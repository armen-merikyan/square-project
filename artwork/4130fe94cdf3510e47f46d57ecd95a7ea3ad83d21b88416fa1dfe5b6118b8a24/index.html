Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between vibrancy and decay, reflecting the duel of 'spark' and 'ash'. The palette merges deep blues and blacks with pale yellows and greens, suggesting a transition from darkness to light, representing both resilience and fragility. The primary contrast runs diagonally, with sparks (brighter colors) clashing against the ashes (darker hues), creating a dynamic tension that evokes memories caught between the past and renewal.
