Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy between confusion and clarity. The chosen palette of navy, black, and dim cyan conveys a lonely yet grounded feeling, while the stark contrast between dark and light creates a rhythm that echoes the central theme of broken symmetry. The arrangement of colors in the 8x8 grid mirrors the idea of nearly perfect reflection disrupted by unexpected color placements, invoking a sense of tension and pulse within the artwork.
