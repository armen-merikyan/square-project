Artist Statement

The emotional tone serves as a juxtaposition between nostalgia and progress. The palette, drawn from 'fear_hope', features dark hues like black and dark violet to embody the weight of nostalgia, contrasted with pale greens and soft gold that suggest the brightness of progress. The stark contrast between these colors creates tension across the split horizontal layout, emphasizing the fight between calm and chaos. Broken pixels create inconsistencies within the systematic grid, illustrating the cracks of memory against the tidy lines of advancement. Overall, this abstract pattern conveys an emotional narrative about the conflict and balance between looking back and moving forward.
