Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The chosen palette of warm coral and cold slate expresses the juxtaposition of warmth and distance. The main collision happens at the center, where the halo pattern of resolve meets the split-screen longing, creating an intersection filled with broken pixels that signify repairs and damage. The negative space ensures quiet amidst the density, capturing the essence of serenity in urgency.
