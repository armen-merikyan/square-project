Artist Statement

This piece embodies an emotional tension between relief and dread through a palette of muted and vibrant colors. The dense pixel arrangement in the top corner represents dread, with a gradual release into pale space that suggests relief. The checker pattern of the dread blends into the staggered stair pattern of relief, creating a visual narrative of tension and release. The broken pixels embody imperfections while emphasizing the layered emotional depth.
