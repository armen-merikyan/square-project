Artist Statement

This 8x8 pixel art composition captures the emotional tension between safety and exposure. The palette integrates soft greens and warm browns, symbolizing comfort, alongside pale and cold colors that evoke vulnerability. The main collision occurs vertically, dividing the piece, reflecting the struggle between shelter and openness. Patterns of 'frame' represent exposure, creating thin structures that guide the eye while 'static' fields convey safety, providing a sense of embrace. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfection, representing vulnerability and the layers of emotional experiences. Overall, the artwork invites contemplation of resilience against fragility, with a clear flow guiding the viewer from warmth to vulnerability.
