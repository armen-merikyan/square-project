Artist Statement

This artwork contrasts restlessness and peace, with a structured diagonal flow inspired by the tension between conflicting emotions. Using a palette of signal green, electric blue, and hot magenta, the heavier presence of dark colors symbolizes the weight of restlessness, while lighter tones represent peace. The main collision occurs in the center, where static gray disrupts the calm palette, echoing the movement and noise of memories. The placement of colors creates a visual rhythm that narrates an ongoing struggle between stability and chaos.
