Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the struggle between clarity and chaos, using colors to symbolize these contrasting emotions. The palette consists of greens and blues representing the 'signal', contrasted against darker tones symbolizing 'noise'. The main collision occurs in the center, where a bright green path disrupts the static gray, while the surrounding borders echo the darker hues, creating a sense of tension and conflict. The overlapping patterns convey a sense of resistance against distortion, embodying the essence of the seed's dualities: exposure and shelter, signal and noise. This creates a vibrant rhythm within an abstract field, suggesting an upward movement from chaos towards clarity, resonating with themes of hope and memory.
