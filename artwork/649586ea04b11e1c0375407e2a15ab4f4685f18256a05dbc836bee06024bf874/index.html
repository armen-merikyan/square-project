Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between confusion and clarity, reflecting the dynamics of inner turmoil against a structured outer frame. The palette combines soft greys and striking reds, representing the warmth of clarity battling the coldness of confusion. The contrast emerges where the saturated red collides with muted tones, creating a visual tension that echoes the tumultuous feelings of the seed. Broken and full pixels form a chaotic yet rhythmic pattern that draws the eye outward, suggesting a struggle between certainty and doubt.
