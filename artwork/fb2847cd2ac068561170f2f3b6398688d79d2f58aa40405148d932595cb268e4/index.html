Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette of deep blacks, vibrant electric blue, and vivid magenta creates a stark contrast that reflects the heaviness of solitude (dark colors) versus the vibrancy of connection (bright colors). The pattern incorporates a diagonal rise to symbolize the struggle between feeling isolated yet yearning for connection. The broken pixels introduce imperfection and disarray, mirroring the complexities of relationships and the tension of hidden emotions. The overall composition maintains a sense of negative space, emphasizing moments of introspection amidst emotional chaos.
