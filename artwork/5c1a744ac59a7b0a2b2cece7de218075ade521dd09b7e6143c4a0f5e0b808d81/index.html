Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional interplay between restlessness and peace. The palette blends calming shades of green and blue with contrasting warm browns and whites, representing peace against the warm vibrancy of motion and chaos. The arrangement creates a central collision between dark and light, with broken pixels adding disorder reminiscent of fleeting memories. This chaotic center intermingles with mirror symmetry at the edges, suggesting the continuous battle between calm structure and disruptive energy. The composition embodies an unresolved argument, where structured patterns collide, reflecting both restful space and encroaching tension.
