Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dialogue between fear and hope. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green represents this duality well: the dark colors embody fear's jagged edges and cold corners, while the soft gold and pale green signify the shimmer of hope and its rising presence. The strong contrast is found in the center, where the gold pixel interacts with the surrounding dark hues, illustrating the struggle and interplay between these emotions. The arrangement forms a scattered, zigzag effect, embodying the disturbance and fluctuation inherent in the themes of rising signals and broken connections. Overall, the pattern evokes feelings of tension and balance, suggesting the precarious nature of our emotional states.
