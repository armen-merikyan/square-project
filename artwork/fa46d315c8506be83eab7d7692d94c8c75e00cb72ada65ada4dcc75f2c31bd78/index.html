Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between longing and resolve through the interplay of warm and cool tones. The palette, featuring stain brown, ash gray, warm ivory, soft gold, and deep wine, suggests a spectrum of emotional states from yearning to safety. The central collision occurs with a series of soft gold 'barcode' patterns intersecting with brown and gray sections that evoke a sense of unresolved tension, reminiscent of repair amidst damage. Layers of warmth against cool quietude create a pulsing beat, reflective of both serenity and urgency shaped by the seed's complexity.
