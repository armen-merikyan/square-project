Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a struggle between stability and chaos, mirrored through the contrasting colors. The palette, 'broken_memory', with warm sepia tones juxtaposed against cool faded blue and dust gray, evokes feelings of nostalgia and instability simultaneously. The central placement creates a feeling of pressure, symbolizing control. The broken pixels introduce an element of unpredictability, reflecting the tension of an inner conflict. The main contrast emerges from the tightly clustered dark tones in the upper half, contrasting with the lighter, fragmented sections below, suggesting a collision of panic and control that resonates within the confines of an empty room.
