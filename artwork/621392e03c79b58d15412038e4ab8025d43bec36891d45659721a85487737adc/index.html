Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through sharp contrasts of light and dark colors. The selected palette, 'rage_calm,' features a mix of deep reds and soft pale blues, signifying a struggle between warm vibrancy and cool detachment. The layout follows a corner invasion, with darker colors emanating from the top-left, creating a heavy atmosphere that meets lighter shades diagonally, allowing for a sense of weight against the ethereal. Broken pixels scattered throughout add a sense of imperfection, further embodying the seed's themes of fragmented memories and connection through carefully placed disruptions in the overall pattern.
