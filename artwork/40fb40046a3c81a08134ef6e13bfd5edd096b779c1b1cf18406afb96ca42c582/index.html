Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a clash between defiance and submission, where bright accents of orange represent boldness scattered amidst cooler tones invoking calmness. The palette merges icy blues with deep blacks, creating a striking contrast that feels alive and dynamic. The main collision occurs at the center, where the patchwork of colors abruptly disrupts the softer pressure band around it, creating a tension that echoes the seed's theme of order vs chaos—and the push and pull between protection and vulnerability. Broken pixels introduce elements of glitch and imperfection, reinforcing the theme of unsteady boundaries. The overall layout is intentionally asymmetric, revealing a controlled chaos that draws the viewer into a deeper emotional narrative.
