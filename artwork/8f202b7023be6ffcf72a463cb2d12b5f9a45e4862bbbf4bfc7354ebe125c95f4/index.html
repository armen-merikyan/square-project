Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a carefully chosen palette of warm yellows and muted purples against deep blues. The central pixel arrangement reflects a protective energy, using brighter squares amidst darker tones, creating a collision of intensity and softness. Here, the broken pixels symbolize moments of exposure, while the structured arrangement denotes strength, exemplifying the push and pull between the two emotions. The overall composition establishes a wave-like flow that conveys movement from outer edges toward the inward sanctuary, suggesting a signal rising through chaos, encapsulating the essence of connection and alienation in emotional landscapes.
