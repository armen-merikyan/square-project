Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between panic and control through a contrasting color palette of warm and cool tones. The composition's zigzag flow reflects a sense of upward struggle, where controlled patterns of deeper tones (representing stability) collide with bursts of vibrant colors (representing chaos). The 'broken edges' add a sense of imperfection, echoing the theme of flawed attempts at control amidst chaos. The central pixels form an abstract arrow, directing energy upward while surrounded by chaotic bursts, illustrating the struggle between order and disorder.
