Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the conflict between quietude and pressure, creating an abstract representation of tension through color and placement. The palette of muted off-white and gray suggests a calm, while compressed reds and dark navy evoke a sense of intensity and depth. The contrast is most evident across the diagonal, where hard edges (dark navy) collide with softer backgrounds (faint peach), symbolizing a push against calmness. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, framing the tension further and enhancing the sense of shattering against the quiet island amidst the chaos.
