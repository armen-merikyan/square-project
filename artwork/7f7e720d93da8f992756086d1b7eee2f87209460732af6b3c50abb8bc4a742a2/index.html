Artist Statement

This composition portrays the tension between confusion and clarity through distinct color placements and patterns. The palette, consisting of sepia, tan, dust_gray, and faded_blue, evokes a sense of nostalgia and complexity, representing the broken memories associated with confusion. The primary contrast occurs between the symmetric and chaotic areas, where clean horizontal lines of clarity disrupt the chaotic dots of confusion. The arrangement embodies the emotional push and pull within the seed, reflecting the struggle for clarity amidst chaotic patterns and the anticipation of resolution through diagonal flow.
