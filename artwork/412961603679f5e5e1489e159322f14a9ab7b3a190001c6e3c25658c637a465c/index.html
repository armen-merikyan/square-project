Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission through stark color contrasts and spatial dynamics. The palette of deep reds and pale blues foregrounds the theme of conflict—the striking red signals defiance, while the quiet blue frames the feeling of submission. The central collision occurs where these colors intermingle, creating a chaotic center surrounded by calmer edges, symbolizing the battle between resisting and yielding. The broken pixel placements echo the imperfection in this struggle, suggesting that even within rigidity, there are disruptions—a metaphor for human emotion.
