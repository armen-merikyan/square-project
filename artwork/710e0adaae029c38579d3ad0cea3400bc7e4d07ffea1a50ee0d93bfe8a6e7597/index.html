Artist Statement

This piece captures the dichotomy of 'spark' and 'ash,' contrasting vitality with remnants. The palette of moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold evokes a resilience against fragility, while the layout invites an open gate of emotional transition. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where sharp, jagged placements of igniting colors meet the subdued tones of ash, creating tension between movement and stagnation. The structured dot pattern clashes with the field-like arrangement of ash, symbolizing memory's echo against erasure.
