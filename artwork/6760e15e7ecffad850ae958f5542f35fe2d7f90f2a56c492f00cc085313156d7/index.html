Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts panic with control, depicted through a split palette of deep navy and more subdued dim cyan. The left side bears a structured crosshatch pattern representing control, while the right side disrupts this with a stair-like pattern symbolizing panic. The perceived weight shifts from the stabilizing navy to the more chaotic tones, suggesting an emotional push from order to disorder. Broken pixels interrupt the patterns, enhancing the feeling of instability amid an overall structured framework, mirroring the struggle between feeling controlled yet overwhelmed.
