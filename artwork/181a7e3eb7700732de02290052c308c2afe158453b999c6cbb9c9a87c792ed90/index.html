Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts panic and control, creating a dynamic between chaos and stability. The chosen 'ancient_fire' palette evokes warmth and tension through burnt orange and dark red, suggesting urgency. The top four rows exhibit a radial pattern symbolizing control with softer gradients, while the bottom four rows reflect panic with erratic spark patterns, emphasizing conflict. The collision occurs where dark nebulous sections interact with bright sparks, conveying tension amidst the swirling chaos. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the theme of instability.
