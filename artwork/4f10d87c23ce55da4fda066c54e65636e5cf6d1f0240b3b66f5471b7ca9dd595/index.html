Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes certainty and curiosity, creating a dynamic tension across the board. The palette includes warm coral for vibrancy, soft amber for comfort, cold slate that represents certainty and restraint, blue-black as the overwhelming force of chaos, and signal cyan as a spark of curiosity. The main collision occurs in the central four squares, where certainty's orderly constellation pattern interrupts the curious stack formation, suggesting a narrative of conflict between desire for structure versus exploration. This interplay creates a visual pulse resonating from the tense edges toward a center that is both inviting yet restrained, embodying a constant push and pull between these emotions.
