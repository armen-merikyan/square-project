Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between calm and pressure, illustrated through a diagonal flow that starts densely and disperses towards the opposite edge. The palette of off-white, dust gray, and compressed red contrasts the heavier dark navy, balancing the soft against the sharp. The patterns of radial pressure and a grid create an interaction where quiet spaces collide with compressed areas, evoking a sense of emotional conflict and release. The broken pixels add intentional imperfection, creating scars in the field of color that suggest moments of disruption amid the quiet.
