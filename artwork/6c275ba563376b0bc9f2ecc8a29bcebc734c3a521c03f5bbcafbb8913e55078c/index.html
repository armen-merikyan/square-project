Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between restlessness and peace, achieved through contrasting patterns and colors. The palette 'melancholy_wonder' offers a soothing yet melancholic feeling, while colors like #575C88 and #BEC3D6 represent restlessness through abrupt cracks in the calm channel structures. The collision occurs centrally with sharp contrasts and interruptions, suggesting an emotional upheaval against a quiet backdrop, which reflects a constant negotiation between chaotic energy and serene spaces. The intentional use of broken pixels creates a delicate imperfection, reinforcing the dynamic interplay of memory and motion.
