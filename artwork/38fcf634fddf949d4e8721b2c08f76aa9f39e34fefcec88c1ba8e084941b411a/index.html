Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the struggle between defiance and submission through an interplay of dense and quiet spaces. The palette combines muted colors with vivid accents to reflect emotional tension. The central collision occurs through the checker pattern of submission (soft collapse) contrasting with the lofty, quiet field of defiance (bright refusal), resulting in a dynamic push-pull sensation. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional imperfections, reflecting the emotional scars from conflicts represented by the seed.
