Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between envy and gratitude, using a dark palette to express weight and tension juxtaposed by hints of gold to represent moments of thankfulness. The placement of black and dark violet dominates, suggesting heaviness, while soft gold introduces lightness to indicate moments of hope. The layout reflects a broken mirror, creating a visual tension that echoes the emotional conflict of rising vs. falling. The alternating edge styles invoke an unsettled, yet rhythmic quality, allowing the contrast of colors to evoke a sense of colliding feelings.
