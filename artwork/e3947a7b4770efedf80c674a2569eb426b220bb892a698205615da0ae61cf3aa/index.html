Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a juxtaposition of intensity and quietude, embodying the push and pull between 'spark' and 'ash.' The chosen palette of 'melancholy wonder' highlights moments of brightness among muted fields, where quick bursts intersect with layered gray tones. The dominant theme emerges in the diagonal collisions, where vibrant colors like soft cyan contrast against lavender gray. This dynamic flow signifies both resilience and fragility, underscoring the ongoing tension inherent in memory and erasure, thus shaping the abstract pattern where pathways are interrupted, suggesting emotional complexity.
