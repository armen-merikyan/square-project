Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between trust and suspicion. The left side, informed by the trust palette of pale mint and soft sky, evokes serenity and warmth, while the right, filled with storm gray and near black, leans into chaotic noise and shadows. The subtle interruptions of lighter colors create a conversation between hope and dread, embodying the split vertical theme of the seed. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, illustrating the fragility within the resilience, emphasizing the push and pull of these emotions in a confined space.
