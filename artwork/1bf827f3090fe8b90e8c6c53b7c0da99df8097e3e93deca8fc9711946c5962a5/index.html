Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The color palette, drawn from muted creams and deep plums, conveys a sense of hunger and restraint. The interplay between the pressure band of darker hues and the quiet field of lighter colors creates a rhythmic ebb and flow across the grid. The center features a concentrated collision of resolve, symbolized by darker colors, surrounded by a soft, longing space utilizing lighter tones, representing the soothing yet unfulfilled desires. The broken pixels introduce imperfections and interruptions, enhancing the sentiment of repair in the midst of damage, contributing to a narrative of healing and hope amidst chaos.
