Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional duality of defiance and submission through contrasting pixel patterns. The palette of muted off-white and dust gray evokes softness, while compressed red and dark navy offer strong visual interruptions, symbolizing defiance. The arrangement of dotted and maze-like patterns suggests a struggle between quiet spaces and bold interruptions, with the center being a strong, upward block of compressed red, illustrating the defiance. The overall density subtly shifts from the periphery to a dynamic core, while the intentional broken pixels create an environmental glitch, enhancing the theme of tension between order and chaos.
