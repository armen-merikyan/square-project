Artist Statement

The emotional tone emphasizes the interplay between chaos and clarity, showcasing a bright signal trying to cut through layers of noise. The selected palette features electric blues and vibrant greens to signify the clarity of signal, layered against deep black and static gray representing the obscuring noise. The center of the composition acts as a collision point, where the clean pulse vibrates, disrupted by random, fragmented static patterns. This setup embodies the struggle between memory and erasure, suggesting both tension and fleeting moments of clarity.
