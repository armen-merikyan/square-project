Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between restlessness and peace, utilizing a palette that combines deep blues for depth and mourning blacks for reflection, while the lighter hues of pale yellow suggest moments of clarity amidst chaos. The conflict is centered along a diagonal axis, where dark tones collide with brighter accents, echoing the push and pull of memory and motion. The open gate layout allows for visual exploration of contrasting feelings and broken pixels add intentional disruptions, representing fragmented thoughts or memories. Overall, it strikes a balance between serene quietness and unsettling movement.
