Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional conflict between relief and dread, utilizing a palette that balances cool tones of pale mint and soft sky against the weighty presence of storm gray and near black. The dense patterns of dots signify the dread, concentrated in the upper left corner, representing an inevitable descent into chaos, while the pulse marks extend into lighter spaces, echoing relief. The main collision occurs along the diagonal from dense to airy, and broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, embodying the struggle for balance between control and chaos.
