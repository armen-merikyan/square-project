Artist Statement

This piece embodies the contrast between rage and calm through a tumultuous center of intense colors framing a serene edge. The palette of deep reds representing anger alongside soft pale blues signifies tranquility, creating a push and pull of emotions. The use of glitch-like patterns colliding with thread-like calm structures visually narrates the theme of overfilled energy meeting quiet containment. The center, vibrant and chaotic, is where tension peaks, while the corners fade into quieter tones, suggesting a journey from chaos to calmness or vice versa.
