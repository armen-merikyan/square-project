Artist Statement

This piece embodies a tension between 'longing' and 'resolve', utilizing a palette of deep blues and pale yellows to evoke feelings of nostalgia against clarity. The color distribution reflects the collision of emotional pressures: dense areas of deep blue represent longing, while the halo of pale yellow suggests moments of resolve. The central cluster of darker tones suggests depth and introspection, while lighter colors radiate outward, creating a distance that emphasizes emotional contrast. The main collision occurs near the center, where dark blue 'mazes' interrupt the pale yellow fields, illustrating the push and pull between desire and acceptance.
