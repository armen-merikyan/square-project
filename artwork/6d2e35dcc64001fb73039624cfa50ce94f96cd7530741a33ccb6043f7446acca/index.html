Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional duality of restlessness and peace through a palette that contrasts dark navy and black with bright dim cyan and white accents. The dark colors represent the weight of restlessness, while the lighter hues suggest fleeting moments of peace. The pattern is a rhythmic braid that intersects with a subtle barcode element, creating tension and fluidity within the grid. The central collision occurs where brighter colors spark against darker areas, echoing a struggle between calm and chaos.
