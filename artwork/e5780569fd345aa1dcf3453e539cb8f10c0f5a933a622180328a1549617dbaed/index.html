Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between envy and gratitude, portrayed through contrasting colors. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green emphasizes a spectrum from chaos (black, dark violet) to hope and lightness (soft gold, pale green). The dense upper area reflects heavy emotion and pressure while the lighter section below provides a release, suggesting a struggle and resolution. The zigzag pattern creates a feeling of movement against static noise, while broken pixels introduce imperfections, evoking the conflict of emotions.
