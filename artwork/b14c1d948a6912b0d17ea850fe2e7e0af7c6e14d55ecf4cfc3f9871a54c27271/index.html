Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between clarity and chaos, illustrating the struggle of a clean signal amid overwhelming noise. The palette features vibrant signal green and electric blue to represent the structured pulse, juxtaposed against the muted tones of static gray and dead black which represent the noise. The central collision occurs from the center dispersing outward, where bright colors intersect with darker fields, creating an unsettling flutter of emotions indicative of memory and erasure. The broken pixels add a layer of imperfection, echoing the theme of disrupted clarity, reinforcing the central idea of fragmented communication in a chaotic environment.
