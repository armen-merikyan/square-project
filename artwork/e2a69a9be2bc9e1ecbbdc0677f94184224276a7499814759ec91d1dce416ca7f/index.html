Artist Statement

The piece explores the tension between confusion and clarity through a fluctuating rhythm of color. The palette, 'lonely_signal', employs deep navy and dim cyan to evoke feelings of depth and introspection, while the black represents weight and noise. The layout reflects fractured symmetry as the outer rows contrast with the central core pattern, invoking a sense of brokenness that clashes with attempts at clarity. In this arrangement, thick outlines resonate with emotional pressure, introducing interruptions amid patterns that oscillate between checker and stair motifs, symbolizing falling pixels. The result resonates with a feeling of balancing chaos against the tendency to find order.
