Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of nostalgia and progress through its contrasting palettes and spatial arrangement. The palette of deep navy, black, and dim cyan communicates the weight of nostalgia, while lighter, fragmented spaces represent the forward thrust of progress. The split horizontal arrangement illustrates the rise and fall inherent in personal and collective narratives. As colors crash and coalesce, they evoke memories of calm amidst the chaos, suggesting an unresolved interplay. The broken pixels add an element of imperfection, enriching the narrative of transition and reflection.
