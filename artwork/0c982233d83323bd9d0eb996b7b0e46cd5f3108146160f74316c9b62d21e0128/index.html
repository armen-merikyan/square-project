Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between loneliness and connection by utilizing a muted color palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple. The arrangement creates an inward flow, contrasting the expansive surrounding with dense clusters of color at the center, suggesting a flame of connection battling against broken memories. Each corner is embedded with deeper hues to represent weight, while bright spots speckled throughout echo lightness. The deliberate placement of negative spaces illustrates the isolation inherent in loneliness, while the busy center nods to the desire for connection, resulting in a visual rhythm that pulses with emotion.
