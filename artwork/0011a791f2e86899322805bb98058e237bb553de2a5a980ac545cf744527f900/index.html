Artist Statement

The artwork captures the essence of 'quiet vs pressure' through a palette of soft and intense colors, emphasizing contrasts between sharp edges and gentle flows. The dense area at the bottom mirrors pressure, while the upper rows fade into a quiet expanse, depicting a release. The interplay of dark navy and faint peach creates a striking tension, resonating with the idea of movement from density to openness. Broken pixels punctuate the structure, symbolizing moments of interruption that echo the conflict of clarity and confusion.
