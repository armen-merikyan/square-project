Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys the tension between panic and control. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold evokes a sense of raw intensity and depth. Control manifests as a structured grid pattern, while panic appears as disordered stripes bleeding outward. The center-heavy layout contrasts the organized control with chaotic elements at the edges, creating a vivid collision that reflects the seed's themes of emotion and movement, with a focus on visual disruption and harmonic dissonance.
