Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes defiance and submission through contrasting color choices and patterns. The palette of shelter green and warm brown evokes a sense of safety paired with tension, while pale skin and cool white suggest vulnerability and openness. The center features a pulse mark pattern, representing defiance, amidst quieter, broken frame elements of submission that create tension around the edges. The uneven border enhances the feeling of interruption, mirroring the seed's theme of chaos versus order.
