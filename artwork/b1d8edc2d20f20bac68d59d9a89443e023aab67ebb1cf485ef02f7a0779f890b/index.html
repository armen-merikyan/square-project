Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between tenderness and rage through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette blends sharp reds representing rage with muted blues to symbolize tenderness, creating a visual tension that captures the essence of the seed. The clash happens centrally, where fierce dots collide with gentle glitch patterns, representing the turmoil of emotions battling for expression. Broken pixels disrupt this interaction, symbolizing the imperfect nature of emotional experiences, resulting in a dynamic yet cohesive composition.
