Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between contrasting qualities, where dark undertones represent weight and pressure, battling against fragments of light that signal release and disruption. The palette of navy and black conveys depth and introspection, while the hints of dim cyan and white introduce moments of clarity and hope. The arrangement emphasizes a quiet structure where the bright spots intermittently clash with the darker surroundings, suggesting a discomfort that seeks to resolve but remains unresolved. Patterns of 'crack' and 'maze' reflect the complexity of emotional experiences, subtly weaving through the grid layout.
