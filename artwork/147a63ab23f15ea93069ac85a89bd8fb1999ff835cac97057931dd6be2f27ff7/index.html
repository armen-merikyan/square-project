Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between defiance and submission, illustrated through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of soft off-white and faint peach represent submission, while deeper hues like dark navy and compressed red symbolize defiance. The center is busy and dense, representing defiance, while the edges fade into quiet gray tones, showcasing submission traits. The collision is in the center where strong angular patterns clash with softer shapes, creating an overwhelming tension that echoes the seed's themes of conflict and balance.
