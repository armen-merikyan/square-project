Artist Statement

The emotional tone grapples with the tension between trust and suspicion, using contrasting colors for a dynamic visual narrative. The palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple conveys warmth versus coolness, suggesting the interplay of light and shadow in interpersonal relationships. The center of the square is dense with warm tones, representing trust, while the border is filled with cooler hues, evoking suspicion. The design incorporates broken pixels as subtle disruptions that reflect fragility and resilience, enhancing the sense of unease against a soft glow, with a diagonal fall pattern emphasizing movement and progression. This square ultimately narrates a story of conflicting emotions and the journey of navigating trust amidst uncertainty.
