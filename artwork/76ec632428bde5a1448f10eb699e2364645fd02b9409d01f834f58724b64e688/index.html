Artist Statement

This abstract piece captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette features warm browns and cool greens, suggesting a balance of comfort and discomfort. The overfilled corner creates a sense of pressure and urgency, contrasting with the quiet fields that symbolize serenity. The collision occurs mainly at the central axis where dense clusters of scar patterns meet the braided shapes of resolve, evoking a narrative of tension and release, perfectly encapsulating the seed's themes.
