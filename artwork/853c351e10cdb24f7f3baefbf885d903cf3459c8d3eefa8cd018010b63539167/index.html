Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork captures the tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette employs muted greens and browns to evoke a sense of shelter contrasted against the starkness of cold whites and open blues that suggest exposure. The central clash occurs where the warm, earthy tones meet the bright, cold accents, symbolizing the struggle between the safety of shelter and the risk of exposure. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, adding a layer of imperfection and complexity that reflects the emotion of connection versus alienation, while maintaining quiet negative space for reflection.
