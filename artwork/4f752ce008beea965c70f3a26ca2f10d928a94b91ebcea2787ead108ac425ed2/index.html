Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette of navy and dim cyan evokes a sense of solitude against a backdrop of chaos. The main contrast occurs at the center with dense dark shades, representing resolve, colliding with lighter tones emerging towards the edges, embodying longing. The layout forms a spiral pattern, drawing the eye inward while maintaining a 'halo border' of empty space, giving a sense of peaceful isolation amidst pressure.
