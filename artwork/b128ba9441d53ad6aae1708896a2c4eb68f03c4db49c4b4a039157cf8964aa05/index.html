Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents the delicate interplay between vulnerability and strength. The selected palette of yellow and muted purple suggests a blend of warmth and calmness against the depth of a deep blue, reflective of tension. The collision occurs centrally, showcasing a soft break of muted colors against vibrant yellow, creating a visual echo of the struggle between opposing forces. This pattern expresses the fragility of safety (muted purples) interrupted by the intensity of exposure (yellows and blues), capturing the essence of the seed.
