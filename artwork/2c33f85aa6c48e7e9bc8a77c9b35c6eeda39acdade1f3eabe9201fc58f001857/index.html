Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dichotomy of loneliness and connection through stark contrasts and an asymmetric pattern. The palette reflects muted tones of sepia and gray, evoking a sense of nostalgia and solitude. The central burst of color symbolizes a moment of potential connection amidst the surrounding emptiness, while the top-weighted layout accentuates a feeling of heaviness and pressure. The broken dots throughout suggest fragmentation, capturing the hidden battles between exposure and concealment, ultimately painting a story of yearning and disruption.
