Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between longing and resolve through its contrasting color palette of deep, dark hues and warm, muted tones. The placement of colors creates a diagonal flow, encapsulating the notion of conflict and connection in an abstract way. The primary collision occurs in the central area, where the intense colors overlap, representing the struggle between emotional states. The presence of broken pixels introduces a sense of imperfection, reflecting on the unfulfilled desires and solid resolutions that define the human experience.
