Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between defiance and submission, captured in contrasting colors that shift from warm burnt orange representing defiance to cooler dark reds symbolizing submission. The layout emphasizes the outer edges as a strong boundary, contrasting with a dense and chaotic inner section, creating a collision of feelings. Broken pixels are used to suggest a sense of imperfection and tension, resonating with the idea of love betrayal. The choice of palette reflects the heat of ancient fire, symbolizing passion and struggle, creating an echo of motion from left to right.
