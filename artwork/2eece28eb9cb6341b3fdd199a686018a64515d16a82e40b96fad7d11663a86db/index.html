Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional nuances of pressure and release through a balance of dark, muted tones contrasted sharply with bright accents. The palette invokes a rhythm where cool colors represent a calm, reflective state (blues and teals), while warm accents (oranges and yellows) emerge sporadically to suggest moments of tension and collision. The placement creates a flow between quiet space and dense clusters, echoing the theme of emotional waves that rise and fall, reflecting the pulse of reality amidst turbulence.
