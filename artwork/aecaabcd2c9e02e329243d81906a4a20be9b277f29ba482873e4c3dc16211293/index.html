Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the delicate balance between safety and exposure. The chosen palette of 'shelter_green,' 'warm_brown,' 'pale_skin,' 'cold_white,' and 'open_blue' conveys warmth and vulnerability, establishing a contrast where softer shades suggest safety while harsher colors represent exposure. The main collision occurs towards the top left corner, where the pressure of darker, rich colors clashes with the lighter, more delicate tones, creating a point of tension. This pattern evokes a sense of conflict and resolution between contrasting emotions, guided by the seed's themes of protection versus vulnerability.
