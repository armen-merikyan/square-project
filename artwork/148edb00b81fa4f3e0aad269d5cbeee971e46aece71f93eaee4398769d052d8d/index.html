Artist Statement

This composition translates the duality of defiance and submission into a rhythmic flow from left to right, using the darkness of submission against the lighter hues representing defiance. The palette of black and dark violet resonates with the struggle and pressure of emotions, while soft gold and pale green create moments of hope and lightness. The contrast emerges primarily from the interplay between darker tones on the left and lighter tones on the right, suggesting a subtle battle and tension. The 'stair_step' pattern imbues an organic rhythm with a structured grid, mimicking the push and pull of the emotional weights. Broken pixels disrupt this flow, embodying moments of imperfection reflective of life's unexpected interruptions.
