Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional interplay of loneliness and connection, where dense clusters of dark colors symbolize the pressure of emotional tension while soft contrasts of lighter hues provide moments of quiet. The palette, drawn from warm reds and cool soft blues, emphasizes the struggle between calm and chaos. The arrangement creates a central void surrounded by pulsating patterns of color that evoke feelings of flow and disruption, capturing the essence of 'collisions of emotion'.
