Artist Statement

This artwork represents an emotional tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting patterns of color and texture. The muted palette reflects underlying tensions, evoking a sense of unease and complexity. The split vertical layout portrays a conflict where soft checker patterns symbolizing trust collide with harsh noise patterns depicting suspicion. The darker colors dominate the grid, accentuating the pressure, while lighter tones provide brief moments of relief, echoing the thresholds of emotional boundaries. Broken pixels create intentional disruptions, emblematic of the fragility inherent in relationships.
