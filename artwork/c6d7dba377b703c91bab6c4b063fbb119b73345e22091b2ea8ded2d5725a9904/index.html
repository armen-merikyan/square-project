Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise). The emotional palette branches from static gray to vibrant signal green and electric blue, allowing for stark contrasts. The grid-like structure simulates the pulse of signals interrupted by waves of color representing noise. The collision occurs centrally, providing a focal point amidst a chaotic background, with broken pixels introducing disruptions that mirror memories and echoes.
