Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of control and surrender, using a palette that balances warm shadows against cool blues to evoke a sense of intimacy amid distance. The arrangement features a densely packed center surrounded by softer fields of color, illustrating the struggle between pressure and release. The collision occurs dynamically in the center, creating a visual echo of overflowing control against yielding spaces, inviting contemplation of a quiet surrender within a structured form.
