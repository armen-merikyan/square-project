Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the conflict between quietude and overwhelming pressure, creating a dynamic interplay. The palette of off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach reflects a balance of soft and hard edges. The main collision occurs in the central rows where darker colors dominate, illustrating a buildup of pressure, while softer hues expand towards the corners, suggesting calmness. This reflects the seed's essence of divergence and resolution, encapsulating both intensity and tranquility.
