Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the artwork reflects a struggle between awe and terror, embodying the tension of contrasting feelings. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes feelings of depth and glimmering hope amidst a turbulent backdrop. The layout maintains a mirror-like quality, suggesting symmetry disrupted by imperfect 'broken pixels' that symbolize the flaws in intensity and emotion. The main contrast occurs diagonally, emphasizing the pull between dense clusters of dark shades and softer highlights, creating an echoing rhythm throughout the grid. Ultimately, the seed encouraged the use of a diamond structure, translating into a pattern where tension is felt through color collision and dynamic movement beneath the surface, resonating the tensions and releases inherent in emotional landscapes.
