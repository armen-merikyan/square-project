Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between signal and noise, capturing the essence of clarity battling against distortion. The selected color palette, featuring a bright green for signal and muted grays and blacks for noise, creates a vibrant clash that represents the struggle of a single channel illuminating the chaotic backdrop. The grid layout allows for a structured rhythm, and the intentional placement of broken pixels disrupts the flow, echoing the sense of interference intrinsic to the theme. The main contrast occurs in the center, where clarity attempts to dominate amidst the surrounding static.
