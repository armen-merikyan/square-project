Artist Statement

This composition evokes the tension between awe and terror through a palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon, contrasting soft edges with hard geometric patterns. The broken dots symbolize chaotic memory, creating a wave of emotional fluctuation. The light and dark colors interplay in an off-center layout, hinting at the constant battle between stability and instability.
