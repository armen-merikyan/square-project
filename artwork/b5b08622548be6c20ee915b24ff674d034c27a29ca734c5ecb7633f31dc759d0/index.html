Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between rage and tenderness, employing a palette of black, red, pale blue, and white to express that dynamic. The contrast is represented through a narrow negative-space channel cutting through dense patterns, emphasizing the collision of sharp, jagged edges of red against soft, muted areas of pale blue, instilling a sense of disturbance. The seed's themes suggest internal conflicts and vast emotional landscapes, driving the arrangement of colors to echo these struggles and release.
