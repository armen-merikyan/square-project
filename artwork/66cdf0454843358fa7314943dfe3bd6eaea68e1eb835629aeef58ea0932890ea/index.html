Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between quiet and pressure, with a distinct palette of soft and muted against deeper, darker tones. The dense accumulation of colors at the top-left corner symbolically represents pressure, while the fading colors towards the bottom-right emanate a sense of release and calmness. The pattern features a veil-like texture that collides softly with a lattice structure, creating a visual representation of disturbance and resolution in opposition. Broken pixels add an element of imperfection, echoing the theme of shattering amid the smooth rhythmic flow of color progression.
