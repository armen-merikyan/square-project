Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between warmth and cold detachment, representing love and betrayal. The chosen palette of deep reds and soft grays creates a resonant tension; darker hues signify betrayal while warmer tones represent love. The main contrast emerges between the bright diagonal rise connecting the top half and the darker, corrupted symmetry below, reinforcing the emotional pull. This arrangement of maze and spark patterns along with soft edges manifests a split yet interconnected narrative of trust and heartbreak, echoing the seed's complex emotional landscape.
