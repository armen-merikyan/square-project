Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the struggle between defiance and submission. The chosen colors from the 'gratitude_envy' palette invoke a sense of tension—dark, grounding greens juxtaposed with bright, energetic honey gold to emphasize the conflict. The central area features a dense cluster of cream and honey gold, suggesting a focal tension that clashes against the quiet, deep olive and darker edges, illustrating a battle between rigidity and fluidity. The uneven borders add a jagged edge to the piece, further indicating disruption and conflict in the emotional landscape.
