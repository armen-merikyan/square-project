Artist Statement

The composition reflects the contrasting emotions of loneliness and connection through an interplay of colors and spatial arrangement. The chosen palette of warm pinks and deep maroons symbolizes passion and solitude, with gray introducing a sense of void. The top-weighted arrangement emphasizes a solitary element at the center, evoking feelings of isolation surrounded by the noise of vibrant colors, suggesting a burst of incongruity. The grid pattern forms a stark contrast against the warm bursts, amplifying the feeling of disconnection while hinting at the potential for connection within the chaotic backdrop. The negative space intentionally left around the bursts further accentuates the emotional void associated with loneliness, inviting contemplation and reflection on the dichotomy of human experience.
