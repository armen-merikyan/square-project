Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between desire and emptiness through a carefully chosen palette that creates a sense of nostalgia. The contrasting colors, from sepia to faded blue, echo the struggle between fullness and void. The pattern of broken dots interwoven with framed areas reflects the conflict of control and chaos, where the central region provides a focal point amidst an overarching sense of turmoil. The hard edges emphasize the sharpness of these emotional collisions, inviting the viewer to experience the unresolved dynamics of pressure and release, while the broken pixels introduce imperfection, mirroring the reality of emotion.
