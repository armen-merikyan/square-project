Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances warmth and vulnerability against cold disillusionment, symbolized through the palette of pinks and dark maroon. The interplay between the maze-like love pattern and the framing of betrayal generates a tension that reflects the internal conflict of the seed. Key contrasts occur in the central grid: warm pinks collide with deeper maroons, while edges are softened to hint at fragility. Broken pixels add imperfection, emphasizing the 'inside vs outside' theme of emotional turbulence, where negative space is strategically used to convey solitude.
