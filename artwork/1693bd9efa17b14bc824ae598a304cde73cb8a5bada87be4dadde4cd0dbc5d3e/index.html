Artist Statement

This abstract artwork explores the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The chosen palette of ripe green, deep olive, and honey gold creates a warm gradation that suggests both the yearning of longing and the firmness of resolve. Densely packed rows embody a spectrum of emotional weight, punctuated by quiet cream fields that allow the viewer to breathe amidst the tension. The central collision between layered greens and sharp honey accents suggests a pulse of urgency against a backdrop of calm, representing the interplay between the two emotions. The arrangement of broken pixels adds intentional imperfections, reflecting damage while hinting at repair, aligning with the seed's narrative of repair versus damage. The overall structure spirals inward, emphasizing a gradual convergence toward resolution, while contrasting edges maintain the theme of stability within chaos, embodying the abstract qualities of the seed without literal representation.
