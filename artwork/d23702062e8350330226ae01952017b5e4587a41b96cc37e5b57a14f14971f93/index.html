Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between relief and dread, employing a contrast of warm yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples. This composition utilizes a broken edges style to suggest imperfection and disturbance, particularly evident in the clash of colors where lighter hues meet darker shades. The patterns of crosshatch and stairs are arranged asymmetrically to emphasize the weight of dread against the lightness of relief, allowing the viewer to experience a visual tension that evokes hope amidst unease.
