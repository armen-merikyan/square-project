Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrast of vulnerability and strength. The palette of deep navy and black represents strength, while the pale dim cyan symbolizes vulnerability and exposure. The composition features a strong central collision, with dark colors framing lighter hues, suggesting a protective boundary around the exposed core. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, enhancing the theme of vulnerability amidst assertive strength. The interaction of the mirror and channels patterns allows a visual dialogue between opposing emotions.
