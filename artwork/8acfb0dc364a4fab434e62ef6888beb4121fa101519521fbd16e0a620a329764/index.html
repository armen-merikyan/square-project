Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional contrasts of tenderness and rage through the interplay of color and structure. The palette of deep red, soft pale blue, stark black, and clean white reflects the struggle between explosive and gentle feelings. The contrast is centered, presenting a collision of jagged, rain-like patterns representing rage amidst softer, fault-line textures symbolizing tenderness. The choice of colors and their placement create a narrative where tension disrupts calmness yet allows for healing and reflection. The broken pixels introduce an element of chaos, illustrating the scars of emotion. Overall, the square speaks to an ongoing negotiation between inner turmoil and a quest for serenity.
