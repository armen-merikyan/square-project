Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dichotomy of joy and grief, with a palette that captures warmth and coolness. The colors of yellow and orange suggest warmth and joy, while deep blue and muted purple embody the heaviness of grief. The stark contrast between vibrant and muted tones creates a tension that resonates with the themes of rising signals and checkered patterns, evoking a feeling of conflict amidst harmony. The layout is a grid that portrays two battling emotional patterns; circles of joy fighting against the radial heaviness of grief. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, mirroring the unevenness of emotional experiences while leaving space for reflection.
