Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between nostalgia and progress, capturing a moment of conflict and balance. The chosen palette of deep reds and soft blues contrasts well, with black providing grounding and white adding clarity. The top half utilizes a calm, orderly blue grid, suggesting nostalgia, while the bottom intentionally disrupts this with chaotic red and black elements, representing progress amidst a struggle. A dip in color density at the center mimics a drip flow, embodying the tension of rising vs. falling emotions and leaving intentional negative space for breathing room amid the turmoil.
