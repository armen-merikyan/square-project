Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of love and betrayal, using a palette that shifts from warm pinks and reds to the cooler shades of gray and dark maroon. The outer borders employ a soft, broken edge style to symbolize betrayal, while the inner square uses contrast and density to represent love's warmth. The main collision is seen in the sharp transitions between the soft edges and the dense patterns, illustrating the tension between connection and rupture. This abstract arrangement draws from the seed by encapsulating the fragmentation and chaos arising from conflicting emotions.
