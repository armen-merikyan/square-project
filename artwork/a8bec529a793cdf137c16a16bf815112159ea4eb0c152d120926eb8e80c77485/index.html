Artist Statement

This artwork expresses the tension between longing and resolve, using the palette to evoke a sense of safety (shelter_green, warm_brown) amid contrasting exposure (cold_white, open_blue). The main collision occurs in the central area, where warmer tones meet cooler edges, suggesting a dynamic push and pull representative of emotional conflict. The pattern of falling pixels creates a flow from comfort to unease, while broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, echoing the themes of repair and damage.
