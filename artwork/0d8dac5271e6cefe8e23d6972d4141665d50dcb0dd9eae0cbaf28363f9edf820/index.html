Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between love and betrayal. The palette of warm pinks and deep maroons captures the essence of love, while the cool gray represents the coldness of betrayal. The top four rows offer a vibrant target pattern, signifying love amid chaos, while the bottom four shift to a crosshatch, representing confusion and fracture. The use of negative space introduces calm amidst the dense structures. The diagonal transitions across each row evoke a rising signal and falling dynamics, creating a narrative of emotional interplay.
