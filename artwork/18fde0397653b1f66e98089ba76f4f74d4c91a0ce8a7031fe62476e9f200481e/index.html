Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers around the duality of safety and exposure, represented through a palette of muted greens and warm browns that suggest comfort, juxtaposed with a stark pale and cool blue for vulnerability. The layout employs mostly empty space, with a collision occurring in the center, where dense textures of the safety pattern overlap with the scar-like marks representing exposure. The distribution of colors creates a tension between warmth and coldness, embodying the duality and complexity of the themes from the seed.
