Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between restlessness and peace. The warm colors represent restless energy, breaking boundaries and encroaching into cooler spaces of tranquility. The palette, derived from 'broken_memory,' combines sepia and faded blue to evoke nostalgia and emotional depth. The edge style expresses receding edges, enabling a sense of motion towards calm. The main contrast, found primarily in the top half, creates a collision that signifies emotional turmoil as it flows downward like falling pixels, hinting at memories descending into clarity.
