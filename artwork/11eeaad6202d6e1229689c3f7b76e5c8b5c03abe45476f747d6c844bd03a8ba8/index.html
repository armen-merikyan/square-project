Artist Statement

This composition reflects the duality of joy and grief through contrasting color placements and patterns. The warm yellows and oranges symbolize joy, while the deep blues and muted purples represent grief. The layout ascends from bottom to top, suggesting a rise in hope layered with the weight of sorrow. Hard edges create a sense of tension, accentuating the conflict between safety and threat. The interplay of light and dark spaces echoes the emotional struggle.
