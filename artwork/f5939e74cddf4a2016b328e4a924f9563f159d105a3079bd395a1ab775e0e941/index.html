Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a tension between softness and severity, illustrated through a palette that balances warm white accents with grounding black and gray tones. The main collision occurs centrally with a dense area of sharp crimson pixels embedded in a halo of softer colors, creating a pulse against a backdrop of static noise. The selection of colors and patterns suggests a journey from calm to intensity, while broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, reminiscent of echoes within the chaos. This arrangement evokes a visceral experience of negotiating guilt and forgiveness against a backdrop of unresolved tension.
