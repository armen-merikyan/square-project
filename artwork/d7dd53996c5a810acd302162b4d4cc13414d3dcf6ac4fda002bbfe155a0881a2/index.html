Artist Statement

The artwork conveys an emotional struggle between restlessness and peace, using a palette of muted cream representing calm and dark hues invoking a sense of lingering turbulence. The arrangement features a grid pattern on one side to signify peace confronting swirling rings that disrupt this order, illustrating the push and pull of memory and motion. The main contrast lies in the collision of muted cream against dark reds and blacks, reflecting the chaotic yet comforting space where emotions conflict. The seed’s themes of interruption and echo are represented as edges fade into the background and color selections juxtapose to build tension.
