Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the interplay between defiance and submission. The palette, drawn from melancholy hues, reflects a push-pull dynamic, where deeper colors represent resistance and lighter shades illustrate yielding. The main contrast arises from dark versus light, creating a channel of quiet disruption amid dense layering, suggesting movement and tension while maintaining a sense of unresolved space. The 'tiny_plus' shape informs the pixel arrangement, where stark breaks and a diagonal rise punctuate the rigid structure, embodying the theme of tension between solidity and fragility.
