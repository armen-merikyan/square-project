Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the vulnerability of exposure with the warmth of safety. The left side features a glitch pattern in cool hues of pale skin and cold white, suggesting fragility and instability. The right side, rendered in warm brown and shelter green stripes, conveys comfort and protection. The main collision occurs at the central line, where the cool colors of exposure hint at an impending disturbance, creating a sharp transition. This duality encapsulates the essence of the seed, resulting in a haunting yet comforting abstract narrative reflecting the interplay of risk and security.
