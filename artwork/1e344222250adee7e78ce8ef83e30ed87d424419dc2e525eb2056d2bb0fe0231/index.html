Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the clash between light (relief) and dark (dread), with the palette favoring soft, pale mint and faded gold against the storm gray and near black. This contrast serves to visualize the pressure exerted by the dark mass in one corner, creating a sense of impending tension that fights against the releasing light throughout the rest of the square, represented by the grid. The patterns echo the seed's themes of chaos (spark) and order (grid), colliding at the center and spilling into quieter zones, providing an abstract narrative of struggle and release.
