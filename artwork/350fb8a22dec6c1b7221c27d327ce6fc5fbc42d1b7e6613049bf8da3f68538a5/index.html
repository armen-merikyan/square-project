Artist Statement

This artwork represents the emotional interplay between pressure and quiet. The palette blends muted tones of off-white and dust gray with bursts of compressed red, evoking a sense of tension between chaos and calm. The distribution generates a high-density region of dark navy and red at the lower left, colliding with the quiet field of faint peach and soft grays, creating an off-balance yet intentional asymmetry. The use of a stepwell pattern for the quiet spaces contrasts sharply with the veil-like pressure of the saturated colors, reflecting the core duality of clarity amidst confusion. The arrangement suggests movement from tightness to looseness across the grid, embodying a visual argument where edges clash softly and sharply, much like life’s unpredictable flow.
