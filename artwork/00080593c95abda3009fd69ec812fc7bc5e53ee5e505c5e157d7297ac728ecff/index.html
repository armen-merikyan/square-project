Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents the struggle between desire and restraint, with a focus on saturation and containment. The palette combines deep, saturated colors like red_oxide and blackened_plum, contrasted with muted cream and iron gray to evoke a tapestry of both longing and limitations. The center is dense with a mirror pattern symbolizing desire, colliding with a lattice structure reflecting restraint, creating a visual tension. Negative space flows through, illustrating a quiet channel that disrupts the pressure around it, reinforcing the theme of sealed edges and locked boundaries.
