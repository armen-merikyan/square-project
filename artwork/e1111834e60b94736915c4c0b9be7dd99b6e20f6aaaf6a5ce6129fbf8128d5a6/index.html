Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a balance of vulnerability and strength through contrasting dense, dark hues against lighter, muted shades. The selected palette of red oxide, blackened plum, muted cream, iron gray, and small orange symbolizes hunger and restraint, representing the weight of strength and lightness of vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center where dense colors clash with a thin, pale hue, suggesting tension and protection. The layout emphasizes an overfilled corner that sublimates into quieter spaces, embodying the idea of pressure and relief, while the application of broken pixels introduces imperfection, hinting at scars from vulnerability.
