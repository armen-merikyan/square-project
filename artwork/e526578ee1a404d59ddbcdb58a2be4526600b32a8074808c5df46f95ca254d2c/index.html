Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the duality of trust and suspicion, represented through contrasting patterns of radial and thread designs. The palette evokes a sense of awe and terror, highlighting a deep emotional landscape. The main contrast occurs in the center, where deeper tones of void black and deep teal collide with the soft glow of star white, embodying uncertainty. Layers create a sense of pressure while leaving spaces in between to maintain balance, reminiscent of resilience against fragility.
