Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between control and surrender, using a palette that juxtaposes warm earthy tones with cooler shades. The central collision occurs in the fourth row, where deep, grounding colors meet softer, more yielding hues. The chosen colors symbolize shelter and exposure, echoing the emotional turmoil of overflow versus containment. The pattern's dense center suggests pressure while the quiet edges stretch towards release, creating a dynamic visual rhythm reflective of the seed's themes.
