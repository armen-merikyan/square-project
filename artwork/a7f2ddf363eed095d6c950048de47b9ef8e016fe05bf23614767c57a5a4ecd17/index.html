Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between softness and severity. The palette features muted powder pink and warm white as a representation of softness, against the starkness of carbon black and steel gray that evoke severity. The pattern oscillates between radial structures that suggest a pulse and stair-like formations symbolizing gentle contacts. The center features a collision of dark and light to visualize the concept of guilt versus forgiveness, with broken pixels adding intentional disruptions that echo the complexity of this emotional landscape. The layout creates a sense of spaciousness, hinting at potential and unresolved feelings, while maintaining clarity and readability within the 8x8 grid.
