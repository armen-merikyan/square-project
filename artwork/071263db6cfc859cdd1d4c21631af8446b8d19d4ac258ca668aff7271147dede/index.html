Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between control and surrender. The left half features warmer colors like alarming reds and pale aquas, suggesting an aggressive overflow of emotions. The right side gives way to cooler mist grays and ink blues, subtly containing the chaos while still allowing some heart-stirring presence. The central clash occurs at the boundary between the two emotional spaces, creating a pulse of movement and interruption where the flame pattern of control meets the softer wave of surrender. This dynamic creates a sense of orchestrated rhythm amidst underlying chaos, capturing the essence of the seed's narrative.
