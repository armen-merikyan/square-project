Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the push and pull between longing and resolve, expressing a delicate balance between hope and bondage. The palette of lime, black, electric blue, and magenta invokes a sense of vibrancy and tension—the lime and magenta represent the urgent flashes of desire, while the black grounds the composition, representing introspection and resolve. The contrast focuses on a central collision of vibrant colors at the center, mimicking a pulse that escapes toward serenity on the edges. The barcode and thread patterns create visual rhythms of connection and separation, transforming the wave into an abstract journey through desire and stability.
