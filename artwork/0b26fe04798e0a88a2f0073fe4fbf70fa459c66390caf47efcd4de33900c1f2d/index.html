Artist Statement

The emotional tone is rooted in the conflict between love and betrayal, reflected in the use of warm reds and cool grays. The palette creates a tension, where pinks symbolize love’s softness, while dark maroon and grays suggest underlying fractures. The left columns utilize a softer split-screen pattern to represent love, while the right incorporates a ring pattern signifying the disruption of betrayal. The main contrast lies in the transition between the warm and cool zones, interrupted by broken pixels that create a visual dissonance, embodying the seed's themes of tension and emotional noise.
