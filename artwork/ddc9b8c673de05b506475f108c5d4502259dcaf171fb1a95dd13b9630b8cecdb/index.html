Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between desire and emptiness. The palette blends deep blacks and cool blues with punctuations of white, reflecting the clash of warmth against the starkness of absence. The primary contrast occurs along a diagonal where dense, dark colors collide with lighter hues, creating an abrupt yet rhythmic flow from the bottom-left to the top-right, mirroring a rise of hopeful longing. Textured patterns of cracks and sparks suggest fragility and light, embodying the emotions derived from the seed, while broken pixels introduce imperfection, highlighting the chaos within structured desires. The layout adheres to the central theme of rising signals, creating a pulsating movement that suggests an ascent despite the surrounding emptiness.
