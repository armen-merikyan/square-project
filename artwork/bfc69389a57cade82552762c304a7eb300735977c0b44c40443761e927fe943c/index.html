Artist Statement

The emotional tone of longing versus resolve is conveyed through a warm and cool color contrast that embodies tension and balance. The palette combines deep wine and warm ivory to represent warmth and desire amidst ash gray and stain brown for the resistive nature. The collision occurs at the center, where pulse patterns clash into a structured grid, symbolizing the push-pull of emotions. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, suggesting scars from past experiences while maintaining a space of clarity and quietness.
