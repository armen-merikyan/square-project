Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the duality of defiance and submission through contrasting color arrangements, with deeper blues and muted grays asserting strength and resilience while softer shades evoke surrender. The emotional collision occurs centrally where the angular resistances intersect with softer gradients, mimicking an inward spiral that suggests both pressure and release, echoing the complexities of struggle and acceptance. The chosen palette reflects a melancholic wonder, allowing quiet fields to juxtapose against sharper clusters, creating visual tension that is dynamic yet unresolved.
