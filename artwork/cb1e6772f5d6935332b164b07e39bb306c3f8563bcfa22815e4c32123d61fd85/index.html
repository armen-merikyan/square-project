Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional oscillation between panic and control through contrasting patterns and colors. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue reflects a nostalgic yet chaotic sentiment, with muted tones grounding the viewer while flashes of brighter color create moments of tension. The collision appears in the checker and ring patterns; the rings emanate from a central void, suggesting stability disrupted by jagged checker patterns, echoing the struggle between randomness and order. The arrangement pushes flow from bottom to top, instilling a sense of rising turmoil as the composition unfolds.
