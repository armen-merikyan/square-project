Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of restlessness and peace. The top half features a veil-like pattern of soft greens and blues, representing tranquility and space. In contrast, the bottom half uses uneven placements of dark browns and warm reds, echoing agitation and fragmented thoughts. The deliberate diagonal edge creates tension, signaling the conflict between chaotic motion and serene stillness. Broken pixels act as disruptions, enhancing the narrative of unresolved emotions and creating an abstract story of memory and movement.
