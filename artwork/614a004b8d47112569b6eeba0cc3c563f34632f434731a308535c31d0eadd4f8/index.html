Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and chaos, mirrored in the color choices. Darker tones like dead black and static gray denote noise, while brighter signal green and electric blue represent attempts at clarity. The main contrast occurs along the center, where vibrant colors collide with darker hues, showcasing the pulse trying to emerge from noise. This arrangement conveys tension and resolution, echoing the seed’s themes of interruption and persistence.
