Artist Statement

This artwork represents the interplay between tension and tranquility through the use of contrasting colors and densities. The palette, featuring dark navy and compressed red, symbolizes the weight of pressure, while the off-white and faint peach hues convey moments of quiet space. The central collision occurs in the middle rows, where dense clusters of darker colors confront surrounding lighter fields, creating an emotional push and pull as envisioned in the seed's themes of resistance and conflict. The rising signal flow elevates the experience, reflecting a journey from heaviness to lightness.
