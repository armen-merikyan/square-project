Artist Statement

This composition juxtaposes the heaviness of dread against the lightness of relief. The color palette of storm gray and near black dominates the center, representing dread, while soft sky and faded gold emerge from the edges, suggesting relief. The main collision occurs at the center, where the dark shades crowd the lighter tones, creating a visual struggle. Broken pixels throughout symbolize imperfections, enhancing the narrative of disarray. Overall, this piece embodies the push and pull between tension and release, with color choices reflecting emotional depth.
