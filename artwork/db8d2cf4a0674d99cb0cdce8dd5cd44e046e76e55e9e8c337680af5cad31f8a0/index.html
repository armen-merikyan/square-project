Artist Statement

This piece conveys the emotional tension between restlessness and peace as it explores the contrasts of structured calm and chaotic motion. The palette of soft powder pink and warm white juxtaposed against sharp crimson and carbon black illustrates both serenity and disturbance. The primary collision occurs in the diagonal bands where the warm colors intersect with cooler, darker tones, suggesting a conflict that is unresolved yet dynamic. The patterned arrangement symbolizes memory versus motion, representing echoing thoughts against a backdrop of stillness. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, reflecting the theme of memory's imperfections, while the overall layout suggests shifting densities across emotional strata, enhancing the narrative of tension and release.
