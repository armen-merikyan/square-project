Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a struggle between defiance and submission, reflected by the jarring contrast of saturated vs. muted colors. The palette combines warm hues like hot coral and blood orange to evoke the heat of conflict, balanced by the coolness of charcoal and hollow blue representing calm acceptance. The collision occurs most dramatically in the center, where bold colors attempt to overwhelm quieter fields, while the edges fade into subtlety, illustrating the tension between order and chaos. The diagonal arrangement of the colors signifies a rise, hinting at progress amidst struggle, embodying the essence of the seed as an abstract narrative.
