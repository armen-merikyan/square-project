Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes vulnerability and strength, represented by the contrasting color palette of deep olive and honey gold against pale cream and ripe green. The top half uses a 'tide' pattern, suggesting fluidity and vulnerability, while the bottom half employs an 'echo' pattern, embodying strength through dense shapes. The primary collision occurs in the center where these two energies meet, creating a dynamic interplay of colors that reflect resilience emerging from fragility. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the concept of emotional scars. The layout emphasizes a split representing the dichotomy, with negative space balanced to sustain visual clarity.
