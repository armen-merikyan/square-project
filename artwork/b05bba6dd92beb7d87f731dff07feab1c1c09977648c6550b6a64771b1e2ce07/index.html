Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension of softness battling against severity. The palette juxtaposes gentle, powdery colors with stark, dark hues to reflect this conflict. The left side embodies softness in light tones, while the right side introduces severity with bold blacks and grays. The main collision occurs centrally, where these emotions blend and interrupt each other, creating an echo of contrast. This arrangement translates the seed's abstract themes into a visual rhythm of pulse and weight, reflecting the struggle between forgiveness and guilt.
