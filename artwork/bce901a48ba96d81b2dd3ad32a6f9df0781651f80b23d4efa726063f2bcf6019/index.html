Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional polarity of confusion and clarity, reflecting the interplay between symmetry and breakage. The choice of a dark palette accented with dim cyan represents a lonely signal amidst the chaos, creating a contrasting atmosphere. The arrangement of colors reflects a grid structure, with calculated interruptions signifying moments of uncertainty, while the soft bleeding edges provide a sense of fluidity and gradual transition. The scattered 'broken pixels' introduce imperfection, embodying the chaotic nature of confusion, while the rhythmic flow seeks to channel instances of clarity through repetition. The overall composition balances density with negative space, invoking a duality, a pulsating visual dialogue between calm and tumultuous emotion.
