Artist Statement

The emotional tone wrestles between strength and vulnerability, captured through a palette that juxtaposes warm ivory and deep wine, symbolizing exposure against darker elements of shelter. The central collision occurs in the center, where dense, rich colors meet the lighter, open spaces, echoing the quiet channel amidst pressure. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, facilitating a sense of imperfection reflective of real emotional experiences.
