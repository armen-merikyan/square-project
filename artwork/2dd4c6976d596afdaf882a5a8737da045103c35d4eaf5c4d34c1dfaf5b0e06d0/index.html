Artist Statement

This composition conveys a struggle between bright signals and pervasive noise. The palette combines deep navy and dim cyan, symbolizing the contrast between clarity and confusion. The collision occurs at the corners, where deep colors are densely packed, creating a visually overwhelming effect that breaks the grid's equilibrium. The arrangement embodies the seed's themes of exposure versus shelter, portraying a center pulse hidden within static layers, suggesting the challenge of finding clarity amidst chaos.
