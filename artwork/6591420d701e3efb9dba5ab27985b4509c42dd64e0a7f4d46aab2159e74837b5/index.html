Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy between defiance and submission, demonstrated through contrasting color placements and structure. The palette shifts from dark cosmic indigos to bright cold golds, signifying the clash of strength and vulnerability. The central pixels evoke a quiet field of firmness, while the surrounding colors weave a soft thread of submission, creating a dance of tension. The broken pixels provide intentional disruptions, contributing to the uneven border, enhancing the sensation of chaotic order in the environment. The main collision occurs at the center vs. edges, highlighting the struggle between assertiveness and acquiescence.
