Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between trust and suspicion, employing a palette that juxtaposes vibrant tones against deep, dark shades to evoke a sense of fragmented clarity. The colors signal green and electric blue represent trust, while the dead black and static gray highlight suspicion. The arrangement features a dominant dark field that interrupts with a few bright accents clustered at the top right, creating a visual struggle between clarity and obscurity. The broken pixels represent moments of doubt and uncertainty, disrupting the overall flow and contributing to the layered emotional narrative. The presence of negative space amplifies the feeling of tension, suggesting that not all is as it seems.
