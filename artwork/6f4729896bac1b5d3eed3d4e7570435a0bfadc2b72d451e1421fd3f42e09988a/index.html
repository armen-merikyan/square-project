Artist Statement

This abstract art reflects the emotional duality of softness and severity. The palette combines the calming qualities of powder pink and warm white with the starkness of carbon black and steel gray, creating a visual tension. The central area thrives with saturated colors representing severity, while softer hues gently encroach from the edges, suggesting a struggle between order and chaos. The contrast, particularly between saturated crimson against muted backgrounds, pings with energy. The broken pixels add intentional disruptions, embodying moments of guilt and forgiveness, while the implied spiral brings an organic rhythm to the composition.
