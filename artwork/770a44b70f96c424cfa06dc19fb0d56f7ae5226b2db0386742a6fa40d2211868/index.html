Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, mirroring the complexities of human experience. The palette, derived from tones of sepia and faded blue, evokes nostalgia while introducing an underlying calmness against chaotic elements. The contrast is established through a broken pattern that portrays overlapping paths, suggesting interference in clarity and balance. The placement of darker and lighter hues across the canvas creates a pulse-like rhythm, punctuated by asymmetries and tension points that echo the seed's dual nature. The final arrangement embodies a narrative of struggle and resolution, with a delicate interplay between dense clusters of confusion and the quiet spaces of clarity.
