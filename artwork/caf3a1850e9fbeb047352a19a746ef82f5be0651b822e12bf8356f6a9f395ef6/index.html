Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the tension between desire and emptiness. The chosen palette of electric blue and magenta emerges as vibrant contrasts against the cool depth of black, symbolizing desire's bright call amidst the void of emptiness. The outer edges present rigid patterns, conveying the starkness of unfulfilled longing, while the inner segments spiral with energy, suggesting hope and vitality. Broken pixels introduce an imperfection, reflective of fragmentation in the pursuit of completeness.
