Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between love and betrayal, utilizing a palette of warm pinks and deep reds juxtaposed against soft grays and dark maroons. The colors represent warmth and affection fighting against despair and emptiness. The top-heavy layout suggests tears or ash falling, embodying both sorrow and passion. The broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection and conflict within the mirrored symmetry, dictating the delicate balance between the highs of love and the lows of betrayal. The arrangement echoes a split screen, with one side more dense and warm, while the other experiences cooler, muted tones, creating a collision of emotions throughout the piece.
