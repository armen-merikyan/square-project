Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between joy and grief through a contrasting palette of warm and cool tones. The joyful yellows and oranges clash with the deep blues and muted purples, creating a visual dialogue of intensity and calmness. The zigzag pattern symbolizes the uncertain path of emotional experience, while the scattered placement disrupts symmetry to represent the chaos within order. The use of broken pixels adds an element of imperfection, echoing the imperfect nature of human emotion. Negative space is thoughtfully incorporated to reflect moments of quiet amidst the chaos.
