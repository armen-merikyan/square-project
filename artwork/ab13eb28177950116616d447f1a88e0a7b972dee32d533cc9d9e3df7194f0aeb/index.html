Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between trust and suspicion, represented through a palette of muted hues and sharp contrasts. The dominant blackened plum and iron gray create a backdrop of suspicion, while accents of small orange symbolize trust, echoing the struggle between openness and doubt. The layout reflects a mirror-broken style, where colors on each side almost mirror each other, yet subtly deviate, signifying the inherent uncertainties in relationships. The echoing pattern flows diagonally, creating a sense of movement that resonates with resilience amidst fragility.
