Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between loneliness and connection, with colors shifting from warm sepia tones (representing a faded memory) to cool, distant blues that echo the isolation. The arrangement emphasizes a top-heavy density that gradually diminishes towards the center, symbolizing a burst of inner warmth fighting against the encroaching coldness of isolation. The palette, 'broken_memory', suggests the remnants of connection while introducing a sense of fading brightness through contrasting tones that collide visually, generating tension. This produces a dynamic interplay between pressure and release, echoing the idea of hidden warmth and exposed vulnerabilities.
