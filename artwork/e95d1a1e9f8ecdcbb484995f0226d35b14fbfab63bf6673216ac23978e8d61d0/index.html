Artist Statement

The composition conveys the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a stark contrast of warm and cool colors, suggesting struggle and resolution. The 'broken_memory' palette encapsulates this duality, with dark muted colors symbolizing submission while brighter accents reflect defiance. The central collision of jagged smoldering orange and deep blue creates a focal point of tension and chaos, evoking a narrative of conflict amidst the calm of surrounding quiet colors.
