Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between rage and calm, reflecting the dichotomy of emotional states. The left half employs a pressure band pattern marked by sharp, warm reds, symbolizing rage and chaos, contrasting against a structured grid of pale blue and white on the right, representing calmness and control. The collision occurs centrally, with jagged edges disrupting the serene horizontal lines, creating a sense of interruption and tension that aligns perfectly with the seed's themes. The palette emphasizes the struggle between the hot intensity of rage and the cool placidity of calm.
