Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between quietness and pressure through an emotional flow. The colors evoke a sense of compressed density and muted spaces, with dark navy representing pressure and off-white symbolizing calm. The collision occurs diagonally across the composition, illustrating a split-screen feeling where dense pixel clusters in dark hues fracture against lighter fields, simulating a journey from chaos to tranquility. The palette was chosen to contrast subdued tones with sharp accents of compressed red, subtly indicating a transition amidst the quietness of the off-white and faint peach areas. This embodies the intricacy of emotional landscapes, showcasing a nuanced balance that evokes contemplation.
