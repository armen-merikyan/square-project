Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional turmoil of rage clashing against the serene calm. The black and red dominate the canvas, symbolizing the explosive nature of rage, while the soft pale blue and white introduce calmness. The jagged edges of the bold, broken dot patterns interrupt softer horizontal lines to visualize a conflict, with moments of quiet space punctuated by dense clusters. The negative space reinforces isolation, reflecting the seed's themes of loneliness and interruption.
