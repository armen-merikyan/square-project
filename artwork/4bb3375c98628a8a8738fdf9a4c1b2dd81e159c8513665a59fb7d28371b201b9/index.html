Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between joy and grief, balancing bright warmth against deep blues. The upper rows represent joy with a radial pattern in yellows and oranges, signifying expansion and spark-like energy. The lower rows reflect grief through muted purples and deep blues, suggesting a weighty, radial descent. The hard edges introduce a feeling of abrupt change, mirroring disruption. The balance shifts between dense joyful colors and the emptiness of grief, with occasional interruptions creating a dynamic rhythm, highlighting the emotional narrative drawn from the seed's exploration of conflicting feelings.
