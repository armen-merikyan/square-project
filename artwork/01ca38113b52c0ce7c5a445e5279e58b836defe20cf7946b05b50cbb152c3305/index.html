Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tone of contrasting loneliness and connection through the interplay of jagged edges and hollow spaces. The chosen palette, with sepia and faded blues, reflects the nostalgic memories of broken connections. The main collision occurs between dense clusters of darker pixels on the right, suggesting loneliness, and lighter, more dispersed pixels on the left, symbolizing connection. This balance creates a rhythm of emotional tension, with the negative space serving to amplify the feelings of emptiness against moments of vibrancy.
