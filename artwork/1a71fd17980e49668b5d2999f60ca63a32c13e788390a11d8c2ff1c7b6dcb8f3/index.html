Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores a dynamic contrast between restlessness and peace, captured through a palette that suggests warmth and coolness. The colors evoke a sense of tension and calmness, with deep wine and soft gold representing inner turmoil while ash gray and warm ivory provide grounding peace. The main collision occurs in the lower left corner, where pulse marks (representing restlessness) disrupt the orderly pattern of broken dots (representing peace), creating a visual tension. The structure and randomness of placements encourage movement while maintaining an underlying balance, reflecting the seed's essence of memory versus motion.
