Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension of softness and severity through a harmonious palette that transitions from a warm, feathered touch to sharp, defined edges. The lighter tones represent softness, spilling into darker, more severe regions, creating a visual weight that pulls the eye upward, mirroring the rising signal theme. The collision of the smooth transitions and rigid pressure bands generates a pulse effect that suggests movement and dynamism, reflecting the interplay of guilt and forgiveness, a space where emotions overlap yet remain distinct.
