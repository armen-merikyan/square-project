Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the struggle between softness and severity, represented through a palette of warm and cool colors. The use of powder pink and warm white introduces gentle pressure against the strong presence of carbon black and sharp crimson. The primary collision occurs in the center, with a dynamic interplay between lattice and spark patterns, evoking a sense of urgency and quiet resolve. The layout rises from a heavier base color upward, symbolizing hope emerging from weight, enhancing the feeling of tension between restraint and release.
