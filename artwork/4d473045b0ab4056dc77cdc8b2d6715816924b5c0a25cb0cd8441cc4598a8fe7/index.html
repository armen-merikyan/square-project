Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between longing and resolve through contrasting colors representing emotional states. The selected palette offers a muted urgency with red oxide and blackened plum to signify repair and damage, complemented by muted cream and iron gray to convey subtle serenity. The arrangement prioritizes density on the left side, with vibrant colors interrupting a quiet field, providing a pulse-like rhythm while evoking feelings of reflection and echo. The broken pixels throughout symbolize fractures in resolve, suggesting both vulnerability and strength, contributing to a layered emotional narrative.
