Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between loneliness and connection, represented through contrasting light and weight. The chosen palette—pink, red, gray, and dark maroon—embodies tension between warmth and isolation. The layout is framed, with a lighter, more connection-driven core amidst darker, weighty edges. The contrast occurs primarily along the right to left flow, where the lighter central area collides with a denser surrounding, presenting echoes of connection amid loneliness. The incorporation of broken pixels signifies memories, creating imperfection in the overall structure, emphasizing the fragility of connection.
