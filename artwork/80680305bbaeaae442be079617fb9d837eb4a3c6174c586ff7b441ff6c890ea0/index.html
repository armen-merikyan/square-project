Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the balance between quietude and intensity, with soft pastel colors representing serenity on the left and denser, darker tones symbolizing pressure on the right. The arrangement creates a diagonal flow where light colors expand against the dark, creating an echo of contrast. The 'crown' shape seed manifests as a subtle radial pattern on the left while the right side disrupts it with soft dots, embodying a collision between sharp edges and gentle curves.
