Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a clash between loneliness and connection through the deliberate arrangement of colors. The chosen palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple reflects warmth (connection) contrasted with the cool depth of isolation. The light colors burst from the center, creating a visual tension against the darker, surrounding frames that suggest both connection and separation. This interplay of colors fosters a sense of unease while hinting at potential warmth, with negative space allowing for quiet reflection on the feelings of exposure and concealment inherent in the seed's themes.
