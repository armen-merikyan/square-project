Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the interplay between vitality and entropy, represented through the sparking warmth of coral and blood orange against the muted presence of charcoal and dust pink. This dichotomy creates a fundamental tension; the central collision occurs in the middle rows, where vibrant accents clash with somber tones, giving way to a quietness in the edges that suggests contemplation versus chaos. The layout of a narrow negative space path slices through the density, guiding the eye and allowing for a sense of mental space, while the broken pixels symbolize memory's scars and the unresolved nature of resilience and fragility. The choice of colors establishes warmth battling against decay, illustrating a dynamic narrative of push and pull.
