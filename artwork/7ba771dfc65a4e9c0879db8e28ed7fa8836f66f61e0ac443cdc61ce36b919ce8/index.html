Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the duality of control and surrender, with a strong focus on the chaos of overflow and containment. The chosen palette, featuring burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold, evokes warmth and intensity but is tempered by darker shades to convey depth and tension. The main collision occurs in the center, where a chaotic burst of color disrupts an otherwise organized structure, representing the intersection of control and surrender. The broken pixel elements introduce imperfections that reinforce the theme of interrupted harmony, aligning with the overarching idea of pressure contrasting against moments of release.
