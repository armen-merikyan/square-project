Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of longing and resolve, creating a complex interaction between muted tones and sharper edges. The palette of red oxide and muted cream evokes a sense of yearning, while iron gray and blackened plum portray a steady resilience. The main contrast is centered, where radial patterns of longing collide with the noise of resolve, creating tension within the symmetry that is eroded at the edges. This reflects the breakdown and repair inherent in the seed's concepts, offering an abstract visual story of emotional flux.
