Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents a dance between longing and resolve, expressed through a palette of deep maroon, red, pink, and gray, conveying a classic conflict of warmth and tension. The layout mirrors itself with subtle disruptions, enhancing the feeling of broken symmetry, embodying longing through soft, scattered gray tones and interruptions of intense red. The dominant broken pixels create scars, signifying damage amidst the search for repair, reflecting chaos against hope. The central collision occurs around the center, where deep maroon forces intersect with pink accents, inviting the viewer to navigate this complex emotional landscape.
