Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys the tension between defiance and submission. The palette of pale aqua, mist gray, alarm red, ink blue, and clear white creates a serene yet urgent atmosphere. The main collision occurs in the center where dark and dense colors (red and blue) clash against the lighter, more subdued edges, reflecting a struggle. The pattern reflects spiraling inward and outward movement, with the concentric arrangement suggesting both attack and retreat, embodying the essence of the seed's emotional conflict without literal representation.
