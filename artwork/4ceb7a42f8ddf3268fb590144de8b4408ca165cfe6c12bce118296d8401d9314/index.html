Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between confusion and clarity through a structured yet chaotic pattern. The use of black and dark violet represents confusion, while soft gold and pale green offer hints of clarity and hope. The center focuses on a 'half_square' shape, symbolizing transition from full to hollow, while the contrast is evident with dark colors clashing against lighter pixels. Broken pixels subtly disrupt the flow, enhancing the overall feeling of fragility within the chaos. The arrangement conveys a wave-like rhythm, inviting the viewer to probe deeper into the emotional complexity of the piece.
