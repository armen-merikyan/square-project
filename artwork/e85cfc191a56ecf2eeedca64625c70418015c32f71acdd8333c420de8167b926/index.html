Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between softness and severity through a deliberate contrast of colors. The palette revolves around soft powder pink and warm white for gentleness, juxtaposed with deep carbon black and sharp crimson to convey intensity and disruption. The layout reflects an eroded symmetry, a stark contrast between defined edges and smudged centers that communicates a dialogue between structure and chaos, embodying the layers of guilt and forgiveness mentioned in the seed. The breakout pixels disrupt the calm, introducing unexpected energy that mirrors the emotional conflict.
