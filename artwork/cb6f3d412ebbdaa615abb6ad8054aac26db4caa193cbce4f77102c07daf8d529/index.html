Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional contrast between defiance and submission. The palette features sharp colors like alarm red and calm pale aqua to emphasize emotional tension. The main collision occurs at the center, where lattice and maze patterns interact, mirroring the dynamic between strong and soft forces in the seed. The arrangement allows for visual imbalance, celebrating the disruptions that create a complex emotional landscape.
