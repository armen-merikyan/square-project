Artist Statement

This composition juxtaposes the vibrancy of 'spark' against the muted tones of 'ash,' representing the tension between vitality and decay. The palette combines vivid red and pink to evoke passion and life, while gray and dark maroon reflect the remnants of restraint and memory. The primary collision occurs at the center, where the patterns of 'halo' and 'embers' intersect, creating a dynamic disruption—a visual representation of resilience amidst fragility. The choice of a broken frame layout enhances the feeling of emotion leaking through punctuated spaces, symbolizing the struggle between sustaining and letting go.
