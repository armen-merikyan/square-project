Artist Statement

This composition evokes the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through an interplay of dense and soft elements. The palette of moss green and stone gray represents the resilience found within fragility, while rust and small gold serve as striking accents that suggest sparks of strength amidst exposure. The main collision occurs in the top left corner where hard edges of strength meet the softer clusters of vulnerability, creating a dynamic visual force. The broken pixels add intentional imperfections to enhance the theme of exposure, suggesting scars rather than clean lines.
