Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between loneliness and connection through the contrasting palette of deep reds and grays. The outer edges, weighted by darker tones, symbolize isolation, while the lighter inner pixels suggest moments of connection. The arrangement captures an intentional shift from concentrated darkness to scattered brightness, evoking a sense of breach and vulnerability. The use of broken pixels introduces an imperfection that speaks to the fragility of relationships and memories, creating an emotional resonance that reflects the seed's themes.
