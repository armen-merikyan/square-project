Artist Statement

This artwork utilizes the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through a palette of deep reds, grays, and pinks, symbolizing raw emotion and vulnerability. The central area bursts forth with intensity while the edges remain subdued, creating a visual tension. The broken pixels echo the imperfections of personal connections, while the crosshatch pattern contrasts the structured grid, suggesting the struggle between hidden emotions and open expression. This layout emphasizes the outpouring of feelings from the center, radiating towards the corners, with a deliberate negative space to reflect isolation.
