Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between restlessness and peace, using a palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold to embody warmth and intensity against depth. The main contrast occurs between dense, dark colors suggesting restlessness and lighter accents signaling moments of peace. The scattered repair layout illustrates the clash of emotions through interrupted lines, while broken pixels disrupt the visual flow, evoking an incomplete journey which resonates with the seed's themes.
