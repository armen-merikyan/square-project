Artist Statement

This composition balances a saturated emotional pull against a stark outer border. The inner 4x4 grid represents desire with a warm tide pattern through vivid colors (hot coral, blood orange), while the outer frame contrasts this with a checker pattern in muted hues (charcoal, hollow blue) that symbolize emptiness and restraint. The primary collision occurs at the center, highlighting the struggle between fulfillment and limitation, creating a dynamic visual tension amid a quiet backdrop.
