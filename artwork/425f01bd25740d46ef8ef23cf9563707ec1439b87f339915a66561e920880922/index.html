Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between certainty and curiosity through layered diagonal flows, where defined thread patterns symbolize certainty and soft quiet fields represent curiosity. The palette, featuring pale aqua and alarm red, evokes serenity against urgency, with clear white providing openings and mist gray creating atmosphere. The collision occurs prominently in the center and edges, interrupting the structured path with broken pixels, representing disruptions amidst smooth transitions. The abstract interplay of colors captures the essence of rising signals, a tide against constraints and the destabilization of order.
