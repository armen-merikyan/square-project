Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between clarity and confusion, embodied in a palette of muted sepia tones and faded blues that suggest nostalgia and depth. The contrast lies in the strict, repetitive patterns of clarity clashing with irregular, chaotic placements of confusion, creating a visual tension. The patterns—organized vs. broken—mirror the seed's themes, where a symmetrical layout is punctuated by deliberately misplaced pixels, invoking a sense of fractured emotions and uncertainty.
