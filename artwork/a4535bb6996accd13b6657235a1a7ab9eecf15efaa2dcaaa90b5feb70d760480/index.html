Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between vibrant energy and subtle decay, suggesting a constant battle between spark and ash. The palette of ripe green and honey gold serves to emphasize life's vitality, while deep olive and cream introduce a sense of wear, echoing the softness of memory. The main contrast occurs at the center, representing a threshold where these emotional states collide – a jagged interruption in the flow, manifesting through broken pixels to symbolize imperfections and fleeting moments. This arrangement creates a rhythm that feels both chaotic and harmonious.
