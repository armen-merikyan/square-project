Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional interplay between longing and resolve through dense, deep shades of red and dark maroon, symbolizing yearning and intensity, while lighter grays represent the quiet distance and order. The central density evokes chaos, characteristic of longing, contrasted sharply against the empty borders indicating resolution and clarity. The pattern flows diagonally, echoing the tension present in the theme, where jagged edges disrupt the continuity, emphasizing the struggle between closeness and emotional distance.
