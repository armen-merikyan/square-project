Artist Statement

This composition harnesses the emotional tone of conflict between clarity and obscurity, represented through a dark and muted palette with bright, active clusters. The colors #192f5d (navy) and #0bc5c5 (dim cyan) stand out as signals against the noise created by various shades of gray and black. The bright cluster is intentionally placed in the center to symbolize the 'pulse' of clarity amidst the overwhelming noise. The choice of colors is designed to evoke feelings of isolation and the struggle to maintain one's voice against chaos.
