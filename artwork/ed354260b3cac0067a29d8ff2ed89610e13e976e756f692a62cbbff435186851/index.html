Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise). The selected palette of navy, black, and dim cyan emphasizes the stark contrast between a lonely signal and overwhelming static. The main contrast occurs around the center where bright accents of white suggest a clear pulse interrupted by darker, static fields. The pattern captures the essence of echoes and interruptions, with broken pixels adding imperfections that represent fleeting memories against a backdrop of noise.
