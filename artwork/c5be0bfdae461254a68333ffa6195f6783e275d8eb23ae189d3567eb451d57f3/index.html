Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of joy and grief, represented through a contrasting palette of warm yellows and oranges against the cool hues of deep blue and muted purple. The left side embodies joy with lighter, rounded shapes, employing a mirrored pattern that evokes reflection, while the right side embraces grief, utilizing jagged edges and dense noise patterns. The collision occurs at the divide, where the joyful vibrancy meets the weight of muted colors, suggesting a tension that interplays throughout the composition. This arrangement echoes the feelings of insecurity versus stability, capturing the essence of the seed's emotional narrative.
