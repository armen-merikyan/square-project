Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between defiance and submission, using a palette of vibrant greens and warm golds against muted backgrounds. The central collision occurs with a bright acid green that suggests defiance while the surrounding deep olive offers submission. The use of broken pixels contributes to an environment of imperfection, echoing struggle and resilience. The layout features two small pulses that shift between tension and release, symbolizing the push and pull of emotional energy.
