Artist Statement

The emotional tone evokes a clash between relief and dread, captured through a palette of vibrant lime and electric blue against the depths of black and magenta. The arrangement depicts a dynamic tension, where bursts of bright colors fight against the surrounding heaviness, illustrating the collision of feelings through patterns of noise and rain effects. The zigzag flow creates movement across the canvas, emphasizing the struggle and release within the emotional landscape. Negative space is preserved, adding to the quietness amidst the turmoil.
