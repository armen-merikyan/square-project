Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between loneliness and connection through its stark contrasts and asymmetric patterns. The palette, inspired by 'ancient_fire', utilizes burnt orange and dark red for warmth, evoking passionate emotion against the coolness of charcoal, representing isolation. The main collision occurs where vibrant dots disrupt the monochrome background, symbolizing connection piercing through a sea of loneliness. The arrangement, flowing from top to bottom like falling pixels, creates a sense of descent that hints at weight, echoing the journey of moving from solitude towards connection while leaving intentional negative space for introspection.
