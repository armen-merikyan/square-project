Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic tension between certainty and curiosity. The palette combines soft teal tones with stark blacks and muted grays to express relief amid dread. The contrast enhances the feeling of rising anxiety and anticipation within the layout of a narrow path through dense color pressure. The broken pixels symbolize interruptions in thought and experience, while patches of warmth interrupt the coolness of uncertainty, creating an ongoing dialogue between order and chaos. The overlapping patterns suggest a turbulent flow, generating a layered depth that invites exploration.
