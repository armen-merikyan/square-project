Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional juxtaposition of pressure and quiet. The selected palette of muted colors like 'off_white' and 'dust_gray' conveys calmness, while the 'compressed_red' creates focal tension. The pattern illustrates a chaotic flow from one corner to another, suggesting a struggle against tranquility, with dense clusters on one side breaking into softer fields. The collision of warm and cool tones reflects the emotional narrative of shattering against an overwhelming tide, bridging clarity with confusion.
