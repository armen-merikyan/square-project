Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between softness and severity, using a palette that evokes warmth and coldness. The gentle pastel hues suggest softness, while the deeper, darker shades impose a sense of weight and severity. The central area becomes a point of collision, where the soft 'thread' patterns interlock with the stricter 'mirror' formations, creating a visual dialogue that speaks to the emotional conflict inherent in the seed. The layout mirrors on either side with slight imperfections, emphasizing the theme of brokenness in perfection. Negative space balances dense areas, reinforcing the need for emotional quiet against the backdrop of dynamic tensions.
