Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between rage and tenderness, using saturated colors to signify intensity and muted shades for quietness. The palette combines reds to convey rage alongside blues and whites for tenderness, emphasizing the contrasting emotions. The main collision occurs in the center where deep red pulses clash with lighter blues, creating dynamic interaction. The arrangement echoes a threshold, where the dense pixel cluster at one edge gradually gives way to a calmer pattern, illustrating the movement from pressure to release.
