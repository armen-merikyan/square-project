Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between pressure and quiet through a contrasting palette and arrangement. The darker shades signify compression and density, while the lighter tones depict soft interruptions and open spaces. The layout features a crowded edge transitioning into a more tranquil center, suggesting a release of tension. The main contrast occurs at the center where the dark navy collides with the off-white, creating a defined boundary that invites reflection. The use of a checker pattern for the pressure areas versus the flood of faint peach and off-white captures the concept of an island amidst the chaos, visually narrating the struggle between clarity and confusion.
