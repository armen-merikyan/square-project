Artist Statement

This pixel art composition explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a juxtaposition of soft and stark colors. Using a palette of pale mint and storm gray emphasizes both the gentle pull of vulnerability and the underlying weight of strength, creating a compelling contrast. The central pixel arrangement suggests a soft collision, with lighter tones representing exposed vulnerabilities amid darker edges that symbolize resilience. The broken pixel placement reflects imperfection and fragility, reinforcing the core message that strength coexists with vulnerability. The layout mimics falling pixels, representing a continuous descent, akin to a tide, creating a sense of motion and undulation across the square.
