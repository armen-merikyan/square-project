Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional dichotomy of safety and exposure. The palette of warm brown and soft greens suggests a protective environment while pale skin tones juxtaposed with cold whites represent vulnerability. The collision occurs vertically, with dense color layers at the bottom ascending through lighter hues, emulating the feeling of climbing towards hope. Broken pixels introduced throughout serve as moments of disruption, enhancing the narrative tension of protection versus openness.
