Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between softness and severity, echoing in the color dynamics. The palette, drawn from 'softness_severity', combines light pinks with stark blacks to signify conflict. The main contrast occurs in the center, where heavy dark hues interrupt lighter shades, symbolizing the tension and collision of feelings. The 'stacked_bands' layout creates rhythm, while the 'braid' and 'threshold' patterns express this duality, offering visual complexity.
