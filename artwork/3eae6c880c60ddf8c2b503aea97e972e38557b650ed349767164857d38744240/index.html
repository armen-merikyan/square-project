Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the artwork contrasts tenderness with rage, encapsulating a sense of conflict through the use of saturated and muted hues. The palette integrates bold reds with softer blues and whites, suggesting warmth clashing against coldness. The main collision occurs diagonally across the square, where dynamic noise patterns from rage overlap with calming veils of tenderness, creating visual tension essential to the piece. This structure reflects an internal emotional battle, represented tangibly through color placement and arrangement.
