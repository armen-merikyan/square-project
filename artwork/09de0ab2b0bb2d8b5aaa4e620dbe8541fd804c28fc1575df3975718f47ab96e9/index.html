Artist Statement

The composition captures the duality of 'joy vs grief' using contrasting light and dark colors to create a sense of tension and release. The color palette incorporates bright yellow and orange for joy that gradually encounters deep blues and muted purples representing grief, forming an emotional wave that shifts across the grid. This pattern illustrates the silent struggle between hidden pain and exposed joy, allowing for visual rhythms that embody the flow of emotions present in the seed.
