Artist Statement

The emotional tone highlights the contrast between joy and grief, represented through warm and cool palettes. Bright yellows and oranges reflect joy, while deep blues and muted purples evoke grief. This creates a collision between light and dark, where the joyful colors flicker against the stability of grief's hues. The layout's split horizontal theme reflects the opposing forces at play, creating tension and movement across the square, emphasizing the emotional variations generated by the seed's concepts.
