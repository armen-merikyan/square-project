Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between vulnerability and protection, depicted through contrasting colors. The palette of shelter green, warm brown, pale skin, cold white, and open blue reflects softness against starkness. The collision occurs near the center where the delicate pale skin overlaps with the heavier warm brown, suggesting the protective enclosure around the exposed core. This assembly illustrates the tension of seeking safety while teetering on the edge of exposure, with the patterns of broken frame and fault line suggesting disruption and continuity. Overall, the arrangement invokes a feeling of gentle conflict.
