Artist Statement

The emotional tone of loneliness versus connection manifests through contrasting dark and light colors. The muted sepia and gray palette evokes a sense of nostalgia and memory, while the occasional bright accents create tension and focus amidst the subdued backgrounds. The overall layout features a small central pattern surrounded by a diverse texture of dots and broken pixels, emphasizing isolation and connection's fragility. The jagged edges illustrate the disruptive nature of loneliness, culminating in an abstract, visually compelling composition.
