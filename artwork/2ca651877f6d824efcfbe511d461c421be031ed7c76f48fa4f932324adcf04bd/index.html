Artist Statement

The emotional tone evokes a struggle between restlessness and peace, represented through a palette of gentle off-white and dusty gray that convey tranquility, contrasted by compressed red vivid accents symbolizing disturbance. The center features a dense cluster of muted colors representing the calm, interrupted by sharp splashes of red, showcasing the argument of light and dark, building tension and depth. The arrangement is an uneven distribution that reflects the chaotic nature of memory and movement, with a significant amount of negative space around this central chaos, emphasizing isolation amidst turbulence.
