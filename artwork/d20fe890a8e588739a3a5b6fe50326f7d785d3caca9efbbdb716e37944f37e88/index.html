Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a tension between restlessness and peace, visualized through contrasting patterns of dots (representing peace) and wave-like forms (symbolizing restlessness). The color palette, drawn from deep cosmic indigo and void black, embodies a dark, contemplative space, while the cold gold and star white accents punctuate it with bright, fleeting memories. The primary collision occurs in the center, where the tension of motion disrupts the tranquility, effectively narrating a story of yearning and stillness. Broken pixels introduce intentional redactions and echoes of the past, sustaining a visual dialogue between memory and current experience.
