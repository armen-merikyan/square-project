Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity through a gradient of structured versus chaotic elements. The palette combines warm brown and shelter green, invoking stability alongside the cold white that hints at the unknown. The main collision occurs in the central row, where warmer tones ebb against cooler ones, paralleling the seed's exploration of rising signals and receding edges. The pulse marks of curiosity disrupt the static certainty, creating a dynamic interplay that invites meditation on movement and stillness.
