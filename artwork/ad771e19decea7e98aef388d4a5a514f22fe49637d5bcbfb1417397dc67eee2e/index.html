Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the interplay between fear and hope, represented through a contrasting palette of dark violet and soft gold. The dark colors symbolize fear, creating an oppressive outer edge, while the lighter color represents hope, radiating from the center. The 'straight_vs_curved' contrast is established by sharp, jagged edges in the darkness that buffer the fluid, scattered dots of light, suggesting a battle between rigidity and softness. This composition conveys tension and release, with negative space representing moments of pause amidst chaotic bursts.
