Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between the severity of dark colors and the softness of pastels, representing the struggle between protection and vulnerability. The palette combines soft hues of powder pink and warm white against the intensity of carbon black and sharp crimson, highlighting the contrast between light and dark. The main collision occurs at the center where the saturated colors densely overlap with lighter shades, echoing the spiral motif in a radial pattern. Broken pixels add imperfection, symbolizing guilt and the complexities of forgiveness.
