Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between control and surrender, represented by a palette that balances warm and cool tones. The selected colors evoke feelings of hope amid pressure, with the collision of bright yellow against muted purple creating depth. The main contrast occurs centrally, where bright colors saturate against darker edges, suggesting containment versus overflow. This pattern emerges as not just a shape but an energetic dynamic crafted from the seed's themes, illustrating emotional tension and uncertainty while allowing for broken pixels as intentional disruptions in the flow.
