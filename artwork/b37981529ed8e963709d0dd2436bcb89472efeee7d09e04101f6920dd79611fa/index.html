Artist Statement

The emotional tone fluctuates between the tension of restlessness and the serenity of peace, captured in a grid of contrasting colors. The palette of warm coral, soft amber, cold slate, blue-black, and signal cyan suggests a dynamic interplay where warmer tones create a rhythmic flow, while cooler shades introduce a sense of depth and interruption, enhancing the visual conflict. The primary collision occurs from the diagonal arrangement of the warmer coral and amber, which stands against the stable blues, symbolizing the argument between motion and memory. Broken pixels are applied subtly along the edges to evoke a sense of imperfection and disruption, enhancing the theme of unresolved emotions.
