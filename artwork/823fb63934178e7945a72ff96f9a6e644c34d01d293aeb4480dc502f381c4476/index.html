Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between defiance and submission, embodying resistance through bold color juxtaposition. The palette includes deep moss green and rust to evoke resilience and fragility, respectively. The main contrast occurs at the center of the composition with angular patterns representing defiance colliding with soft fields of color symbolizing submission. The pattern suggests a chaotic inward spiral, showing energy meeting stillness while leaving negative space for breathing room, embodying the essence of tension.
