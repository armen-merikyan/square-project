Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the tension between defiance and submission through a palette that embodies both warmth and coolness, illuminating the interplay of hard interruptions and soft collapses throughout the piece. The color choices reflect the emotional complexity of submission resonating with the vibrancy of defiance. The main contrast occurs at the center, highlighted by warm brown and open blue, symbolizing a beacon of tension amidst the surrounding chaos. The arrangement employs broken pixels strategically to indicate imperfection, enhancing the artwork's narrative of collision and resolution.
