Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the push and pull between defiance and submission, using the contrasting shades of red and black to emphasize conflict and weight. The pale blue offers a moment of distance, contrasting with the dominant colors for reflection. The layout is mirrored with intentional imperfections to showcase the chaos inherent in structured relationships, visualizing the mental tug-of-war. A bottom-weighted design balances the chaotic elements, suggesting the weight of submission while the lighter top evokes defiance, forming a dense narrative within the pixel structure.
