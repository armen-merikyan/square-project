Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a juxtaposition of longing and resolve, portraying a tension between a muted color scheme of deep blues and muted purples representing longing, and bursts of vibrant yellows and oranges depicting resolve. The palette emphasizes the collision between quiet fields and bright accents, creating a sense of urgency amidst serenity. The arrangement reflects a halo void layout where heavier patterns of stripes (resolve) confront fragmentary patchwork (longing) in a central echo of feeling, embodying both the repair and damage aspects of the seed.
