Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures an interplay between longing and resolve, where the clutter of soft cyan and muted plum suggests the tension of unfulfilled desire against a backdrop of lavender gray providing a sense of calm. The palette reflects melancholy with its cool hues, while the broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, evoking a sense of repair amidst damage. The main collision occurs in the center with dark rain blue against bright moon white, symbolizing the struggle of connection and isolation, creating a compelling narrative of recovery within chaos.
