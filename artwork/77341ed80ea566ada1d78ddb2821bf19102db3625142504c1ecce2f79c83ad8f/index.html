Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between safety and exposure. The palette, comprising soft green, warm brown, pale skin, cold white, and open blue, evokes feelings of both comfort and vulnerability. The main contrast occurs between the soft, enveloping colors and the sharper, more vibrant tones, with broken pixel placements suggesting disruption and imperfection. The arrangement of colors reflects a split vertical layout, emphasizing the struggle between protection and exposure, contrasting dense clusters on one side with more open spaces on the other.
