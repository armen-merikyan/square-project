Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between trust and suspicion. The palette of cosmic indigo and deep teal conveys a sense of depth and mystery, while cold gold acts as a bright accent amidst the darkness, representing fleeting trust. The split between darker hues and lighter tones creates a stark contrast, suggesting a collision between the stable and the unpredictable. The layout features a broken frame that symbolizes fractures in trust and the unpredictability of suspicion, as if the emotions are constantly leaking into one another. The arrangement emphasizes a diagonal flow, invoking a sense of instability against a backdrop of softness. Overall, the composition reflects the complexity of emotional states without becoming literal, allowing the viewer to feel the tension through color alone.
