Artist Statement

This piece conveys an emotional tone of struggle between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise). The palette of deep navy, black, and a dim cyan accent contrasts strong, readable patterns against muted, static backgrounds, representing interference vs clarity. The chaotic noise is dominant in the square with an organized halo pattern of light drawing attention to the center, creating a visual dialogue of tension between the calm shield of darkness and the flickering pulse of brightness.
