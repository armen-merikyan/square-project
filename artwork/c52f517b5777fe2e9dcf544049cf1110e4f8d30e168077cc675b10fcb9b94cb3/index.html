Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between joy and grief through a dynamic interplay of colors. The palette is derived from bright yellows and oranges to symbolize joy, contrasted with deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The composition features a structured grid on the left, representing the stability of joy, while the right side intentionally disrupts this order with 'broken dots,' symbolizing grief's unpredictability. The edges are bottom weighted, creating a sense of heaviness that contrasts with the lightness of the center, echoing the pulse of emotions within the square layout.
