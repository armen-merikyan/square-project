Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between strength and vulnerability. The palette of pale mint and soft sky introduces a calm, delicate atmosphere while storm gray and near black provide grounding and weight, representing strength. The soft transition between light and dark colors suggests a constellation—points of vulnerability amid a heavy backdrop. The main collision occurs at the center, where edgy brush-like patterns overlap, indicating a struggle for dominance while maintaining a soft, fragmented touch. Broken pixels further enhance the narrative of imperfection, suggesting a paradox between safety and exposure.
