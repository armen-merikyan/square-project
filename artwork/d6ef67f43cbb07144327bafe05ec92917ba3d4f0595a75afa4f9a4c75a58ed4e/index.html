Artist Statement

The emotional tone plays on the tension between safety and exposure, using a color palette that contrasts warmth and coolness. The composition features a central pale area suggesting vulnerability, surrounded by darker hues representing enclosure. The arrangement emphasizes an asymmetrical collision where the warmth of the sheltering colors overlaps and breaks against the cooler exposed sides. This evokes a feeling of protection that skillfully intertwines with the uncertainties of exposure.
