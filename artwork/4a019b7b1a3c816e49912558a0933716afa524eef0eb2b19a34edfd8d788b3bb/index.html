Artist Statement

This abstract composition embodies the tension of love and betrayal through a dynamic interplay of warm and cool color tonalities, utilizing a color palette of muted reds, pinks, and dark maroon to evoke warmth, contrasted against the quiet greys that symbolize betrayal. The diagonal flow of colors suggests rising emotions, while broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, embodying the concept of disrupted symmetry. The contrasting sharpness of certain colors colliding with softer shades brings forth an intense emotional narrative, depicting a struggle between connection and loss.
