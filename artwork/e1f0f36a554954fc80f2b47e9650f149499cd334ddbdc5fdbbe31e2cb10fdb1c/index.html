Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between longing and resolve through jagged lines and soft expanses. The color palette of warm coral and cold slate creates a dialogue between warmth and coolness, suggesting emotional depth. The main collision occurs on the diagonal, where the vibrant accents of signal cyan intersect with the muted tones of soft amber, representing the chaotic yet structured nature of the emotions. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, reflecting the unpredictability of human feelings, while the negative space invites growth and explores the unfulfilled connections between emotions.
