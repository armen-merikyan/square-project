Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional dichotomy between control and surrender through the juxtaposition of tightly structured pixels and liberating gaps. The muted tones of stain brown and ash gray evoke a somber atmosphere, while warmer aspects of soft gold and warm ivory suggest a delicate release. The gentle collision occurs in the center where thick outlines meet, illustrating a tension between overwhelming presence and sparse negative space, embodying the struggle between containment and overflow.
