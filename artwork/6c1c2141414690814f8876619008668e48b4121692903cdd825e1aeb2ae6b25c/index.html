Artist Statement

The composition captures the tension between defiance and submission through a palette reflecting guilt and forgiveness. The warm ivory and soft gold suggest a feeling of hesitant embrace, while deep wine and ash gray provide a dissonant clash representing struggle. The central pixels form a chaotic cluster representative of defiance, while the surrounding soft backgrounds create a sense of collapse. The layout captures the feeling of scattered repairs along a diagonal line, with broken pixels indicating conflict from past actions. This arrangement echoes the challenges of navigating borders between emotional states, embodying a narrative of asymmetry punctuated by moments of pause and retraction.
