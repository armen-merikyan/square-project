Artist Statement

This piece conveys a tension between trust and suspicion through a contrasting palette of warm yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples. The soft glow of light represents trust, anchored in a brighter, active central area, while the surrounding darker tones symbolically create a sense of suspicion, resembling a pressure border. The diagonal falls suggest movement and interruption, embodying the emotional conflict between resilience and fragility. The grid pattern represents the suspicious layers, intersecting with the fault lines of trust, creating a dynamic interaction throughout the piece.
