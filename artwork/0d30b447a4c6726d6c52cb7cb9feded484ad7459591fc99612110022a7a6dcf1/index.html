Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a tension between clarity and confusion, signifying the struggle for understanding amidst disorder. The selected palette of muted pinks, reds, and grays evokes feelings of uncertainty countered by moments of clarity. The top half reflects structured patterns, creating a sense of order, which sharply contrasts with the disrupted frames of the bottom half, representing chaos. Movements from the darker tones at the edges to lighter centers signify a pulse—simultaneously vibrant yet unstable. The jagged edges imply brokenness, while the placement of the 'broken pixels' introduces deliberate imperfection, enhancing the emotional narrative of conflict between calm and rage.
