Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between relief and dread, where the colors oscillate between oppressive darks and soft lights, establishing a weighty yet hopeful atmosphere. The chosen palette, with its reds and pinks, infuses the composition with heat against the cool grays, symbolizing moments of calm amidst chaos. The contrast manifests predominantly in the top section, where the bright pinks clash with the deep maroons, creating a visual echo of the broken journey. The arrangement flows diagonally as the lighter shades attempt to invade the darker areas, resonating with the seed’s conflict of motion and stillness, while leaving significant negative space to emphasize the struggle between density and emptiness.
