Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a duality of loneliness and connection, contrasting warmth and coolness. The palette combines deep blue, muted purple for a sense of solitude, while yellow and orange add warmth of connection, juxtaposing against areas of negative space. The center, filled with dominant colors, creates a sharp vs. soft contrast, suggesting emotional intensity and vulnerability. The pattern flows diagonally, where clusters of warm hues interrupt cooler areas, echoing conflict and resolution visually.
