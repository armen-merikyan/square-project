Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional conflict between defiance and submission through its rich, warm palette of burnt orange, dark red, and hints of gold against a charcoal backdrop. The center features a crosshatch pattern representing strength and resistance, while the surrounding broken dots suggest a receding submission, creating a stark contrast. This collision occurs prominently in the center, reflecting the 'quiet channel' layout, where pressures from both sides interrupt each other, inviting contemplation of thresholds and boundaries.
