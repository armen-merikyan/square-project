Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between vulnerability and strength, embodied through the juxtaposition of pale and muted colors against darker, stronger hues. The palette features sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue, representing the complexity of these emotions. The arrangement has a dense center with soft recesses towards the edges, suggesting a struggle between security and exposure. The collision of 'stack' and 'stairs' patterns creates visual dissonance, amplifying the theme of conflict, while intentional broken pixels add a sense of imperfection, echoing the theme of fragility. Overall, the artwork radiates a swirling intensity that suggests both safety and risk, as the interplay of colors captures the essence of the emotional seed.
