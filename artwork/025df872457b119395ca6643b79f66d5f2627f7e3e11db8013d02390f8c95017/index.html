Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between loneliness and connection, reflecting the tension between the isolated core and the vibrant outer edges. The palette comprises lime, electric blue, and magenta against a black backdrop, suggesting bursts of energy against a void. The patterns reflect a checker-like rhythm in the top rows (evoking connection) and a mirrored arrangement in the bottom rows (symbolizing loneliness), creating a dynamic interplay that shifts from calmness to pressure. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, suggesting the fragility of human experiences.
