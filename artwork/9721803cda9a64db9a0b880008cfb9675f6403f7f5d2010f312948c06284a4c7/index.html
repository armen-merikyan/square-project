Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional contrast of vulnerability and strength through a split layout, where the top half visually embodies softness with lighter shades, while the bottom half showcases a dense structure with darker tones. The palette of soft pastels juxtaposed with stark blacks represents a narrative of brokenness intertwined with resilience. The central collision of colors lies in the transition from warm whites to sharp crimson at the midline, reflecting the tension between safety and exposure. The patterns of frame and crosshatch create an overlapping dialogue, encapsulating the feelings of unsettled balance that arise from the seed concepts.
