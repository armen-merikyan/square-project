Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional struggle between clarity and confusion. The chosen palette features deep navy and stark black as the foundational tones to denote the noise, while bright dimensions of dim cyan and a singular white reflect the signal. The main collision occurs in the center, where the clean pulse competes against scattered static influences, creating a tension that invites the eye. The layout reflects an empty space, symbolizing isolation while allowing the colors to engage in a dialogue of interruption and resonance, reflecting the balance of memory against erasure in a vibrant, abstract form.
