Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the dense weight of pressure against expansive quietude. The palette of muted tones (off-white, dust gray) and subtle accents (compressed red, faint peach) creates a dialogue contrasting quiet spaces with vibrant bursts. The main collision occurs near the center where the flame-like pattern (pressure) disrupts the barcode pattern (quiet), suggesting a struggle between tension and release. The seed concept of cracks emphasizes sharp edges transitioning into softer, more open areas, literally shattering the stability of the color distribution.
