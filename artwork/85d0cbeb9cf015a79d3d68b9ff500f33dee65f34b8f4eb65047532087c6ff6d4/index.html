Artist Statement

This piece embodies a dynamic tension between presence and absence, symbolized by the clash of dark denser colors and sporadic bright interruptions. The palette, featuring deep navy interlaced with stark whites and muted cyan, represents the struggle of clarity amidst noise. The main collision occurs centrally, where bright whites interject against a backdrop of deep, dark tones, mimicking how strong signals break through dense interference. The abstract arrangement reflects the idea of signals fighting for clarity amidst overwhelming noise—an emotional narrative of hope against distraction.
