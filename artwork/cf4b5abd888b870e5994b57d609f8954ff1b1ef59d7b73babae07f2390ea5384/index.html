Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between clarity and confusion, divided into two sections. The top half features a palette of soft sepia and faded blue, suggesting clarity in a framework. The bottom half contrasts with higher saturation and chaotic patterns, reflecting confusion. The checkerboard layout introduces intersections that evoke sensation and energy. The interplay between structured frames and disjointed pixels presents a visual narrative of emotion, enhancing the theme of symmetry versus break. The palette's muted tones add depth to the piece, allowing for a more reflective interpretation.
