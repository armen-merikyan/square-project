Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the tension between awe and terror through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette combines deep blacks and dark violets to evoke feelings of fear, while soft gold and pale green introduce hope and balance. The layout rises from bottom to top, symbolizing an escape or aspiration, with the central area holding brighter hues as a beacon amid darker tones. The soft edges and jagged contrasts create a sense of instability that mimics the inner conflict of the emotions represented in the seed.
