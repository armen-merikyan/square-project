Artist Statement

The emotional tone arises from the interplay of softness and severity. The palette of powder pink and sharp crimson offers a visual contrast that reflects tension—softness in the lighter shades balances the severity of the dark colors. The main collision occurs at the center with loops of warm white intermingling with deep carbon black, suggesting a passage of emotional complexity. It embodies the ideas of light versus dark where quiet fields of warm white envelop sharp, crowded crimson, evoking a sense of turmoil resolved through the convergence of stark contrasts.
