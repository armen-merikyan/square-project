Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between containment and overflow, represented by a balance of dark and light tones. The cooler palette embodies control while hot accents suggest surrender. The arrangement sees dark colors clustering at the center, simulating pressure, while lighter colors flow outward, symbolizing release. This dynamic creates a visually striking narrative of tension and resolution.
