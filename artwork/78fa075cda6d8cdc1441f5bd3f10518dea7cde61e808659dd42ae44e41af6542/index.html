Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between love and betrayal through a contrasting palette of warm and cool colors. The core is defined by soft pinks representing love, while the outer layers showcase dark maroon and gray tones indicating betrayal. The use of broken pixels and noise against smoother shapes creates a sense of disarray, reflecting internal conflict. The collision of soft warmth with sharp, dark edges illustrates the dynamic struggle between hope and despair, mirroring the dual nature of relationships.
