Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of relief and dread through a carefully selected palette emphasizing cool and muted tones that evoke a sense of weight and despair, alongside contrasting bright highlights that suggest moments of lightness and hope. The main collision in the center creates tension as dark and strained colors push against lighter, more open spaces, visually representing the struggle between pressure and release. The arrangement of colors mimics a spiral movement, echoing the internal turmoil and potential for conflict as suggested in the seed text.
