Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between love and betrayal through contrasting colors and density. The palette of warm pinks and reds interspersed with deeper gray and dark maroon represents the warmth of love clashing with the coldness of betrayal. The sharp edges of contrast reflect the emotional stakes, while the rising signal layout suggests hope amidst turmoil. The interplay between light and dark hues creates collision points, with broken pixels enhancing an imperfect yet dynamic flow, embodying the complexity of emotion captured in the seed.
