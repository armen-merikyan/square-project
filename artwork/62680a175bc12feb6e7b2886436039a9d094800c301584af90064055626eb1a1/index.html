Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between control and surrender. The palette of navy and dim cyan provides a cool foundation, representing control, while sparse isolated bursts of white signify surrender attempting to invade. The main contrast occurs centrally, where the cooler tones push against the brighter accents, creating a visual stutter. The layout suggests an empty space filled only with fragmented signals, embodying the themes of overflow and containment.
