Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the juxtaposition of joy and grief, which is expressed through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette features deep blues and muted purples representing grief, offset by vibrant yellows and oranges embodying joy. The main contrast is concentrated in the center, where warm tones radiate outward, interrupted by cooler shades, exemplifying the collision of emotions. This arrangement creates a dynamic tension that suggests motion from despair to hope, mirroring life’s complexity. The patterns employed evoke a sense of order versus chaos, with structured segments clashing against more chaotic distributions.
