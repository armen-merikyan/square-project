Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy between love and betrayal through color and arrangement. The palette consists of warm pinks and reds representing love, juxtaposed with stark gray and dark maroon symbolizing betrayal. The pattern flows from top to bottom, evoking a sense of falling emotions, while the broken pixel placements disrupt the symmetry, illustrating the unintended rifts in emotional states. The contrasting blocks of warm and cool tones create tension, embodying both the comfort of connection and the fracture of trust, each pixel adding to an overarching narrative of vulnerability and complexity.
