Artist Statement

This piece reflects the duality of relief and dread through contrasting colors where warm, muted browns (sepia, tan) interact with cool shades (dust_gray, faded_blue). The layout emphasizes a central diamond-seed, suggesting pressure as it radiates outward. The use of broken pixels in the top rows introduces imperfection, echoing the theme of broken symmetry, while the lower rows provide a calming reprieve, suggesting a journey from chaos to calm. The interplay of light and dark creates a rhythm of emotional tension and contrast.
