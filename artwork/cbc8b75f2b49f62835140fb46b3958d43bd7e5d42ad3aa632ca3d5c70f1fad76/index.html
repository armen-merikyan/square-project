Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between control and surrender, embodied through the palette of dark hues and soft golds that convey weight and a glint of hope. The main contrast occurs between a dense dark core and lighter peripheral colors, illustrating pressure against a serene release. The overlapping patterns of crosshatch (control) and noise (surrender) create a visual dialogue of collision, as the seed's themes provide a rhythmic push and pull within the composition.
