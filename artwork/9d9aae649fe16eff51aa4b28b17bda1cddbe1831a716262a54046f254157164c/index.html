Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension of joy and grief through the clash of vibrant and muted colors. The use of bright yellows and oranges represents joy while the deep blues and muted purples symbolize grief. The arrangement contrasts dense clusters of dots reflecting the chaotic nature of grief against lighter, broken patterns suggesting the fragility of joy. A central, symmetrical formation is slightly disrupted to capture the essence of being torn between two conflicting feelings, leading to both harmony and discord.
