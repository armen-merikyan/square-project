Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends quiet with pressure, using a palette that reflects both soft and saturated colors. The dominant quiet hues create a muted field, representing calmness, while sharp contrasting elements introduce pressure that interrupts this tranquility. The abstraction of the seed is expressed through a dense collection of colors at one corner, gradually loosening towards the opposite edge, creating a sense of flow and transformation from crowding to clarity, emphasizing the tension between hard edges and soft zones.
