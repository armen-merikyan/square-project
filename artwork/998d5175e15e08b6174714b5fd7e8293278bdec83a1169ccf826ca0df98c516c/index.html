Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a juxtaposition of nostalgia and progress, symbolized through contrasting patterns—stairs representing nostalgia's structured warmth, and dots marking progress's chaotic climb. The palette, 'ancient_fire', evokes warmth and caution, with burnt oranges and deep reds suggesting memory and emotion while gold adds a glimmer of advancement. The main collision between the structured staircase and the dispersed dots creates a dynamic tension that reflects the struggle between reminiscence and innovation, with negative space providing room for contemplation. This layout invites an exploration of the delicate balance between fullness and hollowness, epitomized by the bottom-weighted design. Each broken pixel represents imperfection, echoing memories that resist uniformity.
