Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional duality of quiet versus pressure through a dense aggregate of colors in the upper left corner, symbolizing pressure that loosens and dissipates toward the bottom right, evoking a sense of release. The palette balances shades of compression (dark navy and compressed red) and soothing tones (off white and faint peach) to enhance tension with softness. The main contrast occurs in the movement from dark, sharp edges toward the airier fields of light colors, creating a narrative of conflict and resolution, as suggested by the seed that emphasizes breaks and tensions in clarity.
