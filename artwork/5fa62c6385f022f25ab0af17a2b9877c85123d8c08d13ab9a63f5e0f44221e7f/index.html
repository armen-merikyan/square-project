Artist Statement

This composition embodies a dialogue between hope and fear through color dynamics. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green symbolizes fear's shadows and the soft light of hope. The outer edges exhibit fractured patterns reflecting fear's jagged edges, while the inner four cells create a maze-like presence of hope, with glowing gold accents suggesting resilience amidst darkness. The arrangement offers contrasts in density and light, conveying the constant push and pull between these two emotions, with deliberate 'broken' pixels enhancing the feeling of imperfection and introspection.
