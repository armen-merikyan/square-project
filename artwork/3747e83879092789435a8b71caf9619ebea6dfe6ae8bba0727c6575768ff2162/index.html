Artist Statement

The emotional tone presents a duality of restlessness and peace. The palette, drawn from the 'guilt_forgiveness' theme, merges deep wine with warm ivory to create an interplay between tension and calm. The vibrant flame-like reds and browns collide with soft, muted ivories and grays to suggest turbulence at the corner, symbolizing a compressed memory against drifting motion. The main contrast occurs at the corner, where richer tones surge against a gentle backdrop, embodying the struggle of movement within stillness. The arrangement captures echoing pulses of emotion without literal representation.
