Artist Statement

The emotional tone derived from the seed reflects a dance between nostalgia and progress, expressed through sharp contrasts of the sepia-based palette. The left side embodies nostalgic maze patterns with muted tones of sepia, tan, and dust gray, while the right side unfolds progress with linear rain patterns in faded blue. The thick edge style creates a frame of tension, and the broken pixels symbolize imperfections in growth, embodying the concept of emotional collisions. This layout emphasizes contrasting emotions while allowing for negative space to breathe, symbolizing the unresolved motions of life.
