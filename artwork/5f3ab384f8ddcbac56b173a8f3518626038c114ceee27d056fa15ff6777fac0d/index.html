Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between confusion and clarity through contrasting colors and an asymmetric pattern, embodying the dualities of soft edges and hard contrasts. The selected palette, derived from 'fear_hope', juxtaposes dark violet and pale green, representing the struggle between the known and the unknown. The arrangement features a mirror-like appearance disrupted by scattered, darker shades, symbolizing the interruptions of clarity amidst chaos. The emotional narrative unfolds through the balance of structured symmetry and chaotic breaks, inviting contemplation on the nature of clarity in confusion.
