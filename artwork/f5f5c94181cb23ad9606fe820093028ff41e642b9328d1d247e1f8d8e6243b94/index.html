Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional clash between panic and control through a zigzag pattern that symbolizes conflict and resolution. The selected palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green conveys a tension between darkness and hope. Contrasting colors create visual interruptions that echo the seed's dual themes, while the arrangement leaves negative space to suggest chaos against structured boundaries. Broken pixels add an element of imperfection, embodying emotional turmoil.
