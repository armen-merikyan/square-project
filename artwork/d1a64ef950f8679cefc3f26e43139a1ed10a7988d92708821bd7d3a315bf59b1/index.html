Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes restlessness and peace, creating a tension evident through the selected palette of black, electric blue, lime, and magenta. The predominance of darker colors represents restlessness, providing a dense backdrop, while bright accents symbolize fleeting moments of peace. The main collision occurs diagonally, where chaos (shattered patterns) meets calm (smoother transitions), reflecting the struggle between motion and memory. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, enhancing the sense of disturbance. The layout follows a scattered repair metaphor, visually depicting the emotional complexity of resolving inner turmoil.
