Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of fear and hope, portrayed through a palette of dark and soft colors. The contrasting black and dark violet create a heavy foundation, embodying fear, while the soft gold and pale green represent hope, subtly placed to suggest a rising signal from the center. The arrangement plays with tension—a sharp interruption at the edges versus a gentle glow in the center. The 'rain' effect represented by vertical disruptions positions the conflict between the oppressive corners and a piercing glimmer of potential, echoing a sense of both anxiety and uplift.
