Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension and interplay between joy and grief. The palette features bright yellows and oranges representing joy, contrasted by muted purples and deep blues symbolizing grief. The arrangement reflects a flow of falling pixels, aligning with the emotional cascade described in the seed. The presence of introduced broken pixels adds an element of imperfection, embodying human experience. The use of asymmetric placement showcases the collision between structured joy and chaotic grief, creating a resonant emotional landscape.
