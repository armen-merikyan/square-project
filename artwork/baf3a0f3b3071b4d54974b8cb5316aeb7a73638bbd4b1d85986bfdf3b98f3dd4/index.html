Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay between vulnerability and strength, where the central pixels represent stability amidst soft, braided edges symbolizing exposed feelings. The palette utilizes warm, earthy tones to emphasize grounded strength juxtaposed with cool, faded blues representing vulnerability. The primary collision occurs at the center, where darker colors converge, creating a focal point that highlights the theme of tension between exposure and shelter. Broken pixels add intentional disruptions, suggesting imperfections and feelings of alienation within connections. The layout emphasizes a central symbol surrounded by pressure, allowing the viewer to engage with the emotional narrative of strength and vulnerability, echoing the seed's themes.
