Artist Statement

This composition conveys the emotional tension between love and betrayal, utilizing contrasting warm and cool colors to evoke feelings of warmth and intimacy alongside cold, harsh realities. The palette consists of pink and red to symbolize love's warmth, contrasted by gray and dark maroon to introduce elements of betrayal and disappointment. The layout follows a vertically split format where the left side embodies the soft and mirrored qualities of love with smoother, rounded shapes, while the right presents sharper, fractured edges reflecting betrayal. The central contrast occurs between the soft warmth on the left and the stark, broken textures on the right, suggesting the instability in emotional connections. Broken pixels enhance the theme of imperfection and destruction in relationships, while negative space maintains a sense of unresolved tension throughout the piece.
