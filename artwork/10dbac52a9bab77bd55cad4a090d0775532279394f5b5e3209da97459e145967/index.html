Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between restlessness and peace, captured through the interplay of calm colors and unsettling contrasts. The palette of greens and browns suggests safety, while pale skin tones and cold whites introduce a starkness, representing the tension of memory against motion. The main contrast occurs centrally, where warm brown clashes with cool white, symbolizing a struggle for balance between calm and chaos. This arrangement creates an interruptive pattern, embodying the seed's themes of argument and edge while maintaining a cohesive abstract form.
