Artist Statement

The emotional tone couples the tension of suspicion with the warmth of trust, represented through a 4:4 color split where darker shades dominate the left, symbolizing suspicion, while warmer, lighter hues occupy the right, embodying trust. The collision occurs in the center, creating a stark contrast that evokes the emotional push and pull between these opposing feelings. The use of the 'fault' shape as an organizing metaphor represents disruptions amid stability, manifesting as broken pixels scattered across the grid, hinting at fragility amid resilience.
