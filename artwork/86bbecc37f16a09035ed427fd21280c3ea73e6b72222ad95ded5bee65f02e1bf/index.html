Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between vulnerability and strength — a balanced collision represented by the dense central island of darker colors embodying strength, surrounded by lighter hues suggesting vulnerability. The palette features pale aqua and mist gray, creating a serene but urgent contrast enhanced by alarming reds and deep blues. The main collision occurs in the center, where a maze-like pattern interacts with the softer stepwell structure at the edges, allowing for an exploration of layers and boundaries while maintaining a serene yet pressured atmosphere.
