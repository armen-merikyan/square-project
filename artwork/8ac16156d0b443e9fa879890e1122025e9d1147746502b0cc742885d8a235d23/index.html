Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between restlessness and peace through contrasting patterns and colors. The chosen palette of muted cream, blackened plum, red oxide, iron gray, and small orange reflects the hunger for calm amidst chaotic memories. The central collision of a patchwork structure imparts movement and energy, while crosshatches create tranquility surrounding it. The quiet channel of negative space is essential, guiding the eye while suggesting a delicate balance between intensity and serenity.
