Artist Statement

This composition juxtaposes the soft tenderness of muted greens and blues against the sharper intensity of reds and blacks, creating a tension that reflects the inner conflict between rage and calm. The palette captures both the oppressive weight and the quiet release inherent in the emotional narrative. The main collision occurs in the center, where the bold colors meet the softer tones, establishing a spiral pressure that draws the eye inward, creating a contrast that embodies the concept of protection versus attack.
