Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional dichotomy of confusion versus clarity. The contrasting palette of electric blue, lime, black, and magenta illustrates the tension between chaotic energy and serene clarity. The dominant colors create a pulse-like rhythm, suggesting movement across the canvas. The inclusion of broken pixels introduces intentional imperfections, embodying the theme of false symmetry. The uneven placement of colors disrupts potential harmony, inviting the viewer to explore the underlying emotional narrative of balance against disruption.
