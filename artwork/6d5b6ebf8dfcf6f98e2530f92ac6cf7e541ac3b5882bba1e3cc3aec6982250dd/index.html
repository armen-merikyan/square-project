Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a complex interplay of trust and suspicion, captured through contrasting colors that create a visual tension. The chosen palette of soft green, warm brown, and cold white evokes safety against the backdrop of interference represented by darker tones. The main collision occurs in the center with a shift from the warm grounding of trust to the hardened, static intrusion of suspicion, emphasizing the psychological struggle between connection and doubt. The pattern emerges as an abstract fault line, characterized by broken pixels that symbolize imperfections and disruptions within the emotional landscape.
