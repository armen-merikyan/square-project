Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, depicted through an overfilled center with contrast at the edges. The palette of navy and black suggests depth and strength, while dim cyan introduces vulnerability's softness. The collision occurs primarily in the center, where dots (strength) and veils (vulnerability) intertwine, hinting at a struggle between exposure and protection, reminiscent of a heart fragment breaking yet lingering in a sheltering embrace.
