Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between 'emptiness' and 'desire', represented through a warm palette contrasting against deep blues. The top half reflects the warmth of desire with vibrant yellows and oranges, while the bottom half uses muted purples and deep blues to embody a sense of emptiness. The broken pixels introduce an element of chaos and imperfection, echoing the heart's fragmented feelings. The contrast occurs at the horizontal split, suggesting a collision between the two emotional states, forming a narrative of longing amidst desolation.
