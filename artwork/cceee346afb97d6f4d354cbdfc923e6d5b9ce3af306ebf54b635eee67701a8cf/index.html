Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents the struggle between longing and resolve, where the palette of lime, electric blue, magenta, and black creates a vibrant tension. The arrangement consists of denser clusters in the center symbolizing urgency and resolve, while the outer edges with quieter tones depict longing, suggesting a boundary that is both firm and fragile. Broken pixels represent the imperfections of these emotions, disrupting the patterns of resolve with hints of damage. The collision of the patterns 'pulse_marks' and 'rain' further emphasizes the inner turmoil of reconciling these contrasting feelings.
