Artist Statement

This composition balances pride as a radiant pulse against the muted weight of shame, utilizing a palette of deep navy and dim cyan to represent the gravity of these emotions. The edge is heavy with dark colors, creating a dense atmosphere that contrasts sharply with the intermittent white pixels, which serve as highlights of release. The grid pressure created by the target and teeth patterns suggests a struggle between two opposing forces. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, embodying the disruption in this emotional symphony. This layout emphasizes an orbiting movement, cycling through feelings of conflict and harmony.
