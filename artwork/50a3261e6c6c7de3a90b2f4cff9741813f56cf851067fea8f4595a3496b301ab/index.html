Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional conflict between panic and control, using a palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon to evoke feelings of love and betrayal. The contrasting colors create a zigzag rhythm across the canvas, embodying the argument grid between chaos and order. The burned edges symbolize the raw intensity of emotions, while intentional broken pixels introduce imperfections, highlighting the struggle between symmetry and disruption. The layout promotes a rising signal, suggesting hope emerging amidst chaos, offset by negative space that invites reflection.
