Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the dichotomy of awe and terror by utilizing a palette that juxtaposes deep blacks with bright whites and soft cool tones. The pattern reflects 'order vs chaos' with one half organized in a checker form representing awe, while the other is disrupted by crosshatch strokes symbolizing terror. This collision creates visual tension, where the contrast between dark and light embodies stability against instability, invoking a sense of unease and beauty.
