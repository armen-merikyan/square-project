Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The soft hues of ice blue and violet gray form the vulnerable center, representing exposure and fragility, while the crisp ink black and warning orange create structured channels, symbolizing strength and safety. The main collision occurs in the center, where the softer and stronger elements interweave, suggesting a dance between protection and exposure. The broken pixels subtly disrupt the harmonies, serving as scars, reflecting the idea of imperfection in strength. The palette, combining clarity and confusion, enhances this tension, allowing for a dynamic emotional landscape within the confines of the abstract square.
