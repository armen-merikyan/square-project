Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette of 'melancholy wonder' features soft and muted tones, communicating an introspective mood. The deep rain blue provides a solid foundation that speaks of strength, while the lighter moon white and soft cyan suggest lightness and fragility, evoking a balance between safety and exposure. The main collision occurs centrally, where the dense stepwell pattern of strength breaks against the lighter ash field, creating a visual representation of the disturbance within a seemingly calm area. This reflects the complexities of strength held against the backdrop of vulnerability, peacefully intertwined yet dramatically contrasting.
