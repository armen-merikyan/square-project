Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between fear and hope, illustrated through a palette of deep blacks and rich hues contrasting against soft golds and pale greens. The arrangement captures a burst of energy from the center, with dark edges encroaching upon it, suggesting the struggle of light against shadow. The split vertical layout enhances this duality, allowing the dense versus empty contrast to emerge. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, reinforcing the emotional narrative of resilience amidst anxiety.
