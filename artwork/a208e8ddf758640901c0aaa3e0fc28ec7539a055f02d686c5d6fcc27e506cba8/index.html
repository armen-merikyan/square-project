Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the duality of trust and suspicion through contrasting colors and patterns. The selected palette combines a muted base with vibrant accents, suggesting the pressure of suspicion against the soft glow of trust. The main collision occurs diagonally, where darker hues clash with lighter ones, creating visual tension. This reflects the emotional pull between the two states, enhanced by a split layout and broken pixels that embody imperfection and uncertainty.
