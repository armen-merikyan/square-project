Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between softness and severity, utilizing a palette that juxtaposes warm and cool elements. The 'powder pink' and 'warm white' provide gentle undercurrents, while 'carbon black' and 'sharp crimson' create abrupt contrasts, suggesting a dialogue between warmth and coldness. The main collision occurs centrally with small yet fierce bursts of crimson against muted tones, producing a pulse-like rhythm against a backdrop of quiet space, reflecting the nuanced emotional interactions of guilt and forgiveness.
