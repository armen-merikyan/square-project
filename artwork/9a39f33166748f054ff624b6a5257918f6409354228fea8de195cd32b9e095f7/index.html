Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between love and betrayal through a contrasting palette of warm pinks and dark maroons, suggesting warmth conflicting with depthless sorrow. The edges are purposefully rugged to signify broken expectations, while the density of colors on the left creates a sense of pressure, contrasted by the emptiness on the right, suggesting loss. The zigzagging patterns represent both the order and chaos inherent in relationships, adding a rhythm of rising and falling emotion.
