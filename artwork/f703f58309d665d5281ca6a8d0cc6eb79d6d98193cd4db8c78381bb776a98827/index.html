Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the push and pull between defiance and submission, illustrated through a stark contrast of dark and muted colors with touches of brightness. The palette leans towards reds and blacks to evoke intensity, juxtaposed with muted cream to soften the edges. The composition features a concentrated cluster of color in the top-left corner, symbolizing pressure and defiance, while the muted tones suggest a sense of submission and retreat in the rest of the space. The central collision happens with the interaction of deep red and subtle cream, creating an inward spiral of tension that invites the eye across the square. The patterns of 'pressure_band' and 'ladder' intertwine, hinting at the struggle between rigid structure and chaotic energy without forming literal images.
