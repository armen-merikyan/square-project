Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between control and surrender. The palette combines warm coral and soft amber with colder tones for contrast, reflecting the dualities at play. The dominant area of warm colors represents containment and control, while the cooler shades linger around the edges, symbolizing surrender. Broken pixels are strategically placed to introduce imperfections, suggesting the underlying struggle. The central collision occurs in the middle rows, where controlled patterns clash with softer, diffused forms, creating a visual narrative of tension and release.
