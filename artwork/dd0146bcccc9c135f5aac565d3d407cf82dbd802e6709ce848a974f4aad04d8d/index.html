Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension of longing and resolve through contrasting textures and colors. The muted sepia and dusty blue forms a contrasting backdrop for stronger accents, mirroring the complexity of human emotions. The center features a dense cluster of grid-like patterns symbolizing resolve, while the surrounding area, filled with a braided pattern, expresses the nuances of longing. The interplay and collision between these forms illustrates a pulse between contained strength and the overflow of yearning. The interrupted broken pixels add a layer of imperfection, representing fractures in the emotional landscape.
