Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy between envy and gratitude, using a palette of electric blue, lime, magenta, and black to symbolize tension and warmth. The layout flows downward, mirroring the falling pixels theme, evoking the sense of tears or raindrops descending. The contrasting dark and bright colors create an interrupted flow, symbolizing the collision between feelings of desire and thankfulness. The cracks and split screens replicate emotional fractures, suggesting moments of disturbance amidst gratitude, while leaving ample negative space encourages contemplation and reflection.
