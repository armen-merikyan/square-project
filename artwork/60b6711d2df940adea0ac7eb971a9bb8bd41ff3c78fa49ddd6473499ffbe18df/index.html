Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a dance between defiance and submission, where bold contrasts echo through the composition. The choice of the 'melancholy_wonder' palette creates depth and subtlety, with muted colors representing submission and brighter tones embodying defiance. The main collision occurs at the center, where dense clusters of color spiral outward, representing attack and protection. This abstract arrangement captures the essence of struggle and balance, stemming from the seed's themes of invasion and conflict.
