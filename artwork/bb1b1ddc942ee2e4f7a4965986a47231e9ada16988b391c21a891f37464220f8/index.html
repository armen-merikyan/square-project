Artist Statement

This 8x8 pixel artwork conveys the tumultuous contrast between rage and calm through a dichotomy of colors and patterns. The left half, representing rage, employs sharp, jagged reds that ascend diagonally, symbolizing explosive energy and confrontation. The right side, characterized by soft pale blues, adheres to a horizontal grid, invoking a sense of serenity and order. The chosen palette deepens the emotional complexity, ensuring that the collision of feelings is palpable. The broken pixels introduce imperfections that suggest struggle and disruption, while the rising signal layout hints at an upward quest against the weight of emotions, embodying both hope and solitude.
