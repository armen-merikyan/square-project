Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the tension of joy versus grief, where vibrant colors clash against muted tones. Inspired by the seed's emotional dichotomy, the bright yellows and oranges represent ephemeral moments of joy, while the darker blues and purples serve as reminders of grief. The central placement of brighter hues is interrupted by a maze-like pattern that suggests order amidst chaos, reflecting the struggle between these opposing feelings. The design creates a focal collision point that emphasizes the emotional contrast, surrounding it with an underlying grid that evokes structured sorrow, hinting at how memories intertwine.
