Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between restlessness and peace, where the colors convey this duality through cooler tones and warmer accents. The palette, inspired by melancholy wonder, supports a dialogue between soft cyan and muted plum, representing the gentle balance against unsettled borders. The main collision occurs around the center with broken pixels that mimic the fragmentation of thought—an interruption reflecting the tension within the seed's complexities. The layout creates a sense of threshold, illustrating the push and pull between calm and agitation without literal imagery.
