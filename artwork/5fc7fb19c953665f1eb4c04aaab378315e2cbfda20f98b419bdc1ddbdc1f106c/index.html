Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission through stark contrasts and patterns. The palette of muted earth tones and soft blues reflects both safety and exposure, setting a calm yet charged atmosphere. The outer border echoes the theme of submission with its subdued warmth, while the inner core pulses with vibrant diagonals disrupting the tranquility. The broken pixels act as intentional scars, suggesting a disturbance in the flow, representing moments of defiance. The main collision occurs at the diagonal center, where the echoing and barcode patterns intersect, highlighting the struggle between control and chaos.
