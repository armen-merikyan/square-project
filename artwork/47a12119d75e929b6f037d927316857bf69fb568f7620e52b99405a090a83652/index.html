Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between quietude and the weight of pressure, evoking a contrast of compressed density on one edge which loosens into a quiet field. The palette of soft off-white and faint peach serves as a muted field against darker tones, illustrating a transition from soft to sharp. The main collision occurs where darker hues clash with lighter ones, suggesting an argument of emotional flow, embodying the seed ideas of symmetry breaking and diagonal tension.
