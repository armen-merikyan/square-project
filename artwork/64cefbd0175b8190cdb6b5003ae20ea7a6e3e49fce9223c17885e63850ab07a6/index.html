Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between restlessness and peace, utilizing the colors navy and dim cyan to represent peace, and black to symbolize restlessness. The 8x8 grid alternates between calm blue tones and dark areas, where interruptions are created through the 'thread' and 'rain' patterns. The dark colors dominate, suggesting the weight of restlessness while the lighter patches of dim cyan provide moments of calmness. The central collision occurs where the dark contrasts meet the lighter shades, suggesting a push and pull of emotions. The overall composition echoes a quiet chaos, reminiscent of the emotional turmoil explored in the seed.
