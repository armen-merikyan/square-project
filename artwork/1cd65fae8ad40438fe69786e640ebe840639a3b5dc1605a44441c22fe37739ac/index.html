Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between longing and resolve, represented through a palette of deep black and cool pale blue that signifies depth and calm amidst chaotic longing. The contrast arises from densely packed dark dots colliding with expansive negative space, reflecting the theme of repair vs. damage. The subtle hints of white and accented color enhance the feeling of disruption and echo across the grid, echoing the metaphor of thread that weaves through the narrative, suggesting a connection between fragmented experiences and unified resolve.
