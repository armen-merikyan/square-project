Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the duality of fear and hope, contrasting shadowy depths with uplifting brightness. The palette utilizes dark violet and black to evoke feelings of fear, while soft gold and pale green suggest a rising sense of hope. The main collision occurs in the top half, where sharp contrasts emerge from the darkness, evoking the tension between despair and aspiration. The zigzag pattern flows upward, reflecting the emotional journey from a dense bottom grounded in fear to a lighter center representing hope, aligning perfectly with the idea of rising signals amidst broken pixels.
