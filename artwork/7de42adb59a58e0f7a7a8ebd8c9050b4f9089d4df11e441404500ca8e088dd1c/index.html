Artist Statement

This artwork presents an emotional contrast between safety and exposure, organized around a dense center reflecting vulnerability and a surrounding area of quieter pixels representing protection. The palette of greens and warm browns suggests safety, while pale skin and cold white denote exposure. The main collision occurs at the center where brighter colors resonate with deeper hues, echoing the push and pull between these emotional states. The arrangement symbolizes the tug of safety against the bravado of exposure, enhancing the tension through optical rhythm, interrupted patterns, and broken pixels revealing dynamic narratives beneath the surface.
