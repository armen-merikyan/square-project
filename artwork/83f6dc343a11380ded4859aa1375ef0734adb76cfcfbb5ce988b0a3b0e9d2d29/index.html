Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes warmth against betrayal, using a palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon to highlight the contrast. The top half embodies love's vibrancy, while the bottom half introduces chaotic, damp grays and muted tones, reflecting fragmentation. The pattern oscillates between dense clusters and quiet spaces, emphasizing the heartbeat of love interrupted by betrayal. The arrangement, with slightly broken pixels, creates a disrupted symmetry that resonates with the duality of emotions represented in the seed.
