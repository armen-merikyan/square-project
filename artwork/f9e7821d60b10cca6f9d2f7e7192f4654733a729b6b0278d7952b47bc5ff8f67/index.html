Artist Statement

This composition embodies the struggle between clarity and confusion, where structured signals attempt to emerge from a backdrop of noise. The palette blends deep navy and black for depth and shelter against dim cyan and a hint of white for the pulse of clarity. The jagged edges create interruptions, emphasizing moments of static against smoother tones. The grid arrangement of colors climbs upward, representing hope and a striving for clarity amid chaos.
