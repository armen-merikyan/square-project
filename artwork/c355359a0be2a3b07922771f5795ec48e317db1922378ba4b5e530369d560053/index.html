Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the tension between loneliness and connection through a carefully selected palette of warm (yellow, orange) and cool (deep blue, muted purple) colors. The dominant deep blue suggests depth and solitude, overshadowed by small bursts of yellow and orange that symbolize the desire for connection. The pattern creates a spiral counterclockwise flow, echoing the push and pull of emotional states. The placement of the lighter colors amid larger dark areas creates a stark contrast, encapsulating the feeling of connection punctuating moments of isolation. Broken pixels enhance the piece's narrative of fractured memories, ensuring that while there is beauty, it is wrought with complexity.
