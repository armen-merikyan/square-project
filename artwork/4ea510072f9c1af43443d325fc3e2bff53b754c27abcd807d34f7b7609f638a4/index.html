Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between trust and suspicion through a balanced yet disrupted pattern. The dark colors represent suspicion and uncertainty, while the soft gold and pale green lend warmth and hope, creating collisions of feeling across the grid. The soft edges of the palette provide a contrast to the darker, sharper edges of suspicion, reflecting the emotional tug-of-war. The central collision occurs at the diagonal axis, symbolizing the split between perceptions—a visual echo of two opposing viewpoints.
