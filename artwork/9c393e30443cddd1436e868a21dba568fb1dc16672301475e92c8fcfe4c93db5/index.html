Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between tenderness and rage through a careful selection of colors and forms. The palette incorporates stark blacks to evoke intensity (rage) contrasted with soft pale blues and whites representing gentleness (tenderness). The main collision occurs in the center where sharp black pixels overlap with rounded light colors, creating dynamic pressure and interruption. The pattern reflects a journey from a quiet corner, symbolizing tenderness, to chaotic black bands indicating the encroachment of rage, embodying the emotional themes derived from the seed.
