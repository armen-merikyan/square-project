Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette, named 'melancholy_wonder', features soft, muted colors that evoke feelings of introspection and fragility, while hints of deeper hues establish strength. The contrast resides in the center where strong, saturated colors collide with softer, more subdued tones at the edges, giving a sense of an overfilled center. The layout is designed to reflect the density of emotions at the center, hinting at pressures that push against the boundaries represented by softer colors at the periphery. Broken pixels add intentional imperfections, illustrating the emotional scars of vulnerability and strength clashing.
