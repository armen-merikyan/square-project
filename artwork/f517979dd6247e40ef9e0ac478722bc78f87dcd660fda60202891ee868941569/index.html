Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional dichotomy of vulnerability and strength through a palette that oscillates between warm and cool tones. The deep blue represents strength and stability, while muted purples and yellows suggest fragility and exposure. The contrasting colors collide in the center, creating a tension that reflects the struggle between resilience and fragility. The pattern employs a static structure on the outer edges, creating a 'ring' effect that portrays vulnerability at the heart of strength, allowing for a visual narrative that speaks to both protection and exposure.
