Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a juxtaposition between clarity and confusion, where the left side uses a warm palette to suggest clarity through bright yellows and oranges, while the right employs muted purples and deep blues to evoke a sense of confusion. The pattern of 'teeth' on the left contrasts sharply with 'frame' on the right, illustrating a battle between structured form and chaotic disruption. The thick outlines on the edges signify boundaries that are tested and broken, while the 'falling pixels' create a movement across the canvas, suggesting a pulse or rhythm that falters midway.
