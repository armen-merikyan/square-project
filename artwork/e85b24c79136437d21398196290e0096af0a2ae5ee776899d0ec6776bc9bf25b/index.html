Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of love and betrayal. The palette combines warm pinks and reds with muted gray and dark maroon, representing affection intermingled with sorrow. The primary contrast occurs where bright colors intersect with darker, grayed areas, symbolizing the conflict between hope and disappointment. The layout plays with symmetry and the broken energy of the seed, creating a 4x4 core of warm tones surrounded by softer hues, with imperfections scattered throughout to signify the fragility of emotions.
