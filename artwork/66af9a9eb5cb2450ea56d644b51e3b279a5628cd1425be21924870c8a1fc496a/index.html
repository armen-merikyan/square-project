Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between vulnerability and strength, captured through a palette tilted towards softer shades while maintaining a strong base. The clash of warmer reds with grays signifies moments of protection versus invasion, creating a vibrancy within the subdued. The primary contrast unfolds at the center, where blocks collide, suggesting an exposed core. The arrangement of patterns — glitch and crosshatch — illustrates uncertainty interrupted by reliable structures. Broken pixels at the edges offer an intentional disruption, enhancing the sense of fragility and defense, emphasizing the overall narrative of stability against pressure.
