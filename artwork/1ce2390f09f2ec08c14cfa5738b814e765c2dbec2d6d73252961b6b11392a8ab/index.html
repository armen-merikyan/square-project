Artist Statement

The emotional tone draws on the tension between confusion and clarity, represented through a contrasting palette of yellows and muted purples. The center’s warm yellows signify clarity surrounded by cool, deep blues which symbolize confusion. The pattern creates an echo of brightness that disrupts the darker edges, while symmetrical elements hint at the brokenness introduced by random broken pixels, embodying the fragmented symmetry suggested in the seed. The arrangement flows outward, suggesting a push-pull between inner clarity and outer chaos.
