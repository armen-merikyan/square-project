Artist Statement

This 8x8 composition captures the emotional tension between 'longing' and 'resolve.' The palette blends deep blacks and vibrant greens with cooler shades of blue, creating a contrast between warmth and distance. The main clash occurs at the center, represented by dense clusters of signal green and electric blue, interspersed with quiet gray tones that evoke a sense of longing. The pattern features interrupted passages resembling a shifting landscape, paralleling the themes of chaos and order through the layered design inspired by the seed. Broken pixels enhance the sense of imperfection, adding depth and character to the piece.
