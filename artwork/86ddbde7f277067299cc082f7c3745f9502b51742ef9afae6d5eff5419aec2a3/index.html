Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between defiance and submission, where sharp contrasts represent the struggle against resignation. The palette utilizes 'pale mint' and 'faded gold' as lively accents to disrupt the predominantly muted 'storm gray' and 'near black' fields. The interaction of the shatter and teeth patterns manifests in a cluster of bright colors along the center, symbolizing defiance, while the surrounding softly recedes to represent submission. Imperfect spacing introduces an element of tension, breaking the monotony with deliberate disruptions, creating a compelling narrative of conflict.
