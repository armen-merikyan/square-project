Artist Statement

This artwork embodies a dynamic interplay between longing and resolve through a palette of muted and saturated tones. The colors reflect a sense of nostalgia (sepia and tan) intertwined with the urgency of repair (dust gray and faded blue). The core collision occurs in the center, where a denser patchwork suggests the complexity of emotions while surrounding quiet fields symbolize serenity, creating a bridge across the tension. The flow spirals outward, reinforcing this emotional narrative.
