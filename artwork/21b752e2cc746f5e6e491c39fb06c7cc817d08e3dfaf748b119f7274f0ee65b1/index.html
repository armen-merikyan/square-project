Artist Statement

This abstract piece embodies the emotional tension between love and betrayal. The chosen palette, featuring soft pinks and deep maroons, illustrates warmth clashing against darkness, symbolizing the conflict within relationships. The layout resembles an argument grid, where chaotic checker patterns representing betrayal collide with the softness of noise patterns signifying love. The sharp edges hint at emotional wounds and tension while creating a juxtaposition between the calmness of some sections and the density of others. Each pixel is intentionally placed to evoke an unresolved narrative of emotional struggle, represented via visual contrast and texture.
