Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between clarity and chaos, manifesting as a readable pulse in a sea of interference. The palette combines bright signal green and electric blue, symbolizing clarity and energy, against the static gray and dead black representing noise and confusion. The collision occurs in the center of the composition, where the clear accents interact with the darker, muted tones. This idea translates the seed into a visual narrative of tension between control and disruption, conveying the struggle of finding coherence amid distortion.
