Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between desire and restraint. The palette includes saturated reds and muted creams, emphasizing the contrast between intense longing and subdued barriers. The main collision occurs in the center, where a vibrant pulse of red contrasts against the stark blackened plum, symbolizing a desperate reach toward a bounded edge. The patterns of broken dots and ripples mirror the chaotic, yet structured conflict of these two emotional states, creating a dialogue of tension and release.
