Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a constant struggle between restlessness and peace, where quiet fields of pale colors juxtapose against intense, chaotic patches of deeper hues. The palette—yellow and orange representing the warmth and stir of motion, contrasted by deep blue and muted purple for calm and introspection—creates a visual narrative of conflict and resolution. The main contrast occurs along a diagonal axis, where channels representing restless energy intersect with broader patches that signify tranquility. The broken pixels add intentional disruptions, embodying the imperfection found in memories and emotions.
