Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork embodies a conflict between defiance and submission, symbolizing the struggle of noise against the quiet. The palette reflects a duality: 'hot_coral' and 'blood_orange' represent the vibrant defiance, while 'dust_pink' and 'charcoal' evoke softness and subtle submission. The primary contrast occurs centrally where the warm colors interrupt the muted tones, reflecting a collision of emotional intensity. This seed directed the composition to oscillate between bold and subdued areas, creating a rhythmic flow amidst juxtaposed patterns that invite a reflection on conflict and resolution.
