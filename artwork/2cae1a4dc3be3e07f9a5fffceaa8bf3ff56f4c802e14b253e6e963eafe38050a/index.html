Artist Statement

The emotional tone speaks to a duality between desire and emptiness, expressed visually through contrasting sections within the grid. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue conveys nostalgia, tension, and introspection. The palette reflects a blend of warmth and coolness, with faded tones suggesting a sense of longing amidst a hazy backdrop of emotion. Main contrast arises from the separation of clean noise and a chaotic maze, where bright and muted colors collide like bursts of pressure against quiet spaces, creating a dynamic rhythm. The placement of broken pixels introduces imperfections representative of memories that disrupt the flow, embodying the idea of continuity versus fragmented recollections.
