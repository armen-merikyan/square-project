Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of vulnerability and strength through a balanced yet disrupted symmetry, employing a palette of warm brown and shelter green to symbolize protective elements against pale skin and open blue, suggesting exposure. The main contrast occurs at the center, where pale skin disrupts the surrounding darker shades, representing a break in the shelter. The layout reflects a mirror image with intentional differences to show the conflict between stability and chaos, resulting in an abstract representation of the seed's themes.
