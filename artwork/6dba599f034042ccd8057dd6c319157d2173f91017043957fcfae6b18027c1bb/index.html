Artist Statement

This composition conveys the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a contrasting interplay of dark and light colors. The palette, inspired by 'mourning_renewal', employs deep blues and mourning black to represent a sense of yearning, while pale yellow and new leaf evoke hope and strength. The dominant presence of darker tones in the center suggests a heavy emotional core, while lighter pixels at the edges create a sense of serene emergence, mimicking the push-pull of emotions as suggested by the seed. The layout follows a halo_void structure, with the main collision concentrated towards the center, representing the clash of longing and resolve, creating an engaging visual rhythm that invites contemplation.
