Artist Statement

The composition reflects the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a palette of muted earth tones and vibrant accents. The background employs serene greens and browns to represent a sense of safety, while bursts of open blue and pale skin hues introduce urgency and the feeling of yearning. The central focus utilizes structured patterns from longing (rain) which struggles against the angular rigidity of resolve (hinge), creating a dynamic interplay. The broken pixels are strategically placed to disrupt the flow and signify imperfection, enhancing the sense of collision between emotions, particularly at the central point of the grid.
