Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through a stark palette of burnt oranges and dark reds, symbolizing warmth and rage clashing against the coolness of charcoal. The pattern's left-right split layout emphasizes the concept of symmetry vs. break, where the left contains more chaotic ring patterns indicating confusion, while the right employs flame patterns to suggest clarity. The placement of broken pixels adds visual imperfection, reinforcing the tension of unresolved emotions. The use of contrast and centrally placed colors creates an echoing pulse that speaks to the viewer's own internal conflict.
