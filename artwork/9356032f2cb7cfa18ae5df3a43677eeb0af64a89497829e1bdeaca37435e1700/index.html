Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a juxtaposition of softness and severity, represented through a palette of warm whites and cool blacks. The sharp contrast between the vibrant sharp crimson and the muted tones of steel gray emphasizes the collision of feelings. The main collision occurs in the center, where the crimson interrupts the surrounding soft colors, representing moments of tension amidst quiet. The layout reflects two pulses that create a dynamic flow, with a central focus reflecting emotional struggle and the gentle ebb of forgiveness framed by severity.
