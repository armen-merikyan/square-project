Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts 'panic' with 'control,' represented by a color palette of dark violet and soft gold against a backdrop of black and pale green. The interplay of the structured 'target' pattern for control, with random 'crack' disruptions for panic, creates an unsettling rhythm across the square. The movement from the dense center to the hollow edges mirrors the ebb and flow of tension and release, manifesting the essence of the seed's exploration of emotional balance.
