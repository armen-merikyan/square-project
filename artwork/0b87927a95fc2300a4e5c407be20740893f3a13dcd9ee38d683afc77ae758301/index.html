Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between strength and vulnerability, utilizing a palette of soft, muted colors with a pale mint and storm gray to symbolize exposure and shelter. The layout showcases a main collision in the center, where the strength pattern of rings contrasts against the vulnerability pattern of pressure bands, emphasizing the tension of incoming signals and collisions of feelings. The chosen colors establish a quiet yet striking visual rhythm that resonates with the abstract narrative of connection and barrier.
