Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a push-pull dynamic between vulnerability and strength. The palette of deep teal and cosmic indigo contrasts against the stark void black and soft star white, creating a sense of tension and balance. The main collision occurs at the center, where vibrant colors punctuate the interplay of dark edges, symbolizing the complexities of strength fighting against vulnerability. Patterns of dots signify vulnerability, while constellation-like clusters represent strength, leading to an overall feeling of interwoven forces.
