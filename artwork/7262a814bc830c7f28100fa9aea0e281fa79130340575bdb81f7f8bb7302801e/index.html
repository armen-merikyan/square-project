Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional push and pull of desire against restraint. The palette features warm reds and muted grays to contrast heightened longing with the sense of holding back. The collision of saturated and muted colors symbolizes the struggle between reaching for what we want while facing inevitable boundaries. The main contrast occurs in the center where the vibrant red hues of desire interact with darker, more restrained tones, suggesting a dynamic tension that pulls the viewer's focus inward and outward, reflecting the emotional narrative of the seed.
