Artist Statement

This work encapsulates the emotional juxtaposition of confusion and clarity through contrasting densities and unsettled patterns. Utilizing a palette of dark shades (navy and black) and a dim cyan, the stark contrasts generate a sense of depth and disturbance. The pixel arrangement oscillates between structured grid patterns representing clarity and broken, irregular placements that evoke confusion. The imbalance creates tension, suggesting a pulse of emotion beneath the surface, reflecting both rage and calm. The pixel bleed edge style further enhances the narrative of imperfection and fluidity, inviting contemplation on the intersections of these emotions.
