Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a push and pull between love and betrayal, creating a dynamic tension throughout the piece. The palette includes shades of pink and dark maroon to represent warmth and passion contrasted against gray tones symbolizing uncertainty and betrayal. The main contrast occurs vertically, where warm hues give way to colder grays, encapsulating the emotional shift. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, emphasizing the fragility of the emotions depicted, mirroring the imbalance between love and betrayal while subtly suggesting a heartbeat or signal between two entities.
