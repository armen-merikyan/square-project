Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between trust and suspicion, with a palette that reflects melancholy and uncertainty. The colors, mainly muted tones of lavender_gray and soft_cyan, provide a soft base, while the rain_blue and muted_plum introduce emotional echoes of doubt. The center features a collision of ripple patterns that suggest trust, surrounded by noise, creating a boundary that evokes suspicion. The main collision occurs centrally, creating a visual narrative of inner stability versus external pressure, leading to a balanced tension across the square.
