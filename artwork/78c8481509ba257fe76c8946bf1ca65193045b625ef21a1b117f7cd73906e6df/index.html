Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes joy and grief, using a palette of vibrant yellows and muted blues to create tension. The joy is expressed through bright colors that resonate with spark-like energy, while grief is highlighted through deep blues that evoke a sense of droop and weight. The pattern reflects an upward and downward movement: the left side radiates warmth and joy in a 'teeth' pattern, while the right side embodies a radial pattern of muted blues resembling the weight of grief. The edge style adds a sense of interruption and chaos, enhancing the emotional dichotomy.
