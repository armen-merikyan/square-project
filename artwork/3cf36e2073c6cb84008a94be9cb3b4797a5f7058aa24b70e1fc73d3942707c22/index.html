Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between quiet and pressure, symbolizing a push and pull in color. The muted yet rich palette of off white and dust gray reflects quietness, while compressed red and dark navy introduce a stark contrast, representing pressure. The main collision occurs at the top edge, where the intense colors exert a force against the soft backgrounds, creating a spiraling dynamic that unravels and releases toward the bottom. This pattern stems from the seed's exploration of clarity and confusion, representing the ebb and flow of emotions.
