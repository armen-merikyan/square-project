Artist Statement

The artwork portrays a dynamic interplay between restlessness and peace. Darker hues of charcoal and hollow blue symbolize the restless aspects, while the soft pinks and warm coral represent tranquil moments. The composition reflects a fractured harmony, where a maze of motion contrasts against a lighter lattice of peace, culminating in a central collision of warm and cool tones that pulls the viewer's eye. The seed's direction inspired the interplay of tight clusters and open spaces, marking tension and release.
