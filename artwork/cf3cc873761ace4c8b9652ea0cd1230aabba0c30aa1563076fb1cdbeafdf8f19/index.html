Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between loneliness and connection, evoking a sense of isolation disrupted by moments of warmth. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue reflects a nostalgic memory, where warm browns signify connection and cool blues symbolize loneliness. The contrast happens prominently in the top rows, where deeper colors suggest weight and pressure, while lighter hues break through in a burst pattern. This interplay creates a narrative of hidden emotions, where elements of connection seep through the facade of isolation, interpreted through a randomized grid that emphasizes vulnerability and resilience.
