Artist Statement

This abstract composition reflects the tension between quiet moments and pressing density through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette combines muted off-white and dust gray to represent quietness with intense compressed red and dark navy signaling pressure. The outer frame introduces a weighted border, representing pressure, while the inner core fades into a crosshatch of softer tones that embody calmness. The main collision occurs in the center, where a distinct shift from dark to light colors signifies the breakdown of pressure into clarity, reinforcing the seed's themes of tension and release.
