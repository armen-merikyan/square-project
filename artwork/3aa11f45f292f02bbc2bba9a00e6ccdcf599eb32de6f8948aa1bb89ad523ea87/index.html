Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dichotomy of defiance and submission through a pattern of angular resistance interspersed with softer, retreating areas. The palette of sepia, tan, dust_gray, and faded_blue flows from warm to cool tones, signifying the struggle between bold declaration and quiet surrender. The intense central area contrasts sharply with the open edges, creating a sense of pressure against the quiet border, while the inclusion of broken pixels reflects the glitches of confrontation. The pulse marks represent the rhythmic tension embedded within the chaos, establish a dialogue of struggle on the canvas.
