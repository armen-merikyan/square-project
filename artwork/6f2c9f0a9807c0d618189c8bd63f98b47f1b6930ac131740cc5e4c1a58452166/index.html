Artist Statement

This 8x8 pixel artwork captures the emotional duality of vulnerability and strength. The palette reflects guilt and forgiveness by emphasizing warm ivory and soft gold as centers of strength against deeper, more muted tones like ash gray and deep wine for vulnerability. The collision of light and dark creates a stacked band effect, highlighting the contrast between security and exposure. Broken pixels are strategically placed to disrupt, suggesting imperfection and resilience amidst the struggle between these emotions. The layered density from top to bottom suggests a flow from vulnerability at the bottom to strength at the top, echoing the complex narratives of personal experiences.
