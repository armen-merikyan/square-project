Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy between loneliness and connection. The palette features black as a nod to solitude and dark violet representing deep emotions, while soft gold and pale green introduce notes of hope and warmth. The main contrast occurs between the dark, dense clusters at the edges, suggesting isolation, and lighter, sparse areas that symbolize connection and openness. The crosshatched patterns echo the idea of connection amid separation, creating an interplay that reflects the tension in the seed's themes. The placement of colors allows for hidden versus exposed feelings, while maintaining large negative spaces that enhance the sense of loneliness.
