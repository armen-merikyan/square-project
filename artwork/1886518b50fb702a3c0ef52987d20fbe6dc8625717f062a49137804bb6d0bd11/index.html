Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tone of quiet versus pressure, using a palette with muted tones and sharp contrasts to illustrate the ebb and flow of tension. The colors flow from darker edges to lighter centers, echoing a sense of confinement that dissipates into space, aligning with the seed's themes of conflict and clarity. The collision occurs in the center, where dense areas of dark navy punctuate the softer whites and greys, creating a visual dialogue that reflects the contradicting forces of pressure and release. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, enhancing the feeling of chaos within the calm.
