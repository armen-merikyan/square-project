Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between love and betrayal, mirrored yet interrupted by misalignment. The palette—soft pinks and deep maroons—evokes warmth and anguish, establishing contrast. The central diagonal rise meets a crosshatch pattern that clashes against sharp edges, representing emotional collision. The arrangement aims to capture a feeling of layered complexity, where warmth struggles against cold, echoing the themes of the seed.
