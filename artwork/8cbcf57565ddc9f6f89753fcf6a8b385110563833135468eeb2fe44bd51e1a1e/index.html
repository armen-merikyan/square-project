Artist Statement

This composition embodies the struggle between 'signal' and 'noise' by juxtaposing a cohesive yet interrupted rhythm against a chaotic background. The chosen palette features 'signal_green' and 'electric_blue' as vibrant accents representing clarity and hope, while 'dead_black' and 'static_gray' evoke the encroaching noise that clamors for dominance. The center collision, where these colors of the pulse interact with the encircling static, illustrates a moment of conflict and connection, reflecting the emotional landscape of the seed, where structure meets chaos and beauty intertwines with disruption.
