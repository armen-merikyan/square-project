Artist Statement

The emotional tension of 'relief vs dread' is conveyed using a palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold to symbolize warmth and threat. The contrast is defined by the dense dark areas that represent dread, intersected by light punctuations of gold, providing relief and hope amid chaos. The layout flows from bottom to top, mirroring the ascent from despair to hope, while broken pixels introduce an intentional imperfection, reinforcing the theme of collision between stability and chaos.
