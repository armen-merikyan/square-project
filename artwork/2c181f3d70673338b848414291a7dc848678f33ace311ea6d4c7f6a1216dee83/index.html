Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the contrast between loneliness and connection through colors that evoke warmth alongside colder elements. The palette, comprising deep reds and soft blues, symbolizes the tension between isolation and warmth of interaction. The main contrast happens at the center, where muted shades of the grid pattern collide with vibrant bursts of red, creating a visual echo of connection amidst expanses of black and blue. The off-center placement of the bursts enhances the feeling of hidden emotions, suggesting a dialogue between the visible and the veiled, reflective of the seed themes.
