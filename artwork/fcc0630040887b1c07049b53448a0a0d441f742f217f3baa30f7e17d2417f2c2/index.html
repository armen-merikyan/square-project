Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece juxtaposes feelings of loneliness and connection, conveyed through a pattern that oscillates between crowded and sparse arrangements. The selected palette, named 'joy_grief', includes warm tones of yellow and orange that suggest connection, contrasting with the deeper blues and muted purples representing loneliness and weight. The design emphasizes an off-center single bright pixel amidst broader muted backgrounds, eliciting a sense of isolation surrounded by the potential for connection. This emotional play is further characterized by broken edges creating a fragmented appearance, enhancing the theme of connectivity versus solitude. The movement from right to left suggests a narrative arc of searching for connection, while the negative space maintains a feeling of emptiness. Overall, the composition invites the viewer to ponder the contrasts of their own emotional landscape.
