Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional interplay between longing and resolve. The palette of warm coral and cold slate establishes a dialogue between warmth and coolness, representing desire and stability. The main contrast is found at the center, where the density of colors collides amidst quieter hues, symbolizing the struggle between chaos and order. Broken pixels are deliberately scattered, suggesting the imperfections inherent in the journey, evoking a deep sense of yearning for healing amidst the sense of containment. The interplay of patterns—veiled longing versus quiet resolution—creates a rhythmic pulse across the canvas, drawing the eye in while allowing spaces for reflection.
