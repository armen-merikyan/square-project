Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vitality (spark) and decay (ash), symbolizing a struggle between hunger and restraint. The palette combines warm ivory and deep wine with a backdrop of ash gray, evoking a sense of fragility against vibrance. The left side focuses on the lively spark, represented by a ripple pattern in warm tones, while the right side contrasts with the ashen, static presence. The interplay creates a central collision where bright accents meet muted fields, emphasizing unresolved tension and emotional depth.
