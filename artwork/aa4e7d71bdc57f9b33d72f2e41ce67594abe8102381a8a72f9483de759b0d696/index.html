Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional turbulence between clarity and confusion, reflected in its contrasting palette of electric blue and magenta against a black backdrop. The jagged edges introduce interruptions that enhance the sense of disorder, while the structured grid of colors evokes a pulse—representing the rhythmic clash of emotions. The placement of lighter colors amid the darker ones portrays sporadic clarity piercing through confusion, creating tension and release. The deliberate asymmetry of color distribution mirrors the broken symmetry suggested by the seed, translating the abstract concept through visual rhythms and collisions.
