Artist Statement

This artwork embodies a tension between clarity and confusion through its color palette and arrangement. The contrasting hues of sepia and dusty gray evoke a sense of nostalgia and instability, while the structured yet chaotic layout suggests an argument between harmonious symmetry and disruptive elements. The chosen palette reflects a sense of broken memory, enhancing the emotional depth. The collision of dark tones against the lighter shades creates visual interest, inviting viewers to explore this duality, represented in the juxtaposed checker and ring patterns. Broken pixels add imperfection, symbolizing the chaos in clarity.
