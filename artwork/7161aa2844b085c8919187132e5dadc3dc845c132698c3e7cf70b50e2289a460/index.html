Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between awe and terror through a contrast of warm burnt orange and dark shades against lighter gold. The rising signal layout shifts from stable to unstable patterns, echoing chaos interspersed with moments of calm. Soft edges create fluid transitions, while broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, resonating with broken memory. The diagonal flow infuses a sense of movement, creating a dialogue between density and quiet space in 8x8 form.
