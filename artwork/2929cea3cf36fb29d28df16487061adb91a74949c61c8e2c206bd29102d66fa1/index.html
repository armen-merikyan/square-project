Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between restlessness and peace, characterized by a palette of burnt orange and dark red which evoke warmth and tension. The contrast between the softer gold and charcoal creates a dynamic boundary, suggesting a collision at the center of the square. The layout features a halo void, with a ring of darker colors surrounding lighter shades, creating a visual narrative of ebbing and flowing emotions. The broken pixels intermittently disrupt the fray, echoing the concept of memory vs motion and deepening the sense of unease.
