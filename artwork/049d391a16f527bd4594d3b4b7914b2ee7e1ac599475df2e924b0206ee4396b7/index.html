Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of 'quiet' and 'pressure.' The top half embodies quietude with soft, muted tones like off-white and faint peach, arranged in a ring pattern that suggests calm radiance. The bottom half confronts this serenity with dark navy and compressed red, organized into a flood pattern representing the dense pressure of life. The collision occurs along the horizontal line where the two emotions meet, suggesting a shattering tension that brings clarity amidst confusion. Unexpected broken pixels create imperfections, enhancing the emotional depth and reinforcing the juxtaposition between the rigid and the soft.
