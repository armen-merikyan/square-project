Artist Statement

The composition reflects the tension between quietude and pressure through a contrast of dense and soft hues. The palette consists of muted colors like off_white and dust_gray, contrasted with accent colors like compressed_red, signifying moments of disturbance. The arrangement features a concentrated cluster of dark navy on the left, embodying pressure, while lighter hues expand towards the right, capturing a sense of release and calm. This dynamic evokes a shattering effect against a backdrop of tranquility.
