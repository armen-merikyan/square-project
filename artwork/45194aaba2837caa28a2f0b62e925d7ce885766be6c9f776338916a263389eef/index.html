Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between panic and control, utilizing an asymmetrical form that conveys chaos within structure. The palette of warm yellows and oranges contrasts deeply with muted purples and deep blues, symbolizing the bright bursts of panic against the dominating calmness of control. The zigzag pattern signifies conflict, while the broken pixel concept incorporates imperfection, inviting the viewer to contemplate the dynamics of joy and grief within this emotional grid. The main collision occurs at the intersection of the vibrant and muted tones, promoting awareness of hidden depth amidst disorder.
