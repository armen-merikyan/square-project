Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between 'signal' as clarity and 'noise' as chaotic interference. The chosen palette of deep black, electric blue, vibrant green, static gray, and hot magenta creates a stark contrast that emphasizes the struggle to retain clarity amidst clutter. The patterns of islands representing noise clash against the solid rings symbolizing signal, predominantly occupying the outer border while the core reveals an intricate dance of pulses. This layout resonates with the theme of interruptions and memory, capturing the essence of moments lost in noise, visually communicating the emotional turmoil between exposure and shelter.
