Artist Statement

This composition reflects a struggle between defiance and submission, using a palette that balances the warmth of red (defiance) against the coolness of pale blue (submission). The top half features concentrated dark colors that symbolize weight and pressure, creating a tension with the lighter hues that filter in from the edges, suggesting an open space versus a heavier central mass. The flow is from left to right, mimicking the idea of noise against a frame, with broken pixels introducing fragmentation and imperfection, embodying emotional conflicts. The rows are deliberately bottom-weighted to signify the heaviness of unresolved feelings while leaving ample negative space as a nod to openness amidst chaos.
