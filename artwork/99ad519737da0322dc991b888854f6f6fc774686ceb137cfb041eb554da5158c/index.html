Artist Statement

This composition embodies the contrast between joy and grief, reflecting the emotional collision of brightness against darkness. The upper rows use vibrant yellows and oranges to signify joy, arranged in a zigzag pattern that creates a sense of movement and rising energy. The lower rows embody grief through deeper blue tones and muted purples, presenting a heavier and more fragmented appearance. The checker against spark motif introduces structure amidst chaos, offering a visual bridge between conflicting emotions. The broken pixels add imperfection, enhancing the emotional depth and complexity.
