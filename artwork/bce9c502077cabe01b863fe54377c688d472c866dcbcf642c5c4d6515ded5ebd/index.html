Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts confusion with clarity, reflected in the use of warm oranges against deep blues to create tension. The palette of yellows, oranges, muted purples, and deep blues evokes a sense of joy entwined with grief. An asymmetric pattern creates visual noise through jagged edges and broken pixels, highlighting imperfection amidst structured order. The interplay of dark and light serves as a pulse through the design, where a thick simulated outline delineates the tension between clarity and chaos, culminating in a narrative that embodies both assurance and disruption.
