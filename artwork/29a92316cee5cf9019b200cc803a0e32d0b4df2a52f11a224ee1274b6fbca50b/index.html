Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the piece encapsulates the oscillation between peace and restlessness. The palette is a blend of muted tones and accents—sepia and tan provide groundedness while faded blue introduces a flicker of energy. The main collision occurs at the center, where lively, erratic elements (embers) interweave with more serene, stable shapes (islands), creating a visually striking contrast. This arrangement reflects the tension of memory and motion, signifying a quiet stutter against a backdrop of potential movement, paralleling the dynamics of the seed.
