Artist Statement

The composition captures the conflict between defiance and submission through a careful balance of warm and cool tones, creating visual tension. The palette of hot coral, blood orange, and hollow blue reflects desire and emptiness, evoking a sense of contrast that aligns with the emotional themes of the seed. The main collision resides in the center, where the stronger 'defiance' colors (hot coral and blood orange, forming a maze) clash with the softer 'submission' tones (dust pink and charcoal, representing islands). This interplay suggests a story of pressure at the center with a quiet channel of negative space, evoking feelings of struggle and resolution.
