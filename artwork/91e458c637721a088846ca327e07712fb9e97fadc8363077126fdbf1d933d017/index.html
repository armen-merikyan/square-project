Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional conflict between restlessness and peace, representing a struggle between dense clusters of chaotic energy (represented by darker tones) and quiet expanses of serenity (lighter shades). The palette of mourning black, deep blue, and pale yellow captures the essence of deep emotional exploration, while the arrangement reveals a central quiet channel disrupted by bursts of tumultuous patterns. The main collision occurs along a diagonal flow, creating an unsettled rhythm that echoes the complexities of memory and motion.
