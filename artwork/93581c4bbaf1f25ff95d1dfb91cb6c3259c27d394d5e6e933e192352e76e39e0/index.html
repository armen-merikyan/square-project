Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between clarity and confusion. The use of electric blue and magenta creates a vibrant pulse against the stark black background, embodying a clash of calm and chaos. The placement of colors forms a near symmetry disrupted by unexpected 'broken pixels' that represent the corrupting influences of confusion. The thick outlines create a sense of weight and structure, while allowing for negative space that provides breathing room amidst the densely clustered pixels. The maze-like pattern evokes a feeling of being trapped in noise, while the rain-like sub-pattern suggests clarity peeking through.
