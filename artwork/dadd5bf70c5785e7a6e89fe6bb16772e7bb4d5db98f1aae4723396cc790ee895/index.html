Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances 'quiet' and 'pressure,' reflected in the choice of colors that oscillate between soft and intense. The palette consists of 'off_white' for a muted calm, 'dust_gray' for weighted areas, 'compressed_red' representing pressure, 'dark_navy' providing depth, and 'faint_peach' softly illuminating quiet spaces. The composition features a dynamic contrast, where the main collision occurs along the diagonal axis, fostering a visual tug-of-war between the dense clusters of deep colors and the sparse, lighter tones. The 'threshold' layout suggests a passage where chaos and calm meet, embodying the struggle depicted in the seed.
