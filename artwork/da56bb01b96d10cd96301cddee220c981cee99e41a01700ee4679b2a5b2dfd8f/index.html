Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a balance between pressure and quiet, using a palette that juxtaposes dense, darker colors with lighter accents. The arrangement forms a cluster in the bottom corner (representing pressure) that dissipates into a lighter space (representing quiet). This evokes a sense of tension and release, influenced by the dynamics of breaking points and soft boundaries. The broken pixel elements add imperfection, illustrating conflict and resolution without literal representation.
