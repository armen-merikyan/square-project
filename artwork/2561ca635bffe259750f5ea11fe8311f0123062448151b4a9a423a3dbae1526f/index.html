Artist Statement

This artwork embodies a tension between confusion and clarity through a split layout. The top half employs dark colors like charcoal and burnt orange to create a chaotic, neglected symmetry, characterized by a broken grid pattern. In contrast, the bottom half introduces lighter shades of gold and muted reds, evoking a sense of clarity and sparkling clarity under the chaos. The collisions between the vibrant colors and the dense black represent internal conflict, while the edge style signifies boundaries being pushed and redefined. This composition invites contemplation on the dynamic between order and disorder, as represented in the generated pattern.
