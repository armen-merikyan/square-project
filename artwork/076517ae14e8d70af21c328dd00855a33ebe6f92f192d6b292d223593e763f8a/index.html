Artist Statement

The artwork reflects the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The central colors burst outward in a radial pattern, representing the struggle of hidden emotions seeking exposure. The palette of deep red and gray symbolizes pain and complexity, contrasting with pops of pink for connection. The contrast lies in the center where dense colors clash with empty spaces, echoing the isolation amidst the desire for connection. This tension communicates a visual narrative of struggle and resolution, embodying the concepts of hidden vs. exposed and connection vs. separation.
