Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between clarity and chaos, reflecting the emotional battle of signal versus noise. The contrasting use of deep blacks with electric blues and vibrant magenta creates a disrupted rhythm, symbolizing the interference of static against a singular pulse. The central focus is amplified by the surrounding spark patterns, while broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, forming echoes of past memories. The piece conveys an overarching theme of a deep emotional struggle for clarity amidst overwhelming noise.
