Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts 'quiet' with 'pressure', creating a visual dialogue. The palette features muted colors like off-white and faint peach to symbolize quiet, blending with denser shades like dark navy and compressed red to represent pressure. The main collision occurs at the top-left corner, where densed clusters of spark-like patterns interrupt a quiet grid, suggesting a battle between calm and chaos. The layout creates a sense of struggle and release, mirroring the conceptual tension in the seed.
