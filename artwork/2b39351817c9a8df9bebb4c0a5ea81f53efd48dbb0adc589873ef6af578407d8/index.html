Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, reflecting an interplay of desire and determination through dense clusters and quiet spaces. The palette incorporates deep teal and cosmic indigo to evoke depth and complexity, punctuated by star white and cold gold as accents that symbolize moments of clarity. The main contrast occurs in the center, where rich saturation meets softer edges surrounding it, creating a visual collision that mirrors the struggle of intimacy and distance. The layout embodies an overfilled center with intentional broken pixels, adding imperfection and depth to the abstract narrative.
