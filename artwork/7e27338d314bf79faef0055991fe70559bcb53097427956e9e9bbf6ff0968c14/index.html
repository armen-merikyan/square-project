Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the delicate balance between trust and suspicion, represented through contrasting muted and vibrant colors. The palette of 'hunger_restraint' evokes a sense of underlying anxiety with blackened plum and red oxide, set against the soothing muted cream, which signifies trust. The dynamic placement of the 'stairs' pattern clashes with the 'static' texture, illustrating emotional interruptions and layered complexities. The main collision occurs at the center, emphasizing a struggle between openness and blockage, reflecting the emotional essence of the seed.
