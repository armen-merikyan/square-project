Artist Statement

This composition balances the nostalgia of sepia and muted tones in the top half with the dynamic, crackling energy of faded blue shades in the bottom. The palette 'broken_memory' reflects the tension between past warmth and present chill, enhancing the feeling of split emotions. The stair and crack patterns contrast across the horizontal divide, suggesting continuity and disruption. The broken pixels add imperfection, implying the fragmented nature of memories and progress. The flow zigzags between the layers, creating a rhythmic balance that reflects a pipeline of emotional currents between nostalgia and progress.
