Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional collision between envy and gratitude, using a muted color palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue to evoke feelings of nostalgia and calm amidst chaos. The contrast lies in the diagonal flow, where colors alternate between soft, quiet tones and more vibrant areas, suggesting a rhythm of rising pressure gradually released through negative space. The arrangement mirrors a sense of layered complexity, akin to memories colliding, while leaving enough quiet moments to allow for reflection.
