Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between trust and suspicion, using a distinct palette that embodies safety (shelter green, warm brown) and exposure (cold white, pale skin). The main collision occurs centrally with warmer hues disrupting cooler backgrounds, representing tension in relationships between security and doubt. The layout features stacked bands to create a flow of emotional density, contrasting radial shapes (indicating trust) with dots (indicating suspicion). Overall, this relationship suggests resilience amidst fragility, with imperfect transitions through broken pixels, representing the scars of trust.
