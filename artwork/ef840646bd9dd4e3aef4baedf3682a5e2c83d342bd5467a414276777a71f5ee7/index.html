Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the clash between longing and resolve, utilizing a palette that balances deep navy with stark white to embody distance and intimacy. The main contrast occurs in the center with a dense, dark field colliding against lighter segments, symbolizing the push and pull of emotions. This pattern flows diagonally, suggesting movement and unresolved tension, with broken pixels creating intentional disruptions that enhance the feel of imperfection intrinsic to emotional experiences. The arrangement emphasizes a mostly empty space surrounding significant color clusters, portraying an 'empty room' of emotion.
