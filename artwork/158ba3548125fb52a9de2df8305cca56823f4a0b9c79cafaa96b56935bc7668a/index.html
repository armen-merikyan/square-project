Artist Statement

The artwork embodies a juxtaposition of restlessness and peace through the use of a contrasting palette that emphasizes cold tones and bright accents. The deep teal (#005B5C) represents calmness while the void black (#000000) conveys restlessness. The diagonal layout fosters a tension between these emotions, with the lighter star white (#FFFFFF) clashing against darker hues, creating an interruption that suggests a struggle. The broken pixels add a layer of imperfection, contributing to the narrative of fragility amidst shelter. This aesthetic reflects the emotional complexity in our memories of motion and stillness.
