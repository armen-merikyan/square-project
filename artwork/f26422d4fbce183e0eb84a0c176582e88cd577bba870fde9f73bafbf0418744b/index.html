Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional conflict of confusion versus clarity through a juxtaposition of structured and chaotic elements. The palette reflects a journey from warm sepia tones to cool faded blue, symbolizing the struggle between a comforting past and a present filled with uncertainty. The main collision happens in the center, where radial patterns of confusion interrupt the grid of clarity, creating tension. The broken pixels represent unsettling imperfections, contrasting with the outlined symmetry that seeks to maintain order.
