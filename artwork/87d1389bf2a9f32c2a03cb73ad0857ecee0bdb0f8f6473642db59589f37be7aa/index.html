Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and chaos. Utilizing navy and dim cyan from the 'lonely_signal' palette evokes a sense of depth amidst disorder. Here, the navy signals a stable presence while the intermittent dim cyan sparks through, mirroring the chaos of noise. The collision of patterns—clear, sharp edges from the signal and soft dots from noise—creates a visual tension central to the grid, where imperfections are intentionally placed. This conflict illustrates the ongoing battle of maintaining signal amidst overwhelming noise.
