Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between love and betrayal through contrasting colors: warm reds and soft pinks represent love, while dark grays and maroons symbolize betrayal. The layout of battling patterns, with flame-like warmth on one side and a maze of despair on the other, illustrates a conflict. Sharp edges create a sense of fracture, while broken pixels introduce imperfection, embodying the complexities of relationships. The chosen colors and patterns evoke feelings of either warmth or cold detachment, revealing an unresolved dialogue.
