Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between emptiness and desire through color contrasts and placement. The palette balances warm soft golds with cool tones of black and dark violet to enhance emotional resonance. The external edges use darker shades that symbolize emptiness, while the inner 4x4 area features vibrant sparks of gold embodying desire, creating a collision of feelings in the center. A few broken pixels introduce imperfection, illustrating how desire disrupts a surface of emptiness, suggesting a story of longing amidst void.
