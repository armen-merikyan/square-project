Artist Statement

This abstract artwork captures the emotional tension between trust and suspicion in a spiral layout. The chosen palette of deep blacks and purples conveys a sense of looming uncertainty, while the soft gold and pale green add glimmers of hope and assurance. The primary collision occurs in the center, where the dense dark colors meet the lighter hues, suggesting a struggle between these two emotional states. The diagonal of the color placement creates a pull, reminiscent of stairs, yet soft edges represent resilience amidst that tension. The broken pixels are strategically placed to evoke a scarred feeling, reinforcing the theme of imperfection in trust. The overall design suggests a movement towards the light, reflecting a journey from suspicion into trust, allowing the viewer to engage with the underlying narrative of conflict and resolution.
