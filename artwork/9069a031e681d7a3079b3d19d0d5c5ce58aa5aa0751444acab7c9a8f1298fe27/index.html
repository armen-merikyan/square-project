Artist Statement

The emotional tone emerges from the interplay of softness and severity, where a palette of warm whites and soft pinks contrasts sharply against stark blacks and crisp grays. The main contrast occurs in the center, representing a chaotic collision between a structured maze-like pattern of severity and a gentle, flowing rain-like softness. The arrangement speaks to the tension of opposing forces, echoing the conflict of guilt and forgiveness, while utilizing broken pixels to introduce layers of imperfection.
