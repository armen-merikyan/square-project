Artist Statement

The emotional landscape of 'trust vs suspicion' is echoed through the contrasting usage of muted tones and vibrant accents, creating a feeling of tension. The palette of deep reds and grays represents the struggle between openness and doubt, while the diagonal and vertical patterns symbolize the split perspectives of these emotions. The hard shadows defined by dark colors clash with the soft glows of the lighter ones, indicating the complexity of human emotions. The composition leads the eye through a dense core with softer fields surrounding it, portraying resilience amid fragility, as the main collision occurs off-center, pulling attention while maintaining a balance of chaos outside.
