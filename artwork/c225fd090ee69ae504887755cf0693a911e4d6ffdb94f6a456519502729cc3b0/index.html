Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between awe and terror, reflected in the choice of a contrasting palette of deep blacks and bright whites alongside pale blues to suggest both calm and chaotic feelings. The layout embodies a rising signal, with pixel clusters creating a sense of pressure on the edges, while the inner pixels represent stability amidst instability. The composition functions as a visual echo, demonstrating the interplay of organized chaos versus fragmented memories through the broken pixels, enhancing the tension between the structured vertical wave and the chaotic dots.
