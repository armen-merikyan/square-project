Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette of muted gray and golden hues creates a soft, reflective space, while sharp dark colors signify the compressed strength of resolve. The arrangement features clusters of dots contrasting with structured bands, reflecting the collision of desire and firmness. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, symbolizing repair amidst damage, resonating with the essence of the seed text. The emotional flow zigzags through the square, whispering stories of yearning and determination through abstract means.
