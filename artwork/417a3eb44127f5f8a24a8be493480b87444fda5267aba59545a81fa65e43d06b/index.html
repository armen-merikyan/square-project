Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between connection and loneliness. The warm palette of burnt orange, dark red, gold, and charcoal evokes a sense of ancient warmth but also discomfort, representing the burning desire for connection surrounded by the coldness of solitude. The central area features more pronounced warmth, suggesting a core of connection, while the edges incorporate darker tones, reflecting isolation. The layout mirrors on the left and right, punctuated by areas of chaotic exposure that disrupt the grid pattern, mimicking emotional bursts amidst structured loneliness.
