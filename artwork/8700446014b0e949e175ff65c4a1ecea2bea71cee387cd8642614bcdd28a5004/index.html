Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between defiance and submission, using a palette that juxtaposes vibrant greens and muted earth tones. The central area is filled with brighter hues to symbolize defiance, while surrounding colors recede into softer shades, representing submission. The collision occurs in the center where the bright colors erupt against the darker, more subdued surroundings, creating a visual pulse that speaks to conflict yet also to the potential for harmony at the edges.
