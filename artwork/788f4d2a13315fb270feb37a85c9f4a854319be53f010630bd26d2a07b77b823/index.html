Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between dread and relief, achieved through the contrasting patterns of heavy dots representing dread in the upper region and lighter hinge patterns symbolizing relief in the lower area. This results in a layout where dark tones dominate, implying the weight of dread, while scattered hints of pale mint and faded gold offer glimpses of relief, clashing against the storm gray backdrop. The arrangement captures a spiral motion, suggesting an organic shift between confinement and openness, emphasizing the release from pressure through the gradual loosening of density.
