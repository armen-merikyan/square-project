Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional interplay between restlessness and peace. The chosen palette of soft mint and storm gray creates a soothing background (peace) contrasted with a dense, energetic grid of near black and faded gold (restlessness). The layout of a small, bright cluster in the center symbolizes moments of peace fighting against looming chaos, embodying the 'island and sea' motif. The static pattern represents interruptions of motion, while the broken pixels serve as visual echoes of pressure and intensity. The main collision occurs centrally, illustrating the tension as bright accents disrupt the surrounding calm fields.
