Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission. The dominant color palette of warm shadow and cool blue represents warmth in conflict with cool calmness, establishing a rhythmic clash. The central region emphasizes defiance with bright hues, while the edges represent submission through muted tones, creating a spiraling effect. Broken pixels deliberately introduce imperfections, symbolizing the chaotic relationship between these emotions. Central darkness collides with brighter colors to represent strong energy pushing outwards against subdued backgrounds, crafting a narrative of confrontational movement against stillness.
