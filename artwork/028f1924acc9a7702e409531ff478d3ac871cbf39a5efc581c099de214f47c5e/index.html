Artist Statement

This composition captures the struggle between clarity (signal) and confusion (noise). The palette combines vibrant accents of signal green and hot magenta with muted tones of dead black and static gray, reflecting the emotional tension of determination against chaos. The main collision occurs centrally, where the organized signal (shatter pattern) confronts the disruptive noise (stripes), symbolizing the fight for coherence within distortion. The upward flow suggests hope, yet it is laced with interruptions and shifts, creating an abstract narrative of resilience and resistance.
