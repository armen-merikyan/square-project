Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a clash between the intense feelings of envy and gratitude, translating into a jarring yet harmonious color composition. The palette consists of black and dark violet for depth and pressure, contrasted by the soft gold and pale green that evoke a sense of warmth and hope. The central theme of imbalance is illustrated through a pattern where dense colors collide with lighter ones, creating a sense of unresolved tension. An edge style of 'pixel bleed' adds to the feeling of imperfection, while leaving negative space allows for moments of quiet amidst the visual chaos.
