Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts between trust and suspicion, represented by cool lavender-gray and soft cyan juxtaposed against the darker muted plum. The palette reflects a melancholic wonder, allowing for both warmth and unease. The main contrast collides at the center, where the noise of trust subtly disrupts the negative cross of suspicion. This creates an echo of resilience amidst fragility, reflecting the struggle between openness and doubt. The seed’s theme of pressure and flow in the layout is captured in the vertical mirror that subtly raises questions of coherence and chaos.
