Artist Statement

The emotional tone hinges on the tension between love and betrayal, represented through contrasting warm and cool colors. The palette of pink and dark maroon suggests warmth and tenderness, while gray provides a somber undertone of betrayal. The composition plays with symmetry and breaks it strategically, suggesting a fractured relationship. The diagonal rise and broken pixel pattern create a feeling of disruption, while retaining negative space to highlight emotional void. The arrangement echoes the seed concepts, presenting a story of love in conflict, with sharp contrasts between soft feelings and harsh betrayals.
