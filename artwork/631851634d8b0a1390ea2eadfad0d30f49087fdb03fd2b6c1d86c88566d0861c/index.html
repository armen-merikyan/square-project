Artist Statement

This composition explores the paradox of tenderness and rage through color collisions and patterns. The palette of deep reds and blacks evokes the intensity of rage, while soft blues and whites introduce a sense of calm, representing tenderness. The main contrast occurs at the center, where jagged, deep colors clash with gentle blue frames, symbolizing an emotional struggle. The layered bands create a dynamic rhythm, echoing the inner conflicts of the seed, suggesting both confrontation and the quiet yearning for resolution.
