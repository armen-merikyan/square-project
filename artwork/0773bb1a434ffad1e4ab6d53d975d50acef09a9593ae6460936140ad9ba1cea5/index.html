Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts restlessness and peace, captured through disrupted lines and quiet visuals. The palette mirrors the struggle between muted tones and vibrant accents—dark colors represent the heaviness of restlessness, while lighter hues symbolize peace. The collision occurs in the central area, where lighter colors cut through the darker background, creating tension and movement. The seed's qualities translate into the patterns where sharp interruptions of color represent memory and motion, yet appear held within a structured grid, reflecting edges of calm amidst chaos.
