Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between the softness of the powder pink and warm white, which suggest gentleness and openness, against the severity of carbon black and sharp crimson that represent strictness and confrontation. The combination evokes a conversation between these emotions, with the central area imbued with the lighter tones while the edges embody darker contrasts. This reflects the idea of 'center_vs_edges'—the center is bright and soft, contrasted by a harsh boundary. The broken pixels, interspersed throughout the grid, add an element of imperfection, hinting at the complexity of forgiveness and guilt. Overall, the layers of color create a sense of echoing patterns, symbolizing the tension and release inherent in the emotional narratives implied by the seed.
