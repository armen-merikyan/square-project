Artist Statement

This artwork conveys a tension between longing and resolve through an interplay of color and structure. The chosen palette, consisting of 'shelter_green', 'warm_brown', 'pale_skin', 'cold_white', and 'open_blue', embodies both warmth and cool detachment, reflecting the emotional dichotomy. The layout emphasizes two central pulsations that pulse away from each other, representing the back-and-forth between desire and determination. The use of broken dots and teeth patterns further enhances this feeling of collision, creating a visual tension that feels intimate yet distant. The main contrast of pressure and quiet space occurs towards the center, which draws the eye and evokes a sense of unresolved motion. Overall, the arrangement embodies the complexity of human emotions—where moments of closeness are separated by vast quiet fields.
