Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional dichotomy between emptiness and desire, signified by a contrast of warm and cool colors. The palette combines muted earth tones with faded blues, allowing for a subtle interplay of warmth and coolness. The layout features stripes indicating desire on one half, while the target pattern in muted shades invites feelings of emptiness. The top-weighted edge style signifies stability against the overwhelming noise of emotions, creating tension. Collisions occur where warm colors abruptly intersect cool tones, representing a struggle between opposing feelings.
