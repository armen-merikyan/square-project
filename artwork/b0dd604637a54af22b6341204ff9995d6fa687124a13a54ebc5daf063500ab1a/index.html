Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional dichotomy of restlessness and peace, using a palette that represents the warmth of comfort and the coolness of unease. The dark colors provide a counterbalance to the warmer patches, highlighting the tension between memories of tranquility and the disruptive nature of motion. The central collision occurs along a diagonal, representing the argument between static peace and dynamic unrest, with broken pixels creating intentional disruptions that symbolize scars of memory. This composition invites reflection on the impact of both states, using a pattern where channels of warmth clash against a darkened background.
