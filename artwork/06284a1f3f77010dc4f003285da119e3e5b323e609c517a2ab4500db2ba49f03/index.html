Artist Statement

This composition represents the emotional struggle between desire and emptiness. The palette includes warm colors like hot coral and blood orange to evoke desire, contrasted with cool colors like charcoal and hollow blue that signify emptiness. The arrangement features a central collision of warmth, creating a sense of pressure, while cooler edges frame the composition, hinting at containment. The pattern type shatter intermingles with veil, visually illustrating the tension between overflowing desires and restricted boundaries, while broken pixels add intentional disruptions, creating an echo of longing amidst the quiet edges.
