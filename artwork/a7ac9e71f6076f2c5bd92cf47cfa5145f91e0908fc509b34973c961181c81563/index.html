Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between defiance and submission, embodied in a palette that melds melancholy and tension. The colors oscillate between soft, muted hues and sharp, bright tones symbolizing resistance. The primary collision occurs at the center, where the brighter soft_cyan and rain_blue create a stark contrast against the muted plum and lavender_gray, suggesting a confrontation of emotion. This arrangement mirrors the seed's themes by implying conflict and interaction between different states of being.
