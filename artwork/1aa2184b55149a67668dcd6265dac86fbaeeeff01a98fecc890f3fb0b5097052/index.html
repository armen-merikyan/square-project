Artist Statement

This composition explores the contrasting emotional states of joy and grief through the use of color and pattern. The palette combines muted blue tones representing grief with vibrant yellows and oranges symbolizing joy, creating a dialogue between the two. The structured outer edges reflect order and the chaotic interior showcases fragmented patterns, suggesting a collision of feelings. The placement of brighter colors in an off-center manner enhances the sense of disruption and complexity, while the use of broken pixels adds to the imperfection of the emotional experience.
