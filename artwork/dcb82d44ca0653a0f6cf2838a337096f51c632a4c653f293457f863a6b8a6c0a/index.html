Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between restlessness and peace, symbolized through contrasting colors. The palette of warm coral and cold slate reflects a struggle between comfort and anxiety. The pattern of 'teeth' embodies peace with its symmetrical elements, while the 'stairs' pattern introduces instability, especially in the center where the colors collide. Light hues create a sense of memory, while darker shades represent motion, making the overall composition deliberate but unsettled, illustrating the complex interplay of emotions expressed in the seed. Broken pixels visually disrupt the smoothness, embodying the idea of a broken harmony.
