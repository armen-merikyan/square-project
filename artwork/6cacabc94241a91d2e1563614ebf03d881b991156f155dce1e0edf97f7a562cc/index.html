Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a balance of vulnerability and strength, with a controlled chaos echoing resilience. The palette of deep navy and dim cyan serves as a backdrop for lighter areas, symbolizing exposure against darker, stronger edges. The main contrast is situated in the center, where lighter pixels meet the heavy tones, creating an interplay of pulse and retreat, reminiscent of the 'halo' shape seed encountered. This visual dance captures the tension of connection and alienation through overlapping patterns and broken pixels that add imperfection.
