Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the struggle between confusion and clarity through a dense arrangement in the top half, echoing chaotic energies with overlapping dark tones and broken patterns. The bottom half transitions into clarity with lighter tones and more structured patterns. The palette consists of navy, black, and dim cyan to evoke feelings of isolation contrasted with luminous white for clarity. The emotional collision is sharp at the midline, creating tension as heavy dark emerges into lighter hints of order. This is visually manifested by a fractured symmetry where dense pixels disrupt clarity, reflecting the theme of confusion versus clarity.
