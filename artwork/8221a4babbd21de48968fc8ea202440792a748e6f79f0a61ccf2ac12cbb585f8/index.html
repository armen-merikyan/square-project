Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve through a palette reflecting warmth and coolness, creating an intimate tension. The warm coral and soft amber colors express a constant yearning, while the cold slate and blue-black provide firm grounding. The main collision between the 'crack' (longing) and 'lattice' (resolve) patterns occurs around the center, suggesting a struggle between chaos and order. The quiet negative space channel runs horizontally, amplifying the feelings of containment versus overflow, enhancing the contemplative quality of the composition.
