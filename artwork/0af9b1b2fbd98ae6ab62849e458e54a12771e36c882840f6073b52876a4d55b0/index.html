Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between fear and hope, encapsulated in a checker and mirror pattern. The palette, consisting of dark violet, soft gold, pale green, and black, reflects the contrast of shadows against uplifting light. Central areas are dense with fear's vibrations—darker colors and checker patterns collide with the lighter, hopeful mirror effect pushing outward. The arrangement and distribution highlight an upward movement against heavy corners, creating a complex narrative around conflict and resolution.
