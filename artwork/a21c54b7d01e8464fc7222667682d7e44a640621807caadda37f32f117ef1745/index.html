Artist Statement

The piece embodies a struggle between defiance and submission, represented through a combination of deep, muted colors contrasted against sharp brighter acccents in the center. The use of 'shelter_green' and 'warm_brown' serves to establish a grounded base, while 'open_blue' acts as an invigorating spark, suggesting moments of rising tension and clarity. Bits of broken pixels introduce an element of rawness and imperfection, echoing the emotional turmoil suggested by the seed. The main collision of colors centers around the middle, demonstrating the balance between the calm of muted colors and the brightness that fights to emerge, creating a sense of tension and release.
