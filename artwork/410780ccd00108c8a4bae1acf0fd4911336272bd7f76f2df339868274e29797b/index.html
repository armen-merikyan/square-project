Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The choice of colors from the 'broken_memory' palette—sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue—evokes a nostalgic yet fragmented feeling. The contrast is pronounced in this piece, with muted tones struggling against brighter highlights to reflect the discord between clarity and confusion. The layout suggests a battle between two patterns, with darker regions appearing more concentrated in the center, while the edges subtly fade away, embodying the essence of empathy in abstract form. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, adding depth to the overall composition and echoing the theme of disruption within harmony.
