Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between safety and exposure, with an emphasis on how protective barriers can coexist with openness. The palette, featuring shelter green and warm brown, creates a warm, secure feel punctuated by cold white representing exposure. The split from left to right creates a visual tension, as 'safety' aligns with darker, heavier colors on the left, while 'exposure' lightens towards the right, creating a gentle crescendo of open space. The main collision occurs at the central vertical boundary where rich warmth meets a light, airy shell, embodying the concept of protection versus vulnerability without literal interpretation.
