Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the emotional pull between vulnerability and strength, utilizing a palette of soft pinks and stark blacks to create a visual tension. The central light colors blend with deep darks, illustrating a balance between exposure and protection. The collision occurs at the center, where the two contrasting patterns—braid for strength and checker for vulnerability—intervene and disrupt, evoking feelings of both stability and fragility. The structure retains negative space, enhancing the sense of impact and resonance.
