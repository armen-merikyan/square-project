Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between the clarity of signal and the interference of noise. The palette, drawn from navy and dim cyan against the backdrop of black and white, suggests a night sky where signals emerge dimly amidst static. The main collision occurs in the central region where the veil of pulse marks intersects with abrupt breaks representing noise, creating an emotional peak that resonates with confusion and clarity. The use of broken pixels introduces a sense of disruption, echoing the complexity inherent in memory versus erasure, while maintaining a quiet field to suggest refuge amid chaos.
