Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between control and surrender, represented through a vibrant green center that bursts with energy against a backdrop of muted earthy tones. The palette, drawn from lively greens infused with calm creams and deeper olives, signifies a balance between density and openness. The collision occurs in the center, where the bright 'ripe_green' feels encased in the heavier 'deep_olive', exemplifying the concept of overflowing versus containment. This dynamic interplay expresses the push and pull of emotion and thought, leading to an abstract interpretation of the seed.
