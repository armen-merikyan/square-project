Artist Statement

The emotional tone here juxtaposes longing and resolve through the use of earthy tones contrasted with lighter accents. The palette of stain brown and warm ivory evokes feelings of remorse and potential resolution, with soft gold providing a glimmer of hope. The main collision occurs at the center, represented by a dense cluster of the darker colors from longing, which interrupts the clearer, lighter areas representing resolve. This arrangement suggests a struggle between the two emotional states and creates visual tension yet acknowledges hope and clarity through the lighter hues. The presence of broken pixels in dense areas enhances the sense of damage and emotional scars, altering the rhythm and reinforcing the narrative of negotiation between conflicting feelings.
