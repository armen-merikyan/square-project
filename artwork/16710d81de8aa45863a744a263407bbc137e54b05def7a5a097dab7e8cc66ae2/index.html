Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between longing and resolve, showcasing a struggle to balance desire with determination. The chosen palette—comprised of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon—symbolizes deep emotional conflict, warmth, and a looming sense of melancholy. The arrangement emphasizes a zigzag flow, illustrating both a heartbeat of urgency and a steady rise, mirroring the push and pull of emotional states. Main contrasts occur at the center, where deep maroon interrupts the pink and gray, creating focal tension amidst softer fields, representing the repair and damage interplay.
