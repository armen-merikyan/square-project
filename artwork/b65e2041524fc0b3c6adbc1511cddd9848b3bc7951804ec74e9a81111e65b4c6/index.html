Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional battle between signal and noise through a dynamic use of colors. Darker shades represent the noise with a chaotic rhythm, while brighter colors highlight the clearer signals attempting to emerge. The palette of navy and dim cyan sets a lonely yet vibrant tone, while the broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, mirroring the struggle between clarity and confusion. The main collision occurs in the center, where rhythmic pulse marks meet static noise, reflecting that delicate balance between clarity and chaos.
