Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between defiance and submission, using a color palette of muted purples and vibrant yellows to create contrast. The edges are densely packed with dark colors representing submission, while the lighter tones of yellow break through, suggesting moments of defiance. The layout plays with diagonal flow, indicating movement from left to right, mirroring the struggle of emotions. This composition uses the 'stairs' pattern for defiance and 'stripes' for submission, creating a rhythm of rising tension against quiet areas, evoking a sense of unresolved conflict.
