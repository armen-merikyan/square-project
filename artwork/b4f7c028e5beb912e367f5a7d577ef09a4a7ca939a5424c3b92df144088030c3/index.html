Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork contrasts the feeling of panic and control, embodying both chaos and structure. The palette uses burnt orange and dark red to evoke intensity and urgency, contrasting these warm colors with deep charcoal to instill a sense of grounding. The arrangement features a grid pattern to represent panic, while the central wave-like formations embody a controlled response. The interplay of the broken edges and full areas creates a visual representation of conflict, while maintaining a sparse negative space that invites reflection amidst the chaos.
