Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the warmth of trust and the coldness of suspicion. The palette uses electric blue for trust's vitality and black for suspicion's depth, with magenta acting as a disruptive accent. The main contrast resides in the diagonal fall, where stable sections of soft glow contend with shadows, symbolizing the tension between safety and doubt. This was inspired by the seed themes, shaping a pattern that feels both protective and fractured.
