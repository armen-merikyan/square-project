Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between fear and hope. The palette juxtaposes the deep shadows of black and dark violet with the soft glow of pale green and soft gold, reflecting a struggle for light within darkness. The left half shows a checker pattern representing fear's chaotic nature, while the right half employs a maze pattern symbolizing hope's intricate pathways. The design resembles a zigzag flow, moving upward, while introducing collisions with disrupted pixels representing broken yet persistent aspirations.
