Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects a struggle between relief and dread, manifested through the contrasting palette of dark and light colors. The main collision occurs at the center, where storm gray pushes against faded gold, creating a tension that represents both weight and openness. This contrast is intensified by a crack pattern that evokes feelings of brokenness, while the noise pattern suggests a release. The top-heavy orientation guides the eye downward, creating a sense of slow sinking relief amidst the chaos. The placement of broken pixels adds intentional disruptions, enhancing the overall narrative of conflict and resolution.
