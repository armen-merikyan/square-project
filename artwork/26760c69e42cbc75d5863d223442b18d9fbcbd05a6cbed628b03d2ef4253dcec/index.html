Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a balance between vulnerability and strength, captured through alternating layers of soft hues and stark contrasts. The palette, dubbed 'fear_hope,' features dark violet to represent strength and control, with pale green symbolizing vulnerability and exposure. The collision prominently occurs in the center, where deep black and dark violet layers meet lighter tones, illustrating a tension between the protective edges and the exposed core. Patterns of hinge and echo interplay, creating a feeling of rising signal and quiet shelter. This artwork is a metaphor for the juxtaposition of challenges faced while seeking connection amidst isolation.
