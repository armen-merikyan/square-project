Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between the lively energy of 'spark' and the subdued decay of 'ash.' The chosen palette merges vibrant green and gold to represent hope and vitality, contrasting with muted olive and cream, embodying restraint and fading memories. The central collision, marked by a jagged burst in the lower center, echoes the diagonal rise flow, creating a dynamic yet fragile interplay. This arrangement reflects the conflict and coexistence of resilience and fragility, with dichotomous patterns representing the push and pull of life's experiences.
