Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the constant struggle between panic and control, evoking feelings through color collisions and contrasts. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue introduces a broken memory motif, balancing warmth and coolness to illustrate the chaotic yet structured nature of the subject. The primary contrast occurs between the dense clusters of dark colors, symbolizing chaos and panic, and the negative spaces incorporated, allowing for moments of calm reflective of control. The layout initiates a diagonal movement, invoking rising and falling emotions, while broken pixels subtly interrupt the pattern, symbolizing imperfections in the state of mind. Overall, this piece captures an abstract narrative of emotional conflict with rhythm and intensity through strategic color placement.
