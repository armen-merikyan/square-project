Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between longing and resolve. The contrast is centered as a small core of warm rust and bone white, suggesting strength amidst vulnerability. The palette evokes resilience, using muted tones like moss green and stone gray, interspersed with bursts of warmth that pulsate like distant memories. The broken pixel touches are scattered, creating intentional disruptions in the grid structure, echoing the feelings of repair against damage. The flow is flickering, suggesting moments of quiet against intense pressure, while the layout embodies a sense of order and chaos through the patterns of longing and resolve. The emotional story unfolds through the interplay of calm and urgency.
