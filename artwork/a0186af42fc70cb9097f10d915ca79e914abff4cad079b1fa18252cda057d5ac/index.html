Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a juxtaposition of hard edges and soft transitions. The dark maroon and gray serve as the grounded strengths, while the lighter pink and red highlight subtle vulnerabilities as they collide. The pattern represents exposure at the center facing against the stable yet eroded edges that bleed inward, showing a push-pull dynamic. This reflects the complex emotional landscape of connection and alienation within the chosen themes.
