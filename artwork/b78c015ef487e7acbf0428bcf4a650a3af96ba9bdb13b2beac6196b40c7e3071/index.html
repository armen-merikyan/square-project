Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional struggle between restlessness and peace, with a palette capturing both tension and tranquility. The warm tones of orange and muted purple suggest calmness, while the deep blues introduce an unsettling depth of motion. The diagonal contrasts create a sense of dynamic tension, with the brighter elements interrupting the quieter background to represent fleeting memories clashing against the urge to rest. The layout evokes a rising signal, symbolizing hope yet underscored by a backdrop of unresolved feelings.
