Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between trust and suspicion through color placement and contrast. The palette of yellow and muted purple embodies warmth and uncertainty. The diagonal flow represents the split between the two emotions, with trust glowing in soft ring patterns and suspicion manifesting as interruptions through stair-like shapes. The main collision occurs in the center, where the contrasting energies meet, symbolizing the struggle between stability and doubt.
