Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the contrast between feelings of loneliness and connection. The palette, consisting of soft gold, dark violet, and pale green, creates a visual representation of warmth against coolness, embodying the tension between isolation and interaction. The arrangement of colors suggests a center that is both protected and overwhelmed, evoking the concept of hidden moments of connection within a dominant sense of separation. The use of broken pixels adds a sense of imperfection and disruption, reinforcing themes of exposure versus concealment. The pattern flows outward, with darker shades representing loneliness gradually yielding to lighter colors of connection, creating a rhythmic echo of emotional depth.
