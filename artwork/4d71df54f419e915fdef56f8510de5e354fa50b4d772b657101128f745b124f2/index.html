Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional fulcrum between longing and resolve. The upper half features a radial pattern symbolizing yearning, conveyed through soft shades of powder pink and warm white, suggesting warmth and intimacy. The bottom reflects resolve with a rain pattern in stronger steel gray and carbon black, signaling stability and firmness. The main collision occurs at the dividing line, illustrating the tension between these two emotions. Broken pixels scattered throughout represent disruptions in the smooth flow, embodying the theme of repair versus damage and the intricacy of the emotional tapestry.
