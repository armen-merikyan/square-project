Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece juxtaposes vulnerability and strength, with a specific focus on collision and contrast. The chosen palette, 'lonely_signal', uses dark navy and black as a dense base, emphasizing strength, while sharp accents of dim cyan add vulnerability in the center. The main contrast emerges at the core, illustrating an exposed light against a thick, rooted exterior. Interruption is found in the broken pixels scattered throughout, symbolizing chaos within order, which embodies the struggle between the inner core and outer frame.
