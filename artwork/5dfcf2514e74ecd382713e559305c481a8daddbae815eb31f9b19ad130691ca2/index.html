Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between defiance and submission, expressed through a palette of pinks and dark maroons symbolizing love and betrayal. The layout features a heavier outer frame contrasting with a lighter inner core, creating dynamics of tension. The top-to-bottom flow emphasizes a cascading effect, embodying falling pixels that hint at a gradual descent, while the pixel bleed edge style adds imperfection and movement, further enhancing the emotional clash. The arrangement creates stark contrasts and overlaps, suggesting unresolved conflict and moments of pause.
