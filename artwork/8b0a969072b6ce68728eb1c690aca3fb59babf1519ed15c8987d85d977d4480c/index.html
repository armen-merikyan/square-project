Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between trust and suspicion. The chosen palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon reflects warmth and caution, evoking feelings of resilience entwined with fragility. The use of a diagonal flow and a 'center_seed' layout creates a tension point at the center, where lighter colors (trust) clash with darker edges (suspicion), depicting pressure at the border. This arrangement gives an impression of soft enclosure interrupted by an edge of uncertainty, suggesting both possibility and threat within an abstract narrative.
