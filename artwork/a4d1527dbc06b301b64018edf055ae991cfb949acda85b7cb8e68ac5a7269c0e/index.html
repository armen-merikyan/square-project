Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a tense exploration of love and betrayal, depicted through contrasting warm and cool colors. The palette includes deep pinks and reds symbolizing love, alongside dark grays and maroons to evoke betrayal. The main conflict occurs where warm colors clash against cooler tones, creating an abrupt intersection. The layout emphasizes diagonal movement and fractured symmetry, embodying the tension of the emotional seed, producing an abstract dialogue on connection and separation in an empty space.
