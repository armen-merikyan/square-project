Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the struggle between clarity and interference in color form. The dark palette represents noise and chaos, creating a static backdrop while lighter, clearer colors pierce through the darkness, symbolizing the signal attempting to emerge. The corner invasion sets a dynamic pressure, creating a visual tension that disrupts serenity. The bright edges signify memories, acting as accents amid the muted background, representing moments of clarity in a sea of confusion. The arrangement intentionally leaves negative space, suggesting areas of quiet amidst the chaos.
