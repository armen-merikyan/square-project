Artist Statement

This pixel artwork embodies the duality of confusion versus clarity through a stark contrast of colors and patterns. The left side, representing confusion, employs a mirrored pattern that disrupts itself, while the right side uses crosshatching to denote clarity. The color palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes tension between depth and light, while maintaining a narrow color flow that radiates from the center. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, echoing the theme of unresolved conflicts within a structured layout. The piece culminates in a visual representation of how clarity can sometimes emerge from chaos, with space that invites contemplation amidst the clustered design.
