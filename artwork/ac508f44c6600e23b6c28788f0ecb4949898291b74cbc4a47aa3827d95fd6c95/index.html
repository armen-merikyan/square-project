Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between trust and suspicion through a rising vertical flow. Warm pinks contrast against dark maroon and muted grays, embodying hope versus doubt. The center features a strong split of colors resembling the noise that interrupts the signal of trust. Broken pixels signify disruption and fragility amidst resilience, giving a visual pulse to the composition. Soft glow colors suggest warmth while hard shadows convey depth, creating a dialogue of emotional collision across the canvas.
