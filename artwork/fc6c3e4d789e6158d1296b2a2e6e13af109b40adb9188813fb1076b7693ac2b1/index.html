Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes confusion against clarity, reflected in the color palette of electric blue, lime, and black contrasting with the lighter magenta. The layout mimics falling pixels, creating a rhythmic descent that implies a flowing tension. The thick outlines emphasize boundaries, while broken pixels suggest imperfections, creating a sense of disruption. The interplay of warm (lime, magenta) against cool (electric blue, black) evokes a dynamic emotional landscape, encapsulating moments of clarity amidst chaotic disruption.
