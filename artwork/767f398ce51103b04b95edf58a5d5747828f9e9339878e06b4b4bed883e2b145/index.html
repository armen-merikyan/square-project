Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a balance between trust and suspicion, creating a visual tension through contrasting patterns. The palette, primarily featuring deep blacks and dark violets, evokes a sense of suspense (suspicion) against softer golds and pale greens that represent warmth (trust). The collision occurs through the barcode pattern of suspicion disrupting the fault line of trust, emphasizing the push-pull dynamic. The layout's broken frame suggests leaning on edges of vulnerability while leaving spaces that invite contemplation, allowing both emotions to coexist within the square's structure. Broken pixels strategically interrupt the flow, reinforcing the rawness of the emotional experience.
