Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between joy and grief through a contrasting palette and an upward movement of colors. The use of yellow and orange represents joy's spark and expansion, while muted purple and deep blue reflect the heaviness of grief. The zigzag flow interlaces bright elements with darker tones, creating a collision between open and closed spaces, suggesting a struggle between hope and despair. The broken pixels enhance the feeling of imperfection, bridging the two emotions in a unified abstract pattern.
