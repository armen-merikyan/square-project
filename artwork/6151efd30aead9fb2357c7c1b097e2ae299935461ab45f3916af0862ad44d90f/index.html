Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional dance between restlessness (represented through jagged patterns and high-contrast colors) and peace (expressed with smoother lines and softer shades). The palette of pale mint and faded gold provides a gentle backdrop while storm gray adds tension. The interplay between the dark and light tones conveys a feeling of conflict, with darker edges crawling inward, suggesting confinement, while lighter patches signal an escape toward tranquility. The main collision occurs diagonally, where darker shades meet lighter patterns, creating both disruption and harmony, echoing the seed's theme of memory versus motion, and the quiet U-turn from chaos to calm.
