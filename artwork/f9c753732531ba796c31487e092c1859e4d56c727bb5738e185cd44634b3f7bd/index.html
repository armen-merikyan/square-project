Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette, inspired by earthy tones, evokes a sense of muted memories—sepia and tan echo the past, while faded blue adds a hint of yearning. The main contrast lies in the pulse of deeper colors against the lighter, more desolate spaces, symbolizing the fractured nature of communication and emotional connection. The arrangement of colors represents the dynamic interplay of pressure and release, creating a narrative of disruption and vulnerability in a grid format, emphasizing the corners where tension is most felt.
