Artist Statement

This artwork captures the push and pull between desire and emptiness through contrasting saturated warm colors against muted cool depths, embodied in a central area of intensity clashing with surrounding quietness. The palette reflects longing and absence using hot coral and hollow blue, while the layout introduces tension where the two emotional patterns collide. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfection and echoes the struggle inherent in the emotional themes.
