Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece contrasts the calmness of peace with the unease of restlessness. The chosen palette of pale aqua and mist gray symbolizes serenity, while alarm red and ink blue evoke urgency and tension. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the barcode pattern representing peace overlaps with the sharp 'teeth' pattern of restlessness, creating an expressive tension that delineates quiet versus dynamic movement. Negative space is used intentionally to emphasize the contrast, allowing for a sense of suffocation amidst the serenity.
