Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between dread and relief, represented through contrasting palettes. The palette of warm pink and red (relief) is juxtaposed with dark maroon and gray (dread), emphasizing the emotional collision. This pattern incorporates a symmetry of expectations in the left half against a breakdown of form on the right, with the radial pattern bleeding into the maze layout, creating a narrative of tension and release. The 'light vs weight' theme is communicated through the contrast of bright colors struggling against dark backgrounds, allowing space for reflection and confrontation.
