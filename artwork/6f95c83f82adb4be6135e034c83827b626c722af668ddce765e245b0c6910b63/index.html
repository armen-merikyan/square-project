Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional turmoil of defiance and submission through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of electric blue and magenta conveys vibrancy and conflict, while black serves as a grounding presence representing weight and structure. The sharp vs. soft contrast highlights the struggle between opposing emotions, with the dominant diagonal flow suggesting movement and tension. The arrangement reflects a tightly woven narrative of interruption and balance, embodying the juxtaposed experiences articulated by the seed.
