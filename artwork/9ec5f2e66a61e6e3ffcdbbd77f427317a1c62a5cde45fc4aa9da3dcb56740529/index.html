Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette of 'softness_severity' blends gentle pinks and warm whites with stark blacks and grays, capturing the contrast between yearning warmth and a grounded resolve. The central collision occurs diagonally across the square, where vibrant accents of sharp crimson disrupt the calmer fields of powder pink and warm white. This tension reflects the emotional cycle of reaching for intimacy amidst the boundaries set by resolve, creating a rhythm that flows outward from the center. The broken pixels introduce intentional chaos, enhancing the sense of longing while maintaining a semblance of order.
