Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between clarity and disruption, using a palette rich in contrasts. The dark tones represent noise and interference, while the vibrant greens and magentas signify the boldness of the signal trying to break through. The central pulse emerges from the chaos, illustrating the conflict inherent in the seed's themes of exposure versus shelter, with the main collision at the center where dark and bright interact. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, mimicking the underlying struggle between memory and erasure. Each color placement is a deliberate choice to evoke rhythm and draw the viewer's eye across the square, highlighting the narrative of survival against overwhelming noise.
