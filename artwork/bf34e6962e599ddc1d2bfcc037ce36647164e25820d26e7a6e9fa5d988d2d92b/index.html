Artist Statement

This artwork expresses the dynamic between pressure and quiet through contrasting colors, representing shame and pride. The palette combines electric blues and vibrant magentas that clash against deep blacks to create tension. The upward and downward flow reflects the emotional rhythm of rising hope against falling doubt. The hard edges deliver a sense of clarity amidst the emotional collisions, while broken pixels introduce imperfection, allowing for moments of quiet amidst chaos.
