Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tensions of 'defiance' and 'submission' through a contrasting palette of deep navy and soft white, symbolizing the struggle between resistance and acceptance. The dense central cluster bursts outward with muted tones, interrupted by occasional bright sparks to signify moments of defiance. The lower density elements represent submission, gradually fading into the periphery. This interplay of light and dark mirrors the conflict inherent in the seed, creating a visual narrative of struggle and release.
