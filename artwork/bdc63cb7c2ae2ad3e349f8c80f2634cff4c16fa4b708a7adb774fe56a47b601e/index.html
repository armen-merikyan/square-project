Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the tension between softness and severity. The palette blends warm whites and soft pinks to signify gentleness and subtlety, countered with carbon black and steel gray to embody strictness and coldness. The main contrast is found at the center, where the pulse marks of soft hues collide with harsh, defined boundaries, creating a dynamic interplay that captures the imagined spiral motion of emotion described in the seed. This abstract representation invites contemplation of emotional depth without literal forms.
