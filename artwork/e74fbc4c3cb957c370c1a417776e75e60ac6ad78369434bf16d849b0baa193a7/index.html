Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between signal and noise woven throughout the seed. The palette combines the vibrancy of signal green and electric blue with the depth of dead black and static gray. The central area is intentionally left sparse, highlighting the contrast where clear pathways of green reflect moments of clarity surrounded by magenta and gray noise patterns. This echoing conflict of emotions translates into a pattern that collides in the center, radiating outwards to emphasize the struggle between pressure and release.
