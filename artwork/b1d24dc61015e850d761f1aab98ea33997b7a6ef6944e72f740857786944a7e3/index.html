Artist Statement

The piece embodies the tension between defiance and submission, utilizing a palette of sepia tones to evoke a nostalgic yet charged emotional state. The contrast is centered, with bold bright colors disrupted by muted shades, creating a pulse of interaction. The uneven border suggests an open channel, while the patterns collide, illustrating the dynamic interplay between control and chaos as dictated by the seed.
