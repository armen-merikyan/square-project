Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve through contrasting densities and rhythmic patterns. The palette of warm ivory, deep wine, and soft gold represents the interplay of warmth and conflict. The collision occurs primarily at the center, where the deep wine stacks disrupt the flowing soft gold stripes of longing. This clash symbolizes repair amid damage, echoing the emotional complexity of the seed. The asymmetric arrangement creates a sense of unresolved motion, suggesting a struggle for balance while preserving negative space for quietness.
