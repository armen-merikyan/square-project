Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrasts of joy and grief. The color palette features vibrant yellows and muted purples, where the former signifies joy and the latter indicates grief. The arrangement mirrors the struggle between stability and chaos, as symmetrical rings of joy clash against fragmented, chaotic dots representing grief. The pulse of emotion is captured through the density of colors in specific areas, allowing for quiet interruptions, while maintaining the necessary negative spaces to evoke feelings of abandonment and hope simultaneously.
