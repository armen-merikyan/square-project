Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between love and betrayal through a fragmented composition. The warm pinks and reds symbolize love, while the muted grays and dark maroon invoke a sense of betrayal. The layout reflects a nearly mirrored image, where subtle distortions introduce disharmony. Main contrast emerges in the central area, where warm colors collide with cooler tones, suggesting tension between opposing feelings. The arrangement creates a rhythm of calm and chaos, inviting reflection on emotional complexities.
