Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between nostalgia and progress, with a palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue to evoke a sense of memory versus the clarity of forward movement. This arrangement emphasizes a vertical split, symbolizing the divide between past and future, while the soft edges create a sense of calm within the chaos. The progression of darker shades to lighter ones introduces a rhythm of rise and fall, echoing the conflicts suggested by the seed. The deliberate placement of broken pixels adds imperfection, embodying the tumult of emotions and feelings of disruption in the pursuit of progress.
