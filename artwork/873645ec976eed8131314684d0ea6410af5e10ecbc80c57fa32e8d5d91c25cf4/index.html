Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension of defiance versus submission with a split horizontal layout. The top half represents defiance through aggressive dark reds and burnt oranges in a barcode pattern, suggesting upward motion and resistance. The bottom half symbolizes submission using soft echoes of charcoal and muted gold colors, evoking a sense of receding and enclosure. The central collision area utilizes overlapping patterns that interrupt the flow, maintaining a dynamic contrast between the two emotions. Broken pixels create intentional disruptions, enhancing the feeling of conflict woven throughout the piece.
