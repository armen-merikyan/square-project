Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances envy and gratitude, represented through warm amber and cool muted purples. The palette of yellow and deep blue underscores both warmth and depth, suggesting an interplay between tension and calmness. The main contrast appears between structured grid patterns of envy on the left and the more chaotic (yet framed) sections representing gratitude on the right. The zigzag-like distribution of colors creates dynamic energy, while broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection. Negative space offers a quiet resolution, preventing overwhelming density and reflecting the seed's themes of balance and interruption.
