Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between fear and hope through a layered interplay of dark and light. The dark palette represents fear, with jagged edges and dense patterns, while soft gold and pale green signify hope, providing a glowing contrast. The arrangement focuses on a few bright pixels amidst darker, heavier blocks, creating a visual representation of rising signals breaking through pressure. The zigzag pattern and broken pixels evoke the emotional complexity of resilience against turmoil.
