Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between love and betrayal, utilizing a contrasting palette of soft pinks and deep maroons to manifest warmth intertwined with harsh edges. The left side is dominated by circular motifs representing love, while the right incorporates chaotic dot patterns symbolizing betrayal. The collision occurs at the center, where warm colors meet dark tones, accentuated by broken pixel placements that evoke imperfection and fragmentation. This embodies the struggle of the emotions portrayed in the seed.
