Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines longing's gentle stretches with resolve's firm structures. The palette elicits a sense of faded memory, layering sepia and faded blue to create depth and distance. The collision occurs in the center where the intense sepia flood meets contained muted tones, embodying the tension between holding onto fragments and the pull of moving forward. The pattern reflects urgency and serenity, with patches interrupting the quiet fields, suggesting a journey through emotional conflict.
