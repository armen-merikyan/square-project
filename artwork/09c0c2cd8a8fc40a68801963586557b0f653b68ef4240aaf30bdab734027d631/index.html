Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the collision between structured control and soft surrender, using the palette of 'clarity_confusion' to evoke a sense of pressure against quiet spaces. Dark hues dominate to reflect control, while bright accents symbolize moments of release. The prominent contrast occurs in the center, where a burst of color meets dense black, representing a confrontation of these opposing emotions. The flow and patterns echo feelings of movement and stanzas of tension throughout the piece.
