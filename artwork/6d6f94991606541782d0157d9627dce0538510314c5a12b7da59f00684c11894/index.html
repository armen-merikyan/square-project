Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between safety and exposure, manifesting in a layout where the saturated center suggests intensity and the borders indicate a struggle to contain that pressure. The selected palette—a blend of soft greens, warm browns, and crisp whites—illustrates protection amidst vulnerability. The density in the center (dominant colors: pale skin, cold white) contrasts sharply with the muted tones at the edges, emphasizing the theme of sanctuary versus openness. The pattern interweaves checker and threshold dynamics, creating a visual echo that depicts the conflict between these two emotional states.
