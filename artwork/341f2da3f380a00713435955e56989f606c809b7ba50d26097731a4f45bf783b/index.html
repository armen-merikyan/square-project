Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting color densities and placements. The palette of shelter green and warm brown evokes safety while cold white and open blue represent exposure and lightness. The strong pulse marks and soft scars of the patterns collide, creating a visual representation of the fragility and resilience within relationships as they interplay in the center dominated by saturation. The edges contrast with their lighter, more vulnerable colors, capturing the essence of the struggle depicted in the seed.
