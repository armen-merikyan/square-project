Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances nostalgia with progress. The palette, featuring soft gold and dark violet, juxtaposes warmth against deeper tones to evoke a sense of warmth and complexity. The top half utilizes a striped pattern to reflect nostalgia, while the bottom half adopts a ring pattern to represent progress, creating a rhythmic interplay. Main contrasts surface from the collision between warmer hues and the grounding darkness, illustrating a transition from comforting memories to forward momentum, suggesting both unity and conflict in the pursuit of change.
