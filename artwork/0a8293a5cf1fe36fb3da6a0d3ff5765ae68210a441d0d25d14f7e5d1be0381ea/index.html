Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a juxtaposition of dense pressure versus muted quietude. The palette is a spectrum from dark navy and compressed red symbolizing pressure to faint peach and off-white suggesting a calm field. The main contrast occurs at the diagonal, where dense, echo-like patterns fade into calmer regions, interrupted by broken pixels that symbolize shatters and edge transitions. The inner 4x4 suggests a struggle, while the outer bands circle back into a quiet state, reflecting the seed's themes of conflict and resolution.
