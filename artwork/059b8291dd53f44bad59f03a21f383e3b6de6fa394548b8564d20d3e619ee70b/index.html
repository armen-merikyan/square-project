Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between loneliness and connection. The palette of yellow and muted purple evokes warmth and grief, symbolizing a struggle between the two states. Interaction of colors top left to bottom right creates a sharp contrast that suggests a movement between isolation and a yearning for connecting, echoing the seed's themes. The broken pixels scattered throughout embody the imperfections of memory and experience, enhancing the abstract narrative.
