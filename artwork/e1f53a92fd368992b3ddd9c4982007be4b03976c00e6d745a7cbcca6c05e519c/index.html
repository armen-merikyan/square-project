Artist Statement

This artwork symbolizes the tension between control and surrender, employing a palette that juxtaposes warm tones with cool ones, reflecting the emotional struggle. The warm colors represent control, yearning to contain, while the cooler shades embody surrender, promoting release. The central area, filled with vibrant warm colors, creates an overflow effect, clashing with the cooler outer hues that strive to contain the intensity. The asymmetric arrangement adds to the sense of dynamic movement as the warm hues pulse outward, interrupted by soft, cool accents, creating a visual echo of pressure and release.
