Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines longing's distant warmth and unfinished approach with resolve's steady rise and locked shapes. The palette features pale mint and soft sky to evoke a sense of hope against the storm gray and near black for contrast, symbolizing chaos. The layout forms two pulses in dynamic opposition, suggesting tension as they rise against each other through the grid. The pattern mixes mirror images of warmth and resolution, with intentional broken pixels adding a sense of imperfection and depth, reflecting the emotional conflict intrinsic to the seed.
