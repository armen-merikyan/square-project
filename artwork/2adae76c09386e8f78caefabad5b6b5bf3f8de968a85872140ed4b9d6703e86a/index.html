Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends longing and resolve, representing an interplay between steady firmness and wistful yearning. The palette of pink, dark maroon, gray, and red evokes a sense of warmth and intensity. The primary contrast occurs in the center, where dense flames clash with the stretched barcodes, echoing emotional tension. This contrast portrays the struggle between repair and damage, symbolized by broken pixels that add intentional imperfections to the composition while preserving a sense of containing an overflow. The arrangement reflects a burst of emotions, suggesting both serenity and urgency through the layered patterns that repeat but never quite resolve into clarity.
