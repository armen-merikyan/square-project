Artist Statement

The contrasting emotional tones of awe and terror are reflected through a balanced yet tension-filled palette of warm yellows and deep blues, symbolizing the clash between stability and disruption. The left side embodies order with a smooth gradient pattern representing awe, while the right disrupts with broken dots symbolizing terror. This division suggests an ongoing battle for control, with sharp contrasts emphasizing this struggle. The edges are softened to suggest both fragility and complexity, creating a rich narrative of contrast.
