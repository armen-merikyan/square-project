Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a contrasting color palette of warm rust and soft bone white against a backdrop of moss green and stone gray. The central pixels evoke an enclosed field, representing submission, while the surrounding area exhibits the flickering push of defiance, creating a layered dynamic. The structure flows from a quiet core to busy edges, embodying the theme of a protective center under pressure from surrounding negativity and echoing disturbances, reflecting a noise of both strength and fragility.
