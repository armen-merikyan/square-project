Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the tension between restlessness and peace, resulting in a dynamic interplay of colors. The chosen palette of soft cyan, lavender gray, and muted plum evokes a melancholy wonder while also suggesting hidden energy. The contrast is sharp between the calming horizontal barcode patterns and turbulent radial bursts that suggest motion and memory in a diagonal formation. The main collision occurs in the center, where the radial disturbance meets the horizontal calm, symbolizing an unresolved tension that leaves space for reflection, thus playing on the seed's themes of movement and stillness.
