Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between defiance and submission through a contrasting palette of rich reds and grays, symbolizing conflict and resolution. The interplay of angular resistance (defiance represented by vibrant reds) and subdued calm (submission in soft grays) creates a visually dynamic composition. The layout evokes an 'open_gate,' suggesting an entry of chaotic emotions spilling into a quiet space. The central area employs a broken pixel pattern to disrupt the order, highlighting the pulse of tension versus tranquility, mimicking the rhythmic clashes of life.
