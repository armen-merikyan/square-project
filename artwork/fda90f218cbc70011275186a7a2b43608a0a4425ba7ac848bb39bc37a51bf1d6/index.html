Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through a radial pattern, reflecting the isolation that can exist at one’s core surrounded by a chaotic outer world. The palette of deep maroon, soft pink, gray, and vibrant red symbolizes the contrast between warmth and solitude. The top-weighted composition creates a sense of pressure and an impending burst, while the asymmetrically broken structure offers a visual echo, symbolizing the collision of emotional states. The arrangement of colors suggests overlapping feelings of hidden and exposed elements, emphasizing the fragility of connection amidst loneliness.
