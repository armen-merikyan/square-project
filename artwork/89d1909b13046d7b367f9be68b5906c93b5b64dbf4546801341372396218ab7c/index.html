Artist Statement

The emotional tone is captured by contrasting loneliness and connection, depicted through a palette of deep blacks and soft golds. The warmer tones in the center represent connection, while the cooler and darker shades evoke loneliness, manifesting in a checkerboard pattern to illustrate interaction. The central cluster is dense, suggesting intensity, while broken pixels disrupt the harmony, embodying the seed's notion of fractured connections. The arrangement flows outward from a weighted center to the edges, simulating a push and pull between feelings as they collide and coexist.
