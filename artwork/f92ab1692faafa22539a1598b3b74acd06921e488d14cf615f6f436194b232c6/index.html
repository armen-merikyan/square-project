Artist Statement

The artwork encapsulates the tension between explosive, fiery elements and tranquil, subdued spaces, embodying emotional contrasts through vivid and muted colors. The palette consists of bold reds to symbolize rage intersecting with calm pale blues and whites, reflecting eroded symmetry and disrupted noise. The main collision occurs at the center, where sharp edges of red remind us of agitated energy amidst a backdrop of cooler shades, effectively depicting a struggle between contained emotions and overwhelming sensations. The layout features stacked bands, subtly transitioning from calm to intense, guided by the patterns reflecting ash fields and threshold waves, which embody the visceral nature of the seed.
