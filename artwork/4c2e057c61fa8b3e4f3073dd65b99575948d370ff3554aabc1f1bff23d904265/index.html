Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between safety and exposure. The palette, featuring soft greens and warm browns, evokes a sense of stability while contrasting with pale and cold tones that suggest vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center where patterns of channels and glitches intertwine, representing the fragile balance between protection and exposure. The rising signal flow leads the eye upward, instilling a sense of hope amidst the complexities of safety and risk.
