Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between restlessness and peace through a diagonal pattern that distorts symmetrically organized segments with erratic color placement. The palette blends soft pastels with stark dark tones, representing the clash between chaos (darker shades) and calm (lighter hues). The focus of contrast occurs at the diagonal’s intersection, symbolizing a disruption in flow, where the lighter colors attempt to breach through the heavy, restless darker sections, suggesting an ongoing struggle.
