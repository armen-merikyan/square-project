Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the emotional duality of longing and resolve through contrasting color blocks and interrupted patterns. The palette is dominated by warm shadows and cool blues, signifying the push and pull of desire and determination. The use of jagged edges and broken pixels creates a sense of imperfection, mirroring the frailty of longing amid a backdrop of resolve. The main collision occurs at the center, where warm tones wrestle with cooler hues, representing the clash between emotional urgency and serene acceptance.
