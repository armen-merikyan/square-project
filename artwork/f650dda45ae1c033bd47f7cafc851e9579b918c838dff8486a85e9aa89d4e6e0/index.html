Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette combines earthy tones of moss green and rust with the starkness of bone white, creating a contrast between the organic and the structured. The layout features two pulsating centers represented by the organized pixel clusters clashing against quiet fields of neutral tones. The glitch pattern for longing and the veil for resolve collide disruptively, reflecting inner turmoil and the pursuit of clarity amidst decay. The broken pixels serve as intentional marks of damage and repair, illustrating the intricate weave of resilience and vulnerability.
