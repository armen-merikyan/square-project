Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The chosen palette, featuring black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green, represents the interplay of shadow and light, underpinning the contrast between chaos and order. The flow of rising pixels from bottom to top signifies hope amidst disarray, while the checker pattern embodies confusion, interrupted by stripes of clarity. The main collision occurs between the dark and warm tones, evoking a sense of dynamic motion and unresolved themes of clarity struggling against disarray. The arrangement is intentionally asymmetrical, creating a visual narrative of tension and release, where the corrupted symmetry encourages deeper contemplation.
