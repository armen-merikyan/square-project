Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional duality of confusion and clarity through a juxtapositional color scheme and pattern flow. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes nostalgia while embodying a sense of fractured memory. The central disparity is highlighted through thick edges that define the areas of density versus negative space, echoing the 'teeth' pattern of confusion against the clarity of a more open layout. A deliberate choice of broken pixels adds to the imperfection, enhancing the narrative tension. Patterns radiate outward, suggesting a drift from chaotic center to structured edges, encapsulating the emotional journey.
