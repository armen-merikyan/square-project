Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'longing vs resolve' is reflected through an interplay of warm and cool tones, symbolizing the struggle between reaching for connection (longing) and establishing boundaries (resolve). The palette of red and pale blue introduces tension, where red signifies urgency and repair while pale blue evokes calm, creating a duality. The main contrast occurs in the top left corner, where dense clusters collide with expansive quiet spaces, illustrating the pressure of emotion. The broken pixels serve as reminders of imperfection within this emotional landscape, echoing the seed's focus on 'brokenness' and 'repair'.
