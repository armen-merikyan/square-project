Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between vulnerability and strength, where the central pixels in shades of deep teal suggest a soft, exposed core, contrasting with surrounding stronger cosmic indigo and void black tones. The palette, evoking awe and terror, highlights feelings of safety and exposure, while the barcode-like pattern in the center signifies fragility. The rising arrangement of the colors symbolizes hope and ascension against a background of weight, echoing the overall concept of 'vulnerability vs strength' as a navigational experience through a cosmic landscape.
