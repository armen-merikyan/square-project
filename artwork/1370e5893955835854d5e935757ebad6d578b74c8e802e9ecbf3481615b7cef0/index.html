Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a tension between trust and suspicion, represented through color as dark vs. light. The palette features black and dark violet, evoking suspicion, while pale green and soft gold introduce trust and hope, creating a dynamic interaction. Main contrast occurs in the vertical split and diagonal fall, symbolizing the struggle between these emotions. The pattern of stairs conveys ascension amidst confusion, while broken pixels imparts an essence of fragility. The structured grid allows the emotional layers to resonate visually, fostering an atmosphere where trust intermittently breaks through the shadows of suspicion.
