Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between joy and grief through a split horizontal layout that reflects contrasting movements—one side displaying bright, warm colors representing joy and an upward flow, while the other side adopts muted, cooler tones invoking grief with a downward pull. The palette of yellows and oranges engages with deep blues and muted purples, creating a vibrant interplay. The jagged edges introduce a sense of chaos, while strategic placement of broken pixels enhances imperfection, symbolizing the conflict between hidden and exposed feelings. This composition invites the viewer to reflect on the complexities of emotions as they intersect, shift, and collide.
