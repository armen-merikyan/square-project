Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the dichotomy of vulnerability and strength. The palette combines soft pastels like powder pink and warm white with darker shades of carbon black and steel gray, creating a visual tension that reflects the fragility of vulnerability against the robustness of strength. The main collision occurs centrally with a dense concentration of warm colors clashing with the surrounding cool tones, symbolizing the struggle of finding safety in exposure. The layout rises diagonally to imply hope and ascendance, while the use of broken pixels adds deliberate imperfections representing the complexities of resilience.
