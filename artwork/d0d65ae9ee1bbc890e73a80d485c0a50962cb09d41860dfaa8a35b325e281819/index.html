Artist Statement

This artwork explores the dual nature of restlessness and peace, visually represented through contrasting patterns of pulse marks and mirror images. The chosen palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon evokes a tender yet tumultuous atmosphere, embodying both warmth and tension. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the heavier colors interact with lighter hues, creating a sense of vertical movement and emotional dissonance. The placement of broken pixels adds intentional disruptions, representing the interruptions that come with unresolved emotions, balancing between calm and chaos.
