Artist Statement

This composition reflects the interplay between restlessness and peace through a clash of light and dark hues. The palette, 'joy_grief', incorporates bright yellows and muted purples, symbolizing the tension between warm emotional bursts and cool disconnectedness. The checker pattern for peace blends against a fault line structure representing restlessness, creating a textured conflict throughout the square. The main collision occurs at the top left corner, overflowing with light, while the bottom right recedes into darker tones, visually narrating a journey from chaos to calm.
