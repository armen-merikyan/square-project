Artist Statement

This abstract piece encapsulates the emotional tug-of-war between certainty and curiosity, conveyed through a palette that contrasts deep blues with pale yellows. The deep blue symbolizes certainty, creating firm boundaries within the grid, while the pale yellow represents curiosity, offering odd accents that disrupt and intrigue. The main collision occurs in the center with overlapping hues, suggesting an interplay of forces. The chosen pattern types evoke a tactile sensation–the cracks of certainty cradled by the 'teeth' of curiosity—richly exploring the tension of rising signals against receding edges. This layout allows for both density and quiet spaces, embodying the theme of movement from bottom to top, reminiscent of tides fighting against channels.
