Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances vulnerability and strength, presenting a spectrum from exposed and fragile to robust and grounded. The color palette, 'ancient_fire', with burnt orange and dark red suggests warmth and intensity, while charcoal and gold introduce depth and glimmer of hope. The primary collision occurs diagonally across the grid, where thick, stacked patterns of warmth clash against thin, pressured bands, creating a tension between safety and exposure. This interplay echoes the seed's concepts of 'center versus edges' and the duality of strength and vulnerability, resulting in a visually engaging narrative without literal representation.
