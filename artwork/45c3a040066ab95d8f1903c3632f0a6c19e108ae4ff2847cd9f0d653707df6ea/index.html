Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between softness and severity, depicted through a palette where soft colors like powder pink and warm white gently collide with sharp crimson and carbon black. The outer border of the composition, representing severity, is contrasted by a lighter, more forgiving inner core, creating a sense of enclosure and tension. This interplay of harsh darker edges against the lighter center embodies the struggle between noise and clarity, signaling a pulse at the core that seeks to emerge but is constrained by the surrounding weight. The arrangement prioritizes negative space to breathe, underscoring the echoes of pulse marks against the radial core, embodying guilt and forgiveness in its broken pixel placements, suggesting imperfection and unresolved emotions.
