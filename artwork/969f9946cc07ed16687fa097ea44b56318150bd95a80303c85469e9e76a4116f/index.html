Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between safety and exposure, characterized by a stark contrast in hues and patterns. The chosen palette of soft greens, warm browns, and pale tones symbolizes safety, while the cold whites and open blues evoke a sense of exposure. The dominant green in the center captures the feeling of safety, while interrupted lines of blue echo outward, suggesting vulnerability. The asymmetrical placement of colors creates a dynamic interplay, with darker, heavier shades at the edges representing pressure and softer colors in the center mirroring release and calm. Broken pixels disrupt patterns, symbolizing the fragility of safety against the echoes of exposure.
