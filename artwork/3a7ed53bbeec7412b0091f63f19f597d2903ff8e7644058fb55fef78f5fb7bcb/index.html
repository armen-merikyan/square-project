Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between calm, muted spaces and areas of dense, dark pressure. The palette of quiet colors like 'off_white' and 'faint_peach' contrasts sharply with deeper tones such as 'dark_navy' and 'compressed_red', embodying the collision of tranquility and urgency. The upper half of the composition is dominated by softer tidal patterns, representing quietness, while the lower half shifts to a denser stair-like arrangement that signifies pressure. The broken pixels throughout act as intentional disruptions, echoing the chaos that shatters the calm. This interplay reveals the struggle of clarity against confusion, creating a visual argument that resonates with the emotional complexity articulated in the seed.
