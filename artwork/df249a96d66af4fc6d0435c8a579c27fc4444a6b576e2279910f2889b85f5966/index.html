Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between defiance and submission, manifested through a palette that contrasts vibrant and muted hues. The core colors — ripe green and honey gold — symbolize defiance, while deep olive and cream illustrate submission. The main collision occurs at the center, with a dense cluster of vibrant colors interrupted by softer, more subdued shades radiating outwards, reflecting the tension of the seed. The edges are stark and defined, suggesting an erosion of symmetry that speaks to a struggle between two opposing forces.
