Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a carefully selected palette that contrasts deep blues and mourning black against a soft pale yellow and new leaf. The layout, structured around a cross current, illustrates the collision of distant echoes with foreground intensity. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, signifying both repairs and scars within the overall design. The main contrast occurs centrally, where the resolved moments intersect with lingering longings, suggesting a narrative of hope amidst imperfection. The selected pattern types—shatter for longing and mirror for resolve—interact to create a dynamic balance, reflecting on the push and pull of emotions.
