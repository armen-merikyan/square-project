Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the duality of confusion and clarity, manifesting in a stark contrast between the inner and outer sections. The chosen palette comprises deep blacks and vibrant soft golds representing chaos and clarity respectively. The outer border employs a sharp, thick outline in dark violet to signify rigidity amidst the confusion of the inner section, where a staircase pattern of pale green mirrors a rising tension. This agitation is interrupted by broken pixels, symbolizing imperfections and moments of clarity amidst noise. The overall layout flows from the outer chaos inward, creating a tension-filled visual narrative that oscillates between dense clusters and quiet spaces, embodying the emotional push and pull of the seed.
