Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional interplay of vulnerability and strength through contrasting color densities. The palette—shades of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon—reflects intimate warmth intersected by stark shadows, emphasizing the tension between exposed softness and robust protection. The main collision occurs in the center where fiery, flame-like patterns of red compete against the stabilizing, split-screen grays, creating an argument grid filled with layered complexity. Broken pixels pepper the design, hinting at imperfections in the boundary where strength meets vulnerability, allowing glimpses of the tumult beneath.
