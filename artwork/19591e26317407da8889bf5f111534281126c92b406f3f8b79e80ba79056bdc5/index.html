Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines heavy dread with moments of relief, invoking a struggle between light and dark. The palette, with faded gold and storm gray, amplifies this contrast. The color placement starts with dense dark corners giving way to lighter fields, signaling a shift from pressure to calm. This dynamic is embodied by the collision of chaotic noise against structured swathes of faded gold. The seed of ancient fire translates into warm accents bursting through the heavy background, creating an abstract representation of battle between constraint and freedom.
