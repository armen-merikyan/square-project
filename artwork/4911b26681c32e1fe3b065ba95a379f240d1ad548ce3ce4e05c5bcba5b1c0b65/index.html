Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional contrast of confusion and clarity through a nearly symmetrical design that embodies brokenness. The palette combines deep blues with warm yellows and oranges, representing clarity amidst chaos. The placement of contrasting colors communicates a sense of pulse; warm hues pierce through the cooler tones, suggesting a vibrational tension. The checker pattern disrupts the otherwise orderly flow, embodying conflict while adhering to the structured grid. Negative space is carefully maintained, inviting visual breathing amidst density. The arrangement fuses silent echoes of calm against the backdrop of confusion, creating an abstract story of visual discord and harmony.
