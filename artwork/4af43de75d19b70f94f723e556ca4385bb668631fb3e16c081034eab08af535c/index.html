Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission, depicted through contrasting patterns and colors. The stark palette of black and white signifies conflict, while the inclusion of pale blue offers a quiet counterbalance, evoking calm amidst turmoil. The placement of intense dark colors at the center represents the unmoved core of defiance, surrounded by softer and receding tones suggesting submission. This dynamic creates an inward spiral effect that visually narrates the collision between the two emotions, allowing for a compelling exploration of pressure and release.
