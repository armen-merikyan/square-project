Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional struggle between emptiness and desire, expressed through a contrasting palette of warm and cool colors. The layout features a clear distinction: the outer rows represent the desire with brighter, warmer colors, creating a sense of aspiration and energy. Meanwhile, the central rows reflect emptiness, employing darker, cracked patterns that symbolically suggest a void. The composition moves left to right, reinforcing the idea of rising tension, where warm shades pulse against cooler tones. Broken pixels introduce a raw, imperfect quality, paralleling the fragmentation of heart and experience.
