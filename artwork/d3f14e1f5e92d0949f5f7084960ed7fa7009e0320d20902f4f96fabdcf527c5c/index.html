Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional duality of confusion versus clarity through juxtaposed colors and patterns. The soft gold (#D6B700) represents moments of clarity, while the dark violet (#3E1E73) embodies confusion with its dense and textured patterns. The palette of 'fear_hope' creates a tension between light and dark, hinting at both fear and optimism. The symmetrical arrangement is disturbed by broken pixels that introduce an imperfect rhythm, reflecting the struggle between symmetrical clarity and chaotic breakdown. Overall, the composition illustrates a pulse of emotions, with noticeable interruptions creating a dynamic visual narrative.
