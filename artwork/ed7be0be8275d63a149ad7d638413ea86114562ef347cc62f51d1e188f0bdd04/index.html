Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between isolation and the desire for connection. The palette consists of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue, symbolizing warmth and faded memories, blending a nostalgic hue with the coolness of detachment. The main contrast lies in the top-left density that bursts into lighter areas, suggesting a movement from loneliness to connection. The crosshatch pattern evokes a sense of complexity and intrusion, mirroring the struggle between hidden and exposed feelings.
