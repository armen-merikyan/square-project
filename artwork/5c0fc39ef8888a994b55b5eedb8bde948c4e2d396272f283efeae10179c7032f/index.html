Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tug-of-war between vulnerability and strength. The palette draws from a mix of earthy greens and warm gold, suggesting growth amidst the conflicts of exposure and shelter. The strong edges of deep olive and cream represent stability, while the acid green and honey gold flow inward, creating a visual cue of rising tension. The main collision occurs at the center where the softer colors of warmth and vulnerability meet the harder edges, creating a visual vortex of emotional energy.
