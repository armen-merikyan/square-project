Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between clarity and static disruption. The color palette of navy, black, dim cyan, and single white evokes the themes of isolation (lonely_signal) and communication, where the brighter pixels represent fleeting moments of clarity amidst overwhelming noise. The main collision occurs in the center, representing the pulse of signal trying to emerge from the surrounding noise, aligning with the layout's theme of scattered repair. By incorporating broken pixels, the piece suggests imperfections and echoes of memory, intentionally creating disruptions to the visual flow.
