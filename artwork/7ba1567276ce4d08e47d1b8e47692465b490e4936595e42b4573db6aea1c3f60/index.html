Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork encapsulates the dichotomy of loneliness versus connection, enhanced by the juxtaposition of density and negative space. The palette, dominated by electric blue, magenta, and black, emphasizes the conflict between warmth and coolness, mirroring the push-pull dynamic of being both isolated and connected. The top-heavy arrangement suggests a feeling of burden, with a few vibrant pixels (representing fleeting moments of connection) breaking through a sea of darkness, symbolizing loneliness. The intersecting crosshatch patterns reflect the complexity of these emotions, while intentional broken pixels add an imperfect human touch, honoring the struggle of the emotional landscape.
