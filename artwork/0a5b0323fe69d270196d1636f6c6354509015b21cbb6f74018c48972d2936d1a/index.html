Artist Statement

The artwork explores the tension between longing and resolve, employing a palette that reflects warmth and depth through burnt orange and charcoal. The arrangement features a lattice pattern interspersed with pulse marks, creating a contrasting rhythm that conveys both desire for connection and the firmness of resolve. The soft edges evoke a sense of gentleness amidst the sharper contrasts, while deliberate broken pixels introduce imperfection and disruption, signifying the complexities of emotions. The main collision occurs at the center where layers of colors overlap, symbolizing the interplay of hope and uncertainty, evoking a story of aspiration amidst challenges.
