Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes restlessness with peace, suggesting a constant push-pull dynamic. The palette blends warm brown and shelter green, symbolizing safety and the earthly calm against the cooler open blue and cold white, indicative of unsteady motion. A diagonal pattern represents the conflict, with the center embodying the calm space—where tranquility resides—while the surrounding colors ripple and converge towards the edges. Key colors collide at the diagonal axis, creating a visual tension that disrupts quiet spaces with vibrant interruptions. The choice of broken pixels emphasizes imperfections in this emotional narrative, representing the scars of memory amidst a backdrop of serenity. Overall, the composition illustrates the struggle between moments of stillness and enduring restlessness, culminating in a dance of color and emotion through the pixel arrangement.
