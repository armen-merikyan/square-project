Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between trust and suspicion, highlighted by a split vertical layout that contrasts order with chaos. The palette, featuring soft sky and storm gray, alludes to the tension between optimism and uncertainty. The horizontal flow mimics a flickering signal, generating both curiosity and apprehension. The collision between the structured ladder pattern of trust and the fractured lattice of suspicion creates powerful visual tension at the center, embodying the push and pull of these conflicting emotions. Broken pixels add intentional imperfection, emphasizing fragility.
