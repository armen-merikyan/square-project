Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through a deliberate arrangement and palette inspired by warmth and muted tones. The choice of burnt orange and dark red evokes a sense of depth and intensity, suggesting both the warmth of connection and the heaviness of loneliness. The grid-like structure instills a cramped feeling, while the broken pixels disrupt this order, symbolizing imperfection and memory. The primary contrast occurs between the dark edges forming a protective barrier and vibrant warm hues that pulse from within, mimicking the struggle between isolation and reaching out.
