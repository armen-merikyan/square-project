Artist Statement

This abstract piece explores the emotional tension between clarity and confusion. The palette of warm reds contrasts with cool grays, embodying a struggle between structured thoughts and chaotic impulses. The central focus features dense clusters of red, implying strong emotion and confusion, while the edges soften into grays representing clarity and resolution. The broken pixels selectively employed introduce imperfection, aligning with the theme of anxiety where clarity seems just out of reach. This layout challenges the viewer's perception, creating a visual echo of rising and falling, reflecting the duality of emotion.
