Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional dichotomy of loneliness and connection through a carefully chosen palette of muted purples and warm yellows. The contrast arises from a central cluster of vibrant colors amidst expansive negative space, symbolizing a burst of connection within the weight of isolation. The pattern alternates between mirrored echoes and subtle overlaps, embodying the struggle between being hidden and exposed, where each color's collision generates tension and release. The arrangement suggests a top-weighted pressure that encourages wonder and interaction, leaving the viewer to navigate their own emotions.
