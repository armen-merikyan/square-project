Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between vulnerability and strength, shown through a juxtaposition of soft pastels against deep maroon and gray, suggesting a narrative of protection over an exposed center. The collision occurs mainly in the center with a radial pattern of pink and pale gray radiating against the darker, bold borders. This reflects the theme of balanced tension, where the emotional states interweave and disrupt in a symmetrical yet chaotic manner, paralleling the complexities of desire and emptiness.
