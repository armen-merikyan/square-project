Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, reflected through a palette of red, pale blue, black, and white. The contrast emphasizes a divide between deep, yearning colors and the stark resolve of the lighter tones, creating an interplay between density and quietness. The broken pixel placements act as intentional scars, symbolizing the conflicts and repairs within the emotional narrative. The overall layout of stacked bands enhances movement across the canvas, suggesting an ebb and flow of emotions that collide and resonate throughout the grid.
