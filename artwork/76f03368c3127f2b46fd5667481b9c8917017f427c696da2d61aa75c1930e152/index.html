Artist Statement

This piece embodies a struggle between calm and chaos, utilizing a palette of muted sepia tones contrasted with faded blue to symbolize the tension. The arrangement reflects falling pixels, capturing the descent from tranquility to agitation; the darker fields interrupt with specks of brightness, suggesting moments of stress within a larger serene backdrop. The central collision is defined diagonally, representing the argument of oppositions, while the broken pixels add intentional disruptions, echoing unresolved motion.
