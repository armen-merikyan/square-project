Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between longing and resolve, creating a dynamic tension that reflects fractured connectivity. The palette of soft pastel mint paired with stormy gray represents a spectrum of hope and melancholy. The main contrast occurs in the center, where the ring pattern of longing clashes with structured patches of resolve, suggesting the complexity of emotional intersections. Broken pixels are dispersed intentionally to invoke a sense of disruption and repair, encapsulating the layered experiences of yearning and firmness in the face of chaos.
