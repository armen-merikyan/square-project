Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional duality of love and betrayal through a clash of warm and cool colors, which symbolize the complex interplay of feelings. The palette, dominated by reds and pinks contrasted with dark maroon and muted gray, highlights the tension between affection and deception. The main collision occurs as the warm, stair-like patterns of love converge into the colder, grid-like structures of betrayal, suggesting an underlying conflict. This is accentuated by a few broken pixels dispersed throughout, representing fractures in what seems whole, inviting viewers to reflect on the fragility of trust and the impact of emotional scars.
