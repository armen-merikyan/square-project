Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional struggle between defiance and submission through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette includes warm coral and soft amber highlighting the defiant aspects against colder hues like blue-black representing submission. The pattern intertwines an upward thread with downward stacking, creating tension at the center where emotions collide. The negative space enhances the feeling of isolation, while scattered broken pixels evoke disruption within the calm.
