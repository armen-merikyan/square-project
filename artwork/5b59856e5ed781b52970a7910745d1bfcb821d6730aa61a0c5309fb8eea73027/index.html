Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between joy and grief by employing a palette that emphasizes contrast: warm yellows and oranges represent joy, while deep blue and muted purple signify grief. The left side is structured with a grid pattern, evoking the weight of grief, while the right side introduces stair-like patterns that suggest joy's expansion. Broken pixels add imperfection, illustrating disruption in the symmetry. The lively yellow and deep blue clash at the center, where the emotions collide, creating a dynamic visual pulse. This arrangement echoes the emotional complexities surrounding love and betrayal, represented through color and abstract forms.
