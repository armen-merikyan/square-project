Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a push-pull dynamic between longing and resolve, represented through contrasting color clusters. The palette incorporates muted tones like storm gray and faded gold, symbolizing both tension and calm, while pale mint and soft sky provide moments of serenity. The main collision occurs central to the grid, where the dense island of deep colors suggests an intense desire, woven with lighter threads of resolve peeking through; this echoes the theme of threads intertwining yet also presenting a potential break. Broken pixels introduce an element of disruption, reinforcing the narrative of repair versus damage.
