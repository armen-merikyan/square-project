Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between control and surrender. The palette of warm shadows, cool blues, and subtle accents creates a scene where contained pressure meets gentle release. The main collision occurs in the center, where the darker hues encapsulate the brighter gold and lilac, embodying the last flicker of light amidst encroaching shadows. This arrangement echoes the seed's themes of conflict and balance, utilizing color to represent emotional tension rather than concrete imagery.
