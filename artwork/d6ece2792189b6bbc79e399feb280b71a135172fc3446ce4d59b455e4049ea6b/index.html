Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes clarity against randomness, symbolizing the struggle between clear signals and noise. The palette features deep navy and black to represent the noise, while dim cyan serves as a signal accent, creating contrast. The main collision occurs in the center where the brighter colors intersect with the darker, more chaotic tones, echoing the impact of memories and interruptions as represented by broken pixels that disrupt the noise texture. This pattern reflects the emotional ebb and flow, ultimately conveying a balance between existence and erasure.
