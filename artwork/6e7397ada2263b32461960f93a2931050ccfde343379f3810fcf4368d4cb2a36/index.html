Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional landscape defined by longing and resolve, created through a palette of muted and soft colors, suggesting a serene yet urgent ambiance. The main contrast occurs in the center where two contrasting patterns, represented by the longing (teeth) and resolve (spark), collide. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, echoing the idea of repair versus damage, while maintaining negative space around the center to reflect the theme of distance in tension. The color choices favor a sense of melancholy wonder, encapsulating the struggle between internal desires and outward strength.
