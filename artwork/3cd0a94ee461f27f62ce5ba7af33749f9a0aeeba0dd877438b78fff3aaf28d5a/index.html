Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension of longing and resolve through contrasting density and quietness. The palette, featuring deep reds and muted creams, evokes feelings of hunger for connection along with restrained urgency, visually represented by clusters of rich colors against softer backgrounds. A collision occurs centrally, where dark tones encounter lighter halos, representing the delicate interplay of desire and stability. The seed's suggestion of distance and brokenness translates into overlapping patterns, forming a narrative of repair amidst chaos.
