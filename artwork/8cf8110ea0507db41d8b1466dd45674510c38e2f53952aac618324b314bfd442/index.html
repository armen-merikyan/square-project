Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tug-of-war between defiance and submission. The palette of warm yellows and oranges contrasts with the deep blue and muted purple, representing brightness against weight. The composition utilizes a mirrored layout with intentional breaks, signifying errors in the perceived harmony. The pattern of crosshatches and cracks emphasizes conflict and tension, while the bottom-weighted design gives a sense of gravity, making the viewer feel both anchored and unsettled. Negative space around the composition adds a feeling of unresolved emotion and potential.
