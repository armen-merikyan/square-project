Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between longing and resolve, represented through juxtaposed patterns of broken pixels and static textures. The palette, drawn from 'awe_terror', uses deep teal and cosmic indigo to evoke depth and introspection, while cold gold acts as a striking accent, illuminating the resolve within darker spaces. The main collision occurs in the center where static and teeth patterns interrupt and overlap, suggesting a struggle between the desire for connection and the feeling of incompleteness. Negative space is preserved along the edges, creating a feeling of vastness and anticipation.
