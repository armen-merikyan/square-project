Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette of deep black emphasizing strength and vibrant lime as a spark of hope. The contrast highlights a small cluster of vivid colors at the center, suggesting exposure amidst a surrounding protection. The broken pixels provide intentional disruptions, simulating flaws in both strength and vulnerability. This pattern portrays the conflict and connection, with abrupt transitions creating inner chaos against a backdrop of stability.
