Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between envy and gratitude, using contrasting colors and patterns. The palette combines warm hues of pink and red with calm gray and dark maroon, symbolizing the duality of feelings. The main collision manifests in the center, where warmth meets the cooler edges, echoing the seed's exploration of emotional dynamics. Patterns are placed asymmetrically to create a visual narrative of rising and falling, illustrating the push-pull of these emotions.
