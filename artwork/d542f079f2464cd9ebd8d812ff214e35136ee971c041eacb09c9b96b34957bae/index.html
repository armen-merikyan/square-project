Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between restlessness and peace. The selected palette of moss green and bone white represents resilience against the subtle pressure of rust and stone gray. Here, the main contrast forms diagonally, where restless maze patterns intersect with more serene constellation shapes. The dissonance created by broken pixels accentuates the feeling of instability, representing moments of disruption that contrast with areas of calmness. Ultimately, the arrangement reflects the delicate balance between motion and memory, evoking a sense of nostalgia disrupted by the demands of the present.
