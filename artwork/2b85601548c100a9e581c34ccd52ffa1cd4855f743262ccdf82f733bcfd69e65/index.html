Artist Statement

The emotional tone is captured through contrasting colors that symbolize 'defiance' with brighter, angular shades and 'submission' with muted, receding hues. The palette features warm ivory and deep wine to create a sense of guilt and forgiveness, mirroring the underlying conflict. The main collision occurs diagonally across the square, where the vibrant 'braid' pattern of defiance intertwines with the 'threshold' areas of submission, creating narrative friction. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, emphasizing emotional scars and echoes of tension, while ensuring enough negative space suggests a moment of quiet amidst the chaos.
