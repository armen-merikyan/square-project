Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional duality of longing and resolve, utilizing a palette of pinks and grays to convey both vulnerability and strength. The contrast between the constellation-like patterns of longing and the sharp interruptions of resolve creates a dynamic collision in the center, symbolizing the push and pull of these emotions. The layout evolves upwards (rising_signal), suggesting hope amidst tension, with negative space strategically placed to enhance the feeling of separation and connection.
