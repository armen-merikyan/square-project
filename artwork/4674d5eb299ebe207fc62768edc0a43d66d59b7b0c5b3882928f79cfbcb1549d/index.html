Artist Statement

This composition balances the tension of spark and ash, using a palette that embodies desire and emptiness. The warm coral and blood orange clash with the cooler charcoal and hollow blue, creating a visual narrative of struggle between bursting brightness and quiet decay. The maze-like pattern of ash occupies the edges while crosshatching from the spark interrupts the center, forming an energetic collision that reflects resilience amidst fragility. The layout, featuring a narrow negative space channel, suggests a quiet path through dense emotional terrain, while broken pixels disrupt the continuity, signifying scars and echoes of memory.
