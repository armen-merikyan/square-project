Artist Statement

The composition embodies the contrast between stability and chaos, reflecting the emotional tones of signal vs noise. The chosen palette of navy, black, dim cyan, and white symbolizes deep shifts from quiet isolation (black) to vivid moments of clarity (white). The main collision occurs in the center where bright accents (white) pierce through layers of dark hues (navy, black), depicting the struggle of clarity amongst overwhelming static. The patterns of embers and hinge create dynamic flows, representing emotional movement and interruptions, allowing the viewer to feel the tension and resolution of memories in a chaotic environment.
