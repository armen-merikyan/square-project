Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between saturated desire and muted restraint. The palette features vibrant oranges and coral hues representing desire, balanced by muted charcoal and blue tones symbolizing restraint. The grid arrangement creates a clear passageway at the center where heated colors collide with cooler tones, symbolizing the conflict of emotional push and pull. This dance between warm saturation and cool muted edges is evident in how the overflow of desire competes against the containment by restraint. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, resembling emotional scars, evoking pain yet deep vulnerability. Overall, the composition conveys an abstract narrative of longing, tension, and the complexities of emotional expression.
