Artist Statement

This composition channels the emotional dichotomy of certainty versus curiosity. The palette of off-white and grey tones creates a subdued background, complemented by compressed red for moments of tension, while dark navy and faint peach offer contrast and warmth. The arrangement reflects a collision between structured frames (certainty) on one side and layered stacks of color (curiosity) on the other, highlighting an ongoing inner dialogue. The flow spirals outward, suggesting a rising signal amidst static. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, embodying the feeling of dread within the framework of order.
