Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes pressure with moments of quiet through densely packed colors transitioning into softer hues, creating a sense of tension and release. The palette includes dark navy to signify pressure and off-white to embody calming quietness, with faint peach and dust gray providing contrast. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the high-density 'flame' pattern of warmer reds confronts the 'pressure band' layout of tranquil colors, allowing the viewer to feel the tension and subsequent release.
