Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy between confusion and clarity. The color palette ranges from deep blacks to pale blues and whites, emphasizing the stark contrast between dense darkness and light. The layout adopts an argument grid where patterns of clarity (represented through spark) and confusion (depicted by checker) overlap and collide. Darker hues dominate one side, betraying chaos and noise, while lighter shades strive for coherence on the opposite side. The broken edges introduce imperfection, echoing the essence of the seed, impacting the viewer's perception and emotional response.
