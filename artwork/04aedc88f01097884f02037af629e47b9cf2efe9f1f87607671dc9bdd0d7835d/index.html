Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dichotomy of confusion and clarity through a balanced blend of colors from the 'love_betrayal' palette. The darker shades represent the thick outlines of confusion, contrasting sharply with lighter, clearer tones emerging from the center. The falling pixel layout suggests a descent into confusion, while the radial clarity emerges from the center, creating a visual pull outward. Broken pixels sporadically disrupt the symmetrical feel, echoing the theme of imbalance and uncertainty.
