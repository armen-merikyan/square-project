Artist Statement

This composition evokes the tension between vitality (spark) and decay (ash), using a palette of soft browns, ash gray, and warm ivory to symbolize fragility and resilience. The diagonal layout creates a clear collision between invigorating bursts of soft gold against muted tones, illustrating the struggle between hunger and restraint. The patterns—'noise' for ash and 'target' for spark—create a visual sense of interruption, echoing the contrast in memory and erasure, making the piece dynamically engaging yet abstract.
