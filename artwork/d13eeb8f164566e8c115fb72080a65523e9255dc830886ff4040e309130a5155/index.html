Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes relief with dread, illustrated by the tension between dark and light hues, with a focus on the top-weighted arrangement symbolizing the oppressive presence of dread. The palette features deep maroons and grays to evoke the weight of dread, contrasted by softer pinks that symbolize fleeting relief. The use of broken pixels introduces an element of imperfection, embodying the struggle between symmetry and chaos, while the layout guides the eye downward, reflecting the flow from top to bottom. The resulting abstract pattern creates a dense, emotional narrative, with clusters of dark colors interrupted by bright accents to suggest a flicker of hope amidst heaviness.
