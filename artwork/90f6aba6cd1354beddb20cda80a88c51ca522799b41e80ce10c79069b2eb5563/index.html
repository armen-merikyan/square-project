Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The chosen palette blends warm browns with cool greens and open blues to evoke a sense of safety against urgency. The contrast lies in the central collision of deep green and warm brown, representing the overlapping feelings of calm and tension. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, mirroring the seed's concepts of repair and damage. The patterns of negative crosses signify longing, while the flooded fields represent resolve, creating an abstract narrative of emotional push and pull.
