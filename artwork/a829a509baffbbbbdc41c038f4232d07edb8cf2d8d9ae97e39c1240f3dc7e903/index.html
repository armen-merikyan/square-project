Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The palette mixes deep, introspective dark violet with soft, hopeful gold, creating a visual dialogue between confusion (darkness) and clarity (light). The thick outlines contrast the soft gradients, representing a clear structure amidst the chaos. Symmetrical patterns are deliberately interrupted by 'broken pixels' to invoke a sense of disruption, while rising patterns suggest an upward flow, hinting at hope. The overall arrangement evokes a pulsating rhythm with darker tones clustering at the base, as if dealing with heavier emotions, releasing towards the top with lighter hues, signifying clarity emerging from confusion.
