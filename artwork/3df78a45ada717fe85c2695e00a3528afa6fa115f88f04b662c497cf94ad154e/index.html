Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between quiet and noise, utilizing a stark contrast of dark and light hues to convey emotional collisions. The palette features deep blacks and grays against soft whites and pastel tones to symbolize the complexity of feelings—ready to burst yet held back. The arrangement reflects rhythmic patterns where calm areas are abruptly interrupted by vibrant highlights, manifesting moments of pressure and release. This interplay creates a sense of movement and emotional depth, resonating with the seed's themes.
