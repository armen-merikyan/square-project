Artist Statement

This composition contrasts the notions of vulnerability and strength, embodying emotional tension through color and placement. The palette leans on warm tones like hot coral and blood orange, signifying strength, and cooler accents from charcoal and hollow blue, embodying vulnerability. The main collision is found centrally, where the coral clashes with the darker shades, creating a focal point that suggests both exposure and shelter. Rows organize emotional strata, shifting from warm, dense colors to lighter shades, implying movement from tension to release, through broken pixels that intentionally disrupt uniformity, evoking a sense of interaction between chaos and control.
