Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between envy and gratitude, visually represented through a palette that juxtaposes dark and vibrant tones. The soft edges and radial patterns create a sense of movement that flows from the corner inward, suggesting the encroachment of emotions. The primary contrast occurs where the deep black meets the bright lime, indicating a collision of feelings that echo the themes of rising versus falling and the push-pull dynamic of emotional states.
