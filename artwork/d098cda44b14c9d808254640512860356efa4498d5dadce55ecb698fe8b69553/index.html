Artist Statement

This composition expresses the duality between joy and grief by juxtaposing bright and muted tones, showcasing the internal conflict. The palette, featuring warm yellows and deep blues, symbolizes the vibrancy of joy alongside the heaviness of grief. The primary contrast occurs in the left section, where vibrant colors pulse against a background of muted hues. This interplay creates tension, mirroring the emotional fluctuations described in the seed. The pattern of falling pixels represents tears or unresolved feelings, allowing the viewer to engage with abstract motion, yet maintain a sense of symmetrically balanced chaos.
