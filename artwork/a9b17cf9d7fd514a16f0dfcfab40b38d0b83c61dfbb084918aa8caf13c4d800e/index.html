Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The chosen palette of soft sky blues and rich storm grays evokes a sense of serenity intertwined with underlying urgency. The contrast between muted hues and brighter accents reflects the collision between hope and despair, as dynamic threads pulse across the canvas, creating a dialogue of movement and stillness. Broken pixels add intentional disruptions, mimicking the fragility of these conflicting emotions.
