Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the emotional tension between clarity and confusion. The vibrant shades of signal green and electric blue pierce through the chaotic static gray and dead black, symbolizing a clean pulse struggling within a dense cloud of noise. The layout of two pulsing centers separated by quiet spaces reflects the theme of isolation against an echoing backdrop, creating a visual narrative of tension and attempts to break through. The placement of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, reinforcing the theme of interference within the flow.
