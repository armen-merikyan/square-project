Artist Statement

The emotional tone speaks to the collision of quick bursts against settled remnants, representing resilience amidst fragility. The palette of deep navy, black, and dim cyan contrasts the softly glowing white, creating a stark interplay between the vibrant and the muted. The dominant presence of black suggests the heavy weight of ash, while the scattered light of white portrays fleeting sparks of energy. The diagonal and spiral flows suggest a movement from the center outwards, emphasizing both tension and release amidst jagged interactions between fragmented pixels. Broken pixels create intentional disruptions, reflecting the moments of fragmentation and memory lost amidst life’s pressures.
