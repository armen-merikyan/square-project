Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The chosen palette of soft cyan, muted plum, and lavender gray invokes a melancholic wonder, serving both as a quiet field and accent colors. The main collision occurs in the center of the artwork, where fragmented dots represent longing, while crosshatching signifies resolve, creating a tension-filled dynamic. The negative space allows these emotional expressions to breathe, embodying the balance of chaos and order.
