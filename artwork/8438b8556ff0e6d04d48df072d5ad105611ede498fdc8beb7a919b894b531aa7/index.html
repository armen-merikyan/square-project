Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise). The chosen palette features deep blacks and vibrant greens representing energy, with accents of electric blue and hot magenta evoking bursts of interference. The noise pattern (fault_line) disrupts the signal pattern (mirror), creating an uneasy but dynamic flow across the square. The main collision occurs in the center, where the bright green signals of clarity persist amidst surrounding gray and black noise, embodying the struggle for meaning amid distortion.
