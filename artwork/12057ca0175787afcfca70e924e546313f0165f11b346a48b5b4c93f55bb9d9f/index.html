Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy between longing and resolve through a careful balance of density and emptiness. The palette, derived from deep navy and dim cyan, reflects a sense of quiet isolation juxtaposed with stark black, symbolizing the compressive strength of resolve. The collision between these colors occurs centrally, where a small dense cluster represents longing, contrasting sharply with the surrounding quieter pixels, illustrating the struggle between chaos and order. Pattern types 'teeth' and 'scar' provide tangential disruption and strength, respectively. Broken pixels act as interruptions, reinforcing the unresolved tension within the composition, resonating with the seed's narrative of distance and intimacy.
