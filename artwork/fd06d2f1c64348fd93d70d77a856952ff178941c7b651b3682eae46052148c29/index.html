Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a tension between longing and resolve—an inner struggle mirrored through a contrasting color palette of warm and cool tones. The combination of lush greens and delicate creams suggests growth and peace, while the deeper olives and golds indicate a solid foundation and determination. The main collision occurs at the center, where broken dot patterns intermingle with a lattice design—representing the push and pull of these emotional states. The negative space enhances the feeling of longing, emphasizing the desire for resolution amidst chaos.
