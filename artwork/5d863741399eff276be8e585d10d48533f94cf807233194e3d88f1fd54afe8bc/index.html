Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between presence and absence, signal and noise. The dark navy and black represent depth and the chaos of noise, while the dim cyan and white punctuate moments of clarity and communication. This composition oscillates between clusters of dense, dark areas interrupted by lighter rings, symbolizing the struggle of readable signals within chaotic noise. The brightest areas form a rising movement, suggesting an upward pulse echoing themes of hope and escape, which aligns with the chosen seed's emotional landscape.
