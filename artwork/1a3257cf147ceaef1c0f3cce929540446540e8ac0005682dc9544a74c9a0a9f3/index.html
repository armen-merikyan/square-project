Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon expresses warmth and depth, while the asymmetrical arrangement creates a dissonant rhythm. The left side features irregular 'dots' representing loneliness, creating large negative spaces, while the right side uses 'rings' for connection, suggesting unity. The visual collision occurs at the center where the contrasting emotions meet, igniting an exploration of hidden versus exposed feelings, resulting in an abstract story of emotional interplay.
