Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between control and panic, illustrated through a palette of sepia and cool gray tones that juxtapose warm and calm feelings. The zigzag pattern symbolizes angular tension, while the stable edges represent the attempt to impose order upon chaotic emotions. The central contrast occurs with striking dark pixels colliding against faded colors, emphasizing instability within a structured form, echoing the core themes of the seed: structured chaos and the tension of emotions.
