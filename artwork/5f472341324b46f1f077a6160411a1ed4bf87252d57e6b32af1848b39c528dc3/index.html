Artist Statement

This composition navigates the emotional terrain of restlessness and peace, embodying both the discomfort of interrupted patterns and the calm of symmetrical tones. The palette blends deep navy and dim cyan, providing a cool richness against stark black, symbolizing chaotic motion interrupted by serene spaces. The contrast is centered, with a predominance of restlessness in the left section, while gentle halos of peace herald a quiet relief to the right. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, echoing the thematic tension captured in the seed.
