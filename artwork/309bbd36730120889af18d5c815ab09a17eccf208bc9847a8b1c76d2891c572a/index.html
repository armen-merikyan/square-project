Artist Statement

This composition evokes the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette, combining soft tones like powder pink and warm white with the stark contrast of carbon black, creates a dialogue across the square while drawing attention to the center, which represents vulnerability. The layout of 'corner_invasion' directs one's gaze from a strong edge into a softer interior. The collision occurs where the braid pattern of vulnerability meets the hinge of strength, forming a boundary that pulses with feeling, creating an interruption against the otherwise stable structures. Broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, punctuating the flowing pattern with intentional disruptions, suggesting a narrative of exposure and protection. The overall square embodies a delicate balance of contrasting emotions, inviting the viewer to explore its depths.
