Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy between confusion and clarity. The palette consists of stark black, white, and shades of pale blue, symbolizing the tension between clarity (white) and confusion (black). The top half utilizes a grid pattern, creating an orderly feel, while the bottom introduces a checker pattern reflecting disorder and broken realities. The main contrast unfolds in the transition from the structured patterns of the top to the chaotic arrangements below, embodying a narrative of falling from order to disarray, with a thick outline hinting at boundaries that blur between these emotional states.
