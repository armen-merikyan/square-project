Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between quiet and pressure through a palette that represents softness and strength, with off-white and faint peach juxtaposed against darker hues of navy and compressed red. The dense clustering of colors at the bottom elevates upwards, suggesting a hopeful escape and collision of emotional tones. The pattern is a blend of checker and flood, creating an interplay of structured quietness against overwhelming density, intensified by subtle broken pixels that symbolize imperfections in life's flow.
