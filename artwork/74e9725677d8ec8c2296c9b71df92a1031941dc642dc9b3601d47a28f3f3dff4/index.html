Artist Statement

The emotional tone is derived from the conflicting feelings of chaos and calm, suggested by the palette of dark violet and soft gold against vivid black and pale green. The contrast manifests through dense clusters of dark hues clashing with lighter accents, creating a rhythm that evokes both quiet and tumult. The layout follows a rising signal, illustrating the clash of hope and despair through a broken grid pattern, leaving space for interpretation in the negative spaces. Thus, the seed translates into an abstract expression of emotional collisions, rhythm, and disruptions, successfully embodying the concept of rising from chaos to clarity.
