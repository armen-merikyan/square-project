Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between longing and resolve, employing a palette of signal green and electric blue against darker tones, signifying a struggle between hope and despair. The layout illustrates a passage where two emotional states collide—longing represented by flowing patterns and resolve depicted through structured bands. Broken pixels disrupt the surface, mirroring imperfection and the scars of experience. The chaotic yet rhythmic arrangement creates a pulse, inviting the viewer into a realm of serenity wrapped in urgency.
