Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between vulnerability and strength through soft contrasts and structured patterns. The chosen palette of muted blues and grays conveys a sense of melancholy while suggesting warmth in the silent spaces. The main collision occurs at the center, where dense blocks of lavender_gray clash with thin stripes of moon_white, portraying a stark contrast between exposure and shelter. The broken pixels represent the imperfections and disruptions in this emotional dialogue, encapsulating the essence of rising signals battling against the weight of experience.
