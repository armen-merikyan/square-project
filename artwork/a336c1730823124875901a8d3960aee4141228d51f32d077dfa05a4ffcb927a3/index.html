Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between trust and suspicion, using a palette that balances soft aquas and bold reds to highlight contrasting feelings. The pattern reveals a spiraling motion that draws the eye inward, suggesting layers of uncertainty encountered in relationships. The collision occurs at diagonal points where the colors meet, creating soft overlaps reminiscent of shadows, evoking a sense of watchfulness and warmth simultaneously. Areas of negative space enhance the feeling of pressure and release, illustrating the delicate dance between reassurance and doubt.
