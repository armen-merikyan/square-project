Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between loneliness and connection, conveyed through a palette that juxtaposes dark violet for weight and softness with pale green for lightness and hope. The contrast emerges from the hard edges of the corner blocks, forging a conflict between dense clusters in the left and open space on the right. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection and complexity, mirroring how isolated moments can be shattered yet still connected through memory. This layout shows an argument grid where the struggle for emotional expression plays out, exemplifying the push and pull of full versus hollow.
