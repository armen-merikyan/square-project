Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between certainty and curiosity through an interplay of structured patterns and chaotic disruptions. The palette is drawn from 'ancient_fire' to evoke warmth and energy, contrasting deep charcoal with glowing gold and burnt orange. The arrangement features a central mass of certainty, represented by staircase patterns in darker tones, interrupted by pulse marks in lighter shades, creating a visual dialogue of collision. The layout emphasizes erosion at the edges, with negative space as unfilled potential, embodying quiet versus clamorous growth.
