Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between envy and gratitude, where the left side displays a structured radial pattern in warm tones, symbolizing the richness of gratitude. The right side contrasts this with broken pixels in cooler, muted shades, interrupting the flow to evoke envy. The combination of negative space and dense clusters creates a feeling of collision and unresolved motion, reflecting the push and pull between the two emotions.
