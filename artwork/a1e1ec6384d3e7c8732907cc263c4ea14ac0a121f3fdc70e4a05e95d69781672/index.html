Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the piece contrasts defiance with submission, utilizing a palette that embodies tension and dynamism. The chosen colors—signal green and electric blue—serve as vibrant accents against a backdrop of dead black and static gray, reflecting the struggle between chaos and order. The layout follows a quiet channel, creating a narrow path through dense patterns of dots representing defiance, while the ladder pattern symbolizes submission. The collision occurs at the center, where the vibrant dots intersect the subdued fields, creating visual tension indicative of the emotional struggle. Broken pixels have been intentionally placed to suggest imperfections and disruptions, embodying the theme of conflict and its inherent complexity.
