Artist Statement

This composition reflects the duality of joy and grief as two contrasting emotional states. The palette progresses from deep blues representing grief to warm yellows symbolizing joy, creating a compelling narrative of emotional tension. The main contrast occurs in the middle rows where cool tones collide with warmer shades, mimicking a wave of transitions—highlighting exposed versus hidden feelings. The layout of falling pixels suggests a descent into these layered emotions, while broken pixels add a sense of imperfection and authenticity to the experience.
