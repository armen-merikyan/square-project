Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between the vibrancy of 'spark' and the muted presence of 'ash.' The palette, featuring lime and electric blue against black and magenta, illustrates a collision of energy and stillness. The crosshatch pattern of sparks collides with the constellation form of ashes, creating breaks in a broken frame layout that frames the chaos within order. The center is dominated by electric blue, representing abrupt moments of clarity amidst dark, soft edges where memory and erasure meet.
