Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the juxtaposition of fear and hope, encapsulated in a palette that transitions from dark to light. The colors portray a sense of pressure, layering dark violet with soft gold to reflect the struggle between dense, jagged edges of fear and the delicate, upward glimmers of hope. The pattern exhibits a radial dynamic with sharp contrasts at the edges and softer transitions within, evoking a feeling of rising tension and a search for balance. This embodies the struggle of finding light amid darkness, creating a rhythmic flow with broken pixels representing fractures in perception.
