Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional tension between fear and hope, with a vertical split representing the collision of these feelings. The dark palette represents fear, using black and dark violet, while soft gold and pale green suggest hope emerging from the void. The contrasting textures reflect jagged edges against soft flows, creating an impression of pressure on the left and a gentle burst of optimism on the right. The arrangement's negative space subtly mirrors the layout concept of 'falling pixels,' ensuring a lightness amidst density.
