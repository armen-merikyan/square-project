Artist Statement

This piece captures the struggle between desire and restraint through contrasting colors and patterns. The warm reds and oranges symbolize the pulsating energy of desire, while muted tones reflect the weight of restraint. The diagonal argument layout creates a visual clash, emphasizing the push and pull dynamic. The checker pattern of desire clashes with the thread-like patterns of restraint, forming a tapestry of emotional intersections. The broken pixels introduce intentional irregularities, echoing the complexities in both feelings.
