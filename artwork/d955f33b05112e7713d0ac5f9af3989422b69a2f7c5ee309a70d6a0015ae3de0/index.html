Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dichotomy of trust and suspicion, represented by a palette that combines deep navy and stark black for mystery and depth, while introducing dim cyan for hints of quiet release. The composition illustrates a balance between structured grid-like patterns symbolizing suspicion on one side, contrasting with a softer, crosshatched area embodying trust. The main collision occurs at the diagonal divide, creating a visual rhythm that pulses between orderly and chaotic, suggesting an interplay of emotion. Negative space is maintained to evoke a sense of calm amidst the tension.
