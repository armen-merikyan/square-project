Artist Statement

The artwork explores the tension between vulnerability and strength. Drawing from the palette of muted 'dust_gray' and 'faded_blue' with warm tones of 'sepia' and 'tan', the composition places blocks of strong colors around a soft, exposed center. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, representing the scars of vulnerability contrasting against the stability of strength. The central collision between strong and muted colors evokes a narrative of resilience found in moments of fragility, creating a serene yet poignant atmosphere.
