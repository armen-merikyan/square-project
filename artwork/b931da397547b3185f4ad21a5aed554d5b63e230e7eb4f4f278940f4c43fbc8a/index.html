Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a turbulent interaction between warmth and coldness, suggesting love’s embrace contrasted with betrayal's sharp edges. The warm pink and red colors symbolize love's intensity, while the grays and dark maroons introduce a sense of betrayal's looming darkness. The main contrast is created diagonally across the square, where the vibrant colors collide with the stark gray, evoking an echo of tension. The layout suggests a falling pattern, resonating with the seed’s themes of descent and cracks, while the broken edges symbolize the imperfections inherent in relationships.
