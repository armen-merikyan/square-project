Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a struggle between clarity and chaos, weaving in contrasting elements of signal and noise. The palette, featuring deep navy and subtle dim cyan, evokes feelings of solitude punctuated by random sparks of white to represent interference. The arrangement places dark hues in the top half to symbolize the noise pushing down, while bursts of lighter colors punctuate the center, suggesting a fragile pulse aiming to break through. The main collision occurs in the center, with lighter hues interrupting the descent of darker tones, portraying a visual tension between an aspiring signal and encroaching noise.
