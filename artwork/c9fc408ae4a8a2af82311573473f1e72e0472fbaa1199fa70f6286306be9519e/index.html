Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between rage and calm through stark contrasts in color and pattern. The palette features hot reds clashing against cool pale blues and whites, symbolizing the chaos of overwhelming emotions wrestling with serenity. The patterns of rings representing rage disrupt the framework of calm, depicted by horizontal frames, creating an echo of unresolved tension. The flow ascending from bottom to top visually suggests hope clashing with despair, while broken pixels introduce deliberate imperfections, echoing the complexity of emotions.
