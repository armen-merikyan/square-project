Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional juxtaposition of fear and hope, represented through contrasting palettes of dark and light. The selected colors of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green capture the tension between heaviness and uplift. The layout, resembling a split vertical flow, accentuates the struggle; dark stripes representing fear clash with glowing rings of hope. The burning edge style emphasizes the urgency of this battle, while broken pixels introduce an imperfect beauty that mirrors our own vulnerabilities. The overall composition invites reflection on how hope persistently glows, interspersed within our own fears.
