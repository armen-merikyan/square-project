Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between clarity and confusion. The palette of black, electric blue, magenta, and lime captures a duality: black signifies confusion and depth, while the vibrant hues suggest clarity and hope. Main contrast emerges from the clash of bright electric blue against deep black, symbolizing clarity interrupting confusion. The abstract pattern utilizes a rising signal layout, suggesting ascent amidst chaos, with broken pixels representing imperfection, serving as a visual metaphor for fragmented clarity amidst confusion.
