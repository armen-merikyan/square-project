Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between the fleeting brightness of 'spark' and the settled, muted quality of 'ash'. The palette of burnt orange and dark red creates depth and intensity, while charcoal provides a stark contrast, symbolizing the weight of restraint and memories. Gold accents act as a 'spark', interrupting the dark fields and creating an energetic collision, translating the seed into a rhythm of bursts and quiet spaces that embodies resilience against erasure. The layout indicates fragmented repair through scattered placement, emphasizing asymmetry and unexpected intersections.
