Artist Statement

This composition embodies the duality of restlessness and peace, using a palette that reflects muted tones and striking contrasts. The dark colors create a feeling of heaviness and anxiety, while the subtle creams and oranges add fleeting warmth, representing peaceful moments. The main contrast occurs diagonally, echoing the 'light vs dark' theme, with broken pixels interspersed, creating imperfections that suggest interrupted memories. The patterns of 'spark' and 'veil' collide in the center, emphasizing tension and the struggle between these emotional states.
