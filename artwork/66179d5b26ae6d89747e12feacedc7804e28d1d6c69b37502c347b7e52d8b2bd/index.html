Artist Statement

The emotional tone of confusion versus clarity is captured through a stark contrast of dark and muted tones against lighter breaks, symbolizing clarity piercing the chaotic confusion. The palette reflects 'broken memory' with colors like sepia and faded blue, creating a sense of nostalgia in a broken yet coherent space. The central density of dark pixels suggests the confusion at the core, while the lighter edges represent clarity. This arrangement narrates the struggle between the full and hollow emotions as they collide.
