Artist Statement

The composition expresses the emotional tension between restlessness and peace through a dynamic interplay of colors—a balance between warm and cool tones. The palette combines vibrant pink and red, embodying restlessness, with muted gray and dark maroon, suggesting tranquility. The central area, featuring clustered warm colors, signifies chaos and motion, while the cooler, quieter edges represent peace and stability. This alteration in density emphasizes the contrast, echoing the theme of collision between opposing emotions.
