Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between love (warm, soft hues) and betrayal (dark, sharp tones) through a stark contrast of colors and texture. The palette of pinks and dark maroons reflects warmth and depth, symbolizing emotional complexity. The placement of colors is deliberate, creating a rhythm where the warmth of the pinks collides with the strength of the dark hues. The pixel layout descends, symbolizing a gentle fall from warmth to isolation, while echoes between mirrored patterns suggest missed connections, embodying themes of both love and betrayal. The negative space is integral, allowing for emotional breathing room amidst the density of color. The abstract nature of the arrangement avoids literal representation but conveys a poignant interplay of feelings.
