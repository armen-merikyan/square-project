Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines tension and clarity, reflecting the dynamic between noise and signal. The palette of deep navy, dim cyan, and stark whites illustrates the contrast of darkness and light, mirroring the battle between interference and readable paths. The upper rows embody the noise with chaotic stripes, while the lower rows present a more structured flame pattern suggesting a clearer signal. The main collision occurs at the center, representing the clash and interplay of emotions.
