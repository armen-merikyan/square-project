Artist Statement

This piece conveys the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, centered around burnt orange and dark red, evokes warmth and intensity, resonating with the theme of 'flame against broken dots,' while the cool tones of charcoal and gold introduce stillness and depth. The arrangement features a heavy outer border symbolizing loneliness, while the lighter core represents fleeting moments of connection. The use of asymmetrical placement highlights the feeling of interruption and echoes of memory, capturing a sense of pressure and release throughout.
