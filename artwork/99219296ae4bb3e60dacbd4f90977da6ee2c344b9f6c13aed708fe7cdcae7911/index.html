Artist Statement

The piece embodies the tension between joy and grief, with the top half representing joy through vibrant colors and structured patterns, while the bottom half portrays grief with darker tones and fragmented patterns. The use of a warm palette (yellow and orange) contrasts against muted hues (deep blue and muted purple), creating a dynamic interplay. Key moments of collision occur where joy's bright patterns intersect with the more chaotic maze of grief, suggesting a constant interplay between these two emotional states, symbolizing the complexity of human experience.
