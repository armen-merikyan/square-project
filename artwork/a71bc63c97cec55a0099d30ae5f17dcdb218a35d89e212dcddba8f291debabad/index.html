Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the dichotomy between love and betrayal, expressed through a palette of pink and dark maroon to symbolize warmth and depth. The pattern flows from bottom to top, creating a rising tension with warm colors juxtaposed against cooler, muted grays. The central contrast lies in the chaotic crosshatch of dark maroon and sharp-edged pink, which collide with soft grays to illustrate the struggle between comfort and isolation. This ‘inside_vs_outside’ theme manifests as broken dots and noise across the canvas, evoking feelings of both connection and solitude.
