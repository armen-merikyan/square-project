Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between confusion and clarity, using contrasting colors to convey misalignment and clarity through structured chaos. The palette incorporates dark shades of black and electric blue, signaling mechanical emotion, while vibrant lime and magenta introduce a jarring yet necessary disruption. The pattern radiates outward with dense clusters of confusion in the center, gradually lightening towards the edges, reflecting the fall of pixels like rain. This asymmetry embodies the contrasting themes of fullness against hollowness, creating an emotional echo that resonates through the piece.
