Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional contrast between joy and grief through a layered arrangement of colors. The palette blends warm yellows and oranges, symbolizing joy, with deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The main contrast occurs in the arrangement where warm colors pulsate against cooler tones, creating a visual tension that reflects the ongoing battle between hope and sorrow. The pattern resembles falling pixels, subtly hinting at the emotional rain of tears and laughter, surrounded by a structured grid that suggests both chaos and order. The broken pixels interrupt the symmetry, embodying the unpredictable nature of emotions, while keeping a significant amount of negative space to represent quiet contemplation amidst emotional turbulence.
