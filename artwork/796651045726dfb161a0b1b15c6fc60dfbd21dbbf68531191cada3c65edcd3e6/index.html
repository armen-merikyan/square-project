Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between envy and gratitude through its contrasting color palette and asymmetrical layout. The dominant colors—dark maroon and red—signify intense emotions, while softer shades of gray introduce a sense of calm and reflection. The zigzag flow enhances movement, echoing the conflict of these feelings, while the center-heavy arrangement suggests an internal struggle. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, mirroring the nuances of personal emotions and maintaining at least 10% negative space to evoke contemplation.
