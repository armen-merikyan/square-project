Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a push-and-pull between restlessness and peace, with dense clusters on the edges representing tension and looser spaces at the center indicating release. The palette of warm ivory, deep wine, stain brown, and soft gold evokes warmth yet contrasts against the cool ash gray background, symbolizing emotional complexity. The main contrast occurs diagonally, creating a dynamic flow that embodies the struggle between motion and stillness, representing both memory and fleeting moments. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, strengthening the theme of unresolved emotions.
