Artist Statement

This composition captures the juxtaposition of joy and grief through a palette of vibrant yellows and muted blues. The warm tones symbolize the expansive and uplifting aspects of joy, while the cooler hues represent the depth and mourning associated with grief. A split pattern alternates between bright and dark, evoking tension where joy collides with grief. The thin outline edges create a stark contrast against the denseness of the colors, enhancing the tension. The arrangement reflects a wave-like flow, creating visual movement and implying the cyclical nature of emotions.
