Artist Statement

The emotional tone emphasizes the tension between longing and resolve. The palette of cosmic indigo and deep teal evokes introspection, while accents of cold gold and star white introduce a sense of urgency and clarity. The main contrast occurs centrally where the deeper hues collide with lighter tones, suggesting a struggle between the weight of desire and the firmness of resolve. The arrangement mirrors fragmented reflections, using broken pixels to enhance the theme of repair versus damage, contributing to a layered, abstract narrative.
