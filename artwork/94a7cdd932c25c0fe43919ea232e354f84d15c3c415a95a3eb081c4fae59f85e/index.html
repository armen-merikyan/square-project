Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between grief and joy through contrasting color placement and pattern. The palette consists of deep blues representing grief and bright yellows and oranges symbolizing joy. The left side exhibits structured dots representing grief's heaviness, while the chaotic noise of the right side reflects joy's spark. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, aligning with the complexities of emotions. This design captures an unresolved tension, with central colors bridging the gap between the two emotional states.
