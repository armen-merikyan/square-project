Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the delicate balance between joy and grief, with colors expressing warmth and coolness. The palette features bright yellows and oranges representing joy contrasted with deep blues and muted purples symbolizing grief. The primary conflict lies in the upper rows where bright warm tones encounter the heavier cool tones, creating a shifting wave-like tension. The arrangement mimics a rising signal, suggesting hope amidst heaviness. There is intentional negative space around the vibrant pixels, embodying moments of quiet contemplation against clusters of emotional density.
