Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between confusion and clarity, utilizing a palette of lime, electric blue, magenta, and black to evoke a mechanical yet emotional atmosphere. The contrast primarily occurs at the intersections, where the more chaotic elements pierce through the quieter areas, representing confusion versus clarity. The arrangement is inspired by the 'rising signal' layout, embodying hope amidst disorder, while the integration of broken pixels introduces a sense of imperfection and unpredictability reflective of real emotions.
