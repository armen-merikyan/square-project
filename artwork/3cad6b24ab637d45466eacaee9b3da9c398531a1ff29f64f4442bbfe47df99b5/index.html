Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between panic and control, creating a visual representation of fluctuating stability. The selected palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue reflects a sense of nostalgia and uncertainty, with muted tones for control and explosive contrasts depicting panic. The grid-like pattern on the left breaks down towards the right, suggesting the collapse of structure into chaos, enhanced by soft edges to evoke a delicate tension. The arrangement promotes a collision of color across the central axis, embodying the theme of conflict between control and unrest.
