Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between panic and control through contrasting color and placement. The darker colors (#000000, #8B0000) evoke feelings of turmoil and chaos, while the cooler tones (#B0E0E6, #FFFFFF) represent clarity and control. A zigzag pattern emerges, where vibrant bursts of color collide against muted areas, illustrating the struggle and dynamic tension between order and chaos. The application of broken pixels introduces imperfection and unpredictability, aligning with the theme of emotional conflict. The layout maintains a flowing descent, symbolizing the continual shift of emotions.
