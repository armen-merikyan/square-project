Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes warm saturation, representing desire, against lower saturation, symbolizing emptiness. The palette features vibrant hues like hot coral and blood orange to evoke warmth and longing, while hollow blue and charcoal introduce a cool, restrained presence. The main contrast occurs along the diagonal, where warm colors collide with cool tones, emphasizing the struggle between overflow and containment. The broken pixels strategically disrupt this pattern, echoing the theme of disruption in desire and the boundaries created by emptiness.
