Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a tension between warmth and coolness, with desire symbolized by hot coral and blood orange that pulse against the cooler charcoal and hollow blue, creating a power dynamic of excess seeking containment. The layout 'halo_void' captures the hollow center where these colors explosively meet, suggesting a central conflict. The collision primarily occurs at the center, where the warm and cool colors intertwine in a spiral pattern, evoking both attraction and restraint. The choice of colors reflects saturation versus muted tones, enhancing the narrative of emotional disparity.
