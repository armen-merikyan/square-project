Artist Statement

This composition navigates the emotional tension between softness and severity, using a palette of powder pink for gentle warmth and carbon black for stark contrast. The bright accents of sharp crimson collide with cooler fields, represented by steel gray. The layout splits the canvas into two distinct areas, creating a passage where the intensity of color meets the softness of the maze-like patterns. The central collision spot signifies an emotional threshold, embodying the push and pull of forgiveness against guilt, amplified through broken pixels scattered throughout to disrupt and evoke tension.
