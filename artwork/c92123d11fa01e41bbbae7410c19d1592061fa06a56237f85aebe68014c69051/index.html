Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between the dense, pressing energy of 'pressure' and the calming expanse of 'quiet'. The palette of off-white and dark navy invokes serene yet stark contrasts, symbolizing the push and pull of these emotions. The main collision occurs diagonally, where deep densities of color meet lighter fields, creating tension and resolution. This pattern emerges from the seed's themes of pressure, distortion, and the interplay of hard versus soft edges.
