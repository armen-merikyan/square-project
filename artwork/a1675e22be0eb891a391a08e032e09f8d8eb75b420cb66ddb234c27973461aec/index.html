Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional struggle between defiance and submission through an intentional color composition. The palette of ripe green, acid green, honey gold, deep olive, and cream evokes a sense of unease, reflecting both vitality and restraint. The bold contrast occurs at the center where the core colors clash against a more subdued border, symbolizing the tension between the two emotions. The structured flow from top to bottom and the jagged edge style emphasize the chaotic elements, while the broken pixels serve as disruptions that echo the imperfections in this emotional narrative.
