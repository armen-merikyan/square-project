Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the contrast between strength and vulnerability, creating a dynamic interplay within the square. The warm ivory and deep wine shades suggest a mixture of protection and exposure. The dense, scattered application of colors on one corner symbolizes pressure, while the light, glitch-like patterns embody vulnerability. Using a palette from the 'guilt_forgiveness' family adds depth to the feeling of tension. The main collision occurs at the overstimulated corner, revealing a soft, fragmented center, thus enhancing a narrative of exposure amidst the struggle for shelter.
