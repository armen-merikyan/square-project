Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission, where sharp angular disruptions (representing defiance) clash against softer gradients of calm (representing submission). The palette incorporates Pale Aqua and Alarm Red to highlight the urgency and serenity of the scene. The main collision occurs in the center, creating an area where patterns clash: cracks defining the defiance theme giving way to a stepwell of submission, symbolizing the push and pull of emotions. The edge style is shadowed, creating a sense of protection yet ambiguity, embodying the conflict of order versus chaos.
