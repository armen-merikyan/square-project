Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve. The palette, featuring cosmic indigo and void black, expresses depth and weight, while star white and cold gold introduce luminosity and contrast. The center of the piece represents the collision of these emotions, using calm, muted colors to signify longing, against brighter accents to embody resolve. The ripple pattern for longing creates a sense of fluidity, while the ladder structure for resolve establishes a firm anchor. Broken pixels are dispersed to reflect repair amid damage, contributing to the overall theme of interruption and strain. This 8x8 grid balances quietness and density, inviting viewers to explore the emotional layers present in each color placement.
