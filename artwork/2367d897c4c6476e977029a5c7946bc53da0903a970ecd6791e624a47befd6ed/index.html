Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between trust and suspicion, symbolized through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of deep black, bright white, and soft pale blue indicates friction between shadowy doubt and illuminating trust. The main collision occurs in the central vertical split, where dark colors represent suspicion intertwining with bright accents of trust. The upward flow in the layout illustrates hope rising through layers of complexity, mirroring the struggles of resilience and fragility, while the broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, embodying the imperfections of trust. This composition results in a harmonious yet tense balance, capturing the essence of the seed's abstract narrative.
