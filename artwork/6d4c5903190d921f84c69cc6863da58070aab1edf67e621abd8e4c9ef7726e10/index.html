Artist Statement

This artwork captures the duality of confusion and clarity by contrasting dense, chaotic patterns with areas of order. The palette reflects a 'broken memory' theme with muted tones of sepia and gray, representing calmness and chaos, respectively. The upper half emphasizes clarity with a structured stair pattern, while the lower half embodies confusion with jagged, erratic teeth patterns, symbolizing conflict. The collision occurs where the structured upper rows meet the lower's disrupted chaos, illustrating a tension between two emotional states.
