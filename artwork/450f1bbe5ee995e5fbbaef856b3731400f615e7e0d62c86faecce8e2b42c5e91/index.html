Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a duality between vulnerability and strength, where soft hues collide with dense anchors. The palette, inspired by the 'broken_memory' theme, uses sepia, tan, and faded blue to create warmth mixed with a touch of nostalgia. The central collision occurs in the center with dense dark colors that press against the lighter, more exposed shades, symbolizing the tension between protection and exposure. Each pattern of strength and vulnerability weaves through the square, creating an abstract representation of the seed's themes of conflict and harmony.
