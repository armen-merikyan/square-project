Artist Statement

This composition reflects an emotional struggle between trust and suspicion, encapsulated in a soft glow of sepia and faded blue contrasted by the harshness of dust gray. The central diagonal line creates a split, suggesting a struggle to find a pathway through noise. The static pattern hints at suspicion while the gentle rain-like texture implies trust. The quiet channel layout allows for negative space to evoke a sense of tension and unease, emphasizing the interplay between these emotions. The jagged edges convey a sense of disruption, with the broken pixels symbolizing imperfections and interrupted flows.
