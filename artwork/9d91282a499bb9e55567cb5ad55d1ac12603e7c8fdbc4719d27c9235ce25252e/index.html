Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between vulnerability and strength through a split vertical layout. The left side is dominated by muted hues of sepia and tan, symbolizing vulnerability with faint echoes of flame patterns, suggesting a fragile foundation. The right side employs a cooler palette of faded blue, establishing a more assertive presence. The intention is to create a contrasting emotional landscape that reflects safety against exposure. The broken pixels add intentional disruptions, enhancing the theme of fragility against strong undercurrents. The palette's logic lies in transitioning from warmth to coolness, signifying life's delicate balance. The central collision occurs at the vertical divide, representing the intersection of vulnerability and strength.
