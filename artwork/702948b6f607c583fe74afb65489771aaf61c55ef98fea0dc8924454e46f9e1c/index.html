Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a struggle between clarity and confusion, reflecting on the tension of perfection versus imperfection. The chosen palette of deep blue, muted purple, vibrant yellow, and warm orange evokes both joy and grief, creating an emotional landscape that oscillates between warmth and depth. The rows feature a hard edge style with a light vs dark contrast to enhance the feeling of split screens and interrupted patterns. The placement of broken pixels introduces imperfection, symbolizing the disruption within symmetry, while negative space invites contemplation amid the chaos.
