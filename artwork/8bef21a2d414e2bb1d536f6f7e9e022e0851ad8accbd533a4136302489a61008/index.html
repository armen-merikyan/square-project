Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between love and betrayal. The palette of warm pinks and reds contrasts with muted gray and dark maroon, symbolizing the intertwining of joy and pain. The layout features mostly empty space, suggesting an isolated moment of reflection (the 'empty room') contrasted by the bold colors that represent passion and hurt. The blend of sharp and soft edges creates a dynamic rhythm, where the confrontation between contrasting hues (density vs. quiet) mirrors the turmoil in interpersonal relationships, all while preserving a sense of abstract integrity. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the theme of a fractured connection.
