Artist Statement

This piece captures the interplay between vulnerability and strength through an emotional palette of pale aqua and alarm red contrasting against ink blue and mist gray. The lightness of the center represents exposure while sharp edges in the surrounding layers suggest protection. The collision occurs prominently in the middle where cooler colors bleed into warmer tones, creating an emotional dialogue of conflict and harmony, reflective of the seed concepts.
