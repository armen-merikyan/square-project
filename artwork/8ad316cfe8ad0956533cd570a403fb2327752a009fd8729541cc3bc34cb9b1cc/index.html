Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between clarity and interference, where the vibrant green pulse battles against chaotic magenta static. The palette juxtaposes sharp 'signal green' and 'electric blue' against muted 'static gray,' representing communication's struggle within noise. The central collision occurs diagonally, emphasizing both signals and their disruptions, while broken pixels serve as intentional imperfections symbolizing the gaps in memory and reality.
