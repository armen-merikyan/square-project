Artist Statement

The emotional tone is rooted in the clash between defiance and submission, using color contrasts to represent the tension. The palette blends 'shelter_green' and 'warm_brown' for grounding with 'cold_white' and 'open_blue' to inject a sense of conflict. The main collision is central, characterized by a zigzag flow that disrupts the perceived order. The arrangement of pressure bands clashes with ring patterns, embodying the push-and-pull dynamic suggested by the seed. This creates a feeling of visual argument where neither side dominates completely yet leaves room for interpretation and balance.
