Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the struggle between control and surrender. The palette of signal green, electric blue, hot magenta alongside the grounding dead black and static gray creates a stark contrast of dominance and repose. The main collision occurs in the center, where the structured maze of control interjects with the flowing stripes of surrender, suggesting the push and pull of emotions battling for space. This creates an intricate narrative captured within the chaos and order reflected in each pixel.
