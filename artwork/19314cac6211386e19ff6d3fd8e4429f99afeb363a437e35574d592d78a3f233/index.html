Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the calm and chaos suggested by the seed. The palette, combined with cosmic indigo and void black, evokes deep introspection against brightness, representing the struggle between peace and restlessness. The main contrast emerges around the diagonal intersections, where structured barcode patterns collide with jagged teeth-like disruptions, symbolizing memories that both ground and unsettle. The placement of conflict hints at a struggle, with broken pixels that interrupt the flow, creating tension amidst the calm fields.
