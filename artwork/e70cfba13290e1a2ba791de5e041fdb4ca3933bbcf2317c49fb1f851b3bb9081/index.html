Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a tension between vulnerability and strength, illustrated through contrasting densely packed dark colors representing strength and light, softer hues symbolizing vulnerability. The palette, 'melancholy_wonder', includes rain blue and muted plum to evoke depth and introspection. The central collision occurs between strong hues at the edges and delicate, lighter shades with broken pixels disrupting an otherwise calm pattern, conveying the fragility and resilience of connections amidst chaos.
