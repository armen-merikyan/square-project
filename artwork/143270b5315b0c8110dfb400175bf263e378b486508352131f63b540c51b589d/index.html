Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through a split vertical layout. The left half embodies loneliness with darker, muted shades—dusty sepia and gray hues creating a sense of isolation and weight. The right side bursts with brighter tan and faded blue accents, symbolizing connection and warmth. The arrangement features stair-like patterns on the left, intersected by dot patterns on the right, creating a visual discussion of hidden versus exposed feelings. The loss of symmetry enhances the concept of imbalance between solitary and communal experiences.
