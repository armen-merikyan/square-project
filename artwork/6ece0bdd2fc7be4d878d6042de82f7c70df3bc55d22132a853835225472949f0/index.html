Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette consists of ripe greens and honey golds, symbolizing growth and warmth amidst deep olives and cream, invoking a sense of safety. The main collision occurs at the center, where broken pixel elements create imperfection, suggesting the fragility of strength. Layers in the layout echo different emotional densities—from soft, warm hues to the cooler, heavier shadows, portraying a journey from safety to exposure. The result is a dynamic abstract pattern that intimately captures the essence of the seed.
