Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrast of softness and severity. The palette, with muted shades of powder pink and warm white, represents softness, while carbon black and steel gray introduce a sense of severity. The placement of pulse marks and channel patterns echoes the tension inherent in the seed's themes, creating a dynamic interplay as lighter hues swirl within darker tones. The central area features a cluster of soft colors, simulating a gentle pull against the harder lines from the severity, representing the complexity of guilt and forgiveness. The broken pixels disrupt the flow, suggesting the fleeting moments where clarity meets chaos. This visual conveys a narrative of navigating the emotional landscapes between hurt and healing, framed by both enclosed whispers of warmth and sharp boundaries of reality.
