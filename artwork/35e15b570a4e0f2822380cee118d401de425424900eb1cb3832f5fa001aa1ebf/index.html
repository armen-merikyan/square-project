Artist Statement

This artwork explores the duality of trust and suspicion, represented through a contrasting palette of somber shades and warm accents. The colors embody feelings of unease, with dark tones capturing suspicion and soft hues illuminating trust. The central collision occurs where the stairs and radial patterns intersect, creating tension that evokes a sense of resilience amidst fragility. The layout emphasizes negative space, allowing areas of calm to exist against more chaotic segments, mirroring the emotional turmoil present in the seed.
