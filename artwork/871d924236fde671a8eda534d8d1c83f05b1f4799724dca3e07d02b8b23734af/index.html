Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the confrontation between trust and suspicion, with a palette that contrasts dark violets representing suspicion against soft golds and pale greens for trust. The intentional placement creates a diagonal flow that embodies a struggle; dark, heavy colors in the upper left represent suspicion, while the lighter hues gradually invade from the opposite corner, signifying trust's persistence. The use of broken pixels introduces discord, emphasizing the imperfections within the themes, creating an abstract rhythm where dense areas disrupt otherwise calm expanses.
