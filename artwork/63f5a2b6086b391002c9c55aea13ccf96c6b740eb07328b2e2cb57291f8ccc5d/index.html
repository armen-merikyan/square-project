Artist Statement

This abstract artwork captures the intense emotional juxtaposition of joy and grief through a contrasting palette of bright yellows and muted blues. The left half employs a 'split_screen' pattern depicting a joyful atmosphere layered against the serene chaos of muted purple and deep blue on the right. Peak bright colors interrupt the darker tones, simulating conflict and collision between emotional states. The choice of colors and arrangement channels the seed's duality, abstractly representing waves of emotion with hidden depths exposed through tension in the pixel arrangement.
