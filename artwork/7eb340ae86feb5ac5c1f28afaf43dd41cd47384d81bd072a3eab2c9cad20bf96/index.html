Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional duality of vulnerability and strength, emphasized through contrasting density and transparency. The palette, 'mourning_renewal', evokes a deep, reflective mood with the dark base representing strength and resilience, while lighter hues suggest fragility. The dominant darker colors fill the center, creating a heavy core that contrasts sharply with the lighter, more fluid edges representing vulnerability. The collision occurs within the central rows, highlighting the struggle between stability and exposure, interspersed with intentional broken pixels to signify imperfections and disruptions.
