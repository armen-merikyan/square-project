Artist Statement

The emotional tone merges trust's stable base with suspicion's dark slivers. The palette of 'melancholy wonder' draws from soft cyan and lavender gray to evoke a lingering tension between warmth and coolness. The main collision occurs at the top left, where deeper hues meet lighter, halo-like patterns creating a fractured but resilient path. The arrangement's asymmetry reflects the split nature of the seed, capturing the push and pull of emotion against a backdrop of static noise.
