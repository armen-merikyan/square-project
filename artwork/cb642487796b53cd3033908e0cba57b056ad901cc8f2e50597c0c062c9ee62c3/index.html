Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of fear and hope through contrasting color placements and patterns. The black and dark violet form the heavier, fearful outer border, incorporating asymmetry to evoke pressure and intensity. The soft gold and pale green in the center signal hope and light, creating a maze-like pattern that resonates with a sense of rising or renewal. The jagged edges symbolize a sharp contrast against the softer flow of the inner colors, and the broken pixels disrupt the rigid uniformity, suggesting vulnerability amidst strength. This composition reflects the interplay of emotions through a visual representation of tension and release.
