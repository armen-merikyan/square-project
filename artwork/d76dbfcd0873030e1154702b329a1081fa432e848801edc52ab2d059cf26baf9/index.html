Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the duality of vulnerability and strength through a dynamic interplay of color and form. The palette, 'desire_emptiness', evokes a warm and heavy sensation with vibrant accent colors against subdued shades, representing the contrast between strength and vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center where intense colors clash with gentle pulses, echoing the theme of protective yet exposed areas. The layout suggests a pressure point in one corner, symbolizing a buildup of emotional weight that seeps through the grid, reminiscent of a star under pressure yet striving to shine against the tide of darkness.
