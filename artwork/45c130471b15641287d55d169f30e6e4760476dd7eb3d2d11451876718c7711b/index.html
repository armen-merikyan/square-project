Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional collision between calmness and rage, using a palette of red and pale blue to symbolize heat and coolness. The left side embodies calm through low contrast maze patterns, while the right side exhibits the chaos of rage with radial bursts of red. The contrasting densities create a visual tension where the two emotions collide, reflecting the seed's themes of pushing against pressure and eventual release. Broken pixels on each side emphasize imperfection and unpredictability, further heightening the emotional engagement.
