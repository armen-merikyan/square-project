Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between longing and resolve through contrasting colors and emotional patterns. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold evokes warmth and intensity, while maintaining a balance of darker tones for depth. The pattern incorporates broken dots and islands, symbolizing the yearning and determination within separate yet colliding spaces. The main contrast occurs in the center, where the vibrant accents (burnt orange and gold) clash against the muted dark red and charcoal, representing a focal point of struggle and resolution amidst a vast tranquil backdrop.
