Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between longing and resolve through its color and structure. The palette combines soft tones of powder pink and warm white with the severity of carbon black and sharp crimson, suggesting a duality of emotion. The layout emphasizes a clear dividing line, representing the threshold of feelings. The colder, darker edges represent resolve, while the lighter, fragmented sections symbolize longing, creating a visual conflict. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, reflecting repair versus damage, echoing the seed's narrative without literal depictions.
