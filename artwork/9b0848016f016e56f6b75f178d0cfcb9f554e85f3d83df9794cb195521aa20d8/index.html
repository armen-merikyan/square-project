Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between trust, represented by soft blues and whites, and suspicion, marked by deep blacks and dark hues. The palette was chosen to create a visual tension with cool calming shades clashing against encroaching dark surfaces. The contrast is at the center, where the dense black intrudes upon the softer pastels, symbolizing suspicion breaking through trust's boundaries. This arrangement translates the seed's themes into an abstract pattern emphasizing emotional conflict through a vertical split and chaotic interaction.
