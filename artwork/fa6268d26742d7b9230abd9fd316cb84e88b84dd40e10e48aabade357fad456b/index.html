Artist Statement

This pixel art explores the emotional dichotomy of fear and hope. The upper half is dominated by dark tones (black and deep violet), reflecting fear's jagged chaos and mirror-like textures through dense patterns, while the lower half is illuminated with soft gold and pale green, symbolizing hope's gentle uprising and positivity. The stark contrast between the heaviness of the top and the lightness of the bottom creates a palpable tension, emphasizing the theme of struggle and yearning. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, mirroring life's inherent uncertainties. Overall, the abstract pattern flows diagonally, suggesting a battle that resides within us, oscillating between tumult and tranquility.
