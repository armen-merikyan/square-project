Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between softness and severity, represented through a combination of warm pinks (softness) and deep blacks and grays (severity). The palette emphasizes the tension between light and dark, where the contrast serves to create a pulse-like rhythm in the arrangement. The collision emerges at the center region of the grid, marked by a dense cluster of darker colors while surrounded by lighter hues. This layout speaks to an island of severity amidst a sea of softness, effectively capturing the seed's abstract narrative.
