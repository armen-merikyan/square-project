Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a delicate balance between longing and resolve, creating a dialogue of compression and release. The palette is anchored in deep navy to evoke serenity, with touches of dim cyan offering an accent of urgency, capturing the essence of tension that comes from broken connections and pulsing rhythms. The pattern features alternating lines resembling a fault line (longing) in a jagged, broken flow against structured, interlocking segments (resolve), facilitating a rich interplay of visuals that embody connection and ruptures. The primary collision occurs centrally, where the two pattern types meet, hinting at conflict yet achieving a coexistence of emotions, with deliberate broken pixels creating intentional disruptions contributing to the overall narrative.
