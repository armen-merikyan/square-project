Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between clarity and confusion, mirroring the duality of signal and noise. The dominant dark tones create a shadowy presence, suggesting the weight of memory and interference, while the scattered accents of cyan represent moments of clarity and escape. The center holds a void, symbolizing isolation, encircled by ripples of lighter tones that try to break through the static, embodying a struggle for presence in the noise. The palette reflects loneliness yet hints at connection, with contrasting colors colliding to create a dance of despair and hope.
