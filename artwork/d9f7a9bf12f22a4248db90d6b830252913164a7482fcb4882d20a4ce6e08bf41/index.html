Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the delicate interplay of longing and resolve through a contrast of warm and cool tones. The palette of moss green and rust evokes a sense of both resilience and fragility, reflecting the emotional tension between reaching out and the steady strength of resolve. The main collision occurs in the center, where a lattice pattern of longing intersects with a split-screen representation of resolve, creating a dynamic visual tension. Negative space allows for clarity, balancing the crowded elements with areas of quiet reflection.
