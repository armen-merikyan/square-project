Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and confusion. The palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple captures the warmth of hope clashing with the coldness of uncertainty. The sharp contrast between the bright yellow and muted shades creates a sense of tension, while the repeated overlapping patterns suggest chaos resistant to resolution. The organic flow from the center outward symbolizes the fight for clarity amidst overwhelming noise, intertwined through pixels that symbolize falling and rising emotions.
