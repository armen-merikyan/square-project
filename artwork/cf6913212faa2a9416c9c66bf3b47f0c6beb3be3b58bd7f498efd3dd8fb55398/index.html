Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between exposure and safety, manifesting through a palette of soft but contrasting colors. The warm brown and green evoke shelter, while cool blues and whites suggest exposure. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense layered colors meet thin stripes, creating an echoing effect of softness battling severity. The scattered repair layout symbolizes the attempts to harmonize these opposing forces. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, emphasizing the fragility inherent in the concepts of safety and exposure.
