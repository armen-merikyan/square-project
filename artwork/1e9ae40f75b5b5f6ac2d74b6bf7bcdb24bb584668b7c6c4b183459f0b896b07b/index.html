Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional contrasts of vulnerability and strength. The palette combines warm coral and soft amber suggesting vulnerability, while cold slate and blue-black bring in the element of strength. The main collision occurs in the center, where the pressure bands representing vulnerability meet the channel-like structures of strength. This abstract arrangement embodies the push-and-pull of feelings associated with exposure versus protection, illustrated through a mix of dense and quiet spaces.
