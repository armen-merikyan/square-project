Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise). The palette of navy, black, and dim cyan creates a shadowy atmosphere that suggests depth while allowing bright white accents to emerge as signals of clarity. The pattern features a collision of shatter and teeth motifs, illustrated as muted colors surround a vibrant core in the center, symbolizing the core pulse trying to push through layers of disruption. This composition embodies a distinct tension with densely packed noise at the edges gradually softening towards a central focus, representing the interaction of exposure and shelter against a backdrop of echoes.
