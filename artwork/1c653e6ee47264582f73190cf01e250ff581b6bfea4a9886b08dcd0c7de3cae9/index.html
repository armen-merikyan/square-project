Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional duality of longing and resolve. The color palette, reflecting earthy moss green and warm rust alongside muted bone white and stone gray, captures a sense of resilience in fragility. The pattern features central collisions of clear shapes amidst a quiet field of broken pixels, suggesting a pulse of unresolved tension. The primary contrast lies in the edges creating both division and connection, mirroring the interplay of structure and imperfection in human emotion.
