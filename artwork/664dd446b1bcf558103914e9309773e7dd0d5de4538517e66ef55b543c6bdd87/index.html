Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between loneliness and connection through a carefully chosen palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon. The off-centered placement of the bright colors contrasts with the surrounding muted tones, embodying a small burst of connection amidst a larger space of solitude. The grid pattern evokes the feeling of isolation, while the soft transitions mimic the complexities of hidden emotional layers. The arrangement suggests a narrative of struggle and warmth, where the 'tiny plus' shape seed represents a flicker of hope against a backdrop of quiet desolation.
