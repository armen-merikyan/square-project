Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the battle between control and panic, using a palette that balances deep navy and black against lighter tones. The left side embodies control with a structured pattern, while the right is an echo of chaos through broken dots and a radial distribution of color. The contrast of stability versus instability is emphasized in the uneven distribution, creating tension. The seed informs the dynamic between the clean and the fractured, crafting a visual narrative of emotional conflict and resolution.
