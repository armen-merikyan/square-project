Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of panic versus control, translating into a contrasting color palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold. The central pattern suggests a 'rising signal' with vibrant segments contrasting against the grounded, dark edges representative of chaos and fear. The broken pixel elements add slight imperfection, enhancing the theme of asymmetry between inner calm and external turbulence, while the layout creates a sense of climbing tension that invites reflection on the delicate balance between order and chaos.
