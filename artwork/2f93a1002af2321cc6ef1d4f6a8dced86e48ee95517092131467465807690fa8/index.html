Artist Statement

The emotional tone leans heavily on contrasting feelings of trust and suspicion, merging warm hues with darker tones to reflect their collision. The palette, composed of 'shelter green' for comfort, 'warm brown' for grounding, 'pale skin' as an accent of humanity, 'cold white' to signify isolation, and 'open blue' representing transparency, invokes a sense of tension. A dominant presence of warmth against isolated cooler colors creates a palpable excitement of trust struggling against the shadows of suspicion. The main contrast occurs in the lower right corner where deeper hues merge, embodying the pressure of doubt. The seed inspired a layout of spreading marks influenced by contrasting emotional qualities, showcasing both the resilience of trust and the fragility of suspicion.
