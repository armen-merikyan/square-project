Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a battle between strict control and soft surrender, manifested through color saturation and pattern dynamics. The palette of cosmic indigo, void black, and cold gold represents the tension of control (darker shades) while deep teal and star white signify open, surrendered spaces. The dominant contrast occurs at the overfilled center with vibrant radials swirling outwards, symbolizing chaos and overflow, while gentle, cracked patterns along the edges evoke a controlled environment, creating a rich interplay of pressure and release, akin to signals breaking through interference.
