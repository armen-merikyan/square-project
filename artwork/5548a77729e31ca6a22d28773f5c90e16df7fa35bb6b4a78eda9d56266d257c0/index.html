Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette combines warm clay and gold against cool blues and lilacs, echoing intimacy and distance. The layout exhibits pressure at one corner, with a shatter pattern representing longing colliding with stacked elements for resolve. The major collision occurs in the upper-left corner where warm colors accumulate, while cooler shades recede, creating a sense of both containment and overflow. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, inviting contemplation on repair and damage, reflecting the seed's narrative.
