Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the emotional duality of envy and gratitude through a balance of dark and light colors. The palette employs deep blacks and reds contrasted by pale blues and whites to represent the weight of envy versus the lightness of gratitude. The shifting patterns create a sense of movement, with zigzag edges suggesting disruption and noise against centered calmness. The placement of colors creates a collision of tones that echo the turmoil of the emotions while allowing for quiet spaces to breathe.
