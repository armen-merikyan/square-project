Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a struggle between pressure and quietude, where dense clusters of color clash against open spaces. The palette features muted colors like 'off_white' and 'dust_gray' representing quietness, combined with the compressed intensity of 'compressed_red' and the dark depth of 'dark_navy'. This arrangement allows for a stark contrast between muted fields and bursts of energy. The layout follows a broken frame, symbolizing interruptions that arise from emotional tensions, while diagonal patterns reflect a sense of argument and conflict. The collision primarily occurs at the center, where the pressure builds, and the tension dissolves towards the periphery, creating a visual narrative of struggle and release.
