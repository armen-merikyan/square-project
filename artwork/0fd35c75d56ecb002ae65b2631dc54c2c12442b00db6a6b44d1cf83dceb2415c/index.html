Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between trust and suspicion, embodying a flow from organized pattern to chaotic disruption. The warm colors representing trust are juxtaposed with the darker, fragmented tones signifying suspicion. The layout, defined by a hollow center surrounded by contrasting radiance, symbolizes the emotional boundaries that fluctuate between reassurance and uncertainty. The textured interruptions and the dagger-like spikes of black suggest moments of vulnerability as trust is challenged. The palette includes a mix of reds and pale blues accentuating this emotional conflict, creating an atmosphere dense with tension and unresolved narratives.
