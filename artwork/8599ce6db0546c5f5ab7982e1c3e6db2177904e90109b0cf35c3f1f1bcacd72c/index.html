Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured in this composition reflects the tension between love and betrayal, embodied through warm reds and soft pinks contrasted with stark greys and dark maroon. The palette symbolizes vulnerability and warmth against a backdrop of coldness and conflict. The layout embodies an argument grid, showcasing a clash between structured 'frames' and disordered 'dots' representing emotional noise. The contrast emerges where vibrant pink is unexpectedly interrupted by dark hues, amplifying feelings of loneliness and disruption. This dynamic evokes a sense of both connection and dissonance, spun from the seed's themes.
