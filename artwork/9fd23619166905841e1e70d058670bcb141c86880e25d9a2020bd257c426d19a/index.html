Artist Statement

This composition portrays a vivid emotional dichotomy between dread and relief through color contrast and spatial organization. The dark tones, represented by storm gray and near-black, evoke feelings of weight and oppression, while the lighter hues of pale mint and faded gold introduce relief and spaciousness. The main collision occurs in the lower corner, where the storm gray and soft sky clash against a lighter, airy upper space, creating a tension that mirrors the struggle suggested by the seed. The arrangement of the colors follows a diagonal layout, enhancing the notion of a conflict spreading throughout the square.
