Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between stillness and motion, represented by gentle colors clashing against dark, restless tones. The palette combines muted off-white and faint peach with deep, dark navy and compressed red, creating a stark contrast. The main collision takes place in the center, where the restless 'noise' pattern disrupts the 'shatter' calmness of the edges. The layout leans towards a diagonal arrangement, enhancing the sense of conflict between memories of peace and the chaos of motion, culminating in a subtle yet impactful narrative of contrast that resonates with the emotional seed.
