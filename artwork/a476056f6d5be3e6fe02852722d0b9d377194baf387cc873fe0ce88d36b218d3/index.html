Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of confusion and clarity, using a palette that juxtaposes vibrant electric blue and magenta with the dark grounding nature of black. The left side, representing clarity, employs a structured, frame-like arrangement of clean lines, while the right side embodies confusion through scattered, rain-like patterns of color that disrupt symmetry. The placement of broken pixels adds to the feeling of chaos interrupting a balanced design, creating an unresolved tension that invites viewers to reflect on their own experiences of clarity pierced by confusion.
