Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the pull between defiance and submission, using a palette of deep blues and muted blacks interspersed with soft yellows to signify conflict and resolution. The main contrast occurs in the center where bright yellow dots collide with the darker surrounding colors, creating a pulse against the quieter edges, symbolizing both tension and release. The seed's themes of 'channel' and 'chaos' translate into a pattern that reflects inconsistent, jagged formations echoing the battle between structure and surrender.
