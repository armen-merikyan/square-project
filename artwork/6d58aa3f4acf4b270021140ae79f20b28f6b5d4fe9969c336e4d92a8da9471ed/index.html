Artist Statement

This pixel art embodies the dichotomy of confusion and clarity through a structured breakdown. The palette of dark violet, soft gold, and pale green hints at emotional weight and subtle hope amidst chaos. The arrangement features harmonious symmetry disrupted by intentional breaks, creating a narrative of 'symmetry vs break'. The thick outlines suggest a repressive edge, while the checker pattern conveys disarray, evoking a visual representation of internal conflict. The faint pulses of color create moments of contrast, echoing the emotionally charged seed.
