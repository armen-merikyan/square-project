Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the interplay between longing and resolve, manifesting a sense of distance bridged by connection, yet interrupted by chaotic undercurrents. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green creates a stark contrast, with black and violet denoting the pressure of longing, while soft gold and pale green represent moments of resolve, introducing warmth amid the cool depths of chaos. The primary collision occurs in the upper left corner, where densely packed checker patterns hint at longing, while softer channels in the remaining space suggest a calmer, steady resolve. This tension cultivates an abstract rhythm of emotional movement, embodying the complexities of intimacy separated by distance.
