Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between loneliness and connection. The palette revolves around dark navy and muted cyan to symbolize depth and isolation, contrasted with faint white highlights to represent connection's fragile moments. The collision occurs at the center, where dense clusters surround a negative space, embodying the push-pull dynamics of feeling hidden versus exposed. Checker patterns suggest the loneliness while radial bursts hint at connections that are attempted but not fully realized, resulting in an asymmetric flow from bottom to top.
