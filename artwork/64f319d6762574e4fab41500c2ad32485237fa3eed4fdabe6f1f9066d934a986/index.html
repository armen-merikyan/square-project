Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional conflict between dread and relief. The top half is dominated by dark, stormy hues that represent the weight of inevitable descent, instilling a feeling of dread. The bottom half transitions to softer, lighter colors representing a release, where pale mint and faded gold suggest moments of exhale and clarity. The collision occurs between the darkest and lightest colors at the center, highlighting the tension and transition between these opposing emotions. The overall layout emphasizes a gradual loosening of pressure from the top to the bottom, embodying the theme of relief emerging from chaos.
