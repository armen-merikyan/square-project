Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between clarity and interference. The palette, using deep navy and dim cyan, evokes feelings of isolation contrasted with occasional bursts of signal and light, adding dynamics to the piece. The tension arises from the collision of smooth threads and rigid stripes, suggesting a struggle for coherence amidst chaos, with the vibrant center bleeding outwards into darker edges—illustrating the push-pull of signal fighting noise.
