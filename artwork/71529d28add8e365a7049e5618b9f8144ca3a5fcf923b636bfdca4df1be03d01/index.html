Artist Statement

This artwork embodies a struggle between defiance and submission, visually represented through stark contrasts and emotional collisions. The palette combines intense reds with soothing pale blues and deep blacks, suggesting a battle of warmth against coolness. The main contrast occurs in the center, where fragmented reds and blacks interrupt a calmer outer layer of pale blue and white, symbolizing a chaotic spark at the core of a tranquil facade. This reflects the inward spiral of conflict and resolution, giving depth to the emotional narrative.
