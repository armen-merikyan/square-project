Artist Statement

The emotional tone emerges from the tension between longing and resolve. The use of 'pale aqua' and 'mist gray' reflects a serene foundation, while 'alarm red' introduces an urgent, vibrant contrast, symbolizing the push and pull of emotions. The main collision occurs at the center where the 'spark' pattern of longing integrates with the steady 'ripple' of resolve, creating a dynamic interplay. The layout begins at the corner, manifesting growth and urgency as colors 'spread diagonally', fostering a rhythm of contained yet overflowing emotions.
