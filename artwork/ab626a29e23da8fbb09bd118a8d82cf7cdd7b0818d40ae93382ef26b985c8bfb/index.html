Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between control and surrender, illuminating how these opposing forces can coexist and interact. The palette of warm oranges and cool blues creates a contrast representing the heat of control against the calmness of surrender. The main contrast occurs centrally, where the structured quiet field of deep blue meets the vibrating lattice of coral and orange, suggesting a rupture in the flow, an emotional collision. The broken color elements introduce imperfections, enhancing the theme of tension as they disrupt the solid color fields. This composition will evoke a sense of dynamic unrest, reinforcing the struggle between containment and overflow.
