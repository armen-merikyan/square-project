Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrasts of loneliness and connection, utilizing a palette of navy, black, single white, and dim cyan to evoke a sense of solitude contrasted with fleeting moments of connection. The dominant black and navy colors represent the weight of loneliness, creating a dense, heavy atmosphere, while interspersed white and cyan offer bursts of relief and light, symbolizing connection and hope. The layout features a protective outer layer reflecting loneliness, with a contrasting core of lighter colors suggesting hidden connections that might erupt from beneath the surface. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, hinting at the complexity of human relationships and emotions. The composition flows with a wave-like movement, capturing the push and pull of emotional states throughout the piece.
