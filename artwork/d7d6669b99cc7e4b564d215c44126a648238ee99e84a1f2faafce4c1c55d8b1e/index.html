Artist Statement

This artwork balances the emotional tones of longing and resolve through sharp contrasts of colors and placements. The palette includes soft 'ripe_green' and warm 'honey_gold' to evoke longing, while 'deep_olive' and 'acid_green' represent the steadiness of resolve. The layout follows a broken mirror, with a central collision of dense clusters and distant echoes reflecting unresolved emotional tension. The negative space invites introspection, while broken pixels create intentional disruptions to highlight imperfection in the emotional spectrum.
