Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes clarity and confusion through a palette emphasizing contrasts between deep blacks and soft pale blues. The layout and flow represent falling pixels, descending from a dense top that feels chaotic, moving into lighter spaces where clarity shines through. Cumulative dark tones clash with lighter accents, creating collisions that mirror the seed’s thematic duality, particularly around the edges where structure succumbs to breakdown. The use of broken pixels embodies imperfection within symmetry, enhancing the emotional conflict of the piece.
