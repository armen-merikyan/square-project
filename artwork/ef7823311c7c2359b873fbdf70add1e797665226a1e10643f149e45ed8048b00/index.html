Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between pressure and quiet, using a palette of soft and dark tones to juxtapose feelings of calm against dense clusters of color. The main collision occurs on the left side, where deep dark navy meets a muted off-white patchwork, creating a dynamic dialogue of sharp versus soft. The central cage shape is abstracted through dense placement, suggesting a strong, weighted core that loosens into softer fields, resonating with the seed's themes of disruption and clarity amidst confusion.
