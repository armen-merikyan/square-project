Artist Statement

This composition depicts the emotional tension between tenderness and rage. The palette focuses on saturated red and muted blue-gray tones, creating a visual dichotomy that reflects an internal struggle. The upward movement of colors on the left juxtaposes against warmer, chaotic shades on the right. The central clash symbolizes moments of conflict, while broken pixels introduce imperfection, representing the fragility of emotions. This interplay of colors and patterns evokes a narrative of vulnerability in the face of overwhelming passion.
