Artist Statement

The composition encapsulates a struggle between defiance and submission, illustrated through contrasting patterns and color usage. The predominant sharp crimson and carbon black create a sense of defiance, while the softer tones of powder pink and warm white suggest submission. The central zone is densely packed, representing an inner turmoil, while the surrounding areas are more subdued to suggest outreach and vulnerability. The horizontal and vertical lines, inspired by the 'cross_current' layout, clash in the center, creating a tense crossing point where emotions collide. Broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, enhancing the feeling of imperfection in this emotional landscape.
