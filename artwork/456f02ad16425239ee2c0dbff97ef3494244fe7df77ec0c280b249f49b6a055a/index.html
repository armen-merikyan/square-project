Artist Statement

The emotional tone emerges from the duality of love and betrayal, captured through contrasting colors and patterns. The chosen palette of warm pink and red juxtaposes against the cool gray and dark maroon, reinforcing the tension between affection and discord. The layout reflects an argument grid, with fragmented sections of dots representing love and targeted patterns illustrating betrayal. The collision points of vibrant colors against muted shades create sharp contrasts, while areas of negative space suggest unresolved emotions. This arrangement embodies the complexities and fragmented nature of relationships, guided by the seed's themes.
