Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the interplay between nostalgia and progress, with colors signifying tension and release. The palette of lime, electric blue, magenta, and black evokes feelings of vibrant energy meeting dark introspection. The contrasting colors collide in the center, with lighter tones pressing against darker edges, creating a dynamic tension. The layout mimics a mirror with planned imperfections symbolizing the unavoidable chaos within structured emotions. The negative space allows for breathing room, embodying the release amidst pressure.
