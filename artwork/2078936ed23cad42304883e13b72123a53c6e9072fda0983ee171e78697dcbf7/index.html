Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional contrast between vulnerability and strength through a visually striking arrangement. The central pixels embody strength ('negative_cross' pattern), holding a firm base with darker tones, while the surrounding elements express vulnerability ('barcode' pattern), arranged in lighter hues that evoke a sense of exposure. The color palette includes pale aqua, mist gray, alarm red, ink blue, and clear white, transitioning from a tranquil base to punctuated moments of urgency. The solid core surrounded by softer, interrupted colors creates a dynamic tension, representing the push-and-pull of these opposing emotions. The main collision occurs at the borders, creating an asymmetrical interaction that invites the viewer to perceive balance amid chaos.
