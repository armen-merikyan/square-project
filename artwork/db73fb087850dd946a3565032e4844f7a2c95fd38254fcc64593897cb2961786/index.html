Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of safety versus exposure through a mix of warm and cool colors. The palette blends warm brown and shelter green for safety, contrasted by cold white and open blue to depict exposure. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense pulse marks overlap with a softer broken frame. The quiet channel layout allows space for reflection amidst the pressure, embodying both vulnerability and stability.
