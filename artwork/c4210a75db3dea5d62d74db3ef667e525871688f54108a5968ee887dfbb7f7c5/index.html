Artist Statement

This piece embodies the interplay of vulnerability and strength through the use of a stark palette. The dominant dark tones represent strength and stability, forming a dense base across the center, while lighter colors emerge at the edges, signifying vulnerability and exposure. The spiral layout creates a pull towards a quiet off-center point, creating a fluid tension. The broken pixels strategically disrupt the harmony, echoing the fragility within strength. Overall, the artwork reflects the constant push and pull between feeling both secure and exposed.
