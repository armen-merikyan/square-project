Artist Statement

The emotional tone derived from the seed suggests a contrast embodied in the palette, with deep burnt oranges and dark reds counterbalanced by lighter tones. The composition plays with symmetry and brokenness, utilizing a top-weighted edge style that captures a sense of descent. The collisions occur in the arrangement of dark masses versus patches of light, representing relief fighting against dread. This interplay creates a dynamic visual tension, reflecting the essence of the seed's exploration of emotional contrasts.
