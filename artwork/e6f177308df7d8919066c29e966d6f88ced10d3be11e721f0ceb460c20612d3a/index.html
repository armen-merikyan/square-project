Artist Statement

The emotional tone of defiance versus submission is explored through a palette that balances the warmth of yellow and orange with the depth of muted purple and deep blue, suggesting a tension between light and weight. The central pattern reflects defiance through diagonal stripes against a backdrop of grid-like structures, hinting at submission. This creates a layered texture where colors collide and contrast, mirroring the struggle between opposing forces. The dominant movement is upward, representing hope amidst weight, while the negative space provides breathing room, allowing the viewer to contemplate the intricate balance of emotions.
