Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances envy and gratitude, depicted through jagged versus structured patterns. The palette of sepia and faded blue accentuates the contrast between lightness and heaviness. Sharp edges flow diagonally, interrupting softer shades, symbolizing the push-pull dynamic of emotions represented by the seed. The arrangement of colors creates visual tension with bright interruptions that resonate against muted backgrounds, echoing through the square composition.
