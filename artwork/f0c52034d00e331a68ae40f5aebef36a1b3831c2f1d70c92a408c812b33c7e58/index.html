Artist Statement

This composition embodies a tension between restlessness and peace through a contrasting layout. The outer border in muted tones represents the calm, while the inner section with dynamic, interrupted patterns encapsulates the unrest. The palette blends soft mint and pale sky colors with storm gray and near-black to evoke both relief and dread. The main collision occurs diagonally as visual elements disrupt patterns, reflecting the restless pursuit of peace. This pattern echoes memory and motion, illustrating the complex interplay of stillness and activity.
