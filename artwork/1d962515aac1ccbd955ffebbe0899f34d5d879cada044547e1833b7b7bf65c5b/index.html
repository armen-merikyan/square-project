Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes warmth and cool detachment, reflecting the conflict of love and betrayal. The chosen palette incorporates soft pinks and deep maroons, embodying love, contrasted with sharp grays, representing betrayal. The main collision occurs through the radial pattern of warmth at the center, surrounded by chaotic gray noise, symbolizing internal struggles. The burned edges hint at damage yet maintain a soft core, suggesting vulnerability. Overall, the arrangement of colors creates a dynamic between calmness and turmoil, telling the story of emotional conflict.
