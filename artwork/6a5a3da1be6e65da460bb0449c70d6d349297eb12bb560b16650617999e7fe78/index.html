Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between trust and suspicion, represented through a palette of dark and muted tones, interspersed with flashes of light. The chosen colors create a boundary of pressure, encapsulating the theme of signal versus noise. The central collision occurs in the drop from warm hues to stark shadows, mimicking interruption and creating an emotional pull between stability and uncertainty. Patterns of ring and ember coexist, suggesting resilience against a backdrop of fragility, leading to a dynamic interplay in the grid's structure.
