Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dichotomy between 'emptiness' and 'desire' through a carefully chosen palette that balances warm and cool colors, suggesting a longing amidst solitude. The dark violet and soft gold encapsulate desire, creating a tension against the emptiness represented by the blacks and greens. Contrast arises in the layout where pixels shift from dark to lighter shades, mirroring the upward signal of hope against the descent of emptiness. The broken pixels add a sense of imperfection, echoing the fragmented feelings of yearning. The arrangement suggests clashing rhythms of aspiration and melancholy, inviting the viewer to explore the narrative of emotional rise and fall without literal imagery.
