Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the essence of longing and resolve, where each color embodies the complex dynamics of expectation and fulfillment. The palette combines signal green and electric blue to evoke a sensation of vitality against the static gray and dead black that anchor the piece in depth and intensity. The main contrast appears in the center where the vibrant green pulses against the muted tones, illustrating the collision of energy and calmness. The broken pixels add intentional disruptions—symbols of both damage and repair—while the arrangement of colors suggests a thread of connection that runs through the artwork, symbolizing unity amidst separation.
