Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the conflict between certainty and curiosity, manifesting in a palette that juxtaposes warm, muted tones with stark contrasts. The colors invoke a sense of a struggle between order (represented by steady browns) and curiosity (expressed by softer blues). The main contrast lies at the center, where the warm sepia tones collide with the cool faded blue, suggesting a rising signal amidst chaotic currents. The wave shape seed influences the upward and downward motions, reinforcing a narrative of tension and release.
