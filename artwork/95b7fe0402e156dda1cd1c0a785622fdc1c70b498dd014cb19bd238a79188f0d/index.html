Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension of joy against grief through the use of a varied palette that balances bright and muted tones. The chosen colors of vibrant yellow and orange evoke joy, while deep blue and muted purple introduce the somberness of grief. The sharp versus soft contrast represents the clash between these emotions, with a layered pattern that enhances the idea of falling pixels to symbolize tears and rising flames—suggesting both loss and warmth, with noticeable corridors of negative space promoting introspection amidst the busyness. The split arrangement echoes the theme of duality, providing a visual narrative that immerses the observer in the emotional conflict suggested by the seed.
