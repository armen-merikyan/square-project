Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between emptiness and desire, using color to suggest a layered story of collision and contrast. The palette features warm tones like burnt orange and dark red reflecting the fiery intensity of desire, while cool colors like charcoal and gold provide interruptions that evoke feelings of emptiness. The layout emphasizes a frame of warm colors contrasting with a core of cool, exemplifying the emotional push and pull. The asymmetrical pattern creates a resonance of noise against structure, mirroring life's disturbances and the signal of longing amidst isolation. Broken pixels introduce an imperfection that echoes our emotional states, creating depth within the seemingly simple arrangement.
