Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through color contrasts and structured chaos. The choice of a navy and dim cyan palette creates both depth and a serene quality, while the interspersing of grays introduces a muted contrast, suggesting an underlying noise between clarity and confusion. The thick outlines emphasize symmetry in the left half, while the right evokes a broken pixel pattern, symbolizing disorder. The piece flows from solid stability on the left to a more chaotic arrangement on the right, visually narrating the strife of confusion transforming into clarity.
