Artist Statement

This piece embodies the dual nature of loneliness and connection through its contrasting textures and colors. The muted sepia and faded blue tones reflect a sense of nostalgia and broken memories, while the intentional off-center placement of darker pixels creates an echo of singularity, representative of loneliness. The warm striations contrast against the cooler hues, suggesting a push-and-pull dynamic between connectedness and the weight of solitude. The patterns blend subtle chaos with gentle waves, referencing lightness and heaviness, while broken pixels add an element of imperfection, solidifying the theme of fractured connection.
