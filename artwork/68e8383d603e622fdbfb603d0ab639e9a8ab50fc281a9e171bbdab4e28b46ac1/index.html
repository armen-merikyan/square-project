Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a duality of joy and grief, captured through a palette of warm yellows and cool blues to illustrate the contrast between expanding joy and fragmented grief. The layout obeys an argument grid where the patterns of grief (muted purples) clash with those of joy (bright yellows and oranges). The main collision point occurs in the center, where brighter colors of joy purportedly break through the somber tones, signifying the struggle for control in emotional expression. Broken pixels add an element of imperfection, enhancing the theme of disrupted signals. The structured layout evokes the feeling of zigzagging emotions as they rise and fall like a bridge trying to connect two opposing feelings.
