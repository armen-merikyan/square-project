Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances vulnerability and strength, depicting an interplay of rigid structures and flowing patterns. The palette of muted cream, red oxide, and blackened plum emphasizes the weight of strength against the delicacy of vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center, where deep colors clash with lighter ones, representing a subtle tension. The patterns of lattice and pulse marks intersect dynamically, embodying the essence of the seed as an abstract narrative of internal conflict and resilience.
