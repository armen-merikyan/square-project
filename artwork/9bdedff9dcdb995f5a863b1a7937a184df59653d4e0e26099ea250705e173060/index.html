Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between nostalgia, represented by sepia and muted tones, and progress, embodied by brighter, more vibrant shades. The asymmetric split draws a clear line, reflecting the battle between remembering the past and moving forward. The drip effect creates a feeling of fluidity, suggesting that these emotions are not static but change with time. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, aligning with the concepts of 'rise_vs_fall' and leaving space for thought and introspection, representing a full vs hollow experience.
