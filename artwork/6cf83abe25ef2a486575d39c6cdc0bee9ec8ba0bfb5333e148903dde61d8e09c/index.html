Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a push-pull dynamic between clarity and confusion. Using a muted palette that emphasizes sepia and tan evokes a sense of nostalgia and broken memory, creating a contrast with faded blue tones that hint at clarity amidst chaos. The layout follows a nearly mirrored pattern, disrupted by a few pixels (broken) to signify confusion, with thicker outlines demarcating distinct blocks of color to contain the chaotic energy. The main collision occurs where clarity meets confusion, marked by an overlapping grid pattern that embodies the struggle between understanding and disorder. The density of colors in the center emits a sense of weight, while negative space around it speaks to emptiness and absence.
