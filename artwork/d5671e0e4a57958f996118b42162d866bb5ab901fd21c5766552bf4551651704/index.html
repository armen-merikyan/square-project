Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between fear and hope, captured in the contrasting dark hues on the left and warm, vibrant shades on the right. The palette combines deep blacks and dark violets to represent fear, while soft gold and pale green symbolize hope. The layout splits the canvas vertically, creating a stark visual collision between despair and aspiration. Textured patterns of crosshatch evoke a sense of disturbance and anxiety, whereas sparks of light flow upward on the right, suggesting renewal. The arrangement echoes the rising signals of hope while grounding them in the weight of fear, illustrated by the jagged edges and broken pixels that disrupt the visual flow, embodying the emotional turbulence of the seed.
