Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between awe and terror through a careful interplay of color. The palette combines warm yellows and oranges with cool deep blues and muted purples, suggesting that beauty can exist alongside chaos. The main contrast occurs between dense clusters of color at the edges and a lighter center, representing stability. The split screen layout echoes the feeling of fragmented memories, with vertical waves hinting at both calm and unsettling forces, creating a visual pulse of attraction and repulsion.
