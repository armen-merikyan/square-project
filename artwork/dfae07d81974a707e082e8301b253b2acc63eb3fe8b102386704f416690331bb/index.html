Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a clash between defiance and submission, channeling energy through upward and downward forces. The palette embodies the 'broken memory' theme with earthy tones, adding sepia and faded blue for depth and a sense of history. The main contrast occurs in the center where darker pixels pulse against lighter ones, interrupted by intentional broken pixels which create a sense of imperfection and unpredictability. The layout focuses on two dominant pulses signifying the struggle between the forces outlined in the seed, resulting in an abstract narrative of tension that resonates with the internal battle of emotions.
