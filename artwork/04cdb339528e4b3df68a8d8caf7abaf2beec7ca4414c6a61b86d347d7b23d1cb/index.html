Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between rage and calm through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette features vivid reds to convey aggression and muted blues to represent serenity. Central collision occurs in the middle of the grid, where jagged, sharp red echo patterns clash with softer, more symmetrical blue sparks. This layout evokes a sense of struggle and balance, reflecting the seed's themes of eroded symmetry and conflict. Overall, the piece conveys a narrative of powerful emotions colliding, while maintaining elements of calmness in the spaces around them.
