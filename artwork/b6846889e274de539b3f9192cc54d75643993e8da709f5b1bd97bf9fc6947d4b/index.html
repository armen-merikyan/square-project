Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension of desire versus restraint, utilizing a color palette that amplifies this contrast. The vibrant hues on the left represent desire, radiating warmth and saturation, while the cooler, muted tones on the right embody restraint, creating a boundary that feels sealed yet fragile. The interaction between these contrasting areas reflects a spiraling pressure, indicative of the struggle between attraction and limitation. The layout divides the emotions vertically, ensuring a clear visual demarcation where the main collision occurs at the center, symbolizing the dynamic push and pull of these feelings. Broken pixel placements introduce elements of imperfection, echoing the chaos of these emotions.
