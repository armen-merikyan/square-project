Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between tenderness and rage through a carefully chosen palette of saturated reds and muted blacks, symbolizing the struggle and interplay of conflicting emotions. The pattern begins with dense, dark undertones (representing rage) that clash with lighter, more gentle hues (representing tenderness), creating moments of quiet interruptions amidst the chaos. The broken pixels reflect the imperfections of memory and experience, contributing to an overall sense of unresolved motion, echoing the themes from the seed. The primary collision point occurs centrally, where the contrasting colors intersect, evoking a sense of crossing currents.
