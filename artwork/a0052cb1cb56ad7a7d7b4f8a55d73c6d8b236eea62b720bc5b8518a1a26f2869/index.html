Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between loneliness and connection, using a palette of bright yellows and muted purples to represent warmth and despair. The arrangement flows from right to left, suggesting a movement of connection being drawn from dark isolation. The contrast lies in the collision of deep blues against bright yellows, emphasizing the struggle between density and space. The diamond shape indicates a central focus, while the noise pattern around it evokes memories and echoes of past connections. Broken pixels create an imperfect surrounding, suggesting memories that fragment yet persist.
