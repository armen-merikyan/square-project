Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between control (represented through structured stripes) and panic (expressed in a radial pattern), capturing the tension of the seed. The palette of electric blue and magenta creates contrast against the stability of black, while the inclusion of broken pixels disrupts the symmetry, enhancing the feeling of chaos. Central placement of dark colors suggests a core of control, radiating outward into brighter, chaotic bursts, illustrating a fight for dominance on the canvas.
