Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional contrast between loneliness and connection, employing a palette of warm colors like burnt orange and dark red against the grounding tones of charcoal. The diagonal flow suggests a movement from the weighted corners where loneliness resides to openings where connection might occur. The warm tones are punctuated by hints of gold that disrupt the pattern, suggesting fleeting moments of connection amid a backdrop of isolation. The placement of broken pixels throughout enhances the theme of memory and disconnection, generating areas of density and echoes of absence.
