Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between clarity and chaos, using a palette that balances deep navy and muted cyan with stark white to symbolize moments of clarity amidst noise. The stripes represent a readable rhythm, while the static pattern evokes broken cells of noise. The main collision occurs centrally, drawing focus, while the surrounding lower-density fields allow for quietness, equating to 'exposure vs shelter'. This abstract arrangement visually narrates the fight for signal clarity against the backdrop of overwhelming noise.
