Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette drawn from the 'love_betrayal' collection. The central area features softer colors to evoke vulnerability, while dense, dark borders create a contrast of strength. The collision occurs predominantly in the center, where a thick border of dark maroon encroaches on the exposed pale colors, creating an intense visual dialogue. This juxtaposition represents the relationship between the desire for protection and the need for exposure, encapsulating the essence of the seed's themes.
