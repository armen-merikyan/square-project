Artist Statement

This artwork captures the juxtaposition of vulnerability and strength through a pale central field that represents exposure, surrounded by layers of deeper colors, suggesting protection and shelter. The palette consists of muted gray tones and bold reds, emphasizing both vulnerability (light pink) and strength (dark maroon) in a contrasting manner. The main collision occurs off-center, where bold colors intersect with softer shades, creating tension and a visual dialogue between conflicting emotions.
