Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a delicate dance between love and betrayal. The palette includes soft pinks and deep maroons that evoke warmth juxtaposed with the starkness of grays, illustrating the tension of closeness against isolation. Key contrasts arise from the broken edges, creating a sense of imperfection and the chaos of emotions, using sharp colors to indicate moments of betrayal amidst the warm background of love. The pattern layout follows a diagonal flow, symbolizing the struggle of two contrasting feelings, where the chaos of betrayal emerges from the calmness of love.
