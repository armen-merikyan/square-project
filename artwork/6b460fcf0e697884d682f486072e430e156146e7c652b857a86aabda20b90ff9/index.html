Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the duality of love and betrayal, captured in an abstract pattern that contrasts warmth with cold detachment. The palette of pink and red represents love, juxtaposed against darker maroon and gray that symbolize betrayal. The dense grid on the left represents connection and intimacy, while the dot patterns on the right signify interruptions and betrayals. This visual dichotomy creates a palpable tension, embodying the seed's themes of conflict and emotional collision, especially where the two halves meet, creating stark contrast and visual disruption.
