Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the dichotomy of trust and suspicion, embodying the struggle between stability and uncertainty. The palette of red_oxide, blackened_plum, muted_cream, iron_gray, and small_orange creates a warm yet complex atmosphere, where muted tones represent suspicion while glows of soft orange suggest glimpses of trust. The collision occurs predominantly at the center, where the chaotic patterns of suspicion (represented by patchwork) intersect with the smoother, round pattern of trust (symbolized by a ring). The arrangement allows for visual tension, with bold contrasts evoking an emotional push and pull, encapsulating the essence of the seed. Overall, the layout emphasizes a central pulse attempting to emerge from layers of interference, suggesting a narrative of resilience amidst fragility.
