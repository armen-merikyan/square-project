Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between clarity (signal) and confusion (noise). The palette incorporates deep blacks and vibrant greens, symbolizing the stark contrast of readable rhythm against static clutter. The central collision occurs in the middle rows, where vibrant green 'flame' patterns face off against jagged gray 'lattice' noise, creating a sense of interruption and chaos. The narrative is an abstract composition of tension, where specific colors dominate yet remain in conversation, reminiscent of a pulse fighting to establish dominance amidst interference.
