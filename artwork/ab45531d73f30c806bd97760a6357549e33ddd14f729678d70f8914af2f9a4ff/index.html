Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional dichotomy of defiance and submission through a blend of muted and vibrant colors, embodying contrast and density. The palette reflects a mix of sepia and gray tones, echoing feelings of nostalgia and conflict. Contrast emerges as bold blocks of darker colors in the corners push against lighter, more subtle shades in the center, symbolizing a battle between pressure and release. The radial pattern and frame layout amplify this tension, with outer layers feeling heavy like a protective barrier, while the core suggests openness and vulnerability. Through this arrangement, the artwork captures the essence of emotional struggle linked to the seed's themes.
