Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that reflects melancholy wonder. The contrasting colors of moon white and muted plum signify the delicate interplay of safety and exposure. The arrangement focuses on a dense center surrounded by lighter, more fragmented edges, creating a visual collision that echoes the theme of brokenness and repair. The scattered repairs symbolize the struggle between holding on and letting go, encapsulating the emotional essence of the seed.
