Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a battle between defiance, represented by jagged, bright colors, and submission, illustrated through softer, muted tones. The palette combines ripe green and acid green to create tension, while honey gold acts as an accent, illuminating the struggle. The sharp vs. soft contrast is prominent in the center, where vibrant, angular pixels disrupt the smoother edges, showcasing a clash at the core. The patterns of threshold for defiance collide with glitch effects for submission across the grid, expressing a narrative of conflict and resolution.
