Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between fear and hope. The palette juxtaposes deep dark colors against soft gold and pale green, creating a sense of contrast. The center diamond represents a beacon of hope surrounded by swirling patterns in darker hues that signify pressure and chaos. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the chaotic feeling around the central point, suggesting a struggle towards light. The intentional use of negative space amplifies the inner glow, culminating in an emotional narrative of collision between despair and aspiration.
