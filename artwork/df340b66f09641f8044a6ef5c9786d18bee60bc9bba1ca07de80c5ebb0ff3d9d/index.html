Artist Statement

The piece encapsulates the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting color densities and patterns. The palette includes vibrant yellows and muted purples representing warmth (strength) and cooler deep blues (vulnerability), creating a dynamic interaction between tension and release. The checker pattern symbolizes exposure and fragility, while the ember pattern evokes a sense of strength and persistence. The collision predominantly occurs near the center, with vibrant hues intersecting with darker tones, suggesting a complex emotional landscape that reflects the central theme of confronting inner fears and strengths.
