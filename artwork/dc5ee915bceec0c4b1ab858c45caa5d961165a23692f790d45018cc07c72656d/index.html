Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the tension between restlessness and peace, represented through a structured but imperfect pattern. Bright colors on one side symbolize restlessness (interruptions, frantic textures), while muted tones suggest peace (soft, soothing spaces). The palette combines 'shelter_green' and 'warm_brown' to evoke safety amidst chaos, contrasted by the tranquil 'cold_white' and expressive 'open_blue'. The main collision occurs diagonally, creating an unsettling push against the steady form. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, echoing the chaos against a backdrop of yearning for calm.
