Artist Statement

This piece captures an emotional tension between hollowness and yearning. The palette of hot coral and blood orange suggests desire’s vibrant pull, while hollow blue and charcoal represent a lingering emptiness. The collision occurs centrally, where saturated colors rise like signals against the subtle blue edges. The arrangement hints at cascading, falling pixels, creating a narrative of competition between filling the void and being restrained, palpable in the soft edge transitions.
