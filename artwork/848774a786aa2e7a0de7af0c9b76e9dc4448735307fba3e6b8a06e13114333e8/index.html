Artist Statement

The emotional tone of defiance versus submission is represented through jagged edges contrasting with soft expanses, symbolizing a struggle between rigid boundaries and fluidity. The palette of ripe green and honey gold provides a dynamic contrast, with colors linked to growth and caution. The main collision occurs in the center with a firm dark edge surrounding a lighter green pulse pattern, suggesting inner conflict amidst chaos. This arrangement captures the essence of an 'empty room' filled with tension and potential.
