Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between heavy pressure and soft quietude. The colors selected from the 'quiet_pressure' palette juxtapose dark navy and compressed red, reflecting pressure, against softer hues like faint peach and off-white, symbolizing quietness. The layout follows a frame-and-core design—dark colors dominate the borders while lighter hues fill the inner space, creating a visual tension that resonates with the seed's themes of contrast and symmetry. The diagonal arrangement leads the eye through a shattering wave of colors, representing the collision between order and chaos. Broken pixels intentionally disrupt the flow, embodying the interruption described in the seed.
