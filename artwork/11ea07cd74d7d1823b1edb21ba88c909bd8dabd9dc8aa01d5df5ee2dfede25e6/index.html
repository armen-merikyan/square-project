Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between panic and control, evoking a sense of tension and unease. The palette of vibrant lime and electric blue juxtaposed against deep black creates sharp visual contrasts that symbolize conflict. Main collisions occur where bright pixels cluster amidst darker tones, suggesting sparks of chaos within an ordered framework. The arrangement is fragmented with broken pixels, echoing feelings of vulnerability against a backdrop of stability, as suggested by the zigzag flow and corner invasion layout.
