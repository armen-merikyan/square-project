Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, using a palette of electric blue and magenta to symbolize urgency and serenity respectively. The contrast is highlighted by a central collision of densely braided patterns suggesting longing, against clear, channel-like formations depicting resolve. Broken pixels interrupt the flow, signaling moments of repair and damage, while the overall layout spirals inward, creating a rhythm of pressure that challenges the boundary of containment. This arrangement invites viewers to feel the push and pull of emotions, echoing the inner conflicts represented by the seed.
