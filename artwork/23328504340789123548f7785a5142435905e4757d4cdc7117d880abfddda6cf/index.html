Artist Statement

The composition reflects the duality of joy and grief through a split horizontal arrangement. The upper half employs bright yellows and soft oranges symbolizing joy, and utilizes a maze-like pattern to create movement and flow. The lower half adopts muted blues and purples to embody grief, incorporating a crosshatch pattern that conveys a sense of tension and heaviness. The main collision occurs at the middle line where the vibrant energy of joy clashes with the subdued tones of grief, creating a visual and emotional starkness that encapsulates the complexity of these conflicting feelings.
