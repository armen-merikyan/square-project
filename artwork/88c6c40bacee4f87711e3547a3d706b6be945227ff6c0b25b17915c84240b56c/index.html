Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between restlessness and peace. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust_gray, and faded_blue reflects both warmth and neutrality, balancing nostalgic memories with an unsettling undercurrent. The main contrast collides at the center, where defined patterns disrupts the quiet channel, creating a palpable tension between the peace of empty spaces and the bustling, organized chaos of color. The arrangement utilizes broken pixels to signify imperfections, enhancing the abstract narrative of fluctuating emotions, as suggested by the seed.
