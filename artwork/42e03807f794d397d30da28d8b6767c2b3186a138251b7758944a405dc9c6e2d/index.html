Artist Statement

This composition reflects the clash between 'restlessness' and 'peace', captured through a palette of darks and light, suggesting the struggle of memory against the motion of fleeting thoughts. The center is densely saturated with deep navy and black tones, symbolizing a chaotic pulse, while the edges use gentle dim cyan and patches of white to represent calm. The contrast between the patchwork of lighter colors and the stuttered dots of darker ones creates a visual echo of the emotional turmoil. The broken pixels enhance the feeling of imperfection, emphasizing the theme of unresolved emotions.
