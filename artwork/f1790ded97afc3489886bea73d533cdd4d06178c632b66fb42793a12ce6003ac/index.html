Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between tenderness and rage through a palette that balances saturated red and muted blue. The hot central pixels interrupt the softer, round shapes of the tender hues, creating a sense of conflict. The stormy blue softens the edges of the fiery red intensity, suggesting an emotional flow from chaos to calm. The arrangement of the colors forms a visible collision, where the sharp edges of rage cut into the flowing pattern of tenderness, representing an inner turmoil that resonates with the seed's themes of conflict and resolution.
