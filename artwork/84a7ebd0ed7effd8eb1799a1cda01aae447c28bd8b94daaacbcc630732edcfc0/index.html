Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between pressure and quiet through a dominant dark palette accented by soft lighter hues. The flow is directed from a crowded cluster in the lower left, symbolizing pressure, toward a faint, airy space in the top right. The contrasting hard and soft edges add complexity, echoing the theme of clarity versus confusion. The use of muted fields combined with splashes of faint peach creates an emotional rhythm, suggesting both density and release.
