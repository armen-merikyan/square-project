Artist Statement

The emotional tone envelops a dichotomy between envy represented by dense dark hues and gratitude depicted through lighter ones. The selected palette of solid blacks and soft whites dissects the flow of 'rage vs calm,' highlighting the stark contrast in emotional weight. The main collision occurs in the center where deeper colors pull against the lighter edges, embodying a struggle within the 'center vs edges' theme. The zigzag pattern intersperses movement through the arrangement, creating an unsettling yet captivating rhythm echoing the chaos of broken mirrors.
