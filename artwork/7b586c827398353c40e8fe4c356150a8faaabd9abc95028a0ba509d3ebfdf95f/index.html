Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of loneliness and connection through a contrasting color palette that emphasizes muted tones against a bright center. The faded blue embodies a sense of calm and solitude, while the sepia elements suggest warmth, creating a tension between isolation and potential closeness. The main collision occurs in the center, where muted tones surround a bright electric pixel, symbolizing the fragility of connection within a broader landscape of loneliness. The top-weighted edge style and broken pixel placements simulate an imperfect environment, reinforcing the theme of hidden versus exposed emotions, echoing the complexity of human connection.
