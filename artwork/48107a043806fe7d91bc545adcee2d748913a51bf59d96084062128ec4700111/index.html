Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between vulnerability and protection, using a palette that contrasts soft greens with pale skin and strong browns. The center remains exposed with cold white, representing vulnerability, while dark greens and browns frame it, creating a protective barrier. The arrangement incorporates both spark and pulse marks, where the collision occurs diagonally at the center, suggesting tension between safety and exposure without literal representation.
