Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through a contrasting palette of muted sepias and cool faded blues. The structure features a broken grid layout, reflecting a false symmetry filled with overlapping paths. Darker tones and jagged edges collide with lighter hues, creating a sense of chaotic energy amidst the calm, allowing the viewer to sense a 'pulse' through the color placement. The strategic use of negative space enhances the feelings of uncertainty and searching for clarity, effectively translating the complexity of the seed into an abstract form.
