Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between love and betrayal through a contrasting palette of warm and cool tones. The use of pink and red symbolizes love, while dark maroon and gray hint at shadows of betrayal. The diagonal rise reflects the emotional journey from despair to hope, and the jagged interruptions suggest broken symmetry. The central arrangement draws the eye inward, revealing layers of complexity, imitating a heart's vulnerability. Each broken pixel subtly indicates imperfection, reflecting the frailty of emotion and the conflict within.
