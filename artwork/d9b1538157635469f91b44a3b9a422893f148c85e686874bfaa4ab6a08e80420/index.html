Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between clarity (signal) and distortion (noise) through contrasting color placements and intensity. The chosen palette of dark navy and muted dim cyan conveys a sense of isolation and highlights with white as the signal's beacon amidst the chaos. The edge style with jagged contrasts represents the disruption of signals, while the dense center creates an overwhelming experience before fading into quieter borders. The piece visually embodies the essence of a recognizable pulse struggling to emerge from a static wave, inviting contemplation of the memory versus erasure embodied in the seed.
