Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the contrast between envy and gratitude, expressed through a divided palette. Dark hues on the left suggest the weight of envy, while lighter colors on the right embody gratitude. The center serves as a focal point of tension, showcasing the noise between these emotions. The checker pattern on the left collides with the frame pattern on the right, creating a rhythmic heartbeat of contrasting feelings. Broken pixels add imperfection, reflecting the unpredictable nature of these emotional experiences.
