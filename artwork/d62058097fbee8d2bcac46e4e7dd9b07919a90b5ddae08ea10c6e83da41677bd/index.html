Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional struggle between longing and resolve through the interplay of warm and cool colors. The palette of ice blue, clean white, and warning orange represents the tension of hope against despair. The symmetrical structure yields to disruption, with broken pixels strategically placed to evoke a sense of damage while hinting at repair. The barcode pattern flows downward, embodying the steady presence of resolve, whereas scattered dots create an echo of longing, merging in the center to symbolize the emotional collision. This abstract representation reflects the complexity of feelings where the longing dominates slightly, establishing a balance but leaving room for contemplation and unresolved emotions.
