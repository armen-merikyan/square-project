Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork traverses the complex interactions of loneliness and connection. A palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple evokes warmth and grief, symbolizing the spectrum of feelings between isolation and interrelation. The layout initiates from a corner, gradually expressing a diagonal tension that contradicts the central focus, suggesting a struggle between exposure and protection. The contrast is specifically laid in the form of bright clusters against darker, muted backgrounds, mirroring bursts of connection hidden within periods of solitude. The broken pixels introduce an erratic nature, adding imperfections that speak to the unstable emotional landscape of human connection and isolation.
