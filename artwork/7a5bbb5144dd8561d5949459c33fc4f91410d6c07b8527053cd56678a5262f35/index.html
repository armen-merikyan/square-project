Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a tension between trust and suspicion, represented through contrasting colors of warmth and coolness. The palette reflects this tension with 'faded gold' representing trust juxtaposed against 'storm gray' and 'near black' symbolizing suspicion. The collision occurs at the center, where soft pastels 'pale mint' and 'soft sky' encounter darker tones. This tension echoes the themes inherent in the seed, suggesting a layered emotional landscape filled with both warmth and shadow, with broken pixels woven throughout to add a sense of imperfection and resilience.
