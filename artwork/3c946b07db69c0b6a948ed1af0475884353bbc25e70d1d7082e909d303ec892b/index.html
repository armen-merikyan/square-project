Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between quiet and pressure through contrasting colors and spatial arrangement. The subdued palette of off-white and dust gray represents a tranquil background, while compressed red adds bursts of tension and energy. The composition flows from the dense, weighted corner towards a lighter, more negative space, symbolizing a release from pressure. The collision of sharp edges and softer fields highlights the conflict between clarity and confusion, echoing the seed’s essence. Eroded symmetry accentuates the impression of shattering against a tide of quiet, presenting a complex emotional landscape.
