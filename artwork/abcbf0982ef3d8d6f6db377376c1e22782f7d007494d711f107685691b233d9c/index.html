Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between quietness and pressure, where muted tones are interspersed with sharper colors to show the clash between these feelings. The palette of off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach creates a subtle yet dynamic contrast. The main collision occurs where the darker colors cluster at the edge, suggesting a heavy weight, while the lighter shades rise upward, illustrating a soft escape from that pressure. The layout, influenced by the seed's themes of struggle and clarity, results in a composition that oscillates between crowded density and lighter, expansive areas, staying true to an abstract expression without literal imagery.
