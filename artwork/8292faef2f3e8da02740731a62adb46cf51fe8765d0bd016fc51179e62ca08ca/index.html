Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional conflict between control (represented by a structured grid pattern) and panic (expressed through the warm, broken orange and red colors). The choice of the 'ancient_fire' palette evokes a sense of organic warmth while the layout allows for significant negative space, enhancing the tension. The placement of dark accents against lighter tones creates a visual echo of pressures colliding, invoking a rhythmic rise and fall, reflective of both chaos and calm. Overall, the central square frame stands as the heart of this emotional interplay, surrounded by a dance of gradients and broken edges.
