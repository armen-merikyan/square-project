Artist Statement

This composition portrays the emotional pull between confusion and clarity. A stark palette of deep black combines with pale blues and whites to create a visual tension reflecting a fight between chaos and definition. The dense black in the center suggests confusion, bleeding outward into more straightforward, lighter hues, resembling falling pixels that form a gradient of clarity. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the sense of disruption while allowing negative space to retain a balance, highlighting the contrast between full versus hollow sections and the sharp definitions at the edges. This arrangement communicates a story of movement and unraveling, where clarity emerges from chaos.
