Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between restlessness and peace, translating the duality into color patterns. The palette of shelter green, warm brown, pale skin, cold white, and open blue reflects grounding hues against chaotic impulses. The layout emphasizes a rising signal, where the lower part of the square shows muted, static colors representing peace, while the upper area contrasts with more intense, pulsating arrangements suggesting restlessness. This collision occurs diagonally, creating an asymmetrical flow that invites the viewer to navigate its complexity, mirroring the turmoil of memory and motion.
