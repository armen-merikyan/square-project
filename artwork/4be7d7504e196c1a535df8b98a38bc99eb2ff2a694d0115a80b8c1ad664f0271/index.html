Artist Statement

The artwork conveys a dynamic emotional tone, emphasizing the tension between longing and resolve. The palette of ripe green, honey gold, and deep olive introduces a warm yet complex atmosphere, where the softer cream acts as a quiet field around more vibrant colors. The main contrast occurs between the dense central cluster of green and gold shades that evoke energy and resolve, and the outer rows filled with quieter tones, suggesting a longing for connection amid space. Broken pixels add intentional disruptions, embodying imperfections and echoes of past memories that resonate throughout the composition.
