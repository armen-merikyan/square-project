Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional duality of confusion and clarity. The top four rows, dominated by warmer tones such as dark maroon and red, represent confusion through chaotic, broken patterns reminiscent of disjointed thoughts and tangled emotions. The bottom four rows shift to cooler shades of gray that imply clarity and order. The collision lies in the abrupt transition from a tumultuous top to a serene base, illustrating the struggle between these two emotional states. The broken pixels scattered throughout symbolize imperfections in clarity while the structured pattern below attempts to establish a sense of calm and control.
