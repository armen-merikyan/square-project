Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve through a careful arrangement of colors and patterns. The palette combines rich cosmic indigo with stark void black and luminous cold gold, which highlights a contrast between dense emotional layers and quiet spaces. The center contains a cluster of pulse-like marks in deep teal symbolizing longing, contrasting with a structured maze of resolve that forms around the edges. This collision creates an impression of a struggle between chaos and order, accentuating the emotional tone and enhancing the dynamic flow in the artwork.
