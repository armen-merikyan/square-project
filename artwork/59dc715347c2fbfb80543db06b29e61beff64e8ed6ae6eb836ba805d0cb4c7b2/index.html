Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dialogue between emptiness and desire. The 'ancient_fire' palette captures warmth through burnt orange and gold, contrasting sharply against the coolness of dark red and charcoal, creating a tension that reflects longing amidst void. The upward flow suggests a rising signal, with placements that echo the conflict of movement and stillness. Hard edges introduce a sense of disruption while broken pixels represent imperfection, emphasizing the pulse between hope and desolation. The negative space ensures the feelings of isolation are palpable, allowing room for interpretation.
