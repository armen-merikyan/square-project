Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between fear and hope through a stark contrast of colors and patterns. The palette combines deep blacks and dark violets to embody the weight of fear, while soft gold and pale green provide a glimmer of hope. The split layout allows fear to invade from one corner, creating jagged edges that disrupt the flow, emphasizing a struggle. Each pixel's arrangement suggests a crossing between chaotic tension and serene glows, echoing the internal conflict of despair versus aspiration.
