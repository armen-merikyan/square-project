Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The palette combines the warm tones of yellow and orange with the cool hues of deep blue and muted purple to evoke a sense of contrast between chaos and calm. The arrangement depicts a central broken symmetry that generates a pulse of energy flowing horizontally while introducing interruptions in the pattern towards the edges, symbolizing the complexity of navigating clarity amid confusion. The use of thicker outlines represents structure while the scattered placement of colors delivers a sense of dynamic noise clashing with contained zones, reinforcing the idea of pressure against release.
