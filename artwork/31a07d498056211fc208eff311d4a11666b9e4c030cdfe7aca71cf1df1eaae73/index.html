Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the tension between pressure and release through contrasting colors and compositions. The palette of deep blues and vibrant oranges creates a dynamic interplay of calm and chaos, representing quiet interludes amid emotional collisions. The arrangement features clusters of brighter colors, suggesting sharp bursts of feeling against a backdrop of subdued tones, thereby visualizing the rhythm and flow of emotions. The collisions take place primarily in the center, where vibrant colors disrupt the more muted areas, embodying the concept of contrast as described in the seed.
