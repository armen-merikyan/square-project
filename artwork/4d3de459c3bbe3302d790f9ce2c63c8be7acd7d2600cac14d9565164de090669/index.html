Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between restlessness and peace, expressed through a disruptive palette of warm and cool colors, conveying a sense of collision and unresolved motion. The colors represent warmth as a patch from the scars of anxiety, while deep blues symbolize tranquility. The vertical and diagonal placements create an uneven rhythm, with patches that interrupt the darker fields, suggesting memories of motion amidst moments of stillness. The broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, representing scars that seek to heal amidst chaos.
