Artist Statement

The emotional tone expresses a tension between loneliness and connection, balancing muted dark colors with small bursts of light. The palette emphasizes navy and black, symbolizing isolation while introducing hints of dim cyan to suggest possibility and connection. The central placement of color contrasts, with dark rows surrounding one or two lighter pixels, creates a sense of hidden potential against a backdrop of obscuring weight, echoing the contrast between loneliness and fleeting moments of connection.
