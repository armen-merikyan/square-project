Artist Statement

The emotional tone reveals a dynamic tension between restlessness and peace. The color palette of clean white and ice blue signals calm while the ink black and warning orange introduce a sense of disruption. The main contrast occurs at the center, where the restless ladder pattern intersects with the peaceful checker pattern, creating an interplay of structure and chaos that mirrors the themes of memory and motion. This composition captures the essence of unresolved emotions through careful placement and contrast.
