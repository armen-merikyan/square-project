Artist Statement

The emotional tone emerges from the contrast between desire, symbolized by saturated hues, and emptiness, represented by muted backgrounds. The palette mixes warm coral and orange against cooler charcoal and blue, suggesting a struggle between warmth and void. The center holds a dense maze pattern symbolizing desire, while edges offer threshold-like quietness, creating a spacious collision around the border. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, reinforcing the narrative of tension in blocked urges and unlimited potential.
