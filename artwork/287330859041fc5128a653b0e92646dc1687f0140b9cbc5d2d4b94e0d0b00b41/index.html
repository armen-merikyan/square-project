Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between certainty and curiosity, cycling through moments of rigid order and sporadic exploration. The chosen palette of greens and creams suggests an organic tranquility interrupted by chaotic gold accents, symbolizing curiosity. The main collision occurs diagonally, where deep olive meets honey gold, representing the push and pull between structured paths and the allure of the unknown. The layout features a quiet channel, creating a clear division while still allowing for density, thereby giving room for both emotions to breathe.
