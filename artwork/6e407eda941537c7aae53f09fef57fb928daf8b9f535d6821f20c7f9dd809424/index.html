Artist Statement

This piece captures the duality of awe and terror by juxtaposing dense clusters of dark hues against sparse bursts of lighter tones. The palette of navy and blacks, representing a deep emotional abyss, contrasts with the dim cyan that signifies fleeting moments of clarity and hope. The layout reflects a mirrored but broken symmetry, where the bottom-left corner exudes a sense of stability while the top-right spirals into chaos, embodying both the calm and tumultuous waves of emotion, evoking a narrative of unpredictable shifts in perception.
