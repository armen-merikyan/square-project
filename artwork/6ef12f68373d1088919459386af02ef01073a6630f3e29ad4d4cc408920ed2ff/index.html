Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork explores the collision between softness and severity, represented through the juxtaposition of delicate colors against a heavy dark mass. The palette employs a warm white and powder pink to evoke a sense of tenderness, contrasting sharply with the carbon black and sharp crimson that introduces an element of severity. The main collision occurs in the central area, where the warm colors struggle against the darker hues, creating a visual tension that reflects the thematic pulse of the seed. This arrangement embodies feelings of guilt and forgiveness through asymmetrical placements, establishing a narrative of emotional conflict over resolution.
