Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes panic with control, reflected in the chosen dark and light palette. The top half employs a controlled 'stairs' pattern in dark colors to evoke order, while the bottom half bursts into chaotic 'teeth' patterns with lighter colors to embody tension. Key contrasts happen within the center and edges, suggesting a battle between calm control and surging chaos. The broken pixels reflect imperfections in emotional states, mirroring real-life struggles. The layout emphasizes a split between two opposing feelings, inviting the observer to navigate the complex interplay of emotions.
