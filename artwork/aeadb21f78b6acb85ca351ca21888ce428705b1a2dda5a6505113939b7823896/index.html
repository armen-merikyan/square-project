Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between restlessness and peace, with a palette balancing vibrant green and electric blue against deeper tones of black and gray. The main collision occurs along a diagonal line, where the crosshatch pattern of unrest meets the quieter stripes of calm, symbolizing conflicting energies. Broken pixels are integrated to reflect tension and imperfection, creating an air of instability amidst structured elements, resonating with the seed's themes of memory, motion, and the push-and-pull of emotions.
