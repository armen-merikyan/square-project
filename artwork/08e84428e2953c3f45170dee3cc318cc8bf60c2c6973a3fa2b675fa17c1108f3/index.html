Artist Statement

This composition embodies the struggle between clarity and confusion through color placement and contrast. The left side, representing clarity, utilizes lighter shades of gray and pink to create a feeling of structure and balance, reflecting a calm pulse. The right side introduces chaotic reds and dark maroons, symbolizing confusion and emotional turbulence. The thick outlines create a jagged boundary, suggesting the tension of breaking symmetry. The negative space and broken pixels add an element of imperfection, mirroring the challenges in achieving clarity amidst noise.
