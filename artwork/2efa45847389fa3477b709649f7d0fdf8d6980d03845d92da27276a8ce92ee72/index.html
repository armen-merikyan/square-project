Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the fluctuating emotions of confusion versus clarity with a sensibility of fractured symmetry. The chosen palette, a deep navy and muted cyan, evokes a sense of solitude while contrasting the clarity of stark white against the enveloping black. The main contrast occurs between the structured rows of color and the deliberate placement of gaps, representing confusion. The thick outline creates definition yet is not flawless, emphasizing the theme of brokenness. Each pixel arrangement serves as a pulse through the square, rhythmically alternating between dense clusters of darkness and sparse moments of light, mirroring the emotional turmoil suggested by the seed text.
