Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette of pale aqua and mist gray serves to create a soothing, serene backdrop, while the alarm red and ink blue introduce striking contrasts that evoke strong feelings of urgency and focus. The main collision occurs centrally, where warm tones intersect with cooler muted fields, symbolizing the intersection of emotion and rationality. The discrete arrangement of broken pixels introduces disruptiveness and imperfection, reflecting the complexities of the themes within the seed.
