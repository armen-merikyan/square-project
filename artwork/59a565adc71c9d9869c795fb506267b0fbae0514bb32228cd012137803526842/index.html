Artist Statement

This composition evokes a tension between clarity and chaos through a deliberate arrangement of colors embodying 'signal' and 'noise'. The palette utilizes sharp contrasts of signal green and electric blue, countered by the static gray and dead black, illustrating the struggle for a readable pulse against overwhelming interference. The collision occurs predominantly in the center, where bright greens intermittently break through a backdrop of darker static tones, suggesting the persistence of hope amidst confusion. The structure mirrors a 'ladder' shape, scaling upward within the grid, promoting the narrative of rising tension and escape.
