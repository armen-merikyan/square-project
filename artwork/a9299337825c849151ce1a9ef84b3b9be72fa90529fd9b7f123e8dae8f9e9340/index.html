Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between love and betrayal through contrasting warm reds and cool grays, establishing a narrative where warmth gradually fades into an unsettling chill. The diagonal rise suggests an upward impulse toward hope, while the broken pixels reflect imperfections in relationships. The half-density structure inside the 8x8 grid evokes an empty room, contrasted by clusters that represent emotional memories. The palette reflects passion and loss, making stark contrasts through disrupted symmetry with gentle edges collapsing into sharper tones.
