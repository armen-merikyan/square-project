Artist Statement

This artwork evokes a struggle between defiance and submission through contrasting color densities and placements. The palette draws from muted tones like dark navy and dust gray to convey a quiet pressure, while compressed red punctuates the center, representing bold defiance amid a quiet field. The main collision takes place at the center where the dense red clashes against the expansive off-white and faint peach edges, symbolizing the emotional battleground. The 'crack' shape seed influences the jagged disruptions in the arrangement, visually manifesting conflict through broken pixel usage.
