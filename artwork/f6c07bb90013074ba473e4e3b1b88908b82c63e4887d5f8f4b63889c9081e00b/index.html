Artist Statement

This composition embodies the struggle between defiance and submission through the interplay of bold burnt orange and dark red against the softer, more subdued charcoal and gold. The stark contrasts create a visual rhythm, mimicking the pulses of emotional collisions suggested by the seed. The dense center, representing pressure, is surrounded by quiet spaces that embody moments of tranquility and release, while broken pixels introduce imperfection, adding to the overall sense of emotional conflict. The layout of argument grid illustrates the competing forces within the composition.
