Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional polarity between defiance and submission using a palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold. The strategy employs a halo void layout, with a bright central focus that signifies defiance, surrounded by a dynamic mix of noise and veil patterns representing submission. The primary collision occurs in the center where vibrant colors meet the softer edges, creating visually stimulating tension. Broken pixels strategically disrupt the composition, reflecting the chaos inherent in the emotional struggle of the seed.
