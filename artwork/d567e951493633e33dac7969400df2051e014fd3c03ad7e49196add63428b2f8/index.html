Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between warmth and coolness, echoing the desire for connection against a backdrop of emptiness. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue creates a visual representation of muted longing. The main contrast is between the warm tones on the left, denoting desire, and the cool ones on the right, representing emptiness. The layout suggests a stark division with complex patterns of desire (maze) clashing against the simplicity of emptiness (stripes), creating an interplay that flows from left to right, highlighting both absence and yearning.
