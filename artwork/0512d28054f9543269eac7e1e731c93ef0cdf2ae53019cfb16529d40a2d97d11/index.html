Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional dichotomy of joy and grief through an 8x8 grid. The palette blends bright yellows and oranges representing joy with deep blues and muted purples embodying grief, creating a tension-filled contrast. The layout ascends from bottom to top, symbolizing hope and perseverance against challenges, while jagged edges contribute to a sense of chaos and conflict. The placement of colors employs a maze-like pattern to signify joyful memories interspersed with dot-like fragments of grief, establishing a balance between open expansion and heavy closeness, echoing the complexities of human emotion.
