Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension of joy and grief through contrasting colors and abstract shapes. The palette utilizes warm yellows and oranges to express joy while deep blues and muted purples symbolize grief. The outer edges are filled with darker tones, representing the heaviness of grief, while the inner section radiates lighter colors, suggesting sparks of joy. The zigzag and ring patterns create a feeling of rising tension and pulse, allowing for a narrative of conflict between order and chaos. The use of broken pixels introduces an element of imperfection, echoing the duality of joy and grief.
