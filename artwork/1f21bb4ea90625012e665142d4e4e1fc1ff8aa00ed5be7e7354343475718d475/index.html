Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional dichotomy between vulnerability and strength as a dynamic interplay. The palette, 'lonely_signal', comprises deep navy and black reflecting strength, while the dim cyan serves as an echo of vulnerability, interrupted by a stark white that denotes exposure. The main collision occurs where these colors overlap, creating areas of tension that represent the struggle between the two emotions. The falling pixel layout symbolizes the descending nature of these emotions, while the broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, mirroring the complexities of human experience. The ring pattern evokes a protective strength around a central point of vulnerability, allowing the viewer to engage with the abstract narrative of struggle and perseverance.
