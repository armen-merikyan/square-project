Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise). The chosen palette of signal green and electric blue provides vibrant contrast against the muted static gray and dead black, which represent interference and noise. The central collision embodies the main pulse amidst the chaos, creating a dynamic flow that visually narrates the struggle of a clear signal trying to resonate through a field of distortion. Broken pixels highlight imperfections, symbolizing the disruptions inherent in communication.
