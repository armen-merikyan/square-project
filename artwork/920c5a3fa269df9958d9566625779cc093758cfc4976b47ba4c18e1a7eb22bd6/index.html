Artist Statement

This composition boldly contrasts vulnerability and strength through the interplay of deep navy and stark whites, suggesting both a protective barrier and an exposed core. The outer edges filled with dark tones represent the strength and shelter, while the glitch patterns in the center hint at fragility and vulnerability. The choice of a quiet navy field evokes a sense of isolation and calm, while the sharper, glitch-like whites and cyan symbolize illuminated disruptions, showcasing the emotional tension between these states. The main collision of colors occurs at the center where vulnerability peaks through the surrounding darker tones, representing the struggle between exposure and protection.
