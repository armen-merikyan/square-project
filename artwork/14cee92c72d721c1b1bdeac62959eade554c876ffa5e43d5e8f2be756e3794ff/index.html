Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece embodies the uneasy balance between trust and suspicion. The chosen palette of earthy greens and browns juxtaposes soft backgrounds with sharp contrasts, suggesting warmth clashing with doubt. The collision of mirrored yet broken patterns echoes the complexity of emotional responses, while the primary diagonal flow invites the viewer's eye to navigate through layers of subtlety and tension. The distribution of broken pixels represents imperfections in trust, allowing space for reflection.
