Artist Statement

This composition embodies a collision of emotional tones: the vibrant reds signify rage and the cooler tones represent calm. The center contains dense red and black layers, suggesting a strong emotional weight, contrasting with the lighter pale blues and whites pushed to the outer edges, symbolizing a sense of space and quiet. The use of the 'hole' shape seed creates an interruption in the pattern, enhancing the feeling of tension as the colors fight for dominance. The radial calm pattern meets the jagged frame of rage, reflecting the theme of conflict beautifully.
