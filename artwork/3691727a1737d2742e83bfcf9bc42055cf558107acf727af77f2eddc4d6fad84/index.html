Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional push and pull of longing versus resolve. The chosen palette, inspired by 'relief_dread', harmonizes pale mint and soft sky to create a calming, melancholic backdrop, while storm gray and faded gold inject tension and contrast. The center is marked by sparse clusters of resolve-like 'teeth' structured pixels, while longing manifests as scattering 'dots' at the edges. This arrangement creates a dynamic where clear boundaries and distant warmth collide, eliciting a feeling of simultaneous serenity and urgency. Broken pixels subtly disrupt the flow, enhancing the sense of fractured understanding and emotional depth.
