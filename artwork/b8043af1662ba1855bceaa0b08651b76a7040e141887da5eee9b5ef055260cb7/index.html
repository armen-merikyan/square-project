Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy between longing and resolve. The palette of pale mint, storm gray, and faded gold conveys a sense of both tranquility and urgency, reflecting the push and pull of yearning versus determination. The center is dominated by a dense cluster of storm gray, symbolizing pressure and conflict, while surrounding hues of pale mint suggest the quiet longing. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, echoing the theme of damage and repair, while maintaining a sense of structured flow. The design follows a spiral pressure layout, emphasizing the tension of movement toward a singular point.
