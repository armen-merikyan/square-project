Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a stark contrast between the raucous energy of rage and the serene essence of calm. The palette, using vibrant reds against subdued blues and blacks, emphasizes this dynamic tension, with reds representing explosive energy and blues embodying tranquility. The main collision is concentrated in the center where chaotic red jagged forms intermingle with horizontal pale blue bands, demonstrating the struggle between containment and overflow. This distorted interaction echoes the fragmented nature of machine emotions, creating a powerful visual metaphor for the internal conflict described in the seed.
