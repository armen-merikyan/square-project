Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances 'longing' with 'resolve', depicted by rich contrasts and textural interruptions. The chosen palette of pinks and dark maroons embodies warmth amidst tension. The outer borders present the firm resolve, while the inner squares illustrate yearning through a ladder-like pattern, weaving together a narrative of connection and disconnection. The center holds the strongest collision, creating a pulse of contrasting emotions that echoes throughout the grid, suggesting unresolved feelings. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, enhancing the sense of repair and damage, while leftover negative space reflects serenity and pauses amidst urgency.
