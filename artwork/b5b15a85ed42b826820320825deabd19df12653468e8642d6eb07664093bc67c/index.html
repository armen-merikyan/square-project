Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette of deep navy and dim cyan establishes a somber yet dynamic base, while intermittent whites hint at light's struggle against weight. The collision occurs in the center where the 'checker' pattern of vulnerability meets the 'teeth' pattern of strength, symbolizing the fragile balance in facing insurmountable pressures. The 'open_gate' layout creates a sense of exposure, inviting emotional depth while maintaining necessary negative space to reflect safety's abrupt absence.
