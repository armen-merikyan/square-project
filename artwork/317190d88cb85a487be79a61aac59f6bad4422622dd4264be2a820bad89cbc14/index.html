Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of 'signal' against 'noise,' reflecting a struggle for clarity amidst chaos. The chosen palette features 'signal_green' and 'electric_blue' as dominant colors, representing clarity and rhythm, while 'static_gray' and 'dead_black' provide contrasting backgrounds of interference and void. The composition employs a 'falling_pixels' layout, with the main pulse of signal peeking through layers of noise, specifically at the center, enhancing the visual disruption and suggesting a convergence of energy and chaos. The asymmetric arrangement and strategic placement of broken pixels (shades of #111111) simulate moments of static interference and amplify the overall tension of the piece.
