Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of love and betrayal. The warm tones of pink and red contrast with the cold grays and dark maroon, embodying the struggle between affection and abandonment. The chaotic layout mimics falling pixels, suggesting a sense of loss while the layered patterns evoke a rising tension. This juxtaposition creates a visual dialogue where the warm colors interrupt the cooler grounded spaces, symbolizing hope amidst despair, echoing the experiences of love's highs and lows.
