Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tone of restlessness contrasted with peace, represented through a careful selection of colors showcasing both bright accents and muted fields. The palette, termed 'melancholy wonder,' contains soft cyan and muted plum against the serene lavender gray and moon white, reflecting internal conflict. The design features a strong tension where warmer hues collide at the center, symbolizing a flame against chaos. The asymmetry of placement strategically interrupts calm zones with restless shapes, creating a visual narrative of unresolved movement and emotional depth.
