Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between awe and terror, using a palette of dark and light tones to symbolize the contrast. The use of deep black (#000000) and pale blue (#A1C8E0) represents the heavy weight of terror against the illuminating spark of awe. The jagged edges and split screen layout suggest a battle between stability and chaos. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, reflecting the 'broken memory' theme, while maintaining negative space to allow the tension to breathe.
