Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between defiance and submission through a contrasting color palette of warm ivory and deep wine. The arrangement features a halo of dark and warm tones around a quiet central void, representing both the chaos of emotion and the calmness of restraint. The main collision occurs at the edges where defiance (constellations of warm and bright colors) meets the submission (soft stripes of muted shades). The layout reflects a channel of movement that resonates with the emotional undertones of the seed, creating a dynamic interplay.
