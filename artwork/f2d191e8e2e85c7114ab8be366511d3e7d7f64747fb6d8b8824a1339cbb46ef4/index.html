Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects tension between restlessness and peace, utilizing a mix of warm sepia and cool faded blue. The collision occurs primarily at the diagonal, where chaotic noise patterns clash with glitch-like order, representing memory's friction against motion. The broad gray fields create negative space, embodying a quiet yet unsettling presence. The palette's combination of muted colors enhances the sense of nostalgia and fluidity, weaving a narrative of struggle between calm and turmoil.
