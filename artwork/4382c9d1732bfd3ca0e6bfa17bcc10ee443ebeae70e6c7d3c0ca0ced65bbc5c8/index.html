Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dialectic between pressure and quietude. The chosen palette of soft neutrals and accents conveys a subtle tension, with darker tones representing density and light colors suggesting liberation. The collision occurs mainly in the central region where deep navy contrasts sharply with the soft peach and gray, symbolizing the clash between overwhelming force and a peaceful respite. The diagonal layout reflects dynamic movement, while the structured bands evoke layers of complexity, mirroring the dual nature of the seed’s themes.
