Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional juxtaposition of pressure and release through the color palette of 'fear_hope', comprising rich contrasts between dark and muted tones. The left half of the artwork symbolizes nostalgia with soft gold and pale green patterns that create a gentle wave, while the right half reflects progress through darkness layered with dynamic crosshatches. Broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, enhancing the narrative of collision in emotions. The layout balances space with a 10% negative ratio, allowing the contrasting emotions to breathe amidst tension—visually depicting the path from pressure to emotional release.
