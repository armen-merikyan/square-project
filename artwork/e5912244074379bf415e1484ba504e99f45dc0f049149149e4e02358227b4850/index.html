Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between control and surrender through a blend of muted and saturated colors. The central cluster of vibrant hues suggests moments of clarity and activity, while the surrounding deep tones create a sense of containment and pressure. The palette of moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold captures resilience amidst fragility. The broken pixels add intentional disruptions, symbolizing the struggle between the signal and the noise. The main clash happens towards the center, where the colors collide and invite emotional engagement.
