Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts between panic and control are captured through the use of a primarily dark palette interspersed with vivid sparks of color, echoing feelings of urgency against a backdrop of uncertainty. The colors promote a feeling of tension, with dark maroon illustrating the weight of panic and pink representing moments of control and hope. The main contrast occurs where bright pixels are surrounded by heavier shades, demonstrating the collision of emotion and the desire for stability. The layout moves from a corner invasion, reflecting chaos creeping in, while broken pixels introduce imperfection, undermining a sense of stability.
