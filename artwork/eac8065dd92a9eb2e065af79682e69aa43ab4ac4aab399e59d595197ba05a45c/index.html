Artist Statement

The composition embodies the tension between control and panic through contrasting patterns: a checkerboard representing control fighting against a chaotic crosshatch illustrating panic. The chosen palette of sepia tones and muted blues suggests nostalgia and introspection, enhancing the emotional gravity. The main collision occurs in the center where both patterns intersect, suggesting a battle for dominance. The broken edges amplify the feeling of imperfection, leading to an emotional resonance that reflects on the struggle between calm and chaos.
