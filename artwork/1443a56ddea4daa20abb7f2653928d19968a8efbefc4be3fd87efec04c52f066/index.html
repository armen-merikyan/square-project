Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between clarity and confusion, with a mixture of warm reds and cool grays creating an interplay of feelings. The palette reflects betrayal through stark contrasts, simulating 'broken' symmetry with disjointed patterns that pulse with an emotional rhythm. The main collision occurs where vibrant pinks clash against muted grays, eliciting a sense of dynamic instability. This configuration embodies both an ascent of hope, represented by the gradually lightening colors towards the top, and the chaotic darkness below, symbolizing confusion and complexity.
