Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between control and surrender, encapsulated through contrasting patterns of stripes and maze structures. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes memories of muted warmth and distant coolness. The primary collision occurs in the center where structured control meets chaotic surrender, creating tension and visual dialogue. The placement of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, echoing the feelings of overflow and false signals influenced by the seed's narrative.
