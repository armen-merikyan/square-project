Artist Statement

This composition navigates the emotional terrain of trust and suspicion through contrasting patterns and colors. The palette features deep blacks to embody suspicion, intermingled with pale blues suggesting trust, creating visual tension. The black and pale blue barcodes channel feelings of suspicion, while the pressure bands in white signify an unbroken path of trust. The collision occurs diagonally, representing a fracture between the two emotions, with softer colors providing a hint of glow against darker fields, mimicking the push and pull of these contrasting sentiments. The broken pixels add imperfection, enhancing the narrative of resilience versus fragility, as they disrupt the structure while still allowing for a thematic coherence.
