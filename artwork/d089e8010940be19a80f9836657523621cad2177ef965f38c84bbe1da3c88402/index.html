Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between clarity and chaos, where a vibrant pulse attempts to emerge from static. The palette incorporates shades of green and blue to symbolize the 'signal,' while gray and black create a sense of 'noise.' The center of the artwork represents the dominant signal, with lighter shades radiating outward, interrupted by dark accents, embodying the concept of thriving amidst interference. Such collisions capture the essence of a broken mirror—fractured yet reflective, hence portraying multiple layers of emotion through asymmetry.
