Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a clash between the quiet stillness of peace and the chaotic pulse of restlessness. The palette, featuring deep wine and warm ivory, reflects the struggle between warmth and coldness, providing a backdrop for the contrasting patterns. The main collision occurs in the center, where restless 'teeth' patterns made with darker colors create tension against smoother, peaceful stripes in lighter tones. This represents the duality of memory and motion, where sharp breaks interrupt a flowing calm, echoing the seed's themes of conflict and resolution.
