Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional conflict between restlessness and peace, expressed through contrasting color fields of pale mint and storm gray, creating a visual tension. The arrangement reflects a diagonal argument, with darker, restless hues colliding against softer, peaceful colors in the center, while the edges are eroded, hinting at an invasion of chaos. Broken pixels signify disruption and uncertainty, enhancing the feeling of a fragile peace amidst unyielding motion.
