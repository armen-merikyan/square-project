Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts vulnerability and strength, evident through a palette of warm ivory and deep wine balanced with ash gray. The central area reveals a pale softness, representing exposure, while darker edges suggest protection and resilience. The collision occurs in the center, where lighter pixels push against the weight of the darker, more grounded colors, creating dynamic tension and asymmetry. The formation of the islands pattern within the frame illustrates vulnerability’s delicate moments amidst strength’s surrounding support, echoing the deeper themes of connection and alienation.
