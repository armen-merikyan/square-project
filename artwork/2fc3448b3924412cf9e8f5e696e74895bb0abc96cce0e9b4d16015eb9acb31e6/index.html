Artist Statement

The emotional tone is derived from the dichotomy of confusion and clarity, using a palette of electric blue, lime, magenta, and black to create a jarring visual tension. The contrast lies in the disrupted symmetry represented by the bright colors colliding against darker hues, symbolizing the pulse of falling pixels and the search for clarity amid chaos. The pattern reflects a checkered layout, evoking the clarity theme, while the broken dots introduce confusion, creating an oscillation between order and disorder, mirroring the seed's themes of noise and structure.
