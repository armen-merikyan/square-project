Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through the interplay of colors and forms. The palette incorporates cool tones like ice blue and violet gray to suggest vulnerability, contrasted with powerful accents of clean white and warning orange symbolizing strength. The layout shows two pulses in the center, creating a collision that embodies the core of the seed—strength safeguarding vulnerability. The delicate lattice pattern subtly intersects with thread-like structures, enhancing the visual narrative of safety against exposure.
