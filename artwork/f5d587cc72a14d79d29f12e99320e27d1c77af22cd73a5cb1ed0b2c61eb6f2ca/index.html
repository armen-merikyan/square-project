Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between confusion and clarity, evoking moments of chaos interrupted by flashes of insight. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green represents fear yet gestures towards hope. The pattern reflects instability with broken pixels scattered throughout, creating a heartbeat-like pulse. The primary collision occurs where soft gold disrupts the darker hues, mirroring the conflict born from symmetry versus break. The arrangement echoes a narrative of balance and disruption, making the abstract visual experience resonate with emotional complexity.
