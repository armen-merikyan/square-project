Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional duality of strength and vulnerability. The palette of pink, red, dark maroon, and gray emphasizes warmth contrasted by the coldness of the edges. The arrangement focuses on a dense center representing strength, surrounded by softer tones suggesting vulnerability, mirroring the conflict and connection between these emotions. The main collision occurs through the juxtaposition of the vibrant center against the quieter edges, embodying the ebb and flow between protection and exposure.
