Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between quietude and pressure through a nuanced palette. The palette intertwines soft hues like faint peach and off white to evoke a tranquil field, contrasted sharply with darker tones like dark navy and compressed red representing weight. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where pressure colors pool, only to shatter and disperse into the quiet, creating a balance of tension and release. The layout mirrors on the left and right, but 4 pixels disrupt this harmony, embodying the 'broken' aspect of clarity and confusion, lending an imperfect beauty to the composition.
