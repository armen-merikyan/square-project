Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the ongoing struggle between restlessness and peace. The palette employs deep blacks and blues to convey a sense of mourning and hidden depth, contrasted by the pale yellow representing fleeting moments of tranquility. The composition centers around a channel motif which carries echoes of fluidity and movement, creating a stark dichotomy at the center of the artwork. Restlessness is depicted through pulsing marks diverging from the center, while the softness of the surrounding color fields offers sanctuary. The well-defined edges light against dark invoke the sensation of constraint on freedom, amplifying the emotional tension.
