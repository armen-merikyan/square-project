Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a deep conflict between strength and vulnerability, where protective measures resist exposure. The palette of cosmic indigo, void black, and cold gold evokes feelings of awe mixed with terror, contrasting muted spaces against bright signals. The central collision occurs where deep teal breaks through the dominant dark colors, suggesting a spark against a flood. This translates the seed's theme into an abstract narrative of opposing forces seeking balance and connection, where structured grids of strength interweave with disordered fields of vulnerability, punctuated by intentional broken pixels that signify disruptions in this tension.
