Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between panic and control. The chosen palette of warm colors (yellow and orange) contrasts with cooler tones (deep blue and muted purple), symbolizing the clash between stability and instability. The outer border showcases a structured layout representing control with soft, smooth transitions, while the inner 4x4 grid disrupts this with jagged, broken elements, emphasizing upheaval. The flow is counterclockwise, suggesting a spiral of thought, where unresolved emotions echo throughout, mirrored by contrast and collision within the design. This arrangement tells an abstract story of tension—both a cry for order and a submission to chaos.
