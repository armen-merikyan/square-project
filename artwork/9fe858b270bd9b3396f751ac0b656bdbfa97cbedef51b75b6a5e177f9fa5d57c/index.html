Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, manifested through color contrasts. The warm tones represent strength while the cooler shades express vulnerability, creating a recurring visual theme of tension and balance. The palette, a blend of intimate colors like rose clay and warm shadow against cooler blues, creates a narrative of emotional depth. The main contrast occurs in the center, where dense patterns collide with minimal empty space, representing the internal struggle of presence versus absence. The patterns chosen—crosshatch for strength and ladder for vulnerability—overlap, forming a braided effect that suggests connection and disruption simultaneously. The rule of broken pixels introduces visual imperfection, evoking a sense of reality within abstraction. This composition invites the viewer to navigate through a landscape of emotional complexity, inviting reflection on the push and pull of human experience.
