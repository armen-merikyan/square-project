Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the conflict between panic and control, where a structured layout represents stability, but broken pixels infuse chaos. The palette of deep blacks and bright whites symbolizes the stark contrast between calm and distress. This interplay creates a tension-filled rhythm, marked by the checker pattern for panic, while the framing lines showcase control's structure. The chaotic sparks contrast against more subdued areas, representing an outer conflict between internal stability and external pressures.
