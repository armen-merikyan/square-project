Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional interplay between awe and terror through a juxtaposition of calm and chaotic color placements. The black and pale blue invoke a serene base, while sharp contrasts of red introduce sudden punctuations of intensity, embodying an emotional collision. The arrangement reflects a broken symmetry that challenges the viewer's perception, where soft edges on one side fade into denser, more chaotic patterns, evoking feelings of instability. The 'stair step' shape influences the visual flow, suggesting a wave of emotion coursing through the piece.
