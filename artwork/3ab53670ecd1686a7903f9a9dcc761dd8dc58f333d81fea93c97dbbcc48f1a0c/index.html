Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between feelings of isolation and connection, represented through a palette of muted sepia and faded blues. The outer frame is composed of darker hues to encapsulate loneliness, contrasting with a lighter, more vibrant core suggesting connection. The placement of colors creates a tension where the central area pulses with warmth, yet the edges remain heavily saturated, evoking quiet desperation and the fear of exposure. This duality reflects the 'inside vs outside' theme, while bursts of illuminated pixels signify fleeting moments of connection amidst the pervasive shadows. The randomness of broken pixels enhances the theme of imperfection, representing the fragility of relationships in a seemingly rigid grid layout.
