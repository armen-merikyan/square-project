Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between trust and suspicion. Utilizing a palette of muted tones (ink_black and violet_gray) against a striking warning_orange, it creates a dynamic contrast that visually represents 'signal vs noise.' The diagonal flow across the canvas suggests a fall into uncertainty, while the repetitive echo pattern implies fragments of doubt ('suspicion'), dramatically interrupted by stable lines of trust. The uneven distribution of colors emphasizes both vulnerability and resilience, portrayed by the sporadic introduction of clean_white, symbolizing clarity attempting to peek through the noise.
