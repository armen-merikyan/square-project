Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a combination of depth and light. The palette features cosmic indigo and deep teal as the base colors, suggesting a serene yet rich foundation. Cold gold and star white introduce warm accents that punctuate the calm, representing moments of clarity amidst a yearning darkness. The central collision occurs where the lighter hues meet the indigo, creating sharp contrasts in the pattern of horizontal frames and crosshatching. This reflects the interplay of contained emotions against the backdrop of breakage and repair, mirroring the concepts of the broken mirror and constant pulse of longing found in the seed. The organized chaos of broken pixels disrupts the rhythm, enhancing the sense of urgency without sacrificing the overall tranquility that lingers in the quieter segments of the composition.
