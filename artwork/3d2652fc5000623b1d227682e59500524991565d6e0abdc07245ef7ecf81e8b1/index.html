Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between softness and severity through gentle gradients and sharp contrasts. The colors shift from warm whites and pale pinks to deep blacks and grays, eliciting feelings of exposure and shelter. The arrangement features broken dots representing severity, clashing with pressure bands depicting softness, captured through a radial pulse pattern that mimics motion. The central collision occurs at the canvas's heart, where gentle colors battle with stark contrasts, embodying the dialogue between guilt and forgiveness while creating a visual narrative of weight and release.
