Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional dichotomy of softness vs severity through a palette that juxtaposes warm and cool tones. The sharp crimson accents fight against the muted steel gray and carbon black, creating a dynamic tension where the softness is overwhelmed by severity. The main contrast occurs at the center, mimicking a pulse that seeks to break free but is held back by darker edges. Intrusions of softness, represented by powder pink, ripple outward, creating a spiral-like effect that signifies the struggle between expansion and restraint.
