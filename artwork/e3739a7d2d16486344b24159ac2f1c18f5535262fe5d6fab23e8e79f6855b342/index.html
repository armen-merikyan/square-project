Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the tension between awe and terror, reflected in the contrasting palette of deep blacks and soft golds. The left side conveys a sense of stability and protection with darker tones, evoking awe, while the right side introduces lighter colors and broken patterns to suggest chaos and instability—a visual mirror to the tension between organized and chaotic thoughts. The arrangement flows vertically, symbolizing how these emotions coexist, while maintaining negative space to suggest unresolved tension.
