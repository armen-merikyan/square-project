Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between open vulnerability and protective enclosures. The palette combines soft greens and warm browns to signify warmth and stability against the cold exposure of pale whites and blues. The central contrast of colors collides with stripes on one side representing exposure and scars on the other symbolizing safety. A disrupted pixel pattern indicates both the fragility and resilience of the interplay between safety and exposure.
