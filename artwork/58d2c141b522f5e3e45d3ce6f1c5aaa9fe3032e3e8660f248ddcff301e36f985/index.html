Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a dance between softness and severity, utilizing a palette featuring a mix of powder pink, warm white, carbon black, steel gray, and sharp crimson to represent contrasting moods. The collision is prominent in the central rows where the pale and muted colors clash against sharp, dark accents, invoking a sense of tension and release. This suggests the constant negotiation between guilt and forgiveness, with the pulse marks indicating movement and rhythm against a background of grounding structural density.
