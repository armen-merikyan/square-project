Artist Statement

The emotional tone leans into the tension between nostalgia and progress, represented through a fractured yet ascending layout. The palette of reds and grays invokes warmth and depth, emphasizing the contrast between full, heavy hues and lighter, airy tones. The split horizontal pattern signals a division between the past and the future, while the drip effect suggests fluidity and continuity. Key collisions occur in the stark transition from dark maroon to pink, creating a visual dialogue that reflects the push and pull of memory against advancement.
