Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional juxtaposition of nostalgia and progress, using a palette inspired by ancient fire to highlight warmth and historical depth. The palette's burnt orange and dark red evoke a sense of warmth and memory, while the charcoal and gold accents represent the sheen of progress and clarity. The layout flows from top to bottom, framing the encounter between historical depth and modern clarity. The vertical split introduces a collision between dark, dense edges and lighter, more open regions, suggesting a weighty past versus the illuminating push of future possibilities. Broken pixels, strategically placed, add imperfection, embodying the reality of change—how nostalgia and progress can coexist in a beautifully flawed tapestry.
