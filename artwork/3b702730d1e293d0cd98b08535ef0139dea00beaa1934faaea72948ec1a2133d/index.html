Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece juxtaposes love and betrayal. The palette of warm pinks and deep maroons speaks to the warmth of affection and the intensity of heartbreak. The layout suggests precipitation, mimicking the feeling of tears or ash falling. Main contrasts occur between the softer, warm tones and the harsh, dark grays that interrupt the flow, embodying betrayal. Each pixel placement creates an echo of memory, suggesting a fragmented story of connection and disconnection, enhanced by the sparse placement allowing for a sense of loneliness against density.
