Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece oscillates between tenderness and rage, represented through color and spatial contrast. The palette incorporates deep reds and blacks to convey intensity and chaos, complemented by pale blue and white to introduce moments of calm and clarity. The main collision occurs in the center, where intense colors clash against muted backgrounds, symbolizing the internal struggle between these opposing emotions. The arrangement of stripes and stacked patterns creates a rhythm that suggests an ebb and flow, echoing the quiet channel from which the emotions rise. This structure reflects the overall narrative of the seed, embodying a mix of fragility and intensity.
