Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a clash between defiance and submission, represented through a combination of bold, dark colors and softer, lighter hues. The palette, with its cool greys and a hint of muted gold, evokes a sense of conflict and resolution, emphasizing the tension between dense areas and negative spaces. The pattern logic features a ripple effect colliding with a grid-like structure, creating visual interruptions and emphasizing the contrast at the center where chaotic energy meets calmness. This ties back to the seed's concepts of inward spirals and the interplay of noise and signal.
