Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between control and surrender through a palette that balances vibrant warmth against muted cool. The collision occurs at the center, where thick layers of 'hot coral' and 'blood orange' press outward, meeting the tranquil 'hollow blue' that suggests a release into openness. This spiral pattern captures the theme of overflowing versus containment with radiating 'hinge' and 'crosshatch' arrangements, evoking a visual narrative of pressure building and eventually releasing.
