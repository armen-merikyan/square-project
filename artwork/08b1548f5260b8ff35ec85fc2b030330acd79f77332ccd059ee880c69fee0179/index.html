Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between calm and chaos, using cool and warm colors to represent emotional collisions. The palette shifts from deep blues and grays symbolizing quietness and depth to vibrant reds and yellows that suggest energy and pressure. The central area of high contrast highlights a collision of color, echoing the rhythm of emotions that pulse and recede like waves. Asymmetrical placements create a sense of unresolved motion, reflecting the complexities of feeling.
