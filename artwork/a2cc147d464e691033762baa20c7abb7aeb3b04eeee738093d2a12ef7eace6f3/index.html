Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the duality of trust and suspicion, using the 'love_betrayal' palette. By placing warmer pinks and reds on the left to signify trust against the cooler grays and dark maroons on the right for suspicion, the artwork embodies a collision of feelings. The broken dots pattern for trust contrasts with the radial pattern for suspicion, creating a visual tension that reflects emotional pressure and eventual release. The shape seed of a square frame encapsulates the concept of boundaries in emotional experiences.
