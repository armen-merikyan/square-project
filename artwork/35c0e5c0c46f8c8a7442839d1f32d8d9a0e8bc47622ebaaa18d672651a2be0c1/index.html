Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve, using cool and warm tones to suggest depth and warmth. The palette includes colors like shelter green and warm brown to create a feeling of contrast between safety and exposure. The main collision occurs in the center of the grid where warm and cool colors interact, representing the pulse of desire against firm resolve. The broken pixels represent moments of disruption, while negative space allows for breathing room, enhancing the feeling of longing intertwined with the sense of resolution. Overall, the composition is an abstract exploration of emotional interplays and patterns.
