Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between love and betrayal through a split layout, where the left side utilizes warm pink and red tones symbolizing love, while the right employs cooler, muted grays and dark maroons representing betrayal. The pattern flows from warm bursts on the left transitioning to a more chaotic noise on the right, suggesting a journey from warmth to emotional disconnection. The broken pixel elements introduce imperfection and evoke feelings of conflict, while the 10% negative space emphasizes the emptiness associated with betrayal, ultimately crafting a visual narrative of contrast and emotional collision.
