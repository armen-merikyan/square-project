Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes explosive rage against serene calm. The palette combines intense reds against soothing pale blues and stark blacks, creating a visual tension. The arrangement of brighter colors breaks through the cooler tones, especially in the upper sections, reflecting an overflow of emotion. The patterns suggest a conflict, with chaotic constellations of red cutting through a structured horizontal calm, symbolizing the struggle between containment and eruption. The edges have a faded border, invoking the idea of boundaries being tested.
