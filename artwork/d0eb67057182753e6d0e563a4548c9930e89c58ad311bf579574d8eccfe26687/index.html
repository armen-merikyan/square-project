Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tumultuous interplay between envy and gratitude, captured through jagged patterns suggesting discord and lingering moments of appreciation. The palette blends deep black as a weighty presence with electric blue as a hint of hope, and magenta introduces an emotional pulse of turbulence. The arrangement zigzags upward creating a visual tension, with bright colors interrupting dark swathes, representing moments of clarity amidst heaviness. Each broken pixel adds to the rawness of the experience, creating a complex narrative that evokes a duality of feelings.
