Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a vibrant palette of yellow and orange contrasted with deep blue and muted purple. The bright colors in the center symbolize defiance, while the surrounding muted tones represent submission, creating a striking contrast. The edge style reflects an uneven border, allowing the center's energy to leak outward, embodying a push-pull dynamic. Pulse marks suggest rhythm and energy while glitches represent disorder. The overall composition uses negative space to enhance the feeling of tension and release.
