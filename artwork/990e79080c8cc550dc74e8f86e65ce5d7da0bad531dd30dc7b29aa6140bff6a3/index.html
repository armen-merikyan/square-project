Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between the stark emptiness and the burning desire, represented through a split color arrangement. The palette of burnt orange and dark red evokes warmth and yearning, while charcoal represents shadow and void, creating sharp contrasts. The composition features a rising zigzag pattern on one side to symbolize desire amid the cool, staggered emptiness, creating a collision of feelings. This pattern reflects the struggle of wanting against the feeling of absence, with negative space enhancing the whisper of loneliness.
