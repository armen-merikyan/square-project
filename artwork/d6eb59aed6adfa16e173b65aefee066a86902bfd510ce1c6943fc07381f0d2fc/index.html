Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a duality between emptiness and desire, echoing the seed's essence of collision. The palette of lime, electric blue, magenta, and black conveys warmth against coolness, emphasizing contrast. The layout suggests an argument of order versus chaos, representing the tension in emotions. The jagged edges and broken pixels mirror the imperfections of feelings. The pattern of mirror and spark creates a visual conversation, with dense blocks of color colliding against negative spaces which provide breathing room, illustrating a struggle for balance.
