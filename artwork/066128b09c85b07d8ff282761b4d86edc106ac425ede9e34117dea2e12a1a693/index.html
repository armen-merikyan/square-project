Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of vulnerability and strength. The palette of muted cream, red oxide, and iron gray expresses an earthy resilience tempered by soft hues, while the blackened plum provides depth and a striking contrast. The primary collision occurs in the center, where sharp edges of vibrant color interrupt the stable, muted surroundings. The arrangement reflects a tension between pressure and release, creating a dynamic visual rhythm that encapsulates the narratives of exposure and safety.
