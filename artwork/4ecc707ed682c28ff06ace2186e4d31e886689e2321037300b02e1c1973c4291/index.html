Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between intensity and calmness, using a palette of warm yellows and oranges contrasted against deeper blues and muted purples. This dichotomy forms the central theme in the arrangement, suggesting a release through movement and rhythm. The collision occurs primarily in the center with dense colors, visualizing a pressure point, while edges are left lighter, embodying submission and quiet. Broken pixels symbolize the imperfections inherent in emotional experiences, enhancing the artwork's depth and complexity.
