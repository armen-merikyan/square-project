Artist Statement

The piece captures the emotional dance between loneliness and connection through a warming palette of soft gold and pale green juxtaposed with dark violet and black. Darker colors create a sense of pressure and depth, while the lighter shades suggest moments of hope and release. The central area is dense with color, while edges transition into quieter spaces, allowing for a rhythmic flow from center outward. The defined patterns emerge in asymmetries, creating deliberate collisions between muted fields and vibrant tones, echoing the seed's narrative of emotional conflicts and moments of harmony.
