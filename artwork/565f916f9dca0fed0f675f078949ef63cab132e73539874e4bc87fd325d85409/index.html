Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between control and surrender, embodied by structured grids transitioning to soft, flowing edges. The palette combines cool hues like ink blue and pale aqua, suggesting a calm space being subtly disrupted by alarm red, which serves as a striking contrast that embodies pressure. The main collision occurs centrally, where the colors disrupt one another, representing the feeling of a weighted core overwhelmed by external forces. This abstract arrangement conveys a narrative of imbalance, suggesting both a weighted pressure and a yearning for release, resonating with the seed's themes.
