Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between tenderness and rage, using the color palette to reflect these contrasting emotions. The deep reds with pale blues create an emotional juxtaposition, symbolizing rage and calmness, respectively. The broken pixels add an element of imperfection, evoking scars of past experiences while the overall layout suggests a hopeful ascent. The main collision occurs in the center, where the warmth of red interacts with the coolness of blue, creating a vibrant focal point that draws the viewer's eye; this arrangement mirrors the inner conflict of the emotional seed.
