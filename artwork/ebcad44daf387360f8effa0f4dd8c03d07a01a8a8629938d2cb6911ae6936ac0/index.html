Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers around the contrasting feelings of dread and relief, depicted through a palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon. The layout imitates a mirror, creating tension through uneven pixel placements, suggesting the pressure and collision of emotions. Light dots represent moments of relief amidst heavy, dark block patterns. The flowing drip effect enhances the quiet yet intense rhythm of these emotional exchanges, embodying the struggles of quiet against overwhelming density.
