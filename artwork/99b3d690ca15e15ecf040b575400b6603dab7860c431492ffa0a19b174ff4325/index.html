Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional conflict between defiance and submission through contrasting colors and dynamic patterns. The palette balances softness in 'powder_pink' and 'warm_white' against the severity of 'carbon_black' and 'sharp_crimson', creating a visual tension. The central collision of 'channels' representing defiance is met with 'islands' of submission around the edges, suggesting an inward spiral of conflict. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfection, symbolizing disruptions within the emotional dialogue, while the negative spaces maintain a sense of reflection and depth.
