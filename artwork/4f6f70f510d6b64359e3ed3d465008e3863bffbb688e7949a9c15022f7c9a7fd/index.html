Artist Statement

The composition embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve, expressed through the use of a subdued yet warm palette featuring red_oxide and iron_gray intersected by the contrast of muted_cream. The overlapping textures reflect a push-pull dynamic, suggesting both intimacy and distance. The main collision occurs in the center, where warm tones collide with cooler shades in a diagonal arrangement, optimizing visual tension while leaving ample negative space to impart a sense of longing. This configuration guides the viewer's eye, creating a rhythmic flow around the edges, echoing the complexity of the emotions drawn from the seed.
