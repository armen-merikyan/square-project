Artist Statement

The emotional tone swings between the calming hues of peace and the intense pulses of restlessness. The palette of pale mint and soft sky creates a tranquil backdrop, while storm gray interspersed with near black introduces moments of tension and disruption. The central collision occurs where the dark shades interrupt lighter tones diagonally, symbolizing the battle between calm and chaos. This abstract layout respects the rule for negative space, allowing breathing room while maintaining tension through mixed patterns of heavy density and quiet channels.
