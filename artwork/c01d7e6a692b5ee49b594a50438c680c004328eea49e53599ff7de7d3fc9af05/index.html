Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between trust and suspicion, represented through a palette of dark navy, soft dim cyan, and accents of white. The use of navy conveys deep suspicion, while the dim cyan suggests a hopeful illumination. The layout features a vertical split, symbolizing the divide between these emotions, with sharp contrasts in color density showcasing the struggle between hard shadows and soft glows. Main collisions occur through blocked spaces filled with dark tones interrupted by lighter accents, mimicking the oscillation between openness and vulnerability. The broken pixel elements create intentional disruptions, enhancing the overall feeling of tension and fragility, encapsulating the seed's essence into a compelling abstract pattern.
