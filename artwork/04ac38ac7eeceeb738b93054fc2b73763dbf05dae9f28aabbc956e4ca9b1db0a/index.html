Artist Statement

The emotional tone emphasizes a struggle between desire and restraint: warmth and pull represented by saturated colors, and containment by muted tones. This palette facilitates a rich conversation in the center, where sharp reds and orange peaks intersect with darker fields, creating a dynamic collision that suggests both urgency and restriction. The layout of two pulses of color rotating near each other across negative space illustrates the push and pull of emotions distantly, reflecting on the abstract qualities of closeness and distance.
