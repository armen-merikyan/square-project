Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional clash of longing and resolve through a contrasting palette of muted browns and grays suggesting weight and nostalgia, accented by warm ivory and soft gold. The central collision occurs where warm and cool bands intersect, creating a tension that reflects both the desire for connection and the structure of resolve. The broken pixels disrupt uniformity, giving a sense of repair and imperfection, while the scattered layout suggests a dynamic pulse interrupted by moments of stillness, aligning closely with the themes from the seed.
