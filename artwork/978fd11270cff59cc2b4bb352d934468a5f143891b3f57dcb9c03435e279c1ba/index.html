Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between desire and restraint, using a palette that reflects the urgency of desire through saturated hues and the cool control of restraint with muted tones. The contrast between the warmer reds and oranges against the cool blacks and grays creates a sense of conflict, with the bright colors attempting to overflow their constrained spaces, creating visual harmony yet emotional discord. The layout of the colors forms lattices of pressure, interrupted by broken pixels that symbolize the imperfections and disruptions inherent in desire. The composition's edge-style suggests containment, while the center pulses with intensity, embodying the struggle to balance these emotional extremes.
