Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dance between vulnerability and protection. The palette, composed of shelter green, warm brown, pale skin, cold white, and open blue, accentuates the tension between exposure (open blue, cold white) and safety (shelter green, warm brown). The central collision occurs with a pale skin color at the heart, representing the precarious balance of being both sheltered yet exposed. The arrangement of colors suggests a rhythmic pulsing, emphasizing the struggle between pressure and release, where broken pixels disrupt uniformity, echoing lives marked by both intensity and gentleness.
