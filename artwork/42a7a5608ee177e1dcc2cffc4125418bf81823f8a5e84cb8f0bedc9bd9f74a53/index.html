Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between longing and resolve, symbolized through color and contrast. The palette of ripe green, acid green, honey gold, deep olive, and cream evokes both warmth and discomfort, reflecting the dualities present in the seed. The main collision occurs at the center where vibrant colors intermingle with broken edges to symbolize the struggle of repair against damage. The framework of 'broken dots' and 'framed' patterns reflects this by providing both continuity and interruption, visually representing the emotional push and pull.
