Artist Statement

This piece evokes the emotional duality represented by envy and gratitude through a balanced yet contrasting color palette of navy and dim cyan against stark black and white. The composition juxtaposes structured, orderly dots symbolizing gratitude with a chaotic crack pattern reflecting envy. The layout creates an invasion from one corner, producing a flow that moves through the piece, embodying both pressure and quiet. The overall impression captures how emotions collide yet harmonize.
