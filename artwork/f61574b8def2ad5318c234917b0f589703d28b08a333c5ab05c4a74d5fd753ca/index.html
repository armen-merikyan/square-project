Artist Statement

This artwork captures the juxtaposition of longing and resolve through a structured layout emphasizing emotional layers. The palette combines softness with severity, reflecting tension between intimate warmth (powder pink, warm white) and stark clarity (carbon black, steel gray, sharp crimson). The main conflict occurs along the center, where shades of warmth clash against cooler, darker tones, suggesting an unresolved narrative. Each row represents a different emotional layer, merging structured density with quiet spaces, echoing the push and pull of human experience.
