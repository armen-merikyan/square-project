Artist Statement

This composition contrasts the concept of trust with suspicion through the use of clean, soft colors embodying a sanctuary against chaotic, sharp colors suggesting threat. The palette reflects serenity and urgency, with pale aquas and mist grays creating a calm core, while alarm red and ink blue represent the electric charge of suspicion. The dominant lower left quadrant symbolizes trust, while the upper right (more chaotic and fragmented) signifies suspicion, echoing the duality of these emotions. The design clashes diagonally, embodying a fall into uncertainty, supported by an eroded edge that suggests vulnerability.
