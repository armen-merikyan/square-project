Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the clash between panic and control, with chaotic stripes representing panic in the top half, while structured broken dots signify control in the bottom. The color palette contrasts deep black and red for intensity and urgency, with pale blue and soft white to echo calm. The striking contrast occurs at the center where panic meets control, creating a tactile collision of emotions. This pattern reflects the seed's themes by portraying inner conflict and an unresolved tension.
