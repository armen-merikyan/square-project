Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a delicate balance between longing (mirrored depths) and resolve (steady axes). The color palette of 'safety_exposure' implies warmth against coolness, enhancing the contrast between emotional states. The more saturated colors create a striking presence in the center, giving form to longing, while surrounding muted tones suggest serenity, defining the edges of the piece. The arrangement features a collision of patterned mirrors and stacked elements, representing conflicting feelings churned through the lens of broken reflection.
