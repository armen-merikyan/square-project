Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a complex interplay between joy and grief, employing a palette of warm yellows and oranges contrasted with deep blues and muted purples. The top half embodies joy with a checker pattern that provides brightness and an open feeling, while the bottom half presents grief in a target-like arrangement, suggesting fragmentation. The thin outline gives a sense of boundary while the broken pixels induce an imperfect flow, emphasizing a collision of emotions that oscillates between lightness and heaviness, echoing the turmoil inherent in the seed's narrative.
