Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a balance between softness and severity, represented through a palette that oscillates between warm pastels and deep contrasts like carbon black and sharp crimson. The center of the grid features heavier, darker colors suggesting weight and severity, while lighter colors dissipate towards the edges, symbolizing softness and openness. The main collision happens in the center with dark hues interfacing with soft textures, creating an echoing contrast that represents a pulse against the backdrop of radial calm. This translates the seed’s dichotomy into an absorbing emotional landscape.
