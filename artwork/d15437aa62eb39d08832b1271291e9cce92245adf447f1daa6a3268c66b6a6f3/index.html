Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes a sense of quiet against overwhelming pressure, using a palette that reflects muted fields and sharp accents. The main collision occurs in the diagonal layout, creating a visual tension that signifies a battle between calm and chaos. The chosen colors, particularly the dark navy and faint peach, suggest depth and fleeting warmth, while the broken pixels introduce imperfections that echo the theme of shattering expectations. This abstract pattern symbolizes the nuanced interplay between silence and noise, with dense zones sparking moments of clarity amidst confusion.
