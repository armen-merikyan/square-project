Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between 'emptiness' and 'desire'. The top half uses warm yellows and oranges, signifying longing and aspiration, while the bottom half employs muted purples and deep blues to represent solitude and void. The contrast between warm and cool colors creates an emotional collision, echoing the complexities of human experience. The upward flow in the pixel arrangement suggests an ascent of desire, interrupted by the cool tones below, symbolizing the weight of emptiness. Random 'broken pixels' introduce a sense of imperfection and reality, reinforcing the pattern's depth and emotional impact.
