Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'spark vs ash' is captured in the palette of warm oranges counterbalanced by cooler grays, reflecting the contrast between sudden energy and remnants of stillness. The conflict between 'hunger' and 'restraint' manifests through jagged edges that interrupt smooth transitions, suggesting the tension of desire and control. The central collision occurs where vibrant colors meet muted shades, creating a focal point that evokes both turmoil and calm. The structure of diagonal flows breaks the square into mirrored yet disrupted fields, enhancing the sense of memory versus erasure as colors collide in reflection and bitterness.
