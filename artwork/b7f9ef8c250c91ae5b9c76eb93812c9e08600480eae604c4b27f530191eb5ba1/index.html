Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between panic and control. The chosen palette—a mix of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon—contrasts warmth with coldness, reflecting the internal struggle. The top-left corner starts with ordered stripes symbolizing control, while the chaos gradually breaks through towards the edges, represented by irregular patterns and broken pixels. The tension peaks at the center, where control meets chaos, creating a visual collision that echoes the seed's theme. The contrasting arrangements evoke an emotional story of struggle, control, and eventual release.
