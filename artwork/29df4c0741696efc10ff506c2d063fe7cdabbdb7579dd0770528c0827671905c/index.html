Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between loneliness and connection, utilizing a muted palette evoking nostalgia and warmth through sepia tones and faded blues. The dark colors dominate the edges, representing the weight of solitude, while the lighter colors at the center suggest fleeting connections. The contrast is pronounced between the dense outer pixels and the sparse inner pixels, encapsulating the 'inside_vs_outside' theme, creating a heartbeat-like rhythm of connection amidst isolation.
