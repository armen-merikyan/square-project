Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between control and panic, expressed through an arrangement where circles representing control clash against jagged edges symbolizing chaos. The color palette combines warm yellows and orange to emphasize tension with deep blues and muted purples introducing a cooler contrast, suggesting depth and complexity. The main collision occurs at the center where bright colors meet darker tones, representing a spark amidst disorder. Negative space is utilized to enhance the feeling of both isolation and connection, inviting the viewer to experience a push-pull dynamic between regulation and fragmentation.
