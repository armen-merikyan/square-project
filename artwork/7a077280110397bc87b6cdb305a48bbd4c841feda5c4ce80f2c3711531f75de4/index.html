Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting dense and pale colors. The selected palette, consisting of muted tones like off white and dusty gray complemented by the compressed red and dark navy, reflects a quiet yet powerful undercurrent of emotion. The central collision occurs where the colors sharply transition, representing the threshold where protection and exposure meet. The pattern captures this with a constellation-like approach on the edges, surrounded by frames symbolizing the structure of vulnerability, supported by a slight presence of broken pixels to introduce an element of imperfection, enhancing the emotional depth.
