Artist Statement

The emotional tone emerges from a dichotomy between desire and restraint, suggesting a pull toward warmth while being confined by darker undertones. The selected color palette, featuring rich reds and muted grays, embodies the tension between vibrant aspirations and a heavy sense of confinement. The bright orange accents symbolize fleeting hope amidst the darker frames around the square, creating a sensation of collision that takes place predominantly at the center. This arrangement of colors pulses against a backdrop of locked, muted spaces, visually conveying the essence of the seed as an abstract story of longing checked by barriers.
