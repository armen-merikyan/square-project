Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between love and betrayal, utilizing a palette of warm pinks and reds contrasted against the cooler gray and dark maroon. The placement follows a mirrored layout that is intentionally disrupted by broken pixels, illustrating the conflict between harmony and chaos. The central shape—abstractly inspired by a fragmented heart—introduces a collision of emotions at the center, where vibrant warmth meets darker, cooler tones, suggesting a nuanced emotional experience of both affection and pain. The flowing pattern subtly echoes chaos with its broken dots while inviting organized structure through the symmetry of the layout, forming a rich narrative of interlinked yet contrasting feelings.
