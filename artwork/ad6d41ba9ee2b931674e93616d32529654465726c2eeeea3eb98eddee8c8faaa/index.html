Artist Statement

This artwork conveys a dynamic tension between spark and ash, using a palette drawn from the themes of warmth and coolness. The deep wine and warm ivory colors create a vibrant core, suggesting vitality, while the ash gray and stain brown communicate depth and restraint. The main contrast occurs along the diagonals, where the rich antioxidants clash and blend, representing a struggle between light and dark. The layout's two pulses create visual tension as they push against each other across the negative space, manifesting the emotional duality of hunger versus restraint within the abstract structure.
