Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between quiet and pressure, utilizing a muted palette with sharp contrasting edges. The top rows evoke a soft, expansive feel, representing quiet through variations of off-white and faint peach, while the bottom rows introduce a darker, denser section with navy and red, suggesting a build-up of pressure. The main collision occurs midway, where the dark tones start to intrude upon the softer hues, embodying the dynamic shift from serene clarity to overwhelming intensity. The pattern and pixel placement symbolize the struggle between embracing calmness and confronting chaos, aligning with the provided seed's themes.
