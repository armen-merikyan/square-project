Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between fear and hope. The use of a limited palette—lime, electric blue, magenta, and black—creates a vibrant contrast, reflecting clarity amidst confusion. The jagged edges and the diagonal invasion of color suggest bursts of noise and patterns of tension, where calm reflects against chaotic interruptions. The pattern of confusion manifests as teeth-like jagged shapes clashing with mirror-like symmetry to evoke a sense of broken rhythm, further enhanced by carefully placed negative space. The collision of sharp contrasts plays with the viewer's emotions, inviting an introspective journey through complexity and simplicity.
