Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay between restlessness and peace, embodied through warm and cool contrasts of color. The palette includes warm coral and soft amber to invoke a sense of gentle balance, while cold slate and blue-black introduce unease and depth. The main collision occurs in the center, where clusters of warm hues disrupt larger cool fields, symbolizing abrupt memory recalls against a backdrop of soothing thoughts. The seed inspired a spiral layout, suggesting continuous motion and reflection, captured through asymmetric distribution of color that echoes both tension and tranquility.
