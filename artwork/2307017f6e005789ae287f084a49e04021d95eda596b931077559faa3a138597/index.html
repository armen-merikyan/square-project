Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between softness and severity through a structured spiral layout that weighs towards darker tones. The palette includes warm whites and soft pinks clashing against deep blacks and grays, suggesting a conflict of lightness versus heavy emotion. The main collision occurs in the center, where dark rigid structures disrupt the flowing lighter islands, creating a dynamic interplay between calm and chaos. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections that echo the theme of guilt and forgiveness, leaving space to breathe amidst the tension.
