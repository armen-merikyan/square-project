Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the sharp intensity of 'spark' against the muted essence of 'ash', creating a dynamic tension that encapsulates both hunger and restraint. The palette draws from 'signal_noise' colors, combining vibrant electric blue and hot magenta against static gray and dead black, establishing a rich contrast. The primary collision occurs diagonally through the canvas, where the checker pattern of 'spark' interrupts the pressure band of 'ash', illustrating the battle between memory and erasure. This arrangement evokes a story of tension and contradiction, exemplifying resilience amidst fragility.
