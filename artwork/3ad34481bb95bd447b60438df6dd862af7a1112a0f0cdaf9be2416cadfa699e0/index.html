Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts 'restlessness' and 'peace,' weaving a narrative of quiet chaos. The palette includes 'pale aqua' and 'mist gray' for a calm background, disrupted by 'alarm red' and 'ink blue,' creating tension. The 'broken pixels' reflect imperfection and fragmentation, while the layout of 'eroded symmetry' emphasizes the struggle between inner balance and outer chaos. The main collision occurs at the center where deep colors intersect with lighter tones, representing a struggle for peace amidst turmoil.
