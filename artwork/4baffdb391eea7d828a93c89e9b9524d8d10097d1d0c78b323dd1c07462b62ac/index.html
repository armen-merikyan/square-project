Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic interplay between certainty and curiosity, embodied in a palette that transitions from deep, solid blacks to hopeful, vibrant greens. This tension creates a dialogue between the starkness of certainty and the wonder of curiosity. The layout follows two pulsing centers that convey motion, interspersed with negative space to encourage exploration. The broken edges represent interruptions and uncertainties. The collision occurs at the center of the layout where darker hues meet lighter colors, emphasizing the contrast between tension and relief.
