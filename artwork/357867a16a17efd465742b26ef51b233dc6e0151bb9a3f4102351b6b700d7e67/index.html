Artist Statement

The piece conveys the emotional tension of loneliness juxtaposed with connection. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green reflects this duality, where the dark hues represent isolation and the lighter accents signify fleeting connections. The central area is dense with dark colors representing a sense of confinement, while the edges are lighter, evoking the essence of outside connections. The pattern’s haphazard maze arrangement suggests confusion punctuated by moments of hope and clarity, resembling a burst of connection amid loneliness.
