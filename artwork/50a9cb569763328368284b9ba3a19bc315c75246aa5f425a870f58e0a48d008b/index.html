Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional contrast of longing and resolve, where the upper rows depict the yearning through a dense arrangement of warm colors accentuating emotional pressure, while the lower rows contrast with an orderly, dotted pattern representing stability and resolution. The palette of signal green and hot magenta visually captures the electric tension and intimacy intertwined with distance. The central collision is highlighted by a shift from a chaotic, warm density to calm resolution at the bottom, embodying the overarching theme of a struggle between desire and stability.
