Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the conflict between defiance and submission, represented through a palette that juxtaposes deep reds with soft grays to illustrate sharp vs. soft contrasts. The pattern's layout channels downwards, creating a sense of upward resistance amidst downward pressures. Main collision occurs at the center, where bright hues of red disrupt the surrounding gray fields, echoing the tensions of conflict. Broken pixels introduce rawness, reflecting subtle scars within the struggle.
