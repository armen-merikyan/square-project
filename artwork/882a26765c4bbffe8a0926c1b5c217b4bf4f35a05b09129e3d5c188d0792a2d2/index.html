Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the contrast between safety and exposure, creating a visual narrative of tension and protection. The palette of gentle greens, warm browns, pure whites, and soft blues evokes feelings of shelter and vulnerability, with the stark contrast between the deeper and softer colors suggesting the coexistence of heavy pressure and gentle release. The central clash occurs where the colder blue meets the warmer brown, illustrating a balance of stability and risk. This pattern echoes the complexities of the seed's suggestions, portraying a dynamic interplay between openness and confinement.
