Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional clash between confusion and clarity through a chaotic arrangement of dark and light colors, representing the battle between clarity and confusion. The palette of deep navy, stark black, and hints of dim cyan portrays deep emotion and isolation, while the glowing white introduces moments of clarity amid the noise. The overlapping patterns create a sense of rhythmic tension, mirroring the pulse of emotions shifting between structured calmness and chaotic disruption. The placement of broken pixels adds to the feeling of imperfection and unresolved tension, enhancing the composition's dynamic energy.
