Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional conflict between joy and grief, using a palette of vibrant yellows and oranges against muted blues and purples. The zigzag pattern of joy, represented by bright sparks, collides with the melancholic dots of grief, creating a push-and-pull effect. The broken edges suggest a fragility inherent in the human experience, where fleeting joy battles against depths of grief. The layout offers a spacious center that invites contemplation, embodying the contrast of chaos and order, perfectly reflecting the seed's duality.
