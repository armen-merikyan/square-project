Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents a juxtaposition of rage and calm, where the center features hot, explosive colors representing anger, while the outer edges embrace cooler tones of tranquility. The palette combines vibrant reds and stark whites against soft pale blues and black, creating a compelling contrast. The chaotic diagonal patterns of red disrupt the horizontal calmness, depicting a struggle between containment and overflow. The ‘diamond’ shape seed influences the arrangement, with explosive color bursts at the center and quiet field colors framing the edges.
