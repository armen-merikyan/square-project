Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines joy's spark with grief's muted tones, creating an engaging tension. The palette features bright yellows and oranges representing joy alongside deep blues and muted purples reflecting grief. The main contrast occurs vertically across the square: the left side radiating vibrant warmth with a structured pattern (joy) while the right side fades into fragmented patterns and darker shades (grief). The jagged textures symbolize both the rising signal of joy and the sharp edges of grief, creating a jagged contrast that aligns with the zigzag theme. Spaces of negative space enhance the feelings of isolation amidst emotion.
