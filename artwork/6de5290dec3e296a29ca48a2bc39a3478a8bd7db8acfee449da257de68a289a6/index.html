Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress, symbolized through an upward movement that contrasts with downward heaviness. The palette features navy and black to evoke depth and introspection while dim cyan introduces a progression tone. The lower half is filled with darker shades, signifying weight and stability, while the top introduces brighter colors representing hope and potential. The layout suggests visual tension and a pull between two emotions, echoing the steady rise of progress against the gravity of nostalgia.
