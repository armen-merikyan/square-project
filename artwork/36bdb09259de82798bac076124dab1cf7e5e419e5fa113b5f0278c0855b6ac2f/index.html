Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and confusion. The palette of deep navy and black contrasts with bright white and dim cyan, illustrating the collision of dense, oppressive space against moments of lightness. Each row represents an oscillation from clarity to confusion, creating a fragmented rhythm that mirrors the seed's themes. The broken pixels interrupt the flow, symbolizing disruption in the quest for clarity, while patterns clash between dense saturated spaces and areas of negative space, maintaining a balance between pressure and release.
