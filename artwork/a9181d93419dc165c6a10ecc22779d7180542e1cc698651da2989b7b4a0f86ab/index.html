Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a duality between clarity and confusion, embodied through a palette of stark contrasts. The left side displays a muted gray-black spectrum representing chaos, brokenness, and confusion; while the right side incorporates whites and pale blues to signify calmness and clarity. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfection, symbolizing ruptures in perceived harmony. The interplay of dark and light creates a rhythm that suggests tension and resolution, while the 'split_vertical' layout naturally separates these emotional states. The overall composition evokes a pulse—a heartbeat navigating between chaos and serenity.
