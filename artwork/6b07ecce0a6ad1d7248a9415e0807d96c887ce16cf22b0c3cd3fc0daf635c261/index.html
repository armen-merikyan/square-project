Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between love and betrayal, represented by a palette of warm pinks and intense reds contrasting against grays and dark maroon. This both conveys warmth and a sense of betrayal. The layout embodies a broken symmetry, with a near-mirror image split by chaotic placements that evoke feelings of discomfort and disruption. The collision occurs diagonally across the square, hinting at a crack in the perceived order, where dense clusters of warm colors clash with colder, sparser sections, emphasizing the conflict between passion and loss.
