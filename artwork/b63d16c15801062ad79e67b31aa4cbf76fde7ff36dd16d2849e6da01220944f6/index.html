Artist Statement

The composition embodies a tension between fear and hope, represented through a contrasting palette of deep shadows and soft lights. The dark corners symbolize fear, while the vibrant soft gold encapsulates the fragile glimmer of hope. Jagged edges provide a sense of instability around a less dense center, while the falling pixel layout conveys a downward motion of emotions. The asymmetry creates a collision of vibrant colors against muted fields, illustrating a narrative of emotional movement from pressure to release.
