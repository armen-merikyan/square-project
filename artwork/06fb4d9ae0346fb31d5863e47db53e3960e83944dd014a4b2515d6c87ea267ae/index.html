Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between 'longing'—represented by warm hues of electric blue and hot magenta, suggesting a depth of feeling and desire—and 'resolve,' characterized by the firm presence of dead black and static gray, which anchor the composition. The palette contrasts the vibrancy of longing against the solidity of resolve. The main collision occurs at the center, where electric blue ripples meet static gray fault lines, creating a push-pull effect that illustrates the emotional struggle. The layout presents longing in a dense center surrounded by an outer layer of resolve, allowing for breathing space and reflecting layers of intimacy and distance.
