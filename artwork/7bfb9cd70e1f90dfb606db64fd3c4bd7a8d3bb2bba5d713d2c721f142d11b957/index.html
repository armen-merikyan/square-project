Artist Statement

This artwork juxtaposes the emotional currents of restlessness and peace. The palette combines dark tones symbolizing tension with vibrant accents suggesting fleeting moments of clarity. The intense contrasts and collision at the edges mirror the struggle between motion and stillness, while the core hosts a gentle pulse of calmer colors to denote moments of rest. The overall layout embodies a tension releasing towards the outer edges, allowing for quiet space within the chaos, as envisioned by the seed's abstract themes.
