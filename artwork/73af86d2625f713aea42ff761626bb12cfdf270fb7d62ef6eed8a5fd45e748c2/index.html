Artist Statement

This piece conveys the tension between relief and dread through a deliberate color balance and layout. The contrast between deep navy and stark white embodies the weight of dread against the flickers of relief. The rows of descending colors symbolize the pull of gravity, while the jagged edges evoke a sense of instability. The broken pixels introduce an imperfection that echoes the complexity of navigating emotions, creating a rhythm that pulses between calm and tension.
