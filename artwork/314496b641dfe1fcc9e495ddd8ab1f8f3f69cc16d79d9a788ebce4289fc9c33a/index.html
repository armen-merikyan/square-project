Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between calmness and agitation, depicted through the juxtaposition of soft moss green and stark bone white against the harsher rust and stone gray. The pattern incorporates restlessness via scattered dots breaking into the patchwork of peace, culminating in a collision toward the center where fragments of tranquility meet turmoil. The layout spirals out from a small off-center point, embodying subtle movement and tension. This convergence reflects the duality inherent in the seed, translating abstract qualities of memory and motion into a visual statement.
