Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve through the juxtaposition of warm and cool tones. The palette blends soft pinks and whites with stark blacks and grays, demonstrating a tension between intimacy (longing) and structure (resolve). The bold crimson acts as a focal point, suggesting an inner turmoil. The arrangement features a dense cluster in the center, representing the clash of emotions, while the edges maintain a quiet field, echoing the feelings of space and distance. This balance of density and emptiness mirrors the ideas from the seed, inviting viewers to interpret their own experiences of longing.
