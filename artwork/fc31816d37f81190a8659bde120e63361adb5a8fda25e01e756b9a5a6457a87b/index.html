Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay of loneliness and connection, manifested through a palette that merges vibrant yellow and orange with muted deep blue and purple. The stark contrast between warm and cool tones creates a sense of pressure against a backdrop of quiet structure. The central 4x4 grid pattern signifies confinement while the glowing edges offer a sense of release, echoing the tension of emotions colliding. The arrangement captures a rhythmic pulse of memory, where moments of intense color intersect with spacious, softer hues, suggesting both isolation and the yearning for connection.
