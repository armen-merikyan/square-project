Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the duality of safety and exposure, reflected in a palette that contrasts warm browns and greens symbolizing enclosure against cool whites and blues representing open air. The top half is dominated by deeper tones, embodying safety, with a jagged pattern signifying the embrace of warmth amid exposure, while the bottom half showcases lighter hues with scattered accents to depict fragile exposure, creating a visual echo that resonates with the themes of pressure and release. The central horizontal division emphasizes the split, with both emotional states colliding in the midsection, generating a dynamic interplay between protection and vulnerability, hinting at a soft yet severe tension.
