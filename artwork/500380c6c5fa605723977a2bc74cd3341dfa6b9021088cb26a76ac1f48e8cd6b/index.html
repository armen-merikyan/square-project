Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between control and surrender, represented in the top half with systematic patterns and dense colors, while the bottom half flows into soft radial interruptions. The palette of deep blacks contrasts sharply with pale blues, representing the struggle between pressure and release. The main collision is centered, creating a visual push-pull effect that resonates with the seed's themes of tension and release.
