Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve through a palette of deep navy and dim cyan, symbolizing isolation and distant warmth respectively. The collision occurs at the center as the darker hues create a dense cluster, interrupted by lighter tones, representing moments of resolve amidst chaos. The use of broken pixels introduces an intentional imperfection, reflecting the flawed journey of emotional navigation. The layout guides the eye through a cross current of conflict, embodying a dialogue between order and chaos, while preserving negative space that evokes a sense of solitude and contemplation.
