Artist Statement

This composition grapples with the tension between love and betrayal, visualized through warm reds and pinks colliding with dark maroons and grays. The layout leads from a vibrant corner to a darker, more chaotic edge, symbolizing the spread of emotions. The broken dots evoke imperfections and disruptions, while the negative space emphasizes isolation amidst chaos, creating a striking contrast between fullness and emptiness.
