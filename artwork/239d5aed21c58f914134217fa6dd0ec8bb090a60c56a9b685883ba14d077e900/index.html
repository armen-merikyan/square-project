Artist Statement

This composition reflects a tension between defiance and submission, using deep contrasts from the 'ancient_fire' palette. The dark red and burnt orange colors create a sense of pressure and conflict, while the charcoal represents void and submission. The layout of falling pixels suggests a cascading emotional release. The broken pixels add imperfection, emphasizing the chaos amid structure. The interplay of contrasting colors and patterns acts as a metaphor for emotional collisions, creating a visual narrative of unresolved feelings.
