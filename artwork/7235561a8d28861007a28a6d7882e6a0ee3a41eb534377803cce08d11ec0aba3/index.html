Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional duality of defiance and submission, using contrasting colors and patterns to evoke a sense of conflict. The selected palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green embodies both tension and hope. The grid pattern represents defiance while the 'teeth' pattern suggests submission, creating a visual dialogue that shifts from dark, weighted areas to lighter, more open spaces. The main collision occurs in the center, where these opposing patterns interact, revealing an interplay of strength and vulnerability that resonates with the seed's themes.
