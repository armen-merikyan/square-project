Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between rage and calm, using sharp contrasts and structured chaos. The left half features deep, dark colors representing weight and compression, while the right half introduces lighter tones that suggest openness and tranquility. The palette of reds and blacks conveys intensity, while the pale blue and white softens the overall impact, leading to a delicate balance of heavy versus light. The arrangement employs broken pixels to symbolize the crack against the mirror, creating a sense of disruption yet harmony in the overall rhythm of the piece.
