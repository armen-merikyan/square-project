Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the clash between the gentle warmth of nostalgia and the sharp, chaotic edges of progress. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue embodies memory's warmth contrasted against restless time. Rows feature transitions, with lighter hues at the top representing hope and faded memories, gradually shifting to deeper, richer tones, suggesting weight and density as we delve into deeper emotional currents. The central layout emphasizes an inner calm surrounded by chaotic patterns, symbolizing the friction between past comforts and present challenges. Here, echoes of structured noise push back against soft, reminiscing sparks to showcase a balance of inner peace amidst surrounding chaos.
