Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between defiance and submission through a palette inspired by lonely signals. The bold contrast between the deep navy and soft dim cyan creates an emotional collision at the center, representing the active struggle amidst an oppressive frame of black. The falling pixels layout conveys a sense of weight and descent, while the checker and spark patterns interlace to suggest ongoing conflict. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, reflecting the complexity of emotional experiences.
