Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between the warmth of 'spark' and the coolness of 'ash', representing resilience versus fragility through its bold contrast in colors. The palette balances 'signal_green' and 'electric_blue' as vibrant accents against the muted 'dead_black' and 'static_gray' that dominate the canvas, embodying the emotional push and pull of hunger versus restraint. The main collision occurs diagonally, where cool and warm tones intersect, creating a visual rhythm of interruptions that reflect the complexities of memory and erasure. The negative space allows the viewer to breathe amidst the chaos, simulating a moment of quiet reflection among jagged edges.
