Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a tension between trust and suspicion, represented through contrasting color densities. The palette combines deep teal and cold gold to explore warmth and distance. The collision occurs diagonally, where a dense cluster of dark colors (representing suspicion) interacts with lighter strokes (representing trust). The division fosters an 'argument grid' layout, where patterns of each emotion weave in and out, symbolizing the complexity of navigating trust amidst doubt.
