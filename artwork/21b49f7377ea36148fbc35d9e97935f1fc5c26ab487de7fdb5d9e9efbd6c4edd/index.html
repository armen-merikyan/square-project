Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette features a blend of 'shelter_green' for protective instincts, 'warm_brown' for stability, and 'pale_skin' to denote exposed softness. An 'open_blue' accent suggests expansive courage, while 'cold_white' creates an ethereal backdrop that contrasts with the grounded tones. The central collision, where vulnerability and strength interact, is highlighted with a pale skin color nestled within a halo of darker, heavier tones—suggesting both safety and exposure. The design's flow is diagonal, echoing the interplay of energies, resembling two pulses drawing together against the tide of negative space.
