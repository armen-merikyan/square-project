Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between confusion and clarity. Darker colors on the left suggest confusion, featuring broken patterns that disrupt the mirror-like quality. The right side introduces lighter hues representing clarity, radiating with a sense of order. The palette balances muted purples and vibrant yellows, creating emotional collision. The asymmetry reflects the struggle between structured expectation and chaotic disruption, resonating with the idea of 'falling pixels'.
