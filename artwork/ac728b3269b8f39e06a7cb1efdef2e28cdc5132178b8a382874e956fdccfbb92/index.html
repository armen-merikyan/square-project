Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting color sections. The warm colors of trust (lamp gold and distant lilac) are interrupted by deeper shadows (warm shadow and cool blue), creating a sense of conflict. The palette choice evokes intimacy while still reflecting underlying distance, suggesting the emotional push and pull. The main collision occurs in the center-left quadrant where lighter hues clash against the darker borders, embodying the struggle between openness and doubt. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, enhancing the narrative of resilience amidst fragility.
