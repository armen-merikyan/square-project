Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between control and surrender, illustrated through a dynamic interplay of colors and patterns. The chosen palette, combining muted cream and blackened plum, establishes a base of restraint with bursts of red oxide adding vibrancy, signifying oppressive control. The central pixel cluster represents overflow, creating a visual collision with the surrounding darker tones, symbolizing pressure. The deliberate use of broken pixels introduces an element of imperfection, making the composition feel alive and dynamic. This collision is intentional, capturing the essence of frustration and the inevitability of release.
