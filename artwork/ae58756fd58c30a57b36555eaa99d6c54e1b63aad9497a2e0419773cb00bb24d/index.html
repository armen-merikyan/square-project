Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette, inspired by gratitude and envy, juxtaposes deep greens with lighter, softer tones to symbolize fragility against resilience. The main contrast occurs at the center where a soft radial pattern collides with a structured ladder pattern, interrupting a seemingly stable background. The layout plays with negative space, ensuring the central conflict draws the viewer's eye while leaving room for contemplation.
