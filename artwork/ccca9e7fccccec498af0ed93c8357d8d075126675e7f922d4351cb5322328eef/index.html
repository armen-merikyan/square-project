Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between clarity and confusion. The chosen palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green creates a stark contrast, where the dark tones impart the weight of confusion, while gold and green interrupt with hope and clarity. The layout features a near-symmetrical arrangement that diverges with broken pixels—placed in specific locations to suggest disruption and disturbance. The flow of the pattern rises in intensity from the dark baseline to the punctuated central accents, suggesting a pulse of emotions colliding and reflecting upon each other.
