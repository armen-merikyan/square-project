Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the tension between restlessness and peace, with an emphasis on a manic undercurrent contrasting against serene patches. The color palette blends warm hues (coral and orange) symbolizing intensity with cool tones (charcoal and hollow blue) embodying calm. Predominantly chaotic patterns of crosshatch suggest unrest, while subtle cracks represent fleeting moments of tranquility. The main collision occurs in the central rows, where chaotic colors collide with muted tones, creating a visual narrative of struggle against stillness.
