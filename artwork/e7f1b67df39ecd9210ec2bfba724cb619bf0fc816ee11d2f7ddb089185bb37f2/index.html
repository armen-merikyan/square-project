Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a balance of longing and resolve, reflecting an inner conflict. The palette of soft green, warm brown, pale skin, cold white, and open blue represents sheltering qualities clashing with exposed vulnerability. The primary contrast occurs in the central dense cluster, illustrating resolve through strong colors, while the surrounding quiet fields imply longing. The broken pixels disrupt symmetry, embodying imperfections in the emotional narrative, creating an interplay of density and space that resonates with both urgency and serenity.
