Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrasts of loneliness and connection through a structured layout that features a stark inner core amidst a dynamic, weighted outer frame. The palette of electric blue, lime, magenta, and black signifies the duality: coolness of loneliness (black) colliding with the vibrancy of connection (electric blue and lime). The placement of darker colors around the edges establishes an emotional boundary, while the lighter center allows for a burst of potential interaction. The patterns of rings and stripes serve to reinforce the push-and-pull of connection, creating a visual echo of human experiences. The result is a rich exploration of emotional space, with structured interruptions and places of quiet dominance, reflecting the struggle between internal and external worlds.
