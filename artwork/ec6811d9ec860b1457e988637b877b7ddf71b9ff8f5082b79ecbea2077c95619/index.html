Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tug between clarity and confusion. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, and charcoal emphasizes the warmth of confusion against the cooler, muted gold. The layout reflects a central seed against a backdrop of overlapping grids that reflect noise and asymmetry. The contrast is heightened where dark colors break through warm hues, suggesting a pulse of uncertainty amidst a desperate quest for clarity. Broken pixels disrupt symmetry, embodying the mental strain of seeking understanding in chaos.
