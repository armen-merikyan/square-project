Artist Statement

This composition explores the duality of awe and terror, creating an emotional tension between structured order and chaotic disarray. The warm palette of yellows and oranges evokes feelings of awe, while muted blues and purples introduce an unsettling contrast, embodying terror. The left side features a clean, wave-like pattern suggesting stability, while the right reflects broken dots, introducing chaos and memories. Key collisions occur in the central area, where the warm hues disrupt the cool tones, creating a dialogue of emotion that speaks to fleeting moments of clarity amidst confusion.
