Artist Statement

The emotional tone is one of tension between defiance and submission, symbolized by contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of hot coral, blood orange, and dust pink evokes warmth and intensity against muted tones of charcoal and hollow blue, reflecting the struggle between bold assertion and gentle surrender. The main collision occurs in the center where bright colors clash with the darker edges, creating a focal point of chaos versus calm, embodying the core themes of confrontation and resolution. The arrangement also incorporates broken pixels that disrupt the flow, suggesting imperfections that enhance the narrative's emotional complexity.
