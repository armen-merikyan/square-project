Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dichotomy of trust and suspicion through contrasting patterns and colors. The palette features muted tones like 'blackened_plum' and 'muted_cream' symbolizing stability and fragility, while vibrant 'small_orange' introduces tension. The left side, filled with distinct 'dots', signifies trust, while the irregular 'shatter' pattern on the right interrupts the field, embodying suspicion. The central collision represents a conflict between assurance and doubt, effectively utilizing broken pixels to enhance the narrative of imperfection and tension throughout the piece.
