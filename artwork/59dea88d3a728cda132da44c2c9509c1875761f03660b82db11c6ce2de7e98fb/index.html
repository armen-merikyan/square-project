Artist Statement

This abstract composition captures the emotional dichotomy between certainty and curiosity. The palette, drawn from 'gratitude_envy', combines ripe green and honey gold, symbolizing growth and value amidst the tension of uncertainty, reflected in acid green and deep olive. The main collision occurs at the center with a burst of clear color against denser, muted backgrounds, embodying the struggle between rigid order and fluidity. Overall, the arrangement oscillates between sections of busy patterns and quiet channels, allowing for moments of contemplation amid energetic bursts, thus echoing the seed’s themes of rising signals and receding edges.
