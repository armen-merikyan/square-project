Artist Statement

The emotional tone dances between panic and control, imbuing this abstract work with contrasting pressures. The selected palette features deep blacks to symbolize chaos and red to represent urgent emotion, while pale blue offers a brief respite and white provides stark contrast, reflecting moments of clarity amid turmoil. The layout rises, instilling a sense of hope and escape from the impending chaos below. Pattern types further accentuate this, with radial symmetry suggesting control and broken dots indicating scattered, chaotic energy. Here, the main contrast lies between the stark, dark edges and the lighter center, embodying the collision of feelings. Ultimately, this piece embodies the struggle between inner stability and external disturbances, expressed through color and form without literal representation.
