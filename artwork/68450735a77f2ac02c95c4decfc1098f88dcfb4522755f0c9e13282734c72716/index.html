Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between quiet and pressure through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of off-white and dust gray provides a muted field that represents quietude, while the deeper tones of compressed red and dark navy introduce the weight and intensity of pressure. The arrangement showcases a tight clustering of darker shades in the center that gradually disperses outward into lighter, quieter hues, illustrating the flow from pressure to release. The broken frame layout evokes the idea of shattering against the tide, allowing calm to seep through intentional gaps. The uneven distribution of colors and the introduction of 'broken pixels' create a sense of disruption that echoes the complexity of the emotional narrative, inviting the viewer to explore the layers of meaning and feeling.
