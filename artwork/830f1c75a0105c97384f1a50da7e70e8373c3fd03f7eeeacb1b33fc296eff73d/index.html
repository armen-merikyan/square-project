Artist Statement

This artwork explores the dynamic tension between vulnerability and strength. The pale mint conveys a quiet, exposed center, symbolizing fragility, while storm gray and near black form a dense perimeter, illustrating protective strength. The contrast of soft sky against the more intense dark tones represents emotional turmoil. The diagonal layout creates conflict and interweaving patterns, with pale fields interrupted by grayscale clusters, suggesting a collision of emotions. The composition's patterns and broken pixels evoke a sense of interruption and complexity, encapsulating the essence of vulnerability set against fortitude.
