Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension of restlessness versus peace. The left side embodies peace with soft amber and warm coral, creating a comforting atmosphere, while the right side introduces cold slate and blue-black, evoking chaos and motion. The broken pixels disrupt the flow, symbolizing interventions in tranquility. The zigzag flow accentuates the dynamic conflict, leading to an eruptive center where emotions collide, enhancing the narrative depth of memory and motion.
