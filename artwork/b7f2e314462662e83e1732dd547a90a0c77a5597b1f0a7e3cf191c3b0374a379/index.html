Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission. The palette features a striking contrast of bright signal green and hot magenta against the deep dead black and static gray, creating a deliberate clash. The arranged colors symbolize the diagonal rise of defiance, marked by sharper interruptions of the brighter hues, while the darker regions represent submission's muted presence. The central collision of colors emphasizes an uneven border that suggests an 'open gate,' allowing the contrasting flows to resonate in an abstract realm. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, enhancing the narrative of resistance and retreat set by the seed.
