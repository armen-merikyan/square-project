Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional contrast of longing and resolve through a visual layering of dense and empty fields. The palette, primarily using deep navy accented with dim cyan and single white, reflects a sense of solitude while simultaneously offering moments of clarity. The core collision resides at the center, where densely packed color marks suggest tension against a broad, quiet space. The patterns of 'teeth' and 'ladder' interact to create an unfinished conversation, embodying the 'cross_current' between desire and stability, flowing in a counterclockwise spiral that invites reflection.
