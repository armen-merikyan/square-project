Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between trust and suspicion, employing the palette derived from 'lonely_signal' with deep navy and black, invoking feelings of depth and uncertainty. The color arrangement has contrasting clusters, where dark tones dominate the left half, symbolizing suspicion, while lighter tones emerge in the right, suggesting trust. The main collision occurs at a diagonal edge, where these two emotional currents meet, creating a visual tension that echoes the intrinsic struggle between vulnerability and guard. The 'broken_frame' layout imposes disruptions in flow, highlighting the cuts in connection and a leaked emotional narrative.
