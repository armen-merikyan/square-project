Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the tension between longing and resolve, characterized by a central island of dense colors symbolizing resolve amidst a quiet sea of distant hues representing longing. The palette draws on deep navy and cooling dim cyan, reflecting both emotional weight and the pull of serene calmness. Key contrasts occur where the strong pulse marks collide with the softer crosshatch patterns, creating visual dissonance. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, echoing the sense of repair and damage, adhering to the seed's narrative of emotional turbulence.
