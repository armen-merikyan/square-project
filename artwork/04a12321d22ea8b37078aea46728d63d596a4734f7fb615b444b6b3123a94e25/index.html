Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the conflict between pressure and tranquility, with the palette merging muted colors like off_white and dust_gray to represent quiet and clarity, while compressed_red and dark_navy suggest tension and pressure. The pattern of dense clusters at one edge gradually releases into softer hues, invoking a sense of both confinement and liberation. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the bright compressed_red interrupts the calm ash_field, encapsulating the struggle of breaking free from a weighty environment.
