Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, using a rich palette that symbolizes depth and emotional complexity. The use of warm ivory and deep wine colors suggests intimacy and warmth battling against the cold, distant ash gray tones representing strength and resilience. The main contrast occurs in the central square, where stronger hues interlace with softer colors, creating a visual echo of safety alongside exposure. The technique of crack and crosshatch patterns embodies the collision of feelings, with broken pixels adding a sense of imperfection that speaks to the delicate balance of safety and vulnerability as one navigates through life's challenges.
