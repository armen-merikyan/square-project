Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the duality of strength and vulnerability through a contrast of bold and subtle colors. The palette consists of rich greens and soft creams, embodying the themes of growth (strength) and gentleness (vulnerability). The layout employs a broken_frame approach, allowing emotion to leak through at the edges. The collision is at the center, where deeper greens represent stability, encircled by lighter tones that signify fragility. This interplay illustrates the dynamic tension of personal growth amidst exposure.
