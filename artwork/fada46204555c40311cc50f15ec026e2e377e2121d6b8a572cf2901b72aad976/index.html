Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between loneliness and connection through a stark contrast of colors and patterns. The palette of black, electric blue, magenta, and lime creates a balance of warmth and coolness, highlighting conflict. The central region is dense and dark, representing loneliness, while the outer parts are punctuated with brighter colors symbolizing connection. Broken pixels disrupt the grid-like nature, suggesting vulnerability and imperfection. Thus, the arrangement not only visually expresses feelings of isolation but also the innate human desire to connect, capturing a complex emotional narrative.
