Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that embodies desire and emptiness. The warm tones of hot coral and blood orange against the cool shades of charcoal and hollow blue create a vibrant yet unsettled balance. The main collision occurs in the center, emphasizing exposure, surrounded by protective waves of darker colors, suggesting a struggle between openness and shelter. The broken pixels act as reminders of disruption amidst cohesion, allowing the empty spaces to breathe, adhering to the concept of vulnerability.
