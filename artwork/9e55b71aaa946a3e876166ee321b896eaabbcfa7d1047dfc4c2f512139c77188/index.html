Artist Statement

This composition expresses the duality of rage and calm through contrasting colors and patterns. The vibrant reds symbolize explosive energy and tension, while the soft pale blue conveys tranquility and calmness. The rings of red create a feeling of overwhelming pressure that radiates outward, while the quiet fields of pale blue provide moments of relief. The arrangement features a central collision of bright and muted colors, emphasizing the emotional battle between contained fury and soothing calm, thus fitting the seed's themes of contrasts and inner turmoil.
