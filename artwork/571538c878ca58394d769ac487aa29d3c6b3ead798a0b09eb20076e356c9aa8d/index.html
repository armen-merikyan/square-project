Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a clash between the delicate nature of vulnerability and the solid grounding of strength. The chosen palette of lime, black, electric blue, and magenta creates a dynamic interplay: vibrant colors signify strength while darker shades hint at vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center where dense black and electric blue fight against thinned areas of lime and magenta, symbolizing a struggle for control. The grid layout embodies this argument, while broken pixels disrupt the flow, echoing the fragility in the midst of strength.
