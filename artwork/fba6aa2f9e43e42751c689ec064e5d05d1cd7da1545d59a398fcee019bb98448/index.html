Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of 'quiet vs pressure' through color and arrangement. The palette features muted tones like off-white and dust gray for a serene backdrop, while compressed red introduces sharp tension, symbolizing pressure. The main collision takes place diagonally across the grid, where softer, loose patterns meet denser, tighter formations, creating a visual tension. Broken pixels interrupt the flow, introducing imperfections that mirror the chaos inherent in moments of clarity and confusion.
