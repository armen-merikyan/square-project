Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of fear and hope, presenting a sharp contrast between dark edges and light centers. The chosen palette of soft gold and dark violet captures the struggle between brightness and heaviness, with broken dots representing fear colliding against hopeful stair patterns. The mirror layout suggests balance disrupted by imperfections, enhancing the feeling of tension and hope rising within uncertainty. This abstract pattern conveys the unsteady dynamics between these emotions, highlighting an internal struggle through color placement.
