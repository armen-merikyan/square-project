Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between defiance and submission, manifesting through sharp contrasts and congested patterns. The palette combines deep navy and black for density, with segments of dim cyan to punctuate the structure. The top rows represent defiance’s angular interruptions, while the bottom rows unfold a softer submission approach, creating a visual tension. The central area is dense, signifying a focal collision, while the edges maintain a quiet space, embodying a veil of separation.
