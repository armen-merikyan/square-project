Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between joy and grief, using a palette that juxtaposes bright yellows and oranges with deep blues and muted purples. The colors represent the vibrancy of joy battling the heaviness of grief, creating a split-screen effect of chaotic order. The main contrast is built through hard edges that divide the bright lower half from the muted upper half, suggesting a flow from bottom to top while incorporating both warmth and coolness. The overlapping radial and stripe patterns create a sense of movement and tension, encapsulating the essence of the seed as an abstract representation of emotional conflict.
