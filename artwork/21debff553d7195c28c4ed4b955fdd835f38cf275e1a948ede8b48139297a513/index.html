Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts love's warmth with betrayal's coldness, reflected in a palette of pink and gray. The 'crosshatch vs checker' pattern creates a visual dialogue, revealing an echo of symmetry disrupted by uneven, burned edges. The contrast erupts predominantly in the center, where the warmth of pink clashes with sharp gray, pulling the viewer's gaze diagonally, evoking both tenderness and tension. The seed's concepts translate into this uneven, abstract landscape, hinting at hidden narratives between love and betrayal.
