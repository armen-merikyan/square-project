Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the interplay of vulnerability and strength through a delicate balance of color densities—an exposed central area surrounded by darker, more robust colors symbolizes the tension between safety and threat. The palette of shelter green, warm brown, pale skin, cold white, and open blue illustrates earthy grounding against atmospheric lightness. The main collision occurs in the center, with soft, thin threads representing vulnerability amidst bolder, fragmented dots of strength, creating a rhythm of interruption and contrast that reflects the nuances of human experience. This composition invites contemplation of the duality of exposure and protection.
