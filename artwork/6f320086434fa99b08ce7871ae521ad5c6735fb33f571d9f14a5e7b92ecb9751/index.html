Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances the glowing vitality of 'spark' against the subdued remnants of 'ash.' The palette combines vibrant reds and pinks with grounding grays and dark maroon, expressing the clash of intensity and restraint. The main contrast occurs in the center where hot accent colors disrupt the cooler areas, echoing the theme of conflict and memory versus erasure. The maze pattern of ash collides with pulsating sparks, illustrating a dynamic struggle between energy and calmness, creating a rich narrative of resilience and fragility.
