Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a balance between eruptive rage and serene calm. The chosen palette of red, black, pale blue, and white creates a stark contrast between aggression and tranquility. Main collisions occur through jagged red lines intersecting a calm pale blue background, reflecting tension and unresolved conflict. The pattern embodies interruptions, leading to a visual narrative of dynamic energy meeting stillness, revealed through the organized chaos shifting across the grid.
