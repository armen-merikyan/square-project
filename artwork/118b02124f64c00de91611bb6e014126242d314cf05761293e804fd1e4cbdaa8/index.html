Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between quietness and pressure. The chosen palette of muted off-white, soft dust gray, and a strong compressed red juxtaposes stillness with dynamic punctuations. The contrast occurs in the upper left corner, where the vibrant red pushes against the serene tones, creating a sense of conflict and explosion against a backdrop of calmness. 'Shatter' patterns weave through the piece, expressing brokenness, and randomness, symbolizing the turmoil within what appears to be a placid surface, reflecting the seed's themes of clarity and confusion.
