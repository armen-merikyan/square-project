Artist Statement

The piece reflects the emotional tug-of-war between joy (represented by bright yellows and soft, warm hues) and grief (denoted by deep blues and muted purples). This contrast is centered around a more vibrant core, pulsating with warmth, while the edges feature broken, scattered dots representing the remnants of grief. The overall tension lies in the asymmetric distribution of these colors, with warmth flowing left, echoing the left-weighted composition, while cool tones retreat into a weighty quietness on the right. The grid reinforces the structured nature of joy, while the dots signal the fragmented feel of grief. This contrast and collision encapsulate the complexity of human emotion.
