Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork embodies the delicate interplay between vulnerability and strength, reflected through contrasting dark and vibrant hues. The chosen palette of 'ancient_fire' signifies warmth and intensity, fostering a feeling of conflict and resolution. Dark hues maintain the gravity of vulnerability, while the gold and burnt orange evoke moments of strength. The main contrast resides in the center, with an explosion of brightness amidst heavier, muted fields, representing the intimate clash of exposure against shelter. The radial and crack patterns symbolize the complexity of connection and alienation, suggesting a narrative of resilience and fragility in a chaotic world.
