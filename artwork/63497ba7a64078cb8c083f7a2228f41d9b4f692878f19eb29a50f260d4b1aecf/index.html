Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette includes muted tones of off-white, dust gray, and faint peach to create a quiet field, while compressed red and dark navy serve as vibrant accents, suggesting the urgent signals of curiosity amidst the calm of certainty. The left half of the grid exhibits a mirrored pattern indicating firmness, while the right half features scar-like interruptions, creating visual collisions. The main contrast is between a solid navy core and the chaotic light edges, evoking the push and pull of curiosity battling against the structured residue of certainty.
