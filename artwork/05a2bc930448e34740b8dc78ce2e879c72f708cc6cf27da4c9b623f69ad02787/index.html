Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes feelings of loneliness against connection, leading to a stark visual representation. The palette of dark maroon, gray, and red suggests an underlying tension. The grid pattern represents connection while the scattered spark patterns indicate moments of isolation. The top weighting of colors displays the weight of emotional burdens contrasted with lighter tones below, symbolizing an attempt to rise or connect. The collision between dense hues and more muted tones creates a visual imbalance that echoes the push and pull between solitude and unity.
