Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tension between control and surrender, highlighting a saturated pull towards a restrained border. The palette, inspired by 'ancient_fire', blends burnt orange and dark red to capture warmth and intensity, while charcoal adds depth and containment. The dense center confronts the surrounding lighter tones, creating a collision of pressure and release. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, echoing the struggle within the emotional framework. This artwork uses asymmetry to emphasize how one emotional state dominates, reflecting the relentless quest for balance amid inherent conflict.
