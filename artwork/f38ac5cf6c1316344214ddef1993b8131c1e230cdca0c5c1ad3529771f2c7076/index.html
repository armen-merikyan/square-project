Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between 'quiet' and 'pressure.' The palette incorporates soft hues like 'faint_peach' and 'off_white' to represent tranquility, contrasted against the harsh 'compressed_red' and dark tones of 'dark_navy,' which signify pressure. The main collision occurs centrally, where dense colors clash with gentle tones, simulating the push-and-pull dynamic of the seed's emotional undercurrents. The arrangement features a flowing transition from crowded to open spaces, embodying both 'density' and 'release' as colors shift across the grid. The deliberate use of a few broken pixels adds imperfection, enhancing the concept of struggle and disruption in order and chaos.
