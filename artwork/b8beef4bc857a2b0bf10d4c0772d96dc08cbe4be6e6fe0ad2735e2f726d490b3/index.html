Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between softness and severity, utilizing a palette that juxtaposes gentle pinks and whites against stark blacks and harsh grays. The darker tones occupy the bottom left, suggesting a weightiness that counteracts the lighter, more liberated top right, creating a sense of dynamic flow. The collision occurs through scattered placements of black and crimson that disrupt the softness of the warm white and pale pink, representing moments of guilt versus the soft touch of forgiveness. The open gate layout allows the eye to escape the confines of dense pixels, promoting an impression of lightness and release.
