Artist Statement

This piece embodies the struggle between vulnerability and strength, illustrated through a palette of deep maroons and soft grays. The contrast stems from dense stripes representing strength against lighter ladder-like patterns suggesting vulnerability. The placement creates a collision of emotions, particularly in the central area, where darker colors predominate, conveying a stable center surrounded by fragile edges. The negative space captures moments of quiet amidst emotional tension, inspired by concepts like safety, exposure, and the horizon's duality.
