Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of longing and resolve through contrasting dot and echo patterns. The muted cream symbolizes serenity and serves as a backdrop, while the vibrant red oxide represents urgency and desire, creating tension. The arrangement of colors reflects a central collision, evoking a feeling of fluctuating emotions—an inner struggle between yearning and determination, punctuated with intent breaks in the pattern to symbolize broken connections.
