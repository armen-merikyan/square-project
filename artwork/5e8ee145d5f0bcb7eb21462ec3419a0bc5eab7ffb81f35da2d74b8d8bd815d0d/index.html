Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting color fields and pressure dynamics. The chosen palette of mourning black, deep blue, new leaf, pale yellow, and stone illustrates the duality of emotion—black and blue representing suspicion and a hard shadow, while the lighter tones reflect warmth and resilience. The main collision occurs diagonally, where soft glows of yellow interact with deep blues, creating a vibrant flare amidst darker corners. This dynamic encapsulates a constant push and pull between these two emotional states, threading through a spiral pressure layout that draws the eye inward, suggesting a journey towards understanding amidst chaos.
