Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between loneliness and connection, captured through dark and muted colors contrasted with small bursts of lighter shades. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green embodies a sense of fear and hope, where the dark colors represent isolation, while soft gold hints at potential connection. The main collision occurs in the center with the burst of gold against a dark background, symbolizing the struggle between hidden vulnerabilities and the desire for openness. The layout follows a mirror-broken pattern, contributing to an echo of disconnection while evoking a sense of pressure and release.
