Artist Statement

The artwork balances heavy, dark colors representing pressure against lighter tones signifying quiet. The edges contain jagged pixel disruptions, showcasing the collisions between pressure and soft calmness, embodying the seed's theme. The muted field colors create a sense of distance while allowing sharp contrasts to echo throughout, symbolizing the push and pull of emotional tension.
