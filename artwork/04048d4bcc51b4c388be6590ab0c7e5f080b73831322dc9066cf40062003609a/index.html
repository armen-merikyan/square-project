Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a conflict between envy and gratitude, with an interplay of muted and vibrant colors. The chosen palette, inspired by 'broken_memory', combines warm sepias and cooler faded blues to depict an emotional landscape filled with soft transitions and jagged disturbances. The contrast of deeper colors against lighter shades creates a tension that suggests rising hope against falling despair, with soft edges blurring the boundaries of each emotional state. Negative space is deliberately utilized to evoke the sensation of an empty room, emphasizing a feeling of isolation yet maintaining a sense of possibility.
