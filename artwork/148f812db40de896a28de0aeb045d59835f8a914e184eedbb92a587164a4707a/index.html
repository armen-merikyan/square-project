Artist Statement

This composition conveys the tension between loneliness and connection. The palette of deep navy, muted blacks, and dim cyan evokes feelings of solitude and dimmed emotions, while the inclusion of a bright white represents the fleeting moments of connection. The weight at the center contrasts with the surrounding voids, emphasizing isolation interspersed with attempts at connection. The crosshatch pattern signifies the complexities of relationships, where each intersection gives a sense of both connection and obstruction, hinting at the duality of the seed's themes. The visual flow zigzags through moments of stark contrast, with the overwhelming dark punctuated by sudden bright moments, symbolizing bursts of hope amidst the weight of loneliness.
