Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the conflict between longing and resolve, expressed through contrasting clusters of color. The palette includes soft tones like powder pink and warm white, reflecting a sense of gentleness and nostalgia, juxtaposed with sharp crimson and carbon black that convey intensity and urgency. The collision arises from the interplay of the channels and constellation patterns, suggesting both connection and chaos. The broken pixels serve as discrete interruptions, creating a visual narrative of repair amidst fractures. Main contrast occurs in the center, where crimson and white punctuate the softer gray tones, embodying the tension between contained emotions and an overflowing heart.
