Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a balance between vulnerability and strength, symbolizing the contrast of exposed and protected spaces. The palette includes soft, off-white and faint peach to represent vulnerability, while dark navy and compressed red evoke strength. The ring and dot patterns create a dynamic interplay, with the rings suggesting fragility and the dots representing resilience. The main collision occurs at the center where these contrasting patterns meet, reflecting an emotional push and pull that resonates throughout the piece.
