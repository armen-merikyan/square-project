Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between envy and gratitude, accentuated through a palette of vibrant greens and contrasting deep blacks. The arrangement echoes tension, with dark colors dominating the edges while sharp, bright hues pulse inward. The soft edge contrasts and the upward/downward flow capture the ambivalence of rising hope against falling despair. The dotted patterns, influenced by both emotions, interrupt the solid fields, symbolizing the constant collision of feelings, evoking the sensation of an empty room filled with meaning.
