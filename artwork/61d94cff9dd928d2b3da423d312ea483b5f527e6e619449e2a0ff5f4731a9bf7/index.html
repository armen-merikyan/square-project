Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the tension between vulnerability and strength, represented through soft, broken edges contrasted against vibrant, stable blocks. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue communicates a sense of nostalgia and memory, evoking feelings of both resilience and fragility. The main contrast occurs at the center, where the soft 'fault line' motif of vulnerability disrupts the more solid, 'noise'-driven strength pattern. This collision creates a visual narrative of exposure that is interrupted by structured protection, reflecting the ambivalence of one's inner conflicts.
