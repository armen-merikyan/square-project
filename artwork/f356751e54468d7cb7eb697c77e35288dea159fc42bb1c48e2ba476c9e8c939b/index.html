Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between the warmth of love and the coolness of betrayal. The palette, comprising warm pinks and reds against cooler grays and deep maroon, illustrates this duality. The main contrast occurs where intense reds and soft grays collide, creating rhythmic interruptions that suggest heartache. The pattern of sharp edges and soft areas reflects the seed's concept of symmetry and break, showcasing a tension of structured versus chaotic elements.
