Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the interplay between fear and hope, manifesting through jagged edges contrasting with soft gold highlights that suggest a glimmer of optimism amidst a dark backdrop. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green embodies tension, where fear is represented by darker shades surrounding a hopeful core. The structured arrangement of colors creates collisions, with the central 4x4 of soft gold spark patterns clashing against the more solid, split-screen outer layer. This layout emphasizes a narrative of pressure from the outside world while allowing a rhythm of hope to emerge at the center.
