Artist Statement

This artwork harnesses the emotional contrast of 'relief' and 'dread' by using dense colors complemented by softer tones. The dark palette emphasizes pressure, while lighter colors provide the feeling of release and exhale. The primary collision occurs at the center, where rich colors converge, gradually loosening towards the edges. This reflects the theme of tension easing into calm, while the broken pixels throughout introduce intentional imperfections and disruptions, enhancing the narrative of tension against release.
