Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes nostalgia and depth, creating a warm yet melancholic atmosphere. The primary collision occurs in the center, where densely packed 'resolve' pixels meet the sparse interruptions of 'longing,' symbolized by single pixels. This layout cultivates a dynamic interplay between pressure and space, mirroring the emotional journey of striving against the backdrop of emptiness. The chosen patterns reflect a patchwork of hope woven through a stair-like structure, emphasizing the journey amidst uncertainty.
