Artist Statement

This piece contrasts confusion and clarity through a split layout, where the outer border represents the chaotic side and the inner 4x4 core conveys a sense of order. The palette, influenced by the themes of love and betrayal, uses dark maroon and red to evoke intensity against softer gray tones symbolizing clarity. The stair-step pattern exemplifies confusion, while the checker pattern in the center embodies clarity. The collision occurs at the transition from the dense outer layer to the more organized inner square, illustrating the tension between disarray and focus.
