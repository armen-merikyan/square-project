Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the contrast of 'fear' represented by jagged and dark elements against the 'hope' of bright points of light. The palette employs black and dark violet to denote fear, alongside soft gold and pale green to convey hope. This interplay creates a visual narrative where darkness encroaches on light. The main collision occurs between the dense dark corners and the central glowing accents, illustrating an emotional struggle. The split layout emphasizes the vertical divide, while the crosshatch pattern brings a dynamic texture that suggests tension and resolution, encapsulating the theme of duality in a cohesive abstract form.
