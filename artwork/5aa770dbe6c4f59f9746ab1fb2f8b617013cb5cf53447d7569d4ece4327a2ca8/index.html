Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes desire, represented through warm coral and blood orange, against emptiness characterized by cool shades of charcoal and hollow blue. The pattern of a maze evokes a saturated pull while the embers suggest a quiet loss. This interplay creates tension, with the main collision occurring in the center, where warmth meets coolness, emphasizing the struggle between overflowing desire and restrained emptiness.
