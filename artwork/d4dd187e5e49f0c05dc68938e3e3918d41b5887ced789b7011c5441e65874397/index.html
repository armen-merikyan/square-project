Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the duality of dread and relief through an asymmetrical distribution of colors. The dense corner, filled with deep storm gray and near black, communicates dread, while the cooler pale mint and soft sky at the opposite edge represent relief. The introduction of broken pixels enhances the narrative of imperfection and disruption, symbolizing how dread can crack through the calm. The overall palette, infused with muted tones and a single accent of faded gold, creates a sense of tension between heavy and light, showcasing a dynamic emotional story.
