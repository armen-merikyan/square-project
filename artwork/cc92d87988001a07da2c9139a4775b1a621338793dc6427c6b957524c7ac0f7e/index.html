Artist Statement

This composition captures the duality of calm and rage through contrast. The palette of deep black, stark white, and subtle blues represents the intense emotional collisions and releases, echoing the pressure of contrasting moods. The upward rise of lighter colors against the darker tones conveys a gradual movement from tension to peace. The arrangement suggests broken and interrupted patterns, reflective of the emotional push and pull, resonating with the seed's themes.
