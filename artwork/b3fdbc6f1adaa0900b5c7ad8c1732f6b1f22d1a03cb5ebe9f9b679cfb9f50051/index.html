Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, with broken pixels interrupting the serenity that vulnerability often brings. The palette of warm coral and deep charcoal accentuates the contrast between the exposed lighter shades and the boldness of darker edges. The colliding patterns of shatter and embers reflect the emotional conflict, centering on the delicate interplay of strength and exposure. The usage of negative space invites the viewer to contemplate the 'threshold' or seam where these emotions converge.
