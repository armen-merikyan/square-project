Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic interplay between pressure and release, represented by the contrasting colors of fear and hope. The palette combines black and dark violet to emphasize a sense of control and stability while incorporating soft gold and pale green to suggest moments of hope and relief. The placement of colors creates a rhythmic flow from dark clusters at the top to lighter hues at the bottom, symbolizing a journey from tension to release. The 'argument_grid' layout introduces a fight between dense pressure and open space, allowing for an unresolved emotional collision that speaks to the complexity of human feelings.
