Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'restlessness vs peace' is depicted through jagged edges and contrasting colors, with burnt orange and dark red representing restlessness, while charcoal and gold signify peace. The interplay creates a visual tension, with a concentrated burst of warm colors in the center suggesting motion and unrest, surrounded by calmer tones that evoke a quiet space. The choices reflect memory intersecting with motion, emphasizing a chaotic pulse in the center that echoes outward, thus visually narrating the seed's emotional dichotomy.
