Artist Statement

This abstract artwork explores the emotional tension between 'quiet' and 'pressure'. The chosen palette of off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach creates a contrast between muted tones and sharp highlights, reflecting the theme of density versus release. The dense clusters of darker colors on the upper left signify pressure, while the lighter colors fading towards the bottom right symbolize a gradual release into calm. The collision of these patterns emphasizes the dynamic interplay of emotions, embodying the seed's narratives of tension and confusion.
