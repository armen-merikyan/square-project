Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional conflict between confusion and clarity through a stark contrast of colors and patterns. The selected palette of electric blue, lime, magenta, and black evokes an energetic tension, while the broken symmetry introduces an imperfection that reflects turmoil amidst an otherwise orderly layout. The interleaving of vibrant colors against a dark background creates a pulse effect, suggesting rhythm and movement while also emphasizing the collision of calm against chaos.
