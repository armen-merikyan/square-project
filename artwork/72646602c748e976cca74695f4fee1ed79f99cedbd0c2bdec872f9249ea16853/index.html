Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between joy and grief through a carefully chosen palette and layout. The outer edges are darker, representing grief and chaos, while the vibrant yellows and oranges in the center symbolize joy, suggesting a rising signal of hope amidst despair. The zigzag pattern along with the fragmented placement creates a sense of movement and interruption, reflecting the push and pull of human emotions. The broken edges represent the imperfections of these feelings, adding to the emotional depth.
