Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'defiance vs submission' is expressed through stark contrasts between dark and light colors, symbolizing the struggle. The palette of navy, black, and dim cyan reflects a sense of depth and isolation, while a single white pixel interjects hope amidst starkness. The main contrast unfolds in the outer frame versus the densely packed inner pixels, creating a visual narrative of pressure and release. This layout encapsulates the seed's themes of falling and weight, suggesting a precarious balance between freedom and containment.
