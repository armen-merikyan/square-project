Artist Statement

This artwork illustrates the conflict between 'defiance' and 'submission' through the use of stark contrasts and contrasting patterns. The selected palette of deep reds and pale blues conveys strong emotions of rage intertwined with a sense of calm. The jagged edges hint at the rawness of defiance, while the split-screen pattern introduces a sense of order over chaos. The layout allows the colors to exhibit a rising flow, suggesting hope that counterbalances the bottom weight. The placement of colors creates a push and pull effect, enhancing the emotional depth stemming from the seed's themes of conflict and duality.
