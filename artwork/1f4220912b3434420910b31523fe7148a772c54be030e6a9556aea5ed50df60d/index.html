Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the tension between longing and resolve, reflecting a dance between quiet and pulse throughout the grid. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of nostalgia and broken memories, while also allowing for a subtle calmness amidst emotional conflict. The collision occurs at the center, where darker tones represent resolve and brighter tones imply longing, creating a dialogue between presence and absence within a framework of soft edges. This layout features a calm clear space with intimate, pulsing centers that reflect the complex interplay of emotions derived from the seed.
