Artist Statement

This abstract piece embodies the struggle between quietude and pressure, using a palette that combines muted tones with sharp contrasts. The dark navy establishes a weighted border, representing pressure, while the faint peach and off-white act as soothing, quieter fields, creating a tensioned balance. The collision between the dense central area and airy outer pixels suggests both confinement and release, mirroring the emotional layers hinted at in the seed. This arrangement evokes a dynamic narrative where structure meets chaos.
