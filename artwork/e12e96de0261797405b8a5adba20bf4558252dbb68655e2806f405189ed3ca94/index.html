Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between defiance and submission, represented through a clash of bold colors and muted tones. The palette features 'ancient_fire' colors to signify warmth and conflict: burnt orange and dark red as dominant expressions of defiance, while charcoal introduces submission's quiet retreat. The main contrast occurs in the center, aimed at the emotional collision point, utilizing a stepwell pattern for defiance and stairs for submission, creating a visual echo of rising and receding forms. The arrangement encourages a viewing experience of tension and balance, underscored by intentional broken pixels that speak to imperfection and disruption.
