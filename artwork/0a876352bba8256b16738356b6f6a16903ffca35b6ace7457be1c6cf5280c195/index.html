Artist Statement

This composition captures a juxtaposition between the entangled feelings of restlessness and peace. The palette reflects a softness contrasted with severity; the warm white (#FFFFFF) represents peace, while shades of black and gray echo the discord of restlessness. Main contrast occurs along the diagonal, where the calm hues meet deep darkness, suggesting a struggle within. The pattern emerges as a dynamic dance between mirror-like symmetry of the soft colors and chaotic target-like interruptions from the darker shades, evoking a vivid sense of memory caught between motion and stillness.
