Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of longing and resolve, juxtaposed with feelings of containment and overflow. The palette blends red oxide and muted cream to express urgency against a backdrop of resilience represented by iron gray. Main contrasts occur in the upper left where overfilled marks collide with clear boundaries in the rest of the grid, symbolizing the conflict between desire and determination. The patterns of broken frames intersect with the structured teeth to create a complex visual narrative of repair and fractured beauty.
