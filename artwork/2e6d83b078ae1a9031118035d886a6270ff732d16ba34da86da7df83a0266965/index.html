Artist Statement

This pixel artwork explores the tension between certainty and curiosity. The palette, derived from deep teals and cosmic indigos, evokes a sense of awe tinged with a hint of dread, reflecting the depths of emotional experience. The composition emphasizes a dominant dark edge, suggesting grounding certainty while lighter pixels hint at curiosity cascading into chaos. The collision occurs near the lower right, where structured lines meet erratic glows, suggesting an interaction where certainty is challenged by unpredictability. The broken pixels interrupt the flow, creating tension and inviting exploration within the grid's confines, embodying the themes of rising signals and receding edges.
