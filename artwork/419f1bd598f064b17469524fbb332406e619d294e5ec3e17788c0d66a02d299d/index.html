Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between isolation and connection. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, and faded blue evokes a sense of nostalgia and yearning. The contrast lies in the dense, dark patches signifying loneliness against lighter, warmer areas that suggest connection. The pattern's broken, checker-like implementation represents the inherent disruption in relationships—where connection feels fractured, creating a push-and-pull dynamic. The layout mirrors around the center, yet hints at imperfection through varied color intensities, amplifying feelings of both exposure and hidden depths.
