Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a delicate balance between vulnerability and strength, where muted hues represent shelter and brighter accents signify exposure. The palette includes safety green and warm brown to anchor the piece, while pale skin and open blue evoke feelings of vulnerability. The central collision occurs in a mirrored layout that breaks symmetry, creating tension on the edges with soft breaks colliding against dense forms. This arrangement suggests a space caught between light and shadow, representing the push and pull of safety and exposure.
