Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension of loneliness versus connection. The color palette of muted sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue creates a memory-like quality, evoking feelings of nostalgia and isolation. The contrast between the dense central cluster (representing connection) and the expansive negative space (embodying loneliness) emphasizes the struggle between these two emotions. The central burst pattern suggests a 'tear' in an otherwise quiet landscape, where individual color dots intermittently collide with muted backgrounds, creating a feeling of fragmentation and connection amidst solitude.
