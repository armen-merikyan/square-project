Artist Statement

The emotional tone derives from the interplay between vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette 'hunger_restraint' includes muted cream for the exposed center, contrasting with dark hues of blackened plum and iron gray that form the protective boundaries. The layout, described as 'last_light', focuses on a small bright cluster, symbolizing the remnants of hope amid overwhelming darkness. The ripple pattern represents strength, while the stepwell pattern conveys vulnerability, creating a dynamic collision in the center. The arrangement balances exposed light against surrounding dark surfaces, inviting contemplation about safety and exposure.
