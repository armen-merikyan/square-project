Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dynamic interplay between strength and vulnerability. The palette emphasizes clarity with a stark contrast of ice blue and warning orange, symbolizing strength amid vulnerability. The collision occurs in the central area, where densely packed ‘channels’ interact with ‘maze-like’ patterns, creating visual tension. The edges are shadowed to suggest protection while leaving intentional breaks for vulnerability. This representation stems from the seed’s themes of emotional dualities, translating them into a complex, yet abstract arrangement.
