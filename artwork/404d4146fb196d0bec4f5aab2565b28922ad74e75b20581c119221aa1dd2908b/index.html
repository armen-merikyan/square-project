Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between joy and grief through a defined palette. The warm yellows and oranges evoke joy, while deep blues and muted purples communicate grief. The layout resembles falling pixels, symbolizing tears against a backdrop of chaos. The contrast between bright and dark creates collisions of emotion, with jagged edges enhancing the feelings of tension and disturbance. The central focal point, a hard edge of color separation, serves as a visual representation of the struggle between the light and dark elements.
