Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between 'longing' and 'resolve' using a palette that dances between muted grays and warm accents. The top half represents longing through a checker pattern in soft sky and pale mint, creating a sense of distance and ethereal quality. The bottom half embodies resolve with a static pattern in storm gray and near black, establishing a grounded, compressed strength. The collision occurs at the midpoint where longing and resolve intertwine, highlighting the push and pull of these emotions, while occasional broken pixels symbolize the imperfections and scars of the emotional journey.
