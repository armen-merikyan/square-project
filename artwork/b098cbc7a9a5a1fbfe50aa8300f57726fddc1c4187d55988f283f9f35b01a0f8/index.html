Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between joy and grief through a deliberate use of color and pattern. The palette blends bright yellows and oranges to represent joy with deep blues and muted purples symbolizing grief. The upper half is denser with vibrant noise patterns, evoking an expanding joy, while the lower half introduces ring patterns in muted tones to convey heaviness and fragmentation. The thin outline around certain shapes interrupts the flow, highlighting the boundaries between opposing emotions. The arrangement creates a visual wave, reinforcing the dynamic conflict between light and shadow, presence and absence.
