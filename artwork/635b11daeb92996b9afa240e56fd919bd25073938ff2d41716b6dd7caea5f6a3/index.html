Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between trust and suspicion. The palette balances softness with severity, employing warm white and powder pink to evoke trust, contrasted sharply with carbon black and steel gray to signal suspicion. The layout adopts stacked bands to emphasize emotional strata. The main collision occurs diagonally, showing the push and pull between these opposing forces, with broken pixels introducing imperfections that reflect the fragility intertwined within resilience. Overall, the piece reinforces the complexity of navigating trust amidst suspicion.
