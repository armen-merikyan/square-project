Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between loneliness and connection, employing a palette that reflects both warmth and darkness. The contrasting colors evoke feelings of intimacy juxtaposed with isolation, with the dark maroon and gray suggesting hidden emotions and unexpressed pain. The main collision occurs in the center, where bursts of pink challenge the surrounding muted tones, creating a dance between exposure and concealment. This pattern represents the 'hole' shape seed; it's not literal but abstractly portrays the emotional void and longing for connection through unexpected interactions of colors.
