Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional duality of nostalgia and progress through a carefully curated palette and arrangement. The black and white colors suggest a timeless contrast between the past (nostalgia) and a striking pale blue indicating the vibrancy of progress. The grid-like placement with a bottom-weighted design introduces an element of tension, while the broken pixels symbolize imperfections and reflections of memories. The swirling pattern hints at the fluid movement from calm to rage, representing the conflicting emotions inherent in reminiscing versus moving forward. By allowing negative space, a sense of void is created that mirrors the feelings of absence and longing.
