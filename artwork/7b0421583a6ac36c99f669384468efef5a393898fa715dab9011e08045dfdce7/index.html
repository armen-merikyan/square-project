Artist Statement

This pixel artwork captures the emotional interplay of vulnerability and strength through a contrasting palette of dark maroon and pale pink, representing strength against a backdrop of exposure. The hard edges juxtapose the scattered marks, implying both the resilience of shelter and the fragility of being exposed. The collision happens centrally, where the pale area intersects with darker hues, expressing a narrative of protection while hinting at the potential for breach. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, adding depth to the overall composition and enhancing the feeling of imperfection.
