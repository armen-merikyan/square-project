Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between the deep void of emptiness and the vibrant pull of desire. The palette consists of warm coral and orange hues that evoke yearning, interspersed with muted charcoal and blue to represent the containment of those feelings. The edge style is blocked, illustrating boundaries that desire cannot breach, while the spiral layout serves to depict movement both towards and away from an implicit center of intensity. The collision occurs centrally where warmer tones congregate amidst cooler backgrounds, inviting contemplation of the struggle between aspiration and restraint.
