Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional juxtaposition of fear and hope. The palette includes deep blacks and dark violets that suggest fear, contrasted with soft gold and pale green representing hope. The edges are jagged, creating a sense of disruption, while the central area remains light to symbolize emerging hope amidst darkness. The placement creates a vertical flow from darkness to light, echoing the theme of rising hope against the weight of fear, enhanced by a crosshatch pattern that suggests complexity and tension.
