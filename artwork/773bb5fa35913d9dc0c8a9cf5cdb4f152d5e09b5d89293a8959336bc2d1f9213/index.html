Artist Statement

This artwork captures the conflict of joy and grief, utilizing contrasting colors to evoke the emotional tension between the two. The palette features warm yellows and oranges to symbolize joy, interspersed with deep blues and muted purples that represent grief. The spiraling flow and falling pixel layout create a sense of cascading emotion, where vibrant bursts collide with cooler tones, suggesting both the weight of grief and the fleeting moments of joy. The asymmetrical pattern and broken pixels enhance the sense of disruption, echoing the imperfect nature of emotional experiences.
