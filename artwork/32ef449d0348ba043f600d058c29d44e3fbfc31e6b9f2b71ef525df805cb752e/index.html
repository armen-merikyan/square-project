Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a juxtaposition of the delicate balance between safety and exposure. The palette combines muted shelter green and warm brown to convey safety, contrasted against a bright pale skin and open blue that suggest exposure. The collision occurs at the center, where the two patterns overlap, creating a dynamic tension. The edge style is left-weighted, reinforcing a feeling of pressure at the borders while maintaining soft, flowing patterns that symbolize release. The abstract composition invites contemplation on vulnerability and protection.
