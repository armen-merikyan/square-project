Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends quiet pressure with bursts of contrasting colors, depicting the collision of pride and shame. The chosen palette, 'broken_memory', with its sepia and faded blue hues, reflects a sense of nostalgia and emotional depth, where muted tones establish a quiet background while vibrant contrasts disrupt the calm. The layout captures a split vertical format, emphasizing the dichotomy between emotions. Main contrasts occur in the central column, where the bright colors collide against darker shades, symbolizing unresolved emotions and tension, with broken pixels introducing imperfection to evoke the chaos behind these feelings.
