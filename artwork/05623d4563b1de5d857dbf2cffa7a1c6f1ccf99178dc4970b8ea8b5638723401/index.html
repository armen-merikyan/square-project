Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the conflict between defiance and submission, using a palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon to symbolize turmoil and warmth. The placement creates a weighted bottom with lighter pixels cascading, reinforcing the sense of falling. Major contrast emerges from the vibrant reds against the muted grays, suggesting an echo of passionate frustration amidst softer tones. This results in a rhythmic wave across the grid that mirrors the push and pull within the emotional narrative.
