Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between rage and calm, reflecting the powerful collision of contrasting feelings. The black and red signify explosive emotion, while pale blue and white evoke tranquility. The arrangement contrasts chaotic stripes against calming waves, creating rhythm through displacement with intention. The edges are heavy with chaos while the center remains calm yet disrupted, accentuating the narrative of emotions colliding and yielding.
