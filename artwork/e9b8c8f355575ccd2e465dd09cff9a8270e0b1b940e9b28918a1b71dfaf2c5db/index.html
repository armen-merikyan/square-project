Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting colors and forms. The warm tones of hot coral and blood orange suggest feelings of trust and warmth, while the cooler shades of charcoal and hollow blue introduce a sense of suspicion and uncertainty. The primary collision occurs diagonally, where the brighter colors meet the darker ones, suggesting a moment of interaction or conflict. The layout features a mirrored structure that is disrupted by deliberate imperfections, echoing the fragility of trust amid suspicion, leaving negative spaces to reflect pauses in emotional tension.
