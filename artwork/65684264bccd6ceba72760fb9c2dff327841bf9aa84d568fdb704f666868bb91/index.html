Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between defiance and submission, expressed through a palette of deep navy, stark black, muted cyan, and singular white, evoking a lonely yet vibrant presence. The colors are placed to create a duality, where warmer darks (navy and black) suggest weight and tension against lighter shades (white and dim cyan) symbolizing fragility and openness. Main contrast and collision occur in the center, where the denser left side meets the more exposed right, representing noise against calm. The layout mirrors this emotional conflict, with slight imperfections introduced through uneven pixel placement, illustrating the unpredictability of submission and defiance.
