Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional contrast between confusion and clarity, mirroring the tension of overlapping pathways. The color palette of lime, black, electric blue, and magenta reflects the duality; lime and electric blue suggest clarity and machine emotion, while black and magenta introduce confusion. The top half bursts with sharp contrasts and vivid colors, symbolizing clarity, against a lower half that deteriorates into sparks of confusion with subtle, fractured placements creating a 'broken' sense. The pattern logic radiates from the center outward, aligning with the seed's concept, while negative space preserves an element of calm against the dense chaos below, reflecting a balance between clarity’s intention and confusion’s intrusion.
