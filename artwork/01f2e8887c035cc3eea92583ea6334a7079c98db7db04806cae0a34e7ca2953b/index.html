Artist Statement

This artwork embodies a dynamic interplay between confusion and clarity, reflected in the contrast of dark, muted tones and lighter accents. The navy and black create a heavy atmosphere, while the dim cyan introduces moments of clarity and pulse. The arrangement simulates rising signals interrupted by chaotic patches, representing both ascending hope and descending confusion. The broken pixels add to the imperfection, with edges that appear sharp yet dissolve into faint geometry, embodying the theme of symmetry versus break.
