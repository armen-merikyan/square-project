Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'longing vs resolve' drives a tension between empty space and structured forms. The palette of cosmic indigo, void black, star white, cold gold, and deep teal highlights an interplay of cool and warm tones, embodying the clash of desire and steadiness. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense clusters of grid-like patterns in cosmic indigo meet the more open, creamy halo of star white, creating a feeling of longing encroaching on resolve. The abstract expression resonates with the nuances of distance in intimacy, allowing for a broken pixel here and there, emphasizing the imperfections in these emotions.
