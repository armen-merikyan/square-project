Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between pressure and quietude, suggesting tension and release. The palette combines muted colors like off-white and dust gray with a striking compressed red for accent, facilitating a visual and emotional dialogue between calm and intensity. The peaks of pressure manifest as dense clusters in the top left corner, while the soft edges around the silent spaces suggest a fading quiet that evaporates towards the bottom right. The interplay of the patterns—crosshatch for pressure and glitch for quiet—creates a dynamic where the sharpness of hard edges contrasts with the softness of the negative spaces, capturing the essence of the seed's thematic exploration of conflict and harmony.
