Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between vulnerability, depicted by softer, muted colors, and strength, represented through bold contrasts. The palette draws from 'ancient_fire' to embody warmth and depth, with dark reds and warm burnt oranges framing the center. The collision happens at the center of the square where the tight, solid ring of strength meets the pale, fragmented target pattern of vulnerability. This segmentation suggests tension and flow, mirroring the complexities of the seed's themes such as 'flood against stack' and 'cross_current' as colors merge and clash, yet find moments of harmony.
