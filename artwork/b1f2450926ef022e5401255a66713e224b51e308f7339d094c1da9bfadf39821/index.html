Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between joy and grief through a split horizontal layout, where the top half employs a bright palette reflecting joy while the bottom half uses darker, muted tones representing grief. The warm yellow and orange signals in the top pixels contrast starkly with the deep blues and muted purples below, suggesting a constant push and pull between light and dark emotions. The noise pattern in the joyful section contrasts with the dot pattern in the grief section, hinting at chaos versus order. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, signifying the messiness of human experience, and the absence of outlines reinforces the fluidity between these emotions, adding to the feeling of abstract echo and resonance.
