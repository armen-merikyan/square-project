Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the push and pull between dread and relief, embodied by a warm palette contrasting with deep blues. The collision occurs at the center where vibrant yellow and orange clash against muted purples, suggesting a burst of light amidst heavy darkness. The maze pattern signifies relief, surrounded by a crosshatch of dread, while broken pixels introduce imperfection and tension. The layout is an argument_grid that forces these emotions to contend within the square, leaving intentional negative space to enhance the sense of unease and release.
