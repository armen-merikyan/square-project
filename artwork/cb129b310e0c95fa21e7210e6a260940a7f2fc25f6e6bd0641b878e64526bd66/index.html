Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between loneliness and connection through a palette of muted sepia, tan, and faded blues, symbolizing nostalgia and emotional weight. The color placement emphasizes the contrast between dense clusters at the center and scattered, negative spaces, creating a visual echo of feelings. Patterns of flame interspersed with target-like shapes reflect the push and pull of intimacy, mirroring the emotional interplay from the seed.
