Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes restlessness against peace, with the palette featuring warm coral and soft amber representing calmness while cold slate and blue-black evoke tension. The contrast lies in the center where restless patterns (target) disrupt the calm maze, symbolizing the conflict between fleeting memories and present peace. Broken pixels are scattered, creating a sense of imperfect memories within a serene context.
