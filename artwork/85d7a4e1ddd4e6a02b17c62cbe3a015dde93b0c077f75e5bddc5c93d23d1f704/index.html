Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between joy and grief through a palette of warm yellows and oranges contrasting muted blues and purples. The pattern embodies both a wave of upward joy and a downward pull of grief, creating a dynamic flow from bottom to top. The jagged edges suggest interruption, while thin outlines create a sense of fragility amidst the density. The arrangement leaves significant negative space, enhancing the depth of feeling by emphasizing the quiet and chaotic interplay between emotion.
