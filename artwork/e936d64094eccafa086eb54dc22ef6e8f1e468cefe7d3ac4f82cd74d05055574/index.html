Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between trust and suspicion, expressed through a dynamic interplay of colors and patterns. The palette blends warm coral and soft amber to symbolize trust and warmth while juxtaposing cold slate and blue-black to evoke suspicion and chill. The 'ripple' and 'braid' patterns collide diagonally, enhancing the emotional push and pull of the composition. Broken pixels add intentional disruption, inviting viewers to explore the layers of meaning and challenge preconceptions, all while maintaining a narrative structure that resonates with the ideas of resilience and fragility.
