Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork captures a duality between confusion and clarity, reflecting the struggle for coherence amidst disorder. The palette of deep blue, muted purple, and vibrant yellow evokes feelings of potential calmness set against chaotic bursts symbolizing confusion. The placement of colors showcases thick outlines that define a central area while leaving edges fragmented and disjointed, illustrating a dance between fullness and hollowness. The presence of broken pixels adds a layer of intentional imperfection, enhancing the sense of fragmentation and movement.
