Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between desire and emptiness. The palette of vibrant 'hot_coral' and 'blood_orange' clashes with muted tones like 'charcoal' and 'hollow_blue,' emphasizing the yearning that feels trapped within a constrained space. The 'rising_signal' layout suggests upward aspiration. The diagonal collisions of the pulse and threshold patterns interrupt the flow, representing the chaos within our wants versus voids. Broken pixels inject a sense of imperfection, reinforcing the theme of unfulfilled desire.
