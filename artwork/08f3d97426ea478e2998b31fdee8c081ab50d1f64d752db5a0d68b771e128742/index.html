Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise). The palette of signal green (#3aff00), electric blue (#00ccff), hot magenta (#ff1f66), static gray (#a3a3a3), and dead black (#000000) creates a vibrant clash, while maintaining a dominant shadowy atmosphere. The primary contrast emerges in the center, where mirrored patterns of green pulse against static gray and magenta disruptions, illustrating the theme of clarity attempting to be heard amid overwhelming interference.
