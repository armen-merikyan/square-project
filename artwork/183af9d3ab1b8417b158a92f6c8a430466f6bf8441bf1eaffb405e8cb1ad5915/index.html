Artist Statement

The artwork conveys a duality of emotions—love and betrayal—through contrasting colors and spatial arrangement. The palette combines warm pinks and reds symbolizing love with cooler grays and dark maroon to represent betrayal. The centerpiece suggests a softness interspersed with sharp edges, echoing the push and pull of intimacy versus alienation. The collisions between the warm and cool tones create moments of tension, while the slight negative space allows for contemplation amidst the chaos, inviting viewers to linger on the intricacies of the emotional narrative.
