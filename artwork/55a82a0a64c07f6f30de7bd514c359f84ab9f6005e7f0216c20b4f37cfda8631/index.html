Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between awe and terror, embodied in the interplay of warm and cool tones. The palette, weighted with soft reds and dark maroons punctuated by gray, reflects love tainted by betrayal. The layout is mirrored yet imperfect, representing the chaotic relationship between order and chaos. The primary contrast arises from the dense clusters on the left, echoing the intensity of terror, while the right suggests calmness, forming an emotional wave. Broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection reflecting our fractured memories, enhancing the overall narrative.
