Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a tension between desire's vibrant pull and restraint's muted grip. Colors from the 'hunger_restraint' palette reflect this dynamic: deep red hues suggest fervent passion, while muted creams and blacks encapsulate the feelings of restraint. The composition has a rising flow that echoes hope yet suggests a contained pressure, with the vibrant elements clashing against the soft backgrounds, creating tension at the center. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, allowing for an imperfect narrative to emerge, representing both violated boundaries and suppressed urges.
