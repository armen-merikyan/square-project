Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a turbulent center contrasting with softer edges. The selected palette features warm coral and soft amber representing defiance, while the cool shades of slate and blue-black suggest submission. The dense center disrupts the serene flow towards the edges, where the 'ladder' pattern reflects a quiet retreat, accentuating the narrative of inward spirals and corner invasions. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, symbolizing scars and echoes of the interplay between strength and vulnerability.
