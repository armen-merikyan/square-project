Artist Statement

The emotional tone arises from the interplay of fear and hope, where dark edges create a boundary suggesting confinement, yet the lighter central pixels suggest an emerging possibility. The palette, balancing black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes a transition from weightiness to uplift. The main contrast occurs in the center with the soft gold and pale green, colliding against the intense dark hues. The zigzag and stripes add tension, mimicking rising and falling emotions as the viewer navigates through the pressure and hopeful glimmers, shaped by the seed's focus on a single pixel confronting vastness.
