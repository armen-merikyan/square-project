Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between clarity (signal) and distortion (noise). The palette, featuring deep navy and dim cyan, evokes a sense of isolation. The black provides a stark contrast against the vibrant hues, enhancing the struggle between order and chaos. The main collision is at the center, where disruptive textures of noise (represented by broken pixels) blend with the clearer pulse pattern. This creates a noticeable tension, inviting viewers to engage with the complexities of emotional signals amidst chaotic surroundings.
