Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional struggle between envy and gratitude, playing with the contrasts of dense color clusters versus open space. The palette of deep blacks, soft golds, and hints of pale green symbolizes the heaviness of envy against the brightness of gratitude. The structure suggests a rising tension on one side (mirroring hope) while the other side feels like it sunk down (representing fear), captured in the arrangement and density of pixels. This interplay creates a soft, almost haunting rhythm that encapsulates an inner turmoil without literal representation.
