Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between restlessness and peace, using a palette that contrasts vibrant electric blues and hot magentas with deep black and static gray to represent conflict and resolution. The main collision occurs at the center, where glitch patterns of electric blue and hot magenta interrupt the constellations of static gray, creating a visual dialogue of chaos versus calm. The influences of light versus dark manifest in the arrangement's placement, where quiet fields of gray provide a soothing contrast to the more active areas, injecting a sense of both memory and motion throughout the square.
