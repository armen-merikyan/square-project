Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between certainty and curiosity, using a palette of deep olive and honey gold to signify the clash between firm boundaries and unresolved paths. The use of broken pixels introduces a sense of imperfection, echoing the inner struggle between the desires for order and exploration. The center of the square holds a bright golden cluster, signifying curiosity, while darker hues surround, representing certainty. The flow of color from the edges inward creates a dynamic feel, where the tension between contrasting emotions is palpable.
