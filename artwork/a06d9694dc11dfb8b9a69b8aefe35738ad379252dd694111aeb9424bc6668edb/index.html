Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captures the dichotomy of restlessness and peace. Using a palette of deep blue and pale yellow against mourning black and stone creates a contrast that embodies both the calm of a quiet space and the unsettled feeling of restlessness. The collision occurs in the center, where sharp accents of light burst against darker tones, representing the tension between motion and stillness. The layout reflects a mirrored structure, enforcing symmetry with intentional disruptions that suggest memory and movement. The patterns evoke channels of peace interrupted by noise, portraying an abstract narrative without direct representation.
