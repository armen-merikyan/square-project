Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents a struggle between clear signals and overwhelming noise. The palette of deep navy and dim cyan evokes isolation but also highlights paths of clarity. The main collision occurs at the center, symbolizing the tension as signals attempt to emerge amidst static chaos. Patterns alternate between defined structures and random noise, creating a visual representation of the interplay between order and disruption.
