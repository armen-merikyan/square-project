Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of vulnerability and strength, where colors manifest the tension between solid foundations and delicate fragility. The palette, drawn from 'mourning_renewal', features deep blues representing strength and mourning, juxtaposed with pale yellows signifying vulnerability and lightness. The layout emphasizes an outward spiral of conflict, with the dominant dark hues at the edges contrasting against the lighter tones that peek from the center, creating a dynamic interplay. The broken pixels symbolize fractured strength, enhancing the dialogue between the robust ladder pattern and the fragile target, ultimately crafting an abstract narrative of emotional resilience.
