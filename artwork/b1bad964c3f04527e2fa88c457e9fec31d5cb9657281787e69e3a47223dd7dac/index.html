Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dichotomy between vulnerability and strength. The palette chosen reflects a blend of fear (black and dark violet) and hope (soft gold and pale green), suggesting the oscillation between dark complexity and light potential. The dominant colors create a dense base in the outer regions, echoing strength, while the pallid interior symbolizes vulnerability. The pulse of contrasting patterns emerges centrally as a collision, showing how these emotional forces interact, with interruptions representing hard edges meeting softer transitions, creating an overall sense of unresolved tension.
