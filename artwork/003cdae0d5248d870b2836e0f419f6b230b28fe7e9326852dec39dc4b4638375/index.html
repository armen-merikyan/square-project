Artist Statement

This abstract artwork embodies the tension between restlessness and peace. The palette features vibrant acid green and deep olive to symbolize the emotional clash, while cream and honey gold offer moments of calmness. The arrangement showcases a spiral pressure layout, with colors colliding at the center to evoke a sense of disarray transitioning to serenity. Broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, enhancing the feeling of imperfection and fluidity between contrasting emotions.
