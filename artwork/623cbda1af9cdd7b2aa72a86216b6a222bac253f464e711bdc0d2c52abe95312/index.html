Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dichotomy of love and betrayal. The interplay of warm tones from the palette—pink and red—evokes warmth and connection, while dark shades introduce an underpinning of anguish and conflict, representing the complexities of relationships. The contrasting patterns of circular targets against sharp angular disruptions create tension, suggesting how love's softness is limited by the presence of betrayal. The rising layout conveys an aspiration to rise above these conflicts, yet the broken pixels introduce a necessary imperfection and vulnerability, reflecting the theme of ancient emotional scars.
