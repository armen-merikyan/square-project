Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between relief and dread; warm colors represent the weight of dread while cooler shades symbolize the potential for relief. The palette reflects an 'ancient fire' with burnt orange and dark red clashing against charcoal and gold. This interaction creates a balance of pressure and light, representing a fractured symmetry and echoing the idea of an empty room filled with unresolved tension. The broken pixel placements add an imperfect touch, enhancing the overall conflict and suggesting a deep emotional narrative without literal representation.
