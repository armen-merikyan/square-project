Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between longing and resolve, with dense clusters contrasting quiet spaces. The warm browns evoke intimacy while cold whites introduce distance. The collision of the lattice and fault_line patterns creates an interplay between structure and chaos, where a focal point emerges amid swirling pressures, representing the complex relationship between aspiration and grounding.
