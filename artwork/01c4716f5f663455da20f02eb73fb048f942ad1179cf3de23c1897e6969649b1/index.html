Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes desire, represented by warm, saturated colors, against the void of emptiness via cooler, muted tones. The palette of hot coral and blood orange signifies urgency and a yearning pull, while charcoal and hollow blue evoke a sense of hollowness and distance. The collision occurs predominantly at the center, where warmth disrupts a quiet field, hinting at a restrained expansion against a dark backdrop. The layout embodies an 'open gate', allowing desire to emerge while still presenting the borders of containment.
