Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between nostalgia and progress, reflected in the choice of deep navy and contrasting bright accents. The palette suggests solitude and dialogue with change. The main contrast occurs at the horizontal split, creating a collision between tranquility above and vibrancy below, embodying tension and release while reflecting on the past versus pushing forward. The broken pixel choices interrupt the flow, enhancing the sense of imperfection in the transition.
