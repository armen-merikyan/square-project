Artist Statement

This composition conveys the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve through a delicate balance of dense and quiet pixels. The palette, comprising bold signal green, electric blue, and hot magenta, reflects a spectrum from urgency to serenity. The primary movement of the piece flows from the central cluster of saturated colors, suggesting a peak of tension, to the surrounding quieter spaces that embody resolution. The impact of broken pixels throughout symbolizes the scars of experiences, while the overlapping patterns of 'scar' and 'noise' create a vivid juxtaposition of conflicting emotions. The main collision occurs in the center, where rich colors entwine, embodying the dynamic interplay of hope and despair.
