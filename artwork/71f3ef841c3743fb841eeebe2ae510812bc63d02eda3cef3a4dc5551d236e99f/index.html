Artist Statement

The composition embodies the tension between restlessness and peace through an interplay of muted and vibrant colors. The palette of soft cyan, muted plum, and lavender gray evokes a sense of melancholy while the deeper hues introduce unease. The main collision occurs diagonally, where radial patterns of unrest intertwine with barcodes of serenity, suggesting movement against a textured background of memory. The shape seed of 'bridge' is represented abstractly through the diagonal flow, suggesting connections and transitions between moods.
