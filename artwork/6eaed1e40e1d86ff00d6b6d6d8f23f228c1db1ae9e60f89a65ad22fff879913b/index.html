Artist Statement

The artwork captures the conflict between restlessness and peace, represented by the chaotic stripes of dark colors meeting the softer, pale mint and gold hues. The layout emphasizes a dense central island against a wider calming field, echoing the idea of a restless heart in search of peace. The color palette uses soft greens for serenity and deeper grays for tension, while broken pixels introduce imperfection, suggesting a battle between tranquility and disorder.
