Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a tension between longing and resolve, expressed through a palette of deep reds and grays that evoke both warmth and detachment. Main contrasts emerge where intense hues collide with subdued shades, creating dense clusters interrupted by empty spaces, reflecting the push-pull of emotions. The pattern evolves from a central diagonal density, symbolizing the idea of a 'ladder against a fault line,' representing both aspiration and instability.
