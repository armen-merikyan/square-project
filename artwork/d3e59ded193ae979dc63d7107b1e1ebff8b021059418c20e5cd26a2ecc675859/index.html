Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, with a palette chosen to evoke feelings of melancholy wonder. The contrast manifests primarily at the center, where pale hues of soft cyan and moon white collide with the deeper, muted plum and rain blue, suggesting an exposed core surrounded by protective gray tones. This interplay of colors illustrates the struggle between collapsing under pressure and grounding oneself with strength, embodied by the layout that emphasizes overfilled corners meeting smooth, retreating edges.
