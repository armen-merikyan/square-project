Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional struggle between fear and hope. The jagged edges and dark corners in the outer layer evoke a sense of unease and tension, while the soft gold and pale green hints at warmth and uplift within the center. The layout emphasizes a contrast with a heavy outer border featuring broken dots, suggesting chaos, while the center glows with hope portrayed through upward trajectories. The zigzag flow from dark to light creates a dynamic that reflects rising signals of positivity emerging from underlying anxiety. The color palette balances dark violets with soft tones, enhancing the deep emotional complexity of the piece.
