Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between joy and grief, captured through contrasting warm and cool colors. The palette of yellow and orange signifies joy, while deep blue and muted purple embody grief, creating a stark visual collision down the center of the grid. The layout suggests upward movement on one side and downward flow on the other, reflecting the themes of order vs chaos and safe vs threatened. The defined edges and a few broken pixels imply imperfection in the emotional landscape, offering a sense of tension that flows diagonally, echoing life's dualities. The moments of negative space introduce a breathing room amid the density, contrasting the emotions effectively, allowing a rhythmic interplay across the artwork.
