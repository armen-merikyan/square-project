Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the battle between longing and resolve, enhanced by a palette of sepia and faded blue, which evokes nostalgia yet stability. The subtle contrast between dense clusters of dark colors and intersections of light shades creates a visual tension, suggesting a pulse that runs throughout. The collision primarily occurs towards the center where the colors clash, symbolizing the struggle between repair and damage. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, reminiscent of fragmented memories, while the quiet channel layout adds an unsettling yet serene flow that echoes the themes of containment and overflow.
