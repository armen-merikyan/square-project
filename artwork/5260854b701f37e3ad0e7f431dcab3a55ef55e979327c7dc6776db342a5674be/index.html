Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between desire and restraint, with deep saturation suggesting yearning, while muted tones symbolize the constraints faced. The interplay between bold and subtle colors creates a visual tension that resonates with the spiral of pressure suggested by the seed. The central patchwork of warm colors contrasts with quiet fields, creating a layered rhythm that captures the complex emotions of the theme.
