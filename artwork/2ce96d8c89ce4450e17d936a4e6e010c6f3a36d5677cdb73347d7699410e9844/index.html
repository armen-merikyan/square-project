Artist Statement

This composition emphasizes the emotional clash between strength and vulnerability using a palette that symbolizes growth (ripe green) and unease (acid green). The dense center represents strength, while the lighter colors create an exposed feeling, highlighting the tension of hard edges against the softness of shelter. The interrupted grid pattern combines with a flowing thread pattern, suggesting a painful interweaving of experiences. The main collision occurs in the center, where dark greens collide with brighter acid tones, visually expressing the struggle between sheltering strength and the exposure of vulnerability.
