Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional duality of vulnerability and strength through a strategically chosen color palette that juxtaposes muted tones with bursts of warmth. The combination of deep wine and soft gold represents strength, while cool ash gray and warm ivory exhibit vulnerability. The layout captures two pulses that interact at the center, with broken pixels signifying imperfections—showcasing the interplay of protection (strength) against the threat of exposure (vulnerability). The zigzag flow enhances the dynamic tension between these contrasts.
