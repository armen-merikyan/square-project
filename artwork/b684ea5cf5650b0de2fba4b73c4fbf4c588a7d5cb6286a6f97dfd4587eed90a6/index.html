Artist Statement

This composition evokes a dance between reassurance and doubt. The palette, drawn from 'melancholy_wonder', features soft hues of lavender gray and muted plum clashing against deeper rain blue for a gentle yet striking contrast. The central area represents the 'trust' theme with a maze pattern, quietly surrounded by 'suspicion' channels that push against the edges, reflecting the pressure struggle inherent in relationships. The placement of the darker tones concentrated toward the borders creates a sense of confinement, while lighter elements in the center suggest an opening, embodying resilience amidst fragility. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, adding complexity to this emotional exploration.
