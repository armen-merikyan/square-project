Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through a juxtaposition of colors and pattern types. The pinks and reds suggest underlying warmth and passion, while the gray and dark maroon introduce a sense of ambiguity and fragmentation. The layout forces a standoff between broken dots and cracks, symbolizing the tension between harmony and disruption. The strong contrasts create visual friction and echoes of uncertainty within an otherwise structured space.
