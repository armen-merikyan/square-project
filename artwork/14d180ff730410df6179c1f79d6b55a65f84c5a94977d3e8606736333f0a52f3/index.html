Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic between quietude and pressure, where dense pixel clusters represent internal tension while softer hues embody calmness. The palette, with off-white, dark navy, and the faint peach, creates a contrast of warmth against the colder tones, suggesting conflict within balance. The main collision occurs at the center, where the intense compressed red meets the surrounding soothing colors, creating an argument between chaos and serenity. The shape seed metaphorically influences the central arrangement, hinting at the organization amidst emotional intersections, echoing a subtle crown-like symmetry amidst the disruption.
