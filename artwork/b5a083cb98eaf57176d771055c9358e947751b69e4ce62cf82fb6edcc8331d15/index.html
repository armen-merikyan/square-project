Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the juxtaposition of dread and relief through a palette featuring cooler storm gray and near black for tension, contrasted by softer tones like pale mint and faded gold to signify release. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense, broken dots of gray meet swirling echoes of pale mint, creating a dynamic interplay between heaviness and lightness. The layout mirrors itself with intentional disruptions, reinforcing chaos while allowing space for calmness, truly reflecting the duality expressed in the seed.
