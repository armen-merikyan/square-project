Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, characterized by a central exposed area surrounded by a denser frame, representing shelter. The palette of ripe green and acid green suggests fresh growth amidst the harsh aspects of honey gold and deep olive, illustrating the emotional clash. The checker pattern for strength contrasts with the maze of vulnerability, creating visual disruptions. The primary collision occurs centrally, where soft and clear colors interact, embodying the emotional struggle of exposure against the need for safety.
