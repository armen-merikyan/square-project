Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between nostalgia and progress through a warm color palette that reflects ancient fire, hinting at memory and growth. The contrast between deeply saturated colors and lighter shades creates a dance of calm versus rage, enhancing the emotional pulse. The layout reflects a falling pixels motif, accentuating the sense of descent, with a bottom-weighted arrangement suggesting heaviness in memories versus the lightness of progress. The broken pixels introduce an intentional imperfection, echoing the nature of our experiences with nostalgia and the fractured beauty of progress.
