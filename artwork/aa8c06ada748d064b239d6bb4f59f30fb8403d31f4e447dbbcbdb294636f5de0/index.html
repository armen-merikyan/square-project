Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between brightness (spark) and subdued remnants (ash), creating depth in the interplay of colors. The palette combines warm reds and muted creams with dark undertones to evoke the contrast: the vibrant accents represent sudden bursts of vitality, while the ashy tones create a grounded weight. The central collision happens diagonally, where the bright elements attempt to disrupt the quiet density, symbolizing resilience against erasure. This arrangement highlights the struggle between memory and fading, embodying the essence of the provided seed.
