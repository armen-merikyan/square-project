Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between defiance and submission, captured through a contrast of dense color blocks and softer edges. The palette combines muted shades with an occasional bright accent to symbolize tension and energy. The main collision occurs at the center with dark hues suggesting resilience while soft colors radiate outward, representing submission or a retreat. The chosen patterns echo the themes in the seed, with a central 'hole' symbolizing vulnerability despite the energetic flow of colors around it, leading to a dynamic but unresolved narrative.
