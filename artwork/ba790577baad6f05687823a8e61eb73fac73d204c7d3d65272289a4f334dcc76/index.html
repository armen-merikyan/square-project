Artist Statement

The emotional tone depicts a juxtaposition between longing and resolve, reflected in the choice of a warm palette that represents desire, contrasted with cool hues suggesting clarity and stability. The flow of colors moves diagonally, illustrating the tension of resolution being sought amidst chaotic feelings. The use of dots to represent longing creates a sense of scattered hopes amidst the steady, rain-like appearance of resolve, leading to a collision in the center, symbolizing the heart of emotional conflict. Broken pixels are used to imply interruptions, highlighting the imperfections in the pursuit of emotional clarity, providing a visual narrative that resonates with the experiences of longing and resolution.
