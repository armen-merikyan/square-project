Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a blend of longing and resolution, where the palette reflects warmth and severity, creating a dialogue between softness and strength. The main collision occurs in the center with sharp crimson interrupting the powder pink and warm white patterns, symbolizing the push and pull of desire against decisive action. The lattice and tide patterns overlap, manifesting the contrast between structured resolve and flowing longing, while the quiet channel layout introduces negative space that invites introspection and distance.
