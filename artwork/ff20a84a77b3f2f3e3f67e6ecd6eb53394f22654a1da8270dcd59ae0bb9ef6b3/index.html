Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette, featuring deep blue and muted purple, evokes feelings of grief and solitude, while hints of yellow and orange suggest warmth and potential connection. The central area echoes loneliness through darker hues, surrounded by a 'burst' of lighter, more vibrant colors, creating a visual representation of connection trying to penetrate the loneliness. The grid structure juxtaposes organized elements against the chaotic burst, illustrating the contrast of being hidden vs exposed. Broken pixels add imperfections, emphasizing vulnerability and the reality of emotional fractures.
