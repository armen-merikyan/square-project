Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic interplay between affection and betrayal. The chosen palette of soft pinks contrasts sharply with the dark maroon and gray suggesting the complexities of love. The arrangement embodies symmetry disrupted by clusters of darker colors, reflecting emotional turmoil and the struggle between harmony and discord. The pattern flow is a zigzag, evoking the push and pull of feelings, while the presence of negative space invites contemplation.
