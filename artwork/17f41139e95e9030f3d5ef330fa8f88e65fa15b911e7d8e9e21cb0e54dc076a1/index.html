Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the clash between joy and grief, structured through a nearly symmetrical layout with broken elements to suggest imperfection. The color palette combines bright yellows and oranges representing joy with deep blues and muted purples that evoke grief, creating a stark contrast that emerges from left to right. The grid of blues suggests a sense of order disrupted by vibrant bursts of yellow, reflecting the pulse of emotions as they collide. This arrangement tells a story of fluctuating feelings, highlighting the beauty and pain of life's contrasts.
