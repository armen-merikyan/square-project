Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress. The color palette features deep blacks and bright whites, symbolizing the stark contrasts in our memories versus aspirations. The upper rows are filled with gradients of pale blue and white signifying calmness and nostalgia, while the lower rows utilize deeper blacks and reds, representing the weight and fervor of ambition. The split layout emphasizes the horizontal divide between past and present, and the presence of broken pixels adds a layer of imperfection, echoing the resonance of memories. The overall flow from light to dark conveys a transition from calm to intensity, where the collision of colors suggests unresolved feelings between our rooted past and dynamic future.
