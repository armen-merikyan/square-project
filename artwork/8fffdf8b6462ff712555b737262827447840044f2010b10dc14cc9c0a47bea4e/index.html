Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between tenderness and rage, symbolized through contrasting color patterns and placements. The palette of red captures the essence of rage, while the pale blue and white hues introduce a sense of calm and purity, creating a push and pull within the composition. The layout, resembling a broken frame, establishes boundaries that suggest an overflow of feelings where tenderness leaks through the intense red spaces. The arrangement creates an interruption at the center, with dark hues colliding with lighter, softer shades, embodying the struggle between overwhelming emotions and the desire for peace.
