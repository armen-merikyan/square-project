Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through color contrast and placement. The palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple reflects warmth and grief, presenting a discourse of joy intertwined with sorrow. The arrangement features a dense burst of colors in the center to symbolize a concealed seed of connection fighting against the void of loneliness represented by the darker edges. Sharp contrasts highlight the struggle for emotional expression, inviting the viewer to feel the push and pull of these contrasting states.
