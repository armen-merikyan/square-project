Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects a delicate balance between vulnerability and strength, represented through contrasting pixel arrangements. The chosen palette includes warm coral and soft amber to convey vulnerability, while cold slate and blue-black embody strength, creating a visual tension. The main collision is placed diagonally where bright accents of signal cyan burst against the softer background, suggesting an influx of energy. The patterns of 'ash_field' symbolize the resilience of strength, while 'fault_line' indicates the fractures of vulnerability. Through asymmetry and intentional disruptions, the square encapsulates a narrative of conflict and connection.
