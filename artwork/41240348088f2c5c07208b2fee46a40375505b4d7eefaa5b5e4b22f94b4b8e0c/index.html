Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts longing and resolve, creating a dynamic tension. The chosen palette 'broken_memory' evokes nostalgia through soft sepia and faded blue; these hues suggest a connection to the past while remaining subdued. The layout incorporates a narrow negative-space path, embodying the feeling of a longing gap, while the clustered patterns of the 'target' and 'ripple' evoke both density and resolution. The collision occurs at the center where colors interweave, emphasizing the intersection of these emotions, with broken pixels representing disruptions in continuity, enhancing the abstract narrative of repair amidst damage.
