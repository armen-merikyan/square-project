Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional struggle between longing and resolve. The palette, comprising rain blue and soft cyan, introduces a melancholic yet soothing atmosphere, juxtaposed by accents of muted plum and moon white, representing the tension and clarity of resolve. The layout spirals toward a focal point, creating a sense of movement that reflects the internal conflict. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, suggesting the imperfections in our journey between hope and determination. The interplay of dense clusters against quiet spaces captures the push and pull of intimacy and distance.
