Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between defiance and submission, signified by a juxtaposition of sharp ladder patterns symbolizing resistance and soft broken dots representing retreat. The palette, 'melancholy_wonder', merges subdued tones with brighter accents, creating a sense of tension and resolution. The main collision occurs in the center, where layers of color converge with broken pixels offering an incomplete repair, evoking feelings of vulnerability amidst strength. This reflects the inward spiral suggested by the seed while maintaining an abstract essence.
