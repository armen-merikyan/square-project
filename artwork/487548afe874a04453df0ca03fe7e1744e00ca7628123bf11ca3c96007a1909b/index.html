Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the warmth of love against the coldness of betrayal, illustrated through a split horizontal layout. The palette incorporates soft pinks and reds for love, contrasted against darker grays and maroons representing betrayal. The top half uses stripes to evoke a sense of connection, while the bottom half features cracked patterns that symbolize disruption. The main contrast occurs at the center, where the warmth of love touches the edges of betrayal, creating tension in the form of broken pixels. This pattern reflects a dynamic emotional narrative where love seeks to fill the space, only to be met with the jagged interruptions of betrayal, embodying the complexities of relationships.
