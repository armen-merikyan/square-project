Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve through an interplay of soft and severe colors. The palette of soft pink, warm white, and sharp crimson contrasts with deeper hues of black and gray, suggesting the push and pull of desire and commitment. The layout features stacked bands, symbolizing the layers of emotion. The intersection of stark blacks with lighter tones creates a significant collision in the center, hinting at the chaos within the quest for order and stability as suggested by the seed influences.
