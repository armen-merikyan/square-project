Artist Statement

This artwork contrasts the emotional tones of pressure and quiet through a dynamic interplay of color. The palette features muted shades such as 'dust_gray' and 'off_white' that represent the quiet moments, while 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy' signify the heaviness of pressure. The dense clusters on one edge create a feeling of compression, while the negative space contributes to a sense of calm. The collision occurs diagonally, where the heavier hues meet the lighter ones, symbolizing the struggle between pressure and calmness. Broken pixels introduce disruptions, suggesting moments of chaos amid the orderly shift from dense to airy spaces.
