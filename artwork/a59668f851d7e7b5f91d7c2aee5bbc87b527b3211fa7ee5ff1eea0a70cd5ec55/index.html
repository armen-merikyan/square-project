Artist Statement

The composition navigates the emotional intensity between rage and calm, using the palette of red, black, pale blue, and white. The sharp reds and blacks embody rage, while the pale blue and white introduce calmness. A central collision of warm spark-like patterns against trailing cooler islands creates rhythmic tension. Broken pixels are interspersed to symbolize disruption in harmony. Overall, the layout communicates a story of conflict and resolution, capturing a dynamic emotional landscape.
