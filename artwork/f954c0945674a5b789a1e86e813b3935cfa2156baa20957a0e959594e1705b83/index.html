Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between joy and grief, utilizing a palette of warm yellows and oranges to represent joy, contrasted against deep blues and muted purples symbolizing grief. The central focus creates an echo of conflict, as joy bursts forth from the corners while grief encroaches, forming a zigzag pattern that vibrates with life yet hints at something heavier. This interplay of color and pattern visually narrates a story of contrasts, where laughter inches forward against looming shadows. The edges are distorted, enhancing the feeling of imperfection, resonating with the seed themes of chaos and structure.
