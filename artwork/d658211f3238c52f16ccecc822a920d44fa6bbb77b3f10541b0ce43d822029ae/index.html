Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between emptiness and desire, using a palette of muted tones that embody 'broken memory.' The contrast lies in the jagged edges of darkness against the warmth of emerging light, echoing the narrative of a central longing disturbed by surrounding desolation. The flow radiates outward, evoking a heartbeat that disturbs the stillness, while broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the emotional weight of both warmth and coolness in the arrangement.
