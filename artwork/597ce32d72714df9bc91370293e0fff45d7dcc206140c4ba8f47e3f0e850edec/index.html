Artist Statement

This composition balances vulnerability and strength through a stark palette of cool and warm colors. The central rich density evokes strength, while pale, lighter edges suggest exposure and fragility. The collision happens prominently in the center—where strong, vivid colors like hot magenta and electric blue clash with hints of static gray and pale greens, symbolizing tension. The chosen palette embodies the emotional contrast between safety and exposure, while the arrangement employs a scattered flow to reflect the unpredictable nature of vulnerability.
