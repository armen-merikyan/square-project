Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the dichotomy of softness and severity through a strategic use of colors and patterns. The top rows embody softness with a palette of powder pink and warm white, expressed through a 'teeth' pattern that feels gentle and inviting. Contrasting this, the bottom rows illustrate severity with sharp crimson and carbon black, implemented in a dot pattern that introduces a feeling of pressure and disruption. The asymmetrical layout emphasizes the collision of these emotional tones, with a heavy concentration of darkness in the lower rows balancing the lighter hues above. This duality reflects the seed's thematic tension and narrative of emotional struggle.
