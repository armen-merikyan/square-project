Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional turmoil of longing juxtaposed with the firm resolve through color and pattern. The palette mixes soft warm clays and cool blues, creating a sense of distance. The grid pattern symbolizes longing, while the ember-like scatter represents an assertive resolve. Main contrast happens centrally, creating a collision between the quiet spread of distant lilacs and the intense heat of warm shadows, symbolic of an unfinished journey towards connection. The layout reflects mirroring but introduces intentional breaks, suggesting that even within order, chaos subtly influences. Broken pixels serve to disrupt the perceived harmony, hinting at imperfections in our desires and efforts.
