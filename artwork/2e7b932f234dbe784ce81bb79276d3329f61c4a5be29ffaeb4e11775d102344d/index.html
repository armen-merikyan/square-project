Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the push and pull between 'longing' and 'resolve', manifesting as a contrast between warm muted tones and sharp edges. The selected palette includes warm, earthy reds combined with deep blacks and grays to embody a rich tension of warmth against desolation. The collision occurs with bold reds and the quiet of muted creams, suggesting memories amidst a fractured calm. The patterns of lattice and mirror intertwine, representing a repaired thread facing the tension of the broken. The layout is a boundary, a seam where two emotional states meet, creating a ripple effect through the grid, embodying the unresolved nature of the emotions.
