Artist Statement

The piece contrasts joy and grief, using a palette of bright yellow and orange to represent joy against deep blue and muted purple for grief. The sharp contrasts and wavy line patterns evoke a sense of collision, while the asymmetry introduces an element of unpredictability, emphasizing the emotional tug-of-war inherent in the seed's themes. The layout plays on symmetry, where slight breaks evoke the fragmentation of emotions. Negative space suggests quiet introspection amidst dense clusters of colors, creating an interplay between calm and chaos.
