Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the tension between longing and resolve, utilizing a palette that juxtaposes warm oranges and yellows against deep blues and muted purples, symbolizing hope against sorrow. The pattern captures this clash through a diagonal flow that guides the eye across the grid, creating a sense of cross-currents as the warm colors rise amidst cooler tones. The collision occurs towards the center, where dense color clusters disrupt the calm negatives, echoing the emotional turmoil of the seed. This artwork reflects the complex dynamics of intimacy and distance inherent in the thematic elements.
