Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between desire, represented by warm, saturated colors, and the emptiness conveyed through muted, low-saturation areas. The palette of hot coral, blood orange, dust pink, charcoal, and hollow blue illustrates this dichotomy. The collision occurs in the center with a dynamic spiral pattern that juxtaposes bold stacking against a backdrop of negative cross, invoking a sense of pressure. The seed's abstract quality is reflected in the spiral layout and the interplay of light and dark, echoing the balance of longing and constraint.
