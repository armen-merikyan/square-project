Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the artwork embodies the duality of fear and hope, utilizing a palette that alternates between dark, grounding colors and soft, illuminating accents. The main contrast lies in the dense black sections that symbolize fear, juxtaposed with small patches of soft gold and pale green that represent hope. The zigzag layout creates an up-and-down rhythm, suggesting the rise and fall of tension, while the broken pixels serve as reminders of imperfection amidst clarity. This chaotic arrangement reflects the inner conflict of 'inside vs outside', capturing the essence of struggle towards hope within darkness.
