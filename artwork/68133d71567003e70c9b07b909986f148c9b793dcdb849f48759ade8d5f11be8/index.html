Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tone of contrasting fear and hope. The palette combines darkness (black, dark violet) representing fear with gentle hope (soft gold, pale green), creating a tension between the two. The left side is dense with darker colors suggesting pressure, while the right evokes light and softness indicating release. The arrangement features a burst effect, where brighter colors intermittently disrupt the flow of darker blocks, suggesting moments of hope amidst layers of fear. This reflects the seed's themes of collision and release within personal emotion, forming a coherent abstract pattern.
