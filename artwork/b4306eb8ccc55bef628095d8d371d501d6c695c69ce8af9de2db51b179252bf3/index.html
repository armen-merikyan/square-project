Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a juxtaposition of bright electric blue against subdued black and deep magenta, creating an echo of conflict. The arrangement suggests structured chaos, with pixels falling erratically while maintaining a central focus point, portraying a battle of order versus chaos. The uneven border signifies vulnerability against an enclosing field, and the intentional use of broken pixels adds an organic touch to the otherwise sharp contrasts, embodying the dynamic narrative suggested by the seed.
