Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the artwork captures a struggle between clear, readable signals against the backdrop of chaotic noise. The palette draws from deep navy and black to evoke a sense of loneliness, while dim cyan offers a glimmer of hope or clarity. The primary contrast occurs diagonally; one side embodies the clean pulse of signal with structured patterns, while the opposite is rife with the interferences of noise, represented by chaotic, broken patterns. This captures the essence of the seed, translating its themes into a dynamic visual narrative.
