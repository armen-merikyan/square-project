Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between vulnerability and protection, embodying the push and pull of safety versus exposure. The chosen palette of warm brown and soft green represents safety, while the pale skin and open blue reflect exposure. The primary collision occurs in the center where the colors intertwine, creating a dynamic tension between the layers, symbolizing the thin boundaries between comfort and risk. The layout presents a dim outer area with a vibrant cluster at the center, encapsulating a moment of light amid surrounding shadows, representing hope against fear.
