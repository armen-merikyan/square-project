Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, illustrated through a careful balance of pale and muted colors contrasted with deeper tones. The palette consists of soft hues that evoke melancholy yet offer moments of stability. The main contrast occurs in the central area, where a lighter pattern interrupts denser rows, echoing the feeling of exposure amidst protective layers. The broken pixels create an intentional disruption, reflecting the fragility in strength as we navigate safety and openness.
