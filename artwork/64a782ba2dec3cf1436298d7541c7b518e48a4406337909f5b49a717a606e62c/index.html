Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the balance between restlessness and peace, expressed through the contrasting warm and cool palette. The layout emphasizes a core of tranquility surrounded by chaotic edges. Central complementarity is defined through soft shades of faint peach and off-white colliding with the dark navy and compressed red at the corners. The arrangement conveys a narrative of stability challenged by encroaching tension, mirroring the diagonal argument of the seed, highlighting memory intersecting with motion. Broken pixels disrupt the peace, representing uneasy echoes.
