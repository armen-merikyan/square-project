Artist Statement

This artwork embodies an emotional dichotomy between defiance and submission, expressed through a palette of deep blues, subtle greens, and contrasting shadows of black and pale yellow. The darker colors invoke a sense of weight and resistance, while the lighter shades offer a glimpse of hope and renewal. The main contrast occurs in the center, where the densest blocks of noise meet softer, more open spaces, reflecting the struggle between chaos and order. This arrangement represents the ebb and flow of confrontational energy—creating a visual echo that resonates with the seeds of conflict and resolution.
