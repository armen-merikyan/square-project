Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between restlessness and peace through dynamically contrasting colors. The palette features vibrant reds and soft grays, suggesting a struggle between intense feeling and muted calm. The main collision occurs in the center where warm colors reflect a flame-like energy, colliding against cooler, restrained tones that emphasize the borders of the piece. Patterns alternate between angular disruptions of restlessness, leading to an irregular, dynamic grid, ultimately creating a visual memory of tension and resolution, fenced by the edges of this square.
