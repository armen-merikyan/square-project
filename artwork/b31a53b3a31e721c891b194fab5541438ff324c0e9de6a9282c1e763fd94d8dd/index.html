Artist Statement

The emotional tone expresses the tension between longing and resolve, achieved through a contrast of warm and muted colors. The palette, drawn from 'joy_grief', integrates vibrant yellows and oranges to evoke warmth alongside deep blues and muted purples that reflect sadness or introspection. The main collision occurs centrally, where patchwork-like longing meets the stronger, veiled resolve, creating an interplay that mirrors both hope and imperfection—symbolic of the brokenness represented by the 'fault' shape seed.
