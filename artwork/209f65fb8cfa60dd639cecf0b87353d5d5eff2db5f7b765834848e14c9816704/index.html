Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between emptiness and desire through contrasting warm and cool tones. The palette includes vibrant yellows and oranges symbolizing desire, while deep blues and muted purples represent emptiness. The arrangement features dense clusters of stripes framing a quieter center, evoking a sense of push and pull. A collision occurs at the edges, where the warmer colors interrupt the cooler backgrounds, suggesting the struggle of desire against the backdrop of loneliness. The broken pixels create a sense of imperfection, enhancing the theme of fragmented aspirations. Overall, the layout flows from top to bottom, emphasizing a rise and fall dynamic within the pixel composition.
