Artist Statement

The emotional tone radiates a duality of trust and suspicion, captured in a dynamic tension between light and shadow. The palette, drawn from 'relief_dread', features soft mint and faded gold against stormy gray and near-black, symbolizing resilience amidst fragility. The layout illustrates a hollow center surrounded by an energetic aura of radial patterns representing suspicion, while a constellation pattern hints at trust in the periphery. The main collision occurs in the center where broken pixels interrupt the flow, echoing the unpredictable nature of human emotions.
