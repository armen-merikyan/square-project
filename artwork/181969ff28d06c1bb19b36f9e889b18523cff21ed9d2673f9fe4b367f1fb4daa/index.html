Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between safety and exposure, showcasing a pale core that contrasts against a darker, enclosing frame. The palette features soft greens and warm browns representing shelter, juxtaposed with vibrant blues symbolizing exposure. The echo of colors creates a pulsating rhythm, where the dominant dark surround intersects the lighter center, suggesting a push and pull between vulnerability and protection. The arrangement of colors follows a dense clustering that fades outward, maintaining negative space to enhance emotional depth, resonating with the core themes of pressure and release.
