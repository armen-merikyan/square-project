Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the duality of envy and gratitude, using a palette of muted reds and grays to evoke tension. The zigzag pattern symbolizes the clash between rising aspirations and descending feelings, embodying the struggle inherent in the emotions. The heavier color presence at the center contrasts with softer edges, illustrating the density of feelings against the lighter themes around. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, aligning with the concept of a mirror broken, giving the piece an unpredictable rhythm that captures the essence of turmoil and calm in flux.
