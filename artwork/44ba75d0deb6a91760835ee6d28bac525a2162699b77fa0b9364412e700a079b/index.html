Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition reflects the tension between control and surrender, embodying the struggle of structured order overshadowed by chaotic flow. The selected palette of black, electric blue, magenta, and lime creates a vivid contrast; black represents control and structure, while the brighter colors signify the freedom and unpredictability of surrender. The main collision occurs in the center, where intense colors disrupt a more subdued background, symbolizing the overwhelming pressure versus the quiet release of letting go. This setup encourages the viewer to consider the dance between rigidity and fluidity, resonating with the seed's themes. Broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, underscoring the idea that control can often be fractured. Ultimately, this abstract piece instills a sense of balance disrupted, a visual dialogue of opposing forces in a confined space.
