Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends relief and dread, symbolizing the struggle between intensity and quietness. The chosen palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon creates vibrancy and weight, reinforcing the emotional collisions. The strong contrast between bright and dark colors emphasizes the tension in the artwork. The pattern exhibits a mirror arrangement with deliberate imperfections, representing unresolved emotional collisions in rhythms and pressures. Patterns of dots signify relief, while areas of darker hues exhibit a sense of dread, creating an engaging dialogue within the composition.
