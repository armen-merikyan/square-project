Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the tension between warmth of trust and the cold shadows of suspicion. The selected palette of rain blue, lavender gray, moon white, soft cyan, and muted plum creates a serene yet contemplative atmosphere. Main contrast occurs at the center, where a gentle glow suggests trust meeting sharp edges of suspicion represented by darker colors. The pattern embodies floods of trust layering against shards of suspicion, with intentional disruptions to suggest the fragility of balance in emotional states, visualizing the concept of a hollow center surrounded by mixed feelings.
