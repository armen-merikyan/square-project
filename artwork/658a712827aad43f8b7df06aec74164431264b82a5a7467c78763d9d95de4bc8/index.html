Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the collision between joy and grief, represented through the contrasting palette of bright yellow and muted purple. The left side uses a structured grid pattern symbolizing joy, while the right side employs a jagged 'teeth' pattern reflecting grief. This clash creates tension in the composition, showcasing how these emotions coexist and interact. The layout splits vertically, emphasizing the contrast between the two emotions, while broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, mirroring the rawness of these feelings.
