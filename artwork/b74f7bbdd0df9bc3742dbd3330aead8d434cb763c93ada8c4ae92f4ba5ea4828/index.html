Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional journey between longing and resolve. The muted tones of dead_black, static_gray, and signal_green create a backdrop that embodies unresolved feelings, while hot_magenta and electric_blue punctuate the arrangement, symbolizing bursts of urgency and clarity. The dense center is layered with pulse_marks, representing longing through a rhythmic pattern, while the fault_line pattern at the borders signifies resolve, contrasting with the inner density. An intentional break in some pixels adds to the visual narrative of disruption and repair, emphasizing the balance between the two emotional states. The arrangement suggests a struggle contained within a defined area, merging chaos and order without literal representation.
