Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a delicate interplay between peace and restlessness, where warmer accents push against cooler fields. The palette includes bright honey gold for tension and deep olive for calm, intersecting to denote conflict. The main collision occurs near the center, where vibrant colors clash, creating a sense of movement, while the spiraled layout emphasizes trajectory amidst chaos. Broken pixels disrupt the pattern, embodying the seed's theme of transformation and tension.
