Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between certainty and curiosity, visualized through ordered and chaotic pixel arrangements. The selected palette of warm coral, soft amber, cold slate, blue-black, and signal cyan reflects this duality. Main collision occurs in the center of the grid where vibrant signals clash with muted tones, symbolizing curiosity turbulent against static certainty. The belief in structure is juxtaposed with whimsical exploration, representing the ebb and flow of emotions, much like the tide against its channels.
