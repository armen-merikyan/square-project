Artist Statement

This 8x8 composition embodies the emotional tug-of-war between longing and resolve. Colors oscillate between deep, muted intensities and lighter, warmer notes, fostering a dialogue of depth and open space. The juxtaposition of the intense blackened plum against the soft muted cream serves as a visual metaphor for yearning versus determination. The layout creates a dense central 'island' of chaos amidst a quieter, expansive field, replicating the feeling of seeking connection within the vastness of emotional distances. The collision, notably in the center, anchors the eye, channeling the interplay of order and chaos, subtly introducing broken pixels to represent imperfections in that journey. This composition captures the tension and release of desire, creating a narrative of intimacy and distance.
