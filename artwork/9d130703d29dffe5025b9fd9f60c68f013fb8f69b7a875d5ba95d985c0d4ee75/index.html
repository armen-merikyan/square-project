Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends joy (represented by bright colors) and grief (indicated by muted tones). The palette chosen features vibrant yellows and oranges against deeper blues and muted purples, symbolizing the clash between light and shadow. The left side encapsulates a structured brightness, while the right displays disruptive patterns that break the order, representing the emotional pull of grief. The central seed, a flame, introduces a heartbeat-like pulse to the composition, suggesting dynamism and warmth against the more subdued chaos of grief. The collision primarily occurs at the center, where the vibrancy meets fragmentation, embodying the duality of the experience. The presence of broken pixels adds imperfection, enhancing the emotional depth of the piece.
