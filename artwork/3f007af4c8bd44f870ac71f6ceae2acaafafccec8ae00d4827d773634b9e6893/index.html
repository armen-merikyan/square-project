Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of loneliness and connection through contrasting color placement, highlighting a deep internal struggle. The palette of reds and blacks symbolizes a burning isolation contrasted against the cool pale blue and white, suggesting the fleeting nature of connection. The dense use of dark colors in the corners creates a pressure that bursts forth in clusters of light, reflecting moments of connection that interrupt the loneliness. The arrangement features a central cluster of brighter colors that is both protected and pressured by the surrounding darker hues, reinforcing an echo of emotional experiences.
