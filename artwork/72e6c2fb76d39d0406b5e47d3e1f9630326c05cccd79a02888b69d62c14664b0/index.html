Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between quietude and pressure, expressed through an asymmetrical arrangement of colors. The dark navy occupies the majority, representing the overwhelming force of pressure, while the soft off-white stands out as an island of calm, creating a visual counterpoint. The blend of muted dust gray and faint peach punctuates the composition, introducing moments of clarity amidst confusion. The overlaps and collisions of color reflect the 'shatter against tide' aspect, where hard edges clash with softer hues, resulting in an intricate dance of emotional contrasts.
