Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a careful arrangement of color and pattern. The palette, rooted in the 'rage_calm' family, juxtaposes deep reds with pale blues, evoking a sense of emotional conflict and clarity. The central collision occurs where the lattice of longing transitions into the glitch of resolve, suggesting a narrative of confusion morphing into clarity. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, representing scars from past emotions, while quiet space provides breathing room for tension. The arrangement mirrors the idea of a cross-current, where different emotional flows intersect, creating a dynamic interplay of feelings.
