Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captures the tension between control and surrender. The palette, drawing from cool blues and muted plums, creates a misty atmosphere that suggests both weight and lightness. The central cluster of darker hues serves as an anchor, while the lighter colors drift outward, symbolizing the release from pressure. The noise of the control pattern collides with the surrender's gentle descent, manifesting at the center where colors collide and disrupt, creating a visual dialogue of tension and release.
