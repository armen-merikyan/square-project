Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional contrasts between trust and suspicion. The chosen palette of blacks, dark violets, soft gold, and pale green creates a tension between dark uncertainty and warm stability. The collision occurs predominantly in the top left corner, where the maze-like disruption of deep colors reflects suspicion, interrupted by small, bright dots representing trust. This visual clash creates an engaging narrative of competing emotions, with the pattern of disruption echoing the tensions suggested by the seed's themes of pressure and resilience.
