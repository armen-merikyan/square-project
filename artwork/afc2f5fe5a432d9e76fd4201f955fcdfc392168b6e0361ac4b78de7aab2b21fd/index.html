Artist Statement

The emotional tone hinges on the struggle between spark (bright energy) and ash (soft decay). The palette, with yellows and deep blues, emphasizes this duality, embodying vibrant life against the muted reminder of loss. The main collision occurs at the center of the square, where warm yellows interrupt the cool blues, manifesting the 'hunger vs restraint' theme. The patterns (hinge vs stepwell) evoke the conflict of movement and stillness, forming a rhythmic interplay reminiscent of memory battling erasure. This arrangement maintains a chaotic harmony reflective of resilience amid fragility.
