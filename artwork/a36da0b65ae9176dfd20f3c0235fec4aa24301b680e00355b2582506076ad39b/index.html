Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a juxtaposition of trust and suspicion, depicted through the contrast between warm and cool colors. The palette features soft amber and warm coral reflecting trust, while cold slate and blue-black symbolize suspicion. The dominant darker tones create a weightiness that signifies both pressure and gravity, aligning with the seed's themes of tension and complexity. The layout flows with descending colors, suggesting tears or rain, interrupted by brighter accents that symbolize fleeting trust amidst a backdrop of uncertainty. This abstract pattern embodies resilience amidst fragility, revealing deeper emotional collisions.
