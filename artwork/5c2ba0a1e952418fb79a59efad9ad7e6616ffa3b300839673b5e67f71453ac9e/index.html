Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette features deep blacks and dark violets to convey strength, while soft gold and pale green introduce elements of vulnerability. The main contrast is centered, where the soft gold cluster disrupts the surrounding dark hues, embodying the protective hope within exposed emotions. Broken pixels scattered throughout create intentional disturbances, suggesting the scars and repairs of emotional experiences, while maintaining negative space that conveys tranquility amidst chaos.
