Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between quiet and pressure through the juxtaposition of darker colors and lighter tones. The palette of off-white and faint peach represents calmness, while the compressed red and dark navy suggest pressure. The dense pixel clusters in the lower left symbolize the buildup of pressure, contrasting with the lighter, softer colors that spread diagonally, echoing a release flowing across the canvas. The arrangement highlights the collision of static pressure and tranquil quiet, allowing for an interplay of emotional tones as they shatter against each other.
