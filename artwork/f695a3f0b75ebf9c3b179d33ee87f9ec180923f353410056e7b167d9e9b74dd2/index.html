Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between control and surrender, using the palette of warm and cool tones to suggest intimacy yet distance. The main collision occurs in the center, with warmer hues representing pressure and cool tones allowing for release. The layout creates a narrow channel that emphasizes the contrast between dense patterns and quiet spaces, embodying a unique push and pull, suggestive of overflow and containment. The broken pixels disrupt the flow, mirroring the emotional stutters inherent in this tension.
