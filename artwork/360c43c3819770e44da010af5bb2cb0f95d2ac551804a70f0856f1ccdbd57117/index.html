Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, with a focus on interruption and density. The selected palette includes shelter green and warm brown, symbolizing safety and warm solidity, while cold white and open blue represent exposure and openness. The central collision occurs with dense checker patterns of strength clashing with the fragmented fault lines of vulnerability, creating a dynamic visual experience that embodies a struggle between these two forces. The arrangement emphasizes the bands of color to represent emotional strata, using fragmentation to express the delicate balance of support and fragility.
