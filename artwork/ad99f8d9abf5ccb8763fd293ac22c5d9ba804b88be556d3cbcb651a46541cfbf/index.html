Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between safety and exposure, utilizing a palette that juxtaposes warm, sheltering colors against cooler, more exposed tones. The central contrast plays out in a soft circular echo of pale skin surrounding deeper brown hues, suggesting a protective barrier. The grid-like arrangement represents exposure through uniformity, while the irregularities signify moments of interruption. The overall layout creates a feeling of a vulnerable core, surrounded by an inviting yet firm embrace.
