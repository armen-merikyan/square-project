Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between shelter and exposure, using a palette of warm browns and cool greens to signify safety versus the fragility of openness. The dominant pressure is conveyed through a thick border, while a softer, paler center suggests gentle exposure and vulnerability. The layout creates a defined area of tension, placing warm colors against cool, evoking a feeling of caution and calmness intertwining in diagonal flows. This contrast culminates at the center where the colors interlace, producing a sense of collision and harmony.
