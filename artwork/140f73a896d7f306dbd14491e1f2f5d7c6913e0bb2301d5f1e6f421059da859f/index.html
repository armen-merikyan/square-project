Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional conflict of rage and calm, juxtaposing sharp, warm colors against soothing cool tones. The palette combines reds and blacks to represent rage with pale blues and white as calming elements. The grid layout allows for contrasting emotional tensions, with a 'flame' pattern of jagged pixels suggesting an attack on the calm, horizontal structure below. Each edge displays broken pixels to convey imperfection and disruption, illustrating the cracks in perceived tranquility.
