Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys the tension between pressure and quiet, creating a sense of movement from density to lightness. The palette of off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach reflects a contrast between soft and sharp, representing moments of calm (quiet) interrupted by bursts of color (pressure). The main collision occurs through sharp reds against the muted grays and whites, suggesting both conflict and harmony. This pattern illustrates the struggle of opposing forces, with strong edges suggesting hard boundaries while allowing soft fields to 'leak' through, echoing the seed's intent of clarity amidst confusion.
