Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts vulnerability and strength, represented through a palette that's dark yet luminous. The main colors evoke awe and terror, with cold gold serving as a bright accent against the deep teal and void black background. The collision occurs at the center, where a maze of vulnerability interlaces with a ripple of strength, depicting a threshold where emotions meet. The layout creates an uneven flow, reinforcing the tension between exposure and protection, integrating the seed's concepts into an abstract emotional pattern.
