Artist Statement

The composition reflects the tension between clarity and confusion through a blend of structured and chaotic patterns. A deep palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold emphasizes feelings of rage and calm — a warm emotional spectrum that evokes intensity. The overlapping checker pattern symbolizes confusion while sections of clarity create visual breaks, embodying the struggle between the two states. The asymmetrical arrangement of colors suggests movement and pulse, resulting in a unique narrative that resonates distinctly as confusion echoes across a backdrop of calm.
