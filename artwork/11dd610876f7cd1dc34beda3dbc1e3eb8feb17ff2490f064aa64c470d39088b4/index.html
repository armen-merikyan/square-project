Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts longing with resolve, where longing is expressed through softer, translucent colors that suggest a stretch or gap, while resolve is depicted with denser, more structured colors signaling strength. The color palette, featuring coral and charcoal hues, emphasizes a blend of warmth against coolness, invoking an intimate collision between desire and steadiness. The main contrast occurs along the diagonal, creating a tension between bright and muted areas, with broken pixels representing deliberate imperfections—echoes of emotional history. This interplay informs an abstract rhythm of pressure and release throughout the square.
