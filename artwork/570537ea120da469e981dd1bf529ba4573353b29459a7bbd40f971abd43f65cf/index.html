Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the artwork represents a balance between restlessness and peace, reflecting inner turmoil intertwined with serenity. The chosen palette 'hunger_restraint' incorporates deep, muted hues that evoke tension (blackened plum, red oxide) while introducing soft patches of muted cream and small orange as soothing accents. The main contrast occurs at diagonal intersections, where dynamic patterns collide, visually suggesting conflict and interaction. The arrangement features broken pixels to symbolize interruptions in calm and moments of emotional disruption, which aligns with the seed's narrative of motion and memory. The structural flow is designed to evoke a drip-like movement, reinforcing the feeling of ongoing change between calmness and chaos.
