Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between vulnerability and strength. The use of a palette featuring soft colors like powder pink and warm white contrasts sharply with the deep intensity of carbon black and sharp crimson. The layout reflects falling pixels, suggesting elements of pressure from above. The central collision of textures, where vulnerable colors meet the stronger hues at the center, embodies the theme of exposed contrast. Broken pixels are incorporated to symbolize imperfections and interruptions, alluding to the dynamic nature of strength intersecting with fragility. This arrangement seeks to evoke a story of emotional resilience beneath the surface of delicate environments.
