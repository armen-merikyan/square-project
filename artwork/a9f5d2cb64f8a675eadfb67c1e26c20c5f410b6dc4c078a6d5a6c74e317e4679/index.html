Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between 'emptiness' and 'desire'. A palette of warm reds and cool grays captures the friction of these opposites, suggesting a struggle where warm colors, representing desire, flicker amidst the cooler tones of emptiness. The grid pattern symbolizes the structured yet conflicted nature of these feelings, creating a rhythmic push and pull across the canvas. The negative spaces foster a sense of loneliness, while the broken pixel elements reflect imperfections and frailty within these desires.
