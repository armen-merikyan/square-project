Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional struggle between restlessness and peace. The chosen palette of electric blue and signal green pushes against dead black and static gray, creating a dynamic contrast. The collision occurs predominantly in the center, where warm tones of magenta clash with cooler shades. The rising layout hints at hope through stuttered movement, reflecting the pulse of memory and fleeting motion while maintaining a calm undercurrent. The broken pixels introduce an intentional disarray, enhancing the overall narrative of emotional tension.
