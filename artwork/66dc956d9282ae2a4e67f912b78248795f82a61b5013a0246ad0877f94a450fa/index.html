Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances vulnerability and strength, reflecting their interplay through the palette of muted plum, soft cyan, and rain blue. The soft colors represent vulnerability, while deeper tones symbolize strength. The collision occurs at the center, where the saturated dark hues meet the lighter ones, creating a dynamic tension. The broken frame layout emphasizes the fragility of shelter against external pressures, allowing for negative space that evokes exposure, enhancing the feeling of uncertainty and connection.
