Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between vulnerability and strength. The palette incorporates burnt orange for warmth and energy, dark red to signify strength, charcoal for depth, and gold for illumination. The central collision occurs in a rising pattern, where light colors disrupt the darker fringe, echoing the essence of protection while exposing the soft breaks at the edges. This arrangement embodies the tension of hope against despair, portraying a balance that mirrors the seed's themes of brokenness and rise.
