Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between longing and constraint, creating a narrative of desire pressing against barriers. The palette blends deep reds with muted creams to depict warmth and heat struggling against cool, dark spaces. The main collision occurs at the center, where saturated colors clash with the subdued edges, representing the battle of existence within defined boundaries, echoing the spiral pressure implied by the seed.
