Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the juxtaposition of vulnerability and strength, where the center appears softer and exposed while the edges are dense and rooted. The chosen palette combines warm and cool tones representing intimacy and distance, with accents of light and shadow overlapping to symbolize collision. The arrangement—the central pixels portray vulnerability through a crosshatch pattern, surrounded by a fault line pattern that represents strength—reflects an upward flow, embodying hope amidst struggle, mirroring the battle between safety and exposure.
