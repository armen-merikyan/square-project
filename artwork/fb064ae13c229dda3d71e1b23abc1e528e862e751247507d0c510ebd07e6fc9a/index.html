Artist Statement

This artwork represents the emotional dichotomy of joy and grief through contrasting colors and shapes. The palette of yellow and deep blue captures the essence of joy's brightness clashing with the heaviness of grief, creating an emotional turbulence. The layout features a dense grid-like structure against lighter dots to signify a conflict between stability and chaos, representing the struggle between two powerful feelings. The left side is weighted with darker tones while the right introduces bursts of yellow, illustrating the pulse of emotions—this offsets joy against grief beautifully. The broken pixels exemplify imperfections, serving as reminders of disruptions in our emotional landscapes.
