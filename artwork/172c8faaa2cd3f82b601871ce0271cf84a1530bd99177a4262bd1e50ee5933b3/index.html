Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the tension between clarity and confusion, reflecting a struggle between symmetry and imperfection. The palette, inspired by 'ancient_fire,' utilizes burnt orange and dark red to evoke warmth and intensity while charcoal and gold add depth and contrast. The left side embodies clarity through a rain pattern in warmer hues, while the right side contrasts with chaotic, stair-like formations in darker shades, suggesting noise. The collision occurs in the center where the meticulously arranged patterns meet disarray, symbolizing the incessant pulse of conflicting emotions. This embrace of broken pixels adds a human touch, illustrating the fragility of perceived order amidst chaos.
