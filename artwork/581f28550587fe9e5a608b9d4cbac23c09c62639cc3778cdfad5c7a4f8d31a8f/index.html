Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between restlessness and peace, depicted through a palette of earthy greens and soft blues which evoke a sense of grounded stability against disruptive threads of dark hues symbolizing chaos and motion. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where warm browns interrupt cooler colors, suggesting the tension between tranquility and unease. The composition uses broken pixels strategically, causing deliberate disruptions within the overall harmony, reflecting the complex emotional landscape described in the seed. The layout comprises stacked bands, reinforcing the idea of shifting density and temperature, thus marrying the two emotional states effectively.
