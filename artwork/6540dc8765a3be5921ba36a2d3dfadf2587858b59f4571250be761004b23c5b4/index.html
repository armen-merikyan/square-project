Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional interplay between longing and resolve. The palette, featuring soft pinks and stark blacks, symbolizes the tension between delicate hope and the firm grounding of resolve. A collision occurs in the center where the warm tones of longing meet the sharper edges of resolve, creating an intense pulse. The use of broken pixels introduces an element of disruption, reflecting the inherent frailty within these emotions. Overall, the arrangement evokes a narrative of healing amidst chaos, where contradictions coexist.
