Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between control and surrender, with colors creating a sense of pressure and release. The palette, 'hunger_restraint,' of deep reds and muted creams emphasizes the struggle of containment versus overflow. A thick outline suggests control with broken pixels disrupting the flow, symbolizing moments of surrender. The main contrast occurs in the center, where dense clusters of color meet open fields, illustrating the collision of emotions. The patterns of spark (control) and ring (surrender) interact, creating a rhythm of conflict and release, echoing the seed's themes.
