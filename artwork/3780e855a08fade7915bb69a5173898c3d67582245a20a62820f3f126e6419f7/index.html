Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette of deep navy and dim cyan to evoke a sense of depth and protection combined with patches of white that suggest light and fragility. The collision primarily occurs in the center, where the warmer tones of white burst through the cooler background, creating a visual metaphor for the struggle between these emotions. The patterns suggest a scattered repair motif, reflecting interrupted flows and a sense of unresolved tension, as the broken pixels deliberately disrupt symmetry, reinforcing the theme of vulnerability amidst strength.
