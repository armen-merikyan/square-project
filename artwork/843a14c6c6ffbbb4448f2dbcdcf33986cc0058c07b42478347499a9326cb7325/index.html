Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the tension between restlessness and peace through contrasting patterns and colors. The dominant dark hues represent chaos and memory, while lighter shades suggest a serene, uncluttered space. The composition's center, where dark and light collide, embodies the tumultuous yet necessary interaction between these emotions. The palette combines navy, black, dim cyan, and white, creating depth and visual tension. Overall, the piece captures a dynamic narrative where structure intermittently breaks, highlighting the push and pull of inner conflict.
