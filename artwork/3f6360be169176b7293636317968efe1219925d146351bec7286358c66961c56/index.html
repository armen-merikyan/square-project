Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between defiance and submission through its contrasting color palette of deep black, striking red, soft pale blue, and pure white. The central tension emerges from the stark juxtaposition of dark, oppressive colors in the lower half, symbolizing the weight of submission, against lighter, more open colors at the top, representing defiance. The 'stairs' pattern reflects the struggle between climbing up toward light versus falling into darkness, while the edge style of broken pixels introduces imperfect conflict and disorder at the borders, echoing the 'noise against frame' concept from the seed. The layout captures an 'argument_grid', where contrasting patterns vie for dominance, creating an emotional push and pull within the abstract square.
