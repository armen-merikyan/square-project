Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the delicate balance between 'longing' and 'resolve.' The palette combines calm whites and blues with stark blacks and vivid oranges, representing serenity against urgency. The main contrast appears at the center where the broken dots of resolve collide with the ash field representing longing, creating visual tension. Broken pixels introduce imperfections that symbolize scars of experience, further enhancing the layered narrative of repair and damage. The negative space forms a quiet channel, cutting through the dense, scattered patterns, suggesting an unresolved journey.
