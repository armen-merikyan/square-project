Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a dichotomy between vulnerability and strength, represented through a palette of stark contrasts. The chosen colors create a dialogue between cold, muted tones and vivid accents, highlighting the struggle between exposure and shelter. The main collision occurs in the center, where soft marks intertwine with jagged, hard edges, symbolizing a tension of connection and alienation. The pattern flows in a countercurrent, allowing for a visual representation of rising signals against the stillness of static fields, embodying emotional depth and dynamism.
