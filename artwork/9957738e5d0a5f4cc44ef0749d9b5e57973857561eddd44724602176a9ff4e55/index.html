Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve, utilizing a palette that combines pale mint and faded gold for warmth, contrasted with storm gray and near black for weight. The upward layout reflects a sense of hope, while the collision of star-like constellations and stacked elements creates an interruptive dynamic. The broken pixels signify moments of damage amidst aspirations, embodying repair and recovery. The dense gradient at the center symbolizes the struggle for clarity amidst noise, reinforcing the themes of tension and resolution.
