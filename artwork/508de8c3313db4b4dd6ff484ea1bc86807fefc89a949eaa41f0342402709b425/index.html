Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between defiance and submission, represented through a palette that balances bright greens with muted creams. The contrast is found in the center, where vibrant colors represent resistance, colliding with quiet fields that symbolize submission. This arrangement creates a dynamic tension echoed in the static and scar patterns, with broken pixels introducing intentional disruptions, evoking the idea of an ongoing battle between order and chaos.
