Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines vulnerability and strength, symbolizing the tension between exposure and protection. The palette of muted plum, soft cyan, and lavender gray suggests a melancholic yet hopeful atmosphere. The central collision is found in the pale interior, surrounded by harder edges of the muted shades, illustrating the struggle between being open and being shielded. The broken pixels disrupt the symmetry, reflecting the imperfections of both vulnerability and strength.
