Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the tension between softness and severity, where lightness suggests hope and fragility while the dark tones evoke weight and gravity. The palette comprises powder pink and warm white to symbolize gentleness in contrast with the carbon black and steel gray representing severity. The main collision occurs at the center, where the bright hues interrupt the dark, creating a pulse of emotional resonance. This composition mimics a spiral action, acknowledging both the gentle unfolding and the restrictive bounds, establishing an abstract narrative of forgiveness against guilt.
