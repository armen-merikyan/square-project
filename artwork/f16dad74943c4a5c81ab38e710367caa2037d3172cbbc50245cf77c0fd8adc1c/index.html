Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between longing and resolve using a palette of deep navy, black, and dim cyan with single white accents. The dark background suggests containment and introspection, while the brighter edges represent the pulse of urgency and desire for connection. The composition flows outward, creating a central collision where longing (echo patterns) meets resolve (steady pulse marks), framing negative space that allows the viewer to breathe amidst the intensity. Broken pixels add an element of disruption that reinforces the theme of repair amidst damage, embodying the emotional complexities hinted at by the seed.
