Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between envy and gratitude, represented through a palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold. The central collision of colors simulates a pressure point where contrasting forces meet. The thick outlines create stability, while the broken pixels invoke a sense of imperfection and raw emotion. The layout emphasizes empty space, allowing the viewer to feel the weight of each color's presence, suggesting a narrative of unresolved tensions.
