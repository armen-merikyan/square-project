Artist Statement

This abstract piece embodies the struggle between rage and calm, utilizing a palette of red, black, pale blue, and white to create emotional tension. The sharp contrast of red and black represents intensity and chaos, while the pale blue and white serve as calming elements, creating a visual balance. The layout reflects a mirrored structure with slight asymmetries, emphasizing the ongoing collision of feelings. The broken pixels introduce imperfections that echo the theme of containment versus overflow, enhancing the overall narrative.
