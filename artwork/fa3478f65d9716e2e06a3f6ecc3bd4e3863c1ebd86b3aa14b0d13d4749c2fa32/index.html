Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy between signal and noise, juxtaposing the clarity of the pulse within a chaotic framework. The chosen palette features dark tones of navy and black to signify the deep noise, while bright dim cyan serves as the singular signal accent, emerging through layers of static and fragments. The central area acts as the collision point where clarity disrupts the surrounding noise, creating a dance between the ordered flow of signals and the disarray of chaos, invoking a sense of tension and resolution.
