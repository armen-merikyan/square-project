Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between control and surrender. The palette, drawn from sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue, evokes nostalgia while facilitating a warm cool contrast. The center is filled with a dense cluster suggesting pressure and overextension, while the edges, softer and less intense, create a surrendering flow. The crossed patterns signify movement and collision, illuminating the push and pull between containment and release.
