Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between restlessness and peace, utilizing a palette that contrasts warm tones with muted grays to evoke a sense of unsettled calm. The color rust acts as a fiery accent within calming fields of moss green and bone white, suggesting a memory of motion that conflicts with the tranquility of the surrounding space. The entrance of broken pixels disrupts the overall flow, merging the defined edges into a chaotic center, emphasizing the dynamic interplay of light and dark. The composition is structured around a hollow center that radiates outward, embodying the idea of a pulse trying to break through static while leaving negative space intact, enhancing the feeling of unresolved motion and echo.
