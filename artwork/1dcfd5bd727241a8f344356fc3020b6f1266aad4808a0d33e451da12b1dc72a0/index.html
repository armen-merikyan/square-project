Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of joy and grief through a careful balance of warm and cool colors. The chosen palette of yellows and oranges, representing joy, contrasts sharply with deep blues and muted purples, embodying grief. The pattern mirrors the themes of rising signals and chaotic order by utilizing a zigzag layout, reflecting emotional highs and lows. The edges are emphasized to signify the boundaries of these feelings, while broken pixels invoke imperfection and realness in emotional experience. Negative space is utilized to symbolize pauses between moments of joy and grief, enhancing the overall emotional impact of the piece.
