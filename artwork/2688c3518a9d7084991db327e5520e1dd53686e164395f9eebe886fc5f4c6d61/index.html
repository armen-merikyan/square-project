Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between envy and gratitude through contrasting color dynamics and patterns. The selected palette of lime, black, electric blue, and magenta evokes a sense of vibrant struggle, where the lively lime and electric blue represent gratitude's warmth, while the black and magenta underscore the depths of envy. The pattern is organized into a grid that oscillates between clarity and disruption—enforcing a dialogue of rising tensions versus falling relief. Negative space highlights the emotional gaps, while broken pixels scatter within, suggesting imperfection and unresolved feelings.
