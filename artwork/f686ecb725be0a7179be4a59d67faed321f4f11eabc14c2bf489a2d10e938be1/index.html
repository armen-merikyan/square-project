Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines nostalgia and progress, represented by a palette of vibrant colors (lime, electric blue, and magenta) against black's depth, suggesting both vibrancy and void. The layout's split horizontal approach creates an interplay where the top section increasingly brightens while the lower half remains darker, highlighting the contrast between hope and melancholy. Main contrasts occur where lime and electric blue collide with more muted tones, embodying the tension between past comfort and forward momentum. The broken pixel elements introduce an imperfection that symbolizes the unpredictability of progress.
