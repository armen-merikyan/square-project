Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the tension between loneliness and connection, evoking a sense of isolation through stark contrasts in color. The palette—featuring deep blacks, stark whites, and a muted blue—creates a visual narrative of both weight and lightness. The main contrast occurs at the edges, with broken, asymmetrical distributions around the center, where a dense cluster of lighter hues provides a rhythmic pulse against a backdrop of darker, more fragmented shapes. This refers to the struggle between feeling heavy and light, while the broken pixels accentuate the imperfect nature of memory, and the sparse arrangements reflect moments of solitude. Overall, it captures the essence of fragmented connection, allowing for gaps that signify emotional absence.
