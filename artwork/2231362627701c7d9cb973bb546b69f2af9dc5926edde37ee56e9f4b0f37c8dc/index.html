Artist Statement

This piece conveys the emotional duality of relief versus dread through contrasting palettes. The lighter colors (pink, soft gray) introduce a sense of relief, while deeper tones (dark maroon, red) invoke dread. The arrangement features a top-weighted design, where the heavy colors concentrate at the top, shifting towards a gradual release at the bottom with rain-like patterns in lighter hues, illustrating a journey from tension to calmness. The clashes between colors create visual tension and emotional conflict, echoing the struggles inherent in the seed.
