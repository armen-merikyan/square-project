Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines vulnerability and strength, represented by softer color fields interrupted by stark contrasts. The palette comprises 'clean white' for clarity, 'ice blue' providing calmness, 'ink black' for depth, 'violet gray' indicating confusion, and 'warning orange' as a striking accent. The center pulse of strength, illustrated through a strong black presence, meets lighter hues that softly drift away, symbolizing vulnerability. Broken pixels disrupt this otherwise structured arrangement, creating visual tension and suggesting the idea of a fractured mirror. The balance between calm and pressure also resonates with the seed, offering a visual impression of emotional instability. Central collisions manifest through the juxtaposition of dark and light, while color transitions encourage a reflective journey across the grid.
