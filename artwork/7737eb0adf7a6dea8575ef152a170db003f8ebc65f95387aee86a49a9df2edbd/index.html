Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between vulnerability and strength, with an emphasis on the balance of lightness and weight. The palette of soft powder pink, warm white, and sharp crimson represents vulnerability, while carbon black and steel gray signify strength. The main contrast emerges in the central area, where the lattice and islands patterns collide, creating an intentional break in density. This composition parallels the struggle of finding safety amid exposure, symbolized by the fractured distribution of colors. The negative space allows for breath within the composition, enhancing the emotional narrative.
