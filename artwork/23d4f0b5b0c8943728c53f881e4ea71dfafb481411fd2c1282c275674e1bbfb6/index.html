Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional conflict of fear and hope. The palette features stark contrasts with dark colors symbolizing fear, such as black and dark violet, while soft gold and pale green represent hope and rising signals. The edges are deliberately left jagged, presenting the chaos of fear, juxtaposed with soft, bright centers signifying hope's subtle emergence. The layout encourages upward movement, reflecting the struggle to rise above despair. Broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, emphasizing the jagged edges of anxiety while maintaining open spaces for light. This interplay invites viewers to reflect on the tension between the heaviness of fear and the uplifting nature of hope.
