Artist Statement

This piece embodies a tension between longing and resolve. The color palette, dominated by deep blue and mourning black, evokes a sense of depth and introspection, while the pale yellow introduces moments of brightness symbolizing hope and renewal. The structured ascent of colors from the bottom to the top reflects a rising signal—an emotional journey from despair towards resolution, embodied through flecks of broken pixels that interrupt the flow, suggesting both damage and repair. The main collision occurs centrally, where the resolve overtakes the longing, showcasing a dynamic push-pull between these emotional states.
