Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between quietness and pressure, symbolizing moments of tension interspersed with moments of calm. The palette, composed of subtle off-whites, soft peaches, and deep navy, mirrors this dynamic, where muted tones suggest stillness and darker colors introduce weight. The primary contrast occurs along the diagonal axis, with denser areas of dark navy breaking through softer peach and gray fields, creating a sense of release amidst tension. This interplay of color, alongside the scattered broken pixels, emphasizes interruption and a unique rhythm of compression and expansion, inspired directly by the seed's call for shattering contrasts.
