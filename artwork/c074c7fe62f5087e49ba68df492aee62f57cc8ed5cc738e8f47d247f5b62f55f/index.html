Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between loneliness and the yearning for connection. The palette, drawing from deep blacks, hints of dark violet, soft gold, and pale green, creates a contrast between the somber weight and the warmth of potential connection. The center showcases a small bright dot, symbolizing connection, surrounded by broken dots denoting loneliness. This creates a dense pressure around the empty space, evoking feelings of isolation against the hope of connection, encapsulating the emotional essence of the seed.
