Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between love and betrayal through its contrasting palette of warm pinks and deep maroons. The arrangement uses symmetry with interruptions to reflect the notion of nearly mirrored feelings disrupted by imperfections. The collision occurs where the soft colors confront sharp edges, symbolizing the delicate balance of trust and deceit. Patterns echo a tension-filled maze that twists subtly, allowing for an exploration of the complexities of love and pain.
