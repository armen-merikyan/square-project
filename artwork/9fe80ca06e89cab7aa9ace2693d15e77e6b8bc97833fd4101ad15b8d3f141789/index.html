Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines longing and resolve, manifested through contrasting clusters that suggest tension between an unfinished pulse of desire versus the stability of resolution. The palette echoes a subdued 'broken memory' with sepia and gray tones, providing a reflective background. Dots collide with sharper edges at the center, creating a visible disruption that mirrors the 'broken' aspects of the seed. The layout leans toward emotional 'near_vs_far' with reduced space on one side, reinforcing the feeling of yearning against a sufficiently held boundary.
