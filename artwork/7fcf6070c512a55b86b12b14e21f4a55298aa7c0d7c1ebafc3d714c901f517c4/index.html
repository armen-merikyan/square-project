Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity. The palette, anchored in deep teal and cosmic indigo, suggests an enigmatic depth. The arrangement features clean, mirrored patterns on one side contrasting with disrupted 'islands' of color that evoke curiosity and the chaos that often accompanies it. The dominant flow descends like a tide, encapsulating the uncertainty of rising signals as the colors intersect, creating a focal point in the center that embodies an unresolved collision. The negative space here creates breathing room, inviting contemplation on the edges of chaos.
