Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between emptiness and desire, represented through a palette of warm reds and cool grays. The center features deeper hues symbolizing desire, while the edges dissolve into cooler tones, suggesting emptiness. The broken pixels create a sense of unease, enhancing the randomness of emotions. The layout flows from the central theme outward, suggesting a rise against a backdrop of desolation, while maintaining negative space for reflective thought.
