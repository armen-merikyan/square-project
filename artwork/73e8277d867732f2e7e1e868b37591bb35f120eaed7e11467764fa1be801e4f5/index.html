Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dialogue between joy and grief, using a palette of yellow and muted purple to convey warmth and sadness. The grid structure interlaces noise and fragmented dots, suggesting hope rising through chaos. The sharp contrast between the bright yellows and deep blues creates a collision that highlights the struggle between order and chaos. Centered chaotic sparks are framed by quieter edges, enhancing the feeling of pressure and release, embodying the seed's themes of rising signals and emotional complexity.
