Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between desire and restraint. The palette of muted cream and red oxide creates a saturated pulse, encapsulating yearning, while the darker hues of blackened plum and iron gray establish a sense of containment and pressure. The pattern is designed with falling pixels, where brighter, warmer colors intrude upon cooler, muted tones, highlighting their conflict. The central collision occurs in the center, drawing the eye and establishing a focal point of tension that suggests the struggle inherent in love and desire.
