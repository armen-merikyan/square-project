Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional dichotomy of confusion and clarity through a palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple. The stark contrast between warm and cool tones suggests a push and pull, evoking a sense of tension and balance. The layout descends like falling pixels, creating a vertical flow that enhances the feeling of disorder breaking into order. The jagged flame-like pattern paired with the structured stair pattern embodies the breaking and rebuilding process in emotions, with negative space utilized to reflect moments of clarity amidst confusion.
