Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between spark and ash, fostering a sense of resilience amid frailty. The chosen palette of soft sky and storm gray reflects a struggle between warmth and desolation, while the faint gleam of faded gold signifies hope amidst the ash. The collision happens diagonally, where the broken dots pattern disrupts the tide motif, symbolizing the ongoing battle between vitality and decay. Negative space emphasizes the quiet yet powerful contrasts in the grid.
