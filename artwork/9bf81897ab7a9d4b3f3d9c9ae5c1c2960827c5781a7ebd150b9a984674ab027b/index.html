Artist Statement

This composition represents the emotional tension between softness and severity, utilizing a palette of muted and bold colors to denote contrasts. The powder pink and warm white provide a backdrop of softness, while the sharp crimson and carbon black define moments of severity. The arrangement leans heavily on the bottom, echoing a rising signal, with the spiral flow enhancing the sense of movement and collision, creating intersections that suggest both noise and harmony.
