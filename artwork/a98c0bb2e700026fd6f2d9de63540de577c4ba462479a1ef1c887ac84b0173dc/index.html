Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional contrast of nostalgia and progress, layered in a diagonal flow. The palette features deep blacks and soft golds symbolizing the weight of memory against the glow of advancement. Dark violet illustrates tension within the contrast, evoking a sense of hidden depth while pale green introduces moments of calm within the chaos. The split horizontal layout enables a collision between full and hollow spaces; dense lower rows create pressure where memories linger, while lighter upper rows suggest an elusive, open future. This interplay captures the dualities implied in the seed, reflecting a desire for balance amid emotional conflict.
