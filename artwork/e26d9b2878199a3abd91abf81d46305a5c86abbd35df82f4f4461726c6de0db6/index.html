Artist Statement

The emotional tone emerges from the contrast between awe and terror, creating a visual narrative of tension. The palette chosen displays deep blacks to assert a threatening void, complimented by pale blues that suggest serenity and awe. Contrasts lie in the duality across the center, where darks press against softer hues, creating a boundary that evokes both stability and instability. The arrangement mimics soft edges with jagged interruptions to portray a dynamic balance, implying a collision of emotions and memories amid chaotic and stable elements.
