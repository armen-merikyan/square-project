Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the tension of fear and the optimism of hope. The palette combines deep blacks and dark violets representing fear, contrasted with soft gold and pale green symbolizing hope. The composition features jagged edges amidst a checker pattern, allowing the soft gold to burst through the dark frame, suggesting light battling against weight. The split vertical layout creates a strong sense of internal conflict, with the center pixel glowing, hinting at a breakthrough amidst darkness.
