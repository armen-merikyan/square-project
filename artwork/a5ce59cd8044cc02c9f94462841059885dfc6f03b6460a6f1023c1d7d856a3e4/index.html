Artist Statement

The emotional tone presents a struggle between clarity and confusion, using a palette of intense colors that suggest both calmness and chaos. The contrast stems from electric blue against dark black, representing clarity piercing through confusion. The arrangement of colors creates a rising signal, where the bottom layers (confusion) gradually lead to clearer hues at the top, embodying a sense of movement and pulse. The jagged edges and broken pixel placements evoke a feeling of interrupted symmetry, making the piece feel both structured and chaotic, reflecting the intricate emotional dance between rage and calm.
