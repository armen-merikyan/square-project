Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between clarity and chaos, utilizing a stark navy and black palette reflecting the heaviness of noise against bright cyan accents for the signal. The center collision creates a dynamic debate between structured lines and scattered marks, representing the struggle for coherence amidst interference. The patterns evoke both a pulse of vitality and a haunting echo of static, honoring the balance between order and disruption.
