Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances longing and resolve, illustrated through a structured breakdown of the layout. The palette combines the tranquility of 'pale_aqua' and 'mist_gray' in the upper rows with 'alarm_red' and 'ink_blue' below to depict tension and urgency. The primary collision, seen in the evocation of longing by the crosshatch pattern, meets the stability of resolve marked by the barcode pattern. This interplay creates a narrative of seeking connection amidst fragmentation, symbolized by the broken pixels creating imperfections that suggest hidden depths beneath the surface.
