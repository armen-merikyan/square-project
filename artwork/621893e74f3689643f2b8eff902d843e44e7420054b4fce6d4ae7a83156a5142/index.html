Artist Statement

The emotional tone draws from the tension between longing and resolve, creating a push-pull dynamic in the grid. The chosen palette of melancholic blues and soft plums establishes a serene yet complex interaction. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense clusters of muted plum push against soft cyan fields, embodying the struggle between chaos and order. This arrangement suggests tension through varying pixel density—intimacy found in contrast and distance. The pattern combines a 'thread' aspect for longing as it weaves through a structured 'grid' of resolve, illustrating intersecting emotions and the journey_upward that hints at aspiration amidst chaos.
