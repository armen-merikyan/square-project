Artist Statement

This composition captures a dynamic interplay between fear and hope. The black and dark violet create a heavy, jagged edge that suggests tension, while soft gold and pale green introduce light and a rising flow of hope. The layout rises upward, enhancing the contrast between the dark corners and the flickers of brightness. The broken pixels add imperfection, emphasizing the emotional collision inherent in the depicted struggle. This abstract pattern conveys complex feelings through methodical color placements, allowing for an exploration of the emotional spectrum.
