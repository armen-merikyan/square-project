Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts restlessness with peace, emphasizing a struggle between dark and light elements. The chosen palette, 'broken_memory,' reflected muted sepia and gray tones, suggesting nostalgia and transient feelings. The central area uses brighter accents to depict fleeting memories in motion, while the darker edges create a sense of closure and containment, delivering a visual narrative of tension and resolution. The intentional disruption of color through broken pixels enhances the feeling of a jagged memory, embodying the clash between calmness and chaos.
