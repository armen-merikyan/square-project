Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a tension between loneliness and connection, represented through an exploration of color placement that balances negative space with targeted bursts of color. The palette of sepia, tan, dust_gray, and faded_blue reflects the warmth of connection intertwined with the coldness of loneliness. The main contrast is realized through a radial pattern of muted colors surrounded by darker tones, creating an echo of loneliness at the center while allowing glimmers of connection along the edges. This shapes a narrative of hidden versus exposed emotions.
