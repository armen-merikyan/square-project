Artist Statement

This composition captures the duality of quietness and pressure, translating the chaos of 'shatter against tide' into a visual form. The palette combines darker colors like dark navy and compressed red for density and pressure, and softer hues like off-white and faint peach to create zones of calm. The main collision occurs through the sharp edges and contrasting colors that disrupt the muted field, embodying the tension between the weighted border and the spacious center. The pattern aligns with the seed's themes of pressure vs. quiet through clustered pixels versus loose spaces, evoking a sense of fragmented clarity in confusion.
