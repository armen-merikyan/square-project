Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between emptiness and desire, signifying a push and pull within a quiet expanse. The chosen palette of dark navy and black conveys a sense of solitude, pierced by moments of light in dim cyan and white, mirroring the collision of hope against desolation. The layout employs a frame-and-core with a radial pattern subtly integrated into the core, suggesting an inner turmoil evolving from the stillness of the surrounding darkness. Light and dark colors create tension, symbolizing rising signals against a backdrop of muted noise, enhancing the narrative of longing amidst barrenness.
