Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between quietness and pressure through its color choices and pixel arrangement. The palette, consisting of soft off-whites, dusty grays, warm reds, dark navy, and faint peach, creates a calm yet intense atmosphere. The main collision occurs in the center, where darker, denser colors converge, symbolizing pressure, while the edges remain softer and quieter, echoing relief and calmness. The 'falling_pixels' layout and 'maze' pattern form a tension between structured chaos and loose tranquility, representing the interplay of quiet and pressure as they disrupt each other throughout the composition.
