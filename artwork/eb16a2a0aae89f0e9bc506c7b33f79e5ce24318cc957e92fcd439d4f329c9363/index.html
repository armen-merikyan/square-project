Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors and patterns. The central area features soft, radial patterns in muted tones symbolizing exposure, while the edges are fortified with dense, dot-like structures representing strength. The palette, drawn from 'hunger_restraint', includes rich reds and plums contrasted with muted creams to evoke a sense of struggle and resilience. The main collision occurs at the center where the exposures and strengths intersect, signifying the delicate balance of emotion.
