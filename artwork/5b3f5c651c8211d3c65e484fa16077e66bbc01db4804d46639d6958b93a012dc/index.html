Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between defiance and submission, represented by a palette that swings from saturated colors to muted tones. The electric blue and signal green punctuate the composition, evoking a sense of resistance, while hot magenta and static gray suggest entrapment and a receding presence. The main collision occurs in the center, where lattice-like patterns of defiance confront the tide-like waves of submission, creating a visual tension. This interplay captures the essence of the seed, translating the conceptual struggle into an abstract dialogue.
