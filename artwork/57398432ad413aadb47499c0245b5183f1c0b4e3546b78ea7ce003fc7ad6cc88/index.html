Artist Statement

This composition navigates the emotional landscape of longing and resolve through a balance of muted and saturated colors. The palette of 'red oxide', 'blackened plum', 'muted cream', 'iron gray', and 'small orange' reflects the undercurrents of urgency yet provides spaces of serenity. The central collision occurs at the center with dense clusters of colors, encapsulating the tension between repair and damage, while the edges utilize quieter tones to evoke a sense of spacious resolution. The patterns — 'tide' and 'crosshatch' — intermingle to illustrate the push and pull of emotional dynamics, reminiscent of threads intertwining yet threatening to break.
