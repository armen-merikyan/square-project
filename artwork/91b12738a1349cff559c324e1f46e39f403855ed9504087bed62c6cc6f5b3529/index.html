Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between envy and gratitude, employing a color palette that embodies fear and hope. The contrast between the soft gold representing gratitude and the dark violet symbolizing envy creates a visual dialogue. The upward movement of colors on the left hints at ambition, while the muted tones on the right suggest heaviness. The irregularity introduced through broken pixels creates a dynamic yet unresolved pattern, symbolizing the jagged nature of emotional experiences.
