Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between quiet and pressure through a distinct palette of muted and bright colors. The dark navy (#1A1D2A) acts as the dense pressure contrasting with the faint peach (#F6E0D6) that suggests softness and calmness. The barcode pattern juxtaposed against the lattice interrupts the flow, creating a dynamic visual tension across the grid, where density collides with openness. The dotted scars represent shattering against the tide, encapsulating the seed's essence of conflict between clarity and confusion.
