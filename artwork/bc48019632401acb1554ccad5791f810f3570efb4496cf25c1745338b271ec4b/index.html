Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional spectrum between joy and grief using contrasting colors to create a rhythmic pulse. The palette merges bright yellows and oranges representing joy with deep blues and muted purples to signify grief. The arrangement has a central focus with bright elements radiating outward, clashing against the darker outer edges, emphasizing the push and pull of emotions. The edge style and broken pixels reflect the fragility of balance in this tension, suggesting an unresolved emotional narrative.
