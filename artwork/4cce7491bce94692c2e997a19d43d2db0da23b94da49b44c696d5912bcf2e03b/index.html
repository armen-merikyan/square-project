Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise). The selected palette features dark navy and blacks, symbolizing depth and weight, contrasted with bright dim cyan and a single white; this juxtaposition represents clarity and moments of exposure amidst confusion. The main collision occurs in the central rows, where the structured pulse of calm blue collides against the more chaotic black and grey tones, portraying the struggle of maintaining clarity in a sea of interference. Patterns of `'broken_frame'` and `'braid'` are utilized to echo the concepts of fractured signals and entangled noise, their collision establishing a visual rhythm that reflects memory and erasure.
