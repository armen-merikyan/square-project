Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between vulnerability and strength, portrayed through a palette of safety and exposure colors. The main contrast occurs in the center where warm brown interacts with pale skin, suggesting an exposed emotion surrounded by dark greens as shelter. This arrangement hints at flooding signals clashing with unshielded light, achieved through a lattice of strength and a ripple of vulnerability, while intentional broken pixels introduce imperfection, representing ongoing conflicts and connections.
