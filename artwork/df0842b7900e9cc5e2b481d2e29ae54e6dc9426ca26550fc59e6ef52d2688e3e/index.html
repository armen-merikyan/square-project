Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between trust and suspicion, realized through contrasting colors. The selected palette of yellow for warmth and deep blue for depth creates a visual tug-of-war, reminiscent of hope fighting against shadowy doubt. The main collision occurs in the center with a bright yellow intersecting deep blue, embodying the struggle of resilience against fragility. The upward flow and rising signal layout support an ascent from pressure to light, while broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, echoing life's uncertainties. The pixel arrangement reflects a balance of calm and conflict, where each color dance leads to a tender resolution.
