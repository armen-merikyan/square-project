Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting colors. The palette combines warm brown and soft green to symbolize trust, with cold white accents representing suspicion. The arrangement features a dense center with darker tones that gradually yield to lighter ones at the edges, embodying the idea of pressure against an emerging softness. The collision of patterns—sharp interruptions from suspicion against a flowing, stacked trust motif—creates both tension and release, reflecting the complexities of these emotions.
