Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a dance between longing and resolve. The palette features warm rose clay and distant lilac to convey intimacy contrasting with cool blue, suggesting emotional distance. The primary collision occurs in the top left corner, creating a dense cluster of warm colors against cooler tones spreading outward, symbolizing pressure. This layout emphasizes a pressure corner, encapsulating the idea of intensity starting seamlessly and radiating to both calm and conflict.
