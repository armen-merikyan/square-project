Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the conflict between control and surrender, focusing on the ebb and flow of tension through the juxtaposition of muted edges and vibrant core. The palette, characterized by soft powder pink, warm white, and severe carbon black, reflects warmth colliding with starkness, creating a compelling narrative of confinement and a yearning for freedom. The central collision occurs in the heart of the square, where a concentration of brightness and warmth radiates against darker edges, symbolizing the struggle between openness and containment. The arrangement intentionally avoids even distribution, allowing for a feeling of overflow at the center and measured retreat at the perimeters, evoking a deep emotional landscape of struggle and release.
