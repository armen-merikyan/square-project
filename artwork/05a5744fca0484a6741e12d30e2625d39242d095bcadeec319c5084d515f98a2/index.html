Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between panic and control through a duality of color and structure. The palette of faded blues and dusty grays symbolizes memory and introspection, while the arrangement suggests a clash between stability (the center) and chaos (the edges). The radial patterns converge towards the center, showing control as an anchor, contrasted by broken, irregular dots around it, representing the unpredictable nature of panic. The flow from the center outward reflects the struggle between retaining order and the encroachment of disorder.
