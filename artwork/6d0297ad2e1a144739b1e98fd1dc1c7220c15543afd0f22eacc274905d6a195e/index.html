Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of love and betrayal through a palette dominated by warm pinks and deep maroons contrasted with gray undertones. The layout suggests a diagonal invasion, with warmer tones starting from the bottom left, representing love, while cooler gray tones encroach from the top right, symbolizing betrayal. The jagged edges and broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection that echoes the theme of conflict and unresolved feelings, creating tension and an interplay of muted clusters against brighter accents.
