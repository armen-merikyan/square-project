Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between clarity and confusion through a contrasting palette of muted sepia tones and cool faded blue. The thick outlines create a visual border that draws the viewer's eye inward, while the deliberate placement of broken pixels disrupts symmetry, embodying the theme of oscillation between order and chaos. The predominantly empty spaces allow for a sense of calm amidst the clutter, mimicking the emotional narrative of falling away from clarity into confusion, evoking a complex interplay that resonates with the initial seed.
