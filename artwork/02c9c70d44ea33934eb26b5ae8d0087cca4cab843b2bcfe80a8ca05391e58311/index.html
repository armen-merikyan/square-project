Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured here reflects the tension between intense desire and the confines of restraint. The chosen palette of deep reds, muted creams, and a touch of gray conveys warmth and vulnerability, with a stark contrast to the heavier, darker shades that suggest boundaries and limitations. The arrangement features a dense center in saturated colors, gradually shaded out towards the edges, echoing the energy of longing pressed against the limits of untouched space. The interplay creates a rhythmic collision of warmth and coolness, emphasizing the push-pull dynamic inherent in the seed's themes.
