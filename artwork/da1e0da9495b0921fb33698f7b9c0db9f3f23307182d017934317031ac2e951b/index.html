Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional interplay between longing and resolve through a palette of pale aqua, mist gray, alarm red, ink blue, and clear white—colors that evoke serenity intertwined with urgency. The dense center symbolizes the weight of longing, creating a stark contrast with the quieter borders. The overlapping patterns of crosshatch and ripple reflect the tension of chaos versus order, illustrating the struggle with unresolved feelings. The main collision occurs centrally, where color density creates a visual thrum against the surrounding emptiness, representing an emotional climax of connection and distance. Broken pixels add an element of imperfection, suggesting scars of experience, while maintaining necessary negative space for balance and breathing room.
