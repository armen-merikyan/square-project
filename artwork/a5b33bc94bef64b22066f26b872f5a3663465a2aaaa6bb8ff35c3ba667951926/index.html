Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional collision between control and panic through its contrasting palette of electric blue and magenta against the grounding black. The diagonal flow creates a sense of pressure releasing from one corner to the opposite, suggesting a journey from chaos to calm. The usage of patterns aligns with the emotions, with a checker pattern on the left representing instability and a crack-like pattern on the right indicating a sense of control. The 2 broken pixels introduce imperfection, reinforcing the theme of disruption and highlighting the balance within chaos.
