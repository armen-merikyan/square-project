Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between defiance and submission, represented through the tension of contrasting colors. The palette of muted creams, dark plums, and vibrant accents of red and orange creates a dynamic interplay. The stark black serves as the dominant background, symbolizing heavy submission, while bright shards of color ripple through as marks of defiance, forming a chaotic yet structured rhythm. The collision occurs primarily at the center, where the bright colors push against the surrounding darkness, suggesting a struggle for dominance. This results in a visually engaging pattern that captures the essence of the seed's emotional dichotomy while maintaining an abstract quality.
