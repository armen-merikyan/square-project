Artist Statement

The artwork expresses the tension between joy and grief by utilizing a palette of bright yellow and orange to signify joy, contrasted against deeper blue and muted purple shades to represent grief. The layout flows upward, suggesting hope or escape, while the vertical wave pattern embodies the fluidity of emotions. Broken edges and sparse placement illustrate the imperfection of feelings, echoing each emotion's unpredictability. The collision happens through alternating dense and empty spaces, emphasizing a push-pull dynamic between heaviness and lightness.
