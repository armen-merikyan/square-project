Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between control and surrender. The palette, softened with muted plum and soft cyan, creates a melancholic feel that resonates with themes of overflow and containment. The dominant color is a gradient of rain blue, suggesting a layered depth of control, while lavender gray serves as the quiet contrast, symbolizing release. The central collision occurs with a stutter step of broken pixels that introduce intentional imperfections, echoing interruptions in the flow. The organized, yet asymmetrical arrangement allows for a tension-filled narrative of pressed emotion against the larger, quiet expanse, reflecting the push and pull of the two emotional states.
