Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between love and betrayal. The palette of soft pinks and deep maroons conveys warmth yet hints at underlying darkness. Contrasts arise from the soft edges of love clashing against the harsh boundaries of betrayal, creating a visual echo of conflict. The pattern is a grid that provides structure but is disrupted by radial bursts symbolizing chaos and noise, captured in the placement of darker colors. The seed's themes translate into an abstract arrangement that juxtaposes warmth and isolation, inviting reflection on the complexity of relationships.
