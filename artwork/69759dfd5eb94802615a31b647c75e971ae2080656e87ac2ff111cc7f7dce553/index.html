Artist Statement

The emotional tone is centered on the conflict between panic and control, utilizing contrasting colors to represent the strife. The palette 'broken_memory' evokes nostalgia and disturbance, combining sepia and dusty grays with cool faded blues to enhance tension. The outer borders are chaotic, marked by directional stripes, while the central area introduces a serene ring-like structure that pushes against the edges, suggesting control amidst chaos. The use of broken pixels symbolizes imperfections in our emotional weather, creating a sense of unresolved motion.
