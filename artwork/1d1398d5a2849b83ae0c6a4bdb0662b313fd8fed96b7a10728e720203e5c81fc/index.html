Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional tension between quiet and pressure, expressed through a dense cluster on the left transitioning into more open, muted spaces on the right. The palette blends dark navy and compressed red to evoke a sense of weight and pressure, while faint peach and dust gray encourage calmness and distraction. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where the intensity of colors is interrupted by softer tones, embodying both conflict and resolution inherent in the seed. The broken pixels introduce a deliberate imperfection, resonating with the idea of shattering against the tide, emphasizing the narrative of collision between sharp and soft surfaces, mirroring the emotional theme of clarity within confusion.
