Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional duality of control and surrender. The palette, drawn from shades of green and brown with accents of pale skin and cold white, creates a feeling of safety and exposure. The distinct contrast occurs at the center where the overfilled warm browns collide with the cooler, lighter shades, symbolizing a struggle against an imposing weight. The patchwork control pattern emanates from this center, while the ring of surrender softens towards the edges, capturing a flow of inward spiral that draws the viewer's eye around the chaotic core.
