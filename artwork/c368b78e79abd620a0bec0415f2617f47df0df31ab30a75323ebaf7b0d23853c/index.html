Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional duality of vulnerability and strength through color contrast and spatial arrangements. The palette relies on rich greens and soft creams, suggesting a connection between nature's resilience and the delicate nuances of human emotion. The central diamond merges a vibrant green with softer cream tones, creating a strong visual anchor contrasted by an outer layer of deeper hues that introduces a sense of both protection and exposure. The collision is palpable where the bright center meets the darker edges, embodying the push-pull dynamic of vulnerability versus strength.
