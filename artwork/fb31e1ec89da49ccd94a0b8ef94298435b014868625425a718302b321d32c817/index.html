Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured is a tension between clarity and chaos, where a readable rhythm (signal) struggles against the background of interference (noise). The palette blends signal green and hot magenta for strong contrast, presenting vibrant interruptions in otherwise muted hues, resulting in a stark visual conversation. The split layout allows clear segmentation of emotion, amplifying the dynamic interplay between the two contrasting states. The main collision occurs towards the center, with broken pixels symbolizing disruptions that echo the theme of memory vs. erasure.
