Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional contrast of longing and resolve through a pattern of flowing pixels that alternate between calm areas and densely packed accents. The chosen palette balances signal green and electric blue to evoke a sense of both urgency and serenity, while the broken pixels represent moments of vulnerability amidst the steady structure. The primary collision occurs in the center, where the dense color meets the lighter rows, echoing the tension between desire and determination. The overall layout suggests a feeling of descent, with colors cascading down, reinforcing the sense of time passing with longing.
