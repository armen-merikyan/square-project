Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the friction between stability and exploration, illustrated through a balance of structured and sporadic patterns. The palette, encompassing intense hues of signal green and electric blue layered upon the muted dead black and static gray, emphasizes a tension between the vibrancy of curiosity and the weight of certainty. The contrast manifests at the center of the grid where bright colors collide against dark backgrounds, symbolizing the struggle of rising signals amidst encroaching chaos. Elements laid out in a downward flow evoke feelings of things descending, suggesting both the weight of burdens and the hope of reaching towards something unknown.
