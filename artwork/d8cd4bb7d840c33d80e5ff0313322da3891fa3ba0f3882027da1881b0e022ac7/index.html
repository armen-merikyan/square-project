Artist Statement

This composition embodies the conflict between safety and exposure, showcasing a pale central area contrasted by a heavier border. The palette of soft greens and warm browns suggests comfort and protection, while hints of cold white and open blue evoke vulnerability. The collision is found in the center where the 'stairs' of exposure intersect with the 'spark' pattern of safety, creating a dynamic interplay. Broken pixels disrupt symmetry, enhancing the emotional tension of the piece, where each side mirrors yet diverges from the other.
