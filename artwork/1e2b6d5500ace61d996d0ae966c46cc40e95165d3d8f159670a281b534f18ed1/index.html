Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes feelings of loneliness and connection, where deeper, darker colors (black and shades of gray) represent solitude. In contrast, electric blue and magenta evoke warmth and vibrancy, suggesting connections. The layout is diagonal, indicative of pressure building from the outside corner, encapsulating the center, which is brighter, symbolizing a burst of connection. The color choices reflect a clash between isolation and warmth, with the central burst contrasting against a field of noise. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfection, reinforcing the emotional complexity of the piece.
