Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the conflict between tenderness and rage, employing a palette of reds and muted blues. The contrasting colors create a dynamic tension, with the deep black background acting as a grounding force, while the soft blue veils suggest an underlying calm against the explosive red stepwell patterns. The collision occurs in the center, where tenderness (blue) meets the outburst of rage (red), creating interest and emotional complexity. Broken pixels disrupt the arrangement, representing imperfection and the interplay of guilt and forgiveness that the seed implies.
