Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between 'emptiness' and 'desire' through color and pattern. The palette, a mix of navy, black, and dim cyan, evokes a sense of loneliness juxtaposed with a yearning warmth. The left half features darker colors that frame and create a sense of void, symbolizing emptiness, while the right half introduces brighter, warmer tones, hinting at desire. The flow is left to right, reinforcing a movement from void to fullness. Key contrasts arise between the tightly clustered dark colors, portraying dense feelings of isolation, and the more scattered, warmer lighter colors illustrating hope or yearning. The inclusion of broken pixels injects imperfection and human experience, further deepening the emotional narrative. Overall, the balance and structure reflect the struggles of rising signals in the chaos of seeking connection.
