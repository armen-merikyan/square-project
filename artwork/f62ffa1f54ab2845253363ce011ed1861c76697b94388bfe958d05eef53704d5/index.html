Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between clarity and confusion, utilizing a palette that juxtaposes stark black and white with soft pale blue to signal both rage and calm. The arrangement employs both symmetry and pronounced breaks, where clusters of color are interrupted by blank spaces—a nod to the emotion of confusion. The thicker outlines introduce a jagged, irregular rhythm, enhancing the sense of brokenness amidst the order. The overall composition pulses upward, evoking an emotional wave that begins with chaotic density at the bottom and gives way to clearer spaces above.
