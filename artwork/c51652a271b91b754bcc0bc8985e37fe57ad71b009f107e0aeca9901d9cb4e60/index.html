Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects tension between trust and suspicion, illustrated through a blend of warm and cool tones. The warm coral and soft amber suggest trust, while cold slate and blue-black evoke suspicion. The layout of scattered repair brings forth abrupt interruptions in patterns—mirroring the conflict of emotions. Key contrasts occur where bright colors break into muted spaces, creating a visual dialogue of connection amidst alienation. The diagonal flow emphasizes both a fall and ascent, reflecting resilience against fragility, with broken pixels representing imperfections in trust.
