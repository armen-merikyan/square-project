Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents the dichotomy between confusion and clarity, using a palette that contrasts electric blue and magenta with grounding black and lime. The vibrant colors collide, representing moments of confusion, while the black provides a stark clarity, reflecting the theme of broken symmetry. The falling pixels layout creates a chaotic yet rhythmic descent, emphasizing the contrast between structured clarity and distorted confusion. The broken edges represent the disruptions in perception, and the layout maintains a negative space to allow for reflection amidst the visual noise.
