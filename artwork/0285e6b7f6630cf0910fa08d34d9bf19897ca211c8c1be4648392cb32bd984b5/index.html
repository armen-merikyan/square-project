Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between panic and control, utilizing stark color contrasts to reflect this duality. The selected palette of lime, black, electric blue, and magenta represents vibrancy and energy, clashing with stable, darker tones to signify control. The top half, reflecting panic, exhibits chaotic dot patterns symbolizing erratic emotion, while the bottom half portrays a more structured maze-like design, suggesting stability. Key clashes occur at the center, where the contrasting patterns meet, creating a striking dynamic that captures the essence of the given seed, portraying a struggle for dominance between emotional extremes.
