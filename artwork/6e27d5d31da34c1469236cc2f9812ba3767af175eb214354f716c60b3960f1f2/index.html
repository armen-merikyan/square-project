Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the tension between panic and control, represented through contrasting color choices and patterns. The palette incorporates yellows and oranges for warmth and vibrancy (representing panic), while deep blues and muted purples offer a calming backdrop of stability (representing control). The main contrast appears at the center where a chaotic ring pattern (pivoting towards panic) collides with the orderly dot pattern (signifying control). This arrangement evokes a narrative of emotional conflict, reflecting a push and pull between feelings of security and disarray.
