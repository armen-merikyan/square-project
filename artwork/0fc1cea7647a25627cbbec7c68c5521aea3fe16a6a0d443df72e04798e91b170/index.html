Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between clarity and distortion, mirroring the tension of 'signal vs noise'. The palette employs dynamic contrasts of bright green and electric blue against muted static gray and dead black, implying a struggle for coherence amidst chaos. The main collision occurs around the center, where scattered bright patches represent the signal attempting to penetrate the noise. This grid of colors evokes a visual representation of the battle between clarity and interference, with broken pixels emphasizing imperfections and the fragmented nature of memory.
