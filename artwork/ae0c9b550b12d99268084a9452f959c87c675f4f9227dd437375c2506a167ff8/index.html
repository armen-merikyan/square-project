Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional dialogue between vulnerability and strength. Utilizing a palette of warm and muted tones, colors like muted cream contrast against heavy blackened plum and iron gray. The central area features a light reflective quality, symbolizing exposure, while the outer sections envelop it with denser hues, creating a sense of protection. The flow from left to right accentuates movement, with a broken layout reflecting imperfections and the need for safety amid exposure. The intentional disruptions mimic scars, emphasizing the tension of vulnerability amidst strength.
