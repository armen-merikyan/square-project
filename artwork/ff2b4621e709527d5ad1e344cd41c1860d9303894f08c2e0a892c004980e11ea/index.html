Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between clarity and chaos, evident in the interaction of patterns representing noise and signal. The palette merges deep navy and black to convey the weight of noise, punctuated by the bright white and dim cyan to express moments of clarity. The primary contrast collides centrally where the structured ring patterns embody signal and the chaotic checker patterns interrupt them, creating a dynamic tension. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, enhancing the sense of chaos and mirroring the imperfections of transmission narratives, reflecting the seed's ideas.
