Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between dread and relief, embodying a narrative of fluctuating tension. The palette draws from earthy sepias and muted blues, embodying weighty memories intertwined with glimpses of relief. Contrasts manifest through a heavy dark mass (dread) colliding with lighter accents (relief) across the rows, creating a dynamic layering that reflects movement and change. Broken edges enhance the notion of imperfection, while patterned densities foster a quiet urgency. The shape seed influence introduces chaos, urging subdued intensity amidst the rhythm.
