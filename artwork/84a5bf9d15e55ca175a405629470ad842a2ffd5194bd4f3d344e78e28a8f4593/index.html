Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes a contained pressure against a backdrop of soft yielding. Utilizing the palette of moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold, the composition reflects the struggle between control and surrender. The center features a clash of colors representing overflow while the surrounding bands echo containment. The visual rhythm creates an unresolved tension as brighter hues disrupt cooler fields, illustrating the theme of pressure versus release. The cut edges suggest fragility and instability, while maintaining a readable structure allows viewers to engage with the emotional narrative.
