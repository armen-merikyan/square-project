Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a juxtaposition of defiance and submission. The palette of yellow and muted purple indicates conflict, with bright warmth symbolizing defiance and dark tones representing submission. The main contrast is visible in the center, where vertical 'ladder' layers clash with the chaotic 'broken dots' from the edges, creating an optical tension. Deliberate breaks in the borders allow colors to leak through, embodying the concept of an uneven channel of emotions—reflecting both order and chaos. The placement creates a rhythm of interruption and flow, encapsulated within the square's boundaries.
