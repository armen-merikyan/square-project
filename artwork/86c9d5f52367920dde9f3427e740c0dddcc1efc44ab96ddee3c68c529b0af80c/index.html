Artist Statement

The emotional tone is rooted in the collision between softness and severity, represented through a palette that blends warm whites with sharp crimson against a backdrop of deep blacks and cool grays. The contrast reflects the push and pull of opposing feelings. The zigzag pattern of severity meets the gentle waves of softness in a central overlap, creating a dynamic tension while leaving the corners largely untouched, inviting viewers to experience the emptiness while still sensing the energy of the collision.
