Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tension between safety and exposure. The selected palette features 'shelter green' and 'warm brown' to convey a sense of warmth and protection, counterbalanced by 'cold white' and 'open blue' representing vulnerability. The main contrast occurs vertically, suggesting a split between dense safety (bottom) and vulnerable exposure (top). Each color placement serves to evoke interactions and interruptions, creating a dynamic dialogue between the two emotional states, where the center reveals the softness weaved through the harder edges.
