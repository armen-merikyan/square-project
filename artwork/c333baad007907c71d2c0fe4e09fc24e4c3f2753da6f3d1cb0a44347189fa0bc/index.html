Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork embodies the clash between fear and hope, reflected in its contrasting color palette of dark violet, black, soft gold, and pale green. The layout suggests a mostly empty space indicating the potential for growth, while the dark hues convey pressure and fear. The grid pattern from hope distinguishes a small light in the center, representing resilience amidst the chaos. The combination of horizontal lines and dense vertical shapes echoes the contradictions in emotional experiences, inviting an introspective connection.
