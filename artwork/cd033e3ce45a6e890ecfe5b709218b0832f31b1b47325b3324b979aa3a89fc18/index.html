Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the clash of joy and grief, showcased through a palette of bright yellows and muted blues. Bright colors form a dynamic central space representing joy, while deep blues and purples create a surrounding heaviness of grief. The layout flows from bottom to top, suggesting a rise from the depths of grief towards the elevation of joy. The deliberate contrast between bright, energetic colors and dark, somber hues highlights their conflict and interplay, with a crosshatch pattern representing joy's vibrancy disrupting the maze-like structure of grief, creating an engaging viewing experience.
