Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a struggle between grief and joy, encapsulated in an 8x8 arrangement where vibrant colors intersect with darker, muted tones. The palette of bright yellows and oranges juxtaposed against deep blues evokes a vivid contrast, symbolizing the conflict between light and shadow. The grid-like order for joy is disrupted by dot patterns representing grief, creating a dynamic interplay as they collide within the composition. The layout suggests a diagonal tension, flowing from joy in the top left to grief in the bottom right, offering a sense of release through hidden connections that invite the viewer to explore layers of emotion.
