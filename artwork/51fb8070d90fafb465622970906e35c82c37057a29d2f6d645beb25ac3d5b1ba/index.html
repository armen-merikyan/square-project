Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captures the tension between longing and resolve. The palette combines warm coral and soft amber to evoke feelings of nostalgia and connection, while cold slate and blue black ground the piece in a sense of calm introspection. The main contrast occurs at the center where deep blue black interrupts the warm tones, mirroring the struggle between desire and fulfillment. The broken pixels create intentional disruptions, suggesting scars of past experiences, while the organized chaos reflects a thread of urgency within a serene backdrop. This design encapsulates the nuanced interplay of emotions derived from the seed.
