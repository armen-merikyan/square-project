Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional dichotomy of vulnerability and strength. The palette of mournful black, deep blue, and pale yellow reflects the depths of emotion—with black embodying strength and stability, contrasting against the softness of pale yellow, representing the exposed and vulnerable center. The main collision occurs centrally, where overlapping patterns of stacked vulnerability meet the structured stepwell of strength, creating a visual tension that resonates with the seed's themes. The presence of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, allowing the composition to breathe and echo the idea of layered experiences finding balance in chaos.
