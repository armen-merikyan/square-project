Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, employing a palette that combines clarity and confusion. The contrast, primarily at the center, represents a bright core tangled in darker shades, symbolizing the struggle of protective desires against the overwhelming force of exposure. Broken pixels disrupt the harmony, indicating imperfection amidst aspiration and restraint—a visual echo of the seed’s thematic ambiguity.
