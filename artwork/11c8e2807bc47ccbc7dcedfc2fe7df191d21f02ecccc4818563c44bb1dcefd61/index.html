Artist Statement

This piece explores the themes of tension between clarity and confusion by juxtaposing sharp contrasts and soft falls of color. The palette of deep blue and muted purple suggests a complex emotional landscape, where moments of clarity punctuate areas of confusion. The central bands of brighter yellow and orange vibrate against the cooler hues, creating a dynamic collision that feels almost mechanical in its precision, yet deeply emotional. Broken pixel elements introduce a sense of imperfection, reflecting the inherent chaos in seeking clarity amidst confusion.
