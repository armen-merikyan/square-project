Artist Statement

This abstract composition explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors and spatial arrangements. The palette of soft greens, warm browns, and pale tones represents the delicate balance of safety and exposure. The dense bands evoke strength, while the scattered echoes symbolize vulnerability, colliding predominantly in the upper left corner, creating dynamic pressure and a sense of movement against the subtler right side. This duality is suggested without direct representation, embracing the complexity of human emotions.
