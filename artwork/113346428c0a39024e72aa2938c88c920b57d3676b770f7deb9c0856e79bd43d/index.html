Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional contrast between vulnerability and strength through the interplay of dark and pale colors. The chosen palette of black, pale blue, and white represents a spectrum from protection to exposure. Strong, dark blocks suggest stability, while the pale, lattice-like patterns convey fragility. The main collision of styles occurs centrally, where the abrasive edges of the strength pattern intersect with the delicate lattice of vulnerability, illustrating the ongoing struggle between these two states. This tension creates a visual dialogue that resonates with the seed’s core themes.
