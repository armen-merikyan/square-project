Artist Statement

This composition evokes a strong emotional tone of confusion versus clarity through a juxtaposition of cool and warm colors. The palette, featuring lime, black, electric blue, and magenta, captures a machine-like emotion that resonates with the delicate conflict in the seed. The arrangement of colors mimics a 'falling pixels' layout, where the top rows converge into dense clusters below, suggesting a pulse - rhythmically oscillating between clarity and confusion. The chaotic interspersing of dark and light provides a visually intriguing collision, reflecting the unresolved state of the emotional narrative.
