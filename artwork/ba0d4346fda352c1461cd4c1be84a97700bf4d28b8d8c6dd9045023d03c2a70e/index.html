Artist Statement

The emotional tone here reflects a tension between confusion and clarity, illustrated through a stark contrast of a dark, dense center that scatters outward with lighter shades. The chosen palette of navy, black, and a single white emphasizes the feeling of isolation against a backdrop of complexity. This represents the dense pressure of confusion at the center, fighting against the clarity of the edges. The jagged disruptions of broken pixels suggest interruption, while the consistent but varied patterns of color suggest movement and flow, echoing the idea of falling pixels. The collision between darker tones and the abrupt lighter inputs creates a striking visual conflict, capturing the essence of the seed's themes.
