Artist Statement

This artwork expresses the tension between 'restlessness' and 'peace.' The palette, selected from tones representing intimacy and distance, merges warm pinks with cool blues and lilacs. The primary contrast occurs at the center, where bright accents collide with muted backgrounds, embodying both chaotic motion and tranquil fields. The arrangement echoes the seed's themes, using a grid to symbolize peace while juxtaposing it with stuttering, broken pixels that disrupt the calm, suggesting a memory in motion.
