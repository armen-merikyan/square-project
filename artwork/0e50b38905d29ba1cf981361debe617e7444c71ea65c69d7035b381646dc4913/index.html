Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, inspired by melancholy wonder, brings forth a soothing yet introspective quality, with rain blue and soft cyan representing quiet resistance, and muted plum and lavender gray embodying submission. The center of the composition features a dense grid pattern, symbolizing defiance, while broken dot patterns dissolve into the edges, signifying submission with an unsettling but necessary collision occurring at the center, creating an emotional pulse that draws the viewer in.
