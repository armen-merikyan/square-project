Artist Statement

This abstract composition captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength by utilizing a palette of muted and bright colors that clash and reinforce each other. The contrasting shades of 'storm_gray' and 'faded_gold' serve to represent the scars of vulnerability against a backdrop of stability. The main collision occurs in the center through a cluster of heavier tones, while lighter shades sweep from the edges, suggesting safety amidst exposure. Inspired by the concept of a 'fault', the imperfect arrangement gives a sense of depth and intimacy, echoing both fragility and resilience.
