Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of safety and exposure, using a palette that embodies both warmth and coolness to symbolize protection and vulnerability. The choice of 'shelter_green' and 'pale_skin' contrasts with 'open_blue' and 'cold_white', highlighting the conflict between calm and tension. The main collision occurs in the central pixels, where warmer tones collide with cooler accents, suggesting an ongoing struggle between the desire for protection versus the need for openness. The pattern reflects a mirrored symmetry with intentional disruptions, emulating an echoing effect that adds depth to this narrative of emotional pressure and release.
