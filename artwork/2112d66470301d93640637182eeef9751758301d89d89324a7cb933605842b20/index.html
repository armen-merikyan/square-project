Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tensions between panic and control, represented through a balance of warm and cool tones. The color palette, a blend of joyful yellows and deep blues, symbolizes the clash between chaotic energy and calm stability. The central area is dense with color, suggesting a spark of control amid surrounding fractured pixels that evoke feelings of instability and unrest. The layout mirrors, but with deliberate imperfections, representing the breaking points of control. The combination of a maze pattern for panic and a ring for control creates a visual narrative of tension and potential release.
