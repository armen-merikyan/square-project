Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, illustrated through a palette that includes burnt orange and dark red to convey urgency, contrasted with charcoal and gold for serenity. The main collision occurs in the center, where pulse marks collide with firm edges, creating a dynamic tension. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, representing the fragility of resolve, while the quiet space surrounding the dense cluster of pixels manifests the feeling of longing, providing a stark contrast. The arrangement embodies a push-pull dynamic, suggesting a journey from chaos to clarity, embodying the narrative of brokenness striving for repair.
