Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic conflict between the warmth of sparks and the heaviness of ash, illustrating resilience amidst fragility. The palette blends warm browns and greens with cold whites and blues, signifying the balance between safety and exposure. Contrasts occur centrally between bright scarlet accents indicating spark against softer, muted ashen hues that recede into negative space. This arrangement symbolizes memory against the backdrop of erasure, with the jagged edges introducing disruption, echoing the chaos of conflicting emotions.
