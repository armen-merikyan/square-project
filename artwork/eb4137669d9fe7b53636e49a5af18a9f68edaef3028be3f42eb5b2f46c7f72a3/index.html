Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrast between softness and severity. The chosen palette of powder pink and warm white represents softness, evoking a sense of warmth and gentleness, whereas deep carbon black and sharp crimson introduce an element of starkness and severity. The composition rises upward, depicting a hopeful escape yet layered with cold, relentless boundaries like graphite strokes. The main clash occurs centrally, where soft and severe patterns intersect, creating a dynamic tension that allows the viewer to appreciate the pulse between vulnerability and strength. Broken pixels scatter throughout, symbolizing imperfections and the remnants of past experiences, contributing to the overall narrative of conflicting emotions.
