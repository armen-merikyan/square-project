Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a balance between joy and grief, capturing the struggle of hope against despair. The palette consists of bright yellows and oranges to signify joy, contrasted by deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The primary collision occurs where bright colors intersect with darker hues, illustrating that moments of joy can disrupt the heaviness of grief. The rising_signal layout promotes an upward motion, hinting at escape while maintaining zigzag patterns that evoke chaos and order inherent in human emotion. Together, these elements create a visual dialogue that resonates with the complexities of feeling.
