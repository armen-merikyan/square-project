Artist Statement

The emotional tone emerges from the clash of love and betrayal, represented by warm reds and muted grays. The palette features pink and dark maroon to symbolize warmth veiled by sorrow. Main contrasts occur through the interplay of dense warm hues intersecting with cooler shades, suggesting tension and break. The broken pixel application creates unease amidst the symmetry, mirroring emotional splits conveyed in the seed. The pattern flows outward, with a subtle diagonal rise enhancing a sense of movement and unresolved tension, yet leaving conscious negative spaces to suggest moments of reflection.
