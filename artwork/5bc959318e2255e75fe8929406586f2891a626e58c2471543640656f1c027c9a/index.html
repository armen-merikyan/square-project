Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the struggle between fear and hope, represented through heavy contrast and dense patterns. The palette, consisting of dark violet for fear and soft gold for hope, creates a palpable tension. The split vertical layout emphasizes the conflict, while the noise and flame patterns create a dynamic interplay and burst of emotional energy. The arrangement allows for both warmth and coolness, with areas of quiet interspersed with bursts of chaotic color, culminating in a visual representation of inner conflict.
