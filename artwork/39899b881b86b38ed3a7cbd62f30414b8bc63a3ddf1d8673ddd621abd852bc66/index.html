Artist Statement

This piece embodies the interplay between joy and grief through its contrasting palette and layout. The outer edge, utilizing deep blues and muted purples, conveys the weighted aspect of grief, while the inner checker pattern of vibrant yellows and oranges introduces a dynamic tension of joy and hope. The zigzag structure suggests order versus chaos, with bright colors interrupting the dark, creating a vibration of emotional waves. Broken pixels add an intentional flaw, representing the imperfection of real emotions. This composition presents a visual dialogue of rising signals against a foundation of melancholy, creating a poignant yet abstract narrative.
