Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between loneliness and connection, illustrated through a palette of warm yellows and muted purples, suggesting a warmth that contrasts against feelings of emptiness. The color positions evoke a sense of weight, where the darker hues felt like broken memory colliding with bright dots of connection. Main contrast occurs in the top left corner with the strong yellow emerging from the muted background, suggesting an invasion of connection amidst scattered moments of solitude. The layout follows a falling pixel pattern, reflecting the descent of emotions from the heights of connection to the depths of loneliness.
