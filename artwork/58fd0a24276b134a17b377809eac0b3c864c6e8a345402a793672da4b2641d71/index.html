Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional contrast between longing and resolve. The palette reflects muted warmth with shades like red oxide and small orange, contrasting against deeper tones like blackened plum and iron gray, suggesting a dynamic between warmth and weight. The spiral pattern creates a flow toward the center, where the dominant feeling of resolve intersects with traces of longing. Disruptive broken pixels add an element of imperfection, while the layout maintains a balance between quiet spaces and dense clusters, inviting contemplation and tension.
