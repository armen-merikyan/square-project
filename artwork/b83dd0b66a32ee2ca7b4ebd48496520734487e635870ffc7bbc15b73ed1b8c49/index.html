Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the dichotomy of loneliness and connection through a split vertical composition. The left side embodies loneliness with scattered muted dots, creating a feeling of isolation and distance, while the right side employs a structured checker pattern to symbolize connection and unity. The palette of sepia, tan, and dust gray suggests warmth yet reflects the distance of memory, while faded blue accents represent fleeting moments of connection. The sharp contrasts between the soft dots and the structured checks reveal an underlying tension between the two emotions, echoing the push and pull of human experience.
