Artist Statement

This artwork juxtaposes the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The use of black and dark violet creates depth and represents confusion, while soft gold and pale green infuse moments of clarity. The arrangement reflects a nearly mirrored structure that contains intentional breaks, evoking the dissonance of confusion. The flowing horizontal stripes suggest a pulse, with stark contrasts providing visual tension, capturing the essence of the seed's struggle between symmetry and chaos.
