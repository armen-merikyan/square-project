Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dichotomy between the vibrancy of sparks and the stillness of ash. The palette of muted gray tones and a few bright accents conveys a balance of pressure and release, resonating with the themes of resilience and fragility. The contrast emerges sharply in the center, where the jagged patterns of ash collide with the softer edges of the sparks, creating an ongoing argument within the space. The arrangement is designed to evoke a sense of dynamic tension between vivid and muted spaces, reflecting an inner struggle that aligns with the seed's themes of memory and erasure.
