Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between defiance and submission, embodied by the contrast of dark versus light colors. The palette reflects 'lonely_signal', where deep navy and black create a sense of isolation, punctuated by stark white and dim cyan to signify moments of clarity amidst chaos. The collision arises in the bottom-left quadrant, where darkness and light vie for dominance, encapsulating the concept of weight versus lightness. The placement of pixels follows a diagonal fall, echoing both the act of falling and a quiet surrender, finally forming an abstract essence of the tension and unresolved emotions parsed from the seed.
