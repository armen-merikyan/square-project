Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between love and betrayal through the choice of a warm palette with muted sharp contrasts. The palette includes colors like pink and dark maroon, representing love and pain respectively. The layout has a significant balance of negative space, creating a sense of emptiness that echoes loneliness and flawed intimacy. The sharp edges against softer colors create visual disruptions, akin to the experience of betrayal within a loving space. Patterns of dots and cracks intersect, illustrating the duality of connection and separation.
