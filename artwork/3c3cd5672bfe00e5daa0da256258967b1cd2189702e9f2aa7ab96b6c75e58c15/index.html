Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between softness and severity through contrasting color placements. The soft hues of powder pink and warm white blend into the rigid structure of carbon black and steel gray, symbolizing the duality of emotions. The center holds a denser cluster of crimson and black, representing a pulse of severity, while the surrounding lighter colors shimmer softly—suggesting quiet space. Broken pixels strategically disrupt the grid's symmetry to evoke strong emotional echoes. The arrangement leads the eye in a spiral, reflecting the counterclockwise movement of complexity and underlying guilt versus forgiveness. This interplay invites contemplation on personal balance amid chaos.
