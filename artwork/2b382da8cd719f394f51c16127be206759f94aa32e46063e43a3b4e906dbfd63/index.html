Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between longing and resolve through its contrasting palette of ice blue and warning orange, juxtaposed against a clean white field that suggests a serene backdrop. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional imperfection, signifying repair amidst damage. The visual flow from right to left, combined with echo patterns representing longing and crosshatch patterns reflecting resolve, creates a dynamic interaction in the central region while allowing the edges to decay softly, symbolizing the wear of emotional experience. This arrangement conveys a sense of unresolved motion amid contained yet overflowing feelings.
