Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between control and surrender through contrasting shades. The choice of a sepia and faded blue palette reflects nostalgia and calmness, while the muted tones symbolize containment. The bright cluster at the center represents pressure and chaos, suggesting overflow against a backdrop of soft, quiet areas. The lattice pattern interspersed with ash-like fields represents a collision of structured control against yielding surrender, creating rhythmic interruptions that echo the inherent push and pull of the emotion captured in the seed.
