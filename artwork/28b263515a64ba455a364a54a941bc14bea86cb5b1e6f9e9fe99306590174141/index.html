Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of confusion and clarity through a structured yet fractured layout. The palette reflects fear and hope, using deep darkness to signify confusion, contrasted with soft gold and pale green to suggest clarity amidst chaos. The arrangement follows a falling pixel concept, echoing a sense of descent, while the left-weighted edge creates imbalance and tension. Key contrasts emerge between dark and light colors across the rows, illustrating the struggle between symmetry and disruption, encapsulating the essence of the seed's emotional narrative.
