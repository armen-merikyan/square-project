Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between the clarity of a singular signal and the chaos of surrounding noise. The palette of navy and dim cyan serves as a representation of the deep oceanic space, contrasting against the stark black and white accents that symbolize piercing signals cutting through the din. The central area presents a denser cluster of color, representing the 'signal,' while lighter colors subtly surround this core, echoing the noise. The broken pixels disrupt uniformity, hinting at memory and erasure, embodying layers of experience within the abstract structure. This layout embraces the idea of islands of meaning amidst overwhelming background static.
