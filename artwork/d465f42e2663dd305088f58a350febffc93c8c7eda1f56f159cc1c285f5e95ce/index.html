Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve through a play of warm and cool tones. The sepia and tan suggest a warmth of desire, while dust gray and faded blue introduce a coldness of uncertainty. The central clash, represented by dense clusters of color interspersed with negative space, reflects the tension of connection vs disconnection. Patterns of thinned threads intertwine with pressure bands, manifesting feelings of intimacy and distance, with the 'root' shape seed echoing the underlying emotional structure. The arrangement demonstrates a collision of emotional currents amidst a stark backdrop, embodying the unresolved nature of longing versus the firmness of resolve.
