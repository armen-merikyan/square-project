Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the feelings of isolation and connection. The palette of warm yellows and oranges amidst cooler deep blues and muted purples captures this duality. The contrast is stark between the cracked pattern alluding to loneliness and the grid representing connection. The central area bursts with warmth, symbolizing the heart of connection, while the outer areas carry the weight of isolation, creating a dynamic interplay of pressure and release through color placement.
