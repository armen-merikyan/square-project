Artist Statement

This piece embodies the contrast between fear and hope through a carefully curated palette of dark and light colors. The black and dark violet signify fear with their oppressive weight, while the soft gold and pale green create a sense of hope and rising energy. The jagged and scattered arrangement represents turmoil, while the central pixels feature soft patterns that signify a longing for peace amidst chaos. The pattern flows from dense to sparse, illustrating the emotional tension and the struggle for balance between these opposing feelings.
