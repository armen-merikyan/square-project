Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around tension between defiance and submission. The palette of cosmic indigo, void black, and cold gold represents the struggles of chaos against a backdrop of quietude. The main collision occurs towards the center with shattered disruptions contrasting against smooth radial patterns, illustrating the defiance of the core amidst a soft surround. The uneven border adds to the chaos, echoing life's unpredictable nature while maintaining a sliver of negative space to signify unresolved tension.
