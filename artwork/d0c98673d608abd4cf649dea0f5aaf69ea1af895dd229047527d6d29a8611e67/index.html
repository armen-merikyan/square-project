Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between the vibrancy of 'spark' and the calm of 'ash.' The chosen palette of soft pink, warm white, and deep grays conveys a contrast of warmth against a background of cool severity. The corner-loaded composition conveys 'hunger vs restraint,' where the center is rich with saturated colors flowing inward, creating an interruption that pulls towards calm. The broken pixels add intentional imperfection, emphasizing the fragility of memory vs erasure.
