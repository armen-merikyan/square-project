Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the delicate balance between confusion and clarity. The palette pulls from muted sepia and faded blue tones to evoke a sense of nostalgia and uncertainty. The left side is denser with colors that collide in warmth, representing chaos, while the right side presents a more rhythmic pattern that suggests clarity. Discrepancies in the patterns create a broken symmetry, echoing the idea of a pulse against noise, and the use of negative space enhances the feeling of unresolved movement.
