Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, symbolized by a central cluster of brighter colors against muted backgrounds to suggest pressure. The warm hues of yellow and orange against the cool deep blue and muted purple create a contrasting rhythm, enhancing the sense of conflict and interaction. The edge style suggests a fluid boundary, where strength is defined yet under constant challenge, while broken pixels emphasize imperfections in the composition, capturing the theme of 'fault' without depicting it literally. The layout is balanced, allowing for quiet spaces amidst bursts of color.
