Artist Statement

This composition captures the conflict between clarity and confusion. The palette leverages deep navy and stark black to convey a sense of depth and void, while dim cyan adds a cooler contrast, acting as a fleeting signal. The patterns of signal (flood) dominate the lower half, while circular noise (ring) interrupts, creating visual tension. The arrangement allows the eye to be drawn upwards, echoing a rise amidst chaos and leaving negative space to sustain the pulse's readability.
