Artist Statement

This composition uses a palette of muted and dynamic colors to evoke the tension between quiet and pressure. The dark navy acts as a grounding force representing density, while faint peach and off-white represent moments of clarity and soft release. The main collision occurs around the central area where colors mirror and interrupt each other, embodying the idea of symmetry versus break. The patterns of echo and mirror provide a visual rhythm that flows from left to right, enhancing the sense of a release from pressure to quietness, vividly capturing the emotional essence of the seed.
