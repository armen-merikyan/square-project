Artist Statement

The artwork embodies a tension between panic and control through a play of dark and light tones. The gloomy palette of navy and black contrasts with sharp whites and dim cyans, creating a visual representation of emotional collision. The grid and crack patterns suggest structured chaos, with the center emphasized to highlight the dichotomy of feeling. The main contrast occurs in the center where dim cyan clashes against the deeper shadows, pulsating outward and drawing attention, while lighter hues echo the sense of release along the edges.
