Artist Statement

The emotional tone stems from the juxtaposition of love and betrayal, reflected in the contrasting color palette of warm pinks and reds against cooler grays and dark maroon. The arrangement creates a rhythmic fall, where dense clusters (representing emotional turmoil) clash with empty spaces (signifying moments of reflection and calm). The cracked edges symbolize fragmentation and imperfection within relationships. The primary collision occurs in the center, where the warmer hues meet the cooler tones, embodying the push and pull between affection and distrust.
