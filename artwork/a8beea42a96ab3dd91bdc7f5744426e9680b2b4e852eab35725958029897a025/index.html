Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the struggle between urgency and tranquility, represented through a dynamic clash of color. The palette features warm and cool hues, invoking feelings of both comfort and chaos. The warm shades dominate the center, representing the feeling of restlessness, while cool tones restrained around the edges signify peace. The overlapping patterns of 'stairs' and 'hinge' amplify the sense of motion and halted flow, creating an emotional narrative through the grid. The broken pixels serve as scars on the surface, alluding to past disquietude in contrast to the calm surrounding it.
