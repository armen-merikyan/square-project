Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a collision between pressure and calm, illustrated through the selected palette of dark violet, soft gold, and pale green, which embody a push-pull dynamic of confusion versus clarity. The layout follows a rising flow, symbolizing hope despite the chaos. The arrangement features varying densities; the top rows are dense with dark colors, creating pressure, while the bottom rows offer moments of lighter hues, suggesting moments of release. The inclusion of broken pixels adds to the sense of confusion, echoing the seed's theme of emotional turmoil and conflicting feelings, while ensuring at least 10% negative space helps retain clarity amidst the chaos.
