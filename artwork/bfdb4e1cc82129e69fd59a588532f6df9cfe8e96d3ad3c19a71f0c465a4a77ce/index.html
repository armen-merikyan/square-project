Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between longing (represented by soft pastels) and resolve (embodied by stronger, darker hues). The palette focuses on muted yet slightly vibrant colors to suggest a warmth that contrasts with a feeling of damage and repair. The main collision occurs where the negative cross pattern representing longing meets the structured crosshatch of resolve, creating a center of emotional conflict. The intentional broken pixels symbolize imperfection and the scars of unresolved feelings. This juxtaposition creates a quiet urgency against a largely calm background, illustrating an interplay of serenity amidst tension.
