Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay between vulnerability and strength, captured through a palette of sepia, tan, dust_gray, and faded_blue, symbolizing warmth against coolness. The main contrast arises from the dense colors in one corner representing strength, while lighter tones echo vulnerability. The shapes generated suggest a flowing movement against a backdrop of stacked layers, where the juxtaposition of pressure and release creates a rhythmic tension within the grid's layout.
