Artist Statement

This artwork plays with the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, employing a palette of ripe green and deep olive to symbolize growth and stability against the more soft tones of cream. The contrasting patterns of grid and mirror create a dialogue of symmetry and disruption, where the main collision takes place in the center. The colors pulse toward each other, evoking a sense of tension and coexistence in the space, illustrating the fragile yet stable balance between exposure and shelter as suggested by the seed.
