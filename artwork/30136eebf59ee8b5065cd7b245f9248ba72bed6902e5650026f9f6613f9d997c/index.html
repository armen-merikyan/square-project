Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between hope (spark) and weariness (ash). The palette of mourning black, deep blue, new leaf, pale yellow, and stone evokes a journey through darkness into renewal, with quiet fields representing ash and crosshatch strokes signifying sparks. The collision occurs centrally, where deep blue and pale yellow intersect, suggesting a struggle between opposing forces. The broken frame layout allows for deliberate gaps, symbolizing leaks of unresolved emotion through jagged edges. Overall, the piece captures a push-pull feeling of resilience confronting fragility.
