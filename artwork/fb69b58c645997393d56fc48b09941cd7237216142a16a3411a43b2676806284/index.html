Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between joy and grief, using a palette of bright yellows and oranges to signify joy against deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The layout symbolizes a rising signal, with clusters of color interplaying in rising waves—a vertical harmony pierced by horizontal breaks. The contrast is sharp where warm colors encounter cool tones, illustrating the battle of emotions. The interplay of dense pixels against emptiness provides a stark reminder of memory and loss, while broken edges reflect the imperfect journey through feelings.
