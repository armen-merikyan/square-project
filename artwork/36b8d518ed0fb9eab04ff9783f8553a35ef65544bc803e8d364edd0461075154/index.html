Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between longing and resolve, represented through an interplay of muted and bright colors which communicate depth and urgency. The palette, combining shelter green for calmness, warm brown for warmth, pale skin for fragility, cold white for contrast, and open blue for clarity, reflects a balance of conflicting emotions. The tension arises in the central area, where echoes of longing collide with the firm marks of resolution, creating a dynamic pulse of interaction and layering. The layout emphasizes a quiet channel, allowing for a sense of spaciousness amidst the dense patterns, representing the coexistence of serenity and urgency.
