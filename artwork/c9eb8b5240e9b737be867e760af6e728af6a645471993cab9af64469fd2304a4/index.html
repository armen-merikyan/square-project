Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between vitality and desolation, represented by warm and cool tones. The palette consists of muted plum for grounding, soft cyan to embody restraint, and vibrant rain blue as the ignition point, suggesting the spark of life against the ash of memory. The diamond shape emerges through a maze of warmth interrupted by hinge-like ash patterns, creating a collision at the center that emphasizes the emotional push and pull between past and present. The layout creates a dense island of emotional intensity amidst softer fields, maintaining a balance that avoids uniformity.
