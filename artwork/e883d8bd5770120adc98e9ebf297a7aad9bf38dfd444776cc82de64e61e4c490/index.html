Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette of muted reds and grays reflects a complex relationship, using dark maroon to suggest depth and sorrow while pink accents represent fleeting connections. The main contrast occurs in the top-heavy design, where the warmth of the pink is suddenly interrupted by the coldness of darker shades, evoking the feeling of hidden emotions crashing against exposed surfaces. The radial and split-screen patterns embody the push and pull of connection and isolation, transforming the seed's narrative into an abstract visual experience.
