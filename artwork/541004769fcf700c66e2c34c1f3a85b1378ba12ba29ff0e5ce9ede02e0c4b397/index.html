Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between safety and exposure, evoking a feeling of vulnerability framed by stability. The palette, featuring 'shelter_green' and 'cold_white,' suggests a protective barrier meeting an open, bright core. Key contrasts occur at the center and right edge where warmer hues interact with cooler ones, creating a collision of feelings that reflect the seed's themes. The arrangement of pulse marks and stairs subtly addresses intensity and retreat, enhancing the sense of a fractal repair structure and leaving quiet space to emphasize the delicate balance of emotions.
