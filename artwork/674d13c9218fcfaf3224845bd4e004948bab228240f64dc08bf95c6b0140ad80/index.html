Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between 'quiet' and 'pressure', using a palette that juxtaposes soft, muted tones with darker, compressed colors. The arrangement creates a diagonal argument where the darker colors suggest inner pressure surging from one corner, contrasting with lighter, more abstract 'quiet' areas. The stepped 'pressure' pattern collides with scattered 'quiet' dots, invoking a sense of conflict amidst calm. Boundary edges are softened, allowing for a visual exploration of clarity meeting confusion, showcasing both hardness and softness.
