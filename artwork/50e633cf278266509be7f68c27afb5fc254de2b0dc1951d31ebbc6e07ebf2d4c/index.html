Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between grief and joy, evoking a complex narrative through color and contrast. The palette combines deep blues and muted purples to signal grief, contrasted by vibrant yellows and oranges representing joy. The layout presents a central clash where joy intrudes into the grief represented by horizontal stripes, creating dynamic interaction and visual conflict. The broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection and fragmentation, enhancing the abstract quality and allowing space for reflection amidst emotional chaos.
