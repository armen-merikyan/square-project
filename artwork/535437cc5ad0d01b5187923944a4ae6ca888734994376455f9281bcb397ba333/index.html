Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between longing and resolve, symbolizing the push and pull of desire and strength. The selected palette of clean white, ice blue, ink black, violet gray, and warning orange captures both clarity and confusion, providing segments of hope against deep shadows. The collision is centered, creating a powerful contrast amidst calculated chaos. The jagged edges imitate the broken nature of the emotions, hinting at scars while the halo and islands patterns merge yet differ, forming a chaotic reflection of inner conflict.
