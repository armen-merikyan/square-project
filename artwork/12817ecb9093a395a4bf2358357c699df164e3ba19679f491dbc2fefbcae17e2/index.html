Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between fear and hope, depicted through a contrasting palette of deep blacks and soft golds. The layout gives an impression of descending pixels like rain, symbolizing tears or fear, while the bright center glows with hope. Zigzag patterns emerge from the center, signifying disruption and inner conflict, surrounded by darker tones representing corners of fear. The broken pixels enhance the rawness of emotion, inviting viewers to explore a narrative of tension and release within the frame.
