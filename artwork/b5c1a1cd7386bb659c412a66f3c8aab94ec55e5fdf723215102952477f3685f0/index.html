Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional contrast between 'longing' and 'resolve'. The color palette, drawn from 'awe_terror', consists of deep teal lending depth and void black creating a quiet foundation, while cosmic indigo and cold gold serve as accents to highlight emotional tension. The main collision occurs in the center where the structured checker pattern of longing meets the heavier and more enveloping veil of resolve, creating an interplay of intimacy versus distance. The arrangement emphasizes the 'halo_void' layout, allowing negative space to breathe and echo the tension between hope and certainty throughout the composition.
