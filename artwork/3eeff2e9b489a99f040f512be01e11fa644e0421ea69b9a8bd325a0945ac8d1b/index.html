Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a conflict between restlessness and peace, achieved through a careful palette of muted greens and browns, interrupted by vibrant accents in pale skin and cold white. The main contrast occurs in the center, where bright spots of color disrupt the dim surroundings, suggesting a brief moment of illumination amidst darker, heavier forms. The selected patterns of spark and flood embody these emotions: dynamic interruptions from restlessness and the enveloping calm of peace. The layout 'last_light' emphasizes a singular bright area, highlighting the struggle between tension and tranquility, while broken pixels introduce an intentional ruggedness, symbolizing imperfection and the scars of memory.
