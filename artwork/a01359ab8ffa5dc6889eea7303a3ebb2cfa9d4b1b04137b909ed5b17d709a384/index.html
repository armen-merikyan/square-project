Artist Statement

This abstract composition explores the emotional dichotomy of confusion and clarity. The top half resonates with clarity, represented by the warm yellows and oranges, organized in a semi-symmetrical pattern that provides a sense of structure. The bottom half embraces confusion, with a mix of muted purples and deep blues breaking the order, suggesting fragmentation and disorder through irregular placements and cracked patterns. The color choice amplifies the emotional tension, as warm hues contrast starkly against the cool depth of the lower section. This interplay creates a visual conflict that mirrors the struggle between finding clarity within the chaos.
