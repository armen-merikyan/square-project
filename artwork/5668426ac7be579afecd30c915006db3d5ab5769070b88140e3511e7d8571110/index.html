Artist Statement

This piece reflects the duality of envy and gratitude through a contrasting palette of deep, absorbing tones and soft, uplifting hints of warmth. The main contrast occurs where the dark violet and soft gold intermingle, symbolizing the push-pull of these emotions. The flowing pattern suggests both rising and falling motion, illustrating the emotional journey from deep longing to transient appreciation, while the broken pixels introduce subtle imperfections, adding depth to the experience.
