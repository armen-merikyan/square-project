Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between longing and resolve, portraying a reflective emotional landscape. The cool shades of rain blue and soft cyan symbolize serenity and contemplation (longing), while the muted plum and lavender gray convey a sense of firmness and structure (resolve). The central collision occurs with a series of broken pixels, representing imperfection amidst a sleek pressure band pattern that runs from edge to edge. This juxtaposition visually narrates the struggle of repair versus damage, captured through dotted patterns overlapping more locked shapes. The use of negative space enhances the feeling of quiet against the pulse of color, inviting a deeper emotional engagement.
