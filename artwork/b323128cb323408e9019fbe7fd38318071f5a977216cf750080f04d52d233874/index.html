Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension of defiance against submission. The top half features glitch patterns in vibrant 'rain_blue' and 'moon_white', symbolizing bright refusal and angular resistance, contrasting the bottom half's muted 'muted_plum' and 'soft_cyan', which evoke a sense of recessive tranquility. The composition navigates a diagonal rise, interrupted by broken pixels, creating a dynamic interplay between chaos and order. The division reflects the struggle at the threshold, between open and closed spaces.
