Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission, using a palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green to evoke feelings of resistance and acceptance. The dense center, marked by hints of soft gold and dark violet, creates an area of conflict and intensity, representing defiance. The surrounding darker shades symbolize submission, binding the central energy within a lattice of quiet pressure. The edges exhibit a struggle against the overwhelming force at the center, introducing broken pixels as scars of disruption that echo the theme of chaos. The composition flows from bottom to top, emphasizing a rising tension, while the interplay of contrasting colors captures the ebb and flow of emotional dynamics.
