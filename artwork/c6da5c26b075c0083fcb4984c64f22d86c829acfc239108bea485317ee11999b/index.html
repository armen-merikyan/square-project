Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between quiet and pressure, creating a landscape of interactions where dark tones suggest weight and stress while lighter hues offer moments of reprieve. The chosen palette combines muted grays with accents of faint peach, representing both the soft horizon and the hard edges of pressure. The main collision manifests diagonally, with dense clusters contrasting with scattered, lighter pixels that represent moments of clarity amidst confusion. This aligns with the abstract seed of the moon, suggesting distance and quiet contrasts without a literal interpretation.
