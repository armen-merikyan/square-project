Artist Statement

This abstract piece evokes the tension between longing and resolve through a carefully curated palette of deep navy and black hues, interspersed with bright white and muted cyan. The arrangement creates a clear collision in the center, illustrating a crossing of emotions with the darker colors representing longing and the lighter ones signifying resolve. Broken pixels add an intentional disruption, mirroring the theme of repair amidst damage. The flow spirals clockwise, suggesting a pulsing rhythm within the structured chaos of the layout, where distant echoes and clear boundaries coexist.
