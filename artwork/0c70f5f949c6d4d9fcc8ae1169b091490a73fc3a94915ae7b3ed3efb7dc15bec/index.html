Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between trust and suspicion. The palette combines soft powder pink and warm white to evoke trust, contrasted by a stark carbon black and sharp crimson to symbolize suspicion. The central area features a soft halo pattern, creating a sense of warmth, while the periphery employs cracked patterns suggesting fracture and unease. The collision occurs primarily in the center, where the balanced emotional tones meet the pressure of suspicion, creating an outward spiral flow that signifies a journey through emotion. Broken pixels enhance the narrative of fractured trust, adding depth and complexity to the exploration of these intertwined feelings.
