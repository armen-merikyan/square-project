Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between control and surrender, utilizing a palette that evokes warmth and ancient fire. The central dense area stands in stark contrast to the muted edges, illustrating a battle of overflowing pressure versus quiet release. The distinct use of burnt orange and dark red creates an intense focal point, suggesting conflict, while the soft grey tones around it offer a sense of surrender. Broken pixels intermittently disrupt the flow, symbolizing stutters and interruptions within the emotional landscape.
