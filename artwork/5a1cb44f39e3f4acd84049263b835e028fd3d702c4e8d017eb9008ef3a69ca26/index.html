Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between defiance and submission, utilizing a palette that combines muted and vibrant colors to represent contrast. The juxtaposition of the soft cyan and lavender gray reflects a subtle quietness against more assertive hues like rain blue and muted plum, forming a central 4x4 area that faces an outer border of darker colors. The main collision of emotions happens at the center, creating a sense of controlled chaos amidst the harmonious quiet of the core. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, enhancing the sense of unrest and collision mirrored in the seed's themes.
