Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between chaos and resolution, represented by a palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold, which conveys warmth and intensity against a backdrop of uncertainty. The confusion is created through a grid-like layout that descends intermittently, simulating falling pixels. The contrast is made prominent by the abrupt interruptions of dark reds against softer burnt oranges, embodying a push and pull between clarity and confusion. The 'thick outlines' are suggested by dense clusters of color, while scattered broken pixels introduce imperfections that echo the sense of fragmentation implied in the seed.
