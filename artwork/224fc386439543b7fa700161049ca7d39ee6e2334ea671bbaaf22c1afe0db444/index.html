Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured in this piece reflects the duality of defiance and submission, underscored by the contrast of heavy and light elements. The selected palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold embodies a fiery intensity and weight, suggesting the strength of love and betrayal. A symmetrical flow generated from the center outward creates a visual impact, with broken pixels introducing imperfection while maintaining significant negative space, allowing tension to breathe. The collision is primarily seen along the edges where lighter colors meet darker forms, suggesting conflict yet unity in the emotional experience.
