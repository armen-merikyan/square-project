Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes fear's jagged edges with hope's glowing center, using dark violet and soft gold to convey tension. The palette mixes emptiness and richness, contrasting sharp and soft for dynamic interaction. The arrangement flows inward, creating a center that fights against the surrounding jagged pattern while preserving negative space to enhance emotional impact. The zigzag pattern represents rising signals within turmoil, highlighting a sense of struggle between inner hope and outer despair.
