Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional contrast of loneliness and connection. The palette of deep reds and pale blues reflects the tension between warmth and isolation. The dark colors represent loneliness, creating a central cluster that is surrounded by lighter shades, which symbolize connection. The burst pattern represents an explosive moment of clarity against an otherwise dense and muted backdrop. There is a strong contrast as darker hues clash with softer tones, creating a rhythmic struggle between connection and separation. The pixel arrangement evokes an ancient, primal feeling while incorporating both exposure and concealment.
