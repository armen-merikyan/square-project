Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, represented through a palette of cosmic indigo and deep teal, symbolizing depth and calm amidst chaos. The contrast develops at the center, where structured ripples of resolve meet the fragmented stairs of longing, suggesting a continuous push and pull. The interplay of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, echoing the narrative of repair versus damage, enhancing the abstract rhythm of the composition.
