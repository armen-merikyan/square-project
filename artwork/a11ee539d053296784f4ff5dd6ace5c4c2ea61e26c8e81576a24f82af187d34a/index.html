Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional battle between confusion and clarity through contrasting palettes of warm and cool colors. The use of yellow and orange reflects warmth and clarity, set against deep blue and muted purple, representing depth and confusion. The collage of patterns—radial vs grid—creates an overlapping tension that symbolizes the pulse of conflicting emotions. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, representing moments of chaos amidst structured clarity, evoking feelings of unresolved tension and quiet relief.
