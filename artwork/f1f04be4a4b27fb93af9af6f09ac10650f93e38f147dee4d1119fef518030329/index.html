Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between confusion and clarity through the interplay of stark vibrancy and muted tones. The selected palette—dark maroon, gray, red, and pink—creates a contrast that is both warm and unsettling, echoing the emotional dichotomy. The center of the grid features a dense cluster of red and pink, symbolizing clarity amid the surrounding confusion of grays and dark maroon. This juxtaposition creates a visual tension that reflects the seed's themes. Pixels fall from the center outward, embodying the concept of 'falling pixels' in a structured way that suggests movement and disarray, while some broken edges within the pattern lend an imperfection that resonates with the theme of confusion.
