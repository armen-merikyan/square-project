Artist Statement

The emotional tone stems from the juxtaposition of fear's jagged shapes against hope's soft glimmers. The dark colors (#000000 and #5C4C93) signify fear, creating a constrictive environment, while the lighter hues (#E6D58E and #A8E9B9) evoke hope, suggesting a possible escape from the heaviness. Main contrasts collide in the central column, where darker shades intersect with the brighter signals of hope, forming a visual zigzag pattern that portrays struggle and resilience. The negative space enhances the composition, allowing for moments of calm amid the pressure.
