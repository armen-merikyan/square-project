Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between defiance and submission. The chosen palette of 'melancholy_wonder' uses soft, cool colors to convey a sense of quiet yet oppressive atmosphere. The arrangement creates a collision at the center where the tension is most palpable, marked by warm hues breaking through softer tones. Eroded symmetry gives a sense of decay and disarray, aligning with the theme of conflict and unresolved boundaries.
