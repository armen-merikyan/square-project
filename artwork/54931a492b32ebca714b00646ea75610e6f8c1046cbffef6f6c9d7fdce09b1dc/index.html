Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the duality of joy and grief, juxtaposed through a checker pattern representing joy on one side, and a spark pattern embodying grief on the other. The palette balances warm yellows and oranges reflecting joy against deep blues and muted purples symbolizing grief. The main collision occurs between the structured joy on the left and the fragmented grief on the right, illustrating the tension between ordered emotions and chaotic feelings. The pixel arrangement reflects a stair-step approach, adding depth to the emotional narrative.
