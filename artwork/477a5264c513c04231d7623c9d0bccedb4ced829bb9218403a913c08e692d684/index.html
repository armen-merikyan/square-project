Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the conflict between panic and control through a distinct contrast of ordered patterns and chaotic disruptions. The chosen palette of soft gold, dark violet, and pale green evokes hope amidst fear, highlighting the struggle for stability against uncertainty. The main collision occurs where dark hues meet brighter accents, suggesting moments of crisis within a structured landscape. The layout supports diagonal tension, while broken pixels introduce subtle imperfections, enhancing the narrative of chaos marring order.
