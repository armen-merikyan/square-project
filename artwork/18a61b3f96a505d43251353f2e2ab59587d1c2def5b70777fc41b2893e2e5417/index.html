Artist Statement

This artwork visually explores the tension between confusion and clarity. Divided vertically, the left side embodies confusion through a radial pattern of descendants in varying shades of gray and muted pink, representing distorted symmetry and a sense of chaos. The right side, contrastingly, employs a brighter palette of pinks and reds, reflecting clarity and vitality, flowing seamlessly from left to right. The edge style, pixel bleed, adds depth and a sense of imperfection, symbolizing the intermingling of ordered clarity and chaotic confusion. The disarray introduced through broken pixels generates an emotional resonance of unresolved conflicts inherent in the theme.
