Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a struggle between feelings of loneliness and connection. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue emphasizes a sense of longing and nostalgia, with muted colors suggesting weight and depth. The contrast lies within the arrangement of densely packed pixels on the left, symbolizing loneliness, and scattered brighter pixels towards the right, suggesting fleeting connections. The layout of falling pixels echoes a rain-like effect, while broken pixels create a sense of imperfection, enhancing the themes of broken memory and fragmented connections.
