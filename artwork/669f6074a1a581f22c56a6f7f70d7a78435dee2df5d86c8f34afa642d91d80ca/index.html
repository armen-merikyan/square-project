Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances 'longing' and 'resolve,' embodied through juxtaposed colors creating an echo of pressure. The palette combines vibrant hues like hot coral and blood orange with muted tones such as charcoal, emphasizing an interplay of warmth and emptiness. The main collision occurs around the center, where patchwork resolves forcefully into thin threads of longing, suggesting both tension and a yearning for resolution amidst chaos. This arrangement reflects the complexities of longing and resolve depicted in the seed.
