Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the juxtaposition of vulnerability and strength, symbolizing protection through motifs of softness and density. The palette of muted colors, such as rain blue and lavender gray, evokes a melancholy wonder, creating a calm environment with sharp bursts of contrast. The main collision resides centrally, where the soft ring pattern of vulnerability meets the dense ash field of strength, suggesting a turbulent interplay of emotions. Broken pixels are subtly scattered, reinforcing the notion of imperfection and disruption amidst strong borders, echoing the push and pull between fragile openness and fortified stability.
