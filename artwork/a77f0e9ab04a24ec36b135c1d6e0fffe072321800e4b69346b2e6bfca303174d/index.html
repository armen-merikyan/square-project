Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between clarity and distortion, represented by a dark, structured upper half (signal) and a chaotic, scattered lower half (noise). The selected palette ranges from deep navy to dim cyan, reflecting the emotional depth of communication and interference. The contrast centers on a structured grid of dark colors above, interrupted by lighter, random dots of white and cyan below, embodying echoes of static. The broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions within the otherwise orderly grid, enhancing the narrative of fractured communication. Overall, this square embodies the dialogue between clarity and confusion, expressed through abstract color placement.
