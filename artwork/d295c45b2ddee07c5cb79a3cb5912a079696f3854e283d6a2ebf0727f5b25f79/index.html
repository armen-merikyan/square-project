Artist Statement

The emotional tension is derived from 'spark' and 'ash', embodying a struggle between hope and despair. The palette of mourning_black, deep_blue, new_leaf, pale_yellow, and stone represents the dichotomy of these emotions: deep blues and blacks signify ashes of restraint, while bright accents depict the bursts of spark. The layout creates pressure in the top left, with a jagged edge that interrupts the flow, symbolizing resilience amidst fragility. As bright lights collide with muted backgrounds, the overall pattern suggests a rhythmic push and pull, evoking the contrast between vibrancy and decay.
