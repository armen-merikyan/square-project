Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between defiance and submission, represented through contrasting colors that evoke strong feelings. The palette, consisting of bright reds and muted grays, highlights conflict and resolution, suggesting resistance against pressure. The primary collision occurs at the center, where vibrant colors intertwine, while the jagged edges represent the surrounding submission, creating an inward spiral effect. The broken pixels act as intentional disturbances, mirroring the disruption of the inner turmoil.
