Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dynamic between longing and resolve, using a palette that represents quiet pressure and disruption. The chosen colors of soft off-white and faint peach denote a sense of warmth and yearning, counterbalanced by the dark navy and compressed red, which symbolize strength and determination. The primary collision occurs in the central row where these emotional tones interact, creating a pulse-like rhythm against the backdrop of muted colors that serve as a reminder of the unresolved tension. The arrangement reflects the idea of split mirrors - close yet distant, with broken pixels introduced deliberately to reflect imperfections and disruptions. This evokes both serenity and urgency, capturing the essence of the emotional seed.
