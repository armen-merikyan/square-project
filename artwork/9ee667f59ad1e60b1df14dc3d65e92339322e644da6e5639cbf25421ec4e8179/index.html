Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity through dense clusters of color and contrasting open spaces. The dense patterns on the bottom invoke certainty, using dark hues to suggest pressure and organization, while the lighter colors above represent curiosity as they softly recede into space, suggesting a sense of exploration. The interplay between the broken pixels symbolizes the disruption of order, creating an echo of anxiety amidst moments of clarity. The main collision occurs at the center, where the dark tones clash with light tones, exhibiting both conflict and balance within the square.
