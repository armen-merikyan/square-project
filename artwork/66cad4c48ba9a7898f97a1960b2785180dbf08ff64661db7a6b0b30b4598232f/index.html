Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a push-and-pull between defiance and submission, represented through dynamic color placements. The palette combines cosmic indigo for depth and mystery, void black for the heavy absence of submission, star white as piercing contrast, cold gold for shimmering defiance, and deep teal as a calming undertone. The center features a dense cluster of darker colors to signify the anchored defiance, while lighter colors spiral outward, creating a tension-filled pattern that captures the essence of the seed's emotional narrative.
