Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between certainty and curiosity through a palette that evokes melancholy wonder. The cool blues and soft greys suggest depth and calm while accenting the chaos of curiosity through muted plums and highlighted whites. The main contrast lies in the lower left corner, with the strong presence of dark hues gradually giving way to lighter tones, creating a rising sensation. The wave shape seed is represented abstractly: motion upward toward certainty collides with curious points of interest, interrupted by lighter waveforms symbolizing exploration amid a structured bottom edge.
