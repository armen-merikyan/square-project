Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the juxtaposition of restlessness against peace, embodied in the contrasting palette of deep wine and ash gray. The placement of the colors follows a spiral layout, creating a dynamic push towards an off-center focal point, while maintaining negative space to evoke a sense of quietness. The glitch and pressure band patterns clash in structured chaos, allowing for moments of tension and calm to emerge, where bright accents confront muted fields.
