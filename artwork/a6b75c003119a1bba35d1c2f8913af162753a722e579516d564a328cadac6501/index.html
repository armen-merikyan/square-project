Artist Statement

This composition captures a clean, rhythmic essence of 'signal' that pierces through a chaotic 'noise,' represented by contrasting colors. The palette uses deep navy and dim cyan to convey a somber yet clear emotional landscape, interrupted by bursts of white, signifying a 'pulse' amid the disorder. The main collision of colors occurs centrally, emphasizing balance and tension between the calm (signal) and chaotic (noise), while broken pixels create intentional disruption, mirroring the theme of a failed connection within the square frame.
