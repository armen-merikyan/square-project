Artist Statement

The emotional tone is one of juxtaposition between softness and severity, represented by a palette of contrasting colors. The warm whites and powder pinks denote gentleness, while the carbon black and steel gray signify sharp contrasts, creating a tension that captures the essence of guilt and forgiveness. The flow mimics falling pixels, with density at the top giving way to lighter hues, reflecting a descent from severity to softness. The central collision at the row 4 position highlights an interruption with spikes of sharp crimson breaking through the softer background, amplifying the pulse marks against the radial pattern.
