Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and chaos, symbolized by the interplay of deep blacks and electric hues. The palette was chosen to evoke a sense of technological interference ('signal') with vibrant 'noise' through color contrasts. The main collision of 'signal' green against 'static gray' occurs centrally, highlighting the pulse of a singular path disrupted by surrounding 'noise.' This arrangement captures the essence of the seed as abstract, transforming elements like 'exposure' and 'memory' into emotional patterns of rhythm and interruption.
