Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between loneliness and connection, encapsulated in a palette of deep, muted colors suggesting darkness and isolation with shimmering accents to evoke fleeting connections. The soft edges create a gentle flow outward from the center, where the main contrast lies between the dense navy and the light dim cyan, forming a visual ripple that echoes the theme of emotional collisions. The composition utilizes negative space strategically, allowing the viewer to reflect on feelings of emptiness and the desire for companionship, represented by the split between cool and warm tones.
