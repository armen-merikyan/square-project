Artist Statement

This composition embodies a tension between joy and grief, represented through a palette of warm yellows and oranges symbolizing joy, contrasted with deep blues and muted purples evoking grief. The layout features upward and downward movements, creating a dynamic tension. The colors interact in a way that highlights their emotional collision, with dense patterns in the lower half signifying heaviness (grief) versus expanding forms in the upper half representing joy. Broken pixels add an element of imperfection, creating a sense of fragility within the vibrant emotion.
