Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between 'longing'—characterized by soft, distant hues of blue and gray—and 'resolve', symbolized through strong bold accents and firm lines of orange and black. The layout emphasizes a split across the horizontal axis, capturing a blend of longing's quiet space with resolve's steady strength. The stark contrast between the top and bottom rows indicates a collision of emotional states: the longing emerges as a gentle ring pattern, while the resolve comes forth as a veil-like drape. The use of broken pixels punctuates the composition, suggesting imperfections and vulnerabilities. The chosen palette reflects clarity amidst confusion, creating a dynamic yet coherent piece of abstract art.
