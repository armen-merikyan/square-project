Artist Statement

The emotional tone reveals a dichotomy of confusion and clarity, captured through contrasting dense clusters of deep blue and muted purple against pockets of yellow and orange. The palette is intentional—warmth from yellows and oranges embodies clarity, while deep blues and purples suggest the complexities of confusion. The main contrast occurs at the center where the warm colors clash with the cooler tones, echoing the tension between clarity and confusion. The broken pixels seem to fall outward, leading the viewer away from the center, reinforcing the concept of confusion by disrupting symmetry.
