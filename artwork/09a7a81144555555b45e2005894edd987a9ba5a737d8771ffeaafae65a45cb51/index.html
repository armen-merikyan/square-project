Artist Statement

This composition reflects an emotional journey from fear to hope using a color palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green. The arrangement creates a diagonal spread, representing a gradual shift from the dense, oppressive weight of darkness to the lightness of soft hope. The use of dots for 'fear' and rain-like patterns for 'hope' evokes tension that contrasts against an empty center. Collision occurs where these patterns meet, suggesting a struggle that resolves into light. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, enhancing the deeper emotional resonance of the piece.
