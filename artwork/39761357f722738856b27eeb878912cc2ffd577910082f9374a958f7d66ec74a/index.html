Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the duality of restlessness and peace through a deliberate juxtaposition of colors and patterns. The palette combines soft tones of powder pink and warm white with stark contrasts of carbon black and sharp crimson. This reflects the emotional tension of movement against stillness. The 'falling_pixels' layout creates a dynamic flow where colors fracture and collide, representing the interplay of memory and motion. The central collision occurs between the warm whites and sharp crimsons, while the surrounding blacks and grays depict the unsettled nature of the edges. This creates a patchwork effect that conveys both a sense of longing and calm spaces.
