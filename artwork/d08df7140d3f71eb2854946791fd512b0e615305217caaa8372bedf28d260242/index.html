Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between nostalgia and progress through a dichotomy of warm and cool colors. The palette features rich dark maroon and vivid red, contrasting with soft gray and pink, symbolizing the passionate clash between the yearning for the past and the drive for advancement. The upper rows are weighted with reds, signifying warmth and nostalgia, while the lower rows introduce grays, representing a cool, progressive outlook. This split emphasizes the emotional battle as colors drip and collide, creating a harmonious yet fragmented visual journey, evoking both memory and aspiration. The composition plays on negative space, creating an echo of unresolved emotions.
