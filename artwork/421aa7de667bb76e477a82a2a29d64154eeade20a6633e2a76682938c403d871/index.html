Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between clarity and chaos, akin to signals fighting through noise. The palette juxtaposes vibrant greens (signal) against muted grays and blacks (noise). The central arrangement showcases a lattice of green interrupted by bursts of hot magenta, representing moments of clarity amidst static. The rising visual flow illustrates the struggle to break free from the dense noise, with edges burned to symbolize the scars left by interference. The structured chaos becomes a dance of memory and erasure, portraying the journey through exposure and shelter.
