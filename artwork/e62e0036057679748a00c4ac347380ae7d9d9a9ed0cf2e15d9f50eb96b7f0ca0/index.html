Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between restlessness and peace through the interplay of deep saturation and muted tones, with colors representing a struggle for expansiveness against a confining border. The palette consists of safe, gentle hues alongside bold contrasts to capture both quiet moments and interruptions, echoing the emotional dynamics suggested in the seed. The main collision of feelings, where rich colors pierce through the quieter fields, occurs mainly toward the center-left, creating a ripple effect that suggests movement and memory through diagonal stripes against a backdrop of rippling calm.
