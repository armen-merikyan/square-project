Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between softness and severity, utilizing a palette of gentle pastels against stark blacks and greys. The soft-grid pattern battles with pulse marks, creating a dynamic contrast at the center—where severity punctuates softness. The black and dark gray on one side embodies severity, while warmer whites and soft pinks emanate softness, allowing for a quiet chaos that expresses unresolved feelings of guilt and forgiveness. The layout features broken edges, symbolizing emotional leakages, adding to the layered story being told.
