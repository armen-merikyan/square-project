Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, encapsulated in a dynamic flow with a central collision. The colors blend warmth and cool undertones, establishing a narrative of exposure versus protection. Accents of cold slate (#4B7BE5) push against the warm coral (#FF6F61), suggesting stress points between elements of softness and assertiveness. The patterns of dots represent strength and stability, while the veil pattern captures the delicate threads of vulnerability. Broken pixels create imperfections, reinforcing the theme of emotional exposure within the solid structure of resilience.
