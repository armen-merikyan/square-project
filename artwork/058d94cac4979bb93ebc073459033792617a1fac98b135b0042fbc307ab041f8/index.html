Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the delicate push and pull of emptiness versus desire, where the linear flow from left to right embodies the rising signal of desire against the stagnant backdrop of emptiness. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue conveys a sense of nostalgia and longing. The main contrast occurs along the edge where the warm tones clash with the cool, creating an interrupted flow that mirrors the seed's theme of collision between ambition (desire) and hollowness (emptiness). The pattern, utilizing broken dots for desire and a stair-like arrangement for emptiness, creates an imbalance, evoking a sense of fragmented emotion and unresolved tension.
