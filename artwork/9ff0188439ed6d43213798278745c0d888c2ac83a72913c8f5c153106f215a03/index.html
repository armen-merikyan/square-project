Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional balance between vulnerability and strength. The palette features burnt orange and gold to evoke warmth and familiarity contrasted with dark red and charcoal, symbolizing depth and tension. The main collision occurs in the center, where pulse marks (strength) break through the surrounding soft threads (vulnerability). Each pixel arrangement echoes a push and pull between safety and exposure, creating a visual representation of conflict and resolution.
