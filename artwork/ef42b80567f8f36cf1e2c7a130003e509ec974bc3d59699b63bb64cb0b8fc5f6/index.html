Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between emptiness and desire, represented through contrasting colors. The chosen palette of deep black and dark violet suggests a void, while soft gold and pale green symbolize longing and hope. The left side employs a maze pattern to evoke emptiness with dark tones, while the right side mirrors the pulse of desire with a warm, golden reflection, creating a dynamic interplay of feeling. Hard edges on the transition evoke the fragile nature of shifting emotions, where desire collides with the stark reality of emptiness.
