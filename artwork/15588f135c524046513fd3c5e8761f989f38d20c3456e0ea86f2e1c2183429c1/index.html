Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the clash between clarity and confusion through a contrasting palette of electric blue and lime against a deep black. The arrangement reflects a rising signal, creating a sense of climbing tension while simultaneously presenting interruptions characterized by broken pixels. The use of symmetry vs. break emphasizes the emotional conflict at play, where pockets of clarity in lime clash with the obscurity of black and the disarray of magenta. This juxtaposition drives the visual narrative of confusion meeting clarity, where dynamic sections invoke a pulse-like rhythm, creating a compelling journey through the chaos.
