Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured is a delicate balance between confusion and clarity. The palette incorporates warm yellows and cool blues that engage in a visual tug-of-war, illustrating emotional tension. The contrast occurs through a broken symmetry—one side pulsating with warmth while the other feels cool and disconnected. The arrangement of colors uses both orderly patterns and scattered disruptions, creating a sense of falling pixels that echoes the theme of disruption against clarity. This composition captures the emotional dichotomy by utilizing layered clusters of muted and vibrant colors, evoking feelings of both chaos and calm.
