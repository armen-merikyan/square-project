Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between love and betrayal, using a warm palette of pinks and reds contrasted with cold grays and dark maroons. The left side embodies love through a staircase pattern, where shapes rise warmly, while the right features a grid representing betrayal, with sharp edges disrupting the symmetry. The slight imperfections in the pattern symbolize the chaos in relationships, presenting a visual clash that evokes both attraction and disillusionment.
