Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between clarity and chaos, embodying the struggle of a distinct signal amidst overwhelming noise. The palette of deep blacks, vibrant greens, and electric blues brings a balance between darkness and vibrant signals, emphasizing the core theme. The dominant use of black represents noise and confusion while the patches of green and blue emerge as glimmers of clarity and focus, creating a sense of conflict. This is visually accented by occasional bursts of hot magenta, acting as interruptions in the flow, resembling fleeting thoughts or moments of clarity. The central collision between these elements invites the viewer to experience both the static interference and the bright signals of hope amidst the chaos.
