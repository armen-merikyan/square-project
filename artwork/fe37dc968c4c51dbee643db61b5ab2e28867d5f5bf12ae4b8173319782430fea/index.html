Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette, featuring warm coral and cold slate, symbolizes the warmth of connection and the chill of isolation. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense clusters of warm coral burst against a soft amber background, reflecting the struggle between intimacy and distance. The broken pixels create disruptions, suggesting imperfections in communication and the fragility of relationships. This arrangement fosters a spiraling flow of emotion, resonating with the seed's themes of tension and contrast.
