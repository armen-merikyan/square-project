Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between envy and gratitude. The chosen palette of sepia tones and muted colors suggests the weight of memories, contrasting the lighter shades which represent moments of gratitude. The jagged edges symbolize the 'broken mirror' aspect of the seed, creating a sense of disruption. Patterns clash in a zigzag flow, while negative space emphasizes the emotional void between contrasting feelings. The arrangement balances heavier, darker areas with lighter spots, encapsulating the struggle of finding peace amid turmoil.
