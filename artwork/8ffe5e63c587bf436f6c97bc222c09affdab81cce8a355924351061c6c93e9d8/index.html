Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension of 'quiet' versus 'pressure' through stark contrasts and conflicting patterns. The left side, representing 'quiet', features soft colors with gentle ripples, while the right side embraces deeper, denser shades expressing pressure. The palette combines off-white and faint peach for calmness against compressed red and dark navy that evoke intensity. The main collision occurs along the vertical split, where the soft ripples of the quieter shades clash with the harsh, rigid rings of the pressure palette, creating a distinct divide that embodies the seed's themes of symmetry contrasting with breaks.
