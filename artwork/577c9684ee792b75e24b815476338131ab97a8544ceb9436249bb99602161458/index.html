Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a battle between joy's brightness and the heaviness of grief. The palette of bright yellows and oranges contrasts sharply against muted purples and deep blues, highlighting this duality. The arrangement employs a rising signal layout, symbolizing hope alongside despair, with broken pixels representing imperfection in both states. The joy pattern of mazes weaves through the grief's cracks, creating tension in the center—it's a subtle push and pull of emotions, represented by the sharper left-weighting contrasted with the rising flow.
