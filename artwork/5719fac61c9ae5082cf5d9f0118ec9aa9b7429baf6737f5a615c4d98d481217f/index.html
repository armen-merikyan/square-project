Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between clear signals and overwhelming noise, symbolizing a struggle for clarity amidst chaos. The palette, dominated by deep blacks, electric blues, and vibrant magentas, reflects this dichotomy. The center pulse of green amidst static gray evokes the feeling of a single heartbeat against a backdrop of interference. This artwork's layout embraces negative space to enhance the sense of isolation and interruption, allowing the viewer to appreciate the contrast between the bright signal and the surrounding noise. Patterns intersect, with magenta sparks disrupting the quiet green, illustrating the collision of memories and echoes, and adding dynamic tension to the composition.
