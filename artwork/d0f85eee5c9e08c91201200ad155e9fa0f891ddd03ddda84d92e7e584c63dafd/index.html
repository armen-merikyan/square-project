Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured in this artwork reflects the dualities of loneliness and connection, as suggested by the edge-focused composition. Utilizing a palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon, the stark contrast between the surrounding darker hues and the soft pink at the center signifies moments of emotional pressure and slow release. Main collisions occur at the periphery where dark maroon and gray collide, creating a tension that reflects the struggle of emotions. The layout hints at a small star at the center while keeping it abstract, maintaining negative space to evoke a quest for connection amidst loneliness.
