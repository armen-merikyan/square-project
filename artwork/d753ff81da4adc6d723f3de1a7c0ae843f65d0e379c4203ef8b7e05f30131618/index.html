Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of vulnerability and strength, with the use of soft and severe colors representing these contrasting feelings. The palette combines warmer pinks and whites with harsher blacks and grays to create dynamic tension. The main collision occurs centrally where the two patterns (stairs and crosshatch) overlap, suggesting a struggle between exposure and protection. The left-right mirroring layout features slight disruptions to emphasize imperfection, aligning with the seed's theme of tension.
