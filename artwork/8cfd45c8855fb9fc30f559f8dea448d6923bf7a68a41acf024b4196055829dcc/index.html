Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between envy and gratitude, using a palette of red and pink to signify warm, intense feelings while gray and dark maroon provide depth and contrast. The composition features a rigid outer border contrasted by a chaotic inner section, symbolizing the push and pull between emotional stability and disarray. The soft edges of the outer layer are infused with gentle colors, gradually giving way to more vibrant and tumultuous tones, mimicking a rhythmic clash of sentiments.
