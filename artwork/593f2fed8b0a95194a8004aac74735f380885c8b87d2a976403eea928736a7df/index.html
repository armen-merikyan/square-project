Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional clash between chaos and control through contrasting colors. The chosen palette of dark violet and soft gold represents the weight of panic against the hopeful spark of control. The placement creates density at the center, suggesting pressure, while the edges dissolve into more muted tones, echoing an unresolved longing. The zigzag pattern introduces tension, creating moments of fracture and flow as it navigates the canvas, embodying the fight between order and chaos.
