Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the tension between emptiness and desire. The selected palette includes deep blacks and dark violets to represent emptiness, balanced with soft golds and pale greens symbolizing desire. The main contrast emerges through a scattered flow of soft gold pixels amidst dense dark hues, suggesting a signal trying to rise against the weight of empty space. This configuration creates an echo of longing, framed by negative space to enhance the feeling of isolation while still hinting at the potential for connection.
