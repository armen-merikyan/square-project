Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between quiet and pressure by contrasting dense clusters of dark colors with softer, softer hues. The palette features deep navy for pressure combined with muted peach and gray tones, creating a balanced play of shadow and light. The main collision occurs in the center, where channels of dark merge into a clearer space, mimicking the conflict of pressure releasing into quiet. This setup resonates with the seed's themes of diagonal arguments and fragmentation, manifesting in the asymmetry and unexpected juxtapositions of color placement.
