Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between quietness and pressure. The palette combines muted tones (off-white and dust gray) to create a base of tranquility, which is pierced by sharp, contrasting colors (compressed red and dark navy). The main collision occurs in the lower-left corner where the weight of the colors suggests pressure, while the upper right offers a quiet expanse, inviting contemplation. The arrangement reflects a wave of energy crashing against calmness—a visual representation of chaos meeting serenity, with broken pixels serving as reminders of the imperfections in this dance.
