Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between vulnerability and strength, reflected in the contrasting use of colors. The palette features warm coral and soft amber to symbolize vulnerability, countered by the cold slate and blue-black, which evoke strength. The main collision occurs diagonally from the top left to the bottom right corner, where warm tones invade cooler spaces, creating an emotional interplay. The broken pixels intentionally disrupt the flow, akin to life's unpredictable nature, suggesting areas of vulnerability amidst comforting strength.
