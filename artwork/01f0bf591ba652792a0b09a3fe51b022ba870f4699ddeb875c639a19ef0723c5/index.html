Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy between clarity and confusion. The palette, rooted in deep navy and contrasting dim cyan, establishes a sense of lonely signal amidst the chaos. The composition flows from the top, with pixel clusters representing falling sensations of confusion, contrasted by lighter pixels representing clarity. Asymmetric placements create moments of interruption, where dark colors collide unexpectedly with hints of brightness, evoking a feeling of struggle between order and disarray, mirroring the seed's themes of symmetry versus brokenness.
