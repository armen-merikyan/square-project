Artist Statement

This artwork embodies a tension between signal and noise, with a predominant focus on readable rhythm amid chaotic elements. The palette, featuring a balance of signal green and static gray with accents of hot magenta and electric blue, underscores the push and pull of clarity versus interference. The main collision occurs in the center where smooth patterns of signal clash with fractal disruptions of noise, emphasizing the struggle for coherence in a world of chaos. Broken pixels scatter throughout to reflect the imperfections and scars of experience, creating subtle disruptions in an otherwise rhythmic flow.
