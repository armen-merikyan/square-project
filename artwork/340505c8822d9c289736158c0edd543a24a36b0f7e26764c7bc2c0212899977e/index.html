Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the contrasts between vulnerability and strength, illustrated through the choice of a 'lonely_signal' palette featuring deep navy and dim cyan, symbolizing the sheltering aspect, while pure black and white highlight exposure. The split vertical layout creates a clear division, allowing the patterns of strength (threshold) to occupy the right side, reinforced by darker tones, while the left—representing vulnerability—features fragmented and lighter thread patterns. The main contrast occurs at the central division, where light and dark collide, suggesting a struggle and interplay between feelings of security and exposure, creating a dynamic, resonant narrative.
