Artist Statement

The artwork encapsulates the emotional struggle between defiance and submission through a dramatic interplay of colors. The palette combines saturated alarm red and pale aqua, representing defiance and calm submission, respectively. The sharp contrast between bright and muted fields creates a dynamic tension, reflecting the conflict inherent in the seed. The placement of broken pixels adds to the sense of chaos and interruption, while maintaining a significant negative space, highlighting the struggle. The composition flows outward from the pressured corner, drawing the eye into a spiral of colors transitioning between resistance and calmness, embodying the themes of invasion and weight.
