Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional narrative of struggle between control and surrender through contrasting color density and pixel patterns. The warm tones of yellow and orange signify bursts of control and dominance, while muted purples and deep blues suggest yielding and calmness. The center is densely packed, representing an overflow of emotions, surrounded by quieter edges that create a sense of surrender. The arrangement of patterns is uneven, with abrupt disruptions mimicking the tension of stuttered steps.
