Artist Statement

The composition reflects a balance of confusion and clarity through a descent of colors reminiscent of falling pixels. The palette of navy, black, and dim cyan creates a moody contrast, suggesting depth and loneliness, while the intermittent use of white represents moments of clarity breaking through confusion. This contrasting rhythm creates a pulsing effect, where the chaotic organization of darker colors interacts with the brighter ones, depicting a struggle between emotional clarity and confusion. The 'thick outline' represented by dense colors reinforces the tension at the center, while soft edges manage to allow negative space that enhances the impact of the 'echoing' effect.
