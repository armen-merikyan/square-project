Artist Statement

This artwork employs the duality of confusion and clarity. The palette uses muted tones of sepia and dust gray to reflect a sense of nostalgia and broken memory, while accents of faded blue punctuate moments of clarity amidst the noise. The composition begins with a dense cluster at the center and gradually fades outward, evoking physical and emotional tension as the colors intertwine. The broken pixels create an imperfect, echoing rhythm across the grid, enhancing the feeling of unresolved motion. The stark contrast between the dense and hollow areas mirrors the struggle between clarity and confusion, resulting in a uniquely abstract narrative.
