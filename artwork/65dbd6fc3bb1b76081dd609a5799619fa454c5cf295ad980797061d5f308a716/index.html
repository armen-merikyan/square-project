Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tone of 'spark vs ash' through a juxtaposition of vibrant and muted colors. The palette selections of lavender gray for ash and soft cyan for spark create a warm vs cool tension, with a prevailing density of cool tones that dominate 65% of the square. The layout evokes falling pixels, with the color clusters suggesting scattered sparks amidst the calm grayness of ash. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, echoing themes of resilience and fragility. The main contrast occurs diagonally, enhancing the dynamic contrast between the two emotional states.
