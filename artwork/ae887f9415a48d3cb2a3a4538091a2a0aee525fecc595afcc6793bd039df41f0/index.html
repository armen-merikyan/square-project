Artist Statement

This artwork interprets the tension between confusion and clarity through a duality in the composition. The top half embodies confusion with tightly woven maze patterns in dark colors, symbolizing density and the noise of overlapping paths. The bottom half contrasts with sharp white horizontal stripes, representing clarity and stability. The palette shifts from navy and black to a slighter clarity with dim cyan and white, mirroring the emotional spectrum from tension to resolution. The use of broken pixels in the maze introduces disruptions, creating a dialogue about imperfection amidst confusion.
