Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of safety and exposure through a rising signal that embodies both vulnerability and warm enclosure. The palette features soft greens and browns to represent shelter, while brighter hues of pale skin and cold white reflect the theme of exposure. The contrast of stepped patterns between the two emotions creates distinct collisions, framed by an eroded border, signifying the fragility of safety. The palette functions to illustrate a balance between warmth and stark vulnerability, echoing the themes of pressure and release.
