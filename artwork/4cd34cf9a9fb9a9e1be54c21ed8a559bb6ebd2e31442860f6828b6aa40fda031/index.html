Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between nostalgia and progress, creating a visual narrative of rise and fall. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue captures both warm nostalgia and cool progress, allowing for a dynamic interplay. The contrast is strongest in the middle of the composition, where lighter shades collide with darker tones, suggesting the struggle between clinging to the past and moving forward. The layout emphasizes a downward flow, while broken pixel placements introduce an element of imperfection, aligning with the theme of fragmented memories.
