Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tug-of-war between 'emptiness' and 'desire’. The palette moves through cooler grays to warms of pink and dark maroon, suggesting a struggle from isolation to yearning. The patterns shift diagonally, simulating a rhythmic rise of desire contrasted with descending emptiness. The use of glowing edges creates a visual tension between voids and hints of warmth, allowing the piece to breathe while maintaining a 10% negative space, symbolizing the unresolved nature of the emotional conflict.
