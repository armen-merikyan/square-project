Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength within structured chaos. The chosen palette of shelter green, warm brown, pale skin, cold white, and open blue creates layers of warmth and coolness, reflecting emotional contrasts. The dominant hues are warm, creating a sense of shelter, while the cooler colors suggest exposure. The central collision occurs between dense, warm clusters and open, soft areas, emphasizing the fragile threshold that encapsulates strength. The broken pixels represent disruptions, symbolizing how vulnerability can manifest within the strength, creating a fragmented, abstract landscape.
