Artist Statement

This piece navigates the emotional spectrum between defiance and submission, with the upper half presenting strong contrasts reminiscent of pressure through a sharp, stair-like pattern in red and gray tones. The bottom half transitions to a softer, checker pattern in muted pinks and dark maroon, suggesting moments of quiet submission amidst the chaos. The broken pixels throughout instill a sense of imperfection, enhancing the theme of emotional collisions, while maintaining a deliberate asymmetry. The palette reflects a struggle between warmth and coolness, driving the narrative of conflicting emotions.
