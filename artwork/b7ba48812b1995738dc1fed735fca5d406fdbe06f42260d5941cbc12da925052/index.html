Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts trust and suspicion, represented by a soft palette of muted colors. The color choices reflect inner warmth and gentle disturbance; pale tones give a sense of trust, while deeper hues emerge in patches, symbolizing suspicion. The dominant presence of faint peach and off-white suggests calmness, while compressed red emerges like a sudden sharp thought, creating visual tension from the corner. This layout allows for an impactful collision of emotions as overlapping patterns evoke resilience and fragility, creating a narrative of an inner struggle.
