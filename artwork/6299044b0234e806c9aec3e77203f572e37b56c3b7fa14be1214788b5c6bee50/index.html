Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances restlessness and peace through uneven pulses of vibrant and muted colors. The palette, rooted in broken memory shades, emphasizes the clash of static and flow, enhancing the fragmentary sense of memory versus movement. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where active threads of color cut through tranquil fields, embodying the central theme of tension against calm. The intentionally placed broken pixels introduce disruptions, adding depth to the quiet channel layout.
