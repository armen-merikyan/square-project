Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between restlessness and peace, with a palette that evokes intimacy and distance. The contrast happens diagonally, where warm shadows collide against cool blues, symbolizing conflict and harmony. The arrangement leans heavily on fluid patterns of rings and constellations, suggesting motion amidst a calm backdrop, reflecting the seed's themes of memory and interruption via abstract emotion.
