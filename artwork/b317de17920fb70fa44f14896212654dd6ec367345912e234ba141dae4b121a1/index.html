Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts feelings of relief and dread. A warm color palette with yellow and orange illustrates relief against cooler shades of deep blue and muted purple that evoke dread. The arrangement reflects a top-weighted pressure, with lighter hues positioned towards the top, clashing with deeper tones at the base—a visual metaphor for emotional struggle. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the narrative of discordance.
