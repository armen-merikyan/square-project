Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional nuance between defiance and submission by utilizing a palette that oscillates between cool and warm tones. Muted plum provides a gentle field base, representing submission, while vibrant rain blue disrupts the calmness, signifying defiance. The pattern diverges diagonally, invoking a sense of motion and tension, as colors collide and merge. The center features higher intensity colors that serve as a point of punctuated resistance against softer, receding edges, emphasizing the theme of open vs closed within the space. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfection, allowing for visual echoes of chaos against a backdrop of order, creating a visual threshold that is both inviting and challenging.
