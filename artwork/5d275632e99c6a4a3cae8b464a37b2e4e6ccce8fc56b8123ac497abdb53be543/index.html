Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between love and betrayal through a contrasting palette of warm pinks and reds against cool grays and dark maroon. The arrangement captures rising aspiration while suggesting unsettling cracks and interruptions, symbolizing the fragile balance within relationships. Love emerges in soft, rhythmic patterns while betrayal interjects sharp angles and broken spaces, illustrating conflict and resolution within the abstract narrative.
