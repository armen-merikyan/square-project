Artist Statement

The emotional tone draws from the contrast of defiance and submission. The palette combines warm and cool tones to create visual tension, with warm shadow representing defiance and cool blue indicating submission. The main collision occurs at the center with dense colors battling against the quiet edge spaces, highlighting the internal struggle. The spiral pattern reflects a motion of pressure and release, directed inward to symbolize the constant push and pull of emotions. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional imperfection, reinforcing the theme of tension.
