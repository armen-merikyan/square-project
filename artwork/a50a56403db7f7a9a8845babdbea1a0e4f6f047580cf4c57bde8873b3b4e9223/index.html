Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between defiance and submission, visualized through contrasting colors that evoke tension. The selected palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple creates a dialogue between warmth and coolness, enhancing the emotional complexity. The main collision occurs in the center, where the 'broken_line' shape is visually represented by the harsh division of dark versus light colors, creating a pressure point. The surrounding pattern echoes the themes of rain and rings, leading to a sense of cascading turmoil and emotional release within a structured pattern, while adhering to the 10% negative space rule.
