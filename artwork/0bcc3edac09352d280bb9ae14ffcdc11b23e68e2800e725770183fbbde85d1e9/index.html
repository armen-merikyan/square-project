Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between confusion and clarity. The chosen palette of bright yellow and muted purple symbolizes joy battling grief, with deep blue serving as an anchor reflecting calm amidst chaos. In the layout, the outer frame represents clarity through a split screen, contrasting with a dense, noise pattern in the center that evokes confusion. Key collisions occur at the boundary between the vibrant outside and murky inner square, creating a sense of pulse and interruption, where the pixels seem to fall and collide.
