Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a juxtaposition of defiance and submission, illustrated through bold color contrasts and jagged edges. The palette invokes feelings of fear (dark violet and black) tempered by hope (soft gold and pale green), aligning with the themes of weight and lightness. The clashes between the left side's mirror patterns and the right side's flame patterns create dynamic tension, making the viewer navigate between open spaces and sudden clusters. The arrangement embraces negative space while suggesting a sense of unresolved motion and emotional collision.
