Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The use of a warm color palette (pinks and reds) depicts warmth and intimacy, while the gray and dark maroon accents provide depth and weight of isolation. The arrangement features a dominant off-center warm core, radiating towards the upper corners, suggesting an 'invasion' of connection overcoming the void of loneliness. This is abstracted through a burst pattern, echoing the seed's essence of ancient fire. The interaction of the colors forms a visual pulse, reflecting the paradox of seeking connection amidst loneliness.
