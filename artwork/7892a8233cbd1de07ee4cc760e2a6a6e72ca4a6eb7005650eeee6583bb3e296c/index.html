Artist Statement

The composition embodies a dialogue between restlessness and peace. The chosen palette of soft powders and sharp accents creates a contrast between a gentle, expansive field (#FFFFFF, #FAD8D9) and deeper, anxious tones (#CC4B4B, #333333). Emulating the tension of restless memories, the central collision of vivid crimson against cool greys implies disruption, as sharp crimson patterns illustrate movement while warm whites provide a grounding space. The arrangement diverges into the corners from the center, suggesting a push-pull dynamic of emotions. Broken pixels create intentional imperfections reinforcing the theme of memory, further enhancing the sense of unease against a calm backdrop.
