Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between pressure and quiet, with a dense arrangement of colors in the center transitioning to softer tones at the edges. The palette balances off-white and faint peach to evoke a sense of calm overcoming chaos, while dark navy and compressed red introduce sharp contrasts, creating a visual conflict. The collision of these patterns emerges from the zigzag flow, where densely aggregated dark tones at the center give way to lighter, mirrored regions on the periphery, representing a shattering of silence against a heavy tide.
