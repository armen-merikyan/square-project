Artist Statement

The artwork expresses the tension between exposure and safety through a contrasting palette of 'shelter_green' and 'pale_skin,' representing a protective barrier against vulnerability. The central vertical split emphasizes the collision of patterns; the negative cross overlaps with stacked forms, creating rhythmic pressure along the center while leaving spaces of openness. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, suggesting scars from past encounters. The balance of emotional tones creates a dynamic interplay of calmness and tension, capturing the essence of safe exposure.
