Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between joy and grief through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette includes vibrant yellows and oranges symbolizing joy, juxtaposed with deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The zigzag pattern of joy against the checker-like texture of grief creates visual conflict, aligning with the seed's themes of contrast and disruption. The layout starts from a corner, symbolizing an invasion of joy into the heavier grief area, with broken pixels adding imperfections that hint at the complexities of emotion.
