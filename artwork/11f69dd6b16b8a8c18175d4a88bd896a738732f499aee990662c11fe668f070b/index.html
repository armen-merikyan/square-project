Artist Statement

This abstract composition conveys the emotional tension between tenderness and rage, utilizing a palette of cool blues and warm reds. The collision occurs in the center, symbolizing the struggle between protection and attack, with soft hues providing contrast against sharp, jagged lines. Broken pixels punctuate the flow, representing interruptions in both emotions, while the descending layout mimics rain, suggesting a cathartic release. The overall structure retains readability and evokes a sense of layered complexity, aligning with the seed's themes of internal struggle and external expression.
