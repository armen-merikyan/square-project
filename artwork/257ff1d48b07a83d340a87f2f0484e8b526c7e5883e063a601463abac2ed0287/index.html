Artist Statement

The composition reflects the emotional dichotomy between panic and control through contrasting patterns. The warm yellows and oranges represent energy and intensity, while deep blue and muted purple serve as a calming counterbalance, signifying control amidst chaos. The interplay of structured grids and erratic stairs embodies the conflict at the center versus the edges of the canvas, leading to a visual tension that suggests an emotional struggle between stability and anxiety. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the sense of unease while maintaining a touch of negative space to prevent overwhelming density.
