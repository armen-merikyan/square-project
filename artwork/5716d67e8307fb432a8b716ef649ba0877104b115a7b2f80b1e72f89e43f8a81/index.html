Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between nostalgia's warmth and the sharpness of progress. The palette captures the tension with yellows and oranges symbolizing hope and grief contrasted with deep blues and muted purples representing introspection and weight. The main contrast occurs in the lower rows, where warm colors drip upwards against cooler hues, suggesting a rising signal of hope battling against the weight of nostalgia. Driven by the seed's themes, this abstract pattern embodies an echoing motion of unresolved tension.
