Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a collision of bright and muted colors. The palette incorporates ripe green and acid green to represent the vibrant energy of defiance, while deep olive and cream symbolize the softness of submission. The contrast occurs diagonally from the corner, where bold colors surge upward against a backdrop of quieter tones. The use of broken pixels introduces an element of disruption, aligning with the themes of chaos and order, fostering an unsettled yet compelling visual experience.
