Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between yearning and limitation, with a palette that reflects warmth and intensity of desire against the cool, muted tones representing restraint. The saturated colors in the center create a powerful pull towards the edges, which are framed by darker, sealed colors creating a locked-in feeling. The tension is emphasized by the patched arrangement of warm hues conflicting with the structured ladder-like cool tones, suggesting a struggle between freedom and confinement. The broken pixel elements serve as reminders of imperfection within the emotional states depicted, further enhancing the narrative of desire held back by restraint.
