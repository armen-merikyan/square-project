Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional interplay between vulnerability and strength. The palette of deep black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green reflects themes of fear and hope. Strong geometric patterns clash at the center, where the darker colors embody the weight of strength, while soft gold and pale green punctuate the composition, suggesting moments of exposure and warmth. The collision occurs centrally, creating a dynamic tension that draws the eye and evokes the struggle between opposing forces.
