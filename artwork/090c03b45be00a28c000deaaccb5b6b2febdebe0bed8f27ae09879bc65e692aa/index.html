Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy between fear and hope through contrasting colors and textures. The dark corners represent fear, while soft gold hints at rising hope. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes charged emotional states. Jagged edges create a sense of anxiety, while the gentle stripes suggest a soft yet persistent hope. The placement of colors favors a corner invasion, creating tension as darker colors push inward, while lighter colors attempt to rise against them, suggesting conflict and resolution within the same space.
