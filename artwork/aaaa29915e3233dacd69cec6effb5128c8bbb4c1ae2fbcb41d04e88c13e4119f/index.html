Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'longing vs resolve' is captured through contrasting dim and vibrant colors within a structured yet chaotic grid. The palette combines deep navy and black for weight and seriousness, interspersed with soft dim cyan to evoke serenity and tension. The center presents a collision of patterns—longing's fault lines gently intersect with the firmness of resolve’s crosshatch, creating a dynamic center with lit edges framing the surrounding quiet field. The broken pixels interject as subtle reminders of damage amid the resolve, representing the unresolved feelings present in the center's chaos. This ambient interplay reflects the emotional struggle and delicate tension of trying to reconcile fractured experiences with desired steadiness.
