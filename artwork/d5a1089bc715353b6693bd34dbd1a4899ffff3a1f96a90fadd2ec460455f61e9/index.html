Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the tension between longing and resolve, expressed through an interplay of dark maroon, red, pink, and gray hues. The palette conveys a journey from warmth to the coolness of reservation. The main collision occurs at the center, where crosshatch patterns overlap with echo marks, representing the struggle between contained emotions and the urge to overflow. By incorporating broken pixels within the borders, the piece resonates with imperfections, suggesting damage yet striving for repair. The layout creates a hollow center ringed by colors to evoke a sense of longing and unresolved feelings in the abstract arrangement.
