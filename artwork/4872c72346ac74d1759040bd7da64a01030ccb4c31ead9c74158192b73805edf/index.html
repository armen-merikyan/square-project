Artist Statement

This composition embodies the conflicting emotions of fear and hope through a striking contrast of dark and light colors. The palette combines dark violet and black to evoke the tension of fear, while soft gold and pale green represent the glimmer of hope. The layout is centered, ideal for contrasting the dense edges with a lighter core. The jagged edges create an unease, while the crosshatch pattern signifies a complex interplay between the two emotional states—layered yet fractured. The presence of negative space provides a breath of calm, suggesting the weight of fear countered by the lightness of hope, captured in the abstract arrangement.
