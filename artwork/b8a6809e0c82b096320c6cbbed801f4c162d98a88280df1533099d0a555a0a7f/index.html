Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between desire and restraint, reflected through a warm color palette with a core conflict at the center. The chosen colors evoke readiness and longing, contrasting with muted edges to signify barriers and restraint. The use of saturated hues for desire collides with darker, restrained shades at the corners, creating a sense of depth and tension. The spiral and lattice motifs reflect how the essence of these emotions intertwine, embodying an unresolved push and pull that resonates with the seed's themes of pressure and sealed boundaries.
