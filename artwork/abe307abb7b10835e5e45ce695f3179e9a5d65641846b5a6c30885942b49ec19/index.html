Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension of control and surrender through a contrasting palette of warm ivory, stain brown, deep wine, and soft gold shades. The center appears overfilled with dark colors representing pressure, while the edges utilize lighter tones to suggest a yielding softness. The collision of structured checker patterns in the edges versus the cracked texture in the center illustrates the interplay between containment and overflow. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, representing disruption within the control. The shift from dark to light visually narrates the struggle between rigidity and freedom.
