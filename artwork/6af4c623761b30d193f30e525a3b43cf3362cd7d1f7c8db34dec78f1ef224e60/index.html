Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension of 'signal vs noise' with a heavy center that contrasts sharply against the muted edges. The bright, saturated colors (signal green and electric blue) dominate at the core, emulating a vibrant pulse striving for clarity amidst the static represented by the darker shades of dead black and static gray. The use of hot magenta interrupts the flow, suggesting fleeting moments of clarity as it collides with the surrounding noise. This aligns with the dynamics of the seed, where memory and exposure struggle in a chaotic landscape.
