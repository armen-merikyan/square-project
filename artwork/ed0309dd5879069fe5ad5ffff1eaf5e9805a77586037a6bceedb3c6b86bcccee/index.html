Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the conflict between emptiness and desire, which translates into color choices and arrangements. The use of saturated hues like hot coral and blood orange symbolizes longing, while hollow blue represents the void. The center features a dense cluster of warm colors surrounded by cooler, muted tones, creating a contrast that evokes a sensation of being both drawn in and kept at bay. The negative space embodies emptiness, while broken pixels represent disruption in the pursuit of fulfillment.
