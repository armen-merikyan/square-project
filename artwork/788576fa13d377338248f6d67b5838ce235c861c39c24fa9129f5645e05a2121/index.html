Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional dichotomy of nostalgia and progress through contrasting colors. The palette combines warm yellows and oranges representing nostalgia with cool deep blues and muted purples that symbolize progress. The bottom-weighted composition creates a sense of grounding with the warm colors, while the cooler tones drip upwards, suggesting an oscillation between calm and tension. The 'tiny_cross' shape seed is abstractly interpreted in the interplay of colors, encapsulating the struggle and balance between reminiscence and forward movement.
