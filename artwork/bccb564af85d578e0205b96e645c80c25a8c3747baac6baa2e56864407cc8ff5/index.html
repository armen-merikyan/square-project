Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes moments of restlessness against serene peace, reflecting internal conflict. The chosen palette of navy, black, and dim cyan establishes depth while allowing for areas of quiet light. The main collision occurs diagonally, where darker tones converge with lighter patches, symbolizing the struggle between chaos and calm. This arrangement leads to an abstract flow, suggesting movement yet interrupted by stillness, echoing the complexities of memory and motion.
