Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents a delicate balance between sharp vibrance (spark) and muted decay (ash). The palette of warm yellows and oranges intersects with deep blues and muted purples, creating a vivid contrast. The diagonal arrangement symbolizes the tension between hunger and restraint, while broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, echoing memories of the past. The central patterns collide—a braided warmth of sparks enveloped by jagged edges of ash—creating a dynamic composition that expresses resilience amidst fragility.
