Artist Statement

The composition captures the tension between the themes of emptiness and desire through a carefully chosen palette of warm red tones juxtaposed with cool blacks and pale blues. The contrasting colors create an emotional clash throughout the arrangement. The left side features a dense pulse of warm reds, symbolizing desire and warmth, while the right transitions into cooler tones, embodying emptiness. The layout subtly mirrors across the vertical axis, with intentional disruptions in the arrangement that signify emotional dissonance, reflecting the fragmentation of the heart. This abstraction of the emotional concept manifests in a rhythm that moves from left to right, symbolizing an ascent towards a desire that is often interrupted by feelings of isolation.
