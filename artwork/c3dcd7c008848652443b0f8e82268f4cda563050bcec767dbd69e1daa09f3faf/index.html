Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between pressure and quietude. The palette of off-white and faint peach creates a soft backdrop, representing quiet, while the dark navy and compressed red introduce pressure and intensity. The layout emphasizes an overfilled corner that collides with softer spaces, creating a visual drama of chaos vs calm. The resulting pattern reflects both disruption and clarity, embodying the seed's themes of conflict and resolution without invoking specific imagery.
