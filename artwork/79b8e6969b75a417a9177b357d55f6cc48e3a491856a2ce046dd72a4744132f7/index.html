Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the bright, expansive yellows of joy against the muted, fragmented blues of grief. The palette enhances this duality with vibrant oranges flaring from the center, illustrating optimism amidst sorrow. This composition features a tension-filled contrast where joy occupies the top half, signifying ascension and hope, while grief clings to the lower half with depictions of decay. The stair-step pattern suggests a movement, echoing life's balance between emotional highs and lows, while the hard edges delineate the struggle between safety and threat.
