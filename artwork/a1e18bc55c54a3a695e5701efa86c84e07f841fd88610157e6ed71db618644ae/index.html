Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tug-of-war between safety and exposure. The palette, with 'shelter green' and 'warm brown' as dominant colors, reflects a gentle yet stable environment contrasted by the cool 'open blue' for exposure. The edges are jagged, enhancing the feeling of breaks and imperfections, while the center showcases a vivid collision of pressure bands creating a rhythmic interplay between density and openness. The seed's themes of duality inspired the vertical split, facilitating a dynamic interaction between the softness of shades and the severity of boldness throughout the composition.
