Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tensions of love and betrayal through a juxtaposition of warm and cold colors. It employs a palette of pinks and reds symbolizing love, contrasted with dark grays representing the coldness of betrayal. The layout reflects a diagonal rise, illustrating hope while also confronting the broken symmetry of a fractured bond. Sharp edges in some pixels create a sense of tension, while softer tones and spaces allow for breathing room. The collision of colors and patterns symbolizes the complex dynamics of relationships as they oscillate between warmth and coldness.
