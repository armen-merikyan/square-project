Artist Statement

The emotional tone is captured by juxtaposing deep longing with steady resolve. The palette of 'melancholy_wonder' includes soft blues and muted plums, reflecting a serene yet urgent narrative. The main contrast is centered diagonally where the uncertainty and yearning emerge from the black and dark gray edges, clashing against the calm lavender-gray and soft cyan channels, creating a sense of tension and release. This translates the seed's narrative into an abstract arrangement of spacious echoes and close-knit patterns.
