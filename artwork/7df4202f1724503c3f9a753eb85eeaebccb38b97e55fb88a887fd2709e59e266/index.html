Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between joy and grief, utilizing contrasting colors to symbolize the emotional collision present in the seed. The palette is derived from muted blues representing grief and vibrant yellows and oranges reflecting joy. The symmetrical arrangement of warm and cool tones portrays a push and pull dynamic, with the soft energetic wave patterns suggesting movement and rhythm amidst chaos. The lack of clear outlines contributes to the sense of fluidity and ambiguity, evoking a feeling of hidden depths within the visual experience. The arrangement is designed to maintain a balance of density and negative space, allowing the viewer to navigate the emotional landscape.
