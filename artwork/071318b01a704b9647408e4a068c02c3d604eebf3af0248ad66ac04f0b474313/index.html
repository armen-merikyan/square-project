Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a tension between loneliness and connection, represented by the interplay of warm and cool colors within an asymmetrical layout. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a nostalgic atmosphere, contrasting lighter hues that suggest connection against darker ones that symbolize isolation. The pattern features fragmented lines that evoke broken memories, with strategically placed pixels creating a visually dynamic narrative of tension and unity. The flow from the left to the right embodies a journey from solitary weight to hopeful light, while the broken pixels introduce imperfection, emphasizing the theme of fragmented human experience.
