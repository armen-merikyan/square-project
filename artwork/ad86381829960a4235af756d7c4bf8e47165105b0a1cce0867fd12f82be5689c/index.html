Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional conflict between quietness and pressure. The selected palette includes off-white and faint peach to symbolize calmness, contrasted with compressed red and dark navy to manifest tension. The tension plays out through a checker pattern on the left and an embers pattern on the right, creating a noticeable collision at the center of the grid. This clash represents an argument between two emotional states, enhancing the sense of unbalance and intensity inspired by the seed's themes of counterflow and fractured clarity. Negative space enhances the feeling of gentleness amidst chaos, inviting contemplation amidst the tumult of colors.
