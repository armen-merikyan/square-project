Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between vulnerability and strength, symbolized through a soft pale center juxtaposed against a stark dark border. The color palette of faded mint, soft sky, storm gray, and near black illustrates the tension of exposure versus protection. The main contrast occurs in the center, where subtle textures intersect, encapsulating the essence of hope rising against hardship. The asymmetrical arrangement represents disturbance in a seemingly stable environment, mirroring the complexities of emotional interplay.
