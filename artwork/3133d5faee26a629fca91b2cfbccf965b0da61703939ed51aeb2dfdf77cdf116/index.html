Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between love and betrayal, using a palette of warm and cool colors to evoke contrasting feelings. The top half features soft pink and gray dots representing love, while the lower half contrasts with deep maroon and dark hues, indicating betrayal. The radial pattern clashing with organized dots creates visual friction, signifying a dissonance in emotional states. The edge style introduces brokenness, reflecting the theme of imperfection in relationships. Negative space is maintained to allow emotional clarity amidst complexity.
