Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional duality of certainty and curiosity. The palette, featuring warm ivory and deep wine against ash gray, represents the contrasting emotions, implying warmth and tension. The central area bursts with soft gold and warm ivory, symbolizing curiosity breaking through the backdrop of structured uncertainty. The zigzag flow mirrors the internal conflict of rising signals about to clash with receding edges—locations of light battling against darkness. Broken pixels introduce disruption, hinting at imperfection amid a structured grid, echoing the uncertainty within confined spaces.
