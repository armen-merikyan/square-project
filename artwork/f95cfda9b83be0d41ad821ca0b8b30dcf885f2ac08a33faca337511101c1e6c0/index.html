Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a tension between loneliness and connection. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold evokes a sense of ancient warmth, reflecting a deep, hidden fire. The contrasts emerge in the dense outer layers, suggesting protection, while the lighter center attempts to burst forth, symbolizing a desire for connection. The checkered pattern in the inner square employs spatial oscillation between connection and isolation. The broken pixels create a sense of imperfection, embodying unresolved feelings as they collide with the stability of the structured outer layer. The visual narrative tells of a core yearning for connection entwined with the weight of solitude.
