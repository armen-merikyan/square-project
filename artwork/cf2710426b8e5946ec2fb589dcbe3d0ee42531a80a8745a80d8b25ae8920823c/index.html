Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between quiet and pressure through a clash of colors and patterns. The palette, comprising muted tones like off-white and dark navy, represents calmness and pressure, respectively. The outer border (dark navy) creates a sense of containment, while the central core (light tones and compressed red) symbolizes a dynamic inner world. The asymmetry in color placement, marked by clashes and interruptions, embodies the emotional contrasts conveyed by the seed. This push and pull suggest a thoughtful narrative of tension and release, perfectly illustrating the complexities of feelings underlying the theme.
