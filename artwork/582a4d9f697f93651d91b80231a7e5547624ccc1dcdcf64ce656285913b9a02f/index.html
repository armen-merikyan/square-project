Artist Statement

The emotional tone here reflects a dynamic struggle between peace and restlessness. The palette, drawn from 'broken memory', utilizes muted sepia and tan hues contrasting against more vibrant shades of faded blue, suggesting the clash of calm versus chaos. The collision occurs centrally, where the dense pattern of 'teeth' overlaps with the patchwork of peace, representing the struggle of light and dark within this emotional framework. The arrangement of broken pixels introduces a sense of imperfection, mirroring the concept of memory as fractured yet vivid.
