Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the contrast between defiance and submission through a vibrant and muted palette. The palette, drawn from 'joy_grief', creates a dynamic interplay of warmth and coolness, suggesting a pulse of internal conflict. The upper half features a gradient of yellows and deep blues, embodying defiance and energy, while the lower half employs muted purples and soft oranges to suggest submission and calm. The collision occurs at the horizontal divide, where stark contrasts of color evoke a sense of tension and unresolved emotion, aligning perfectly with the seed's concept of duality and weight. The patterns, arranged in stairs, create a visual echo of this push-and-pull, reinforcing the overall narrative of struggle and balance.
