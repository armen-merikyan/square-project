Artist Statement

The composition reflects a struggle between feelings of loneliness and the yearning for connection. The palette suggests depth and intimacy, using dark hues like '#000000' and '#001f3f' to represent isolation while introducing bright accents of '#FF4136' to symbolize warmth and connection. The central pixel pattern fights against the darker edges, creating a burst effect that resonates with both emotional pressure and desire. The checker pattern represents isolation, juxtaposed with the crosshatch stability of the surrounding pixels, emphasizing the contrast between inner and outer worlds.
