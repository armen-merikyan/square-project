Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the dichotomy of restlessness and peace, contrasting a chaotic grid with soothing rings, representing the struggle between motion and stillness. The palette uses deep blacks and icy blues to reflect tension, while warm oranges introduce sparks of activity. Main contrast lies in the top left, where warm accents collide with a dark field, creating a dynamic visual tension that echoes the abstract themes of memory and disruption.
