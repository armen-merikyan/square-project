Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the tension between loneliness and connection through the chaotic interplay of color and spatial patterns. The chosen palette reflects faded memories, with sepia and tan evoking warmth while embodying nostalgia and desolation. The center features a stark contrast between dark and light tones, emphasizing exposure juxtaposed with hidden elements. A burst pattern inspired by flames speaks to the intense emotion of longing and connection amidst the quiet oppressive edges, creating an echoing effect that portrays both isolation and a yearning for unity.
