Artist Statement

This composition seeks to explore the tension between joy and grief through the juxtaposition of a bright, warm palette against darker, cooler tones. The bright yellows and oranges represent joy, depicting warmth and openness, while the deep blue and muted purple create a sense of heaviness and fragmentation associated with grief. The layout exhibits a central clustering of brighter colors, while the edges fade into deeper tones, resembling the ebb and flow of emotions and the unresolved nature of the experience. The zigzag pattern suggests movement, mirroring the unpredictability of emotional states.
