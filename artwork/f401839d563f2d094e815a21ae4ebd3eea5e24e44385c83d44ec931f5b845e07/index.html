Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duel of joy and grief through contrasting color placement. The palette focuses on bright yellows and oranges to symbolize joy, juxtaposed against muted blues and purples representing grief. The arrangement features a diagonal rise, embodying hope striking out of chaos. The 'spark' and 'rain' patterns map out a transition from structured order on one side to disrupted chaos on the other, echoing the intimate interplay of these two emotions. The use of broken pixels softens rigid edges, reflecting the complexities of human feelings.
