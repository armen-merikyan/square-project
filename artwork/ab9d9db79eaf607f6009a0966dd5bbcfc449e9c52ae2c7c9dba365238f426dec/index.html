Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between longing and resolve, manifested through a palette of warm and cool hues. The selected colors, such as rose clay and cool blue, evoke intimacy against distance, reflecting the essence of longing while maintaining a structured resolution through the negative cross pattern. The collision lies centrally, where the crosshatch of longing meets the negative space, symbolizing the clashing yet serendipitous nature of emotions. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, mirroring the concept of a mirror broken, enhancing the visual narrative while creating a sense of overflow and containment. The layout maintains a near symmetry with slight distortions, reinforcing the theme of an unresolved emotional state that teeters between repair and damage.
