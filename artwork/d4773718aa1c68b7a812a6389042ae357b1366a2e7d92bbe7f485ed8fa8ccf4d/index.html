Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic balance between control and surrender. The palette, drawn from impressions of awe and terror, comprises deep indigo and cold gold contrasting against void black to signify a struggle between contained pressure and soft yielding. The main collision occurs in the center, where black and gold collide with overlapping glitched patterns, symbolizing an overfilled center. The use of broken pixels suggests disruption within the otherwise structured design, enhancing the sense of conflict. This pattern expresses an inward spiral flow, a dialogue between confinement and release in an abstract format.
