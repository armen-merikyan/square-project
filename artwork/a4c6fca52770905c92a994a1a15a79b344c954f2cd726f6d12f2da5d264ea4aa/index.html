Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between joy and grief, using a palette of warm yellows and cool blues to create a visual dialogue. The left side displays denser clusters of muted purples representing grief, while the right emerges with brighter yellows and oranges embodying joy. The asymmetry introduces a stark contrast, echoing the conflict of love and betrayal. The arrangement establishes a rhythm, where the central area pulsates with warmth, disrupted by cooler tones at the edges, suggesting both a protective and a broken barrier.
