Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between clarity and chaos, reflecting the seed's themes through color and contrast. A dominant 'signal green' is interrupted by 'hot magenta' and 'static gray,' representing the readable rhythm amidst noise. The central collision occurs around the center, where sharp contrasts invite tension, and the corner areas feature muted tones, echoing the concept of exposure versus shelter. The broken pixels serve as disruptions in the otherwise structured flow, enhancing the story of struggle between clarity and interference, mirroring concepts of memory versus erasure.
