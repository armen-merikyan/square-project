Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a dynamic range between clarity and confusion, represented by the stark contrast of the warm tones against cool grays symbolizing clarity among chaos. The palette, dominated by dark maroon, pink, and gray, echoes themes of love and betrayal. The defined thick edges create a juxtaposition with the softer inner pixels, symbolizing the tension between structure and disorder. This layout conveys a narrative of disarray while the falling pixel pattern suggests a gradual movement from a center of confusion toward the clarity of the outer edges.
