Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tone of tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of lime, black, electric blue, and magenta evokes a sense of vibrant yet uneasy energy. The sharp edges of suspicion are represented by the black and dark tones, while the trust emerges in the lime and electric blue, creating a dynamic collision at the center that embodies the push and pull of these emotions. The placement of soft glow colors alongside darker hues creates an echo of resilience against fragility. The maze-like patterns of suspicion interrupt the clean lines of trust, representing the conflict between stable bases and unsettling reflections in this abstract composition.
