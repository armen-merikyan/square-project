Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between trust and suspicion through a juxtaposition of patterns and colors. The palette combines soft cyan and lavender gray to evoke a sense of calm and melancholy, while deep rain blue and muted plum introduce an element of tension and interruption. The central collision, where the two patterns overlap, represents the struggle between stability (trust) and instability (suspicion). Each broken pixel serves as an intentional disruption, creating an imperfect symmetry that highlights the emotional nuances of the seed. The layout showcases an argument grid where the diagonal flow suggests movement, hinting at the dynamic balance between these conflicting emotions while maintaining a clear negative space that draws the viewer's eye across the canvas.
