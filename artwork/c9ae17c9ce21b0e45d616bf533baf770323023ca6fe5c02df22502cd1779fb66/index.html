Artist Statement

Emphasizing the contrast between envy and gratitude, the color palette includes burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold, symbolizing warmth and discomfort. The layout reflects falling pixels, evoking a sense of descending emotion, where pressure from the edges resembles outside noise clashing with a quiet center. The main collision occurs between the harsh dark tones and the warm, vibrant colors at intervals, suggesting a dance of feeling between despair and appreciation. The seed's focus on boundaries and soft edges manifests as irregular pixel arrangements that capture the essence of emotional tension resolved in a subtle echo.
