Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension of longing and resolve through color contrasts and patterns. The palette features a mix of electric blue and magenta, symbolizing the vibrancy of yearning, against a deeper black that conveys the weight of resolve. This contrast creates a visual pulse and a sense of collision in the center, stimulating a narrative of striving and overcoming. The split-screen and negative cross patterns create an interaction that embraces both the quiet space and the vibrant tension, embodying the essence of repairs and damages symbolically through broken pixels.
