Artist Statement

This piece evokes a strong emotional dynamic between loneliness and connection. The outer rows, darker shades represent feelings of isolation, setting a stark backdrop that contrasts with the lighter central core, representing connectivity and warmth. The palette of sepia and faded blue draws on the nostalgic quality of memories, while the grid layout suggests entrapment but is interrupted by small bursts of lighter color, akin to fleeting moments of connection amidst persistent solitude. The use of broken pixels represents imperfections in these connections, reinforcing the tension between hidden and exposed emotions.
