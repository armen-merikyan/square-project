Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, featuring pale aqua and alarm red, represents the calm (defiance) that clashes with urgency (submission). The layout emphasizes a corner overwhelmed with bright, chaotic marks, symbolizing emotional pressure against a background of subdued elements. This collision creates an echo of tension and resolution that reflects the central theme of opposing forces, as the pattern shifts from the vibrant chaos to the calm areas, embodying a story of balance and conflict.
