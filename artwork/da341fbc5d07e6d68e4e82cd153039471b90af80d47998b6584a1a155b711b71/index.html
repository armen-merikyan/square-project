Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional conflict between defiance (warm hues) and submission (cool hues) through a split layout, reflecting layered emotional complexity. The chosen palette of yellows and muted purples contrasts the brightness of defiance with the subdued tones of submission. The flowing diagonal rise reflects the struggle between these opposing forces. Each section is visually balanced but interrupted by broken pixels, symbolizing the rawness and imperfection of emotions, leaving negative space to emphasize tension. The arrangement creates a visual tension, hinting at battle within the emotional spectrum, aligning with the seed's nuances.
