Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines the fiery intensity of rage with the soothing calm of quietness, creating a vivid juxtaposition. The selected palette of red symbolizes the heat of rage, while the soft pale blue represents calmness, intertwined with shades of black and white that act as a balancing backdrop. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where warm red embers surge against a calm field of blue, suggesting a collision of feelings. The pattern plays with the concepts of cracked calm and vibrant sparks of excess, echoing the themes of containment versus overflow, while the scattered arrangement evokes tension and flow, embodying the seed's abstract qualities.
