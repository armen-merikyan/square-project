Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the duality of 'loneliness' and 'connection', represented through a palette of warm yellows and oranges against a backdrop of muted blues and purples. The use of soft edges fosters a feeling of gentleness; the colors collide in an asyndetic arrangement, especially in the top left where the warmth of yellow meets the coldness of blue, suggesting an attempt at connection amidst the void. The layout emphasizes balance yet introduces broken pixels to signify imperfection. The overall pattern sways from the left to the right, evoking a pulse of emotional struggle, reflecting the seed’s themes of weight and memory. This intention captures the complex feelings of being alone while yearning for connection, creating a rich tapestry of abstract expression.
