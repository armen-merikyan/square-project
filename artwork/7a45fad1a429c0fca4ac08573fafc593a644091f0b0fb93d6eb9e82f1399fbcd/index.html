Artist Statement

This composition plays with the emotional tension between defiance and submission. The palette of pale mint, storm gray, and faded gold creates a feeling of quiet unease, where the dark shades indicate the weight of submission, while the lighter tones embody defiance. The collision occurs centrally, where the ripple of defiance clashes against the tide of submission, creating a dynamic flow from left to right. The intentional imperfections represent disruptions in the expected harmony, reinforcing the theme of resistance against surrender.
