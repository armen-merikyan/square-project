Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, using a palette that balances warm yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples. The layout starts from the corner, introducing vibrant colors that transition into deeper tones, suggesting the movement from clarity into chaos. The broken edges and stair-like patterns create a sense of instability, while the use of negative space allows moments of respite, enhancing the emotional contrast within the grid.
