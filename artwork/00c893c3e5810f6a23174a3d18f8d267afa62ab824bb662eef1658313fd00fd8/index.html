Artist Statement

The emotional tone synthesized from longing and resolve creates a tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette reflects resilience with moss green representing life and hope, while rust symbolizes decay and a sense of loss. The main contrast emerges in the center, where the sharp edges of the rust and muted bone white regions collide against the soft, contained green, reflecting conflict yet harmony. The broken pixels symbolize scars of past damage amidst a backdrop of serenity, creating depth and an abstract narrative of healing.
