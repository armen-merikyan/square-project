Artist Statement

The emotional tone highlights the struggle between defiance and submission, creating a vivid push and pull in the artwork. The palette, featuring deep blacks, vibrant greens, electric blues, and contrasting magentas, reflects both chaos and tranquility—a visual representation of tension. The main collision occurs at the center, where sharp edges of color cut through softer hues, symbolizing interruption and defiance against a backdrop of submission. The chosen abstract patterns evoke visual rhythms, embodying the dynamics of confrontation and surrender, while the broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection and irregularity, deepening the narrative.
