Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between the clear and chaotic, represented by the selected palette of navy, black, single white, and dim cyan. The corner invasion layout allows the 'signal' to emanate from one edge, creating a central tension in the square. The contrast emphasizes whispers of clarity pushing through layers of noise, with broken pixels indicating imperfections and interruptions in memory. This culminates in a balance of calm space broken by sporadic static, evoking feelings of isolation amidst a chaotic environment.
