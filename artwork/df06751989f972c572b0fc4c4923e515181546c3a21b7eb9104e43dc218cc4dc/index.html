Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes warmth and intimacy from 'love' with the harshness of 'betrayal.' The palette merges pink and dark maroon to reflect duality; warm colors representing affection clash with deeper hues for conflict. The dense outer rows symbolize pressure and noise, while softer, fragmented inner colors suggest vulnerability and resonance. The asymmetry simulated by broken pixels enhances the feeling of instability, playing on the contrasts of sharp edges against softer backgrounds, embodying a narrative of tension and unresolved emotions.
