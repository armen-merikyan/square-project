Artist Statement

This composition captures the duality of restlessness and peace through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette combines signal green and electric blue to represent the vibrancy of motion against the heaviness of dead black and static gray, embodying moments of anxiety and calm. The arrangement features a centralized clash where bright colors interrupt a predominantly dark field, symbolizing the tension and desire for resolution. The use of broken pixels throughout adds a layer of imperfection, mirroring the conflicts inherent in memory and emotion, while maintaining a quiet space around the edges for reflective balance.
