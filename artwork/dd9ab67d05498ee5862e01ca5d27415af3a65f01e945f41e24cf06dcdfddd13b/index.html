Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between envy and gratitude, utilizing a palette of pinks and reds to evoke warmth and intensity, contrasted against deeper grays and dark maroon suggesting underlying complexities and shadows of fear. The design follows a rising flow, where warm colors ascend from the bottom, symbolizing hope and aspiration, while jagged edges introduce a sense of interruption and chaos, creating a dialogue between the softness of gratitude and the sharpness of envy. The arrangement of colors suggests a collision of emotional states, enhancing the abstract narrative without literal representation.
