Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between rage and calm through a palette of intense reds and soothing pale blues. The emotional tone oscillates with sharp, jagged interruptions of red pushing against structured blues, embodying a struggle between chaotic energy and quiet grace. The main collision occurs toward the center, where radial patterns of red's explosive energy meet the horizontal calm of the blue, creating a visual conversation of containment versus overflow. The broken pixels introduce intentional interruptions that mirror emotional scars within the larger calm context.
