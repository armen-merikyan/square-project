Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between relief and dread through a juxtaposition of colors and patterns. The palette includes 'pale_mint' and 'faded_gold' that suggest warmth and comfort, balanced against 'storm_gray' and 'near_black' representing weight and unease. The central collision occurs where dark colors meet lighter ones, creating a visual 'pressure corner' that draws the eye inward, invoking a spiral motion away from chaos. The arrangement of broken pixels intentionally disrupts order, suggesting imperfections and the complexity of the emotional interplay.
