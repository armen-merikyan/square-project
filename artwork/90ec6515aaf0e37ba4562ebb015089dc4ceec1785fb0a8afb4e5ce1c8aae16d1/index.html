Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the contrast between pressure and quiet, where the palette shifts from muted tones to deeper, weightier colors. The darker colors represent compressed density, while the lighter hues evoke a sense of soft horizon, embodying both tension and release. The composition is structured to favor density at the corner, spreading diagonally to create a flow that intertwines sharp edges with softer areas, suggesting interruption and collision. This evokes a story of maintaining calm amidst an impending tide of chaos, drawing on the seed's themes of symmetry and break.
