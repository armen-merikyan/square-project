Artist Statement

The emotional tone of awe versus terror is captured through a dynamic use of the machine_emotion palette featuring electric blue, magenta, lime, and black. The contrasting colors create a tension between the idea of awe represented by softer hues and the stark depth of terror conveyed through darker tones. The layout flows like rain, introducing a scatter that speaks to chaos, while broken pixels symbolize imperfection and fractured memories. The use of broken dots against sharper, spark-like patterns enhances the collision of feelings, creating an abstract narrative of fluctuating stability and unrest.
