Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between love and betrayal, captured through contrasting colors. The palette incorporates warm hues (pinks and reds) to represent love, while the darker gray and maroon evoke feelings of betrayal. The left-right flow mirrors the concept of symmetry, with broken pixels introducing imperfections that symbolize betrayal's disruption of harmony. The juxtaposition of rings (representing love) and sharp edges (betrayal) creates a dynamic interplay, conveying the complexity of emotions. The right side features subtle displacements, embodying the underlying tension.
