Artist Statement

The artwork embodies a dance between clarity and chaos, where a bright path of signal struggles amidst a sea of static noise. The palette of deep navy and dim cyan provides a stark contrast against hollow black, reflecting a push and pull of emotional landscapes. The central pulse, represented by brighter colors, competes with darkness, creating tension in the overall flow. Patterns of interruption flood the canvas, illustrating 'signal vs noise' as an abstract conflict of memory in timeless waves.
