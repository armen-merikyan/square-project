Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the conflict between longing and resolve. The palette, 'resilience_fragility,' emphasizes muted greens and warm rust tones to suggest organic growth and decay, creating a rich emotional depth. The main collision occurs toward the center, where the barcode and spark patterns intertwine, symbolizing the tension between structured desire and chaotic resolution. The layout adopts an argument grid, showcasing the fight for control through asymmetric placement and broken pixel interruptions, illustrating emotional scars and unresolved urges that compete for dominance.
