Artist Statement

This composition navigates the tension between exposure and safety, employing a palette that contrasts soft, warm browns and greens against pale, cooler tones. The central arrangement draws on the idea of fragmented vs. whole, representing vulnerability within a protective wave of colors. The echo effect produced by asymmetric placements not only highlights the central contrast but creates visual tension that plays with the viewer's perception, illustrating the emotional weight of safety and exposure through juxtaposition.
