Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional duality of restlessness and peace through the use of dense and fragmented color patterns. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green reflects a spectrum from deep disturbances to gentle balance. The main collision occurs diagonally across the square, where the maze-like structure of fragmented colors interacts with the more serene stacked bands. The arrangement embodies a dynamic interplay of tension and calm, suggesting both movement and stillness. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, enhancing the narrative of conflict between competing emotions.
