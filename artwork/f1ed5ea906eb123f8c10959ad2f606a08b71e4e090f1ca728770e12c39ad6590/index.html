Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the push and pull between longing's warmth and resolve's rigidity. A palette of muted purple, deep blue, and vibrant orange reflects this dichotomy, creating a sense of depth and contrast. The collision is prominent in the center, where the warm colors disrupt a cooler backdrop, embodying chaos within order. The 'ladder' metaphor emerges as a diagonal pattern, representing both aspiration and struggle. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, mirroring emotional scars.
