Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between panic and control, illustrating the tension of fluctuating stability. The selected palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon embodies the love-betrayal theme, with warm colors representing passionate emotions battling the cooler grays that symbolize stability. The layout indicates an invasion from the corner, with the grid pattern of panic spreading diagonally. This is juxtaposed against broken dots representing control, creating visual tension. The main contrast emerges in the explosive reds against the subdued grays, emphasizing the chaotic energy of the center fighting against a stabilizing backdrop. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, suggesting instability amidst an ordered framework. The overall composition invites reflection on the emotional dichotomy inherent in the seed's concepts.
