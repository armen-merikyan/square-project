Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between dread and relief through a dual pattern of dense saturation and open space. The chosen palette of pale mint, soft sky, storm gray, near black, and faded gold represents a gradient of emotions, where cooler tones evoke a sense of dread and warmer accents signal moments of relief. The main contrast occurs between the dense storm gray and the soft sky, positioned along the diagonal, suggesting a struggle between tension and release. The seed's instruction to incorporate broken lines and irregular placements manifests in the uneven distribution of colors, emphasizing the abstract narratives of pressure and elation.
