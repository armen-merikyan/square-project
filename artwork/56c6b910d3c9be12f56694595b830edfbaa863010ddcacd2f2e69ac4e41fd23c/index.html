Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the stark contrasts of loneliness and connection. The palette features deep reds and grays, symbolizing raw emotion (loneliness) and warmth (connection). The main contrast occurs between dense gray clusters at the center and broken, warmer colors towards the edges, representing hidden connections. The pattern is informed by the seed's duality, with the 'tiny_plus' shape suggesting a central vulnerability amid bursts of warmth, embodying the tension of a hidden fire yearning for exposure.
