Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between chaos and control, using a palette of muted sepia and gray to represent memory and tension. The left half embodies panic with a noisy, jagged pattern, while the right half explores calm through a mirrored symmetry. The central pixels collide with contrasting colors, forming a visual spark that symbolizes the ongoing argument between joy and grief, creating an abstract narrative of unresolved emotions.
