Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between nostalgia as a warm, grounding presence and the cool, stark contrast of progress. The palette reflects the 'ancient fire' theme, using burnt orange and dark red to evoke memory and warmth, while charcoal and gold represent clarity and aspiration in contrast. The layout suggests movement with upward pixels on one side and downward on the other, illustrating the rise versus fall dichotomy. The brushstroke pattern of nostalgia flows like stairs, while progress rains down, echoing the drip element. This arrangement creates collisions in visual rhythm through juxtaposed warm and cool colors, leaving spaces that symbolize both tension and release.
