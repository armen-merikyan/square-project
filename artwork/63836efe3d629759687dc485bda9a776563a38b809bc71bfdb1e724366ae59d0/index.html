Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the tension between love and betrayal, represented through a stark palette of warm reds and soft grays to evoke warmth versus isolation. The top four rows feature a checker pattern reflecting love's warmth in half the square, while the bottom four rows utilize noise to convey the discord of betrayal. The sharp contrast creates a collision point where the two emotions meet, mirroring the struggle between connection and distance. The broken pixels add a sense of imperfection, suggesting unresolved emotions, with empty spaces symbolizing feelings left unspoken, enhancing the overall narrative.
