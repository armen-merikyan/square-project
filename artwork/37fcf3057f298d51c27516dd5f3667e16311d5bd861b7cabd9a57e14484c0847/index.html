Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between loneliness and connection, illustrated through a palette that contrasts warm sepia tones with cooler faded blues. The central area shows a burst of warmth, symbolizing connection, while the edges fade into dusty grays, embodying isolation. The placement of broken pixels creates an impression of imperfection, aligning with the themes of hidden vs exposed emotions. The flow of the colors moves top-down, invoking a sense of weightiness from loneliness that gradually opens up towards connection.
