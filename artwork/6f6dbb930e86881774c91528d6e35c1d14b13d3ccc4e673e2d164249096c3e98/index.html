Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts defiance's bright, angular resistance with submission's softer, receding forms. The palette of powder pink and sharp crimson represents warmth and intensity, juxtaposed with the cooler tones of steel gray and carbon black, expressing tension. The central collision of patterns—bright angular splits against soft braiding—creates an inward spiral that visually signifies pressure and release, while negative space forms a quiet channel in this dynamic narrative. The arrangement evokes a sense of conflict between standing firm and yielding, embodying the themes seamlessly through color and placement.
