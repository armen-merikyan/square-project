Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between trust and suspicion, using a warm palette of yellow and orange to represent trust, juxtaposed against the deep blue and muted purple that convey suspicion. The composition features a collision at the center, where vibrant hues meet darker tones, suggesting a struggle between clarity and uncertainty. This aligns with the shape seed of 'shell', as it represents both protection and fragility within the context of emotional exchanges. Broken pixels are used to create a deliberate imperfection, enhancing feelings of unease and vulnerability.
