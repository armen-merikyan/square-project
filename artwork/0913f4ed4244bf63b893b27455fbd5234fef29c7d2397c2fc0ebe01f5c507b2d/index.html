Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the struggle between trust and suspicion, represented through warm coral and orange hues juxtaposed against muted charcoal and hollow blue. The main contrast feels centered at the diagonal fall, where trust interrupts suspicion, dominating the square while leaving evident signs of fracture via broken pixels. This design reflects an open gate flows with lightness into a field of static, suggesting a struggle for clarity amidst doubt.
