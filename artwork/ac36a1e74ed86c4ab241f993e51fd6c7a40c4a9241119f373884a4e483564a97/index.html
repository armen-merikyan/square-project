Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between tenderness and rage, using a palette that juxtaposes saturated colors of red with muted blues. The main contrast occurs centrally, where the bright red stack pattern representing rage collides with the soft mirror pattern of pale blue, suggesting a struggle in space. This artwork reflects the push and pull of emotions, with broken pixels representing scars of past experiences, creating a static interference against a quiet background, embodying the notion of a hidden struggle.
