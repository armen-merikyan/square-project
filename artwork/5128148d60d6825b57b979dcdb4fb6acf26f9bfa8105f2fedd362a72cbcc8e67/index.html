Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between quiet and pressure through a strategic color distribution. The palette features muted hues like 'off_white' and 'dust_gray' that convey peace and stillness, contrasted by the deeper tones of 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy', representing the weight of pressure. The arrangement tilts towards the left side, embodying density and compressive energy, while the right side releases into more muted tones, highlighting the contrast. The layout maintains a nearly symmetrical mirror, yet incorporates strategic 'broken' patterns to suggest imperfection and conflict, akin to the quieter aspects battling the heavier forces. The main collision occurs along the diagonal, where the darkest tones meet the softest hues, creating a dynamic balance through visual interruption.
