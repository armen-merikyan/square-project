Artist Statement

The emotional tone merges tenderness and rage through a contrast of soft pastels and sharp blacks. The palette emphasizes a balance of pale blues and reds against deep blacks, symbolizing the inner emotional landscape. The main collision occurs in the central and lower rows where saturated rage collides with muted tenderness, creating a visual pulse. The use of broken pixels disrupts the expected patterns, embodying the tension and release inherent in the emotions represented by the seed.
