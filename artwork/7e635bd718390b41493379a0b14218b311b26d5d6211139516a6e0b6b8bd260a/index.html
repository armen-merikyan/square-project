Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional duality of joy and grief, using a palette that juxtaposes bright, warm colors with deep, cool tones. The upward and downward movement of colors symbolizes hope and despair in flux. The contrast of bright yellows and oranges with muted purples and deep blues evokes an emotional tension, with warm accents interrupting the cool depths. The pattern embodies a spiral flow, suggesting both an orbit of complexity and an exploration of hidden versus exposed feelings, allowing for a meditative reflection on the nature of emotion.
