Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional push and pull between panic and control, employing a palette of warm yellows and oranges juxtaposed with deep blue and muted purple. The bright colors command attention, suggesting sparks of panic, while the cooler tones offer a sense of stability. The arrangement utilizes an asymmetric flow, with pixels rising on one side and descending on the other, creating visual tension. The placement embodies the chaos of emotion with clusters of color against negative space, evoking a feeling of an empty room filled with conflicting energies.
