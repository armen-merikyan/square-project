Artist Statement

This piece evokes a tension between longing and resolve through a palette of cool blues and warm highlights, suggesting emotional layers. The contrasting colors represent the interplay between hope and closure, while the falling pixel layout mirrors tears or rain, echoing the feeling of longing. The barcode pattern adds structure to the chaos of broken dots, highlighting the complexity of emotions. The main collision occurs centrally, where darker and lighter shades intersect, enhancing the duality of feelings.
