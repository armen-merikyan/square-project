Artist Statement

This composition explores the interplay between calmness and disturbance, using a palette that balances vibrant yellow and orange against deeper blues and muted purples. The warm tones represent moments of panic while the cool colors offer a sense of control. The jagged edges symbolize the disruptive nature of emotional collisions, where the quiet is broken by sudden bursts of pressure. The placement of colors creates a visual rhythm that rises from chaos at the bottom to calmness at the top, embodying hope amidst turmoil.
