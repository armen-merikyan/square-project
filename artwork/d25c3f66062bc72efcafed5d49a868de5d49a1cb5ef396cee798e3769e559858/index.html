Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, utilizing a palette of deep blue reflecting calm and muted purple illustrating chaos. The hard edges of the arrangement enhance the contrast, while the split-screen pattern radiates a feeling of fractured symmetry. The falling layout represents a descent into complexity, with darker shades pooling at the bottom, illustrating unresolved emotions and echoes of clarity disrupting chaos through selective bright accents, creating a pulse in the visual rhythm.
