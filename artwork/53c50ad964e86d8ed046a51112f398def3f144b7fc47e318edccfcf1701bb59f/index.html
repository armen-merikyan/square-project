Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between tenderness and rage through a palette that reflects inner conflict. The use of deep blacks interspersed with soft pale blues and stark whites creates a dramatic contrast that embodies emotional collision. The layout features jagged edges symbolizing rage on one side, while smoother, partial patterns represent the softness of tenderness, suggesting a narrative of conflict and resolution. The main contrast occurs in the center with a burst of white against black—this collision reflects the push and pull of guilt and forgiveness, creating a rhythmic climb towards emotional release.
