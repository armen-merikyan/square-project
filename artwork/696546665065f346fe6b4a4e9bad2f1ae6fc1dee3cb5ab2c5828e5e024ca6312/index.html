Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy between restlessness and peace. The palette of pale mint, soft sky, storm gray, near black, and faded gold conveys this tension. The main contrast occurs in the central area, where storm gray punctuates pale mint, mirroring the conflict between movement and stillness. The layout portrays two pulses—one calm and another restless—interacting across the canvas. Broken pixels create intentional disruptions, embodying the theme of unresolved motion and fragmentation.
