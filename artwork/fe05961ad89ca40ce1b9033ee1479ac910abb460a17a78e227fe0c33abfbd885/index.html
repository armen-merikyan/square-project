Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between softness and severity, reflecting the emotional landscape suggested by the seed. The palette combines `powder_pink` and `warm_white` with the sharper tones of `carbon_black` and `sharp_crimson`, allowing a visual play between gentle and harsh. The flow is spiral counterclockwise, representing a gradual build-up as we trace the pattern with our eyes. The primary contrast occurs in the center, where soft hues collide with dense, dark accents, capturing the essence of guilt and forgiveness. Broken pixels scatter throughout, symbolizing imperfections and unresolved feelings within this emotional contradiction.
