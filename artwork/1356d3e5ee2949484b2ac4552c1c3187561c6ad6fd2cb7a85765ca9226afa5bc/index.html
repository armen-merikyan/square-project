Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between softness and severity. The palette blends warm whites and soft pinks to evoke a gentle emotion, set against the starkness of blacks and grays representing severity. The arrangement features a distinct pulse of center pressure, where 'severity' sharply intersects with the surrounding softness, creating an understandable yet abstract collision. The quiet field spaces enhance the main contrast, emphasizing echoes of feelings rather than objects.
