Artist Statement

The emotional tone emphasizes the contrast between the isolation of loneliness and the warmth of connection. The selected palette, 'broken_memory,' with sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue, evokes a sense of nostalgia and depth, supporting the themes of hidden vs exposed feelings. The central area is more densely populated with muted colors, suggesting a burst of emotion, while the outer edges introduce negative space surrounded by soft, blended contrasts. The layout follows a mirror-broken arrangement to reflect the tension between connection and solitude.
