Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between yearning and determination, represented by bending flows of color. The chosen palette of faded gold, soft sky, and storm gray establishes a narrative of contained brightness against somber depths. The main collision occurs diagonally, where soft hues ripple into deeper shadowy zones, symbolizing the push-pull between longing and resolve. Broken pixels are dispersed to suggest imperfections and scars, reinforcing the narrative of repair versus damage. The overall structure echoes a diagonal ascent, emphasizing the striving upward motion inherent to resolve, while also leaving quiet spaces as moments of introspection.
