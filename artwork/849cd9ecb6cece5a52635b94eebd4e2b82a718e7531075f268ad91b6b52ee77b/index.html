Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between love and betrayal through a divided color narrative and contrasting shapes. The top half uses a warm palette of pinks and reds to convey love's vibrant essence, while the bottom half cools into darker maroon and gray tones to illustrate betrayal's shadow. The pattern's interruptions represent moments of conflict within a seemingly harmonious flow. Key collisions occur at the edges between emotions, where lighter and darker colors meet, symbolizing the contrasting forces at play within relationships.
