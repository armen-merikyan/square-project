Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between awe and terror through a contrasting palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold. The outer border in darker hues represents terror, encroaching upon the core's warmth, symbolizing awe. The zigzag flow creates a dynamic interaction, while broken pixels signify imperfection and memory. The center's stability contrasts against the chaotic edges, suggesting both a sense of safety and an underlying threat.
