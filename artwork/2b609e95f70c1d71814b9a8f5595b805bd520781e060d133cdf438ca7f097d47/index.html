Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between trust and suspicion. The palette, consisting of soft pinks and harsh dark maroons, illustrates the conflict between warmth and shadow. The lighter colors in the left half embody trust, forming a stable rhythmic pattern, while darker hues on the right introduce suspicion, fragmenting and colliding with the former, creating emotional unrest. The main contrast occurs along the vertical split, where the organized arrangement meets chaotic disruptions.
