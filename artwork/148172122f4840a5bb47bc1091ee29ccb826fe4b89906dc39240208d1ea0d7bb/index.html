Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between confusion and clarity. The chosen palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green reflects a dichotomy—darkness representing confusion, while soft gold and pale green represent moments of clarity. The chaotic checker pattern juxtaposed with smoother areas reflects the emotional impact of broken symmetry and falling pixels. The placement of colors creates dense clusters countered by negative space, evoking a pulse that flows diagonally, symbolizing the internal struggle between order and chaos.
