Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a variety of color contrasts and patterns. The palette chosen—a blend of ice blue, violet gray, and warning orange—suggests clarity clashing with confusion, while clean white provides a quiet backdrop representing calm amidst chaos. The main collision occurs at the center, where stark contrast and angular patterns define the tension between the two emotions. The 'ladder' shape seed is abstractly represented through stacked patterns on opposing sides, symbolizing a rise against or submission to external forces. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections to echo the unpredictability of this emotional journey.
