Artist Statement

This abstract composition explores the emotional tension between tenderness and rage through a diagonal layout where soft blue channels represent tenderness, contrasting against explosive red jagged pixels symbolizing rage. The interplay of saturation and muted tones echoes the complexity of guilt and forgiveness as colors bleed into one another. The composition is intentionally disrupted by broken pixels, enhancing the narrative of inner conflict and emotional dissonance, suggesting the struggle between vulnerability and anger.
