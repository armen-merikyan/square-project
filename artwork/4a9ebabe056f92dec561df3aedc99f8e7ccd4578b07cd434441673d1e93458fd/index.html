Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of restlessness versus peace. The palette combines pale aqua and clear white to symbolize tranquility, contrasted against alarm red and ink blue representing agitation. The arrangement flows in a spiral pattern, embodying the pressure of motion at the center while allowing lighter colors to spread outward, promoting a sense of calm in the area. Broken pixels are intentionally placed to create disruptions, mirroring the uneasy nature of memory and motion. The primary collision occurs diagonally, creating visual tension that resonates with the concept of unresolved arguments. Overall, this abstract composition captures the ongoing dialogue between disorder and serenity.
