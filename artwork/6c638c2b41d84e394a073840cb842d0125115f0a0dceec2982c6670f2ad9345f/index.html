Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional dichotomy of defiance and submission through color and placement. The chosen palette of deep blacks and dark violets contrasts with soft gold and pale green, embodying a sense of quiet hope amidst chaos. The layout conveys spiral pressure, where colors coil toward an off-center point, creating a tension between control and disorder. The broken pixels disrupt symmetry, illustrating scars of defiance against the backdrop of yielding submission. This interplay evokes a deep narrative of struggle, marking the threshold between conflicting emotional states.
