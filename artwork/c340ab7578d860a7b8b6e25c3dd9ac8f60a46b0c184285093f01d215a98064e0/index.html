Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the tension between rage and calm, utilizing a palette of fierce reds and soft blues. The sharp contrast between the predominantly dark outer area and the subtle inner core creates an intense visual conflict. The jagged placement of red within the soft grid of pale blue signifies interruption and pressure breaking through serenity, embodying the seed's themes of collision and weight, where the calm core is threatened by the surrounding chaos.
