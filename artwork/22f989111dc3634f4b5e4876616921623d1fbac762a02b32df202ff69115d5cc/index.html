Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the chaos of 'panic' with the structure of 'control'. The palette of navy, black, and dim cyan evokes isolation and depth, suggesting a lonely signal amidst turmoil. The left side mirrors rhythmic control in a structured pattern, while the right exhibits explosive 'panic' through a flame-like chaos of colors. This collision provides tension, reflecting the theme of inner turmoil versus external stability, where warm colors intersect muted tones, expressing the abstract narrative of conflict and emotional struggle.
