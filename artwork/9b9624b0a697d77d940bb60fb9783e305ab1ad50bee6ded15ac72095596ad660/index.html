Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of 'emptiness' and 'desire' through contrasting tones and textures. The warm colors (red, pink) symbolize desire while the cool grays represent emptiness, creating a visual struggle. The placement features heavy dark maroon on one side, echoing a rise and fall motif, while the opposite holds softer tones, amplifying the sense of longing. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, reflecting the heart's fragmented journey. The noise against radial pattern symbolizes the chaos of longing amidst silence, inviting tension between calm and pressure throughout the composition.
