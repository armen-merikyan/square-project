Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between longing and resolve through contrasting pixel patterns. The palette uses clean white and ice blue to evoke clarity and serenity, layered with ink black for depth and violet gray for introspection. A strong pulse of warning orange adds urgency. The lattice structure on the left represents resolve, while the barcode pattern on the right symbolizes longing, creating a visual echo. The center features an intentional collision that disrupts the symmetry, manifesting the theme of repair and damage as broken pixels interject to form a narrative of conflict and harmony.
