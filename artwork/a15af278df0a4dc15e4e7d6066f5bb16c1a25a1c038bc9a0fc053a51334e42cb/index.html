Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve. The palette of sepia, tan, dust_gray, and faded_blue suggests nostalgia and depth, reflecting on memories. The contrast lies in the broken frame, symbolizing interruptions in emotions, while the lattice of longing collides with a structured grid representing resolve. This interplay creates a feeling of distance yet implies intimacy through proximity, illustrating the complex emotional landscape shaped by the seed.
