Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the tension between desire and restraint. The palette mixes warm, saturated reds and muted plums to evoke a push-and-pull sensation. The main collision occurs in the center where the bright peaks of warm colors compete against cooler, contained edges, creating a vivid burst amidst a restrained backdrop. Patterns of pressure and stacks play against each other, embodying the struggle encapsulated in the seed's themes of conflict and dichotomy, thus translating abstract emotions into a visual narrative.
