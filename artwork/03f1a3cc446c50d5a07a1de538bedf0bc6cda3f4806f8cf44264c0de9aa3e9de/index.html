Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between restlessness and peace through the use of a muted palette contrasted with vibrant accents. The arrangement creates a diagonal collision, where the darker edges creep in from one corner, representing anxiety and interruption, while lighter areas reflect calm moments and softness. The use of broken pixels embodies the theme of flawed memories, enhancing the overall feeling of fragmented tranquility.
