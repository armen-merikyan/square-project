Artist Statement

This square embodies the tension between longing and resolve through color and texture. The burnt orange and dark red evoke warmth and urgency, capturing the emotional struggle. Horizontal stripes symbolize resolve while constellations of broken pixels disrupt, suggesting interrupted desires. The main collision of colors occurs in the center, where the passionate reds clash with soothing charcoal, creating a visual narrative of conflict and hope amidst chaos.
