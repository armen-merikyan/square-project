Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between confusion and clarity. The palette of reds, blacks, and pale blues encapsulates both agitation and calm. The contrast is established through the left side of deep black and rich red, representing confusion, creating heavy density amidst scattered pale blue dots, while the right side uses whites and softer hues to represent clarity. This interplay emphasizes the split emotions, culminating in a chaotic arrangement that echoes the center-outward flow, where the broken pixels symbolize disruption amidst structured clarity.
