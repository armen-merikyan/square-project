Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a contrasting palette of muted purples and vibrant yellow accents. The central area, representing vulnerability, is exposed with lighter hues, suggesting fragility. Surrounding the center, deeper blues symbolize strength, creating a protective barrier. The edges are intentionally broken, embodying hard edges and disruption, while maintaining a wave-like flow that guides the eye from the edges inward, creating an interplay of tension and release. The balance between quiet spaces and dense clusters conveys a narrative of connection versus alienation, depicting a journey of navigating external pressures against internal shelter.
