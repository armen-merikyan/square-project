Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tensions of relief and dread through contrasting palettes and disrupted patterns. The palette blends light textures like pale mint and faded gold with heavy storm gray and near-black, aimed at visualizing the push and pull between chaos and order. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where the bright mint interrupts the darker shades of gray, symbolizing relief penetrating dread. Broken pixels create a fragmented quality, enhancing the chaos while preserving a sense of openness in the design, reflecting the intricate balance between pressure and reprieve induced by the seed's themes.
