Artist Statement

This piece evokes the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette combines deep blacks to evoke longing with vibrant greens and blues symbolizing resolve amidst chaos. The arrangement features a broken line of distinct dots in the lower left, suggesting a diagonal push contrasted by dense areas of color that evoke boundaries. This captures the narrative of movement—an unresolved journey toward coherence amidst chaos. The interplay of colors showcases how yearning spikes against firm resolve, creating an underlying tension throughout.
