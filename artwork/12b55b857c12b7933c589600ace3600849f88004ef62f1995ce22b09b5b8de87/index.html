Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional struggle between rage and calm, displaying a dominant split between deep, intense colors signifying rage on the left and soft, calming hues representing tranquility on the right. The use of reds and blacks evokes pressure and conflict, while the pale blue and white create a sense of spaciousness and peace. The jagged patterns on the left contrast with the horizontal, serene bands of color on the right, illustrating the push and pull of these opposing emotions. The 'tiny_cross' shape seed is abstractly represented by the interplay of color densities created by the arrangement. The overall flow is upward on the left, symbolizing a rising signal, while the right maintains a grounded presence, signifying calm.
