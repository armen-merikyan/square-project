Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers around the tension between vulnerability and strength, represented by the use of bright yellows and muted purples. The palette reflects a contrast where strength (deep blue and orange) dominates but is subtly interrupted by the vulnerability expressed through pale/internal shades. The collision occurs primarily in the center, with brighter, more vibrant colors pushing against calmer tones, symbolizing safety and exposure. The layout mirrors the notion of broken symmetry, creating a sense of imbalance and dynamic interplay that resonates with the theme of resilience amidst fragility.
