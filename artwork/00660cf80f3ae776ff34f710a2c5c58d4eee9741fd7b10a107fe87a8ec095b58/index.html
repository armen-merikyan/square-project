Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the dichotomy of longing and resolve through a blend of deep tonal contrasts and expansive quiet space. The palette centers around cosmic indigo and void black, offering a backdrop of mystery and depth, symbolizing longing. The accents of star white and cold gold introduce sharp contrasts, representing resolve and urgency, while deep teal provides a steadying rhythm amidst the tension. The design employs jagged edges and broken pixels to express the imperfect nature of emotions, symbolizing a journey of repair versus damage, and the intersection of memory with erasure. The main collision occurs at the center, where clashing patterns of static longing meet pressure bands of resolve, embodying a compelling emotional narrative. The layout, originating from the corner, gradually spreads across, creating a visual echo that enhances the sense of distant warmth amid the urgency, allowing the viewer to engage deeply with the unresolved tension of the composition.
