Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a duality between confusion and clarity, manifested through a palette of dark violet, soft gold, and pale green. This palette evokes a sense of tension—dark hues suggest depth and uncertainty, while the contrasting softer colors introduce a hint of hope or potential clarity. The layout reflects an invasion from one corner, demonstrating the theme of confusion as it disrupts the symmetry; the broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, emphasizing chaos amidst an underlying structure. The flow from top to bottom portrays a pulse of emotion, where the darker colors dominate at the top, giving way to lighter accents that represent clarity emerging from confusion, creating a dynamic interplay of contrast and collision between ordered structure and chaotic interruption.
