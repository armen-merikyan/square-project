Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional dance between longing and resolve, using a palette of warm and cool tones to represent each feeling. The collision occurs centrally with a dense arrangement of warm shades juxtaposed against the cool, empty spaces. The layout reflects a cross-current, visualizing the tension between controlled strength and chaotic yearning. Each color placement was carefully selected to create a push-pull dynamic that tells an abstract story of desire and resolution.
