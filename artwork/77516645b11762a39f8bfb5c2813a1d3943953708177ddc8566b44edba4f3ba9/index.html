Artist Statement

This composition embodies the struggle and interplay between defiance, represented by the bold, angular placements of colors, and submission, suggested through the softer, more muted areas. The palette of cosmic indigo and void black provides depth and contrast, creating an emotional tension that underscores the narrative of conflict. The center, predominantly filled with dynamic broken pixels, conveys a strong sense of defiance, while the corners and edges soften this intensity with layers of quiet colors like cold gold and deep teal, facilitating a balance between chaos and order.
