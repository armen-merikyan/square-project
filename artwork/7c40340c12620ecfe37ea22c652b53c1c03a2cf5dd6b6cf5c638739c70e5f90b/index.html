Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional struggle between panic and control, using a stark palette of black, white, and shades of blue to represent the contrasting feelings. The central area uses a split-screen pattern to invoke control, while the edges blur into a crack pattern, representing panic bleeding into the controlled center. The edge style is soft to create a sense of a fading boundary, where the central calm meets the chaotic outside, highlighting this emotional battle. The strategic placement of colors allows for moments of quiet juxtaposed with dense clusters, creating an unresolved tension.
