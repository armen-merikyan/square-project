Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between safety and exposure through contrasting colors and patterns. The chosen palette features warm browns and shelter greens to evoke feelings of safety, while cool tones of open blue and cold white suggest vulnerability. The central collision takes place in the middle rows, where broken dots representing safety fight against stacked lines representing exposure, creating a dynamic interplay. The layout emphasizes a vertical split, reinforcing the theme of tension and balance, while intentional disruptions in the pixel pattern introduce a sense of imperfection, echoing the unpredictability of such contrasts.
