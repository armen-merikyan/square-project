Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between the vulnerability of exposure and the embrace of safety. A central pale skin tone (#F5D6B0) symbolizes fragility, while surrounding shades of shelter green (#4A7C56) and warm brown (#8B5A2D) create a protective barrier, suggesting the fluid dynamics of pressure against release. The layout, informed by an 'open gate', emphasizes the contrast where a soft flow enters from the right, colliding with dense barriers on the left. This interplay yields a quiet space against denser clusters while evoking a feeling of echoing threats and gentle reprieves.
