Artist Statement

This composition portrays the emotional tension between love and betrayal, using contrasting colors to create a visual echo of duality. The warm reds and pinks symbolize love, while the darker grays and maroons represent betrayal and chaos. The placement of colors suggests a rising signal of hope amidst fractured elements, embodying the 'rising_signal' layout. The edges feature irregular shapes, embodying 'burned_edges' and imperfections that signify turmoil within harmony. The pattern of dots represents love's embrace and the maze reflects the entrapment of betrayal. The arrangement of empty spaces adds to the feeling of loss and potential, allowing the viewer to reflect on the balance of these emotions.
