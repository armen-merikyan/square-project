Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between the vibrant energy of 'spark' and the subdued essence of 'ash'. The palette harmonizes warm accent colors like hot coral and blood orange with cooler, muted tones of charcoal and hollow blue to suggest the tension between desire and emptiness. The main contrast occurs at the center, where the 'spark' patterns collide with 'ash' elements in a chaotic yet structured dance, representing the dynamic conflict of memory against erosion. Broken pixels intentionally disrupt the more uniform areas, symbolizing imperfections inherent in recollection while leaving space that suggests uncertainty and stillness.
