Artist Statement

This composition embodies a tension between dread and relief. The palette of pale mint, soft sky, and faded gold is contrasted against storm gray and near black, creating a dynamic interaction. The darker shades represent the weight of dread, while the lighter hues emerge as relief. The spiral flow adds complexity, suggesting a push and pull between these emotions. The broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, emphasizing imperfection and fragments of relief amidst looming shadows.
