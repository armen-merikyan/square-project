Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional dance between 'panic' and 'control' through a deliberate use of warm and cool colors. The palette mixes vibrant yellows and oranges, which symbolize the intensity of panic, with deep blues and muted purples representing calm control. The upper half embodies a noisy chaos created by clashing pixel placements, while the bottom reflects a control paradigm with structured circular patterns. Jagged edges disrupt symmetry, reflecting moments of imbalance and emotional bleed, mirroring the core seed concept of 'machine emotions'. The interactions and contrast culminate in a visual narrative of tension and contradiction, inviting the viewer to explore their own emotional responses.
