Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional duality of longing and resolve through its contrasting patterns and colors. The palette combines cosmic indigo and deep teal to represent depth and introspection, while the stark star white serves as a bright accent, symbolizing moments of clarity. The primary contrast occurs in the center with a dense cluster of colors embodying longing, creating a pulse that radiates outward. This reflects the seed's exploration of repair and damage, mixing sharp edges with smooth transitions to evoke an emotional landscape where desire meets determination.
