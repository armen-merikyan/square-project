Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between trust and suspicion through the use of warm tan and cool faded blue. The palette references an ash field subtly through sepia and dust gray, evoking memories of loss and resilience. The primary contrast occurs in the vertical split, where the soft, mirrored patterns of trust collide with the harsh cracks of suspicion, creating a visual tension that echoes the seed's themes of pressure and fall. The placement of darker shades around the edges suggests boundaries and borders, while the lighter tones in the center create a soft glow, representing hope amidst uncertainty. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, echoing the fragility of trust.
