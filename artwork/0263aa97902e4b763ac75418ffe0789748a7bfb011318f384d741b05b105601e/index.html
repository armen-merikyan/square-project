Artist Statement

The composition juxtaposes feelings of relief and dread through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette oscillates between the soft mint and pale gold, representing relief, and storm gray paired with near black, symbolizing dread. The spiral layout infuses a sense of movement and tension, with the collision occurring predominantly in the center, where darker shades spiral inward against lighter ones, creating visual and emotional friction. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the sense of chaotic tension against a structured backdrop.
