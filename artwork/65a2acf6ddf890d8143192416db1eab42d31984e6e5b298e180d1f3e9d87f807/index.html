Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances confusion and clarity, represented by a palette that juxtaposes grays with vibrant reds, denoting tension and relief. The dominant dark maroon creates weight, while scattered pinks symbolize bursts of unexpected clarity within chaos. The primary contrast occurs where the structured grid of pixels clashes against the organic, chaotic interruptions—this clash represents the seed's themes of broken symmetry and emotional turbulence. The layout suggests a rising flow, capturing the tension between upward momentum and falling disruption, reflecting the 'rising_signal' composition idea.
