Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a dynamic interplay between the stark contrasts of vulnerability and strength. The palette of cosmic indigo, void black, star white, cold gold, and deep teal communicates awe and terror, symbolizing the weight of experiences. The main collision occurs in the corner where deeper shades invoke a sense of dense pressure, while lighter colors radiate outward, creating a spiral that reflects both protective strength and the fragility of exposure. Broken pixel placements add intentional disruptions that mirror the imperfections of strength at its most vulnerable points.
