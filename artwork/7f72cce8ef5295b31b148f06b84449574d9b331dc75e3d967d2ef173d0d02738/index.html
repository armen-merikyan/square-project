Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork highlights the tension between longing and resolve, creating a landscape of organized chaos. The palette consists of clean white for clarity, ice blue for calmness, and warning orange for urgency, contrasting with the grounding ink black and violet gray. The center features a collision of stripes signifying resolve, framed by broken pixel imprints suggesting longing. This dynamic interplay creates a sense of decay against a structured core, embodying the complex emotions derived from the seed.
