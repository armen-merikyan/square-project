Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dichotomy between restlessness and peace, represented by a striking contrast of deep blacks and vibrant electric blue. The layout splits the canvas vertically, with the left side embodying chaos through disjointed 'islands' of accents (lime and magenta), while the right side offers a 'threaded' calmness, utilizing soft fading tones. A sense of motion is suggested as colors jostle for space, structured yet unbalanced, evoking the feeling of a silent argument. The seed's elements are expressed through the tension of colors colliding at the center, embodying memory and motion, while leaving ample negative space to emphasize tranquility on the right side. This structure allows the viewer to experience the push and pull of emotion without a literal representation, focusing purely on abstract interaction.
