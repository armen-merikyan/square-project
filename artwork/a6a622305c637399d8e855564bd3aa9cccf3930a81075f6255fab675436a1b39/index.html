Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the duality of joy and grief through contrasting color densities and arrangements. The left side employs bright yellows and oranges, capturing the essence of joy in a spark pattern, symbolizing exuberance and openness. The right side contrasts this with deeper blues and muted purples, reflecting the heavy and fragmented nature of grief in a split-screen layout. The uneven distribution creates weight on the left, invoking a gravitational pull towards the vibrant. The 'arrow' motif is abstracted into the color transitions, representing directionality amidst emotional turmoil. Overall, there’s a blend of structured symmetry clashing with breakages, highlighting the intersection of contrasting emotions.
