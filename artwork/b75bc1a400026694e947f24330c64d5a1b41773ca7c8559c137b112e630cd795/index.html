Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of loneliness and connection. The palette utilizes deep black and dark violet to suggest isolation, contrasting with the soft gold that symbolizes the flicker of warmth and hope. The composition is weighted towards the left, with broken pixels creating an imperfect rhythm that embodies memory's fragility. The dense area on the left collides against an expanse of pale green, illustrating the pull between emotional weight and the lightness of connection. This arrangement captures the abstract story of isolation threatening connection, echoing through the gaps.
