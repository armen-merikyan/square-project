Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between longing and resolve through contrasting dark and light elements. The palette utilizes navy for depth and emotional weight, with dim cyan providing highlights that evoke moments of clarity amidst chaos. Broken and irregular pixel placements serve to symbolize disruption and imperfection in the emotional narrative, accentuating the central diagonal collision that runs through the center, suggesting a struggle between unresolved feelings and firm resolution.
