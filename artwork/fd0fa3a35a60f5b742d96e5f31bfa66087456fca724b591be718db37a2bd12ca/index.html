Artist Statement

The emotional tone emerges from the interplay of longing and resolve, with a palette reflecting guilt and forgiveness to highlight complexity. The colors of deep wine and warm ivory create tension, while ashy gray provides a quiet space. The main collision occurs at the center, where the 'islands' of resolve meet the halos of longing, suggesting an unresolved narrative between stability and yearning. Broken pixels disrupt this flow, representing scars and repairs, enhancing the theme of contrast within the abstract pattern.
