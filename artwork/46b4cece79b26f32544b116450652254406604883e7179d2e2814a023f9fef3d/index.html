Artist Statement

The composition captures the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity, illustrated through a carefully selected palette that balances muted tones with sharp accents. The darker edges symbolize boundaries and order, while the scattered lighter shapes represent curiosity and exploration. The intentional disruption of color placement—particularly the clash of dark navy and compressed red in a chaotic pattern—reflects the tension between these two emotions. The main collision occurs in the center, creating a focal point of chaos that draws the eye toward the edges where order prevails. The seed's influence is seen in the island metaphor—structures of solid color contrasting against the unpredictable flow of curiosity's patterns, creating a visual landscape that is both contained and dissolving, echoing the complexity of the emotional narrative.
