Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between softness and severity, symbolizing the push and pull of human feelings. The palette blends soft tones like powder pink and warm white with sharper contrasts from carbon black and sharp crimson, creating a visual dialogue between gentle curves and striking edges. The primary collision occurs in the center, where the 'spark' pattern of light color interrupts the dense, dark waves of 'ripple' patterns, manifesting feelings of guilt and forgiveness within a structured chaos. The layout allows for a stark contrast in density, facilitating both calm openness and the encroaching severity of darker hues. The intentional breaks in the pixels add an element of imperfection, reflecting the nuanced journey of emotion.
