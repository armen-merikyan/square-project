Artist Statement

This composition reflects the struggle between clarity and static interference, visualized through a diagonal layout that emphasizes a readable rhythm of color among chaotic disruptions. The chosen palette of signal green, electric blue, and hot magenta embodies the contrasting identities of signal (clear and structured) versus noise (random and scattered). The use of radial patterns for noise and a checkered pattern for the signal creates visual tension at the center, where these emotions collide. Imperfections in the form of broken pixels add depth and complexity, symbolizing the scars of interference, while remnants of clear paths suggest hope and resilience amidst chaos.
