Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the piece conveys a struggle between emptiness and desire. The palette mixes warm reds with cool blues against a stark black background, creating vivid tension. The upper rows, filled with black, represent emptiness, while the lower rows burst with reds and blues symbolizing desire and warmth. The patterned disruption suggests a rising signal inviting complexity amidst stillness, capturing the internal conflict and resonance between these opposing emotions.
