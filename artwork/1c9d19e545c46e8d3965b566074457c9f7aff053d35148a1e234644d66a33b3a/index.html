Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic tension between loneliness and connection. The palette of muted purples, deep blues, yellows, and oranges evokes warmth while maintaining an undercurrent of grief. The contrast lies where deep blue and muted purple clash against the brighter yellows and oranges, suggesting bursts of connection amidst hidden loneliness. The central block yet fragmented patterns represent the struggle between exposure and internal barriers, creating an abstract reflection of the given seed.
