Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between desire and emptiness through color contrasts and spatial dynamics. The palette of hot coral and blood orange symbolizes desire's vibrant yet overwhelming nature, while the dark tones of charcoal and hollow blue represent the chill of emptiness and restraint. The central area is heavy with desire, creating a saturated pulse that confronts the quiet spaces surrounding it, reflecting the struggle to overflow against containment. The arrangement introduces disrupted patterns signifying the collision of these emotions, allowing the viewer to sense both the yearning to break free and the weight of limits.
