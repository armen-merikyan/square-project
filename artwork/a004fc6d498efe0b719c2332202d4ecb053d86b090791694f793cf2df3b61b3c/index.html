Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes dread with relief, illustrating an inevitable descent towards shadow while simultaneously invoking the calm of cool release. The palette blends somber storm gray and near black with understated pale mint and soft sky, representing the tension between heaviness and lightness. The composition's flow and layout reflect a fragmented descent from the top, where the weight of darkness clashes against the whisper of light, achieving a constant push and pull through interrupted gradients and broken pixels. The primary collision occurs in the center, highlighting the tension of spiraling chaos meeting loose echoes of relief.
