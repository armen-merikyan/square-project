Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece plays with the tension between longing and resolve. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold reflects warmth and complexity, embodying both the intensity of longing and the strength of resolve. The main contrast occurs through the diagonal layout, where longing's tides of swirling colors crash into the resolve's structured patterns, embodying a fractured yet dynamic relationship. The irregular placement of the colors signifies the brokenness and repair present in the emotional landscape, creating a visual narrative that speaks to the push-and-pull between these feelings.
