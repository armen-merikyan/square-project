Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between ignited ambition (spark) and residual memory (ash). The hot and bright colors push against cooler, muted tones, creating a visual tension that reflects the conflict inherent in personal resilience amidst decay. The palette is a deliberate choice to represent contrasting states—red for energy and vitality balanced with cool pale blue and black suggesting restraint and the weight of memory. The composition centers around a dense red area, suggesting an overwhelming vitality that bleeds outward, interrupted by cooler colors at the edges. The patterns collide and interact, forming a conflictual yet dynamic visual narrative of regeneration and neglect.
