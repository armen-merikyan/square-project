Artist Statement

This abstract composition embodies the tension between quietness and pressure, utilizing a muted palette dominated by off-white and dust gray with accents of compressed red and dark navy. The layout shifts from dense clusters in the corners to open quiet spaces towards the center, illustrating the struggle of pressure breaking against clarity. The broken pixel elements create intentional imperfections, mimicking emotional scars within the overall harmony of the piece.
