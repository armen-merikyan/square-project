Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between safety and exposure. The palette features muted greens and browns versus brighter skin tones and blues, representing warmth and coolness in contrast. The main contrast occurs in the center, where lighter colors interrupt the denser outer hues, symbolizing vulnerability amidst protection. The lattice pattern from exposure collides with the threshold from safety, creating a complexity that resonates with the seed's themes of protection against the elements, echoing the push-pull dynamic.
