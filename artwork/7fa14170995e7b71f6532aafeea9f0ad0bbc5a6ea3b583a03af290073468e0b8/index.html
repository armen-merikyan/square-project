Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between clarity and confusion using a dense palette of black, pale blue, red, and white. The top rows embody confusion with flame-like patterns, while the bottom rows suggest clarity through structured stair-like patterns. The collision between the structured and chaotic is emphasized by the hard edges that define the shapes, creating a stark contrast. The seed influences the fragmented visuals, symbolizing falling pixels and broken patterns, suggesting a narrative of unresolved emotions.
