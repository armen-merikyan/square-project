Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between longing and resolve, using dark violet and soft gold to evoke emotions of yearning against a backdrop of stability. The dominance of dark violet in the upper rows conveys a sense of unfulfilled desire, while the soft gold interspersed in the lower rows offers moments of resolution and clarity. The diagonal flow symbolizes the struggle of forces, creating a palpable sense of interruption. Each pixel placement creates a rhythm that oscillates between dense and empty spaces, echoing the emotional pull of the seed.
