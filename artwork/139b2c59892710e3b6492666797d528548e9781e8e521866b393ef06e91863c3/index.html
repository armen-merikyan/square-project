Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between fear and hope through contrasting colors and an asymmetrical layout. The palette of deep blacks and violets contrasts with soft golds and greens, symbolizing the struggle between dark, oppressive thoughts and the glimmer of optimism. The imperfect symmetry hints at disturbances, with a chaotic zigzag flow running through the edges. The darker tones dominate the corners, creating a feeling of being enclosed, while soft gold hints at emergent hope within the structure, evoking the balance between pressure and release. The arrangement of colors creates a rhythm that reflects emotional waves, embodying an abstract representation of rising signals of hope against a background of fear.
