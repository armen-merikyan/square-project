Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the struggle between control and surrender, with a palette that reflects melancholy through cool tones punctuated by warmer accents. The collision occurs predominantly at the center, where the warm lavender pushes against the soft cyan, symbolizing the overflow. The asymmetry in placement and broken pixels illustrates the tension in trying to contain what seeks to overflow, creating an abstract pattern that feels both structured and chaotic.
