Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the conflict between desire and emptiness, enhanced by a palette of dark and muted colors. The contrast arises from warm and cool tones, where the black serves as the anchor representing emptiness, while the dim cyan hints at the yearning of desire. The arrangement flows from the center outward, emphasizing a fight between the inner dynamics of warmth and coolness, echoing the push-pull of longing. Broken pixels introduce subtle chaotic moments, reflecting a heart fragmented amidst a minimalized presence.
