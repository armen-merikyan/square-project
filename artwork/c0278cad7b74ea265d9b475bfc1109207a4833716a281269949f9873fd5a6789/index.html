Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dance between confusion and clarity, using a palette that embodies intensity ('burnt_orange', 'dark_red') and grounding elements ('charcoal', 'gold'). The pattern reflects the idea of chaos with broken symmetry and a flow from bottom to top, creating pressure that lifts against quiet spaces. The color collisions, particularly where the warm hues meet darker tones, suggest internal conflicts, while broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, reinforcing the theme of emotional collision.
