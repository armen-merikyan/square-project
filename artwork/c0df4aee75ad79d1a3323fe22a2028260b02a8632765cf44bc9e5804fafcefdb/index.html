Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional interplay between certainty and curiosity by contrasting dense, dark colors with bright sparks of light. The selected palette of burnt orange and dark red reflects a smoldering tension, symbolizing both fear and fervor. The layout's center is tumultuous, dominated by deep colors that represent chaos, whereas the softer borders offer glimpses of order and curiosity. The bright accents of gold create dynamic interruptions within the dense central mass, embodying a struggle for light amidst the weight. The overall arrangement reflects the battle between straight and curved paths, mirroring the seed's ideas of rising signals and receding edges, creating a visual metaphor for tension and release.
