Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between love and betrayal, symbolizing the duality within relationships. The palette features warm pink and red alongside somber gray and dark maroon, embodying the warmth of love and the coldness of betrayal. The main contrast occurs in the dense upper half where vibrant colors clash against softer shades, representing conflicting emotions. The pattern of rising colors mirrors hope, while fragments of density suggest the interruption of peace, thus leading to an abstract storytelling of turmoil and resilience without literal representation.
