Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between 'signal' and 'noise', representing a clear pulse amidst static interference. The chosen palette, dominated by navy and dim cyan, evokes a sense of isolation and focus, with striking white accents providing clarity against the overwhelming noise. The collision occurs in the center, where the brighter colors conflict with darker shades. The layout features significant negative space to amplify the sense of solitude, with selective use of broken pixels to suggest the imperfections inherent in memory and experience.
