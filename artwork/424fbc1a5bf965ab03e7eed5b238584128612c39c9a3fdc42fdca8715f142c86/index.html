Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the tension between confusion and clarity, using a palette that juxtaposes stark black and white against soft pale blue. The central concept of 'symmetry vs break' is depicted through a nearly mirrored arrangement, disrupted by broken pixels that add imperfection and depth, thereby introducing a sense of chaos underlying the calm. The contrast between light and dark creates a pulsating rhythm across the canvas, as the interplay of checker patterns (representing clarity) and broken dots (for confusion) evoke conflicting emotional states. This square embodies a narrative of struggle and resolution within abstract bounds.
