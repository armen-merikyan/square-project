Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a duality of exposure and shelter, represented through a color palette that contrasts soothing pastels with darker, grounding tones. The 'pale mint' and 'soft sky' offer an exposed center, suggesting vulnerability, while 'storm gray' and 'near black' provide the protective edges that symbolize strength. The main contrast occurs in the center, where colors collide amidst a subdued background, evoking a feeling of tension and potential release. The zigzag flow of colors enhances this emotional narrative, guiding the viewer's eye through moments of stark confrontation and gentle softening. Broken pixels add intentional imperfections, amplifying the sense of raw authenticity and emotional complexity.
