Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional interplay between trust and suspicion, represented by contrasting colors: warm muted creams suggest trust, while deep blackened plums evoke suspicion. The dominant use of cream (63% coverage) creates a soft glow interspersed with harsh blackened accents (13% coverage) that signify disruptions and echoes. The pattern of 'stairs' reflects the rising tension and layered unpredictability, while the 'rain' motif suggests a gentle persistence that seeks to pierce through noise. Overall, the contrasting colors and patterns converge in the center, creating a quiet boundary that symbolizes resilience amid the friction of suspicion.
