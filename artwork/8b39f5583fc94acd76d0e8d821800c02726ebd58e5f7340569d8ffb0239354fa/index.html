Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension of trust versus suspicion through opposing color fields and intricate patterns. The selected palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold creates a warm, earthy base against the starkness of shadows, embodying the concept of ancient fire. The 'tide' wave pattern in warm hues suggests trust's soft embrace, while the 'crosshatch' strokes of dark red repel with suspicion. The main contrast occurs at the center, where these colors collide and intertwine, creating dynamic tension with a balance of disrupted flow and structured chaos. This visual story reflects the push-pull of trust and skepticism, inviting the viewer to contemplate the delicate dance of emotions.
