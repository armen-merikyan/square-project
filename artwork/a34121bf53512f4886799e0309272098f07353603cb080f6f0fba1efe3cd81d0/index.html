Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between joy and grief. The warm yellow and orange hues suggest joy, while deep blue and muted purple evoke a sense of sorrow. The layout reflects a vertical flow from grief below to joy above, signifying a journey through emotional influence. The use of random cracks and broken pixels contributes to the theme of imperfection inherent in both joy and grief. The contrast between dense, warm areas and cooler expanses generates a feeling of conflict and balance, where joy attempts to break through the heaviness of grief.
