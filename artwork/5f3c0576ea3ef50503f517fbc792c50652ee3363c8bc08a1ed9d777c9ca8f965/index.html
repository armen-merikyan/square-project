Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between vibrant desire and oppressive emptiness. The palette combines warm and cool colors, with hot coral and blood orange exuding the urge for connection, while charcoal and hollow blue impart a sense of cold emptiness. The design emphasizes a stark contrast between densely packed colors in the center symbolizing desire, colliding with the sparse crosshatch pattern depicting emptiness on the edges. Broken pixels disrupt the rigid border, suggesting a struggle against containment, perfectly embodying the theme of 'hunger_vs_restraint'.
