Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through an arrangement of muted tones contrasting against sharp bursts of color. The palette features shades of sepia and faded blue, evoking nostalgia and a melancholic warmth. The broken pixel patterns symbolize the fragility of connection, contrasted against dense, dark segments that represent isolation. The layout begins in the corner, gradually expanding through a gradient of intensity, illustrating the struggle of reaching out yet feeling withdrawn. The top-weighted design adds visual gravity, while the varying patterns enhance the narrative of disconnection fighting against the need for belonging.
