Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tones of contrast and pressure, exploring the dichotomy of quiet and rhythmic release. The palette chosen includes deep, muted colors juxtaposed with bright accents to represent the interplay between tension and tranquility. The dense clusters of dark colors suggest compression and weight, while vibrant colors like neon green and bold red interrupt the space, signifying sudden feelings of joy and surprise amidst a calmer background. As the eye travels, these collisions create a visual rhythm that mirrors the complex emotions described in the seed.
