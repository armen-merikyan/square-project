Artist Statement

This 8x8 pixel arrangement embodies the emotional contrast of defiance and submission. The deep blue and muted purple serve as a somber, heavy backdrop, suggesting the weight of submission, while bright accents of yellow and orange pierce through, representing defiance and resistance. The central section features a tumultuous interaction between the pressure bands of defiance and the softer patchwork of submission, creating a dynamic clash. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, symbolizing the constant struggle between chaos and order, where tension builds around a quiet center, revealing the underlying battle of emotions.
