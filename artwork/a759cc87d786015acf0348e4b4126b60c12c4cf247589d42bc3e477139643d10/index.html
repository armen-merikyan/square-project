Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between a readable signal and overwhelming noise. The palette embraces contrasting colors—vivid greens and blues suggesting clear communication against dark and static greys, representing confusion and disruption. The main collision occurs along the diagonal, where vibrant spatial rhythms clash with muted zones, illustrating disruption in the flow. The half-square shape seed hints at imbalance and echo, informing the placement of stacked colors juxtaposed by cracked, static areas. Volume and density vary across the grid, fostering either calmness or interference throughout the square.
