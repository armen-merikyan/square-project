Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between envy and gratitude, represented by dark low contrasts and bright accents. The selected palette of black, electric blue, lime, and magenta conveys tension and movement, where the stark contrasts symbolize rising and falling emotions. The top rows, filled with fragmented patterns, evoke envy’s chaotic nature, while the bottom rows offer structured grids signifying gratitude’s grounding force. This arrangement creates a visual disturbance that hints at internal conflict, echoing the emotional weight of the seed.
