Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional duality of quiet versus pressure, illustrated through a gradient of color density. The palette of muted off-white and soft peach contrasts with deep navy and compressed red, reflecting pressure's intensity against the calm stillness of the quiet. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the densely packed maze pattern of dark colors encounters the softer pulse marks of lighter hues, suggesting a dynamic interplay between chaos and serenity. The layout reflects a struggle for control, translating the seed's themes into a visual argument, with broken pixels serving as disruptions that highlight imperfections amidst the tension.
