Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a warm coral center surrounded by cooler shades. The soft amber represents the exposure, while the cold slate and blue-black provide contrast, suggesting an environment that feels both protected and invaded. The pattern combines stairs and a grid to create a structured yet fluid rhythm, symbolizing the wave-like flow of emotions. The contrast resides primarily in the center, where the warm coral juxtaposes the cooler colors, creating a visual 'spark' amidst the 'flood.' Intentionally placed broken pixels bring a sense of imperfection, enhancing the idea of vulnerability.
