Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional contrast between safety and exposure. The palette integrates 'shelter_green' for soothing stability, 'warm_brown' for a gentle weight, 'pale_skin' representing vulnerability, 'cold_white' for stark openness, and 'open_blue' symbolizing expansive freedom. The main contrast occurs diagonally, with the saturation of 'warm_brown' pressing against the delicate lines of 'cold_white', creating a dynamic tension. The broken pixels offer a sense of imperfection, echoing the vessel-like nature of our emotional states, where softness and severity weave together.
