Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between joy and grief, reflected through a palette of warm yellows and oranges interacting with deep blues and muted purples. The conflict and resonance arise from zigzag patterns symbolizing joy's dynamic nature against the grief depicted in the ring-like structure, creating an impression of both structure and chaos. The broken edges introduce a sense of imperfection and unresolved feelings, while negative space invites contemplation.
