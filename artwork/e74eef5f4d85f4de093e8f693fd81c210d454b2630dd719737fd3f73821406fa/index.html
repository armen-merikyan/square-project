Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional conflict between trust and suspicion through a nuanced palette of deep darks and soft lights. The interplay between black and dark violet represents the weight of suspicion, while the soft gold and pale green offer a gentle counterbalance of trust. The design's central dynamic collision occurs in the middle, where radial patterns of suspicion meet the more chaotic noise patterns of trust, creating a vibrant tension that ebbs and flows across the composition. The structured randomness adds a feeling of resilience amidst fragility, reflecting the struggles conveyed in the seed text.
