Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dialogue between defiance and submission through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, 'broken_memory', underscores a sense of faded clarity with sepia and faded blue tones, while the dusty grays introduce subdued emotional tension. The center of the grid, punctuated by brighter accents, represents defiance—a focus of emerging strength amid chaos. In contrast, the muted fields surrounding symbolize submission, creating a delicate balance of pressure against quietness. The layout allows for disrupted patterns—crosshatch and stack—clashing at the center, where intensity peaks and reveals 'broken pixels' as vital scars in the narrative, evoking a sense of imperfect repair and resilience.
