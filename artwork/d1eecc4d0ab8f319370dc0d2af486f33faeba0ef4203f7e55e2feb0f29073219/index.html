Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between vulnerability and strength, using the palette of soft powder pink and warm white to suggest a delicate core, set against the severity of carbon black and steel gray that represents hard edges and resilience. The main contrast occurs in the center, where the pale colors collide with darker shades, evoking a sense of both exposure and shelter. The layout splits the canvas into sections, with fault lines and cracks suggesting ruptures in the emotional landscape, while broken pixels add intentional imperfections, reinforcing themes of connection and alienation.
