Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional interplay of desire and restraint. The palette, including deep reds and muted creams, reflects the passionate yet controlled nature of these feelings. The blackened plum and iron gray create a rich contrast, resembling tension between warm bursts and cool containment. The central pattern of saturated noise disrupts and overflows against the structured, dot-like restraint, symbolizing the struggle between free expression and self-imposed boundaries. Emotional intensity peaks at the center, creating a focal point that suggests both conflict and resolution.
