Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts joy and grief, using a palette of vibrant yellows and muted purples to represent the dichotomy. The left-weighted layout creates a sense of pressure, with stripes of joy breaking through the teeth-like patterns of grief, leading to interruptions and echoes throughout the grid. The square frame serves as a central seed, surrounded by dynamic tension that captures the struggle between cohesion and fragmentation.
