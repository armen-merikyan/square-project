Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between joy and grief, using a palette that balances bright oranges and yellows against muted blues and purples. The center is a chaotic blend of deeper hues, suggesting grief and emotional weight, while the outer areas radiate bright tones, symbolizing joy and potential expansion. The symmetry is interrupted by a peculiar arrangement of broken pixels, invoking a sense of imbalance, echoing the feelings suggested in the seed. The vertical flow mimics a falling pattern, enhancing the overall feel of descent, which serves to elevate the emotional discourse around the pulse of joy fighting through a backdrop of grief. These choices create an abstract narrative of duality, collision, and the space where sorrow and happiness coalesce.
