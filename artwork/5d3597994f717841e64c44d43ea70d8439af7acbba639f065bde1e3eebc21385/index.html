Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between clarity and interference. The chosen palette of signal green, electric blue, and hot magenta contrasts sharply against the muted shades of dead black and static gray, embodying the conflict between signal (clear, readable paths) and noise (static disruption). The 'thread' and 'stripes' patterns clash in the center, illustrating the collision of structured rhythm against chaotic bursts, reflecting the essence of the seed. Negative space is preserved, allowing for visual breathing, while broken pixels add intentional disruptions, suggesting fragments of memory and fading echoes.
