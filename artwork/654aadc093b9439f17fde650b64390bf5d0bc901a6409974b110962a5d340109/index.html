Artist Statement

This artwork explores the contrast between loneliness and connection through a geodesic pattern that emanates from the center, represented by dark colors, suggesting a hidden core under pressure. The palette includes navy and dim cyan to evoke depth and calmness while creating a stark collision with the darker elements. The flow suggests a pulsating energy, with broken pixels symbolizing the imperfect nature of human relationships. The inner burst faces the quiet outer edges, emphasizing the emotional struggle of connection against isolation.
