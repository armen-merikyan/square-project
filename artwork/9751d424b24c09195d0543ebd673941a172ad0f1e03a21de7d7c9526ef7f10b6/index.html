Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, expressing a delicate balance through contrasting hues and forms. The palette blends cool 'shelter_green' and 'cold_white' with warm 'warm_brown' and 'pale_skin', signifying the interplay of protection and exposure. The main collision of pressure and softness occurs in the top left corner, where dense colors are intermingled with lighter shades, representing a flood against a stack. The edges taper into eroded forms, maintaining an organic flow while creating a sense of asymmetry and movement. The chosen abstract theme reflects a central seed of a 'ladder', manifesting as a complex arrangement—suggesting ascent yet frailty. This leads to an overall atmosphere of contemplation, showcasing the push and pull between opposing emotions without literal representation.
