Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between restlessness and peace through a stark contrast in color and pattern. The selected palette of cosmic indigo and void black provides a strong emotional depth while the hints of star white and cold gold add contrasting lightness, suggesting a conflict between quiet symmetry and uneven rhythms. The layout of falling pixels evokes a sense of tears or rain, emphasizing the interplay of motion and memory. The dominant dark colors interrupt with bright accents, mirroring the emotional disruption precluded by restlessness against the backdrop of an anchor of peace on one side.
