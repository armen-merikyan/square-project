Artist Statement

This composition embodies a contrast between dense pressure and quiet release through its pixel arrangement. The palette blends muted tones of off-white, dust gray, and faint peach with stronger accents in compressed red and dark navy. The off-white and dust gray provide a calm foundation, while the red introduces tension, suggesting pressure. The layout sees the colors split diagonally, creating a collision at the center where soft hues meet sharper edges, manifesting the struggle between quiet and pressure. Broken pixels punctuate this tension, adding an element of imperfection and shattering that encapsulates the emotional journey.
