Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between envy and gratitude through a contrasting palette of muted sepia and faded blue. The left side, representing envy, spills in jagged, flame-like patterns, while the right side embodies gratitude through soft dot patterns, maintaining a sense of lightness. The irregularity introduced by broken pixels emphasizes a mirror-like quality disrupted, enhancing the feeling of imbalance. The negative space invites contemplation, making the viewer reflect on the delicate dance of emotion in their lives.
