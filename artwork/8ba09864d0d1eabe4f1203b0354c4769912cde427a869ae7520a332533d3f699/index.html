Artist Statement

This artwork navigates between nostalgia and progress, illustrating the push and pull of memory and advancement. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of time and recollection, while providing a soft contrast against sharper, more vibrant moments, creating tension. The pattern mimics a crosshatch layout for progress and rain-like flows representing nostalgia, creating an asymmetry that suggests a dynamic emotional landscape. The collision occurs where dark and muted colors interact with softer shades, highlighting contrasts and evoking feelings of yearning and movement. The bottom-weighted design emphasizes the heaviness of nostalgia while allowing the upper segments to suggest potential, capturing the struggle and harmony between contrasting emotions.
