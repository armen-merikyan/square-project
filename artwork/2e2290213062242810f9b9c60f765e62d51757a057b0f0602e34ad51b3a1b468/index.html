Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects an interplay between clarity and disruption, symbolizing the struggle of a bright signal against the overwhelming presence of noise. The palette, dominated by shades of signal green and electric blue with accents of hot magenta, evokes a sense of urgency and visibility amidst static gray and dead black, suggesting a vibrant path amidst confusion. The main collision occurs in the center, where brighter colors clash with darker hues, signifying moments of clarity deeply embedded within chaotic noise. The spiral layout enhances the forward movement, capturing a sense of exposure countered by sheltering layers, embodying the essence of the seed.
