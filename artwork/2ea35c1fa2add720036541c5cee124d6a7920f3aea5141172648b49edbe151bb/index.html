Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between desire and restraint, represented by a palette that juxtaposes warm, vibrant colors against cooler, muted shades. The core of the artwork features a collision in the center where hot coral (#FF6F61) radiates outward, symbolizing desire, while surrounding hues of charcoal (#333333) and hollow blue (#A9C3E0) invoke feelings of restraint. This dynamic interplay captures the essence of pressure and release, achieved through the contrasting flow within the lattice layout. Broken pixels are integrated as marks of imperfection, enhancing the narrative of tension and interruption.
