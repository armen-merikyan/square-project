Artist Statement

The emotional tone in this artwork reflects the battle between defiance and submission, conveyed through contrasting colors and geometry. The palette includes off-white and dust gray to create a serene backdrop, while compressed red introduces tension. The collision occurs in the center, where angular patterns rise against the quiet fields, symbolizing resistance. This arrangement speaks to the dynamic struggle and pulse of life, subtly echoing the seed's themes of chaos and order rather than depicting them literally.
