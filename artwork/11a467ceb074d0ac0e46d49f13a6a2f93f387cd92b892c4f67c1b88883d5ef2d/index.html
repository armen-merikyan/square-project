Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between fear and hope. The left side represents fear through darker tones with jagged patterns, creating a feeling of pressure and confinement. The right side, illuminated with soft gold and pale green, instills hope through circular patterns suggesting growth and possibilities. The glowing edges contrast between the two halves, denoting a crossing point between fear's heavy density and hope's uplifting lightness. The split layout symbolizes the juxtaposition of descending anxiety against the ascent toward optimism, with broken pixels adding an imperfect, raw quality to the transition.
