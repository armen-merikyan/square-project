Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between dread and relief, using a palette that contrasts dark, stormy colors with soft, light hues. The collision occurs predominantly towards the bottom left, where dense clusters of darker shades (storm_gray and near_black) are juxtaposed with the quiet fields of pale_mint and soft_sky, representing a struggle that begins tightly but loosens into air. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, adding to the chaotic feeling of the dread emotion while maintaining a minimal negative space, essential for emphasizing the sense of relief as it pours out from the structured chaos.
