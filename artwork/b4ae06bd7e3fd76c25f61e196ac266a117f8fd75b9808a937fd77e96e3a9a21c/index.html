Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between joy and grief, creating an undercurrent of conflict. The palette combines bright yellows and oranges for joy with deep blues and muted purples for grief, establishing a vivid contrast. The layout follows a rising signal, with clusters of warm colors at the bottom transitioning to cooler tones above, symbolizing the struggle between uplift and weight. This split conveys the push and pull of emotion, accentuated by jagged edges enhancing the sense of chaos. The patterns are defined by stripes representing grief and dots for joy, creating a rhythmic interplay that echoes the themes of hidden vs. exposed emotions.
