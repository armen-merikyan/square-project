Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of joy and grief through a contrasting palette of vibrant and muted colors. The chosen colors—bright yellows and oranges representing flames of joy—collide with deep blues and muted purples that evoke the weight of grief. The layout follows a near-mirrored symmetry, with slight disruptions that allow for a sense of chaos amidst order. The top half embodies brightness and openness while the bottom anchors in deeper tones, suggesting a journey from light to shadow, echoing the emotional swell. The subtle broken pixels create a sense of fragmented memory, enhancing the theme of hidden versus exposed feelings.
