Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional landscape between longing and resolve, using a palette that balances cool and warm tones to evoke depth and warmth. The primary contrast occurs where the muted lavender-gray clashes with the vibrant soft cyan, signifying the struggle between chaos and order. The barcode pattern of longing interplays with pulse marks of resolve across the canvas, creating a dynamic interplay that suggests both connection and distance. The layout intentionally maintains negative space to emphasize the emotional tension, reflecting the themes of intimacy and distance.
