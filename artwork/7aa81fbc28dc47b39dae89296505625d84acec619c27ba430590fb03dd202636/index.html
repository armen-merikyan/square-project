Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between panic and control. The palette combines deep navy and black to reflect a sense of insecurity, while hints of dim cyan symbolize fleeting moments of clarity. The layout features a mirrored structure disrupted by broken pixels, creating feelings of instability. Contrasting patterns of noise and cracks symbolize the struggle between chaotic thoughts and the desire for order. The main collision occurs where the navy and black intermix, highlighting the emotional conflict inherent in the seed.
