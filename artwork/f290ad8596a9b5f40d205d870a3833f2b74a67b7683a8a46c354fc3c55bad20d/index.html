Artist Statement

This artwork captures the dichotomy of restlessness and peace through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette is constructed from warm yellows and oranges juxtaposed with deep blues and muted purples, representing the heat of turmoil against the calm of tranquility. The collision occurs diagonally across the grid, where vibrant accents collide with muted tones, suggesting a push-pull dynamic of emotion. The falling pixel layout symbolizes interrupted thoughts and memories moving from an unsettled chaos to silence, while broken pixels create irregularities that enhance the theme of tension within structure.
