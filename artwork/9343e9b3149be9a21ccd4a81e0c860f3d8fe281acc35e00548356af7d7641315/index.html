Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a balance of warmth and tension, inspired by love's vibrancy contrasting with betrayal's darkness. The palette incorporates soft pinks and reds to represent love, disrupted by dark maroon and gray as reflections of loss and disillusionment. The layout is centered, with a main structural contrast between the orderly rings of love and the chaotic checker patterns of betrayal. This creates a sense of pulsing motion, with collisions at the edges to evoke feelings of fragmentation and the ache of resilience.
