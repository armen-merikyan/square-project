Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the dichotomy of trust and suspicion, expressed through a palette that oscillates between muted warmth and cool distance, creating a sense of tension and resolution. The layout suggests an overwhelming force (dark tones) infiltrating the lighter spaces, with darker blocks interrupting flow, representing suspicion intruding upon trust. The central clash occurs around the diagonal, conveying instability and the idea of resilience through shadowy forms, while lighter hues suggest hope amid the descent of pressure.
