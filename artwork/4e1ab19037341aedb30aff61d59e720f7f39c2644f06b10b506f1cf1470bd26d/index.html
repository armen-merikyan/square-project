Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the clash between pride and shame, evoking visual tension through contrasting colors. The selected palette, with its deep reds and grays, represents the raw intensity of these feelings, suggesting a struggle between vibrancy (pride) and muted tones (shame). The upward movement signifies a push against gravitational shame, portraying an abstract ascent and descent of emotion. The arrangement emphasizes a diagonal rise with sections of dense color and deliberate spaces for quiet, instilling a sense of unresolved tension and release, reflecting the conflicting emotional collisions derived from the seed.
