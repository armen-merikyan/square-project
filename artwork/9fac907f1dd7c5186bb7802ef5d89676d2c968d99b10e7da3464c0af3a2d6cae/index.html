Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the ongoing struggle between vulnerability and strength, reflected through a stark contrast of sharp-edged, dense outer colors and softer, muted inner tones. The palette employs sepia and faded blue to evoke nostalgia and melancholy, respectively, suggesting a narrative of longing and pressure. The sharp versus soft contrast emphasizes the tension of the central 4x4 area which is more subdued and vulnerable while being encircled by a stronger, darker border. The intentional placement of broken pixels mirrors imperfection and disruption within this tension. Overall, the arrangement tells a story of conflict amidst a structured narrative of strength surrounding softness, providing a sense of protection intertwined with exposure.
