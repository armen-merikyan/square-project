Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, with a dense, dark core representing strength surrounded by lighter, softer hues signaling vulnerability. The palette incorporates 'signal_green' and 'electric_blue' for a dynamic interplay while maintaining 'static_gray' and 'dead_black' as grounding fields, ensuring a stark contrast that echoes through the arrangement. The central collision of darker tones symbolizes resilience amid a delicate outer layer, achieving a balance between pressure and release amidst the negative spaces, forming a reflective horizon.
