Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between chaos and order. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green conveys the struggle of panic (darkness and chaos) against control (light and clarity). The main contrast occurs at the center where soft gold clashes with the surrounding black, representing a burst of order amidst turmoil. The pattern flows inward, emphasizing the 'outside-inward' emotional movement, while the jagged edges introduce a sense of brokenness, enhancing the emotional depth. The structured layout of falling pixels mirrors the descent of control into panic, creating a compelling abstract narrative.
