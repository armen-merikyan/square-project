Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a dynamic interplay between softness and severity. The palette, featuring powder pink and warm white, conveys a delicate lightness, while the dark accents of carbon black and sharp crimson introduce a stark contrast. The layout suggests a mirrored dichotomy, disrupted by intentional broken pixels that evoke a sense of interference, reflecting the essence of 'signal vs noise'. The main collision occurs centrally where pressures collide, accentuating the tension of softness versus severity, forming a spiral rhythm that encapsulates an emotional pulse amongst static disruptions.
