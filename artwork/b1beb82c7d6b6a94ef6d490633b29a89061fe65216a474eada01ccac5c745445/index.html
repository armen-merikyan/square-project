Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between strength and vulnerability, showcased through a palette that combines muted, grounding colors with sharper accents. The 'guilt_forgiveness' palette emphasizes the tension between dense darks and bright highlights. The main collision occurs in the center with darker shades representing strength, while lighter, softer hues seep in from the borders, suggesting vulnerability. The broken pixels create intentional disruptions, echoing the feelings of exposure against a protective frame. This pattern forms an abstract story of conflict and coexistence, where strength attempts to shelter vulnerability yet remains susceptible.
