Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between longing and resolve through contrasting color strategies and spatial arrangement. The palette, rich with electric blue and hot magenta, conveys urgency and a push against a backdrop of static gray and dead black, representing calm and stability. The collision occurs at the center where the barcode motif of resolve intersects with the broken pixel array of longing. This creates a visual echo of the emotional themes reflected in the seed, suggesting an interplay of hope and despair, clarity and ambiguity.
