Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of longing and void, captured through warm yellow and orange hues contrasting with cool deep blue and muted purple. The layout starts from the corner depicting a slow, almost invasive rise of desire against a backdrop of emptiness, creating tension. The pixel bleed edge style introduces a sense of imperfection, mirroring the struggle between aspiration and desolation. Main contrast occurs in the central area where warm colors collide with cool tones, symbolizing the battle between feelings. The broken pixels add to this discord, emphasizing emotional fragmentation.
