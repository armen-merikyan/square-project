Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a palette of dark maroon, pink, and gray. The composition features a central band of soft, light colors, symbolizing exposure, surrounded by darker shades representing strength and protection. The structured rows create a sense of stability while the broken pixels introduce unexpected disruptions, echoing the underlying struggle between pressure and relief. The top-to-bottom flow implies a journey of rising signals and interactions, culminating in a vivid engagement of colors that invite contemplation.
