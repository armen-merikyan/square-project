Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances warmth and coolness, representing love through colors like pink and red, while betrayal is represented by darker gray and maroon hues. The arrangement creates a stark contrast between the soft maze of the top half and the aggressive mirror pattern of the bottom half, depicting an internal emotional struggle. This tension culminates with sharp, hard edges around softer colors to signify the conflict of feelings, with broken pixels symbolizing imperfection and raw emotional experience.
