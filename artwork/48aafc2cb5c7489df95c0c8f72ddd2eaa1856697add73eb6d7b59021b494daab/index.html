Artist Statement

The emotional tone is established through the interplay of restlessness and peace, creating a dance between chaos and calm. The color palette choosing muted sepia and tan echoes memory and nostalgia, while faded blue serves as a calm relief amidst the restlessness. The main contrast occurs in the center, where softer halo patterns of peace collide with jagged shatter patterns representing unrest. The edges are sealed to further emphasize containment yet echo the struggle with closed corners, enhancing the emotional tension.
