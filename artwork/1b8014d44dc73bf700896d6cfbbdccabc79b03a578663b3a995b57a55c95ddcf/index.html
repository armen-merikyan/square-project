Artist Statement

This artwork captures the duality of confusion and clarity by using a palette inspired by contrasting emotional states: vibrant yellow and muted purple. The top half utilizes a radial pattern with deep blue and orange hues to represent chaos and distortion, while the bottom half shifts into a structured ring pattern, echoing a sense of order. Broken pixels are introduced to signify imperfection and interruption within the overall rhythm. The placement of colors creates tension between warm and cool elements, emphasizing the struggle between clarity and confusion.
