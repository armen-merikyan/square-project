Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The selected palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of nostalgia and past, while contrasting the darker hues with lighter ones to highlight moments of clarity and intention. The main collision occurs in the center, where the structured grid of longing meets the circular patterns representing resolve, creating an intricate dance of resolution amidst desire. The mirrored edge with intentional disturbances symbolizes the dynamic interplay between order and chaos, capturing the essence of the seed's themes.
