Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the juxtaposition of emptiness and desire, represented through a blend of warm and cool colors. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, and gold communicates warmth and yearning, contrasting sharply with charcoal, portraying a sense of void. The left side employs a grid pattern to signify structured desire, while the right side shifts to a radial composition suggesting chaos and fragmentation, embodying emptiness. The thick outlines contribute to a feeling of separation, heightening the contrast between the two emotional states. This layout creates a dynamic tension; the interplay of warm and cool tones evokes an unresolved emotional story, revealing the complexities of longing against the backdrop of isolation.
