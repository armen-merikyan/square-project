Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between softness and severity. The palette features soft powder pink and warm white tones juxtaposed against the harshness of carbon black and sharp crimson, embodying the internal conflict suggested by the seed. The center of the grid serves as a focal point of collision where soft spark patterns clash with radial severity, creating pulses of energy amidst negative space. This arrangement reflects an interplay of emotions, highlighting guilt and forgiveness as they oscillate within a structured chaos.
