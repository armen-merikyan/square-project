Artist Statement

The emotional tone here revolves around the struggle between panic and control, where the dark colors signify the weight of anxiety, while lighter, broken-dot patterns break through to suggest fleeting moments of hope. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue embodies a sense of nostalgic tension, enhancing the feeling of broken memories contrasted against the urge for stability. A diagonal spread from the bottom left showcases a pull between dense dark areas and negative space, capturing the dynamic interplay between chaos and calm.
