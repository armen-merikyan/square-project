Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional duality of envy and gratitude. The palette, drawn from 'broken memory,' utilizes muted, warm hues like sepia and tan contrasted with cooler tones like faded blue, symbolizing the tension between aggressive envy and serene gratitude. The arrangement features a strong focal point in the center, surrounded by a zigzag pattern invoking both chaos and order, while strategic broken pixel placements introduce a sense of imperfection and fragility. The primary collision occurs at the center, where warm hues clash subtly with cool shades, evoking the struggle and push-pull of these emotions.
