Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, where a dense base of darker colors represents strength, while a pale core embodies vulnerability. The palette features saturated reds and pinks against gray and dark maroon, showcasing deep emotional conflicts. The central cluster contrasts with the surrounding muted tones, symbolizing exposure against the encroaching darkness. This reflects themes of pressure and delicate balance, as the broken pixel elements disrupt the flow, suggesting the fragility of strength in the face of vulnerability.
