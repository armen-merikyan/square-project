Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between defiance and submission, represented through a division in color and structure. The left side exudes a deep maroon intertwined with soft gray, signifying defiance's strength wrapped in vulnerability. In contrast, the right side is punctuated with lighter shades of pink, creating a sense of submission intertwined with hope. The deliberate asymmetry and division suggest a bottom-heavy weight that echoes the tension of falling and rising, aligning with the 'falling pixels' theme. The patterns alternate between mirror and dotted, enhancing the conflict and weaving a narrative of emotional friction and unresolved tension.
