Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional interplay between vulnerability and strength, utilizing a palette drawn from mourning and renewal. The outer 4x4 area, characterized by darker shades, represents strength with its stable contrast, while the inner square, painted in softer tones, signifies vulnerability. The collision occurs where these areas intersect, creating a tension that echoes the duality of being exposed yet enclosed. The arrangement disrupts the symmetry, echoing the seed's themes of competing forces and emotional currents.
