Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the clash between envy and gratitude, portrayed through contrasting color placements. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon symbolizes the warmth of gratitude versus the sharpness of envy, marking a collision of feelings with a dominance of dark maroon representing heavy emotional weight. The layout suggests the structure of an argument, where organized radial patterns flow into chaotic asymmetries, echoing the overwhelming nature of conflicting emotions. The presence of broken pixels evokes imperfection, symbolizing the shattering of a mirror, while maintaining a 10% negative space on the canvas to signify introspection amidst turmoil.
