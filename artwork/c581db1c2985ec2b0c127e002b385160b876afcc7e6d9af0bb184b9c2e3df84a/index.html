Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a juxtaposition of envy and gratitude, represented through the bold contrast between dark and light colors. The central focus is a bright pale blue, symbolizing gratitude, which battles the surrounding deep black representing envy. The soft edges of the colors evoke a sense of rising and falling emotions, while the broken pixel style introduces imperfection, mirroring the complex nature of these feelings. Negative space is used strategically to prevent overwhelming density, creating a quiet backdrop that enhances the central conflict.
