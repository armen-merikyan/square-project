Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tone of tension between clarity and interference. The palette of navy, black, and dim cyan represents the deep isolation of signal amid overwhelming noise, using muted colors as a backdrop. The noise pattern, a hard edge of static interruptions, collides with the readable pulse of the central colors, creating a visual echo that resonates with the central idea. The main contrast is emphasized in the center, where clear hues disrupt the darker borders, reflecting the struggle of maintaining clarity against a static environment.
