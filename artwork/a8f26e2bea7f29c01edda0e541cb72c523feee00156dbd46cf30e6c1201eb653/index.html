Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The choice of a black and soft gold palette represents the struggle of stark contrasts — black for the chaotic side of confusion, and soft gold for the hopeful clarity. The left side features a mirror-like pattern with dark violet and pale green hinting at confusion due to their visual weight, while the right side has subtle stripes that promote clarity. The broken pixels in strategic places invoke the feeling of disruption amidst the structured communication of clarity, forming a bridge of tension and release across the square. The composition flows from top to bottom, suggesting a downward spiral and an eventual lift, echoing the pulse of turbulent emotions.
