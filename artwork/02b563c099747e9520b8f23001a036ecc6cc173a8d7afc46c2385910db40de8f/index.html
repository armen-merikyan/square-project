Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional collision between fear and hope, embodying a tension that spirals from a central diamond shape towards a chaotic outer region. The palette of dark violet and soft gold represents the interplay between the dense pressure of negative emotions and the glimmer of potential release. Dark corners create jagged edges symbolizing fear, while soft gold hints at bright potential amidst the chaos. The layout lends a sense of descending motion, capturing the essence of falling pixels, suggesting an emotional release that is always in flux.
