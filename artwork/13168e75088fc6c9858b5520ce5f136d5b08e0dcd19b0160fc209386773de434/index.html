Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between defiance and submission, expressed through a chaotic corner filled with vibrant colors and a serene negative space. The selected palette of moss green and rust evokes organic resistance against the permanence of bone white, symbolizing resilience amidst fragility. The main interaction occurs at the bottom left corner, where the bold, broken frame pattern creates a visual spark, symbolizing invasion and pressure, contrasting with the subdued threads of submission that stretch toward the upper edges. The arrangement signifies a struggle within the space, while the broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, enhancing the narrative of conflict.
