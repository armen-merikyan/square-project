Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between restlessness and peace, utilizing a warm and cool palette to highlight this contrast. The colors red and pale blue represent conflict, while black and white offer moments of both containment and release. The layout begins with warm tones in the corner, suggesting flame and intense restlessness, which diagonally clashes against cool calming colors that provide interruption and a sense of distance. The broken pixels create disruptions echoing the initial emotional turbulence while maintaining a core of calm at the center, emphasizing a balance that is both chaotic and serene.
