Artist Statement

The emotional tone fluctuates between defiance and submission, expressed through a dense center of signal colors clashing against the more somber edges. The palette of signal green, electric blue, dead black, static gray, and hot magenta captures the tension between noise and clarity. The main collision occurs at the center where defiance manifests as jagged patterns (channels), while submission is represented through softer, pulsing shapes (pulse marks) along the edges, creating a dynamic interplay of energy and restraint. This composition reflects the suggestion from the seed, emphasizing the struggles within the space, thus generating a visual representation of tension and emotional depth.
