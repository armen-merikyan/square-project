Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the contrasts of joy and grief, embodying the dynamic interplay between light and dark. The palette combines bright yellows and oranges symbolizing joy with muted purples and deep blues representing grief. The main collision occurs at the center, where vibrant colors clash against deeper tones, reflecting the conflict hinted at by the seed. Patterns of cracking and stripes create visual tension, emphasizing the feel of rising signals and zigzag movements through the canvas, suggesting a narrative of an emotional bridge over complex feelings.
