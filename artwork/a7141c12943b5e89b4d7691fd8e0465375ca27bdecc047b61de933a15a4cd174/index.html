Artist Statement

This composition embodies the nuances of panic and control, merging a vibrant palette representing warmth and optimism with cool, muted tones that convey stability and weight. The central 4x4 area is filled with a burst of striated colors, suggesting movement and conflict, while the outer borders maintain a calmer, more uniform tone, creating a frame for the chaos within. The combination of structured stripes against scattered broken pixels reflects the tension of trying to maintain control amid disruption.
