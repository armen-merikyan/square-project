Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dichotomy of loneliness and connection, showcased through the contrasting warmth of sepia and the coolness of faded blue. The arrangement plays with density on the left and negative space on the right, symbolizing isolation versus the human craving for connection. The palette evokes a sense of nostalgia, resembling broken memories, while the pattern collision of solid blocks against scattered broken dots enhances the feeling of displacement, suggesting the intrusion of connection amidst isolation.
