Artist Statement

This composition represents the emotional tug-of-war between softness and severity. The choice of a palette blending warm whites and powder pinks with deep carbon black and steel gray emphasizes the delicate tension. The collision occurs primarily in the center, where bright light tones clash with heavier dark shades, suggesting a struggle between gentle ease and strict boundaries. The pattern's flow mimics a ripple, invoking a spiral motion that intertwines light and shadow while retaining a sense of openness, emulating an emotional landscape that feels both layered and balanced.
