Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional duel between spark and ash, suggesting a breadth of tension from warmth to coolness. The palette of soft cyan, muted plum, and lavender gray evokes a melancholic yet vibrant atmosphere, representing the conflict between vitality and decay. The main collision occurs in the center where the bright colors of the spark pattern clash against the dense, grid-like ash patterns, symbolizing resilience meeting fragility. Negative space creates a quiet channel, accentuating the struggle and interplay of pressure around the edges.
