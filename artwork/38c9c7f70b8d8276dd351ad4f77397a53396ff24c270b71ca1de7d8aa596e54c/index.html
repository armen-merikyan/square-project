Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between longing and resolve, using a palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue to evoke nostalgia and depth. The background is predominantly quiet, allowing for expressions of longing via scattered dot patterns (representing distance and separateness) colliding with structured flame patterns of resolve that create a sense of order and strength. The main contrast happens in the center of the square, signifying the tension point where feelings collide, with broken pixels introducing intentional disruptions that reflect imperfections in both emotions.
