Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength through a soft color palette of muted blues, grays, and pale hues, suggesting an emotional landscape that oscillates between calm and turmoil. The checker and pressure band patterns create a sense of collision and interruption in the center, where darker tones represent strength, contrasting with lighter shades that symbolize vulnerability on the edges. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional imperfections, mirroring the theme of a 'broken mirror' while maintaining an exploratory and reflective journey across the grid.
