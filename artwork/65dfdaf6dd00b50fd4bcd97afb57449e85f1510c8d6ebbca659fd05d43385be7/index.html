Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between joy and grief through a juxtaposition of patterns. The palette blends bright yellows and oranges, symbolizing joy, against muted purples and deep blues, representing grief. The top half embraces joyful grid-like structures, while the bottom is filled with a checker pattern reflecting disarray and sorrow, honoring the split-screen concept. The hard edges signify a boundary between these emotions, creating a stark contrast that evokes feelings of both safety and threat. The placement and density shift from calm to pressure, suggesting a narrative of tension amidst tranquility.
