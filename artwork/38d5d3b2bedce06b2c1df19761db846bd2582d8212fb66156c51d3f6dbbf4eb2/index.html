Artist Statement

This piece contrasts the emotional tones of longing and resolve through the use of high-contrast colors. The palette combines cool tones of ice blue and violet gray with an accent of warning orange to highlight moments of intensity. The main collision occurs in the center where dense clusters of color interact with quiet spaces, symbolizing the push-pull dynamic between chaos and order. The uneven distribution of colors reflects the unpredictable nature of emotions, while incorporating intentional broken pixels emphasizes the imperfection of the experience.
