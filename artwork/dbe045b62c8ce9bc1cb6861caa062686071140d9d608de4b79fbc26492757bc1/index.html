Artist Statement

This composition delves into the emotional tension between longing and resolve, depicted through contrasting patterns: a ladder representing aspiration amidst a chaotic braid symbolizing complexity. The chosen palette of mourning_black, deep_blue, and pale_yellow evokes a somber yet hopeful atmosphere. The main collision emerges from the clash of dense areas featuring deep hues against lighter, emptier spaces, creating a visual echo of the emotional narrative that aligns with the provided seed. Intentional broken pixels introduce a layer of imperfection, mirroring the faults in striving for connection amidst the void.
