Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between longing and resolve, utilizing a palette of deep blacks and warming golds to symbolize the conflict between emptiness and a firm foundation. The corner is densely packed with dark colors representing the weight of longing, while lighter hues spread outward, suggesting a release towards resolve. The patterns of stacking and crosshatching interact, promoting a dialogue of pressure and relief. Broken pixels are strategically placed to signify interruptions in emotion, embodying the imperfect journey from longing to resolution.
