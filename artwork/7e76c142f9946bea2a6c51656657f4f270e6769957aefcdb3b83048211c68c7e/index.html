Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between lightness and heaviness. The palette, a mix of powder pink and carbon black, symbolizes the duality of softness and severity with gentle tones contrasted by harsh darks. The main collision occurs at the center where the pale pinks and warm whites clash sharply against the deeper blacks and grays. The layout suggests an open space being filled gradually, echoing the push and pull of guilt and forgiveness. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, hinting at the chaotic beauty of these emotional contrasts as they swirl in a spiral, creating a dynamic movement across the grid. It reflects the notion of struggle and release within human emotion, emphasizing both the gentle embrace and the harsh boundaries we all navigate.
