Artist Statement

This composition contrasts the deep emotional tension of 'longing' with the firm grounding of 'resolve.' The palette incorporates 'guilt_forgiveness' colors—stain brown and deep wine evoke a sense of weighty contemplation, while warm ivory and soft gold offer moments of hope and illumination. The main contrast occurs centrally where checker patterns of longing interrupt framed borders of resolve, creating a visual pulse. The layout, 'two_pulses,' reflects an interaction between distant warmth and clear boundaries, embodying the emotional fractures and repairs described in the seed. The broken pixels add intentional disruption, representing scars of past damage that both inform and enrich the abstract narrative.
