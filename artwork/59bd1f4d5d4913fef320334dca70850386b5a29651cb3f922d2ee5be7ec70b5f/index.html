Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional conflict between restlessness and peace, using the palette 'serenity_urgency' to highlight this dichotomy. Colors like pale aqua and mist gray represent tranquility, while alarm red and ink blue evoke urgency and motion. The main collision occurs in the center, where clusters of red vibrate against calm fields, suggesting a struggle between animosity and calmness. The 'hole' serves as a metaphor for the tension created by feeling lost amidst familiarity. The intentional broken pixels add depth and imperfection, mirroring the unresolved nature of life's journey.
