Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between trust and suspicion. The color palette, featuring signal green and electric blue against static gray and dead black, creates a vivid contrast representing conflict. The arrangement balances the structured grid of suspicion with the flowing threshold of trust, focusing on the diagonal fall that segments the square. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, symbolizing fractures in trust. The central cluster hints at resilience amidst a field of noise, illustrating the ambiguous nature of communication and connection.
