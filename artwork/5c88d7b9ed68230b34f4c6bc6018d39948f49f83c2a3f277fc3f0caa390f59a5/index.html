Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the struggle between longing and the encroaching emptiness. The palette blends warm coral and orange tones representing desire against deep charcoal and soft hollow blue, invoking a dance of saturation and restraint. The primary contrast is concentrated at the center, where warm tones clash with cool, suggesting a collision of feelings. This interplay draws inspiration from the seed, creating a visual story of contained yearning and splintered boundaries.
