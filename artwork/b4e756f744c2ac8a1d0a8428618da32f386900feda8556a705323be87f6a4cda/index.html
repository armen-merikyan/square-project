Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between vulnerability and protection through color choices. An interplay of warm browns suggests safety, while cool whites and subtle skin tones evoke exposure. The pixel arrangement creates a diagonal flow that embodies the invasion of one emotion over the other, with bright accents disrupting the calm fields, creating an echo of conflict. The palette embodies shelter in warm earthy tones, while colder colors represent stark exposure, suggesting a dialogue between these opposing forces in an abstract manner.
