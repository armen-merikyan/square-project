Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength using a palette of warm corals and cool blues. The broken pixels disrupt the calm, suggesting imperfection amidst stability. The central contrast between the hot coral and charcoal creates a 'sweep' effect, inviting the eye across the grid while emphasizing the struggle between exposure and safety. The horizontal and vertical flows collide in the center, creating a tense interaction reflecting the emotional dichotomy of the seed.
