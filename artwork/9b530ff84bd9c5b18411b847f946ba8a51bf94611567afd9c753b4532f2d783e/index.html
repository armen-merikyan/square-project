Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'spark vs ash' suggests a conflict between energy and decay. The chosen palette of deep red, soft pale blue, and stark black represents dynamic tension versus muted, settled tones. The layout showcases a battle of patterns: 'quiet field' representing spark occupies the dominant portion, while 'stairs' pattern symbolizes ash creates jagged interruptions, achieving a collision centered diagonally. This evokes themes of memory versus erasure, highlighting resilience amidst fragility.
