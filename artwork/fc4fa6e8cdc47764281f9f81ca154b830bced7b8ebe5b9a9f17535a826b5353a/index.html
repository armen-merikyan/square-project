Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between spark and ash through a palette featuring warm and cool tones, highlighting moments of ignition against muted backgrounds. The rising signal layout leads the eye upward, while the jagged edges reflect the struggle between memory and erasure. The central collision occurs through the checker and shatter patterns, creating an imbalance that evokes loss and hope, inviting viewers to experience the duality of resilience and fragility.
