Artist Statement

The emotional tone illustrates the complex interaction between longing and resolve, where the contrasting feelings coexist yet disrupt each other. The palette, featuring warm hues like hot coral and blood orange juxtaposed with cooler shades like hollow blue and charcoal, reflects this tension. The main collision occurs in the center where elongated shapes in vibrant colors crash against quieter, static elements, creating a visual pulse that oscillates between serenity and urgency, embodying the seed's dynamic qualities. The abstract pattern embodies a narrative of fragmented connection, with broken pixels symbolizing scars and the ongoing journey of repair amidst chaos.
