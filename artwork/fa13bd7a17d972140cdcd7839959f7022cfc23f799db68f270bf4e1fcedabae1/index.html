Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between clarity and confusion, highlighted by an interplay of tan and faded blue colors with sepia to evoke nostalgia and complexity. The arrangement reflects a broken symmetry, where horizontal and vertical patterns clash, creating a pulse that suggests both a rise and a fall. The negative space enhances the sense of disarray amidst the structured grid, embodying the essence of 'falling pixels'. The collision occurs primarily in the center where the chaotic grid meets the more organized patterns, illustrating a struggle between stability and instability.
