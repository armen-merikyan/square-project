Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between softness and severity. The palette consists of powder pink and warm white representing softness, contrasted by the starkness of carbon black and sharp crimson reflecting severity. The collision happens centrally where the light and dark play against each other, illustrating the clash of guilt and forgiveness. The layout features stacked bands shifting in density, allowing for a sense of movement and emotional layering, embodying the seed's essence of contrasting feelings and interruptions.
