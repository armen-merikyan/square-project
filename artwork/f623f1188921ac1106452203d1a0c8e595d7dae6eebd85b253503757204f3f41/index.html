Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between the vibrant sparks of hope and the settling ashes of memory. The palette combines warm rust and gold accents with cool moss green and bone white to illustrate resilience juxtaposed with fragility. The collision occurs in the center of the grid, where warm dots (depicting sparks) interact with the rain-like patterns of cool colors. This creates a rhythmic dialogue of pressure and release across the space, gracefully revealing the complexity of memory and resistance.
