Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between dread and relief. The darker shades dominate the top half, representing dread with a checker pattern emphasizing weight and noise. The bottom half uses lighter colors in a broken frame pattern, signifying a soft escape and release. The collision occurs at the central division where dark and light meet, creating a visual tension. The use of colors from the palette underlines this contrast, with 'storm_gray' and 'near_black' representing dread, while 'pale_mint' and 'soft_sky' evoke relief.
