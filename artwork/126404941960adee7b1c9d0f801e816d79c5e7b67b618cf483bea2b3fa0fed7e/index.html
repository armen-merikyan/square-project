Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition captures the delicate balance between longing and resolve. The chosen palette of moss green and rust evokes a sense of resilience amidst fragility. The main contrast occurs in the middle, where thin bands of longing press against edges of resolve, creating visual tension. Broken pixels are intentionally placed to suggest scars of past occurrences, adding layers of depth and complexity to the work. Overall, the layout seeks to illustrate the interplay of stability and unsettlement, embodying an abstract narrative shaped by the seed's themes of reflection and contrast.
