Artist Statement

This composition contrasts the heaviness of dread with the lightness of relief, using dense hues like storm gray and near black in the top half to signify pressure, while the bottom half employs pale mint and soft sky for release. The palette evokes feelings of tension through muted colors, then open space through brighter tones, culminating in a clear visual echo of relief contrasted with oppressive dread. The broken pixels evoke fragmentation, enhancing the emotional narrative of conflict and resolution.
