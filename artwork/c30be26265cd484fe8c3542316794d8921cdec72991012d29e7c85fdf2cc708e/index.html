Artist Statement

This composition reflects the interplay of clarity and chaos, exemplifying the emotional dichotomy of 'signal vs noise.' The chosen palette of navy and dim cyan conveys loneliness contrasted by moments of bright white to symbolize clarity, delivering a visual dialogue between defined paths and scattered interference. The center pulse acts as the primary contrast, where the bright white interrupts the darker tones, manifesting as an echo of memory against a backdrop of noise. Broken pixels disrupt uniformity, creating tension and suggesting the lingering presence of chaos even in moments of clear intention.
