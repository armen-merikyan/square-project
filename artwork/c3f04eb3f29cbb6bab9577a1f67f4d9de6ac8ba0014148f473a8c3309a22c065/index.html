Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between restlessness and peace, utilizing a palette that juxtaposes calm hues like pale aqua and mist gray with vibrant alarm red and ink blue, symbolizing emotional disturbances and clarity. The placement of colors follows a diagonal argument, creating a sense of motion contrasted by the quiet fields of clear white. The main collision occurs in the center, where the restless frame pattern sharply intersects with the peaceful split-screen, embodying the inner struggle of memory against motion.
