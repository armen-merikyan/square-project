Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts restlessness with peace, using the palette of muted mint and storm gray to suggest a tense equilibrium. The outer border employs darker tones to symbolize restraint, while the lighter inner 4x4 section represents fleeting serenity, creating a visual tug-of-war. The use of the 'embers' and 'checker' patterns captures the stutter of memory and fleeting moments, allowing significant collision at the center, where tension and calm collide. Broken pixels introduce imperfections that echo the central theme of disturbance against stability.
