Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between confusion and clarity, represented by a palette of electric blue, lime, black, and magenta. The contrast is most pronounced in the swirling chaos of the pattern, where the bold colors collide with the underlying darkness, creating a thick outline that defines the boundaries yet feels broken. The abstract pattern reflects falling pixels that symbolize a descent into confusion, with areas of density that echo calm, reinforcing the theme of pulse through sudden contrasts and interruptions.
