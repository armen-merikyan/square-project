Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between panic and control through an interplay of structured grid patterns and chaotic zigzags. The palette reflects rage with dark blacks alongside pale blues, creating a stark contrast that symbolizes the clash between order and chaos. The burned edges evoke a sense of urgency, while strategic use of broken pixels disrupts the symmetry, enhancing the feeling of imperfection and unresolved emotions. The negative space facilitates a breath between dense clusters of color, suggesting moments of chaos within the overall structure.
