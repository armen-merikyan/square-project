Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between isolation and togetherness, captured by contrasting shades and patterns. The palette, featuring black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green, balances weight and lightness while evoking feelings of fear and hope. The central black pixels represent darkness and loneliness, while soft gold interrupts, symbolizing connection, against pale green's hints of vitality. The arrangement creates a fragmented echo of connection through broken pixels and asymmetry, mirroring the complexities of relationships amid solitude.
