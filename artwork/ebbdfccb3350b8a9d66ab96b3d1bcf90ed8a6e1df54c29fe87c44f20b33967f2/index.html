Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the duality of emptiness and desire. The palette, consisting of deep blacks and soft golds, visually represents the struggle between warmth (desire) and coolness (emptiness). The darker colors create a sense of void, while the soft gold provides a fleeting connection, creating a tension that rises from the center outwards. The pattern features alternating stark contrasts that suggest a collision of feelings, with patches of noise and structure echoing the seed themes like rising signals and burned edges.
