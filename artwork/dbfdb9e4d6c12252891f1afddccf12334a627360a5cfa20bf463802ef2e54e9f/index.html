Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'longing vs resolve' is represented through a palette reflecting warmth and coolness, addressing the complexities of desire and determination. The chosen colors—shelter_green for longing and cold_white representing resolve—contrast to evoke a tension-filled interaction. The main collision occurs diagonally, where soft green villages clash with stark whites, suggesting a journey across vast fields of uncertainty. Broken pixels create intentional disruptions that echo the theme of repair and damage, enhancing the sense of unease while negative space builds a quiet backdrop for the pulsating emotional narrative.
