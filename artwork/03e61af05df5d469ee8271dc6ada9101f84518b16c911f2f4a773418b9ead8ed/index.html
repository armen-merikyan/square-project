Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between panic and control, using the palette 'broken_memory' to capture feelings of nostalgia and disarray. The contrast lies between dark sepia and tan tones representing weight and rigidity, while faded blues punctuate moments of brightness and clarity, echoing sparks of hope. The arrangement mimics the mirrored layout with intentional breaks, where certain pixels disrupt symmetry to signify chaos within order, creating an abstract narrative of conflict and resolution.
