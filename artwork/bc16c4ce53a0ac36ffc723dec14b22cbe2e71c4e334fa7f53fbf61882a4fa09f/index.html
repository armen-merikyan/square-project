Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tone of pressure and release through a dichotomy of dense clusters and quiet spaces. The color palette juxtaposes deeper, muted tones against sharper, vibrant hues to evoke tension and tranquility. The main collision occurs where bright colors intersect with shadows, suggesting moments of emotional intensity amidst a backdrop of calm. This dance of light and dark reflects the seed's themes of rhythm and interruption, creating an abstract narrative through color dynamics.
