Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts certainty's stability with curiosity's disruptions, embodying the tension between order and chaos. The warm palette of hot coral and blood orange suggests urgency and conflict, while cool hollow blue provides calmness and depth. The pattern emerges as a spiral, with pulse marks colliding in the center, emphasizing a dynamic disruption against structured backgrounds. Broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, adding layers of complexity to the composition, reflecting the push and pull of curiosity against certainty.
