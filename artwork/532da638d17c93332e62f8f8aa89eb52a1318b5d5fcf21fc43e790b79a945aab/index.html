Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between vulnerability and strength, reflected in the palette of muted gray tones contrasted with vibrant pinks and rich reds. The layout features falling pixels, symbolizing emotions that cascade like rain. The main collision occurs in the center where the dense dots of strength meet the chaotic noise pattern, representing vulnerability. Broken pixels add intentional imperfections, enhancing the sense of exposure and the tension between shelter and openness.
