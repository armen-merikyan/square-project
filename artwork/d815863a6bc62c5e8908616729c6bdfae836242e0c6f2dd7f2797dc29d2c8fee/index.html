Artist Statement

This composition reflects a tension between 'spark' and 'ash,' capturing the friction of vitality against decay. The chosen palette of muted plum and soft cyan invokes a sense of melancholy while the lattice and barcode patterns create dynamic movement across the grid. Key contrasts occur where the lighter, vibrant hues collide with the subdued gray shades, manifesting emotional conflict in both density and quietude. The layout emphasizes a small, bold center of action, surrounded by an expansive calm, echoing the concept of a 'tiny plus' amidst the vastness of existence.
