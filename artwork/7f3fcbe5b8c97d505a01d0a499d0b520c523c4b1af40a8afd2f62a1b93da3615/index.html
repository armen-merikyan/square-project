Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the conflict between strength and vulnerability. The selected palette of 'melancholy_wonder' captures this duality, using soft cyans and muted plums to illustrate the tender, yet resilient aspects of the theme. The central area showcases a concentration of the 'ash_field' pattern, emanating a sense of grounded stability amidst an outer layer of 'teeth' patterns, suggesting the chaotic nature of vulnerability. The significant collision occurs at the edges where 'vulnerability' in softer shades meets the grounding 'strength' at the center, effectively communicating the push and pull of these emotions while maintaining a balance of negative space.
