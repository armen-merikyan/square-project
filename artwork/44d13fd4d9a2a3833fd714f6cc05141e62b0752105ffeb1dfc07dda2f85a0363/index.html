Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional dichotomy of joy and grief through its color palette and layout. Utilizing bright yellows and oranges to represent joy while juxtaposing them against muted purples and deep blues to signify grief, the arrangement creates a visual tension. The palette chooses colors dense with meaning—yellow for warmth and hope, mixed with deeper tones that suggest loss and introspection. The left-weighted symmetry speaks to the imbalance often felt between joy and grief, with deliberate broken pixels introducing an element of imperfection, enhancing the narrative of the emotional struggle. The interplay of the grid and mirrored motifs embodies the pulse of life, drawing parallels to the rhythm of emotions that ebb and flow, reflecting moments of love and betrayal that intertwine within our experiences.
