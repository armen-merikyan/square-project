Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances defiance and submission, creating tension through the use of a dark palette contrasted against muted and brighter tones. The colors reflect conflict; deep blue embodies submission while pale yellow represents defiance. The central collision occurs in the off-center design, where 'wound' suggests both vulnerability and strength, creating a visual narrative of resistance against containment. The layout leaves significant negative space, enhancing the emotional weight and allowing the piece to breathe, symbolizing the thresholds between identities.
