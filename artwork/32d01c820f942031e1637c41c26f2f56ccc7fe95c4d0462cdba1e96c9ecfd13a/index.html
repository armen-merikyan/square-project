Artist Statement

The composition expresses a dynamic interplay between pressure and quiet, utilizing a palette that juxtaposes muted and saturated hues. The dense concentration of #000000 and #B03030 on the lower right embodies pressure, while soft shades like #F5F5DC and #A9A9A9 in the upper left suggest a calm expanse. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfection and echo the theme of shattering against the tide, while the overall layout creates a layered narrative transitioning from chaos to serenity. The sharp vs soft contrast is heightened where dark edges abruptly meet faint backgrounds, enhancing the emotional depth of the piece.
