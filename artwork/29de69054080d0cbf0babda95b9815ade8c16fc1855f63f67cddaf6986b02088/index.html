Artist Statement

The composition explores the emotional tension between quietude and pressure through contrasting color clusters. The palette incorporates muted tones like off-white and dust gray, evoking a soft field, while compressed red and dark navy embody weight and density. The main contrast occurs in the top left, where dense pixels collide with an open space, suggesting a breaking point amidst a quiet expanse. The chosen layout of falling pixels mirrors the ethical duality of pressure building against a backdrop of release, enhancing the momentum and visual rhythm.
