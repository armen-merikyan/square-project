Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional duality of loneliness versus connection via a muted color palette that evokes nostalgia and depth. The colors of sepia and faded blue create a sense of ancient memory, while soft edges and broken pixels introduce an imperfect but intimate rhythm. The primary contrast is at the center, where a lighter colored pixel suggesting connection clashes with surrounding darker tones representing isolation. The layout suggests a mirror, with subtle asymmetries creating tension, reinforcing the conflicting emotions and leading to a burst of color that symbolizes a desire for connection amidst solitude.
