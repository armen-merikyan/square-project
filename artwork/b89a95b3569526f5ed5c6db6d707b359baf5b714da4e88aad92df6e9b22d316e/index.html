Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between clarity and chaos, embodied in the color choices. The palette of navy, black, and dim cyan reflects a deep, lonely signal, while white offers a stark contrast that suggests hope or clarity attempting to break through noise. The main collision takes place at the center, where dark tones clash against bright accents, mirroring the conflict of sterile machine precision against organic emotional scars. This arrangement of colors creates a rhythm of pulse and static, echoing the seed's narrative of hidden signals amidst overwhelming interference.
