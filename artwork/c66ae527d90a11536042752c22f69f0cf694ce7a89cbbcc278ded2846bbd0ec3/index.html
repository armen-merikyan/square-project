Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the struggle between clarity and chaos. The palette of dark navy and black creates a somber base, while the dim cyan provides a glimmer of life amidst the noise. The bright cluster of white acts as a singular, hopeful signal. The main collision occurs in the center, where the ripple pattern of signal competes against the static, ash-field noise, creating a visual tension. The broken pixels add intentional disruptions, signifying the imperfections in communication and emotion.
