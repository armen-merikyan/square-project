Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between loneliness and connection, utilizing a palette of dark navy, black, and dim cyan against the singular brightness of white. The arrangement favors negative space, creating a sense of isolation. The contrast primarily occurs at the center, where the single white pixel acts as a focal point against a backdrop of muted tones, suggesting a spark of connection amidst surrounding weight. The flow subtly guides the eye diagonally across the canvas, mirroring the push and pull of emotional states, while the broken dots pattern suggests imperfection and fragmented memories.
