Artist Statement

The piece embodies the emotional tension of longing versus resolve, expressed through a palette of warm sepia and cool faded blue, echoing the contrast of distant warmth against firm boundaries. The main collision occurs at the center where longing's negative_cross pattern meets the resolve's teeth, effectively illustrating the struggle between desire and certainty. Broken pixels are scattered throughout, serving as intentional disruptions that narrate the imperfections of this emotional journey. The quiet spaces emphasize the gaps in connection, creating a sense of intimacy and distance in the surrounding dense patterns, effectively visualizing the complex interplay between these emotions.
