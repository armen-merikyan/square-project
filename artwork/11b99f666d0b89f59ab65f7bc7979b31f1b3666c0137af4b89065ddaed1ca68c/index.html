Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the restlessness of chaotic memories and the serenity of passive peace. The palette features warm 'honey_gold' and 'ripe_green' for moments of tranquility, contrasted sharply against cooler 'deep_olive' and 'acid_green' to echo uncertainty and tension. The dominant sector (about 65% of the square) is filled with dense colors towards one corner, indicating pressure, while lighter tones subtly spread against it, suggesting a gradual release. This represents the interplay between memory and motion, where fragments collide yet still resonate within defined boundaries.
