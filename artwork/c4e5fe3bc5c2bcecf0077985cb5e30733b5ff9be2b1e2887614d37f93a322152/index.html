Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional dynamic between longing and resolve through contrasting color placements and visual patterns. The chosen palette blends serene greens and browns with active accents of open blue and pale skin tones, reflecting a balance between tranquility and urgency. The main collision occurs in the center, where a cluster of vivid tones interrupts the darker surroundings, embodying the tension and pulsing energy of the seed's themes. The 'islands' and 'patchwork' patterns embody the concepts of separation and connection, representing the emotional interplay through deliberate asymmetry.
