Artist Statement

The composition balances the emotional dynamics of restlessness and peace. The dark tones (#000000, #222222) create a base of stability reflecting peace, but the piercing bright accents (#00FF00, #0000FF) invoke a sense of restless motion. The collision occurs in the diagonal band of color, creating tension through oscillation between calmness and disturbance. The patchwork of colors suggests memories weaving through moments of stillness and activity, reflecting the seed's essence of motion and memory. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, echoing the journey between chaos and tranquility.
