Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between envy and gratitude through a palette characterized by warm oranges and cool blues, symbolizing conflict and resolution. The center embodies the weight of these emotions with a dense cluster of muted purples, while the edges flow in a zigzag pattern that creates a sense of movement and interruption. Broken pixels are integrated to enhance the theme of imperfection, allowing for moments of visual chaos that contrast with the stable center. The final arrangement reflects a balance between pressure and release, echoing a narrative of struggle and acceptance.
