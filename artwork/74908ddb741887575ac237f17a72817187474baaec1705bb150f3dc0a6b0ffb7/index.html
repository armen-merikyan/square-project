Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes themes of vulnerability and strength, creating a dynamic tension reflected in the color choices. The selected palette, 'clarity_confusion', utilizes icy blues for vulnerability and inky blacks for strength, with a striking warning orange accent. The layout emphasizes a spiral pressure flow leading to a pale center, suggesting a conflict between exposure and protection. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, creating visual disruptions that echo the emotional journey provoked by the seed.
