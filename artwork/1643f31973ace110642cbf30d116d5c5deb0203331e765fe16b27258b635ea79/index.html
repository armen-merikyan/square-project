Artist Statement

This artwork conveys a strong emotional tone of contrast between relief and dread. The palette combines 'pale_mint' and 'faded_gold' for relief, representing warmth and soft return, while 'near_black' and 'storm_gray' embody the weight and uncertainty of dread. The central collision occurs in the middle rows, where dense dark colors interrupt lighter tones, symbolizing chaos and stability battling for space. The rising signal layout fosters a sense of upward movement, offering hope amid tension.
