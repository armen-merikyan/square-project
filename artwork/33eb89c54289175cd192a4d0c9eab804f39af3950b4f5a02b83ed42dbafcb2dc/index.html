Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve through contrasting patterns and colors. The palette of deep pink, red, gray, and dark maroon expresses the complexity of emotional tension, while the density in the center works against the quiet edges, embodying an overflow that clashes with containment. The barcode pattern represents the unease of longing, while the flame pattern signifies resolve, both colliding predominantly in the center. Broken pixel disruptions introduce a sense of fragility, echoing the theme of mirroring brokenness. Overall, the artwork radiates an unresolved pulse that reflects the interplay of serenity and urgency.
