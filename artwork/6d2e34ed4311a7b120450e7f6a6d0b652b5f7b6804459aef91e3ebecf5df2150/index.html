Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between stability (trust) and disruption (suspicion). The palette combines soothing soft blues and grays (trust) with alarm reds and deep blacks reflecting suspicion. The main collision occurs diagonally, where a structured pattern of calming colors is interrupted by jagged accents of red, representing moments of doubt. The layout suggests falling energy, emphasizing a downward motion that mirrors the emotional weight of suspicion against the backdrop of trust's firmness.
