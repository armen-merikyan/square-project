Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tug-of-war between restlessness and peace. The palette combines deep teal and void black for a profound sense of calm, contrasted with cosmic indigo as a restless push. The grid pattern represents structured chaos, while the ember-like sparks of star white create interruptions. The main collision of deep teal and cosmic indigo is present in the center, representing a struggle between soothing depths and frantic motion, capturing the essence of the seed's tension.
