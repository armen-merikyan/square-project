Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between clarity and confusion, expressed through contrasting colors and patterns. A palette of muted purple and deep blue represents confusion, while yellows and oranges symbolize clarity. The central area, dense with warm colors, radiates outward, intersecting with cooler hues, creating a sense of shifting focus. The layout suggests a corner invasion of warm tones that collide with the cooler regions, enhancing a feeling of disorder. Broken pixels randomly disrupt the clean lines, embodying imperfection while adhering to a strict edge style. The interplay between dense clusters and quiet spaces evokes the duality of emotions, illustrating a journey from chaos towards clarity.
