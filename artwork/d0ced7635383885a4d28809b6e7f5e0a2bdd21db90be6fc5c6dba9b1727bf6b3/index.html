Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between fear and hope. The dark colors represent fear, creating jagged pressure along the corners, while the soft gold and pale green symbolize hope, rising gently through the center. The contrast of sharp edges and soft forms illustrates the struggle between these two emotional states, creating an abstract expression of conflict and resolution. The placement of colors follows a fluid wave pattern, dictating the visual flow from dark to light, while broken pixels add an organic imperfection to the piece, suggesting the unpredictability of emotions.
