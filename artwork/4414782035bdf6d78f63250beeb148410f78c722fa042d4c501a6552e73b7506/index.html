Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, using deep blacks and muted oranges to create a sense of turbulence and contrast. The dark palette evokes confusion with its dense feel, while gold highlights suggest fleeting clarity. The falling pixel layout introduces a rhythm that mimics both noise and structure, echoing the pattern of thoughts battling for coherence. The interplay of broken edges and stark contrast provokes a sense of unresolved emotion, where clarity attempts to break through confusion, illustrated through abrupt disruptions and lingering shadows.
