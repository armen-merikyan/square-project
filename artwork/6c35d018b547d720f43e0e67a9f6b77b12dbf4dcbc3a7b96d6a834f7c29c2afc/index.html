Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the conflict of clarity (signal) against a background of confusion (noise). The dark blues and blacks represent the chaotic noise, while the dim cyan serves as bright accentuating signals trying to emerge. The central collision of colors creates a chaotic pulse resembling a scarred landscape struggling for clarity. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, reflecting the message that while signals can break, they persist amidst noise, thus crafting a story of resilience and echo.
