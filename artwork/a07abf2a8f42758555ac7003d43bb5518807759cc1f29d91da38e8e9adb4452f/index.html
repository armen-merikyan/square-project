Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between hope and fear, creating a dynamic interplay where darker colors represent pressure and anxiety while lighter shades signify potential growth and release. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green creates a stark contrast that is visually engaging, emphasizing the 'outside inward' flow where darker pixels cascade down, creating a sense of blockage against the vibrancy of the edges. The structured pattern of fear through grids juxtaposes the hopeful rising stripes, echoing the collisions of these emotions.
