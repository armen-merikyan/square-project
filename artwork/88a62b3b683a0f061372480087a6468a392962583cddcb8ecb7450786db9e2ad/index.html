Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the tension between loneliness and connection. The palette features deep navy and black symbolizing solitude, punctuated by dim cyan for subtle connections. The contrast is highlighted through a central diamond shape in dark, surrounded by a maze of lighter hues that teeter between connectivity and isolation, evoking a burst of feeling amidst structured chaos. The patterns of crosshatch and maze create an interplay of hidden vs exposed, portraying a narrative of reaching out while feeling enclosed.
