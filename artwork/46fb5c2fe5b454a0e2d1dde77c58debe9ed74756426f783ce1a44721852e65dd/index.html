Artist Statement

The emotional tone is captured between the tension of panic and the steadiness of control. The color palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue reflects nostalgia and complexity, merging subdued warmth with cooler shadow tones that suggest quiet chaos beneath apparent order. The main contrast occurs as broken dots of tan disrupt the otherwise orderly grid of darker hues, symbolizing the sparks of an argument or conflict. The placement evokes a zigzag pattern, fostering a sense of movement from chaos on the left to calm on the right, hinting at a journey from discord to resolution. This layered dynamic fulfills the seed's emotional narrative without limiting it to literal representations.
