Artist Statement

The emotional tone intertwines loneliness and connection. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold emphasizes warmth contrasted with darkness, representing a struggle between isolation and community. Main contrast occurs at the center, where densely packed burnt orange bursts outward, fluctuating with the cooler charcoal, symbolizing hidden depth. This pattern creates a crosshatch effect, echoing the feeling of being surrounded yet disconnected, resonating with the seed's themes.
