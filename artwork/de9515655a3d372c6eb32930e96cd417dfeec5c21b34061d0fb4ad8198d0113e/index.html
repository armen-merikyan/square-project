Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the tension between longing and resolve, captured through contrasting patterns and colors. The palette comprises deep maroons and muted reds symbolizing resolve, while soft pinks hint at the fragility of longing. The jagged edges disrupt the smooth noise pattern to represent damage but also refine the resolve, creating a fascinating collision at the center—where the longing intersects with resolve. This yields an overall sense of imbalance yet completion within the confines of the frame, illustrating the emotional narrative of struggle and connection.
