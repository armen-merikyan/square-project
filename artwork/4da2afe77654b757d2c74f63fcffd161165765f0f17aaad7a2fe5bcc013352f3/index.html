Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captures a juxtaposition of longing and resolve, channeling both into a rhythm of rising hope. The palette of 'intimacy_distance' colors reflects warmth and coolness, suggesting a delicate balance: rose clay and warm shadow symbolize warmth and yearning, while cool blue and distant lilac evoke a sense of introspection and calm. The main contrast emerges at the center, where warm tones collide with cooler hues, representing the tension between resolution emerging from longing. The layout 'rising_signal' captures an upward motion, contributing to the visual narrative of hope and release. Broken pixels strategically interrupt the flow, embodying imperfections in the journey and adding depth to the emotional landscape.
