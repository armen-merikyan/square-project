Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the tension between trust and suspicion, expressed through contrasting colors. The palette of 'safety_exposure' blends deep green and warm brown for stability, intersected by cold white and open blue to hint at uncertainty and tension. The main collision occurs across the center, where brighter colors disrupt darker fields, representing the struggle between security and doubt. The pattern of crosshatch (trust) gently layers atop a halo pattern (suspicion), visually illustrating their interplay amidst broken pixels, reinforcing the idea of imperfection in this emotional landscape.
