Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension of emptiness versus desire through color contrasts and spatial arrangements. The palette of deep reds and contrasting cool tones reflects the warmth of desire and the chill of emptiness. The central area pulsates with intensity, where brighter colors like red collide with shadows of black, symbolizing the heart's longing amidst the void. The upward flow suggests a rising signal of hope, while darker edges perpetually threaten this ascent, representing the ever-present risk of falling back into emptiness. Each pixel placement creates a rhythmic narrative without resorting to literal forms, transforming the emotional seed into an abstract dance of light and shadow.
