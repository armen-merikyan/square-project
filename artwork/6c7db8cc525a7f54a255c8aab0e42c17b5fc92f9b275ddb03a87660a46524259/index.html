Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional dichotomy of fear and hope through a contrasting palette of deep blacks and soft golds. The use of broken pixels introduces an element of disruption, echoing the jagged fear intertwined with the glowing hope. The layout creates a confrontation of jagged edges and smooth flows, depicting the struggle of emotional tensions. The collision occurs in the center, where the intense colors clash, portraying a vivid tension amidst organized chaos.
