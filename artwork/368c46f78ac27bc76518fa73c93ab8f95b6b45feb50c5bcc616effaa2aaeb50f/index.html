Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the dichotomy between quietness and pressure, reflecting a gentle pulse against a tight, dark push. The palette consists of muted tones of off-white and dust gray, representing the quiet, with bursts of compressed red and dark navy suggesting underlying tension. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the patterns of 'shatter' and 'channels' intertwine, portraying the struggle between softness and sharpness, clarity interrupted by complexity. This reflects the seed's themes through a layered, abstract composition, inviting contemplation.
