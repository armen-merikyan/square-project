Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between defiance and submission, where defiance is visually represented by bold colors occupying the center, symbolizing a resilient core. The palette incorporates shelter green and open blue for depth, juxtaposed against warm brown to signify the submission fabric surrounding the dense island. This collision of colors creates a tension that evokes a narrative of resistance and retreat. The main contrast occurs at the center, where the green and blue clash against the subtle warmth of brown, representing the ongoing push and pull of these emotions.
