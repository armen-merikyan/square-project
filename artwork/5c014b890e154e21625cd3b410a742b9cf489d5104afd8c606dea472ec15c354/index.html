Artist Statement

This piece conveys the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through a contrasting palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green. The choice of colors reflects a push-pull dynamic: soft gold symbolizes clarity, while dark violet embodies confusion. The arrangement utilizes a rising signal layout to suggest continuous movement upward and out of chaos. The dominant presence of black creates a foundational tension, while places of negative space allow for moments of calm amid density. The interaction of colors creates a pulse effect, enhancing the contrast between vibrant and muted areas, echoing the theme of balancing clarity against confusion.
