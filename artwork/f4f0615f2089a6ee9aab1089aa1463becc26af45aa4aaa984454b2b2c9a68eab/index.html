Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dichotomy between nostalgia and progress. The chosen palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold evokes warmth and depth, representing rooted memories versus the harshness of forward momentum. The contrast occurs at the center, with a heavy lower section filled with deep colors suggesting weight and history, while lighter, split patterns suggest emerging progress at the top. This creates a visual tension conveying emotional struggle and unresolved motion.
