Artist Statement

The composition captures the conflict between tenderness and rage through a palette of saturated reds and muted blacks, representing emotional weight and lightness. The central collision occurs diagonally, where jagged red pixels interrupt the calmness of softer blue hues, symbolizing a struggle for dominance. This arrangement is influenced by the heart fragment shape seed, reflecting vulnerability and repair amidst turmoil, framed within a soft, quiet channel of white and pale blue.
