Artist Statement

This composition evokes a tension between awe and terror, utilizing deep hues and soft edges to create a juxtaposition of stability and chaos. The palette of blacks and dark violets captures the solemn feeling of darkness, while soft gold and pale green offer hope amidst uncertainty. The placement of colors creates a visual 'split' — one side holds dense, muted tones symbolizing terror, while the other hints at a brighter space, reflecting awe. The broken pixels enhance the sense of fragility, embodying the idea of memories fractured yet significant.
