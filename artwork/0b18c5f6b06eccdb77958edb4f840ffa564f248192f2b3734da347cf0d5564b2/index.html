Artist Statement

This composition represents the emotional struggle between restlessness and peace, highlighted by a contrast of warm and cool colors. The palette utilizes burnt orange and dark red to signify turmoil, while charcoal and gold create a sense of grounding serenity. The main collision occurs along a diagonal axis where brighter colors are interrupted by deeper, darker hues, embodying the tension between the desire for motion and the pull towards calm. The overall abstract shape echoes the seed's theme by merging flowing energy with static fields, symbolizing an incomplete journey towards stability.
