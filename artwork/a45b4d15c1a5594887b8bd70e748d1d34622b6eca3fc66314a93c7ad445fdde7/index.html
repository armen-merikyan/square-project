Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between loneliness and connection, represented by contrasting color clusters. The palette features sepia and faded blue, encompassing memories in muted hues. The main contrast resides on the left side, where denser colors symbolize weight and isolation, while lighter tones on the right suggest openness and connection. The seed's tension is illustrated through a grid pattern in darker shades that breaks toward the right, allowing space to breathe, echoing the duality of filled versus hollow.
