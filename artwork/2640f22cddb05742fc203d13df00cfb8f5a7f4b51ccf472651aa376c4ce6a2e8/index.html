Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional duality between restlessness and peace. The palette chosen—cool ice blue (for tranquility) amidst stark ink black and warning orange (representing unrest)—creates a contrast filled with tension and allure. The patterns play out where the black and orange collide sharply in the center, while the blue diffuses outward, emphasizing a gradual motion towards calmness. The layout serves as a threshold, allowing emotional interaction as these hues suggest a back-and-forth struggle between peace and disquiet. The broken pixels add intentional disruption, representing the fracturing of serenity.
