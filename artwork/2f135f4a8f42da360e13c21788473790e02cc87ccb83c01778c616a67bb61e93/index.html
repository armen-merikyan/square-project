Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between safety and exposure, embodied in the palette of warm brown and shelter green that suggest security, juxtaposed with the cold white and open blue indicating vulnerability. The contrast is central, with a split layout that emphasizes the collision between heavy and thin patterns—targeted structures on the left that suggest safety, woven with fault lines on the right representing exposure. This creates a rhythmic flow and a fragmented yet cohesive visual narrative, echoing the complexities of protecting oneself while being exposed.
