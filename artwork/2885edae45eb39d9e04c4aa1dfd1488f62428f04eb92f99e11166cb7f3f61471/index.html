Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between tenderness and rage, using a palette of deep reds for intensity, contrasting against soft pale blues and whites to evoke calmness. The collision occurs at the diagonal flow across the square, where dense clusters of red create a sense of pressure, while the lighter colors establish a gentle release. The shape seed 'eye' symbolizes observation yet remains abstract, suggesting layers of emotional complexity without defined imagery.
