Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between confusion and clarity, manifesting as a mirrored layout hemmed with broken pixels that depict dissonance. The color palette of deep black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green contrasts richly, with black providing grounding clarity against chaotic hints of violet, suggesting confusion. The arrangement employs a split maze pattern—chaotic intersections clash with defined spaces, revealing an emotional narrative of density juxtaposed with emptiness. The corners remain intentionally muted, allowing the center to emanate soft gold, symbolizing the hope that conflicts arise from disarray. Thus, every pixel forms a story stemming from the central seed of complexity, illustrating a neurotic dissection of emotional weight interspersed with serene intervals.
