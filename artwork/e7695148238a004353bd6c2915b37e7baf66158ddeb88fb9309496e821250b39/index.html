Artist Statement

This composition reflects the intricate interplay between defiance and submission, manifesting through a left-weighted arrangement that captures the emotional collision. The colors chosen from the 'love_betrayal' palette — rich reds and soft grays — symbolize passion versus surrender. The pattern suggests a descent, echoing falling pixels in a digital landscape, creating a sense of weight and noise. The structure is balanced by negative space on the right, allowing the intensity of color on the left to breathe, thus embodying the ongoing tension between opposing forces.
