Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the warmth of trust with the shadows of suspicion. The palette combines soft aquas and greys with sharp reds and deep blues, lending the piece both a serene and urgent feel. The collision occurs at the center, where darker colors invade the lighter, symbolizing a struggle between trust and suspicion. The abstract patterns formed are jagged and broken, aligning with the seed's theme of resilience and fragility, while also suggesting a subtle edge of noise beneath calm surfaces.
