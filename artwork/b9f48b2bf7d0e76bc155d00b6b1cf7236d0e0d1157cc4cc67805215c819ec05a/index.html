Artist Statement

The composition explores the emotional contrast between longing and resolve through the interplay of warm and cool colors. The color palette incorporates deep reds and pinks to evoke warmth and intimacy, contrasted with cooler grays and dark maroon, symbolizing distance and resolution. The main collision occurs at the center, where the 'spark' pattern from resolve meets the 'echo' of longing, creating visual tension. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions that mirror the complexities of emotional states, while the spiral pressure layout enhances the sense of movement and progression.
