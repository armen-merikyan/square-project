Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duel between panic and control, using a palette that reflects depth and tension. The dark colors symbolize the controlled chaos, while the soft gold hints at hope and joy amidst grief. The grid layout conveys the structure seeking to contain chaos, while zigzag patterns introduce disruption and unpredictability, reflecting the inner conflict. Broken pixels represent imperfection and vulnerability, making viewers conscious of the tension between aspirations of order and moments of upheaval. This piece embodies the push and pull between feeling overwhelmed and grasping for composure.
