Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional struggle between restlessness and peace. The palette of warm shadow and cool blue suggests conflict and intimacy, with the vibrant lamp gold accentuating moments of light amidst darker patches. The predominant use of grid pattern symbolizes peace, while tide-like disruptions represent restlessness, colliding at the center to signify tension. The broken edges offer a sense of imperfection, inviting the viewer to explore the narrative of emotion through contrasting colors and placements.
