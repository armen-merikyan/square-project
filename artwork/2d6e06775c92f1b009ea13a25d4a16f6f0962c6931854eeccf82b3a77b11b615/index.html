Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between fear and hope. The palette features dark violet and black representing fear, contrasted against soft gold and pale green, symbolizing hope. The arrangement emphasizes dense clusters on the outer regions, leading to a single bright pixel that evokes hope at the center—a rising signal amidst encroaching shadows. The jagged, heavy edges create a sense of pressure, while the inner ring of lighter colors suggests resilience and rising energy, echoing the conflict expressed in the seed.
