Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay of chaos and calm, represented by contrasting shades of the palette. The sepia and tan suggest a lost memory, while dust gray and faded blue introduce rhythmic quietness amidst pressure. The layout flows diagonally, embodying the tension between control and panic, where darker edges collide with lighter centers, creating centers that are both protected and attacked. Broken pixels add imperfections, enhancing the sensation of disruption, while maintaining open negative space to create balance.
