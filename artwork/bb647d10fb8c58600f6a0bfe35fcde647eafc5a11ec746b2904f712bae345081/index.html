Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes loneliness and connection through a palette that oscillates between dark and light. The contrasting colors create a visible tension, highlighting the dichotomy of feelings. The left half employs a stair-like pattern reflecting loneliness, while the right half is adorned with stripe patterns suggesting connection. Key collisions occur in the border areas where the dark violet meets the soft gold, creating an uncomfortable but intriguing shift that embodies the essence of the seed. This pattern invites reflection on our fragmented memories and the yearning for connection within isolation.
