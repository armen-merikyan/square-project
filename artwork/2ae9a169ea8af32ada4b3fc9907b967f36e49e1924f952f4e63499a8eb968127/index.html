Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrasts of longing and resolve through a structured yet disrupted pattern. The palette, featuring vibrant accents of electric blue and hot magenta against deep tones of dead black and static gray, highlights the tension between the two emotions. The arrangement emphasizes a halo void, with dense clusters near the center contrasting a spacious border, symbolizing both intimacy and distance. The use of broken pixels suggests flaws and imperfections, reflecting the disorder within the search for resolution.
