Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'spark vs ash' evokes a sense of contrast between vitality and decay. The selected palette—a blend of ripe green, acid green, honey gold, deep olive, and cream—reflects a balance between vibrant life (spark) and subdued, weary tones (ash). The main collision occurs in the diagonal arrangement, where vibrant accent colors break against the muted backgrounds, creating tension and dynamic movement. The composition encourages exploration of resilience and fragility amidst life's contrasts, represented through jagged and soft interrupts in pixel arrangements.
