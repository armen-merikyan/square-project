Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece explores the tension between softness and severity. The selected palette of powder pink and warm white evokes gentleness, while carbon black and sharp crimson introduce sharp contrast. The layout features a saturated center, emphasizing the push and pull between light and dark, while maintaining a few open spaces that convey a sense of quiet. The 'teeth' and 'veil' patterns interact dynamically, with density clashing against scattered openness, representing forgiveness amid guilt. The main collision manifests at the center, signifying the emotional turmoil within a structured environment.
