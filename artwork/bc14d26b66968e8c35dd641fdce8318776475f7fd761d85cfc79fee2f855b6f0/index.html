Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between clarity and chaos, symbolizing the struggle of a singular signal amidst surrounding noise. The palette of deep navy and muted dim cyan serves to create a calm yet oppressive atmosphere, with the stark white representing piercing clarity—a bright route that interrupts the static. The main collision occurs in the center, where brighter patches disperse through darker areas, suggestive of hope fighting against entropy. By embracing imperfect broken pixels, the artwork illustrates the fragility of memory and communication, allowing a narrative of interruption, distortion, and fleeting moments to emerge from the chaos. Patterns of radial and channel-like arrangements reflect the conflict of exposure versus shelter, and the delicate balance between memory versus erasure.
