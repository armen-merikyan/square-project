Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between awe and terror, captured in the contrasting palette of deep blacks, dark violets, soft golds, and pale greens. The interplay of these colors reflects a delicate balance between order and chaos, with the left half suggesting stability through a soft gradient of black and dark violet while the right introduces a fractured chaos with scattered golds and pale greens. The structured pattern of the left meets the broken, fragmented style on the right, creating an intriguing collision that embodies both awe and terror. Each block of color serves to emphasize the tension between these emotions, with occasional breaks reinforcing the idea of a disordered memory amidst a stable past.
