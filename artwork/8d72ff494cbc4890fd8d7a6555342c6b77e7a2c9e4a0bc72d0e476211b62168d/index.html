Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between restlessness and peace. The palette of pale mint and faded gold reflects moments of calmness disrupted by storm gray and near black, symbolizing restlessness. The main collision occurs diagonally, with warm hues flaring against cooler tones, suggesting a vibrant yet uneasy pulse. The intensity of the colors, combined with broken pixels, reinforces the notion of interrupted thought and fluctuating emotions, resonating with the idea of movement against stasis.
