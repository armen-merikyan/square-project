Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between fixed certainty and the fluidity of curiosity. The palette merges warm browns of safety and cool blues representing exploration—signifying a battle between grounding and seeking. The collision occurs at the center where structured cracks of certainty break into checker patterns of curiosity, representing the constant push and pull of inquiry against rigid borders. This interplay results in an abstract representation of the tide of curiosity pushing against the established order.
