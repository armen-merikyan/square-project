Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between panic and control, where soft gold and pale green symbolize hope and release, contrasting sharply with the depth of black and dark violet, representing the weight of pressure and uncertainty. The arrangement employs a diagonal flow, where darker, pressure-filled colors collide with lighter tones, creating a tension that suggests an impending shift. Broken pixels disrupt the symmetry, embodying the inherent chaos in collision, and reflecting the emotional scars inherent in this dynamic.
