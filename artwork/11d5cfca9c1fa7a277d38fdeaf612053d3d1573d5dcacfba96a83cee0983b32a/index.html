Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between restlessness and peace, represented by the contrasting application of colors. The palette combines warm and cool tones, reflecting both intimacy and distance. The calm stair-like pattern invokes peace, while the quiet fields reflect restlessness with jittered interruptions. The main contrast occurs at the diagonal intersection—the collision of warmth alongside coolness demonstrates the struggle between serenity and agitation, echoing the seed's themes of movement and memory. The use of broken pixels introduces deliberate imperfections, emphasizing fragility in the balance of emotions, creating a dynamic landscape of color and feeling.
