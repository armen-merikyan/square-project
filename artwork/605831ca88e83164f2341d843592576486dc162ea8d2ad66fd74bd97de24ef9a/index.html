Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between emptiness and desire, achieved through a palette of dark navies and cool blacks, contrasted with soft whites and dim cyans. The flow from left to right represents the rise of desire against the backdrop of persistent emptiness. Broken pixel placements create an imperfect visual noise, echoing the theme of longing against the spatial void, while the density along the edges brings tension to the overall composition, leading to a central focus that feels both inviting and isolating at once.
