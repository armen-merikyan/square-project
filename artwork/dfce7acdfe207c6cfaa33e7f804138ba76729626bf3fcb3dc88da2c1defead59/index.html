Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic battle between vibrance and softness, capturing the essence of sparks and ash. The palette unites the warmth of stain brown and soft gold with the subdued nature of ash gray, showcasing contrasts in resilience and fragility. The main collision occurs at the center, where vibrant colors clash against the gray, echoing themes of memory versus erasure. The pattern reflects a diagonal descent, infused with broken pixels to create organic disruption, symbolizing the interplay between growth and decay.
