Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between love and betrayal. Using a palette of pinks and dark maroon creates warmth contrasted by shadows, representing comfort fractured by loss. The layout alternates between soft stripes and a maze-like disturbance, where the collision occurs at the center, divided by a sharper edge. This duality symbolizes the battlefield of emotions—where love embodies warmth while betrayal introduces harshness, suggesting an unresolved struggle in a confined space.
