Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional struggle between rage and calm through a striking contrast of dark and light colors. The palette of deep blacks and vibrant pale blues reflects intensity against serenity, while the arrangement suggests a clash of forces—a dominant dark mass with scattered lighter elements resembling sparks of emotion. The use of broken pixels amplifies imperfection, symbolizing the frailty of balance in emotional states. The calm is structured with horizontal patterns, while the rage disrupts this with jagged interruptions, embodying the theme of 'light vs weight.'
