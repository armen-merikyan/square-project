Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional duality of 'spark' and 'ash', employing a contrast of vibrant colors representing energy against muted tones symbolizing decay. The palette, featuring clean white and warning orange as highlights, evokes a sense of scattered light and hot accents clashing with burnt quiet. The layout suggests a split horizontal flow, with a jagged edge effect giving a sense of interruption and dynamic movement. The collision is centered, emphasizing the struggle between growth and loss, akin to memory versus erasure, where the upper section (spark) is more prominent than the grounded bottom (ash). The interplay of colors and patterns captures the essence of resilience amidst fragility, making the viewer contemplate the tension between vitality and entropy.
