Artist Statement

The emotional tone is underscored by the contrast of loneliness and connection, manifesting through a dynamic interplay of dark and light colors. The palette, dominated by navy and black with hints of dim cyan, creates a deep feeling of solitude, while sporadic white highlights symbolize fleeting connections. The main collision occurs where the bright pixels intersect with the darker hues, suggesting moments of interaction amidst shadows. The burst pattern directed from the center reflects the struggle between hidden depths and exposed bursts of warmth, encapsulated in a grid that evokes both structure and chaos, mirroring the jagged nature of human relationships.
