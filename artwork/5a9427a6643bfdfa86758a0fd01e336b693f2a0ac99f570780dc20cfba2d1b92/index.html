Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between relief and dread, represented through the choice of a warm palette balanced against deep darkness. The colors #FFA500 (burnt orange) and #CD5C5C (dark red) emerge against a backdrop of rich charcoal tones, suggesting the fight for light amidst overwhelming weight. Symmetrical placements inspire balance, yet flawed iterations of color hint at brokenness. Impacts of lighter colors are interspersed, causing visual echo and tension, embodying the seed's theme of symmetry versus chaos.
