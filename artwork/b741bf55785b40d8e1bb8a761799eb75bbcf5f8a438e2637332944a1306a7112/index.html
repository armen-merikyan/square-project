Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a juxtaposition of chaos and clarity, reflecting the battle between signal and noise. The color palette consists of deep navy and dim cyan for calmness, contrasted by black to symbolize depth and static interruptions. Main collisions occur along the center, where the dense band of dark colors punctuates the lighter ones, evoking a pulse amidst a sea of interference. The broken pixels add an imperfection, symbolizing memory's transient nature. The abstract arrangement mirrors the layered complexities of sound and silence, echoing the effects of exposure and shelter as they clash.
