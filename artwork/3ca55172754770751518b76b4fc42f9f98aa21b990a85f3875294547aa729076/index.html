Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional contrast of longing and resolve through a striking interplay of color and arrangement. The palette, comprising warm hues like honey gold and deep olive, suggests a sense of yearning, while cream acts as a quiet background, providing the necessary negative space. The positioning of vibrant colors in a spiral pattern creates a sense of dynamic tension, with the deepest hues clustering towards the center, representing resolve, while light and muted tones recede, symbolizing longing. The arrangement suggests an interruption in the emotional flow, as if the colors are in a constant push and pull, embodying intimacy and distance. Broken pixels disrupt the clarity of the colors, introducing an imperfection that signifies the conflict between the two emotional states.
