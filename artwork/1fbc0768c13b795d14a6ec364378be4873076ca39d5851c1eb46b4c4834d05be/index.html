Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the tranquility of peace and the disruption of restlessness. The palette combines warm coral and soft amber to evoke moments of calm, contrasted by colder shades like blue-black and cold slate, symbolizing the weight of unrest. The composition's center holds a lighter field—soft amber—encircled by darker edges, emphasizing a collision between light and dark that suggests a sense of tension and release. Broken pixels are introduced as intentional imperfections, walking the line between structure and chaos, representing fleeting moments of memory amid constant motion. The path of visual flow is diagonal, contrasting with central calm, echoing the push and pull of emotions.
