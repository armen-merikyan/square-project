Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress through contrasting colors. The palette employs sepia and faded blue tones, representing warmth, memory, and calmness against more vibrant shades symbolizing forward motion and intensity. The pattern creates a sense of falling: the darker values in the top rows evoke introspection, while lighter, more saturated colors at the bottom signal energy and movement upward, creating a visual echo of the duality expressed in the seed. There is noticeable asymmetry, where bold colors interrupt neutral fields, articulating a sense of conflict and resolution over the span of the composition.
