Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between rage and calm. The palette uses stark contrasts with red as the dominant color, symbolizing rage, while pale blue and white provide the containment. The arrangement features chaotic red dots creating a visual clash mid-square, interrupted by calm, horizontal pale blue frames around the edges, suggesting the struggle for containment amidst overflow. This representation of emotional conflict resonates with the seed's themes of noise versus clarity and erosion against structure.
