Artist Statement

This 8x8 abstract composition explores the emotional spectrum of fear and hope, utilizing a palette of deep blacks and subtle golds to symbolize the contrasting forces at play. The use of jagged and soft edges conveys instability and comfort respectively. The main contrast occurs at the center, where the darkness (fear) meets the light (hope), creating areas of collision and merging that suggest a burst of emotion. The overall layout maintains significant negative space to suggest emptiness, while irregular patterns reflect the unpredictable nature of human emotion. This layout echoes the seed's themes of split and conflict, embodying an abstract representation of emotional tension.
