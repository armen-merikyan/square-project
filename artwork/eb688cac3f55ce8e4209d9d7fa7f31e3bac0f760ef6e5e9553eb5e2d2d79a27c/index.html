Artist Statement

The piece embodies the tension between clarity and confusion, using contrasting colors and patterns to reflect the emotional interplay. The dark tones represent confusion, while the soft gold hints at clarity. The arrangement of colors creates a motion from center outwards, evoking the feeling of chaotic energy emanating from a solid core. Broken pixels add visual disruption, contributing to the theme of fragmentation amidst the search for order.
