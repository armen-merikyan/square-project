Artist Statement

The artwork embodies a tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting color densities. The darker shades represent suspicion's weight, while the lighter hues offer glimpses of trust's soft glow. The diagonal layout creates a visual push-pull, with the darker colors clustered in the lower left representing suspicion trying to overwhelm trust, which softly glows in the upper right, embodying resilience. This arrangement uses broken pixels to add intentional disruption, suggesting conflict and imperfection within the emotional narrative.
