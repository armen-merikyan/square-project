Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between awe and terror through a contrasting palette of dark and light colors. The choice of naval and black tones creates a strong base reflecting terror, while the dim cyan and subtle whites introduce moments of awe, echoing the emotional complexity suggested by the seed. The layout shifts from dense, dark hues at the top to lighter accents at the bottom, due to the split horizontal layout. Broken pixel placements disrupt uniformity, hinting at instability within the structure, inviting the viewer to navigate the narrative of conflicting feelings.
