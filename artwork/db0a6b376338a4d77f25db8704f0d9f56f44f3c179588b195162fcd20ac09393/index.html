Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The core palette of muted off-white and dust gray suggests fragility and quietness, while compressed red punctuates the composition with bursts of emotional intensity. The layout focuses on a small bright cluster that creates a striking contrast against the overall dimness, representing fleeting moments of strength amidst pervasive vulnerability. The flow suggests fractured motions with a barcode-like pattern reflecting strength on one side and a stepwell texture signaling vulnerability on the other. This collision is most pronounced at the center, where the two patterns mingle, creating an intentional interplay between chaos and balance.
