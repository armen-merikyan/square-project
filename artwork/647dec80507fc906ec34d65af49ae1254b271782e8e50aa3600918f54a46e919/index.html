Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tone of longing intertwined with resolve through a tension of color and placement. The palette of off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach presents a nuanced conversation between calm and chaos. The dominant dusty gray represents resolve, while the softer off-white and red hints at longing. The primary collision occurs at the center, where radial patterns of longing intertwine with streaks of resolve, creating an echo of a fractured boundary. The quiet spaces suggest the weight of unfulfilled desires amidst strong, structured patterns, embodying the duality present in the seed.
