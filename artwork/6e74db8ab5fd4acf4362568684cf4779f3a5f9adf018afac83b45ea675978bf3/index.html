Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between loneliness and connection, using a palette that balances warmth and coolness. The stark contrast between deep black, pale blue, and bright white evokes a struggle between quiet spaces and dense clusters. The layout frames a central pixel suggesting a single point of focus surrounded by rhythmical pressure from the other colors, representing both a connection and a sense of isolation. It captures the pressing emotional flow, suggesting that within spaces of quiet, there are collisions that imply deeper narratives of emotional experience.
