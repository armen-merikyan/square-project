Artist Statement

This composition reflects a dance between vulnerability and strength through color density and layout. The palette of soft pink and warm white conveys an underlying tenderness, while the sharp crimson and carbon black inject moments of intensity. Collisions between the soft 'rain' pattern of lighter tones and the stacked strength of darker shades create a visual narrative of protection and exposure. The center, dominated by pale colors, represents a fragile space within a firm outer boundary, suggesting safety amid chaos.
