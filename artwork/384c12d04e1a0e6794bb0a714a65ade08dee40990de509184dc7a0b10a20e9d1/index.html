Artist Statement

This composition embodies the struggle between defiance and submission, represented by a contrast of warm and cool tones. The palette, consisting of yellow and orange for warmth, juxtaposes against deep blue and muted purple for a sense of calm. The arrangement flows from the center outwards, embodying the pulse of conflict, while soft edges suggest vulnerability. The broken pixel elements introduce a layer of imperfection, echoing the nuances of emotional states, while keeping a portion of negative space to ignite thoughts of openness versus enclosure, perfectly balancing intensity and calm.
