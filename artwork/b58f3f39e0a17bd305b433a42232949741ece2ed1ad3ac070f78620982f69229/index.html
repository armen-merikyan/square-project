Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension of desire and restraint through contrasting colors and spatial arrangement. The palette includes bright, saturated hues like hot coral and blood orange to represent the pull of desire, while charcoal and hollow blue convey the calm of restraint. The sharp contrast occurs along the edges, creating a dynamic tension where the brighter colors suggest overflow against the muted backgrounds, echoing the push and pull of emotional states. The layout features a halo structure, with vivid colors radiating from the center, reinforcing the idea of desire pressing outward while restrained by the darker hues. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, symbolizing the unexpected turns in emotional journeys.
