Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts loneliness and connection, expressed through a palette of warm reds and soft blues against stark blacks and whites. The outer rows evoke a sense of isolation with deeper, darker tones, while the inner square gradually brightens to hint at fragile connection. The use of broken pixels symbolizes the imperfections of communication and connection, creating tension against a backdrop of negative space that signifies unfulfilled potential. This arrangement captures the delicate balance of feeling exposed versus protected, reflecting an abstract narrative of seeking connection amidst isolation.
