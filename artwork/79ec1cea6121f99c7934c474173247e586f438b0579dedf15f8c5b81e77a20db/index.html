Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a dense and chaotic corner juxtaposed with quieter areas. The use of deep colors like blackened plum creates a heavy emotional tone, while muted cream offers respite, akin to a veil over the chaos. The collision occurs prominently in the top-left corner, which is saturated with rich colors, suggesting a bursting energy that contrasts sharply with more subdued colors spreading across the grid. This represents the spiral of conflict and resolution inherent in the seed's themes.
