Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between loneliness and connection, with an emotionally charged palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue representing fragmented memories and longing. The pattern emphasizes a central block of darker shades that contrasts with lighter colors radiating outward, symbolizing the push and pull between isolation and the desire for connection. The weight toward the top creates a sense of imbalance, reflecting the struggle to bridge the gap between the inner self and external perceptions.
