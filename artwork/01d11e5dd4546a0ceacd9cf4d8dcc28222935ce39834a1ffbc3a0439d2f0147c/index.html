Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette of ripe green and honey gold to symbolize growth and warmth against the contrast of deep olive and cream, which evoke a feeling of safety and shelter. The collision occurs at the center, where the 'tide' pattern of vulnerability disrupts the 'constellation' of strength, creating an abstract dynamic that suggests conflict yet harmony. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional imperfections, symbolizing the scars left by emotional exposure. This design encourages viewers to consider the threshold where these feelings intersect, inviting a reflection on their personal experiences of resilience and fragility.
