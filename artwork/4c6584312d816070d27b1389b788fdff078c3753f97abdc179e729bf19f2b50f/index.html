Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece embodies the struggle between control and surrender, manifested in a dynamic composition that reflects tension in the center while allowing for gentle release toward the edges. The chosen palette of muted rose and cool blues creates a contrast that visually narrates the push and pull of these emotions. The main collision occurs at the center where darker hues of control clash with lighter tones of surrender, suggesting a battle for dominance. The 'rain' pattern (control) meets the 'constellation' pattern (surrender) around the center, creating a layered, textured effect that reinforces the narrative of pressure giving way to relief.
