Artist Statement

This composition captures a dynamic emotional tension between envy and gratitude. The color palette, 'ancient_fire', presents a mix of burnt orange and dark red, symbolizing the heaviness of envy against the brightness of gratitude. The layout mimics reflection but intentionally diverges in certain areas to represent the imperfect balance of these feelings. The palette creates high contrast between rich darks and warm, inviting lights, with central pixels emphasizing the emotional tug-of-war. The use of broken pixels introduces a level of imperfection, echoing the natural chaos between these contrasting emotions.
