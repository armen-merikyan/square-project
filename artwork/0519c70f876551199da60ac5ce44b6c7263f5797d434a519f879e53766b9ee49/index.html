Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between warmth in love and the sharp cold of betrayal. The palette combines soft pinks and reds with dark maroon and gray, symbolizing affection challenged by harsh realities. The main contrast occurs in the center where soft, warm stripes collide with the maze pattern on the edges, creating a tension that reflects the central theme of symmetry versus break. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, emphasizing the fragility of love amidst betrayal. The layout follows a frame-and-core approach, allowing the viewer to feel the duality of these emotions and their chaotic interrelation.
