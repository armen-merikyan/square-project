Artist Statement

The piece encapsulates the tension between restlessness and peace through a palette of warm and cool tones. The reds and pinks embody the vibrant pulse of restlessness, while grays provide the calming influence of peace. The main collision occurs diagonally, where warmer hues push against the cool boundary, suggesting the idea of containment met with emotional overflow. This arrangement reflects the fluidity of memory and motion, capturing the essence of the seed through chaotic yet structured patterns.
