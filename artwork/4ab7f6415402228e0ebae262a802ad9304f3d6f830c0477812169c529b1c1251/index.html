Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional tension between isolation and connection. The palette of deep navy, black, and dim cyan evokes a sense of loneliness, while the inclusion of a single white pixel offers a stark contrast suggesting a glimmer of connection. The arrangement echoes a top-weighted layout where the darker colors dominate the corners, hinting at barriers, while the lighter color gradually emerges from the center, representing potentiality and fragility in connections. The noise pattern reflects the chaotic nature of attempts at connection amidst the backdrop of solitude, with broken pixels interspersed to symbolize imperfections in the emotional landscape.
