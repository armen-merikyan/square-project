Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between signal and noise, where a solitary pulse strives to assert itself amidst overwhelming static. The chosen palette of dark navy, black, and dim cyan creates a sense of depth and isolation punctuated by a bright white that acts as a beacon, representing the strong signal. The contrast of muted and bright colors generates a dynamic visual narrative of struggle and resilience. The collisions are concentrated at the center, where bright interrupts static, echoing the central theme of the seed: a battle against chaos. Negative space enhances the sense of quiet amidst the clamor, allowing the viewer to reflect on the push and pull of each element.
