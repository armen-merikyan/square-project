Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between quietude and pressure, embodied by the arrangement of dense, dark colors contrasted against softer, lighter hues. The palette employs muted shades of off-white and faint peach to create a calm backdrop, while compressed red and dark navy introduce an element of tension, suggesting a chaotic interplay. The top right corner is congested with darker colors, symbolizing pressure, while the bottom left features a lighter field, representing release. This formation captures the 'open gate' layout, where contrast and flow embody the themes from the seed.
