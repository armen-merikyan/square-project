Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between desire and emptiness, creating a visual tension that oscillates between warm saturation and cool dullness. The palette combines warm hues like hot coral and blood orange to evoke desire with cooler tones like charcoal and hollow blue that signify emptiness. The primary contrast occurs at the top left corner with vibrant colors that seem to overflow, while the right side utilizes muted shades to create quiet pressure. This arrangement reflects the seed's notion of restraint against the yearning for expansion, resulting in a conversation of dominance and disruption throughout the square.
