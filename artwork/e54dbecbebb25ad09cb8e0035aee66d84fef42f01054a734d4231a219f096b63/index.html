Artist Statement

This square reflects the tension between loneliness and connection through color placement. The dark palette evokes a sense of isolation, while the brighter accents suggest potential connections. The arrangement features a dense cluster of darker hues on one side that interrupts the lighter colors, representing the weight of loneliness against the possibility of connection. The use of broken pixels adds to the feeling of imperfection in memory, creating disjointed echoes of presence amidst absence.
