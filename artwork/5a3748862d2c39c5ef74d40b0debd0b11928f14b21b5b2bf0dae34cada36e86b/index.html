Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between trust and suspicion. The palette of hot coral and blood orange represents warmth and vibrancy associated with trust, while charcoal and hollow blue bring a cool tone of suspicion. The main collision occurs at the center where static patterns of trust overlap with broken dots of suspicion, creating a dynamic conflict. The layout invokes a broken frame, leaving intentional gaps suggesting vulnerability and intrusion. Imperfections, created through broken pixels, further emphasize emotional scars and disruptions, echoing the inner struggles between these contrasting feelings.
