Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between defiance and submission through a symmetric pattern eroded at the edges. The palette combines warm clay tones with cool blues, reflecting intimacy and distance, mirroring the emotional contrast. The main collision occurs centrally, where bold, noisy patterns challenge the softer flood patterns at the edges. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, emphasizing the theme of chaos against calm. This abstract pattern embodies the spiraling inward dynamics suggested by the seed, resonating with themes of pressure and release.
