Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between rage and calm, using the palette of red, pale blue, white, and black to signify intense emotion against tranquility. The main collision occurs in the top-left corner where vivid reds burst into cooler blues, representing an overflow of chaotic energy within a calm field. The layout emphasizes this by pushing bright accents to the edge while tranquil hues recede, echoing the concept of containment versus overflow. Broken pixels are integrated as disruptions, enhancing the raw emotion of the arrangement.
