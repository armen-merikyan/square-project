Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between confusion and clarity, expressed through a palette of deep navy and black contrasted with the starkness of white and dim cyan. The arrangement creates a pattern where dense clusters of dots reflect confusion, surrounded by areas of negative space that breathe clarity. The collision occurs in the center where the 'crack' theme is illustrated by stark color shifts and broken pixels, making the symmetry feel fractured. This conveys the struggle between chaos and calm, echoing the seed's themes of disorder and resolution.
