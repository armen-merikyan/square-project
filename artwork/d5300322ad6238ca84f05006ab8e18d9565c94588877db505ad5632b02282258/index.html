Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the conflict between clarity and confusion. The color palette, sourced from 'joy_grief,' combines vibrant yellows and muted purples to signify the contrasting feelings. The layout features a fragmented grid pattern, symbolizing symmetry breaking, while disruptions from radial patterns represent the chaotic aspects of confusion. Key contrasts lie in the juxtaposition of warm and cool tones, with colors colliding in the center to invoke a sense of pulse and rhythm, embodying the struggle between order and disorder.
