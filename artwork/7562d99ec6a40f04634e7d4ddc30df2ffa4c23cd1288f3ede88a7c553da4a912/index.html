Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between trust and suspicion, depicted through a contrast of clear patterns and jagged disruptions. The color palette, anchored in warmer pinks and reds, symbolizes trust, contrasted with the colder gray tones representing suspicion. The central collision, occurring in the inner 4x4 space, embodies an echo of trust, while the outer borders disrupt the flow with jagged edges that suggest suspicion. This layout draws from a frame-and-core concept, where the outer chaos frames the inner order, reinforcing the theme of stability versus insecurity.
