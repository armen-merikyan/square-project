Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between desire and restraint through a clash of saturated and muted colors. The palette has been carefully selected to balance bright reds and soft creams, illustrating the push and pull of emotional states. The layout suggests a fractured repair path, with bright colors breaking through a darker static structure. The focal collision occurs centrally, where warm colors intersect with muted edges, creating a visual dialogue that captures the essence of love and betrayal.
