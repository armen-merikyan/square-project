Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional duel between calm and rage, utilizing a palette where vibrant reds and blacks clash with soft blues and whites. The cooler colors dominate, establishing a tranquil base while chaotic red elements erupt, symbolizing the overflow of fierce emotions. The composition features jagged red patterns cutting through a descending flow of blue, embodying the idea of interruption and collision. Negative space is preserved, creating a veil of quiet amidst the explosive colors, suggesting a suppressed tension. This arrangement evokes a sense of anticipation and unresolved conflict, shaped by the language of the seed.
