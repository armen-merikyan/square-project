Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette derived from 'ancient_fire' to convey warmth and intensity. The main contrast lies in the dense orange center against the darker borders, evoking a sense of both security and exposure. The arrangement presents an interplay of dense clusters colliding with open spaces, illustrating the struggle between being guarded and unshielded. Broken pixels disrupt uniformity, enhancing the narrative of imperfection in the experience of vulnerability and strength.
