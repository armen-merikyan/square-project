Artist Statement

The artwork encapsulates the emotional tug-of-war between overwhelming desire and suffocating emptiness. The palette features bold, warm colors representing desire (hot coral, blood orange) contrasted with cool, muted tones (dust pink, hollow blue) that evoke feelings of emptiness. The central collision occurs in the center with vibrant colors surging outward, interrupted by a receding dark edge suggesting containment. The arrangement echoes a spiral motion, contributing to the dynamic interplay between overflowing intensity and restrained quietude, reflecting the seed's exploration of contrasting emotional states.
