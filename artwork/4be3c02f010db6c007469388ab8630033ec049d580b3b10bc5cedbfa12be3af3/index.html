Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette featuring moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold reflects a complex interplay of serenity and urgency. The moss green dominates, representing a feeling of 'contained longing,' while the rust serves as a strong contrast indicating 'resolve.' The arrangement, with a cluster of colors in one corner, embodies a pressure dynamic where the colors seem to overflow, representing a yearning that is both filled and hindered. The broken pixels add layers of imperfection, depicting the scars of experience. Overall, the piece tells a story of emotional collision through patient quietness and sharp intervention, inviting viewers to explore the nuances of feeling within abstract form.
