Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork embodies the struggle between defiance and submission, reflected in the palette choice of muted tones contrasted with vibrant accents. Darker colors create a sense of confinement and resistance at the borders, while brighter colors pulse from the center, illustrating the tension of unyielding strength conflicting with the push to retreat. The broken pixels symbolize flaws and disruptions, aligning with the chaotic themes of the seed. The overall composition emphasizes density at the center, signifying strength, while the edges exhibit a sense of struggle to contain that energy, evoking a powerful narrative of conflict and resolution.
