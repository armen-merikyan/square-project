Artist Statement

This composition conveys the tension between restlessness and peace through a defined palette. The warm tones like hot coral and blood orange symbolize restlessness, while the cool shades like hollow blue represent peace. The arrangement allows for a stark collision towards the center, suggesting a clashing emotional argument. Broken pixels interrupt the flow, embodying the disturbances of memory versus motion. The layout maintains significant negative space while disrupting balance, reflecting unresolved feelings.
