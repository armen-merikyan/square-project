Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between desire and emptiness through a vibrant yet obstructed palette. The palette primarily features warm tones of coral and orange to symbolize desire, contrasted against deep charcoal and hollow blue representing emptiness. The collision occurs centrally, where the saturated broken_frame pattern of warmth collides with the cold, structured crosshatch of restraint, creating a visual discord that embodies the inner struggle. This interplay invites viewers to reflect on the emotions evoked by the push and pull of conflicting desires.
