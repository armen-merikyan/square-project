Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette employs deep blacks and electric blues to represent strength, while pale grays and a hint of hot magenta symbolize vulnerability. The central area is defined by intertwined 'flood' and 'ripple' patterns, presenting a collision where the bold colors dominate yet allow gaps for lighter shades to penetrate, illustrating the theme of exposure in a world that is both protective and threatening. The arrangement of colors creates dynamic tension, while the intentional placement of broken pixels adds a sense of imperfection and realism.
