Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between quiet and pressure through a contrasting color palette. The muted tones of off_white and dust_gray represent the quiet fields, while the bold compressed_red and dark_navy symbolize the pressure. The composition features denser clusters in the bottom-left, creating a sense of weight that gradually loosens towards the top-right, embodying the concept of release. The arrangement reflects a diagonal movement, suggesting tension and counterflow, while broken pixels introduce imperfections that echo the theme of shattering against tide, enhancing the overall dynamic.
