Artist Statement

The emotional tone articulated in the seed is captured through the contrast between control and surrender. The palette of hot coral and hollow blue embodies tension—warmth battling with coolness, highlighting the inner conflict. The most significant collision occurs at the center where chaotic patches of color disrupt a measured approach, representing the stutter step between surrendering and retaining control. The broken pixels create a sense of imperfection that adds depth, akin to a hinge swinging between two states of being. The layout showcases vast negative space surrounding a dynamic, off-center pattern—emphasizing the loneliness of a singular signal fighting for dominance amidst overwhelming pressure.
