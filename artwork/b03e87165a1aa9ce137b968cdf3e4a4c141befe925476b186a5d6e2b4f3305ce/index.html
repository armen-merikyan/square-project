Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette features pale aqua and mist gray to symbolize distant warmth and tranquility, coupled with alarm red for urgency and ink blue for depth. The main collision occurs diagonally, where dense, tightly packed colors express the impulse of longing, contrasting with the sparse, structured resolution in the upper and lower rows, highlighting the theme of order versus chaos. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, evoking the unpredictability of emotions. The overall placement suggests a serene yet conflicted atmosphere, echoing the push and pull of intimacy and distance.
