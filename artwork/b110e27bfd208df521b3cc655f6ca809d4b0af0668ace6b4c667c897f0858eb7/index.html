Artist Statement

The composition captures a conflict between quietness and pressure through a carefully chosen palette of off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach. The outer border, representing pressure, utilizes darker tones with sharp edges for a weighted feel, while the inner area portrays a quieter mood with softer hues, creating an effectively contrasting visual. The pattern collision occurs diagonally, where the shatter motif disrupts the calm barcode structure, ultimately suggesting a narrative of internal struggle between tension and release.
