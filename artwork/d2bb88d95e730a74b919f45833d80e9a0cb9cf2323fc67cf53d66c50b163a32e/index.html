Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts restlessness with peace, reflected in the chaotic pattern on the left and structured spacing on the right. The palette of signal green, electric blue, and hot magenta introduces a tension between tranquility and agitation. The main collision occurs diagonally from the center to the edge, where disorder meets calm. This arrangement encapsulates the seed's essence, displaying the push and pull between memory and movement, using colors and placements to evoke a story of opposing emotional states.
