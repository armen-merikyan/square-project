Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between longing and resolve through contrasting colors and fragmented patterns. The palette of deep black and vibrant red conveys urgency and intensity, while pale blue introduces moments of calm reflection. The main collision occurs in the central grid area where dense blocks of red clash against softer, quieter blue segments, echoing the pulse of conflicting emotions. Broken pixels disrupt the pattern, symbolizing damage and repair, while maintaining an overall serene structure amidst urgency. The arrangement of color sequences creates a rhythm that recalls both yearning and strength, aligning with the conceptual exploration of a ladder as abstract aspiration.
