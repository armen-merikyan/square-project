Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional interplay of joy and grief through contrasting warm and cool tones. The palette, including yellows and deep blues, creates a dynamic strife between light optimism and heavy melancholy. The layout mimics falling pixels, echoing the descent of emotions—an array of light rings symbolizing joyful memories confronts dark, separated sections indicating sorrow. The deliberate unevenness introduces a sense of broken symmetry, suggesting an imbalance inherent to the human experience. The collisions happen where vibrant yellows clash against muted purples, embodying a spectrum of joy punctuated by grief.
