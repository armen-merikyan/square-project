Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional duality of longing and resolve. The warm coral and soft amber tones symbolize distant warmth and unfinished approaches, while the colder slate and blue-black suggest a clear boundary and firm axis. The layout presents a tension between a foggy area of longing on the left and a structured, resolved space on the right, creating a visual argument. Broken pixels scattered throughout symbolize disruption and imperfection, echoing the seed's themes of repair versus damage. The composition favours a serene quiet field contrasted with sharper accents, enhancing the overall sense of conflict and balance.
