Artist Statement

This piece embodies a struggle between tenderness and rage. A palette of saturated reds contrasted with muted pale blues conveys the intensity of these emotions. The primary collision occurs vertically, where strong reds descend like a jagged storm against the soft blue surroundings, echoing a turbulence within calmness. The edge style is soft and feathered, allowing the more aggressive colors to merge and bleed subtly into the quiet blue, symbolizing how these emotions can coexist. This layout represents a 'falling pixels' theme, crafting a narrative of emotional tension amidst the chaos, suggesting turmoil anchored in the tender, quiet moments.
