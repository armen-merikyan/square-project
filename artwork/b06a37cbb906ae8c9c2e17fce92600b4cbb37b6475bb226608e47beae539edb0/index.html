Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tug-of-war between inertia and yearning, employing a palette that blends vibrant and muted colors. The collision of hot coral and blood orange represents desire, vibrant and longing, while the hollow blue and charcoal signify emptiness and restraint. The center of the composition is where these emotional contrasts meet, creating a stark visual tension. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the artwork's narrative of dissatisfaction and yearning under constraints.
