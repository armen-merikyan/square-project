Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension of confusion versus clarity through its stark contrast of colors and patterns. The palette, rooted in deep navy and black with accents of dim cyan and white, hints at the loneliness of signal noise, evoking a sense of isolation amidst chaos. The left side employs a 'stripe' pattern to convey clarity while the right side with its 'crack' pattern represents confusion, contrasting sharply against the thick outlines that create a visual boundary. Broken pixels create intentional disruption, enhancing the theme of unresolved tension.
