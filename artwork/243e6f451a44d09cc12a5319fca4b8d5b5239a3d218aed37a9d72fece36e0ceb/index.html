Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tug-of-war between longing and resolve. The palette, featuring deep blacks and soft blues, creates an atmosphere of yearning and muted hope. The contrasting elements of dense clusters of color against quiet fields of white highlight the struggle between chaos and order. The main collision occurs in the central cluster of black and red, representing a profound emotional turmoil that disrupts the calm, echoing the seed's themes of intimacy and distance. The 'falling pixels' layout reinforces the descent of these emotions, while broken pixels introduce imperfection, symbolizing the ongoing battle within.
