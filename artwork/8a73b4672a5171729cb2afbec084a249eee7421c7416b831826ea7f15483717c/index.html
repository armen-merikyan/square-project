Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between dread and relief, captured through a palette of muted grays for weight and inviting mint and soft sky for openness. The collision occurs at the center where the dark pulse marks clash with lighter teeth patterns, forming an intentional disruption. The seed inspired a layout that emphasizes a hollow center suggesting an echo of chaos amid the order of soft framing colors.
