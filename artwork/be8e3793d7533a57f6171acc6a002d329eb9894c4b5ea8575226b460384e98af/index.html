Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between panic and control, illustrated through contrasting colors and disrupted patterns that symbolize conflict. The selected palette's warm tones (burnt orange, dark red) embody chaos and intense emotions, while the cooler charcoal and gold signify calm and control, creating tension throughout the composition. The dominant contrast occurs in the center, where jagged, broken pixels interrupt a harmonious layout, symbolizing the way chaos encroaches upon a controlled state. This arrangement reflects the seed's conflict on both structural and emotional levels.
