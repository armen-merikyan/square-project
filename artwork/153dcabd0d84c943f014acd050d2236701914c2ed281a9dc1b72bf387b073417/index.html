Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of love and betrayal. The palette, consisting of warm pinks and deep maroons, evokes passion while stark grays introduce a contrasting chill, representing betrayal. The left half signifies love, with softer, rounded arrangements, and the right side displays sharper arrangements, indicative of discomfort and fracture. The central broken pattern where colors collide hints at a moment of tension and disillusionment. Overall, it captures the journey from warmth to coldness, a visual story told through deliberate color placement and abstraction.
