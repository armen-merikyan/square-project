Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension of awe and terror, laid against a backdrop of order and chaos. The warm tones of orange and yellow contrasting with the deep blue and muted purple suggest conflicting feelings, while the broken pixels create a sense of instability and fragmentation, echoing the themes of memory and perception. The layout shifts between the left and right halves, maintaining a mirror-like quality disrupted by intentional disarray, fostering a dialogue between calmness and pressure through the arrangement of contrasting colors and shapes.
