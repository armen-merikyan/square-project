Artist Statement

This composition depicts the emotional struggle between joy and grief through its color placement and contrast. The palette blends warm hues of yellow and orange symbolizing joy, contrasted against muted purples and deep blues representing grief. The left-weighted arrangement signifies an emotional imbalance, while the subtle patterns evoke a sense of pulse and disruption. The presence of broken pixels introduces a hint of imperfection, echoing the theme of betrayal within love, creating a visual narrative that oscillates between hope and sorrow.
