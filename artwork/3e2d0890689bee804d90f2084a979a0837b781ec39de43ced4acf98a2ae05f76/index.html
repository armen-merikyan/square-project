Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension of loneliness versus connection through a stark contrast of filled and empty spaces. The muted purples and deep blues represent isolation, while the yellows and oranges symbolize warmth and potential connection. The center features a burst of colors, echoing the concept of a 'tear' through loneliness, surrounded by darker hues that create a weighty border, implying protection yet also confinement. The pattern flows from top to bottom, fostering a sense of dynamic movement between the peaks of connection and troughs of loneliness, while crosshatching invites an intricate interplay in the grid of emotions.
