Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between pressure and quietude through a juxtaposition of sharp and soft colors, echoing the seed's themes. The palette features muted 'off_white' and 'dust_gray' for the quiet spaces, contrasting with the dense 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy' representing pressure. The main contrast occurs at the center, where the pressure builds but is interrupted by lighter elements that suggest clarity amidst chaos. The layout of a hollow center reflects a dynamic tension, while broken pixels disrupt the flow, representing imperfections like scars or echoes of memory.
