Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, illustrated through a vibrant palette of deep blues and warming oranges. The arrangement suggests a dynamic interplay, with strong, rooted blocks contrasting against soft, echoing hues, encapsulating a feeling of pressure and release. The overt collision happens centrally, where strong colors clash with lighter, muted fields, symbolizing the duality of desire and emptiness. The design embraces asymmetry to reflect life's unpredictable nature, marked by intentional broken pixels that serve as emotive disruptions amidst the flow.
