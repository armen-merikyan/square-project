Artist Statement

The artwork juxtaposes the emotional extremes of restlessness and peace through a bold palette of deep reds and soft grays. The restlessness emerges through dynamic, frenetic patterns at the edges, while patches of serene gray create resting places among the chaos. The main collision exists in the center, where deep maroon meets lighter pink, symbolizing a struggle for peace amid turmoil. This tension reflects the seed's themes of motion and memory against a backdrop of tranquility, creating a rhythmic yet discordant energy.
