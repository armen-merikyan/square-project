Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the contrast between joy and grief, using a palette that harmonizes warm and cool tones. The colors yellow and orange symbolize joy and openness, while deep blue and muted purple represent grief and heaviness. The patterned arrangement captures a zigzag movement, with vibrant colors fighting against darker tones, enveloping the central space yet leaving room for air. The hard edges offer a sense of conflict, while broken pixels suggest the raw imperfection of emotions. The layout is intentionally sparse, embodying the negative space rule to allow reflection amidst the emotional tension.
