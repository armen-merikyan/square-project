Artist Statement

Emotional tone reflects a struggle between light and dark, capturing the nuances of pressure and release. The selected palette of black, white, pale blue, and red creates a visceral contrast that embodies both envy (darkness) and gratitude (light). The pattern is defined by sharp, irregular disruptions in an otherwise soft gradient, emphasizing emotional tension and resolution. The layout allows for breathing space, suggesting release amidst collisions. Each pixel's placement is intentional, fostering an interplay of density and quietness, echoing the complexities of emotional experiences.
