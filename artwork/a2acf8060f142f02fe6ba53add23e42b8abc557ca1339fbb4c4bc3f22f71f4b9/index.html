Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a delicate balance between longing and resolve, suggesting both a search for connection and a firm stance. The chosen palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon represents the depth of emotions layered between warmth and grief, with darker shades grounding the vibrant reds. The main collision between longing (spark pattern) and resolve (noise pattern) occurs in the center, where the contrasting patterns collide, symbolizing the tension felt between desire and determination, while the surrounding negative space permits breathing room in the design. Broken pixels amplify feelings of imperfection, aligning with the drawn-out emotional narrative.
