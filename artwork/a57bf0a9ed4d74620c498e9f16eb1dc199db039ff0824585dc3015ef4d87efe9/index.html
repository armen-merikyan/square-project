Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional tone of confusion and clarity, represented through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of deep blue and muted purple serves to create a sense of calm amidst chaos, reflecting the juxtaposition of clarity against confusion. The center is defined with thick outlines, while the surrounding pixels disrupt this order with stripes, suggesting falling and breaking, indicative of the 'machine emotion' aspect. The main contrast arises between the dense central area and the broken, hollow edges, creating a visual tension that mirrors the emotional landscape of the seed.
