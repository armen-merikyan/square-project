Artist Statement

This abstract composition explores the emotional tension between strength and vulnerability. The palette, featuring soft powder pinks against stark carbon black and sharp crimson, reflects the delicate balance between exposure and shelter. The strong lines of black and gray create an anchoring presence while the lighter colors pulse outward, embodying vulnerability. The collision occurs centrally where the softer colors meet the hard edges, creating an area of tension, representing the coexistence of both emotions. The rising visual flow embodies hope as colors ascend from dark to light, illustrating a journey inward toward connection.
