Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between clarity and distortion, allowing one bright path to pulse through layers of muted tones. The palette, combining vibrant signal green and electric blue with static gray and dead black, enhances the contrast of a clear focal point amidst the noise. The main collision occurs around the center, where the vivid colors interject into a sea of subdued tones, suggesting a struggle for clarity and presence amid confusion. The layout's mirror/broken structure embodies the inherent imperfections of communication, echoing the complexities of exposure and shelter.
