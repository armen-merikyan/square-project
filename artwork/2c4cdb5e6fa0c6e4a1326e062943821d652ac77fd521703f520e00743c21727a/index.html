Artist Statement

This artwork expresses the emotional tension of longing versus resolve through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, rooted in deep blues and subtle yellows, mirrors the themes of mourning and renewal, representing both the sadness of broken connections and the brightness of new beginnings. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions that embody the struggle between repair and damage. The central collision of dark and light hues creates visual tension, as the dark colors dominate while the softer yellows serve as highlights, suggesting moments of light amidst a darker emotional landscape. The alternating patterns of shatter and mirror resonate with the ideas of fractured reflections and unresolved emotions, culminating in a complex narrative of tension and release.
