Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between confusion and clarity through colors that oscillate between vibrant and subdued. The palette combines lime and electric blue to evoke a sense of energy and spontaneity, contrasted with black and magenta which convey depth and disarray. The main contrast occurs in the upper-right corner, where bright colors collide with darker tones, symbolizing a struggle between chaos and order. The seed prompted a pattern that reflects fractured symmetry, illustrated by alternating arrangements of bold colors that simulate the feeling of noise against clarity, fostering a visual representation of emotional disturbance and release. The layout suggests a rising signal, reinforcing the notion of hope amidst confusion.
