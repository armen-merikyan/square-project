Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between emptiness and desire through a juxtaposition of cool and warm colors. The palette features vibrant greens and blues that symbolize desire, contrasted with deep blacks representing emptiness. The diagonal rise mimics the longing associated with desire, whereas the lower rows maintain a cooler, quieter tone that suggests feelings of isolation. The pattern is split-screen, representing the inner conflict of yearning and absence. Broken pixels randomly disrupt the smooth flow, embodying the imperfection and fracture of emotions.
