Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional juxtaposition of vulnerability and strength. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon symbolizes love, betrayal, and the conflict between exposure and shelter. The center features a dense cluster of dark maroon, representing strength, while softer pink accents suggest vulnerability around it. The broken pixels serve as scars, creating a path of scattered repair, reflecting the collision of these emotions. The dominant density in the center contrasts with negative spaces and scattered light colors, providing balance yet tension.
