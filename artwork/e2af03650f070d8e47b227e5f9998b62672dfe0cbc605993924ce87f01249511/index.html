Artist Statement

This piece contrasts softness with severity, embodying the tension between delicate and harsh emotions. The palette consists of powder pink and warm white as gentle tones representing softness, while carbon black and sharp crimson provide stark interruptions indicative of severity. The light colors occupy the outer edges, with dark highlights in the center, creating a pulsating rhythm that echoes the interaction of guilt and forgiveness. The collision occurs through sharp crimson islands amid a field of softer tones, symbolizing a turbulent emotional relationship. The negative space is maintained to reflect the openness of the 'gate' in the concept.
