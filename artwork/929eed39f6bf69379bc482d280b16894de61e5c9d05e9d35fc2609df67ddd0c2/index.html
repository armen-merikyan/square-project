Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between rage and calm, symbolized by the explosive red and the tranquil pale blue. By placing the hotter, saturated reds in the top-left corner, the piece evokes a sense of overflowing energy. The cooler tones create a calming resistance, particularly in the lower-right, which is more muted to allow negative space that emphasizes the chaotic edge. Broken pixels disrupt the smoothness, suggesting interruptions in calm or violent eruptions. This arrangement captures a dialogue of emotional push and pull, resonating with the seed’s themes.
