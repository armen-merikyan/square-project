Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional dichotomy of rage and calm through an interplay of dark and light colors. The upper half, dominated by deep blacks and fragmented reds, evokes explosive energy, while the lower half features a lighter palette with soothing pale blues and whites that signify calmness. The sharp contrasts create a collision between the chaos of emotion and the order of tranquility. The arrangement hints at upward movement, representing tension rising and falling, influenced by the seed's themes of orbit and open vs closed spaces.
