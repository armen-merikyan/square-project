Artist Statement

The artwork captures the dual emotion of joy and grief through contrasting colors and patterns. The left side, representing joy, utilizes bright yellows and oranges—sharp, spark-like qualities—to create a sense of expanding energy. In contrast, the right side portrays grief with deep blues and muted purples, channeling heaviness and reflection. The central area serves as a stark reminder of the feelings colliding, with broken pixels suggesting imperfection and interruption, adding to the narrative of conflict between joy and sorrow. This tension is mirrored in the layout's left-weighted symmetry, suggesting a subtle imbalance that echoes the fragility of emotion.
