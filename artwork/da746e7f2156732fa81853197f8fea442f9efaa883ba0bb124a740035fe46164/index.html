Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional dialogue between burst and residue, with a bright, warm palette symbolizing the vitality and intensity of 'spark' clashing against the muted, cool tones representing 'ash'. The diagonal arrangement seeks to reflect the tension inherent in resilience versus fragility, with a pressure corner layout accentuating the struggle as the bright colors overflow into denser, quieter spaces. Key color placements establish a vivid contrast at the top-left, creating a dynamic interaction that embodies the emotional narrative of the seed.
