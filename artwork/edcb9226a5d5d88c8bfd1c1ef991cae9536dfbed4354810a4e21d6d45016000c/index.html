Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances longing and resolve, using contrast to evoke a dialogue between quietness and urgency. The palette comprises muted tones of off-white and dust gray for a subtle atmosphere, contrasted with the compressed red and dark navy that inject tension and depth. The main collision occurs in the upper left corner, embracing density amidst the quiet. The 'fold' shape seed inspires a layered composition, with checker patterns suggesting a scattered rhythm against flowing ash fields, illustrating the push and pull of intimate connections nested within broader, chaotic currents.
