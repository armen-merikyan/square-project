Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between emptiness and desire, captured through a contrasting palette of warm reds and cool grays. The collision occurs between the vibrant pink and deep maroon, representing desire breaking through the cool, muted tones of gray symbolizing emptiness. The layout follows a nearly mirrored symmetry with small disruptions that reflect the theme of broken signals and loneliness. The diagonal flow enhances a sense of rising tension, while fragmented patterns showcase the inner struggle, allowing for contemplation amidst the chaos.
