Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional struggle of relief battling dread through the interplay of dark and light colors. The selected palette of deep blacks juxtaposed with pale blues creates a visceral contrast, emphasizing emotional collision. The 'grid' pattern representing relief is subtly interrupted by jagged edges and broken pixels, embodying the disarray of dread. 'Top_weighted' layout directs the viewer’s gaze downward, giving weight to the darker hues and a sense of looming pressure, while amidst the quiet of the negative space, pockets of light emerge to signify fleeting moments of calm. The arrangement reflects the constant balance between dread and the search for relief, with color choices chosen to evoke contemplation and depth.
