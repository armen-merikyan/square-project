Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys the tension between longing and resolve as a dynamic interplay of warm and cool tones. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes a nuanced dialogue of fear and hope, with dark hues suggesting depth and longing, while soft gold represents resolution and warmth. The main collision occurs centrally where a patchwork of longing clashes against a structured stepwell representing resolve, embodying a shift from emotion to steadiness. Broken pixels create intentional disruptions, invoking the mirror of repair and damage.
