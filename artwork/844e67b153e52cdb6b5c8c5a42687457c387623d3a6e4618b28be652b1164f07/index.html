Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between confusion and clarity, using a jagged contrast of colors. The chosen palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green symbolizes the interplay of fear and hope, creating a pulse of emotion across the pixels. The left side mirrors the right, with intentional disruptions that represent broken symmetry. This choice creates a narrative of uncertainty disrupted by moments of clarity, with dense clusters of darker tones colliding against brighter accents, embodying the struggle of 'falling pixels' against a backdrop of 'crosshatch noise.'
