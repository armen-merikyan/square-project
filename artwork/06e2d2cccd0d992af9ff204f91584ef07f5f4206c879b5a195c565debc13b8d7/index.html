Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between joy and grief through contrasting use of color and pattern. The palette includes warm yellows and oranges to signify joy, contrasted with deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The orderly progression on one side reflects rising hope, while the chaotic dispersal on the other conveys fragmentation. Broken pixels create imperfections that mirror emotional experiences. The overall composition spirals upward, symbolizing the journey from sorrow to uplifting resilience, interspersed with moments of stillness that allow for reflection.
