Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a clash between calm and intense pressure, embodying the seed's themes of collision and release. The palette of red, pale blue, black, and white creates a strong contrast—red embodies rage and intensity, while pale blue offers a soothing counterbalance. This arrangement achieves a striking division, with the top half representing calm through a soft, spaced pattern, contrasted with a chaotic, jagged pattern in the lower half that echoes the dynamics of pressure. The irregular placement of colors serves to illustrate the narrative of emotional clashes and tensions, evoking a sense of unresolved motion and complex feelings.
