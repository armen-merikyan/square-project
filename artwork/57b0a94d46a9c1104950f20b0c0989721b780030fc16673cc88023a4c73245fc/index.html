Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through contrasting shades and patterns. The palette features deep blacks and dark violets symbolizing longing and the muted tones of pale green and soft gold representing resolve. The quiet channel layout allows for a stark contrast between dense clusters of emotion and negative space, with broken pixels introducing deliberate imperfections that echo the theme of repair versus damage. The collision occurs centrally, where the patterns disrupt each other, invoking a feeling of urgency amidst serenity.
