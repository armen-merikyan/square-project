Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between clarity and confusion, represented through the contrasting use of deep, muted tones and soft, pale colors. The palette is derived from 'broken memory,' invoking a sense of nostalgia mixed with discomfort. The collision occurs where dark colors clash against lighter shades, creating a visual pulse of tension. The layout reflects a broken symmetry, with a structured outline containing an inner chaos, embodying the struggle between these contrasting emotions. The arrangement reflects a notion of falling pixels, as some areas appear dense while others remain sparse, representing the ebb and flow of understanding.
