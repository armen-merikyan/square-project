Artist Statement

This composition carries the emotional tone of conflict between chaos and control. The chosen palette of deep navy and dim cyan reflects the struggle, contrasting dark undertones with bright accents. The soft edges represent the tendency of chaos to bleed into control, creating a sense of unease. The layout flows upward, symbolizing hope mingling with despair. The placement of brighter pixels occasionally challenging the darker grid pattern mirrors the tension between order and disorder, while the broken pixels introduce an imperfect rhythm, embodying the unpredictability of emotions like panic and control.
