Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional struggle of defiance versus submission through a dualistic color composition. The palette features deep navy and black, expressing weight and darkness, contrasted against luminous dim cyan and white, signifying light and openness. The central lines of lighter colors cut through darker hues, suggesting a burst of energy and raising tension, where light 'falls' into shadow. The mirror layout introduces intentional dissonance with slight pixel irregularities, symbolizing how both states can coexist yet remain unresolved.
