Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between restlessness and peace. The palette of sepia and faded blue portrays a contrast between memory and motion—sepia evoking nostalgia while faded blue represents serenity. The outer border is densely packed with dark tones, symbolizing restlessness, while the lighter inner pixels reflect peace, creating a visual collision. The repeated patterns of echo and fault line create tension and release, allowing the viewer to feel both the weight of the past and the lightness of present calm.
