Artist Statement

The emotional tone illustrates a dynamic tension between restlessness and peace, represented by the clashing patterns of 'hinge' and 'tide'. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold captures the theme of ancient fire, evoking warmth battling the coldness of dark spaces. The dense center, filled with warm hues, clashes with the cooler edges to symbolize the struggle of calmness within chaotic thoughts. The broken pixels create intentional disruptions, enhancing the feeling of unsettled restlessness, while the negative space permits a breath of tranquility within chaos.
