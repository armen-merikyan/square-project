Artist Statement

The composition explores the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress through a contrast of rich and muted colors. The chosen palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold suggests warmth and deep emotional resonance. The split horizontal layout emphasizes the dichotomy, creating a visual echo between the top and bottom halves. The patterns of frame and crosshatch symbolize a boundary and integration, resulting in a tension that hints at moving 'forward' while being grounded in memory. The broken pixels intersperse the structure, reflecting imperfections of both nostalgia and progress.
