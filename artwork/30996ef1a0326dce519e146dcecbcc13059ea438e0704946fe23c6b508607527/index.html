Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension of control versus surrender, using a palette that features warm coral and orange against cooler blues and greys. The placement creates a visual narrative of pressure released from the center while dense clusters dominate the periphery, embodying a struggle between containment and overflow. The main collision occurs at the center where controlled flame patterns clash with burst-like broken dots, representing the weight of choice and the pull of emotional release.
