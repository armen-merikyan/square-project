Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the duality of joy and grief, symbolized through contrasting colors and densities. The palette alternates between deep blues representing grief and warm yellows and oranges symbolizing joy, creating a dynamic emotional tension. The arrangement of colors reflects a wave-like rhythm, evoking an upward flow of hope amidst the undertow of loss. The thin outlines in certain areas create a subtle crosshatch, emphasizing moments of fragmentation while maintaining a sense of coherence. The overall composition delivers a visual narrative of rising from emptiness towards brightness, capturing the essence of human experience.
