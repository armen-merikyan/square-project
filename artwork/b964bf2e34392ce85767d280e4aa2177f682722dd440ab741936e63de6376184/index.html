Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between tenderness and rage through a juxtaposition of warm and cool tones. The selected palette of saturated red against muted pale blue and black suggests a vibrant clash—a hot center encaged by cooler shades, representing the protective nature of tenderness amidst the aggressive bursts of rage. The main contrast originates in the center, where warmth collides with the coolness of surrounding colors, emulating a spiral pressure dynamic that moves from a fragile core outward. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, adding to the narrative of guilt and forgiveness as it echoes throughout the composition. Overall, this piece embodies a delicate balance of emotions that bleed into one another, capturing the essence of the seed text without literal representation.
