Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between 'longing' and 'resolve.' The palette of soft pink, warm white, and stark black contrasts provide a visual dialogue between delicate yearning and firm boundaries. The radial pattern of longing collides with the sharp fault lines of resolve, creating a dynamic interplay across the grid. The broken pixels serve as deliberate disruptions that symbolize repair amidst damage, enhancing the sense of urgency and serenity. The main collision occurs centrally, drawing the viewer’s eye while maintaining a field of negative space that embodies quiet reflection.
