Artist Statement

The palette reflects a contrast of clarity and confusion, with clean whites and ice blues symbolizing defiance against the darker ink black and violet gray representing submission. The grid pattern in the top rows collides with the tide pattern below, creating emotional tension. The center evokes a pulse of defiance, while the surrounding pixels smoothly recede, embodying submission. This juxtaposition and the intentional broken pixels suggest a struggle between two emotional states, leaving open space as silent commentary on unresolved feelings.
