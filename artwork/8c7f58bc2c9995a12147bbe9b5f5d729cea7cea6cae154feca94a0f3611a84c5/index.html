Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dance between joy and grief. The palette combines bright yellows and oranges representing joy, juxtaposed against deeper blues and muted purples conveying grief. The left-weighted layout illustrates an asymmetry that symbolizes the struggle between the two emotions, creating a collision that resonates at the center. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfection, echoing the theme of betrayal while maintaining at least 10% negative space for a sense of breathability amidst tension.
