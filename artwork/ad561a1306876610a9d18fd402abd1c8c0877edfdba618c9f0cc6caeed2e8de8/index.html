Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between restlessness and peace, represented by a palette that combines deep teal as the quiet field and cosmic indigo as a contrasting pulse. The layout reflects a mirrored structure that introduces slight disruptions, echoing the theme of memory against motion. The main collision occurs in the center, where the pattern types of 'dots' for peace and 'tide' for restlessness intersect, introducing dynamic tension through contrasting shapes and colors. The broken pixels create intentional disruptions, suggesting an unresolved state, while leaving enough negative space for clarity amidst chaos.
