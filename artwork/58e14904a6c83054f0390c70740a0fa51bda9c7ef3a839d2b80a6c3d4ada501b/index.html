Artist Statement

This composition explores the duality of fear and hope, represented through dark and light palettes. The jagged, broken patterns symbolize fear while the structured grid reflects hope, creating a tense interplay. The glowing edges of the lighter colors contrast sharply against the dark base, suggesting an emergent potential amidst pressure. The seed 'flame' inspires a dynamic tension, evident in the collision of dark and light, guiding the viewer's eye towards a journey from the shadowed edges toward a central light, embodying a sense of upward flow and resonance.
