Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the struggle between loneliness and connection, manifesting as a dense concentration of dark colors that suggests weight, contrasted with softer hues symbolizing hope and connection. The palette combines deep blacks and dark violets representing loneliness with touches of soft gold and pale green that feel vibrant against the weight, creating an echo of connection amidst the void. The layout features descending pixels to emphasize a rain-like flow from the top, representing tears or falling ash, evoking the weight of broken memories. The dominant left-weighted design introduces imbalance, capturing the feeling of corner invasion and the tension between fullness and hollowness. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, reminding us that connections may be fragile and scattered, enhancing the story of lonely echoes in a world filled with noise.
