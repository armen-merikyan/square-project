Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between clarity (signal) and confusion (noise) through color placement. The palette balances vibrant colors like signal green and hot magenta against deeper tones of dead black and static gray, enhancing the tension in the artwork. Main contrast occurs vertically, where the left signals order and clarity, while the right embodies chaotic interference. This is visually represented through stripes versus cracks, creating a dynamic interaction in the layout.
