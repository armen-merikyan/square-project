Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the stark contrast of 'spark'—a sudden brightness depicted by vibrant colors—and 'ash'—the muted grays symbolizing residue. The palette of mourning_black and pale_yellow creates a tension that captures the 'hunger_vs_restraint' theme, with predominant dark tones representing restraint and lighter accents indicating fleeting sparks of vitality. The main collision happens diagonally, where flame-like patterning intersects with channeling forms, echoing a visual struggle between these emotions. Broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, embodying the fragility and resilience described in the seed.
