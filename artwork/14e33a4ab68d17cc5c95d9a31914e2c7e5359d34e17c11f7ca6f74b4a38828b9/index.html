Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between clarity and distortion. The palette of navy, black, and dim cyan evokes solitude and depth, while the single white adds punctuated clarity—representing signals striving through noise. The primary collision occurs centrally, where bright accents juxtapose against darker clusters, creating an echoing effect of pulsating rhythm against static interference. The lattice patterns serve to emphasize both the signal and noise, conveying an intricately layered, yet chaotic emotional landscape.
