Artist Statement

The artwork juxtaposes the tension between fear and hope, utilizing the dark palette to convey fear while integrating softer hues for a sense of hope. The piece features an empty center with densely populated corners to emphasize isolation, with broken pixel elements that disrupt the flow and create an imperfection echoing emotional turmoil. Contrasts occur between jagged edges (fear) and smoother transitions (hope), with a zigzag pattern suggesting conflict and duality. The arrangement invites the viewer to contemplate the delicate balance between these feelings.
