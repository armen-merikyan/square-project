Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional duality of rage and calm. The palette intertwines aggressive reds with soothing pale blues, creating a striking contrast that emphasizes the collision of these feelings. The top of the grid is densely packed with deep reds, suggesting an eruption of rage, which gradually transitions into bursts of tranquil white and pale blue, evoking calmness. This arrangement mirrors the 'falling_pixels' layout, where warm colors dominate and are interrupted by cool, creating a visual tension that aligns with the concept of conflicting emotions expressed in the seed. The layout and color placements reflect an upward struggle amidst underlying calm, lending to a sense of unresolved motion.
