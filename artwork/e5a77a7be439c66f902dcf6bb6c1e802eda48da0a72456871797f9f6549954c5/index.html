Artist Statement

This composition evokes an emotional interplay between the feelings of panic and control. The chosen colors of deep blue and muted purple provide a contrasting base, representing stability and chaos. Small bursts of yellow and orange punctuate the composition, reflecting sparks of energy fighting against the weight of darker shades. The arrangement flows diagonally, creating a sense of dynamic tension, where vibrant colors collide with darker, heavier tones, suggesting both conflict and balance.
