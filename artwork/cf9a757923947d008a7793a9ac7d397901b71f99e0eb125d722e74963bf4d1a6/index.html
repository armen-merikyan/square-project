Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the tension between control and surrender. The use of deep, soft hues conveys a sense of pressure and confinement, while lighter accents symbolize moments of release. The palette selection, featuring black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green, creates a contrast that fosters a discussion of weight versus lightness. The center features an 'overfilled' group of soft gold pixels, creating a collision with the surrounding darker colors that emphasize feelings of suffocation yet hint at hope and potential escape. The 'last light' layout maintains both an active center and ample negative space, showcasing an intricate dance between density and calmness.
