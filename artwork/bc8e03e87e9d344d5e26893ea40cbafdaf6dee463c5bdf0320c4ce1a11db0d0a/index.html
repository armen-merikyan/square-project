Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a duality where love and betrayal intersect. The palette blends warm pinks and reds with cooler grays and dark maroon to signify affection battling uncertainty. The piece utilizes upward motion with crosshatch patterns to represent 'inside vs outside', with burnt edges indicating a sense of harm. Color placements create tension and harmony, where the clash of bright accents and muted fields represents unresolved emotional currents, echoing the complexity of relationships.
