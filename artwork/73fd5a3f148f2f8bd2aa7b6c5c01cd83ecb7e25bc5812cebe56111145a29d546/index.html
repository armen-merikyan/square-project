Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'confusion vs clarity' manifests through the jagged interplay of colors and patterns. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes nostalgia and fragmentation, mirroring the broken memory concept while maintaining a level of recognition amidst chaos. The main contrast occurs with the rich, deep tones of sepia clashing against lighter faded blue hues, representing clarity breaking through confusion. The flow of diagonal falling pixels creates a sense of movement, while the arrangement keeps some sections dense and others open, reflecting the struggle for control between orderly symmetry and chaotic breaks. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, allowing moments of disruption to peep through the seemingly orderly layout.
