Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between joy and grief, using a warm palette to evoke a sense of conflicting feelings. The deep blue anchors the piece, contrasting sharply with vibrant yellows and oranges that represent joy's explosive nature. The arrangement reflects a split-horizontal layout, embodying the coexisting forces—chaos above and order below, with a rhythmic zigzag flow. Patterns of dots suggest grief's heaviness, while the ascending stairs evoke joy's upward movement. Broken pixels at strategic points reinforce imperfection, adding depth to the overall narrative.
