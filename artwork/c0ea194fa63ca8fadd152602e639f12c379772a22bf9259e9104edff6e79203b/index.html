Artist Statement

This abstract artwork portrays the collision between dread and relief through a dynamic use of color that combines dark hues with warm accents. The palette embodies fear (black and dark violet) battling against the lightness of soft gold and pale green, creating a visual tension indicative of emotional conflict. The pattern reflects rising signals, where colors project upward but are disrupted by the 'broken edges' style, signifying a struggle and a release from pressure. The interplay of dense dark colors with punctuated lighter ones suggests an intricate emotional dance, filled with unresolved rhythms, echoing feelings of hope amidst heaviness.
