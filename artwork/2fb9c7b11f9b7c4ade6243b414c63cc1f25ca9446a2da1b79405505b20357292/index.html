Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a battle between quietude and overwhelming pressure, reflected in the contrasting palette. The dark navy and compressed red signify pressure and tension, while the off-white and faint peach represent quiet spaces. The palette facilitates a dynamic interplay where the left side symbolizes the dense weight of pressure, featuring sharp, jagged patterns, while the right side offers softer, more fluid transitions. The main collision occurs at the center, where the transition between a crowded texture and an open space illustrates the emotional struggle eloquently.
