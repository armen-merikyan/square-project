Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the conflict between clarity and confusion, embodied through a palette of red, pale blue, black, and white. The dense clusters of contrasting colors mimic confusion, while the strategic placement of lighter tones introduces clarity. The interplay between the two reveals a tension—dark edges contrasted with spaces of white, representing both thick outlines and hollow spaces. The arrangement encapsulates the motion of 'falling pixels', as some colors collide starkly at the center while others veer outward, creating a story of unresolved emotional rhythms, where the patterned dots intermingle with stripes, enhancing the duality theme.
