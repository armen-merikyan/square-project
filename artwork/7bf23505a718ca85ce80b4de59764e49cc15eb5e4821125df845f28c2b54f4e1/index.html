Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional journey of loneliness intersecting with connection, showcasing a stark contrast between dense, intimate clusters and vast negative spaces. The palette of blacks and soft golds represents the tension between despair and warmth, while the top-weighted layout amplifies this contrast. The center-seeded pattern emerges as an echoing grid, creating a sense of ancient history and modern fractures. The broken pixels contribute to the narrative of imperfection and the struggle between hidden and exposed emotions.
