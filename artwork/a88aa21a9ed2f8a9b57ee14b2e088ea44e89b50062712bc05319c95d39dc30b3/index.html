Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the tension between restlessness and peace, embodied through a palette that balances deep blue's calmness with mourning black's intensity. The positioning of colors creates a chaotic upward flow that collides with the tranquility represented in pale yellow, suggesting fragmentation within stability. The central diagonal pattern embodies both memory and motion, while broken pixels add an element of disruption that signifies unresolved emotions. This artwork emerges from the interplay of structure and chaos, reflecting the complexities of human experience.
