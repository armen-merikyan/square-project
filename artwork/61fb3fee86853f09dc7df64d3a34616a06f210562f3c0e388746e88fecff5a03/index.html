Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between envy and gratitude, captured through a palette of warm yellows and oranges juxtaposed against deep blues and muted purples. The layout suggests a rising signal, emblematic of hope and escape in the face of noise, while the jagged edges introduce a sense of brokenness that resonates with feelings of envy. The main contrast occurs between warm and cool tones, creating collisions that symbolize inner turmoil and resolution. This emotional dichotomy is represented through upward and downward motion, symbolizing the struggle between aspiration and heaviness.
