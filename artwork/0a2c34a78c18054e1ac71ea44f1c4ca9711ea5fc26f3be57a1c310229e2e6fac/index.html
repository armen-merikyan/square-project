Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a battle between clarity and chaos, represented by the contrasting patterns in the top and bottom halves. The chosen palette, consisting of vibrant greens and contrasting greys, reflects the sharp pulse fighting through static. The collision occurs through the intersection of bright accents and muted backgrounds, echoing the tension of ‘signal vs noise’ while the broken pixels add disturbances that evoke imperfection in the machine's rhythm. The abstract layout emphasizes the struggle for definition amid disorder, grounding the composition in emotional resonance.
