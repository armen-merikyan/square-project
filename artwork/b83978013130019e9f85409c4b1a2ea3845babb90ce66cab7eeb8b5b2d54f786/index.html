Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates emotional tension between envy and gratitude through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of red and pale blue depicts a struggle; red symbolizes pressure and intensity, while pale blue offers calmness and serenity. The arrangement creates a rhythmic push and pull, with dense clusters of dark colors emphasizing internal conflict, surrounded by quieter spaces that suggest release. The jagged edges further illustrate the chaotic interplay of emotions, while broken pixels introduce imperfection, reflecting the complexity of human feelings.
