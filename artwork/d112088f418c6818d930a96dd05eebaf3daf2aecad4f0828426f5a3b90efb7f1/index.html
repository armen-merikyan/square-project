Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, illustrated through a pale exposure at the center framed by deeper tones. The palette comprises soft aqua and grey to represent vulnerability, while alarm red and ink blue provide strength, creating a striking contrast. The upper half signifies vulnerability with stacks of softer colors, while the lower half contrasts with sharper, shattered edges representing strength. The focal collision occurs at the center, where the two emotional currents meet, emphasizing the conflict between open exposure and protective strength.
