Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects an ongoing struggle between restlessness and peace, represented through a chaotic interplay of colors. The palette includes warm ivory and soft gold to suggest gentle warmth, contrasted starkly with deep wine and ash gray which evoke tension and restlessness. The main collision occurs in the top left corner, where intense colors collide, symbolizing pressure and motion, while the lighter hues spread outward, embodying calmness. This chaotic distribution reflects the seed's complexity of memory and motion.
