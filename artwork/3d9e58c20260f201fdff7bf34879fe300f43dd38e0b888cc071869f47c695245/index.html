Artist Statement

The artwork is a visual representation of loneliness vs connection, with a heavy center that bursts outward, symbolizing connection attempting to bridge the confines of loneliness. The palette of burnt orange and dark red channels the warmth of ancient fire, which stands against the coolness of charcoal, creating tension. This collision manifests in the center, where colors intersect, while edges retreat into muted tones, suggesting a struggle between exposure and concealment. The pattern oscillates between stripes of loneliness and bursts that represent connection, creating a rhythm of emotional push and pull.
