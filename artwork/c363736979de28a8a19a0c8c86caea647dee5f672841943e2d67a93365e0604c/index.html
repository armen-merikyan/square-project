Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a juxtaposition of joy and grief, represented through a palette of bright yellows and muted blues. The upper four rows evoke joy through a checker pattern, featuring vibrant yellow and orange colors, suggesting lightness and expansion. In contrast, the lower rows illustrate grief with cracked patterns using deep blue and muted purple, conveying heaviness and depth. The contrast between the horizontal divisions emphasizes the emotional struggle between order and chaos, whereby the bright colors rise against the somber tones, while the layout allows for moments of negative space, reflecting silence amidst chaos. The placement of colors suggests a bridge between tensions, creating an abstract representation of emotional collision.
