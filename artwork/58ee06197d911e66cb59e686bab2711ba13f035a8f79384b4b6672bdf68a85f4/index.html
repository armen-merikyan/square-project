Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a deliberate play of colors and patterns. The palette of deep black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green reflects a shift between stark contrasts, suggesting moments of introspection and clarity against a backdrop of chaos. The diagonal flow emphasizes the journey from the corner, where soft gold emanates a sense of warmth, toward the darker chaos at the edges, encapsulating the concept of crossing currents and fault lines. Broken pixels are integrated to evoke scars, creating an imperfection that reflects the complexity of emotional states. The overall composition balances density with quiet spaces, inviting the viewer to ponder the interplay between hope and fear.
