Artist Statement

The emotional tones of control and surrender create a dynamic tension within the composition. The palette reflects a juxtaposition of ripe greens and honey golds against muted creams, symbolizing growth and warmth while maintaining an underlying subtlety. The dominant color is a deep olive, representing control that anchors the piece, while patches of softer colors yield to suggest moments of surrender. The main collision occurs centrally, where darker dots embody control amidst lighter patchwork patterns that explore the theme of yielding. The arrangement aims to evoke contemplation, encouraging the viewer to navigate feelings of pressure versus release.
