Artist Statement

This composition conveys a dynamic emotional landscape characterized by defiance and submission, expressed through contrasting shades of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue. The palette evokes memories that balance warmth and coolness, creating an unsettling tension. The central area features a stark contrast amid surrounding colors, suggesting pressure and release. The diagonal flow emphasizes collision, while the grid and crosshatch patterns illustrate the struggle between rigidity and fluidity, ultimately culminating in a palpable emotional pulse.
