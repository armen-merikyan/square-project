Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between trust and suspicion, where warm colors symbolize trust (burnt orange and gold) clash with deeper shadows of suspicion (charcoal and dark red). The pattern of halos representing trust softly intersects with the stepwell pattern of suspicion, creating a visual dialogue of overlap and interruption. The main contrast occurs along the horizontal lines, mirroring the division and potential for connection, guiding the eye through layers of complexity and subtle gradients of feeling.
