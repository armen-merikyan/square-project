Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, utilizing a palette drawn from feelings of melancholy wonder. The contrasting textures of a lattice pattern for strength and a ripple pattern for vulnerability create an engaging dialogue, where the center represents exposed fragility surrounded by a dense, protective lattice. The main collision occurs in the central rows, where the colors clash both visually and emotionally, evoking the push and pull of these feelings. The layout intentionally divides the canvas, suggesting a threshold where safety and exposure collide, mirroring the dynamic interplay inherent in the seed text.
