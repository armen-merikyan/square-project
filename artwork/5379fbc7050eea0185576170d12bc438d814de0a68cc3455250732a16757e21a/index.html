Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the tension between longing and resolve, represented by a combination of warm burnt orange and deep dark red set against muted charcoal. The palette captures the duality of an energetic yet restrained atmosphere. The outer halo border formed by the darker colors symbolizes the boundaries of resolve while the inner chaotic arrangement and patterns reflect the unresolved nature of longing. The collision happens centrally where distinct patterns of 'noise' and 'stairs' intertwine, forming rhythm amidst structured chaos, reinforcing the concept of connection and distance.
