Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the fragility of vulnerability against the fortitude of strength. The palette balances warm reds and pinks with grounded dark maroon and gray, echoing despair and resilience. The main contrast occurs in the center, where a bright cluster of color represents the heart of conflict, surrounded by jagged edges and darker hues that symbolize pressure and disruption. This collision creates a narrative of unresolved tension, sparse areas reflecting vulnerability while nests of color suggest strength in their clusters.
