Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a sense of tension between longing and resolve, mirrored in the palette of muted warmth with stark contrasts. The selected colors create a narrative of a delicate balance between desire and clarity, with broken pixels symbolizing imperfect yet essential repairs. The palette includes soft off-whites and grays, representative of longing, interrupted by vibrant reds and deep navies, which establish boundaries and clarity. The main contrast occupies the center, where radial patterns of longing overlap with the strong, linear stripes of resolve, embodying the collision of emotions and suggesting an urgent pulse amidst serenity.
