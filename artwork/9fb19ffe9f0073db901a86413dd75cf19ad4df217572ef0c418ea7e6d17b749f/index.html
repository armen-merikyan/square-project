Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional interplay between longing and resolve. The palette incorporates ripe green and deep olive, invoking a sense of organic life alongside feelings of uncertainty, while the honey gold adds a spark of warmth and possibility. The central collision occurs in the fourth and fifth rows, where the deep olive and honey gold patterns collide, symbolizing the tension between reaching for something and staying grounded. The broken frame of colors suggest both imperfection and the journey toward resolution, encapsulating the essence of the seed text as an abstract narrative.
