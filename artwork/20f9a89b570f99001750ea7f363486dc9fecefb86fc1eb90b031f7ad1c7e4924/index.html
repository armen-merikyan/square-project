Artist Statement

This piece evokes a turbulent emotional landscape, embodying restlessness and peace. The palette, a balance of moss green, bone white, and rust, reflects nature's fragility and resilience. The darker elements evoke a sense of weight and pressure, while the lighter tones suggest fleeting moments of calm. The collision of dense clusters of rust and moss green mimics the duality, pulsing at the center, creating a flickering tension against the surrounding quieter spaces, echoing the themes of memory and motion from the seed.
