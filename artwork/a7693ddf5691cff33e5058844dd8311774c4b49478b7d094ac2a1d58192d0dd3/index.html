Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between loneliness and connection, depicted by contrasting areas of dense warmth (colors drawn from the earthy palette) against isolated cool sections. The palette, inspired by 'broken memory,' evokes a sense of nostalgia with sepia and faded blue, harmonizing the perceived warmth of connection against the chill of isolation. The collision predominantly occurs at the grid's edges, featuring a soft pixel bleed that symbolizes hidden layers yearning to break through, while a top-weighted asymmetry leans toward a central burst of color, representing vulnerability against exposure.
