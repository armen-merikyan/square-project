Artist Statement

The composition captures the interplay between control and surrender through dense, saturated centers contrasted by thinner edges. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a nostalgic, broken memory, grounding the chaotic center with muted tranquility. The central clash represents surrender, as it spills outward, while the jagged borders impose a sense of containment. Intentional disruptions—broken pixels—introduce imperfection, allowing the piece to breathe amidst the tension, illustrating the struggle between pressure and release.
