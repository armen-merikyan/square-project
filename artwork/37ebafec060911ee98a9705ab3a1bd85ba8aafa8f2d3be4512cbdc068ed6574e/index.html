Artist Statement

This abstract composition captures the clash between panic and control through jagged contrasts and organized chaos. The color palette of black and pale blue signifies the balance between rage and calm, with bursts of white representing moments of clarity against a backdrop of emotional turmoil. The arrangement of colors creates a dynamic rhythm that shifts from serenity to tension, suggesting a narrative of conflict and resolution. Notably, the use of broken pixels interrupts symmetry, creating a sense of unease while leaving a healthy negative space that embodies the unresolved feelings of joy and grief.
