Artist Statement

This abstract artwork explores the tension between control and surrender, represented through a carefully selected palette of muted greens and warm rust tones. The thicker outlines symbolize the feeling of control, while the softer inner segments depict a sense of yielding. The main collision occurs centrally, where the vibrant rust battles with the surrounding calm moss green, eliciting a pulse of emotional tension. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfection, enhancing the theme of struggle and compromise.
