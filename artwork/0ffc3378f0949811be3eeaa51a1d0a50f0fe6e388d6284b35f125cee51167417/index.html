Artist Statement

This piece explores the interplay between vulnerability and strength through contrasting color fields. The palette of soft powder pink and stark carbon black emphasizes emotional tension—softness versus severity. The main collision occurs at the center, where dense black patches encounter lighter, fragmented colors to represent the fragile boundaries between opposing forces. The broken pixels introduce disruptions, embodying the theme of imperfection and complexity in our emotional landscapes.
