Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a conflict between fear and hope, represented through contrasting dark and light colors. The palette incorporates deep blacks and dark violets to evoke fear, while soft gold and pale green introduce elements of hope and illumination. The layout is split vertically, with the outer frame embodying the dense pressure of fear, while the inner core processes hope's delicate glow. The collision occurs through haphazard stripes in the fearful section, juxtaposed with sharp 'teeth' patterns symbolizing hope's upward moment. This balance creates an abstract rhythm, embodying the tension of light meeting dark.
