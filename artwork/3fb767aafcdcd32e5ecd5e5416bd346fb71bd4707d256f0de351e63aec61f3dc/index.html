Artist Statement

The emotional tone is defined by the interplay between light and dark, suggesting both pressure and release. The palette features deep blues and vibrant contrasting yellows, reflecting collision and rhythm—where muted tones create a sense of quiet against urgent bursts of brightness. The main contrast occurs in the center, where the brightest colors collide with darker shades, creating visual tension that embodies emotional conflict. The seed inspired an abstract pattern of echoes, pressures, and rhythmic flows, transforming feelings into a geometric emotional landscape.
