Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the hot intensity of rage with the soothing calmness found in muted colors. The chosen palette of dark reds and pale blues reflect this emotional dynamic. A significant collision occurs in the top left corner, where vibrant reds clash against subdued blues and whites, evoking tension and movement. The structured zigzag pattern represents the chaotic signal of noise while the softer horizontal elements convey calmness, creating a striking visual story of emotional conflict and resolution.
