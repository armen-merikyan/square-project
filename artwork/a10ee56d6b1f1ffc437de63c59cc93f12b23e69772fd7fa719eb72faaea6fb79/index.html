Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dance between longing and resolve, with longing colored in soft pinks that evoke tenderness and vulnerability, while resolve commands dark maroons and grays representing strength and stability. The palette emphasizes emotional tension through warm vs. cool contrasts. The left side highlights patchwork longing, with broken pixels creating disruptions, while the right side showcases the firm teeth-like pattern reflecting resolve, with a concentrated collision point in the center suggesting tension. This layout encapsulates the push and pull of emotion, reflecting the essence of the seed text without representing a literal image.
