Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tumult of confusion battling against the desire for clarity. The chosen palette, featuring vibrant yellows and muted purples, represents the tension between light (clarity) and shadow (confusion). The radial pattern symbolizes the swirling chaos at the core, while the hard edges create a stark contrast that interrupts the flow. This collision manifests visually in the abrupt shifts in color density and the asymmetry introduced by the intentionally broken pixels, suggesting imperfections in our perception of order. The dual narrative of symmetry versus break is echoed in the arrangement, with dense clusters on one side and open spaces on the other, encapsulating the ongoing struggle for understanding amidst confusion.
