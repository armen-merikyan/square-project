Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece speaks to the juxtaposition of longing and resolve, manifesting through the tension of dense color arrangements clashing with quiet spaces. The palette 'ancient_fire' embodies longing through warm hues of burnt orange and gold, adding an underlying emotional warmth. Charcoal and dark red represent the steadfast nature of resolve. The central collision occurs at the heart of the grid, where bold colors meet softer edges, enhancing the feeling of distance and resonance, while the broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, underscoring the imperfect journey of transitioning between emotions. The diagonal flow guides the eye across the canvas, mirroring the title's theme of exploration and resolution in an abstract form.
