Artist Statement

This composition captures the delicate balance between vitality and decay, emphasizing the tension between the quick bursts of spark and the soft remains of ash. Utilizing a palette of off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach creates a muted yet poignant atmosphere, where red accents symbolize bursts of energy amidst the stark gray representing moderation and exhaustion. The central focus exhibits a radial clash of colors that suggests a struggle while the edges hold a thicker outline reflecting jaggedness and unresolved emotions. This composition resonates as a story of resilience against fragility, an ongoing conflict within the viewer’s psyche.
