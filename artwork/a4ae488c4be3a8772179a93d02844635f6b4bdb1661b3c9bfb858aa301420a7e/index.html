Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between dread and relief, represented through sharp contrasts of heavy darks and bright lights. The palette of black and pale blue embodies a clash between weight and calm, with white pixels creating moments of relief amidst the tension. The arrangement features dense clusters at the top, suggesting a weighted atmosphere, which gradually breaks down towards the bottom, introducing more negative space to imply release. The broken edges and jagged transitions between colors symbolize the cracks of emotion, embodying the tension of symmetry versus break.
