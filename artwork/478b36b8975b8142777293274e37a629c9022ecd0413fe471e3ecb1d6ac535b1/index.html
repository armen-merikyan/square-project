Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captures the simultaneous feelings of longing and resolve. The chosen palette 'melancholy_wonder' includes muted hues that evoke a sense of depth and instability while providing moments of calm. The primary collision occurs with the dense center represented by the bold tones of rain blue and muted plum, juxtaposed against the softer lavender gray and moon white fields that contain the intensity. This arrangement embodies the tension of yearning versus determination, where the predominant longing is illustrated in the wider fields while resolve is illustrated through the concentrated center. The broken pixels act as the scars of a journey, encapsulating the theme of repair versus damage, suggesting fragmentation within unity.
