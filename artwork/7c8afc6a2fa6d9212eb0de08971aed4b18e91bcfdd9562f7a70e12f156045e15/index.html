Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between defiance and submission, using contrasting colors and dense pixel arrangements to reflect emotional struggle. The chosen palette—comprised of ripe green for defiance and creamy tones for submission—presents a battle of vibrancy against quietude. The main collision occurs centrally, where darker hues interrupt lighter fields, representing the push and pull between order and chaos. The irregular edges create a visual 'keyhole' effect, inviting viewers to perceive fleeting glimpses of stability while acknowledging the underlying turmoil.
