Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes vulnerability and strength, reflected in the use of a muted palette of sepia tones and a soft faded blue. The strong center, designed with deeper contrast, symbolizes strength, while the surrounding lighter shades represent vulnerability, creating a sense of calm uncertainty. The pattern features radial interruptions from the center, suggesting moments of exposure amidst protective layers. This arrangement creates a visual collision where the bold colors meet the tender hues, embodying the dynamic push and pull of human emotion.
