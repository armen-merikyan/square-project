Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional contrast of longing and resolve, presented through a palette of ripe green and warm honey gold, symbolizing hope and calm against chaotic undercurrents. The central diagonal whisper of dark greens represents the clash of order and chaos, interrupted by scattered lighter pixels representing moments of clarity amidst a dense background of deep olive and cream. The main collision occurs through the contrasting shades that evoke intimate distances within an empty environment, suggesting both solitude and unity.
