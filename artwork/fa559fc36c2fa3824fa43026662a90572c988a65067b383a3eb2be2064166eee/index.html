Artist Statement

This composition evokes a dynamic emotional interplay between the concepts of control and surrender. The palette utilizes deep blacks to symbolize structure and restraint, countered by pale blues representing calmness and an overwhelming flow. The main contrast occurs at the center, where saturated blocks collide with the surrounding muted tones, creating a visual tension that mirrors the emotional struggle of the seed. The arrangement reflects the notion of an island of pressure against a vast, yielding sea of color, emphasizing the push and pull of emotional states. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, underscoring the imperfection inherent in these feelings.
