Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between spark and ash, embodying resilience and fragility. The palette oscillates between deep navy representing ash's weight and light dim cyan as a counterpoint, a 'spark' of hope. The collision occurs in the center, where dense dark pixels meet scattered lighter accents, suggesting a struggle between preservation and erasure. This contrast drives the narrative, where calmness breaks into volatility, reflecting the nuanced emotional journey captured by the seed.
