Artist Statement

This abstract composition embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission through contrasting colors and patterns. The selected palette, featuring shades of pink and red against muted grays and dark maroon, evokes passionate struggle. Defiance emerges through echoing patterns in the center, while submission unfolds in softer, grid-like structures at the edges, creating a visual push and pull. The spiral layout enhances a sense of movement and pressure directed towards the center, where the most intense colors collide, symbolizing the heart of the conflict.
