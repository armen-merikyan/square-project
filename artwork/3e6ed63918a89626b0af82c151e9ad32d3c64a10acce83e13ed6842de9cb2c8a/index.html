Artist Statement

This abstract piece captures the emotional tug-of-war between restlessness and peace, employing a palette that suggests intimacy and distance. The chosen colors—warm clay, cool blue, and warm shadow—contrast and collide, echoing the tension of memory against motion. The layout 'falling_pixels' implies a descent—a sense of surrender to the restless energy while leaving pockets of calm throughout the composition. The central collision occurs diagonally, a band of contrasting colors that represents a wave of motion against a backdrop of quiet aspects, maintaining the balance between disruption and serenity.
