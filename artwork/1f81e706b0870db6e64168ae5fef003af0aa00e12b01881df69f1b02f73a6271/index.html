Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clear signals and overwhelming noise, with a palette that juxtaposes dark navy and black against bursts of dim cyan and single white. The dense lower half symbolizes the chaos of noise, while the bright cluster in the center represents the surviving pulse. The main contrast happens at the center where ordered structure forms amidst chaotic backgrounds, resulting in an abstract narrative of attempt and survival.
