Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between panic and control. The palette of dark navy, deep blacks, and subtle cyan conveys a sense of loneliness and turmoil, highlighting the contrast between the chaotic edges and the structured center. The zigzag flow of contrasting colors creates a visual disruption, emphasizing the argument between control and chaos. Imperfections are introduced through broken pixels, enhancing the raw emotional experience, while the layout invites the viewer’s gaze upward, suggesting a fragile hope amidst the distress.
