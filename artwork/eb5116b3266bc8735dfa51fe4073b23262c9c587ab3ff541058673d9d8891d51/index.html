Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The color palette, encompassing shades of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon, symbolizes love entwined with betrayal. The contrast between the muted gray and the vibrant red represents the struggle between despair and determination. The grid-like structure created by the pixels conveys a sense of separation and connection, while broken pixels disrupt this orderly pattern, suggesting scars of past experiences. The collision of pressure bands and flowing teeth-like patterns reflects the ebb and flow of emotional states, allowing viewers to feel both the serenity of its quiet fields and the urgency that lies beneath.
