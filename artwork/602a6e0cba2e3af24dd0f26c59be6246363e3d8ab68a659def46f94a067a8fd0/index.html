Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects a juxtaposition of rage and calm, embodied by a stark contrast between vibrant reds and darker, cooler blues. The palette of red and pale blue resonates with the themes of explosive passion versus tranquil restraint. The layout is split vertically to reinforce this dichotomy, with chaotic 'broken dots' patterns in the left half symbolizing rage, while cooler 'radial' patterns on the right embody calmness. The main collision occurs at the center, where the hot hues encroach upon the cool space, suggesting tension and unresolved energy. Broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, mirroring the turmoil of emotions and imparting a sense of imperfection passing through rigidity. This composition speaks to the balance of opposing forces within the psyche, a visual echo of internal conflict and harmony.
