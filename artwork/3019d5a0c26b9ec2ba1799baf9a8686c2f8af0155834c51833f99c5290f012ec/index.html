Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette includes muted sepia and faded blue hues, creating a sense of nostalgia and a distant memory. The soft, broken_dots pattern signifying loneliness disrupts the checker pattern of connection, resulting in a dynamic tension across the canvas. The main contrast occurs on the edges, where denser color intersections represent emotional collisions. The placement of broken pixels creates intentional imperfections, evoking themes of both hidden feelings and the exposure of inner thoughts, reflected in the top-weighted layout which draws the eye upward, suggesting a desire for connection from moments of solitude.
