Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between panic (represented by the jagged disruptions of brighter colors) and control (illustrated through the calm, dark frames). The palette of lime, black, electric blue, and magenta provides a stark contrast; the bright lime sparks against the deep black, creating a dynamic environment of conflict. The outer frame represents the calm order, while the inner chaos flickers with unexpected bursts of color, suggesting unresolved feelings and the jagged edges of both emotional states. The layout, where dark colors frame an energetic core, captures the essence of structured chaos, echoing the seed's themes of tension and imbalance.
