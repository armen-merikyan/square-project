Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts control with surrender, utilizing a palette that reflects warmth and coolness. 'Warm coral' and 'soft amber' evince controlled emotion while 'cold slate' and 'blue black' represent surrender. The central collision occurs with a dense cluster of colors in the middle, signifying pressure, surrounded by calming edges that suggest release. This interplay embodies the tension of choice, where the signal dot is illustrated through overlapping patterns that collide yet can blend.
