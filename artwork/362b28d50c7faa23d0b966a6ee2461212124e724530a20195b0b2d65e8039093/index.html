Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the weight of pressure and the calm of quiet. The palette of muted tones (off_white, dust_gray, faint_peach) introduces a subtler interference which reflects 'quiet,' while deeper hues (compressed_red, dark_navy) create the confines of pressure. The central collision transpires diagonally, where heaviness meets gentler notes, suggesting a shattering of tension. This interplay connects to the seed’s themes, balancing hard edges with soft veils and allowing negative space as a metaphor for clarity amidst confusion.
