Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the duality of dread and relief, using warm and cool tones to create a striking contrast. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue reflects a state of broken memory. The dominant dark colors suggest weight and pressure, while brighter tones offer glimpses of relief amidst chaos. This tension is illustrated by a central presence surrounded by dense patterns, drawing the viewer into a rhythmic flow that alternates between calm and sudden pressure. The overall composition mixes structured and chaotic elements, allowing for a lingering sense of unresolved emotions.
