Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between love and betrayal. The palette of warm pinks and reds contrasts with dark maroon and gray, symbolizing passion intertwined with betrayal. The pattern resonates with a rising diagonal flow, showcasing hope amidst conflict. The collision of soft noise against harsher stripes embodies the split emotions, while selectively placed broken pixels create intentional imperfection, reflecting fracture in symmetry. The arrangement balances negative space against densely clustered colors, conveying a story of complex feelings.
