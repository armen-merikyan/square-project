Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the notions of safety and exposure, with a focus on delicate contrasts. The chosen palette features soft greens and warm browns representing safety, while cooler whites and blues symbolize exposure. The arrangement places cool colors on the left, subtly cascading towards warmer tones on the right, creating a visual 'rising signal' effect, illustrating the tension between safety and exposure. The central area remains lighter, suggesting an open space amidst the heavier borders, crafted with broken pixels to signify imperfect protection against the noise of exposure.
