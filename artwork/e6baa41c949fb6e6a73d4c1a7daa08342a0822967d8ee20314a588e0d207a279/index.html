Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a struggle between vitality (spark) and desolation (ash). The palette comprises vibrant shades to represent spark—specifically signal green and electric blue—contrasted with muted tones like static gray and dead black to signify ash. The main collision occurs diagonally across the grid, where vibrant colors clash with dark remnants, embodying the tension between life and loss. The jagged edges and broken pixels disrupt the flow, symbolizing memories of what is lost and the resilience in moving forward, creating a palpable tension.
