Artist Statement

This work represents the emotional tension between tenderness and rage, using a palette of saturated red and soft pale blue to illustrate the collision of feelings. The outer border, featuring a continuous ladder pattern in red, embodies the explosive energy of rage, while the central 4x4 area, filled with muted tones and cracked textures, symbolizes the gentle overlaps of tenderness. The main contrast occurs at the edges, where the unequivocal reds meet the soft blues, illustrating the conflict between chaos and calm. This arrangement reflects the duality in the seed's narrative, emphasizing both emotional discharge and quiet introspection.
