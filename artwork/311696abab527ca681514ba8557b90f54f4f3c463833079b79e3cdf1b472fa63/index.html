Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers around the conflict between vulnerability and strength, encapsulated in a palette of hot coral, blood orange, dust pink, charcoal, and hollow blue. The center is saturated with warm tones, representing strength and confidence, while cooler and darker hues at the edges signify vulnerability and exposure. The main collision occurs at the center where vibrant colors intermingle, creating a chaotic yet harmonious energy, reflecting the interplay between protection and openness as described by the seed.
