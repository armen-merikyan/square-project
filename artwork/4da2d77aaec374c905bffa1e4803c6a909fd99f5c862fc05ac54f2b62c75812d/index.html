Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the brightness of joy, represented by vibrant yellows and oranges, against the somber hues of grief, captured in muted blues and purples. The palette's contrast signifies a dynamic battle, evoking feelings of both warmth and melancholy. The pattern employs a split design, creating a horizontal divide that symbolizes order against chaos, while edges are softened to imply a gentle flow. The imperfect 'broken' pixel placement introduces unexpected moments, echoing the complexity of hidden versus exposed emotions, with clusters of lighter colors punctuating darker areas, suggesting bursts of hope amidst sorrow.
