Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a contrast between panic and control, embodying a struggle of movement and restraint. The palette of yellow and muted purple illustrates joy and grief, with vibrant yellow sparking against the somber backdrop of muted hues. The upper half is layered with radial patterns in warmer colors, suggesting dynamic chaos, while the lower half employs a controlled ring structure, creating stability. The edges echo a sense of burned disruption, inviting subtle imperfection into symmetry, representing the paradox of tension in balance.
