Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional struggle between vibrant life (spark) and quiet, fading remnants (ash). The palette combines warm, earthy tones with stark contrasts to evoke a sense of resilience amid fragility. The diagonal layout promotes movement from the corner, where warmth and brightness clash with muted grays and soft whites, illustrating a dynamic interplay. Broken pixels add intentional imperfection, emphasizing the theme of memory alongside erasure. The overall rhythm flows from the top left, creating a compelling echo effect across the grid.
