Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between clarity and confusion through a palette that juxtaposes darkness with sharp highlights. The navy and black create an enveloping space representing confusion, while the dim cyan serves as moments of clarity. The choice of a ring pattern amidst the chaos suggests continuity against distortion, with the thick outline providing a border that invites the viewer's gaze inward, constructing a visual pulse. Broken pixels create an element of imperfection, enhancing the emotional dissonance of the composition, while the diagonal flow mimics a swirling movement that embodies both rage and calm.
