Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a battle between signal and noise, employing a palette that includes muted blacks and greys to symbolize interference, highlighted by vibrant accents of green and magenta that represent the clear, readable pulse. The main collision occurs in the center, where the green rhythm momentarily disrupts the surrounding static. The jagged edges echo the eroded symmetry theme, while broken pixels add a sense of imperfection, simulating interruptions of memory and noise. This abstract arrangement embodies the tension between chaos and clarity, creating a visual rhythm that invites exploration of the dynamics within the emotional landscape.
