Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between confusion and clarity through a rich palette derived from 'ancient_fire'. The choice of dark reds and burnt oranges creates an emotional weight, symbolizing chaos and rage, while gold adds a touch of clarity and warmth. The layout suggests a split emotional reality, with the lower half featuring a dense crosshatch pattern signifying confusion, clashing against vast negative space above that evokes clarity. Broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, representing the intrusions of chaos amidst an otherwise structured environment. The diagonal flow hints at movement from confusion to potential clarity, yet the asymmetry ensures unresolved tension remains.
