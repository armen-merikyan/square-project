Artist Statement

The artwork captures the tension between loneliness and connection through a stark division in color and arrangement. The left half embodies loneliness with solitary, muted tones reflecting isolation, while the right embraces connection through warmer, interwoven colors that suggest togetherness. The chosen palette of deep reds and pale blues illustrates a vivid emotional clash, highlighting the central contrast where the two emotional states collide. Jagged transitions between the colors evoke a feeling of disturbance, representing broken memories and interrupted connections. The inclusion of broken pixels adds imperfection, enhancing the theme of memory's fragility. This layout creates asymmetry, with the weight of the left heavier and the right lighter, conveying the complexity of emotional experiences.
