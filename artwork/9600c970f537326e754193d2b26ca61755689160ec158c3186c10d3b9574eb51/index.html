Artist Statement

This piece navigates the emotional terrain between awe and terror, employing a palette of muted yet warm colors to evoke both comfort and unease. The top half illustrates the awe with warm pinks and soft red transitions, mimicking a gentle upward wave, while the lower half features unsettling dark maroon tones that crack and fragment, representing chaos and terror. Contrasts between the smoother top and the harsher textures below create a sense of collision, where both emotional states coexist in tension. The choice of broken pixels adds an element of imperfection, reinforcing the theme of fractured experiences. Overall, the arrangement suggests a struggle between stability and instability, mirroring the thematic implications of the seed.
