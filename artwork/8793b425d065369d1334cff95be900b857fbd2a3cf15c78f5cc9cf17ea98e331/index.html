Artist Statement

This composition evokes the tension between opposing forces—defiance and submission—by utilizing a palette that juxtaposes saturated, vibrant greens with muted earth tones. The angular placement of brighter colors suggests defiance, while the more subdued colors form a stable base below, representing submission. The broken pixels disrupt the smooth flow, creating echoes of interruption within an otherwise structured layout. The grid’s descending motif enhances the sense of inevitable pressure while still allowing for negative space, embodying both quiet and intensity in an abstract expression.
