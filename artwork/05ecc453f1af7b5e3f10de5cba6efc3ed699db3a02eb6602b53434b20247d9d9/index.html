Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between vulnerability and strength, illustrated through a palette of signal green and electric blue as vibrant accents, contrasted against static gray and dead black, which suggest heaviness and distance. The main collision occurs in the center with a dense cluster of deep colors, representing the struggle between the protective nature of strength and the exposure of vulnerability. This abstract arrangement weaves strong edges with frail interiors, mimicking the tensions and layers of the seed's themes.
