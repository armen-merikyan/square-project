Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between restlessness and peace through a palette of muted sepia and faded blue. The intersection of the radial pattern representing peace combines with the chaotic crosshatch of restlessness, creating a dynamic visual narrative. The main collision occurs at the center, where the peace spiral is interrupted by restless strokes, symbolizing a moment of hesitation within serenity.
