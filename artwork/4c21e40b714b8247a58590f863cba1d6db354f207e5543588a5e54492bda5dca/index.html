Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through a dynamic use of color and structure. The palette, featuring deep navy and dim cyan, reflects a sense of isolation and subtle motion, while the inclusion of black introduces moments of stark contrast. The layout evokes falling pixels, creating a sense of descent and release. Colors collide and echo against a symmetrical backdrop, suggesting a broken yet coherent space where clarity momentarily emerges through the chaos, illustrating the struggle between order and disruption.
