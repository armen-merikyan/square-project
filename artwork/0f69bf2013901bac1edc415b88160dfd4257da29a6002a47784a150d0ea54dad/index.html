Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through contrasting color placements. The choice of a dark palette with shades of navy and black evokes depth and uncertainty, while subtle hints of dim cyan introduce moments of clarity breaking through. The crowding of darker pixels in the lower half represents confusion, while the light tones in the upper half suggest emergent clarity. Broken pixels throughout disrupt the patterns to signify the 'falling pixels' motif, while the jagged edges create a sense of tension and interruption, reflecting the emotional conflict within the seed. The layout reflects an argument between symmetrical and broken patterns, lending a rhythmic flow as colors pulse against one another.
