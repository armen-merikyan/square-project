Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through contrasting patterns and colors. The warm yellows and oranges suggest a hopeful clarity amidst the deep blues and muted purples representing confusion. The placement of brighter, denser colors in the left half introduces a sense of pressure, while the right side follows a cleaner gradient, echoing the theme of symmetry vs. break. The broken pixels scattered throughout disrupt the flow, evoking the 'falling pixels' motif, creating a sense of imperfection and dynamic tension.
