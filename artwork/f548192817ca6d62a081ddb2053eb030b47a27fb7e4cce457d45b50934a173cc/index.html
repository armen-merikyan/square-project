Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tone of control versus surrender, utilizing a palette that evokes quiet pressure: soft off-white and faint peach juxtaposed with dark navy and compressed red. The collision occurs at the center, where the stable, stepwell pattern of control meets the glitching, disrupted surrender. The choice of colors highlights the contrast between contained pressure and the release from it, reinforced by the double outline replicating the stability of control alongside the chaotic flow of surrender.
