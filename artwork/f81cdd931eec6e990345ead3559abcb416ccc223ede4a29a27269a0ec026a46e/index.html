Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between vulnerability and strength using a palette that balances warm and cool tones. The collision occurs centrally, where the depth of charcoal contrasts with the warm vibrancy of blood orange. The structure represents the emotional strata, with dense, broken frames of strength wrapping around the pale, airy patchwork symbolizing vulnerability. This arrangement echoes the themes of exposure and shielding while allowing for the flow of energy between the two states.
