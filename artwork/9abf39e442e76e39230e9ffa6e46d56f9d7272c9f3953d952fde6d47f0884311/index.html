Artist Statement

The emotional tone is centered on the duality of trust and suspicion, mirrored in a palette of soft pinks versus stark blacks and grays. The pattern captures the pulse of trust with gentle curves, contrasting sharply with the jagged edges of suspicion. The main collision occurs in the center, creating tension between vibrant accents and muted backdrops, reflecting the constant interplay of these emotions within the structure of the 8x8 grid. This composition symbolizes how trust seeks to illuminate amidst pervasive shadows of doubt.
