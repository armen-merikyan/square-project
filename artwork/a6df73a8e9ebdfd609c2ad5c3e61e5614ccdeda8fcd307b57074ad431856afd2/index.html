Artist Statement

This composition illustrates the tension between pressure and quietude, reflecting the seed's emotional contrast. The selected palette utilizes muted tones of off-white and dust gray to create a calm backdrop, punctuated by compressed red and dark navy to signify the pressing force. The structure features stripes representing pressure coexisting with fragmented target patterns that evoke a sense of quiet interruption. The main collision occurs diagonally across the canvas, highlighting the struggle between these forces. The use of broken pixels introduces an intentional imperfection, echoing the theme of conflict and resolution.
