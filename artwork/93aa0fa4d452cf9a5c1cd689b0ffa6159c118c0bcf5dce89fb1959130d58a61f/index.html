Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through its contrasting colors and patterns. The dark palette signifies isolation, with colors like black and dark violet representing the depths of loneliness. In contrast, soft gold and pale green add touches of connection and hope, hinting at the possibility of reaching out despite the surrounding darkness. The layout emphasizes a rising signal, mirroring the inner struggle of seeking connection. The use of hard edges creates a sense of looming pressure, while broken pixels inject a sense of imperfection, echoing the unpredictable nature of human connections. Overall, the artwork pulsates between the solitude of dark shades and the warmth of golden hues, representing an ongoing battle for connection amidst feelings of isolation.
