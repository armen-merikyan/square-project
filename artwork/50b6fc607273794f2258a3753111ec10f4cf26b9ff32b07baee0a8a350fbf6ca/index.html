Artist Statement

The emotional tone emerges from a delicate interplay between structured signal and chaotic noise. The palette features deep navy to signify the foundational calm, broken by scattered dim cyan and stark white accents reminiscent of erratic signals. The main contrast collides in the center, where dark shades create a stark backdrop for intermittent bright spots, embodying the tension between clarity and interference. This configuration reflects the seed's essence, translating the abstract concepts of pulse and disruption into a striking visual narrative.
