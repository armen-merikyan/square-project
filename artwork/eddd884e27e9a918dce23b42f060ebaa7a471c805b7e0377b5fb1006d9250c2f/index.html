Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the duality of shelter and exposure. The palette of greens, browns, and soft hues conveys warmth and safety, while the contrast of the cool blues and whites suggests an unprotected, open space. Main contrasts occur in the central pixels where the colors collide—this tension between safety and exposure is heightened by jagged edges that disrupt the calm flow. Pattern arrangements reflect a rising signal, suggesting hope and escape, while allowing the dominance of 'safety' to create a gentle enclosure amidst the interruption of the exposed colors.
