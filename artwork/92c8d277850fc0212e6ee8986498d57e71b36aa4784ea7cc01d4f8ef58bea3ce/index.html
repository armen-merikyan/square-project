Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dichotomy of envy and gratitude, manifested through a stark contrast between dark and light colors. The palette combines deep blacks with soft whites and pale blues, creating tension. The arrangement gives a sense of falling from dark heaviness at the top to lighter shades below, symbolizing a struggle and shifting perspectives. Broken pixels add imperfection, reflecting the ‘mirror_broken’ concept, while zigzag patterns suggest a dynamic interplay of emotions, capturing the essence of weight vs lightness.
