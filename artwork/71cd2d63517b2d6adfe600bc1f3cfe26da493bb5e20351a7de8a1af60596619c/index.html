Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between pressure and quietness. The palette employs muted shades like off-white and dust gray to evoke calmness, contrasting against the darker navy and compressed red, which suggest weight and intensity. The central collision occurs diagonally, where the soft radial pattern of quieter tones meets the more structured ladder pattern of dense colors, illustrating an interplay of order and chaos. This captures the essence of the seed's thematic conflict, showcasing breaks and interruptions in a balanced yet dynamic composition.
