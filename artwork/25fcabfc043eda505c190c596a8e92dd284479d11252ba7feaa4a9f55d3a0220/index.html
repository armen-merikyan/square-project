Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between restlessness and peace. The palette of warm ivory, deep wine, and soft gold symbolizes calm moments and warmth, contrasted by stain brown and ash gray, which represent unease and conflict. The diagonal collision of broken dots and islands creates an argument grid, showcasing a struggle for dominance in emotional tone. The piece features disrupted patterns and exposed spaces, encapsulating the complexities of memory and motion within a cohesive abstract narrative.
