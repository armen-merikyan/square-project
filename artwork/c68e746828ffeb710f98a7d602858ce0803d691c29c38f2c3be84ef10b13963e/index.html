Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between loneliness and connection, illustrated through a predominantly dark palette with bursts of vibrant colors, symbolizing moments of connection. The left-weighted layout disperses warmer tones from the corner, contrasting against cooler backgrounds to evoke a sense of invasion and disruption. The arrangement suggests a fragile balance between connection and solitude, with significant negative space representing emptiness, while broken pixel elements add a layer of imperfection, hinting at fractured memories.
