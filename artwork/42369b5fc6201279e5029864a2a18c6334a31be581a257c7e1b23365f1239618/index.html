Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between restlessness and peace. The palette, dominated by deep blues and muted yellows, invokes a dreamlike state where memory and motion collide. The outer edges feature a grid-like structure in darker tones representing restlessness, while the inner section gracefully radiates lighter, calming colors. This contrast is reinforced by the diagonal flow, creating a sense of movement across the piece. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, symbolizing life’s disturbances. Overall, the composition speaks to the struggle between chaos and serenity, encapsulated in the interplay of color and form.
