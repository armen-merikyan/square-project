Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between joy and grief through its dual color scheme of warm yellows and muted purples. The top half embodies joy with rounded, expanding elements, while the bottom conveys grief through fragmented patterns. The interplay of bright accents against darker hues creates a clash, echoing a split between order and chaos. The vertical flow suggests movement up toward hope while simultaneously descending into heaviness, reflecting the complexity of human emotion.
