Artist Statement

This composition evokes the emotional tone of 'control vs surrender' by contrasting structured grid-like areas with more fluid, loose arrangements. The palette of ripe and acid greens paired with warm honey gold hints at underlying tension, suggesting both growth and conflict. The main collision occurs in the center, where saturated colors disrupt the radial pattern, embodying overflowing pressure versus quiet release. This stuttering motion reflects the staccato, hesitant steps of yield and resistance, weaving a narrative of struggle and release through the pixel arrangement.
