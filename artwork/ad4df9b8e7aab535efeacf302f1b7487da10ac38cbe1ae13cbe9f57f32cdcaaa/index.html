Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between restlessness and peace, manifesting through contrasting colors and arrangements. The palette drawn from muted tones like sepia and dust gray evokes a sense of faded memories juxtaposed with brighter accents suggesting movement and unrest. The layout creates a diagonal split that amplifies the sense of collision, with softer colors on one side and more jarring contrasts on the other, making the viewer feel both the pull of peace and the push of motion. The arrangement captures the essence of memory and motion, creating a visual representation of their dynamic interplay.
