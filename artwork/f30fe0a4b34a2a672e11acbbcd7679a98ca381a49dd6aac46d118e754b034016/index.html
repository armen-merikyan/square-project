Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a stark contrast between loneliness and connection, utilizing a palette of lime, black, electric blue, and magenta to embody both vibrancy and depth. The central configuration represents the tension of being isolated yet yearning for connection, achieved through a ring pattern that surrounds a single focal point. The layout emphasizes negative space, showcasing vulnerability in the core while the outer layers suggest persistence and energy through densely packed color. The clash of warm and cool tones embodies the struggle between warmth of companionship and the chill of solitude, creating a dynamic juxtaposition throughout the artwork.
