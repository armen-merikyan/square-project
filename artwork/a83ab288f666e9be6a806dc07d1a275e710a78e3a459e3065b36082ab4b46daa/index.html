Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between peace and restlessness through color and pattern. The outer layer uses darker shades to represent peace, providing a stillness that is contrasted by the core's chaotic crosshatch pattern in brighter colors. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green creates a feeling of depth and warmth. The main collision occurs at the center where the restless pattern interrupts the calm, suggesting a struggle between memory and motion. This arrangement prompts a dynamic yet abstract narrative without forming identifiable shapes.
