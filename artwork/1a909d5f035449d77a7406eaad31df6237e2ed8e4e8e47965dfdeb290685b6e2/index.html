Artist Statement

The composition embodies the tension between longing and resolve, utilizing a palette of pinks, reds, and muted grays to express emotional depth. Saturated colors in one area clash with softer tones in another, manifesting the crosscurrent dynamics of desire versus determination. The broken frame layout invites fragmentation, enhancing the feeling of intimacy and distance, while an overall density in the lower half gives way to a spacious top, suggesting a diagonal fall. This aligns with the emotional seeds of conflict and resolution amidst chaos.
