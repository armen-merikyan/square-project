Artist Statement

This artwork explores the duality of confusion and clarity using a palette inspired by ancient fires, emphasizing warm tones like burnt orange and dark red set against a backdrop of charcoal and gold. The chaotic checker pattern embodies confusion, while the structured edges symbolize clarity. The left side embraces warmer colors, representing rage and pressure, while the right transitions to calmer tones, showcasing clarity and release. The arrangement of pixels intentionally disrupts symmetry, creating moments of interruption amidst a structured base, leading the viewer through an emotional journey from turmoil to peace.
