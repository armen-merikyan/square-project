Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through contrasting warmth and darkness, suggesting a struggle between isolation and the longing for interaction. The chosen palette of burnt orange and dark red evokes a sense of internal heat amidst the charcoal void, while the layout creates a mirror effect with intentional breaks, symbolizing disrupted connections. The central 'hole' shape signifies absence surrounded by faint noise, emphasizing broken memories and the quiet yet pervasive feeling of solitude.
