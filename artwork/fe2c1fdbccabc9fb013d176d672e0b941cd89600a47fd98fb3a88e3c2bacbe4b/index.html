Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the warmth of trust against the coldness of suspicion. The palette features warm ivory and soft gold as dominant colors representing trust, while deep wine and ash gray introduce the tension of suspicion. The layout showcases uneven reflection with high contrast in the overfilled corner, creating a visual disturbance against smoother transitions. The broken pixels serve to interrupt the flow and suggest fragility within the structure, embodying the clash of emotions from the seed, resulting in a dynamic composition that resonates with tension and release.
