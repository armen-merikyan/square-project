Artist Statement

This piece conveys the tension between awe and terror through a contrasting palette of warm yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples. The organization reflects a 'rising signal' layout, signaling hope and upward movement, yet is marred by broken pixels that evoke a sense of instability and disorder. The stripes symbolize awe while the scattered dots mimic the chaos of terror. The central crack motif introduces an imperfection that interrupts the flow, capturing the delicate balance of these emotions.
