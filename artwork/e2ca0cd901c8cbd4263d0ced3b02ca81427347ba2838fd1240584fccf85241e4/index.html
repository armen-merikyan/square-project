Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the tension between love and betrayal through a palette of warm pinks and reds contrasted with cool grays and dark maroon. The emotional tone flows between warmth and coldness, creating a pulse-like rhythm of conflict. The grid layout suggests an argument, while the patterns embody love's flame-like warmth battling against the cold, maze-like structure of betrayal. The broken pixels add a layer of imperfection, enhancing the narrative of tumultuous emotions, as the colors collide and intertwine to indicate the struggle of balancing love and betrayal.
