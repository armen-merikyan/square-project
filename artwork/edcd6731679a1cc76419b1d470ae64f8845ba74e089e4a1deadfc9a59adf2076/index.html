Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a blend of deep, grounding colors and vibrant accents. The palette includes rich greens and subtle creams, representing both the yearning for connection and the steadfastness of resolve. The main collision occurs in the center, where a halo pattern of longing radiates against the sharp confrontational spark pattern of resolve, creating a visual pulse that both attracts and repels. The use of broken pixels adds an element of disruption, symbolizing both repair and damage. Negative space allows the two emotional forces to breathe, mirroring the balance between serenity and urgency.
