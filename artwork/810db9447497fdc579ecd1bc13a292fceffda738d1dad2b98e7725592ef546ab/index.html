Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic interplay between strength and vulnerability, using a palette of electric blue, lime, black, and magenta to convey contrasts of lightness and weight. The central area represents an exposed core symbolizing vulnerability, while surrounding layers represent strength in a braid-like pattern. The colors are intentionally placed to create a tension-filled collision, with the bright lime and magenta accenting against the deep black, reflective of the struggle between exposure and protection.
