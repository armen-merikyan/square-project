Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the contrast between quiet space and pressure through a palette of soft and dark colors. The quiet attributes are represented by off-white and faint peach, while pressure is suggested with compressed red and dark navy. The collision occurs diagonally, with a heavy concentration of darker colors at one edge, loosening into lighter hues, reflecting the interplay between tension and calm. The broken pixels in the art subtly interrupt the flow, symbolizing the fragmented nature of clarity amidst confusion.
