Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between vitality (spark) and the remnants of fading energy (ash). The palette draws on 'cosmic indigo' and 'void black' to elicit a sense of vastness and depth, while 'star white' and 'cold gold' punctuate this with flashes of intensity. The main contrast manifests in a jagged collision of dynamic diagonal patterns (spark) against muted frames (ash), creating an interplay of tension and release. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, symbolizing the scars of memory against the drive for continuity, emulating the complexity of resilience against fragility.
