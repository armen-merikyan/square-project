Artist Statement

This artwork expresses the emotional tension between pressure and quietude. The palette featuring 'off_white', 'dust_gray', 'compressed_red', 'dark_navy', and 'faint_peach' evokes a sense of contrast, with muted tones grounding the visual chaos. The center is dense with darker colors symbolizing pressure, while lighter hues radiate towards the borders, creating a visual flow and moments of quiet amidst the chaos. The arrangement reflects a shattering tension clashing against a calm tide, echoing the seed's themes of symmetry and break. The intentional 'broken pixels' introduce a layer of disruption, enhancing the tension and releasing energy outward for a dynamic composition.
