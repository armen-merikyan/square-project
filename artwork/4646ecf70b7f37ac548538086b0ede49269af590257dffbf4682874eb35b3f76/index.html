Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the delicate balance between vulnerability and strength, employing a palette that evokes warmth with 'rose_clay' and 'lamp_gold' contrasting against the coolness of 'cool_blue' and 'distant_lilac.' The main tension is at the center where the thin shells (dots) of vulnerability collide with the firm rings (strength) encircling them, representing an exposure within a protective barrier. The pattern suggests a gradual erosion of symmetry toward the edges, symbolizing the journey of vulnerability against rigid strength.
