Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts loneliness and connection through the use of deep shadows (black) and vibrant highlights (electric blue, magenta). The top rows embody isolation with a ring-like pattern in darker shades, while the bottom half bursts with radial patterns in brighter colors, suggesting connection. The collision occurs around the center where darkness meets light, creating tension and an echo of both emotions. The palette reflects a spectrum of feelings, enhancing the visual narrative of hidden versus exposed.
