Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the clash between panic and control, symbolized through the palette of deep blacks and soft golds which highlights the contrast. Black suggests depth and chaos, while soft gold adds a sense of subtle hope and control. The pattern emphasizes a radial structure that is interrupted by vertical stripes, echoing the tension within the center versus the edges, suggesting a movement from chaos at the center towards order at the edges. This juxtaposition creates moments of collision where color vibrancy contradicts darker zones, inviting the viewer to explore the emotional narrative of instability and balance.
