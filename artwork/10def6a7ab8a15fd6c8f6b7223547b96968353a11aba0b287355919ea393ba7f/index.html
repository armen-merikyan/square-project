Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the juxtaposition of vulnerability and strength, creating a delicate balance in the composition. The palette of soft pinks and warm whites contrasts sharply with deeper blacks and grays to evoke feelings of both exposure and fortitude. The main collision occurs in the center, where lighter hues, representing vulnerability, clash with the heavier, darker strokes symbolizing strength. This interplay creates tension, reminiscent of a fragile vessel struggling against the tide of an overwhelming force. The patterns of stairs (strength) and scars (vulnerability) intertwine, forming an 8x8 grid that reads as an abstract narrative of safety intertwined with exposure.
