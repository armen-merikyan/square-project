Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between panic and control through a juxtaposition of structured and chaotic elements. The palette features vibrant electric blue and lime, symbolizing energy and urgency, contrasted with deep black and magenta, representing disorder and introspection. The main contrast surfaces in the left half with tightly arranged control patterns, transitioning into scattered, broken forms on the right that suggest emotional distress. This zigzag flow from top to bottom visually narrates a clash between stability and chaos, echoing the emotional complexities of the seed themes.
