Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the push and pull between control and surrender, capturing the essence of pressure and release. The palette of cosmic indigo, void black, star white, cold gold, and deep teal evokes feelings of awe and terror, with stark contrasts creating tension at the intersection of dense and quiet spaces. The lattice and island patterns collide in the center, where color interactions symbolize the struggle between overflow and containment, creating a visual echo of conflict.
