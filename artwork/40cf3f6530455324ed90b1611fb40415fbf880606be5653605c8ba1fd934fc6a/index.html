Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between 'emptiness' and 'desire', utilizing a palette that reflects warmth and deep contrasts. The transition from the warm hues of coral and orange to the muted shades of charcoal encapsulates a journey from an internal void towards a yearning for fulfillment. The center holds a denser color formation, symbolizing desire, while the edges fade into lighter shades, echoing an emptiness that surrounds it. This contrast provides a visual pull, creating a sense of dynamic tension, where color clusters represent desires at odds with the palpable boundaries of restraint. The zigzag flow further echoes the conflict, suggesting motion and interruption as opposing emotions collide.
