Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a spectrum of intensity—from the quietude of muted colors to the clashes of vibrant shades. The palette embraces deep blues and greys for emotional weight and tension, contrasting with luminous yellows and soft pastels that signify release. The primary clash occurs in the center with bursts of bright colors intermittently disrupting the calmer surroundings, representing the interplay between pressure and release. This arrangement symbolizes the underlying rhythmic pulse of emotions colliding and resolving.
