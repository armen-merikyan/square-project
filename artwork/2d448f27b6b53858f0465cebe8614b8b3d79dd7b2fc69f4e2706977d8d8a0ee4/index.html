Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the pull between restlessness and peace, utilizing contrasting palette elements. The palette 'melancholy_wonder' harmonizes muted tones of rain blue and muted plum with bursts of soft cyan, establishing both tension and tranquility. The main collision occurs at the center, where peace (represented by pulse marks in soft cyan and moon white) interrupts the dynamic disruptions of restlessness (fault lines of muted plum and lavender gray) at each diagonal edge. The broken pixels were introduced as intentional elements of imperfection that reflect the inherent struggles presented by the seed, enhancing the dialogue between stasis and movement.
