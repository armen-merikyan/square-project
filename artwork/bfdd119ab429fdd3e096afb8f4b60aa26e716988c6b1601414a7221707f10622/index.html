Artist Statement

The composition embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The palette of navy, black, and dim cyan creates a cool, quiet base while introducing a stark contrast with fragmented white highlights that suggest clarity amidst confusion. Broken pixels disrupt symmetry, emphasizing a shift from solid ground (clarity) to an ambiguous outer edge (confusion). The pattern formation radiates outward from the center, echoing the idea of scattered thoughts emerging from a core of understanding. The negative space contributes to the breathing room necessary for interpretation, inviting the viewer to grapple with the nuances between fullness and hollow silhouettes.
