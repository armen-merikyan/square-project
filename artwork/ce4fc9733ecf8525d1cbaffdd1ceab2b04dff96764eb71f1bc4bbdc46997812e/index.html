Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a tension between nostalgia (warmth, memory) and progress (cool, stark transitions). The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green reflects the complexities of these emotions. Central to the contrast is the bottom-heavy arrangement where the dark colors form a dense space, representing nostalgia, while lighter shades above suggest aspirations and progress. The patterns—crosshatch for nostalgia and split screen for progress—create a rhythmic disturbance in the interplay of light and shadow, echoing the push-and-pull of growth versus remembrance.
