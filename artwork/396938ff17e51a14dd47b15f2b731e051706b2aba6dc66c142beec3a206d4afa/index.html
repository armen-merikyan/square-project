Artist Statement

The artwork conveys the emotional dichotomy of longing versus resolve by juxtaposing a structured patchwork at the center with a soft halo border that suggests a lingering distance. The palette of muted plum, rain blue, and lavender gray establishes a melancholy mood complemented by pale moon white accents that interrupt the density of longing. The main collision occurs at the core, where the inner patchwork of resolve conflicts with the surrounding frame of longing, encapsulating the emotional struggle depicted in the seed.
