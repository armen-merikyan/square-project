Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on a tension between the vibrant essence of 'spark' and the muted decay of 'ash'. The palette incorporates warm tones like 'lamp_gold' and softer hues such as 'cool_blue' to reflect this juxtaposition, suggesting both attraction and restraint. The collision occurs diagonally, where the warm burst of yellow interacts forcefully with the cooler blues, embodying conflict and fragmenting memory versus erasure. The patterns of 'target' and 'fault_line' create an organic rhythm that amplifies this dynamic, while broken pixels add intentional imperfections, suggesting resilience amidst fragility. The layout invites the viewer to explore this emotional dialogue, embodying the essence of light against shadow, order against chaos.
