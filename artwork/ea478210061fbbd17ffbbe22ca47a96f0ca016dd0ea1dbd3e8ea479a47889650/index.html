Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension of 'relief vs dread' through a stark division of light and dark colors. The palette, inspired by 'broken memory,' utilizes sepia tones that invoke nostalgia and dustiness alongside faded blues representing calm. The collision occurs through the broken pixel arrangement, creating jagged edges and an imbalance that reflects the struggle between relief and tension. The pattern alternates between symmetry and disruptive elements, highlighting the psychological push and pull central to the seed's emotional narrative.
