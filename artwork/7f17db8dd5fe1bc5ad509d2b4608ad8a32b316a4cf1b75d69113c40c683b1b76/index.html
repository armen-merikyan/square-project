Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys the tension between nostalgia, represented by muted, earth-toned colors and soft patterns, and progress, depicted through brighter contrasts and dynamic forms. The selected palette of sepia and faded blue symbolizes memory and modernity, with the darker colors grounding the composition. The main contrast occurs in the vertical split, where deeper tones collide with lighter hues, echoing a balance of weight and levity. The seed-inspired patterns create a push and pull between past and future, forming a pulse of color variation that invites contemplation.
