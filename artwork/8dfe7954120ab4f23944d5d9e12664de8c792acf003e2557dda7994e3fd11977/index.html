Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the tension between panic and control, using a palette of burnt orange and dark red to evoke a sense of intensity and chaos, contrasted by grounding charcoal and gold. The top half represents control with a grid-like pattern, while the bottom half captures panic through jagged flame-like arrangements. This duality creates a visual narrative of order colliding with chaos. The broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection and unpredictability, resonating with the theme of turmoil and unease.
