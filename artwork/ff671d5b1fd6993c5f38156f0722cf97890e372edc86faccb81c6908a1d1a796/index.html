Artist Statement

This abstract composition embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette comprising deep navy and dim cyan symbolizes melancholy intertwined with a flicker of hope, while the stark black represents darkness and obstacles. The main collision occurs centrally, where the pulse marks of resolve intersect with the echoing patterns of longing. Broken pixels are strategically placed to reflect the theme of repair versus damage, allowing imperfections to resonate within the orderly structure. The arrangement favors quiet fields, allowing for breathing space amidst the chaotic interplay of emotions.
