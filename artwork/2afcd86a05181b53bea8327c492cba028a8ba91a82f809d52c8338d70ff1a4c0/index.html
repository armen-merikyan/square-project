Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the conflict between love and betrayal. The warm palette of pink and red represents love, while the muted gray and dark maroon convey betrayal. The top half of the artwork features stripes, symbolizing the alignment of love, contrasted by the bottom half's rings that echo betrayal's disruptive nature. The jagged edges and broken pixels create a sense of imperfection, highlighting the unresolved emotions. The main contrast lies in the transition between the soft warmth of love and the sharpness of betrayal, creating a palpable tension within the abstract arrangement.
