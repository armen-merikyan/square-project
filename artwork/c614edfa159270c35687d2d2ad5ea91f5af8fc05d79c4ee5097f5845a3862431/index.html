Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between defiance and submission, using contrasting colors to evoke a dynamic interplay. The palette blends tones of calm tranquility (pale aqua, mist gray) with intense urgency (alarm red, ink blue), creating a visual pulse that mirrors the seed's conflict. The composition employs a staggered, stacked-band layout, reflecting fluctuating intensity from bottom to top, where the bright alarm red serves as an interruption in a predominantly cool field. The broken pixels scattered across the canvas signify flaws in order, enhancing the narrative of chaos disrupting perceived tranquility, while the diagonal rise pattern conveys a subtle but persistent push against boundaries.
