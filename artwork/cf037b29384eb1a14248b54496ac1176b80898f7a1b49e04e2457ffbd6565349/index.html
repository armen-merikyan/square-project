Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a dynamic interplay between vulnerability and strength, captured through the use of a muted color palette. The dominant colors suggest a foundational strength (#4A4E69, #22223B) contrasted by delicate exposures (#F9F7F7, #B9D6D8). The contrast is marked by a bold center fleck of compressed red (#9A0036) peeking through, symbolizing sparks of courage amidst the overwhelming weight of pressure. The flowing pattern from the edges inward creates a sense of a protective layer forming over the vulnerable core, interspersed by gaps to emulate the forgotten spaces of connection and alienation. The intentional broken pixels emphasize the imperfections of resilience, adding depth to the narrative beneath the surface.
