Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between safety and exposure. The palette consists of soft shelter green and pale skin contrasted with the stark open blue and cold white, suggesting a dynamic interplay of safety and vulnerability. The main collision occurs at the center, where the colors pulse against each other, reflecting pressure and release in an echoing manner. The arrangement of colors fosters a sense of layering and movement, creating a juxtaposition of dense clusters against quieter fields, representing the delicate balance of protection and exposure.
