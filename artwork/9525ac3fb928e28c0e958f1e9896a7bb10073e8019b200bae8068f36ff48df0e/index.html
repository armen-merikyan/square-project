Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between loneliness and connection, where the central area acts as both a point of vulnerability and a seed for potential connection. The palette consists of navy and black for depth, combined with dim cyan and a single white for moments of clarity and hope. The arrangement creates density in the top-left corner and gradually releases to more negative space towards the bottom-right, symbolizing the journey from isolation to potential reconnection. The maze-like pattern intersperses with rings, echoing the struggle between being hidden and exposed. The overall balance leans on creating an emotional charge that resonates with inner conflict, poised to burst forth.
