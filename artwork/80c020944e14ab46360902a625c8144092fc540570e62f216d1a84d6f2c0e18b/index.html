Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of panic and control, represented through a systematic order interspersed with chaotic breaks. The palette of warm and cool tones reflects joy and grief, establishing an emotional tension. The arrangement features a symmetry of organized patterns on one side juxtaposed with jagged disruptions, symbolizing moments of chaos cutting through a controlled existence. The hard edges of the colors create a visual collision that captures the essence of structured anxiety, with broken pixels echoing imperfection and instability.
