Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the essence of collisions and contrasts between pressure and release. The chosen palette of navy, black, and dim cyan creates a deep, reflective atmosphere, while the white introduces a stark interruption, enhancing the sense of contrast. This plays into the outer border representing pressure (black and navy) and the inner 4x4 suggesting quiet rhythm (dim cyan and white). The 'frame_and_core' layout supports this juxtaposition, with a spiral counterclockwise flow to echo the dynamics of emotion. The pixel arrangement considers broken pixels, creating an impression of imperfection and emotional weight, emphasizing the core theme of duality.
