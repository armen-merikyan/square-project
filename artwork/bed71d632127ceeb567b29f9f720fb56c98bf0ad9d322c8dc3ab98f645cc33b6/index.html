Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension of tenderness versus rage. The palette of deep reds and muted whites suggests oppression and fragility, where darker tones prevail to represent rage as a dominating emotion. The main contrast occurs where soft white and pale blue noise patterns collide with jagged, explosive red threshold bands, symbolizing the ongoing struggle between calm and chaos. The layout creates a gradient effect from dense and saturated colors at the bottom to lighter and more forgiving tones above, echoing a climb from intensity to release. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, reflecting the unpredictability of emotional experiences. Overall, it tells a story of grappling with inner turmoil and the ongoing process of forgiveness and healing.
