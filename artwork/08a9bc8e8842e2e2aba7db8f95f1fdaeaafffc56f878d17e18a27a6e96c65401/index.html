Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, captured through sharp contrasts and layered patterns. The palette, consisting of soft cyan, moon white, lavender gray, and muted plum, hints at a melancholic wonder, suggesting moments of subtle beauty amidst stark realities. The main collision is at the center, where a dense cluster of soft cyan circles disrupts the pale white background, embodying the impact of exposure. The layout follows an 'island and sea' concept, isolating a bold section against a quieter expanse, symbolizing both connection and alienation. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, echoing the fragility of human experience amid resilience.
