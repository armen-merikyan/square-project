Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a complex interplay between nostalgia and progress, expressed through a palette that blends warm reds with cool grays. The use of dark maroon represents weight and history, while pink introduces a burst of optimism and hope. The composition's rising signal layout emphasizes upward movement amidst disruption, creating a tense collision between the dense clusters of dark colors and sporadic lighter tones, reflecting both a struggle and an aspiration. The pattern of frames and cracks symbolizes the fragmentation of memories against a backdrop of progress, encapsulating the tension between full vs. hollow and the weight of nostalgia versus the potential of the future.
