Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between defiance and submission. The palette, drawn from deep navy and dim cyan, reflects the loneliness of signals amidst noise. The structured pattern mimics 'islands' of defiance in the center, contrasted with the flowing 'pressure band' motifs around it. The main collision occurs centrally, where these two emotional elements clash, creating a vibrant yet uneasy balance. The broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, echoing the theme of conflict and resolution.
