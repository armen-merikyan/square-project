Artist Statement

This artwork explores the intricate balance between love and betrayal. The left side is filled with warm reds and pinks, representing affection and connection, while the right side contrasts sharply with cool grays and dark maroon, symbolizing betrayal and emotional isolation. The pattern of paired shapes on the left suggests unity, while the stair-like pattern on the right evokes a sense of conflict and estrangement. The choice of colors and their placements create a tense collision in the center, where the warmth clashes with the coolness, suggesting an emotional struggle inherent in relationships.
