Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette leans towards deep, cosmic colors to evoke awe and terror, with cosmic indigo and deep teal creating a strong foundation, providing stability and depth. Accenting colors like star white and cold gold introduce a sense of tension and light, representing vulnerability amidst strength. The central collision occurs at the core of the piece, where a barcode pattern of deep teal interacts with lighter colors, creating a feeling of being exposed yet protected. The 'broken pixels' add an intentional disruption, echoing the theme of vulnerability in strength, capturing the essence of a fragile yet resilient state.
