Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes control and panic, manifested through contrasting colors and patterns. The 'ancient_fire' palette invokes warmth and intensity, with burnt orange and dark red suggesting turmoil, while charcoal and gold introduce a sense of structure and restraint. The main contrast occurs between the organized, linear grid of the control side and the chaotic pixel bleed of the panic side, creating a dynamic visual tension that embodies the core of the seed. The layout and flow guide the eye from the center outward, echoing the struggle of maintaining control against turbulent feelings.
