Artist Statement

The emotional tension between confusion and clarity manifests through the use of dense clusters of gray and distant sparkles of burnt orange and gold. The palette rooted in 'ancient_fire' invokes warmth yet contrasts sharply with the charcoal, introducing an overwhelming feeling of pressure. Central placement of vibrant colors disrupts the otherwise muted grid, mirroring the struggle of clarity rising from confusion. The arrangement allows echoes of radiant against shadowy intersections, instilling a dynamic conversation between order and chaos, reflected in the broken pixels that suggest imperfection as an intrinsic quality of reality.
