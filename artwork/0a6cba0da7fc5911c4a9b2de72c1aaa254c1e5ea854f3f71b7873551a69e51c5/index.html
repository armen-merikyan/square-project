Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between vulnerability and strength, captured through the juxtaposition of soft and sharp colors. The palette, rooted in muted 'quiet pressure' colors, conveys an uneasy harmony: off-white represents exposure, while dark navy evokes a sense of strength. The main collision occurs in the central area, where soft ripple patterns (vulnerability) meet dense threads (strength), symbolizing the interaction between exposure and shelter. This creates a visual echo of fluctuating feelings, offering a dynamic array of emotional narratives.
