Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between longing and resolve, depicted through color saturation and spatial dynamics. The palette features warm hues like hot coral and blood orange representing urgency and desire, contrasted by cool charcoal and hollow blue, evoking serenity and detachment. The composition centers around a dense core, symbolizing overwhelming emotion, while the borders represent the struggle to contain it, creating a push-pull dynamic. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, imbuing a sense of unresolved tension that reflects the seed's themes.
