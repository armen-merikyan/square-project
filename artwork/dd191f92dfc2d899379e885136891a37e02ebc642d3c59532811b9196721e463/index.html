Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between quietude and pressure, achieved through a palette of soft and dark hues. The primary contrast emerges from the dense, saturated center, symbolizing pressure, which then dissipates into the softer outer bands suggesting tranquility. The arrangement of stripes and braids captures a flow of zigzag that creates visual interlocks and breaks, echoing the seed's themes of collision and clarity amidst chaos.
