Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between clarity (signal) and disruption (noise) through color and form. The palette of navy and dim cyan creates a stark contrast against the black, suggesting depth and shadow, while the white serves as a whisper of clarity amid chaos. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense segments of dark and light clash, simulating the echo of a pulse striving for clarity against a backdrop of interference. This evokes the tension and complexity suggested by the seed, where the stark edges clash against smooth gradients, representing the chaos of broken signals.
