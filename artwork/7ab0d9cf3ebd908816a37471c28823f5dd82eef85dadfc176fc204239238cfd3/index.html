Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between control and surrender, with an overfilled center suggesting pressure against the backdrop of softer edges. The palette of 'melancholy wonder' creates a soothing foundation with hints of warmth, fostering a sense of gentle release amidst the drama. The main contrast occurs centrally, where bright colors push against the subdued hues, indicating an overflow of emotion against the tranquil confines of control. This gives rise to an abstract pattern that mirrors the stutter-step of fluctuating tension, embodying the essence of the seed.
