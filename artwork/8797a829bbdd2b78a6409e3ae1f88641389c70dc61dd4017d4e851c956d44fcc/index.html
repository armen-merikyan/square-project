Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a palette of rich, muted colors that create depth and texture. The contrasting elements blend rippled patterns of longing with the noise of resolve. The intersection occurs in the central part of the grid, where the strong tones collide with broken pixels, introducing reminders of imperfection and emotional scars. This discord reflects the seed's themes of repair and damage, inviting viewers to contemplate a journey fraught with tension yet illuminated by brief moments of serenity.
