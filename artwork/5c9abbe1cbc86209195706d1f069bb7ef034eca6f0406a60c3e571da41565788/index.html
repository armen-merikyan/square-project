Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the juxtaposition of trust and suspicion, evident in the contrasting color palette of warm oranges and yellows against deep blues and muted purples. The flow creates a zigzag pattern, emphasizing the idea of collision and pressure, while the central stair-step shape implies movement and escalation. The balance of positive and negative space enhances the feeling of unresolved tension, allowing the viewer to feel the interplay between calm and pressure.
