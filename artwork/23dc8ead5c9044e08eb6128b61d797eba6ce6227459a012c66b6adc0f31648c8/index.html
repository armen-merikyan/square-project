Artist Statement

The artwork reflects the duality of confusion and clarity through stark contrasts and abstract patterns. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold creates a dynamic tension between warmth and darkness, emphasizing the emotional conflict. The arrangement features thick borders of dark red surrounding chaotic arrangements of colors in the center, hinting at clarity trying to break through confusion. The use of broken pixels throughout introduces intentional imperfection, echoing the emotional turmoil conveyed in the seed, culminating in a visually compelling tumult that transitions from the center outward, striking a balance between order and chaos.
