Artist Statement

The emotional tone portrays the dichotomy between loneliness and connection. The palette combines dark violet and soft gold to symbolize ancient fire and hope amidst sorrow. The contrast lies between tight clusters of pale green dots representing connections amidst broader empty black spaces echoing loneliness. This configuration mirrors a central 'tear,' where the inner bursts attempt to reach out yet remain confined, creating an abstract narrative of struggle and longing.
