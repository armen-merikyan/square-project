Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the conflict between love and betrayal, symbolizing warmth disrupted by coldness. The palette is a balance of passionate reds and soft pinks clashing against dark maroon and gray, representing warmth fighting chaos. The main contrast surfaces through the collision of warm and cool colors, creating a tension as one side evokes longing while the other hints at loss/struggle. The pattern suggests a wave of warmth disrupted by sharp edges of chaos, symbolizing the interference of emotions in relationships.
