Artist Statement

This abstract work embodies a clash between rage and calm through a spectrum of colors. The palette of deep blacks and fiery reds contrasts against soft blues and whites, illustrating emotional conflict. The upper rows represent calm, with horizontal expansions of pale blues and whites suggesting space and serenity, while the lower rows embody rage through jagged patterns of red and black, indicating a primal energy. Each color placement reinforces the upward and downward movement proposed in the layout, capturing a dynamic tension that echoes the emotional states of the seed.
