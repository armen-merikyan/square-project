Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances the heat of desire with the coolness of restraint. Using bright colors like hot coral and blood orange conveys saturated passion, while muted shades like charcoal and hollow blue introduce a sense of containment. The pattern features stacked and fault line elements, illustrating a tension that spirals out from the center. The central collision occurs within the inner square, creating a dynamic conflict that suggests the struggle between desire's push and restraint's pull.
