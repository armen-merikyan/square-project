Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a delicate balancing act between restlessness and peace, represented by the juxtaposition of dark storm gray with light pale mint. The palette echoes relief with faded gold and soft sky, layering quiet fields with accents. The main collision occurs diagonally from the corner, where restless patterns of gray disrupt peaceful stripes of mint, symbolizing the tension between memory and motion. Each color's placement creates an oscillation, embodying the continuous push and pull inherent in the theme.
