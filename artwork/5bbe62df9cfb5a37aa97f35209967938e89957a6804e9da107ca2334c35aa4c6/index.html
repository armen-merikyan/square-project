Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the juxtaposition of longing's delicate, glitch-like patterns against resolve’s structured, crosshatch type. The palette, including warm coral and cold slate, symbolizes the warmth of intimacy clashing with the coolness of distance. The central collision occurs around the middle of the square, where dense clusters of warm hues interrupt the cooler, quieter background, suggesting an emotional pull between longing and resolve.
