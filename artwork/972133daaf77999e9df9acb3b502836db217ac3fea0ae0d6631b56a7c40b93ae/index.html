Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between longing and resolve, utilizing a palette that juxtaposes warm pink and red with deep gray and dark maroon. The design features high contrast, with bold, overlapping patterns symbolizing the collision between desired connection (longing) and grounded strength (resolve). A central void represents a broken, literally echoed pattern, highlighted by broken pixels that introduce imperfections and nuances, enhancing the narrative of an insatiable quest for balance amidst the noise.
