Artist Statement

This artwork contrasts the heaviness of grief with the lightness of joy, utilizing a palette of warm yellows and oranges against muted blues and purples. The upper half is dense with flame-like patterns signifying joy, while the lower half is fragmented with broken dots representing grief. The flowing wave pattern symbolizes the ongoing tension and undulation of emotions. The slight negative space invites the viewer to breathe, reflecting hope amidst struggle.
