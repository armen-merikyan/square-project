Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between love and betrayal through a contrasting palette of warm pinks and cool grays. The outer layers represent expectations and warmth, while the inner structure, fragmented grey tones, symbolize betrayal's cold grip. The arrangement reflects chaos with dense clusters of color clashing against areas of negative space, evoking a sense of unresolved conflict and emotional disturbance—mirroring the inner battles of trust versus doubt.
