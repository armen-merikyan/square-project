Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, utilizing a palette that intertwines soft blues and grays with punctuations of muted plum. The contrast is heightened in the center where darker colors collide with lighter shades, suggesting a moment of breaking or exposure. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection and disruption, reflecting the fragility of strength amidst vulnerability. This square invokes feelings of unease yet beauty, echoing the balance between protective shelter and exposed emotion.
