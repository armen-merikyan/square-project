Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the dichotomy of restlessness and peace through a juxtaposition of dense, dark colors that create a feeling of tension against lighter, softer hues that promote calmness. The chosen palette includes cosmic indigo for a touch of depth and mystery, void black representing struggles and challenges, and cold gold to evoke a feeling of warmth emerging from darkness. The collision occurs predominantly in the central area of the square, where intense dark colors intertwine with lighter tones, suggesting both conflict and resolution. The layout has an open gate style, allowing movement and flow amidst structured chaos, reflecting the interplay of memory and motion defined by the seed.
