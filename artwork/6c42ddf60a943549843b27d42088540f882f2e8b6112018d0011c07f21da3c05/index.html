Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional duality of longing and resolve through contrasting tones and patterns. The chosen palette features muted colors like 'off_white' and 'dust_gray,' symbolizing a quiet yearning, while the 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy' introduce pressure and firmness. The arrangement incorporates a central collision of dense clusters and empty spaces, visualizing the struggle between chaos and order. The 'scar' pattern hints at emotional wounds and the pursuit of stability, leading to a fragmented harmony across the square.
