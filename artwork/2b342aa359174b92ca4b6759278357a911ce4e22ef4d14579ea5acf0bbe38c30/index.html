Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting textures and tones. The palette of soft pink, warm white, and deep black highlights the emotional dichotomy; pale colors represent exposure while dark tones suggest strength. The main collision occurs in the center with a concentrated burst of pink surrounded by heavier shades that create an enveloping frame, hinting at both shelter and exposure. The patterns—radial strength meeting crosshatch vulnerability—reflect the push and pull of emotions, resulting in a visual dance of interruption and flow.
