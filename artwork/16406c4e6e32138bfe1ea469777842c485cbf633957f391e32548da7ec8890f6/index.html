Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between fear and hope through distinct patterns and color placement. The dark palette reflects fear, with deep blacks and violets holding the corners, creating a sense of pressure and confinement. In contrast, soft gold and pale green introduce small areas of hope, appearing as glimmers of light against the darker backdrop. The zigzag pattern suggests conflict, with sharp transitions between colors evoking a sense of movement. The overall flow from outside to the inward center emphasizes the struggle and bittersweet balance. The arrangement of colors creates an experience of emotional collision, reflecting the dynamic interplay of despair and optimism.
