Artist Statement

The emotional tone suggests a conflict between pride (warm colors) and shame (cool colors). The palette of yellow and muted purple allows for a vibrant push against a softer backdrop, echoing feelings of conflict and resolution. The layout is split vertically to denote the opposing emotional forces, while the use of stair patterns creates a sense of ascent and descent, paralleling the pressure and release theme. The broken pixel elements suggest imperfection amidst structure, reflecting the inherent clashes in emotion.
