Artist Statement

The contrasting emotional tone of restlessness and peace is reflected in the bold use of colors. The palette includes bright coral and orange tones to express urgency and tension, juxtaposed against muted shades of charcoal and soft blues representing calm and introspection. The center of the grid is densely populated with warm colors, clashing against the cooler edges that signify a struggle to contain this vibrancy. The pattern created by intersecting threads of color embodies the diagonal arguments and dissonance of memory and motion, manifesting a quiet yet chaotic energy.
