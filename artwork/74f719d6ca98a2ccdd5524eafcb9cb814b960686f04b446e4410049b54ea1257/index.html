Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the delicate balance between confusion and clarity, conveyed through a palette of deep blacks and hints of gold, representing clarity within chaos. The arrangement reflects broken symmetry with contrasting layers of solid and fragmented colors that pulse between dense clusters and quiet spaces. The top half embraces a spark pattern of soft gold and pale green to symbolize clarity, while the bottom introduces darker shades that evoke a sense of confusion and disarray, suggesting the influence of broken pixels and broken grids. The pulses of color create a rhythmic balance, emphasizing the collision between reason and chaos.
