Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette blends crisp ice blue (representing a cold yet serene longing) with warm warning orange (symbolizing the urgency of resolve). The main collision occurs in the center with a noise pattern intersecting a ripple pattern, reflecting the push-pull of emotions. Broken pixels introduce areas of imperfection, representing the scars of damage while the 'half_square' shape organizes the chaos into a coherent yet abstract form.
