Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between pressure and quietude. The palette, featuring off-white and darker hues, represents a balance of clarity and confusion. The dense center collides with lighter edges, symbolizing the struggle between weight and release. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, adding a dynamic aspect to the symmetry versus break theme, enhancing the emotional push and pull inherent in the seed's thematic elements.
