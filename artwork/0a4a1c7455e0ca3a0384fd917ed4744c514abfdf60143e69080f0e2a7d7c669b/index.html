Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between strength and vulnerability, with the burnt orange and dark red radiating strength while the softer charcoal and gold reflect vulnerability. The arrangement creates a dynamic intersection where strong, bold colors collide with gentle, faded hues, depicting the struggle of opposing forces. The main collision happens towards the center of the square, reminiscent of currents meeting, creating a visual tension that suggests both conflict and harmony amid chaos. The falling pixel layout mimics the descent of emotion, allowing for negative space to breathe amidst the clustered expressions of strength and vulnerability.
