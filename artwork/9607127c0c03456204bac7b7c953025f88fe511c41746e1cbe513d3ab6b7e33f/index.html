Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between a warmth of protection and the chilling essence of exposure. The selected palette reflects a balance between soft greens and warm browns, expressing shelter's embrace against the cool, open blues that signify exposure. The main collision occurs in the central diagonal wedge, where warm and cool colors interact, suggesting tension and movement. Patterns of negative crosses representing safety intersect with ember-like marks of exposure, creating an emotional dance across the canvas that speaks to vulnerability and strength.
