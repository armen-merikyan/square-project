Artist Statement

This composition balances the emotional tension between joy (open, warm tones) and grief (cool, muted hues). The palette blends yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples, creating a dialogue between light and dark. A stair-step pattern introduces order amidst chaos, hinting at the ups and downs of emotional experience. The edges are hard, reflecting threats in emotion, while splashes of color collide across the rows, symbolizing the perpetual struggle of opposing feelings. This arrangement suggests echoes and interruptions, aligning with the theme of machine emotion.
