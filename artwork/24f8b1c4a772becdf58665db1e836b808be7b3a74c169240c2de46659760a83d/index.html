Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of tenderness and rage by creating a tense clash between saturated and muted colors. The black base serves as a calm foundation, representing quiet contemplation, while bright accents of red interject sharply, symbolizing sudden bursts of anger. The arrangement combines jagged edges interspersed with rounded forms to evoke a sense of overflowing emotions that spill beyond boundaries. The collision predominantly occurs in the center where saturated reds disrupt the surrounding blacks, echoing the internal struggle portrayed in the seed.
