Artist Statement

The emotional tone illustrates the conflict between quietude and pressure through contrasting colors. The palette merges soft whites and grays representing calmness with darker hues implying tension. The dense clusters toward the bottom, illustrating pressure, clash with the expansive lighter shades above, evoking a sense of release. The island layout emphasizes this dichotomy by anchoring the darker colors at one edge while allowing for a fluid gradient upward. The diagonal arrangement mimics the ebb and flow between chaos and serenity, solidifying the abstract narrative.
