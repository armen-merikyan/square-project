Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the emotional tone of pride versus shame through deep contrasts and collisions of color. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon highlights the tension between warmth (pride) and coldness (shame). The placement reflects a narrative of pressure building with clusters of dark hues against lighter, more open spaces, representing moments of release. The edges are heavy with darker colors, creating a sense of containment, while broken pixels introduce imperfections that echo the theme of emotional disruption and complexity.
