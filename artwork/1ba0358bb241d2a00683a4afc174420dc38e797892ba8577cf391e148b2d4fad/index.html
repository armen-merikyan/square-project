Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity, illustrated by the juxtaposition of structured patterns against more fluid forms. The muted palette of off-white and dusty gray conveys a sense of quiet pressure, while the compressed red and dark navy serve as strong accents representing curiosity and chaos. The central collision occurs through radial patterns clashing with pressure bands, creating a tension-filled center that pulls focus, while the edges recede, suggesting an ebbing tide of thoughts and feelings.
