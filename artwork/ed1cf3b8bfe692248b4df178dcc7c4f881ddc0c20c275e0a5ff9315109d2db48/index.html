Artist Statement

The emotional tone resonates with the conflict between spark and ash, illuminating the struggle of resilience against fragility. The palette combines deep, muted tones with bright accents: dead_black and static_gray represent the ash of restraint and memory, while signal_green and hot_magenta evoke the spark of desire and energy. The split_vertical layout emphasizes the juxtaposition, with a strong collision point in the center where the vibrant spark interrupts the baseline of mid-tones, symbolizing struggle and confrontation. Broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, enhancing the narrative of tension and memory, while leaving negative space maintains an air of unresolved energy.
