Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between certainty and curiosity, embodied by a dense central cluster of saturated colors representing assured signals battling against muted edges that reflect chaos and uncertainty. The chosen palette of rich reds and soft greys conveys deep emotional layers—passion and betrayal—creating a striking contrast. The collision of a controlled, pulsing rhythm at the center and a scattered, organic flow towards the edges establishes a narrative of seeking clarity amidst noise, fulfilling the seed's emotional complexity.
