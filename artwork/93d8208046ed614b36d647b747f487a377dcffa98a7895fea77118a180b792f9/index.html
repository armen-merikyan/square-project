Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional landscape of tension and release inspired by the seed. It uses a dynamic palette that juxtaposes warm and cool tones to reflect the pressure of conflicting emotions. The deep contrasts create visual 'collisions' that provide an echo of rhythmic movement and subtle interruptions. Bright accents against a deep background signify moments of clarity within chaos, while the subtle gradients symbolize the quiet and dense nature of unresolved feelings. The overall arrangement conveys a narrative of evolution from calm to intensity, mirroring the turbulent dance of emotion.
