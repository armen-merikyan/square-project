Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork captures the tension between emptiness and desire. The palette of warm oranges and cool blues illustrates this dichotomy, with contrasting colors that suggest movement from the left to the right, representing a journey from chaos to order. The structured stripes of desire on one side meet a chaotic frame symbolizing emptiness, creating constant interplay. The broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, echoing the fragmented heart theme while contributing to an overall sense of unresolved emotion.
