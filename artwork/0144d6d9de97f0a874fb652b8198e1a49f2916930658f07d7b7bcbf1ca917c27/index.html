Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece is defined by the struggle between control and surrender, represented through the contrasting elements of the palette. The soft, warm colors illustrate the idea of surrender—powder pink and warm white suggest openness, while the starkness of carbon black and sharp crimson bring a sense of control and pressure. The inner pulse of tension, evident in the concentrated placement of darker colors at the center, creates a colliding sensation between the soft edges and the bold core, embodying the theme of overflow vs. containment. The abstract composition oscillates between a measured rhythm and a release, captured in the layout of small centers that pulse outward against a quiet field, culminating in a striking visual representation of emotional duality.
