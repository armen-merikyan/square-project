Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional struggle between restlessness and peace through a contrasting palette of soft pinks and deep maroons that merge and clash throughout the canvas. The central area, dominated by dark maroon, represents tension, while gentle patches of pink create moments of peace around it. Disrupted pixel placements suggest a sense of chaos against calm. The diagonal flow of the arrangement guides the viewer's eye, contributing to the sense of movement and memory within the underlying pattern.
