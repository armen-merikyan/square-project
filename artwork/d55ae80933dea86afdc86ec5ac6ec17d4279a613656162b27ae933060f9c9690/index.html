Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between fear and hope. The chosen palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green captures the duality of these emotions, with darker colors representing fear's engulfing nature, while the lighter hues suggest the gentle rise of hope. The layout creates a split that reflects the split vertical theme, using a mirror imagery of color placement. The glowing edges give a sense of relief in the midst of darkness, while the broken pixel elements introduce imperfections, embodying the chaotic struggle between light and dark. The arrangement of colors emphasizes a collision between vibrant gold and the depth of the darker shades, illustrating a burst of hope amidst the surrounding fear.
