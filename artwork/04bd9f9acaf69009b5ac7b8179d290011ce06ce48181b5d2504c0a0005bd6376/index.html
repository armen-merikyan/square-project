Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between awe and terror, represented through a stark contrast of warm and cool tones. The palette combines muted purple and deep blue to evoke a sense of depth against bursts of yellow and orange, reminiscent of light amidst shadow. The layout is split vertically to signify order versus chaos, with the left representing awe through a rain-like pattern of softer edges, while the right embraces terror through more chaotic textures. Broken pixels create a sense of imperfection, enhancing the feeling of instability, while negative space ensures the composition breathes, allowing the viewer to navigate the emotional landscape of this juxtaposition.
