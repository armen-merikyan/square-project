Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of signal versus noise through contrasting patterns and color placements. The palette centers around deep navy and dim cyan, embodying the tension between darkness (noise) and bright signals (lighter hues). The layout, influenced by a mirror effect, introduces intentional disruptions that echo the idea of memory versus erasure. The center pulse signifies the clear signal amid static interruptions, with broken pixels enhancing the sense of chaos and imperfection. The use of contrasting areas creates a dynamic rhythm while maintaining clarity through organized placements.
