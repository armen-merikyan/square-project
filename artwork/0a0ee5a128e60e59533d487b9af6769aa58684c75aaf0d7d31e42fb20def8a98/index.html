Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a dialogue between feelings of loneliness and connection, expressed through patterns of thick edges and center pressure. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue represents the longing for connection amidst a backdrop of muted tones. The contrasting light and dark colors collide at the edges, suggesting an invasion of connection within a lonely expanse. The pattern replicates a grid-like noise suggesting both structure and chaos, with broken pixels to symbolize fractured memories woven into the composition.
