Artist Statement

This composition juxtaposes the themes of confusion and clarity through hard edges and contrasting colors. The outer border features darker, muted shades representing confusion, while the inner core, though slightly fragmented, aims for a clearer, brighter expression. The use of sepia, tan, and dust gray provides a nostalgic feel, while the faint blue introduces a calming yet complex tension. The broken pixels create a sense of imperfection and disruption against a backdrop of organized chaos, symbolizing the emotional struggle between clarity and confusion. The main contrast lies in the outer layers battling with the bright inner core, suggesting an unresolved emotional state.
