Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between the vibrancy of 'spark' and the softness of 'ash'. Using a palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon, warm hues depict sudden bursts of energy while cooler shades suggest restraint and calm. The primary conflict occurs at the center where bright pink and red collide with muted gray and dark maroon, embodying a struggle between intensity and tranquility. This layered structure mimics a stacked bands layout, representing fluctuating emotional layers. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, allowing memories and emotions to seep through amidst the contrasting chaos.
