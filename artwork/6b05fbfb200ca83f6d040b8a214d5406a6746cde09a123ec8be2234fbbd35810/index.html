Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances the yearning of 'longing' with the firmness of 'resolve'. The palette, rooted in 'gratitude_envy', invites warmth through ripe greens and honey gold while contrasting with deeper tones. The collision happens centrally, where broken dots from the 'resolve' pattern meet the frame from 'longing', creating an intentional disruption at the heart of the piece. This pattern symbolizes the tension between desire and stability, inviting an echo of emotion while maintaining clarity through structured placement.
