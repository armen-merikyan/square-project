Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances nostalgia and progress, manifesting in contrast between muted hues and vibrant accents. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon suggests warmth amid emotional turmoil. The main contrast occurs at the center, where light and dark interact, echoing a split between aspiration (progress) and reminiscence (nostalgia). The placement of colors creates a disrupted harmony, reflecting a narrative of rising against past memories, with drip-like gradients adding imperfection, highlighting a sense of flawed beauty.
