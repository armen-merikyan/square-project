Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between longing and resolve, with one color field representing an unfulfilled yearning and another symbolizing clarity and stability. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of nostalgia and broken memories, contrasting the warm tones of hope with cool tones of detachment. The collision occurs centrally, where the striated pattern of longing meets the fault line of resolve, suggesting a dynamic struggle. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, amplifying the feeling of incompleteness and repair. The layout adheres to a split horizontal structure, where the top half is dominated by the yearning (longing) and the bottom half by the stability (resolve). The balance between these colors signifies a nuanced emotional experience.
