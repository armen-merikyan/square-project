Artist Statement

This artwork embodies a tension between chaos and order. The top half, representing confusion, utilizes darker, muted tones from the 'ancient_fire' palette to evoke depth and unrest, suggesting broken symmetry and overlapping paths. The bottom half transitions to brighter, clearer tones, mirroring the potential for clarity and order. The strategic placement of broken pixels introduces an element of disruption, reinforcing the emotional contrast. The burnt orange and dark red colors create a warm vs. cool tension against the charcoal background, embodying an emotional landscape of anxiety trying to break through to clarity.
