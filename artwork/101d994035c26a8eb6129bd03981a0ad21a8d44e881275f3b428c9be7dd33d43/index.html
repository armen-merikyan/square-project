Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between loneliness and connection. The color palette utilizes deep navy and black to evoke a sense of solitude, countered by spots of bright white and dim cyan, symbolizing fleeting connections. The main contrast occurs at the edges, where dark tones are surrounded by lighter pixels, creating a visual collision that embodies the push and pull of human emotions. The pattern of broken lines resonates with the seed's theme of broken memory, while maintaining an abstract essence through the structured placement of colors, leaving intentional negative space to highlight the feelings of isolation.
