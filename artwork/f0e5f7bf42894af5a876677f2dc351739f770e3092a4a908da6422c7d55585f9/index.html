Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'fear vs hope' creates a dynamic tension throughout the piece. The palette, featuring deep blacks and dark violets contrasted with soft gold and pale green, establishes the duality of fear's density and hope's illumination. The main collision occurs between the dark, jagged edges that evoke fear and the soft, glowing shapes that suggest hope. The layout mimics rain falling, with an uneven pattern, creating a sense of chaos against the calm brightness. This reflects the seed's complexity in emotional struggles, highlighted by occasional broken pixels that symbolize imperfections in both feelings.
