Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrasts of joy and grief through color choice and composition. The palette combines bright yellows and oranges representing joy, with muted purples and deep blues symbolizing grief. The upper half of the composition is bright and radiates warmth, while the lower half is dense and muted, representing heaviness. The broken pixels introduced for imperfection enhance the chaotic elements of grief amidst the orderly layers of joy. The interplay creates an emotional wave, echoing the struggle between these two states, encouraging viewers to contemplate the hidden depths within each feeling.
