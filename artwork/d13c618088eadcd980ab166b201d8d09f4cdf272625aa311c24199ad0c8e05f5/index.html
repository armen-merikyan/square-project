Artist Statement

The emotional tone suggests a struggle between clarity and chaos, embedded in a dark palette with sharp accents. Using a base of navy and black conveys a depth of noise, while scattered bright patches of white and dim cyan signify moments of clarity and signal. The main contrast occurs at the center where a brighter cluster intermingles with darker elements, visually representing the collision between noise and signal, echoing the seed's themes.
