Artist Statement

The emotional tone emerges from collisions of warm and cool colors, creating a sense of dynamic tension. The palette features deep blues and vivid yellows that contrast sharply, suggesting both pressure and release. The arrangement of darker shades in the corners represents quiet spaces, while brighter hues in the center form clusters echoing rhythm and chaos. The interruptions of color create a sense of unresolved motion, embodying a journey through contrasting emotional landscapes.
