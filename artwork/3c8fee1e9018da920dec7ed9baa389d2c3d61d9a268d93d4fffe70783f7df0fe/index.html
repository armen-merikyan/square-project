Artist Statement

The emotional tone emphasizes a tension between restlessness and peace, using a muted palette of sepia and faded blue to represent broken memories. The weight of darker tones contrasts with luminous patches of light to signify fleeting moments of tranquility. The central arrangement of 'embers' collides with quiet fields, depicting fragments of motion against a gentle backdrop, while broken pixels create intentional disruptions, lending to the impression of unresolved memory.
