Artist Statement

The emotional tension between longing and resolve shapes this piece, represented through a juxtaposition of deep teal and cold gold for warmth and depth, reflecting desire and strength. The contrast emerges at the center where rings of deep teal pulse against fragmented bursts of cosmic indigo and cold gold, creating an echo that draws the eye while also leaving negative space that signifies absence or waiting. This interplay between filled and empty, dense and quiet, mirrors the theme of repair vs damage and emphasizes the broken threads of connection suggested in the seed, allowing for a dynamic flow of emotion.
