Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of love and betrayal through a palette of warm and cool colors that evoke feelings of both connection and disruption. The use of pinks and reds represents love, while dark maroon and gray signify betrayal, introducing tension with each color collision. The jagged edges create a sense of brokenness, echoing the emotional turmoil, while the rising signal layout suggests hope amidst chaos. The patterns subtly intertwine, embodying both maze-like complexity and simplicity, achieving a balance between density and emptiness, leaving just enough negative space for reflection.
