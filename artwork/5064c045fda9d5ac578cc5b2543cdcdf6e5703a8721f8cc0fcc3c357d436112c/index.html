Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of signal versus noise. The palette of deep navy, stark black, and subtle dim cyan reflects the weight of chaos and static, while punctuations of white signify fleeting signals of clarity and hope. The main contrast occurs where the dense noise is broken by clear signals, emphasizing the conflict between turmoil and beauty. The vertical flow of pattern highlights how the readable rhythm attempts to assert itself against a backdrop of disorder, creating a sense of movement and disruption, poetically interpreting the seed's essence.
