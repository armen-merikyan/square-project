Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the contrast between softness and severity, embodied in the chosen palette of powder pink and carbon black, suggesting a gentle warmth against a harsh boundary. The main collision occurs at the center, where soft islands of color conflict with stark fault lines, creating a visual argument of control and chaos. Negative space invites reflection amid the dense colors, while broken pixels serve as scars of the emotional tension embedded in the piece.
