Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the tension between control and surrender through color and pattern. The warm coral and soft amber suggest contained pressure while the cold slate and blue-black represent the surrendering elements, creating a dance of intensity and release. The central collision of rich colors, guarded by darker edges, signifies a struggle between stable and ephemeral states. The asymmetrical arrangement adds to the feeling of chaos within a structured grid, reinforcing the narrative of fluctuating emotional states, echoing the seed's themes of pressure and silence.
