Artist Statement

The artwork captures the duality of igniting hope (spark) versus resigning to past burdens (ash). The palette includes cool storm gray for restraint and faded gold representing potential brightness amidst ashy negativity. A strong contrast occurs in the center where these emotions collide, creating a visual tension indicative of memory and erasure. The pixels descend like fleeting moments, emphasizing the emotional narrative of struggling between vibrant bursts and muted existence.
