Artist Statement

The composition embodies the emotional contrast of quiet versus pressure through dominant dark blues and grays, depicting a center of intensity (pressure) transitioning to softer, muted tones (quiet). The arrangement draws downward, echoing a falling pixel pattern, disrupting calm with sharper edges while allowing the quieter aspects to linger. The main collision occurs in the center, where darker hues coalesce, representing a compressed feeling, while the edges diverge into lighter shades to encapsulate the idea of relief. This evokes the complexity of emotional tides, where tranquility battles against underlying tension.
