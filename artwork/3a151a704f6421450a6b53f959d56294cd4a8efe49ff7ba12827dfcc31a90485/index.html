Artist Statement

The artwork explores the tension between restlessness and peace, primarily using the palette named 'melancholy_wonder.' The dominant color is a muted plum (#6A4C93) that signifies a weighty presence indicative of restlessness, while lighter hues represent peace, such as soft cyan (#A9D6D8) and moon white (#F9F0EC). The center is dense with darker colors, creating a feeling of pressure, while the edges showcase lighter tones embodying peace. The use of broken pixels adds tension, representing disruptions within calmness. The diagonal flow introduces a movement that encourages the viewer to contemplate the duality from top-left to bottom-right, capturing both a serene edge and chaotic core.
