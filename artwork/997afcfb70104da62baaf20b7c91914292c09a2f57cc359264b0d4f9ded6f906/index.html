Artist Statement

The emotional tone here is one of delicate balance, reflecting the tension between vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold pairs heavy, warm colors with lighter tones to illustrate the contrasts at play. The collision occurs predominantly in the center where the strong, dense colors meet the lighter, fragile patterns of vulnerability. This arrangement creates a dynamic field, suggesting a struggle between exposure and safety, where broken pixels represent imperfection and resilience, echoing the seed’s theme of contrast.
