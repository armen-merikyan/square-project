Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between fear and hope through its dense vs. empty contrast. The left side uses dark colors evoking fear with dense arrangements, jagged edges, and a pattern that feels like static—chaotic and heavy. The right side shifts to soft gold and pale green, suggesting hope; the grid pattern creates a rising light effect. The collision occurs where the two emotional states meet, with broken pixels introducing an element of imperfection, enhancing the narrative of struggle and resilience.
