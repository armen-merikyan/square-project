Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between quiet and pressure, with the palette featuring muted tones of off-white, dust gray, and faint peach contrasted against deeper shades of compressed red and dark navy. The clustered pixels at the top left corner symbolize pressure, creating a collision with expansive quiet that spreads throughout the rest of the grid. This movement allows for both density and a release of tension across the square, mirroring the themes in the seed text.
