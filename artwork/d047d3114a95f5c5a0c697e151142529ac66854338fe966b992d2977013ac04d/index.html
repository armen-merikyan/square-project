Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a struggle between love and betrayal, represented through contrasting colors of warmth and darkness. The palette of pink and dark maroon symbolizes love's warmth while introducing elements of cold gray to depict betrayal, resulting in tension. The layout employs a split vertical structure, creating a feeling of disconnection. The placement of soft pinks on one side clashes with the harshness of dark colors, fostering a clash of emotions, while the 'broken pixels' add an imperfection that mirrors emotional turbulence.
