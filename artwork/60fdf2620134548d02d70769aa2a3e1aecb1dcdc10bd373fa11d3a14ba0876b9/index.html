Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the tension between safety and exposure through a palette that combines 'shelter_green' with 'open_blue', representing safety and exposure respectively. The collision occurs on the right side of the grid where cooler hues (the echo of exposure) intermingle with warmer tones, suggesting a nuanced emotional narrative. The hollow center acts as a vessel, emphasizing the contrast between dense colors around it and negative space, embodying a feeling of soft severity and gentle weight. The artwork honors the idea of the pressure border influencing the rhythm of color placement, with broken pixels lending an imperfect, intentional texture that echoes vulnerability.
