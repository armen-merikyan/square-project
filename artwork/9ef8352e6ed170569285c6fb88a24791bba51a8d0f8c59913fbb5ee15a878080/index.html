Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between defiance (expressed through bright coral and blood orange accents) and submission (seen in the softer pinks and dark charcoal). The palette evokes a sense of desire intertwined with emptiness. The central collision, where the vibrant colors clash with muted tones, forms a visual tension that resonates with the themes of signal vs. noise and inward spirals. The layout mirrors on either side but is disrupted by broken pixels, embodying the idea of a door being both an entry and a barrier, provoking thought without literal depiction.
