Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a dance between vulnerability and strength, embodied through a palette that contrasts warm browns and greens with cool whites and blues, suggesting a narrative of safety layered with exposure. The collision occurs in the center, where strength dominates, creating a broken frame of resilience against the more vulnerable scar-like patterns on the edges. This arrangement reflects the push and pull of emotions highlighting both the sensation of protection and fragility, ensuring the 8x8 structure evokes depth and a quiet tension, inviting contemplation.
