Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between loneliness and connection through a contrasting palette of deep navy and delicate white. The upper rows reflect a sense of isolation with staggered placements reminiscent of stair patterns, while the bottom rows feature a structured checker pattern embodying connection. The flow shifts diagonally from the top left to bottom right, suggesting movement from solitude to community. Broken pixels inject imperfection, adding depth and evoking memories that tether the emotions of the piece, encapsulating the push and pull of personal experiences.
