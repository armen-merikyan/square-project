Artist Statement

This piece conveys the tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors and areas of density. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of nostalgia and fragility, reflecting the emotional undertone of 'broken memory.' The main collision occurs at the center with a bright cluster of color surrounded by darker tones, visually representing the struggle between safety (vulnerability) and exposure (strength). The pattern mimics a heart fragment while maintaining an abstract feel, ensuring clarity even in its emotional complexity.
