Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a struggle between restlessness and peace, illustrated through jagged edges and broken pixels. The palette, a blend of vibrant yellows and greens against muted earth tones, accentuates tension and balance, embodying the conflict of memory and motion. The main collision occurs in the top left corner, where the intense colors 'spill' into the calmer sections, creating dynamism amidst quieter hues. This reflects the core seed themes as abstract qualities, emphasizing the tension between containment and overflow.
