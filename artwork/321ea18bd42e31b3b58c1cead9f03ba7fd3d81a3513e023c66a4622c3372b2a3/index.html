Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between loneliness and connection, illustrated through a contrast of dense clusters and negative space. The chosen palette of deep blacks and soft pale blues creates a visual tension, with black symbolizing the weight of loneliness and pale blue embodying fleeting connections. The main collision occurs in the corners, where brighter elements disrupt darker background, conveying moments of connection trying to emerge from isolation. The arrangement of pixels resembles falling droplets, hinting at emotional weight leaving negative space in between, enhancing the abstract nature of this piece.
