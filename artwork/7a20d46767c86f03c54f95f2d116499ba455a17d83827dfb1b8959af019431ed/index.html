Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional conflict of panic and control through a carefully chosen palette of pinks, reds, dark maroons, and grays. The warm tones suggest an intense emotional heat, representing panic, while the cooler grays offer a counterbalance of control. The pixel bleed edge style enhances the feeling of imperfection, mirroring the chaotic interplay between two patterns: a flame representing urgency and fiery emotion, conflicting against a stair pattern that suggests ascendance and stability. This piece creates a visual tension, with abrupt interruptions and asymmetries that reflect the struggle of maintaining composure amidst chaos.
