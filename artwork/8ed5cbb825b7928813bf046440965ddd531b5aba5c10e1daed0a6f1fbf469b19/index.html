Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured here is one of tension between clarity and confusion, represented using a palette that juxtaposes calmness with vibrancy. The choice of black and dark violet delivers a sense of depth while the soft gold and pale green introduce moments of brightness, echoing the contrast between ordered symmetry and unexpected ruptures. The collision occurs between the heavy darks and the light accents, manifesting the struggle to find balance amidst discord. The broken pixels accentuate this imperfection, amplifying the erratic rhythm produced by the alternating patterns. Overall, the arrangement presents a narrative of conflict and resolution through color placement and symmetry, echoing the core message of the seed.
