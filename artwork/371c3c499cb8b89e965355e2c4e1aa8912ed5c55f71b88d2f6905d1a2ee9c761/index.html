Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between softness and severity through contrasting tones and visual collisions. The color palette juxtaposes warm pinks and whites against sharp blacks and grays, embodying gentle versus harsh elements. The central pixels dominate the grid, evoking heaviness and gravity while allowing lightness to break through at intervals, symbolizing moments of reflection amidst pressure. The patterns merge in a spiral, suggesting a counterclockwise flow—a dynamic of release and resistance. Broken pixels add intentional disruptions, creating a sense of imperfection that resonates with the emotional weight of guilt and forgiveness.
