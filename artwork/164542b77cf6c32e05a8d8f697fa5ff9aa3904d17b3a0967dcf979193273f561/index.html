Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dichotomy between bright sparks of vitality and the muted palette of ash, symbolizing hunger against restraint. The palette of soft pink and harsh crimson contrasts with deep grays, emphasizing the tension between warmth and coolness. The layout is split vertically, with dynamic patterns of 'islands' representing sparks of connection on the left, colliding with quiet fields of ash on the right, allowing for a visual representation of conflict and balance. The main collision occurs near the center, allowing both themes to intertwine, yet maintain a distinct identity.
