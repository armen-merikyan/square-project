Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrast between dread and relief through a carefully chosen palette that reflects a spectrum from deep stormy hues to lighter, calming shades. The density of the storm gray and near black at the top corner reflects a sense of dread that gradually loosens into pale mint and soft sky, suggesting a release. The radial pattern transitioning into ripples symbolizes the push and pull of emotion, with pixel disruptions acting as scars of experience amidst the chaos. The main contrast occurs at the top left, where chaos gives way to a more serene right side, mirroring the journey from pressure to relief.
