Artist Statement

The composition encapsulates the emotional tension between love and betrayal. The warm pinks and dark maroons interact to portray the contrasts inherent in passion and deceit. The arrangement features stripes representing love, layered against dots illustrating betrayal, with an emphasis on negative space to showcase loneliness and abandonment. This layout mirrors the empty room concept, where the central clash of colors introduces a feeling of unresolved tension, symbolizing the struggles of inner vs. outer conflicts.
