Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve, depicting an inner landscape of contrast and conflict. The palette, consisting of lime, black, electric blue, and magenta, embodies a spectrum of intensity, with lime signifying vibrant hope, while black anchors the piece in profundity. The central collision occurs where the dense clusters of electric blue pulse marks interrupt the quieter expanses of lime and black, simulating the sense of an unresolved journey. This tumultuous engagement reflects the emotional journey articulated in the seed — a search for intimate connection against the backdrop of firm boundaries and chaos. The structured placement of colors underscores the push and pull of longing and resolve, allowing the viewer to sense the depths of each feeling in oscillating patterns.
