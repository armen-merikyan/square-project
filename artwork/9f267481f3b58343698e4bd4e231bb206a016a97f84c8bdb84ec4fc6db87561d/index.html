Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional interplay between certainty and curiosity through a structured yet interrupted pattern. The chosen palette of off-white and dark navy creates a quiet intensity, while subtle shifts from pale peach to compressed red accentuate moments of tension and curiosity. The main contrast lies at the center, where radial patterns echo certainty, colliding with channel-like interruptions that suggest curiosity. This structure symbolizes the tension between order and chaos, embodying the seed's essence in an abstract framework.
