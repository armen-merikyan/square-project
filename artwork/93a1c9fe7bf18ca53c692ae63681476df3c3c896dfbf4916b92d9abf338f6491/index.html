Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between fleeting brightness and enduring ashes. The palette, influenced by the contrasting warmth of 'spark' (represented by bright yellows) and the muted earthiness of 'ash' (gray tones), creates an underlying rhythm. The dominant warm yellows (60%) clash with quiet grays (40%), symbolizing the internal struggle of resilience against fragility. The collision occurs in the center of the square, where bright hues disrupt the cooler tones, echoing the fight between memory and erasure. The broken pixel aspect offers subtle imperfections, hinting at the scars left behind in this emotional landscape.
