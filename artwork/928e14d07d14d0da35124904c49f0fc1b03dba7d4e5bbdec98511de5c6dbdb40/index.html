Artist Statement

This composition channels the tension between 'emptiness' and 'desire' through a warm vs cool palette, emphasizing contrasts. The top four rows represent desire with warm golds and soft greens in a checker pattern, suggesting movement and pull. The bottom four rows depict emptiness through muted blacks and dark violets in a maze formation, evoking a sense of quiet isolation. The interaction between the two halves creates a feeling of conflict and balance, while broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, reflecting the complexity of emotions.
