Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between trust and suspicion. The palette of dark mourning black, deep blue, and the soft pale yellow reflects the gravity of suspicion against the warmth of trust. The collision occurs diagonally, where the static pattern symbolizing suspicion interacts with the rain-like flow of trust. The hollow center suggests both fragility and resilience, embodying a split between chaos and order, with broken pixels creating imperfections that enrich the narrative of uncertainty.
