Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the composition reflects the dualities of loneliness and connection. The chosen palette—dark violet representing loneliness and soft gold symbolizing the warmth of connection—contrasts the oppressive weight of dark tones with the brightness of lighter hues. This tension creates a feeling of pressure within the structured grid. The layout employs a dense outer layer with soft edges to represent protection against the harshness of loneliness while allowing for a central zone of calm, represented by lighter colors. The use of broken pixels introduces feelings of imperfection and tension. The mixture of striped patterns (representing loneliness) against chaotic noise (for connection) forms a visual narrative that embodies the push and pull of emotional experiences.
