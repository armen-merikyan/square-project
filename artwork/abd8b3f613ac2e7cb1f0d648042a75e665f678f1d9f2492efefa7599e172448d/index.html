Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the tension between longing and resolve, with the left side dominated by a stair-like pattern of deep blacks and vibrant greens, representing yearning and instability. The right side contrasts this with broken dots of electric blues and static grays, suggesting clarity and strength. The collision occurs in the vertical split, where longing meets resolve, providing a visual representation of the emotional struggle. The inclusion of broken pixels symbolizes the scars and repairs inherent in the process of finding balance.
