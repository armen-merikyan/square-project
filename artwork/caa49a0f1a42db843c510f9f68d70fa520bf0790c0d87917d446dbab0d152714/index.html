Artist Statement

This composition plays on the tension between quiet and pressure, with a dominant presence of dark navy to evoke a sense of weight and compression on the left side. The right side, marked by faint peach and dust gray, offers a sense of lightness and space. The collision occurs diagonally, where the crosshatch patterns of the compressed red introduce an interruption within the calm pulse marks of the quiet side, creating a dynamic shift. The broken pixels add intentional disruptions, enhancing the theme of shattering against the tide of calming space. This piece illustrates the interplay of forces, embodying the abstract dialogue suggested by the seed.
