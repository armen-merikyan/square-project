Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a push and pull between longing and resolve, creating a dynamic landscape of color. The palette of deep blacks and soft pale blues suggests emotional depth, countering the stark reality of reds and whites that represent firm boundaries or resolutions. This interplay creates a stark contrast; longing is represented with smoother transitions (pale blue), while resolve pushes through with sharp incidences of red and black. The central collision occurs towards the center where the colors intersect and overlap amidst negative space, embodying the tension captured in the seed.
