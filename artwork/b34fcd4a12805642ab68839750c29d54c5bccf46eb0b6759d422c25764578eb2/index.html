Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional conflict between defiance and submission, utilizing a palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple to represent warmth and coldness, joy and grief. The color choice captures both vital energy and subdued tones, creating a dynamic interplay. The composition's bottom weighting with gradients leads the viewer’s eye upward through contrasts of density and openness, suggesting a struggle between chaos and calmness. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, further emphasizing the emotional tension inherent in the seed's narrative.
