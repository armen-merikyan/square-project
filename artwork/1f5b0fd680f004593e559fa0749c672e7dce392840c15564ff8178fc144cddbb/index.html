Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the juxtaposition between confusion and clarity, captured through fragmented symmetry. The palette of reds and grays symbolizes intensity and depth, with darkness representing confusion and clarity interspersed within the chaotic array. The layout allows pixels to flow downward, mirroring a cascade of thoughts—some aligned, others broken. The use of thick outlines creates a stark division, emphasizing the tension between stability and disruption, while the specks of broken pixels introduce imperfection to the otherwise structured form.
