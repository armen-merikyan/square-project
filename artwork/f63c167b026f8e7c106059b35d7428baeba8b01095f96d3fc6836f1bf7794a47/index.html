Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a clash between the intensity of rage and the serenity of calm, represented through a palette of stark contrasts: saturated reds and soft blues. The arrangement features jagged, glitch-like patterns emanating from the center, symbolizing explosive emotion, while cooler tones flow steadily around them, portraying a quiet field of resolution. This collision of emotion is emphasized by an 'island and sea' layout, where the red pixels disrupt the tranquil blue field, creating a dynamic visual tension.
