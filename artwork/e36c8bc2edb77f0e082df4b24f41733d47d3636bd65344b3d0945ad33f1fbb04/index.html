Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between clarity and chaos, with the signal represented by a vibrant green accenting through a dynamic interface of static gray and muted heats of hot magenta. The palette selection emphasizes the clash of readable rhythms against disruptive echoes. The collision occurs predominantly in the center, where sharper edges sharply interrupt the softer static fields, symbolizing the struggle between the signal and noise as it seeks a thread of coherence within the dissonance.
