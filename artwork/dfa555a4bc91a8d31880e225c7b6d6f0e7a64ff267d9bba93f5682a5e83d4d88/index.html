Artist Statement

This composition juxtaposes the emotion of warmth and vitality from 'spark' with the stoic stillness of 'ash'. The top half features warm, vibrant hues representing sparks of energy and hope, while the bottom half showcases the cooler grays and blues symbolizing restraint and reflection. The palette, comprising shades like warm_shadow and cool_blue, creates a striking contrast, punctuated by 'broken pixels' to signify interruption—the trauma of memory fading into erasure. The defined split layout enhances the visual dialogue, inviting contemplation of resilience against fragility. The bright central pixels echo the clash of these emotions, forming a dynamic tension across the piece, while leaving quiet spaces to emphasize emotional weight.
