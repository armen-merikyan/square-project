Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional conflict between panic and control by layering dense patterns against spaces of quiet. The navy and black establish a foundation of introspection, while dim cyan delivers flashes of urgency like sparks. The central checkered pattern suggests control, juxtaposed with chaotic crosshatches that evoke a sense of instability. The overall layout plays with negative space, emphasizing the struggle between the enclosed and the expansive, embodying a subtle cosmic dance, where clusters of color collide against emptiness.
