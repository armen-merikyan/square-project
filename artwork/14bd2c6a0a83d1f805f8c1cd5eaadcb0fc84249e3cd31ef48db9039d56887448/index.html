Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between love and betrayal, symbolized by a stark contrast in color arrangements. The palette leans into warm reds and soft pinks to echo love, juxtaposed with dark grays and maroons representing betrayal. The sharp vs. soft contrast is evident in the placement of vibrant colors against muted backgrounds, while a diagonal rise suggests a longing or upward struggle. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, invoking the emotional fractures inherent in relationships, suggesting echoes of hope amidst confusion.
