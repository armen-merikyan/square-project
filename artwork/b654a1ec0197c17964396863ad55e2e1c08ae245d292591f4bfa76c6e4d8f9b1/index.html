Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional pull of 'emptiness' contrasted against 'desire'. The outer border represents a sense of cool detachment with dark shades, framing a more vibrant core of warmth where desires collide. The use of pixel patterns—stripes for emptiness and dots for desire—creates a visual tension, suggesting movement from cool, stark edges toward a central burst of warmth. This reflects the rising versus falling dynamic as colors ebb and flow, interspersed with negative space to symbolize isolation, punctuated by warmer tones that express longing.
