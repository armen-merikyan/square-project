Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between longing and resolve. The palette transitions from pale aqua to alarm red, suggesting an interplay of serenity and urgency. The central collision is marked by a subtle shift from ink blue to clear white, embodying the push and pull of nascent hope against the backdrop of subdued yearning. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, creating a sense of narrative depth without defining forms. The pattern balance—between split screen for longing and braiding for resolve—visually represents conflicted emotions in a seemingly contained framework, echoing the 'open gate' layout that invites confrontation of these feelings.
