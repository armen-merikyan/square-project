Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between quietude and pressure. The palette presents a muted field with an emphasis on off-white and faint peach, representing serenity, contrasted by dark navy and compressed red, embodying intensity. The main collision occurs toward the center, where pressure bubbles up, while softer hues dominate the borders. This arrangement reflects the seed’s themes of conflict and relief, shaping an abstract structure that mirrors a struggle of density dispersing into calm.
