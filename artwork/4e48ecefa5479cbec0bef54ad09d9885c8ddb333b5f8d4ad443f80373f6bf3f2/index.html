Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional clash between restlessness and peace, employing a palette that reflects the warmth of soft gold and ash gray to represent tranquility, contrasted with the deep wine and stain brown denoting turmoil. The main collision occurs in the center, where mirror patterns intermingle with a rain-like sequence, reflecting the tension of memory and motion. The diagonal argument of contrasting colors suggests a battle between the urge to move and the desire for stillness.
