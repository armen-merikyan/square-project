Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts longing for resolve amidst broken dreams and urgency. The palette combines lime and magenta for vibrancy, punctuated against the calmness of black, mirroring the desire for calm amidst chaos. The main collision occurs at the center, where intense colors disrupt quieter sections, creating a sense of movement and tension, reflecting the seed's themes of pulse and interruption through jagged edges and broken forms.
