Artist Statement

This composition evokes a tension between longing and resolve. The palette of muted cream, iron gray, red oxide, blackened plum, and small orange reflects a struggle for union. The edge style, described as 'pressure border', introduces contrast with a softness at the center representing longing, while assertive colors at the periphery suggest resolve. The defined halo void layout emphasizes the separated emotional layers, cultivating a visual dichotomy between quiet spaces and denser clusters of color. Broken pixels represent the imperfections of thought, akin to scars and indicative of a path towards repair.
