Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between nostalgia and progress through a split horizontal layout. The top half utilizes soft, muted tones of sepia and faded blue to evoke a sense of nostalgia, while the bottom half contrasts with sharper, more intense shades of gray, suggesting a sense of urgency and the forward motion of progress. The light_vs_dark contrast creates a palpable divide, symbolizing the push and pull of memory against action. The placement of colors mimics a falling pixel effect, drawing the eye downward, revealing layers of emotional depth that oscillate between calm and intensity. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, resonating with the theme of fragmented memories permeating through the linearity of progress. Overall, this composition tells the story of a struggle between holding onto the past and pushing forward into the future.
