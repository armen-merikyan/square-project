Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the push and pull between longing and resolve, creating a dense center with quiet edges. The chosen palette of pale aqua, mist gray, alarm red, ink blue, and clear white reflects serenity intertwined with urgency. The main collision happens in the center where deep colors overlap and disrupt the softer borders, symbolizing the tension between chaos and order. This pattern aligns emotionally with the seed's themes, illustrating a dynamic interplay where density meets emptiness, forming a visual narrative of conflict and resolution.
