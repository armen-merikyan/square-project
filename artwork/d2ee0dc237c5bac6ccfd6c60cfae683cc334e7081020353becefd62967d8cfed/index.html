Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional interplay between softness and severity. The palette, featuring powder pink and warm white, represents softness, while sharp crimson and carbon black introduce severity. The center of the piece is saturated with sharp contrasts, creating a collision where warmth meets cold, emphasizing tension. The looped flow from corner to corner creates a spiral visual dynamic, echoing a sense of confinement and expansion. This follows from the seed's influence, which suggests themes of push and pull, light versus dark, and the interplay of guilt and forgiveness manifesting through abstract pixel placement.
