Artist Statement

This artwork juxtaposes the emotional tones of joy and grief, using a balanced yet intentionally fractured arrangement to highlight the contrast. The palette features vibrant yellows and oranges representing joy, contrasting with deep blues and muted purples that speak to grief. The left-weighted structure hints at a burden or asymmetry, while the central colors act as a pulse of calm amidst turbulence, emphasizing the collision of emotions. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfection, reflecting the plight of the emotional experience.
