Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the dichotomy between longing and resolve through the interplay of colors. The palette, dominated by dark navy and muted cyan, evokes a sense of depth and introspection, suggesting a longing illuminated by the faintest notes of light. The use of black and dim cyan creates tension, resonating with the stark contrasts in the emotional landscape. The edges are both defined and fragmented, reminiscent of a broken mirror where reflections of desire and certainty collide. The central collision happens in the middle rows, where dense clusters of color represent the struggle of unresolved emotions, against a backdrop of negative space that breathes serenity. Patterned scars and constellations thread through the square, illustrating the beauty of imperfection and the delicate balance of feeling. Overall, the composition invites the viewer to dwell in the space between longing and resolve, where each shade tells a part of the intertwined story.
