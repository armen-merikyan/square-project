Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the battle between confusion and clarity, with top rows embodying clarity through structured patterns and a cool palette, while the bottom rows illustrate confusion using labyrinthine patterns and darker hues. The palette of navy, dim cyan, and fine whites signifies isolation against a backdrop of disturbance, while the vertical collision of structured lines versus chaotic breaks creates a narrative of tension and release. The broken pixels lend a nuanced imperfection, reinforcing the struggle between symmetry and disruption.
