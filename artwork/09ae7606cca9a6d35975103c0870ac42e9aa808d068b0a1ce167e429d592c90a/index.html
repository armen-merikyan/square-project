Artist Statement

This composition embodies the clash between defiance and submission through its color block arrangement. The warm tones of rose clay and lamp gold suggest strength and assertiveness, while the cool blues and distant lilacs offer a sense of softness and retreat. The left half resonates with defiance, utilizing a tide pattern with a brighter color palette, while the right half reflects submission, with an echo pattern that creates a more muted atmosphere. The central collision is characterized by the sharp alteration of colors, representing the tension between chaotic energy and the quiet longing for closure, resulting in a pulse of emotions throughout the abstract grid.
