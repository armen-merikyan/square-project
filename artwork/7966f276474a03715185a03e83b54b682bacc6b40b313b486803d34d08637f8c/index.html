Artist Statement

This artwork juxtaposes the emotions of fear and hope, symbolized by dark colors representing fear and lighter tones suggesting hope. The center presents a soft gold pixel, breaking through the dense black and dark violet edges, embodying the tension between pressure and release. The jagged edges of black create a sense of unease, while the golden central pixel offers a focal point of warmth and aspiration. The use of stripes and dots gives rhythm to the arrangement, enhancing the collision of emotional states.
