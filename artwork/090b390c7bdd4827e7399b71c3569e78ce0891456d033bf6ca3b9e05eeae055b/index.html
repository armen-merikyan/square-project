Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'longing' suggests a yearning presence, utilizing muted greens to convey calm and distant warmth, with active broken pixels creating a sense of imperfection. In contrast, 'resolve' introduces saturated honey and olive tones, depicting strength and clarity, colliding with the more fluid longing. The palette evokes feelings of gratitude and envy as they meet, with one corner dominated by muted green patterns flowing towards the center where a vibrant clash occurs. This asymmetry invites the viewer to explore the balance between longing and resolve, resembling a vessel striving for completeness, layered with echoes of both feelings.
