Artist Statement

The artwork explores the interplay between defiance and submission through contrasting colors and shapes. The warm palette of burnt orange and dark red symbolizes defiance, while the cooler charcoal represents submission. The arrangement emphasizes a top-down flow, with denser color blocks at the bottom suggesting weight and an undercurrent of pressure. Broken pixels introduce imperfection and echo the emotional turmoil. The interplay of dots creates visual noise, mirroring the conflict inherent in the seed.
