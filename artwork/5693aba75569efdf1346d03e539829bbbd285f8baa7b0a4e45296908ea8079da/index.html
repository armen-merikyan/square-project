Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between control and surrender through a palette of deep reds and soft blues, symbolizing the heat of struggle against the coolness of yielding. The central clash occurs with a burst of intense color, depicting the overwhelming pressure in contrast to calm borders, marking a clear interruption in the flow of serenity and chaos. The stair-step pattern suggests movement between levels of pressure, while the uneven distribution creates a sense of dynamic tension, reflective of the stutter-step struggle portrayed in the seed.
