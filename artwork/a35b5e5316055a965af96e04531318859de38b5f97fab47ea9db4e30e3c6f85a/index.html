Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional struggle between love and betrayal through contrasting colors and patterns. The warm shades of pink and red echo love, while the dark maroon and gray emphasize betrayal, creating a visual conflict. The use of rings represents the hopeful impulses of love, while dots signify the fractures of betrayal. The asymmetry and broken pixels add to the theme of disruption, evoking a space filled with unresolved emotion. The canvas is left with negative space, inviting reflection amidst the chaos.
