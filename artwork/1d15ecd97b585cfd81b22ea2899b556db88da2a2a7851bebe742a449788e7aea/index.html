Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dichotomy of loneliness and connection, using a palette that combines the vibrancy of magenta and electric blue against the depth of black and lime. The stark contrast creates collisions of feeling, with electrifying spots representing connection amidst a backdrop of loneliness depicted by negative space. The top-weighted layout instills a sense of gravity, echoing the tension of finding connection within isolation. The network-like burst of pixels mimics a crosshatching texture that encapsulates the shared yet fragmented nature of human experience.
