Artist Statement

This composition explores the juxtaposition of joy and grief through color placement, where a warm palette representing joy collides with cooler tones of grief. The arrangement creates a subtle wave-like flow, reflecting the push and pull of these emotions. The central rows hold denser warm colors transitioning into muted tones that draw the eye downwards, invoking a sense of heaviness versus uplift. The scattered broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the emotional dissonance without defining a literal shape.
