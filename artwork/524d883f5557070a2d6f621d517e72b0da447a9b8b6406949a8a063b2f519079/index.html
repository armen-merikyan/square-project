Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a volatile tension between rage and calm, evoking chaotic energy alongside serene containment. The palette features vibrant reds against subdued pale blues and blacks, symbolizing the interplays of anger and tranquility. The contrast peaks where the jagged reds cut across the soft horizontal bands of blue, representing the interruption of calm by surging emotions. This visual story is shaped from the seed's essence through a rhythmic pulse of stacked bands, fostering a sense of collision.
