Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the tension between relief and dread through a stark contrast of light and dark colors, suggesting layers of emotion. The use of lime and electric blue provides vibrant accents that struggle against the weight of black and dark tones, representing the emotional dichotomy. The arrangement allows for a central focus with the brighter colors, offset by surrounding darker hues that evoke feelings of dread. This 'center_seed' layout highlights the battle within the emotional spectrum, while the broken pixels introduce imperfections, enhancing the narrative of struggle and interruption. The maze pattern of dread is subtly echoed by the noise pattern of relief, creating a rhythmic interplay across the square.
