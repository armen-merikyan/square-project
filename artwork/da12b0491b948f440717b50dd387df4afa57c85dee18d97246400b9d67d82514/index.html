Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of love and betrayal, with a palette of warm pinks and deep maroons contrasting against cool gray shades. This interplay suggests both intimacy and discord—warm hues flow from the bottom to top, embodying rising hope, while sporadic dark accents and gray clusters interrupt this flow, creating collisions of feeling. The pattern is driven by the notion of imperfection, with broken pixels subtly integrated to enhance the abstract storytelling.
