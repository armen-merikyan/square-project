Artist Statement

The artwork conveys the contrasting emotions of tenderness and rage through the use of a saturated color palette, predominantly featuring deep reds and muted blues. The patterns of jagged grids symbolize rage while soft textures suggest tenderness, creating a dynamic tension. The central collision of colors represents the intense emotional struggle where the fiery core of rage disrupts the surrounding calmness, demonstrating the interplay of contrasting feelings as described in the seed.
