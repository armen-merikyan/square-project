Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between control and surrender, captured through a palette of soft pinks and intense crimson, suggesting conflict and contrast. The arrangement emphasizes a dense center, symbolizing an overflow of emotion, surrounded by borders that dissipate into lighter hues like warm white, echoing a gentle release. The patterns of 'pressure_band' and 'tide' visually collide in the center, representing the tension of control against the yielding of surrender. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions to symbolize imperfections in this emotional journey.
