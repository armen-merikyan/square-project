Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between longing (soft hues, open spaces) and resolve (strong, defined boundaries). The palette combines clean white and ice blue to signify clarity and calmness, with ink black and warning orange representing chaos and urgency. The contrast occurs centrally where the soft and dense collide, enhancing the narrative of crossing emotions. The use of broken pixels adds intentional disruption, allowing feelings of both intimacy and distance to emerge within the structured grid.
