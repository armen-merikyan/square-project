Artist Statement

This abstract composition reflects the tension between loneliness and connection through contrasting patterns and colors. The top-weighted layout symbolizes pressure while emphasizing the solitary central seed, represented by a deep sepia. The pale, faded blues create a haunting echo, representing connection yet feeling distant. The crosshatching evokes feelings of isolation, while the glowing edges introduce a subtle warmth, suggesting hope amidst the loneliness. The carefully placed negative space adds to the sense of isolation, offering a visual reprieve. The palette draws from 'broken memory,' reinforcing the theme of nostalgia and emotional conflict.
