Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional collision of joy and grief, represented via hints of structured versus chaotic contrasts in color placement. The warm tones of yellow and orange suggest joy, while the deep blue and muted purple bring forth the heaviness of grief. The arrangement showcases a clean, orderly progression on one side while breaking into more chaotic patterns on the other, illustrating the push and pull of these emotions. Each broken pixel adds to the imperfection, enhancing the narrative of pressure and release.
