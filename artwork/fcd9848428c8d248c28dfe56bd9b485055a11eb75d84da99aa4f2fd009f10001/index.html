Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between restlessness and peace. The palette combines warm tones (hot coral and blood orange) for intensity and cool hues (charcoal and hollow blue) for calm. The dance between dense clusters at the left and a lighter field towards the right creates a diagonal argument, symbolizing struggle and resolution. The chosen edge style of open corners suggests movement and release, while the broken pixels interrupt the flow, embodying the chaotic yet hopeful journey of memory and motion.
