Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of 'spark' versus 'ash.' The palette combines soft mint with storm gray to represent resilience against fragility. The contrast of colors intensifies at the center, reflecting a collision of emotions as vibrant colors (representing spark) intermingle with muted tones (representing ash). Each quadrant features a recognizable pattern that disrupts the viewer's eye, symbolizing the tension present in the seed's narrative. The jagged edges of the central shape evoke memory and the scars of experience, while the outer pixels provide a calming frame that hints at rest and restraint. Negative space emphasizes the contrast, allowing the central clash to resonate powerfully against the surrounding calm.
