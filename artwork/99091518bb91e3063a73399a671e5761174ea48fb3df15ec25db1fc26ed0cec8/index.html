Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the struggle between restlessness and peace. The color palette includes soft mint and sky against storm gray and near black, representing calmness battling dark turmoil. The patterns of channels (representing restlessness) collide softly with fault lines (embodying peace) towards the edges, suggesting a dynamic yet controlled interaction. Color placement emphasizes a flow from dense dark to lighter hues, with negative space allowing for breathing room amidst turmoil. The collision occurs mainly at the center and towards the upper left, illustrating the tension between motion and stillness.
