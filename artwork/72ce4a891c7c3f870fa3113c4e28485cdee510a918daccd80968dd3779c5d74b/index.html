Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between control and surrender. The selected palette of muted hues contrasts with saturated accents, illustrating the dynamics of pressure vs release. The dark navy provides a controlled backdrop while the compressed red represents bursts of intense emotion at central intersections, symbolizing the 'overflow vs containment' theme. The two pulses of color evoke a feeling of stutter-step movement, conveying alternating rhythms of tension and release throughout the grid.
