Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle of clarity amidst chaos, with colors representing signals of hope merging into waves of noise. The palette combines vibrant greens and blues as signals, contrasted against shadows of gray and black that evoke static and interference. The main collision occurs in the center, where sharp magenta interruptions disrupt the signal's flow, symbolizing conflict. This abstract pattern shapes a narrative of seeking clarity in turbulent environments, resonating with the seed's themes of exposure and memory.
