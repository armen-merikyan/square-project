Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between rage and calm, embodying a clash where fiery reds encroach upon the cooler blues and whites, suggesting a struggle for dominance. The palette of red, pale blue, black, and white reflects this duality: red epitomizes anger while pale blue embraces tranquility. The primary contrast occurs at the top left where intense red, representing rage, collides with a softer blue, embodying calm. This collision and the jagged, shattering pattern of rage disrupt the regularity of the calmer, more contained lines, creating a visual representation of emotional struggle. The broken pixels add imperfections, enhancing the volatility and tension within this abstract setting.
