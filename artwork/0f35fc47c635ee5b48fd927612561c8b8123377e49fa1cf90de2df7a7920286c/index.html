Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between clarity and interference, with a vibrant pulse represented by the signal green cutting through the static gray and dead black. The layout evokes a rising signal, with broken pixels introducing intentional disruptions that symbolize scars of noise. The collision occurs centrally as elements of pattern intersect and blend, embodying an ongoing tension between order and chaos.
