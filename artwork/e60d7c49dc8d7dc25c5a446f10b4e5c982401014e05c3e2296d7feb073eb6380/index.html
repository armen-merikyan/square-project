Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of softness and severity, showcasing a stark contrast between dark and light. The palette blends powder pink and warm white with carbon black and sharp crimson, creating a visual tension that echoes the seed’s theme of collision between gentle openness and strict boundaries. The layout uses a border of severity surrounding a softer inner core, creating a palpable shift in the viewer's experience. The main contrast occurs at the center, representing a pulse where emotions collide and intertwine, suggesting dynamic interactions of forgiveness and guilt amidst open gates and spirals.
