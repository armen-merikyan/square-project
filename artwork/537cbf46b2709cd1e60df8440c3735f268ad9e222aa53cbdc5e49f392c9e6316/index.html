Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between restlessness and peace through contrasting patterns and colors. The palette, with its vibrant 'signal green' and 'hot magenta', suggests bursts of energy against the calming 'static gray' and deep 'dead black', embodying the clash of motion and stillness. The main collision occurs diagonally, representing a struggle as elements seem to burst forth while others recede, creating a sense of both escape and entrapment. This dynamic interplay reflects the seed's essence of conflicting emotions, leading to a visual narrative of fleeting calm amidst a backdrop of vibrant activity.
