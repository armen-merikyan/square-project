Artist Statement

The emotional tone is one of duality, capturing the tension between awe and terror. The palette intertwines dark navy and dim cyan to evoke a feeling of depth against stark white, suggesting both illumination and threat. The collision occurs at the center where brighter whites disrupt the heavy darkness, illustrating the struggle between stability and chaos. The vertical flow and soft edges hint at a wave-like movement, symbolizing the push and pull of these emotions while incorporating broken pixels to add an imperfection reflecting broken memories.
