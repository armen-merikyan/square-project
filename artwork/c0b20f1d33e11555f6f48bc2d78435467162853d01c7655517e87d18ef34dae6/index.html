Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between love and betrayal, represented by a dichotomy of warm and cool colors. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon suggests vibrancy and turmoil. The left side is dense with warm colors, symbolizing love, while the right side utilizes cooler grays and dark maroon to evoke feelings of betrayal and chaos. The collision occurs where the warmth of pink splinters into the darker shades, suggesting a break in harmony. The arrangement of repeating patterns signifies echoes of memory and emotional tension, creating an abstract representation of the alternating highs and lows of deeper relationships.
