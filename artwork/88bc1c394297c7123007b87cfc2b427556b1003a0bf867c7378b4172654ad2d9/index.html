Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a delicate balance between strength and vulnerability, evoking both stability and fragility. The chosen palette of off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach illustrates a spectrum of pressure, where muted hues suggest a quiet struggle and accent colors punctuate the tension. The center serves as a collision point where the density of dark navy meets the lighter hues, symbolizing the heart of vulnerability surrounded by protective strength. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, echoing the complexities of life’s battles. The abstract pattern transcends literal interpretation, instead exploring the nuanced emotions of safety and exposure.
