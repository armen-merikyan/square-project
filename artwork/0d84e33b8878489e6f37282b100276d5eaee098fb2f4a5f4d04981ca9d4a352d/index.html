Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette of muted purples, yellows, and deep blues. The collision manifests at the center where the 'strength' patterns dominate while the softer 'vulnerability' shades creep in from the corners, creating a shift from density to quiet space. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, mirroring life's fragility amidst resilience. The layout channels a diagonal invasion, emphasizing a strong foundation yet left open to exposure.
