Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between 'relief' and 'dread' through contrasting colors and patterns. The top rows, embodying relief, showcase warm burnt oranges and golds in smooth, ring-like patterns, suggesting warmth and safety. In contrast, the bottom rows representing dread incorporate dark reds and charcoals in chaotic noise patterns, evoking a sense of anxiety and depth. The layout is split horizontally, emphasizing the weight of dread against the lightness of relief, with a deliberate imbalance that enhances complexity. The arrangement culminates in a striking visual dialogue, reflecting a struggle between calmness and chaos, embodying the essence of the seed.
