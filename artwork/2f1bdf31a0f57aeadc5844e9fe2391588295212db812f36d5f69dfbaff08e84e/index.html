Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the push and pull between vulnerability and strength. The warm hues of red and dark maroon represent strength and rootedness, while soft pink and gray highlight vulnerability amidst the tension. The visual collision occurs on the diagonal, where the braided strength meets the flooded softness, creating a complex interplay that embodies both emotions. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, adding a layer of imperfection that mirrors the chaos of opposing feelings.
