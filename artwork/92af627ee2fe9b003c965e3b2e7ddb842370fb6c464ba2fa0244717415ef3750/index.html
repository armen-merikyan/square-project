Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity through a dynamic arrangement of colors. The palette – a mix of muted blues and stark blacks accented with warning orange – creates a contrast between stable and provocative thoughts. The main collision occurs at the center, where dense clusters of dark colors suggest firm boundaries while lighter colors spiral outwards, invoking space for exploration and questions. This reflects the seed's intangible concepts such as order against chaos and the rise of an inner signal amidst external distractions.
