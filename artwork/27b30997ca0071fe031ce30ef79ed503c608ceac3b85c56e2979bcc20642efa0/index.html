Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between safety and exposure, employing a palette that juxtaposes warm browns as a protective layer against cool whites that suggest vulnerability. The random distribution echoes a constant interplay, reflecting the theme of pressure versus release in the color placements. The central area, lighter and more open, introduces airy contrast, inviting contemplation while the darker edges provide a sense of encasement, creating a dynamic interplay of soft and severe. By leaving deliberate spaces, the composition maintains an emotional quietness amidst density, visualizing the duality of safety and exposure.
