Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between longing and resolve through a structured barcode pattern juxtaposed with broken, jagged edges. The palette, featuring electric blue and hot magenta, evokes a vivid contrast that represents emotional intensity. The main collision occurs in the center where the structured longing patterns intersect with irregular bits of muted gray, symbolizing damage and tension. This reflects the emotional theme of repair amid chaos, presenting serenity alongside urgency in its composition.
