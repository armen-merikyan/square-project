Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts pressure, depicted through dense clusters, against the quiet represented by soft backgrounds. The chosen palette mixes muted off-white and dust gray for subtlety, while compressed red and dark navy punctuate the canvas with sharp edges, suggesting tension. The main collision occurs along the diagonal axis, where these two emotional forces meet, embodying the struggle between calmness and chaos. This pattern reflects the seed's themes of counterflow and fragmentation while creating a dynamic rhythm.
