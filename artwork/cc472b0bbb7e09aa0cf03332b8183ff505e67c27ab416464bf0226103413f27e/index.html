Artist Statement

The artwork reflects the interplay between vulnerability and strength through a contrasting palette of warm and cool tones. The central area features a pale core, representing exposure, which is surrounded by deeper, more saturated colors that evoke shelter and strength. The main collision occurs diagonally, where intense shades meet softer fields, symbolizing the tension between safety and risk. This abstract representation embodies the emotional tension suggested by the seed, transforming it into a visual echo that speaks to both fragility and fortitude.
