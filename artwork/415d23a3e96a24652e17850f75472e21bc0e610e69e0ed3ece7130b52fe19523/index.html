Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between trust and suspicion, illustrated through an interplay of dark and light hues, symbolizing pressure and quietude. The palette features earthy greens and warm rust against cool grays, enhancing the contrast. The dominant dark central area reflects suspicion, juxtaposed with lighter edges signifying trust. The diagonal gradient serves as a visual metaphor for falling action—instability. Broken pixels disrupt uniformity, reflecting imperfections and uncertainty, creating a constant ebb and flow of emotional resonance. The grid's structure remains intact, yet offers a layered, complex visual experience.
