Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece navigates the duality of relief and dread through an interplay of colors and patterns. The chosen palette of soft sky and faded gold represents moments of relief, contrasting with storm gray and near black that evoke the heaviness of dread. The top half employs a lighter ash field pattern to symbolize the calming aspects of relief, while the bottom half contrasts this with a dense crosshatch, suggesting chaos. The main collision resides in the center, where the two emotional patterns intersect, creating a visual tension that embodies the theme of conflict and resolution.
