Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, reflecting an unfinished narrative of connection and disconnection. The palette blends earthy greens and warm golds, symbolizing a hopeful richness clashing with underlying unease. The main collision occurs in the center, where the deep olive and honey gold colors meet, representing the heart of the emotional struggle. Patterns of thread signify connections, while the flood elements invoke a steady flow, suggesting a push and pull between intensity and calm. The use of broken pixels in strategic places enhances the feeling of imperfection and disruption, echoing the theme of repair amidst damage.
