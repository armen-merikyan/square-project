Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional interplay between longing and resolve. The color palette consists of dark violet and pale green, juxtaposing warm and cool tones to create a tension that reflects a pulse of connection and fracture. The 'falling_pixels' layout features significant negative space, mimicking the distance and contained emotions inherent in the seed. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, serving as scars from past damages. The checker and ripple patterns interweave, demonstrating conflict between desire and determination. The central collision occurs in the middle where darker hues meet lighter accents, creating a visual weight that resonates deeply within the 8x8 grid.
