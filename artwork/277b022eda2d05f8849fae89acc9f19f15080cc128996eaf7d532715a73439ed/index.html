Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between softness and severity, utilizing a palette that juxtaposes warm whites and tender pastels with deep darks. The left side encapsulates a light, fluid softness with gradient colors, while the right showcases a dense, structured severity with stark contrasts. The main collision occurs centrally, where the soft hues gradually confront the harsh blacks and grays, suggesting a struggle and interplay of emotions. The arrangement allows for a gradual release to the viewer's eye, reflecting the 'open_gate' metaphor implied by the seed.
