Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between restlessness and peace, utilizing a palette of vibrant and muted tones to create a stark contrast. The chosen colors—lime, electric blue, and magenta—serve as dynamic accents, while the black acts as both a barrier and an anchor. The grid appears dense at central regions, symbolizing memory and motion clashing through the chaotic placement of the bright colors amidst the darker tones. The layout, inspired by a quiet channel, allows for visual pathways of light cutting through shadow, thus demonstrating the interplay of emotion encapsulated in the seed.
