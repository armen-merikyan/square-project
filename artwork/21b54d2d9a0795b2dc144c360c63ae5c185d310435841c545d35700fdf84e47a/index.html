Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing, represented by the cold depths of dark colors, and resolve, embodied by the structured, brighter hues that rise diagonally. The palette of navy, black, and dim cyan conveys a sense of melancholy yet stability, allowing the lighter accents to punctuate the heavy emotions. The main contrast occurs where the maze-like patterns of resolve push against the echoing gaps of longing, creating a visual and emotional echo chamber throughout the artwork. This dynamic illustrates the persistent struggle and interplay between these opposing feelings, embodied in an 8x8 format that suggests both intimacy and distance.
