Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between clarity and distortion, reflecting the battle between signal and noise. The palette blends dark navy and black as deep contrasts with the singular white accent, representing clarity amidst chaos. The layout introduces deliberate breaks, echoing the theme of exposure and shelter. The collision mainly occurs in the center where the 'signal' pattern of white gridlines intersects with the chaotic black noise, suggesting the struggle to emerge from confusion. Ultimately, the seed prompts a visual narrative of interruption and the fleeting clarity that struggles against overarching noise.
