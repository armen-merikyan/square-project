Artist Statement

The composition explores the interplay between pressure and quietness through contrasting textures and colors. The palette, dominated by muted tones like dust gray and faint peach, emphasizes a calm yet weighted atmosphere. Dark navy serves as a backdrop to showcase the tension of compressed red, representing pressure. The left side symbolizes quiet with soft patterns, while the right side erupts in sharper, more aggressive forms to create an emotional collision. This arrangement reflects the seed's narrative of contrasting feelings and the motion of shattering against stillness.
