Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional contrasts of pressure and release through a carefully selected palette. The deep navy and dim cyan evoke a sense of quiet introspection, while the harshness of black represents the weight of pressure. The arrangement of colors creates an asymmetric flow, suggesting sudden interruptions amidst a sense of calm. The split-screen pattern aligns with the theme of confusion against clarity, emphasizing a tension between dense clusters of color and negative space, signifying a moment of release from chaos.
