Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between softness and severity. The use of powder pink and warm white at the top invokes gentleness and flow, while the bottom half dives into carbon black and steel gray, embodying strictness and structure. The slight biases in color placement introduce a contrast that symbolizes the internal struggle between guilt and forgiveness. Broken pixels serve as disruptions in the overarching harmony, representing an ongoing emotional story of seeking balance amid turmoil.
