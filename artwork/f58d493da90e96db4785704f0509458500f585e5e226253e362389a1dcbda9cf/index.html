Artist Statement

This abstract piece embodies the emotional tension between clarity and confusion, using a palette of lime, black, electric blue, and magenta to contrast the themes. The deliberate placement of colors creates a visual echo where the left side offers a semblance of symmetry, while the right disrupts it with 'broken pixels' that symbolically represent confusion. The rising intensity of the pattern flows diagonally, suggesting a pulse that oscillates between calm and chaos, enhanced by the 'flame' pattern for confusion and a 'split_screen' for clarity. Negative space is thoughtfully included to avoid overwhelming density, allowing the viewer to breathe in this emotional interplay.
