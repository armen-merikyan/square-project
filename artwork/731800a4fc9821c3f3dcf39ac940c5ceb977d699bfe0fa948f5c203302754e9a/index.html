Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional duality of loneliness and connection through a contrasting palette of warm and cool tones. The interplaying of muted purples and deep blues creates a sense of introspection, while the vibrant yellows and oranges burst forth as fleeting moments of connection. The arrangement of colors features a dense center that radiates outward, suggesting both a burst of internal emotion and a push against external barriers. The use of broken pixels incorporates an element of imperfection, evoking the inherent chaos of human connection against the backdrop of solitude. The grid pattern intersects with organic bursts, reflecting the tension between structure and freedom.
