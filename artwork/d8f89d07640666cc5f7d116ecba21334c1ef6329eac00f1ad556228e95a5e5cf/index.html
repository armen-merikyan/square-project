Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around a tug-of-war between 'longing' and 'resolve'. The palette reflects this duality; soft colors like 'pale mint' and 'soft sky' symbolize a yearning for calmness, while denser tones such as 'storm gray' and 'near black' provide a grounding contrast that hints at determination. The layout is split vertically, with the left side showcasing the softer, longing qualities through a 'crack' pattern, while the right side presents the structured 'broken_dots' pattern representing resolve. The main collision occurs at the center, where colors and patterns interrupt across the divide, suggesting a beautiful tension that echoes the complexity of emotions involved. Broken pixels introduce disruptions, enhancing the sense of both repair and damage, effectively expressing the themes outlined in the seed.
