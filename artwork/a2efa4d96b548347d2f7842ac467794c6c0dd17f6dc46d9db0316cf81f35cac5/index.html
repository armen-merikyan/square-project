Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of vulnerability and strength, visually represented through a palette of dark, muted colors contrasted by select brighter tones. The use of navy and black indicates strength and stability, while dim cyan introduces an element of gentle pulsation and vulnerability. The main contrast emerges from the tension between dense, dark blocks and the lighter dots that suggest exposure and fragility. This interplay crafts a visual narrative of rising signals amidst a background of sheltering depth, encapsulating the complexities of connection and alienation.
