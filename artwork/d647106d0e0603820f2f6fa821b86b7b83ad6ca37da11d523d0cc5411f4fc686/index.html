Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between panic and control through a contrasting palette of muted and vibrant colors. The soft edges contrast with sharp accents, symbolizing the fight for dominance in an argument grid layout. Pops of vivid orange and deep sepia exhibit the bursts of panic, while soothing faded blue enhances moments of control. The broken pixels at scattered points create intentional imperfections, suggesting chaos within order. The result is an abstract rhythm of emotion that captures the dynamic interplay between conflicting states.
