Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between 'loneliness' and 'connection,' represented by a mix of warm and cool colors to evoke tension and juxtaposition. The palette of yellow and muted purple stands for warmth and depth, while deep blue adds a sense of calm yet echoes a feeling of solitude. The arrangement features a dense cluster of colors in one corner, gradually tapering off into negative space, reflecting the push and pull of emotional connectivity. The 'crosshatch against grid' pattern creates lines of division yet allows colors to blend at the intersection, signifying moments of connection amidst isolation. The use of broken pixels adds imperfection, embodying the fragility of human relationships.
