Artist Statement

This artwork expresses the delicate balance between quietness and pressure through color placement. The dark navy (#001F3F) serves as the base, creating a heavy atmosphere of compression, while the faint peach (#FFD8B1) acts as a gentle contrast, introducing moments of clarity amidst confusion. The pressure builds in the lower diagonal with compressed red (#FF6347), symbolizing emotional weight, while the off white (#F8F8F8) and dust gray (#B3B3B3) punctuate the upper section with lighter areas that suggest quietness and release. The structured organization of colors reflects both a diagonal argument and an interplay of hard edges with softer transitions.
