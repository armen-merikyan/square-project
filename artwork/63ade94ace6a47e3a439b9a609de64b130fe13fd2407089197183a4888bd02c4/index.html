Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the duality of love and betrayal. The palette combines warm pinks and reds representing love, juxtaposed with cool grays and dark maroon symbolizing betrayal and absence. The main contrast surfaces at the central diagonal, where the soft flames (love) interact with the harsh dot patterns (betrayal), creating tension and unresolved emotions. This layout illustrates the encroachment of love within the conflict of betrayal, echoing the seed's themes of inside vs outside and the dual nature of relationships.
