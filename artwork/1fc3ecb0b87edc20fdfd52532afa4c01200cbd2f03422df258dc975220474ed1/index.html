Artist Statement

The emotional tone of relief and dread is explored through the juxtaposition of vivid and muted colors. The selected palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple emphasizes the collision between light and dark. The layout features a grid pattern representing dread, contrasted with broken dots that suggest relief. The top-weighted edge style draws attention upward, while the broken pixel elements create visual interruption, enhancing the sense of tension and release within the abstract composition.
