Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes softness and severity, allowing for a playful yet tension-filled atmosphere. The soft hues of powder pink and warm white spread across the grid, creating a gentle environment, while stark contrasts of carbon black and sharp crimson punctuate the composition, providing abrupt moments of severity. The center vs edges layout emphasizes the openness of the 'gate' while the broken pixel method disrupts the organization, helping reflect both guilt and forgiveness through imperfection. The pulse marks emerge from the black and crimson, suggesting a fluctuation between the weight of remorse and the lightness of release, ultimately framing a narrative of emotional complexity.
