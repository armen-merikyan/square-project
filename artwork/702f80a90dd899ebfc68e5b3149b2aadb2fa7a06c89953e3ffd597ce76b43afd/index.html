Artist Statement

This artwork captures the conflict between vulnerability and strength using a blend of soft and rich colors to evoke emotional depth. The palette intertwines warm and cool shades representing desire and stark reality, with deep olive and honey gold serving as a stable central point that contrasts with the flowing lines of ripe green and acid green. The main tension arises from the spiral layout where dense clusters collide with open spaces, illustrating the struggle between the desire for protection and the exposure of the central point, showcasing broken pixels as irregularities that mirror the fragility of human emotion.
