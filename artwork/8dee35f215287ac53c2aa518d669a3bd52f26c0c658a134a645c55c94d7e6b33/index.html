Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise), creating a tension-filled landscape where defined pulses of color battle against static disruptions. The palette employs shades of vibrant green and electric blue as signals of clarity, while deep blacks and static grays suggest noise’s chaotic influence. The structure follows an eroded symmetry, highlighting the central pulse of color disrupted by static blocks. Broken pixels add intentional imperfection, illustrating scars of interference in a battle for clarity.
