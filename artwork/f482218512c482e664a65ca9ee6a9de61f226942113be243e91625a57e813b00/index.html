Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between longing and resolve, presented through a contrast of light and dark colors. The palette combines cool whites and blues with dense blacks and grays to symbolize hope against despair, creating a push-pull effect. The main collision occurs through a bright cluster on the left facing a dense dark area on the right, embodying a struggle between clarity and chaos. The patterns of shattered longing intertwine with a grid of resolve, forming a rhythm that symbolizes the push-and-pull of emotion.
