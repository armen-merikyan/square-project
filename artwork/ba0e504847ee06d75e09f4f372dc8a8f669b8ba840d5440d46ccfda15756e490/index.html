Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dynamic conflict of rage and calm. The palette, featuring bold reds and deep blacks juxtaposed with soft pale blues and whites, creates a visual tension, embodying the clash between explosive energy and serene tranquility. The main collision occurs at the center, where jagged red 'noise' pixels collide with horizontal blue 'threads', creating a spiraling effect outward, symbolizing the push and pull of emotions. This arrangement mirrors the seed's narrative of eroded symmetry and oscillation between chaos and order.
