Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between defiance and submission, utilizing a striking palette of soft pinks and sharp crimson against deep black and gray. The palette represents 'softness_severity,' suggesting a clash between the harsh realities of resistance and the allure of surrender. The dominant color is carbon black, creating a dense atmosphere, while powder pink and warm white permeate the space, allowing for quiet moments amidst the chaos. The rows are arranged to create pulses of color that counterbalance—one quadrant takes on a fiery energy reflecting defiance, while another subdues into softer tones representing submission. Broken pixels in the pattern disrupt the visual flow, echoing the unpredictability of emotions, creating a collision where edges blur. The central theme of 'two small centers' allows for exploration of inner conflict amidst spaciousness, leaving negative space that speaks to an unresolved tension between opposing forces.
