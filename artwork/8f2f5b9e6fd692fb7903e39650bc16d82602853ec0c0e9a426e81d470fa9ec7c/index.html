Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captures the duality of longing and resolve. The palette combines pale mint and storm gray to evoke feelings of yearning against a backdrop of clarity. The contrast emerges in the center where dense strokes of color meet the soft sky and quiet faded gold surrounding a fragmented heart pattern. This collision represents the struggle between chaos and order, creating a visually compelling narrative that engages the viewer's emotional resonance. Rows intentionally incorporate broken pixels to symbolize the imperfections in life's journey, accentuating the central theme of tension and release.
