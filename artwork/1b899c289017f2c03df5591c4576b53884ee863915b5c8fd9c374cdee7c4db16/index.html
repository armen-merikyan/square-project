Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece balances the tension between safety and exposure. The warm browns and greens evoke feelings of shelter and warmth, while the icy blues and pale colors introduce a vulnerability. The outer rows depict a 'safety border' feeling, reinforced by warmer shades, while the inner rows employ a chaotic maze-like pattern, symbolizing 'exposure'. The main collision occurs in the center, where the cool hues disrupt the warmer field, creating an echo of tension and release. This visual narrative captures a journey from vulnerability to protection, illustrating the delicate interplay of emotions molded by the seed.
