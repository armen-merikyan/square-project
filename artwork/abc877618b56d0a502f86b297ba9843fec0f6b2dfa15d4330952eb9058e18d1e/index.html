Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between love and betrayal, captured through a palette of warm pinks and deep maroons contrasting with grays. The top half embodies love through interconnected patterns, while the bottom half, representing betrayal, uses sharper, jagged patterns to interrupt flow. The broken pixels add imperfection, symbolizing the fragility of relationships. The diagonal rise in the arrangement suggests a movement towards resolution, yet the sharp corners hint at unresolved pain.
