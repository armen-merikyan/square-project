Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork embodies a dance between vulnerability and strength, captured through the use of muted and saturated colors. The palette incorporates 'off_white' and 'faint_peach' as soft contrasts to the much darker 'dark_navy' and 'compressed_red'. This allows the viewer to feel both the weight of strength and the delicate nature of vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center where the powerful saturated colors interrupt the surrounding softer hues, creating a visual tension mimicking the struggle of exposure against safety. The 'corner_invasion' layout dictates an angular spread of these emotions, emphasizing the qualities of confinement versus openness in formation. Broken pixels introduce unpredictability, mirroring life's imperfection in moments of turmoil and subtle strength.
