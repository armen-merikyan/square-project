Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension of longing and resolve through juxtaposed clusters and quiet spaces. The palette, featuring warm browns and shelter greens, reflects safety intertwined with exposure, mirroring the complexity of emotions. The main collision occurs in the center, where stacked colors clash against the constellation-like spread of brighter accents, representing the unresolved relationship between desire and strength. Broken pixels disrupt the continuity, adding an intentional imperfection that echoes the theme of repair. The arrangement utilizes both dense clusters and open spaces to convey the push and pull of feelings.
