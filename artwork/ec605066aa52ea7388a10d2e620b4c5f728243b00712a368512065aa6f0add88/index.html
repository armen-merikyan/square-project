Artist Statement

This piece translates the interplay between confusion and clarity into a visual representation using the love_betrayal palette. Dark maroon and gray represent confusion, creating depth and complexity, while bright pink signifies clarity, interrupting the darker tones. The composition embraces a rising signal layout, with thick outlines suggesting structure amidst chaos, while grid patterns interlace to evoke confusion. Pulses of pink disrupt the monochrome gloom, creating an emotional tension that mirrors the seed's essence, where symmetry is almost present but always broken, generating a feeling of unresolved motion.
