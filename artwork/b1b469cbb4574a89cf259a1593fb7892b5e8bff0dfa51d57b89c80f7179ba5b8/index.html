Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional dichotomy of desire and restraint through a turbulent interplay of saturated and muted colors. The central section radiates bright colors symbolizing desire, while muted tones radiate from the edges, reflecting restraint. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, aligning with the theme of tension created by desire vs restraint. The palette of hot coral and blood orange builds a heated core, juxtaposed with the calming hollow blue and charcoal at the borders. The main contrast establishes itself at the center, where vibrant colors collide with the cooler edges, immersed in a chaotic spiral flow, mirroring the journey from tension to release.
