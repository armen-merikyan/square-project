Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured here embodies a struggle between warmth and heartbreak. The color palette features warm pinks and reds juxtaposed against dark maroon and gray, representing love's vibrancy contrasted with the shadows of betrayal. The layout adopts an asymmetrical pattern to reflect the 'broken pixels' theme, evoking feelings of an empty room filled with tension. Main contrasts lie between the lighter and darker hues, emphasizing an emotional shift. The split screen pattern on one side displays warmth while the other side is interrupted by darker chaos, achieving a blend of harmony and disruption, echoing the seed's duality.
