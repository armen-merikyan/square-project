Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a tension between safety and exposure, employing a palette of soft greens and warm browns to convey a sense of protection intertwined with vulnerability. The collision occurs in the center, where darker edges frame a lighter core, suggesting a protective barrier that feels both safe and exposed. The glitch patterns allow for an echoing effect, representing the conflict between firmness and fragility. The choice of colors reflects warmth against the coldness of exposure, emphasizing the delicate balance inherent in the seed's themes.
