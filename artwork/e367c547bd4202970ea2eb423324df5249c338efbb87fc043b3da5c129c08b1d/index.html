Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances fear and hope, represented through a contrasting palette of dark and soft colors. The top four rows embody 'fear' with tightly packed dark tones, resembling a jagged edge, while the bottom four utilize lighter hues of 'hope', reflecting rising signals with scattered dots. The collision occurs at the transition line between tension and release, symbolizing a shift from oppressive to uplifting. This evokes a sense of deep emotional conflict, representing the struggle and interplay of these feelings.
