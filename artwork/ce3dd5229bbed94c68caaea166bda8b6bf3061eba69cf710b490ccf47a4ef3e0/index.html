Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional contrast between panic and control, illustrating the battle between chaos and order. The chosen palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green reflects a spectrum from deep uncertainty to a glimmer of hope. The left side employs a structured 'mirror' pattern for control, while the right dissolves into a chaotic 'spark' of color, creating tension through collision. The artwork ebbs and flows, indicative of rising signals of emotion, with pockets of negative space enhancing the sense of imbalance. The spark of gold offers brief moments of joy amid the chaotic undercurrent of panic.
