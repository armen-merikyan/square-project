Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between 'longing' and 'resolve', with a primary focus on seeking clarity within chaos. The palette—shades of navy and black interspersed with dim cyan and white—reflects distant warmth intertwined with clear boundaries. This creates a stark contrast, with a central collision where both emotions meet, represented by a broken frame pattern amidst a flame-inspired flow. The arrangement intentionally juxtaposes dense clusters against quiet spills of negative space, illustrating an unresolved tension that emanates from the seed concept of repair versus damage.
