Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between quietness and pressure through contrasting colors. The chosen palette of off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach reflects a balance of soft blooms and dense structures. The central collision occurs in the right half, where warm hues interrupt the cooler, muted tones, suggesting a struggle between chaos and calmness. The layout emphasizes a dim background giving way to a bright cluster, embodying the idea of pressure loosening into release while incorporating broken pixels to enhance depth and imperfect beauty.
