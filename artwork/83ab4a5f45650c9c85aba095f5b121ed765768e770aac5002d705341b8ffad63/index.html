Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of restlessness and peace, where the center houses a tension represented through collision and pattern disruption. The palette incorporates muted moss green and stone gray to evoke calm, balanced with rust for unrest. The contrasting patterns of dots and glitches depict a pulse of memories against the backdrop of stasis. The primary collision occurs in the center, where peace (green) is disrupted by hints of tumult (rust), signaling a constant motion against stillness. Overall, the composition reflects a memory patchwork intertwined with a spectrum of emotional highs and lows.
