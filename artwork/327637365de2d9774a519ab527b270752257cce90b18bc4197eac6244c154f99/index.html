Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of loneliness and connection through contrasting colors and patterns. The dark palette symbolizes isolation, while soft gold hints at hope and connection, embodying warmth against the heavier tones. The top-weighted layout represents a struggle upwards, with broken pixels enhancing the imperfect nature of human connections. The contrast occurs mainly between the deep dark tones and the bright gold, suggesting bursts of connection within a broader atmosphere of loneliness. The pattern oscillates between dense stripes representing loneliness and crack-like formations suggesting fleeting connections.
