Artist Statement

This abstract composition navigates the emotional dichotomy of longing versus resolve. The palette combines deep blacks for yearning and soft pale blues for clarity, allowing for a striking contrast that embodies the unresolved nature of the theme. The central collision occurs where dense black colors interrupt lighter fields, suggesting a push-pull dynamic. The layout echoes a strong vertical and diagonal flow, capturing the tension and movement of trying to bridge the gap between unresolved feelings and the will to overcome them, using broken pixels as intentional disruptions that symbolize imperfections in the journey.
