Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'restlessness vs peace' is represented through a mix of vibrant and muted colors, symbolizing the internal struggle and contrast. The chosen palette emphasizes 'signal_noise' with deep blacks and electric blues creating tension against warmer shades like hot magenta, representing patches of peace. The spiral layout introduces a dynamic motion, with the center as the focal point where vibrant colors converge, echoing the themes of memory and motion. The strategic placement of broken pixels creates intentional disruption, suggesting the tension and fragility of the balance between calm and agitation, while ensuring at least 10% negative space to allow the composition to breathe.
