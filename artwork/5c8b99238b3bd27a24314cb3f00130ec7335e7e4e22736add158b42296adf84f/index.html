Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a push-pull dynamic between safety and exposure, embodied in the chosen palette that features soft greens and browns juxtaposed with cold whites and pale tones. The contrast centers in the grid's left side, illustrating the protective warmth of shelter against the crisp coldness of vulnerability. Patterns are disrupted by broken pixels that represent the scars of exposure, while the overall composition flows from left to right, emulating tension and release.
