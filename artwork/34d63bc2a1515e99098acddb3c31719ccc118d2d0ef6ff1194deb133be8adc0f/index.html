Artist Statement

This artwork conveys a dynamic emotional landscape where 'nostalgia' and 'progress' intersect. The chosen palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple represents joy amidst grief. The warm hues clash against the cool tones, representing the contrast of past experiences and future aspirations. The core of the piece features broken pixels, symbolizing imperfection and interruption, while the diagonal flow creates a sense of movement from the corner, suggesting an invasion of emotions. Key collisions between vibrant and muted colors create moments of tension and release throughout the composition.
