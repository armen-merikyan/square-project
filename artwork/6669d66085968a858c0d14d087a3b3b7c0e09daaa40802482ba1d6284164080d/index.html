Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between fear and hope, contrasting darker shades with glowing hints of gold and green. The split layout and mirror-like symmetry evoke a sense of duality, while broken pixels symbolize imperfection and disruption. The top left is heavier and darker, suggesting fear and weight, while the bottom right lightens with soft gold and pale green, signifying hope and upward flow. This arrangement creates a visual contradiction, capturing the essence of 'burst' and 'crosshatch,' leading the viewer through a dynamic experience of emotion.
