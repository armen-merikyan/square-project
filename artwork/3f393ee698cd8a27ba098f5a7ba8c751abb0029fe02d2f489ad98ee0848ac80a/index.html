Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between softness and severity through a blend of soft pastel tones and stark darks. The warm whites and powder pinks represent an inviting softness, while the carbon black and steel gray inject a sense of severity and weight. The arrangement features a diagonal separation, with severe colors on one side and soft tones on the other, creating a pulse of emotional tension. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, representing the imperfections inherent in navigating guilt and forgiveness, while the echoing patterns of light versus dark lend a rhythmic quality, suggesting an ongoing dialogue between contrasting emotions.
