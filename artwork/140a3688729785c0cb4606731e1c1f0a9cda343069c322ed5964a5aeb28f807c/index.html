Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the collision between quietness and pressure, representing distinct facets of human experience. The palette balances cool tones (#f8f1ec, #e4e0de) with rich accents (#ed4a3e, #002b5c) to emphasize vibrancy against a tranquil backdrop. Each pixel's placement promotes tension - rows 1-4 convey the weight of pressure piled against the quietude of rows 5-8, creating an uneven yet harmonious division. The 'teeth' pattern with broken pixels suggests imperfection amid coherence, mirroring life’s intricate balance between chaos and calm.
