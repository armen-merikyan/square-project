Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress, represented by contrasting color patterns. The palette, consisting of soft sepia and faded blue hues, conveys a sense of warmth and calm, invoking feelings of nostalgia against sharp, cooler tones that symbolize forward motion. The layout split horizontally presents a transition from the rich depth of nostalgia below to the lighter dispersion of progress above. A deliberate arrangement of dots and drips evokes memories, while jagged edges suggest the friction of change. The layout invites the viewer to contemplate the flux between stability and advancement, with the flow diverging to symbolize movement. Negative space is preserved, emphasizing the contrast between fullness and hollowness—a reminder of what remains unfulfilled amidst the progression.
