Artist Statement

This composition contrasts the explosive energy of rage with the quiet, tender spaces of calm. The palette includes deep blacks and muted blues to create a stormy background, emphasizing the internal struggle, while soft whites and pale blues provide a gentle touch, representing tenderness. The main collision occurs in the central area where muted shades intersect harsh edges, reflecting the tumult of emotions. Inspired by the 'ladder' shape, the pattern conveys a sense of ascent amidst chaos, symbolizing a climb through various emotional states.
