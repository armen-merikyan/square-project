Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional contrast of 'spark' and 'ash', invoking a dynamic interplay between warmth and coolness. The palette, featuring cosmic indigo and cold gold, reflects the tension between immediacy and erosion, illustrating the struggle between vitality and fatigue. The collision of radial and ring patterns brings focus to the center, while jagged edges and broken pixels add an element of imperfection, akin to scars of memory. The negative space serves as an echo of restraint amid the vibrant bursts of color, symbolizing the conversation between energy and stillness.
