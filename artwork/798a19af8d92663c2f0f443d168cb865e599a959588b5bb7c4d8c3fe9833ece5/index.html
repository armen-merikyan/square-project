Artist Statement

This composition captures the balance between strength and vulnerability through a dynamic clash of patterns. The center emerges as a focal point representing vulnerability, surrounded by harsh, darker edges that embody strength. The palette of soft gold and warm ivory represents warmth and connection, while deep wine and ash gray introduce tension and contrast. The intersecting patterns of cracks and ash fields reflect the narrative of exposure and shelter, suggesting a fragile connection amid hard edges.
