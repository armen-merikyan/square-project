Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between longing and resolve, captured through warm and cool color contrasts. The warm whites and soft pinks represent longing, while the sharp crimson and dark blacks embody a sense of resolve. The pattern integrates tight clusters of vibrant colors against expansive negative space, creating a dynamic collision of emotions. The design is grounded by a central tension, echoing the idea of a broken thread amidst serene urgency.
