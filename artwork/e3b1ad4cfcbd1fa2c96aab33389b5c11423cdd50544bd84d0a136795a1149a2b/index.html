Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts joy and grief: the left side embodies the joy with rounded, warm colors suggesting expansion and pathways, while the right side displays the heavy, fragmented tones of grief, echoing chaos through rainfall patterns. The palette blends vibrant yellows and oranges against muted blues and purples, enhancing the division and suggesting a narrative of struggle between safety and threat. The stair-step arrangement heightens the feeling of ascension or decline, while broken pixels represent the imperfections inherent in both emotions.
