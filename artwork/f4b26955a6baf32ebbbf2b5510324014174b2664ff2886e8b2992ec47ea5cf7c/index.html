Artist Statement

This composition captures the struggle between the bright, dynamic presence of 'spark' and the settling, muted quality of 'ash.' The palette contrasts soft pinks and warm whites against deeper blacks and grays, mirroring the tension between energy and stillness. The central area exudes intensity, while the edges feel almost muted, highlighting the conflict. Key collisions happen diagonally across the square, symbolizing the push-and-pull of memory versus erasure. The patterns intertwine—tidal motions clash with a split-screen approach, creating an unresolved narrative of resilience and fragility.
