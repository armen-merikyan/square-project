Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve through juxtaposed colors and placement. The chosen palette of moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold conveys a sense of resilience against fragility. The dense cluster of colors in one corner symbolizes pressure and struggle, embodying longing, while the more tranquil space represents resolution and calmness. The main collision occurs at the dense corner where longing's complexity meets resolve's clarity, creating a poignant tension that resonates throughout the piece, echoing the emotional narrative.
