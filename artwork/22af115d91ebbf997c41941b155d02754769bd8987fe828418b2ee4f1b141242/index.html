Artist Statement

This piece reflects the tension between nostalgia and progress, represented through contrasting dark and light hues that embody a struggle between stability and change. The palette of deep navy and black represents the weight of nostalgia, while hints of dim cyan evoke a sense of forward motion and clarity. The split layout, with darker colors dominating the lower half, signifies the heavier pull of memory against dreams of progress. The broken pixel scheme interrupts the flow and enhances the emotional juxtaposition, while preserving negative space to represent the void and potential of what lies ahead.
