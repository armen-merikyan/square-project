Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between trust and suspicion, using a palette that reflects warmth (muted cream, small orange) coupled with deeper tones (blackened plum, iron gray) to illustrate hidden uncertainties amidst an otherwise inviting ambiance. The main collision occurs in the center where the lighter tones disrupt the darker background, suggesting a signal of hope amidst noise. The arrangement evokes a feeling of pressure and flow, with broken pixels symbolizing moments of fragility within an overarching structure of resilience.
