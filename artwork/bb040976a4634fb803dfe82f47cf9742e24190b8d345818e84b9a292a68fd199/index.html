Artist Statement

The emotional tone presents a duality, with one side embodying trust's warming glow and the other the cold shadows of suspicion. The palette combines ripe green and honey gold to create a warm zone while deep olive and acid green hint at underlying tension. The pattern features soft breaks and dots for trust, clashing with angular, sharp patterns symbolizing suspicion. The main collision occurs in the center, where patterns interruption evokes a dance of conflict and resolution, mimicking life’s unpredictable shifts between comfort and wariness.
