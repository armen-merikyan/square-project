Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between confusion and clarity, captured through a palette of strong contrasts: black representing confusion and white symbolizing clarity. The rows create a layered impact, where the dark edges (black and grays) suggest the feeling of constriction and pressure, contrasting with sporadic white dots that echo moments of clarity. The pattern resonates with 'broken pixels,' as broken patterns signify moments of interruption in clarity. The asymmetry emphasizes a journey from chaos ('falling pixels') toward an emerging sense of order, represented by the white pixels radiating outward. This arrangement illustrates the emotional weight of searching for clarity within confusion.
