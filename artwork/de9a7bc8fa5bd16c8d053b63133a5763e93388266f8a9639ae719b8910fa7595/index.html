Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between joy and grief through contrasting color placement and a mirror-broken layout. The palette blends warm yellows and oranges with deep blues and muted purples, suggesting a vibrant joy that collides with a somber grief. The top rows, expressing joy, adopt a radial pattern, while the bottom reflects grief through a grid motif, creating a visual echo of alternating emotional currents. The split horizontal flow reinforces the idea of a dichotomy, while the incorporation of broken pixels adds a sense of imperfection and depth to the overall story.
