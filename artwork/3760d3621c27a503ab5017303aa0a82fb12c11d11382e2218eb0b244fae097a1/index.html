Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between restlessness and peace, leading to an arrangement filled with sharp contrasts. The palette, predominantly featuring warm coral and cold slate, juxtaposes warmth with coolness to signify tension. The main collision occurs in the center with the 'teeth' pattern representing restlessness intersecting the peaceful 'halo' forms, echoing the complexities of memory and motion amidst negative space. This creates a rhythm of interruptions that reflect the ongoing battle between tranquility and disturbance, as the colors pulse and recede across the composition.
