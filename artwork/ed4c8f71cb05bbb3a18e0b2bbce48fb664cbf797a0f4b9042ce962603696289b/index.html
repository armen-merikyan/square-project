Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional clash between panic and control through an uneven flow of pixels reflecting structured chaos. The palette of dark colors interspersed with soft gold represents the underlying tension between fear and fleeting hope, highlighting how control can be fragile amidst panic. The composition features stark contrasts in symmetry, creating a sense of balance disrupted by 'burned edges', reinforcing the feeling of fractured stability. The zigzag arrangement fosters a dynamic rhythm, implicating the argument of opposing forces in a visual dialogue.
