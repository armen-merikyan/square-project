Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette combines warm earthy tones with cool, distant colors, representing strength and vulnerability respectively. The main collision occurs between the light, soft background (#D8C4D0, #E3D6D3) and the dense, darker tones (#775D5E, #3D353F), creating a visual struggle. The abstract pattern mirrors the seed's themes of safety versus exposure, with broken pixel edges introducing imperfection and unpredictability, embodying the idea of a fractured mirror and closed corners that evoke a sense of both protection and fragility.
