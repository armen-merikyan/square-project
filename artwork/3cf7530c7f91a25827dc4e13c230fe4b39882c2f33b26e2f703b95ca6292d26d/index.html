Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between 'panic' and 'control,' reflected in the use of a contrasting palette of vibrant lime and electric blue against deep black and soft magenta tones. The left side of the grid follows a structured pattern, echoing control, while the right side disrupts that order with random placements, evoking a sense of chaos. The arrangement suggests a collision between a stable center and surrounding instability, with the scattered vivid colors representing sparks of emotion fighting against a backdrop of emptiness, alluding to the theme of an ‘empty room’—a space filled with potential yet fraught with unease.
