Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the contrast between restlessness and peace through a careful balancing act. The palette of soft whites and ice blues represents tranquility, while the deep blacks and violet grays inject a sense of turmoil. The central collision occurs between the gentle rhythm of the dot patterns representing peace and the complex, interwoven braids symbolizing restlessness. The layout creates a visual tension, with strong horizontal and vertical lines, invoking the chronic push and pull of memory and motion. The broken pixel placements introduce a sense of interruption, mirroring feelings of being unsettled, contrasting with calm sections of color that offer brief moments of respite.
