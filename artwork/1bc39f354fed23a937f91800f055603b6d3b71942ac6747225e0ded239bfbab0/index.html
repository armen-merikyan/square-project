Artist Statement

This piece embodies a tension between trust and suspicion, represented through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of muted browns and greens conveys a sense of safety against splashes of cold white and pale skin tones, suggesting fragility. The main contrast occurs in the diagonal section, where stripes representing trust clash with scar patterns indicating suspicion. This interplay creates a dynamic flow, aligning with the seed concept by emphasizing unresolved emotional collisions and the complex layers of connection and doubt.
