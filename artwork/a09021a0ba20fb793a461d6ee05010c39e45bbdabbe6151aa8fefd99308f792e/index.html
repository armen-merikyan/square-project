Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between clarity and confusion through a rhythmic arrangement that juxtaposes dense clusters of muted tones against pockets of sharper contrasts, manifesting a visual pulse. The palette of faded blue, sepia, tan, and dust gray reflects the themes of broken memory—suggesting nostalgia intertwined with moments of clarity. The top half's checker pattern represents confusion with overlapping textures, while the bottom half's frame hints at clarity and structure, aligning with the contrasting emotions derived from the seed.
