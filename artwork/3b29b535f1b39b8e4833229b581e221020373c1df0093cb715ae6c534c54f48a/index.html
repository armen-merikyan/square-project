Artist Statement

The artwork captures the contrasting emotions of panic and control through a tension-filled palette of burnt orange, deep red, and charcoal, suggesting instability against a backdrop of muted calm. The warm elements create a vibrant core overshadowed by the darker surroundings, representing the battle between chaotic inner feelings and a striving for order. The arrangement reflects upward movement, symbolizing hope yet interrupted by pockets of density, showcasing a dance between love and betrayal, pushing towards an expressive collision of colors.
