Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission, using a palette of ripe green, deep olive, and honey gold to symbolize contrast in feelings. The central density represents defiance while the expansive lighter colors suggest submission. The arrangement carries a stutter-step flow, emphasizing interruptions with broken pixels, creating a visual echo of quiet spaces against more energetic chaotic clusters. The pulse marks against glitches add dynamics, reflecting the ongoing struggle between order and chaos.
