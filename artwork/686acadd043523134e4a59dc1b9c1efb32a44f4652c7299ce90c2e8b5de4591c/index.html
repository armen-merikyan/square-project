Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between defiance and submission, with a dense, saturated center dominated by crosshatch patterns in deep blues and muted plums, representing defiance. The soft cyan and lavender gray hues around the edges symbolize submission, creating a gradual receding contrast. The main collision occurs as the vibrant center pushes against the quiet channels at the edges. This design captures the tension between chaos and order, creating a visual echo of struggle and continuity.
