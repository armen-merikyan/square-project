Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the conflict between isolation and connection, represented by the substantial contrast of deep reds interspersed with pale blues that echo a sense of warmth against coolness. The layout follows a mirrored but broken pattern, illustrating how loneliness intrudes into the potential for connection, creating a sense of dissonance yet inviting engagement. Key moments of collision occur where the darker colors intersect with lighter hues, hinting at tears or disruptions in the overall grid, evoking the hidden vs exposed theme where the burst of color commands attention within the boundaries. The palette, rooted in reds and blues, embodies rage tempered by calm, reflecting the ancient fires of passion against the backdrop of cool isolation. This arrangement suggests bursts of life amid surrounding darkness, echoing the inner turmoil often felt in social contexts, igniting a captivating tension within the abstract form.
