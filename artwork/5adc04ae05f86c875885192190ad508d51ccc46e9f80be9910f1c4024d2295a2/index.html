Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay between 'longing' and 'resolve', manifesting as a contrast between warm and cool colors. The palette captures intimacy with warm tones (rose clay, lamp gold) colliding against cooler backgrounds (cool blue, distant lilac). The arrangement centers on a hollow core (representing void) surrounded by bursts of color that signify both hope and fragmentation. The composition balances between an upward pull from a dense outer ring and the calm stillness of the inner void, illustrating the push and pull of emotions. Broken pixels are strategically placed, adding intentional disturbances in the flowing pattern, displaying the beauty in imperfection and suggesting a sense of repair amidst damage.
