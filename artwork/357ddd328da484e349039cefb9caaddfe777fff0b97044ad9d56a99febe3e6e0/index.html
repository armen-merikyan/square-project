Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between joy and grief through a color palette that alternates warm yellows and oranges with cool muted blues and purples. The layout evokes a falling pattern, reminiscent of tears or sparks, while the zigzag pattern of brighter accents against subdued tones symbolizes a rising signal amidst grief. The placement of broken pixels introduces a sense of imperfection, enhancing the emotional impact and suggesting a delicate balance between despair and hope.
