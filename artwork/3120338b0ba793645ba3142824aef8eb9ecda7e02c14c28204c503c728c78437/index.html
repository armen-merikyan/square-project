Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between safety and exposure through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette combines soft greens and warm browns, representing safety, interwoven with the coolness of blue and white, symbolizing exposure. The main contrast occurs at the center, where the patterns of crack and crosshatch collide, creating a visual echo that suggests vulnerability and protection. The diagonal flow reinforces the idea of invasion and transition, while the broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection and complexity, embodying the emotional score derived from the seed.
