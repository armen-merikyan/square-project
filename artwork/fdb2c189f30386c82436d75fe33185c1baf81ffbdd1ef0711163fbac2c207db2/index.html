Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional contrast between 'envy' and 'gratitude', using a palette that emphasizes warmth versus coolness. The composition flows from the bottom to the top, indicating a rise in hope against the backdrop of inner turmoil. The cool blues representing gratitude create a soft but firm impact, colliding with the stark intensity of red representing envy. The patterns are intertwined – a mirror reflecting both emotions and a ring suggesting cyclical feelings. The use of negative space enhances the notion of an empty room filled with unexpressed emotions, allowing for moments of quiet amid the rising tension.
