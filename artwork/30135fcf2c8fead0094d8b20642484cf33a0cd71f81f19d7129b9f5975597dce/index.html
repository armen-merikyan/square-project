Artist Statement

This abstract composition explores the emotional contrast between control and surrender. The palette combines muted cream and iron gray as foundational colors, representing the contained pressure, while red oxide and small orange punctuate the composition as accents of warmth and release. The main collision occurs in the center, created by the juxtaposition of densely packed 'pulse_marks' against the softer 'pressure_band' pattern, capturing the essence of tension and resolution. The edge style of faded borders adds a quality of quiet space, enhancing the feeling of separation between distant echoes.
