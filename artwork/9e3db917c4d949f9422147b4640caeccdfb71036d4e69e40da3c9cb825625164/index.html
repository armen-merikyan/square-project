Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between rage and calm. The left side employs a calm palette with low-contrast pale blues and whites in a checker pattern to evoke spaciousness and tranquility. The right side contrasts sharply with aggressive reds and blacks arranged in a target pattern, suggesting conflict and intensity. The diagonal flow creates movement, while broken pixels on the red side interrupt the order, reflecting the emotional cracks against serenity. The overall composition suggests a battle between tension and ease, mirroring the seed's emotional complexity.
