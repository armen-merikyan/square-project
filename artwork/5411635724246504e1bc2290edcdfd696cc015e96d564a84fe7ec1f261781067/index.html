Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between sparks of warmth and the quiet residue of ash. The palette combines 'rose_clay' and 'lamp_gold' for warmth against the cool tones of 'distant_lilac' and 'cool_blue.' The collision occurs diagonally, with warm colors clashing against cooler fields, creating a flicker of interaction. The use of broken pixels adds intentional disruption, mirroring the theme of resilience amidst fragility.
