Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends confusion with clarity, expressed through a stark palette of deep navy, black, and dim cyan that suggest a single, distant light fighting through a chaotic environment. The pattern flows upward, resembling a wave, embodying hope trying to rise amidst confusion. Jagged edges give an impression of broken symmetry, while the 'stairs' pattern injects a sense of disarray, where each step struggles against the weight of the darker colors, showcasing the collision between pressure and release. The concentration of dark tones interspersed with lighter accents creates visual tension, echoing the sentiment of emotional turmoil contrasted by brief moments of clarity.
