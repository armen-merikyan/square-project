Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between loneliness and connection by utilizing a palette rooted in darkness accented by stark contrasts. The dark navy and black serve as a grounding backdrop, representing loneliness and isolation. The limited use of dim cyan and white punctuates this with moments of connection and exposure, suggesting light breaking through amidst the shadows. The arrangement reflects a top-weighted flow, where the colors above pulse downwards, creating a sense of pressure and release. The structured grid, disturbed by broken pixels, creates a visual metaphor for the challenges inherent in finding connection. The patterns—rain for loneliness and maze for connection—intersect, evoking the chaos and complexity of emotional experiences.
