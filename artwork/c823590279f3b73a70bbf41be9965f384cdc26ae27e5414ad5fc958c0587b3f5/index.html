Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional conflict of rage versus calm through a diagonal arrangement. The palette employs deep reds and stark blacks representing rage, contrasted against soft pale blues and whites symbolizing calmness. The sharp, irregular 'pulse_marks' pattern of red disrupts the calm horizontal fields of blue, capturing the tension of an overwhelming emotion invading tranquility. Broken pixels add a sense of imperfection, suggesting that both emotions are in constant conflict yet entwined, finding resolution not in harmony but in dynamic disarray.
