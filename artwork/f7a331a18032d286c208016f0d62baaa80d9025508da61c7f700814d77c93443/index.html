Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances strength and vulnerability, where muted colors suggest vulnerability amidst stronger accents. The use of pale mint and faded gold indicates a lightness that contrasts sharply with the near black, symbolizing the collision of shelter against exposure. The main contrast occurs at the center, echoing the notion of an open heart amidst a protective frame. The broken pixels are strategically placed, introducing intentional disruptions that remind us of fragility within strength. The chosen palette reinforces themes of relief and dread, capturing the essence of emotional layers in a structured yet reactive layout.
