Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the juxtaposition of quiet acceptance against the assertive nature of pressure. The palette blends muted 'off_white' and 'faint_peach' for the quiet spaces, while 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy' introduce density and urgency. The main collision occurs diagonally across the square, where light and dark colored clusters meet, mirroring the evolving struggle between calm and pressure. This pattern illustrates a visual narrative of dichotomy capturing the essence of the seed while maintaining abstraction.
