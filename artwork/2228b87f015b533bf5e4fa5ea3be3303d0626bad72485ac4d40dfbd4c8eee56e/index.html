Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between tension and tranquility, using the palette of powder pink, warm white, carbon black, steel gray, and sharp crimson to embody both softness and severity. The main contrast arises in the center, where warmer colors pulse against the darker tones, suggesting a point of collision that evokes a narrative of interruption—an escape from rigidity into a realm of fluid emotion. The flow is diagonal, creating a rhythm of movement, while the broken pixels disrupt the visual harmony, embodying imperfections of guilt and forgiveness.
