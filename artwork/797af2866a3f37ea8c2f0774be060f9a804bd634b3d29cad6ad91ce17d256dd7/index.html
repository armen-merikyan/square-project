Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dance between longing and resolve, captured through contrasting hues and spatial arrangements. The selected palette of powder pink and sharp crimson conveys warmth and urgency, while carbon black and steel gray introduce depth and intensity. The presence of warm white creates a quiet field, allowing dense segments of colors to pulse in the center, echoing the desire for connection amidst chaos. The arrangement favors asymmetry, emphasizing the tension of confrontation and separation, as selected colors collide and overlap, forming a visual representation of emotional complexity.
