Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between safety and exposure through gradients of warmth and coolness. The palette combines 'shelter_green' and 'warm_brown' for security and softness, while 'pale_skin' and 'cold_white' represent vulnerability. The central collision occurs between soft stripes of exposure and a heavier threshold framework that attempts to encapsulate the lightness of the center. This balance creates an emotional rhythm, wherein shades of lighter colors play a subtle echo against the broader weight of the darker hues. The broken pixels serve as interruptions, symbolizing the scars of vulnerability in the quest for safety.
