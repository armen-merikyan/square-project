Artist Statement

This pixel artwork captures the emotional dichotomy between joy and grief through contrasting warm and cool colors. The palette emphasizes glowing yellows and oranges for joy, clashing against muted blues and purples representing grief. The upward flame-like patterns symbolize joy's vibrant expansion, while the darker, cracked elements reflect the heaviness of grief. The balance exists in the arrangement, where bright colors intone the struggle against muted tones, creating a visual rhythm that echoes the emotional narrative of hidden versus exposed feelings.
