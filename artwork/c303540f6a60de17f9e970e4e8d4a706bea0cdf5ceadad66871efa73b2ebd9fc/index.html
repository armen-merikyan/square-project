Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between vulnerability and strength, reflected in the palette of earthy greens and warm browns against pale tones, representing safety amidst exposure. This creates a dynamic contrast, as soft colors intersect with stronger shades. The flow from corner to corner suggests movement and tension, while the broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, symbolizing vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the central region, where delicate interplays between strong, stable edges and soft, exposed centers create a sense of narrative tension.
