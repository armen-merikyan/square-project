Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional contrast of loneliness and connection through a striking balance of warm and cool tones. The central pixel placement symbolizes isolation amidst a chaotic gradient, signifying hidden emotions under the weight of social interaction. The palette, drawn from 'ancient fire', evokes warmth and depth, reinforcing themes of connection overshadowed by loneliness. The burst and grid-derived patterns create a sense of pressure, while the soft edges promote a whisper of interaction, suggesting both presence and absence.
