Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece traverses the dual landscapes of confusion and clarity, with a stark black contrasting against vibrant electric blue and magenta. The vibrancy symbolizes clarity, while the blacks create an illusion of confusion amidst the structured chaos. The arrangement flows from a dense center outward, asserting a sense of echoing waves of pressure that shift with each pixel. Patterns of precision and randomness collide visually, portraying the tension between filled and hollow spaces that resulted from the seed's dichotomy.
