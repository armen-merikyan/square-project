Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork highlights the tension between confusion and clarity, emphasizing the dissonance of emotional states. The broken pixel pattern reflects the theme of disruption, creating a feeling of imbalance through the arrangement of darker, muted colors against lighter tones. The palette, inspired by 'broken_memory', contains sepia and faded blue that evoke nostalgia contrasted with chaotic drama. The main collision occurs in the center, where the darker fragments scatter outward, mirroring the struggle between the hollow space and thick edges, echoing the seed's notion of fragmentation and continuity. This composition resonates with an abstract narrative of searching for harmony amid disorder, symbolizing the potential for clarity emerging from confusion.
