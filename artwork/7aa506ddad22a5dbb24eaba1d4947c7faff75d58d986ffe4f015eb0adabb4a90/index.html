Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between softness and severity by contrasting gentle pastels against harsh darks. The use of colors like powder pink and warm white creates a feeling of warmth and softness, while carbon black and sharp crimson introduce severity and sharpness. The main collision occurs in the center, where warm and cool colors interweave, creating a pulse-like rhythm, symbolizing the push and pull between opposing emotions. The layout of densely packed colors in the center contrasts with a quieter field around, inviting a sense of meditation and reflection.
