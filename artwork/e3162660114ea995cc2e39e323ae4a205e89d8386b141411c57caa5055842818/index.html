Artist Statement

The piece embodies the emotional collision between dread and relief through a palette of warm burnt orange and gold contrasting against dark red and charcoal. The pattern of falling pixels creates a visual rhythm of descending pressure, where the lighter colors attempt to rise against the weight of the dark masses. This interplay suggests a push and pull of emotions, reflecting moments of tension and release.
