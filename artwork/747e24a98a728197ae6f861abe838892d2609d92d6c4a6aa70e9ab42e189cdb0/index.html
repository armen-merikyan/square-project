Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'defiance' versus 'submission' is captured through a clash of sharp contrasts and soft transitions. The palette, 'melancholy_wonder', incorporates cool tones with muted hues to evoke introspection and conflict. The collision manifests at the center where darker, sharper colors create a stark contrast against the softer, quieter borders. This dialogue suggests a struggle between the chaotic and the orderly, reflecting the seed's thematic elements through a visual rhythm of pulse and interruption.
