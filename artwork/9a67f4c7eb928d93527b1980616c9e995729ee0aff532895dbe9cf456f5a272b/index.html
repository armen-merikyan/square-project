Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional duality of love and betrayal, utilizing contrasting colors to represent affection's warmth against betrayal's coldness. The palette of pink and red evokes love’s warmth, while dark maroon and gray symbolize the cold starkness of betrayal. The collisions occur at the edges, where vibrant colors clash against muted shades, creating a visual tension that suggests the complexity of human emotions. The layout centers on a fragmented core, surrounded by irregular placements to highlight chaos and disorder, reflecting the heart's fracture amidst its desire for connection.
