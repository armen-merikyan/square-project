Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between defiance and submission through color contrast and placement. The pink and red hues represent the intensity of emotions, while the gray and dark maroon add depth and nuance. The arrangement features a central cluster (defiance) surrounded by chaotic patterns of complementing colors (submission), creating a balance of pressure and quiet. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfection, reflecting the complexities of emotional collisions, with a rhythmic flow that suggests movement and release.
