Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between defiance and submission, using a palette that combines deep navy, muted black, and soft dim cyan to evoke a sense of isolation and quiet conflict. The contrast lies in the balance between the dark, heavy-weighted pixels against lighter, airy touches that suggest a lingering tension, like suspended emotion. The falling pattern from left to right symbolizes a journey of shifting dynamics, echoing the push and pull between openness and closure, which results in a rich yet fragmented visual narrative.
