Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional duality of sparks of vitality against ashen remnants of the past. The chosen palette conveys a delicate tension between the muted gray tones that signify restraint and the vibrant accents of faded gold that represent moments of clarity and hope. The arrangement emphasizes diagonal interruptions, creating tension across the square which symbolizes the struggle between memory and erasure. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, echoing the concept of resilience intertwined with fragility. The artwork uses abstract shapes to hint at layers of experience rather than direct representation.
