Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a battle between defiance and submission, captured in the bold contrasts of dark navy and compressed red against soft fields of dust gray and faint peach. The central square evokes tension, marked by glitch-like disruptions, while the outer edges soften into submission. The collision occurs primarily in the center, where dark and bright accents punctuate the quiet backdrop, illustrating the push and pull of the feelings described in the seed. The choice of colors reflects a mixture of dark intensity and soft calm, framing the chaotic struggle within a structured boundary.
