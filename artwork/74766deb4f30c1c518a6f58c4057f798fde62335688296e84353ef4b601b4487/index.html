Artist Statement

The artwork conveys a struggle between control (depicted in darker, muted shades) and panic (expressed through bright, expressive bursts of color). The palette of deep blue, muted purple, soft yellows, and oranges reflects a juxtaposition between sorrowful weight and vibrant energy. The top rows symbolize control through a tight, dense arrangement of dark colors, resembling a machine-like rigidity. The bottom rows represent panic with an outward radial pattern that feels erratic against the structured upper half. The asymmetry and interaction between these contrasting emotions create a dynamic visual flow, emphasizing the emotional tension of the seed.
