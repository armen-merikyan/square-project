Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality between fear and hope, where dark colors invoke a sense of pressure while soft gold and pale green hint at resilience and light. The pattern reflects a zigzag flow, with jagged edges emphasizing conflict. The warm vs. cool contrast emerges at the borders, creating a pulsing rhythm that suggests inner turmoil, interspersed with moments of quiet release. Centralized clusters of hope attempt to break through darker surroundings, symbolizing an underlying struggle amidst the chaos.
