Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress through distinct color patterns and arrangements. The top half reflects nostalgia with darker shades, signaling a depth of memory and the weight of the past, while the bottom half showcases lighter, cooler colors symbolizing progress and clarity. The contrast creates a 'drip' effect of emotion as colors transition in asymmetry, representing the inevitable collisions and disruptions between these two states. The broken edges and slight imperfections build a narrative of tension, mirroring the push and pull of human experience.
