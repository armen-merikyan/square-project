Artist Statement

The artwork captures the conflict between restlessness and peace, represented by the chaotic outer border clashing with the calm inner core. The warm palette of coral and orange suggests urgency, while the cooler shades of charcoal and blue invoke tranquility. The diagonal arrangement disrupts the flow, echoing the memory and motion theme while the inclusion of broken pixels symbolizes interruptions in thought. The dominant use of warm colors creates a tension with the sparse cool hues, allowing the observer to reflect on the contrast of feelings.
