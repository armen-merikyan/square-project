Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of restlessness and peace through contrasting patterns, with the gentleness of lavender gray representing tranquility and the vibrancy of rain blue invoking tension. The top half employs a hinge pattern suggesting stability, while the bottom disrupts this with energetic sparks of soft cyan and muted plum. The central collision occurs where these contrasting elements intersect, creating a visual metaphor for conflict, reflecting the struggle between motion and memory. The arrangement evokes both serenity and chaos, echoing the fluidity of emotional experience.
