Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the juxtaposition of loneliness and connection through a radial pattern, suggesting a spiral of feelings that oscillate between closeness and distance. The palette of soft pinks, deep reds, and muted grays embodies the theme of love and betrayal, creating an underlying warmth that is often shadowed by darker tones. The edge style maintains a thin outline, enhancing the contrast between filled and hollow spaces, suggesting emotional barriers. The placement of colors creates a central focus, surrounded by negative space, illustrating the tension of connection amidst loneliness.
