Artist Statement

This piece reflects a tension between confusion and clarity through a palette of warm and cool tones, layered in pattern. The stark contrast of dark maroon and pink symbolizes emotional turmoil, while the expansive gray tones offer a sense of calm. The arrangement features larger areas of confusion on the left, punctuated by sharp blocks of clarity on the right. Broken pixels disrupt symmetry, creating a chaotic echo that aligns with the seed's themes of disruption and pulse. The overall feeling is one of unresolved emotional movement, captured visually in a quasi-symmetrical but ultimately broken grid.
