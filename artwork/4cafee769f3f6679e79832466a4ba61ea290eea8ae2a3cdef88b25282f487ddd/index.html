Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension of awe versus terror through a balance of soft edges and contrasting colors. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon invokes feelings of vulnerability and intensity, enhancing the duality of stability and chaos. The composition features a nearly symmetrical layout disrupted by broken pixels, suggesting an imperfection that aligns with the theme of broken memory. The flow of the color pattern shifts from stable, soft hues on one side to chaotic, distorted patterns on the other, embodying the collision between order and chaos.
