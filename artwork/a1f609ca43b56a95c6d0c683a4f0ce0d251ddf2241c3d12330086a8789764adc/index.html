Artist Statement

This composition evokes a tension between trust and suspicion through the use of muted and saturated colors. The palette of sepia and faded blue suggests a sense of nostalgia and loss, creating a warm yet uncertain boundary. The main contrast occurs between the scattered dark hues representing suspicion and the lighter tones symbolizing trust, forming an intentional clash in the lower half of the cube, which conveys a fall like tears. The layout mimics the falling pixels concept, as colors descend unevenly, portraying emotional depth and conflict without representing tangible forms.
