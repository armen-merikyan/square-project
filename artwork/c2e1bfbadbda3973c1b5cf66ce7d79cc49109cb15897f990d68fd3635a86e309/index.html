Artist Statement

The emotional tone in this artwork reflects the tension between longing and resolve. The palette blends deep teal and cosmic indigo to evoke warmth and depth, while the contrasting cold gold adds tension and urgency. Broken pixels simulate disruption and a sense of repair amid the clutter. The main collision occurs at the center, where clear boundaries of resolve (represented by jagged edges and pressure bands) confront the softness of longing. The diagonally stretched layout captures the feeling of desire spreading outwards while occasionally colliding with the strength of resolve, creating a pulse-like rhythm in the abstraction. Thus, the overall composition transmits a journey from serene quietness to urgent resolution.
