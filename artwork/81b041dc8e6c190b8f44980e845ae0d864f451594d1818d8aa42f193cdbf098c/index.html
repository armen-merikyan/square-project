Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the vibrant energy of 'spark' against the subdued remnants of 'ash.' The chosen palette, featuring red for fire and warmth alongside black and pale blue for depth and coolness, conveys this dichotomy. The primary collision occurs diagonally from bottom-left to top-right, representing a struggle between vitality and exhaustion. The patterns of 'hinge' for spark and 'negative_cross' for ash create tension and interruption across the grid, reflecting the interplay of resilience and fragility.
