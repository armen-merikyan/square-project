Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a battle between defiance and submission. The palette of red, black, pale blue, and white reflects stark contrasts—red assumes warmth and intensity, black adds pressure and depth, while pale blue and white introduce calmness and space. The main collision occurs in the central rows where the patterns of defiance and submission collide, creating a visual tension that resonates with the seed's themes of disruption and spiral structure. The layout highlights the stark division between the aggressive angular patterns representing defiance at the top and the softer, scar-like textures embodying submission below. The usage of broken pixels emphasizes imperfections that act as reminders of previous conflicts, supporting the narrative of resilience amid restraint.
