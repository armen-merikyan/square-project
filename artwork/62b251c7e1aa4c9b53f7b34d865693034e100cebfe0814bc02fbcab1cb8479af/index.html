Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity. The palette of pale aqua and alarm red emphasizes urgency amid serenity, creating a stark contrast. The diagonal layout showcases certainty on one side as a structured ripple pattern, while curiosity disrupts with chaotic grid formations on the opposite, suggesting a tide that ebbs and flows. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the theme of tension and reflection in the dynamic between order and chaos.
