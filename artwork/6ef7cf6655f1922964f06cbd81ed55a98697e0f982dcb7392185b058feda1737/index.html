Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between envy and gratitude, creating a dynamic interplay in color and placement. The selected palette of navy, black, and dim cyan evokes loneliness, a quiet contrast between depth and void. The layout focuses on 'falling_pixels,' with density in the top rows resembling a cascade downwards, creating a sense of pressure and release as colors interact. The collision occurs at the edges, where darker colors create jagged boundaries, highlighting the soft edges of gratitude at the center, suggesting vulnerability and hope amidst the chaos.
