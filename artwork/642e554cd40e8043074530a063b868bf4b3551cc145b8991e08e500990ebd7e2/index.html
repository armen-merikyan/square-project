Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a tension between warmth and coldness, reflecting trust (soft sky, faded gold) and suspicion (storm gray, near black). The palette inherently creates contrast: the 'soft glow' colors are juxtaposed with deeper, darker shades representing 'hard shadows.' The main collision occurs at the center, where darker colors penetrate lighter areas, embodying the conflict and interplay of contrasting emotions. The diagonal fall guides the gaze, while subtle 'broken pixels' disrupt the flow, embodying fragility amidst resilience, translating the seed into a compelling abstract narrative.
