Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic interplay between restlessness and peace. The chosen palette, comprising muted greens and earthy browns alongside contrasting pale and open blues, signifies a safe space that evinces calm yet hints at underlying tension. The collision occurs at the diagonal stretch from corner to corner, where dense clusters of darker hues are interrupted by brighter, lighter fields, echoing the oscillation between tranquility and a desire for motion. The pattern diverges, with clustered pixels at one edge flowing into calmer, more negative space—alluding to a crack that disrupts the serenity. This structure captures the essence of the seed’s abstract qualities, promoting an engaging exploration between opposing emotions.
