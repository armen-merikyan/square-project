Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between vulnerability and strength through a contrasting palette of deep blues and pale yellows. The center of the grid embodies vulnerability with a soft lattice of pale yellow, while the surrounding edges display a structured patchwork of dark hues, representing strength. The main collision occurs around the central pixels, where the two emotional tones blend and interrupt one another, evoking a sense of both protection and openness. The negative space has been kept intentionally minimal to enhance the feeling of exposure while maintaining balance in the composition.
