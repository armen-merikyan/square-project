Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the juxtaposition of loneliness and connection through a dominant left side invoking feelings of isolation with muted, darker colors. The right side, symbolizing connection, is vibrant and noisy. The contrasting palette of deep reds and grays reflects the tension between warmth and coldness, suggesting hidden vulnerabilities. The burst layout creates an apparent central focus, echoing a sense of pressure and release. The crosshatch pattern on the left embodies the tangled feelings of loneliness, while the chaotic noise on the right evokes energetic connection.
