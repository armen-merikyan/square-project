Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between restlessness and peace through a juxtaposed palette. The tighter clusters of darker colors signify the pressure of restlessness, while softer shades represent an underlying peace. The collision of static and flowing patterns suggests dynamic motion interrupted by moments of calm. The edges are intentionally closed, emphasizing the contained chaos within, while the occasional bright accent pixels reflect glimpses of clarity amidst disturbance.
