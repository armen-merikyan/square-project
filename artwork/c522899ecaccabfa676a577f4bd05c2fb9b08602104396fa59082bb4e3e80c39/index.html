Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional contrast between chaos and tranquility. The palette of red, black, pale blue, and white captures a spectrum from intense pressure to calming clarity. The sharp contrasts between the deep black and stark white create a sense of collision, while the pale blue introduces a soft rhythm, providing a visual balm to the tension. The pattern structure establishes a central focus with radial bursts that evoke both confusion and clarity, reflecting the emotional collision described in the seed.
