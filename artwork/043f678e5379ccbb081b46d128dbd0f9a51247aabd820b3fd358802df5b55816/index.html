Artist Statement

This piece conveys the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The palette, inspired by 'broken_memory', evokes a sense of faded memories and contrasts sharply with itself, aligning with the emotional pairing. Darker hues dominate the top rows, invoking a sense of compression and density, while the lighter bottom rows introduce moments of clarity, creating a visual break. The jagged edges of the darker colors contrast against the smoother lighter shades, symbolizing the struggle between chaotic thoughts and clear insights. The deliberate placement of 'broken pixels' throughout adds an element of imperfection that enhances the overall feeling of disarray and unresolved emotion.
