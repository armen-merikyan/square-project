Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between panic and control, reflecting a chaotic inward pull against a structured, calm perimeter. The palette, featuring deep navy and dim cyan with highlights of white, embodies a stark contrast between controlled calm and frenetic energy. The arrangement of broken dots (control) interspersed with rain-like streaks (panic) creates a collision of emotion where the center is dense, representing turmoil, while the edges remain hollow, suggesting retreat and containment.
