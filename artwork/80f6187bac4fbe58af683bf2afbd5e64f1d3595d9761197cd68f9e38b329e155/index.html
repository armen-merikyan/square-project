Artist Statement

This composition embodies a tension between loneliness and connection, represented through contrasting dark and muted tones of sepia and gray against bursts of faded blue. The palette reflects a 'broken memory,' invoking feelings of nostalgia and the weight of isolation. The top-weighted layout suggests a descent of emotions, where color density shifts abruptly, creating collisions of feeling in the center versus the edges. The broken pixel addition emphasizes imperfection and vulnerability, enhancing the emotional narrative without literal imagery.
