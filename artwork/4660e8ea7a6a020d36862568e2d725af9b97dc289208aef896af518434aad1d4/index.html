Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and disruption, emphasizing the tension between signal and noise. The chosen palette of navy, black, dim cyan, and white creates a somber yet striking contrast, with navy representing the deep layers of noise and crisp white symbolizing the fragile pulse of signal trying to emerge. The central collision occurs in the middle rows where the 'pulse' of bright white interrupts the darker static surrounding it, creating a focal point that suggests an underlying order amidst the chaotic arrangement. The patterns of fault lines and patchwork create a sense of dynamic motion while leaving quiet negative space to evoke a feeling of unease and anticipation. The overall composition mirrors the complexity of modern existence, balancing moments of clarity with the constant presence of interference.
