Artist Statement

The artwork captures a dynamic interplay between the emotions of restlessness and peace. The chosen palette of ice blue and clean white represents moments of calm, while ink black and warning orange signify turmoil and chaos. The collision occurs centrally, where the patterns of 'noise' and 'fault line' intersect, creating a stark division and contrasting rhythm. This evokes the tension of memories in motion versus the desire for stillness, reflected in the textured and broken pixel placements. The layout suggests a seam where these feelings clash, inviting contemplation of balance amidst inner turmoil.
