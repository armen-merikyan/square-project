Artist Statement

The composition captures the emotional turmoil of restlessness juxtaposed with serene patches of peace. The selected palette—mourning black, deep blue, new leaf, pale yellow, and stone—evokes a feeling of renewal and reflection, with darker colors representing tension and lighter shades invoking calmness. The main contrast occurs across the diagonal, where dense clusters of darker colors collide with lighter spaces, echoing the push and pull of conflicting emotions. The seed of 'ladder' symbolizes ascension and complexity, translated into a chaotic arrangement that suggests movement amid stillness.
