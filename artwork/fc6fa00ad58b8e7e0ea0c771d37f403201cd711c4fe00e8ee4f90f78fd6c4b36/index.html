Artist Statement

The composition captures the emotional tug between control and surrender, utilizing the 'fear_hope' palette to evoke the clash of shadow and light. The dominant deep colors create a controlling pressure, while the subtle warm accents hint at release within a tangled center. Strong lines of dark violet and sparse soft gold mimic the patterns of pressure bands and stairs, illustrating the conflict in a halo void layout. The main collision sustains energy in the inner ring, creating a focal point of tension surrounded by moments of quiet space.
