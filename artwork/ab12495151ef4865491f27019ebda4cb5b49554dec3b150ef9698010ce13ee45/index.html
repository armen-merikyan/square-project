Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between panic and control, captured through the use of stark contrasting colors—the black and red signify urgency and anxiety, while the pale blue and white create moments of clarity and calm. The layout mirrors itself with certain pixels slightly misaligned, reflecting the instability of control against the chaos of panic. The radial pattern from the edges collects towards the center, symbolizing a draw towards the calm amidst the turmoil, while the split-screen illusion reinforces this tension with ongoing movement towards balance.
