Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between fear and hope through a structured layout where the top rows reflect fear and the bottom rows embody hope. The dark color palette emphasizes fear with dense, jagged patterns, while soft gold and pale green offer a contrast of hopeful rising signals and gentle touches. The zigzag pattern creates an interruption, echoing a struggle between sharp and soft sensations. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, emphasizing the fragility of hope within a darker, more oppressive context.
