Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the clash between the ephemeral spark of potential and the settling ash of restraint. The chosen palette of soft warmth and harsh severity conveys this dichotomy; muted shades of powder pink and warm white are pierced with sharp crimson and carbon black, representing the pulsating moments of desperation amidst a backdrop of calm resignation. The main clash occurs at the center, where 'spark' and 'ash' colors break and overlap, creating visual tension. The layout suggests descending movement, echoing the theme of falling pixels akin to sparks dissipating into ash while leaving spaces of quiet gray, symbolizing untouched memory. This arrangement illustrates the discourse of aspiration versus limitation, resulting in an intentional, abstract engagement.
