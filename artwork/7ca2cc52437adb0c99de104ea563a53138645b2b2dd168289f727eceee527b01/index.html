Artist Statement

This piece embodies the complex interplay of envy and gratitude, employing a palette of deep reds and soft grays to evoke tension and balance. The upward movement of colors signifies rising hope while the downward tones suggest a sense of gravity, creating a dynamic flow. Negative space represents moments of pause, emphasizing the internal conflict between the two emotions. Color contrasts emerge where soft edges meet hard transitions, embodying collision and resolve as emotions shift within the empty room of the experience.
