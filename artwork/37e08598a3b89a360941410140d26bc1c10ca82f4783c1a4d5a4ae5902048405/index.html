Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, represented through a warm palette interspersed with cooler shades. The main collision occurs in the center where the fiery hues intersect with cooler tones, symbolizing the conflict and harmony in the emotional narrative. The colors, derived from an emotional palette of desire and emptiness, climb upward to reflect hope and pressure, creating a rising signal that echoes across the grid, evoking a sense of climbing towards resolution despite the underlying chaos.
