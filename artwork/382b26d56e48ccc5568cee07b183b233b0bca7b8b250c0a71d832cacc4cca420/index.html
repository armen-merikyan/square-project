Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension of joy and grief through a distinct color palette and structured patterns. The outer edges utilize deep blues and muted purples to suggest a sense of heaviness and fragmentation, representing grief, while a stair-step pattern of warm yellows and oranges in the center conveys an open and expanding joy. The arrangement creates a split-screen effect that embodies the struggle between order and chaos, with hard edges reinforcing the boundaries between emotions, suggesting safety amidst threats. Color placement creates a collision at the intersection of these patterns, emphasizing the complexity of human emotion.
