Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between defiance and submission, conveyed through a palette that captures both fear (dark colors) and hope (soft gold). The contrast emphasizes dark versus light, representing resistance versus acceptance. The pattern features dense, horizontal stripes symbolizing defiance, interrupted by jagged cracks representing submission, forming an abstract narrative of conflict. The use of broken pixels adds imperfection, inviting discomfort, while the bottom-weighted composition dramatizes the emotional struggle inherent in the seed.
