Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension of desire and restraint through a split layout, where the top half emphasizes warmth and passion, using vibrant red and small orange against muted cream and blackened plum. The bottom half introduces cooler tones with restrained marks, employing iron gray to signify containment. The collision happens centrally, where bright elements bleed into the cooler edge, embodying the struggle between urgency and control. The chosen palette contrasts heat and calm, generating a pulse that resonates with the emotional dynamics described in the seed.
