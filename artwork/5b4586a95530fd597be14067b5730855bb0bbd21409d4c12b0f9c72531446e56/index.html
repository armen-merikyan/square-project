Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the brightness of joy against the somberness of grief, depicted through a vibrant palette of yellow and orange clashing with deep blue and muted purple. The forced relationship between light and dark illustrates a zigzagging path of emotions—from the rising signal of joy (bright colors) to the ash-like quality of grief (bluish tones). The composition employs a radial pattern for joy with expanding blocks, interrupted by teeth-like edges of grief, creating a sense of balance yet disturbance. This duality represents the chaotic beauty of contrasting feelings, with broken pixels adding subtle discomfort, maintaining negative space for emotional breathing.
