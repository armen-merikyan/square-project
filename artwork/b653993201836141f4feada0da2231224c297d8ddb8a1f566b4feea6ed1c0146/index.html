Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts trust and suspicion, mimicking a pulse against static. The palette, inspired by melancholy colors such as rain blue and muted plum, evokes feelings of calm and uncertainty. The main collision occurs in the center where horizontal and vertical patterns of subtle stripes and halos intertwine, creating a tense crossing that embodies the essence of trust battling suspicion. Broken pixels are strategically placed to suggest imperfections and echoes, enhancing the drama and complexity of the design.
