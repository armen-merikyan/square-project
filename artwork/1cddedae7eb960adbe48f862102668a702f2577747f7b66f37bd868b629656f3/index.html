Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes trust's stable, warm hues against suspicion's cool, unsettled tones. The chosen palette—muted purples for a soft glow, deep blue for stable undercurrents, and contrasting yellows and oranges for the pressure zones—captures the essence of both emotions. The main contrast occurs horizontally, where deep blue defines a center channel representing trust, interrupted starkly by bands of muted purple and yellow, evoking a sense of conflict and instability. The collision happens along the center, where patterns of soft enclosure meet jagged, interrupted pathways, symbolizing the tension between trust and suspicion. This pattern mirrors a split, hinting at resilience within fragility.
