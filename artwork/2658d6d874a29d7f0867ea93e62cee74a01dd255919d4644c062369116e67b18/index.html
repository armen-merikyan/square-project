Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between longing and resolve through a palette that juxtaposes warm, muted tones with a single, strong accent color. The sepia and tan represent the warmth of longing while the sharp contrasts with dust_gray and faded_blue highlight moments of clarity and determination. The arrangement creates a downward cascade that conveys a sense of falling or drifting, suggesting a progression from chaotic emotions to a more structured resolve. The broken edges throughout act as reminders of interruptions in this journey, combining both intimacy and distance within the pixel placements, emphasizing the emotional collisions within the narrative.
