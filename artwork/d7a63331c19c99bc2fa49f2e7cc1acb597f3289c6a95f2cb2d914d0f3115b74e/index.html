Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between restlessness and peace, accentuated by contrasting elements that collide and communicate through color. The palette of muted creams, deep reds, and dark hues speaks to both hunger and restraint, while broken pixels interrupt clean lines, creating the sense of movement amidst tranquility. The transition from light to dark on the diagonal creates an argument between stability and chaos, giving space to breathe while simultaneously engaging with the encoded restlessness of memory and motion. The pattern reflects a fragmented space, typical of an empty room filled with unexpressed sentiments.
