Artist Statement

This artwork embodies a tension between longing and resolve through its contrasting color fields. The palette draws from muted sepia tones and soft blues, evoking nostalgia while simultaneously suggesting a pathway toward clarity. The layout features stacked bands, creating a sense of contrast between the density of the longing and the structured resolve. The central collision occurs where darker colors meet lighter shades, representing a movement from confusion to clarity. Broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, hinting at damage yet also suggesting repair, reminiscent of the complex interplay between hope and uncertainty inherent in the seed.
