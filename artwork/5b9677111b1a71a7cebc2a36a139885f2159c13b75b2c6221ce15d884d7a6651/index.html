Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dichotomy between trust and suspicion, effectively represented through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of deep black and vibrant magenta emphasizes tension, while the electric blue introduces a pulse of uncertainty. The outer ring of darkness symbolizes suspicion, while the inner core of cooler colors suggests trust. The collision occurs centrally, where the bright accents intersect with the muted shadows, echoing the uneasy balance between warmth and coldness, trust and doubt.
