Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a balance of joy and grief, represented by bright yellows and muted blues. The palette contrasts warm joy with cool grief, creating a dance between light and shadow. The arrangement features sharp edges and asymmetric patterns, suggesting the disorientation of grief intertwined with the spark of joy. The upward movement on one side clashes with the downward flow on the other, embodying the tension between safety and threat, order and chaos.
