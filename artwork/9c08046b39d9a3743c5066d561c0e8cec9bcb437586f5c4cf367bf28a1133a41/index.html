Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional duality of spark versus ash, capturing the tension between bright exuberance and muted decay. The palette, dominated by vibrant reds and soothing grays, creates a strong contrast that speaks to exhilaration and melancholy. The diagonal layout emphasizes the struggle, as the 'spark' colors in jagged patterns compete against the smooth, somber 'ash' stripes. This dynamic interplay resonates with the themes of resilience amid fragility, where the dominance of warm tones suggests vitality but is interrupted by cooler shades that remind us of loss. The arrangement thrives on collision, with broken pixel elements adding intentional imperfections that echo life’s unpredictability. A 15% negative space allows for breath, enhancing the emotional resonance of both light and dark elements. Overall, this configuration transforms the seed's essence into a compelling, abstract emotional narrative.
