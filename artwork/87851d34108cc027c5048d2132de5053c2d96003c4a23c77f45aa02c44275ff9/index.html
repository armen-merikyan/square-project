Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between restlessness and peace, utilizing a palette derived from muted sepia and soft blues, creating a contrast of warmth against coolness. The collision manifests centrally through a pattern that juxtaposes patchwork with sharp, jagged interruptions, symbolizing disruption within tranquility. The shape of flame evokes a sense of energy that contrasts with the calm, forming tension and motion in the arrangement.
