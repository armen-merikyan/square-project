Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, manifesting in a palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue. The soft, muted hues evoke memories and reflections, contrasting with sharper, deeper colors that symbolize strength and boundaries. The arrangement allows for a spacious layout where the central focus represents the collision of longing and resolve, with broken pixels introducing subtle disruptions, resembling moments of uncertainty. Overall, the 8x8 grid embodies an interplay of quiet and density, mirroring the complexity of human emotion.
