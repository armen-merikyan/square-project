Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between longing and resolve through an interplay of soft and severe colors. The palette blends the warmth of powder pink and warm white with the starkness of carbon black and sharp crimson, creating a visual conflict that represents the push and pull of the emotional themes. The broken patterns evoke a sense of imperfection, embodying the concept of repair versus damage, while the layout introduces a crossing current that symbolizes unresolved tension. The central collision between warm and cool tones captures the essence of yearning amidst moments of steadiness, resulting in a richly layered narrative.
