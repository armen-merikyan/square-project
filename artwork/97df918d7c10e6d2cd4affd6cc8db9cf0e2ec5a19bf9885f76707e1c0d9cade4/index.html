Artist Statement

This piece represents the conflict of restlessness against peace through a palette of warm coral and soft amber contrasting with cold slate and blue-black. The design reveals a diagonal flow from the top corner to the opposite edge, suggesting motion and struggle while maintaining patches of tranquil color. The interplay between glitch-inspired disruptions and areas of static calm evokes a memory of interrupted peace, embodying the themes of motion and recollection. The collision occurs at the center, where the restless and serene collide, creating a compelling tension.
