Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between restlessness and peace, utilizing a palette of vibrant and muted tones to reflect emotional contrasts. The black and electric blue provide a grounding darkness, while magenta and lime suggest bursts of dynamic energy. The layout flows spirally, creating a sense of motion and tension that collides in the center. Broken pixels symbolize the imperfections of memory and the friction between opposing emotions, creating interruptions throughout the piece.
