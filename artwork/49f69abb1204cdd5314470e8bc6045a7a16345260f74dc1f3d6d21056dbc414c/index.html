Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the turmoil and tranquility found in the collisions of various feelings. The palette of warm yellows and muted purples symbolizes joy and grief, respectively, suggesting a nuanced contrast. Deep blues add depth and a sense of rhythm, conveying a quiet yet powerful presence. The jagged edges and asymmetrical patterns enhance the sense of tension and release, creating a dynamic interplay of color. The arrangement prioritizes negative space, emphasizing the weight of emotion while allowing moments of silence amidst the pressure. The vibrant yellows clash with subdued purples and blues, invoking a heartbeat of emotion—a rhythm that echoes the complexities of human experience.
