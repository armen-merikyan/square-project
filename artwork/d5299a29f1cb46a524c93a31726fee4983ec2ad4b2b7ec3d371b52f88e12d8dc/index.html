Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the warmth of love against the coldness of betrayal, represented by the palette of pinks and grays. The tumultuous flow created by the falling pixel layout mirrors the descent of emotions from joy to sorrow. The stark contrast between vibrant pinks and darker, muted hues illustrates the collision of warmth and emptiness—signified through the deliberate placement of warmer colors on the left, gradually transitioning to cooler tones on the right. The 'broken pixels' add a sense of imperfection, mirroring the fracturing of heart. This abstract pattern conveys a story of emotional tension, representing the struggle between love and betrayal without literal imagery.
