Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between love and betrayal through a contrast of warm and cool tones, creating a split emotional landscape. The outer edges utilize darker shades of maroon to represent betrayal, while the inner area employs warmer pinks to evoke love. The crosshatch pattern invokes feelings of conflict and interruption, while the soft edges allow for a gentle transition from one emotion to another. The composition guides the eye from bottom to top, suggesting a journey through the layers of feeling. The arrangement of pixels reflects a balance of pressure against quiet spaces, where moments of desolation intermingle with the warmth of connection.
