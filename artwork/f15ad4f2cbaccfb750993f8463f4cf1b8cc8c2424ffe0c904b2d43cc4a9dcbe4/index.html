Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between loneliness and connection through a structured division that features dark, muted colors contrasted by brighter accents. The palette, consisting of black, dark violet, and soft gold, portrays a sense of melancholic depth alongside moments of hope. The arrangement resembles a pulse emanating from the center, where softer colors emanate a quiet release against a backdrop of pressure, suggesting a balance between dense feelings and the need for space. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, enhancing the feelings of interruption and unresolved motion within the visual narrative.
