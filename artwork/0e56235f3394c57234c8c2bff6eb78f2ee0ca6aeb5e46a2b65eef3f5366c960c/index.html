Artist Statement

The composition embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, contrasting soft edges with hard blocks. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold symbolizes the intensity of emotions and the clash of inner and outer worlds. The main collision occurs in the center, where density from strong colors meets the fragility of lighter ones, representing a passage or seam where these feelings interact. This abstract nature reflects the seed as it translates emotional oppositions into a visual dialogue, creating an engaging rhythm of pressure and release within the 8x8 grid.
