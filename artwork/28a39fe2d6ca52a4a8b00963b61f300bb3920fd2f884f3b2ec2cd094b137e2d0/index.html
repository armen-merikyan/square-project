Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional struggle between defiance and submission through a contrast of colors and shapes. The dominant void black represents the defiant, unmoving center while surrounding colors create a softer, more submissive edge. Cosmic indigo and deep teal weave opposing diagonal flows, breaking the perceived order with cold gold accents that pulse against a backdrop of quiet star white, creating echoes of chaos and calm. This tension plays out within an overfilled center, suggesting pressure at the core while imbuing the edges with a hint of submission, embodying the push and pull of identity.
