Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of safety and exposure, presenting an open central space surrounded by contrasting protections. The palette features warm browns and soft greens to evoke shelter, juxtaposed with cooler whites and blues signifying exposure. The main visual collision occurs in the center where pulses of warmth and coolness interact, creating a rhythmic echo visually symbolizing the tension between safety and vulnerability.
