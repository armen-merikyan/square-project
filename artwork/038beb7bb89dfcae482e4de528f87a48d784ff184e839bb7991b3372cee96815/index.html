Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between joy and grief through a split layout, emphasizing upward movement contrasted with downward waves. The palette blends bright yellows and oranges (joy) with deep blues and muted purples (grief), creating a visual tug-of-war. The edges are irregular, mimicking the break in symmetry, and the patterns overlaying the colors create a rhythmic pulse that embodies the conflict of hope against heaviness. The negative space reflects moments of quiet amidst the dense color blocks, reinforcing the emotional weight and elevation.
