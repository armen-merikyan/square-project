Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of restlessness and peace, using a palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue. The light vs. dark contrast is expressed through the interplay of muted tones and sharper, more vibrant accents. The rising signal layout creates a sense of upward movement, symbolizing hope amidst chaos. The abstract nature is emphasized through fragmented patterns, which reflect the crack motif, suggesting unresolved tension. The collision of checker patterns representing peace against a background of disruptive, cracked designs evokes a struggle for balance.
