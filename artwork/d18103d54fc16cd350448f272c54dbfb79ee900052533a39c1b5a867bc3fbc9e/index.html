Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between clarity and confusion—signified by the pulse of coherent signal amid the chaotic noise. The palette of navy, black, dim cyan, and white contrasts the darker, muted elements with brighter, more vibrant accents, mirroring the conflict inherent in the theme. The main collision occurs diagonally where the brighter colors attempt to break through the static shadows, creating a vibrant yet tense atmosphere. The patterns of islands and constellations represent the clarity struggling for dominance against a backdrop of distractions, reflecting the broken mirror concept. This arrangement balances dense clusters with negative space to enhance the emotional dialogue between order and chaos.
