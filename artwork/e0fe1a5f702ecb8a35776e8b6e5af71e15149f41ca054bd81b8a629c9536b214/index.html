Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional conflict between restlessness and peace through contrasting colors and pixel arrangements. The palette is inspired by the themes of safety (shelter_green and warm_brown) and exposure (cold_white and open_blue), creating a balance between warm and cool tones. The dominant color on the left evokes a sense of peace, while the right side embodies restlessness with interruptions in the flow of pixels. The main collision occurs diagonally, emphasizing tension between structured islands ('peace') and chaotic thresholds ('restlessness'), framing the square's narrative of emotional transition.
