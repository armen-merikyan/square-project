Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional dichotomy between quiet and pressure, employing a palette of muted tones and sharp contrasts. The dark navy serves as the base for dense clusters of pressure, while off-white creates isolated fields of quiet, resulting in a visual tension that invites contemplation. The main contrast occurs at the diagonal, where the weighted colors collide, introducing an element of interruption within the structured grid. This follows the seed's guidance of balancing pressure against moments of clarity, a shatter against the tide of emotional noise.
