Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and distortion, echoing the themes of signal versus noise. The palette of deep navy and dim cyan against stark black creates a stark contrast, emphasizing the pulse of moments against an overwhelming background of randomness. The noise is expressed through broken dots in the top rows (noise), while the bottom rows (signal) present vivid pulses. The collision occurs at the center where the dynamic forces of light and dark merge, revealing moments of clarity amidst chaos.
