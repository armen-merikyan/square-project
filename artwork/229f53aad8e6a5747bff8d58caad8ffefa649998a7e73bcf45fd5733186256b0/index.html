Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between rage and calm through the use of contrasting color palettes and patterns. The palette incorporates deep, intense reds representing rage against a backdrop of cooler, muted blues symbolizing calm. The main collision occurs at the center, where the jagged red channels disrupt the smoother lines of the pale blue, creating a visual roar against a tranquil field. The use of broken pixels enhances this sense of imperfection, echoing the mechanical emotion of the seed while maintaining an overall balance of negative space and dominant color.
