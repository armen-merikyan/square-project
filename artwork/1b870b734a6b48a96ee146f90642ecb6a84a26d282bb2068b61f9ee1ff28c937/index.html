Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between isolation and intimacy, with the top half embodying 'loneliness' through a muted palette of dark reds and charcoals, creating an oppressive feeling. The bottom half contrasts with a burst of warmth and gold, representing 'connection' and a sense of illuminated hope. The crosshatched patterns contribute to a visual rhythm that echoes the push and pull of the emotional themes, while the tear shape subtly disrupts the overall grid, suggesting imperfection and the fragility of connections.
