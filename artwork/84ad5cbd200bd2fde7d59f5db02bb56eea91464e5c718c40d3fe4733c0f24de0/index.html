Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional duality of quiet and pressure. The palette of off-white, dust gray, and compressed red reflects the tension between soft calm and dark weight, with layers colliding in the center. The pattern of 'teeth' creates jagged interruptions against the soft 'stepwell' spaces, guiding the eye through pressure zones and negative space that suggest release. The placement ensures a dense collection on the left, gradually dispersing towards the right, symbolizing the push against 'still' quiet.
