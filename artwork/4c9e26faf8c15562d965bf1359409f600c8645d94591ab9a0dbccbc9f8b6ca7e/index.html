Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a push and pull between quiet and pressure, using a palette that contrasts soft pastels with deep saturations. Dark navy creates a weighted backdrop, accentuated by compressed red as an explosive highlight, suggesting moments of sudden clarity against the inherent chaos. The diagonal flow of muted warm tones creates a gradient, interrupted by sharper contrasts that emerge unexpectedly, embodying the theme of a fractured narrative. This arrangement allows the tension to dominate while still maintaining a space for quiet reflection.
