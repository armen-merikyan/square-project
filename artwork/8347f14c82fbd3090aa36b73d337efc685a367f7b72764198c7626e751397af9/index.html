Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional interplay between emptiness and desire, using a palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue to embody nostalgia and complexity. The structured order on one side encounters chaotic fragments, creating tension. The warm tones clash subtly against cool blues, suggesting a rhythmic push and pull between calmness and pressure. The glowing edges provide an ethereal quality, highlighting areas of emotional turbulence and quiet reflection, while broken pixels add a sense of imperfection, mirroring real emotional collisions.
