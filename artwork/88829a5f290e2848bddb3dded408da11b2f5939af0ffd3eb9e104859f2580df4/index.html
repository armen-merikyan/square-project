Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay of joy and grief through color and placement. Bright yellows and oranges create openings of joy, contrasting with heavy deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The palette symbolizes a dance between warmth and coolness, while the alternating patterns suggest a rhythmic conflict between order and chaos. By placing dense clusters of 'grief' colors against sparser 'joy' hues, the piece illustrates that moment of emotional collision where hope meets sorrow; the thin outlines maintain clarity amidst the turmoil.
