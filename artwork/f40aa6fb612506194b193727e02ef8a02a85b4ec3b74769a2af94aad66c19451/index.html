Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional spectrum of joy and grief through an interplay of warm and cool colors, creating tension between bright expanses and dark clusters. The palette, mixing vibrant yellows and muted purples, represents joy as warmth and grief as heaviness. The placement favors the lower left, symbolizing a weighty experience, while the upward layout signals hope breaking through grief. The patterns interlace a grid of joy against the drips of grief—a reflection of the coexistence of these emotions.
