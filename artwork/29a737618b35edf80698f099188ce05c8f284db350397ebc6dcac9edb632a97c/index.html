Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a dance between confusion and clarity, representing the oscillation between pressure and release. The palette of deep blacks, vibrant reds, and soft blues embodies conflict and calm, respectively. The collision occurs primarily in the central area, where darker fields clash against the cooler hues, suggesting a struggle. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, reinforcing the theme of disrupted symmetry. The abstract pattern reflects these dynamics through asymmetric placements that evoke a sense of unresolved motion.
