Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between joy and grief through a rising signal layout. The warm colors of yellow and orange symbolize joy, while the deep blue and muted purple introduce an element of grief. The zigzag pattern reflects an interruption between the expanding joy and the drooping grief, creating visual conflict akin to the push and pull of emotions. The thin outlines enhance the contrast of the vivid colors against the background, while broken pixels create a sense of imperfection that resonates with the theme of emotional struggles. The negative space is maintained to reflect moments of quiet amidst the emotional turbulence.
