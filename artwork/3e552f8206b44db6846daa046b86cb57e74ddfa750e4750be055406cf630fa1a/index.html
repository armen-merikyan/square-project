Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between desire and emptiness, captured through contrasting saturated colors of hot coral and blood orange against muted hues of charcoal and hollow blue. The central collision occurs in an elliptical pattern, where the warm saturated pull of desire intersects with cooler, reserved areas representing emptiness. The layout evokes scattered repair, with broken pixels creating intentional disruptions, emphasizing the unresolved nature of the contrasting feelings.
