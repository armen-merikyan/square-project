Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the dynamic interplay between softness and severity. The palette blends warm whites and powder pinks to convey softness with sharp crimson and carbon black to evoke severity. The composition reflects a central collision of bright colors surrounded by deep tones, resonating with the seed's themes of tension and release. The checker and crosshatch patterns create a rhythmic disruption, echoing the pulse against radial dynamics. The layout prioritizes light to showcase a small bright area against a darker background, embodying the conflict between openness and boundaries.
