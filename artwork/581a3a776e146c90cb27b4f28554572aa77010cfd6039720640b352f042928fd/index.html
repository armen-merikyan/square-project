Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between fear and hope, illustrated through the use of a stark palette dominated by dark hues contrasted with glowing edges. The composition evokes a split feeling, with dark corners representing fear while the noise patterns suggest rising hope. The layout embodies a mirror effect, with intentional disruptions to create tension in the symmetry. The selected colors and arrangement create a dynamic balance, where bursts of gold symbolize resilience against the oppressive weight of darkness, giving a nuanced representation of the emotional conflict.
