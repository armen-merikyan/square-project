Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between joy and grief through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette combines bright yellows and oranges representing joy, with muted purples and deep blues symbolizing grief. The placement of lighter colors in a left-weighted manner creates an emotional push against a backdrop of darker patches, signifying the struggle between uplifting moments and heavy emotions. The spiral flow suggests movement, while the broken pixel pattern alludes to imperfection and disturbance within the emotional fabric.
