Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured here is one of contrast—between the warmth of spark and the coldness of ash. The palette blends vivid reds and soft grays, symbolizing a dance between resilience and fragility. The collision occurs diagonally, where the bright, warm colors push against the cooler, muted tones creating tension. The chosen layout, 'corner_invasion', positions the spark element in the top left, with ash radiating outward, echoing the struggle of memory against erasure, while maintaining an abstract structure.
