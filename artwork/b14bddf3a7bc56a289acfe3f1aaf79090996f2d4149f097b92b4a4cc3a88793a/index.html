Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between panic and control, represented by sharp contrasts of deep navy against stark white, symbolizing the chaos and clarity of argument and resolution. The use of 'lonely_signal' palette reflects isolation and urgency, while the diagonal flow suggests an inescapable descent into tension. Collisions between saturated colors and muted tones emphasize the fractured symmetry of the composition, evoking a sense of unresolved conflict, where bright pixels spar against darker edges amidst a space that feels half-empty, encapsulating both joy and grief through a delicate balance of presence and absence.
