Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between suspicion and trust, captured through a palette of muted and soft colors. The primary colors of soft cyan and lavender gray represent warmth and openness, while deeper hues like rain blue and muted plum introduce shadows of doubt. A diagonal collision emerges from the contrast of dense colors in the lower half against lighter, open bands above, embodying the psychological tension of trust versus suspicion. The fragmented structure throughout magnifies the themes of resilience and fragility, fueling a narrative of navigating emotional landscapes.
