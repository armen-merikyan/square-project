Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork explores the tension between loneliness and connection through a stark contrast of dark and bright colors, representing feelings of isolation and warmth. The palette, primarily composed of deep navy and dim cyan, evokes a sense of weight and depth, symbolizing loneliness, while the inclusion of white hints at moments of connection breaking through the dark. The layout is split horizontally, with the top half showcasing the loneliness in crosshatch patterns, creating an atmosphere of noise and tension, while the bottom half features a more connected, open grid representing connection. The pulse-like zigzag flow and top-heavy weight reflect the fluctuating nature of human emotions. Broken pixels add a sense of imperfection, reinforcing the theme of hidden versus exposed emotions, and the use of negative space invites contemplation, allowing viewers to experience the quiet amidst the noise.
