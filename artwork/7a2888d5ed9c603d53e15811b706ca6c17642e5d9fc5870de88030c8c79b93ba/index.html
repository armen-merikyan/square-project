Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between fear and hope through contrasting elements. The palette, consisting of dark violet for fear and soft gold and pale green for hope, creates a striking dynamic. The use of checker patterns represents the fear with jagged edges, while the noise pattern conveys the gentle rise of hope. The split vertical flow emphasizes the collision of these emotions, marked by a dense center surrounded by lighter, broken edges, suggesting the struggle and eventual burst of hope against a backdrop of fear.
