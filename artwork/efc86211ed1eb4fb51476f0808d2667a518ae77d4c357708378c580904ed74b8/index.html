Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the collision of fear and hope, represented through a palette that juxtaposes dark violet and soft gold, symbolizing density and light. The sharp vs. soft contrast creates a rhythm that flows left to right, emphasizing the push and pull of emotions. The layout of falling pixels allows for a sense of quietness amidst pressure, while patterns reflect tension through stripes and noise, creating a visual narrative of unresolved motion.
