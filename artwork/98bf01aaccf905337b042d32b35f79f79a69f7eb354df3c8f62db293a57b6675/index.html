Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece contrasts between the isolation of loneliness represented by darker, muted colors at the top and the warmth of connection shown through vibrant accents at the bottom. The palette, using deep blacks and pale blues with hints of white and red, highlights the tension of feeling both hidden and exposed. The burst of brighter colors at the lower half illustrates moments of connection bursting forth from surrounding shadows, with a zigzag flow suggesting interconnectedness amidst fragmentation. The layout employs a split horizontal design, where the loneliness pattern features dense, maze-like complexity while the connection section provides spacious framing, leaving ample negative space to evoke breath and relief.
