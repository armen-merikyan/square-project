Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes confusion and clarity, illustrated through the maze-like pattern of dark and muted colors against brighter accents. The chosen palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes an atmosphere of tension and hope. The main contrast occurs at the center where disordered arrangements of deep colors interact with the softer hues, symbolizing the collision between clarity and confusion. The layout follows a rising signal, giving a sense of ascent despite the chaos. The broken pixels scattered throughout provide a layer of imperfection, reflecting the disturbance in symmetry.
