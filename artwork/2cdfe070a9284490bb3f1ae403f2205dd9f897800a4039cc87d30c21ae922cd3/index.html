Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between softness and severity, embodying tension. The palette blends powder pink and warm white for softness, countered by carbon black and sharp crimson, presenting a stark emotional dichotomy. The contrast is centered through broken frame patterns colliding with gentle checker patterns, suggesting an upheaval of calmness by the sharpness of severity. The spiraling flow creates a sense of unravelling, with a bottom-weighted emphasis suggesting burden, while allowing negative space to resonate, enhancing the emotional pulse through subtle breaks in the pixel arrangement.
