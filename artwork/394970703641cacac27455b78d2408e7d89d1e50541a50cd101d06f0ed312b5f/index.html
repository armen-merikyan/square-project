Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork oscillates between confusion and clarity through the use of stark black and white contrasted by shades of pale blue and red. The palette reflects both tension and calm, aiming to elicit a sense of turbulence punctuated by moments of clarity. The layout emulates falling pixels, which visually represent the descent from chaos to clarity. The jagged edges and the stairs pattern embody the underlying tension of brokenness against attempts of symmetry. Collisions of dense black and vibrant red invoke moments of interruption amidst the structured pale blue lines, creating a narrative of struggle and resolve.
