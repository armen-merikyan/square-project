Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between longing and resolve, using warm yellows and oranges to convey yearning, contrasted by deeper blues and muted purples symbolizing strength and composure. The palette creates a harmonious split, with the warm colors occupying a larger portion to represent the dominant longing. The broken pixels scattered as irregular highlights within a structured grid illustrate moments of imperfection and interruption, enhancing the themes of repair and damage. The main collision occurs towards the center, where vibrant stripes meet muted fields, creating a visual pulse that resonates with the seed's emotional depth.
