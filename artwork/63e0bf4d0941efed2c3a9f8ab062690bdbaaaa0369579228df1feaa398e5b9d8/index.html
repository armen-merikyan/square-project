Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between longing and resolve. The chosen palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple reflects joy and grief, suggesting the complexity of emotions. The collision occurs mainly in the center, where broken dot patterns symbolize longing, against enclosed ring patterns representing resolve. The layout maintains a broken symmetry, enhancing the feeling of disruption yet seeking wholeness, effectively transforming the seed's concepts into a visual story.
