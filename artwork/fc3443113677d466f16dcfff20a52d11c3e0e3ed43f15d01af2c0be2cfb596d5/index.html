Artist Statement

This piece explores the contrast between awe and terror, reflecting a journey through structured chaos. The palette, contrasting soft gold and dark violet, evokes feelings of hope entwined with fear. The top half, representing awe, features a noise pattern with gentle gradients, while the bottom embodies terror with radial patterns creating a sense of compression. The arrangement evokes a collision of feeling, where the softer edges and broken pixels introduce instability, reflecting broken memories amidst a quest for balance.
