Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional duality of spark and ash, suggesting a narrative of contrast and collision. The palette blends bright yellows and oranges, embodying quick bursts of energy against muted purples and deep blues, representing the softness of ash. The main contrast occurs at the center, where vibrant checkered patterns intersect jagged tide-like structures, creating a visual dynamic that reflects resilience blossoming amidst fragility. Negative space enhances this impact by providing a field for the eye, inviting contemplation.
