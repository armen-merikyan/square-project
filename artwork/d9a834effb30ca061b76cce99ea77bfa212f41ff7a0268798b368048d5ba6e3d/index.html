Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and confusion. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold contrasts warmth with coolness, embodying tension. The top half suggests clarity with structured patterns (spark), while the bottom half conveys confusion through broken dots, emphasizing a chaotic interplay at the center where clarity falters. The thick edges provide a defined boundary, enhancing the sense of division and pressure, juxtaposed with negative space for breathing room.
