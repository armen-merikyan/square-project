Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents the tension between yearning (longing) and determination (resolve). The palette combines vibrant hues like hot magenta and electric blue to illustrate intense feelings with dead black and static gray providing a muted counterbalance, symbolizing emptiness and distance. The contrast is primarily between the dense clusters of warm colors that suggest chaos, while the larger areas of gray evoke a sense of quiet space. The asymmetry in color pattern highlights the collision between longing and resolve, with the central cluster crowded, portraying urgency, while the periphery remains stretched and sparse, echoing a feeling of unresolved tension.
