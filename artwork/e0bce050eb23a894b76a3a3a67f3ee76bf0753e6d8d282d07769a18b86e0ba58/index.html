Artist Statement

Exploring the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve, the composition utilizes contrasting colors to emphasize tension and tranquility. The palette of soft pinks and steel grays represents a delicate interplay between warmth and severity, creating a space for both yearning and determination. The center of the artwork features a dense collision of hex colors, mirroring the 'broken mirror' concept, while the broken pixels add intentional imperfections that signify the repair vs damage motif. This rhythmic arrangement allows for a feeling of pulse and flow, evoking an emotional journey through the quiet channel theme.
