Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting color placement and patterns. The selected palette of 'signal_green', 'electric_blue', 'dead_black', 'static_gray', and 'hot_magenta' evokes a feeling of duality, where the vibrant colors signal trust while the darker tones bring suspicion. The main collision occurs diagonally, where soft glowing greens and blues suggest trust, and harsh blacks and grays introduce an element of doubt. This interplay forms a fractured space of uncertainty, resonating with the seed's themes of resilience and fragility without resorting to literal imagery.
