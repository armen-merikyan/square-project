Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between the yearning for completion (longing) and the strength of acceptance (resolve). The palette, consisting of clean white, ice blue, and ink black, merges clarity with confusion, reflecting the delicate tension between hope and despair. The composition features blocks of darker colors colliding with lighter hues, creating a central dense area surrounded by quieter tones, representing a calm aftermath of conflict. The broken pixels disrupt the flow, symbolizing damage and repair, resulting in a pulsating rhythm that echoes the emotional push and pull within the artwork.
