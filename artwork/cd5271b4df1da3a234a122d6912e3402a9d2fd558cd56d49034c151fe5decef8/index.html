Artist Statement

The composition explores the interplay between vulnerability and strength through contrasting patterns. The chosen palette of moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold evokes a sense of resilience amidst fragility. The layout mirrors a broken reflection, with left and right edges almost identical, reflecting the tension and conflict present in the seed's themes. The dense patchwork of strength in the center collides with the mirrored softness of vulnerability on the edges, while broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions that narrate stories of exposure and safety.
