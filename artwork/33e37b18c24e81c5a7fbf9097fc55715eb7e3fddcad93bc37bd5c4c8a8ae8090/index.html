Artist Statement

The emotional tone of confusion is juxtaposed with moments of clarity, creating a dynamic tension. The palette, inspired by 'ancient fire', incorporates burnt orange and dark red to evoke warmth amid confusion. Charcoal and gold provide stark contrast, enhancing the visual narrative. The center radiates a 'spark' of clarity surrounded by a 'frame' of confusion, where horizontal and vertical alignment suggest a tension between orderly structures and broken patterns. As the thick outline represents rigidity, it contrasts with the chaotic center, evoking a story of seeking understanding amid confusion.
