Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes saturated desire with muted restraint, creating an internal conflict. The palette combines deep reds and subtle creams, representing the warmth of longing against the coldness of suppression. The main collision occurs in the center, where vibrant reds clash with the muted hues, symbolizing the struggle between urge and control. The layout is split horizontally, allowing the patterns of 'broken_frame' above and 'noise' below to visually interpret the themes of overflow vs containment, with broken pixels introduction to signify disruption.
