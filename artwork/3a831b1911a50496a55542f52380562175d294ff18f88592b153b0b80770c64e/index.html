Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece contrasts restlessness and peace through a palette of warm and cool tones. The warm coral and soft amber evoke a sense of calm while the cold slate and blue-black introduce a sense of disturbance. The main collision occurs diagonally across the square, where ordered bands of color shift suddenly into a fragmented pattern, suggesting a movement from tranquility to disarray. The use of broken pixels symbolizes the interruption of peace by restlessness, enhancing the abstract narrative.
