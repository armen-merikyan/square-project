Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between fear and hope, using a palette that juxtaposes dark violet with soft gold and pale green. The contrast arises from the sharpness of dark colors against the softness of gold, creating a rhythmic fluctuation that reflects the push and pull of emotions. The falling pixel arrangement suggests a descent, embodying release amidst pressure. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfection, echoing the collisions in emotional experiences.
