Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between 'spark' and 'ash', capturing the vividness of ignited contrasts with the softness of settled remnants. The palette incorporates 'red oxide' and 'small orange' as fiery accents, juxtaposed against muted cream and iron gray to symbolize restraint and memory. The diagonal argument creates dynamic tension, where jagged edges of the 'ash' barcode pattern collide with the clean threads of the 'spark'. This collision happens centrally, with a dominant color flowing from the corner, creating a visual journey from dense to light, depicting the memory’s inconsistencies and a struggle for resilience.
