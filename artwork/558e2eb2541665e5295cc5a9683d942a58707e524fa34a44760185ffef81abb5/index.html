Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between 'quiet' and 'pressure', utilizing a palette that evokes softness (off-white, faint peach) against darker, more confining colors (dark navy, compressed red). The main collision occurs in the diagonal flow from the dense left corner, representing pressure, to the lighter right, signifying release. The arrangement uses a 'broken frame' layout to introduce punctured spaces that reflect moments of clarity within confusion, echoing the seed themes of contradiction and conflict.
