Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between longing and resolve, manifesting through a clash of color patterns. The palette, drawn from cosmic indigo, deep teal, and cold gold, contrasts the weight of yearning against the steadiness of determination. The collision is prominent at the center, where rich colors ripple into calm fields, embodying the push-and-pull of emotions. The flow, utilizing a ladder-style structure for resolve against rippling patterns of longing, creates an architectural feeling, capturing both the desire for connection and the firmness of self-restraint.
