Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between rage and calm. The palette, featuring stark reds and muted pale blues, captures the explosive interplay of emotions. The dark hues suggest a sense of containment while vibrant colors radiate outward, illustrating chaos breaking through. The main collision occurs in the corner, with jagged, aggressive patterns asserting dominance, contrasting against smoother, horizontal fields of tranquility. This arrangement reflects the core ideas of pressure and overflow embedded in the seed.
