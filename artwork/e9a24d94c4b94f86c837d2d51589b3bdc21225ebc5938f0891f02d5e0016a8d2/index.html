Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of awe and terror. The chosen palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon creates a clash of warmth against a backdrop of shadow, representing stability versus instability. The main contrast emerges from the split layout—left cool tones symbolize awe while right warm tones embody terror—creating a collision that evokes emotional tension. The patterns of mirroring and flame suggest movement, further enhancing the feeling of broken memories amidst organized chaos, aligning with the seed’s themes.
