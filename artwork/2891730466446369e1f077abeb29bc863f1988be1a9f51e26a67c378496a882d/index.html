Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a palette reflecting ancient fire, with burnt orange and dark red symbolizing defiance and charcoal for submission. The main collision occurs at the center, where bold dots clash with glitch patterns, creating a pulse of activity against an otherwise soft backdrop. Eroded symmetry at the edges emphasizes chaos and quietude, leaving intentional negative space that invites contemplation.
