Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between nostalgia and progress through a structured grid pattern on the right and a chaotic crosshatch on the left. The use of black and shades of blue represents the depth of nostalgia, while the stark whites signal forward movement and clarity. The contrast arises from abrupt transitions at the center, illustrating the collision of emotional states. Broken pixels randomly disrupt the grid, signifying the imperfections in progress and the weight of nostalgia, embodying the story of duality.
