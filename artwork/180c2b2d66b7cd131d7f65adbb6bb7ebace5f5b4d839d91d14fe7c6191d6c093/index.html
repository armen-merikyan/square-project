Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette of electric blue, magenta, and lime against a black background creates a stark contrast, with the vibrant colors signifying bursts of connection amidst a backdrop of isolation. The placement of these colors forms a central spark surrounded by chaos, symbolizing hidden desires that yearn for expression yet remain concealed. The top-weighted design echoes the idea of pressure and protection, while the fractured layout introduces a sense of disturbance, artfully illustrating the struggle between being seen and unseen.
