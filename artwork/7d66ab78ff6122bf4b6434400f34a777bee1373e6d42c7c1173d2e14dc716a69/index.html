Artist Statement

This square captures the emotional tension between quietness and pressure. The palette features a transition from soft 'off_white' and 'faint_peach' to the dense 'dark_navy' and 'compressed_red', reflecting the conflict between calm and intensity. The layout rises, emphasizing hope, while the collision of dense blocks of color on the bottom left with softer tones toward the top right creates a sense of movement and release. The broken pixels disrupt symmetry, embodying shatter against a steady tide, enhancing the narrative of shifting emotions.
