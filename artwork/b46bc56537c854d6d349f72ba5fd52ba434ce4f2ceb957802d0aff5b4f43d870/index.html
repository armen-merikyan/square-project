Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dichotomy of trust and suspicion, where rich, dark hues represent suspicion's weight and uncertainty, contrasting with lighter, soft colors that convey trust. The palette of 'relief_dread' combines pale mint and soft sky to invoke warmth amidst the storm gray and near black that create tension and concealment. The main collision occurs in the center, dense with color, illustrating the struggle between overwhelming emotion and calm clarity. The layout emphasizes an organized chaos, with broken pixels scattered to disrupt symmetry, echoing the erratic nature of signals amidst noise, while also allowing quiet spaces to breathe life into the piece.
