Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between control and surrender, represented by a dense center contrasting with softer edges. The palette utilizes navy and dim cyan to evoke a sense of loneliness and distant signals, while the black acts as a boundary. The dominant dark colors create an imposing structure reminiscent of control, while the lighter tones suggest surrender and yield. The main collision occurs at the center where intense saturation meets softer borders, illustrating the struggle between pressure and release. The choice of broken pixels enhances the idea of disruption and the challenge of containment.
