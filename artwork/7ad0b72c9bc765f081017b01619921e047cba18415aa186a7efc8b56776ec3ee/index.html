Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress through a structured yet fragmented palette. The dark red and charcoal hues convey a sense of weight and history, while hints of burnt orange and gold suggest flashes of optimism and innovation. The split in the design allows for contrasting flow, where the upper half reflects calm nostalgia in muted shades, and the lower half indicates a progressive movement with brighter tones interspersed. Placement of broken pixels adds imperfection, emphasizing the struggle of balancing feelings of history and forward momentum.
