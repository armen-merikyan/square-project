Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts defiance and submission, illustrated through the visual tension of the pixel arrangement. The palette employs warm yellows and oranges to convey defiance against cool deep blue and muted purple representing submission. The arrangement showcases a bottom-weighted structure, creating a feeling of rising pressure where vibrant colors interrupt muted regions, suggesting an oscillation between hope and confinement. This reflects the seed's themes, resonating with feelings of love and betrayal in an abstract narrative.
