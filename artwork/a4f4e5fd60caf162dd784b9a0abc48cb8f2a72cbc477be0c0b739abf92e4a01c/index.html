Artist Statement

The artwork juxtaposes hot and cold tones to embody the emotional duality of 'rage vs calm'. The selected palette features explosive reds and cool pale blue hues, expressing an inner conflict. The dominant black hex colors represent containment, creating a boundary against the bright center. The flow of color descends diagonally, suggesting a relentless movement, reinforcing feelings of pressure and explosion. The patterns of fault lines and rings collide in the center, creating a dynamic tension that reflects the essence of the seed.
