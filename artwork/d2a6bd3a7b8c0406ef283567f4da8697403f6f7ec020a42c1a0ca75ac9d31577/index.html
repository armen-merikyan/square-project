Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the push and pull between control and surrender, utilizing a palette that merges warmth and severity. The warm pink and white colors create a sense of containment, while the stark black introduces an edge of disruption. The collision occurs centrally, where the framing structure meets the openness of the negative space, illustrating a stark contrast. This arrangement implies a surrender to pressure, as the contrasting colors seem to overflow from the confines of the frame. Each pixel embodies a breath—some contained, others spilling into chaos, maintaining an abstract narrative without literal representation.
