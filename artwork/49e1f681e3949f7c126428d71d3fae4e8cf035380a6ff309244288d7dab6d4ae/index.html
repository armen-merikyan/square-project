Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between joy and grief. The upper half is bright, featuring vibrant yellows and oranges that suggest warmth and openness, showcasing a pattern of lively noise against calmer hues. The lower half contrasts sharply with muted purples and deep blues, symbolizing heaviness and fragmentation, structured in a jagged pattern that impacts the cohesiveness. The intentional placement of broken pixels introduces a sense of imperfection and disarray, aligning with the theme of a near-mirror image disrupted by the chaos of grief. The palette transitions from light to dark, emphasizing the clash between radiant joy and enveloping sorrow.
