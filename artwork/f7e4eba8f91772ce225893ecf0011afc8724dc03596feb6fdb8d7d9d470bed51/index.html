Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between joy and grief. The left side features a structured pattern with warm, vibrant colors symbolizing joy, represented through a radial pattern suggesting expansion and energy. The right side introduces broken pixels and muted, heavier colors to signify grief, creating a staircase pattern that evokes a sense of decline and weight. The central area echoes this duality with contrasting colors colliding, suggesting a pulse between the two emotions. The use of glowing edges highlights the stark contrasts, visually representing the interplay of love and betrayal.
