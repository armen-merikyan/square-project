Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between restlessness and peace, creating a visual dialog within the 8x8 grid. The color palette includes deep greens and golden tones, suggesting organic growth against artificial division, encapsulating the theme of contrast between emotion and stability. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the darker tones intrude into the lighter patches, symbolizing the struggle between chaos and serenity. Patterns of uneven spacing and slight variations in color saturation evoke memories and motions, inviting contemplation without literal forms.
