Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the clarity of a solitary signal against an overwhelming noise, embodying a struggle between presence and absence. The palette features a striking 'signal green' and 'hot magenta' juxtaposed with somber 'dead black' and 'static gray'. The left side showcases calming, rhythmic waves while the right erupts in chaotic 'islands', representing noise. This collision occurs in the center, where the clear pulse meets unpredictable disruptions, echoing the essence of memory versus erasure. The piece captures the tension of finding clarity in confusion, using broken pixels to emphasize flaws and disruptions reminiscent of fading memories.
