Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between control and surrender through contrasting color dynamics. The palette combines warm coral and soft amber for warmth versus cold slate and blue-black for chilliness, representing conflicting emotions. The center is dense with coral and amber, illustrating overwhelming pressure, while the edges, lined with cooler tones, express struggle and containment, leaving room for quiet interaction. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, suggesting moments of surrender amidst the pressure. Main contrasts occur where warm colors intensify in the center against subdued cools at the borders, narrating a fluctuating emotional landscape in this abstract format.
