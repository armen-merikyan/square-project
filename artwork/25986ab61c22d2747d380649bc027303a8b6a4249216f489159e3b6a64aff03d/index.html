Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the interplay of restlessness and peace through a contrasting palette. The soft aqua and gray tones symbolize calmness, while the intense reds and blues evoke feelings of urgency and disturbance. The central 'knot' is highlighted through radial patterns that radiate outwards, depicting the conflict between motion and stillness. Broken pixels create intended scars that enhance the art's emotional depth, while the pattern arrangement echoes the 'stair-like' quality of peace clashing with the 'radial' nature of restlessness.
