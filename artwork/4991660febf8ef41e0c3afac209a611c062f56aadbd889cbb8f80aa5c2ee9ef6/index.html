Artist Statement

The composition captures the emotional conflict between control and surrender, balancing bold bursts of color against softer shades. The vibrant palette of signal green and hot magenta clashes with the muted tones of static gray and dead black, illustrating the struggle of containment breaking free. The center serves as a focal point where the clash intensifies, representing an overfilled space yearning for release, while quieter edges embody a marginal comfort in surrender. The arrangement allows both the tension and tranquility to exist simultaneously, reflecting the seed's core emotional dynamics.
