Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between defiance and submission, highlighted through contrasting colors. The palette of electric blue and magenta signifies the intensity of conflict, while black introduces depth and weight. The zigzag flow captures the motion of falling pixels, emphasizing the concept of interruption. The noise pattern clashes with the calming ring of submission, creating a visual dialogue. The layout balances the tension of the outer frame against the core, embodying the push and pull of emotional forces.
