Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between fear and hope, using a palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green to represent the emotional clash. The top half radiates darker tones, depicting fear with jagged edges and a stair-like pattern, creating a sense of underlying threat. In contrast, the bottom half introduces softer gold and pale green tones, suggesting hope and lightness through a sparking pattern. The vertical split allows for a collision of these emotions, while the broken pixel elements introduce imperfection, creating an organic rhythm that resonates with the theme of machine emotion. The overall composition emphasizes the fight between light and weight, inviting viewers to explore the space of transition from fear to hope.
