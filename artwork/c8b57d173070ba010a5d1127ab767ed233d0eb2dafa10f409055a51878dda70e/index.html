Artist Statement

This piece illustrates the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The chosen palette of red, black, pale blue, and white reflects the intensity of rage contrasted with the calmness of clarity. The use of sharp and soft contrasts results in a battle of solidity versus fragility, represented through the arrangement of colors. The interplay between chaos and order is apparent with dense clusters of dark hues colliding with lighter accents of pale blue and white, suggesting unresolved motion and emotional disruption, akin to "falling pixels". The pattern emulates a 'crack' against a 'split screen', capturing the idea of a fractured symmetry within the grid layout. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, echoing the inherent conflicts in thought and emotion.
