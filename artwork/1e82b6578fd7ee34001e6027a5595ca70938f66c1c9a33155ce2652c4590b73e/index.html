Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the delicate balance between safety and exposure, embodying the tension of a warm protector versus a cold void. The selected palette combines earth tones with soft hues to evoke feelings of gentleness amidst stark contrasts. The collision occurs in the center where the pressure of heavier, darker tones meets lighter, more open colors, creating a spiral movement that suggests both warmth and unease. This spiral reflects the themes of the seed, emphasizing pathways of pressure against released space.
