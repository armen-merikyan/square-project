Artist Statement

This composition evokes the complex interplay of love and betrayal through color contrasts and patterns. The palette, consisting of warm pinks and dark maroons, embodies the warmth of love contrasted against the shadows of betrayal. By employing a diagonal rise, the layout suggests growth and movement, while broken dots and checkers add variance and depth. The main collision occurs in the center, where the sharp contrasts of dark maroon clash with soft pink, creating a tension that reflects the emotional turmoil of the seed. Empty negative space allows for reflection amidst the chaos.
