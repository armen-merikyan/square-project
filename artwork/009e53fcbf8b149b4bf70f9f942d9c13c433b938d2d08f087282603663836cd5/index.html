Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of defiance and submission through an interplay of angular resistance and lowered axes. The palette of soft pink, warm white, and sharp crimson against deep blacks portrays both warmth and severity, signifying the tension between a hopeful ascent and a receding barrier. The composition's upward flow represents an escape from confinement, while broken pixels introduce disruption, echoing glitches that challenge the established order. The central collision emphasizes a jagged boundary reflecting varying intensities of resistance and acceptance.
