Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between restlessness and peace, manifesting in a palette that balances warm and cool tones. The hot coral and blood orange evoke a sense of agitation, while dust pink and charcoal introduce calmness. The composition features a diagonal clash between the two emotions, where the brightness of the flame-like patterns encounters the shatter-like disruptions. This arrangement creates a dynamic interplay, symbolizing both movement and stillness, allowing the viewer to sense a push and pull in the visual narrative.
