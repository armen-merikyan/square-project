Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension of panic versus control through a contrasting palette of warm yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples. The zigzag pattern suggests dynamic movement and chaos at the edges, while symmetrical rings at the center represent order and control. The placement of broken pixels introduces imperfections, enhancing the theme of conflict and the interplay between joy and grief. Overall, the design captures a story of emotional collision and release amid structured chaos.
