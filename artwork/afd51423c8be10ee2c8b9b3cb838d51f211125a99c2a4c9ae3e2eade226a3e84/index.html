Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between fear and hope, using a palette that juxtaposes dark tones against a few lighter hues. The layout builds pressure in the outer rows with dark colors, symbolizing fear, while a small central core introduces hope through light, representing resilience. The contrast occurs where dark jagged edges encounter soft gold, heightening the emotional collision. The arrangement leans toward sharp vs. soft dynamics, featuring broken pixels to suggest imperfection and uncertainty, creating a visual echo of tension that embodies the seed's themes.
