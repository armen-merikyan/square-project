Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a tension between tranquility and agitation. The pale aqua and mist gray symbolize peace, while the alarm red disrupts this calm with urgency. The contrast lies between the dense presence of the dark colors at the edges versus the brighter colors that pulse from the center, illustrating a vibrant struggle. The broken pixels reflect interruptions in the narrative, presenting a visually engaging piece that engages viewers and invites them to explore the depth of emotion represented.
