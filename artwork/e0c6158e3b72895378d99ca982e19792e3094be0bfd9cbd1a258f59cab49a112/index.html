Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional dichotomy between longing and resolve, utilizing a palette that reflects warmth and emptiness. The dominant tones of hot coral and blood orange create a sense of urgency, while dust pink and hollow blue offer moments of serenity. The main collision occurs in the center, where structured lines of resolve intersect with softer shapes of longing, representing the tension between aspiration and acceptance. The broken pixels introduce imperfections that echo the complexities of the themes, creating an interplay of contained and overflowing feelings.
