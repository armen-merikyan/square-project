Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between calm and chaotic memories, symbolizing restlessness through darker hues, while peace is suggested by lighter, brighter patches. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue captures moments from memory, resonating with both warmth and nostalgia. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the brighter pixels intrude upon darker clusters, creating a disruptive yet harmonious visual tension. This arrangement evokes a sense of fragmented calm amidst unsettling patterns, representing motion and stillness intertwined.
