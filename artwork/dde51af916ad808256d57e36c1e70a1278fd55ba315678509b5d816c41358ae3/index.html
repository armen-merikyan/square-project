Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured is a dynamic struggle between the warmth of 'spark' and the subdued presence of 'ash'. The chosen palette incorporates 'compressed_red' as a bold accent, contrasting against 'dust_gray' and 'off_white' which suggest the remnants of restraint and memory. The layout sees 'spark' occupy the top-right diagonal, vividly asserting itself against the cool shadows of 'ash' which dominate the bottom-left, symbolizing resilience amidst erasure. The broken pixels are strategically placed to convey disruption, contributing to a sense of jagged edges—portraying the tension between order and chaos vividly. This arrangement aims to evoke the internal conflicts represented in the seed, inviting viewers into a conversation of emotional balance.
