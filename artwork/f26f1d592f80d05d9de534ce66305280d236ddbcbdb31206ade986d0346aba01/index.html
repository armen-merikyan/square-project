Artist Statement

The emotional tone delves into the contrast of clarity and distortion, with a vibrant green representing the signal amidst a sprawling network of muted colors embodying noise. The palette includes 'signal_green' and 'electric_blue' as dominant bright colors reflecting clarity and rhythm, contrasted against 'static_gray' and 'dead_black' that embody the chaos and interference. The main collision between these colors occurs in the center, emphasizing the struggle between the readable pulse and the surrounding static, creating a visual tension that narrates the complexity of communication amidst distractions.
