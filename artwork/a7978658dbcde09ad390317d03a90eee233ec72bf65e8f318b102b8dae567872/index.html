Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the struggle between defiance and submission, represented through sharp contrasts and uneven layouts. The palette of warm coral and cold slate symbolizes the tension of opposing forces. The dominant dark colors (blue-black and cold slate) create a heavy, anchored center, while the warmer, brighter edges pulse outward, interrupting the calm. This arrangement visually depicts chaos at the borders, contrasting with the quieter center, suggesting a flow of energy trapped and pushing against the boundaries. Broken pixels add intentional disruptions, enhancing the narrative of conflict between rigidity and fluidity.
