Artist Statement

This artwork captures the delicate balance between joy and grief through a nearly mirrored arrangement disrupted by a fragmented edge. The palette features vibrant yellows and oranges representing joy, contrasted against deep blues and muted purples evoking grief. The grid increases pressure with joyful cracks emanating towards the center, while broken pixels illustrate the tension of imperfection and emotional weight. The left-weighted design represents a struggle between the two emotional states, creating an unresolved dynamic.
