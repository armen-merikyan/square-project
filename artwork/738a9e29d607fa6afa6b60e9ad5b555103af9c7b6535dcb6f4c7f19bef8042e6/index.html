Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional dynamic of collision through contrasting colors that create a sense of pressure and release. The palette, consisting of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green, reflects the tension between fear and hope. The layout of the inner core and outer frame illustrates loneliness surrounded by connection, creating a duality of emotions. The use of broken pixels adds an element of imperfection, enhancing the sense of disruption across the canvas. Every pixel's placement contributes to a rhythm of movement that feels both introspective and expansive.
