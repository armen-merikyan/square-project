Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between trust and suspicion, represented through color dynamics and spatial density. The palette of warm pinks and reds contrasts against the gray and dark maroon, symbolizing warmth and stability clashing with shadowy watchfulness. The layout emphasizes pressure in the corner, suggesting a buildup of emotion, while diagonal flows create a sense of movement and disturbance. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, allowing for a narrative of resilience through fragility. The central collision occurs at the corner, encapsulating the essence of signal versus noise.
