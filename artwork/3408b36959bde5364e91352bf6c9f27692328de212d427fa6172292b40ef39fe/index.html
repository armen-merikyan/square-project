Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured here reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette, featuring cosmic indigo and void black, evokes a sense of awe and terror, embodying both resilience and exposure. The pattern alternates between stacked light fields and shattering dark edges, representing the collision of soft vulnerability against the hard outlines of strength. The center serves as the primary area of contrast, where the opposing emotional currents meet, while a careful distribution of broken pixels adds intentional imperfections, symbolizing the scars of emotional upheaval. The zigzag flow of colors suggests a dynamic interaction, enhancing the sense of rising signals amidst the flood of heaviness.
