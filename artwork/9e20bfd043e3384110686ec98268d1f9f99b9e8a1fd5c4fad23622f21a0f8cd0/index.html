Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional tension of joy and grief, represented by warm colors (yellow, orange) colliding with deeper, muted tones (deep blue, muted purple). The center features a bright, open yellow contrasting against a maze-like structure of deeper hues around it, suggesting layers of grief surrounding the joy at the core. The piece’s layout features a left-weighted asymmetry, with the grid pattern representing the structured experience of joy, while the broken-edge style captures the interruptions caused by grief, creating a dynamic visual narrative of conflict and resolution.
