Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that evokes melancholy wonder. The main collision occurs at the center, where a bright cluster of soft cyan stands against muted plum, illustrating the fight between light and softness. The asymmetric flow embraces a left-weighted design, embodying how strength can emerge from vulnerability while the jagged edges represent breaking norms. The broken pixels intentionally disrupt the flow, adding depth and texture to the piece.
