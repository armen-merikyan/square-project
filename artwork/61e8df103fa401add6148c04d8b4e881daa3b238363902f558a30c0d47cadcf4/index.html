Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between strength and vulnerability, using contrasting colors to create resonance. The balance of warm coral and cold slate provides a striking juxtaposition, representing vulnerability exposed against the hard edges of strength. The pattern reflects a center pulse with scar-like interruptions around the edges, allowing the viewer to feel the push-pull between the two emotions. The main collision occurs in the center, where deep color contracts sharply against lighter shades, symbolizing the fragile yet resilient human condition.
