Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between clear signals and chaotic noise. The palette juxtaposes the vibrating clarity of 'signal green' and 'electric blue' against the muted yet intense 'static gray' and 'dead black'. The collision occurs in the center, where the bright colors emerge from the depths of muted shades. The broken pixels add imperfections that suggest scars of struggle within the chaos, echoing the emotional turmoil reflected in the seed. A careful balance integrates negative space, allowing the vibrant pulse to stand distinct from the ominous noise, embodying the concept of navigating through life's discord.
