Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between fear and hope. The palette, featuring dark colors (black, dark violet) and lighter contrasts (soft gold, pale green), represents the heaviness of fear against the sprouting light of hope. The main contrast occurs in the zigzag pattern of colors that creates sharp interruptions, while soft, cracking spaces embody hope's gentle ascension amidst fear's dense shadow. The seed themes translate into an arrangement that embodies emotional depth — with dark corners encasing a soft, golden center, conveying an upward flow amidst rising tension.
