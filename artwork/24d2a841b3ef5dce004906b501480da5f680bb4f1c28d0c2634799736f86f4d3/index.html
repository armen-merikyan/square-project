Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a push-pull between restlessness and peace. The palette, featuring soft gold and deep wine, introduces warmth against cooler grays, symbolizing tension and tranquility. The main collision occurs diagonally across the square, tying in the concept of movement versus stillness. The glitch pattern weaves through the stripes of peace, allowing for a visual narrative of interruption and echo, symbolizing memories that disrupt tranquility.
