Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between fear and hope through deep colors and structured patterns. The palette, consisting of dark violet for fear and soft gold for hope, creates a striking contrast that plays out across the square. The layout establishes an outside-inward flow, enriching the inner space with bursts of lighter colors and checker patterns that suggest jitter and unease. The edges are left soft, embodying a sense of vulnerability against jagged patches that hint at fractured emotions. Through deliberate color placement and hidden negative space, the piece conveys a narrative of aspiration caught within a backdrop of anxiety.
