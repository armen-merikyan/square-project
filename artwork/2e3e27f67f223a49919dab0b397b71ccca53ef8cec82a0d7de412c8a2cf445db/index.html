Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts trust and suspicion, with a clear split reflecting a rising signal. The palette includes clean white and ice blue for trust, alongside ink black and warning orange for suspicion. The interaction between the structured quiet field of colors and the disruptive stripes illustrates the tension between stability and doubt. The collision occurs in the center, where the patterns clash, embodying the complex interplay of these emotions while the upward layout invokes a sense of movement and hope amidst chaos.
