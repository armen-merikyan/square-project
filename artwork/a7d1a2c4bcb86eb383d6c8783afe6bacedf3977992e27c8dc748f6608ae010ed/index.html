Artist Statement

This composition reflects a tension between the stability of certainty and the unpredictability of curiosity. The chosen palette comprises earthy and calming hues, featuring shelter green and warm brown for stability, while open blue introduces an element of curiosity. The main collision occurs in the center where the structured channels (reflecting order) clash with the braided patterns (symbolizing chaos), creating a visually dynamic experience. Broken pixels enhance this tension, providing a sense of imperfection and organic flow. This interaction embodies the seed's complexities, presenting a narrative of rise and fall through abstract arrangement.
