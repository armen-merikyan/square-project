Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional conflict of rage versus calm. The palette mixes aggressive reds and deep blacks that evoke turmoil, contrasting sharply against pale blues and whites that suggest tranquility. The central ring pattern disrupts the smoother cracked lines, illustrating the tension of containment versus overflow, triggered by the seed concepts of noise and calm. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the emotional disruption inherent in the clash of these two moods.
