Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between envy and gratitude through stark color contrasts, with deep navy and black suggesting weight, while hints of dim cyan signal the lighter, more hopeful feelings. The zigzag pattern reflects the staircase metaphor, representing upward struggle against noise, echoing the emotional push and pull. The broken pixels create an imperfect rhythm, capturing the chaos of conflicting emotions. Main contrast lies between the darker and lighter spaces, creating a visual struggle that prompts reflection.
