Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between trust and suspicion, illustrated through a palette that merges dark depths with a vibrant electric blue. The collision occurs at the center, where stark magenta stripes (representing trust) intersect with scar-like patterns (representing suspicion), creating a dynamic tension. The color choices reflect resilience against fragility, with the dark boundaries suggesting pressure while electric blue suggests a break in noise, echoing the complexity of clear versus obscured intentions.
