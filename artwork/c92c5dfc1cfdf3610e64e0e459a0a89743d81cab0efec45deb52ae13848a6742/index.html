Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between strength and vulnerability through a palette of pale aqua and alarm red against a grounding mist gray and ink blue. The two pulsating centers represent vulnerability with their softer tones, while the surrounding edges evoke strength with denser, darker colors. The collision occurs at the center where soft gradients meet sharp contrasts, symbolizing a struggle between exposure and shelter, embodying both the fragility and resilience in our human experience.
