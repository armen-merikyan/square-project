Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between clarity and clutter. The contrast palette of bright 'signal_green' (#00FF00) and intense 'hot_magenta' (#FF00FF) against a muted background of 'static_gray' (#7D7D7D) creates a vivid collision—representing the struggle of a singular, clear signal trying to emerge from overwhelming noise. The 'stepwell' pattern intertwines with 'crosshatch', producing a rhythmic pulse at the center, suggesting a heartbeat amidst the chaos. The intentional broken pixels add an element of disruption, enhancing the feeling of memory loss and interference, embodying the seed's theme of transient connections in the face of static existence.
