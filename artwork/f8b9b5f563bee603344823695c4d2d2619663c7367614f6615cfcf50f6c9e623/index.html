Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between envy and gratitude through a contrasting palette of sepia and muted blues, embodying warmth in tones that signify longing and appreciation. The edges are designed with a bottom weighting, and the falling pixel layout suggests descending feelings that echo tears and rising hope; the radial pattern for gratitude collides against a checker pattern for envy, creating a push and pull of emotional forces where soft edges meet, eviscerating clarity and invoking introspection.
