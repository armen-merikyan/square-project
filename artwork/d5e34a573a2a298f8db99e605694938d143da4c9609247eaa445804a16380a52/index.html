Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the contrast between quiet and pressure. The palette includes muted tones like off_white and faint_peach to evoke a sense of calm, while compressed_red and dark_navy introduce moments of intensity. The central conflict emerges diagonally, where the crowded checker pattern meets a more fragmented split-screen design, illustrating the idea of pressure as it collides with the quiet. This arrangement captures the emotional tension between these forces: the dark edges representing pressure and the lighter center mirroring moments of clarity and release. The broken pixels create intentional disruptions, enhancing the theme of shattering against the tide of dense emotion.
