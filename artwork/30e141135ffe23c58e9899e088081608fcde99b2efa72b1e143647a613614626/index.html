Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional interplay between trust and suspicion, represented through contrasting shades and their spatial arrangement. The palette of cosmic indigo and void black evokes a balance between depth and uncertainty, while star white and cold gold accentuate moments of clarity in a complex environment dominated by deep teal interruptions. The central collision occurs around the middle left, where the dark tones sharply contrast with bright accents, creating a visual pulse that embodies tension and resolution. This tension is further interrupted by strategically placed broken pixels, symbolizing imperfections and doubts within the overarching theme of resilience against fragility.
