Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts rage with calm through a palette of dark reds and muted blues, depicting chaos and serenity. The top half employs sharp, jagged edges of reds against a soft blue background, symbolizing explosive feelings that are confined within an overarching calm. The choice of hard edges evokes tension while broken dots suggest fragility. A mindful arrangement and the pattern of displacement illustrate interplay between pressure and release, signifying the conflict between overwhelming emotions and serene moments. The main collision occurs at the border between the red and blue, representing an emotional chasm between two contrasting states.
