Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes certainty with curiosity, highlighted by a palette of warm coral and soft amber contrasting against cold slate and blue-black. This creates a visual narrative of stability and chaos, where the warm colors symbolize certainty rising from the cool depths. The center of the grid features a collision of noise and frame patterns, reflecting the chaotic inquiry spirit, while broken pixels represent interruptions and unresolved tensions. Negative space enhances the flow, allowing the eye to move naturally through the composition, yet inviting contemplation of the layers and depths below.
