Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts fear's dark tension and hope's subtle glimmer. The palette of black and dark violet contrasts with soft gold and pale green, creating a jagged edge between despair and light. The collision happens vertically, where the darker hues press against the lighter ones, signifying weight and release. The abstract pattern reflects a struggle between ascent and descent, with pixels arranged to evoke a flow of energy moving inward and outward.
