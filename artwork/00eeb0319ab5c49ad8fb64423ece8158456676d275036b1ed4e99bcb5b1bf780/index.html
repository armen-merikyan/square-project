Artist Statement

This artwork channels the emotional tension between safety and exposure through contrasting color fields and patterns. The palette consists of cool and warm tones that represent vulnerability (cold white and open blue) against protective warmth (shelter green and warm brown). The core of the piece is dominated by the soft rippling effect of the pale skin color, surrounded by more structured negative cross patterns in darker shades. The color arrangement suggests a central push-pull dynamic, manifesting the tension of exposure intertwined with safety, reflecting the rhythms of pressure and release. The chosen layout, 'two_pulses,' emphasizes this interaction by leaving negative space to invite contemplation and layering textures, indicating an interplay of presence and absence.
