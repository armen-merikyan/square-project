Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between emptiness and desire through a stark contrast of warm and cool colors, encapsulated by burnt oranges and dark reds against charcoal and gold. The upward movement suggests aspiration while the downward shapes hint at a pull towards emptiness, creating a dynamic visual flow that reflects emotional complexity. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, emphasizing the fragility of these emotions. The layout encourages a sense of space and reflection, while maintaining 10% negative space to enhance feelings of loneliness and fragmentation.
