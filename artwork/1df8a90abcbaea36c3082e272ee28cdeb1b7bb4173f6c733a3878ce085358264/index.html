Artist Statement

The emotional tone here oscillates between longing and resolve, expressed through contrasting density and calm. The palette of ripe green, acid green, and honey gold introduces warmth against deep olive and cream tones, showcasing a sense of hope amidst uncertainty. The arrangement plays on a quiet channel that cuts through a densely patterned space, capturing the tension of longing as it collides with the firmness of resolve. The main contrast occurs centrally, where the colors interrupt each other, symbolizing the emotional pull and the struggle for balance. The broken pixels signify the scars left by past damage, emphasizing a journey of repair intermingled with the pulse of life, creating a compelling narrative woven through color and form.
