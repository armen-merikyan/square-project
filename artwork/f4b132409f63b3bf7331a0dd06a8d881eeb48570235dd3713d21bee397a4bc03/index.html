Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the contrast between restlessness and peace, captured through an uneven flow that transitions from dense clusters at the edges towards open spaces. The palette of stain brown, ash gray, warm ivory, soft gold, and deep wine conveys a sense of muted warmth and rich depth. The main collision occurs diagonally, where restless stepwell patterns of dark colors meet the tide-like movement of lighter hues, creating a dynamic tension in balance. The seed's themes of motion and memory guide the palette choice, infusing a sense of unresolved layering and echoed feelings into the abstract pattern.
