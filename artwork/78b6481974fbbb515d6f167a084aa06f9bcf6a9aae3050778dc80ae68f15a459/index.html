Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between love and betrayal using a palette featuring warm pinks and reds contrasting with muted grays. The central area, filled with soft cracks and stripes, symbolizes the chaotic struggle of these emotions. The warm colors evoke feelings of attachment and affection, while the cool grays represent the emptiness and betrayal felt within. There is a deliberate imbalance, where dense clusters of warm hues clash with cool empty spaces, suggesting a fight for emotional control. Each collision of color expresses a layered narrative of conflict and connection, embodying the essence of the seed through abstract patterns rather than literal forms.
