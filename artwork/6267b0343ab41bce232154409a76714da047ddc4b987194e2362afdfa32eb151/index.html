Artist Statement

This artwork captures the dynamic tension between structured signals and chaotic noise. The palette of greens, blues, and magentas represents a spectrum from clarity to disruption. The bright green serves as a focal pulse surrounded by muted gray and black, creating a stark contrast. The main collision of bright signal in the center is interrupted by shards of magenta and gray, invoking a feeling of static interference, while the strategic placement of quiet tones invites an exploration of noise within a structured frame. The arrangement conveys an emotional battle, reflective of machine-like precision and organic chaos as depicted by the abstract patterns. The broken pixels add intentional disruptions, mirroring the theme of damage and repair, resonating with the idea of a fractured signal in a noisy world.
