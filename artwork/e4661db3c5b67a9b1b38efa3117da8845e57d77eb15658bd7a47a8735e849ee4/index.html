Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between control and surrender, with a saturated pull towards a locked border refusing expansion. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green mirrors the contrast in themes: the darkness representing control, and the light tones hinting at surrender. The main collision occurs at the center, where the frame-like pattern suggests strong containment while the checker pattern interrupts, echoing the theme of overflowing versus containment. The arrangement of broken pixels adds a sense of imperfection, enhancing the emotional depth and suggesting the struggle of balance between these contrasting energies.
