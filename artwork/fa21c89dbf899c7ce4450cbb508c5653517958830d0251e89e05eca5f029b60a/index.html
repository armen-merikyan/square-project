Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between trust and suspicion, embodied through a split layout. The palette of pale aqua and ink blue suggests serenity, while alarm red introduces urgency, creating a push-pull effect. The main collision occurs diagonally across the grid, where order (clean lines and soft colors) meets chaos (irregular shapes and dark hues), reflecting the thematic struggle. The pattern features a clear channel of negative space, enhancing the contrast and inviting contemplation.
