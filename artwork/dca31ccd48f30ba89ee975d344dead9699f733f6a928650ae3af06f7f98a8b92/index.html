Artist Statement

This composition plays with the tension between vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette features contrasting electric blue and magenta against a primarily black backdrop, symbolizing the inner strength that often hides beneath vulnerability. The placement of colors and the use of broken pixels represent the imperfections that both emotions carry. The central focus is a dense cluster of electric blue, surrounded by a swirling mass of softer hues, creating a battle between quiet fields and an overwhelming presence, embodying the push and pull of strength against fragility.
