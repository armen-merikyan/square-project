Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents the struggle between clarity and confusion, with the palette blending deep navy and bright whites to symbolize the collision of signal and noise. The stark contrast emerges in the center, where structured light meets the chaotic dark edges, evoking a feeling of tension and interruption. The patterns of stacked light amidst the static create a rhythmic pulse as they oscillate with the uneven, broken pixels reflecting the essence of machine emotion.
