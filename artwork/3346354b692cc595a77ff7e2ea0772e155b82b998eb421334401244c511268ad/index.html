Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the juxtaposition of defiance and submission through contrasting colors and patterns. Using a palette of deep reds to symbolize defiance and pale blues for submission creates an interplay of aggression against serene acceptance. The pattern suggests a split screen where bold reds crowd from the bottom left, embodying weight and pressure, while cooler tones float from the top right, reflecting lightness and openness. The hidden versus exposed contrast highlights moments of tension, clashing where colors collide, leading to an unresolved emotional landscape.
