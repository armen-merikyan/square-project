Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between clarity and confusion through a palette of dark maroon, gray, pink, and red. The jagged edges create a sense of disturbance while the falling layout evokes a rain-like descent into chaos. The contrasting hues symbolize the battle between calmness and rage, seen in the structured yet disrupted placement of the pixels. The asymmetrical distribution of dark shades against lighter tones illustrates moments of clarity piercing through confusion, creating a visual pulse that echoes the emotional conflict derived from the seed.
