Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette of moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold to illustrate resilience intertwined with fragility. The main contrast is found at the center, where soft patchwork textures collide with hard frames, echoing the struggle between exposure and shelter. Broken pixels create intentional disruptions, enhancing the narrative of imperfection and connection amidst alienation.
