Artist Statement

This composition conveys the emotional struggle between connection and loneliness through a contrasting color palette of muted sepia and gray tones alongside the faint whispers of faded blue. The outer frame represents the pressure of loneliness, creating an enclosing barrier, while the inner core embodies connection's more vibrant hues. The layout acts as a visual metaphor for the tension between feeling trapped and the longing to reach out. This interaction is highlighted through scattered broken pixels that maintain an imperfect organic quality, symbolizing the chaos inherent in emotional collisions. The patterns chosen—"stairs" for loneliness and "split screen" for connection—further emphasize this push-pull dynamic, allowing the viewer to engage with the feelings of isolation contrasted with subtle connections throughout the piece.
