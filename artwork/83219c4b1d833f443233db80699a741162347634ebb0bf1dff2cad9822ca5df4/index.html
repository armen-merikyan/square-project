Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between confusion and clarity through a contrasting palette of deep black, dark violet for chaos, and soft gold, pale green representing moments of clarity. The use of broken pixels creates intentional disruptions in an otherwise structured layout, embodying the emotional pull between order and disorder. Central focus emerges from the clash of colors, evoking a sense of interruption and unresolved motion, where dense patches reflect confusion against quieter areas symbolizing clarity.
