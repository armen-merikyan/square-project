Artist Statement

The emotional tone is shaped by the tension between longing and resolve. The chosen palette of clean white, ice blue, ink black, violet gray, and warning orange reflects clarity amidst confusion. The contrast is centered in the middle rows where black and orange collide, representing the struggle between a strong resolve and a quiet, aching longing. The layout mimics falling pixels, symbolizing tears or broken hope, while the hinge pattern conveys longing and the dots signify resolve, thus creating a dynamic 'pulse' that reflects the core themes from the seed.
