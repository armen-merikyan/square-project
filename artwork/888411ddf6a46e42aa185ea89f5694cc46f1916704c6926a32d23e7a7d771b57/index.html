Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the emotional tension between longing and resolve through the use of contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, rich with cosmic indigo and void black, symbolizes depth and the unknown, bringing forth feelings of longing and distance. The cold gold hints at the presence of resolve, asserting itself within the sprawling composition. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the signals of longing (represented by the scar pattern) clash with the structured inset of resolve (the halo pattern), suggesting an ongoing internal struggle as lines of geometric restraint and organic reach intersect. Negative space is utilized to emphasize the emptiness felt in longing, while the riot of colors in other areas captures moments of intensity.
