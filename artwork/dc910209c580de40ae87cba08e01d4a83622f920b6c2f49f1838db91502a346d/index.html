Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dialogue between softness and severity, with the left half embodying gentleness through light pinks and whites, while the right half employs darker shades creating weight and boundaries. The patterns, with warm stairs juxtaposed against rigid crosshatches, represent conflict and interaction. The central collision occurs where warm colors disrupt the cold, mirroring the push and pull of guilt and forgiveness, ultimately conveying both tension and resolution.
