Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional duality of loneliness and connection through contrasting patterns and tones. The palette of warm yellows and oranges (representing connection) clashes against muted purples and deep blues (symbolizing loneliness). The top-weighted layout creates a sense of pressure building downward, depicting the struggle of hidden feelings bursting through. Crosshatching in the top half hints at connection and interweaving, while radial patterns in the lower half evoke isolation. This juxtaposition captures the tension and beauty of human emotion, invoking a poignant reflection on being both connected and isolated.
