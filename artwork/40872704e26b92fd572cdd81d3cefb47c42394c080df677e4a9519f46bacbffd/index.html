Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between clarity and confusion. The palette, from deep navy to muted cyan, symbolizes the solitude of thought threaded with moments of clarity, represented by bright white accents and contrasted against the black depths of chaos. The broken symmetry evokes the dissonance of a nearly balanced arrangement interrupted by intentional imperfections, portraying a journey of frustration and resolution. Key contrast occurs where bright pixels collide with darker tones, emphasizing the abruptness of clarity amidst turmoil.
