Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between clarity and chaos, represented through the use of sharp contrasts and muted colors. The palette features a deep static gray and black with punctuations of vibrant signal green and hot magenta, manifesting the conflict between signal (clarity) and noise (chaos). The primary collision of color occurs vertically; the left side embodies the clean pulse (signal) while the right side suggests invasive static (noise). This arrangement merges the smooth rhythm of broken dots on the left with the disruptive stacks on the right, creating a dynamic emotional landscape that is compelling yet unresolved.
