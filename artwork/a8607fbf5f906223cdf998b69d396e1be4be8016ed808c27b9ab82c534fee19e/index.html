Artist Statement

This abstract piece embodies the emotional tension between 'quiet' and 'pressure.' The palette features soft tones like off-white and faint peach to represent the 'quiet' aspect, contrasted with darker shades like dark navy and compressed red, symbolizing the pressure. The main collision lies in the lower center where the heavier colors disrupt the lighter ones, suggesting an interplay of density breaking through a calm surface. The layout erodes symmetry at the corners, enhancing the sense of pressure equilibrium shifting into chaos, connecting back to the seed's themes of clarity and confusion.
