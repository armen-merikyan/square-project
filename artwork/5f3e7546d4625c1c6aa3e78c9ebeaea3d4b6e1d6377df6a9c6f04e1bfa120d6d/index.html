Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between softness and severity, achieved through a contrast of warm powder colors and deep blacks. The palette balances gentle warmth with stark severity, creating a layered depth that embodies emotional complexity. The collision occurs in the center where soft pinks and whites intermingle with the cold dark edges, suggesting a pulse of life against a backdrop of restraint. This aligns with the seed's themes of conflict and release, swirling gently around a central focus reflecting the dynamic nature of guilt and forgiveness.
