Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the duality of desire and restraint through a saturated palette that contrasts with muted tones. The darker hues spread from the corner, representing desire that pulsates with a vibrant energy, whereas the edges are sealed with subtle shades, hinting at the restrained emotions at play. The diamond-like arrangement symbolizes the sharpness of conflict, while the broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, enhancing the sense of chaos within the structure. Overall, this piece depicts the struggle between longing and limitation, encapsulated in an abstract pattern of color and disruption.
