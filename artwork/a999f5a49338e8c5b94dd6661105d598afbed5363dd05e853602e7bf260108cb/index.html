Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The 'ancient_fire' palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold represents warmth yet evokes isolation. The contrast between the dense center and the lighter surrounding colors echoes the feeling of pressure in lonely moments, while the negative space symbolizes the void of connection. The pattern reflects a burst of energy against an empty room layout, suggesting a struggle between exposure and concealment through the use of scattered dots and crosshatching.
