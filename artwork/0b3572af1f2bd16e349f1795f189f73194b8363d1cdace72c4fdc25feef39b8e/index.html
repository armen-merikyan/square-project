Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between envy and gratitude, establishing a tension that embodies both longing and appreciation. The 'broken_memory' palette reflects muted tones, creating a somber atmosphere with the potential for warmth. The main contrast appears in the zigzag progression from dark to light, symbolizing the struggle between heavy, rooted emotions and lighter, upward aspirations. The pattern embodies a rising signal; a gradient push from depth (darker hues) to light (faded blue) creates an echo of hope amidst chaos while maintaining a delicate 10% negative space that provides breathing room for reflection.
