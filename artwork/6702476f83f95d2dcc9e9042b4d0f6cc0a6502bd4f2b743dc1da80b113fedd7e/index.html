Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tone of awe and terror through contrasting colors from the 'ancient_fire' palette, where burnt orange and dark red collide against the starkness of charcoal and gold. The layout suggests an invasion of order by chaos, with the warm tones radiating from the bottom left corner, symbolizing awe, while the disruptive crack pattern signifies the encroachment of terror. Each color represents different emotional densities, bringing forth a narrative of pressure and subsequent release. The glowing edges highlight the tensions between the patterns of color, prompting the viewer to perceive the interplay of emotions and the space surrounding the chaos, creating a delicate balance over 10% negative space to reflect resolution amid tension.
