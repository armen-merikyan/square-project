Artist Statement

This piece embodies the contrasts of confusion and clarity through a mix of dense patterns and negative spaces. The use of pink and red colors suggests emotional warmth and tension, while gray serves as a balancing neutral. The distribution of colors creates both tight clusters and areas of emptiness, echoing the seed's themes of center versus edges and fragmentation. The thick outlines are hinted at by the patterns of color that flow outward, mimicking falling pixels, while maintaining an abstract presence without literal representation.
