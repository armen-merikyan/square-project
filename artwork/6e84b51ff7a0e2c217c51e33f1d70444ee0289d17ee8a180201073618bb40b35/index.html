Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between confusion and clarity, with a rhythmic pulse that echoes through the pixel arrangement. The palette of warm yellows and oranges contrasts with the cool depth of muted purples and deep blues, encapsulating the emotional spectrum of clarity versus chaos. The symmetry is subtly disrupted with broken pixels on one side, enhancing the feeling of conflict. The abstract pattern emerges as a response to the emotional score, allowing viewers to experience the interplay of structured order and chaotic elements without literal representation.
