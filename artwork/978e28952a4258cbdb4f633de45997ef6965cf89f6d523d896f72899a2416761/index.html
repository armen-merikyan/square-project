Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, using a palette of muted sepia, tan, and soft blues to evoke a sense of fading memories and broken patterns. The contrasting dark shades form a rhythmic pulse, signifying the clash between chaotic noise and serene spaces. The placement of colors creates a sense of rising from the dense lower pixels, suggesting gradual clarity amid confusion. The broken arrangement introduces imperfection, mirroring the inherent dualities and asymmetrical motions within the seed's emotional theme.
