Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between strength and vulnerability, with a central theme of conflict represented by the collision of the 'pressure_band' and 'flame' patterns. The palette of moss green and stone gray reflects resilience and fragility, while subtle accents of bone white and small gold bring disruptions of light against a heavy backdrop, illustrating strength juxtaposed with vulnerability. The main contrast occurs at the center, where soft pixel breaks evoke the fragility of the design amidst denser, stronger patterns. Negative space is reserved to introduce an element of pause and safety among the chaos.
