Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the struggle between panic and control, using contrasting tones of dark and luminous colors. The selected palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green represents fear and hope, illustrating a chaotic tension that coexists with a yearning for order. The arrangement of colors features a stark collision in the center of the grid, where dark colors converge and spark against the lighter hues, representing the core of the emotional conflict. Broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, aligning with the theme of imbalance and uncertainty stemming from the emotional tension encapsulated in the seed.
