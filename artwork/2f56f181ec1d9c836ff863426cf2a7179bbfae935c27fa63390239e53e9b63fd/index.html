Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between trust and suspicion, using warm colors (oranges and yellows) for confidence and cooler shades (deep blue, muted purple) to signify caution. The contrast is prominent in the central vertical split where warm and cool intersect, symbolizing the tension between two opposing forces. Patterns of chaotic noise versus structured paths create an emotional rhythm, illustrating resilience in fragility. The color placements evoke a dialogue of pressure versus softness, with broken pixels adding intentional disruptions, subtly suggesting the imperfections inherent in trusting relationships.
