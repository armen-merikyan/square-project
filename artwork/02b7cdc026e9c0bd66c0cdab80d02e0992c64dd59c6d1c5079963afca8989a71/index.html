Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between confusion and clarity, reflected in a palette of deep blacks and soft pale blues. The contrasting colors evoke feelings of rage and calmness, creating a visual story of rising pressure against subtle clarity. The pattern's symmetry is disrupted in certain areas, suggesting the 'broken_pixels' theme from the seed, enhancing the visual tension. This results in a dynamic feeling as the colors pulse downwards from top to bottom, allowing for a sense of falling sensation through varied density and contrasting order.
