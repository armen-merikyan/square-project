Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between awe and terror. The chosen palette of deep blacks and the bright yet soft pale blue represents the struggle between stable calmness and chaotic fluttering. The contrasting patterns of crosshatch representing awe and spark illustrating terror collide at the center, creating a visual narrative of contradiction. Negative space allows for breath within the arrangement, emphasizing the feeling of broken symmetry and disruption invoked by the seed’s core concepts.
