Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captured the tension between clarity and confusion, reflecting the struggle of opposing forces. The palette, consisting of deep blue and muted purples juxtaposed with vibrant yellows and oranges, highlights this contrast vividly. The deep blue suggests depth and calm, while the yellow serves as a beacon of clarity amidst the chaos. The primary collision occurs in contrasting the structured grid of colors with moments of broken pixels, suggesting interrupted clarity and scattered thoughts that bleed outward from the center. The arrangement strategically places denser clusters at the edges, simulating an emotional ripple that communicates an outward flow from a central seed, embodying both confusion and clarity in its dance.
