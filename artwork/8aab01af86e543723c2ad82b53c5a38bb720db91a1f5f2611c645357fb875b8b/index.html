Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tug-of-war between loneliness and connection. The palette, a mix of soft pinks and deep maroon, signifies warmth that conflicts with the cold grays that evoke isolation. The asymmetrical arrangement emphasizes a pull from left to right, disrupting the visual balance—mirroring the often fragile nature of connections. The broken pixels represent shattered memories, with clusters of colors suggesting fleeting moments of intimacy against quieter spaces of solitude.
