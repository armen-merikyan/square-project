Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between clarity and chaos, where the sharp contrast of black (#000000) represents noise while the dim cyan (#008B8B) symbolizes the fleeting signal. This composition focuses on a central collision of these emotions, where the static patterns disrupt the flow of signals, creating a sense of disarray and unresolved motion. The negative space emphasizes isolation amidst interference, presenting the concept of signal lost within noise, echoing the seed's theme of juxtaposition.
