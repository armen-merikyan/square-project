Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a vivid exploration of joy and grief, contrasting warm oranges and yellows of joy against deep blues and muted purples of grief. The layout employs a falling pixels pattern that symbolizes the tears of grief descending while vibrant flames of joy intermittently break through, creating a visual tension. The color choices embody playful warmth against heaviness, highlighted by broken pixels to suggest imperfection, reflecting how these emotions coexist chaotically yet beautifully. The asymmetric arrangement emphasizes moments of collision and release, embodying the complexity of human emotions.
