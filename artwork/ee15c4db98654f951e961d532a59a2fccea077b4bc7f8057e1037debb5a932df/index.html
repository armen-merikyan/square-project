Artist Statement

This piece uses a dual emotional tone of confusion and clarity, embodied through contrasting patterns. The palette of navy, black, white, and dim cyan evokes loneliness but also sharp clarity, symbolizing the tension between chaotic overlapping paths and structured maze designs. The main collision occurs in the center where darker shadows clash with brighter whites, suggesting the pull between feeling lost and finding clarity in the chaos. The broken pixels create intentional disruptions that align with the seed's themes, enhancing the sense of confusion while maintaining a stark, structured flow.
