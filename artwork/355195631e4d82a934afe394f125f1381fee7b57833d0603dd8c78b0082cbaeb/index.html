Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts restlessness with peace, represented by a palette of muted tones and a single bright accent. The colors include an off-white for a subtle calm, dust gray for restlessness, compressed red as a focal point, dark navy for depth, and faint peach for warmth, creating a balance of cool and warm anxieties. The central area displays a dense cluster of the red to symbolize motion and conflict, surrounded by darker shades that evoke stillness, creating a 'last light' effect where the saturation rolls off to muted corners, embodying peace amidst turmoil. This layout illustrates a struggle where chaos meets tranquility, linking to the seed's themes of motion and memory through rhythmic placement of pixels. The barcode pattern for restlessness intersects with the ring for peace, reflecting their emotional collision throughout the space.
