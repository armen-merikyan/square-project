Artist Statement

This composition illustrates the emotional tension between 'quiet' and 'pressure'. The palette uses subdued tones like 'off_white' and 'dust_gray' for quiet, contrasted with more striking 'compressed_red' and deep 'dark_navy' to evoke a sense of pressure. The arrangement prioritizes density on one corner to represent a buildup of pressure, while the opposite side feels more spacious, illustrating a release into quiet. The grid's structure allows for a clear conflict, yet the soft edges of colors like 'faint_peach' provide a sense of resolution amidst tension.
