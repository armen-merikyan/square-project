Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between restlessness and peace, illustrated by a palette that juxtaposes cool and warm tones. The central area, framed by deeper colors, signifies a sanctuary of calm amid a backdrop of restless motion represented by vibrant, unsettled hues. The arrangement escalates from lower to upper rows, suggesting a rising signal of hope but also an impending disruption, evoking a sense of unresolved tension through the use of scar patterns interrupting the structured frame.
