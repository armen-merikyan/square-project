Artist Statement

This piece juxtaposes warmth and coolness to portray the stark contrast between love and betrayal. The chosen palette of pink and dark maroon reflects warmth and intensity. The placement creates a sense of rising tension, moving diagonally to suggest hope amidst disappointment. Sharp contrasts in color collide at central points, representing moments of conflict and clarity, reflecting the nature of fractured relationships. The uneven edge styling hints at the vulnerabilities within emotional landscapes, enhancing the feeling of distortion versus symmetry.
