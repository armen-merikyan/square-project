Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between yearning and determination, reflected in the distinct palette of warm and cool hues. Using deep wine contrasts with soft ivory, suggesting a pull between warmth and the desire for safety. The center exudes a dense saturation representing resolve, while the edges mirror the longing with broken, muted colors. The main collision occurs diagonally, where longing resides on the periphery, interrupted by moments of resolve at the center. The inclusion of broken pixels symbolizes imperfections along the fault line of desire and determination, further enhancing the abstract narrative.
