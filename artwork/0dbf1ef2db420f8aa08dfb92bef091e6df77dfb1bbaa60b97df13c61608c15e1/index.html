Artist Statement

Drawing on the emotional dichotomy of nostalgia and progress, this artwork uses contrasting shades of darkness and light to reflect the tension between the past and the future. The palette leans towards deep maroons and grays, evoking a sense of weight and historical depth, while bright pinks suggest moments of hope and vibrancy. The arrangement is weighted towards the bottom, emphasizing a 'fall' of nostalgia against a lighter top, representing 'progress.' Broken pixels disrupt the flow, embodying life's imperfections, while the interplay between noise (nostalgia) and dots (progress) creates a rhythmic tension. The negative space heightens the emotional impact, allowing viewers to contemplate the quiet moments amidst the chaos of contrasting feelings.
