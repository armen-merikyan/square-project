Artist Statement

This piece embodies a tumultuous emotional landscape, exploring the contrasts of love and betrayal. The use of a warm color palette—ranging from pinks to dark maroon—articulates affection and warmth, while the grays and blacks introduce elements of betrayal and chaos, signifying cracks in relationships. The layout juxtaposes a vibrant inner core against a darker, chaotic outer frame, highlighting the theme of pressure juxtaposed with a calm center. The broken pixel elements instill a sense of imperfection and interruption, visualizing the friction between love and betrayal. The composition flows like a heartbeat, erratic yet rhythmic, with spatial contrast enhancing the emotional tension throughout the piece.
