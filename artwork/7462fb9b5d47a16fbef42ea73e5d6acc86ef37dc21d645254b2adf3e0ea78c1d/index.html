Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between loneliness and connection through a dynamic crosshatch pattern that creates a visual rhythm of pressure and release. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue reflects warm and cool contrasts, suggesting a balance between inner warmth and outer coldness. The main collision happens at the center, where a burst of dense, dark colors symbolizes emotional intensity, while lighter hues around it represent exposure and vulnerability. The layout, weighted towards the top, emphasizes a feeling of disruption and imbalance, aligning with the themes of hidden versus exposed, crafting an abstract story of conflict and resolution.
