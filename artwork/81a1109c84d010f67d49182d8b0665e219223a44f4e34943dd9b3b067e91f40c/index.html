Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the tension between loneliness and connection, utilizing a warm vs cool color palette: bright yellows and oranges contrast with deeper blues and muted purples. The colors are arranged to create a burst effect centrally, signifying connection, surrounded by darker hues that embody loneliness, with negative spaces emphasizing isolation. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, representing moments of vulnerability, while the overall structure suggests a crosshatch pattern layered against a grid, mirroring the push and pull between inside and outside. The result is a layered abstract rhythm that channels both disconnection and emotional warmth.
