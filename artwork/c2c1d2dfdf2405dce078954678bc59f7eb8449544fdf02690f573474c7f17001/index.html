Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends longing and resolve, creating a tapestry of yearning against the strength of structure. The palette merges soft mint and storm gray to evoke tension between serenity and urgency, illustrating the struggle of repair versus damage. The collision occurs prominently in the center with overlapping 'teeth' patterns, while peripheral 'dot' motifs represent distant echoes. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, imbuing the piece with depth and imperfection, ultimately suggesting a narrative of fractured harmony.
