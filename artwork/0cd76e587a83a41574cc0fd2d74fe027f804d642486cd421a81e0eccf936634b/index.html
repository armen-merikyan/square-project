Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dialogue between vulnerability and strength, embodied through the color palette of 'serenity_urgency.' The pale aqua and mist gray evoke a sense of shyness and exposure, while the alarm red and ink blue provide stabilizing, dense energy. The main collision occurs in the center, with halo patterns suggesting a protective strength amidst dot patterns that symbolize vulnerability. The asymmetry and broken pixel elements introduce intentional imperfections, enhancing the narrative of a fragile yet resilient center surrounded by a tumultuous exterior. Overall, the piece embodies a continuous pull between exposure and refuge, resulting in a complex emotional rhythm.
