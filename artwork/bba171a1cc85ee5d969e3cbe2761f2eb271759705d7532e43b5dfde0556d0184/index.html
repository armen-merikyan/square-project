Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the conflict between control and surrender, visualized through a blend of dark and muted colors alongside vibrant accents. The careful selection of 'mourning black' suggests containment and restraint, while the soft 'pale yellow' represents moments of release. The use of 'deep blue' as a stabilizing backdrop contrasts with bursts of 'new leaf' to evoke feelings of new beginnings breaking through the pressure. The pattern features a central collision where a dominant color field resonates with the surrounding edges, interrupting the quieter spaces and creating emotional tension. Overall, it tells a story of balancing pressure with an innate drive for release.
