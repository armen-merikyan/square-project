Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between certainty and curiosity, represented by a vibrant yet controlled palette of greens and golds that suggest both growth and conflict. The collision is centered, where darker colors mimic order pulsing against lighter, restless hues, echoing a dichotomy of structured chaos. Subtle broken pixels introduce disruptions, enhancing the idea of pondering edges and gradual ascents or recessions, echoing the tidal themes suggested by the seed.
