Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between control and panic. The palette 'ancient_fire' embodies this tension with deep burnt oranges and dark reds symbolizing chaos, contrasted by muted charcoal which suggests control. The arrangement creates a symmetrical mirrored effect that is subtly disrupted, illustrating moments of interruption together with an echo of patterns. The negative space acts as a breath between frantic colors, adding depth to the narrative of conflict and resolution.
