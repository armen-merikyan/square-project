Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances fear's shadowy presence with hope's soft luminance. The dark palette reflects the depth of fear, while the soft gold and pale green signify glimmers of hope. The left side portrays fear with a rain-like pattern, morphing into the right's upward stripes of hope. Main contrast arises from dense, dark clusters colliding with light areas, suggesting a balance between anxiety and aspiration. The vertical split reflects the weight of emotions, and broken pixels introduce imperfection, echoing the human experience.
