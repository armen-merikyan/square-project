Artist Statement

This square embodies the emotional dissonance of certainty and curiosity through contrasting structures. A muted palette of sepia and faded blue evokes nostalgia and introspection, while scattered accents introduce unexpected disruptions, suggesting curiosity's persistent nature. The pattern arrangement leads with dense fields converging toward a narrow path of negative space at the center, symbolizing a journey from chaos to clarity. Broken pixels along the edges reflect the imperfections inherent in this exploration.
