Artist Statement

The emotional tone of longing juxtaposed with the resolve creates a visual narrative of tension. The chosen palette of warm and cool colors—yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple—suggests a contrast between warmth (longing) and coolness (resolve). The main collision occurs at the center where crosshatch patterns intersect with broken dots, symbolizing the complexity of emotions lurking beneath the surface. Quiet spaces allow for breathing room, representing serenity amidst urgency. The use of broken pixels adds imperfection, signifying damage and repair interlaced within the emotional landscape, while also adhering to the layout of a threshold that implies a passage or seam between two emotional states.
