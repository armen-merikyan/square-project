Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between safety and exposure through a palette that juxtaposes warm browns and greens against the coolness of pale skin and open blue. The bright elements in the center represent 'exposure', creating a sense of fragility, while the surrounding darker tones function as a protective 'safety' border. The echo of density is felt through heavy layering and crosshatch patterns, suggesting a braid against external pressure. The placement invites a visual exploration from calm to tension.
