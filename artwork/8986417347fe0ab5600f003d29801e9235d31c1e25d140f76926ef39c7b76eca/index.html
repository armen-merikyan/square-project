Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of vulnerability and strength, weaving a narrative of exposure against shelter. The palette favors warm coral and soft amber to signify warmth and fragility alongside the cool tones of cold slate and signal cyan to evoke strength and signal connection. The main clash occurs centrally, where the warm hues meet the cool ones, suggesting an energetic collision. This layout functions as descending pixels, reinforcing a sense of falling yet building resilience through the density of color contrast.
