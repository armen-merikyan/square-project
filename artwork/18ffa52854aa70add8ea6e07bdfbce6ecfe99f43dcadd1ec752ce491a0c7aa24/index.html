Artist Statement

This composition explores the juxtaposition of safety and exposure through a delicate balance of greens and browns suggesting comfort, contrasted with pale skin tones and whites representing vulnerability. The vertical split draws attention to a tense central collision point where the stacked safety pattern meets the barcode exposure, invoking a feeling of pressure released in sudden bursts. The palette embodies warmth and coolness, creating a narrative of shifting emotional states.
