Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional dynamic between joy and grief, represented through a warm palette of yellows and oranges juxtaposed with muted blues and purples. The vibrant upper section signifies joy, implemented through a striped pattern, while the lower half embodies grief, characterized by a stair-step arrangement that conveys a feeling of heaviness. The contrast occurs along a jagged edge creating tension and highlights the interplay between these two emotional states, emphasizing the struggle between order and chaos. Negative space is utilized to suggest moments of release, enhancing the narrative inherently captured in the seed.
