Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances restlessness and peace, with a contrasting palette of warm and muted colors reflecting the inner struggle. The colors are arranged in dynamic patterns: warm accents clash with cool, muted fields, embodying the conflict of memory versus motion. The main collision occurs in the center where bright tones intersect with dark shadows, creating an 'overfilled center' that suggests entrapment in noise while edges form chaotic, broken pulses of relief.
