Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional duality of panic and control through its use of color and composition. The palette of yellow and orange represents warmth and vibrancy, indicative of hope and release, while muted purple and deep blue signify the weight of grief and pressure. The tension is primarily created through asymmetrical patterns, where brighter colors collide with darker hues, evoking a sense of unrest. The growing density in certain areas suggests a rising current of emotion, dynamically contrasting with negative space that represents the moments of calm amidst chaos.
