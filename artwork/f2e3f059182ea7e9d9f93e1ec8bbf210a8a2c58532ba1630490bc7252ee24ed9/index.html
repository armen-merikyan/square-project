Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional dichotomy of clarity versus confusion through contrasting colors and patterns. The chosen palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon evokes a sense of both warmth and turmoil, reflecting love's duality. The outer rows represent clarity with symmetrical color distributions, while internal rows embody confusion with crack-like patterns, creating visual tension. The interplay between smooth and jagged edges heightens the frame-and-core layout, illustrating the struggle between structured order and fragmented chaos.
