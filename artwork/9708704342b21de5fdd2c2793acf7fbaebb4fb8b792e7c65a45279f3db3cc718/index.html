Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between loneliness and connection through contrasting colors and patterns. The colors of deep maroon and gray evoke a sense of isolation, while brighter pinks burst forth in clusters, symbolizing fleeting connections. The arrangement is top-weighted, with density shifting towards the top edges, creating a feeling of heaviness that contrasts with the lighter, more liberated bottom half. The broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, reflecting the fractured nature of connections in the face of loneliness. This layout plays out a narrative of hidden turmoil against the backdrop of fleeting moments of warmth.
