Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between clarity and chaos. The palette of deep navy, dim cyan, and stark black represents a void filled with static, while the lonely white accents serve as piercing signals cutting through noise. The arrangement features distinct patterns where the lattice of order interacts with chaotic hinge structures, creating a dynamic pulse that draws the eye across the composition. The edges are heavily weighted towards darker tones, enhancing a sense of boundaries blurred by interference. A central clash is felt where bright whites collide with deep blues, symbolizing a struggle against drowning static. The result is an abstract representation of the ongoing battle between signal and noise, shaped by the metaphor of a broken mirror reflecting distorted truths.
