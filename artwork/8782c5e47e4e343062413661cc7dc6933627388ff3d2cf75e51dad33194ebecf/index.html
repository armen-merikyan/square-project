Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between a sense of peace and a restless undercurrent, represented through contrasting colors and shapes. The use of signal green and electric blue introduces vibrancy, suggesting movement, while dead black and static gray serve as calming elements, grounding the composition. The main contrast occurs diagonally across the square, where the calm meets the chaos, embodying the tension between stability and disturbance. The layout's spiral pattern reflects a dynamic pull towards a center, suggesting unresolved motion, while the broken pixels punctuate the structure, introducing an element of imperfection, akin to moments of spontaneous disruption in memory.
