Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines awe and terror through a contrast-laden palette. The use of deep navy and black juxtaposes a bright, singular white point, symbolizing a sudden release amid pressure. This tension creates collisions between light and dark, with the pattern transitioning from a quiet, calm base to dense structures, illustrating the rise of emotion, echoing the seed's theme. The jagged edges capture a raw, chaotic feeling without forming recognizable shapes, reinforcing the abstract narrative.
