Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between calmness and agitation, captured by the contrasting elements in the composition. The palette, inspired by 'ancient_fire,' embodies warmth with burnt orange and dark red juxtaposed against the coolness of charcoal and gold, creating a tension that echoes the seed's themes. The main contrast manifests in the center, where the fiery 'flame' pattern meets the subdued 'negative_cross,' illustrating a collision of energy and stillness. This composition transcends literal interpretation, instead suggesting the push and pull of memories and movement in a space of quiet chaos.
