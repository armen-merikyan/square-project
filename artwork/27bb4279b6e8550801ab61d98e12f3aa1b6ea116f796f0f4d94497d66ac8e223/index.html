Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between rage and calm, illustrated through a palette of intense reds and soft blues. The red creates sharp contrast against the pale blue, representing the conflict between pressure and tranquility. The arrangement mirrors an upward and downward motion, where the reds gravitate towards the center, embodying rage, while the blues spread out, suggesting calm. This collision encapsulates the seed's concepts, translating them into an abstract visual narrative of tension and release.
