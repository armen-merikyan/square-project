Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between defiance and submission, manifesting a dense, saturated center with sharp, angular edges juxtaposed against softer, broken fields. The palette juxtaposes warm sepia tones with cool faded blues, illustrating the conflict and resolution within the structure. The primary collision occurs in the central area where the lattice of defiance meets the cracks of submission, creating visual tension. This design reflects the seed's themes of order vs chaos and the nuanced dialogue between boundaries and openness, ultimately embodying the complex emotional landscape.
