Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, symbolizing a fragile core surrounded by a dense exterior. The palette of indigo, black, and cool gold enhances this juxtaposition—the soft warmth of gold juxtaposes against the raw depth of black, embodying tension and resilience. The main collision occurs centrally, where the radial strength pattern collides with the echo pattern of vulnerability. This interaction forms a compelling visual narrative of pressure and release, representing the delicate balance of emotion shaped by the seed.
