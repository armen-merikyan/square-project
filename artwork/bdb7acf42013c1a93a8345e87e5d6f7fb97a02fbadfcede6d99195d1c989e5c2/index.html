Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a tension between longing and resolve, reflected through soft pinks and stark black accents, embodying the complexity of repair and damage. The palette juxtaposes gentle warmth against serious severity, conveying resilience within vulnerability. The collision occurs centrally, where tighter 'hinge' patterns of longing interact and overlap with the 'constellation' patterns of resolve, creating a narrative of struggle and strength. Negative space acts as a reflection on the emotional contrasts, inviting viewers to ponder the distance and connection inherent in the seed's themes.
