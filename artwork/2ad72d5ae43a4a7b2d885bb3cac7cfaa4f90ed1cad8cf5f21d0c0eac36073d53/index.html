Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy between longing and resolve, using colors that juxtapose warmth and coolness to evoke tension. The chosen palette features - off white and faint peach to suggest warmth and hope, while - dark navy and dust gray express a sense of longing and cold distance. The central conflict occurs in the combination of warm stripes representing resolve, intersected by darker threads symbolizing longing, creating a visual pulse. A moderate amount of negative space mirrors the feeling of yearning. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions echoing the theme of repairs and damages, representing emotional scars amidst a backdrop of quiet pressure.
