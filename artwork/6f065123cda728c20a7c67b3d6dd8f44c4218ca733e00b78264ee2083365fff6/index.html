Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional interplay between emptiness and desire, framed within a central radial shape that gives an illusion of warmth versus coolness. The palette features sepia and faded blue tones to convey a sense of nostalgia while introducing a tension through the use of muted grays. The main contrast arises from the warmer colors in the center, radiating outward toward cooler edges, echoing the fluctuations of heartbeats and the battles of loneliness. Broken pixels introduce an imperfection that suggests the fragmented nature of longing, enhancing the abstract narrative of desire caught in emptiness. The result is a compelling dynamic between pressure and release, inviting viewers to ponder their own emotional signals.
