Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between control and surrender. The palette consists of muted tones complemented by a bright accent, conveying a feeling of quiet pressure with hints of release. The central collision occurs at the center, where the structured control pattern of the checkerboard interacts with softer, flowing threads. The broken pixels symbolize disruptions in the otherwise orderly surround, adding layers of complexity while adhering to the 10% negative space rule to emphasize the emotional struggle between confinement and freedom.
