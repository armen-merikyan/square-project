Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the struggle between clarity and chaos, with a palette highlighting vibrant contrasts as green and magenta punctuate muted shades of gray and black. The main collision takes place diagonally, representing the clash between the readable rhythm and static scatter of thoughts. The interplay of the negative cross pattern suggests a path through confusion, while broken pixels serve to create intentional disturbances, echoing the idea of fragmented memory amidst a sea of noise.
