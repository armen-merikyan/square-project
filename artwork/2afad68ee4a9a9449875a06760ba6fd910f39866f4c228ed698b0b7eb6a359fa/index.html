Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the tension of trust versus suspicion, represented through a palette that contrasts soft hues of warmth with stark tones of severity. The chosen colors like powder pink and sharp crimson play against carbon black and steel gray to manifest the underlying pulse of resilience and fragility. The main collision sits diagonally, where the warmer top section, suggesting trust, fights against the cooler, darker bottom, echoing suspicion. This creates a visual narrative where patterns ripple and distort—an interplay of calm and pressure reflecting the seed's themes.
