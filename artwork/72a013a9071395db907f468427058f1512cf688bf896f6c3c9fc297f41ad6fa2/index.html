Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve. The color palette, representing mourning and renewal, juxtaposes deep blues against pale yellows representing hope. Broken pixels scattered throughout signify damage and repair, echoing the tension between the two emotional states. The center of the grid features the most vibrant colors, reflecting a powerful collision between desire and fulfillment, while keeping quieter hues towards the edges to create a sense of space and release. The upward flow embodies aspiration, with dense clusters of color rising from darker tones, suggesting a resolve to overcome.
