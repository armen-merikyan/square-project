Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a dichotomy between loneliness and connection, reflecting the complexities of quiet moments filled with tension. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of nostalgia and broken memories, enhancing the theme of emotional collision. The contrast between warm and cool colors creates a visual tension, while the layout suggests a rising signal, symbolizing hope amidst pressure. The placement of colors, with heavier shades at the edges and lighter ones towards the center, emphasizes the push and pull of emotion, with broken pixels adding an element of imperfection and interruption.
