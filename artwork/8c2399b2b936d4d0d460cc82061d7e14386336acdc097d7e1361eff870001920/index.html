Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between dread and relief, represented through dense clusters of dark colors juxtaposed against lighter hues. The selected palette of dark grays and muted mint colors embodies a balance of chaos and calm, echoing the seed's themes of pressure and release. The primary contrast emerges from the center, where dark colors collide with light, creating a spiral effect that suggests both chaos and resolution. Broken pixels add intentional disruptions, enhancing the sense of imperfection in this emotional landscape.
