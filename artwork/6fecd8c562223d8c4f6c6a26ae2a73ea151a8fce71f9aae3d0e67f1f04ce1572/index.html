Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between restlessness and peace through a calculated clash of colors and patterns. The palette merges warm coral and soft amber with the cooler tones of blue-black and cold slate, creating a visual narrative of conflict and balance. The central collision emerges from the dynamic tide pattern of restlessness (represented by warmer hues) against a backdrop of soft, quiet noise symbolizing peace. The edges and corners bear marks of erosion, emphasizing movement's disruptive nature while maintaining an undercurrent of stability in the center. This artistic interplay reflects the essence of memory and motion, creating an echo of emotional intensity that invites exploration and introspection.
