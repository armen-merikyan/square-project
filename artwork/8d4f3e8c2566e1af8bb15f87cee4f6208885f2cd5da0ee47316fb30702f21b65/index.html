Artist Statement

This abstraction embodies the emotional tension between trust and suspicion. The palette consists of muted tones ('storm_gray' and 'near_black') for suspicion contrasted against lighter hues ('pale_mint' and 'soft_sky') to represent trust, creating an engaging dynamic. The main collision occurs near the center, where darker shades interrupt the lighter fields, exemplifying the push and pull of these emotions. The chosen layout, open_gate, facilitates a flow that visualizes the gradual interplay of hope and doubt—showing how proximity alters perception and creates an echo of emotions within the grid.
