Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines the tension of clarity (signal) against the chaos of distortion (noise). The palette leverages deep navy and black to represent the overwhelming presence of noise, contrasted by bright white accents symbolizing fleeting signals. The main collision occurs at the center with lighter colors surrounding darker shades, illustrating the battle between clear signals and intrusive noise. As colors disrupt each other, they represent the complexity of memory versus erasure, tying back to the seed's theme of interference and clarity in communication. The composition overall reflects a striking contrast between the dense areas of dark colors and the interruptions of light, creating a visual representation of the seed's underlying emotional theme.
