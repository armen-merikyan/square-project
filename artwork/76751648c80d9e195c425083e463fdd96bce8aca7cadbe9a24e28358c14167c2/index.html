Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers around the struggle between clarity and chaos, symbolizing the tension between signal and noise. The palette features deep blacks and vibrant hues, reflecting noise through static gray and dead black, while signal is represented by the bright signal green and electric blue, suggesting clarity. The main collision exists in the center, where the radial pattern from the signal clashes with the lattice of noise, creating a dynamic interplay that echoes the themes of exposure and shelter.
