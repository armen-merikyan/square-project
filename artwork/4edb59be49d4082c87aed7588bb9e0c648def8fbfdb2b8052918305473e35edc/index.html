Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the duality of loneliness and connection through its spatial arrangement and color dynamics. The use of dark shades in the corners evokes a sense of weight and isolation, while the brighter colors within suggest warmth and potential connection. The deliberate placement of broken pixels creates moments of interruption and imperfection, mirroring the inherent struggle in forging connections amidst loneliness. The palette, inspired by ancient fire, contrasts the warm burnt orange and gold with the deep charcoal to emphasize the conflict, while the structured grid amidst free spaces embodies the tension between connectivity and isolation.
