Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a juxtaposition between trust and suspicion, where the cool, faded blue accents suggest a sense of calm and quiet contrast, while the warmer sepia and tan shades introduce an underlying tension. The palette embodies a 'broken memory' feel, reinforcing the theme of emotional collisions. The main contrast occurs centrally where muted colors collide with brighter hues, creating a rhythmic interlude of subtle tension across the canvas. The flow represents a diagonal rise, guiding the viewer's eye from bottom to top, mirroring an emotional build-up that seeks release amidst the fractured order of the shapes.
