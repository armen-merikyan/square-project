Artist Statement

The composition reflects a tension between certainty and curiosity. The palette of deep blue, pale yellow, and mourning black creates an emotional narrative where the darker tones embody certainty, and the lighter ones signify the sparks of curiosity. The central two pulses represent rising signals amid a backdrop of almost chaotic touches of color that clash and blend, suggesting an ongoing push-pull dynamic. Broken pixels add imperfections that symbolize the uncertain nature of exploration within rigid structures.
