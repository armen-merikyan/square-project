Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The muted red oxides and blackened plums evoke deep feelings of yearning, while the iron gray and small orange introduce elements of strength and clarity. The central collision of longing's islands and resolve's ash fields creates a dynamic interplay of spaced patterns and abrupt interruptions, with a calm quiet field of muted cream to balance the intensity. The 'open_gate' layout facilitates a flow inward, emphasizing the contrast between distant echoes of yearning and the strong, locked shapes of resolve. Broken pixels scatter through the grid, symbolizing imperfection and the essence of repair versus damage.
