Artist Statement

The artwork illustrates the tension between restlessness and peace using a palette that juxtaposes warm browns and cooler greens, creating an emotional dialogue. The asymmetrical placement of cooler colors encroaching upon warm hues suggests the struggle of seeking calm in chaotic moments. The collision occurs at the central diagonal, where conflicting patterns of maze-like restlessness clash against the subdued flames of peace, echoing the lush textures of memory amid chaos, leading to an abstract narrative of struggle and serenity.
