Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional duality of softness and severity, where the warm palette represents healing and gentleness while the cooler tones illustrate strict boundaries. The contrast is centered, suggesting a collision between calm and fracture, with broken pixels acting as spaces of disruption amidst a soft radial flow, evoking feelings of pulse and rhythm in a chaotic world. Its abstract pattern captures the essence of conflict and reconciliation without literal representation.
