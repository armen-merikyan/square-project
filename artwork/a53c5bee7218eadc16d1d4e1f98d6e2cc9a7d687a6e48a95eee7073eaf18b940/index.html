Artist Statement

The composition captures the emotional struggle between longing and resolve, using a palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold to represent warmth and depth. The main collision occurs in the center, where a dense cluster of darker colors interrupts the lighter fields, reflecting the pulse of emotions. The chosen patterns—embers for longing and mirror for resolve—create a stark contrast that echoes through the grid, illustrating an unresolved tension while leaving ample negative space to emphasize the emotional gaps.
