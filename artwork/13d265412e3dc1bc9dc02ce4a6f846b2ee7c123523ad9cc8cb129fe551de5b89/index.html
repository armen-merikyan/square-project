Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional duality between trust and suspicion through contrasting color fields. The palette features deep navy and black for suspicion, contrasted by the soft glow of dim cyan representing trust. The central collision occurs where darker blocks meet the lighter ones, symbolizing tension and resolution. The climbing pattern reflects a hopefulness emerging from uncertainty, while the broken pixels scatter throughout to signify disruptions in trust, achieving an uneasy balance.
