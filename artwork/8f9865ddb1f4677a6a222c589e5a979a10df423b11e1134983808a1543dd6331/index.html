Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between desire and emptiness, articulated through a palette of warm and cool tones to signify conflict. The main contrast emerges from the vibrant warmth in the center against the surrounding cooler, muted colors, creating a visual echo of inner turmoil. The layout follows a mostly empty landscape with a diamond shape centerpiece that represents desire, while jagged edges suggest the challenging journey towards fulfillment, creating a resonant emotional tension throughout the piece.
