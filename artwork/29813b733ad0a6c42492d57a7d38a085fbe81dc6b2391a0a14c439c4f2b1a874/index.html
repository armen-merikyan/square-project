Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional interplay of desire and restraint through warm reds and muted creams, visually represented in a grid pattern mixed with channel disruptions. The main collision occurs centrally, where saturated colors attempt to break free from the cool boundary of grays, symbolizing the push-and-pull of yearning against containment. The palette rightly encapsulates the struggle: lush warmth suggests desire while the grays denote restraint, creating a vibrant yet controlled emotional landscape.
