Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional terrain between strength and vulnerability, characterized by a soft yet defined center, surrounded by contrasting colors. The palette draws from warm and cool tones, suggesting intimacy while maintaining distance. The collision happens around the center, where thin edges meet bold blocks, representing the tension between exposure and protection. The use of broken pixels introduces an element of unpredictability, echoing the complexities of connection and alienation that the seed expresses.
