Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality between trust and suspicion through contrasting patterns and colors. The warm reds and soft pinks represent trust, creating a cozy flame-like flow, while the stark and dark charcoals introduce an undercurrent of suspicion with dotted interruptions. The pressure corner layout accentuates a dominant color scheme, reflecting resilience through stable bases against the fragile whispers of self-doubt and observation. Each collision of warm and cool tones creates a visual tension, echoing the complexities inherent in human relationships.
