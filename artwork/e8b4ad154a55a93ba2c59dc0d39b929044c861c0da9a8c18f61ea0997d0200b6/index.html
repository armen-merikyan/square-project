Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between loneliness and the desire for connection. The palette features muted sepia and faded blue, suggesting nostalgia and quiet sadness with moments of warmth. The main contrast occurs in the center, where a burst of lighter colors disrupts the darker edges, symbolizing a struggle between exposure and the comfort of hidden spaces. The crack pattern enhances the sensation of fragmentation in feelings, while the mirror layout subtly disrupts expectations with a few broken pixels, emphasizing imperfection and the complexities of human emotions.
