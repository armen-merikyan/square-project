Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tumultuous interplay of contrasting emotions through a balance of dense clusters and quiet spaces. The palette features deep blues and vibrant yellows that create a visual dialogue of pressure and release. The strategic placement of darker tones in the corners signifies compression and intensity, while bursts of lighter colors invoke moments of relief and rhythm, embodying the emotional collisions described in the seed. Each row reflects a gradual transition, echoing the dynamics of tension and tranquility.
