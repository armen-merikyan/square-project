Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tone of tension and release. The palette combines electric blue and magenta for vibrancy, contrasted with the depth of black for intensity. The sharp vs. soft contrast is felt through abrupt color transitions amid a calmer background, suggesting a rhythmic interplay of pressure and quiet. The layout reflects mirrored chaos, with broken pixels creating an imperfect harmony that interrupts the visual flow, embodying the emotional collisions woven into the seed.
