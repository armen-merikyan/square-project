Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional contrast between vulnerability and strength through a pallette of melancholic colors that evoke a sense of both exposure and shelter. The light hues represent vulnerability, while deeper shades symbolize strength, creating a visual tension. The grid layout suggests a struggle between these two emotions, with the main collision occurring at the center where light meets dark. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional imperfections, embodying the scars left by emotional experiences.
