Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the tension of control versus panic, using a palette that reflects love and betrayal through warm reds and cool grays. The checker and ring patterns create an interplay of static control and chaotic movement, emphasizing the contrast between quiet moments and bursts of pressure. The main collision occurs where the deep maroon meets soft pink, symbolizing the struggle between safety and chaos. This abstract pattern illustrates an emotional landscape that conveys the intensity of internal conflicts and rhythmic shifts.
