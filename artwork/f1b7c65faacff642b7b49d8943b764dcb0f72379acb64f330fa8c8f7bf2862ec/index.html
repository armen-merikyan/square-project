Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress, with a color palette reflecting faded memories (sepia, dust gray) juxtaposed against vibrant accents (faded blue, tan). The split horizontal layout creates a visual dichotomy—one half embraces softness and the past, while the other introduces a structured rhythm of forward momentum. The jagged edges symbolize the interruptions and imperfections of memory. The central area draws the eye with a subtle imbalance, suggesting a narrative conflict between longing and ambition.
