Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between clarity and confusion. The palette focuses on strong contrasts with shades of red and black symbolizing chaos, while pale blue and white introduce moments of clarity. The primary conflict arises in the intersection of dense black and sharp white segments, reflecting the push and pull of clarity against confusion. The falling pixel layout creates a rhythm that echoes the sensation of a disintegrating structure, allowing the viewer to experience the unsettling dance between harmony and disruption.
