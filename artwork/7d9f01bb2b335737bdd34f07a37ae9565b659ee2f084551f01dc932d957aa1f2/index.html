Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between panic (turmoil represented by saturated shades) and control (calmness depicted with soft earth tones). The palette of sepia, tan, and dust gray contrasts with bursts of faded blue, symbolizing the struggle of spark against weight. The pixels depict a near symmetry disrupted intentionally, representing the 'broken' aspect of control in chaos, with dense clusters emerging from a quiet backdrop. Light and dark tones create a dialogue of weight versus lightness, echoing the seeds' themes of inner turmoil and stability.
