Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between vulnerability and strength, represented through a palette of soft and muted tones alongside deep contrast colors. The emotional tone shifts from a central pale white that reflects exposure to surrounding darker colors that signify strength and shelter. The collision occurs at the center where the dense clusters of dark colors mingle with the lighter fields, creating a dynamic interaction. The soft edges enhance the feeling of vulnerability while the broken dots pattern reinforces the strength of the outer frame. Overall, the arrangement captures the essence of interwoven emotional states through structured chaos.
