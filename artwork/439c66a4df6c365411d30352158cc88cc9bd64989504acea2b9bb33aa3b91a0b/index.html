Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the struggle between clarity and chaos through an interplay of colors and patterns. Dark hues like navy and black embody the noise, while the contrasting dim cyan introduces signals of clarity amid the congested palette. The top-heavy layout hints at the weight of noise falling like ash, while the fractured presence of white suggests light moments trying to breakthrough. Collisions occur as dim cyan interjects into areas of darkness, creating focal points that symbolize memories amidst the din of static. The placement of broken pixels introduces intentional imperfections, echoing the theme of struggling clarity amidst interference.
