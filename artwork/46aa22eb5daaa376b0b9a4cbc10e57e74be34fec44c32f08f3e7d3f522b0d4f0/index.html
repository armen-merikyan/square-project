Artist Statement

This piece embodies the contrast between sparks of vitality and the settled dust of memories. The chosen palette of rain blue, lavender gray, moon white, soft cyan, and muted plum evokes a melancholic yet wondrous tone, reflecting both resilience and fragility. The main collision occurs at the center where the soft cyan, representing a spark, collides with the checker pattern of muted plum, symbolizing ash. The layout emphasizes erosion, maintaining an open vs. closed contrast that suggests tension between space and density, allowing whispers of memory to intersperse with vibrant moments. The use of broken pixels adds intentional imperfections to communicate the process of erasure against remembrance. Overall, the interplay of colors and patterns captures the delicate balance of presence versus absence.
