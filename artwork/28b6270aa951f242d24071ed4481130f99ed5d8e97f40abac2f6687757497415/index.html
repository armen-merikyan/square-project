Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition navigates the palpable tension between desire and restraint. The selected palette of rich reds and muted creams creates a warmth that speaks to longing, while iron gray introduces a holding back effect, emphasizing restraint. The bright small orange accents interrupt the dark, moody blacks, which in turn cluster toward the edges, mapping a boundary of emotional saturation. The central collision of the warm island patterns representing desire meets the more structured ladder-like restraint patterns, forming a dialogue that embodies the struggle between reaching out and holding back—an abstract reflection of the seed concepts.
