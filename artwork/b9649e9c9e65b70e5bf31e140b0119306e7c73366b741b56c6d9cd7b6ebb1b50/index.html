Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional contrast between panic and control through an alternating pattern of chaotic rain and structured checker, reflecting the underlying tension of the seed. The color palette, 'broken_memory', focuses on sepia and faded tones to evoke nostalgia and instability, suggesting an empty room caught between safety and anxiety. The main collision occurs in the middle of the piece, where the orderly checker pattern intersects with the erratic rain pattern, representing the struggle between feeling grounded and lost.
