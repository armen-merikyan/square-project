Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between connection and loneliness, reflecting a stark emotional landscape. The palette, dominated by deep maroons, soft pinks, and muted grays, evokes both warmth and isolation, embodying the struggle inherent in human relationships. The core contrast emerges at the center, where dark maroon signifies loneliness while the lighter pinks bleed into it, suggesting a burst of connection attempting to break through. The layout subtly reveals a flow from the bottom left to the top right, symbolizing an ascent from isolation towards greater connection, all while incorporating negative space to evoke the feelings of emptiness that often accompany loneliness. The selective use of broken pixels creates an imperfection that mirrors the intricate nature of emotional experiences.
