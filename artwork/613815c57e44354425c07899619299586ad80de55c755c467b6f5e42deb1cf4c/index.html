Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the clash between clarity and confusion, represented through a palette of red and gray. The outer border embodies clarity with structured patterns in subdued tones, while the inner core erupts with chaotic splashes of deeper reds, illustrating a pulse of emotion amidst what appears orderly. The sharp vs. soft contrast accentuates the tension, with a striking collision at the center disrupting the symmetry, invoking a sense of fleeting calm being overwhelmed by tumult. The broken pixels create imperfections, underscoring the theme of distorted perception within familiar confines.
