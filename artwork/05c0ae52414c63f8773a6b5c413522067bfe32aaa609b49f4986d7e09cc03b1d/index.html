Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between fear and hope through contrasting dark and soft hues. The outer edges, featuring darker colors, symbolize fear, while the lighter, golden center suggests hope and illumination. The zigzag pattern evokes a rising signal, creating a dynamic rhythm that implies conflict and movement. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, echoing life's uncertainties. The interplay between dark corners and a bright center visually narrates the dual nature of emotional experience, making the viewer confront the balance between pressure and relief.
