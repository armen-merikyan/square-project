Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between trust and suspicion through the use of a palette that emphasizes both darkness and warmth. The deep blue and mourning black juxtaposed with pale yellow creates a sense of conflict, indicative of containment versus openness. The dominant pattern emerges from a soft grid representing trust on one side and an irregular rain-like disruption on the other, suggesting anxiety and uncertainty. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the structured grid abruptly meets chaotic marks, embodying the struggle between stability and fragmentation.
