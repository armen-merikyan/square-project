Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between clarity and confusion, accentuated by a palette inspired by faded memories in sepia and cool blues. The left side boasts a radial pattern of soft tans and grays suggesting calm clarity, while the right side bursts with more chaotic, darker tones, simulating confusion's noise. The main contrast occurs at the center where the structured pattern breaks, creating anticipation and tension, embodying a visual dialogue of symmetry disrupted by imperfection. Broken pixels add subtle irregularities, reinforcing the theme of instability inherent in the emotions explored.
