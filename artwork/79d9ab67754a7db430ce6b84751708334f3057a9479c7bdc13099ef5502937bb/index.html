Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between clarity and confusion, driven by the interplay of signal and noise. The palette of dark navy and black with sharp dim cyan accents creates visual tension, representing the struggle of integrity against chaotic distractions. The dense center resonates with clean lines while the corners exhibit fragmentation, mimicking echoes of disruption. In this design, the collision occurs prominently around the center, where structured patterns meet unpredictable chaos, illustrating a narrative of resilience amidst overwhelming noise.
