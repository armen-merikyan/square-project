Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between clarity and confusion through a pattern that oscillates between symmetry and imperfection. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue reflects a nostalgic ambiance, evoking a sense of broken memory and the interplay of warm and cool tones. The main contrast arises from the juxtaposition of densely packed pixels against areas of negative space, which reflects the conflict between chaos and calm. The 'ring' pattern, symbolizing confusion, intersperses with 'spark' motifs of clarity, presenting an abstract interpretation of the emotional journey detailed in the seed.
