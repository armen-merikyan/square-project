Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between clarity and chaos, represented by a palette of navy and dim cyan illuminating broken pixels against a foundation of darkness. The arranged colors evoke a pulse rising amidst interference, highlighting the competition between ordered rhythm and scattered static. The piece's layout emphasizes an upward movement, suggesting a continuous search for connection amidst confusion.
