Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between the vibrant 'spark' and the subdued 'ash'. The palette features muted tones of sepia and gray, representing settled dust and faded heat, alongside brighter accents signifying scattered light and bursts of energy. The central composition is characterized by a light halo around a darker void, creating a sense of depth and conflict. The jagged edge pattern contrasts with the smoother, tide-like waves, representing the collision between resilience and fragility. Overall, the arrangement elicits a feeling of dynamic tension, where one emotion subtly disrupts the other, leading to a complex interplay of colors and patterns that capture the essence of the seed's themes.
