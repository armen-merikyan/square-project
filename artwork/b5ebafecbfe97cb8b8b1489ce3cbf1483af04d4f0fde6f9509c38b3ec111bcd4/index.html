Artist Statement

This composition captures the dynamic contrast between restlessness and peace through an interplay of dense dark tones and light fields. The palette consists of muted grays contrasted with pale mint and faded gold to suggest a journey from unease to solace, where dark shades pulse with energy against the calm of the lighter hues. The main collision occurs in the center where the dense island of darker pixels disrupts the tranquility of the surrounding area, prompting a visual dialogue of tension and release. The patterns of stripes interwoven with pulse marks convey the conflicting emotions inherent in the seed.
