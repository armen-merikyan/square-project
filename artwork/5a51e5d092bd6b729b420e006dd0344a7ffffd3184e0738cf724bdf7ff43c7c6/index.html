Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a battle between clarity and static interference, symbolizing the struggle to maintain a coherent pulse amidst chaos. The palette of deep navy and dim cyan represents the quiet fields of signal, juxtaposed against black for stark noise. The central collision of colors occurs in the middle of the grid, where a bright accent disrupts the dark tones, evoking a sense of urgency and tension from static echoes. This layout plays into the 'falling_pixels' concept, contributing to a perceived descent of chaotic energy.
