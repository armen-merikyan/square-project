Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission, utilizing contrasting colors to illustrate the collision of these states. The palette of warm ivory and deep wine oscillates between boldness and softness, where warm tones reflect defiance while cooler shades suggest submission. The center of the piece showcases a density of dark colors, representing the weight of defiance, while the lighter shades push towards the edges, creating a contrast that emphasizes space, echoing the theme of invasion and pressure. The 'eye' shape seed loosely inspires the circular patterns, allowing for a spiral movement that suggests inward and outward dynamics, reinforcing the dance between holding back and breaking free.
