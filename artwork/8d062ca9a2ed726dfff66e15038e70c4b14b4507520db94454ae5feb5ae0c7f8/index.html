Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy between restlessness and peace, employing a flowing layout that spirals toward a central tension. The palette incorporates warm coral and soft amber to evoke a sense of warmth and tranquility, juxtaposed against cold slate and blue black for depth and unease. The collision occurs predominantly in the center, depicting the struggle between calm and turmoil, while maintaining a quiet field to suggest unresolved tension. Broken pixels introduce imperfect disruptions, akin to fleeting thoughts or memories, reinforcing the sense of motion and memory.
