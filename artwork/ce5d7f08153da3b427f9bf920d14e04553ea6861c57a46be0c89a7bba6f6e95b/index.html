Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette of warm and cool tones to highlight emotional contrasts. The deep blue serves as a stable backdrop, representing strength, while vibrant orange and muted purple create a sense of vulnerability and uncertainty at the edges. The layout focuses pressure on the bottom left corner, suggesting a chaotic accumulation of emotion, as stronger colors clash with the quieter spaces in the opposite direction. The arrangement reflects the balance between exposed light in the center and the oppressive weight of the edges. Broken pixels appear to symbolize scars or interruptions, enhancing the overall narrative of resilience amidst fragility.
