Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional contrast between safety and exposure through a structured, rising arrangement. The palette utilizes 'shelter_green' and 'warm_brown' to symbolize protection against the vulnerability of 'pale_skin' and 'cold_white'. The soft colors are layered amidst heavier tones, creating a collision that visually represents the tension of safety versus exposure. The lattice and pressure band patterns overlap in a vertical split, emphasizing a balance and gradual rise, marking an optimistic journey through emotional states. Broken pixels interrupt the flow to symbolize the imperfections of both safety and vulnerability.
