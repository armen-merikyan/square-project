Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a juxtaposition between softness and severity, representing the tension between light and dark elements. The palette combines warm pinks and whites with striking black and gray tones to demonstrate this contrast. The collision occurs centrally, where deeper colors interrupt the lighter tones, symbolizing deeper emotions surfacing. The spiral flow pattern echoes the seed's theme of orientation, leading the eye inward, while broken pixels add intentional imperfections, suggesting vulnerability within the strength of the structure.
