Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay between loneliness and connection, represented through contrasting tones. The chosen palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green embodies the tension of despair against hope. The arrangement clusters towards the center, creating a palpable pressure that echoes loneliness, while the lighter colors attempt to break free, suggesting connection. The broken pixels introduce imperfection and unpredictability, enhancing the emotional collision. Vertical flow signifies a rise amidst the chaos, visually narrating a struggle from darkness to light.
