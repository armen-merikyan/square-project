Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the warmth of safety and the brightness of exposure. The palette of soft greens and browns provides warmth and grounding, while pale tones contrast with deeper accents, creating a sense of tension. The main collision occurs at the center, where dense constellations of brown interact with static patches of pale skin tones, symbolizing the delicate interaction of protection versus exposure. These choices translate the complexity of the seed’s themes into an abstract narrative.
