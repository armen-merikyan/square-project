Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional conflict between defiance and submission through a palette of serene and urgent colors. The primary collision occurs at the center, where the vibrant, warm hues from the 'defiance' pattern clash with the cooler, muted shades from the 'submission' pattern. This reflects the seed's themes of strength and fragility, creating a textured interplay reminiscent of a flame restrained by a calm mist. The layout emphasizes a dim overall atmosphere with a concentrated area of brightness, symbolizing persistence against overwhelming forces.
