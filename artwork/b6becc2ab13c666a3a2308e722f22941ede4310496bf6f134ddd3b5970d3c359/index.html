Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional juxtaposition of vulnerability and strength through a palette of warm yellows and muted purples. The central area employs dense, rooted colors as a stronghold against the pale, exposed edges suggesting fragility. The main collision occurs in the center, where strength meets vulnerability, creating a dynamic tension that resonates with the seed's themes. Broken pixels introduce subtle disruptions that add to the feeling of imperfection and connection versus alienation.
