Artist Statement

The emotional tone suggests a complex interplay of longing and resolve. The palette, inspired by 'resilience_fragility', integrates moss green and rust to convey both softness and strength. The collision occurs in the center, where dense clusters of dark hues meet lighter shades, emphasizing the struggle between containment and overflow. This mirrors the seed's exploration of brokenness and the desire for repair, echoing a narrative of quiet urgency and introspection.
