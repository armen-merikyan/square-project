Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between restlessness and peace through color and pattern. The palette combines warm and cool tones, reflecting intimacy and distance, with intentional broken pixels added to represent disruption and memory. The main contrast occurs at central and edge points, where stark blacks meet muted rose clays, suggesting a collision of emotions. The layout mirrors on both sides but has intentional discrepancies to symbolize the chaotic interplay between settled peace and unsettled movement.
