Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between coherent signals and chaotic noise. The palette, featuring deep navy and dim cyan alongside stark blacks and bright whites, evokes a feeling of isolation and contrast. The primary collision occurs at the center, where the grid-like structure representing 'signal' interacts with the ripple pattern of 'noise', suggesting an ongoing struggle for clarity amidst chaos. The 'quiet channel' layout allows for visual breathing space, capturing the essence of a fragmented pulse trying to resonate in a dense environment.
