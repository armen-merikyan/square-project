Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between defiance and submission through a careful arrangement of contrasting colors and patterns. The choice of the 'serenity_urgency' palette introduces a calm aqua and pale gray as backbone colors, while alarm red injects bursts of urgency. The inner core shows a conflict pattern of islands representing defiance, while the outer border embodies submission through a soft ash field pattern. The main collision occurs in the center with the strong red accent cutting through the cool blues, representing the heart of conflict and resolution. The asymmetric arrangement maintains tension and interruption, channeling a narrative of ongoing struggle and balance.
