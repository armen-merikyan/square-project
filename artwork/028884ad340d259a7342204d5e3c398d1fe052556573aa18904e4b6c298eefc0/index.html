Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tone of 'longing' and 'resolve' through contrasting cool and warm colors. The palette features safety_exposure tones, favoring soft greens and deeper browns, manifesting a passage from warmth to distance. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the structured resolve meets fragmented longing, creating tension. This layout embodies the edge style of open corners, allowing the viewer's eye to meander and reflect on the threshold between emotions.
