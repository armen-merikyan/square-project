Artist Statement

The artwork encapsulates the tension between quietude and pressure, utilizing a palette that juxtaposes muted off-white, dust gray, and faint peach with the deep impact of compressed red and dark navy. The central collision occurs where compressed pixels, emblematic of pressure, clash against the soft gradients of negative space, embodying moments of quiet. This shattering against a tide of density creates an abstract narrative of movement, suggesting a journey through chaos towards clarity. The irregular border denotes a hard edge, while the scattered broken pixels add intentional disruption, reinforcing the thematic elements of contrast and emotional complexity, reflecting the duality of quiet and pressure.
