Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between clarity and confusion, using a palette of warm yellows and deep blues to create emotional contrasts. The center starts with a bright yellow, symbolizing clarity, while the edges gradually shift to darker hues, representing confusion and broken structures. The use of the flame pattern reflects a volatile movement, where the bright center seems to dissolve into darker, abstract chaos, symbolizing a struggle to maintain clarity amidst overwhelming noise. The layout flows from the corner, signifying an invasion of confusion into a clear space, and the presence of broken pixels introduces intentional imperfection.
