Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between safety and exposure, using a palette that balances warmth and coolness. The greens and browns suggest stability and protection, while the pale and blue tones evoke openness and vulnerability. The main contrast occurs centrally, where the dense, warm brown strands intertwine with the cool pale skin and open blue threads, symbolizing the tension between protection and exposure. Patterns collide, creating a sense of overlapping narratives that suggest both shelter and rawness throughout the square.
