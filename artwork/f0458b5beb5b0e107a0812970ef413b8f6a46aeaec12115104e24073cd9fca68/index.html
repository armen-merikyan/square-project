Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of loneliness and connection through a center-focused pattern. The palette, named 'broken_memory', comprises muted tans and grays evoking nostalgia and remoteness. This choice heightens the contrast with punctuated bursts of faded blue that symbolize fleeting connections amidst deep-seated isolation. The central hexagonal cluster embodies a feeling of enclosed space, while the crosshatch pattern evokes the noise and pressure surrounding emotional connections. The play of color promotes tension between hidden and exposed spaces, achieving a burst of emotional response as colors collide and echo across the grid.
