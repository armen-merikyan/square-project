Artist Statement

The artwork conveys the emotional tone of vulnerability and strength through a balance of contrasting elements. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of memory and fragility, while maintaining a grounded base. The layout features two pulses of color in the center that clash and interplay against a lightly textured background. This collision embodies the tension between safety and exposure. The broken pixels disrupt the flow, suggesting the imperfections in resilience, mirroring the fragility of strength amidst vulnerability.
