Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captures the duality between confusion and clarity. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green creates a tension where dark colors suggest chaos and confusion, while the softer hues indicate a longing for clarity. The contrast occurs predominantly in the center, where dense clusters of deep colors collide with lighter shades, illustrating the struggle between order and disorder. Each pixel represents an aspect of the seed's themes—broken symmetry reflects the idea of imperfection, while the arrangement and layering echo a pulse, reminiscent of emotional highs and lows.
