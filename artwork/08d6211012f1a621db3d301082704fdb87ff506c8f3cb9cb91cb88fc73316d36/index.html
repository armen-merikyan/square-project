Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between restlessness and peace. The palette of dark navy and hints of dim cyan represents the chaotic noise of memory and motion, while quiet whites engage as peaceful accents. The main contrast occurs at the center, where dense dark clusters meet lighter fields, symbolizing a struggle between intrusive thoughts and tranquil moments. Patterns of 'ladder' disrupt 'broken frame' elements, suggesting echoing memories amidst calm reflections, creating a dynamic visual dialogue without literal representation.
