Artist Statement

This piece embodies the struggle between defiance and submission through sharp contrasts of color and spatial dynamics. The warm shadow (#5d3a29) and warm gold (#b88a3e) create a sense of resistance at the center, suggesting defiance with their bold presence. The cool blue (#4a90e2) and distant lilac (#b7b4db) evoke submission, receding softly toward the edges, forming a boundary of quiet resilience. Broken pixels have been placed to emphasize imperfections, akin to the glitches mentioned in the seed, representing the unpredictable nature of conflict. The open corners reflect possibilities while the diagonals intensify the pulse of the piece, leading the eye across the canvas in a dynamic spiral flow.
