Artist Statement

This square explores the emotional tension between trust and suspicion using a palette that reflects the interplay of stability and chaos. The left side employs warm tones suggesting trust, represented in soft glow-like colors, while the right is filled with sharper, glitchy tones indicative of suspicion. The diagonal fall in color density suggests a subtle decline in certainty, with soft patterns subtly colliding against hard edges to create a sense of structured disarray. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, enhancing the abstract quality of resilience and fragility as per the seed's emotional landscape.
