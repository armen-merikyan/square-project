Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tone of confusion confronts clarity through a deliberate arrangement of colors reflecting the duality of structured chaos. The selected palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue suggests nostalgia and fragility, drawing on the theme of broken memories. Main contrasts appear in the central dark colors, breaking outward into lighter shades, representing a gravitational pull of clarity battling against encroaching confusion. The radial pattern clashing with the structural layout reflects the struggle between stability and disruption, creating an echoing tension in the artwork.
