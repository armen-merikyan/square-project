Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through a divided layout. The outer rows feature a muted sepia-toned palette representing confusion, while the center contrasts with vibrant dots suggesting clarity. The random placement of broken pixels disrupts symmetry, enhancing the theme of fragmentation. This combination evokes introspection and dynamic movement, symbolizing the pulse of emotions amidst chaos.
