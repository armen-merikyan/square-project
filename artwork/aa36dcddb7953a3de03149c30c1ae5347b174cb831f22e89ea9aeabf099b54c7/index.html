Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends the tension of pressure against the tranquility of quiet. Colors from the 'quiet_pressure' palette articulate this dynamic; darker tones indicate the overwhelming pressure while lighter shades suggest a space for relief. The collision occurs in the top left corner, representing a crowding of thoughts, with softer colors veiling the rest of the space, reflecting the aftermath of pressure. This arrangement creates an oscillation between a dense cluster and a dispersing calm, embodying the seed's narrative of conflict and resolution.
