Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional struggle between defiance and submission. The palette employs pale aqua and alarm red to create a dynamic tension, juxtaposing serenity and urgency. The main contrast occurs at the center, where the interplay of broken dots and embers creates a visual collision reflecting resistance and softness. The edges are intentionally darker, providing a weighted perimeter that contrasts with the lighter center. This arrangement mirrors the inward spiral concept, embodying a push-pull dynamic that channels internal conflict.
