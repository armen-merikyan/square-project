Artist Statement

The emotional tension between longing and resolve is represented through a palette of soft, contrasting colors which create a serene yet urgent atmosphere. The use of powder pink signifies warmth and vulnerability, while carbon black introduces severity and contrast. The collision occurs where the structured frame of resolve meets the fragmented echoes of longing, symbolizing the inherent conflict between stability and desire. Broken pixels subtly disrupt the flow, enhancing the sense of imperfection and leakage in the composition.
