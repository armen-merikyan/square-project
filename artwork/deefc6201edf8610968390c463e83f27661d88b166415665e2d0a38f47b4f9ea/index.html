Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional contrast between emptiness and desire, represented through warm and cool colors that clash. The palette consists of burnt orange and dark red for warmth, juxtaposed against charcoal for depth, evoking the starkness of longing. The layout mirrors on both sides with broken edges and scattered pixel placements, creating intentional chaos in the pursuit of completion. The primary collision occurs in the center where lighter dots of warmth meet darker cools, creating a sense of tension and unresolved yearning, symbolizing heart fragmentations. The spiral flow encourages the eye to navigate this emotional landscape, leading to a realization of yearning and solitude.
