Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic tension between softness and severity, as represented by the contrasting palette of warm white and sharp crimson against deep black and steel gray. The main collision occurs at the center of the grid, where the soft fault line pattern of pale colors brushes against the strict ring of darker hues, creating a visual push-pull effect. The seed inspires this layered interaction, suggesting themes of conflict and resolution through radial pulses echoing the spiral motion, integrating both quiet and dense areas effectively.
