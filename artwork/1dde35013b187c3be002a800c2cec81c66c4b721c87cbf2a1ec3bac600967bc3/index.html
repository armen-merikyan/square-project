Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the struggle between 'emptiness' and 'desire', captured by contrasting warm and cool palettes. The palette blends dark violet for depth, soft gold for aspiration, and a touch of pale green to initiate growth, evoking a sense of hope amidst gloom. The arrangement prioritizes upward movements on one side, counteracting downward tendencies on the other, creating a visual pulse. This dynamic layout encapsulates the argument between spectrums of feeling, emphasizing a collision of desire against the backdrop of emptiness rather than literal interpretation.
