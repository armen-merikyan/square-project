Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional contrast between pressure and quietude, reflected visually through a dense outer border and a soft, expansive inner core. The muted tones of off-white and faint peach signify moments of calm and clarity, while deeper shades of compressed red and dark navy suggest weight and intensity. The collision occurs where the dense border meets the lighter, more airy center, invoking a push-pull dynamic. The chosen palette embodies the struggle between these emotions, allowing for a narrative of tension and resolution while maintaining abstract integrity.
