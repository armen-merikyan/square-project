Artist Statement

This artwork contrasts the emotions of envy and gratitude, where dark colors represent the weight of envy, while softer shades reflect gratitude. The use of jagged edges and broken pixels creates a sense of imperfection, symbolizing the struggle between these emotions. Main contrasts occur between dense black areas and scattered blue hues, suggesting an inward pull yet an urge to push outward, conveying both tension and release.
