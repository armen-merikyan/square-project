Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension of 'awe' and 'terror' through a mixture of warm and dark colors. The palette, which is derived from 'ancient_fire', combines burnt orange and dark red to evoke intensity, set against the grounding charcoal and shimmering gold. The layout of falling pixels suggests a gentle descent that contrasts with the sudden dark bursts, reflecting the collisions of emotions. The negative spaces balance the density of the color, symbolizing moments of release amidst the pressure. The interplay of patterns—frame and grid—creates visual tension while suggesting a continuous narrative of conflicting feelings.
