Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress through a carefully chosen palette and contrasting patterns. The burnt orange and dark red evoke warmth and familiarity, signaling nostalgia, while the charcoal and gold introduce an element of timeless progress and aspiration. The horizontal split layout underscores a feeling of dichotomy—where one half is densely packed, symbolizing the weight of nostalgia, while the other is more open, representing the potential of progress. Broken pixels create a sense of imperfection, suggesting that both emotions are in flux. Main contrasts occur between the surrounding darker shades and the lighter core, establishing a visual echo of tension, grounding the viewer in an unresolved state of growth and recollection.
