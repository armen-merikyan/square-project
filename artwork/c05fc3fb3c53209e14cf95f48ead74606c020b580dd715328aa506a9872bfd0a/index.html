Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve. The palette of soft gold and deep wine contrasts with ash gray and warm ivory, creating layers of tension and release. The chaotic overlaps suggest longing, while clear borders assert resolve, embodying a fight for emotional control across the canvas. The arrangement favors density in the lower left, contrasting against a quiet upper right, symbolizing the push and pull of human emotion. Broken pixels introduce deliberate imperfections, hinting at the fragility of the themes addressed in the seed.
