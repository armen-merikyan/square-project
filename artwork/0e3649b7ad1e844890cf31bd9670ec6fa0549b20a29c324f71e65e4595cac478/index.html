Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension of 'longing' and 'resolve' through contrasting patterns of broken edges and structured cores. The chosen palette of pale aqua, mist gray, alarm red, ink blue, and clear white reflect a delicate balance between serenity and urgency. The main collision occurs in the center 4x4, where the crosshatch pattern of resolve meets the chaotic brokenness of longing. This duality creates a visual dialogue, amplifying feelings of tension, pulse, and vulnerability.
