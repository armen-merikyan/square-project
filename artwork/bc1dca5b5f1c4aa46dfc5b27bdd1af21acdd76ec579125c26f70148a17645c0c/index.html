Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between loneliness and connection, depicted through an asymmetrical layout that invites the viewer's eye to wander. The palette features warm yellows and oranges contrasting with muted purples and deep blues, symbolizing the warmth of connection against the coolness of isolation. The main collision occurs at the center, where the glow of warmth intermingles with a dense cluster of darker colors, suggesting a whimsical yet melancholic dance between hope and emptiness. The use of broken pixels creates imperfections, embodying the fragility of memory and emotional states. This abstract pattern captures the complexity of human emotions flowing from left to right, presenting a spiral of connection amidst spaces of solitude.
