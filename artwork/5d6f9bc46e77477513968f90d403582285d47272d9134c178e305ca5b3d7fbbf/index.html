Artist Statement

The emotional tone fluctuates between the starkness of control and the chaotic burst of panic. The palette of warm yellows and muted purples against deep blues evokes a sense of joy and grief, illustrating the struggle between these two states. The sharp contrast is evident in the radial patterns emanating from the center, representing control, while unexpected pixel bleed defines the edges, suggesting an encroaching chaos. The arrangement creates an echo of movement, oscillating between calm and pressure, where the structured core meets the fragmented outer spaces.
