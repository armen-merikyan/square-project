Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between defiance and submission, expressed through the palette of dark maroon, red, gray, and pink. The darker shades suggest a weight of submission while the bright pinks signal defiance. The pattern echoes a tide flowing inward, with more crowded pixels in the center (defiant) and a softer, quieter edge indicating submission. The primary collision occurs at the center, causing waves of tension reverberating outward, creating a nuanced balance between chaos and calm.
