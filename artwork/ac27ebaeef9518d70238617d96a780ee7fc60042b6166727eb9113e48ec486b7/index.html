Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the collision between pressure and quietude, illustrated through a palette featuring subdued sepia and gray tones contrasting against soft, washed-out blues. The dark and muted colors create dense clusters, suggesting the weight of emotion, while lighter hues intersperse, representing moments of release. The pattern flows outward as if peeling away layers, hinting at the rhythm of emotional release amidst tension. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfection, aligning with the theme of flawed human experiences and emotions.
