Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece juxtaposes softness and severity, representing a gentle touch against a backdrop of stark boundaries. The selected palette features powder pink and warm white symbolizing softness, while carbon black and sharp crimson embody severity. The main contrast emerges at the center, where soft glitch patterns collide with the rigid ladder structures, creating a dynamic push and pull. This arrangement captures the essence of the seed's spiral and pulse motifs, illustrating an inherent tension that is both inviting and foreboding.
