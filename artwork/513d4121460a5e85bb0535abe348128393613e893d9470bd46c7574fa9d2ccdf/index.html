Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between panic and control, captured through a contrasting palette of dark reds and burnt oranges that signify volatility against a charcoal backdrop representing stability. The layout is designed with an outer frame of deep colors to signify control, while the inner core adds chaotic spark patterns to illustrate panic. The jagged edges create visual disruption, aligning with the seed's themes of brokenness and the duality of being trapped inside versus outside. Emotional collision stems from the stark contrast of ordered and chaotic placements, suggesting the internal struggle of emotions vying for dominance.
