Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity, with a rising signal motif reflecting hope and upward movement against a backdrop of chaotic patterns. The color palette combines soft sky and pale mint for calmness and relief, contrasted by storm gray to indicate uncertainty and near black to represent depths of curiosity. The mix of echo motifs across the grid, with broken pixels creating intentional disruptions, highlights the interplay of order versus chaos, and the collision occurs centrally, creating a dynamic focal point. This causes a gentle ebb and flow of curiosity, signifying the object's cage and its potential for escape.
