Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between the sharpness of 'spark' and the muted essence of 'ash'. The chosen palette, including sepia and faded colors, reflects this duality of resonance and decay, portraying both resilience and fragility. The main collision occurs diagonally across the square, where brighter accents of 'spark' clash with the subdued hues of 'ash', creating a visual narrative of tension and balance. This interplay between the soft negative space and the pulsating color forms tells a story of struggle and recovery, embodying the seed's themes.
