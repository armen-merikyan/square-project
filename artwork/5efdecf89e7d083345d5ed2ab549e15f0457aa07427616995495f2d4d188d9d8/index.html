Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition is a battle between clarity and interference, using a palette of dark navy and dim cyan juxtaposed against whites to represent the sharpness of signals amidst noise. The contrast playfully collides at the center, where the bright white acts as a pulse trying to navigate through the chaos. The open gate layout allows for a sense of flow, suggesting that while the signals are clear, they are constantly interrupted by noise. Patterns of static and interruption are represented through a mix of broken pixels scattered throughout, creating an imperfect but rhythmic visual. The choice of colors and arrangement narrates a story of struggle and resilience within a cacophony.
