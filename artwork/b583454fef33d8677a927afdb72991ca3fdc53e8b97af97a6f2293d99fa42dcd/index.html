Artist Statement

The emotional tone of longing versus resolve is portrayed through an interplay of soft and firm colors. The palette, consisting of deep greens and warm browns, reflects the desire for safety contrasted with exposure. The primary collision occurs at the center where pulses of color disrupt the quiet channel created by the negative space. Broken pixels introduce a sense of repair amidst damage, forming an abstract landscape that represents the struggle between serenity and urgency. This composition utilizes varying intensities to give depth and evoke emotional resonance.
