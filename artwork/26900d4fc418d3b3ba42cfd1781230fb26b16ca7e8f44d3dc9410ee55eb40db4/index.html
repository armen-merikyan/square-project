Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through color and pattern. The left side reflects loneliness with muted greys and maroon to evoke isolation, using a crosshatch pattern to create a sense of distance and separation. In contrast, the right side bursts with vibrant pinks and reds, symbolizing connection, employing a noise pattern that conveys the chaotic energy of togetherness. The split vertical layout emphasizes the emotional divide, while a blend of broken pixels introduces unpredictability, representing imperfections in relationships. The central area showcases both emotions colliding, hinting at the complexity of moving between isolation and community.
