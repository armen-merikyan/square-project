Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between control and surrender, creating a rhythmic interplay. The palette, drawn from 'hunger_restraint', employs deep reds and muted creams to suggest emotional weight while evoking the struggle between containment and overflow. The central area, framed in darker hues, symbolizes the pressure of control, while the outer rings in lighter tones represent a gentle release. The collision occurs in the middle, where warm colors interlace with cooler tones, illustrating the dynamic conflict within. Broken pixels introduce intentional discrepancies, symbolizing imperfections and interruptions in the flow.
