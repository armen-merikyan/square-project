Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between joy and grief by balancing bright yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples. The left side is filled with a sparking pattern representing joy, while the right, marked by interrupted dot elements, reflects grief. The layout balances symmetrical order with strategic breaks to create emotional resonance. Color collisions emerge where warm yellows meet the cool depth of blues, suggesting a pulse of life amidst sorrow, creating a dynamic shift across the composition.
