Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between joy and grief through a visual dialogue of color and pattern. The palette mixes bright yellows and oranges to represent joy against deep blues and muted purples symbolizing grief. This contrast is accentuated by a checker pattern that emulates grief's heaviness, while bright crack-like bursts suggest moments of joy erupting through. The arrangement intentionally leaves negative space to signify the unresolved emotional distances, creating a visual argument between order and chaos. The burned edges imply a sense of damage or history, while the zigzag flow indicates a struggle between the competing emotions.
