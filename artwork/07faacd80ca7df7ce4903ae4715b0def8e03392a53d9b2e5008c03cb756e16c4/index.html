Artist Statement

This composition explores the push and pull between vulnerability and strength, represented through the contrasting textures of crosshatch and stepwell patterns. The palette of faded blues, sepia, and dust gray symbolizes fragility while the density of some colors and patterns signifies strength. The collision occurs primarily in the center where the strong, dense colors meet the exposed, lighter tones, creating a dynamic tension that resonates with the theme of connection and alienation.
