Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the conflict between stability and exploration, using a palette of clean white for clarity, ice blue for calmness, ink black for certainty, violet gray for ambiguity, and warning orange as an accent of curiosity. The main contrast occurs at the center where black and orange clash, representing the tension between order and chaos. The pattern is derived from the 'flame' of certainty merging with the 'grid' of curiosity, leaving structured negative space reflecting unresolved curiosity in the outer regions.
