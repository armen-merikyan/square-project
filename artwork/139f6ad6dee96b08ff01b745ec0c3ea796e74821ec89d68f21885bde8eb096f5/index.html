Artist Statement

This composition contrasts the gentle tones of softness with the hard lines of severity, creating a tension between the bright accents and muted fields. The palette, featuring powder pink and steel gray, evokes feelings of warmth and coolness alike, representing guilt and forgiveness in stark contrast. The main collision occurs in the center, where sharp crimson accents disrupt the soft pink and warm white, manifesting the emotional pull of the seed into a dynamic rhythm. The layout allows for open spaces representing quiet, with deliberate breaks letting the severity leak through.
