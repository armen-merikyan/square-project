Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and chaos, embraced in the composition as 'signal vs noise.' The palette blends deep navy and dim cyan to evoke feelings of solitude and tension. The contrast between the dense black fields representing noise and the bright, scattered white and cyan signals creates an interruption that invites contemplation. This collision creates a central pulse amid the chaos, shaped by the abstract qualities derived from the seed, emphasizing the theme of navigating through confusion.
