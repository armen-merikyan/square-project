Artist Statement

The artwork contrasts themes of control and surrender through a swirling spiral layout that suggests both pressure and release. The palette features warm yellows and oranges that symbolize contained emotion, while deep blues and muted purples evoke a surrendering atmosphere. The dominant color, a rich deep blue, creates a sense of depth and isolation in the center, which is interrupted by bursts of vibrant yellow, reflecting moments of overwhelming brightness amidst a tranquil background. Sparse usage of broken pixels creates intentional imperfections that echo the theme of interruption and conflict. The arrangement oscillates between densely packed lines and open spaces, embodying the dance of tension and relief.
