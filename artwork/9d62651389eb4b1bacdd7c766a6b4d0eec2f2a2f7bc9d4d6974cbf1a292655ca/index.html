Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional duality of pressure versus release, utilizing the palette of red, pink, gray, and dark maroon to evoke strong feelings. The sharp versus soft contrast creates a dynamic tension, particularly in the central arrows where darker shades collide with lighter tones. The mirrored layout with slight disruptions symbolizes the imperfect nature of emotional experiences, while negative space allows the viewer to engage with the open areas, reflecting the moments of release amid the chaos.
