Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission. The palette of electric blue, lime, magenta, and black creates a stark contrast, highlighting the conflict. The bright accent colors represent defiance while the darker tones suggest submission, forming a dynamic interplay across the canvas. The arrangement centers on a bright cluster with quieter areas surrounding it, creating a sense of pressure. The ring pattern hints at inward spirals and corners, unsettling yet inviting engagement.
