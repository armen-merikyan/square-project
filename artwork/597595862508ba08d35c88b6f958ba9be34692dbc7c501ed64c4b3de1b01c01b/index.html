Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve, represented through soft, muted colors and bold, striking accents. The chosen palette of deep blue and muted purple reflects a calm yet intense feeling, with yellow and orange offering bright bursts that symbolize resolve amidst a background of longing. The layout features a mirrored structure disrupted by broken pixels, illustrating the repair-damage dichotomy. The main collision between calm muted fields and energetic edges occurs at the center, creating a visual pulse that suggests a journey from serenity to urgency.
