Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'restlessness vs peace' guides the composition, expressing a delicate tension between calmness and chaos. The selected palette of greens for stability, browns for grounding, and pale tones for memory reflect a spectrum of emotional states, promoting visual contrast. The main collision occurs diagonally, where structured static patterns meet dynamic pulse marks, suggesting a struggle for stability amidst disruptive forces. The broken pixels punctuate the tranquility, resembling scars of experience, while negative space emphasizes moments of quiet reflection.
