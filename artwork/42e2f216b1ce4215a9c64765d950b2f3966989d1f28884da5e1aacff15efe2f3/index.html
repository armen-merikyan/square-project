Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of joy and grief by juxtaposing vibrant yellows and oranges against muted purples and deep blues. The left side, representing joy, uses a structured frame-like pattern to suggest expansion and brightness, while the right side, embodying grief, employs fragmented teeth-like shapes that impart a sense of heaviness and sorrow. The top-heavy edge style reinforces the flow from bottom to top, creating a sense of rising tension and unresolved feelings as colors collide,. This data-driven approach encapsulates the relentless struggle between order and chaos.
