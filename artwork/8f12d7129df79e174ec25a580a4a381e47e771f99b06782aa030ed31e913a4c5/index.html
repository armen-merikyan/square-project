Artist Statement

The composition plays on the emotional tension of isolation against the chaos of noise. The chosen palette of navy, black, single white, and dim cyan enhances the contrast between clear, readable patterns and chaotic interference, embodying the struggle between signal and noise. The main contrast occurs in the center, where bright dots represent the signal, interrupted by static-like patterns that flow towards the edges, creating a visual echo. The seed's essence is captured through contrasting colors and placements that suggest distance and isolation amid overwhelming echoes.
