Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between restlessness and peace, using a palette that suggests faded memories and subtle emotions. The dominant color is a muted sepia, symbolizing a quiet nostalgia, while the restless accents of dark gray and sporadic faint blue create a sense of agitation and interruption. The layout emphasizes a heavy edge of tumultuous colors, contrasting with a lighter core region, encouraging a visual cadence that conveys the push-pull between stillness and motion.
