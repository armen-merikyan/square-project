Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the tension between joy and grief, represented through a vibrant palette that oscillates between warm yellows and oranges (joy) and muted blues and purples (grief). The rising signal layout suggests hope and ascendance, while the crack pattern of grief contrasts sharply with the circular motion of joy. Broken pixels disrupt the pattern, embodying the complexity of emotion, while negative space allows for breath and reflection in the chaos of colored clusters, creating an immersive emotional landscape.
