Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of spark and ash, representing resilience against fragility. The palette of deep blues represents deep emotional currents, while pale yellows mimic fleeting moments of brightness. The diagonal arrangement creates movement across the square, depicting the tension between hunger and restraint. Jagged edges disrupt symmetry, highlighting imperfections inherent in memory and experience. The collision of colors in the center portrays a moment of conflict, representing life's unpredictable nature.
