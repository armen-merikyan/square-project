Artist Statement

The piece illustrates the emotional tug between longing and resolve, utilizing the 'broken_frame' layout to visually contain tensions while leaving room for expression. The palette, inspired by safety and exposure, balances warmth (warm brown and shelter green) against coolness (cold white and open blue), reflecting feelings of comfort against urgency. The crosshatch and pressure band patterns intermingle, weaving a narrative of continuity interrupted by intention, particularly evident in the mixed placement of dark and light tones, where blue pulses against green's softness. The main collision is central, symbolizing the intersection of conflict and harmony, representing the struggle to mend while still acknowledging damage.
