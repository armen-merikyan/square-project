Artist Statement

This composition expresses the tension between defiance and submission through color and pattern. The palette of yellow and muted purple reflects the clash of warmth and serenity. Bright yellow accents emerge as echoes of defiance amidst a sea of deep blues, allowing viewers to sense a quiet chaos within the strokes. The main collision point is in the center, where angular 'ladder' patterns of defiance intersect with the soft, circular 'ring' shapes representing submission. Broken pixels are scattered throughout, creating a sense of disruption and imperfection, fitting the underlying theme of chaos versus order.
