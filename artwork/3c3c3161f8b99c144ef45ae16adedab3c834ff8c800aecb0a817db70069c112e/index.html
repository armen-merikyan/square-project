Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a delicate balance between safety and exposure, with an open center surrounded by contrasting deep colors representing protection. The palette combines warm browns to signify softness and pale skin tones to evoke vulnerability, contrasted with cool whites hinting at exposure. The collision occurs vertically, with a defined shift of open blue wrapping around softer tones, symbolizing the push and pull between shelter and exposure. Broken pixels introduce subtle disruptions, enhancing the theme of vulnerability. The layering effect creates visual depth, suggesting varying emotional strata beneath a composed surface.
