Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between safety and exposure, utilizing a palette that evokes both warmth and coldness. The contrasting colors of 'shelter_green' and 'cold_white' create a layered feel of protection against vulnerability. The central arrangement heightens the feeling of collision, with the flow of pixels rising, echoing a sense of aspiration. Shadows and light intertwine, where warm browns subtly pulse against cooler tones, creating an interplay of pressure and release. The addition of broken pixels simulates hidden scars of exposure and imperfection, enhancing the narrative of resilience within exposure.
