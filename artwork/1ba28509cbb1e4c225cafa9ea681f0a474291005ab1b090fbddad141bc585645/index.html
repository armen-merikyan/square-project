Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrast of vulnerability and strength through the interplay of colors. The dark violet tones represent strength, serving as a dense base that anchors the composition, while soft gold hues signify vulnerability, introducing a delicate, shimmering quality. The broken pixels disrupt the evenness, symbolizing the imperfections and fragility inherent in strength. The layout spirals toward the center, creating a visual tension that echoes the theme of conflict between safety and exposure. The color palette, named 'fear_hope', amplifies this dialogue between resilience and fragility, where darker colors dominate the edges, creating a sense of encirclement around a softer, luminous core.
