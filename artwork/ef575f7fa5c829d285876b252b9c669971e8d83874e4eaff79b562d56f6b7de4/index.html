Artist Statement

The emotional tone leans towards tension between certainty and curiosity. The color palette of ripe greens and honey gold symbolizes growth and warmth, while deep olive introduces a sense of calm amidst chaos. The layout is dominated by a central dense feeling that contrasts with lighter edges, suggesting a struggle between structured order and organic flow. The use of broken pixels creates intentional disruptions that illustrate the emotional fluctuation inherent in curiosity against certainty. The primary collision occurs centrally, where dense colors command attention, while lighter ones retreat, echoing the seed's narrative of interrogation and rebirth.
