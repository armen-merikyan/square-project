Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that suggests mourning yet also renewal. The darker colors represent fortitude and deep-seated emotions, while the lighter hues introduce a sense of exposure and fragility. The main collision occurs in the center, where the dense colors push against the softer, lighter elements, symbolizing the struggle and interplay between protection and openness. The layout follows a pattern of scattered repair, indicating that even amid brokenness, there are attempts to seek healing and brightness.
