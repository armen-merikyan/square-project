Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting colors and patterns. The dark hues represent suspicion, forming a boundary that is interrupted by brighter, softer tones symbolizing trust, creating 'pulse marks' amid the static. The layout mirrors vertically, yet imperfections add depth, creating echoes of both feelings. The soft gold against darker shades presents a glow of hope amidst uncertainty.
