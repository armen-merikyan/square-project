Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between restlessness and peace, using a palette that blends both warm and cool tones. The palette emphasizes intimacy (rose clay and warm shadow) alongside distant depths (cool blue and distant lilac), presenting a narrative of compression against expanded space. The collision occurs primarily at the center, where a radial pattern representing restlessness interacts with the structured grid of peace, forming a compelling visual tension. The use of broken pixels represents fragmented memories within the silent calm, urging an abstract exploration of emotional duality.
