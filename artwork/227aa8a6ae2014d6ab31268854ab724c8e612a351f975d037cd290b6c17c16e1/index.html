Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between envy and gratitude, expressed through the contrasting palette of deep navy and muted black. The soft edges create a feeling of fluidity while marking the transitional spaces, embodying the tension of rising feelings against falling uncertainty. The predominant dark colors suggest an enveloping weight, while occasional lighter tones punctuate the piece, symbolizing fleeting moments of gratitude amidst overwhelming emotions. The composition embodies a nearly symmetrical layout, disrupted by intentionally misplaced pixels, reinforcing the core seed themes of conflict and emotional collision.
