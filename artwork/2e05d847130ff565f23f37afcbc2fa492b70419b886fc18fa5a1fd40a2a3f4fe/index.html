Artist Statement

This composition evokes an emotional tension between control and surrender, featuring a muted color palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue, symbolizing a nostalgic yet conflicted memory. The pattern reflects control through a lattice structure on one side that exudes calmness, while the other side shows a pressure band with irregular placements invoking surrender. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense layers intersect with negative space, illustrating the push and pull of these opposing forces.
