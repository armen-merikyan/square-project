Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension of 'quiet vs pressure' by contrasting dense, crowded segments with soft, muted fields. The selected palette utilizes 'quiet_pressure' colors, featuring compressed red and dark navy as the pressure element, while off-white and faint peach introduce a calming influence. The main collision occurs diagonally across the square, where the pressure is concentrated in the top-left corner, loosening gradually as it flows towards the bottom-right. This dynamic creates a visual narrative that echoes the seed's themes of conflict and resolution, while remaining abstract and avoiding literal interpretation.
