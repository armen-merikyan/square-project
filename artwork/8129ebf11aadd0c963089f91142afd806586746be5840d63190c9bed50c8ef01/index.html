Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between 'quiet' and 'pressure,' using an off-white field to represent quietude and a ring of dark navy to convey pressure. The palette's soft colors are interwoven with irregular accents of compressed red to create visual collisions. The main contrast occurs at the center, where light and dark intermingle, suggesting a shattering of calm. This interplay of color embodies the emotional duality from the seed, weaving a narrative of clarity amidst confusion and structured disorder.
