Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts joy and grief, represented through the palette of warm yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples. The use of thick outlines emphasizes structural tension, while the split-horizontal layout represents the duality of emotions. Joy is depicted through the 'target' pattern, creating spark-like bursts of color, while grief emerges as cracks, visually creating ruptures in the otherwise serene flow, echoing the concept of waves and interruptions. The presence of negative space encourages reflection amidst the dense clusters, suggesting both isolation and connection.
