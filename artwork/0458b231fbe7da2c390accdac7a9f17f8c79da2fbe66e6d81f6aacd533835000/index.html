Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a push and pull between the vibrant craving of desire and the passive silence of emptiness. The palette contrasts the warmth of saturated hues like 'hot_coral' and 'blood_orange' representing desire, against muted 'charcoal' and 'hollow_blue' embodying emptiness. Main collisions occur along the center where color densities collide, emphasizing the theme of restrained desire pushed against the edges of quietness. The layout suggests a mirroring effect that falters at a few broken pixels, symbolizing the imperfection in the emotional struggle.
