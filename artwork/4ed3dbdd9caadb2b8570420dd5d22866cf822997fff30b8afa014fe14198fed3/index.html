Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition reflects the dynamic interplay of vulnerability and strength, where the coexistence of exposed and dense elements creates a palpable tension. The selected palette includes vibrant hot coral and blood orange, representing strength, contrasted with muted charcoal and hollow blue, symbolizing vulnerability. The central collision occurs with a dense crosshatch pattern at the center, surrounded by shattering fragments along the edges, encapsulating the struggle between stability and disruption. The composition conveys a narrative of pressure and release, teetering between resilience and fragility.
