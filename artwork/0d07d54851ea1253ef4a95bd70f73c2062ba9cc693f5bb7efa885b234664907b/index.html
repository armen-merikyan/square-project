Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between restlessness and peace through contrasting patterns and systematically chosen colors. The palette combines deep wine and ash gray to echo a sense of heaviness intertwined with warmth, suggesting both a weight and a gentle warmth. The interaction of glitch-like accents in a stripe format assembles a clear diagonal argument within an open gate layout, creating an energetic burst that disrupts stillness, sustaining an emotional landscape of oscillation between calm and chaos. The broken pixel elements insinuate memories that juxtapose smooth transitions, capturing the essence of motion against the structured calm.
