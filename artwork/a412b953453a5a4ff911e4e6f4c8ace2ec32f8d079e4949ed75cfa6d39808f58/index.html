Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve, utilizing a palette rich in muted warm tones of rose clay and warm shadow juxtaposed with cool accents from distant lilac and lamp gold. The flow is directed inward, with longing represented by expansive frame patterns creating a sense of distance and desire while resolve intrudes through dense pulse marks, reflecting a determined intensity. The collision occurs prominently at the center, where the patterns overlap, suggesting a conflict between serenity and urgency. Broken pixels disrupt the grid, hinting at repairs and imperfections, embodying the theme of damage versus restoration. This creates a visual narrative of struggle, hope, and emotional depth within the structured chaos.
