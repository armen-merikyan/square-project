Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece balances longing, with its hints of unfinished warmth and desire, against the steadfast, compressed strength of resolve. The palette, named 'lonely_signal', consists of dark colors enhancing a sense of depth and intimacy, crafted with navy and black contrasted by a vibrant single white and a dim cyan that serves as a quiet field. The key collision occurs in the center, where dense colors build a strong presence, interrupted by the halo-like white border, representing a struggle at the edges. This aligns with the seed's theme of tension between chaos and order, creating a dynamic flow through the arrangement of colors and patterns that embody the feelings of distance and intimacy. The asymmetry of the shape elements and deliberate placements of broken pixels create a sense of disruption, enhancing the tension and movement across the grid.
