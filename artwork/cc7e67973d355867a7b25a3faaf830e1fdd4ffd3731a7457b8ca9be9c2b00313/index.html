Artist Statement

The composition reflects the emotional conflict between clarity and chaos, embodying the tension of 'signal vs noise.' The palette is dark, with navy and dim cyan representing depth and interference, punctuated by stark black and white for contrast. The layout features dominant spaces of emptiness representing chaos, with a central pulse of color that symbolizes clarity. This collision of control and disorder is visually manifested in asymmetric patterns disrupting the quieter fields.
