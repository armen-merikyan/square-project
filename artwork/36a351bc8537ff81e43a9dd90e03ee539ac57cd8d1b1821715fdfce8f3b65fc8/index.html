Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The selected palette of warm pinks and deep maroon represents love and betrayal, offering a poignant contrast. The thick outline establishes a boundary, enhancing the idea of broken symmetry and chaos versus calm. The layout, with colors cascading down, evokes falling pixels, suggesting a descent into confusion, while the structured pattern gives a sense of clarity but is disrupted by intentional imperfections. The primary contrast occurs in the darker hues piercing through lighter colors, creating a visual tension that mirrors the emotional state described in the seed.
