Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through contrasting colors and patterns. The use of deep reds and grays reflects the tumultuous nature of confusion, while the lighter tones suggest clarity. The central pattern radiates outward, creating a visual push away from a solid core, portraying a struggle between the dense presence of confusion and the desired clarity. The 'broken pixels' introduce elements of chaos, while the overall composition adheres to the 'empty room' layout, ensuring negative space evokes a sense of calm amidst the tumult.
