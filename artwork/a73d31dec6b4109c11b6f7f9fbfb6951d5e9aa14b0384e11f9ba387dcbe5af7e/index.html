Artist Statement

This artwork contrasts the emotional intensities of love and betrayal, using a palette of warm pink and red against the coolness of gray and dark maroon. The outer border in dark maroon represents betrayal with its cracked, broken edges, while the warm pinks in the center signal love's vibrancy. The placements reveal a narrative of tension and harmony, with the collision of colors creating echoes of emotional discovery. The intentionally broken pixels contribute imperfections, mimicking the disorder in relationships.
