Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette reflects warmth and coolness, intertwining yellow and muted purple to symbolize yearning interlaced with strength. The collision occurs primarily in the center where vibrant hues clash against softer tones, portraying the pull of unresolved emotions. The composition employs broken pixels to mimic disruptions in the emotional flow, while the contrasting patterns of flame and noise evoke a tension between aspiration and reality, depicting the intricate dance between hope and despair.
