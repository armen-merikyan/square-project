Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends the warmth of love with the chilling undercurrent of betrayal. The palette of soft pinks plays against deep maroons, echoing the tension between affection and heartbreak. The contrasting patterns—'teeth' of love and 'spark' of betrayal—create a dissonance that manifests in irregular pixel placement, suggesting a mirrored image broken by few deliberate disruptions. The layout achieves a visual dialogue between symmetry and chaos, asserting a conflict within the abstract form.
