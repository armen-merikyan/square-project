Artist Statement

This artwork evokes the emotional collision between emptiness and desire through a palette of pinks and dark maroon, highlighting contrast and pressure. The jagged edges create an unsettling boundary, while the upward and downward movement of colors suggests a spiraling conflict. Areas of density alternate with quiet spaces, creating a rhythm that mirrors the emotional tension inherent in the seed's themes, leading to unresolved complexity.
