Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional duality of trust and suspicion through a stark color palette. The contrasting dark tones signify suspicion while the warmer, lighter hues hint at trust. The main collision occurs in the center with spiral patterns disintegrating towards the corners, reflecting the ongoing battle between these emotions. Splotches of broken pixels add intentional disruptions, symbolizing the uncertainties in relationships and perceptions shaped by the seed.
