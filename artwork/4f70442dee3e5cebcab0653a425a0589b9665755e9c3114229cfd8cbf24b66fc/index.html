Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between panic and control, suggesting an underlying tension. The palette, which includes varying shades of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon, evokes themes of love and betrayal while maintaining a delicate emotional spectrum. The predominant contrast arises between the softer pinks and harsher dark maroons, representing stability meeting instability. The outer border, utilizing a frame pattern, embodies control, whereas the inner section uses dot patterns to evoke the chaos of panic. This duality creates a visual orbit, highlighting interplay amid contrasting emotions and amplifying the concepts of confinement and freedom.
