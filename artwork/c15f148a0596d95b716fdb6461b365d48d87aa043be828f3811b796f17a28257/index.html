Artist Statement

This artwork captures the duality of joy and grief through its contrasting palette of vibrant oranges and muted blues, creating a dynamic tension. The top half embodies joy with bright, spark-like orange hues, while the bottom embraces grief with deeper blue tones, echoing a sense of heaviness. The misplaced pixel in the transmission creates a beautiful disturbance—symbolizing imperfection and the nuanced human experience. The palette was chosen to reflect warmth and coolness, allowing for a powerful emotional narrative. The central design shifts from bright warmth to cool depth, visually representing the conflict between joy and grief.
