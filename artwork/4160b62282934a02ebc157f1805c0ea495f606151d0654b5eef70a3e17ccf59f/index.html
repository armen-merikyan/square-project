Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the tension and interplay between love and betrayal. The chosen palette of warm pinks and reds contrasts sharply against deep grays and dark maroons, symbolizing warmth clashing with darkness. The main contrast arises diagonally, where vibrant colors collide with muted tones, illustrating the 'rising signal' and 'diagonal fall' concepts from the seed. Each color placement mirrors emotional fragmentation, with broken pixel placements suggesting imperfection and chaos within structured patterns.
