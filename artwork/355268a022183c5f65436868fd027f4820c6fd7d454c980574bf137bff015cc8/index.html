Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional tones of loneliness and connection through jagged edges and contrasting patterns. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon reflects conflicting feelings of love and betrayal, creating visual tension. The layout, starting from a corner and spreading diagonally, emphasizes the collision of these emotions. Key contrast occurs between dense areas of dark maroon and isolated pixels of pink, evoking a sense of pressure and release. This abstract pattern represents the complexity of feelings without literal symbolism, suggesting a story of grappling with emotional intensity.
