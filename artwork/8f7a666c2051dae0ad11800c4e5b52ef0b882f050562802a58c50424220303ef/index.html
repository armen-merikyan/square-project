Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional struggle between longing and resolve, using contrasting patterns that collide and intertwine. The palette of sepia and faded blue suggests a nostalgia, while the quiet gray allows for moments of reflection amid the broken dots pattern of resolve. The dense assembly of dark colors punctuates the longing, allowing for a central collision of lighter tones, representing moments of clarity and strength in the midst of chaos. Overall, the pixel arrangement echoes the themes of tension and searching amidst disorder.
