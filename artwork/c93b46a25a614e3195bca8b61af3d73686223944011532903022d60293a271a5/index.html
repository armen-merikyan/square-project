Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a balance of distinct color fields. The palette blends warm and cool tones, embodying intimacy against distance. The layout features scattered repairs, with brighter accents interrupting darker lattice patterns to signify unresolved feelings. The main collision occurs in the center, where darker hues meet with bursts of light, reflecting a push-pull dynamic between chaos and order, density and emptiness.
