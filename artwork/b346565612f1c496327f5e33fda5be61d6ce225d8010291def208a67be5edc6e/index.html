Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between defiance and submission, relying on a palette that shifts from warm tawny hues to deep, muted colors. The dominant colors create a visual weight complemented by lighter accents, reflecting a struggle between chaos and order. The collision occurs centrally with strong crosshatch patterns asserting defiance against softer lattice layouts, capturing the intricate interplay of resistance and yielding. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions along the edges, creating a sense of imperfection and tension.
