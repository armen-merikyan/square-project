Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the vivid sparks of creativity against the lingering ash of remnants, suggesting a dynamic tension. The palette of burnt orange and gold captures the warmth of sparks, while dark red and charcoal embody the desolation of ash. The main contrast is found in a bright cluster on one edge, symbolizing resilience amidst the pervasive quiet of ash, creating a visual conversation of survival. The diagonal flow enhances the sense of movement and tension, mirroring internal struggles.
