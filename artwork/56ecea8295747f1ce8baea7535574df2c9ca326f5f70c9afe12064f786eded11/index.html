Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between quiet and pressure through contrasting densities. Using a palette of off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach provides a subtle yet striking balance. The quiet fields of faint peach and off-white create a soft horizon, while the dark navy and compressed red provide the pressing density that collides at the center, representing the thrust of the emotional narrative. The diagonal flow from the top-left to the bottom-right introduces an argument between calm and tension, while the pattern alternates between quiet fields and cracks that represent pressure. This interplay captures the essence of symmetry vs. break, reinforcing the central theme of the seed.
