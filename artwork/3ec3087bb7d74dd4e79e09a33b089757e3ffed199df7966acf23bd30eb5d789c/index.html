Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between restlessness and peace through a balance of color density and spatial arrangement. The palette of shelter green, warm brown, and cold white creates a landscape of cool tranquility contrasted with the lively warmth of warm brown, suggesting moments of frenetic energy amidst a calm background. The glitch pattern introduces disruptions signifying restlessness, while the grid gives a sense of structure that hints at peace. The collision occurs in the center where chaos meets calm, inviting the viewer to feel the pull between these opposing emotions.
