Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between clarity and confusion through a balanced color palette of deep black and dark violet contrasted with soft gold and pale green. The palette evokes fear and hope, suggesting a fight between chaos and order. The main contrast occurs in the central region, where the checker pattern symbolizes confusion, while broken dots around the edge hint at clarity. The chaos of the mixed checker pattern collides with the structured flow of the broken dots, representing a heartbeat in an unstable state, hinting at the conflicting emotions defined by the seed.
