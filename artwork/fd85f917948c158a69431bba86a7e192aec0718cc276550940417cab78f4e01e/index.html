Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotions between nostalgia and progress through contrasting patterns and colors. The upper half features mirrored soft blues, evoking a sense of faded memories, while the lower half employs sharp stripes of deeper tones, signaling the push of advancement. The interplay between full and hollow spaces encapsulates the weight of nostalgia against the backdrop of an advancing future. Broken pixels are strategically placed to introduce chaos—a representation of imperfections in both the past and future.
