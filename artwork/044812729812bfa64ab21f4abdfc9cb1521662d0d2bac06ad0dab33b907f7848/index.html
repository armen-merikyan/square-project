Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between confusion and clarity, evoking a sense of chaotic harmony. The palette consists of bold reds and stark blacks, interspersed with pale blues and whites to create contrast and a pulse-like rhythm. The placement of colors emphasizes collision: deep blacks crash against vibrant reds, reflecting internal conflict, while the soft blues offer moments of calm clarity. The arrangement disrupts typical symmetry, allowing for a sense of fragmented balance, echoing the seed's themes of confusion versus clarity. The broken pixels add imperfections that symbolize the emotional chaos amidst the search for understanding.
