Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, represented through a palette of soft and muted tones (bone white, stone gray) contrasting with warmer, stronger shades (rust and small gold). The central area captures light, symbolizing the spark of strength, while the surrounding darker tones reflect vulnerability. The pattern varies between clusters of dense dots and scattered constellations, representing the collision of protection and exposure. Broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, echoing the real-life imperfections in these emotions.
