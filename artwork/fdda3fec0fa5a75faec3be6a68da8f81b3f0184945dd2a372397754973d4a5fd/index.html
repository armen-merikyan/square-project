Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the dichotomy between clarity and confusion, which is reflected through a palette of vibrant burnt orange, deep charcoal, and sharp dark red. This juxtaposition creates a strong emotional tension and a narrative of conflict. The layout is established around an argument grid, where crosshatch patterns of confusion collide against targeted bursts of clarity. The choice of hard edges amplifies the sense of collision, while broken pixels strategically disrupt symmetry, embodying moments of emotional disturbance. The primary clash occurs at the center, where warmth meets darkness, creating an unresolved interplay that invites reflection.
