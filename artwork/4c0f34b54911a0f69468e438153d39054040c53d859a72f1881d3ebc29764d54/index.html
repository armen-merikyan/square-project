Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between strength and vulnerability, suggesting a dynamic interplay of solid forms and delicate spaces. The palette, featuring soft sky and faded gold against storm gray and near-black, embodies the contrast of lightness and depth, mirroring the delicate balance between protection and exposure. The main collision occurs in the center, where static patterns of strength interrupt the quiet fields of vulnerability, while broken pixels introduce organic disruptions, creating scars that resonate with the theme of a mirror broken. This tension between the two emotional realms is visually articulated through layered color placements and strategically maintained negative space, allowing for a resonant exploration of the emotional seed.
