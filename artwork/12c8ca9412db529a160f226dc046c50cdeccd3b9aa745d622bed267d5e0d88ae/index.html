Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between panic and control, visualized through a stark contrast of deep, muted base colors against bright, unstable sparks. The palette of sepia, tan, and faded blue evokes a nostalgic yet unsettling atmosphere, echoing feelings of emptiness and conflict. The pattern features dense dark colors that represent weight and control, with lighter pixels that symbolize brief moments of hope or panic. The arrangement suggests a wave-like flow, where bright elements interrupt the dense canvas, embodying a struggle for equilibrium. This design captures the essence of a heavy backdrop punctuated by fleeting yet vibrant interruptions, representing the inner turmoil of love, betrayal, and the pull between stability and chaos.
