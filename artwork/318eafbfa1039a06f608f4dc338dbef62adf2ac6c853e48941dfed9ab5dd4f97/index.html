Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the tension between loneliness and connection, represented through the contrast of warm and cool colors. The palette of yellows and blues evokes feelings of joy intertwined with grief. The central pixel arrangement suggests a burst of warmth amidst a cool exterior, illustrating the struggle for connection within isolation. The 'mirror_broken' layout creates a visual echo, enhancing the idea of hidden vs. exposed. Asymmetry adds to the emotional complexity, with bursts of orange and yellow against a muted backdrop, symbolizing hope and warmth in contrast to pervasive loneliness.
