Artist Statement

The composition explores the emotional tension between joy and grief, represented by warm and cool colors. The palette blends vibrant yellows and deep blues, demonstrating the collision of brightness and low tones. The pattern moves upward on one side, while the other is left sparse, suggesting a dynamic flow of rising joy against the backdrop of underlying grief. The broken pixel elements introduce imperfection, reflecting the rawness of these emotions. Aspects of hidden vs. exposed are subtly indicated through the arrangement, with some colors interspersed more densely than others, creating an abstract wave of feelings that evokes both memory and present experience.
