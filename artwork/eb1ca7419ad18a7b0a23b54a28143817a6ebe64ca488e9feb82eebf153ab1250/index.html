Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between restlessness and peace, using a palette that conveys both serenity and urgency. The colors transition from pale aqua and mist gray representing calm, to alarm red and ink blue suggesting a dynamic shift. The central collision occurs at the diagonal seam where the ripple pattern of restlessness interrupts the structured crack of peace, creating a visual dialogue of contrasting emotions. The palette choice supports the narrative of juxtaposition, with muted fields giving space for the vibrant accents to resonate.
