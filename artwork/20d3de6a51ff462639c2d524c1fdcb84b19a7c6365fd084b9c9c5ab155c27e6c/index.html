Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tug between fear and hope through contrasting colors and placements. The darker elements symbolize fear, presenting jagged patterns and dense clusters that evoke anxiety, while the soft gold and pale green represent hope, suggesting light and upward movement amidst the chaotic backdrop. The vertical flow mimics descending rain of emotions, with broken pixels introducing imperfection that reflects our human experience. The layered complexity of dark hues against bright accents creates a collision of feelings, where hope peeks through moments of despair, echoing the seed's exploration of tension and resolution.
