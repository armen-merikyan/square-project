Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a push and pull between longing and resolve, embodying a sense of distant warmth coupled with clear boundaries. The palette of guilt and forgiveness, featuring warm ivory and deep wine, suggests a narrative of healing versus damage. Main contrast occurs in the center, where soft gold disrupts ash gray fields. The shapes and textures, influenced by the seed, create an interruption of memories—glitch against calm—symbolizing the complexity of emotions in a minimalist arrangement.
