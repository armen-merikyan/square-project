Artist Statement

The emotional tone is driven by the contrast between the muted earth tones and the soft, cool hues, suggesting a dance between desire and emptiness. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue embodies a recollection of fleeting memories. The main contrast occurs between the darker tones that represent pressure and the lighter hues suggesting quiet release. The layout captures a corner invasion method, with the central 'window' shape subtly implied through color transitions, creating tension and rhythm that mirrors emotional collisions.
