Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay between awe and terror, with a focus on quiet moments surrounded by bursts of pressure. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes both fear and hope. The primary contrast occurs along the boundaries between muted and bright colors, amplifying the sense of collision. The vertical rising layout signifies hope breaking through the weight of emotion, while broken pixels disrupt the flow, suggesting imperfection and chaos amid an otherwise structured pattern.
