Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines the brightness of 'spark' and the dullness of 'ash.' The palette of melancholy tones—rain blue, lavender gray, moon white, soft cyan, and muted plum—represents duality, with 'rain blue' and 'moon white' forming the brighter fields to evoke sparks and moments of clarity against the ashen, muted hues. The main contrast manifests in the center, where vivid forms collide with softer, gray patterns, visually conveying tension between vibrancy and calm. The arrangement embodies conflict and resolution through jagged edges and flowing gradients, aligning with the seed's thematic struggles.
