Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the struggle between defiance and submission through its juxtaposed color patterns. The dark hues represent the unyielding center, while the softer tones depict the encroaching edges of submission. The palette (black, dark violet, soft gold, pale green) creates a tension between hope and fear, echoing the emotional weight of the seed. The central area collides with the softer borders, creating an asymmetrical balance that visually narrates a journey from quiet retreat to bold emergence, symbolizing a threshold of emotional states.
