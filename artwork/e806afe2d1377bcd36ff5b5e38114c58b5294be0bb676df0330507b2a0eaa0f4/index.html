Artist Statement

This artwork juxtaposes feelings of loneliness and connection through the contrast of warm and cool colors. The central area radiates warmth (orange and yellow) to symbolize connection, while the edges fade into muted purples and deep blues suggesting isolation. The pattern is designed with a crosshatch effect, drawing the eye inward while subtle breaks in the pattern evoke a sense of imperfection, symbolizing the struggle between connection and isolation. The layout features a mirror effect with slight inaccuracies, enhancing the emotional tension between proximity and distance.
