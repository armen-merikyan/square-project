Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between peace and restlessness, represented by a muted palette of sepia and dusty blues to invoke a sense of nostalgia and memory. The contrast is manifested in a diagonal disruption of bright accents against subdued fields, with the main collision at the center forming a dynamic tension that embodies both motion and stillness. The incorporation of broken pixels enhances the sense of imperfection, reflecting the chaos within calmness and the struggle against stillness.
