Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the duality of 'spark vs ash,' contrasting hot acuteness against the subdued remnants of loss. The emotional tone is defined by quick bursts of lime (#00FF00) and electric blue (#00FFFF) against the backdrop of black (#000000) and soft gray (#888888). The central collision occurs at the vertical split, where vibrant colors clash with muted fields, illustrating hunger and restraint. The patterns mimic latticework on one side, suggesting structure and energy, while threshold patterns on the other evoke the quiet weight of memories. The overall composition maintains a sense of fragmented continuity, expressing resilience in the face of fragility.
