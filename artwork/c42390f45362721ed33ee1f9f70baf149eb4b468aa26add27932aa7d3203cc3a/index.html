Artist Statement

This piece explores the duality of vulnerability and strength through a contrasting palette. The soft break of the pale yellow and cream colors depict exposure, while the deeper greens work as a protective layer. The diagonal layout emphasizes the tension between these emotions, with the pale tones pushing into the deeper hues, symbolizing ongoing conflict or resistance. The pattern of cracks represents vulnerability while the weighty forms suggest strength. This composition blends feelings of safety and exposure, creating an emotional interplay that mirrors the complexities of human experience.
