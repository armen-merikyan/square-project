Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a delicate negotiation between softness and severity, depicted through the contrasting presence of shadows and light. The palette combines warm whites and sharp crimson to evoke feelings of warmth clashing against coldness, where the powder pinks subtly interrupt the stark blacks. The main contrast occurs centrally, with density in the middle suggesting a pulse of energy against a quieter background, characteristic of an island in a vast sea. The 'scar' shape seed influences this pattern by creating interruptions, reflecting both the gentleness of forgiveness and the hardness of guilt.
