Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a deliberate juxtaposition of pressure and quietness, manifested through a dense center that gradually releases toward the edges. The palette combines dark navy and compressed red to symbolize pressure, while off-white and faint peach offer moments of quiet calm. The center serves as the focal point of dense energy, while subtle shifts in color around the border create a sense of ebbing tension. This conveys a story of confrontation between chaos and solace, led by the abstract metaphor of a 'ladder' which suggests ascent amidst struggle.
