Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the duality between vulnerability and strength by juxtaposing dense, rich colors with softer, muted tones. The palette consists of deep wine, warm ivory, and ash gray, suggesting a narrative of emotional depth and subtle complexity. The central area is dominated by saturated colors representing strength, encased by lighter hues illustrating vulnerability. The collision occurs around the center, with patterns that evoke cracks and breaks, symbolizing moments of vulnerability amid strength. Each broken pixel adds intentional imperfection, embodying the tension between the secure and the precarious, while creating a visual story of protective boundaries against soft breaks.
