Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between 'trust' and 'suspicion'. The clean, structured edges using a dark palette symbolize trust, while the chaotic inner core reflects suspicion through disarray. The collision occurs where the dark colors of the outer frame meet the brighter, more vibrant center pixels, creating emotional intensity. The selected palette of navy, black, and dim cyan conveys a sense of loneliness against the contrasting purity of white. The pattern reflects a dynamic interplay where order and chaos coexist, resonating with the seed's themes of emotional collisions and release.
