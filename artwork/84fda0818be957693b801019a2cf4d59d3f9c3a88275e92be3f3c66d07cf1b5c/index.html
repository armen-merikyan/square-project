Artist Statement

This piece captures the conflict between clarity and confusion through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette leans into warmer tones like burnt orange and dark red, which evoke intensity and confusion, juxtaposed against the cooler charcoal for calm and neutralizing effects. The arrangement creates a broken grid that reflects false symmetry, embodying the emotional dichotomy of order disrupted by chaos. Each pixel placement speaks to the tension between the orderly and the erratic, where colors pulse against one another to emphasize emotional collisions.
