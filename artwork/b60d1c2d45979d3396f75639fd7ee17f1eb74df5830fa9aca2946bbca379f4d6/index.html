Artist Statement

This composition evokes the tension between communication (signal) and chaos (noise). The palette blends deep blues with bright whites for clarity and vibrancy. Colors on the left create a crowded, static space representing noise, while the right introduces cleaner, lighter tones, signifying a clear but fragile signal. The arrangement emphasizes this contrast by using jagged patterns on the left and smoother lines on the right, creating a visual collision at the center which symbolizes the struggle of clarity amidst chaos.
