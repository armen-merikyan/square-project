Artist Statement

This square art piece encapsulates the emotional tug-of-war between restlessness and peace. The color palette of muted cream, iron gray, and blackened plum presents a haunting calm, while bursts of small orange and red oxide introduce energy, symbolizing the interjections of chaotic memory and motion. The arrangement is designed as an 'island' of restfulness surrounded by a sea of dynamic tension. The primary collision occurs in the center where the warm accents clash against the cool, darker hues, creating a visual spark that represents a moment of disruption in tranquility. The layout and choice of broken pixels hint at imperfection and the memory of movement, encapsulating the theme beautifully without literal visuals.
