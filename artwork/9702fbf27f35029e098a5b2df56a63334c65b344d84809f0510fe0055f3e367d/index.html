Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances joy and grief, using a vibrant palette that conveys hope against a backdrop of heaviness. The warm yellows and oranges on the left evoke joy while contrasting with muted purples and deep blues on the right, capturing the essence of tension between these emotions. The split layout enhances this contrast, creating a clear division between experiences, while broken edges disrupt the symmetry, adding complexity. The visual rhythm flows in a zigzag pattern, simulating a stair-step movement that reflects the duality of feeling safe yet threatened, amplifying the emotional narrative.
