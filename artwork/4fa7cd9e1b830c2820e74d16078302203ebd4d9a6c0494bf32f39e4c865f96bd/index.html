Artist Statement

This composition captures the juxtaposition of longing and resolve through the use of soft, muted colors on the left side, representing a sense of yearning, while the right side employs brighter hues and sharper contrasts, reflecting determination and firmness. The emotional tone shifts diagonally, where the collision between the soft, noise-like patterns of longing and the structured, spark-inspired patterns of resolve creates a vivid narrative of tension and convergence. The negative space thrives in the center, allowing the emotions to breathe amidst their collision.
