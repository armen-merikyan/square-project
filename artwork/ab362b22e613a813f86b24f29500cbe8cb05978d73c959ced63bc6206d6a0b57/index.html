Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional spectrum between chaos and tranquility. The palette integrates muted sepia and faded blue tones, suggesting a memory-like quality, with confusion manifested through disjointed patterns and the clash of gradients. The left side embodies clarity with orderly stripes in lighter shades, while the right side disrupts that harmony with flame-like erratic swirls, creating visual tension. The broken pixels enhance a sense of imperfection, embodying the confusion that punctuates moments of clarity.
