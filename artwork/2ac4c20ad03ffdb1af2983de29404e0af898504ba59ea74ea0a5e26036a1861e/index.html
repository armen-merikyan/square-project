Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the interplay between restlessness and peace, captured through contrasting dark and light colors. The palette of deep black (#000000) and vibrant red (#FF0000) serves as dynamic accents against softer tones like pale blue (#B0E0E6) and white (#FFFFFF). The main collision occurs centrally where the red sparks of restlessness intersect with the tranquil pale blue, symbolizing a struggle between chaos and calm. This pattern reflects the duality of memory and motion, as colors fragment and coalesce, depicting a vibrant yet unresolved journey.
