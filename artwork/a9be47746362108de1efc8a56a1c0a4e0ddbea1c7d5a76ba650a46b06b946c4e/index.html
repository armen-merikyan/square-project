Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between signal and noise, using a palette of deep navy, contrasting with dim cyan to suggest a readable pulse amid chaos. The main collision occurs in the central area of the square, where broken dots disrupt the organized flow of maze-like patterns, reflecting the idea of interference. The inclusion of broken pixels symbolizes scars within this structured chaos, promoting a sense of imperfection and fragility that resonates with the seed's themes.
