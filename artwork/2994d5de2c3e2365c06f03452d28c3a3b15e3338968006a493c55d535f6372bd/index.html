Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between spark and ash, symbolizing sudden bursts of energy overshadowed by remnants of restraint. The palette, featuring faded golds and stormy grays, reflects this juxtaposition—delicate yet impactful. The layout offers a quiet channel, with a dominant dark central band interrupted by lighter colors, illustrating a collision of past memories and present urgency. The jagged edges hint at destructive forces, while the broken pixels represent moments of imperfection, ensuring a chaotic beauty as sparks ignite amidst lingering ashes.
