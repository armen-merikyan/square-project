Artist Statement

The artwork conveys an emotional tension between vulnerability and security. The chosen palette of 'shelter green', 'warm brown', 'pale skin', 'cold white', and 'open blue' reflects this dichotomy, with cool tones suggesting exposure and warmth suggesting safety. The main collision occurs in the center, where the soft, quiet fields meet the bold, fault-like disruptions, echoing the impact of emotional pressures. The arrangement of colors creates a dynamic interplay representing the fragility of safety amidst the echoing reality of exposure.
