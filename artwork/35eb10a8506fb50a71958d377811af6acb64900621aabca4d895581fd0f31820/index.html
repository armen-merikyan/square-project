Artist Statement

The composition explores the emotional tension between awe and terror through the placement of deep navy and dim cyan, exposing inner conflict. The left side represents awe with a cool color palette, featuring more structured, frame-like arrangements, while the right side, implying terror, is filled with chaotic dots that break the order. A soft edge style allows the viewer's eye to edge into both zones without sharp boundaries, reflecting an overarching blend of stability and instability, echoing the seed’s concept of a protected versus an attacked center.
