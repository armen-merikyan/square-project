Artist Statement

This artwork captures the schism between joy and grief, resembling a duality of experience. The chosen palette incorporates bright yellows and oranges signifying joy, contrasted by deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The layout, which is nearly symmetrical with intentional disruptions, conveys a sense of order fractured by chaos. Color placements create collisions of emotional intensity, mirroring the underlying tension between the two emotional states. The patterns of dots symbolize joy's vibrancy while flame-like transitions melt into grief's heaviness, maintaining a visual flow from bottom to top.
