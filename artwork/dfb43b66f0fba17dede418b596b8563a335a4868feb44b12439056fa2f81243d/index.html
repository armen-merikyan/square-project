Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission, represented through the bold contrast of deep wine (#4B1F20) against the softer ash gray (#B7B0A4). The layout emphasizes a narrow channel of quiet ivory (#F4E1C1) that cuts through dense clusters of noise-like patterns, while broken pixels punctuate the surface, symbolizing struggle and imperfection. The palette interplays warmth and coolness, illustrating both resistance and acceptance, while the collision occurs in the central band where colors disrupt and blend, evoking the turmoil of emotion. The overall flow from edges to center reflects the inward spiral of complexity, highlighting the dynamic space between chaos and calm.
