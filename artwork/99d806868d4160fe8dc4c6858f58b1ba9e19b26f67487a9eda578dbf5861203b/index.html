Artist Statement

This composition captures the duality of 'quiet' and 'pressure' by featuring a diagonal invasion of soft tones symbolizing quietness transitioning into dense darker shades embodying pressure. The palette is dominated by muted colors with off whites and faint peach blending softly at the top left, while compressed reds and dark navy pull weight towards the bottom right. The collision manifests through a sharp shift where quiet becomes heavy, suggesting a struggle against a tide of pressure, with broken pixels introducing intentional disruptions in the pattern to evoke a sense of imperfection and depth.
