Artist Statement

This piece conveys the tension between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise). The selected palette of deep blacks, electric blues, and vibrant magenta creates a striking contrast, embodying moments of coherence breaking through disarray. The collision occurs centrally, where a checker pattern represents the 'signal' surrounded by a swirling disturbance of static gray, reflecting the emotional conflict inherent in the seed. This arrangement suggests a struggle to maintain focus amidst the chaos of memory and experience.
