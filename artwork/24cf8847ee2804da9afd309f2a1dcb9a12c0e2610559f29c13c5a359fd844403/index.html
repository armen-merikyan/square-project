Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional duel between restlessness and peace, achieved through contrasting color placements and patterns. The palette is drawn from 'melancholy_wonder' to express softness alongside tension, using muted tones for the base to suggest peace, while brighter hues signal moments of restlessness. The broken dots pattern denotes calm, while broken frames encapsulate the frenetic energy of memory and motion. The main collision of light occurs diagonally across the square, a flicker of chaos amidst the dominant lower-toned, tranquil space, symbolizing the inner struggle.
