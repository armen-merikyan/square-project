Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece juxtaposes clarity against confusion, utilizing contrasting qualities of the selected palette: pink and deep maroon for warmth and intensity, gray for neutrality, and darker tones to represent chaos. The outer frame emulates clarity through a subtle, ordered pattern, while the inner core introduces confusion through a checker-like arrangement that is intentionally disrupted by scattered, broken pixels in vibrant pinks. This collision between uniformity and disruption encapsulates the essence of falling pixels, echoing a theme of emotional turbulence and calm. It reflects the struggle between order and chaos, echoed through color and form, allowing the viewer to experience the pulse of conflicting emotions in the arrangement of colors.
