Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts quietness with pressure, using a palette that emphasizes muted colors alongside an accent of compressed red to signify tension. The layout features a dense cluster of colors on one side that dissipates into airy spaces on the opposite edge, illustrating release against pressure. The tension peaks diagonally where the darker navy meets the bright red, symbolizing a breaking point in the pattern and reinforcing the conversation of pressure vs. release.
