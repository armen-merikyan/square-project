Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between trust and suspicion, reflected through a palette that oscillates between serene aqua and urgent red. The soft gradients suggest warmth and openness, while sharp contrasts evoke unease and fragmentation. The arrangement creates a central cluster of depth, symbolizing resilience amidst an outer layer of ash-gray tones that conveys suspicion. The collision of trust's soft glow and suspicion's hard shadow manifests as a textured pattern, creating a visual narrative of inner conflict and balance.
