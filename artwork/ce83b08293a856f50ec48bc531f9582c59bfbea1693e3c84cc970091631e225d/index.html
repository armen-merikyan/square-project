Artist Statement

The dominant emotion of 'resolve' is represented through the firm, structured lines of burnt orange and gold, suggesting clarity and intent. In contrast, the dark red and charcoal evoke feelings of 'longing,' creating a tension between the two. The layout offers two main centers that pulse independently, echoing a struggle between connection and separation. Broken pixels introduce deliberate imperfections, symbolizing repair against damage, while the quiet background (charcoal) enhances the emotional weight of the stronger colors. This arrangement creates a rhythmic, engaging narrative of conflict and resolution.
