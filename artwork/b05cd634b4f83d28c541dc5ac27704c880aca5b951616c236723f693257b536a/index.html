Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between dread and relief. The palette of pale mint and faded gold provides a soothing contrast against storm gray and near black, symbolizing a transition from heavy pressure to an exhaled calm. The main collision occurs at the center where darker colors create a sense of entrapment, loosening towards the edges with lighter tones, suggesting a slow release. The broken pixels create disruptions that symbolize the imperfection inherent in navigating these emotions, echoing the seed's themes of chaos and order.
