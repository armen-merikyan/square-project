Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends longing and resolve through contrasting elements. The palette, comprising muted grays and a soft red against a faint peach, emphasizes the tension between intimate warmth and distant coldness. The primary collision occurs in the center, where the structured 'frame' pattern of resolve intersects with the 'ladder' of longing, symbolizing a journey through uncertainty. This composition reinterprets the seed's themes into a visual narrative of emotional struggle.
