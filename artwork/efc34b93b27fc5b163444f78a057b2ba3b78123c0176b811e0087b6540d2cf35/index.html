Artist Statement

This art piece reflects the emotional dichotomy of trust and suspicion through a structured grid. The outer border represents suspicion with dark hues of #2C2A3B and #4E4B59, creating a pressure zone. The inner core, utilizing warm tones like #D6C7B6 and #AA5A2A, symbolizes trust and resilience in the face of pressure. The collision happens at the central axis where the trust colors intercept the suspicion colors, providing a rich contrast that hints at conflict and resolution. Through broken pixels, the piece feels dynamic, portraying how trust can break amidst suspicion.
