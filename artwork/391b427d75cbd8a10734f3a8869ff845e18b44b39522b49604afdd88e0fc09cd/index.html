Artist Statement

This artwork captures the struggle between density and quiet through a palette that reflects emotional tension. The use of deep blacks (#000000) juxtaposes with vibrant accents like bright oranges (#FF5722) and soft blues (#BBDEFB) to convey moments of pressure interspersed with calmness. The chaotic arrangement reflects the 'collisions' indicated by the seed, with contrasting elements colliding and creating visual rhythms. The overall effect is both chaotic and soothing, reflecting the complexity of human emotions.
