Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the weight of pressure against the lightness of quiet. The palette embraces muted tones like dark navy and dust gray, highlighting a sense of restraint, while accents of compressed red offer sharp interruptions within softer landscapes of off-white and faint peach. The collision manifests near the center, where the dense color pattern of pressure clashes with the expansive, soft flame-like formations of quiet, embodying a contrasting battle of emotions. This layout reflects the tension presented in the seed through an abstract representation of shifts between weighting pressures and spaces of release.
