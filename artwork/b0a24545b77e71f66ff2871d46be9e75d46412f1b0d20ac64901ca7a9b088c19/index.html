Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between openness and protective barriers. The palette, featuring cosmic indigo, void black, and cold gold, captures awe and terror, embodying strength with dense colors against the vulnerability of the pale star white. The main contrast occurs at the center, where static noise collides with subtle texture, allowing for rhythmic interruptions throughout the grid. This interaction symbolizes the struggle of vulnerability and strength, where each color’s placement embodies the broader emotional journey described in the seed.
