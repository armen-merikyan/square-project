Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional conflict between rage and calm. The palette juxtaposes deep blacks with stark whites and pale blues to evoke tension and tranquility. The collision occurs in the center, where the darker, jagged pixels rush against the serene horizontal blue stripes, illustrating a struggle between chaos and quiet. The layout mimics falling pixels, suggesting a descent into emotions, while the broken edges and negative space introduce imperfection and uncertainty. This pattern effectively represents the seed's abstract qualities in a visually engaging manner.
