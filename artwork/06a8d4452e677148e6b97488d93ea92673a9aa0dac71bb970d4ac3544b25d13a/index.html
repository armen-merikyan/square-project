Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between the heaviness of grief and the buoyancy of joy, represented by a palette contrasting deep blues and muted purples with vibrant yellows and oranges. The center employs dense dot patterns symbolizing grief, while the edges employ crosshatch patterns for joy. This juxtaposition creates a powerful collision in the center while allowing some negative space, contributing to the overall narrative of tension and release.
