Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'Island of Tension' reflects the struggle between defiance and submission. The central hex colors, representing defiance, are strong and vibrant, creating a visual collision with the softer, more muted colors of submission that surround them. The palette chosen incorporates rich greens and hints of gold, symbolizing the chaotic push for independence against a backdrop of quieter, subdued tones. The dense arrangement in the center creates a feeling of pressure, while the fewer colors in surrounding areas echo a sense of calm and retreat, suggesting a deeper narrative about conflict and resolution within personal spaces. The seed’s metaphor of an island signifies isolation and strength in the face of external pressures, reinforcing the emotional contrast through geometric placements. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, embodying the glitches and interruptions of life in this ongoing struggle.
