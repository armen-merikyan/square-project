Artist Statement

The emotional tone emphasizes a struggle between defiance and submission. The palette 'hunger_restraint' blends warm reds and muted creams with cool blacks and grays, painting a picture of passionate resistance surrounded by shadows. The dominant blackened plum coats the outer frame, representing submission and quietude, while the marked angular patterns of bright red and small orange interrupt the 4x4 core, emphasizing defiance. The broken pixels symbolize fractures in the emotional landscape, adding to the depth of tension and release.
