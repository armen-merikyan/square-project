Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between trust and suspicion. The palette blends soft, soothing colors (pale aqua, mist gray, clear white) representing trust with more intense colors (alarm red, ink blue) suggesting suspicion. The dominant light colors create a calm space, interrupted by sharper contrasts, emphasizing the core theme of collision between opposing emotions. The central area is lighter, with darker edges creating a border of pressure, mirroring the struggle between openness and caution.
