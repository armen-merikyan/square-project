Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between fear and hope, symbolizing an internal struggle. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green embodies darkness and light, with softer colors representing hope trying to break through fear's heaviness. The layout mimics a nearly symmetrical mirror that is disrupted by jagged edges symbolizing fear, while the radiant elements in the center signify the hope making its way through. The collision occurs at the edges, where sharp contrasts of darkness against halos of light create dynamic tension. Each broken pixel enhances the sense of imperfection, reflecting the inherent struggle of emotion represented in the design.
