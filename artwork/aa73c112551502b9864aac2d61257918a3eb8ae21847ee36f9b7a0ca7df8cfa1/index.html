Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends the intensity of suspicion and trust through a structured yet complex arrangement. The palette of lime, electric blue, and magenta against black creates a vibrant contrast, symbolizing the collision between warmth and coldness. The primary contrast occurs in the center, where dense colors collide, embodying pressure, surrounded by lighter hues that suggest release. The pattern flows downward, creating a dynamic rhythm that reflects emotional waves and interruptions, evoking a feeling of ever-changing tension.
