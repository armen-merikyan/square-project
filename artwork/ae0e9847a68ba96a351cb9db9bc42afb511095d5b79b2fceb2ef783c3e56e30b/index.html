Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the emotional tension between longing and resolve, with the contrasting feelings represented by a dominant darker palette (longing) and a lighter accent (resolve). The main collision occurs in the center 4x4 area, where the juxtaposition of muted cream and iron gray forms a visual pulse, reflective of urgency met with serenity. The chosen palette mirrors emotional scars and the intricate dance between chaos and calm, while the broken pixels add intentional disruptions that echo the complexities expressed in the seed.
