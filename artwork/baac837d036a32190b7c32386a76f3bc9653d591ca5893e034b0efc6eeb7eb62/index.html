Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tension between defiance and submission. The palette of muted colors like dark navy and faint peach creates a quiet pressure, while contrasting elements of compressed red punctuate the composition, symbolizing bright refusal at its core. The outer border suggests order but melds into chaotic patterns, demonstrating a struggle of space and form. Broken pixels disrupt the surface, embodying the essence of glitch and the imperfection of resolution, representing the struggle between serenity and tension.
