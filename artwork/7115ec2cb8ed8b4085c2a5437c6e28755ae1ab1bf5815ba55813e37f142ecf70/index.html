Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the push and pull between restlessness and peace, utilizing a palette of pale aqua and mist gray to represent peace, while alarm red emphasizes the urgency of chaos. The dominant color blocks of pale aqua create a serene foundation, while interspersed accents of alarm red disrupt this calm, creating a dichotomy within the piece. The diagonal arrangements capture the dynamic movement of argument and conflict, with broken pixel placements acting as scars of disruption or fleeting moments of tranquility within the noise of the scene. The main contrast occurs along the diagonal flow, where the two opposing emotional forces collide, representing a conversation between memories of peace and the urgency of motion. This reflects the complexity of human emotions through an abstract language of color and form.
