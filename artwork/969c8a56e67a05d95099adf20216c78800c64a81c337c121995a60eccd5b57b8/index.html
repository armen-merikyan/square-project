Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between panic and control through the deliberate placement of colors and patterns. The palette, inspired by 'broken_memory', features muted sepia and tan tones contrasted with sharper faded blues, representing the struggle between chaotic emotions (represented by the crosshatch pattern) and a sense of control (embodied by the rain pattern). The structured arrangement flows in a zigzag, suggesting instability while still maintaining central heaviness and an overall balance of negative space, reflecting the emptiness of the room and the internal chaos. The artwork evokes a narrative of a small spark of clarity fighting against a weighty backdrop of confusion, aligning with the seed's themes of emotional conflict and balance.
