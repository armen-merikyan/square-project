Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional battle between clarity and chaos. The palette is anchored in deep navy and black, suggesting the pervasive weight of noise, while dim cyan represents the pulses of signal that attempt to break through. The central area is dense with color variations, creating a striking interruption among quieter edges that frame the chaos. The collision occurs where the darker hues confront lighter cyan patches, illustrating the struggle between order and noise. The broken pixels intentionally mar the clarity, adding to the layered complexity without forming a literal shape, instead embodying the conceptual friction of the seed.
