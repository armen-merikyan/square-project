Artist Statement

The palette features bold contrasts of deep black and vibrant electric blue, reflecting the tension between loneliness and connection. The arrangement creates a zigzag flow, with heavier black tones on the left representing the weight of solitude and fragmented memories. The right side introduces crisp blues and magentas that symbolize connection and the yearning to bridge that divide, creating a pulsing emotional rhythm. This blend of solid colors amidst strategic negative spaces evokes a sense of both pressure and release, embodying the complex emotions described in the seed.
