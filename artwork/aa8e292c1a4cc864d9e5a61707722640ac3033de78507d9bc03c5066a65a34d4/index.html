Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between defiance and submission using a palette that embodies resilience and fragility. The use of moss green and rust creates a grounded feeling, while bone white provides a stark contrast to signify submission. The patterns of static defiance mixed with the staircase flow of submission create a push-pull effect, with the primary collision occurring in the center where both emotions intersect. The uneven border and broken pixels introduce a sense of chaos, reflecting the invasion theme—where defiance begins in the corner and gradually spreads across the canvas, battling against the subdued center.
