Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the struggle between defiance and submission reflected through a contrasting color palette of muted earth tones and cool blues, symbolizing tension and balance. The arrangement emphasizes a left-weighted composition filled with heavy shadows on one side, interrupted by faint highlights, suggesting a push and pull of intensity and calm. The main collision occurs between dark and light; the falling pixel arrangement hints at chaos against a stark, repetitive framework that symbolizes structure amid uncertainty. Thus, the abstract pattern becomes a visual representation of emotional conflict inspired by the seed.
