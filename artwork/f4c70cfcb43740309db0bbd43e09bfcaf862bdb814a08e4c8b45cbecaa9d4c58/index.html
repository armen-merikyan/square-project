Artist Statement

This composition captures the dichotomy of loneliness and connection through a split-screen pattern that implies a maze without walls. The palette features warm burnt orange and dark red as a nod to the intensity of emotion, clashing against the cool charcoal, symbolizing solitude. The collision of dense colors on the left side contrasts with the sparse clusters towards the right, suggesting the feeling of weight against lightness. Utilizing broken pixels within the 'hole' shape embodies the imperfection of memory and connection, while maintaining significant negative space, enhancing the emotional tension.
