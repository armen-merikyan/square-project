Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional interplay of joy and grief through a dynamic grid pattern opposing a ring texture, symbolizing the structured and the fragmented. The palette features vibrant yellows and oranges representing joy, contrasted with muted purples and deep blues signifying grief. The primary collision occurs at the center where the joyful grid clashes against the grieving ring, evoking a push and pull of emotional states, while the intentional broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection and ambiguity, reflective of the complex nature of love and betrayal.
