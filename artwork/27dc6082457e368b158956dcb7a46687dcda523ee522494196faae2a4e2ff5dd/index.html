Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between longing and resolve, where 'longing' manifests as soft pulses, contrasting with 'resolve's firm grid-like structure. The palette of guilt forgiveness—with deep wine, warm ivory, and ash gray—reflects emotional depth while the burnt edges signify both vulnerabilities and resilience. The central collision creates a dynamic pulse at the center of the 8x8 grid, indicative of an internal struggle that balances serenity and urgency.
