Artist Statement

The emotional tone carries a clash between envy and gratitude, represented by the tension of contrasting colors and a structured pattern versus chaotic breaks. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon evokes warmth but also a sense of weight. The top rows embody gratitude's stable grid pattern, while the bottom reflects envy with radiant sparks breaking through the grid, creating a tension that vibrates through the composition. The diagonal flow suggests a rise from calm to pressure, capturing the emotional dichotomy inherent in the seed's concepts.
