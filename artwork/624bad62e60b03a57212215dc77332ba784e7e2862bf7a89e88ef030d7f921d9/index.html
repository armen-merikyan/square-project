Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a sense of ambivalence, where longing and resolve collide. The palette of ice blue and violet gray evokes both serenity and urgency, encapsulating a spectrum of emotion. The main contrast occurs where dark and light boundaries meet at the center, creating a fracture-like effect that represents the interplay of strength and vulnerability. 'Broken pixels' introduce imperfection, symbolizing damage and repair, enhancing the overall narrative. The pattern of stacked bands suggests layers of emotional density, resonating with the themes of containment and overflow.
