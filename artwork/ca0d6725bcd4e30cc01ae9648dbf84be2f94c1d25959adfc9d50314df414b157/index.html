Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a delicate tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette, influenced by 'joy_grief,' incorporates bright yellows and oranges to denote strength while deep blues and muted purples suggest vulnerability. The main contrast occurs at the central seam where the two emotional patterns collide—stripes representing strength clash with a grid for vulnerability, creating an echo of tension. The composition invites the viewer to reflect on the interplays of exposure and protection, enhancing the emotional engagement through color placement.
