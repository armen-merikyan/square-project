Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the fleeting brilliance of 'spark' with the somber residue of 'ash', suggesting a complex interplay of resilience and fragility. The selected palette, featuring deep blacks and cool dark violets contrasted by soft gold and pale green, encapsulates this tension. The upper half is dominated by warmer, energetic hues, embodying an upward burst and hope, while the lower half holds cooler, subdued tones that signify reflection and restraint. This collision plays out horizontally, allowing the viewer to experience the ebb and flow of emotions, interrupted by fractured pixels that signify layered complexity and narrative depth.
