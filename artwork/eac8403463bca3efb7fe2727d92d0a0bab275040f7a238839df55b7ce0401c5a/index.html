Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between certainty and curiosity through a contrasting palette of warm and cool tones. The predominant colors, rose clay and cool blue, create a visual dialogue between stability and exploration. The central collision occurs with intersecting grids, where straight lines and curves represent structured certainty breaking into the fluidity of curiosity. Broken pixels disrupt the grid, symbolizing the unpredictability of curiosity as it meets the order of certainty.
