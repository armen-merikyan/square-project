Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between envy and gratitude. The palette of burnt orange and dark red evokes a sense of warmth but also intensity, reflecting the struggle between these emotions. The arrangement features upward movements on one side versus downward patterns on the other, creating a dialogue of hope and despair. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, symbolizing the fragility of emotions while contrasting with stable colors that echo gratitude. This interplay creates a vibrant yet structured field, suggesting the complexities of human experiences while respecting the negative space rule.
