Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a dynamic interplay between longing and resolve, capturing the essence of desire for connection amidst a sense of firm boundaries. The chosen palette of muted greens, warm rust, and soft whites reflects fragility interwoven with resilience. The main contrast occurs at the center, where the lattice structure of resolve meets the stair-like pattern of longing, creating an echo of tension. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, representing scars and imperfections, while the negative spaces allow for breath and contemplation, ensuring the composition feels both grounded and expansive.
