Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between 'spark' and 'ash'. The left side features vibrant 'spark' colors, like 'small_gold' reflecting sudden energy and ignition, while the right side incorporates muted 'ash' tones like 'stone_gray' representing settled dust and memory. The main contrast occurs in the center, where the warm tones collide with cooler shades, creating a dynamic visual argument. The layout follows a vertical split to accentuate the sense of urgency in the 'spark' versus the stillness in 'ash'. Broken pixels disrupt the symmetry, symbolizing the scars and disruptions of memory against fleeting moments of brightness.
