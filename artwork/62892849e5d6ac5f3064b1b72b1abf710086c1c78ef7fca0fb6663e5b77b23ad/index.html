Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the conflict between warmth and pressure, echoing the duality of tenderness and rage. The palette of saturated red and muted blues creates a visual tension, with red sharply confronting a soft blue field. The collision of patterns reflects the struggle between gentle overlaps of tenderness and jagged intrusions of rage, primarily concentrated in the center, symbolizing intense inner conflict. This arrangement embodies an intimate yet chaotic space, inviting viewers to navigate through the stark contrasts.
