Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The top half employs a 'barcode' pattern, reflecting the complexity and desire to connect, using soft pinks and deep maroons to evoke warmth and urgency. The lower half transitions to a 'braid' pattern, where steadfast gray and dark maroon create a sense of resolution and stability, counterbalanced with spaces that hint at the unresolved. The collision of patterns occurs in the middle, representing the tension of a broken boundary, enhanced by intentionally placed 'broken' pixels, suggesting imperfections inherent in the emotional journey.
