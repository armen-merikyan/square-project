Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between loneliness and connection, echoing the contrasts of pressure and release. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue suggests a nostalgic yet fragmented memory, embodying both warmth and coolness. The soft edges and open vs closed contrast create a sense of flow from the outer edges toward the center, with lively colors colliding against muted fields, symbolizing the struggles of loneliness surrounded by a thin layer of connection. The broken pixel insertion heightens the sense of imperfection, drawing the viewer into a moment of unresolved tension and emotion.
