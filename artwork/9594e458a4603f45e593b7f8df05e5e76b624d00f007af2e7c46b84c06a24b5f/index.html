Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between safety and exposure. The palette leverages deep greens and warm browns to invoke a sense of shelter, contrasted against pale skin tones and cold whites that represent vulnerability and openness. The main collision occurs in the lower left corner, where darker, denser colors meet lighter pulses, suggesting a struggle between pressure and release. The layout builds tension by filling the corner, while soft edges blend into quiet negative spaces, reflecting the emotional dialogue of the seed.
