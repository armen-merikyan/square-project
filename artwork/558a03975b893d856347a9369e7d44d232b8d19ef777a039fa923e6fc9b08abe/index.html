Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends 'defiance' with 'submission', showcasing a clash between strength and softness. The palette combines 'red_oxide' and 'small_orange' as bold accents against muted 'blackened_plum' and 'iron_gray' to represent tension and struggle. The arrangement has a strong diagonal division with 'defiance' dominating the left side, creating a sense of movement and expansion, while 'submission' settles on the right in a quieter, relaxing layout. Broken pixels disrupt flows, echoing the inner conflict suggested by the seed.
