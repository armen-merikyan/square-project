Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a juxtaposition of tenderness and rage, reflecting a struggle between calmness and intensity. The chosen palette features deep reds evocative of rage, contrasted with muted pale blues symbolizing tenderness. The main contrast occurs through jagged reds that invade the tranquil blues, suggesting an emotional rupture. The layout captures an 'empty room' feeling, where tension is suggested through the interplay of these colors, reflecting interrupted calmness. The broken pixels add an element of imperfection, indicating scars of emotional experiences. The overall arrangement speaks to the complexity of feelings, revealing an underlying narrative of guilt and forgiveness within the tension of opposite moods.
