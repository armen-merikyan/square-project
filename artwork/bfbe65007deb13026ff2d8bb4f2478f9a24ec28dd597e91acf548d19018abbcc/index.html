Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between joy and grief, illustrated through contrasting colors of bright yellow and muted purple. The arrangement reflects order vs chaos in a diagonal spread, creating tension where joy's spark-like colors meet grief's heavy hues. A zigzag pattern echoes the instability of emotions, while broken edges represent fragmented feelings. The layout invites the viewer to engage with the complexity of the intertwined emotions, suggesting a continual push and pull between joy and grief.
