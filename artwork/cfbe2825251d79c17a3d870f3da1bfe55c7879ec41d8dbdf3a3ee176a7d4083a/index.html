Artist Statement

This composition explores the clash between exuberance (spark) and quietude (ash), utilizing a palette that evokes awe and terror. Dark, muted tones occupy the outer edges, presenting a stark contrast to the vibrant center, symbolizing the tension between memory and erasure. The collision is most visible in the center where diagonal patterns intertwine, creating a pulse of energy against a backdrop of stability. This arrangement embodies the emotional landscape conveyed by the seed, suggesting both resilience and fragility through its movement and contrast.
