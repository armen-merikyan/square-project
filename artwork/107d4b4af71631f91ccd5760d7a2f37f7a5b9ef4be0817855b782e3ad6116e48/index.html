Artist Statement

This composition embraces the emotional duality of clarity and confusion through contrasting warm and cool tones. The palette features a mix of yellow and deep blue, illustrating the tension between optimism and depth. The layout captures a 'falling pixels' effect, where colors drip down to suggest an emotional release while leaving intentional gaps of negative space, embodying moments of pause and reflection. This rhythmic descent positions warmth alongside cooler, muted purple sections, accentuating the collision between clarity and chaos, enhancing the abstract narrative of pulse and disruption.
