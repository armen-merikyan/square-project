Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through a split horizontal design. The palette, rooted in 'ancient_fire', juxtaposes burnt orange and dark red against charcoal and gold, symbolizing warmth amidst chaotic distortions. The top half utilizes a crosshatch pattern, evoking clarity with structured lines, while the bottom half employs crack patterns, signifying breakdown and disorder. The edge style creates a weight towards the bottom, reflecting emotional gravity, while the broken pixels add subtle imperfections, enhancing the theme of fragmented clarity. The collision of solid colors against more muted tones builds a narrative of conflict—central clarity struggles under the demands of confusion.
