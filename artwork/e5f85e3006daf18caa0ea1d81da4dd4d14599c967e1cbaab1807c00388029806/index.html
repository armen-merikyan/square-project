Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional contrasts between longing and resolve, utilizing a palette that balances warm tones of rose clay and lamp gold with cooler shades of distant lilac and cool blue. The interaction of 'spark' and 'frame' patterns creates a dynamic rhythm, where the central collision occurs at the intersection of warm and cool, symbolizing the push-pull of emotion. Broken pixels introduce imperfect elements that speak to the state of being 'mirror broken', reinforcing the themes of repair and damage. Overall, this composition offers a visual dialogue between serenity and urgency through its abstract arrangement.
