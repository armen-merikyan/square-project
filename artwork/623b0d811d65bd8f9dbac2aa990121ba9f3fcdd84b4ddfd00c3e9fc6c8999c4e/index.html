Artist Statement

The emotional tone fluctuates between noise and signal, reflecting tension and clarity through a palette that combines deep navy and bright white. The collision occurs in the center, with sharp edges of static colors contrasting against smoother, softer hues. The seed's essence is discernible in the abstract composition, embodying the struggle between order and chaos, where the structured halos of signal fight against chaotic threads of static. This duality is illustrated through the layout where dark colors dominate, punctuated by sharp bursts of light.
