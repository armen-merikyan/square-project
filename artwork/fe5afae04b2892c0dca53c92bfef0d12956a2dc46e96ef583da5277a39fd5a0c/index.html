Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. A palette of navy and dim cyan reflects isolation, while the stark contrast of black and white pixels suggests the sharp interactions and interruptions of connection. The top-weighted design creates a visual heaviness, offset by smaller bursts of light – mirroring how loneliness often feels heavy, while connection can erupt unexpectedly. The patterns echo a sense of hidden depths and the interplay of exposure versus concealment around a distinct center, symbolizing the struggle between intimacy and distance.
