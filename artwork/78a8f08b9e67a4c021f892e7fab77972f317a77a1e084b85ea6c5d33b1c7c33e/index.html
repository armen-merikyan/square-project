Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between restlessness and peace, embodied through a palette that juxtaposes warm rust and bone white with muted moss green and stone gray. The arrangement creates a dynamic collision, where bright colors ignite tension in the quieter, cooler fields. This is particularly visible at the center, where a dense cluster of vibrant hues disrupts the surrounding calm. This clash signifies the movement of memory against the weight of peace, illustrating a battle that is resolved yet ongoing. The edge style of thick outlines emphasizes the boundary, mirroring the 'closed_corners' and 'edge_crawl' themes, while broken pixel elements introduce intentional imperfections that symbolize disruptions in memory.
