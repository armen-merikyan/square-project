Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tension between defiance and submission, using a palette that evokes safety and exposure. The contrast between warmer tones (shelter_green and warm_brown) and cooler elements (cold_white and open_blue) creates a dynamic interplay. The grid-like structure representing defiance clashes with the noise pattern symbolizing submission, reflecting the core of the seed — the push and pull of emotional states. The main collision occurs in the center, representing a threshold where order meets chaos, with intentional broken pixels introducing an element of imperfection, enhancing the feeling of disruption.
