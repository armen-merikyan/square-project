Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece plays with the contrast between the warmth of desire and the void of emptiness, creating a dynamic tension throughout the pixels. The chosen palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold reflects this emotional interplay, evoking both warmth and depth. The pattern, influenced by falling pixels, descends from intensity at the center to quieter hues at the edges, which visually narrates a release from pressure to calm. The collisions occur where vibrant colors meet subdued tones, creating visually compelling interruptions that mirror the complexity of emotional experiences.
