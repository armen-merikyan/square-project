Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension of joy and grief through the interplay of bright, spark-like yellows and muted, blue-toned grief colors. The pattern reflects a corner invasion of joy, bleeding through into a chaotic field, suggesting a struggle between order and chaos. The use of radial patterns hints at joy while noise embodies grief. The asymmetry allows for an interpreted collision, featuring bright yellow pixels interrupting the muted backdrop, creating a balance of emotional pressure and release.
