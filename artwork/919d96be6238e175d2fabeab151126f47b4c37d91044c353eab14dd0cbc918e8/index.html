Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay of defiance and submission, represented through contrasting shades of sepia and muted gray. The palette echoes feelings of broken memory and past struggles, while the jagged edge style enhances emotional instability. The layout frames a chaotic center symbolizing defiance, surrounded by patterns of submission, creating an overall tension that pushes boundaries. The arrangement and color clashes suggest subtle conflicts and resolutions, enhancing the narrative of emotional conflict.
