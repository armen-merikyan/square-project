Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between joy and grief through a dynamic arrangement. The palette harmonizes warm yellows and oranges with deep blues and muted purples, facilitating a conversation between lightness and heaviness. The radial pattern of grief contrasts with the dispersed joy of broken dots that hint at light piercing through darkness. The use of negative space creates a deliberate interruption, inviting reflection on emotional density versus emptiness. The overlapping colors result in a rich texture that embodies the complexities of both emotions while allowing for moments of quiet amidst the visual chaos.
