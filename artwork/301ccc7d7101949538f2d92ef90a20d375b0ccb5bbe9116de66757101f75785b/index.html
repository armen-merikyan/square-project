Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, using a palette that juxtaposes warm browns and greens with cool whites and blues. The collision occurs prominently in the center, where saturated colors create a dense cluster, surrounded by softer, quieter edges representing the contrasts in emotional weight. The seed's themes of tension and repair manifest as densely woven patterns that hint at both containment and overflow.
