Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between restlessness and peace, using a palette dominated by navy and black to symbolize depth and serenity, contrasted by dim cyan as an accent. The center is dense with dots representing restless motion, while patchwork at the edges provides calm, creating a layered visual interaction where the intensity of the center battles against the peace of the boundaries, echoing the seed's themes of memory and motion.
