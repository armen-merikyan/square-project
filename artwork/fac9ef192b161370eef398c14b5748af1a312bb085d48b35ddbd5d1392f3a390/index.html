Artist Statement

The composition explores the emotional spectrum between confusion and clarity through contrasting patterns and colors. The top half features deep, dark gradients that represent confusion employing a ring pattern, evoking feelings of disorder and broken paths. The lower half introduces stripes in lighter tones representing clarity with a sense of order and calm. The stark contrast between these styles creates an emotional collision, echoing the inherent struggle to find clarity amidst chaos. The chosen palette of blacks and pale blues emphasizes the tension between depth and lightness, while the play of negative space maintains a balance, ensuring not to overwhelm the viewer.
