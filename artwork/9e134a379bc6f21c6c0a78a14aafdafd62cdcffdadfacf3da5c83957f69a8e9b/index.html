Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection using a vibrant, warm palette juxtaposed with cooler tones. The outer edges feature dark maroon and gray, representing isolation, while the central area bursts with red and pink, symbolizing connection. The interplay of dense colors against negative space suggests a pulse, highlighted by 'crack' and 'teeth' patterns subtly emerging within. The arrangement emphasizes the struggle between the weight of loneliness and the potential for connection, echoing the tension outlined in the seed.
