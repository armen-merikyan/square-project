Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the battle between confusion and clarity, utilizing a palette rooted in warm (yellow, orange) and cool (deep blue, muted purple) tones. The colors create a tension between bright and muted fields, symbolizing clarity interrupted by chaos. The contrast peaks in the center where overlapping paths collide, suggesting a moment of conflict before resolution. The rain pattern represents confusion, while the framing elements encapsulate clarity. The broken pixels disrupt symmetry, enhancing the emotional discord. Negative space exists to evoke breath, offering moments of clarity amid chaotic transitions.
