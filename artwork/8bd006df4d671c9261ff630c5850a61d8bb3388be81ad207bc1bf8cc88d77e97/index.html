Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured here reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes nostalgia and fragility, creating a soft yet complex atmosphere. The collision of the crosshatch pattern representing vulnerability with the ladder-like pattern symbolizing strength is evident in the dense center against the lighter edges, suggesting a battle between protection and exposure. This arrangement disrupts the viewer's perception, echoing the intertwined nature of these emotions.
