Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of calm and rage through a split layout where the left side embodies calm with soft pale blue, allowing for a gentle transition into the chaotic reds and blacks of the right side. The crack pattern suggests a fragmented tranquility, while noise on the other side communicates explosive pressure. The central concept of 'collisions' is represented through hard edges on the left transitioning into chaotic bursts on the right, engaging the viewer in the emotional push and pull.
