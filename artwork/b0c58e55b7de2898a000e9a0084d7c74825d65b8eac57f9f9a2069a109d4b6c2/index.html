Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between restlessness and peace. The palette blends soft, muted tones with striking accents to represent the constant push and pull of memories and motion. The main collision occurs in the center with bright crimson contrasting against gentle grays, invoking a visceral interplay between movement and stillness. The abstract pattern reflects diagonal arguments of memory, with fragmented sections suggesting shatter and quiet fields coexisting.
