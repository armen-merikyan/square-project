Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates through the contrasts of defiance and submission, captured in a palette of electric blue, magenta, and black. The contrast between bright electric blue and deep black creates a striking collision, signaling defiance against a weighty submission. This pattern embodies a vertical flow, reminiscent of falling pixels, where the vibrant colors disrupt the muted background. The visual echo of color placement reflects the dichotomy of open vs. closed spaces, balancing weight and lightness.
