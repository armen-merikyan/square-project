Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through contrasting color intensity. The dark colors represent confusion's depth, while brighter shades hint at clarity's hope. The split layout emphasizes dissonance, where the top rows pulse with chaotic flame patterns, while the lower rows exhibit a maze-like clarity. The intersection of patterns suggests the struggle between order and chaos—the falling pixels scatter from the center, creating an outward movement, symbolizing clarity striving to break through confusion.
