Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece embodies a tension between loneliness and connection, using contrasting colors and spatial arrangements to suggest collision and release. The selected palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon evokes themes of love and betrayal, highlighting the subtle emotions that arise through connection and disconnection. Main contrasts occur between warm and cool tones, particularly with the clash of deep maroon against soft pink, creating a visual dialogue of emotional intensity. The 'falling_pixels' layout, coupled with stairs and maze patterns, creates a sense of descent and movement, emphasizing pressure in the upper regions while allowing for negative space at the bottom, symbolizing a release. The application of broken pixels introduces a layer of imperfection that speaks to the complexity of emotions.
