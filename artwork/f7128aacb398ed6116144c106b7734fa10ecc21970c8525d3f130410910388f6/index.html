Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress through a play of color and arrangement. The palette, inspired by 'broken_memory', features muted tones of sepia and faded blue, representing nostalgia as a soft, warm undertone, while the cooler, grayed colors suggest a progressive energy. The composition flows from darker hues at the top to lighter ones at the bottom, emulating a 'falling_pixels' layout that narrates a gradual descent from memory's weight to a more liberated state. The central collision occurs where the sepia and gray tones meet, highlighting the internal struggle of 'full_vs_hollow', creating a balanced yet dynamic interaction of emotions.
