Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between quiet and pressure. The muted pastel shades represent the quiet, while darker, more saturated colors embody pressure. The dense cluster of dark hues at the top left symbolizes pressure, gradually dissipating into softer, lighter tones, illustrating a release. The random placements of lighter colors echo moments of clarity within confusion. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, reflecting the fragmented nature of experiences colliding with tranquility. A diagonal flow leads the eye across the square, reinforcing the argument made by the contrasting tones.
