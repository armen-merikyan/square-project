Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between fear and hope, using a palette that juxtaposes dark hues against glimmers of light. The contrast manifests in the boundary where dense blacks and deep violets frame a brighter center, embodying the burst of hope amid heavy emotional weight. The split layout encourages a feeling of division yet connectivity, resonating with the themes of collision and continuity suggested by the seed, while mirrored patterns introduce complexity and reflection.
