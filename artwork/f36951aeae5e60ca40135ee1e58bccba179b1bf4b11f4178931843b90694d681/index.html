Artist Statement

This composition conveys the emotional interplay between longing and resolve. The selected palette of ripe green, acid green, honey gold, deep olive, and cream creates a contrast between warmth and coolness, enhancing the feelings of yearning and strength. The main contrast occurs at the center of the grid, where threads of longing (represented by warm tones) intertwine with the stronger, patchwork-like resolution (cooler hues). This collision reflects the dynamics of repair versus damage, embodying the complexities of emotion and the delicate balance of tension and release in the pattern, while leaving enough negative space to evoke a sense of contemplation and serenity amidst urgency.
