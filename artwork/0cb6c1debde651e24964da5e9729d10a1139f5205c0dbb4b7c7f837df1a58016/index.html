Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors that collide softly. The muted palette of sepia and faded blue suggests a broken memory, with a pale center symbolizing exposure surrounded by deeper, heavier tones that provide a sense of protection yet remain open to interruption. The collision happens at the center where warm and cool colors meet, representing the instability of safety. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions that symbolize the fragility inherent in the theme.
