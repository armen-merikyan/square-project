Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The palette of lime, electric blue, magenta, and black converges to create stark contrasts, playing with the idea of symmetry versus disruption. The left side, with grid patterns in light colors, illustrates clarity, while the right side embraces chaotic intersections of darker hues, invoking confusion. The intentional displacement of pixels adds a visceral sense of brokenness, enhancing the emotional narrative. These tensions reflect the struggle and strife within one's thoughts, culminating in a visual representation of the seed's themes.
