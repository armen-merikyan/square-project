Artist Statement

This piece embodies the struggle between vulnerability and strength through a color palette that juxtaposes muted, dark tones with soft highlights. The main collision occurs in the central area, where the subtle spark of pale green meets the dense black, suggesting a tension that represents the fracturous nature of exposure. The strong presence of dark violet serves as an anchor, while the soft gold mimics the spark of hope amid clouds of uncertainty. The pixel arrangement follows a falling pattern that emphasizes the emotional weight of the piece, echoing the theme of ‘exposure’ while creating a sense of shelter through the surrounding darker edges.
