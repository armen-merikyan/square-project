Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tug between longing and resolve, illustrated through a dense upper region that conveys unresolved feelings, meeting a more stable bottom section that embodies strength. The palette, 'broken_memory', reflects warmth with sepia and faded blue, juxtaposing muted tones with stark contrasts. The main collision occurs diagonally, facilitating a visual narrative of tension easing into stability, echoing the seed’s themes of cross currents and fault lines. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, embodying the dissonance between desire and certainty.
