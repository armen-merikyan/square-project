Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional extremes of awe and terror through a careful choice of colors and their arrangement. The palette blends warm reds with cool grays to create a dance between tension and calm. The left side follows a structured pattern embodying order and stability, while the right disrupts this with soft edges and broken pixels suggesting chaos and terror. The arrangement flows upward, indicating the struggle to rise above fear while the touches of brokenness evoke memories of instability. Overall, it resonates with the theme of navigating the duality of serenity and anxiety.
