Artist Statement

The emotional tone emphasizes the struggle between loneliness and connection, illustrated through stark contrasts and soft transitions. The chosen palette of navy, black, and a hint of single white evokes heaviness and solitude, while the dim cyan introduces a whisper of connection. The arrangement prioritizes a central negative space, interrupted by densely packed colors, suggesting moments of pressure amidst isolation, akin to tears breaking through silence. This pattern mirrors the concept of hidden versus exposed emotions, with hard edges both framing the square and accentuating the loneliness of the inner space.
