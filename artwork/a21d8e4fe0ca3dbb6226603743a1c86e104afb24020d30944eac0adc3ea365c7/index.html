Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of joy and grief, where warm hues of yellow and orange represent joy, while muted purples and deep blues embody grief. The contrasting colors create a visual tension, with a central light source (yellow) surrounded by darker shades, symbolizing struggle and release. The use of a grid for joy serves as a structure against the chaotic crosshatch of grief, representing the push and pull of these conflicting feelings. This pattern hints at hidden complexities vying for exposure throughout the composition.
