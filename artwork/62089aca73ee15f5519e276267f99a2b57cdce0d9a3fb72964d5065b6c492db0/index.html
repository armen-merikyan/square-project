Artist Statement

This composition captures the vibrant tension between joy and grief, utilizing a palette of bright yellows and deep blues to represent hope and sorrow respectively. The split in the layout symbolizes the emotional dichotomy, where warm colors expand and collide against cooler tones that hold weight. The broken pixel pattern enhances the idea of imperfection, depicting how grief often interrupts moments of joy. Each row's arrangement signifies the rhythm of emotional oscillation, with bright accents creating sparks amidst a muted backdrop.
