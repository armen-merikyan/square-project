Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between defiance and submission. The chosen palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold suggests warmth and intensity, symbolizing the passion of defiance contrasting against the subdued nuances of submission. The dense areas clash with the more open spaces, creating an impactful collision, while the left-to-right flow symbolizes the journey from conflict to resolution. The broken pixels create a sense of imperfection that underscores the theme of struggle and tension.
