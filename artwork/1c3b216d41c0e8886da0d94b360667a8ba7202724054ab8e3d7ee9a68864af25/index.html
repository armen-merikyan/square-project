Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission, showcasing a sharp contrast in diamond-like patterns against soft veils. The palette combines earthy hues, with bright accents and deep neutrals to suggest resilience amidst fragility. The main collision occurs centrally, with bold colors competing for dominance against muted tones, allowing the narrative of struggle and resistance to unfold at the heart of the piece.
