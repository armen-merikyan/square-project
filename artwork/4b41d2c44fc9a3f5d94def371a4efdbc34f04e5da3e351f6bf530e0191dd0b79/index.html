Artist Statement

This composition conveys an emotional tension between longing and resolve, using contrasting patterns and colors to symbolize the struggle between these feelings. The left side, representing longing, features a grid pattern in soft, muted greens and warm honey gold, suggesting a search or reach for connection. In contrast, the right side, embodying resolve, employs a channel pattern with deeper olive and cream dynamics, offering a sense of structure and stability. The collision occurs along the central divide, where colors intermingle, creating a feeling of tension and complexity, reflecting the emotional journey captured by the seed.
