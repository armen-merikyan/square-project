Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve, represented by a palette that includes both vibrant and muted colors. The chosen colors evoke a sense of warmth (ripe green, honey gold) and coolness (deep olive, cream), contrasting the deep satisfaction of resolve with the ache of longing. The main collision is located at the center diagonal, where broken dots disrupt a flowing pattern, symbolizing the tension between containment and overflow. The layout reflects a corner invasion, emphasizing urgency while maintaining negative space to represent quiet reflection. Overall, the composition portrays an abstract journey of feeling, transitioning from hope to frustration.
