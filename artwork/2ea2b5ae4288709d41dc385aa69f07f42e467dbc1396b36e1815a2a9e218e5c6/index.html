Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around a juxtaposition of quiet and pressure, conveyed through dense clusters of dark colors that give way to softer tones. The palette features off-white and faint peach lending a sense of calm, contrasting sharply against the darker hues of compressed red and dark navy, representing pressure. The composition utilizes a symmetric layout eroded at the edges, symbolizing a shift from clarity to confusion, with intentional disruptions (broken pixels) creating a fragmented narrative reflecting the struggle between firmness and softness. The main collision of contrast occurs diagonally, enhancing the narrative of tension erupting in gentle invitations to pause.
