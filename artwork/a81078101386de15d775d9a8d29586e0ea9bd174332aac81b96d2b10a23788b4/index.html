Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between softness and severity, using a palette of muted colors contrasted with sharp accents to depict emotional duality. The dominance of dark shades symbolizes severity, while warmer tones punctuate the composition, suggesting moments of softness and forgiveness. The main collision occurs toward the center, where the dynamic tension of warmer hues meets the grounded weight of darker shades, echoing both the pulse of conflict and the gentle pull of resolution.
