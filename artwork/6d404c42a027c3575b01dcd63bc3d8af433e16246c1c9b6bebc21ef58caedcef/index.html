Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between tenderness and rage. The chosen palette, featuring deep reds and soft blues, reflects the conflict: rage in bold red, seen in the jagged patterns, and tenderness in muted blues, flowing gently across the canvas. The layering of colors mimics emotional strata, highlighting the collision of sharp and rounded shapes. The center serves as the main confrontation point where emotions collide, creating a visible disturbance in the pattern, representing a break between quiet and chaotic feelings.
