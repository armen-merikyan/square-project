Artist Statement

This pixel composition captures the emotional landscape of longing vs resolve through the juxtaposition of warm and cool tones. The palette blends moss green and rust with neutral bone white and stone gray, reflecting both density and fragility. The layout of diagonal wedges echoes the tension between desire and determination. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense clusters of moss green and rust meet the quiet interlude of bone white, creating a pulse that resonates throughout the piece. The abstract organization adheres to the seed's metaphorical qualities and enhances an intimate yet distant feeling, speaking to the emotional core of the contrasts involved.
