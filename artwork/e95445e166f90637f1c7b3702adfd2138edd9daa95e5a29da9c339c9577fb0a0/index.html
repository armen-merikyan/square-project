Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between restlessness and peace. The palette of sepia, faded blue, tan, and dust gray emphasizes muted tones with a few brighter interruptions to symbolize sudden thoughts or memories piercing through calm. The shatter pattern expresses disruption while the radial elements offer a contrasting symmetry, creating a visual conversation about the balance between motion and stillness. The main collision occurs diagonally, creating a visual dynamic through the grid, while closed corners maintain a sense of containment.
