Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through a careful arrangement of color. The palette of deep navy, black, and dim cyan contrasts with the occasional stark white, suggesting moments of clarity against the backdrop of noise. The layout reflects nearly symmetric arrangements that are disrupted by splashes of confusion, where darker shades collide with lighter ones, creating a sense of disarray. This arrangement of colors evokes a pulse—an ebb and flow of intensity that mirrors the complexities of emotions captured in the seed.
