Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress through a defined palette of dark, muted colors, reflecting nostalgia's weight, against bright accents representing progress's urgency. The bottom-heavy layout accentuates the psychological gravity of the past, while the scattered bright colors create moments of optimism, suggesting a struggle for balance between stillness and movement. The patterns of rings and stairs suggest echoes of memories and steps toward the future, capturing the essence of a fight between heaviness and lightness.
