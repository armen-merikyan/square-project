Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between restlessness and peace, represented by a palette that juxtaposes calm neutrals with vibrant accents. The chosen colors—deep olive and cream evoke tranquility, while honey gold and acid green introduce a sense of energy and tension. The main collision occurs at the center, where the brighter colors attempt to disrupt the muted edges, creating a dynamic interplay of light versus weight. This abstract arrangement captures the essence of the seed as a collision of layered emotions and contrasting pressures.
