Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional struggle between restlessness and peace through contrasting colors and arrangements. The dominant dark colors signify the weight of unrest, while patches of bright colors represent fleeting moments of serenity. The composition employs a stepwell pattern to illustrate motion against a backdrop of structure, conveying a sense of upward tension. Broken pixels are placed strategically to disrupt the visual flow, echoing the collision of emotions, while the closed corners create a feeling of confinement, enhancing the narrative.
