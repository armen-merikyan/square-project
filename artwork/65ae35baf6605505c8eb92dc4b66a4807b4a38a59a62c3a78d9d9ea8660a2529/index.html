Artist Statement

This composition balances the intensity of rage with the gentleness of tenderness, creating an emotional interplay. The palette is composed of saturated reds to invoke rage, contrasted against muted pale blues and whites representing tenderness. The core collision occurs at the diagonal, where harsh edges of red pixels bleed into softer blue, capturing the contrast of flowing tenderness against explosive rage. This reflects a deep emotional journey, where warmth and coolness confront each other within the confines of the square layout.
