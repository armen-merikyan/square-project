Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tug-of-war between restlessness and peace. The color palette reflects vivid signals with electric blue and hot magenta representing restless energy, juxtaposed against the calm of static gray and dead black that symbolize peace. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where the vibrant colors clash with darker shades, creating a visual tension that echoes the concept of movement and stillness, influenced by the seed’s thematic elements of memory and motion. The arrangement of pixels mimics a falling pattern, enhancing the sense of descent and turmoil, while broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, suggesting fragments of memory and moments of unease.
