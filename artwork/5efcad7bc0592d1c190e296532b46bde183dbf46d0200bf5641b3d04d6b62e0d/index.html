Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional collision felt between panic and control, utilizing a palette that mixes warm and cool tones. The contrast between vibrant pinks and deep maroons signifies the struggle between a light presence and the weight of darker emotions. Stripes and framing patterns suggest movement from right to left, drawing the eye into the central chaos before erupting outward into chaotic layers, embodying the contrasting emotional forces outlined in the seed.
