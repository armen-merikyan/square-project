Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents the tension between 'spark' and 'ash', juxtaposing warm bursts against cool, settled tones. The palette, featuring 'moss_green', 'bone_white', 'rust', 'stone_gray', and 'small_gold', evokes resilience while hinting at fragility. The main collision occurs in the center where brighter tones of rust and small gold contrast sharply with the muted tones of stone gray and moss green, capturing a sense of dynamic tension. The split-screen and grid patterns create a constant play of density and rest, illustrating conflict and resolution through color placement. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, echoing the theme of resilience against loss.
