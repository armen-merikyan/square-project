Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between longing and resolve through contrasting colors and structured patterns. The use of a muted palette with ice blue and violet gray creates a sense of depth and introspection, while the sharp accents of warning orange act as signposts of clarity amid confusion. The central collision of colors in a diagonal configuration signifies a journey, suggesting movement from a dense, longing state towards the clarity of resolve, with quiet spaces interrupting the flow to represent intimacy and distance. Broken pixels add an element of imperfection, enhancing the emotional narrative of struggle and hope.
