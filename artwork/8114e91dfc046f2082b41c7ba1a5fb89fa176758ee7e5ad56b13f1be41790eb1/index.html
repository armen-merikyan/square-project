Artist Statement

The emotional tone leverages the duality of confusion and clarity, manifesting through fragmented patterns and sharp contrasts. The palette features warm reds against cool grays, expressing tension. The main collision occurs where dark maroon meets vibrant pink centers, symbolizing chaotic yet passionate undercurrents. The broken edges evoke a sense of interrupted symmetry and layered meanings created by the patterns, reflecting the seed's narrative of a conflicted internal journey. Color placements create a visual rhythm that echoes confusion while suggesting clarity as each pixel fights for attention within a constrained space.
