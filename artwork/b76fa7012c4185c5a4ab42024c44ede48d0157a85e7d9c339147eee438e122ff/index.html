Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between fear and hope, represented through a careful interplay of deep dark colors and lighter accents. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes a transition from despair to potential, where the darkness represents fear and the lighter tones symbolize hope. The main contrast occurs where the jagged edges of dark shades clash against the soft, flowing stripes of gold and green, creating a narrative of struggle and uplifting emergence. The zigzag pattern flows through the composition, echoing the rising signals of hope struggling against the heaviness of fear, while leaving strategic negative space to symbolize moments of quiet reflection amidst chaos.
