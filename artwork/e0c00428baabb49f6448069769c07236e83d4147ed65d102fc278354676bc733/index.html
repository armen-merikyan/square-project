Artist Statement

The emotional tone speaks to the juxtaposition between 'emptiness' and 'desire', creating a narrative of conflict and resolution. The chosen palette—dark navy, black, subtle whites, and a dim cyan—reflects loneliness and the struggle against overwhelming pressure. The left side of the grid employs radial patterns symbolizing desire, while the right side uses stripes for emptiness, creating tension through contrasting patterns. The broken edges enhance the theme of imperfection and interruption, allowing a sense of realism in abstraction. The main collision occurs where the dense dark colors meet lighter shades, suggesting an emotional clash between hope and despair, an unresolved dynamic captured visually.
