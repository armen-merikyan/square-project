Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a tug between longing and resolve, captured within a palette of ripe greens and honey gold to evoke warmth and tension. The main contrast occurs in the center, where solid greens collide with lighter tones, symbolizing the intersection of stability and fragility, while broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, suggesting repair amidst damage. This arrangement mirrors the fractured yet persistent nature of desire fading into resolution, all while maintained along a flowing bottom-to-top movement, encouraging a sense of upward hope amidst unrest.
