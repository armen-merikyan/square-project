Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, using a palette that oscillates between dark navy and bright white to create a striking contrast. The main collision occurs in the center with dense clusters of black and dim cyan swirling outward, simulating a chaotic maze of thoughts that diffuse as they reach the edges. The pixel arrangement alludes to the feeling of falling, where weight and pressure are embraced at the center while allowing for negative space to represent clarity. The broken pixel strategy reinforces the confusion motif while a refined outer layer instills a sense of structure.
