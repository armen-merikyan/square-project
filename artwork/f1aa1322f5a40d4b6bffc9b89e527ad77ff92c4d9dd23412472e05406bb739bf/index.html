Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between joy and grief through contrasting color clusters. The warm yellows and oranges symbolize lightness and joy, while the muted purples and deep blues introduce heaviness and grief. The palette evokes a dynamic interchange between brightness and depth, creating a sensation of rising motion over stillness. The dominant position of bright blocks surrounded by deeper shades embodies the struggle between the two emotional states, suggesting a zigzag of feeling as the brighter colors intermittently break through the darkness, mirroring the complexities inherent in navigating joy and sorrow.
