Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between chaos (panic) and structure (control). The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold evokes warmth and intensity, aligning with feelings of anguish and calm. The contrast occurs where the dense, fiery colors clash with the cooler, muted tones, symbolizing confrontation. The zigzag pattern, with a few broken pixels, disrupts symmetry, representing the fragility in the balance of order and disorder, echoing the seed inspiration.
