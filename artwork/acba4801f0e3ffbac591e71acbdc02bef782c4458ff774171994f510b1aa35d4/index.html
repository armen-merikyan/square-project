Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a central dense arrangement in muted colors. The palette features soft and pale tones juxtaposed against deeper hues, creating a quiet calamity and an anxious sheltering border. The main collision occurs at the center, where strength radiates in dense rings, overtaken by the vulnerable, soft pastels, suggesting a struggle to contain one's inner vulnerabilities amidst the push of external pressures.
