Artist Statement

The artwork reflects the emotional tension between loneliness and connection, utilizing a palette of deep blues and blacks to evoke isolation, contrasted with hints of dim cyan and white symbolizing hope and connection. The arrangement creates a push-pull effect, creating a visual representation of bursts of connection breaking through a predominantly dark atmosphere. The top-weighted layout emphasizes the heaviness of isolation, while the scattered elements embody moments of connection that pierce through the surface. The deliberate inclusion of broken pixels introduces imperfection symbolizing the fragmented state of emotional experiences.
