Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece embodies a critical juxtaposition between desire (warm, vibrant colors) and emptiness (cool, muted colors), showcasing a saturated warmth that reaches towards the darker, contained perimeter. The palette of hot coral, blood orange, and dusty pink signifies desire, while the deep charcoal and hollow blue represent emptiness. The central collision point manifests where the warm hues collide fiercely with the cooler containment, creating a center outburst balanced by the calmness of the perimeter. This arrangement reflects the seed's essence through contrasting pressure and release, where the vibrancy finds itself held back by the darker boundaries, mirroring the emotional complexity of seeking fulfillment yet encountering limitations.
