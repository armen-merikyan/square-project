Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional landscape of envy versus gratitude through a palette reflecting warmth and depth. The sharp contrast between muted purples and vibrant yellows symbolizes the tension between feelings of desire and appreciation. The central clustering of deep blues represents concentration while the outer spaces echo a more muted, softer approach. This arrangement suggests a struggle from within, echoing the inward pull of these contrasting emotions, largely influenced by the seed 'tear' and its duality of fear and hope. The jagged edges of some pixels represent imperfection and the emotional noise that interrupts serenity.
