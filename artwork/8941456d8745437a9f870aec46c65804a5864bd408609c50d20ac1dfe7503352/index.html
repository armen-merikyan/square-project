Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between softness and severity. The palette, featuring soft powder pink and warm white against sharp crimson and deep carbon black, creates a striking contrast that conveys inner conflict. The layout of a narrow negative-space path is framed by dense rippled patterns of softness, interrupted by haloes of severity, suggesting a transition from calm to pressure. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, mirroring the unresolved emotions of guilt and forgiveness that the seed evokes. The central collision happens in the transition area, where chaos meets quietness, reflecting the ongoing struggle within oneself.
