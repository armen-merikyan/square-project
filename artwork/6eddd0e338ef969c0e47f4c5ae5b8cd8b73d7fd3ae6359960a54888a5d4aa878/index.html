Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes confusion and clarity, illustrated through a tension between dense, dark sections and lighter, more open spaces. The palette of red, black, pale blue, and white supports this conflict, with red providing intensity and the blacks grounding it in confusion. Light colors punctuate the darker areas, creating a rhythm that echoes the contrast of symmetry and break. This abstract arrangement suggests an unresolved motion—where clarity fights through chaos, represented by the 'broken' elements within a structured layout. The use of 'crack' patterns layered against 'frame' elements embodies this narrative well, presenting a visual story of oscillation between chaos and order.
