Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between rage and calm through a clash of colors and patterns. The palette combines deep reds and blacks representing rage with soft pale blues and whites for calm. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the chaotic 'patchwork' of reds aggressively invades the serene 'radial' flow of blues, creating a visual representation of emotional overflow against containment. The broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, reflecting the imperfections of emotional expression.
