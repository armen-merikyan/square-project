Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between joy and grief, represented by a mix of vivid colors and darker hues. The palette incorporates bright yellows and oranges to signify joy and muted purples and deep blues to embody grief. The interplay of vibrant dots against darker target motifs brings forth moments of collision, echoing the rising signal of emotions amidst the chaos. The zigzag pattern creates movement that balances between structured control and erratic disruption. Negative space is maintained to symbolize the underlying calm that often resurfaces amidst conflicting feelings, mirroring life's complexities.
