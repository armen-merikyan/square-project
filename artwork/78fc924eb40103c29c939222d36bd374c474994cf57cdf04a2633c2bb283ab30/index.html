Artist Statement

The emotional tone wrestles between dread and relief, captured through a dark palette invoking weight balanced by bright sections to signify hope. The colors represent ancient fire, where burnt oranges and dark reds clash sharply against hues of gold, creating a narrative tension of struggle and release. The asymmetrical arrangement introduces breaks in symmetry, reflecting the themes of disturbance and balance suggested by the seed. The bottom-heavy composition mirrors the climbing flow, suggesting a gradual rise from tension to a lighter resolution.
