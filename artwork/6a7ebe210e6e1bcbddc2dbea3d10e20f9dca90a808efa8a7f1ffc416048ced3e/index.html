Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts envy, depicted through cool dark tones, against gratitude's warmth, represented by brighter hues. The palette chosen enhances this contrast, with heavy use of black and pale blue for envy, while white introduces lightness and calm for gratitude. The zigzag pattern mimics an uneven pulse, reflecting the tension between these emotions, while the broken pixels add imperfections, symbolizing glimpses of a 'mirror broken'. The placement evokes a sense of an imbalance in the emotional landscape, creating visual echoes and interruptions.
