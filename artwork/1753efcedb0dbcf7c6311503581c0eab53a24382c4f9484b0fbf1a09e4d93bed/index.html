Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the duality of softness and severity, using a palette that balances gentle warmth and piercing darkness. The soft hues of powder pink and warm white collide with sharp crimson and carbon black in an asymmetric diagonal layout, establishing an engaging tension that reflects inner conflicts. The main contrast resides at the diagonal intersection, symbolizing a pulse of feeling amid chaotic static, creating a vivid narrative of struggle and release. Each broken pixel serves as a reminder of imperfection, enhancing the themes of guilt and forgiveness.
