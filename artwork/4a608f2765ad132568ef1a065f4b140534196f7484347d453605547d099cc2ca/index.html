Artist Statement

This composition portrays the duality of longing and resolve through contrasting textures and colors. The cool tones of ice blue and violet gray represent longing with their soft warmth, whereas the strong, defined areas of black and orange depict the resolve. The broken pixels add a sense of imperfection, mirroring the idea of damage amidst attempts at repair. The central focus is a collision of soft and sharp, creating tension that invites contemplation.
