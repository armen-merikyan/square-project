Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the collision between trust (represented by soft gold and warm ivory) and suspicion (embodied through deep wine and ash gray). The palette creates a tension, simultaneously evoking warmth and uneasiness. The chosen layout shows a pressure corner, suggesting an overflowing intensity that contrasts with quieter areas of the square, illustrating waves of noise against a backdrop of calm. The broken pixels intersperse the continuous patterns, adding a sense of imperfection and dissonance, embodying the chaotic nature of the emotions. The main contrast occurs in the bottom left corner, where warm tones collide with the cooler tones, signaling a struggle between safety and doubt.
