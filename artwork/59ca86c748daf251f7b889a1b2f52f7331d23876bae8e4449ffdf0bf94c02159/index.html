Artist Statement

This artwork explores the dynamic tension between softness and severity through a carefully chosen palette of warm whites and sharp blacks. The contrast creates a push-and-pull effect, symbolizing hunger versus restraint. The arrangement of colors emphasizes the collision between gentle veils and crisp thresholds, reflecting the spiral of conflicting emotions. The central area is denser with softer tones, gradually transitioning to sharp contrasts at the edges, maintaining a spacious negative area that embodies unresolved feelings. The irregularities signify the intersection of guilt and forgiveness, creating an abstract narrative of emotional complexity.
