Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between dread and relief. The palette features muted sepia tones and faded blues, which create a calming yet melancholic atmosphere. The contrast emerges through the placement of dark, solid colors on one side, representing dread, while lighter, broken dots scatter across the opposite side, symbolizing relief. The asymmetry enhances the conflict, resonating with the idea of a dark mass opposing lighter energies, echoing the seed's themes of balance and imbalance. The edge style suggests a fading into darker areas, reinforcing the emotional pressing weight.
