Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between control and surrender through the interplay of darker and lighter colors. The palette showcases a balance of clarity and confusion with contrasting hues of ink black and warning orange against a backdrop of ice blue and clean white. The central collision occurs where darker pixels converge, suggesting a build-up of pressure before a release into the lighter surrounding pixels, embodying emotional overflow and containment. The patterns of rings and targets reflect chaotic interruptions within a more orderly structure, perfectly aligning with the seed's duality.
