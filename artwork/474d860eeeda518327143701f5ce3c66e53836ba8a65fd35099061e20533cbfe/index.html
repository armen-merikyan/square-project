Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between certainty and curiosity, with the left side dominated by warm coral and blood orange tones, representing certainty and a direct pulse, while the right side showcases hollow blue and charcoal shades, invoking curiosity and echoed reflections. The main collision occurs at the center where the warm accents of the left disrupt the cool tranquility of the right, suggesting a tide against channels. Broken pixels are interspersed, symbolizing disruptions and the complexity of navigating between two emotional extremes.
