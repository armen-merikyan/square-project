Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a struggle between vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors and an asymmetric layout. The palette of warm coral and soft amber introduces warmth and humanity, while the cold slate and blue black create a sense of depth and tension. The main collision occurs in the center with a mix of dense blocks (representing strength) surrounded by softer dots (representing vulnerability), creating a visual echo of protection amidst exposure. The shape seed influences an abstract expression of shelter with a broken mirror effect, suggesting both structure and imperfection.
