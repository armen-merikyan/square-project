Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional clash of 'spark' and 'ash'. The palette combines warmth from 'warning_orange' and coolness from 'ice_blue', representing moments of ignition against fading memories. The intentional asymmetry creates a tension where bright colors disrupt darker tones, suggesting hunger and restraint. The layout focuses on a diagonal flow, with contrasting colors colliding in the center, evoking a struggle for balance and a deep emotional resonance with the seed.
