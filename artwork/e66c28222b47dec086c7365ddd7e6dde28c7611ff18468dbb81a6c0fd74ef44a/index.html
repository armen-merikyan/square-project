Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between envy and gratitude. The contrasting palette of electric blue and magenta symbolizes vibrant discontent against the calmness of black, while lime green introduces an unexpected brightness, suggesting hope. The jagged edges represent the clash of emotions, creating an asymmetric flow where darker tones sink while lighter tones rise, mirroring the concepts of weight versus lightness. The irregular placement of the broken pixels evokes a sense of disarray, emphasizing how both feelings coexist, resulting in a turbulent yet beautiful visual narrative.
