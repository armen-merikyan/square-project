Artist Statement

This abstract piece captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission. The color palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon represents the passionate conflict, where warm tones signify defiance against cooler, subdued tones symbolizing submission. The left side of the grid embodies defiance with a ring-like pattern, encircling the center, while the right side employs a broken frame, suggesting collapse and submission. The central area is intense with pulsating reds, creating a hard interruption against softer fields of gray. The uneven border conveys a sense of chaos, emphasizing the contrast between order and disorder.
