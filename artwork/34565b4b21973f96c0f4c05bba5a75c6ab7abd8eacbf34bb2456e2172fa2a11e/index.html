Artist Statement

The artwork reflects a tension between quiet and pressure, using a palette of soft and muted colors to create a sense of calmness that contrasts with tighter, darker areas representing pressure. The flow follows a diagonal invasion, suggesting a gradual encroachment of pressure from one corner, with the middle section acting as the main collision space. The arrangement creates a visual dialogue of density versus spaciousness, echoing the struggle between these emotions.
