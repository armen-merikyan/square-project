Artist Statement

This composition embodies a dialogue between softness and severity. The warm whites and powder pinks create a gentle resonance that contrasts sharply with the cold boundaries of carbon black and sharp crimson. The layout emphasizes a corner pressure, where dense clusters command attention, while the lighter tones unfurl toward the edges, hinting at open spaces. The broken dots pattern interacts with a mirror-severity effect, creating a rhythm that echoes the seed's themes of emotional tension and unresolvable conflicts.
