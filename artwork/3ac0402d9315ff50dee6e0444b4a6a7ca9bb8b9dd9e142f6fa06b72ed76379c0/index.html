Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between control and surrender through a patchwork of cosmic indigo and void black, layered with accents of cold gold and star white. The dominant dark tones represent control, while the bright accents signify moments of surrender, colliding in the center. The structured layout maintains a sense of flow, interrupted by broken pixels, suggesting fragmented memories in a sea of dense emotion.
