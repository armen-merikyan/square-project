Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the balance between trust and suspicion, using a palette that conveys awe and terror. The top half represents trust with a noise pattern, characterized by soft hues of cosmic indigo and deep teal set against chaotic black. The bottom half illustrates suspicion through a negative cross pattern, using stark whites and cold gold. The main collision occurs along a central diagonal, where dark and light colors intersect, creating tension and visual dialogue. This layout emphasizes the struggle between stability and fragmentation, embodying the abstract narrative of resilience against fragility.
