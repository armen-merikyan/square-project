Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dichotomy of defiance and submission through contrasting color placement. The warm burnt orange and dark red represent defiance, creating an intense focal point in the corner that symbolizes pressure and invasion, while cool shades of charcoal provide a quieter, enveloping backdrop of submission. The broken pixels disrupt the flow, embodying the scars of conflict. The layout directs a strong corner pressure, with the overall pattern suggesting a chaotic yet contained rhythm, creating resonance between dense clusters and empty spaces.
