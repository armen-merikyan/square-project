Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between the clear signals and overwhelming background noise. The palette features deep navy and black for the noise, contrasted with dim cyan and white to represent the signal. The layout showcases a dense cluster of dark colors in the center, mimicking an island of clarity surrounded by chaotic noise on the periphery. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, mirroring the theme of fragmentation and pulse amidst interference.
