Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a dynamic conflict between containment and liberation, mirrored in the arrangement of colors. The palette of ripe green and honey gold symbolizes potential growth and warmth against a backdrop of deep olive and acid green, representing pressure and tension. The central explosion of color suggests a release, while the softer outer hues provide a quiet contrast. The 'falling_pixels' layout allows the dense bursts of color to cascade, evoking the metaphor of water flowing freely while also hinting at the heaviness of unyielded emotion. The intention of broken pixels creates disruptions to enhance the theme of control versus surrender, adding depth to the abstract narrative.
