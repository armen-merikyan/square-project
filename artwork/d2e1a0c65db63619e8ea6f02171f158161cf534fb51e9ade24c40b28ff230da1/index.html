Artist Statement

This composition conveys the emotional tension between 'quiet' and 'pressure' through a contrasting layout. The palette includes muted colors like faint peach and off-white representing quietness alongside darker tones like dark navy and compressed red symbolizing pressure. The main collision occurs where the pressure dots emerge in dense clusters in the lower half, breaking against the softer, lighter hues above. This creates a visual rhythm resembling a rising signal, where the upward flow from bottom to top suggests hope and escape amidst layers of emotional complexity.
