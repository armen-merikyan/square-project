Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the tension between 'longing' and 'resolve', evoking a sense of yearning complemented by grounding stability. The chosen palette of ripe green, acid green, honey gold, and deep olive reflects a spectrum from hope to groundedness. The main contrast occurs vertically, with longing colors stimulating the left half while resolve colors fill the right, suggesting a push-pull dynamic. Broken pixels signify imperfections where longing fractures against a resolute base, creating a visual rhythm of interruption and quiet.
