Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between control and surrender. The palette of deep black and white serves as a stark contrast, creating a rhythm between density and quiet space. The thick outlines signify control while the loose patches of pale blue represent surrender. The central collision occurs where dark pixels converge, suggesting overflow, while lighter areas around the edges signify release, echoing the seed's themes.
