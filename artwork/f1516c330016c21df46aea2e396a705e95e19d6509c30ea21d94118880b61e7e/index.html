Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette, derived from fear and hope, uses dark violet to signify depths of loneliness, contrasted sharply against soft gold representing warmth and connection. The arrangement follows a layout that creates a rising sense of hope, with a heavier cluster at the bottom depicted through deeper hues. Asymmetrical placements serve to reflect interruptions in connectivity, with moments of soft resonance through pale green evoking hidden nuances within the overall narrative. The pattern is broken with faint, scattered pixels to signify imperfection, using negative space to underscore isolation.
