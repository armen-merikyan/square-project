Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between loneliness and connection through the interplay of warm and dark tones. The palette, featuring burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold, evokes feelings of ancient fire, suggesting both warmth and isolation. The center area heavily emphasizes negative space to denote loneliness, while the contrasting edges feature dense clusters, creating a sense of connection. The asymmetrical placement of warm tones within a maze-like structure leads to a feeling of interruption, symbolizing the bursts of connection in a sea of loneliness. Overall, this abstract piece captures the complexity of emotional interactions, featuring a balance of pressures and releases across the grid.
