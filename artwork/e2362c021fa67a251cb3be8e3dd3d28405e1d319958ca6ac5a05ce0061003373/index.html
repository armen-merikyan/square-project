Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a conflict between the heaviness of pressure and the stillness of quiet. The chosen palette of muted neutrals and darker shades symbolizes tension, with the darker colors representing the weight and crowding of pressure. The collision occurs mainly in the top-left corner, where dark colors abruptly disrupt a lighter, softer field, illustrating a wave of emotion breaking against quietness, while the broken pixels weave in organic interruptions, emphasizing the fragility of balance. The structure follows a diagonal flow, moving from density into expansive negative space, inviting a feeling of relief as the colors dissipate towards the bottom right.
