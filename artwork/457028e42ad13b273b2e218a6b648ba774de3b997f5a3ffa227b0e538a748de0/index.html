Artist Statement

This artwork captures the complex interplay between joy and grief. The palette features bright yellows and muted purples, symbolizing the struggle between light and darkness. The asymmetrical arrangement mirrors a pulse, reflecting life's rhythmic ups and downs. The top-weighted edge creates a sense of falling emotion, while the placement of broken pixels introduces imperfection, emphasizing how joy and grief can coexist in our feelings. The central focus is enriched by the contrast of warm and cool tones, generating a visual tension that narrates an abstract story of emotional conflict.
