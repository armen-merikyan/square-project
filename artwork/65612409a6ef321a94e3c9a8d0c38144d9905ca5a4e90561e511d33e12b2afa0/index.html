Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional contrasts of longing and resolve through a palette of muted and vibrant colors. The gentle hues of rose clay and distant lilac represent longing, whereas the sharp accents of lamp gold mirror resolve. The composition features a central collision of rich color noise and structured gradients, evoking a rising tension. Broken pixels fragment this narrative, symbolizing disruption and the quest for repair within a backdrop of serenity hinted by negative space. The falling pixel layout brings a sense of rhythm to the piece, enhancing the emotional dialogue between strength and fragility.
