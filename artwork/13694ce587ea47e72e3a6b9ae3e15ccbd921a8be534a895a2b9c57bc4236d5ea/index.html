Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension through the use of color contrasts representing longing and resolve. The palette of sepia and faded blues emphasizes the feeling of nostalgia intertwined with moments of clarity. The primary contrast occurs in the center, where dark sepia rings, symbolizing longing, collide with the crosshatch patterns of resolve in muted blues. This arrangement creates a visual pulse that resonates with themes of broken reflections and the struggle between repair and damage, while the intentionally broken pixels reflect moments of imperfection and fragility.
