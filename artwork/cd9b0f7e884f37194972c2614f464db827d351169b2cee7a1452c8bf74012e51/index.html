Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents a delicate balance between the feelings of loneliness and connection. The palette combines deep black with electric blue and vibrant magenta, symbolizing isolation and warmth. The main contrast occurs between dense clusters on the left and expansive emptiness on the right, portraying a 'left weighted' visual tension, reminiscent of unbalanced emotions. The asymmetry, along with broken pixels creates imperfection, subtly echoing memory, and suggesting the quiet yet tangible presence of connection amidst solitude.
