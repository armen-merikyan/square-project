Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the juxtaposition of vulnerability and strength through contrasting color placements. The chosen palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold provides a warm yet chaotic interplay, symbolizing the clash between strength (represented by bold, warm hues) and vulnerability (through softer and muted tones). The main collision occurs toward the center, creating a focal point amid the surrounding scattered pixels, embodying an echo of conflict and resolution. This arrangement encourages a sense of tension and layered emotion, compelling viewers to explore the connection and alienation inherent in these themes.
