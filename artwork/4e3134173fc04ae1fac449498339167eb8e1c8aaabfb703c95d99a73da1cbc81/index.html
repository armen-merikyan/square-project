Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that juxtaposes darker tones with bright accents. The layout features an exposed center surrounded by dense, dark colors that represent strength as they shift outward in ripple patterns. The contrast is stark, with broken pixels creating disruptiveness, symbolizing vulnerability amidst the steady collection of shadows. The use of static gray and dead black provides a foundational complexity, while the electric blue and hot magenta provide vibrant interruptions echoing moments of safety exposed against the tide of darker elements. The overall emotional theme captures an unresolved motion between safety and exposure, reflected in a spiraling outward flow.
