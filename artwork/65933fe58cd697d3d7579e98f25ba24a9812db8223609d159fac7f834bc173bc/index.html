Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition reflects the conflict between peace and restlessness. The palette incorporates deep blue and pale yellow to symbolize calm and vibrant memories, respectively. The pattern features a dense arrangement of broken dots (peace) in the center clashing against an irregular ring (restlessness) around the edges, creating a stark contrast that visually represents the tension in the seed. The quiet channel of negative space in the grid emphasizes the struggle between tranquility and scattered thoughts, capturing a moment of internal conflict.
