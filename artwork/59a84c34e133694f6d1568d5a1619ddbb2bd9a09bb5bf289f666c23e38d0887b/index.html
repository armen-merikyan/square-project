Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork captures a juxtaposition between loneliness and connection, employing a palette influenced by the warmth of 'ancient fire'. The left side of the composition utilizes a ring pattern, suggesting isolation through dark reds and charcoal, creating a heavy, somber mood. The right side contrasts this with a checker pattern in burnt orange and gold, evoking warmth and connection, creating a visual dialogue between the two emotions. The layout emphasizes a split vertical arrangement that reflects the inner conflict of feeling both weight and lightness. The placement of a few broken pixels enhances the sensation of fragmentation in memory and experience, while negative spaces maintain a sense of solitude amidst the patterns.
