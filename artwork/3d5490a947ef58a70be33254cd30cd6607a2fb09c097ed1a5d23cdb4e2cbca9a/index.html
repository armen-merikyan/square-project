Artist Statement

The artwork conveys the emotional tension between 'rage' and 'calm' through a contrasting palette of deep black, fiery red, and serene pale blue. The composition establishes a threshold where vibrant red 'shatter' patterns collide into the soft 'glitch' forms of pale blue, showcasing chaos against tranquility. The main collision occurs in the center, where intense colors erupt against quieter spaces, illustrating the struggle between containment and overflow. The asymmetrical placement of broken pixels emphasizes the eruption, creating an emotional landscape that invites viewers to explore the dichotomy of control versus chaos.
