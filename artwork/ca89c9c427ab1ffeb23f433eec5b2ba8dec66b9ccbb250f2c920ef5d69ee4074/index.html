Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between certainty and curiosity, reflected through a palette that balances the warmth of shelter green and warm brown with the cool calm of cold white and open blue. The palette supports a collision where saturated colors in the center convey intensity, while the edges fade into quiet fields, suggesting uncertainty. The main contrast occurs in the center with thick broken lines representing certainty, interrupted by small hints of curiosity via lighter accents. The arrangement echoes a rising signal amidst chaos, with pressure pulsing inwards but sharing space with recessing edges, embodying the essence of tension between calmness and the noise of unknowing.
