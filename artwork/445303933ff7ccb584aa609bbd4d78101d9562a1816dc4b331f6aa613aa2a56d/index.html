Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts clarity with confusion, represented through a split layout. The left half showcases burnt orange and warm tones to evoke clarity, while the right employs dark reds and charcoals, manifesting chaos. The palette's 'ancient fire' theme suggests intensity and depth, creating a push-pull effect. The collision occurs at the center line, highlighting the broken symmetry of the arrangement, allowing the viewer to navigate between feelings of calm and tumult. The overall pattern emerges from the interplay between structured warmth and disruptive coldness, echoing the seed's suggestion of dual emotions.
