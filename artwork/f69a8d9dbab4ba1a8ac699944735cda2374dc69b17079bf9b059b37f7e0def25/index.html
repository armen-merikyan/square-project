Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional pull between restlessness and peace. The palette features warm shadows mixed with cool blues, embodying tension and tranquility. The calm static patterns (peace) interrupt the dynamic flame-like elements (restlessness), with the main contrast center-stage from the top left to the bottom right. The arrangement evokes a narrative of memories fading into motion, with broken pixels creating disruptions that add depth and texture to the overall composition.
