Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional duality of rage and calm through an interplay of cool and warm colors. The palette contrasts deep blacks and fiery reds against pale blues, symbolizing the struggle between oppressive weight and delicate lightness. The jagged edges and broken pixel patterns reflect disruption and imperfection, while the rising and falling pixel layout portrays tension. The main collision point occurs through the center where the vibrant reds disrupt the cool blues, capturing the essence of the seed's themes of interruption and emotional conflict.
