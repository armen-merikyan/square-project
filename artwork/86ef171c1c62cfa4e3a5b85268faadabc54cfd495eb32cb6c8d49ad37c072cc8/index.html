Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the dichotomy of confusion and clarity, expressing a struggle between symmetry and its disruptions. By utilizing a palette of electric blue, magenta, and black, I create an energetic contrast that symbolizes clarity and confusion. The flow spirals outward, guiding the eye through contrasting areas of dense color and negative space, enhancing the sense of pulse and tension. The broken pixels introduced represent imperfections amid the pursuit of order, making clear divisive diagonals that scatter across an otherwise coherent grid. The arrangement suggests a narrative of volatility amidst calm, with collisions of warm magenta against cool black embodying conflict and resolution.
