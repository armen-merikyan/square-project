Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The soft pastel colors suggest a serene backdrop while darker shades pulse with urgency, reminiscent of a heartbeat. The collision occurs centrally with dark and light pixels, embodying both a broken and repaired thread. The chosen palette of pale mint, storm gray, and faded gold reflects light, softness, and subtle disarray, while the static longing pattern embraces the overwhelming resolve pattern. This tension creates a dynamic that feels both contained and overflowing, illustrating a narrative of incomplete resolution.
