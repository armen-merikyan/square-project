Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between restlessness and peace through a zigzag flow and diagonal layout. The chosen palette—shelter green and cold white for tranquility, and warm brown and pale skin for warmth—contrasts sharply against the too-cool, calm blue, depicting a restless interplay of emotions. The central collision arises from layered ripples of restlessness in the lower left, pushing against the protective, mirror-like symmetry of peace in the upper right, emphasizing the emotional battle.
