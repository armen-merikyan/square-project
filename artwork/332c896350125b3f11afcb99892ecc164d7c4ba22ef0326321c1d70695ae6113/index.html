Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that embodies quiet pressure. The colors mirror lightness against heavy depth, encapsulating the duality of exposure and shelter. The layout emphasizes a diagonal spread from a corner, allowing colors to collide and overlap, with the contrast primarily centered around the midsection, evoking a powerful dynamic. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, echoing the theme of vulnerability. This arrangement deliberately balances quiet spaces with dense clusters, mirroring emotional landscapes of desire and emptiness, maintaining a readable structure that invites contemplation.
