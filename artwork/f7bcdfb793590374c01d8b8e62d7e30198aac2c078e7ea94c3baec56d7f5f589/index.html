Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between 'spark' and 'ash', representing bursts of energy opposing moments of stillness. The palette of ripe green and cream serves to symbolize growth and serenity, while the darker hues introduce a sense of restraint and quietude. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the bright 'spark' colors clash with muted 'ash' tones, creating a dynamic interplay that resonates with the seed's themes of conflict and memory.
