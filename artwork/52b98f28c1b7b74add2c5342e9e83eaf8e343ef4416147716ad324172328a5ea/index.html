Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dance between tenderness and rage, represented by a diagonal layout where rage occupies the top left corner with bold, explosive colors. The palette of deep reds and muted blacks contrasts against soft pale blues and whites, illustrating the push and pull of these emotions. The broken pixels serve as scars across the surface, disrupting smoothness and evoking a sense of conflict. The main collision happens where tender colors try to penetrate the aggressive red, suggesting a quiet channel of hope amidst chaos. The seed's abstraction is evident in the use of stair-step patterns, indicating a climb through complex emotions, while the overall flow maintains a pulse of energy and contrast.
