Artist Statement

This abstract piece explores the emotional interplay of longing and resolve through the use of a contrasting palette of deep reds and pale blues. The dense black forms represent the weight of longing, while the lighter colors signify moments of resolve that punctuate through the background. The arrangement showcases a central axis of tension, with darker clusters at the edges and lighter hues intertwined, creating a dynamic push and pull within the square. Broken pixels appear intermittently, adding intentional disruptions that mirror the theme of repair and damage, while negative space offers a quiet contrast, inviting the viewer to ponder the balance between containment and overflow.
