Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends longing with resolve, reflecting a journey of tension that interacts vigorously yet tenderly. The palette of deep blue, muted purple, yellow, and orange signals feelings of warmth and urgency, creating contrast between the steady yet vibrant hues. The main collision occurs in the center of the grid, where the intense yellow and deep blue overlap, suggesting a pulse of energy amid a structured chaos. The patterns of rings and veils express the intertwined nature of these emotions, while broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections that mimic life’s disruptions, ensuring dynamic movement across the composition.
