Artist Statement

This pixel art embodies the emotional spectrum of softness versus severity, highlighted through contrasting colors that embody both warm and cool tones. Utilizing a palette of powder pink and sharp crimson for softness, juxtaposed with carbon black and steel gray for severity, the piece reveals a dynamic conflict. The center is dominated by darker shades to suggest protection and weight, while the surrounding lighter hues spiral outward, creating a sense of an open gate and movement. Broken pixels introduce imperfection into the maze-like severity, suggesting moments of interruption and forgiveness amidst the firm edges.
