Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of joy and grief, utilizing a palette that contrasts vivid yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples. This choice evokes a visual tension between light and shadow, representing the interplay between uplifting and heavy emotions. The layering of colors in a descending pattern mimics the notion of rain, suggesting tears or overwhelming feelings. The jagged edges create a sense of disruption while the organized arrangement introduces a semblance of order, paralleling the chaotic nature of grief contrasted with the simplicity of joy. The visual structure aims to reflect a heart fragmented yet striving for balance and connection amidst emotional turbulence.
