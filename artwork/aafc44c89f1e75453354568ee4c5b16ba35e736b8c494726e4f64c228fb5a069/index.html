Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between vulnerability and strength, illustrated with a palette of dark violet for depth and fear, soft gold for hope, pale green representing the delicate balance of exposure and shelter, and black for intensity and strength. The main collision occurs in the center, where gentle shatter-like breaks interrupt dense clusters of dots, showcasing the struggle between two pulses of emotional states—emphasizing the sharpness of hard edges clashing against vulnerability. This arrangement symbolizes a dynamic interplay of internal and external forces.
