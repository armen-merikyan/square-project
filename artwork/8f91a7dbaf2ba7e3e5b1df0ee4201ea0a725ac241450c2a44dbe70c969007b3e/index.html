Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts 'awe' with 'terror,' embodied in the interplay of warm and dark shades. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold reflects a landscape of heated conflict and mystery. The hard edges evoke a sense of instability, with broken pixels punctuating the otherwise ordered chaos. The flow from top to bottom mimics a spiral of rising tension, while the recurring patterns of ring and noise create a dialogue between the sublime and the fearful. The collision occurs at the center where opposing emotions meet, creating a visual rift that suggests both harmony and disruption.
