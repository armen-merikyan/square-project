Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between clarity and confusion, using a palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple to evoke warmth against melancholy. The outer edges symbolize clarity with bold yellow and deep blue, while the inner grid employs split-screen patterns of orange and muted purple to reflect confusion. The layout intentionally employs broken pixels to disrupt symmetry, illustrating the conflict in perception. The main contrast occurs between the warm, welcoming outer colors and the cooler, fragmented inner colors, which embodies a sense of unresolved tension and emotional complexity.
