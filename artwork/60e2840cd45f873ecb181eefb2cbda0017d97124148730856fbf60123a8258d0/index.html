Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a clash of colors and patterns. The bright accents of pink and red represent defiance, sharply contrasting with the muted tones of gray and dark maroon symbolizing submission. The layout, resembling an open gate, allows a chaotic flow of pixelated glitches to disrupt the otherwise calm veil pattern, creating a dynamic interplay that echoes the seed's themes. The central chaos meets a refracting quietness at the edges, representing the struggle between order and chaos, with intentional broken pixels adding to the disruption.
