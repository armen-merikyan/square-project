Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the tension of control against the ease of surrender. The warm palette of yellows and oranges counters the coolness of deep blue and muted purple, introducing a dynamic contrast that symbolizes pressure and release. The center is designed to appear overfilled and saturated, while the outer edges express containment. The collision of dense colors in the center with pale rippling patterns creates a feeling of unresolved motion and complexity.
