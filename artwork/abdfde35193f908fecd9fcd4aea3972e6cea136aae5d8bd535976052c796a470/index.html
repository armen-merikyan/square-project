Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between confusion and clarity through a fragmented yet engaging 8x8 pixel grid. The palette, featuring deep blacks (#000000), vibrant reds (#FF0000), and soft blues (#A3C1DA), reflects the emotional dichotomy—a clash between intensity and calmness. The broken layout includes patches of noise and symmetrical forms, disrupting the order which hints at underlying chaos. The predominant contrast occurs in how the black edges display a stark barrier, emphasizing the laid-back blues and explosive reds that evoke fluctuating emotions. Overall, the piece conveys a story of oscillation between chaos and peace, mirroring the sentiments from the seed.
