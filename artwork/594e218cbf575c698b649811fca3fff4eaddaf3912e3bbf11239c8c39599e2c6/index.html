Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between joy and grief, utilizing a palette of muted purple to symbolize grief and vibrant yellow and orange to represent joy. The contrast emerges sharply at the horizontal split, with dense clusters of warm colors symbolizing joy at the upper half and scattered, cooler tones symbolizing grief below. The interplay of broken pixels introduces imperfections, akin to how emotions often collide and disrupt, evoking a sense of fragmentation yet connection. The wave-like arrangement encourages visual flow, suggesting an ebb and flow of feelings, characterized by sharp transitions that reflect life's complex emotional landscape.
