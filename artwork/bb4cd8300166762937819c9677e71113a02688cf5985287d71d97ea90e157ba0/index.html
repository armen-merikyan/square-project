Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes awe and terror, leading to an exploration of contrasts. The selected palette embodies shades of sepia, tan, and dust gray to evoke feelings of nostalgia and fragility. The arrangement employs a split screen concept, creating an inner quiet space surrounded by external chaos, resulting in a tension between dense, darker edges and lighter, broken pixels within. The 'tiny face' element subtly influences the central focus but remains abstract, leaving space open for individual interpretation of emotion.
