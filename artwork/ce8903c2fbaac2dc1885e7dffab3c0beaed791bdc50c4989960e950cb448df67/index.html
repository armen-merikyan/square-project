Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between feelings of emptiness and desire, illustrated through a split vertical layout. The warm hues (yellow, orange) on the left represent desire, contrasted against cool tones (deep blue, muted purple) on the right that evoke a sense of emptiness. The checker pattern symbolizes the clashing energy of desire, while the dotted pattern suggests an absence. The burned edges suggest fragility and imperfection, reinforcing the theme of collision and tension between warmth and coolness. The arrangement suggests a rising signal transitioning from a dense cluster of desire to quiet spaces of emptiness, echoing the seed's emotional narrative.
