Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dance between vulnerability and strength, conveyed through a palette rich in contrast. The dominant dark tones evoke a sense of safety, while soft gold and pale green serve as bright accents, symbolizing exposure and hope. The main collision occurs at the center, where the lighter colors clash with the darkened edges, illustrating the struggle between protection and exposure. The seed's essence shaped this dynamic pattern as a metaphor for the delicate balance between fragility and resilience.
