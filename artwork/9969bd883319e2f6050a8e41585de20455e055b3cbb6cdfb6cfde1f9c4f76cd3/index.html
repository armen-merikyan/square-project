Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates an interplay between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that suggests grounding (iron gray, muted cream) against the potential chaos of exposure (blackened plum, red oxide). The dominant darker hues create a central tension, surrounded by lighter colors that denote fragility and contrast, while maintaining 'overfilled_center' composition as the main conflict. The soft light (muted cream and small orange) suggest a rising signal against a backdrop of darkness and control, illustrating a narrative of emergence and retreat, where hard edges create certainty amidst vulnerability.
