Artist Statement

This artwork explores the interplay between quiet and pressure through a palette that harmonizes soft tones with deep contrasts. The use of off-white, dust gray, and faint peach offers a calm underpinning while compressed red and dark navy introduce intensity. The layout follows a spiral pattern, with dense colors clustering towards the bottom left, creating a weighted feel that contrasts against the lighter upper right, embodying the emotional push and pull from quietude to tension. This visual narrative reflects a gradual release juxtaposed with pockets of density, mirroring the complexities of clarity and confusion.
