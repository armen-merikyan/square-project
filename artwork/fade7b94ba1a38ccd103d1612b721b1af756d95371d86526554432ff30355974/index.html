Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through a palette of warm red and cool gray tones. The outer edges create an impression of isolation with darker, muted colors, representing weight and broken memories. The inner 4x4 core, using brighter pinks, symbolizes the fragility and hope of connection, creating a vital contrast. The placement of the colors creates a sense of echo and interruption, suggesting the push and pull of human relationships. The asymmetric arrangement signifies disconnection, while subtle overlaps evoke the complexity of emotions. This division visually echoes the seed’s themes through its abstract expression.
