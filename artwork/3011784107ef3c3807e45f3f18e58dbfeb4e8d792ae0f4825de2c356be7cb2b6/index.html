Artist Statement

This artwork navigates the emotional binary between vulnerability and strength through a color palette that reflects fragility (pale green) against stability (dark violet). The play of broken dots and static patterns creates a collision at the center, symbolizing the tension of contrasting emotions. The upward flow mirrors hope amidst fragility, while the darker edges establish a sheltering boundary, leaving negative space that highlights the exposed center. These elements culminate in an abstract expression of the themes present in the seed.
