Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between clarity and interference, representing a central pulse that struggles through static. The palette of signal green, electric blue, and hot magenta contrasts with the shadowy tones of dead black and static gray, illustrating the conflict of order (signal) versus chaos (noise). The arrangement utilizes broken pixels to symbolically show disruptions in the signal, creating a dynamic pattern where clear accents emerge amidst a chaotic backdrop. The primary collision occurs at the center, where the vibrant pulse confronts the surrounding noise, echoing the seed's themes of tension and interruption. The balance of dense clusters in the corners and negative space in the center allows for visual breathing room, mirroring the struggle of maintaining clarity in a cacophony.
