Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tensions between love and betrayal through a careful arrangement of color. The warm hues of pink and red represent love, while the dark maroon adds an element of emotional weight and betrayal. The contrast of dense patterns in certain regions against the quiet, negative space suggests a pulse of life and emotional conflict. The use of stripes and sparks plays into the rhythm of love's warmth contrasting with the jagged interruptions of betrayal. The overall composition employs an almost chaotic arrangement, reflecting the complexity of relationships and emotions.
