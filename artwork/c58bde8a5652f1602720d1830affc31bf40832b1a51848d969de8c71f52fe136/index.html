Artist Statement

The emotional tone suggests a conflict between defiance and submission. The palette of shelter green, warm brown, pale skin, cold white, and open blue creates a visual representation of warmth meeting coolness, echoing the tension of opposing feelings. The center of the square colored with bolder tones (defiance) contrasts with the softer edges (submission), representing the struggle between a protective core and invasive outer influences. The intentional use of broken pixels adds an element of chaos within structured segments, reflecting the sensation of interferences. The inner 4x4 block comprises dense noise patterns (submission) while the edges showcase sharper teeth-like interruptions (defiance), symbolizing the collision of energies.
