Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between clarity and distortion, represented by a palette of deep navy and muted cyan against stark blacks and whites. The primary contrast arises from the center, where clear 'signal' shapes collide with chaotic 'noise', symbolizing disrupted communication. The layout of falling pixels suggests a descent into chaos yet retains a stable core, embodying the seed's theme of broken mirrors and the tension within structured environments. The arrangement of colors reflects an imbalanced yet readable structure, echoing the theme of imperfection in signal versus noise.
