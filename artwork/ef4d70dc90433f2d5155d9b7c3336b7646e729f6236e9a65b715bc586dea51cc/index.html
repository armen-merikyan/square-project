Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the duality of awe and terror by contrasting stable geometric patterns with broken dots representing chaos. The palette includes deep reds and blacks juxtaposed with softer pale blue and white, illustrating the emotional tension. The central area is more ordered, representing awe, and slowly transitions to chaotic broken elements at the edges, reflecting terror. This interplay encapsulates the struggle between stability and instability, creating an evocative narrative through the pixel arrangement.
