Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between love and betrayal, embodied through contrasting palettes that evoke warmth against cooler, darker elements. The choice of pink and red shades symbolizes love, while dark maroon signifies betrayal, creating an emotional collision. The layout is intentionally asymmetric, leaving negative spaces that suggest emptiness while a sharp zigzag flow through the composition mimics the impact of a broken connection. The pattern reflects the idea of paired pixels, reinforcing intimacy yet interrupted, signifying distance and unresolved feelings.
