Artist Statement

The emotional tone is captured by the clash between defiance and submission. The palette incorporates a balance of pale aqua and alarm red, representing a gentle peace contrasted by urgent disruption. The center features a dense collection of alarm red and dark inks that pulsate outward into more muted tones, symbolizing conflict at the heart of tranquility. The 'diamond' shape proposed is abstractly suggested through the organized rhythm in the center, while broken pixels introduce ideas of glitch and interruption. The visual focal point creates tension and movement, with visual echoes of rising chaos meeting the calming edge, portraying the struggle between control and disarray.
