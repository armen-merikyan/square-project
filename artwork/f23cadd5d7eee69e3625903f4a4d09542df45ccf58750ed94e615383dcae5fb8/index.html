Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between rage and tenderness, with color dynamics emphasizing this duality. The palette of red and black invokes intensity while pale blue offers a moment of calm, reflecting the contrast of subdued tenderness against explosive anger. The collision at the center indicates a struggle, with dense, jagged patterns of red representing rage sharply interrupting softer, broken frames of blue. The layout embodies a crossing current of emotions, allowing space for reflection and release amidst the chaos. Broken pixels disrupt symmetry, enhancing the theme of internal conflict and resolution.
