Artist Statement

This composition navigates the emotional landscapes of 'longing' and 'resolve.' The chosen palette of moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold reflects both fragility and resilience. The top left is dense in deep tones, symbolizing a yearning void, while the bottom right features lighter hues signaling a sense of resolution. The collision occurs through the interplay of braids and floods, mirroring the pull of longing against the strong currents of resolve, while broken pixels disrupt the flow, mirroring the seed's themes of disruption and repair.
