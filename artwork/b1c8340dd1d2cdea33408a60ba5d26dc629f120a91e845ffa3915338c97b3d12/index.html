Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional struggle between fear and hope. The palette of deep blacks and dark violets creates a sense of foreboding, while the softer tones of pale green and soft gold introduce a glimmer of hope and positivity. The left half features darker, jagged patterns symbolizing fear and compression, while the right employs broken dots with lighter colors, hinting at rising positivity. The central zigzag flow portrays the tension of these emotions, embodying a visual narrative of struggle towards light amid darkness, with a subtle imperfection mimicking broken resolution.
