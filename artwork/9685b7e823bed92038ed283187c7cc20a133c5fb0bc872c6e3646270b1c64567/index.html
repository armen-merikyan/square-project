Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between chaos and order through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette incorporates electric blue, magenta, and black to symbolize technology and emotion, creating a stark contrast between control (black) and panic (electric blue and magenta). The layout features pixels that descend from the top, signifying a release from control, while the grid pattern for panic suggests rigidity. The arrangement includes broken patterns to evoke imperfection and a sense of struggle. Key contrasts arise where sharp edges meet softer colors, emphasizing the conflict between these emotions.
