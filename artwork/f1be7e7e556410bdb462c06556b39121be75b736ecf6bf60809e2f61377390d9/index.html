Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between certainty and curiosity using a palette that transitions from a serene aqua to an alarm red. The light colors envelop a darker core, representing a protective halo around uncertainty. The arrangement employs a fault line pattern to convey the rigidity of certainty contrasted by a split-screen effect, introducing curiosity with unexpected marks. The collision of hues is centered in the middle, creating a dynamic pulse, implying conflict and resolution within the context of a rising signal or tensioning tide.
