Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a juxtaposition of tension and tranquility. The palette, combining deep reds with muted creams, evokes a sense of conflict (restlessness) against a peaceful backdrop. The striking accents interrupt and ignite the composition, emphasizing the emotional collision. The main contrast occurs diagonally across the grid, where rich tones meet softer hues, capturing the essence of unresolved movement. This translates into layered fragmented patterns that mirror both memories and fluidity, echoing the seed's themes of restlessness versus peace.
