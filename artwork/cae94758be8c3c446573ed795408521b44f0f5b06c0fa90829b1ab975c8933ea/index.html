Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional struggle between vulnerability and strength. The palette features hot coral and blood orange as vibrant accents, signifying strength and urgency, contrasted with cooler shades like charcoal and hollow blue, representing vulnerability. The central collision occurs with a dense cluster of warm colors surrounded by cooler tones, evoking a fluid dynamic of pressure versus freedom. The diamond shape seeds a feeling of sharpness and intensity, layered with patterns that playfulness that reflect the push and pull of emotional states.
