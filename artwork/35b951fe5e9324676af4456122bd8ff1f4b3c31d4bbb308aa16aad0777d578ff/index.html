Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between stability and chaos, reflecting panic and control. The chosen palette of deep blacks, muted violets, and soft gold reflects fear and hope, capturing the nuances of emotional tension. The dominant dark tones represent control, while intermittent golds and greens symbolize sparks of panic, with density drawn toward the edges. The arrangement conveys an upward vs downward motion, with contrasting colors clashing yet harmonizing in their instability, narrating the interplay of emotions informed by the seed.
