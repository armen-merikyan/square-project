Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional duality of safety and exposure, represented through a contrasting color palette that balances soft browns and greens with vibrant touches of pale skin and open blue. The central density hints at an inner vulnerability, while the edges maintain a thin but present barrier, creating a sense of tension and letting the viewer feel both safety and exposure simultaneously. The layout allows for a collision at the center, where the warm brown dominates, reflecting a protective core amidst a lattice of other colors. Broken pixels disrupt the continuity, adding intentional imperfections that echo the complexities of navigating safety and exposure.
