Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between softness and severity, represented by a palette that oscillates between gentle pastels and stark blacks. The palette includes powder pink for softness, warm white for openness, and carbon black for severity, with sharp crimson adding a jarring accent. The main collision occurs in the center, where the gentle flow of the negative cross pattern meets the strictness of the crosshatch, suggesting a pulse amidst noise. This abstract structure embodies the struggle of clarity in a chaotic space, creating a dynamic interplay throughout the square.
