Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, highlighted through a palette of mourning black and deep blue portraying depth and introspection, contrasted by pale yellow for warmth and hope. The layout features a quiet channel of negative space that flows through dense patterns, symbolizing safety amid exposure. The contrast between thick grid-like structures representing strength and soft split-screen areas evoking vulnerability creates an unsettling yet harmonizing collision at the center. The broken pixels serve as an intentional disruption, enhancing the feeling of imperfection and resilience, weaving the emotional narrative of struggle and stability.
