Artist Statement

This composition embodies the struggle between pressure and quiet, where compressed density on the left is met with soft, muted colors on the right. The palette of dark navy, faint peach, and compressed red juxtaposes pressure against subtlety, creating a rhythmic tension throughout. The use of a narrow channel of negative space invites viewers to reflect on the quiet amidst density, while the broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, symbolically shattering the tension. The central collision of these themes occurs diagonally, where the eye travels from the dense left side to the more open right, embodying the emotional journey of conflict and resolution.
