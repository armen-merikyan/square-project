Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the contrast between envy and gratitude, visualized through a split layout. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon articulates this tension—warm hues signify envy while deeper shades evoke gratitude's weight. The left side features dotted patterns reflecting envy’s sporadic nature, while the right employs a sparking texture to express gratitude’s illumination, creating a visual dialogue. The layout and edge styles guide the viewer's eye inward, enhancing the feeling of internal conflict and resolution.
