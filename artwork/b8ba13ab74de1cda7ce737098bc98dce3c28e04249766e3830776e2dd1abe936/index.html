Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between vulnerability and strength, with the palette creating tension through warm and cool tones. The colors evoke desire and emptiness, creating a narrative of safety versus exposure. The central arrangement features layers of strong braid patterns colliding with flame-like vulnerabilities at the edges, invoking a sense of both protection and risk. The main contrast occurs at the center where the strength colors, like hot coral and blood orange, clash against the vulnerability of hollow blue and dust pink, creating a dynamic tension that suggests both cohesion and fragmentation.
