Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between defiance and submission, reflected in the clash of bold versus subdued colors. The chosen palette of cosmic indigo, void black, star white, cold gold, and deep teal emphasizes a cosmic void that runs beneath the surface tension, while broken pixels signify moments of imperfection and tension. The main contrast occurs in the center where a block of deep teal disrupts a chaotic background of indigo and black, reflecting the emotional collision of strength against surrender. This configuration creates both pressure and a quiet pulse, encapsulating the dynamic tension within the seed's abstract narrative.
