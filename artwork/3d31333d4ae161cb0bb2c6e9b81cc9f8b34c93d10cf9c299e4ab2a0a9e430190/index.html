Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between love and betrayal through the contrasting colors of warmth (pinks and reds) representing love against cool grays and dark maroon symbolizing betrayal. The palette is intentionally selected to evoke feelings of warmth and depth, while the layout features a symmetrical outer border with a corrupted inner core depicting emotional conflict. The edges exhibit sharpness while embracing a fragmented feel to illustrate brokenness. The diagonal flow contributes to a sense of rise and fall, suggesting the unresolved dynamics between the two emotions.
