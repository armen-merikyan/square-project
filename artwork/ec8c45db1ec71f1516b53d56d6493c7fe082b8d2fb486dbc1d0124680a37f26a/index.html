Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a cacophony of noise against a singular, clear pulse. The color palette combines vibrant green and electric blue as signals with muted shades of gray and black representing noise. The primary contrast occurs at the center, where the bright colored maze representation of signal collides with overlapping, halo-like noise that distorts the edges. This interplay creates a vibrant yet tense visual narrative of clarity battling against interference, embodying the essence of the seed's themes of conflict and interaction.
