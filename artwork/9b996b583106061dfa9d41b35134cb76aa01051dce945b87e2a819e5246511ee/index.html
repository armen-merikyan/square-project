Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional essence of 'collisions of emotion' through a palette that oscillates between warm, aggressive tones and cool, soothing colors. The blacks and greys express pressure and density, while pops of vibrant colors symbolize moments of release and joy. The primary contrast occurs in the center with brighter colors disrupting the muted tones, reflecting the inherent tension of emotional collisions. The alternating pattern creates a visual rhythm, portraying the push and pull of feelings.
