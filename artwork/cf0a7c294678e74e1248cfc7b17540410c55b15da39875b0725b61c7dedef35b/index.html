Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between vulnerability and strength, represented through warm brown and pale skin tones contrasting with the coolness of open blue and cold white. This palette evokes a sense of safety within exposure. The main contrast occurs in the center where the strength of dark warm colors meets the delicate cracks of the pale colors, resembling the fragility of an exposed shelter. In this 8x8 grid, the outer pixels reflect a resilient protective layer, while the contrasting center suggests a soft break, echoing the seed's themes of protection and exposure.
