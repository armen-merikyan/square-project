Artist Statement

The emotional tone intertwines defiance against submission, creating a clash of contrasting energies. The chosen palette of soft grays and a striking red in the center embodies this tension; the warm hues signify presence while cool tones suggest retreat. The main collision occurs in the center, where a dark navy core is encircled by softer fields, allowing the narrative of protection and vulnerability to emerge. The abstract pattern is shaped by uneven placements, broken pixels, and an inherent dichotomy that reflects the struggle between order and chaos, represented by the ordered repetitions of off-white and dust gray against a bold contrasting red. The design maintains visual clarity while ensuring emotional depth through static pulses and field variations that drive engagement and contemplation.
