Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between feelings of panic and control. The palette of deep reds and stark whites evokes a sharp contrast, symbolizing tension and clarity. Colors are layered to create a 'rising signal' effect, with sharp contrasts between controlled dots and chaotic crosshatching. Dark colors dominate, suggesting a pressure cooker effect, with bursts of white signifying moments of clarity. The zigzag pattern indicates conflict, and the arrangement denotes the challenge of finding harmony within chaos.
