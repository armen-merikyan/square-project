Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the duality of quiet and pressure, capturing a symphony of contrasts. The 'quiet' elements are symbolized by faint peach and off-white hues, setting a soft foundation that represents calmness and subtlety. In contrast, the rich dark navy and compressed red serve to evoke tension and energy, creating an atmosphere that oscillates between serenity and the weight of pressure. The layout, influenced by a broken frame, establishes a sense of restriction at the edges where the dark colors congregate, while the lighter colors tend to flow towards the center, suggesting a release from pressure. This dynamic interaction conveys a visual narrative of tension and relaxation, enriched by deliberate disruptions from broken pixels. The overall composition remains abstract and avoids literal interpretations, focusing instead on the interplay of colors and emotions.
