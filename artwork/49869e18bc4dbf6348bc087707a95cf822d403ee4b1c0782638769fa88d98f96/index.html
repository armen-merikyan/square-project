Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between restlessness and peace, capturing the feeling of a dynamic struggle. The color palette consists of deep blues and blacks to convey restlessness contrasted with pale yellows, symbolizing moments of peace. The arrangement of pixels is dense in the bottom left, suggesting pressure, and loosens towards the top right, representing release. The diagonal flow of colors illustrates motion and direction, creating a visual argument between the two conflicting emotions. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, mirroring moments of chaos within calm.
