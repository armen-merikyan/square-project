Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between clarity (signal) and distortion (noise), utilizing a palette of deep navy and muted dim cyan to suggest a sense of isolation amid the chaos. The center pulses with bright white accents, representing clear communication amidst a static backdrop of darker shades. The arrangement showcases a radial pattern amidst a stair-like disruption, emphasizing the collision between structured order and chaotic interference. Negative space is strategically left to enhance readability, ensuring the square communicates the abstract, emotive contrast of human connection versus machine disruption.
