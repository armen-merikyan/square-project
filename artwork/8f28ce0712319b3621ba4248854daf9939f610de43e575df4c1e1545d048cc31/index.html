Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional duel between vulnerability and strength through a juxtaposition of light and darker tones. The palette of soft gold, ash gray, stain brown, and deep wine creates a gentle yet weighted atmosphere, with muted colors reflecting the tension of exposure against shielding. The center reveals a warm ivory hue, reminiscent of fragile light, surrounded by fault lines of deeper colors suggesting strength struggling against the flow of vulnerability. The layout features an open edge that invites contrast into the square, presenting a collision of feelings where light delicately interacts with darker layers, creating a narrative of safety intermingling with risk.
