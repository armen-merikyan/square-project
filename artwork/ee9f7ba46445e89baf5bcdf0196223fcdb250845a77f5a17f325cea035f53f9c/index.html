Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between control and surrender, illustrated through color clashes between the warm, vibrant palette of reds and pinks, representing emotional overflow, and the cooler, grounding grays that embody containment. The central collision is marked by a bright pink square amidst subdued gray, resembling the struggle between uncontained expression and an urge for structure. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions that echo the narrative of oscillating balance between pressure and release, making this composition a vivid representation of the seed's abstract qualities.
