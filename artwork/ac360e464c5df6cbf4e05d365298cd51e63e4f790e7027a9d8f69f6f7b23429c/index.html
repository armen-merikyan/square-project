Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional dichotomy of relief and dread, using a palette of soft sky and storm gray to evoke tension. The dominant dark mass represents dread, which occupies the majority of the square, while the lighter islands create a sense of relief amidst the chaos. The interplay of muted backgrounds with vibrant accents displays a collision of feelings, channeling the seed's themes of pressure and broken lines into an abstract pattern that conveys struggle and release.
