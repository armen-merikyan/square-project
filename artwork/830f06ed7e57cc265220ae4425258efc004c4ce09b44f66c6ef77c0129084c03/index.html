Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between the overwhelming sense of panic and the need for control. The palette leans towards muted tones of sepia, tan, and dust gray to create an atmosphere of quiet melancholy interrupted by hints of faded blue, symbolizing moments of clarity amidst chaos. The primary contrast occurs where calm sepia hues meet aggressive noise patterns, representing conflict. This interplay embodies the abstract story of a mind oscillating between stability and turbulence. Each row embodies rising pressure and moments of release as colors ebb and flow, creating both tension and occasional harmony.
