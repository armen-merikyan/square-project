Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the conflict between love and betrayal, resulting in a palette of warm reds and soft grays to symbolize tenderness marred by dark maroon, suggesting tension. The chaotic arrangement of patterns, inspired by 'broken_dots' for love and 'spark' for betrayal, creates a dance of control and disarray. The contrast between dense clusters in warm tones against cooler, empty spaces evokes a narrative of rising emotional intensity clashing with moments of stillness. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, reinforcing the concept of fractured connections.
