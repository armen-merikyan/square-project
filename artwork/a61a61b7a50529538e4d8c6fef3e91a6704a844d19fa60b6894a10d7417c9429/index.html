Artist Statement

The emotional tone portrayed here is a juxtaposition of longing and resolve, illustrated through contrasting patterns and colors. The palette includes pale aqua and mist gray for calmness, punctuated by alarm red as an accent signifying urgency. The outer border showcases a static pattern of longing, while the inner core reflects a constellation of resolve. The broken pixels represent the scars of experience, echoing the idea of repair against damage. The main collision occupies the center, where the static longing pattern meets dynamic resolve, creating a pulse of interaction that whispers both calm and pressure.
