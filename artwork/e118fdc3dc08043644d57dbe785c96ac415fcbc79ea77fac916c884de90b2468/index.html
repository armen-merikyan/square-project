Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a palette of warm charcoal and burnt orange, symbolizing both calm vulnerability and the intensity of strength. The main contrast occurs in the center, where light colors emerge to indicate exposure, surrounded by darker bands representing shelter. The pattern features islands of warmth breaking through static patches, creating collisions that evoke feelings of both connection and alienation, with broken pixels adding a sense of imperfection, emphasizing the struggle within this emotional landscape.
