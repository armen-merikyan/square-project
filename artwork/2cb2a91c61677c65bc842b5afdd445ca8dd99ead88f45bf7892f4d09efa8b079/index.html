Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a battle between tenderness, represented by muted blues and whites, and rage, depicted through saturated reds and blacks. The color palette creates a contrast where soft, gentle overlaps of tenderness clashing with explosive, jagged formations of rage evoke a sense of turmoil and unresolved tension. The layout, an island of chaotic red on a sea of calm, enhances the feelings of containment and overflow. The center of the square serves as the main collision point, where jagged red patterns invade the tranquil field, suggesting a struggle between conflicting emotions.
