Artist Statement

The artwork expresses the duality of confusion and clarity, using a palette that features deep reds and grays to create emotional tension. The central area displays strong contrast, where bold red teeth patterns interrupt muted gray backgrounds, creating a sense of collision between chaos and order. This is enhanced by the rising layout, suggesting motion upward from a base of confusion towards potential clarity, while leaving necessary negative space to prevent overwhelm. The broken pixels symbolize imperfection, suggesting that clarity is always intertwined with confusion. Overall, the composition reflects the emotional complexity of navigating inner turmoil.
