Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between defiance and submission, depicted through a stark juxtaposition of colors. The palette includes deep black and blue representing defiance, set against the muted tones of pale yellow and stone that hint at submission. The central keyhole shape creates a protective yet confrontational space, while broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, embodying the tension of the theme. The collision occurs prominently in the center, where the contrasting patterns of islands and crosshatch interlock, symbolizing the battle between chaos and order, inviting the viewer to navigate the emotional landscape.
