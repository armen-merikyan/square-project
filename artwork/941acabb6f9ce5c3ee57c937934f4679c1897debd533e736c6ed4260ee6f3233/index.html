Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette, inspired by ancient fire with burnt orange and dark red, signifies warmth when connection prevails, while the charcoal and gold hint at the quiet void of isolation. The arrangement features a dense cluster of darker tones on the left, symbolizing weight and loneliness, contrasted by scattered lighter hues suggesting fleeting connections on the right. This imbalance evokes the struggle, with 'broken pixels' adding imperfection to the narrative, enhancing the emotional depth. The overall flow moves from left to right, illustrating the journey from isolation towards an attempt at connection, creating a dynamic visual resonance.
