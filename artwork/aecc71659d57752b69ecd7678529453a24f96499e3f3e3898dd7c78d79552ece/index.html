Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of love and betrayal through a structured layout of contrasts. The warm palette of pink and red evokes love, while the dark maroon introduces an undercurrent of betrayal. The fragmented grid emphasizes tension, with areas of density representing emotional turmoil and negative space allowing for introspection. The collision occurs where the warm colors meet the darker hues, hinting at the struggle between harmony and conflict. The fracturing pattern symbolizes the complexities of relationships where affection is often intertwined with pain.
