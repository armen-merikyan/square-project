Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between love and betrayal through a dichotomy of warm and cool colors. The palette of soft pinks and deep maroons suggests warmth and tenderness, contrasted by grays that evoke a sense of isolation and imbalance. Main contrasts appear in the diagonal flow, where warm tones collide against cool, creating a sense of emotional conflict. The patterns, representing love's mirrored emotion against the unpredictability of betrayal, further accentuate this contrast. The placement of broken pixels adds a feeling of imperfection, hinting at unresolved sentiments.
