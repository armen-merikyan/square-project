Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between loneliness and connection, visualized through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of warm yellows and oranges suggests fleeting moments of connection, while deep blue and muted purple embody the weight of loneliness. The center is a bright, singular dot, symbolizing isolation, surrounded by darker tones that represent broken memories, creating a dynamic clash. The rising layout mimics a hopeful emergence from despair, while the soft edges maintain a sense of vulnerability throughout.
