Artist Statement

The composition reflects the dichotomy of tenderness and rage through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette mixes saturated reds and muted blues, illustrating the intensity of rage against the softness of tenderness. The dominant red forms jagged stripes, while the calming blue appears in a ladder pattern, creating a tension that culminates in a central collision. The layout flows from dense emotion at the top to a quieter field at the bottom, suggesting a progression from chaos to calm. Broken pixels create an imperfection that enhances the emotional narrative.
