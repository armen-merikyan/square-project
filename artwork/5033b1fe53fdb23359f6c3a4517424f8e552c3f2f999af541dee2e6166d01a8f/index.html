Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the duality of tenderness and rage. The color palette of saturated red, muted black, and soft pale blue resonates with the themes of intensity and calm. The rage emerges from the corner with sharp, jagged red pixels, colliding with the static and tender pale blue fields, reflecting a struggle between chaos and calm. This collision occurs along the diagonal, creating a tension that invites the viewer into an unspoken dialogue between the contrasting emotions. The occasional broken pixels serve as scarring, hinting at past conflicts; they enhance the complexity rather than distract, ensuring space for contemplation that resonates with the themes of guilt and forgiveness. Negative space is preserved for quiet reflection, while the flow disrupts expectations, inviting a closer look at the dynamism in stillness.
