Artist Statement

This piece conveys a tension between safety and exposure through its color palette, evoking warmth from the 'shelter_green' and 'warm_brown' juxtaposed with the cooler 'pale_skin' and 'cold_white.' The open center suggests exposure, while the surrounding colors frame it in a protective manner. Key contrasts occur at the vertical split, creating a flow from heavy darker hues on one side to lighter shades on the other, which embodies the collision of pressure and release inherent in the seed.
