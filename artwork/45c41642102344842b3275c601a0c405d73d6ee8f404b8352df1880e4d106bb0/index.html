Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension and release evident in the seed. The warm colors of burnt orange and dark red contrast sharply with the cool shades of charcoal and subtle gold accents. The left side, representing confusion, employs a chaotic crosshatch pattern, while the right side embodies clarity with a smoother ring pattern. The 'broken pixels' idea introduces intentional irregularity, reinforcing the theme of unexpected disruptions in emotional flows. Overall, the piece suggests a narrative where pressure leads to release, creating a balanced yet dynamic visual tension.
