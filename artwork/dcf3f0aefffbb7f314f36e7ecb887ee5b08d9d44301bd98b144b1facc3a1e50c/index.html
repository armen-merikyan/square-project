Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette featuring black, electric blue, and magenta reflects the stark contrast of isolation versus the warmth of togetherness. Dominant dark shades symbolize loneliness, while the electric blue and magenta accentuate the idea of connection, creating a visual collision in the center. The right-weighted layout suggests an invasion of connection while echoing remnants of loneliness, manifesting as flashes amongst the deeper hues. The choice of broken pixels enhances the theme of fractured memories, emphasizing the imperfection in emotional experiences while maintaining a whisper of hope. The rising flow symbolizes an upward movement through the tension, indicating progress through the layers of social interaction.
