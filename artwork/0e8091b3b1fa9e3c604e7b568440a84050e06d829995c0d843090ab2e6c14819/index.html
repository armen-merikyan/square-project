Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional struggle between control and panic. The left side, using a structured zigzag pattern of black and pale blue, embodies control and order, while the right side disrupts this with chaotic pops of red in a ring pattern, reflecting feelings of panic. The stark contrast between warm and cool colors heightens the tension, evoking a sense of layered complexity where feelings collide yet also create dynamic beauty. The central area features a subtle interplay of colors, symbolizing a spark amidst the chaos, anchored with 'burned edges' represented in shades of gray, creating an imperfect yet compelling composition.
