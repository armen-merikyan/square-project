Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between warmth and coldness, represented by a palette that contrasts soft pinks with harsh dark maroon. The layout captures love in the top rows through framed patterns, creating a comforting space, while betrayal in the bottom rows uses a grid-like arrangement, suggesting confinement and sharp edges. The collision occurs in the transition between these emotions, allowing for a whisper of intermingled colors. The seed's themes of inside versus outside and crosshatching against noise create an abstract rhythm where warmth embraces coolness yet remains interrupted by sharper tones.
