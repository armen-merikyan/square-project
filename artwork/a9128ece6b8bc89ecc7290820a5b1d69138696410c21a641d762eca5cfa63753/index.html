Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between relief and dread through the interplay of dark and light colors. The palette consists of muted storm gray and near black, indicating introspection, while soft sky and faded gold provide accents of calm and fleeting joy. The collision occurs predominantly in the center, where a dense cluster of dark tones interacts with lighter hues, creating a visual tension that suggests an unresolved emotional struggle. The choice of a broken pattern reflects the chaotic nature of the emotions explored in the seed.
