Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between joy and grief through contrasting color choices and arrangements. The palette uses deep blue tones to represent grief, contrasted with bright spark-like yellows and oranges that embody joy. The flow is designed from bottom to top, evoking a sense of upward movement and resolution. The stair-step pattern suggests structured progression yet introduces soft edges to allow for emotional nuance. The sharp contrast of the darker colors against vibrant highlights creates a dynamic interplay, embodying both safety and threat within the same frame, achieving a layered complexity without forming literal representations.
