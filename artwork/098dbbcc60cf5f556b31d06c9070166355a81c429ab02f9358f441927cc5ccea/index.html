Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes warmth and heartbreak, represented through the palette of pinks and reds for love against gray and dark maroon for betrayal. The layout is a split that reflects a fractured symmetry, where the lower half features chaotic patterns, while the upper region offers a more layered and split-screen feel. The contrast lies in the density of colors colliding with open spaces, creating a rhythm that echoes the themes of love's warmth interrupted by betrayal's cold edges, symbolized by fragmented placements within the structure.
