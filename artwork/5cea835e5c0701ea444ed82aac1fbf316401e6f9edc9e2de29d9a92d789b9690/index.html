Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between softness and severity. The chosen palette, featuring powder pink and sharp crimson, represents the gentle and severe aspects respectively. The layout conveys an asymmetrical balance, with static patterns on one side contrasting against a dynamic crosshatch on the other. The dominance of darker tones on the left creates a grounded weight, while lighter shades spiral outward, symbolizing an opening or release. This collision at the center reflects an interaction between contrasting emotional states, creating a sense of unresolved tension and energy.
