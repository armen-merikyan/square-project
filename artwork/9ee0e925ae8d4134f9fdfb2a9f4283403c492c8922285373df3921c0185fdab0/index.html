Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between calmness and rage, using a palette contrasting deep reds for intensity and pale blues for tranquility. The collision occurs through sharp, jagged reds breaking into the soft blues, illustrating conflict and balance. The chaotic 'frame' pattern disrupts the calm 'glitch' pattern, visually representing the clash of emotions, reflecting the chaos amidst the calmness suggested by the seed.
