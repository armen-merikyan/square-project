Artist Statement

The piece explores the emotional tension derived from the interplay of quiet spaces and dense clusters, representing the complexities of human emotions. The palette contrasts deep, muted tones (#1B1F23, #384E5B) with brighter accents (#FF6B6B, #FFD93D), creating a dynamic rhythm. Clusters of dark colors signify depth and pressure, while lighter colors symbolize moments of release and quiet. The result is an abstract narrative of friction and harmony, echoing the essence of the seed's emotional score.
