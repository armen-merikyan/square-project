Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between softness and severity, highlighted by a palette of powder pink, warm white, carbon black, and sharp crimson. The contrast resonates in the center where dark colors collide with lighter tones, creating an impression of conflict and harmony. The patterns of soft, flowing 'constellations' intermingle with stark, structured 'threads,' symbolizing an internal struggle and resolution. Negative spaces intersperse the design, enhancing the feeling of a gentle descent, akin to fallen tears or unresolved emotions.
