Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between restlessness and peace through its striking palette and structured chaos. The dominant hues of cream and honey gold create large areas of calm while the vibrant greens introduce an element of energy and urgency. Contrast peaks in the lower left corner, where the density of color disrupts the otherwise spacious layout, echoing moments of hesitation against soothing intervals. The broken pixels mimic imperfections of memory and the struggle between a settled mind and persistent thoughts, crafting a narrative of collision and resolution towards the center of the canvas.
