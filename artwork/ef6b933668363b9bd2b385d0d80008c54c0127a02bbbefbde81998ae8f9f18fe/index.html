Artist Statement

The composition reflects the emotional tension of 'longing' vs 'resolve', where the contrasting colors of warm coral and cold slate represent a delicate balance of desire and steadiness. The outer border in warm hues suggests resolution and unwavering support, while the inner core of the grid showcases a web-like lattice mimicking the complexity of longing. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, echoing the transient nature of emotions and the notion of reaching despite obstacles. The soft amber serves as a quiet interlude, providing breathing space amidst the denser arrangements. The overall layout creates an energetic push-pull, where color intensity fluctuates, leading to rich visual narratives that embody the seed's essence.
