Artist Statement

This composition visually narrates the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve. The palette employs sepia and faded blue hues to evoke nostalgia and calmness, while bursts of tan contrast against dust gray, symbolizing the unresolved tension between the two emotions. The primary collision occurs diagonally across the square, where clusters of tan (representing resolve) interject with faint echoes of gray and blue (symbolizing longing). This dynamic arrangement creates a visual pulse, embodying the relationship of contained desire against firm determination.
