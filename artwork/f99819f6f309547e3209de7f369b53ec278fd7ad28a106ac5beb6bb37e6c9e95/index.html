Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between clarity and disruption, embodying the struggle of a distinct signal amidst a chaotic noise environment. The palette of deep navy and black enhances the feeling of depth and isolation, while the dim cyan introduces a soft light, representing moments of clarity. The central collision occurs in the middle of the square, where concentrated bright accents interrupt the muted fields, creating a rhythm of tension and release. This arrangement captures the essence of battling between memory and erasure, allowing the viewer to feel the presence of an echoing conflict.
