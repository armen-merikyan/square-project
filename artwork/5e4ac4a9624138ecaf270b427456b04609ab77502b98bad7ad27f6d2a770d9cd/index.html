Artist Statement

This abstract composition embodies the emotional tension between quietude and pressure. The color palette, sourced from subdued tones like off-white and dust gray contrasted with the intensity of compressed red, captures the vibrancy of life amid an encroaching weight. The pattern strategy adopts a 'static' field to represent the quietness while 'stairs' convey the clashing pressure, creating an impression of rising intensity. The central diagonal flow represents the conflict and balance between opposing emotions, while broken pixels introduce purposeful disruptions, embodying confusion and clarity as they scatter through the space. Overall, a sense of unease and tranquility coalesce in this structured disarray, echoing the layered meanings of the seed.
