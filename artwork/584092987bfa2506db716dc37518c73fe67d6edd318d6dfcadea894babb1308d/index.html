Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between feelings of loneliness and connection, represented through placement and palette. The chosen colors of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evoke nostalgia and a sense of distance. The main contrast occurs in the center, which symbolizes a hidden connection amidst loneliness, celebrated by the burst and top-weighting that crowd the edges in more muted tones. This arrangement reflects the inner turmoil of connection versus isolation, conveying tension and the fragile nature of relationships.
