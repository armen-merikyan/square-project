Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of defiance and submission through contrasting blocks of color. The chosen palette mirrors the interplay of warmth and coolness with yellow and deep blue indicating tension and calm. The dominant use of muted purple creates a weighty presence that frames the lighter oranges, illustrating a bottom-heavy composition reflective of emotional gravity. The pixel arrangement reflects a left-to-right flow, echoing the act of falling and rising, while establishing an abstract rhythm that celebrates both conflict and resolution.
