Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents a struggle between defiance and submission, depicted through the use of deep black to convey weight and pale blue to signify lightness. The left half bursts with contrasting black dots, symbolizing defiance, while the right cascades with a softer white dot pattern, representing submission. This creates a visual tension that falls from top to bottom, echoing the seed's themes of conflict and balance. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the emotional struggle conveyed through color contrasts.
