Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tone of restlessness intermingled with peace through contrasting color fields and placements. The palette of soft cyan, lavender gray, and muted plum introduces a balance of calm and restlessness. The collision occurs in the center, where muted hues are jaggedly interrupted by brighter accents, symbolizing the push and pull of conflicting feelings. Broken pixels represent memory, echo, and imperfection, enhancing the interplay between structured peace and spontaneous motion. The arrangement captures the essence of the seed, translating abstract qualities into a visual experience of tension and release.
