Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the struggle between fear and hope. The chosen palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green suggests a transition from darkness (fear) to light (hope). The combination of jagged edges and soft golden tones creates a stark contrast that captures the collision of emotions, while the broken pixels introduce imperfection, representing the disruption and unpredictability of these feelings. The layout is mostly sparse with a dense cluster of golden hues hinting at the 'inside versus outside' concept, embodying a glimmer of hope amidst the darkness.
