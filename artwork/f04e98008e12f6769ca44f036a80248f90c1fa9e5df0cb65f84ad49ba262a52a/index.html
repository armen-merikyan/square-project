Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between isolation and the yearning for connection, represented by a sparse yet intentional arrangement of colors. The palette, derived from 'broken_memory', features muted sepia, tan, and faded blue hues, evoking nostalgia while contrasting with darker tones that symbolize loneliness. The main contrast occurs where dark dots 'invade' a lighter field, embodying the struggle of reaching out amidst isolation. This dynamic interplay illustrates the push-pull of connection and solitude, echoing the essence of the seed into a visual narrative of pressure and release.
