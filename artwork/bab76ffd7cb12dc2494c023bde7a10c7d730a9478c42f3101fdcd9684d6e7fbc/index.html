Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay of vulnerability and strength, conveyed through a color palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon. The palette brings warmth and intensity, contrasting vulnerability with the hard edges of strength. The main collision occurs centrally where softer colors seem to flood against denser and darker hues, creating a sense of tension and dynamic movement—the rising signal motif. This arrangement symbolizes the struggle between inner fragility and outer resilience, echoing the seed's themes of connection and alienation.
