Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional struggle between loneliness and connection through a contrasting color palette of warm reds and soft grays. The main collision occurs with the central filled square, surrounded by darker, muted tones that signify isolation, giving way to brighter accents that symbolize the yearning for connection. The arrangement employs a 'stairs' pattern to reflect loneliness and a 'checker' pattern for connection, with broken pixels introducing an element of imperfection, suggesting that even in connection, feelings of loneliness may linger. The entire composition echoes the seed’s inspiration—between the stark contrasts and emotional collisions, it tells a story of an inner desire for connection amid an overwhelming solitude.
