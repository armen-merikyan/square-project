Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tensions between longing and resolve, contrasting warmth and coolness. The palette employs muted purple and deep blue to evoke a sense of depth and introspection, while hints of yellow and orange serve as accent colors for emotional highlights. The composition features a diagonal flow embodying dynamic tension, disrupted by a halo pattern on one side and a structured, mirrored pattern on the other, symbolizing the chaotic search for connection against a backdrop of stasis. The central collision occurs along the diagonal, where the more chaotic, warm colors meet the cooler, resolved hues, illustrating the push and pull of emotion.
