Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the complexities between defiance and submission, paralleling light and weight. A palette of electric blue and magenta conveys the vibrancy of defiance, while muted black layers reflect submission and weight. The contrast peaks at the center, suggesting a collision of energies where the struggle is most felt. The layout splits the canvas vertically, enhancing the theme of opposing emotions, with patterns suggesting sparks on one side and teeth-like serrations on the other, embodying both conflict and resolution.
