Artist Statement

The composition explores the emotional dichotomy of joy and grief through contrasting color placements. The warm tones of yellow and orange signify joy, spreading from the top left corner, while deeper hues of muted purple and deep blue represent grief, creating a visual tension. The interplay of broken pixels introduces imperfection, reflecting the reality of emotional states. This layout captures dichotomy through a diagonal rise, suggesting an overarching struggle between light and dark, love and betrayal, echoing the seed.
