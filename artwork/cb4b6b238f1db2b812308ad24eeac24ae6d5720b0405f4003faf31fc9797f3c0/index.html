Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between clarity and confusion. The chosen palette—ranging from muted gray to dynamic red—underscores the contrasting states: the calm integrity of clarity and the chaotic disruptions of confusion. The patterns suggest a maze of clarity in the top half, while the bottom half displays the 'target' layout evoking fragmentation and noise. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfection, symbolizing the disruptions in perceived symmetry, leading to an unresolved visual narrative of tension and imbalance.
