Artist Statement

The emotional tone is deeply contrasted, symbolizing the tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette combines clean white and ice blue, conveying clarity amidst confusion, with warning orange adding moments of intensity. The main collision occurs in the center with thin, vibrant lines representing vulnerability intersecting with dense, stable blocks of darker hues, embodying strength. This reflective pattern echoes themes of exposure and shelter, inviting the viewer to consider the nature of connection and alienation.
