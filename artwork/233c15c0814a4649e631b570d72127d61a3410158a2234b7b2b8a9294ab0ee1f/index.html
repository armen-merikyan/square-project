Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette, featuring soft corals and deep charcoals, emphasizes a contrast between exposed warmth and protected shadows. The main collision occurs at the center, where strong crosshatch patterns overlap with delicate thread-like forms, symbolizing the interplay of being exposed yet guarded. The larger negative spaces provide a sense of emptiness, enhancing the feeling of vulnerability. The asymmetrical layout reflects a deliberate imbalance, resonating with the underlying themes of the seed.
