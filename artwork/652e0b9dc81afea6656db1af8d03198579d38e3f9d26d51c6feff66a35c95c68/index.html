Artist Statement

This composition expresses the emotional duality of defiance and submission through a chaotic central focal point wrapped in subdued outer pixels. The palette features bright yellows and muted blues/purples to represent warmth clashing with cool tension. The defiant, shattering patterns (yellow/orange) surge from the center, colliding against the softer, rain-like patterns of deep blue and muted purple at the edges. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, creating visual 'scars' that echo the emotional conflict. The use of negative space aids in emphasizing the dynamics of open versus closed within this tension, making the viewer feel the push and pull inherent in this opposition.
