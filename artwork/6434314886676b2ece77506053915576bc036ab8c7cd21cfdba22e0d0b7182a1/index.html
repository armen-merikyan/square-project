Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between defiance and submission, using a 'lonely_signal' palette that juxtaposes deep navy and black with light dim cyan and white. The layout appears almost symmetric but subtly deviates, showcasing a mix of checkered patterns symbolizing defiance against maze-like formations indicative of submission. The density shifts from the top right towards bottom left creating a visual weight, while broken pixels introduce imperfection, symbolizing underlying complexity in emotions. The intersection of dense clusters and open spaces embodies the ongoing conflict within the narrative of love and betrayal.
