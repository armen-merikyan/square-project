Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts between relief and dread, encapsulated by a balanced palette of intense lime and electric blue against the heaviness of black and dark shades. The arrangement flows from the top, creating a visual metaphor for descent—a release of structure into chaos. Main contrast occurs between the luminescent accents and the dark foundations, evoking a struggle between lightness and weight, reflecting the seed's themes of breaks versus symmetry. The pixel placement introduces an asymmetry that symbolizes disruption within a rigid emotional framework.
