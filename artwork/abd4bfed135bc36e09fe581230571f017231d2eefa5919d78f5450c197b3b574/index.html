Artist Statement

The emotional tone fluctuates between rage and calm, using the palette of reds and blacks for intensity and blues and whites for serenity. The main contrast occurs where the red jagged patterns cut through the calm blue maze, symbolizing an emotional collision. The diamond shape seed inspires a dynamic layout, where stark edges create a sense of disturbance amidst the quiet flow, reflecting the push and pull of emotions.
