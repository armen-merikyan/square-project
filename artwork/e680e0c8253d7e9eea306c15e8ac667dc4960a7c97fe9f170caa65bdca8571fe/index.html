Artist Statement

The piece juxtaposes deep emotions of fear and hope, blending jagged edges with soft light. The palette features stark contrasts, using dark colors to represent fear and pale tones to symbolize hope. The arrangement reflects a rising signal from the darkness, creating tension in the composition. The placement of brighter pixels interrupts the dense backgrounds, embodying the fight between suffocating fear and piercing hope. The broken pixels add imperfection, resonating with the theme of struggle. The result is a dynamic visual narrative that is both balanced and asymmetric, embodying depth and emotional conflict.
