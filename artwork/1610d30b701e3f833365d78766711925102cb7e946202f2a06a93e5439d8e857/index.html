Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional conflict of confusion versus clarity through a careful blend of warm and cool tones. The pink and red suggest intensity and chaos, while grays and dark maroon provide grounding and contemplation. The main contrast is found at the center where colors collide, reflecting the chaotic patterns of confusion as they break through a mirrored structure representing clarity. The broken edges create tension, while the flowing arrangement evokes both pulsation and disruption, aligning with the seed's abstract nature.
