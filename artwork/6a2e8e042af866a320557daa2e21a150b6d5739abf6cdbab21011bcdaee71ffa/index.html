Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between clarity and interference, reflecting how isolated signals compete within a noisy environment. The palette comprises deep navy and subtle dim cyan representing 'signal', contrasted with black to symbolize 'noise'. The static black background enforces the feeling of isolation, while sporadic bright embers in the blue and cyan create accents that punctuate the dark field, symbolizing fleeting moments of clarity amid chaos. The collision of clean blue channels interspersed with dark noise creates a rhythmic pulse throughout the grid, embodying a struggle for presence and memory within conceptual echoes. Pattern-wise, the grid layout represents a dense island of clarity vying for space against the overwhelming sea of noise, allowing the viewer to interpret the tension visually.
