Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between clear signals and overwhelming noise. The selected palette of signal green, electric blue, hot magenta, and static gray represents clarity amidst chaos, with shades juxtaposed to evoke rhythmic pulses against fragmented interference. The main collision occurs in the center, embodying a pulse that fights through the static noise, while broken pixels disrupt uniformity to signify scars of memory and erasure. This visual narrative captures the essence of the seed's emotional landscape, illustrating a journey through chaos toward clarity.
