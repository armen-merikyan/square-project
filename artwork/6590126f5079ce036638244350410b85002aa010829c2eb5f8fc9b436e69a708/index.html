Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional dance between longing and resolve. The muted palette reflects the quiet pressure of longing, while the sharper, warmer accents introduce moments of resolve. The barcode pattern interrupts the broken dots, creating a visual tension, embodying the core conflict of the seed. The arrangement flows right to left, with the darker tones framing the lighter core, illustrating the balance of contained urge against a backdrop of introspection.
