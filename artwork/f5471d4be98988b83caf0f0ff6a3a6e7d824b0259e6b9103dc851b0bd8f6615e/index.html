Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional contrast between confusion and clarity through its hesitations and structured chaos. The palette of dark violet and soft gold represents clarity amid confusion, with the dark tones enveloping bright accents to evoke feelings of pressure and release. The pattern emulates a blend of rigid frames and flickering flames, portraying a delicate balance between stability and chaos. The juxtaposition of symmetry with disruptive broken pixels illustrates the struggle between clear paths and interferences, represented by the placement of colors that pulse outward, creating a dynamic flow.
