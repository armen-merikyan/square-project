Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional contrast between relief and dread through a carefully chosen palette of muted greys and vibrant accents. The use of storm gray and near black conveys a sense of dread, while the faded gold introduces a momentary brightness, suggesting relief. The layout begins in the corner, with a dense cluster of dark colors that transitions into lighter, more open sections, creating a visual tension that embodies the struggle between heavy emotion and lighter moments. The broken pixels act as intentional disruptions adding a sense of imperfection and organic feel, aligning with the ‘spiral counterclockwise’ theme as they introduce unpredictability within the structured layout.
