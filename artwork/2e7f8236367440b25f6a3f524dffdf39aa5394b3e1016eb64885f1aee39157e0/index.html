Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional duality of longing and resolve through contrasting colors and patterns. The warm hues of ripe green and honey gold represent longing, while the steadiness of deep olive and acid green signifies resolve. Broken pixels scattered across the canvas introduce intentional disruptions, symbolizing the tension of repair versus damage. The layout, favoring a pressure corner, captures the feeling of overflow and urgency, creating a dynamic flow that interrupts serene spaces. The primary collision occurs in the top left, where warm colors clash with the grounded resolution of deeper shades, encapsulating the struggle between desire and determination.
