Artist Statement

This composition symbolizes the ongoing tension between vulnerability and strength. The soft hues of powder pink and warm white convey the fragility of vulnerability, while the strong contrasts of carbon black and sharp crimson represent the fortitude of strength. The layout emerges diagonally, suggesting an invading force of strength from the corner, while patches of noise signify disruptions in the vulnerable areas. Broken pixels give an impression of imperfection, reinforcing the narrative of exposure versus shelter—inviting contemplation of how these emotional states coexist. The architectural wave shape is abstractly present through the flowing patterns that guide the viewer's eye from corner to corner, creating rhythmic visual tension.
