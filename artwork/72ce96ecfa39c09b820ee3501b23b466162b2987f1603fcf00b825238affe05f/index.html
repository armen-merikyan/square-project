Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between softness and severity, with a divided palette that emphasizes both emotional states. The warm whites and powder pinks represent softness, spilling into the stark contrast of carbon black and sharp crimson, embodying severity. The predominant block of dark colors hints at pressure, while the lighter hues provide gentle interruptions. The layout creates a passage where these contrasting emotions meet, reflecting the complexity of guilt and forgiveness through jarring yet soothing interactions in the arrangement.
