Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between clarity and distortion, illustrating a central pulse fighting to be heard amid chaotic noise. The color palette blends deep navy and soft dim cyan, representing calm and disturbance, respectively. The dark colors signify the noise at the outer edge, while brighter shades concentrate in the core, reflecting the 'signal' fighting through the disorder. Broken pixels introduce intentional scars, enhancing the chaotic feel, while allowing clear zones for the pulse to emerge, making the contrast between noise and signal palpable.
