Artist Statement

This composition reflects the duality of fear and hope through a stark contrast of darkness and light. The color palette leans towards deep blacks and purples to signify fear, with brighter tones of pale green and soft gold representing hope. The top half features angular, jagged placements reflecting tension and fear, while the bottom half uses tooth-like patterns to suggest upward growth and optimism. The split layout emphasizes emotional collision and resolution, with a delicate balance of negative space to avoid overwhelming sensations, maintaining an abstract harmony.
