Artist Statement

This abstract composition reflects a duality of vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, consisting of muted cream and blackened plum, captures an emotional tension—warmth meets darkness, portraying the inherent struggle between protection and exposure. The use of stripes to symbolize strength contrasts sharply against the rain-like patterns of vulnerability, especially evident in the center where they collide, emphasizing the battle between solid structure and fragile existence. Broken pixels intermittently disrupt the visual flow, reinforcing the theme of imperfections amid strength. The layout mirrors on the left and right, with slight disruptions creating a sense of imbalance, echoing the complexity of human emotion.
