Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts vulnerability and strength, woven into a textured interplay where the darker shapes (strength) radiate from the center while lighter colors (vulnerability) punctuate the edges. The chosen palette of deep blue, mourning black, and pale yellow represents renewal amidst loss. The broken frame layout allows emotional 'leaks' through breaks, emphasizing the rawness of these opposing forces. The main collision sits centrally, where the radial strength of dark colors collides with the mirrored patterns of lighter shades, creating a tension that hints at dynamic interactions of resilience and fragility.
