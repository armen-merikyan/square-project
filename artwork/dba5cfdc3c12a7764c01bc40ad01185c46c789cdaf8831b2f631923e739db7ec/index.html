Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through contrasting color pairings. The dark palette evokes a feeling of depth and complexity, while subtle accents suggest moments of hope and connection. The pattern features overlapping flow—soft ripples representing longing intermingle with firm threads of resolve, creating a dense center with a subtle halo effect around it, signifying a boundary between the emotional states. The collision occurs predominantly in the center, embodying the struggle of these feelings. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, echoing the imperfections in the emotional experience.
