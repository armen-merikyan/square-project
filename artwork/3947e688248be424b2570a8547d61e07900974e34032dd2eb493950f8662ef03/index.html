Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension of desire battling against restraint. The palette includes rich hues of red_oxide and muted_cream, suggesting saturated intentions and restrained boundaries. The contrast lies in the upper left corner, where warm colors swirl amidst a lattice of cools, echoing the collision of these emotions. The saturated colors reflect the hunger for connection, while the muted tones represent restrictions, forming a narrative of enclosed yet yearning energy. The broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, enhancing the sense of unresolved tension throughout the piece.
