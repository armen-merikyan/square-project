Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition encapsulates the tension between longing and resolve, manifesting through an interplay of warm and cool colors. The palette features soft gold and deep wine to represent warmth and compression of feelings, while ash gray and warm ivory introduce negative spaces that contrast with the density of emotion. The main collision occurs centrally, with a patchwork that interrupts a halo void, suggesting the push and pull between connection and distance.
