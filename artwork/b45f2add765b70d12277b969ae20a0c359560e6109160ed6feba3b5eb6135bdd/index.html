Artist Statement

This piece conveys the emotional tonal shifts between confusion and clarity using a palette that juxtaposes dark violets against soft golds and pale greens. The structured yet corrupted layout mirrors the imbalance in emotions, with the floating dark hues representing chaos, while the lighter colors suggest clarity and hope peeking through. The use of falling pixels enhances the tension of descending confusion, while broken pixels create intentional imperfection, interrupting the visual flow. The collision of dense and sparse areas captures the pulse of conflicting emotions.
