Artist Statement

This composition embodies an emotional contrast between love and betrayal, represented through a palette of warm pinks and reds juxtaposed against cool grays and dark maroons. The left half employs a grid pattern suggesting order and connection, while the right incorporates noise, evoking chaos and fragmentation. The interplay of sharp versus soft edges creates a visual tension, symbolizing the fragility of relationships. The arrangement captures a journey from warmth to disarray, embodying the seed's themes of duality and emotional conflict.
