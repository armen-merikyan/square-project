Artist Statement

This composition reflects the duality of tenderness and rage through the juxtaposition of soft and sharp colors. The palette features deep reds and muted blues, representing the intensity of rage against a backdrop of calmness. The main contrast emerges in the upper left corner, where jagged patterns of red collide with gentle, flowing strokes of blue, suggesting a conflict that is both tangible and emotional. The flow tilts upward, suggesting a climb or progress, while the arrangement of colors hints at a tumultuous landscape of feelings.
