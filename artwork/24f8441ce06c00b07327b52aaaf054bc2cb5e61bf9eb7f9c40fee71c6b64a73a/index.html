Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys the delicate balance between joy and grief, represented through a palette of yellow and deep blue to evoke optimism and depth. The contrasting arrangement features a dense left side (grief) interspersed with dotted patterns suggesting fragmentation, while the right side (joy) displays light, expanding punctuations, creating visual tension. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfection, symbolizing disruption within symmetry, fostering a sense of unresolved emotion within the overall harmony of the piece.
