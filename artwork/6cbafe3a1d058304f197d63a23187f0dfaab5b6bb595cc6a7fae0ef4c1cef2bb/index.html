Artist Statement

The emotional tone highlights a dichotomy between restlessness and peace. The chosen palette of deep reds, grays, and soft pinks reflects turmoil and calm, creating visual tension. The primary contrast arises in the center, where jagged 'crosshatch' patterns disrupt smoother 'braid' textures, encapsulating the idea of conversation between two emotional states. Broken pixels intermittently disturb the surface, representing moments of disruption amidst calm, seamlessly translating the seed's themes into color and space.
