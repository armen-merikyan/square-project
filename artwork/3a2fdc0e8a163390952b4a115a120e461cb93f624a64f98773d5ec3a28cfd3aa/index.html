Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic struggle between loneliness and connection. Using a palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue creates a sense of nostalgia and warmth while suggesting broken memories. The top four rows showcase the weight of loneliness using muted colors framed with structured patterns, creating an echoing effect against the more vibrant connection-emphasized lower rows, symbolizing the constant pull between solitude and unity. The placement of broken pixels introduces imperfection, enhancing the abstract story of fragmented connections surrounded by emotional noise.
