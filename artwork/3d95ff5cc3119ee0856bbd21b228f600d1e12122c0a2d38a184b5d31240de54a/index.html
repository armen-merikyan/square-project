Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dichotomy of desire and restraint through a carefully chosen palette that balances warmth with muteness. The reds and oranges symbolize desire's heat, while deep plums and muted creams indicate restraint. The central collision of colors illustrates the struggle between saturated and contained emotions, with dynamic lattice patterns pressing against saturated areas to evoke pressure. This conflict is represented in a grid format that invokes both movement and containment, anchoring the viewer in the tension of the emotional narrative.
