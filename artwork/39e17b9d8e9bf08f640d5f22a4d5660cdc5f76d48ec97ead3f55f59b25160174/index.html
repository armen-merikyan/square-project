Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between clarity and confusion, using a palette of muted sepia tones and faded blues drawn from the theme of 'broken memory.' The outer sections, arranged with a structured palette, represent clarity, while the inner core, illustrating the chaotic elements, incorporates more noise through color collision. The defined edges contrast with the erratic breaks, creating a dynamic interplay of pulse and stillness, reflecting the interplay of symmetric versus broken patterns dictated by the seed.
