Artist Statement

This artwork conveys a dialogue between vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette represents shelter with earthy tones contrasted by pale highlights to suggest exposure. The main collision occurs in the center where dense echoes of warm brown intermingle with cold whites, creating an emotional tension. The arrangement utilizes islands of color to suggest vulnerability, while the echoing structure suggests resilience. Broken pixels interrupt the patterns, introducing flaws that evoke a sense of imperfection, enhancing the narrative of connection versus alienation.
