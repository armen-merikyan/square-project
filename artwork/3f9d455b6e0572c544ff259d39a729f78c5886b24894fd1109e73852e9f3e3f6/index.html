Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength through a striking contrast of colors and structured patterns. The palette features deep blacks and earthy reds symbolizing strength alongside delicate creams and grays representing vulnerability. The main collision occurs at the center, creating a dense base that contrasts with the lighter, more open spaces surrounding it, suggesting a struggle between protection and exposure. The pixel arrangement reflects falling pixels, initiating a visual narrative of rising signals amidst a flood of emotions.
