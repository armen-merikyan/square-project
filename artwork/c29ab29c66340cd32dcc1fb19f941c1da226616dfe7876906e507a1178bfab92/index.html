Artist Statement

This abstract composition explores the juxtaposition of control and surrender through a dynamic interplay of colors and shapes. The palette of vibrant lime and electric blue contrasts with the deep, absorbing black and intense magenta, embodying the tension between containment and release. The structured patterns of control are mirrored in the corners, while the channels of soft surrender elegantly flow towards the center, creating a visual stutter that symbolizes the push and pull of emotions. The placement of broken pixels adds intentional disruptions, enhancing the narrative of a signal struggling to emerge from noise, emphasizing the theme of 'overflow vs containment.' This layout enhances both the horizontal flow from the corners to the center and the chaotic interaction of colors, crafting a cohesive yet differentiated abstract space.
