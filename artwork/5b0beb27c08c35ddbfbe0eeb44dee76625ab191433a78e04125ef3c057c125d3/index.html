Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the duality of nostalgia and progress, depicted through a palette representing warmth and depth. The contrasting hues of muted purple and deep blue create tension, suggesting a collision between the past and the future. This encounter manifests as a bottom-weighted grid with broken dots on the nostalgic side, elevating the feeling of cherished memories disrupted by the sharp sparks of progress on the other. The layout enhances this, with a split vertical design, allowing for clear separation while echoing the inner struggle of balancing past and future.
