Artist Statement

The composition captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through contrasting colors and structured patterns. The selected palette of signal green, electric blue, dead black, static gray, and hot magenta creates a feeling of vibrancy against a muted backdrop, symbolizing the internal struggle of yearning for completion while navigating chaos. The main collision occurs near the center, where the lively accents intersect with darker elements, embodying the push and pull of desire versus stability. The layout, featuring a broken frame, reflects the leakage of emotion and connects to the seed concept of a ladder against a fault line, suggesting both ascent and impending breakdown.
