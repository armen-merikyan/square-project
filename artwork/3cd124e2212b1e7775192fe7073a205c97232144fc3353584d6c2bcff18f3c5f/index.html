Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between emptiness and desire, using a contrasting palette of warm yellows and oranges against the deep blues and muted purples to represent the struggle between warmth and coolness. The central diamond shape is echoed by broken pixels, enhancing the feeling of fragmentation within the composition. The layout creates a sense of spaciousness, evoking the desire for connection amidst isolation, with asymmetrical placement of colors suggesting an unresolved push-and-pull dynamic.
