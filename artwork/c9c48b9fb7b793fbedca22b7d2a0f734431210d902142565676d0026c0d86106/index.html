Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dichotomy between longing and resolve, using contrasting colors to express the tension between these feelings. The palette, drawn from desire and emptiness, combines warm coral and orange with cool charcoal and blue, representing the collision of warmth and calm. The main contrast occurs in the center, where a radial pattern of resolve interrupts the crosshatch of longing, suggesting a struggle between acceptance and yearning. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, enhancing the theme of repair versus damage while portraying an echo of unresolved emotions.
