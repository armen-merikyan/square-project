Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between restlessness and peace, reflecting a dynamic tension that ebbs and flows. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold symbolizes warmth contrasted against shadows, representing the flicker of unrest against a backdrop of calm. The collision primarily occurs in the center, where warmer pixels interrupt cooler, more restrained fields, symbolizing memories jarring against a peaceful state. The abstract pattern emerges from the edge-crawl of darker pixels interspersed with patches of brighter colors, illustrating a layered motion that seeks resolution, akin to the tension of memories responding to the call of the present.
