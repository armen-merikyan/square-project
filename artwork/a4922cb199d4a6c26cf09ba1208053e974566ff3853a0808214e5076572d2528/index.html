Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork captures the tension between vulnerability and strength. The selected palette, inspired by broken memory, employs muted sepia and faded blue colors that evoke a sense of nostalgia and longing. The main contrast occurs in the center, where warm tan shades collide with the cooler faded blue tones, creating a dynamic pulse between sheltering warmth and exposed fragility. This interplay reflects the seed's themes of vulnerability, strength, and emotional currents, resulting in a unique abstract pattern that invites contemplation.
