Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines tenderness and strictness, reflecting a conflict where softness struggles against severity. The palette, rich with powdered pink and sharp crimson, introduces warmth contrasted by cold black and steel gray, facilitating tension between the inner and outer elements. The layout indicates that the outer border (severity) is shaped by cold and dark colors, while the inner 4x4 (softness) utilizes softer tones. The collision occurs predominantly in the center with an overlapping of soft scars against stern islands. This arrangement embodies an unresolved push and pull between expansion and confinement, beautifully captured within the 8x8 structure.
