Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional conflict between love and betrayal, using a palette of warm reds and cool grays to evoke contrasting emotions. The asymmetrical layout creates a sense of pressure as the colors collide, with the dark maroon serving as a deep resonance beneath lighter hues. The presence of broken pixels adds an element of imperfection, illustrating the fragility of love amid feelings of betrayal. The contrast occurs along a diagonal flow, suggesting a crack in harmony and a rising tension.
