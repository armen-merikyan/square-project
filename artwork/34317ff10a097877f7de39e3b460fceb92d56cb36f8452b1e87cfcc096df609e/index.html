Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that balances serene pale aqua and mist gray against stark alarm red and ink blue. The collision occurs in the center where 'pressure_band' patterns meet 'islands' of color, creating a visual representation of conflict and harmony. The layering of darker colors along the edges contrasts with lighter shades, suggesting a protective barrier that remains imperfect, echoing the fragility of strength and the resilience amid vulnerability.
