Artist Statement

Drawing upon the emotional tension of signal versus noise, this composition channels a sense of a solitary pulse trying to resonate through a chaotic environment. The color palette features contrasting tones: signal green and electric blue for clarity intermingling with static gray and hot magenta that evoke the noise and disruption. The split vertically layout emphasizes tension, with the left half representing the focused signal and the right half embodying the chaotic noise. The broken frame pattern introduces irregularities, enhancing the notion of interference while allowing the underlying rhythm to emerge. The central area serves as the primary collision point, symbolizing the struggle of clarity amidst distraction.
