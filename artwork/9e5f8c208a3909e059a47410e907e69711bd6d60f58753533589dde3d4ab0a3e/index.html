Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional contrasts of loneliness and connection through an interplay of deep contrasts and layered textures. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold reflects ancient fire, evoking warmth amidst solitude. The main collision occurs where deep tones meet, echoing the hidden pain of disconnection and the distant possibility of connection at the center—a single burst of brightness representing fleeting moments of warmth. The grid pattern speaks to the structural boundaries of loneliness while allowing for a subtle noise of connection to weave through, establishing a narrative of tension and potential.
