Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends longing and resolve, reflecting a struggle between scattered chaos and structured calm. The palette of cosmic indigo and cold gold evokes a sense of wonder and uncertainty, suggesting a yearning for serenity amidst disruption. The primary contrast occurs through a dense cluster of deep teal against a backdrop of void black, representing the tension between overwhelming emotions and the yearning for peace. The seed's influence unfolds through a layered composition where patterns of channels collide with stepwells, embodying the complexity of the emotions at play.
