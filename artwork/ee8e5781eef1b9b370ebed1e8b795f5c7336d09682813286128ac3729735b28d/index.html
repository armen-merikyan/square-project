Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold. The colors create a strong central presence reminiscent of resilience surrounded by jagged interruptions representing fragility. The dark reds and charcoals dominate, symbolizing strength, while the burnt orange offers warmth yet hints at vulnerability. The main collision, where the bright colors clash with darker hues, occurs at the center, suggesting an ongoing struggle within. This arrangement evokes a narrative of conflict and balance, embodying the themes of pressure and release as expressed in the seed text.
