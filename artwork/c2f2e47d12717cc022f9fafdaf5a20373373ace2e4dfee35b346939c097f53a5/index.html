Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette combines warm shadow and distant lilac to create a sense of intimacy and distance, highlighting the contrasting feelings. The center features a strong lattice pattern that shows stability, while surrounding islands create undercurrents of vulnerability. A solid black pixel introduces a moment of interruption. The main collision occurs at the center, where the strong pattern collides with more fragmented, delicate forms around it, illustrating the battle between exposure and shelter.
