Artist Statement

The work reflects the emotional struggle between envy and gratitude, using a contrasting palette of dark navy and light dim cyan to signify heavy feelings clashing with lightness. A mirroring layout embodies conflict while soft edges create a sense of fluidity, tension residing in the central area while edges retreat, leaving negative space. The pattern alternates between clusters of noise and radial bursts, representing the push-and-pull of the emotional landscape.
