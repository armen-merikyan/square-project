Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between defiance and submission. The use of clean white and ice blue represents a sense of order and calm, while the ink black and warning orange introduce chaos and interruption. The placement of colors suggests a struggle at the center, where bright accents clash with softer tones, embodying the push and pull of defiance against submission. The diagonal rise in color placement emphasizes movement, while the uneven border incorporates broken pixels to evoke a sense of imperfection and raw emotion, maintaining at least 10% negative space for balance.
