Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette features moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold to convey resilience and fragility. The main contrast occurs between the broken, cracked texture representing longing and the rhythmic, structured barcode symbolizing resolve. This collision happens near the center, embodying the struggle between containment and overflow. The deliberate placement of broken pixels creates a textured imperfection, aligning with the emotional narrative of repair versus damage.
