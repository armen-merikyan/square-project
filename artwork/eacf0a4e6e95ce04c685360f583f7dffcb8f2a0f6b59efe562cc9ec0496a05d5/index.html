Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores 'fear' and 'hope' through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green embodies the tension between darkness and light, with black and dark violet representing fear through jagged edges. In contrast, soft gold and pale green from hope emerge as bursts of color in the center, symbolizing potential. The arrangement favors a diagonal spread of fear from the corners, with brighter colors framing the center, creating a sense of an inward pull towards hope. The layout disrupts the peace with jagged structures, yet the center glows, representing a tender resilience amidst darkness. This clash illustrates the continuous battle between despair and optimism, echoing the emotional complexities of the seed text.
