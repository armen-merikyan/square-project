Artist Statement

This piece contrasts the tension of 'rage' and the tranquility of 'calm', capturing the struggle between these conflicting emotions. The chosen palette of deep blacks and stark whites, with accents of pale blue and sharp red, illustrates the dynamic of opposition, where calm provides a backdrop to the piercing rage. The pattern collides with radial thrusts of red breaking through serene black and blue backgrounds, symbolizing two emotional states vying for dominance. The asymmetric placement of colors suggests movement and conflict, while the soft edges create a boundary that hints at calmness amidst the chaos.
