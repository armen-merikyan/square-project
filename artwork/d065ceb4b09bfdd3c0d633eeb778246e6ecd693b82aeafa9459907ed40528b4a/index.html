Artist Statement

The emotional tone of longing meets the steady resolve in a juxtaposition of colors that evoke tension and connection. The palette of clean white and ice blue signifies clarity amid confusion, while ink black and violet gray represent the unresolved aspects. The collision occurs at the center, rich with noise patterns, contrasting with the longing's veil patterned edges, creating a sense of struggle and interplay. The broken pixels interrupt this narrative, providing a fresh perspective on the balance between damage and repair.
