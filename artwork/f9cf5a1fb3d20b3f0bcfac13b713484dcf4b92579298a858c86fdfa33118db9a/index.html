Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional contrast between dread and relief through a structured yet imperfect layout. The palette features muted tones of sepia and faded blue, conveying a sense of nostalgia and shadow. The top-weighted arrangement suggests heaviness while breaking symmetry creates tension. The central flow spirals outwards, reflecting a struggle between dense regions of muted colors and small accents that pop with lightness, representing relief amidst the heavier elements. The broken pixels subtly interrupt patterns enhancing the feeling of fragmentation, echoing the seed's themes of relief and dread.
