Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a blending of pride and shame due to their inherent contrasts, suggesting resilience amidst conflict. The chosen palette of deep blue, muted purple, yellow, and orange evokes a movement from grief to joy, representing the emotional transitions. The central collision occurs between the warm yellows and oranges against the cooler, muted tones, symbolizing the push and pull of conflicting emotions. Pattern placement follows a rising signal layout, allowing for a sense of ascending hope through color density, while broken pixels introduce intentional imperfection, reflecting the chaotic beauty in emotional experiences.
