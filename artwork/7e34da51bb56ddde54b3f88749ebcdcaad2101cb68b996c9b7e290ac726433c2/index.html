Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the rigidity of control with the fluidity of surrender. The chosen palette of deep reds and soft pale blues reflects the struggle between pressure and release, with the dark colors representing control while the lighter hues suggest openness. The central conflict lies in the blend of dense clusters on the left balancing against lighter whispers on the right, illustrating the push and pull of these contrasting emotions. The pattern embodies the 'channel' shape, mimicking the flow of energy, with an overarching sense of deliberation vs. ease, capturing the essence of the seed text.
