Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the delicate balance between vulnerability (represented by soft tones) and strength (depicted through darker shades). The palette combines pale mint and soft sky as a foundation (calm) with storm gray and near black to invoke tension and depth. The main collision occurs in the center, where warm colors (pale mint) meet cool contrasts (storm gray), illustrating the conflict between exposure and safety. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, creating a sense of fracture, echoing the vulnerable aspects of the seed while maintaining a stronger outer layer.
