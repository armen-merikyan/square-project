Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette blends sepia, tan, and faded blue to evoke a sense of nostalgia and subtle warmth, allowing the eye to drift between the colors. The left side carries a heavier weight with deep browns, while the right introduces lighter tones, suggesting a push-pull dynamic. The use of broken pixels engenders imperfection and echoes the concept of fragmented memories. The contrast arises in the transition from dark to light, creating a visual narrative from weight to lift, dense presence to gentle absence.
