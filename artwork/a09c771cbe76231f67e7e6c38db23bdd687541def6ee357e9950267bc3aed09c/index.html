Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the duality of restlessness and peace, represented through contrasting color placement. The palette of rich greens and warm golds reflects both calmness and agitation. The central focus, a busy convergence of colors, portrays memory and tension, while surrounding muted tones create negative space, enhancing the sense of conflict. The diagonal arrangement indicates movement, with one side reflecting harmony and the other embodying unease, signifying the struggle of returning to peace amidst chaos.
