Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional conflict between vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors and dense patterns that collide and interrupt each other. The palette combines warm coral and orange tones with cool blue hues, suggesting warmth and protection against the backdrop of empty spaces. The main collision occurs at the center where the strong lattice pattern meets the soft pulse marks, emphasizing both tension and balance. The layout, with a quiet channel cutting through dense fields, reinforces the sense of flow, mirroring the journey through vulnerability to strength.
