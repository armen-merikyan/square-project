Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, with hues that reflect the collision of these contrasting states. The palette features warm coral and orange which symbolize strength, intertwined with softer shades of pink and deeper charcoal that signify vulnerability. The center of the square is dominated by the vibrant tones, representing strength, while the periphery is softened, illustrating the fragility of vulnerability. The broken line motif represents the constant interplay and interruption between these emotions, creating an evocative visual rhythm. The distribution of colors creates a scattered repair layout that reflects the complexity of intertwining emotions.
