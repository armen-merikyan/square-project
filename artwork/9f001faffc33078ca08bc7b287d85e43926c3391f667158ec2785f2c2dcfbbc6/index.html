Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between defiance and submission. The contrasting palette of warm reds and cool grays signifies love that can shift to betrayal. The top half bursts with energetic, noisy patterns symbolizing defiance, while the bottom half portrays a more structured, subdued layout representing submission. Main contrasts emerge where the vibrant reds collide with muted grays, creating a visual narrative of tension and emotional push/pull.
