Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between confusion and clarity, conveyed through a palette that juxtaposes deep navy and dim cyan with stark pockets of white. This contrast creates a visual tension, suggestive of a symmetry that is interrupted—akin to broken pixels. The flow, moving diagonally, helps suggest a pulse, with structured grid patterns in the top half representing clarity and more chaotic arrangements below representing confusion. The negative space is maintained to evoke a sense of unresolved tension in the composition.
