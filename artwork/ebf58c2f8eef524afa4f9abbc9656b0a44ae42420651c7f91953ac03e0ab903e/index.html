Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between control and surrender using a palette that embodies emotional conflict. The colors transition from deep maroon to soft pink, illustrating the struggle of containment against the release of feelings. The checker pattern represents control, while the stripes invoke a sense of surrender. The central collision, where these two patterns meet, creates a focal point that highlights this emotional negotiation. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional disruption, echoing the imperfections present when navigating control.
