Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between nostalgia and progress, symbolizing a split horizontal flow. The top half is filled with pale blues and whites, suggesting calmness and nostalgia, while the bottom half incorporates deeper blacks to evoke the weight of progress and change. The jagged edges emphasize collision, and the overall sharp contrasts reflect conflicting emotions. The checker pattern at the center juxtaposes structure against softer stripes around, embodying the lingering shadows of the past against the clarity of the present.
