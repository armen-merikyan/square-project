Artist Statement

The emotional contrast of love and betrayal is depicted through a palette of warm pinks and reds versus muted grays. Dense clusters on the left represent passionate love, while intermittent empty spaces on the right symbolize betrayal's disillusionment. The placement of chaotic stripes that clash with flowing lines captures the collision between order and chaos, echoing the fractured heart. This arrangement creates a dynamic tension that resonates with the seed's themes of rising signals amidst falling despair.
