Artist Statement

This artwork explores the conflict between loneliness and connection. The palette, a blend of deep navy and muted dim cyan, underscores contrasting feelings. The broken pixels simulate interruptions in connection, hinting at fragmented memories or fleeting moments. The layout employs a zigzag flow from right to left, with darker colors framing a lighter core, evoking emotional tension. The negative spaces create isolation while the central colors suggest warmth, balancing solitude with connection.
