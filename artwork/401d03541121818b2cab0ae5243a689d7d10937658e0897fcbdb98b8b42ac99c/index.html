Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional duality of relief versus dread through a contrasting palette that captures intense feelings. The warm burnt orange and gold evoke relief, while the dark red and charcoal embody dread, pushing against each other dynamically. The layout 'rising_signal' emphasizes upward movement, suggesting a battle between hope and weight. The pattern combination—rings for relief and noise for dread—creates a rhythm of tension and release, while broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, symbolizing the cracks in stability. The central light versus dark shapes merge in a way that captures collision and imbalance without clear definitions, fostering an abstract interpretation of emotional struggle.
