Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between clarity and chaos, where the vibrant accents symbolize the 'signal' trying to emerge from the environmental 'noise.' The palette of navy, black, dim cyan, and a single white accent represents isolation and effort amidst darker tones, creating contrast and depth. The main collision occurs in the center, where the organized pulses of color interrupt the darker noise patterns, evoking conflict and resolution. The layout embodies 'two pulses' as the vibrant patterns flow through negative space, suggesting movement and a struggle for dominance, reflecting the seed's themes of exposure and memory.
