Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines warmth of love with the starkness of betrayal, using a palette of pinks and reds for warmth juxtaposed with dark maroon and gray reflecting conflict. The layout emphasizes a vertical split—left warm, right cool—illustrating the contrast between love and betrayal. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, suggesting cracks in the heart. This arrangement creates tension and chaos against noise, embodying the struggle between intimacy and distance, ultimately presenting a narrative of fractured relationships.
