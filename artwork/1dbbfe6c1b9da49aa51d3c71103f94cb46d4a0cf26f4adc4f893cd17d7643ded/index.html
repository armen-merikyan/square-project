Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between desire and emptiness. The selected warm colors (yellow and orange) symbolize desire and light, contrasting against the cool muted purple and deep blue that signify emptiness and depth. The layout centers tension around a core conflict of bright versus dark, capturing a narrative echo of struggle and resolution. The patterns of noise and dots create a feeling of pressure and release, while broken pixels introduce imperfection and complexity, embodying the emotional collisions hinted at in the seed.
