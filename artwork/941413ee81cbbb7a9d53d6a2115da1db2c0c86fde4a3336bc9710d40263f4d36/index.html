Artist Statement

The emotional tone of "Echoes of Signal" explores the tension between clarity and interference. The palette, 'lonely_signal', combines navy and black for depth, juxtaposed with dim cyan and white to represent hope and clarity amidst chaos. The main contrast appears centrally with a defined pulse-like arrangement of bright colors, interrupted by darker, disrupting fields, echoing the collision between signal and noise. The broken pixel elements are scattered throughout to convey imperfection, symbolizing echoes of memory and interruption, allowing the viewer to feel the narrative of resilience against chaotic noise.
