Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tension between relief and dread. The palette combines soft pink and deep maroon to evoke warmth and heaviness, contrasting the delicate light tones against the darker hues. The arrangement suggests a top-to-bottom flow, with light colors illuminating the top, while darker tones sink below, representing gravity and weight. The subtle interjection of broken pixels indicates imperfection, reflecting the dichotomy of calm and impending dread through engaging hues and placements.
