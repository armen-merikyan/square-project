Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between opposites: defiance and submission. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green symbolizes the tension between hope and fear. The thick outer edges represent a restrictive frame while the soft gold pixel in the center embodies lightness amidst weighted darkness. The layout's center seed structure creates a dichotomy where stark contrasts—dark versus light—interact, suggesting unresolved dynamics of betrayal and love. The flowing vertical arrangement mimics falling pixels, while jagged boundaries echo noise, reflecting chaos against order.
