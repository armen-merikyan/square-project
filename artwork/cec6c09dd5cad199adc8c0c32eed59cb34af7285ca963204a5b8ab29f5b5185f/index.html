Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional tension between defiance and submission through contrasting colors and forms. The palette includes muted tones like sepia and dust gray to represent subdued submission, while brighter accents like faded blue evoke defiance. The central collision occurs with angular, interrupted ripples of defiance meeting the softer, ash-like fields of submission, creating an aggressive yet introspective dynamic. The ratio leans slightly towards submission, establishing an overall tone of hesitance interacting with bursts of resistance, embodying the abstract concepts from the seed without literal representation.
