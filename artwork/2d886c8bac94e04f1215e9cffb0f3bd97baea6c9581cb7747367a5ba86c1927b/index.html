Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tug-of-war between rage and calm, using a palette of deep red and pale blue to evoke intense feelings. The arrangement emphasizes a sprawling calm that is disrupted by jagged bursts of red, representing moments of rage. The composition follows a rising flow, creating a visual tension that mirrors the internal struggle suggested by the seed. The careful placement of colors allows a glimpse into the collision of emotions, where calm blues bleed into aggressive reds, emphasized by broken pixel colors that create a sense of imperfection. The top-heavy design echoes feelings of weight and imbalance, while the negative space remains to suggest openness and the idea of calmness being overshadowed. Overall, this abstract piece expresses the complex dynamics of emotions in a controlled yet chaotic manner.
