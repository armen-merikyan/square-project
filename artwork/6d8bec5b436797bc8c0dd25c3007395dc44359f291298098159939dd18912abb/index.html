Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve, reflecting the ongoing struggle of repair amidst chaos. The palette, 'ancient_fire', evokes warmth and intensity, using burnt orange and dark red to signify urgency contrasted with the grounded charcoal, representing serenity. The contrast is emphasized through the scattered application of color, creating an interplay between the bright patches of gold against a predominantly dark field, symbolizing hope amidst damage. The layout, resembling a 'scattered repair', positions the brighter pixels strategically across the canvas, suggesting incomplete paths of restoration that echo with unresolved motion and imply a tension between containment and overflow. The intersection of colors and the arrangement creates a sense of rhythmic pulse, resonating with the heart of the theme.
