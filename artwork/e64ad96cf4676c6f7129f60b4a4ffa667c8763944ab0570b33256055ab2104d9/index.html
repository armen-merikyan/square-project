Artist Statement

This abstract artwork embraces the emotional tension between love and betrayal, using a palette of warm reds and cool grays to symbolize conflicting feelings. The composition features bright colors that clash against darker backgrounds, creating a sense of collision. The central pattern—a maze—reflects complexity, while the surrounding areas of dense color evoke chaos and uncertainty. Broken pixels introduce imperfection and tension, echoing the fragility of relationships, while strategic negative space enhances the emotional pull, suggesting the ebb and flow of connection and estrangement.
