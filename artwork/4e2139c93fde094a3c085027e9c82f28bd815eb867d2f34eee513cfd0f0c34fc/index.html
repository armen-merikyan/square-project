Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, represented by a palette that juxtaposes dark navy with bright cyan, symbolizing the struggle between desire and strength. The main contrast is centralized, where the deep navy represents unresolved longing while the brighter cyan denotes moments of calm resolve. Scattered repair patches form a disjointed connection across the 8x8 grid, illustrating gaps filled with bursts of light, echoing the idea of bridging desires and asserting strength. The layout suggests a fractured but hopeful convergence, maintaining a subtle negative space that enhances the central collision.
