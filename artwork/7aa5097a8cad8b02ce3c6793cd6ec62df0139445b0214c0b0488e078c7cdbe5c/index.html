Artist Statement

The composition captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, with dark tones representing strength and lighter shades symbolizing vulnerability. The palette combines dense, muted hues with bright highlights to create a dynamic contrast in the center. This suggests a narrative of protection amidst potential breakdowns, where stronger edges encircle a fragile core, echoing the themes in the seed. The broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions that amplify the feeling of tension and imperfections inherent in vulnerability.
