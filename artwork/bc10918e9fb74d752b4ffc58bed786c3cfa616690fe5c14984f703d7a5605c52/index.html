Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between certainty and curiosity, represented by a palette that oscillates between warm coral and cold slate. The main collision occurs at the center with a vibrant signal cyan disrupting the warmer tones, symbolizing curiosity rising against a backdrop of certainty. Negative space is employed to allow the eye to breathe, enhancing the contrast between dense, maze-like patterns and smoother halo forms. This reflects the tension and dynamic between control and exploration as suggested by the seed.
