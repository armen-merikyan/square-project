Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of loneliness and connection through a careful arrangement of colors. The chosen palette of dark maroon, gray, and pink evokes feelings of isolation juxtaposed with warmth, symbolizing hope. The grid pattern reflects the loneliness, while the sporadic brighter colors signify attempts to connect, disrupting the uniformity with moments of vibrancy. The arrangement creates a tension between vast emptiness and scattered warmth, illustrating the push and pull of human emotions. The flow follows a zigzag design to enhance the sense of movement across the canvas, embodying a struggle between stillness and emotion, creating intentional breaks in the pattern to echo the theme of broken memory and connection.
