Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional interplay of longing and resolve through the juxtaposition of dynamic pulse marks and structured stepwell patterns. The palette of 'signal_noise' colors conveys both vibrancy and depth, with electric blue and hot magenta representing urgency and life, while dead black and static gray introduce a meditative contrast to evoke moments of reflection. The central collision happens where the vibrant highlights clash with the quieter tones, illustrating the tension between desire and acceptance. The use of broken pixels further reinforces the theme of imperfection and the nuanced beauty of repair amidst damage, reflecting the emotional journey within the grid. The layout is deliberately split horizontally to emphasize the dual nature of the feelings represented.
