Artist Statement

This composition represents the emotional juxtaposition of strength and vulnerability through the contrasting patterns of grid and glitch. The left half (strength) displays a stable grid structure in deeper greys and blacks, signaling rootedness, while the right half (vulnerability) disrupts with softer glitch patterns in warm whites and pinks, suggesting fragility. The central area, where these two halves collide, produces an intensity of color transition, inviting the viewer to navigate between solidity and delicacy. The palette reflects a balance of softness and severity, emphasizing the narrative of a broken mirror—fragile yet beautifully complex.
