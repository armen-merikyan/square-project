Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dichotomy of control and surrender. The left side leans into controlled, even patterns, utilizing muted sepia and dust gray tones that evoke a sense of restraint. The right side contrasts this with vibrant faded blue rings that embody yielding and open energy. The collision occurs at the center, where the stark transition from uniformity to swirling chaos highlights the tension between containment and overflow. Broken pixels are integrated into both the control and surrender patterns, creating intentional disruptions that suggest imperfection and the complexities of emotional release, allowing viewers to engage with the abstract narrative.
