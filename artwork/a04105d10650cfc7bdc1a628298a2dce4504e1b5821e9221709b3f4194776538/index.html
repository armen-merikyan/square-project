Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette consists of clean white and ice blue reflecting serenity, while saturated tones like violet gray and warning orange evoke urgency. The main contrast occurs at the center, symbolizing a collision between desire and determination, reflecting brokenness through glitch patterns. The layout features an open gate inviting a dynamic flow, allowing the quieter fields to exist alongside more agitated, densely packed patches. This balance signifies the push and pull in the emotional narrative.
