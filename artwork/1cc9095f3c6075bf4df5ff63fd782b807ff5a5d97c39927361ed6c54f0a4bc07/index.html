Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a palette that balances soft tones and saturated colors, echoing the clash between these opposing forces. The layout creates a sense of tension, with brighter pixels representing resilience at the center surrounded by softer, muted colors suggestive of submission and a quiet backdrop. The diagonal placement of the colors symbolizes a rising pulse, giving a rhythmic flow that contrasts with the scattered placements, embodying the chaos of the themes. Broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, creating a sense of depth and texture that heightens the emotional gravity.
