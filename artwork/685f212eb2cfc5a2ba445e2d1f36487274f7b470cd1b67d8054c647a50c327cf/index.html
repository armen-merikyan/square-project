Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a struggle between the envy of starkness and gratitude for complexity. The palette combines electric blue and lime to represent intensity, contrasted against black and magenta, denoting weight and depth. The main collision occurs diagonally, where structured maze patterns reflect gratitude on one side, while jagged mirror fragments signify envy on the other. This dichotomy creates tension, embodying the theme of duality within the abstract representation.
