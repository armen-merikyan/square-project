Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between control and panic through a dark, muted palette accentuated by jagged edge patterns. The deep blacks symbolize areas of control while the fragmented lines of dim cyan evoke feelings of chaos. The color placement creates a central collision point, reflecting a spark caught between the two states. The outer layer of solid color contrasts with a chaotic core, representing the internal argument and emotional grid outlined by the seed.
