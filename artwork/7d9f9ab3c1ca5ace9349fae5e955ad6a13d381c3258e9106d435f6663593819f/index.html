Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the tension between certainty and curiosity—solid blocks of color reflect certainty, while shattered accents signify curiosity. The chosen palette of warm coral and cold slate embodies the duality of warmth against the cool detachment of chaos, creating a guided visual journey from the dense top to the softer bottom. The collision occurs centrally, where colors clash and overlap, mirroring the struggle of contrasting emotions. This grid structure evokes an ongoing dialogue, reflecting rising signals amidst receding edges, while incorporating broken pixels to suggest the imperfections of this balance.
