Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a battle between structure and chaos, where the top rows symbolize 'signal' in muted tones achieving a soft rhythm, while the bottom rows represent 'noise' with sharp contrasts creating interference. Utilizing a palette of navy, black, dim cyan, and white introduces a sense of isolation yet maintains clarity in the 'signal'. The main contrast occurs as the structured patterns of 'signal' collide with disruptive patches in the 'noise', evoking the essence of tangled communication within a digital landscape.
