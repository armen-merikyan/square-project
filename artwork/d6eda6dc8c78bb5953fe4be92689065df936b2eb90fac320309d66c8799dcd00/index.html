Artist Statement

This piece explores the interplay between 'restlessness' and 'peace' through a palette defined by stark contrasts of light and dark. The use of ice blue and warning orange punctuates the composition, representing fleeting moments of clarity against a backdrop of confusion. The tension is expressed diagonally, with darker shades clustering in one corner, symbolizing pressure, while the lighter colors escape towards the opposite edge, suggesting a yearning for calm. Broken pixels disrupt the visual flow, embodying the theme of memory versus motion, leaving an imprint of urgency juxtaposed with quietude.
