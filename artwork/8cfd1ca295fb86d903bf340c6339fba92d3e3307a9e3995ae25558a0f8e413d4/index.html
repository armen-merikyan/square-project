Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between certainty and curiosity through the use of a high-contrast palette of muted and vibrant colors. The palette, 'melancholy_wonder', combines soft cyan and muted plum to invoke a sense of quiet thought against a backdrop of restless exploration. The dominant color of soft cyan symbolizes certainty, taking up about 65% of the space, while waves of muted plum and lavender gray break across it, illustrating curiosity. The directed upward flow suggests hope or escape, while intentional broken pixel placements act as disruptions, akin to sudden thoughts or questions. The main collision occurs centrally, where the vibrant and muted colors collide, creating a ripple effect that evokes a dialogue between the two emotional states.
