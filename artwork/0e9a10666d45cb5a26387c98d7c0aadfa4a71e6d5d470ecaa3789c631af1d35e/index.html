Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection, with an off-center pattern that underscores isolation amidst a sparse arrangement. The muted palette of sepia, tan, and faded blue reflects a sense of nostalgia, evoking broken memories. The predominantly dark left side contrasts sharply with lighter hues on the right, symbolizing a delicate push against isolation. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, mimicking fragmented thoughts, while the sparse layout allows for a temporal breath of space, emphasizing the solitude. The composition flows from left to right, echoing the struggle between overwhelming weight on one side and the fleeting lightness on the other.
