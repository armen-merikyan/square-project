Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between yearning and determination. The palette features deep blues and muted yellows, suggesting both a somber depth (longing) and a hopeful glimmer (resolve). The contrast occurs predominantly at the center where radial patterns (longing) collide with the fault lines (resolve), mirroring the broken yet hopeful aspects of the seed's meaning. The arrangement allows negative space to invoke a sense of unfulfilled desire, while broken pixels represent the scars that come from unresolved feelings.
