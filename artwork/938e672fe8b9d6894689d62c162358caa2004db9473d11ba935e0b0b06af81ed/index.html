Artist Statement

This piece contrasts the depths of grief, depicted through dense blue tones, against joyful bursts of orange and yellow. The layout mirrors itself but breaks with corrupted pixels that interrupt the flow, reflecting emotional conflict. A radial pattern radiates outward in the joyful colors while grief's crack-like patterns create a fragmented tension, implying the struggle between joy and sorrow. The contrast of dense vs. empty creates a dance of visual weight while leaving negative space to suggest introspection.
