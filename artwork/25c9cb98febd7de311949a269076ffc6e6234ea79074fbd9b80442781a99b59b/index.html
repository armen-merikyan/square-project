Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between tenderness and rage through a contrast of sharp and soft colors. The palette, chosen from deep reds and muted blues, symbolizes rage’s intensity and tenderness’s calmness, respectively. The bright accents emerge from a dense base, illustrating the struggle between emotional states. The main collision occurs in the center with jagged red interactions cutting into a serene blue background, emphasizing conflict amid the softer tones. The composition's layout, with a quiet field of negation on the edges, enhances the emotional depth and complexity, representing the multifaceted nature of inner turmoil.
