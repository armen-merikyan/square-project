Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between loneliness and connection through a palette of deep navy, stark black, and a hint of dim cyan—a reflection of isolation contrasted by fleeting connection. The top-weighted layout suggests a burden, with dark colors dominating the upper sections, while the central burst of dim cyan introduces a point of interest, representing a moment of potential connection. Crosshatching patterns expand outward, creating a fluctuation against the quiet black, indicating hidden layers of emotions. Each row is carefully crafted to maintain negative space, emphasizing feelings of vulnerability and the struggle against the overwhelming presence of isolation.
