Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between longing and resolve, illustrated by a palette of soft sky and storm gray. The contrast between the quiet mint and faded gold forms a dominantly serene base with contained, warm accents echoing the sense of urgency. The main collision occurs diagonally from the center, where soft echoes of longing clash against the steady, structured rings of resolve, creating visual tension and narrative depth that embodies the seed's emotional complexity.
