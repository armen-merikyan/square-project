Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection, using a contrasting palette of deep reds and cool pale blues. The top half, representing loneliness, employs a maze pattern that evokes isolation through sporadic warmth against a backdrop of muted space, enhancing subconscious feeling of separation. The bottom half mirrors this with a frame-like pattern that celebrates connection via structured unity in the cooler tones, suggesting a formation amidst chaos. The central pixels collide at the center with contrasting colors, representing both a source of light and tension. Broken pixels disrupt the otherwise structured flow, adding an element of imperfection and emphasizing the chaos of emotional experiences. Overall, the piece balances negative space and density, allowing the viewer to navigate feelings of being both disconnected and strongly bound to others, reflecting the seed’s essence.
