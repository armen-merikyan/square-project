Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between clear signals and overwhelming noise, drawing from the emotions embedded in the seed. The palette mixes deep navy and black for a sense of density, alongside dim cyan for a pulse of clarity and alertness, introducing contrast in layers. The collision of patterns suggests a push and pull between a central pulse — akin to a heartbeat — interrupted by static and disarray. This dynamic creates an emotional environment that speaks of fragility within chaos, captured through the arrangement of colors where deeper tones dominate but lighter shades intervene to provide moments of clarity.
