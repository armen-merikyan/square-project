Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette features soft off-whites and faint peaches representing vulnerability, contrasted with the deeper tones of dark navy and compressed red symbolizing strength. The layout flows upward, suggesting hope and resilience rising from the darkness. The central placement of off-white offers a calm, exposed core underneath jagged edges, representing both protection and the potential for external pressure. The broken pixels disrupt the structure, reflecting moments of vulnerability, interspersed within the dominant strong shades, creating a visually dynamic collision between safety and exposure.
