Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between love and betrayal, represented through an intricate dance of warm and cool colors. The chosen palette, a mix of pink and dark maroon, plays on the notion of warmth versus a haunting depth, reflecting longing and heartbreak. The pattern emerges from a rising signal layout, suggesting hope or escape, disrupted by strategic broken pixels that introduce an imperfect symmetry. Main contrasts lie between the vibrant pinks and the deep maroons, creating a collision of emotions while ensuring enough negative space that invites contemplation.
