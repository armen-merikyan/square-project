Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a dynamic interplay between tension and serenity, reflecting the contrasts in the seed. The palette features bold, vibrant colors against quieter, muted tones, suggesting bursts of emotion colliding with states of calm. The main contrast occurs between the warm reds and oranges that signify pressure and intensity, juxtaposed with soft blues and greys that illustrate quietness and rhythm. This creates a textured composition where colors pulse around each other, symbolizing the rhythm of emotional experiences.
