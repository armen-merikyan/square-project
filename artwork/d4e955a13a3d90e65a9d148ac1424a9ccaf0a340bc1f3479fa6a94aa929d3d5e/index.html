Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional clash between defiance and submission, represented through color and placement. The palette, 'hunger_restraint', leverages warm reds and muted creams against deeper blacks and grays to establish a contrast between urgency and calmness. The central pixel area pulses with defiant static patterns, while the edges create a frame of submission, echoing the layout of 'open_gate.' The main collision occurs at the center, echoing the struggle of resistance versus acceptance, with broken pixels introducing imperfections that signify vulnerability within this emotional conflict.
