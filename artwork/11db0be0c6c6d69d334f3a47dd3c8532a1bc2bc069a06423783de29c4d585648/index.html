Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between softness and severity, utilizing a palette that merges gentle powder pinks and warm whites with dark accents of carbon black and sharp crimson. The center is densely saturated with the soft tones, producing a gentle warmth, while the edges consist of stark darks that create a contrasting boundary. The use of stacked patterns in the center represents resilience and comfort, while the dotted patterns at the edges reflect a harshness and the intensity of severity. This interplay creates a sense of conflict and balance, effectively celebrating the emotional narrative from 'guilt to forgiveness'.
