Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between restlessness and peace through its contrasting colors and patterns. The palette draws from deep moods, contrasting the quiet depth of mourning black and pale yellow as a metaphor for light amidst darkness. Central positioning of darker tones creates a pressure point, while lighter accents project moments of peace, symbolizing fleeting memories. The disrupted pattern mimics the shatter theme, embodying chaotic energy against a dense, quiet backdrop, presenting an emotional dialogue between stillness and disturbance.
