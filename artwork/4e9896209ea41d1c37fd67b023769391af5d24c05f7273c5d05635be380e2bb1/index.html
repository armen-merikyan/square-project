Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength through a limited color palette of deep maroon, muted grays, and a soft pink that serves as a quiet field. The center displays a pale core suggesting exposure, interrupted by stronger blocks of darker hues indicating protection. A radial pattern of the lighter color collides with dense, structured patterns of darker tones, creating a visual narrative of conflict and balance. The main collision occurs centrally, mirroring the emotional core where strength and vulnerability intersect.
