Artist Statement

This artwork juxtaposes the emotional tones of vulnerability and strength through a palette that balances soft mint and soft sky colors with deeper tones of storm gray and near black, creating a duality. The quiet fields represent strength at the center, while the edge patterns illustrate vulnerability. The collision occurs predominantly in the horizontal center, where lighter and darker shades interact sharply, suggesting tension and interaction between the two emotional states. The broken pixels are scattered strategically, introducing imperfection that symbolizes the fragility and unpredictability inherent in the theme, while maintaining a sense of structure and balance throughout.
