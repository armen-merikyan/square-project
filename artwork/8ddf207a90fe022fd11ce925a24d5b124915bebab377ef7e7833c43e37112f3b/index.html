Artist Statement

The composition embodies the duality of softness and severity, using a palette of powder pink and steel gray to contrast gentle touches against cold boundaries. The light colors create a soft ascent while the darker shades at the bottom introduce gravity, illustrating the emotional weight of unresolved feelings. The 'falling_pixels' layout reflects a descent of emotion, captured in the channels of gray that interrupt soft areas, just as guilt and forgiveness can disrupt tranquility. The main collision occurs at the center, where soft pinks meet stark blacks, emphasizing tension and release, evoking a narrative of internal conflict.
