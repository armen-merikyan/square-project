Artist Statement

The artwork encapsulates the tension between vulnerability and strength through its color juxtaposition and structural layout. The center houses 'pale interior' colors, symbolizing exposure, while the dense 'thick edge' colors create a protective border, representing strength. The chosen palette features softer hues like distant lilac and warm shadow against sharp accents of cool blue and lamp gold, emphasizing the contrast. The collision occurs at the edges where strong colors rise to meet the weak, while broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, enhancing the sense of vulnerability and making the connection more resonant.
