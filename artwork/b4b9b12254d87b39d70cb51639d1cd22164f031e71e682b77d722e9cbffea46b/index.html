Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between defiance and submission, illustrated through the palette of stark black, pale blue, and varying whites which heighten the tension. The contrast comes from squares of black pushing against softer, lighter hues, representing the conflict of weight versus lightness. The placement suggests an upward flow, reflecting a desire to break free, interrupted by bottom-weighted clusters that suggest obstacles. The chosen colors and arrangement create a sense of movement and collision, mirroring the push and pull of emotions described in the seed.
