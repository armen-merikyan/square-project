Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between nostalgia's warmth and the sharpness of progress. The selected palette, consisting of electric blue and magenta against a backdrop of deep black, symbolizes this duality—blue embodying calm nostalgia while magenta injects intensity associated with progress. Contrasts emerge from densely layered pixels providing a chaotic foundation, interrupted by a few lighter spaces that evoke a sense of airiness, implying moments of introspection amidst the frenzy. The asymmetrical layout introduces broken pixels, offering imperfection that resonates with the themes of rise versus fall, evoking feelings of tension at the center of the square while the edges provide a grounded weight. Overall, the piece embodies the unresolved conflict between reminiscence and forward movement.
