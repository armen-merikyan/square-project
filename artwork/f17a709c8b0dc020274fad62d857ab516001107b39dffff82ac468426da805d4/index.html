Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a dance between restlessness and peace, with the colors suggesting a saturated pull toward calm restrained by chaotic motions. The palette of red, black, pale blue, and white reflects a contrast of warmth and coolness, emphasizing the tension. The primary collision occurs diagonally from the lower left (restlessness) to the upper right (peace), with the lighter, soothing colors battling the darker, more aggressive hues in a constant push and pull. The pattern logic draws from the 'hinge' of chaos transitioning into the structured 'ladder' of harmony, allowing the viewer to feel the struggle for serenity amidst turmoil.
