Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between joy and grief through a carefully chosen palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple. Yellow and orange represent the spark of joy, while deep blue and muted purple evoke the heaviness of grief. The pattern—mirrored with fragments—is intended to provide a sense of symmetry tainted by subtle breaks, symbolizing the disruption of joy by grief. The left-leaning density of colors creates a dynamic pull, suggesting weight and balance. The clash of bright and muted tones centers the piece, allowing emotional contrasts to breathe in the surrounding negative space.
