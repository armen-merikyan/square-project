Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between safety and exposure—a protective outer layer contrasts against a vulnerable core. The palette reflects this duality, with earthy browns evoking security, while cooler blues suggest openness. The main collision occurs in the center, where pale and dark colors interact, representing the tumult between exposed feelings and sheltering warmth. Broken pixels introduce imperfect echoes of uncertainty, enhancing the overall theme of navigating vulnerability.
