Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between control and surrender, illustrating a struggle between dense clusters of pressure and the gentle release into open space. The palette of stain brown and deep wine evokes warmth and tension, while soft gold adds a hint of optimism. The main collision occurs centrally, where the darker shades of brown create a thick outline, contrasting the softer tones that spread outward, illustrating a release. The intertwining braid and dot patterns embody the complexity of the conflict between containment and overflow.
