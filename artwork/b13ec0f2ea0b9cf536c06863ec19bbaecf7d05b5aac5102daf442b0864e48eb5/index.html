Artist Statement

The composition reflects the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve, evoking a sense of friction and resolution through color contrasts. The palette, comprising deep blacks and pale blues with sharp reds, portrays a grappling between depth and intensity. The dominant black and white evoke clear boundaries of resolve, while the broken pixels echo longing and vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center, where stark contrasts meet, creating an unsettling yet captivating tension that embodies the narrative of repair and damage.
