Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured here is one of dynamic tension, reflecting the conflict between submission and defiance. The color palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple conveys contrasting emotional currents, with yellows and oranges representing the warmth of defiance and deep blues symbolizing the calm but pressured state of submission. The composition follows an argument grid layout that emphasizes the struggle between the two emotional extremes. The main collision occurs at the center, where brighter colors clash against muted shades, and broken pixels suggest imperfection and transience. This abstract representation embodies the rhythm of release amidst pressure, encapsulating the nuanced interplay of emotions.
