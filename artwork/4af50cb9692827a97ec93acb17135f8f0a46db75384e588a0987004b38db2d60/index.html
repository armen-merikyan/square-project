Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between restlessness and peace, highlighted by the contrasting palettes of muted tones and bright accents. The colors reflect guilt and forgiveness, creating a palette that is predominantly soft browns and grays, invoking a quiet field. The main collision occurs along the diagonal, where warmer gold tones interrupt the cooler ash gray and deep wine. This layout infuses a sense of dynamic movement against a backdrop of calm, creating a visual tension that intrigues and engages the viewer.
