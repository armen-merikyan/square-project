Artist Statement

This piece captures the interplay between vulnerability and strength through the use of a muted palette and broken edges to symbolize exposure against a dense backdrop. The color choices represent an emotional spectrum: off-white for exposure, dark navy for strength, with punctuations of compressed red indicating tension. The main contrast occurs horizontally across the center, suggesting an intrusion of strength into the vulnerable space. The abstract pattern emerges from the tension created by irregular placements, representing how vulnerability can protect or expose amidst strength.
