Artist Statement

This composition confronts the contrasting emotions of joy and grief via a split layout, with bright yellows and oranges conveying joy on the left against deep blues and muted purples representing grief on the right. The collision occurs in the center with bright colors that fight for space against darker tones, symbolizing the struggle between light and weight. The wave shape subtly emerges through rhythmic placements, enhancing the emotional pull of the piece, portraying an ongoing, dynamic conflict between exposure and concealment.
