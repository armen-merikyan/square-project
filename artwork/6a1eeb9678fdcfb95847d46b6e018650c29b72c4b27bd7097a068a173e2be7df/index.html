Artist Statement

This piece explores the delicate balance between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that juxtaposes cosmic indigo and deep teal against stark contrasts of void black and star white. The spatial arrangement reflects a mirror broken layout, emphasizing the tension of exposed softness meeting thick boundaries. The dominant presence of dark colors suggests safety, while sporadic lighter colors symbolize moments of fragility. The main collision occurs at the center, forming an intentional disarray that echoes themes of resilience amidst uncertainty.
