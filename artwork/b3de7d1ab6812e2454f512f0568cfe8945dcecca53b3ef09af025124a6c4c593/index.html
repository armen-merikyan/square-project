Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the tension between desire (warm hues) and emptiness (cool, muted tones). The palette includes sepia and faded blue for a sense of nostalgia and tranquility, with contrasting edges creating a clash between order and chaos. The central area evokes rising signals amidst the noise of fragmented patterns. The checker and grid arrangements interplay, with broken pixels adding imperfections to symbolize disruption in longing. The interaction between rich, warm tones and cooler, emptier spaces highlights the essence of unfulfilled desires, creating a rhythmic yet fragmented visual narrative.
