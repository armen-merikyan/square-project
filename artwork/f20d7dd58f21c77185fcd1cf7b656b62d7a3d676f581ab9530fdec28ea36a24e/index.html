Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional conflict between panic and control, embodied through the contrast of muted sepia tones against bright faded blue sparks. The palette reflects a nostalgic 'broken memory' vibe, with the warm browns suggesting stability while the cool blues inject an element of unrest. The layout descends, reminiscent of rain or tears, embodying a flow that spirals counterclockwise, establishing movement that feels both heavy and unstable. The arrangement of colors creates a collision between dense clusters of deep shades and bright interruptions, highlighting the tension and emotional struggle embedded in the seed. Imperfections are introduced through broken pixels to symbolize the chaos within the search for control, adding depth and complexity to the composition.
