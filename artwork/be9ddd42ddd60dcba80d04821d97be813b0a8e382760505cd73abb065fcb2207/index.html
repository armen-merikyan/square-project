Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects an interplay between awe and terror, using a palette that oscillates between dark violet and soft gold. The blacks signify the underlying pressures of terror, while the pale greens and golds suggest hopeful emergence, creating a rhythmic contrast. The rising layout evokes a sense of aspiration against suppressed feelings, while broken pixels introduce imperfection, embodying the theme of collision. This abstract pattern suggests movement from quiet spaces into denser emotional territories, marked by sharp color contrasts that invite the viewer to explore the tensions at play.
