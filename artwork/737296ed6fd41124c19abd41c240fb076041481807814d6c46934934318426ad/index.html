Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between hope (spark) and despair (ash). The chosen palette reflects a transition from vibrant golds to muted grays, suggesting memories that linger yet are dulled by time. The central clusters of golden tones collide with darker shades, representing the struggle between aspiration and resignation. The layout retains negative space as a quiet backdrop, amplifying the chaotic interaction of brighter accents against softer fields, giving the piece a sense of ongoing conflict and resilience.
