Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between chaos (panic) and structure (control). The palette, inspired by shades of pinks and dark maroon, evokes feelings of warmth intertwined with deeper emotional conflicts. The stark contrast between lighter and darker colors creates a tension-filled environment where bright accents punctuate the more muted fields. The layout of the central focus and surrounding noise symbolizes chaotic emotions attempting to breach control while maintaining some negative space. This arrangement reflects moments of joy lodged against grief, creating a narrative of unresolved feelings and dynamic emotional shifts.
