Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength. Ripe green and honey gold represent stability and growth, while deeper hues like acid green and deep olive introduce elements of disruption and intensity. The layout adheres to a frame and core structure, emphasizing the contrast where the anxiety of vulnerability collides with the centered strength. The barcode pattern for vulnerability creates dynamic interruption, while quiet fields for strength provide grounding amidst the chaos, allowing for a visual narrative of exposure and protection.
