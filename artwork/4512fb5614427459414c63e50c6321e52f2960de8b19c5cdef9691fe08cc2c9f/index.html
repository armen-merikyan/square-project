Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional dichotomy of joy and grief through a central layout that holds tension between bright and muted tones. The color palette juxtaposes sunny yellows and oranges symbolizing joy against deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The central area features a pattern that emphasizes order through a grid, while the outer layers create chaos with broken arrangements, suggesting a protective yet turbulent environment. This abstract arrangement captures the essence of emotional pressure and release.
