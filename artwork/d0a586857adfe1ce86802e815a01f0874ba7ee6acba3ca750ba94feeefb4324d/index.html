Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents a struggle between defiance and submission, highlighted by a dominant quiet field color amid disruptive accents. The contrasting palette of dark navy against stark white creates tension, embodying the conflict within the borders. The main collision occurs at the center, where hard interruptions (dark colors) meet the soft collapse of lighter shades, reflecting the seed's themes of chaos vs. order and open vs. closed spaces. The inclusion of broken pixels introduces imperfection, resonating with the idea of uneven borders, while the echoing diagonal lines suggest a rising tension that permeates the composition.
