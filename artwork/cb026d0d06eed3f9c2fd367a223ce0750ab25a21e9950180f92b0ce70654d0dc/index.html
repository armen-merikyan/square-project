Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between the fleeting nature of 'spark' and the settling presence of 'ash'. Colors like moss green and rust symbolize resilience and fragility respectively, creating a visual story of contrast between vitality and decay. The pattern features a dominant muted rust field, interrupted by jagged accents of moss green, suggesting a 'last light' amidst shadows. This layout promotes a focal area where brightness collides with darkness, hinting at an unresolved tension that mirrors the conflicts in life and memory.
