Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional struggle between control and surrender. The palette of melancholy wonder combines deep blues and soft grays, suggesting a tranquil yet charged atmosphere. Dark colors frame the canvas, representing control, while lighter tones break through, symbolizing surrender and release. The main contrast occurs at the center, where dense, dark colors collide with ethereal whites and soft cyans, creating an intentional disruption that signifies tension. The contrast and arrangement reflect the balance between a locked border and an opened edge, embodying the core themes of the seed.
