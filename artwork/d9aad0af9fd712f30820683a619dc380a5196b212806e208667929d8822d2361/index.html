Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a battle between rigidity and fluidity, manifested through sharp contrasts and soft fades. The chosen palette of cosmic indigo, deep teal, and cold gold captures both the depths of control and the occasional bright burst of surrender. The main collision occurs at the center with intense color placement that represents pressure, surrounded by softer edges to suggest release and quiet. The layout starts in the corner, reflecting the invasion of control towards the center, while broken pixels add intentional disruptions that embody the chaotic nature of the theme.
