Artist Statement

This artwork plays on the emotional dichotomy of vulnerability and strength, expressed through a contrasting palette of warm and cool tones. The color choices suggest a gentle embrace with elements of tension and respite, reflecting a constant push/pull between exposure and protection. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense warmer hues collide with lighter, cooler colors, symbolizing a struggle between lightness and weight, safety and exposure. The 'broken pixels' offer imperfections, creating a subtle disruption mirroring the themes within the seed.
