Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional duality of restlessness and peace. The warm browns and accentuated flames collide with the cooler greens and whites, creating tension through contrast. The layout follows a spiral pressure, suggesting movement towards an unseen center, echoing how memories and motion interlace. Broken pixels subtly disrupt the flow, representing shattered calm amidst the search for tranquility, while the strategic use of negative space enhances the overall sense of unbalance.
