Artist Statement

This artwork represents the duality of fear and hope through contrasting colors and patterns. The left side embodies fear with jagged edges represented by dark tones and broken dot patterns. The right side radiates hope through an upward flow of soft gold and pale green, suggesting light breaking through the darkness. The subtle interplay of darkness and light creates a narrative of tension and release, as sharp elements collide with softer tones, embodying the emotional contrast of the seed.
