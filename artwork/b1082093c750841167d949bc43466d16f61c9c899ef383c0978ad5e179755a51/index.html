Artist Statement

This piece embodies the collision of calm and rage through a carefully balanced palette of stark contrasts. The dominant colors, black representing rage and pale blue signaling calm, establish a pulsing rhythm across the canvas. The flat checker patterns on one side compete against an erratic arrangement of 'broken dots' on the other, creating a visual tug-of-war that captures emotional tension. The sharp edges add intimacy to each color block, emphasizing moments of pressure and quiet release within the turmoil.
