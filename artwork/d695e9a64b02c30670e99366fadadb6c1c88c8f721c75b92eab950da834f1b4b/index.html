Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a balance between the calming influence of quiet spaces and the dense pressure of clustered colors. The palette combines soft hues like faint peach with darker tones like dark navy, creating contrast that embodies the struggle of clarity amidst confusion. The collision occurs in the center, where compressed red gathers sharply against the muted background, illustrating tension against release. The patterns of soft dots encounter the jagged edges of fault lines as they erode away towards the edges, representing the gradual transformation from pressure to quiet.
