Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional juxtaposition of softness and severity through a palette that balances warm whites and gentle pinks against sharp blacks and grays. The central area is dense with warmer hues representing softness, while the edges employ heavier, darker shades symbolizing severity. This contrast evokes a tension between the inviting calmness at the center and the starkness of the periphery, encapsulating the battle between guilt and forgiveness. The pixel arrangement deliberately interrupts with broken pixels, creating scars amidst the softness, encouraging a narrative of emotional conflict and resolution.
