Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends restlessness with peace, creating a visual dialogue between unsettled energies and tranquil spaces. The selected palette of guilt and forgiveness-inspired colors, such as stain brown and soft gold, evoke warmth amidst unease. The arrangement reflects contrasting patterns, with radial forms suggesting dynamic motion colliding at a dominant center, where deep wine tones invite attention. The layout prioritizes pressure in a corner, with a wide quiet space seeing minimal interruptions, embodying the seed’s dual themes of memory versus motion, suggesting both fragmentation and continuity.
