Artist Statement

The emotional tone emphasizes the tension between longing and resolve, represented by a palette of deep reds and grays, suggesting warmth and uncertainty. The main contrast occurs in the center, where gray and dark maroon colors collide with a fault line pattern, replicating a tension of brokenness. This composition mirrors itself yet disrupts it through the strategic placement of broken pixels, creating a visual echo of conflict and serenity as each side wrestles for dominance, embodying both a gentle push and a firm grasp.
