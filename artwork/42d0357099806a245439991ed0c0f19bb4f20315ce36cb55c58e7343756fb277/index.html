Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between pressure and quietude, using a palette of muted grays and soft contrasts. The dense edge areas reflect the feeling of being crowded and pressured, while the central quiet spaces create a pause. The patterns collide in the center, where muted colors split, allowing cleaner, softer shades to weave through, echoing the idea of fragmentation against resistance. Broken pixels in strategic areas add to the feeling of tension and imperfection, while maintaining a structured yet expressive form.
