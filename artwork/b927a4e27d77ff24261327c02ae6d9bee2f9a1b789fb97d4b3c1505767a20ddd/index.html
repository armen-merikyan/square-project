Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between quietness and pressure through a balance of densely packed and softly flowing color patterns. The palette of muted hues with accents suggests both calm and intensity. The main contrast occurs on the right, where compressed reds and dark navy collide with off-whites and faint peaches, symbolizing an eruption of emotion against a backdrop of serenity. The alternating flow and broken pixel elements introduce intentional imperfections that echo the theme of disruption within symmetry.
