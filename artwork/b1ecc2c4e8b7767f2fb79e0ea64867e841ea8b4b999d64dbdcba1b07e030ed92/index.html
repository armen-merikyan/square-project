Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve. The palette, 'broken_memory', features muted sepia and faded blues that evoke nostalgia and introspection, with dusty grays providing a contrast that suggests repair and disrepair. The central area features a cluster of darker tones to symbolize 'resolve', while lighter shades define 'longing' around the periphery. The pattern logic uses a frame of tenderness edging against a sharp negative cross, creating an encapsulation of desires unmet but firmly contrasted by the strength of resolve. The broken pixels distributed throughout provide intentional disruptions, enhancing the emotional narrative of memory and recovery.
