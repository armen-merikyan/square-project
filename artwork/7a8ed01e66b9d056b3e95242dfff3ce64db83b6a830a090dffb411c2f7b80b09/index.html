Artist Statement

The emotional tone of awe vs. terror is embodied in the palette selection of electric blue, magenta, lime, and black, contrasting vibrant emotion with stark depth. The layout follows a frame and core structure, creating an outer border that expresses chaotic anxiety (black) and an inner core invoking awe (vibrant colors). The flowing pulse suggests movement from chaos to order, while broken pixels introduce a sense of instability and memory loss, contributing to the ongoing unrest. The interplay between structured grids (for awe) and stripes (for terror) emphasizes conflict and interaction, resulting in a dynamic, abstract narrative that reflects the emotional duality of the seed.
