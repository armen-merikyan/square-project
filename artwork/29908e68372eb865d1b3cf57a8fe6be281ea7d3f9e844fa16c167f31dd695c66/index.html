Artist Statement

This artwork embodies a dialogue between confusion and clarity, utilizing an 'ancient fire' palette to evoke warmth amidst shadows. The core contrast emerges through the use of jagged and soft elements, representing broken structures and flowing backgrounds. Dark colors anchor the central area, symbolizing clarity's core, while burnt orange and gold radiate outward, echoing the themes of conflict and motion. The disrupted patterns reflect falling pixels, suggesting uncertainty and a lack of symmetry, reinforcing the emotional tension throughout the piece.
