Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between panic and control. The palette features dark maroon and shades of pink, creating a contrast that reflects the struggle between warmth (control) and intensity (panic). The jagged edges evoke chaos, while the rising pattern alludes to a journey from despair to hope. Key contrasts occur in the dense clusters of chaotic color that clash against lighter, more orderly transitions - echoing the emotional collision suggested by the seed.
