Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between restlessness and peace. The chosen palette features navy and black as the grounding dark tones, complemented by dim cyan for fluidity and a single white pixel as a focal point of hope. The main contrast occurs between the left and right halves, where clustered dark pixels on the left symbolize restlessness, creating a chaotic pulse, whereas the right maintains calm symmetry with greater spacing and a halo-like effect. This diagonal argument between chaos and serenity mirrors the seed's themes, allowing memory and motion to collide without specific imagery while evoking a strong emotional resonance through color placement and pixel arrangement.
