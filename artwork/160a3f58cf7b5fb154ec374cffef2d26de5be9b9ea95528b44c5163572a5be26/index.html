Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between clarity and confusion, using a palette of burnt orange and dark red to convey warmth and discomfort against the cooler yet neutral backdrop of charcoal and gold. The center-heavy layout creates a focal point around which a checker pattern extends outward, embodying the chaos represented by confusion, while negative spaces symbolize clarity. The arrangement echoes the seed's themes—falling pixels and broken dots—resulting in a dynamic yet harmonious composition that plays with perception and emotional resonance.
