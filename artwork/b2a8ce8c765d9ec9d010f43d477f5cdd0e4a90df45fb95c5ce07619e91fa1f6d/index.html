Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between tenderness and rage by using a palette of red for passionate intensity and pale blue for calmness. The composition reflects a main collision in the center, where the warm red tide pattern disrupts the cooler glitch of pale blue, representing a struggle within an emotional channel. The uneven borders create a contrast between chaos and softness, while the broken pixels symbolize imperfections and the scars of conflicting feelings. This setup delivers a strong narrative of emotional overlap, inviting the viewer to navigate the quiet spaces filled with unresolvable tension.
