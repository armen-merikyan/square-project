Artist Statement

The emotional tone interprets the interplay of joy and grief through a balanced yet fragmented structure. Joy is represented by warm yellows and oranges, symbolizing brightness and expansion, while grief is inherent in deep blues and muted purples, evoking heaviness and drooping subtleties. The composition exhibits a central focus with tension radiating outward—grief encircles joy, creating collisions that suggest a battle between warmth and coolness. The pattern structure reflects this dynamic exchange, with soft edged noise patterns of joy clashing against the rigid crosshatch of grief. This layout mirrors a center under pressure, surrounded by distant echoes of emotion, with broken pixels introducing an element of imperfection, highlighting life's unpredictability.
