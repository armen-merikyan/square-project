Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between love and betrayal through contrasting warm and cool tones. The pink and red palettes evoke feelings of affection, while dark maroon and gray introduce a sense of danger and unease. The layout features a diagonal rise, suggesting movement away from stability, with broken pixels disrupting symmetry to symbolize the inherent conflicts within relationships. The pattern combines rings of warmth with harsh, jagged edges, creating a dynamic interplay of emotion, reflecting both nurturing and harsh realities.
