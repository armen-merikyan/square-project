Artist Statement

This abstract composition captures the emotional dichotomy of certainty and curiosity, using a palette that oscillates between warm and cool tones, suggesting both comfort and unrest. The colors of ripe green and honey gold dominate the center, indicating stability, while the contrasting acid green and deep olive along the edges reflect curiosity and the chaotic pursuit of the unknown. The layout creates a collision at the center, where high density meets the empty spaces, evoking a sense of tension as if order pervades chaos. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, symbolizing the imperfections in navigating these emotions.
