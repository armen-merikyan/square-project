Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The upper half uses dark colors with stripe patterns to convey chaotic energy and confusion, while the lower half adopts a structured grid formation and lighter hues for clarity. The transition symbolizes the pulse of conflicting emotions, with the edge style creating a glow that emphasizes the boundary between the two states. The intentional breaks in symmetry with irregular pixel placements reflect the theme of interruption.
