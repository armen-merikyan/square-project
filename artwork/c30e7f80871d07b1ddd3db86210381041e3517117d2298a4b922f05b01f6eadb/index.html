Artist Statement

The emotional tone dances between restlessness and peace, using the palette to reflect a sense of melancholy wonder. The muted tones of lavender_gray and muted_plum suggest calmness, while the sharp contrasts of rain_blue and soft_cyan reflect the underlying motion and restlessness. The layout emphasizes a diagonal split, where the chaotic frame pattern disrupts the tranquility of the flame-like shapes, creating a central collision of emotions. Broken pixels provide intentional imperfections, symbolizing memories shattering towards a quieter space. The overall arrangement gives a sense of tension and release, echoing the theme of disruption and calm.
