Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a dichotomy between longing, represented by warmer, muted tones, and resolve, illustrated through sharper, more saturated accents. The palette of pale aqua and alarm red enhances this contrast, creating moments of tension against a mist gray backdrop. The collision arises at the center, where the 'thread' pattern interlaces with 'stairs,' symbolizing the interconnectedness of these feelings amidst moments of reflection. This pattern reflects an interplay of intention and imperfection, with broken pixels disrupting the flow.
