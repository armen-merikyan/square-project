Artist Statement

This piece contrasts the emotional weights of tenderness and rage through a palette of saturated reds against muted blues and blacks. The hot, aggressive reds occupy the upper left, resembling tumultuous feelings breaking through, while the softer blues provide a soothing counterbalance, hinting at forgiveness and gentleness. The collision happens at the mid-right where saturation and muteness meet, creating a visual echo of tension and calm. The arrangement embodies a quiet channel of experience that climbs from dark depths towards explosive light, offering a journey from chaos to peace.
