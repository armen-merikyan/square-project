Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the tension between dread and relief through a contrast of dark, muted tones and lighter, airy shades. The dominant dark colors create a visual weight, embodying dread, while interspersed lighter colors suggest a release, allowing for a rhythm of contrast across the grid. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, mirroring the unpredictable nature of emotion. The central collision of dense, dark patches and lighter airy spaces echoes the shifting dynamic between chaos and order that the seed inspired.
