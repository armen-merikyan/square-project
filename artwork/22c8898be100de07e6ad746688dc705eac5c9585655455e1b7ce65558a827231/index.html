Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette includes warm hues like hot coral and blood orange, juxtaposed with cooler tones of hollow blue and charcoal, encapsulating the conflict of emotions. The main collision is central, where warm and cool colors meet, creating a visual echo of yearning versus certainty. The 'falling_pixels' layout evokes a sense of descent or loss, while broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection. Patterns reflect a veil of longing that overlaps with the steady tide of resolve, demonstrating a complex emotional narrative without recognizable imagery.
