Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrasts between pride and shame, creating a rhythm of upward and downward movement. The palette of dark violet and soft gold evokes tension and hope against a backdrop of deep black and pale green, suggesting the complexities of emotional collision. The layout emphasizes a diagonal spread, capturing both pressure and quiet moments, while the broken edges contribute to a sense of imperfection and organic flow. The distribution of colors creates a dialogue between density and space, reflecting the push and pull of emotions.
