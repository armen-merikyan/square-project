Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the tight confines of pressure against the sprawling openness of quiet. The palette features muted tones of 'off_white' and 'dust_gray' to evoke a sense of calm, contrasted by 'compressed_red' to represent pressure points. The main collision occurs at the center with dense reds, contrasting sharply with softer surrounding colors that create a visual echo of stillness interrupted by dynamic bursts. The layout reflects a border that almost encloses the space, with deliberate breaks mirroring the constant tension of the seed's themes. This suggests a narrative of fluctuating states between calm and chaos.
