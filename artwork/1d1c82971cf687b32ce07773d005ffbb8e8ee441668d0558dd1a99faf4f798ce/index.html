Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension of certainty battling against curiosity through a juxtaposition of structured dots and erratic framing. The chosen palette of 'intimacy_distance' combines warm and cool tones, thereby enhancing the contrast between order and chaos. The central conflict is accentuated by vibrant 'lamp_gold' interrupting the quiet field of 'distant_lilac', mirroring the push and pull of a rising signal against a backdrop of noise. The layout suggests a scattered repair, where broken pixels represent unresolved curiosity amidst the backdrop of certainty.
