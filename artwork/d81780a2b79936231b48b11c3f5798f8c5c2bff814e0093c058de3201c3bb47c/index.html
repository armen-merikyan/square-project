Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and chaos, embodying the concept of 'signal vs noise.' The palette of navy and black conveys depth and shadow, while dim cyan offers a moment of brightness, representing the signal. The central area is saturated with dark tones, contrasting against lighter pixels at the edges that symbolize exposure and fleeting moments of clarity. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, enhancing the narrative of conflict and memory. The main collision occurs toward the center, where dark elements clash with lighter ones, creating a dynamic interplay, reminiscent of echoes versus a steady pulse.
