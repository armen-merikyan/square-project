Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a balance between restlessness and peace, where the chaotic barcode pattern clashes with a structured patchwork. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon conveys deep emotional intensity while allowing for softer contrasts that suggest moments of tranquility. The main collision occurs both diagonally and centrally, simulating a tension between light and dark, reinforcing the idea of flametouched edges against a backdrop of muted patches. This layout emulates a fragmented memory, where scattered pieces signal the noise of restless thoughts juxtaposed against a serene core.
