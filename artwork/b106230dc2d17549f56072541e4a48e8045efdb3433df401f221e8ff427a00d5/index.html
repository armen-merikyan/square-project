Artist Statement

This composition engages the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette of pale mint, soft sky, storm gray, and faded gold illustrates fragility and resilience through color contrast—soft hues suggest vulnerability while darker, denser colors represent strength. Central collision occurs between the light and dark hues, signifying a struggle between exposure and protection, framed by an uneven border created with darker tones. Broken pixels disrupt the surface, embodying imperfections in this emotional journey. The checker pattern interspersed with stepwell formations amplifies the push and pull of vulnerabilities amidst solidity, translating the seed into an abstract narrative on emotional conflict.
