Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional dichotomy of confusion and clarity through a structured yet disrupted layout that echoes the seed's themes. The warm tones of yellow and orange clash against deep blue and muted purple, representing the inner struggle of clarity battling confusion. The arrangement features almost mirrored symmetry, disrupted by intentional broken pixels, illustrating the imperfect nature of perception. The color choices enhance the contrasting emotions, with the bright yellows and oranges pulsating amidst the cooler blues, creating visual tension and dynamic flow.
