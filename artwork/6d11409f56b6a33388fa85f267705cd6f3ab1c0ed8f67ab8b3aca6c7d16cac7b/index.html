Artist Statement

This artwork expresses the emotional duality between confusion and clarity. The selected palette of deep navy and dim cyan hints at isolation (navy) while also inviting tranquility (cyan). The pattern employs rising signals that clash and intertwine, representing the tension between clarity and confusion, especially in the abrupt transitions between dark and light areas. The broken pixels add an element of imperfection, emphasizing emotional boundaries and unresolved feelings.
