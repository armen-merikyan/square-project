Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between tenderness and rage through a careful juxtaposition of soft and harsh colors. The palette centers around deep blacks, ensuring a strong contrast with softer pale blues and whites that symbolize tenderness. The layout features a scattered repair pattern where portions of broken pixels—symbolizing rage—bleed into muted areas of tenderness, creating visual conflict and resolution. The main collision occurs centrally, demonstrating a compelling push-pull between calmness and tension. This reflects the seed's concepts of emotional dichotomy, with lines of saturation and space echoing the deep internal struggles depicted in terms of climbing from chaos to serenity.
