Artist Statement

This composition explores the dichotomy of loneliness and connection through opposing patterns and colors. The palette of deep blue, muted purple, bright yellow, and orange creates emotional tension: the coolness of deep blue signifies loneliness, while bright yellow symbolizes connection. The grid-like arrangement showcases a sense of rising energy, supported by bursts of color that provide visual intrigue. The top-weighted structure emphasizes the struggle of hidden feelings against exposed bursts, creating a palpable sense of tension and resolution through color collisions and spatial arrangement.
