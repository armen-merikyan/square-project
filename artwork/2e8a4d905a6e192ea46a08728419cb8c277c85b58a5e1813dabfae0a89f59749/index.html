Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional battle between love and betrayal through contrasting colors and patterns. The warm hues of pink and red symbolize love and are interspersed with cooler greys and dark maroons representing betrayal. The diagonal flow mimics a rising signal, yet the interruptions and cracks highlight the essence of chaos and unresolved tension. Each broken pixel adds to the imperfection and fragility of emotions, symbolizing the fractures within human connection. The placement of dense clusters against negative spaces reflects the feelings of pressure and calm, creating a dynamic visual tension.
