Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a clash between longing and void, with warm colors suggesting desire while cooler tones illustrate emptiness. The selected palette 'ancient_fire' introduces burnt orange and dark red to evoke warmth amidst a backdrop of charcoal, symbolizing the heaviness of longing without fulfillment. The zigzag flow guides the eye upward, signifying aspiration amidst a background that absorbs light. Patterns of stripes and checkerboard create a visual tension, echoing the struggle between motion and stagnation; bright pixels interrupt darker areas, symbolizing fleeting moments of hope against pervasive emptiness.
