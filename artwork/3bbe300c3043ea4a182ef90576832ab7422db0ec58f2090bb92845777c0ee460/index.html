Artist Statement

This composition captures the interplay between joy and grief, represented through contrasting palettes. The top features bright, warm colors symbolizing joy (yellow and orange), cascading downward like an uplifting wave. The bottom harbors muted purples and deep blues, invoking grief and sorrow, creating a tension-filled split. The use of broken pixels introduces an element of imperfection, echoing the conflict between safety and threat. Each row's color shift represents a journey through emotional complexity, while maintaining a structured layout reflecting order amidst chaos.
