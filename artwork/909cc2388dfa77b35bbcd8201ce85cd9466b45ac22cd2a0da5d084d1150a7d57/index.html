Artist Statement

The composition reflects the duality of confusion and clarity through a split horizontal layout. The top half (cluttered with dark reds and blacks) embodies emotional pressure and confusion, while the bottom half (light blues and whites) symbolizes calm and clarity. The palette juxtaposes warm and cool tones to enhance the sense of tension. The broken pixels create imperfections, invoking a sense of unresolved feeling amidst the visual chaos.
