Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional duality of restlessness and peace, illustrated through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of clean white, ice blue, and violet gray embodies the serene aspects, while ink black and warning orange add tension and interruptions that symbolize restlessness. The layout suggests a rising signal, where the stark contrast occurs diagonally from the bottom left to the top right, creating a dynamic visual flow. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, enhancing the feeling of collision between calm and unease. This work encapsulates a narrative of struggle and resolution, inviting contemplation of movement versus stillness in a structured but abstract form.
