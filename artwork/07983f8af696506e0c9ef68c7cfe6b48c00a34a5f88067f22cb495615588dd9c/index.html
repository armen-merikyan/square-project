Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between restlessness and peace, represented through a dynamic interplay of colors. The palette combines deep teal and cosmic indigo for depth and calmness, against the stark contrast of void black and bright star white. The main collision of colors occurs diagonally, suggesting movement and tension that interrupts the calmness. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional imperfections, echoing the theme of a memory transgressed by motion.
