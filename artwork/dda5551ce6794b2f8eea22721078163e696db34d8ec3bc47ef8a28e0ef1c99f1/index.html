Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the conflict between restlessness and peace through a dynamic interplay of colors reflecting the emotional tension. The chosen palette enhances this effect, with deep wine and tan suggesting warmth and agitation, while ash gray and warm ivory provide balance and calm. The layout features scattered patches that appear like fractured memories, embodying breaks against the steady pulse of barcode-like designs, creating a sense of interjection amidst chaos. The structure is designed to favor peace at the corners while the central area clashes with lively pulse marks, illustrating an unresolved tension across the grid.
