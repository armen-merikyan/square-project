Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The chosen palette of deep blacks and soft golds express the weight of yearning against moments of clarity and resolution. The main contrast occurs between the dense patches of dark violet and the smoother waves of gold that ripple through, suggesting a struggle between chaos and order. Broken pixels are interspersed throughout to signify the jagged journey of emotion, conveying a sense of rupture in the expectations of intimacy while maintaining the central flow—a tension that rests amid quiet intervals.
