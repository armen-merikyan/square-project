Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between defiance and submission, represented by the striking contrast of vivid colors against muted tones. The palette of signal green, electric blue, and hot magenta symbolizes vibrancy and energy, while static gray and dead black provide grounding and quietness. The arrangement features clusters of colors that spiral inward, echoing the inward spiral theme, while the pressure reflects an involution against outward growth, hinting at an invasion of energy. Broken pixels disrupt the continuity, creating a sense of imperfection that resonates with the theme of defiance. The main collision occurs centrally, where vibrant colors clash against the darker edges, embodying the fight between resistance and surrender.
