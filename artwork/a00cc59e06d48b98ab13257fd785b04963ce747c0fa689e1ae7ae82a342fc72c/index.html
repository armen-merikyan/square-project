Artist Statement

The emotional tone conflicts between quietness and pressure, reflected in the color palette. The off-white and faint peach offer a soft field representing tranquillity, while dark navy and compressed red create tension and density. The layout emphasizes a quiet center with bursts of pressure at the borders, suggesting a struggle between calm and disruption. This reflects the seed's themes of hard edges meeting a soft horizon in an abstract dance of color and form.
