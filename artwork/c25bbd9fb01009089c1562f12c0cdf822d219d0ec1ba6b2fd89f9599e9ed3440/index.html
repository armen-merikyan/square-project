Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends trust's warm embrace with suspicion's lurking shadows. The palette of muted plum, rain blue, and soft cyan creates a calm ambiance with undertones of doubt. The checker pattern on the left represents suspicion, clashing consistently against the organized target design on the right. The collision occurs diagonally from top left to bottom right, emphasizing the tension between order and chaos, evoking a sense of conflict and resilience amidst fragility.
