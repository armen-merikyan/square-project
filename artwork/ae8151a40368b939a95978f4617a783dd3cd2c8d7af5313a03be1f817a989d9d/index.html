Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between clarity and distortion, depicting a struggle of a singular bright signal against an encompassing noise. The palette includes electric blues and vibrant greens that symbolize the 'signal,' set against muted grayscale tones representing interference and chaos. The arrangement of dots interspersed with pulse marks creates a dynamic interaction, with a dominant dark background that allows bursts of color to disrupt its surface, embodying the emotional conflicts described in the seed.
