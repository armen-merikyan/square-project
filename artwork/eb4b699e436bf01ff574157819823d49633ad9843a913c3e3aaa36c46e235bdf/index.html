Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension of confusion versus clarity through a balance of jagged edges and rising signals. The palette, inspired by 'broken memory', combines sepia and faded blue, creating a nostalgic feel that contrasts with the chaotic feeling represented by the rain pattern. The central pixels are denser and darker, symbolizing confusion, while the edges are lighter and more spacious, suggesting clarity. This interplay captures the struggle of finding clarity within chaos, where pixels seem to fall outward yet maintain a sense of ascent.
