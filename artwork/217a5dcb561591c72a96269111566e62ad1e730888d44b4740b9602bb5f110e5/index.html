Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity. The choice of deep teal and cosmic indigo symbolizes the depths of certainty, while the lighter tones of star white accentuate curiosity's openings. The primary collision occurs in the center where the structured dot patterns clash with more erratic stack patterns, representing contrasting forces in an abstract dialectic. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, representing the unpredictable tide against a calculating signal, embodying both chaos and order.
