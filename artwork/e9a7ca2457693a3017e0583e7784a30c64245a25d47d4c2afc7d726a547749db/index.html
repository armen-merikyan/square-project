Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between defiance and submission, reflected through its contrasting color palette and structure. The deep wine and warm ivory evoke emotional pressures, suggesting a battle of wills. The rising signal layout creates an upward movement, while the crack pattern indicates defiance through stark interruptions. The pulse marks of submission create a softer, enveloping presence. The center serves as the point of collision, where the bright, defiant accents burst against the quieter, soft backgrounds. The inclusion of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, enhancing the emotional narrative of conflict and resolution.
