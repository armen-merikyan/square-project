Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents a contrast between fleeting sparks of inspiration (represented by vibrant yellows) and the settled residues of ash (soft grays). The palette utilizes dark violet to invoke depth and gravity while hints of soft gold provide bursts of hope and excitement. The collision occurs in the split layout, where the left side embodies energetic sparks, while the right dissolves into ashy densities. The patterns evoke a tension between movement and stillness, reflecting the internal struggle between memory and erasure. Broken pixels on the ash side serve as reminders of what once was, creating an imperfection that embodies the story of resilience against fragility.
