Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional struggle between loneliness and connection through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, featuring black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green, embodies the tension between darkness (loneliness) and soft illumination (connection). The maze-like patterns on the left symbolize isolation, while the structured, stair-like patterns on the right suggest pathways to connection. The central focus creates a radical burst of color and tension, intertwining quiet space with dense color clusters, representing the push and pull of inner emotional landscapes.
