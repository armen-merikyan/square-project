Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a dynamic interplay of color and pattern. The palette includes deep black for grounding, electric blue for vibrant contrast, and magenta for moments of intensity, all framed by quiet lime suggesting serenity. The use of broken pixels introduces interruptions that mimic the feeling of repairing against damage, creating a sense of collision that enhances the narrative. The dominant dark tones create a weighty background, while bright accents emerge at the center, embodying the theme of a thirst for resolution amidst chaos.
