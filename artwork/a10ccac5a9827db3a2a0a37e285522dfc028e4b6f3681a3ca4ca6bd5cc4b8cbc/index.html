Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability (exposure) and protection (safety). The warm brown and shelter green represent the notion of a gentle, secure center, while the pale skin and cold white echo the feeling of being exposed. The arrangement gives prominence to shelter, leaving a rippling impact with accents of blue providing a sense of calm contrasts against the structured boundary created by the weight of the brown. The pixel placement suggests upward movement, reinforcing themes of hope and transcendence amidst the complexity of feelings.
