Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork explores the conflict between desire and restraint. The palette combines warm tones of hot coral and blood orange to represent desire, with the cooler charcoal and hollow blue to signify restraint. The saturated colors spiral towards the center, where desire collides with the muted edges, encapsulating tension. The placement of broken pixels symbolizes imperfection in this push-pull dynamic, with a clear flow from top to bottom, suggesting a descending emotion. Overall, the grid maintains a readable structure while allowing for a profound exploration of emotional depth.
