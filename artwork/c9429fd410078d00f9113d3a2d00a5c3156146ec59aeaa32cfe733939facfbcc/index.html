Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a tension between pressure and quietude, utilizing muted colors to instill a sense of calm interspersed with sharp, contrasting elements representing pressure. The palette of dark navy, off-white, and compressed red serves to establish a gradient range, suggesting movement from density to lightness. The primary contrast emerges in the center, where the dense ripple pattern collides with softer glitch patterns, embodying the interplay of calm and disturbance that resonates from the seed concepts.
