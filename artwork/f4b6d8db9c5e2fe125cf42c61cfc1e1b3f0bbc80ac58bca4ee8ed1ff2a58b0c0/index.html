Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a balance between longing and resolve through a palette of greens and warm tones, illustrating hope and the weight of unresolved feelings. The contrasting colors create a rhythm of rising and falling, with the deep olive and acid green interspersed to evoke both serenity and urgency. The layout suggests an 'open gate' with bursts of lighter honey-gold that create a collision at the center, interrupting the denser fields. The broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, representing the repair versus damage motifs from the seed, while leaving negative space to enhance the tension between the two emotional states.
