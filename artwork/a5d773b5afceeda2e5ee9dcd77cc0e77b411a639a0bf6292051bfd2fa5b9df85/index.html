Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between awe and terror through a contrasting palette of deep sepia and faded blue shades. The pixel arrangement reflects a dualistic flow from the top to the bottom, highlighting the clash of order and chaos. The softness of edges contrasts with sharper interruptions, creating a rhythm that oscillates between a feeling of stability and instability. The broken pixels introduce noise, representing fragmented memories and introspection within this emotional landscape.
