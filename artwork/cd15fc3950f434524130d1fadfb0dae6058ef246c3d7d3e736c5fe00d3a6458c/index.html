Artist Statement

The composition reflects the emotional tension of 'awe vs terror' through contrasting areas of dense color and empty space. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of nostalgia and unease, with darker hues representing terror and lighter tones symbolizing awe. The outer edges are filled with armored stability, while the center exhibits a broken maze-like pattern, suggesting both comfort and vulnerability. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfection, championing the theme of chaos versus order, as inner contrasts challenge the calmness of the frame.
