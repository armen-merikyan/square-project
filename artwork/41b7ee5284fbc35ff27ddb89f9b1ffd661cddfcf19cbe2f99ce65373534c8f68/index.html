Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between confusion and clarity through contrasting elements. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes a duality of emotions—darkness contrasted with light, confusion with clarity. The outer border features a thick outline in a dark palette, representing confusion, while the inner section employs a ring pattern with soft gold and pale green, symbolizing clarity amidst chaos. The 'broken pixels' introduce an imperfection, echoing the theme of broken symmetry, manifesting a pulse of emotion through rhythmic patterns. The overall effect is a dynamic interplay of order and disarray, provoking thought and introspection.
