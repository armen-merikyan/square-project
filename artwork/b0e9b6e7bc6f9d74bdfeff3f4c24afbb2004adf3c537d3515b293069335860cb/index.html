Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of loneliness and connection, articulated through a palette evoking broken memories in sepia, tan, and faded blue. The arrangement features ascendant dark hues contrasted with scattered lighter tones, creating tension and suggesting a pulse between absence and proximity. The bottom half introduces a burst of lighter shades against a heavier top, symbolizing the struggle between isolation and potential connection. This juxtaposition echoes the seed's themes through shapes and color, demonstrating both oppression and hope.
