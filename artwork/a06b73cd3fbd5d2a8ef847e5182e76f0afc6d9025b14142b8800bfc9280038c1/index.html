Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition centers on the interplay between vulnerability and strength, expressed through color dynamics. The dark violet (#3E1A78) represents strength, outlining a thick boundary around the soft gold (#E3CDA8) that embodies vulnerability, creating a protective 'pressure corner' at the lower right. The pale green (#7BBE90) threads through the design as a symbol of renewal, linking disparate areas while the lack of symmetry introduces a deliberate dissonance. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, reinforcing themes of imperfection and resilience amidst pressure. This abstract arrangement captures a narrative of resisting overwhelm while navigating emotional currents.
