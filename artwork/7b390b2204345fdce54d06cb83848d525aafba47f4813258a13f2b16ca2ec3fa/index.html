Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the artwork captures the tension between longing and resolve, using a palette that conveys both depth and warmth. The rich greens and muted yellows create a layered density that resonates with the push and pull of emotional states. The central collision occurs where the vibrant patches of color intentionally disrupt the quieter, cream fields, symbolizing the struggle between repair and damage. This pattern mirrors the abstract qualities of threads intertwining, illustrating both the fragility and strength of our connections.
