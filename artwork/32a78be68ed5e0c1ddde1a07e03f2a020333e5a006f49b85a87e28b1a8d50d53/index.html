Artist Statement

The artwork conveys the emotional dichotomy between 'defiance' and 'submission', using a palette that combines warmth with coolness to illustrate tension. The bright contrasts symbolize defiant bursts amidst the subdued tones of submission, creating a clash at the center that serves as the focal point. The chosen colors suggest safety ('shelter_green', 'warm_brown') and exposure ('open_blue', 'cold_white'), embodying the space between protection and vulnerability. The broken pixels represent glitches, adding to the overall sense of interruption and a chaotic pulse against a quiet backdrop, while adhering to the asymmetry dictated by the flow of zigzag.
