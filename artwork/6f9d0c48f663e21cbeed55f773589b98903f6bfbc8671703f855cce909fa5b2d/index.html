Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between defiance and submission, embodying an unyielding center clashing against softer edges. The chosen palette of rain blue and muted plum reflects a melancholic yet vibrant atmosphere, where cooler tones signify defiance and warmer accents suggest submission. The collision occurs at the center, where a ‘hinge’ of intense colors interrupts the flow of quieter spaces, evoking a sense of struggle amidst calm. This dynamic reflects the seed's narrative through contrasting patterns of broken dots and hinge shapes, translating emotional complexity into a structured abstract form.
