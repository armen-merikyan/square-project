Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, represented through a contrast of dense black and dark violet sections against quiet pale green and soft gold highlights. The layout begins in the corner, with dark hues representing confusion, bleeding into lighter areas that symbolize clarity. Patterns of broken pixels disrupt symmetry, enhancing the theme of disarray, while maintaining a subtle rhythm of order through the gold and green, guiding the viewer's eye across the canvas.
