Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between tenderness and rage. The palette blends saturated reds from rage with muted blues and whites representing tenderness, creating a visual dialogue. The dominant red tones collide sharply against delicate blue rings, reflecting the emotional struggle of inside versus outside perceptions, echoing the concepts of guilt and forgiveness. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, emphasizing emotional scars, while the arrangement suggests a rising tension in the center, shared between warmth and coolness.
