Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between darkness and light, representing the collision of nostalgia and progress. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green creates a visual representation of heavy dark masses fighting against bright accents. The arrangement emphasizes the contrast between the dense, dark colors symbolizing pressure and the soft gold and pale green, suggesting release. The structured pattern, incorporating both stripes and radial flows, echoes the dynamic of pulsating emotions, evoking moments of conflict and resolution.
