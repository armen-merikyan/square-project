Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between longing and resolve, achieved through contrasting vibrant and muted colors that intersect, suggesting emotional depth and complexity. The palette evokes serenity with pale aquas and muted grays, while sharp alarm reds and deep ink blues add urgency and intensity. The contrast is centered, with the alarm red as the focal point interrupting the cooler tones, representing the core tension between the contained and the overflowing emotions. The 'open_gate' layout invites a flow of color from the corner, enhancing the feeling of pursuit and yearning through diagonal threads that weave across the grid, harmonizing with the broken pixel concept to suggest past scars and the process of repair.
