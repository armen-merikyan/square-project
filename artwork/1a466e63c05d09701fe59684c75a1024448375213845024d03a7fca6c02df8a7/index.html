Artist Statement

This 8x8 pixel art piece embodies the emotional contrast between longing and resolve. Drawing from the seed concepts, the palette combines warm coral and soft amber to suggest warmth and connection, juxtaposed against cold slate and blue black, which convey distance and introspection. The arrangement features scattered broken pixels, representing the unresolved feeling of longing, while denser rings formed by vivid colors evoke a sense of resolve. The main collision occurs centrally, where warm and cool colors intermingle, symbolizing the tension between hope and melancholy. This abstract arrangement encourages the viewer's eye to navigate through the intimate dance of colors, amplifying the subtle interplay between serenity and urgency.
