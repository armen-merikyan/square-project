Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, expressed through a palette of pale aqua for serenity and alarm red for urgency. The central collision reflects a dense saturation of hues with broken pixels interrupting the flow, suggesting both repair and damage. The layout emphasizes overfilled central density surrounded by quieter borders, implying a struggle to contain emotion. The checker pattern for longing collides with the shatter pattern for resolve, creating an abstract narrative of tension and release.
