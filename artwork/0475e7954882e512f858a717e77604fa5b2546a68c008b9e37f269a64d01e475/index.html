Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between trust and suspicion. The palette combines pale aqua and mist gray for warmth and balance, accentuated by alarm red as a signal of tension. The arrangement places the dark blue and white in a semi-diagonal spread, creating a sense of disturbance amidst a calm field. The center pulse of colors reflects the fragility of trust contrasted with the dark shadows of suspicion, while the broken pixels symbolize imperfection. The overall composition hints at a split, taking cues from the seed's themes to evoke a narrative of resilience through irregular patterns and interrupted calm.
