Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tone of confusion and clarity. The palette of deep navy and black combined with hints of dim cyan and sparse white suggests a turbulent emotional landscape—a collision of heavy presence and moments of lightness. The contrast of densely packed dark colors at the edges and brighter, more open spaces in the center highlights the struggle between pressure and release. The shapes formed through alternating stripes and fragments mirror the inherent chaos of emotions, creating tension through color placement and broken pixels that add layers of imperfection.
