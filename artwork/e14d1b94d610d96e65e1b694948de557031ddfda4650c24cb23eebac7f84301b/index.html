Artist Statement

The artwork explores the tension between 'signal' and 'noise', using the bright 'signal_green' and 'electric_blue' as the main pulse clashing against the muted 'static_gray' and 'dead_black'. This reflects the chaotic interaction between clarity and distortion. The design features asymmetric patterns with dots to symbolize consistent rhythm interrupted by crosshatch noise, resonating the emotional layers inspired by the seed. The broken pixels introduce intentional flaws, embodying imperfections in communication. The dominant presence of 'signal' colors composes 65% of the canvas, with 'noise' serving as the chaos that disrupts, positioned heavily towards the right, suggesting an ongoing struggle for clarity amidst chaos.
