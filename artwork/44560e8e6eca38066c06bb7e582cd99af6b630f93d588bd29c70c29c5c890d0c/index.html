Artist Statement

This composition embodies the struggle between loneliness and connection through a juxtaposition of dense, dark colors that suggest isolation, contrasted with sparse bursts of light representing moments of connection. The palette favors strong contrasts of deep reds and blacks against pale blues and whites, reflecting the intensity of emotional experiences. The pattern evokes a crosshatch effect that seems chaotic yet structured, symbolizing the tension of feeling both hidden and exposed. Predominantly dark at the top, the arrangement gives a weighted impression that gradually lightens towards the lower rows, representing a balance between despair and hope.
