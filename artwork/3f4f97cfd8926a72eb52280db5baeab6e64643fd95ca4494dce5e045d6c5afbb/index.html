Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between loneliness and connection, framed by a thick border of dark colors that represent solitude, drawing the eye inward towards a lighter, fragmented core area. The palette includes deep blacks and dark violets juxtaposed with soft gold and pale green, symbolizing the heaviness of isolation against the delicate hope of connection. The contrast unfolds through the edges and the hollow spaces within, creating a rhythm of pressure and release, where broken pixels enhance the theme of imperfection and memory. The spiral flow from dark to light signifies a movement from loneliness to a flicker of connection, echoing the emotional weight of the piece.
