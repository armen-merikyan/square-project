Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between warmth and exposure, embodying a delicate tension. The palette features 'shelter green' for safety, complemented by the 'cold white' of exposure, creating a dynamic interplay. The layout, emphasizing two pulsating centers, highlights conflict — warmth pushing against vulnerability. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, adding depth to the abstract narrative. The collision of the warm and cool colors at the center reflects the emotional struggle of seeking shelter while confronting the elements.
