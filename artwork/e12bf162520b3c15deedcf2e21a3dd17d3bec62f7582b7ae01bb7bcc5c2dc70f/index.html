Artist Statement

This piece captures the duality of restlessness and peace, represented by contrasting regions of bright and muted colors. The palette features deep blacks and vibrant hues like electric blue and hot magenta, creating emotional tension. The arrangement reflects a split-screen pattern dominating one side, embodying tranquility, while the other side is filled with interwoven threads of energy, reflecting movement and discord. The center shows a defined collision between calm and chaos, where the patterns overlap, creating a pulse of visual rhythm. Broken pixels add to the sense of imperfection and disruption, aligning with the themes of memory and motion.
