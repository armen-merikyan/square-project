Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a delicate tension between longing and resolve, represented through an evolving arrangement of colors. The palette, 'signal_noise', utilizes electric blue and hot magenta as dynamic, vibrant accents juxtaposed against deep black and static gray for contrast. The main collision occurs at the center, where the bright hues disrupt the calm, demonstrating a struggle between density and emptiness. This resonates with the seed's themes of unresolved emotions and opposing forces, creating an abstract narrative of interaction and motion.
