Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tug-of-war between longing and resolve. The palette, a mix of hot coral and blood orange, contrasts beautifully with the serene charcoal and hollow blue, creating a vivid narrative of desire entwined with steadfastness. The lower rows explode in density, showcasing a 'ladder' pattern for longing, while the upper rows present a veil-like structure for resolve. The collision is primarily centered but reflects a flowing top-to-bottom movement, fostering a space where urgency meets serenity, capturing the essence of the seed as it translates into an emotional rhythm.
