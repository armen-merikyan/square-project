Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a tension between 'awe' and 'terror', captured in the palette of muted and faded colors, symbolizing the fragility of memory. The contrast of dark and light colors illustrates the push and pull of chaos against order, with a central square representing stability amidst fragmented patterns. The arrangement creates a visual echo of pressure, using broken pixels to suggest imperfection and remind of fleeting memories, while maintaining negative space to amplify the sense of instability.
