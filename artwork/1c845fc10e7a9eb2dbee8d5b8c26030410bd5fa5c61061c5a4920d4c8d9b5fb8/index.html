Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy between vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette, inspired by the 'hunger_restraint' theme, incorporates rich reds and deep grays to represent both exposure and strength. The intersecting 'ladder' and 'target' patterns symbolize the delicate balance between protection and exposure, with the central column (the quiet channel) representing the tension point. Broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, suggesting the fragility of strength amidst vulnerability.
