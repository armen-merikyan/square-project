Artist Statement

The emotional tone stems from the juxtaposition of vulnerability and strength, suggesting a complex interplay between exposure and protection. The palette, drawn from 'lonely_signal', features deep navy and black to evoke a sense of strength, countered by dim cyan and white, representing vulnerability. The main contrast occurs where the dense, dark colors dominate the center, symbolizing a protective core in this abstract composition, while lighter colors attempt to break through, embodying exposure. The pattern merges ember-like strength with a checker-like vulnerability, creating a dynamic collision at the center that visually narrates the struggle between these contrasting emotions.
