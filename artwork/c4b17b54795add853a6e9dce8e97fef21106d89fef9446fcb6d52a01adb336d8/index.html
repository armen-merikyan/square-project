Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tug-of-war between control and surrender, using a palette influenced by cosmic indigo and void black to evoke depth and mystery. The contrasting patterns embody the structured tide of control against the yielding stepwell of surrender. The center showcases the main collision, where dense marks pulse outward, spilling into negative space, hinting at overflow while maintaining an edge of containment. Broken pixels add intentional imperfection, echoing the inherent struggle within the seed's themes.
