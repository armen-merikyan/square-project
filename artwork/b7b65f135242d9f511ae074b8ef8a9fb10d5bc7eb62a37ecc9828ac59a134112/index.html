Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between trust and suspicion. The chosen color palette reflects this duality: the deep electric blues and bright magenta represent vibrant moments of trust, while the darker blacks and static grays symbolize the unease of suspicion. The layout forms a diagonal contrast where the stripes representing trust intersect with maze-like patterns signifying suspicion, creating an engaged visual collision. This reflects the inner struggle experienced when navigating between confidence and doubt. The incorporation of broken pixels introduces imperfections, enhancing the theme of resilience versus fragility, offering a dynamic and layered composition.
