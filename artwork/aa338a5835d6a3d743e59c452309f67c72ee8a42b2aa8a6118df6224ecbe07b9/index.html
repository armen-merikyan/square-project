Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a structured pattern. The color palette reflects both softness and severity, featuring soft pinks and warm whites juxtaposed against sharp crimson and deep blacks. The main collision occurs at the center, highlighting a tension within a nearly enclosed border, symbolizing the spectrum between safety and exposure. Broken pixels interrupt the flow, reminiscent of vulnerabilities, while dense areas of dark colors represent strength, creating a poignant visual narrative.
