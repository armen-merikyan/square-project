Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional struggle between 'longing' and 'resolve'. The palette, derived from warm sepia and cool faded blue, creates a tension that's expressed through a dominant lattice structure representing 'longing' against a spiraled backdrop resembling 'resolve'. The collision occurs in the center where the darker colors break the lighter lattice, mirroring the complexities of emotional thresholds. Broken pixels are introduced to signify disruptions, enhancing the sense of repair amidst damage. The layout promotes a visual spiral that guides the eye, embodying both pressure and serenity.
