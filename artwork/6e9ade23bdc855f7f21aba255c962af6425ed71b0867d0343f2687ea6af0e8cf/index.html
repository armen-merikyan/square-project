Artist Statement

This composition evokes the emotional tension between desire and emptiness. The palette, drawn from hues of warm coral and cool charcoal, reflects a saturated pull against a distant boundary. The main contrast occurs at the center, where vibrant colors collide with darker hues creating a visual tension, while the edges maintain a sense of quiet containment. Broken pixels act as subtle disruptions, echoing the struggle inherent in the emotional themes represented. The abstract layout and flow echo a spiral inward, emphasizing the dynamics of overflow and restraint.
