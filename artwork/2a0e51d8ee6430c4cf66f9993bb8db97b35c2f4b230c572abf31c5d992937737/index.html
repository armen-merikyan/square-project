Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a duality of longing and resolve, reflected in the choice of an 'ancient fire' palette with burnt orange and dark red suggesting warmth and depth of feeling, while charcoal and gold provide boundary and texture. The central clash occurs in the third row, where vibrant colors disrupt muted textures, embodying the push and pull of longing versus resolve. The composition's stacked bands create a rhythm, with the quiet field enhancing this tension, suggesting moments of serenity amidst urgency.
