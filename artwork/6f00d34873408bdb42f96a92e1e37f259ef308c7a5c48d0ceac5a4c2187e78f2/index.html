Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between clarity and confusion through the contrast of color and arrangement. The chosen palette of dark maroon, pink, gray, and red conveys conflicting emotions and intensity. The main contrast occurs at the edges, where the soft transition of pink meets the starkness of dark maroon, creating colliding feelings of wariness against warmth. Falling pixels and broken structures hint at confusion, while outward patterns suggest a quest for clarity. The seed of 'diamond' shaped the abstract through its duality, mirroring the clash between structured chaos and emerging clarity.
