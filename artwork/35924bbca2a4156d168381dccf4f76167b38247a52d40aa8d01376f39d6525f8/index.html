Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between strength and vulnerability, where a dense center suggests stability while the edges represent fragility. The palette captures awe and terror, with deep teal and cosmic indigo dominating the theme. The main contrast occurs in the center, where cool colors collide with warmer accents—symbolizing the push and pull of emotions. The arrangement of colors employs a staircase pattern for strength and a noise pattern for vulnerability, visually expressing the tension and balance from the seed's duality.
