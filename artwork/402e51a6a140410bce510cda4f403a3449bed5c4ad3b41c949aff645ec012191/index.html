Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the battle between trust and suspicion through a dense center representing noise surrounded by softer edges suggesting quiet trust. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of broken memory, emphasizing a warm vs. cool tension. The main contrast occurs in the center, where a dynamic clash of darker lattice patterns (suspicion) disrupt the softer ring patterns (trust), creating an engaging visual pattern that narrates instability and resilience.
