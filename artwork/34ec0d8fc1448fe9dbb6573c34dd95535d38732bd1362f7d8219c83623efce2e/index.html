Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between panic and control, represented by the sharp contrasts of the palette and asymmetrical placement of colors. The use of 'broken_memory' colors reflects a fading clarity and layered emotions, while the interplay of sharp zigzag patterns and smooth flows mimics the chaotic arguments in life. The structure suggests bursts of activity against quieter spaces, symbolizing moments of grief punctuated by sparks of joy. The choice of negative space enhances the feeling of uncertainty and imbalance, echoing the main contrasts between order and chaos.
