Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between defiance and submission. A palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold creates a warm yet weighted atmosphere, representing the fiery tension between the two emotions. The contrast occurs primarily along the edges, where dark tones collide with lighter elevations, hinting at an inner core of resilience amidst the noise of conflict. The layout divides the frame into two emotional states, celebrating the interplay of openness versus confinement through a fractured grid, resonating with the weighty feeling suggested by the seed.
