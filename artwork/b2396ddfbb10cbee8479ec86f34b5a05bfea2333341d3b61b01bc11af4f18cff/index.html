Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between envy and gratitude through contrasting colors that evoke feelings of warmth (gratitude) and coolness (envy). The palette, consisting of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue, suggests a broken memory, hinting at both nostalgia and longing. The layout mirrors on the left and right sides, but a few broken pixels create an interruption, symbolizing the unpredictability of emotions. The main contrast occurs at the edges, where soft colors collide with deeper shades, reflecting the struggle between inner calm and external noise. The gradient of colors from the center outwards suggests a movement from a safe place towards the chaotic edges, embodying the narrative of rising versus falling feelings.
