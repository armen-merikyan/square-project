Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional pull between love and betrayal, utilizing a palette of warm pinks and reds contrasted against cooler gray tones to evoke deeper feelings of conflict. The layout rises diagonally, suggesting hope yet revealing broken pixel patterns reminiscent of betrayal. The interplay of sharp and soft edges introduces tension, while overlapping grid patterns symbolize hidden complexities in relationships. Empty spaces serve as a reminder of absence, maintaining essential negative space to encapsulate the emotional depth.
