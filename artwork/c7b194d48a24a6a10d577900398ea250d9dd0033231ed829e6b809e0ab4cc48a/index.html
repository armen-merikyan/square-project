Artist Statement

The emotional tone vibrates between the juxtaposition of 'spark' and 'ash,' invoking a dynamic tension of potential energy versus settled remnants. The chosen palette of soft pinks and stark blacks suggests a warmth and severity, echoing the relationship between ignition and the aftermath. The main collision occurs in the center, where vibrant strikes of color disrupt the muted embers, symbolizing resurgence amidst decay. This reflects a story of resilience and the moment of choice, as the jagged edges of the layout allow for a sense of movement through the square, creating a path of evolving emotional narrative.
