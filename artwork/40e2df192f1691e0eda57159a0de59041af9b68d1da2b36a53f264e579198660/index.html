Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the dichotomy of love and betrayal, utilizing a palette of warm pinks and dark maroons to emphasize emotional tension. The outer edges are dark and intense, symbolizing betrayal, while the inner 4x4 grid is soft and warm, suggesting love. The collision occurs where the soft shapes subtly interrupt the darkness, creating a dynamic contrast that suggests both tenderness and fracture. The patterns of noise and stairs, combined with broken pixels, convey imperfection and the complexity of human emotions, encapsulating the seed's narrative into an abstract, visual tension.
