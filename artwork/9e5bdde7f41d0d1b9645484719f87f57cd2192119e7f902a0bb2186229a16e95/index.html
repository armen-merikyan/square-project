Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional clash between defiance and submission through contrasting tones and patterns. The palette of soft blues and muted purples evokes a sense of melancholy wonder, illustrating both resistance (in the bright, flame-like accents) and retreat (in the receding ring-like forms). The dominant color shifts paradoxically create a flickering contrast, centering on the tension where the vibrant colors invade the quieter fields. The broken pixels symbolize scars of conflict, emphasizing the theme of fractured harmony.
