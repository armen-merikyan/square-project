Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between defiance and submission, with a palette that uses warm corals and cool blues to create tension. The bright colors represent defiance, colliding against muted fields that imply submission. The main contrast occurs in the center, where bold accents clash with softer hues, symbolizing the confrontation of these emotions. The abstract pattern evolved from the notion of a spiral, suggesting inward movement and complexity, while the broken pixels disrupt harmony, echoing the emotional conflict.
