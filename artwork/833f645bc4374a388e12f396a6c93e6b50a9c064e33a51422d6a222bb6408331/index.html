Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the conflict between restlessness and peace through an interplay of colors. The palette, composed of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green, signifies a struggle between darkness (restlessness) and light (peace). The contrast is primarily centered, creating a clash where vibrant gold meets the somber black and violet edges, echoing a sense of tension and unresolved motion. The patterns of stripes (peace) and frames (restlessness) intersect chaotically, suggesting a dynamic yet fractured state of being. The edge style is left-weighted, highlighting a sense of imbalance towards the darker side, representing the difficulty in achieving tranquility amidst chaos.
