Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between clear signals and disruptive noise. The palette of signal green and electric blue symbolizes clarity amidst the shadows of dead black and static gray, embodying an emotional spectrum of hope versus confusion. The stark contrast is focused in the central rows, where the crosshatch pattern of signal colors dramatically clashes with the pressure bands of noise, illustrating the struggle of a readable pulse against interruptions. The use of broken pixels adds intentional disruption, enhancing the theme of memory and erasure.
