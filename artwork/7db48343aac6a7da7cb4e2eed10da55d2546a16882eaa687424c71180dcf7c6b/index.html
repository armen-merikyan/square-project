Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between clarity and chaos, with a central rhythm of 'signal' represented in clean whites and dim cyans, contrasted against the static 'noise' of darker shades. The navy and black create a grounding, textured backdrop, while the use of broken pixels introduces disruptions that mirror the emotional conflict inherent in the seed. The pulse of clear accent colors cuts through the dense noise, showcasing a struggle for clarity in an environment of interference.
