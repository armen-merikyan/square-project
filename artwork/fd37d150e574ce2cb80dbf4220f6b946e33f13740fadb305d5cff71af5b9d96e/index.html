Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional interplay of longing and resolve. The palette, featuring moss green and rust, conveys fragility and resilience, suggesting the tension between brokenness and repair. The dense center in rich tones contrasts with lighter edges, symbolizing an overflow of emotion that strains against boundaries. Broken pixels add intentional imperfections, mirroring the theme of damage and healing. The central collision creates a visual pulse that draws the eye, representing the heart's dual nature of yearning and affirmation.
