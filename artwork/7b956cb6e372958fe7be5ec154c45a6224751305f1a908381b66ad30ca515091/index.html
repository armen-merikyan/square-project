Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between confusion and clarity through the juxtaposition of warm and cool colors. The palette, inspired by fear and hope, utilizes dark violet and gold contrasting against black and pale green to create emotional depth. The organized checkerboard pattern evokes confusion, while the subtle interruptions of positive space signal clarity. The broken edges emphasize the asymmetry, enhancing the narrative of disharmony amidst a structured background.
