Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the tension between love and betrayal through a palette of warm and darker shades. The warm pink and red signify love, while dark maroon hints at the betrayal. The interaction between orderly stripes and jagged edges illustrates the struggle between chaos and order. The main contrast occurs at the edges where the darker tones seep in, creating a fragmentation effect that speaks to heartbreak and disruption. The pattern's flow starts with warmth radiating outward, interrupted by dense, darker areas that encapsulate moments of tension and emotional strife, embodying the abstract story of the seed.
