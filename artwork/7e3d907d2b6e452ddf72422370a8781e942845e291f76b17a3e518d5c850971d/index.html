Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of defiance and submission through an interplay of colors and patterns. The chosen palette—lime, black, electric blue, and magenta—highlights the tension between brightness (defiance) and darkness (submission). The arrangement features descending patterns that suggest ‘falling pixels’, while the bottom-weighted design evokes gravity and a sense of weighted emotion. The contrasting colors collide at the center, representing the heart of conflict and resolution, rising from a space of calm to a dense cluster of noise and energy.
