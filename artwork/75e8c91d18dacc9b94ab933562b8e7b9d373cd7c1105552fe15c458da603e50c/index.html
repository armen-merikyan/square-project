Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission, captured through a palette that resonates with melancholy and wonder. The colors 'rain_blue' and 'muted_plum' evoke a somber space, while 'moon_white' and 'soft_cyan' provide a contrast that suggests quiet moments amidst chaos. The diagonal rise emphasizes a pulse of energy, while the broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, reflecting the struggle between order and chaos. Main contrasts collide at the center, representing the ongoing conflict of emotions, leaving surrounding pixels to echo against this core. The layout visually suggests an eroded symmetry, further enhancing the sense of imbalance.
