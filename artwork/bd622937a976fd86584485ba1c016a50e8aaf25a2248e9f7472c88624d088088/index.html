Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional duality of longing and resolve, utilizing a palette that oscillates between muted tonality and stark contrast. The choice of off-white and dust gray as base colors suggests a quiet pressure, while the compressed red introduces urgency and intensity. The layout features a central collision where dense, maze-like patterns of longing break against the steady, tide-like resolution in a weighted diagonal. Broken pixels are strategically placed to symbolize disruption and fragility within the tension of emotion. This abstract arrangement captures the essence of unresolved feelings, reflecting both serenity and urgency.
