Artist Statement

This artwork evokes a tense interplay between clarity and static interference. The palette of deep navy and dim cyan contrasts sharply with the surrounding black, symbolizing the struggle of signal within noise. The two predominant pulses represented by brighter accents (cyan and white) clash within a dark field, suggesting moments of clarity fighting against the overwhelming noise. Broken pixels are scattered strategically to interrupt the smooth surfaces, adding a layer of chaos that mirrors the emotional dichotomy present in the seed.
