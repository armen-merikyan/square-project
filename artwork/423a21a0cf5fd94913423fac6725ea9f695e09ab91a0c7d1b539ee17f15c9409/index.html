Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dichotomy between the yearning for connection (longing) and the certainty found in resolve. A palette of warm ivory and soft gold confronts cooler shades of ash gray and deep wine, embodying the battle between desire and acceptance. The vibrant core presents a dense, chaotic arrangement, while the quieter border represents suppressed emotions. The main collision occurs at the center, creating a focal point that resonates with the viewer, while the influences of the edge evoke an echo of emptiness, reminding us of the struggles faced between intimacy and distance.
