Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the unrest of restlessness with the calm of peace, symbolized through a palette of muted colors that echo melancholy yet strong feelings. The arrangement of warm and cool colors encapsulates the tension between chaos and quietude, with broken pixels disrupting the flow to represent the static of memories. The primary collision occurs diagonally, where deep blues meet soft accents, signifying moments of clarity piercing through noise. This visual narrative helps convey a sense of unresolved emotion and the struggle to find peace amid restlessness.
