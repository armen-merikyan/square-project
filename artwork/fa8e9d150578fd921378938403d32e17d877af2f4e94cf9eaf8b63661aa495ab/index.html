Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress through a stark palette, showcasing a bottom-weighted contrast. The palette, rooted in sepia and dust gray, evokes a sense of memory and imperfection. The arrangement features a central axis of dark and light, illustrating a pull between full vs hollow experiences. The top half employs a radial pattern symbolizing progress with brighter tones, while the bottom half leans towards nostalgia with muted shades creating a sense of weight. Broken pixels introduce unpredictability, mirroring the emotional fractures in the theme of rise and fall.
