Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the dichotomy of restlessness and peace. The palette of warm ivory and deep wine, combined with ash gray and stain brown, creates a contrast between the serenity of pale fields and the unsettling vibrations of darker depths. The primary collision occurs in the central area, where the stacked patterns of restlessness collide with the structured teeth of peace, creating the tension of uncertainty amidst calm. The 'quiet_block' abstract seed shapes this interaction, with border heaviness contributing to a sense of closed corners, while dynamic diagonal movement suggests ongoing motion, perfectly embodying the seed's themes.
