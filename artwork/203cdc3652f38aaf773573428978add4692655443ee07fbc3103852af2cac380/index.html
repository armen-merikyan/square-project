Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between softness and severity through a balance of muted and intense colors. The warm whites and powder pinks suggest gentle emotional layers that contrast sharply with the deep black and crimson, representing severity. The layout features a ‘broken frame’ that hints at emotional leaks, while the directional flow from left to right creates a sense of gradual release. The density is weighted towards the bottom, channeling the feeling of grounding amidst chaos, and the broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, adding depth and complexity.
