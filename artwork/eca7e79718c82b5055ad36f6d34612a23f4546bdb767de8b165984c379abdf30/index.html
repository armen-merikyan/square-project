Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between panic and control by using a palette that incorporates muted hues alongside brighter accents. The contrast between deep sepia and muted gray represents the weight of control, while sparks of faded blue break through as moments of panic. The layout and diagonal flow emphasize a sense of rising tension, with asymmetric patterns that evoke the push and pull between stability and unrest. This arrangement offers a visual story of chaos attempting to find grounding, with layered density emitting from the edges inward to suggest inner conflict.
