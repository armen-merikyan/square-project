Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue conveys a sense of nostalgia and isolation, while the patterns evoke movement—grid for loneliness and radial for connection. The central clash occurs in the middle row where dense clusters of dark and muted tones create a stark contrast to the lighter, more open spaces above and below, suggesting a struggle between exposure and concealment. The top-weighted edge style enhances the feeling of weight pressing down, emphasizing the theme of connection being overshadowed by loneliness. The interplay of colors is designed to evoke a narrative of hidden emotions and the yearning for connection amidst isolation.
