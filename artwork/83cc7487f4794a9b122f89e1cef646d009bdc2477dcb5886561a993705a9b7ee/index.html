Artist Statement

This composition balances the emotional contrasts of longing and resolve through a rhythmic interplay of muted and saturated colors. The selected palette, named 'relief_dread', integrates pale mint and soft sky for a sense of calm, interspersed with storm gray and near black, symbolizing depth and weight of yearning. The collision forms near the center, where the 'braid' pattern of longing overlaps with the 'flood' pattern of resolve, creating a dynamic pulse that feels both urgent and steady. The shape seed of a cage suggests containment and an underlying sense of breakage, reflected in the subtle use of broken pixels that disrupt the otherwise structured flow, adding a layer of complexity to the emotional narrative. Negative space encircles the dense regions, providing clarity and calm amidst the tension, allowing the viewer to feel the push and pull of these conflicting emotions.
