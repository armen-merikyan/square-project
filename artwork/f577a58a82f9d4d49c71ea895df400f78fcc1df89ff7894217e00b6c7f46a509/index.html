Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a delicate struggle between longing and resolve, embodied in the use of a muted color palette that evokes serenity while allowing urgent accents to disrupt the calm. The selected colors underscore a sense of repair alongside the lingering presence of damage, echoing the idea of a broken mirror. The main collision occurs in the center, where the vibrant alarm red collides with the calm pale aqua, suggesting an unresolved tension between the two emotions. This arrangement creates a rhythmic pulse that moves throughout the structure, varying from quiet fields to dense clusters, aligned with the seed's narrative of conflicts and echoes.
