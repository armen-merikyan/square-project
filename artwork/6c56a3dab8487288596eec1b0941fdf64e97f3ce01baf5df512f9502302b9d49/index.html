Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between emptiness and desire, with the left side representing a target pattern signifying loneliness and void, while the right employs a radial pattern suggesting yearning and dynamic movement. The warm tones of red and pink contrast the cooler grays and dark maroon, depicting emotional collisions. The interplay of soft edges with broken pixels adds to the feeling of imperfection and unresolved motion, evoking a sense of longing amidst isolation.
