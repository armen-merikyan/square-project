Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes loneliness with connection, mirrored in the chaotic yet structured layout. The palette of yellow and muted purple embodies warmth against detachment, with deep blue infusion representing the weight of absence. The main contrast occurs in the left-dominant structure against scattered accents of light, suggesting the fluctuation between solitude and fleeting contact. The arrangement encapsulates 'inside vs outside' with the meaningful gaps acting as broken memories, mirroring the core emotional dichotomy derived from the seed.
