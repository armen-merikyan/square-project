Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts loneliness and connection, depicted through the soft boundary of colors from the muted palette of sepia, tan, and dust gray, interspersed with bursts of faded blue. The central 'tear' shape represents vulnerability, surrounded by a cage of darker hues, symbolizing both hidden feelings and the longing for connection. The top-weighted layout emphasizes a pull toward the center, creating a visual tension that reflects the push and pull between isolation and connection. Mixed patterns among the colors shape a silent dialogue of solitude versus the warmth of reaching out.
