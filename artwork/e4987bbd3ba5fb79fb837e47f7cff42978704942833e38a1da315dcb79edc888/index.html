Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the interplay between vulnerability and strength, creating a tension that emerges from the colors' dynamics. The 'joy_grief' palette enhances this contrast—warm yellows and oranges suggest strength and energy, while muted purples and deep blues evoke introspection and vulnerability. The heavy presence of yellows in the lower left corner symbolizes the overfilled pressure of both emotions colliding. The main collision, marked by sharp contrasts and echoes radiating from the center, illustrates the struggle between exposure and shelter, inviting a deeper analysis into the emotional landscape. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, reinforcing the theme of vulnerability amidst strength.
