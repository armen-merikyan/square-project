Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes 'longing' with 'resolve', represented through a palette that combines muted tones with occasional bright accents. The contrast between deeper hues and lighter colors creates a visual tension that evokes an unresolved push and pull. The layout reflects the notion of distance and intimacy, with the main collision occurring centrally where darker shades meet brighter pixels, mimicking a bridge where yearning meets stability. The chosen ‘door’ shape seed subtly informs the arrangement, representing transitions and thresholds in feeling.
