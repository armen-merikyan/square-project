Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional contrast between longing and resolve through a dance of muted greens with bursts of warmth. The deep olive and ripe green evoke a sense of urgency and calm resolution, while honey gold serves as an accent highlighting the breaking points in the tension. The central collision occurs at the outset of the pixel arrangement, symbolizing the push and pull between distant hopes and tangible realities. The layout creates a halo of complexity around a quiet center, allowing space for reflection amid the chaos.
