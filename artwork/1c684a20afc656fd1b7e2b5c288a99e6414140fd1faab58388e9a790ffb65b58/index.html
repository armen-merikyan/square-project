Artist Statement

This composition embraces the emotional duality of longing and resolve, using a palette of deep blues and soft yellows to create a contrast between cool and warm. The dominant dark tones evoke a sense of yearning, while the light accents suggest moments of clarity and growth. The layout features a broken frame, with lit edges creating tension through intentional interruptions by broken pixels, allowing the emotions to bleed into one another. The spiral flow unfurls outward, illustrating the push and pull between serenity and urgency.
