Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between panic (expressed through chaotic, dark hues and uneven distribution) and control (represented by structured, lighter colors). The palette balances the rage of deep reds and blacks with calming pale blues and whites, suggesting both conflict and resolution. The placement of lighter colors in a jagged pattern creates echoes of sparks against heavy, dark backgrounds, demonstrating the struggle and interplay between order and chaos. The central movement flows upward in a zigzag, creating an optical climb while maintaining a significant presence of negative space to emphasize imbalance and absence, encapsulating the abstract dilemma.
