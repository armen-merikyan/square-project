Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the contrast between relief and dread, illustrated through a palette of pale mint, soft sky, storm gray, near black, and faded gold. The overlapping quiet fields and broken frames create a tension where light shapes struggle against dense dark areas, particularly centered in the square. This arrangement visually disrupts the viewer's perception, invoking a feeling of unresolved emotional collisions that mirror the complex interplay of relief and dread suggested by the seed.
