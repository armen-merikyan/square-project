Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension of trust against suspicion. The palette features deep blacks and vibrant electric blue alongside soft magenta, suggesting both an overarching darkness and bright accents that conflict. The central contrast, positioned diagonally, illustrates the tension between structured paths (trust) in electric blue dots and the scattered disruptions (suspicion) in black and magenta. The juxtaposition creates a rhythm of movement, echoing the push and pull of our emotions based on the seed's themes of pressure and resilience.
