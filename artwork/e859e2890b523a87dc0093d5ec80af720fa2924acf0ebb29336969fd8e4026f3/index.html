Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between restlessness and peace, embodied in a palette that juxtaposes clean whites with darker hues, suggesting clarity battling confusion. The central collision occurs where the structured grid of the restless pattern breaks into the quieter negative cross, creating moments of interruption that evoke a sense of struggle. This interplay mirrors the seed's themes of memory versus motion, illustrating the complex dance between stasis and change.
