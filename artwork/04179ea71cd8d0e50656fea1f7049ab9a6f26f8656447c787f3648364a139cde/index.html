Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional dichotomy of vulnerability and strength. The palette merges pale mint and soft sky to convey softness while storm gray and near black introduce strength, creating a tension-filled backdrop. The radial pattern of vulnerability collides with the constellation pattern of strength, forming a visual chaos at the center, representing struggles and interruptions. Broken pixels disrupt the order, symbolizing the fragility of connection amidst disruptions, embodying both exposure and shelter in an abstract interplay.
