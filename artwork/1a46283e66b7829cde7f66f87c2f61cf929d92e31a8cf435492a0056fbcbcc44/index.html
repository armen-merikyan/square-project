Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between rage and calm as one dominates the space with saturated reds and blacks, while cooler blues and whites attempt to establish a grounding presence. The palette features stark contrasts to suggest collision; jagged reds cut through the tranquil blue fields, embodying chaos against serenity. The flow spirals inward, reflecting an intrinsic struggle, with off-center placements enhancing the sense of imbalance. Broken pixels disrupt patterns, symbolizing imperfection amidst emotional conflict, echoing themes of containment versus overflow.
