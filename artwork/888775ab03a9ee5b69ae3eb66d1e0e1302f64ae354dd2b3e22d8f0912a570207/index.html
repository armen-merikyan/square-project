Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between defiance and submission, where the deeper navy represents calmness and submission, while the bright dim cyan and scattered white reflect defiance and disruption. The central area contrasts with the darker edges, encapsulating the struggle between quiet acceptance and vibrant resistance. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, embodying glitches that echo the tension. The dominant navy creates a vast, open space, while brighter colors pulse outward, manifesting the core emotional conflict of the seed into an abstract dialogue.
