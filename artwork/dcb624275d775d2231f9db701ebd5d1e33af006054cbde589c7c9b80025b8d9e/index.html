Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between emptiness and desire, using a palette of saturated coral and orange against muted charcoals and blues to represent a struggle. The main collision occurs at the center, where vibrant colors pulse inward, echoing desire against the quiet perimeter of emptiness. This pattern creates a spiral flow, visually mirroring the seed's themes of containment and overflow, suggesting movement and interruption while maintaining a sense of vast negative space.
