Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve through the use of a muted palette contrasted with abrupt accents. The serene and cool tones dominate the space, establishing a quiet underlying pulse, while warmer, sharper edges and broken pixels introduce moments of tension and disruption, simulating the conflict between repair and damage. The central distribution of colors reflects a 'center_seed' layout with clusters of serenity interrupted by brighter elements, expressing both a contained essence and a sense of overflow.
