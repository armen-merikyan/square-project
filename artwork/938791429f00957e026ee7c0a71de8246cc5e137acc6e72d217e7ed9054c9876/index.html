Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between loneliness and connection by contrasting dark, dense colors and lighter, more ethereal tones. The black and pale blue palette reflects both weight and lift, suggesting a push and pull of emotions. The arrangement creates collisions where dark areas clash against lighter bursts, representing fragmented memories as they attempt to connect. The layout follows a rising signal, inviting a sense of upward movement, while leaving deliberate negative space to enhance feelings of loneliness amidst connections.
