Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between vulnerability and protection. The palette consists of muted shelter green and warm brown invoking a sense of safety, contrasted with the stark cold white and pale skin tones that represent exposure. The collision occurs in the central region where the ripple pattern of exposure clashes with the threshold pattern symbolizing safety. The arrangement highlights a hollow center, informed by the seed's emotional qualities of pressure and release. Interruptions and breaks enhance the feeling of fragility amidst structure, resonating with the idea of a delicate balance.
