Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional contrast of strength and vulnerability through a palette that juxtaposes rich, dark tones against lighter, softer hues. The composition features a heavy base symbolizing strength at the bottom, which supports a pale, exposed center representing vulnerability. The deliberate placement of sparse, cracked elements introduces a sense of imperfection, enhancing the narrative of exposure versus shelter. At the center, a collision occurs as lighter tones interact with dark edges, suggesting a tension between safety and risk, mirroring the complexities of human emotion. This arrangement disrupts evenness, creating a visual weight that delivers an emotional impact reflective of the seed's themes.
