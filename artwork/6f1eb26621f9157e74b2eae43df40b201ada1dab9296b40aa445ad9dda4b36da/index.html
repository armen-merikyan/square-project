Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between 'quiet' and 'pressure,' using a palette of muted and sharp contrasts. The colors reflect a spectrum from calm off-white and faint peach to darker, compressed reds and dark navies. The collision occurs at the center where the dense pressure of reds meets the soft edges of lighter colors, suggesting a struggle between chaos and calmness. The arrangement allows for a sense of shattering symmetry, capturing an emotional interplay of serenity versus density, as inspired by the seed.
