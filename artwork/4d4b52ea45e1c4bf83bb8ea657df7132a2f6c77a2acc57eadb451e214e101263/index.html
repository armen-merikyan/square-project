Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the tension between 'spark' and 'ash', with the selected palette reflecting hues of energy and stillness. The contrast arises from the 'electric_blue' and 'hot_magenta' accents colliding with the deeper 'dead_black' and 'static_gray', representing vitality against decay. The radial pattern of gray evokes a sense of ash spreading outward, while jagged interruptions of bright colors symbolize fleeting sparks. This composition resonates an exploration of resilience and fragility, with the main collision occurring centrally, embodying a shift from calm (darker tones) to bursts of vibrancy (lighter hues). Negative space enhances the notion of emptiness amidst a struggle for control.
