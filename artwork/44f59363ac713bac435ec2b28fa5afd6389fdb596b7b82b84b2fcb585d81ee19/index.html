Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic interplay between softness and severity. The chosen palette combines gentle powder pinks and warm whites for softness with stark carbon black and sharp crimson for severity. The layout splits the canvas vertically, accumulating warmth and gentle textures on the left, contrasting sharply with dense, cold textures on the right. This creates a visual tension that feels alive, echoing the pulse of conflicting emotions. The transitions and disruptions between colors serve as not just boundaries, but moments of intensity and release, symbolizing the complexity of forgiveness and guilt embedded in human experience.
