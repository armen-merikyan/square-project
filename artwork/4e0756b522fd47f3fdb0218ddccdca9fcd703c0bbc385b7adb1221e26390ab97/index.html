Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes warmth and strength against a backdrop of subtle chaos. The palette—comprised of off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach—offers a spectrum between quiet warmth and the gravity of resolve. The main collision occurs diagonally from the top-left to the bottom-right, symbolizing the struggle between longing and firmness, reflecting the tensions found in human connections. Boundaries soften with a double outline, while intentional broken pixels evoke moments of imperfection and intimacy. This arrangement leaves ample negative space, reinforcing the themes of distance amidst connection.
