Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between longing and resolve, with warm hues representing desire and cool shades suggesting distance. The layout features a halo of deep colors at the border, echoing the feeling of resolve, while the inner core employs glitch patterns symbolizing longing through fragmentation. The main collision emerges at the center with overlapping dots, creating a visual tension that resonates with the emotional narrative of the seed.
