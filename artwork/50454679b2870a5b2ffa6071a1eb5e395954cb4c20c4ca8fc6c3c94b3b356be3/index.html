Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between quietude and pressure. The outer border, painted in deep, muted colors like dark navy and compressed red, conveys a sense of weight and compression, representing pressure. The inner area, using softer tones like off-white and faint peach, embodies a quiet, expansive space, fostering a feeling of release. The main collision occurs in the transition zone, where soft and hard edges meet, visually suggesting a struggle between dense clusters and open areas. The random placement of broken pixels amplifies the feeling of shattering against a tide, creating an intentional disruption that mirrors the chaos inherent in the seed phrase.
