Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrast of spark versus ash, pairing vibrant accents with muted, settling tones. The color palette of reds and creams evokes hunger and restraint, while the juxtaposition between dense patterns of crosshatching and negative cross elements reflects tension and release. The center of the composition features a collision of the two patterns, where the brighter colors fight against the darker, more subdued shades, symbolizing the battle between vitality and decay. The quiet channel layout provides a visual path through the turmoil, emphasizing the push and pull of energetic versus stillness, mirroring the themes of memory and erasure.
