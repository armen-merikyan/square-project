Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension of longing and resolve through contrast and color placement. The palette represents a mix of warmth and neutrality, featuring deep wine and soft gold to signify longing, while ash gray and stain brown provide a sense of grounding associated with resolve. The collision occurs in the center, where warmer tones intersect with cooler ones, embodying a struggle between emotional extremes. The falling pixel layout creates a visual flow that suggests dripping memories or unresolved feelings, contributing to the overall narrative of repair and damage.
