Artist Statement

This artwork balances the themes of restlessness and peace through a palette emphasizing warm sepia and cool faded blue hues. The quiet fields of tan and dust gray offer contrast to sporadic bursts of deeper colors, reflecting the tension of memory and motion. The main collision of patterns occurs in the diagonal arrangement, where echoes of light against darker shades create a flow of unrest, interrupted by moments of calm. This entanglement captures the emotional journey between tranquility and the chaos of thought, embodying the essence of the seed.
