Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette of 'mourning_renewal'—featuring deep blue, pale yellow, and mourning black—evokes both a calm introspection and a sense of weight. The main contrast occurs centrally, with the gentle pressure_band pattern suggesting vulnerability clashing against the noise of strength. Misalignments in the mirror layout highlight imperfections, adding an element of chaos within symmetry, while maintaining an overall structure that evokes protection. The placement of colors and thoughtful use of broken pixels create a narrative of tension and complexity, mirroring the contrasts found in the seed.
