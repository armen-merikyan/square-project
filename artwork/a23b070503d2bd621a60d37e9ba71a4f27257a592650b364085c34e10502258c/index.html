Artist Statement

The emotional tone is an interplay between tenderness and rage, conveyed through a palette of saturated reds contrasted by muted blues. The harshness of red suggests a compressed anger while the soft blues evoke tenderness. The layout follows a pressure corner, where red intensely occupies the top left, suggesting a buildup of rage, while the calming blue occupies the rest, creating a gentle counterbalance. The tiny_cross serves as a metaphor for conflict, with collisions visible through broken pixel motifs in the red, bleeding into soft blue dots, echoing a struggle for forgiveness amidst turmoil.
