Artist Statement

This composition embodies the struggle between control and surrender. The selected palette of mourning black, deep blue, new leaf, pale yellow, and stone evokes both depth and renewal, emphasizing the emotional intensity related to each concept. The central collision occurs with the dark tones contrasting against lighter hues, symbolizing the push and pull of pressure versus release. The two pulsing centers create an inward spiral flow that captures a sense of tension, suggesting movement surrounding the quiet spaces, echoing feelings of constraint and unexpected freedom.
