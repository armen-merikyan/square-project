Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the contrasting emotions of awe and terror through a carefully curated palette of dark and light colors. The use of black and pale blue against white creates a stark contrast that evokes tension. The top half is dominated by dark materials, symbolizing terror, while pale blue and white soften the lower half, suggesting awe. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, echoing the chaos within order, aligning with the seed's themes. The arrangement of colors develops a wave-like movement, invoking a sense of oscillation between the two emotional states.
