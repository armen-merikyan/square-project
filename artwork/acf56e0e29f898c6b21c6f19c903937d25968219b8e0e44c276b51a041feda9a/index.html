Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between explosive rage and serene calm. The palette combines reds and blacks for the rage, contrasting with pale blue and white to embody calmness. The dense center is marked with hot reds and chaotic dots, introducing tension, while a lattice of pale blue surrounds, creating a quiet space. The central clash represents an overflow that struggles against a containment on the edges, echoing the seed's suggestion of eroded symmetry and machine emotion, highlighting the clash between calm and chaos.
