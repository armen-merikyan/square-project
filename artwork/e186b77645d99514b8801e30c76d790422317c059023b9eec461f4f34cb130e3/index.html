Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between joy and grief, using a palette that shifts from bright yellows and oranges to muted blues and purples. The bright colors signal moments of joy, while the darker shades express grief, colliding in areas to emphasize emotional conflict. The abstract pattern is informed by the concepts of order vs chaos and the dual nature of the human experience, with an intertwining checker pattern that creates a fractured harmony, suggesting both hidden and exposed emotions.
