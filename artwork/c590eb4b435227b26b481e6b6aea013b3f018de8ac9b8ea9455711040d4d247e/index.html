Artist Statement

This composition blends emotional tension of vulnerability and strength, represented through a soft center of pale greens contrasting sharply with dark violets and shadows. The broken pixel placements symbolize imperfections in strength, creating a narrative of beauty in fragility. The color palette, taken from fear and hope, plays on the interplay of darkness and light, with the main collision occurring at the center, suggesting rise and fall, safety against exposure. It evokes a sense of anticipation and quiet struggle, encapsulating the essence of the seed.
