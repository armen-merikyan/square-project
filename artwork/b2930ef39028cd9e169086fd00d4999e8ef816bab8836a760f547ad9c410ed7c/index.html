Artist Statement

The emotional tone dances between pressure and quietude. The palette captures this duality with darker weights of dark navy and compressed red representing pressure, while off-white and faint peach introduce soft, quiet spaces. The main collision occurs in the center with dense colors transitioning to lighter hues at the edges, symbolizing a release of tension. This arrangement reflects the dialogue between chaos and calm, with a careful balance that suggests a shattering against an unseen tide, echoing the seed's themes of conflict and resolution.
