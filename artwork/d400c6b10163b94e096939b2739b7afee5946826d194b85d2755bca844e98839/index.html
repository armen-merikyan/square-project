Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the interplay between quiet spaces and dense clusters of color, a visual metaphor for emotional collisions. The palette oscillates between cool and warm tones, emphasizing the contrast of pressure against quiet interludes. The use of deep teal (#007F7F) suggests hidden depths, while bright coral (#FF6F61) acts as an emotional accent, creating moments of tension that echo throughout the piece. The arrangement is intentionally asymmetric, allowing for discord to arise from the organized chaos, resulting in a rhythm of release and pressure that mirrors the complexity of human emotion.
