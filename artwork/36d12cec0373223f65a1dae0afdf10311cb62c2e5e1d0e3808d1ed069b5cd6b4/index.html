Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between trust and suspicion, realized through a clash of smooth, flowing patterns and jagged interruptions. The palette, drawn from warm yellows and oranges contrasted against deep blues and muted purples, creates a dynamic interplay of warmth and shadow. Key contrasts occur in the middle, where the soft flow of the 'tide' pattern collides with the fractured lines of 'fault_line', creating a central chaos amidst a generally trusted structure. This tension epitomizes the duality in the seed's themes, producing a compelling visual experience that resonates with the narrative of resilience and fragility.
