Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette of 'melancholy wonder' colors—rain blue, lavender gray, moon white, soft cyan, and muted plum—evokes a sense of introspection and depth. The layout suggests a rising flow, with the void of negative space at the bottom symbolizing openness and vulnerability, while the upper blocks of muted tones grow denser, representing strength. The main contrast occurs at the center, where soft veil-like patterns of pale colors collide with bold fault lines that interrupt the flow, creating a narrative of fragile resilience. The broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection and complexity, enhancing the emotional depth observed in the arrangement.
