Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between dread and relief, capturing a palpable tension. The palette of muted storm gray and faded gold suggests an emotional depth, where gray embodies dread and gold signifies relief. The main contrast lies at the center, where the jagged checker pattern (symbolizing chaos and pressure) collides with the fluid tide pattern (symbolizing calm and release). This duality creates a dynamic visual rhythm, embodying the seed's theme of pressure versus release, where density is concentrated at the edges, gradually dispersing towards the center.
