Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve, integrating a palette that transitions from soft pinks to deep maroons, reflecting both warmth and intensity. The contrast between densely packed dark colors and lighter accents creates an intersection of desire and determination. The arrangement suggests falling pixels, with clusters of pinks and reds colliding with muted dark maroon and gray, mirroring the idea of an emotionally charged scene where intimacy meets distance. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, illustrating fractures in emotional states.
