Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of love and betrayal, encapsulated through a palette that juxtaposes warm pinks and deep maroons against gritty grays. The center features a space where warm colors crosshatch softly, surrounded by muted edges representing isolation and fractured trust. The contrast happens dynamically in the bottom two rows, where a collision of warmer and cooler tones creates tension, suggesting emotional upheaval. The use of broken pixels infuses imperfection, enhancing the sense of a lonely signal amidst an overarching noise.
