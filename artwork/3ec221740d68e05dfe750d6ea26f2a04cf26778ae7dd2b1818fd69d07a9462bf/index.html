Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between envy and gratitude, with a focus on the central pixels representing conflict against the softer edges. The palette of navy and dim cyan aids in invoking feelings of loneliness contrasted by moments of warmth. Sharp contrasts arise in the center, creating a collision against the softer tones around, reflecting the noise of competing emotions. The pattern flows from the center outwards, suggesting a gradual release that feels echoing and unresolved.
