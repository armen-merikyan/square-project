Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines tenderness with rage, expressed through contrasting palettes of saturated reds and muted blues. The palette includes bold red (#FF0000) representing rage, juxtaposed with soft pale blue (#A0C1E3) representing tenderness. The main contrast occurs in the center, where jagged red pixels, representing chaotic rage, clash with gentle maze-like patterns of muted colors, creating a sense of collision. This reflects the seed's theme of emotional turbulence and conflict, forming an abstract rhythm that resonates with feelings of guilt and forgiveness, creating a layered narrative.
