Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between safety and exposure, symbolizing a delicate balance with soft barriers against harsh elements. The selected palette uses shelter green and warm brown to suggest a protective atmosphere, contrasted by pale skin and cold white to evoke vulnerability. The main collision occurs vertically, splitting the lighter and darker tones, representing the internal struggle between comfort and vulnerability. This interplay suggests a narrative of resilience through fractured beauty, embodying the essence of the seed's themes.
