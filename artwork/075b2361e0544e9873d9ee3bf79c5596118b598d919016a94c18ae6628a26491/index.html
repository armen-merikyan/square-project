Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between defiance and submission through a juxtaposition of sharp, intense colors against softer hues. The palette, featuring alarm red and ink blue, symbolizes urgency and depth, respectively. The dominant red clusters push outward from a dense center, while the cooler tones provide a calming backdrop, suggesting an underlying struggle. This collision occurs in the middle of the grid, reflecting the emotional theme of conflict rooted in contrasting forces. The broken pixels add intentional disruptions, reinforcing the chaotic dynamics of this abstract narrative.
