Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of clarity battling confusion. The chosen palette of electric blue, lime, and black creates a stark contrast, representing clarity and confusion respectively. The arrangement reflects a near-symmetrical pattern disrupted by strategic breaks—serving as interruptions and representing emotional tension. The 'spark' and 'noise' patterns convey the pulse of this conflict, with dense clusters colliding against empty spaces to enhance the feeling of chaos amidst structure.
