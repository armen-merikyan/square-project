Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between joy and grief through the chosen palette of bright yellows and muted blues. The upper rows convey a spark of joy with brighter colors, transitioning into deeper, darker tones that reflect grief. Hard edges create a sense of structure, contrasting the fluidity often associated with emotions. The pattern resembles a split screen, suggesting both the calm and chaos of processing feelings, while the varying shades create rhythmic waves, echoing the layered experiences of human emotion.
