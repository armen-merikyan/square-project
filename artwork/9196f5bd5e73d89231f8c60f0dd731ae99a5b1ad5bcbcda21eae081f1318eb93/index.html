Artist Statement

This abstract composition embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission. The palette chosen, 'melancholy_wonder', features muted plum and soft cyan to create a serene backdrop while contrasting sharply with the ring patterns in rain blue. The central area serves as an anchor, surrounded by pulse marks that fluctuate in intensity, suggesting a rhythm of inner conflict. The arrangement flows outward, signifying the struggle between chaos and order, while maintaining negative space that allows the colors to breathe, creating a feeling of unresolved tension.
