Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between clarity and chaos, with muted gray tones representing noise and sharp electric blue and green acting as signal, creating a pulse that cuts through. The collision occurs centrally, where a focus of brighter colors disrupts the static background, echoing a struggle for clarity. The uneven distribution of colors reflects the inherent disorder in communication, influenced by the seed's themes of brokenness and harsh contrasts.
