Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between 'spark' and 'ash', representing sudden bursts of energy colliding with remnants of restraint. The palette of 'relief_dread' features muted gray tones of 'storm_gray' and accents of 'faded_gold', illustrating the push and pull of energy versus decay. The primary contrast occurs centrally, with sharp diagonals from the bright 'near_black' interrupting soft gradients of pale and faded tones, echoing fragments of memory within the drama of a falling pixel layout.
