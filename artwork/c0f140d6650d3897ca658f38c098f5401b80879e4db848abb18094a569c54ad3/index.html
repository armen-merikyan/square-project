Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a delicate balance between longing and resolve, using a palette that blends sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue. The contrast mainly lies between soft, quiet fields and dense clusters of color. The collision occurs centrally, where longing emerges in the form of pale blues and grays surrounded by deeper, richer tones of brown, creating a feeling of a broken yet yearning landscape. This pattern depicts the duality of strength and weakness, much like a thread stretched taut yet remaining intact, embodying both repair and damage within a structured yet fragmented layout.
