Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the clash between transient brightness ('spark') and residual quiet ('ash'). The palette combines impactful contrast with cool blues and muted grays, evoking a sense of struggle and resilience. The main collision occurs at the center, where jagged patterns of 'spark' clash against the softer 'ash' edges, showcasing the struggle between energy and calm. The layout opens invitingly, hinting at a flow from bright to muted, embodying memory's battle with erasure.
