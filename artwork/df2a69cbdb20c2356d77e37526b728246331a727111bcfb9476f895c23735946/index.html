Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between joy and grief, employing a palette of vibrant yellows and muted blues. The upper half features warm hues representing joy expanding through crosshatch patterns, while the lower half deepens into cooler tones indicating the heaviness of grief expressed through stripes. The central pixel configuration introduces a light-to-dark contrast, suggesting a protective boundary that oscillates between exposure and concealment. This arrangement simulates emotional waves, creating a visual narrative of turmoil and comfort through an orbit around a core of contrasting feelings.
