Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the raw emotional tension between defiance and submission. The palette of cosmic indigo, void black, star white, cold gold, and deep teal creates a dynamic contrast between the quiet, subdued areas and the bold, dark elements signifying resistance. The main collision occurs in the central rows, where brighter colors intersect with darker shades, suggesting a battle between calmness and pressure. The jagged edges and uneven distribution of colors encapsulate the themes of chaos and order, while areas of broken pixels introduce an element of unpredictability, echoing the glitch aspect intertwined with pulse marks. Overall, the composition carries an abstract narrative of exertion against retreat, accentuated by an intricate dance of contrasting colors.
