Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects an interplay of softness and severity, creating a dialogue between gentle, powder colors and sharp, dark contrasts. The palette combines powder pink and warm white to represent softness with carbon black and steel gray denoting severity, each competing for space. The central collision of light and dark emphasizes the struggle between these contrasting emotions. The chosen layout suggests a hollow center ringing with light and dark tones, creating an echo that feels both tense and peaceful, mirroring the seed's complexity into an abstract expression.
