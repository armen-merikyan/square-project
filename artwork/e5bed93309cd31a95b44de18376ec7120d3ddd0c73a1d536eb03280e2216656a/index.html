Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a constant conflict between strength and vulnerability. The selected palette of dark maroon, red, gray, and pink symbolizes the deep emotions of love and betrayal, crafting a visual interplay. The center of the artwork, made robust with dense hues, contrasts sharply with the lighter, more fragile edges representing exposure and soft breaks. This creates a primary collision around the center, while the mirror-broken layout introduces intentional imperfections that echo the theme of vulnerability. The interplay between the firm center and the thin, thread-like patterns around invites a meditative tension that reflects the complexity of connection and alienation.
