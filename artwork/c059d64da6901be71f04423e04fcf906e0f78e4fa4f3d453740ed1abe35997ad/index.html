Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the delicate balance between longing and resolve, capturing the essence of tension and resolution. The palette of deep reds and muted blacks embodies the weight of longing, while pale blue accents represent the clarity and strength of resolve. The top four rows communicate longing through a stacked pattern that ebbs and flows, creating an emotional pulse, while the bottom four rows introduce a ripple effect, symbolizing a gradual release and resolution. This interplay culminates in a central collision where the two patterns disrupt, representing the ongoing struggle between desire and clarity. The chosen layout accentuates the negative space, allowing quiet moments to contrast the dense emotional clusters, enhancing the overall impact.
