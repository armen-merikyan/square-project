Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the conflict between clarity (represented by the structured grid) and confusion (represented by radial patterns), embodying the tension of pressure and release. The chosen palette of warm and cool tones—yellow and orange as vibrant accents paired with deep blue and muted purple—creates a dynamic interplay that highlights the collisions of emotions. The main contrast emerges from the juxtaposition of warm versus cool, suggesting a dialogue between chaos and calmness. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, amplifying the sense of confusion and disarray within the orderly grid.
