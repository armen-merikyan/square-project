Artist Statement

The artwork captures the contrasting emotions of rage and calm, using a color palette of reds and soft blues to signify warmth and tranquility. The predominant chaos of red forms echoes collision while softer pale blues create a boundary of calmness, emulating the push and pull of tension. The layout features an off-center configuration with jagged patterns of red bursting against lighter hues, simulating an emotional eruption contained by muted calm. This design reflects the struggle of opposing forces, echoing the seed's theme of interruption while leaving enough negative space to represent quietness.
