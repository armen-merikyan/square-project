Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between panic and control, reflected in the contrasting patterns of ring and crosshatch. The chosen palette of navy, black, and dim cyan conveys a sense of loneliness and deep introspection. The left half represents the chaotic aspect of 'panic' with swirling ring motifs, while the right embodies 'control' through structured crosshatching, creating a rhythmic symmetry disrupted by imperfection. The interplay between densely packed colors and negative space enhances the feeling of unresolved emotional tension.
