Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional interplay of longing and resolve. The chosen palette combines electric blue and hot magenta for tension, while static gray serves as a quiet field to reflect emptiness. The main contrast emerges diagonally, where dense clusters of color collide with open spaces, symbolizing the push and pull of desire against determination. This composition reflects the abstract nuances of intimacy and distance through interrupted patterns, portraying an unresolved yet intimate emotional landscape.
