Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between restlessness and peace, expressed through a split composition where one half embodies stillness and the other dynamism. The palette, 'melancholy_wonder', combines soft hues such as 'rain_blue' and 'lavender_gray' with deeper tones like 'muted_plum' to create tension. The central diagonal strong contrast, where vibrant 'soft_cyan' clashes with more muted shades, suggests a motion versus memory conflict. Broken pixels strategically interrupt the flow, embodying the theme of disruption in peace and restlessness. This abstract arrangement focuses on emotional echoes and the quiet vs. stuttered interplay of colors.
