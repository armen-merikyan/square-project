Artist Statement

This abstract artwork embodies the dichotomy between spark and ash, represented through the juxtaposition of vibrant and muted hues. The palette, featuring soft gray, warm ivory, deep wine, and golds, resonates with the emotional tension of resilience versus fragility. The pattern of stairs on one side introduces dynamism, while the glitch style on the other disrupts the rhythm, creating a collision at the center. This contrast invites contemplation about the interplay of opposing forces, mediating the experience of hunger and restraint, emphasized by broken pixels that symbolize imperfection and memory's erosion.
