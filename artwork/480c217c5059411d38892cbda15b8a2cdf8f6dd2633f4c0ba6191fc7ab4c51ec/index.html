Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional juxtaposition of longing and resolve. The color palette of muted cream, red oxide, and blackened plum evokes a sense of warmth and depth, symbolizing the complexities of human desire while the gray tones emphasize the strength and firmness of resolve. The main contrast occurs in the central area where dense clusters (red oxide and blackened plum) interrupt the soft fields of muted cream, suggesting an emotional collision. This creates a visual tension that echoes the themes of intimacy and distance, portraying the nuanced struggle of connection versus separation.
