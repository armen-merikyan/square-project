Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between defiance and submission, reflecting the emotional turmoil through contrasting patterns and colors. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue symbolizes a sense of broken memories and emotional weight, with lighter tones suggesting lightness trying to break through murkier hues. The contrast is pronounced in the zigzag flow, where darker colors encroach upon lighter spaces, portraying a struggle between opposing forces. The broken pixel elements introduce imperfection, enhancing the feelings of conflict and unresolved tension as the center appears under siege by edges of heavy color, suggesting a battle between opening and closing off emotionally.
