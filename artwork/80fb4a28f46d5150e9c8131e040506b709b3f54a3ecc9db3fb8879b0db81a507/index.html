Artist Statement

This artwork captures the interplay of explosive emotion and serene calm. The palette contrasts deep blacks and fiery reds to represent rage against soft blues and whites depicting calmness. The layout features two dominant centers in a zigzag pattern, punctuated by broken pixels, suggesting an interruption of quiet by intense energy. The cool colors create a calming field that punctuates the aggressiveness around the edges, mirroring the emotional tension of the seed's themes.
