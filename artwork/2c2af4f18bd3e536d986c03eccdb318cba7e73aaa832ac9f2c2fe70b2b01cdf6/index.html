Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between hope (love) and despair (betrayal). The palette features warm pinks and reds contrasting with cool grays and dark maroons, symbolizing the tension between love's warmth and betrayal's cold edges. The layout rises suggestively, echoing hope, while the combination of radial and split screen patterns reflects the complexities of emotions colliding. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, hinting at cracks within a heart. The canvas maintains negative space to prevent overwhelming chaos, further emphasizing the emotional struggle.
