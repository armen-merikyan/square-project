Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The dark palette of blacks and grays evokes a feeling of isolation, while the burst of pale blue and white interrupts the space, representing moments of potential connection. The layout is structured to have a thick outline of darkness encasing an inner ring of lighter hues, symbolizing a protective barrier that both isolates and invites. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfections, reflecting the fragility of memories and connections. The flowing pattern from left to right carries this narrative of transition from weight to lightness, embodying the struggle between feeling full and hollow.
