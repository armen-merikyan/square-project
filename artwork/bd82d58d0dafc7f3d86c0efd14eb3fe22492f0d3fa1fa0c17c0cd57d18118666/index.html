Artist Statement

The artwork captures the duality of joy and grief through contrasting colors and patterns. Bright yellows and oranges signify joy, suggesting brilliance and expansion, while muted blues and purples convey grief with a sense of fragmentation. The layout features an orderly center that gradually breaks apart, embodying the chaotic friction between the two emotions. The zigzag flow adds dynamic tension, allowing the colors to clash yet resonate, illustrating the struggle between order and chaos.
