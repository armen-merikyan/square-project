Artist Statement

The emotional tone of confusion versus clarity is captured in the interplay of dark, dense colors against vibrant highlights. The chosen palette, 'ancient_fire,' suggests warmth and intensity, symbolizing the chaotic swirl of thoughts. The main contrast occurs between the dark border, representing confusion, and the lighter inner core, embodying clarity. This clash serves as a visual metaphor for the center's magnetic pull against the edges’ chaotic demeanor, echoing the seed's themes.
