Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between loneliness and connection, presenting a burst of central colors that symbolize a contrast against darker edges, suggesting the hidden depths of emotion. The palette comprises pinks and reds to signify warmth and connection, while grays and dark maroon introduce an element of isolation. The arrangement features clusters of connected colors contrasting against expansive negative space, embodying the profound sense of being alone amidst connection, portraying a visual echo of the seed's essence.
