Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between clarity and chaos, with a predominance of noise represented by deep navy and black fields, disrupted by intermittent signals in dim cyan and stark white. The palette creates contrast, where dark spaces amplify the bright interruptions. The main collision occurs near the center, symbolizing the clash of both emotions—a pulse attempting to emerge amidst static. The seed concept of a 'broken mirror' translated into fragmented patterns evokes a sense of disarray, where the interference creates a distortion that still hints at a readable rhythm.
