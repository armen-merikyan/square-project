Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of deep reds and muted blues conveys the weight of suspicion against the calm of trust. The structure features a dark center representing suspicion, encircled by lighter hues suggesting trust, thereby embodying the halo void layout. The collision of brush-like crack patterns emphasizes the fractures in perception, while the broken pixels act as reflections of instability, reinforcing the theme of resilience amidst fragility.
