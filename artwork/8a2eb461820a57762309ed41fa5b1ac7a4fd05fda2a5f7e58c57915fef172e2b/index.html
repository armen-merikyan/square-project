Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette reflects a mourning yet renewing quality, with the deep blues suggesting depth and introspection, while pale yellow hints at hope and resolution. The top half embodies ‘longing’ with scattered, stacked patterns, exhibiting a sense of unfinished paths. The bottom half represents ‘resolve’ through a structured stepwell pattern signifying stability. The collision between chaotic stacks and orderly steps occurs at the middle, creating a dynamic yet serene visual tension that represents a journey between these emotional states.
