Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional struggle between restlessness and peace. The palette predominantly features cool static gray and dead black, creating a quiet foundation, while hot magenta and electric blue disrupt with vibrant accents, signifying the tumultuous nature of memory and motion. The main contrast occurs in the center where crosshatch patterns of warm tones clash against cooler, striped backgrounds, echoing a battle of contain versus overflow. The tension of diagonal arguments forms a narrative as pixels break and bleed into each other, reflecting interruptions in the emotional landscape.
