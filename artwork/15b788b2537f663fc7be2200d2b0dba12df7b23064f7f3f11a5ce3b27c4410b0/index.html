Artist Statement

This abstract piece captures the tension between confusion and clarity through contrasting colors and pixel arrangements. The palette of yellow and orange represents warmth and clarity, while deep blue and muted purple symbolize confusion and turmoil. The juxtaposition of radial patterns and checkerboard motifs reflects the emotional collisions of symmetry and breakage. The placement of colors suggests a narrative flow from subdued chaos to structured calm, embodying the pulse of inner turmoil that alternates with moments of clarity. The arrangement of broken pixels introduces imperfection, enhancing the theme of emotional disturbance against the stability of clarity.
