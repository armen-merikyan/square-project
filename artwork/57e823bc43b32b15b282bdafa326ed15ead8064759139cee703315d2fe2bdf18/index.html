Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes peace with restlessness, creating a central tension. The palette 'joy_grief' with warm oranges and muted purples contrasts with deep blue, allowing for expressive conflict within the square. The main collision occurs in the center, where noise distorts the quiet veil surrounding it. Broken pixels disrupt the structured pattern, suggesting the fragility beneath peace. Overall, the layout amplifies the idea of threshold, depicting the passage from tranquility to stirring chaos.
