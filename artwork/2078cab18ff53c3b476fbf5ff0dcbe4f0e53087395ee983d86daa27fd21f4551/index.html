Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between love and betrayal through a palette of pinks and grays, conveying warmth and cool detachment. The main contrast is created by alternating soft pinks with harsh grays, generating an emotional collision. The layout features an asymmetrical distribution of colors, mirroring the broken symmetry of the emotional seed, while strategic negative space emphasizes feelings of isolation and quiet reflection, representing an empty room filled with echoing memories.
