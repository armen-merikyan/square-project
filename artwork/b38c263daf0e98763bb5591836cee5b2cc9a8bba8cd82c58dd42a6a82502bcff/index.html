Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between fear and hope through a careful balance of dark and light colors, representing the struggle for light amidst encroaching darkness. The palette consists of deep blacks and dark violets symbolizing fear, juxtaposed with soft golds and pale greens that evoke hope. The arrangement leans toward the bottom left, creating a sense of rising, reflective of the idea of 'rising signal.' This creates a dynamic flow, with hope emerging from the corner while fear dominates the rest of the canvas. Broken pixel patterns and a predominance of negative space suggest chaos and imperfection, amplifying the conflict between the two emotions. Overall, the layout expresses an upward progression amidst the fractures of the surroundings.
