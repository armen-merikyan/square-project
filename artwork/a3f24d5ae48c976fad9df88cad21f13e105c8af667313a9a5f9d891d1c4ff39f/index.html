Artist Statement

The piece captures the tension between defiance and submission through a clash of colors and patterns. The darker tones symbolize defiance, creating a chaotic yet structured frame that interrupts the lighter, quieter fields representing submission. The palette, drawn from 'clarity_confusion', offers a stark contrast, emphasizing the struggle for control. The collision occurs primarily in the center, with jagged borders representing fractures and disruptions, while maintaining a sense of open space to avoid claustrophobia.
