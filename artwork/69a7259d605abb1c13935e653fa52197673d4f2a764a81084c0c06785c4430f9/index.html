Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional tension between quiet and pressure through the interplay of muted and dense colors. The palette, featuring off-white and dust gray for calm and compressed red and dark navy for intensity, creates a visual dialogue of contrast. The main collision occurs diagonally, where a denser cluster of red starkly interrupts a muted background, symbolizing pressure breaking into a quiet space. The inclusion of broken pixels introduces imperfection, representing shattering moments against the tide of clarity. The overall pattern, with its sharp edges and soft interruptions, encapsulates a complex emotional landscape that invites contemplation.
