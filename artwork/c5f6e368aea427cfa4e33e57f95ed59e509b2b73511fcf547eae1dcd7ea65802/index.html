Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional clash of panic and control through sharp, contrasting colors. The chosen palette of deep navy against bright dim cyan reflects the tension between chaos (represented in angular, radial patterns) and order (the underlying stripes). The symmetry of the central structure, juxtaposed with broken edges, illustrates how perfection is disrupted by external forces. Main contrasts occur at the border where the chaos of radial patterns exacerbates the calm, controlled stripes. The arrangement invites a sense of movement and unresolved tension, echoing the emotional dichotomy embedded in the seed.
