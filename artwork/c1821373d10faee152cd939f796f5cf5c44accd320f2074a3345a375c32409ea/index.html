Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional duel between defiance and submission. The palette transitions between deep red and pale blue, symbolizing aggression versus calmness. The contrast emerges prominently in the center, with dense clusters of darker shades clashing against lighter hues, creating a rhythm of conflict. The layout reflects a mirror that stutters—repeated colors on each side interrupted by specific offsets, speaking to the theme of imbalance—while broken pixels add an element of imperfection, resonating with the idea of love and betrayal. Negative space is strategically employed to maintain breathability and tension.
