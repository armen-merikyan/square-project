Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between clarity and distortion, illustrating a readable pulse (signal) attempting to emerge amidst overwhelming noise. The selected palette features stark contrasts: vibrant 'signal_green' and 'hot_magenta' emerge against a backdrop of muted 'static_gray' and 'dead_black'. The collision of colors occurs centrally, where the bright cluster struggles against the pervasive dark, portraying an urgent moment in the flow of sound and silence. Patterns of the flame represent the signal, flickering amidst a persistent ash field of noise, leaving intentional gaps as echoes of memory.
