Artist Statement

This composition juxtaposes 'quiet' and 'pressure' through a flow from left to right, echoing the tension described in the seed. The palette features subdued 'off_white' and 'dust_gray' against striking 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy', creating an emotional impact. The main contrast occurs at the center, where dense clusters created by 'compressed_red' clash against the soft expanse of 'off_white', suggesting both confrontation and release. The 'open_gate' layout implies an ebbing tide, while the broken edges hint at imperfections in harmony.
