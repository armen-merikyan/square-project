Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the dense and dark against light and muted fields, embodying the clash of pressure and quiet. The chosen palette enhances this, with 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy' representing the pressure, while 'off_white' and 'faint_peach' offer a quiet release. The main contrast occurs at the center of the square, where pressure and quiet collide, suggesting a core of tension amidst outward calm. The arrangement creates a braided pattern of interaction and interruption, reflecting the abstract struggles of clarity in confusion.
