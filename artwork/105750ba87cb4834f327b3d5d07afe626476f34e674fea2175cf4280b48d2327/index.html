Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a dance between the warmth of safety and the crispness of exposure. The palette, featuring earthy greens and warm browns juxtaposed with pale skin tones and cold whites, reflects this dichotomy. The outer border in deeper colors represents the protective aspect, while the lighter inner grid signifies vulnerability. The main collision occurs at the center where softer and sharper tones blend and compete, illustrating the push-and-pull of protection versus exposure throughout the abstract formation.
