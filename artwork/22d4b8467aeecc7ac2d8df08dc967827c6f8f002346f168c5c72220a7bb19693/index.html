Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The warm shades symbolize vulnerability while the darker, cooler tones represent strength, creating a dynamic dialogue through color contrast. The dense center signifies strength with a 'target' pattern interspersed with less saturated hues embodying vulnerability, showcasing a protective core that battles against the softness of the surrounding area. The center pulse layout creates focus, making the interplay between safety and exposure palpable and impactful.
