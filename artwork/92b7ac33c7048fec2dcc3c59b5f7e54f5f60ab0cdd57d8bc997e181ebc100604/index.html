Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between joy and grief through a contrasting color palette of vibrant yellows and muted blues, representing light and heaviness. The arrangement suggests a flow where joy bursts upwards, a metaphor for lightness, while grief stays anchored below, creating a sense of collision as bright colors counter the somber depths. The absence of outlines and broken pixels enhances the feelings of imperfection and exposure, embodying the balance between hidden emotions and their expression. The layout is dictated by falling pixels, resonating with the descending weight of grief amidst flickering joys.
