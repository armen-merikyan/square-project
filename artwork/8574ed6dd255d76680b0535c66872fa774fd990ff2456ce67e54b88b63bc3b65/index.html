Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the conflict between fear and hope, using a palette of deep blacks and violets against softer golds and greens. The contrast between jagged edges (fear) and soft centers (hope) creates a visual disruption that mirrors the inner turmoil of the emotions. The layout mimics a mirror broken by a few deliberate misalignments, embodying the 'inside vs outside' concept where hope emerges amidst a chaotic background. The upward flow of the composition suggests rising signals of hope, while the darker tones represent fears, interspersed to create a sense of both pressure and release.
