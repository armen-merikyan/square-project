Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between relief and dread through a contrasting palette of soft sepia tones against harsh dark shades. The arrangement emphasizes a descending pattern that captures a sense of movement and emotional gravity; bright shades represent moments of relief amidst darker hues representing dread. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, reinforcing the theme of instability while allowing negative space for reflection.
