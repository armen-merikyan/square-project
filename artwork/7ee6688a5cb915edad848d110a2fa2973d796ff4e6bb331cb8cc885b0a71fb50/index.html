Artist Statement

The emotional tone pivots between constraining control and liberating surrender. The selected palette of muted cream, red oxide, and blackened plum reflects this tension, providing warmth against the starkness of iron gray and hints of small orange as accents. The main collision occurs at the center with more pressure-building colors, suggesting overflow, while the softer edges hint at release and openness. The layout pattern mimics stacked bands, where each row symbolizes different emotional strata, echoing the overlapping themes of stability and flux.
