Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a tension between quietude and pressure, symbolizing the struggle between calmness and chaos. The palette of off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach illustrates soft versus hard contrasts, emphasizing the pressurized density with the muted fields representing quiet. The main collision occurs diagonally, where sharper, darker colors crash against the softer, lighter shades, suggesting an argument of emotions parallel to the idea of breaking against a tide, with broken pixels adding imperfection to reinforce the shattering feeling against tranquility.
