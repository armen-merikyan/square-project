Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between restlessness and peace through a contrast of jagged, energetic accents against serene, low-pulse backgrounds. The use of navy and dim cyan suggests quiet depth while black provides an unsettling yet grounding element, fostering a duality of experience. The main collision occurs diagonally where scar-like marks disrupt a grid structure, symbolizing a clash between stability and chaos. The edge style softens with a shadowed border, indicating a fading boundary between these contrasting emotional states.
