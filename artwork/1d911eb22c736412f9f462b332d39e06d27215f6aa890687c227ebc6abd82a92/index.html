Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dance between defiance and submission through its stark color contrasts and asymmetric placement. The palette consists of warm coral and soft amber that signal defiance, contrasting sharply with the cool slate and blue-black for submission. The patterns of stripes and mirroring create a visual rhythm that clashes in the corners, emphasizing pressure while maintaining negative space to reflect moments of quietude. The main collision occurs at the top-left, where dense warm tones confront the cooler, more subdued colors, embodying the battle between chaos and order.
