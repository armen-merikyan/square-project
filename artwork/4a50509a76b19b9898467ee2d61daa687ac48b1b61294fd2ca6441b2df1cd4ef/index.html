Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional dichotomy between longing and resolve. It employs a muted palette of sepia, tan, and faded blue to evoke nostalgia and warmth, juxtaposed against denser gray areas portraying uncertainty. The collision occurs centrally, with semi-structured blocks of color locking together, highlighting the tension in the theme of order versus chaos. The 'spiral_pressure' layout drives this tension, as paths converge towards a quiet center, echoing feelings of intimacy and distance. Broken pixels provide intentional disruptions, making the artwork feel both fragile and resilient.
