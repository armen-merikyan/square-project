Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between defiance and submission through a palette of vibrant and muted tones. The bright reds symbolize defiance, creating sharp contrast against the soft grays representing submission. The pattern collided between a checkerboard of defiant elements and pressure bands of softer hues, suggesting a dynamic and unresolved emotional state. The central region is darker, hinting at turmoil, while lighter edges embody calm and submission, respectively portraying the complexity of conflict and resolution.
