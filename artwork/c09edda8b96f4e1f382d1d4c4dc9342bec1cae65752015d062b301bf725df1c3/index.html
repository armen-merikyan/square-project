Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve, represented by the interplay of deep blacks and shimmering soft golds. The palette, inspired by the fears and hopes, creates an underlying pulse of urgency against serene depths. The main collision occurs towards the center where dark violet encroaches on lighter shades, illustrating the push and pull of desire and determination. The broken pixels introduce an imperfection that narrates the story of repair amidst damage, reinforcing the abstract nature of feeling without literal imagery.
