Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional struggle between defiance and submission, using a palette of warm and cool tones to signify conflict. The bright yellow and orange colors indicate defiance, while muted purple and deep blue reflect submission. The arrangement creates a diagonal rise, suggesting hope and tension, with the central collision representing strong defiance surrounded by softer submission. Broken pixels disrupt the static flow, symbolizing the challenges faced at thresholds in life.
