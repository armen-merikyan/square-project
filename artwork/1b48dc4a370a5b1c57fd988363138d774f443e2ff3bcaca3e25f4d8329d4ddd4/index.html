Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension of defiance and submission through a contrasting palette and placement. The colors alternate between warm coral and cold slate to illustrate the struggle between warmth of defiance and the coolness of submission. The main pulse of color rises diagonally from the bottom left to the top right, with jagged interruptions reflecting the concepts of order versus chaos. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, mirroring the tension inherent in this emotional conflict. The balance of colors suggests both the protection of a center and the dissolution of order at the edges.
