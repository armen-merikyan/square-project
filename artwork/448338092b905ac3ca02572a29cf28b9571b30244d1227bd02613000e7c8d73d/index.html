Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between certainty and curiosity through a contrasting palette of muted plum and soft cyan, balanced against the tranquility of moon white. The colors represent order (muted plum) and chaos (soft cyan), both colliding in the center where they dance like waves. The negative space emphasizes the rising signal, inviting exploration, while broken pixels add a sense of imperfection. The overall composition pulses gently, hinting at rising and receding tides, allowing each color to reflect the emotional interplay between stability and wonder.
