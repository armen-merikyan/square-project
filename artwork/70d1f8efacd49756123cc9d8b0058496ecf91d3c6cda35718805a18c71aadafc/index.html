Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional collision between longing and resolve. The palette, 'lonely_signal,' utilizes deep navy and dim cyan to create a serene but urgent atmosphere. The main contrast occurs at the edges, where fragmented shapes of black interplay with dispersed white dots, representing broken mirrors and signals. This interplay of colors conveys a sense of yearning against the backdrop of established boundaries, echoing the seed's themes of decay and reaffirmation without depicting them literally. The central area simulates a ladder, denoting progress through tension, with interrupted flows emphasizing both repair and damage, while maintaining negative space to suggest unresolved emotions.
