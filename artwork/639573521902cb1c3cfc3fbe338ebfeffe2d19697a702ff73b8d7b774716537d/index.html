Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, employing a palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green to signify hope amidst chaos. The intentional imbalance in color distribution creates a contrast that feels both unresolved and compelling, echoing the seed's themes of symmetry versus break. The presence of broken pixels lends a sense of imperfection that enhances the narrative of disarray within structure. The overarching visual flow climbs upwards, suggesting a pulse that oscillates between stability and disorientation.
