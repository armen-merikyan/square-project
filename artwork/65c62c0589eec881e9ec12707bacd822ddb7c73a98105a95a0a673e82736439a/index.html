Artist Statement

This composition captures an emotional tone of duality, reflecting both vulnerability and strength through color placement. The palette features 'pale mint' and 'soft sky' representing exposed, vulnerable spaces with 'storm gray' and 'near black' embodying strength and protection. The main contrast occurs at the center with a bright area surrounded by darker tones, symbolizing the collision of feelings. The arrangement of strong blocks and thin lines resonates with the emotional depth of the seed, creating a visual dialogue around exposure and shelter.
