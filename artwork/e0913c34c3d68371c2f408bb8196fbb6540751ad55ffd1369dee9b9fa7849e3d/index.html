Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The dark navy and black colors represent confusion, creating a heavy, unyielding outer border. In contrast, the central area employs a lighter palette of dim cyan with scribbles of white, symbolizing clarity that breaks through the darkness. The use of sharp edges and a 'frame and core' layout emphasizes the collision between these contrasting emotions, while the broken pixel concept introduces an inherent imperfection. This arrangement suggests a tumultuous journey from chaos in the outer layers to a clearer center, echoing the seed's themes of falling pixels and interrupted symmetry.
