Artist Statement

This 8x8 pixel art composition explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, utilizing a palette dominated by static gray and electric blue to embody the contrasting forces. The top half represents strength with dense, stable colors forming an anchored base, while the bottom half illustrates vulnerability through fragmented, softer shades. The collision occurs prominently in the center where patterns from both sides intersect, creating a visual harmony of tension. The intention was to evoke a sense of dynamic interplay between exposure and shelter, seen in the broken pixel technique that introduces intentional imperfections, reflecting life's inherent unpredictability.
