Artist Statement

This piece explores the interplay between fear and hope through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of dark violet, soft gold, pale green, and black reflects the weighty emotional context: dark corners symbolizing fear and a soft glow at the center representing hope. The arrangement utilizes a split vertical layout, where the left side features deeper, darker colors while the right blooms with lighter, more vibrant hues, creating tension and relief. Jagged edges along the borders evoke unease while a central gold pixel acts as a beacon of hope amidst the chaos, illustrating a burst of resilience.
