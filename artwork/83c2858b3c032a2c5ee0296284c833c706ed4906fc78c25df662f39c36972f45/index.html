Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between relief and dread, using a palette that contrasts warm yellows and oranges with deep blues and muted purples. The pattern suggests a conflict, where light (representing relief) struggles against heavy dark masses (dread). The arrangement incorporates broken pixels, signifying imperfection, and utilizes a mirror-broken layout creating a deliberate asymmetry. The echo of colors across the canvas highlights both collision and interplay between contrasting emotions, culminating in a composition that feels both balanced and disrupted.
