Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of longing and resolve, where soft, muted colors transition into more defined, firm edges, suggesting a struggle between containment and overflow. The palette, derived from 'intimacy_distance', juxtaposes warm muted tones with cooler, clearer colors, amplifying the contrast. The collision happens in the center, where pressure bands merge into noise patterns, creating a visual 'threshold' of emotion, signifying the tension between vulnerability and strength. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections that speak to repair amidst damage.
