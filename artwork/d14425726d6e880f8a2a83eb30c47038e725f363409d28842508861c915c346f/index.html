Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes relief and dread, revealing a struggle between light and dark, hope and despair. The palette, drawn from yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple, conveys warmth against encroaching shadows. The composition climaxes at the center where a bright yellow block—a symbol of relief—interrupts the cooler depths of muted purple and deep blue, embodying both tension and a sense of escape. The pixel layout ascends, invoking the visual of climbing, creating a rhythm of pressure as the darkness surrounds the brightness.
