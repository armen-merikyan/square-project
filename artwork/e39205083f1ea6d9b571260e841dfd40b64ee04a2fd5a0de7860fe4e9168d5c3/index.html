Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between loneliness and connection, evoked through a tension-filled grid. The palette of sepia and faded blue reflects nostalgia, contrasting warmth against cool tones to symbolize connection amid isolation. The main contrast emerges at the edges, where deeper colors cluster, suggesting pressure, while lighter spaces hint at exposure and release. The seed's ideas of 'inside_vs_outside' drive the structured patterns, creating a rhythm that resonates with the struggle between seeking connection and the feeling of being hidden away.
