Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength through dark and light contrasts. The palette features deep blacks and steel grays alongside softer pinks and whites to create an emotional depth contrasting the dense strength with a delicate vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center where dark colors interrupt lighter ones, symbolizing the fragile core surrounded by an overwhelming mass. Broken pixels are scattered to represent imperfections and scars that enrich the emotional narrative, echoing the interconnectedness of strength and vulnerability.
