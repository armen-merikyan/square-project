Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between clarity and confusion through juxtaposed color placements. The chosen palette, drawn from the 'lonely_signal', employs blacks and dark blues to represent confusion, contrasted with hints of dim cyan, which symbolizes moments of clarity. The negative space is carefully maintained to allow the viewer to absorb the chaotic interactions without overwhelm. The pattern reflects a break in symmetry with asymmetrical placements and 'broken pixels' that boldly interrupt the composed flow, echoing the theme of conflicting emotions inherent in the seed text.
