Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a dance between moments of quiet reflection and intense pressure, embodying the collisions inherent in human emotion. The palette juxtaposes deep navy (#1A1A4B) against bright bursts of orange (#FF7F00), illustrating tension. Darker tones crowd the corners, suggesting weight and pressure, while lighter colors in the center release into calmness, creating a dynamic emotional rhythm that reflects the seed's essence.
