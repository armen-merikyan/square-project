Artist Statement

This piece navigates the emotional tension between defiance and submission, embodied through a palette of cosmic indigo, void black, and cold gold that evokes feelings of awe and terror. The sharp diagonal pulse marks interject amid a dense black background, creating moments of interruption that symbolize conflict. The broken frame layout allows emotion to escape through the gaps, representing the struggle between order and chaos. The use of broken pixels alongside solid blocks reflects uneven strength, reinforcing the ongoing push and pull between the opposing emotions. As such, the arrangement communicates a powerful narrative without literal representation.
