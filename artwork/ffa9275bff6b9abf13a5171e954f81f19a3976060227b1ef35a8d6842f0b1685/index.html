Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the struggle between restlessness and peace, manifested through a palette of warm and cool colors. The deep blue signifies tranquility, while the vibrant yellows and oranges create a sense of energy and motion, representing tension. The arrangement forms a central collision of bright colors against a darker background, emphasizing that chaotic interaction. The 'ember' shape seed informs the warm accents that clash with the cooler patches, suggesting an ongoing dialogue between heat and calm, where patterns of fault lines interrupt the peaceful checker layout, projecting a narrative of conflict and resolution.
