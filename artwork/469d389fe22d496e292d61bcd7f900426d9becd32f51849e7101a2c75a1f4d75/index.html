Artist Statement

The piece contrasts nostalgia and progress through a heavyweight black border representing nostalgia, filled with a variety of muted grays and blacks to evoke memory and reflection. The vibrant white and dim cyan drip pattern in the center symbolizes progress—transitioning upward, bright colors representing hope and forward motion against the darker perimeter. The split between light and dark creates a sharp contrast that suggests both a calming and jarring duality. The arrangement follows a bottom-weighted spiral, illustrating the push and pull between the two emotional states, creating a sense of tension yet balance.
