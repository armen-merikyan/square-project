Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork captures the struggle between light and dark, evoking nostalgia and progress through contrasting vivid and muted hues. The palette of warm yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples illustrates emotional collisions, symbolizing joy and grief. The primary contrast occurs in the diagonal from the top left to the bottom right, where lighter shades disrupt darker ones, creating visual tension. The layout suggests a corner invasion, enhancing the sense of encroaching emotion, while broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection and variance, mirroring the complexity of feelings. Overall, the interplay of colors and patterns generates a rhythm of emotional pressure and eventual release.
