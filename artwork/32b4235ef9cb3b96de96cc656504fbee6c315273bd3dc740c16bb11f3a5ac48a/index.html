Artist Statement

The emotional tone relies on the juxtaposition of trust and suspicion. The chosen palette of muted plum, rain blue, and soft cyan creates a sense of melancholy and wonder, reflecting tension. The contrast has a sharp division, where trust (represented by softer shades) occupies the upper left corner and suspicion (represented by darker and more fragmented pixels) dominates the lower right. The interaction mimics the idea of a pressure corner with the visual conflict leading to a diagonal fall, where one color interrupts another, embodying the concept of resilience against fragility. The arrangement captures the essence of uncertainty and dynamic emotional shifts as colors collide and blend.
