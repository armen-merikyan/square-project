Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress, featuring a split horizontal layout that signifies the duality of 'rise vs fall.' The upward movement from bottom to top symbolizes hope and progress, while the controlled pattern evokes a sense of nostalgia through its mirroring effect. The dark, muted palette, with its contrasting soft gold and pale green accents, reflects the complexity of these emotions, creating a visual collision between density and quiet moments. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, emphasizing the natural chaos in progress while maintaining a delicate balance with negative space, yielding an overall sense of unresolved motion.
