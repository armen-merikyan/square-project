Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between restlessness and peace through a carefully chosen palette and pixel arrangement. The use of deep blacks and icy blues conveys a sense of weighty calm, while sharp accents of orange and gray illuminate the edges, representing ruptures in stillness. The central collision takes place diagonally, where peace meets agitation — a fault line exhibiting the struggle within, symbolizing memory against motion. The broken pixels intersperse throughout to signify moments of disruption and echo, as if capturing fleeting thoughts amidst tranquility.
