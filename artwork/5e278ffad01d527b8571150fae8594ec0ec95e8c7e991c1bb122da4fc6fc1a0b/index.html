Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece embodies the struggle between tenderness and rage. The palette, primarily featuring deep reds and blacks, represents the intensity of rage, while the soft pale blue and white introduce a sense of calm tenderness. The pattern is organized as a chaotic fight over an 8x8 grid, where the jaggedness of sharp edges (representing rage) interrupts the gentler, softly repeating motifs of tenderness. The main collision occurs centrally, with dense red pixels flooding into the quieter pale blue, highlighting the conflict. This arrangement encapsulates the tension between an overwhelming emotional outburst and the need for serenity, turning the seed’s abstract qualities into a visual narrative.
