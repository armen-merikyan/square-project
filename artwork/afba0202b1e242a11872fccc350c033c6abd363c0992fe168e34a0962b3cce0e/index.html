Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between confusion and clarity. The palette combines warm oranges and yellows with deep blues and muted purples to create a conversation between warm luminosity and cool depth. This contrast speaks to the chaos of 'confusion' while 'clarity' emerges in the cleaner sections of the grid. The broken pixels introduce elements of unpredictability and disruption, mirroring the emotional push and pull as colors collide and intermingle, creating a visual rhythm that echoes the 'pulse' concept.
