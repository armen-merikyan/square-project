Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodied in this artwork reflects the push and pull between longing and resolve. The palette of moss green and rust symbolizes both resilience and fragility, capturing the essence of the emotional seed. The main collision occurs in the center, where intense patterns of longing meet the calm walls of resolve, creating a tension that is visually palpable. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, allowing for narratives of repair against the backdrop of damage, suggesting an ongoing dance between hope and despair.
