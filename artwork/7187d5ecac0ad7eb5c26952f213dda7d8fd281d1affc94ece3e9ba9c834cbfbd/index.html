Artist Statement

This piece juxtaposes the emotional tones of confusion and clarity using a palette of black, pale blue, red, and white. The contrast lies heavily in the interaction between saturated colors and muted spaces, echoing a narrative of tension and resolution. The layout of falling pixels introduces a dynamic flow, which symbolizes the descent of clarity against the backdrop of chaos, evoking a sense of movement and interruption. This creates a visual resonance that speaks to the push and pull of emotions, thereby embodying the essence of the seed through abstract expression.
