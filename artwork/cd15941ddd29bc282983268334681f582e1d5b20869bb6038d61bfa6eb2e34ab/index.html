Artist Statement

The emotional tone of restlessness versus peace creates a dynamic tension portrayed through a contrasting palette. The selection of colors, including soft powder pink and stark carbon black, reflects the push-and-pull between calm and agitation. The diagonal arrangement suggests movement, with radial patterns representing restlessness surrounded by soft channels symbolizing peace. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, mirroring unresolved tension, making the piece feel alive and in motion while maintaining areas of quiet. The primary collision occurs toward the center, where dynamic colors interact with calm tones, capturing the essence of the seed.
