Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission using a palette of electric blues, magentas, and contrasting blacks and limes. The structure evokes a sense of a protected center clashing against an outer chaos, where the main contrast occurs in the middle rows. The presence of broken pixels symbolizes imperfections and disruptions, creating an abstract story of struggle and resilience, guided by the 'quiet_channel' layout that allows for open spaces amid dense patterns.
