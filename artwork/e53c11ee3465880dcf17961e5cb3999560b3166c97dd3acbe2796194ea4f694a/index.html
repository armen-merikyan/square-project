Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the tension between 'spark' and 'ash', embodying both resilience and fragility. The palette mixes warm and cool tones, with bright accents symbolizing bursts of hope (spark) against muted fields representing calm decay (ash). The main collision occurs diagonally, where warm tones meet cooler shades, illustrating the struggle between these opposing forces. This layout, influenced by the 'open_gate' concept, signifies an emotional barrier being breached, highlighting areas of intensity and calm.
