Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of vulnerability and strength. The palette is based on the 'broken_memory' theme, utilizing sepia and faded blue to evoke both warmth and coolness. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense dot patterns of strength in darker shades confront the lighter stepwell patterns of vulnerability, creating a visual dialogue of tension. The stark contrast between dark and light emphasizes the struggle between shelter and exposure, while the diagonal layout represents a dynamic interaction, allowing each emotion to press against the other in a dance of resilience and fragility.
