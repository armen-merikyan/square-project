Artist Statement

This composition captures a profound emotional dichotomy, embodying the tension between trust and suspicion. The palette, drawn from 'melancholy_wonder,' employs cool blues and soft grays to evoke a sense of calm but unsettled introspection. The left side resonates with trust, using shatter patterns that create fractures in soft hues, while the right embodies suspicion, encapsulated in a split-screen approach with darker, more impenetrable tones. The main contrast emerges at the vertical center, where light and shadow clash, suggesting a threshold where resilience and fragility collide. This abstract arrangement reflects the complex emotions of conflict: tension, isolation, and the yearning for connection amidst doubt.
