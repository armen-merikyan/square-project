Artist Statement

The emotional tone examines the tension between longing and resolve, represented through a palette of serene aqua and urgent red, reflecting both calm and pressure. The contrasting patterns of 'shatter' and 'broken_dots' illustrate echoes of memory and the persistent pulse of urgency. The collision occurs centrally, with dense sections of red surrounded by the quieter aqua field, symbolizing a threshold between turmoil and resolution. The pixel imperfections act as intentional disruptions, enhancing the sense of emotional scars and repairs.
