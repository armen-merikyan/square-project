Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between restlessness and peace. Utilizing a palette of muted and warm colors, the visual narrative unfolds through a blend of calm fields and jagged accents that symbolize movement and interruption. The main collision occurs diagonally across the center, where a structured pattern meets chaotic elements, fostering a sense of unease against tranquil backgrounds. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, adding to the theme of memory versus motion, suggesting memories that are at once soothing yet restless.
