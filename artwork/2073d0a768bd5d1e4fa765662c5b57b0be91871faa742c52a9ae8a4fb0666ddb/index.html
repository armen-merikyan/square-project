Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional contrast between relief and dread, where dense clusters of darker tones signify dread, while lighter areas symbolize relief. The spiral layout creates a dynamic flow from the pressure of chaos, represented by harsh blacks and grays, to softer hues of mint and gold, suggesting the easing of tension. This visual tension resolves into negative space, evoking a feeling of liberation amidst chaos.
