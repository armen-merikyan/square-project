Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece addresses the tension between loneliness and the quest for connection. The selected palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue reflects a sense of nostalgia and ancient warmth. The main contrast arises from the vibrant bursts of color that represent connection amidst wider, muted areas that evoke solitude, creating a sense of dialogue between spaciousness and summoning warmth. This blend of emotional states manifests through checker patterns for connection, while areas of negative space suggest isolation, thus shaping the abstract arrangement into a narrative of seeking warmth in solitude.
