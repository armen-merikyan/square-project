Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork explores the tension between longing and resolve, manifesting as a dynamic interplay of colors and forms. The selected palette 'joy_grief'—a blend of deep blue, muted purple, yellow, and orange—embodies a comforting warmth contrasted with the cool solitude of longing. The structure reflects a near symmetry disrupted by intentional broken pixels, mirroring the emotional landscape of broken resolution. The collision of stacked and stair-like patterns symbolizes the push-pull of urgency versus serenity, with the main contrast centered around the fourth column where brighter yellows meet deeper blues, creating a vivid focal point that captures the viewer's gaze.
