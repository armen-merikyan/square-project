Artist Statement

The artwork captures the tension between vulnerability and strength, using an intimacy_distance color palette. Darker tones represent the weight of vulnerability, while lighter colors symbolize strength. The central area is dense with dark colors, creating a strong focal point, while the lighter edges and occasional broken pixels signify exposure and fragility, creating a visual echo of the contrast between the two emotions. The spiral clockwise flow enhances the feeling of continuous conflict and resolution.
