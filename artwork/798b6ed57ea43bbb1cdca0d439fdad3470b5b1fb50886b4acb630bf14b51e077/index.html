Artist Statement

This composition embodies the friction between softness and severity. The palette combines cool carbon black and steel gray, representing severity, contrasted with powder pink and warm white, symbolizing softness. The central area is lighter, creating a sense of exposure, while darker shades surround to illustrate boundary and density. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfection, echoing the conflicted emotions surrounding guilt and forgiveness. The spiraling marks add a dynamic rhythm, suggesting a flow that pulses outward, while the overall layout maintains clarity amidst emotional chaos.
