Artist Statement

This abstract artwork captures the tension between pressure and quiet through a sharp contrast of density and softness. The palette merges muted colors like off-white and dust gray with the bold compressed red and dark navy to signify a struggle, evoking both a crowded path and a soft horizon. The main collision occurs at the center where dense, saturated colors clash with the lighter edges, suggesting a battle between chaos and calm. The arrangement of pixels embodies the conflicting emotional tones from the seed, emphasizing irregular patterns to represent disruption and the interplay between hard edges and softer spaces.
