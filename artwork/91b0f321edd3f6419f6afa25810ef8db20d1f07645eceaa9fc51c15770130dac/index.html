Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional dance between confusion and clarity through a duality of patterns reminiscent of 'stairs' and 'mirrors'. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of nostalgic distortion, enhancing the chaotic collision found in the center. Rows showcase a stark contrast between dense color clumps and negative space, representing moments of clarity amidst confusion, highlighted by broken pixels and the thick outlines that suggest a shifting balance. The pattern pulses like a heartbeat, with color interruptions that embody the emotional weight of the seed's themes.
