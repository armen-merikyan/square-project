Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between trust and suspicion, utilizing a palette of warm and muted colors. The contrast between vibrant yellows and deep blues reflects the balance of warmth (trust) against shadows (suspicion). Broken pixels suggest imperfection and disruption, while the layout creates a visual pulse that plays between negative space and dense color clusters, embodying the struggle between these two emotions. The main collision occurs diagonally, enhancing the feeling of movement between the opposing forces.
