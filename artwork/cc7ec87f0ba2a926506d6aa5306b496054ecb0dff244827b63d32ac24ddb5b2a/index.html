Artist Statement

This composition explores the dichotomy of softness and severity through a balance of muted and bold colors. The palette features gentle pastels alongside harsh blacks and reds, emphasizing the emotional tensions of guilt and forgiveness. The layout is structured as stacked bands, suggesting a layering of feelings—where severity punctuates softer tones. The quiet spaces contrasted with densely packed colors represent the push and pull of emotional weight, while the intentional broken pixels disrupt the flow, embodying imperfections in the emotional landscape. The spiral flow adds a sense of movement that softly spirals into the center where the main collision occurs, rendering the juxtaposition between emotional states palpable.
