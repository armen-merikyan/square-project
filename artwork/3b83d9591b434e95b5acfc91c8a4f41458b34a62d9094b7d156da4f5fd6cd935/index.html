Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends restlessness and peace, captured through a palette of muted and warm tones that contrast with brighter accents, evoking a sense of tension and calm simultaneously. The use of sepia and dust gray provides a nostalgic foundation, while splashes of faded blue introduce a sense of motion against a static backdrop. The main collision occurs diagonally, representing the tension between motion and memory, mimicking the emotional struggle of finding peace amidst chaos. The broken pixels emphasize imperfections, adding depth and narrative to the abstract story.
