Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between longing and resolve, where longing creates a feeling of distance and yearning with its 'stepwell' pattern, while resolve manifests a structured presence through the 'glitch' pattern. The palette of soft pinks and stark blacks emphasizes the collision of warmth against severity. The main contrast occurs along the diagonal, where the warmer colors gradually meet the cooler tones, creating a pulse-like rhythm. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, echoing the theme of 'repair vs damage' as they disrupt but also highlight the surrounding structure.
