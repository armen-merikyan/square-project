Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between trust and suspicion. The chosen palette reflects a melancholy wonder, with shades that evoke feelings of both warmth (from soft cyan and moon white) and coolness (from lavender gray and muted plum). By creating a split vertical flow with a diagonal fall, the pixel placement emphasizes the collision between the two emotions: the overwhelming presence of trust suffocating the edges with darker suspicion. The interrupted patterns illustrate wary trust contrasted by sharp energies of suspicion. Focused clusters in the center suggest resilience amid the daunting presence of doubts, capturing the essence of conflict as a visual narrative.
