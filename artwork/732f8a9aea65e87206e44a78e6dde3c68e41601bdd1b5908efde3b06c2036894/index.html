Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork explores the tension between confusion and clarity. Utilizing a palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green, the composition creates a profound contrast that reflects complexity against simplicity. The main collision occurs in the top half, where the intensity of dark colors begins to recede into lighter tones, mirroring the struggle for clarity. The 'mirror_broken' layout emphasizes near symmetry with deliberate distortions in color placement to evoke feelings of frustration and calm. This interplay of colors suggests layers of emotion—each pixel falling like soundwaves, an echo of unresolved tension.
