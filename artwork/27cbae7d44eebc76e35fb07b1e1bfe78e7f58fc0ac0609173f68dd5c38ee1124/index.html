Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional dichotomy of joy and grief through a color palette that balances bright, warm hues against deep, muted tones. The contrast lies predominantly in the center, where joyful yellow and orange pulsate amidst surrounding waves of deep blue and muted purple, mimicking the push and pull of complex emotions. The deliberate asymmetry and disrupted patterns suggest movement and unrest, echoing the inner battle represented by the themes of betrayal and tenderness.
