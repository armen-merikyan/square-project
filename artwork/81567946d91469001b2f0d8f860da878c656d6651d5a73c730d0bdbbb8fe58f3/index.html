Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between certainty as a firm structure (represented by darker colors) and curiosity as exploratory energy (shown through brighter accents). The palette features deep hues contrasted with vibrant highlights to create a sense of pushing against boundaries. The main collision occurs at the center, where the patterns of certainty and curiosity meet, creating a visual dialogue of tension and exploration, reflecting the emotional dimensions captured in the seed.
