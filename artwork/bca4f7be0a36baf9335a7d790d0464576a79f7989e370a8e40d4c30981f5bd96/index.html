Artist Statement

The emotional tone here reflects the struggle between clarity and confusion, represented by the split in color density and placement. The chosen palette of muted sepia tones and faded blues suggests nostalgic fragility, while the sharp contrast of dark edges against softer centers illustrates the tension between structure and chaos. This pattern showcases intersections of feeling through chaotic overlaps, with deliberate broken pixels enhancing the sense of imperfection. The layout allows negative space to emphasize both clarity and confusion, promoting an emotional ebb and flow.
