Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between restlessness and peace, with a dominant dark palette representing restlessness. The structured grid embodies memory and motion, while iridescent patches of light symbolize fleeting moments of peace. The collision happens primarily in the center where the chaotic grid intersects with serene patches—creating an echo of past emotions. The uneven spaces reflect a narrative of interruption and depth, inviting contemplation amidst the chaos.
