Artist Statement

This piece explores the dynamic between longing and resolve, utilizing a warm palette contrasting with cooler tones to symbolize emotional tension. The layout features intersecting patterns that echo the concepts of 'repair' and 'damage', with thin edges outlining the shapes to express clarity amidst chaos. The central collision occurs in the middle of the square, where rich, deeper colors interrupt softer, muted tones, reflecting the push-pull of desire and fulfillment. Broken pixels are strategically placed to enhance the feeling of imperfection and urgency, embodying the seed's thematic essence.
