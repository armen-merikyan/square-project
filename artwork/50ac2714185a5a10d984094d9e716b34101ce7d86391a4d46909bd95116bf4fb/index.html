Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic interplay between vulnerability and strength. The palette features dusky reds and grays, embodying both the warmth of strength and the coldness of vulnerability. The contrast primarily occurs at the center, where a dense pool of darker shades converges with lighter hues, symbolizing the exposed essence amidst a protective frame. The 'quiet channel' layout allows for structural tension, creating a pathway that reveals the deep interplay of opposing feelings, resembling a mirror fracturing and revealing hidden layers.
