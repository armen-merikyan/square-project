Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between safety and exposure through a dense center representing protection and lighter colors at the borders evoking exposure. The palette contrasts the warmth of 'shelter_green' and 'warm_brown' with the cooler tones of 'cold_white' and 'pale_skin', creating a dialogue between comfort and vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center, where the colors intertwine, suggesting a complex balance of emotions. The echoes of exposure ripple outward, forming a stark yet delicate frame of negative space that invites the viewer to reflect on the interplay of protection and openness.
