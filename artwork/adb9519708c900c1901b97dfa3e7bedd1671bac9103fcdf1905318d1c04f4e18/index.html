Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between certainty and curiosity, represented by a palette of navy and dim cyan for solidity against floating instances of black and white to symbolize intangible thoughts. The collision occurs at the center, where structured steps meet chaotic dots, symbolizing clashing emotions. The seed informs the layout of grounded patterns emerging from a chaotic void, emphasizing a feeling of tension and unresolved motion.
