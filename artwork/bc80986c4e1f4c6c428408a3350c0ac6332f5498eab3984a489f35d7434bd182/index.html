Artist Statement

The emotional tone is defined by the collision of contained pressure and soft yielding. The palette features mourning black and deep blue for grounding, while pale yellow introduces an element of tension. The main contrast occurs at the centermost point, where dense hues clash with more open spaces, suggesting a pulsating signal amidst noise. The 'hole' shape seed informs the fragmentation, resulting in broken pixels symbolizing intervention of control over the feeling of surrender. This pattern captures the narrative of trying to maintain clarity while being overwhelmed.
