Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between softness and severity, using a palette that juxtaposes powder pink and steel gray for a dialogue between light and dark. The center features a collision of softer hues suggesting guilt and forgiveness, contrasted against strong anchors of sharp crimson and deep carbon black. The arrangement reflects a spiral motion, hinting at nuance and infinite depth, creating a visual rhythm where the warmth of softness interrupts the rigor of severity. This dynamic interplay mirrors the emotional duality from the seed, conveying an abstract narrative of conflict and resolution.
