Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional dichotomy of 'desire' and 'restraint'. The warm colors (hot coral, blood orange) represent the pulsing energy of desire, contrasted against muted tones (charcoal, hollow blue) that symbolize restraint. The contrasting colors collide in the center, creating a vibrant tension where warm echoes meet cool boundaries, illustrating an internal struggle. The spiral layout, governed by dense clusters and subtle curves, enhances the sense of pressure. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, symbolizing the unpredictable nature of this emotional interplay. The layout leaves areas of negative space, ensuring clarity amidst the chaos of emotional conflict.
