Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette blends warm browns and shelter greens, suggesting resilience, while pale skin and cold whites introduce elements of fragility. The main collision occurs at the center, where the split-screen pattern meets the checker arrangement, representing strength's confrontations with vulnerability. The layout favors dimness yet allows a bright cluster at the border, emphasizing the last light of hope breaking through a pressure-filled space. The deliberate inclusion of broken pixels creates intentional disruptions within this narrative of conflict and balance.
