Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a deep sense of loneliness contrasted with fleeting connection. The palette of navy and black evokes feelings of solitude and depth, while hints of dim cyan introduce moments of potential connection. The top-weighted layout conveys an imbalance where loneliness dominates, while the rows are split to signify the struggle between the two emotional states. The crosshatch pattern builds a barrier of complexity overlaid with bursts of connections, represented by lighter shades, creating a rhythm of hope against despair.
