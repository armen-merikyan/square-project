Artist Statement

This composition embodies a tension between control and panic, illustrated through contrasting warm and cool tones. The chosen palette of yellows and deep blues reflects joy and grief, creating an emotional push and pull. The placement of muted purples introduces breaks and disruptions to an otherwise orderly zigzag pattern, suggesting chaos within control. The broken pixel elements create unexpected moments of imperfection, enhancing the underlying theme of symmetry versus fracture. Overall, the artwork evokes a feeling of unease and complexity, mirroring the conflict described in the seed.
