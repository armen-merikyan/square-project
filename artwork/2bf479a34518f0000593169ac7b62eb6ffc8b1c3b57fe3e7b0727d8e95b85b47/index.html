Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay between the vibrancy of 'spark' and the subtlety of 'ash.' The palette features off-white and faint peach to signify quiet spaces, contrasted by the compressed red and dark navy reflecting deeper emotions. The collision takes place diagonally where 'spark' (compressed red) meets 'ash' (dust gray) creating an interruption in the flow. This arrangement utilizes a feeling of imbalance yet captivating beauty, while avoiding any realistic representation, instead showcasing the effects of the themes through color placement and intensity.
