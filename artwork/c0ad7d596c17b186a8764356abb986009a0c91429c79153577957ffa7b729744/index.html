Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between love and betrayal through a split-pattern that symbolizes conflict and resolution. The warm palette of pinks and reds embodies love, while the gray and dark maroon introduce feelings of betrayal and chaos. The layout reflects a rising signal, suggesting hope amidst turmoil, while the jagged edges evoke brokenness. The main collision occurs in the center, where light and dark colors intersect, amplifying the emotional dichotomy. This abstract arrangement, with its mix of density and negative space, invites viewers to contemplate the fragility of relationships and the duality of emotions.
