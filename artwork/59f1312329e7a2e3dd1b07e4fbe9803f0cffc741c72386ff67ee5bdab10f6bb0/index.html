Artist Statement

This composition navigates the emotional tension between softness and severity. The chosen palette of powder pink, warm white, carbon black, steel gray, and sharp crimson creates a juxtaposition, highlighting warmth amidst the cold pressure of black. The center showcases a persistent pulse (sharp crimson), surrounded by a scar pattern (severity) that interrupts the flow of soft dotted textures (softness). This creates deliberate collisions in density, echoing the battle between structure and chaos, while maintaining a light vs dark contrast that harmonizes in negative space.
