Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between restlessness and peace, illustrated through a palette of red, pink, dark maroon, and gray. The main diagonal flow creates a dynamic tension across the square, with vibrant colors invading the peaceful areas, echoing a conflict between motion and stillness. The contrasting colors represent the split-screen of emotions—the bright accents symbolize restlessness while the muted backgrounds invoke peace. Broken pixels add imperfections, enhancing the feeling of disruption within the overarching calmness, producing an evocative abstract narrative.
