Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between joy and grief, using warm oranges and bright yellows to evoke joy, contrasted against deep blues and muted purples that represent grief. The palette creates a visual metaphor of conflict and harmony, reflecting the human experience of joy and sorrow. The placement of brighter colors in a radial pattern on the left suggests an explosion of joy, while the right side, with its stripes of deeper colors, bends to a heaviness that represents grief. This juxtaposition creates a rich visual narrative of emotion. The arrangement follows a vertical split, embodying the push and pull between two contrasting states, yet the broken pixels add imperfection to the overall harmony.
