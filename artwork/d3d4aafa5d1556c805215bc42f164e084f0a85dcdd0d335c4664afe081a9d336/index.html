Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dynamic between defiance and submission through a palette that embodies both calm and tension. The contrasting colors, where deep grays symbolize defiance and soft pastels represent submission, create an uneasy balance. The halo layout evokes a void, emphasizing the central clash of emotions. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, reinforcing the chaotic interplay; the center suggests strength while the edges depict vulnerability, forming a complex rhythm of conflict.
