Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between desire and restraint. The palette combines saturated reds and muted creams, embodying lust and muted control. Saturated colors dominate the corners, creating explosive contrasts, while the inner grid remains restrained. The central pattern of pulse marks clashes with a structured crosshatch, symbolizing how desire strives against the boundaries of restraint. The spiral flow introduces a dynamic rhythm, echoing the push-pull of emotions, with broken pixels signifying disruption and longing for release.
