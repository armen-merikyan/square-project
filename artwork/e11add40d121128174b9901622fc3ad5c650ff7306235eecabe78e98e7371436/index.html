Artist Statement

This composition interprets the emotional dichotomy of restlessness and peace through a chaotic clash of colors. Using shades from the 'gratitude_envy' palette, warm tones like honey gold and acid green represent restlessness, while pale cream illustrates peace. The workflow suggests a spiral pressure where colors spiral around an off-center point, reflecting disrupted calm. The uneven borders and intentional broken pixels create a sense of imperfection, enhancing the theme of balance amidst chaos. The collision of bright accents against muted backgrounds creates an engaging visual narrative, embodying the tension highlighted in the seed.
