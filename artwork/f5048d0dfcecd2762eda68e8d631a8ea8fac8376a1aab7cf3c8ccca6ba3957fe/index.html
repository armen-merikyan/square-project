Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional duality between restlessness and peace through the dynamics of color placement. The selected palette of soft blues and muted purples reflects a melancholic wonder, with tranquil hues dominating the layout. The peaceful crosshatch pattern suggests serenity, while the interrupted barcode elements introduce a sense of fragmentation and tension, echoing the restlessness inherent in the seed. The central collision at the grid's heart embodies a struggle between calm and chaos, evoking a narrative of unresolved emotions.
