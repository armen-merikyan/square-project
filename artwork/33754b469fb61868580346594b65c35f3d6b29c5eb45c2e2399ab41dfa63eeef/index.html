Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the tension between love and betrayal, utilizing contrasts in warm and cool tones to evoke a sense of conflict. The palette is anchored in red and grey, symbolizing passion interwoven with uncertainty, while the layout mimics a fractured mirror, highlighting imperfections and dissonance. Color collisions arise where warm pinks interrupt the cooler greys, creating a push and pull effect that represents both connection and fracture. This composition embodies the internal struggle described in the seed, ultimately forming an abstract pattern that resonates with themes of emotional complexity.
