Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between calmness and rage, represented by the juxtaposition of soft pale blues and explosive reds. The palette reinforces the clash, with darks framing the bright sparking signals. The layout depicts pixels falling like raindrops, creating sharp contrasts as they encounter heavier, denser colors below, suggesting a struggle for dominance. The 'door' shape seed morphs into an abstract concept of boundaries and opening, while broken pixels imbue the composition with imperfection, mirroring emotional turmoil.
