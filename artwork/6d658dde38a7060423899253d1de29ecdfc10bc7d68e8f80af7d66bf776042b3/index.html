Artist Statement

This piece explores the juxtaposition of confusion and clarity through an interplay of dark and vibrant hues. The palette of black and dark violet evokes a sense of depth and uncertainty, while soft gold and pale green introduce warmth and hope. The central focus creates a form of internal conflict—one side reflects order with structured colors, while the other disrupts with erratic patterns reflecting broken symmetry and chaotic emotion. The absence of order is manifested through broken pixels and layered density, inviting the viewer to contemplate the tension between coherence and disarray.
