Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional duel between relief and dread. The palette juxtaposes the cool relief of pale mint and soft sky against the menacing storm gray and near black, suggesting an inevitable tension. The dense center of broken pixels symbolizes dread, contrasting with the loosening edges that embody relief. The collision occurs at the center, where heavy textures interact with lighter hues, illustrating the battle between overwhelming pressure and the search for space. The noise pattern in the relief areas creates a sense of breathing space, offering a momentary escape from chaos.
