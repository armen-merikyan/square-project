Artist Statement

This piece embodies the duality of clarity and confusion, represented by the juxtaposition of warm and cool tones. The palette, drawn from 'ancient_fire', emphasizes burnt orange and dark red, symbolizing warmth and intensity, contrasted with the depth of charcoal. The placement creates a dense cluster of colors at the bottom, suggesting pressure, while the top features quiet negative space, providing a stark contrast that evokes a sense of tension amid calm. The use of 'broken pixels' introduces imperfection, echoing life's complexity. The overall arrangement tells a story of emotional peaks and valleys, reflecting the rhythmic ebb and flow of feeling, punctuated by sudden interruptions in color.
