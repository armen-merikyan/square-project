Artist Statement

The emotional tone of awe juxtaposes with terror, reflecting a delicate balance between beauty and chaos. The chosen palette of deep blues and muted purples alongside bright yellows symbolizes contrasting feelings. Warm yellows emerge from the depths of dark blues, creating an echo of both hope and uncertainty. The arrangement echoes a vertical wave, suggesting upward motion while incorporating broken pixels that introduce a sense of disruption. This collision emphasizes the fragility of stability versus the chaos of broken memories, creating an abstract dialogue where each pixel reflects a fragment of emotional complexity.
