Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a palette of quiet pressure. The colors oscillate from warm muted tones to cooler depths, reflecting the contrasting feelings. The main collision occurs in the center where compressed textures of red and navy meet, suggesting both urgency and serenity. The use of broken pixels fosters an imperfect rhythm, echoing the essence of repair versus damage, thus embedding a narrative of longing while maintaining a boundary that exhibits resolve.
