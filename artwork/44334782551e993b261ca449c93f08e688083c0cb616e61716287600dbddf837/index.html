Artist Statement

The emotional tone emphasizes the interplay between longing and resolve, captured in a palette that combines muted and vibrant tones. The diagonal layout forces a collision of dense patterns on one side with a sparser arrangement on the other, symbolizing the struggle between order and chaos. The broken pixels create intentional disruptions, enhancing the sense of unresolved tension. The arrangement tells a story of yearning and strength challenged, with quiet spaces accentuating the weight of emotions.
