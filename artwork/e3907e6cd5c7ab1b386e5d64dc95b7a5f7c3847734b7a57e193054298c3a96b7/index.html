Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between calmness and rage. A sharp contrast between warm reds, representing the explosive nature of rage, and cooler pale blues and blacks embodies the attempt to contain that intensity. The use of jagged, fault_line patterns signifies interruption and collision while softer ring patterns create spaces of calm, forming a nuanced emotional landscape. The layout erodes symmetry slightly towards the corners, suggesting encroaching chaos. The deliberate use of broken pixels introduces imperfections, enhancing the abstract narrative of containment versus overflow.
