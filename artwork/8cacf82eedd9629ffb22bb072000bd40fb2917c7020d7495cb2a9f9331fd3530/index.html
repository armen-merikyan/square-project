Artist Statement

The emotional tone wrestles between restlessness and peace, embodied through a contrasting palette of vibrant greens and muted creams. The outer colors represent a calming border, while the interior, marked by chaotic patterns, suggests fragmented memories and restless motion. The sharp diagonal elements introduce tension while the negative space ensures a sense of room for breathing and reflection. This arrangement reflects the interplay of calmness and chaos, creating an engaging visual narrative.
