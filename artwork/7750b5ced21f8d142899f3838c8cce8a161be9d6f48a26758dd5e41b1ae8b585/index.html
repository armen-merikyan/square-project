Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tone of longing and resolve through contrasting colors and patterns. The top four rows utilize a barcode pattern in muted tones, representing the yearning for connection and warmth, while the bottom four rows display a patchwork of stronger hues, symbolizing strength and clarity. The palette, featuring stain brown, ash gray, warm ivory, soft gold, and deep wine, reflects the weight of guilt and the brightness of forgiveness. The central collision occurs between the two patterns and colors, where longing meets resolve, creating a dynamic visual narrative. The overall composition reinforces the emotional tug-of-war, evoking a sense of depth and juxtaposition.
