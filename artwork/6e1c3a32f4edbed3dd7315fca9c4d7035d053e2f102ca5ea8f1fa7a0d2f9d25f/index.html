Artist Statement

This artwork represents the conflict between defiance and submission through a visual pattern that captures a sense of pressure and quiet. The palette features sharp crimson and powder pink contrasting with carbon black and steel gray. The vivid colors symbolize defiance, while the muted tones suggest submission. The central collision occurs between these bright accents and the quieter fields, creating a rhythmic push and pull. Neglected negative spaces amplify the sense of emptiness and draw attention to the pulsating centers, evoking an emotional narrative of struggle and transformation.
