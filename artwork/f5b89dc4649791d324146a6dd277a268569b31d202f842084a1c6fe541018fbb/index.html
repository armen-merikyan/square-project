Artist Statement

This artwork conveys a dynamic interplay between the themes of emptiness and desire. The palette, consisting of sepia tones mixed with softer cool colors, represents nostalgia and longing. The arrangement features stronger colors on one side, creating a contrasting collision of warmth against cooler hues. The placement invokes a feeling of weight, as if something is both rising and falling, echoing the emotional tension from the seed's themes. The distribution leaves ample negative space, embodying the idea of loneliness while the broken pixels add to the sense of imperfection and fragmentation.
