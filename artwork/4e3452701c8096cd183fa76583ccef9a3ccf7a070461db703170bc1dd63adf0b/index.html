Artist Statement

The emotional tone dances between grief's fragmented blues and joy's bright yellows. The palette contrasts a deep blue and muted purple that conveys sorrow against vibrant yellows and oranges representing joy. The left side emphasizes a structured radial pattern of joy while the right disrupts it with asymmetries reflecting grief, embodying the tension between the two emotions. The layout mirrors visually but intentionally places broken pixels to represent life's imperfections, creating echoes of past joys and current sorrows throughout the composition.
