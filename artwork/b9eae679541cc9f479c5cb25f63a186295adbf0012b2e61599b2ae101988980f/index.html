Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional interplay between vulnerability and strength through a dense center surrounded by lighter, more muted colors. The palette, which includes deep blacks and pale blues, emphasizes the contrast of a stable core against an exposed perimeter. Broken pixels disrupt the symmetry, reflecting the collision between pressure and release, while the overall flow suggests a pulsing movement—a quiet strength pressed against an evolving vulnerability.
