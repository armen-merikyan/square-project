Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dance between trust and suspicion, with a palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold embodying warmth and intensity. The composition contrasts structured frames on one side against chaotic noise on the other, emphasizing the emotional collision outlined in the seed. The edges are intentionally burned to suggest impermanence and urgency, while negative space creates breathing room, allowing the tension between structured trust and unpredictable suspicion to resonate.
