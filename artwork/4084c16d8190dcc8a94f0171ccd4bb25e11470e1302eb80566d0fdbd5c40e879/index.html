Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes warmth from love against the cool detachment of betrayal. The chosen palette consists of pinks and reds expressing warmth and affection, counterbalanced by gray and dark maroon, embodying coldness and disappointment. The primary contrast occurs in the central area where bright colors meet darker tones, creating an echo of warmth against the cold noise of betrayal. The seed's concepts inspired a layout where a mirrored design subtly interrupts itself, evoking feelings of tension and unresolved emotions, capturing both intimacy and rupture.
