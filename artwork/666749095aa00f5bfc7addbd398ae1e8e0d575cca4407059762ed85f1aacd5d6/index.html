Artist Statement

The artwork reflects the emotional tension between fear and hope through a palette that juxtaposes dark hues with a subtle accent of soft gold. The sharp edges of dark violet echo the jagged nature of fear, while the warm soft gold represents a glimmer of hope. The layout is designed to have pixels that descend, reminiscent of rain, creating a sense of pressure. There are strategic placements of lighter pixels amidst darker tones, suggesting a rising signal of hope against a backdrop of fear. The pattern of jagged and soft transitions embodies the inner conflict expressed in the seed.
