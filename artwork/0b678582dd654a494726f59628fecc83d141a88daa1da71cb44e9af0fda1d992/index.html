Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between longing and resolve through contrasting color patterns. The palette, comprised of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue, evokes a sense of nostalgia and memory, while sharp and soft contrasts manifest the emotional collision. The central focus is a boundary where longing's fractured patterns of fading warmth intersect with the steadfast, structured lines of resolve. Broken pixels represent the scars and disruptions in emotional states, enhancing the artwork's depth. Overall, this grid expresses a narrative of unfinished connections and resolved paths, with calm negative space creating breathing room between emotional bursts.
