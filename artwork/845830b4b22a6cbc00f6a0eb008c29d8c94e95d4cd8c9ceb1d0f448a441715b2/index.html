Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between longing and resolve, captured through a juxtaposition of warm and cool colors. The palette, derived from 'softness_severity,' includes comforting soft pinks and stark blacks, portraying a spectrum of emotion. The patterns of pulse marks and grids collide primarily at the center, suggesting an interaction of intimacy and distance. The scattered layout incorporates broken pixels to create an asymmetrical balance, emphasizing a narrative of repair within damage, mirroring urges of serenity and urgency.
