Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents a dance between confusion and clarity, captured through a palette inspired by deep blue and muted purple representing complexity, and the brightness of yellow and orange signifying clarity. The layout embodies an argument grid, reflecting competing patterns of rain (confusion) and frame (clarity). Key contrasts occur in the central vertical axis, where deep blue meets vibrant yellow, suggesting a collision of emotions. Occasional broken pixels introduce imperfection, inviting the viewer to engage with the chaos amidst moments of clarity.
