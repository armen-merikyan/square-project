Artist Statement

This composition represents the emotional conflict between defiance and submission, crafted through the interplay of warm and cool colors. The palette, drawn from 'mourning_renewal', evokes depth and transformation, with the dark tones symbolizing resistance set against lighter glimmers of submission. The collision occurs in the center where the rigid ladder-like patterns of defiance disrupt the softer grid of submission, creating an intricate dance of tension and release. The broken pixels introduce nuances of imperfection, reinforcing the theme of an ongoing struggle between opposing forces.
