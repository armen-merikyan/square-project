Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between defiance and submission through a stark palette of mourning colors. The darker hues symbolize resistance and struggle, while the lighter shades represent moments of quiet submission. The main contrast occurs in the central rows where broken dots (defiance) collide with echo patterns (submission), interrupting each other's spaces. The asymmetry and broken edges add to the feeling of imbalance, echoing the seed's themes of chaos and order.
