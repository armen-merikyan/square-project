Artist Statement

This composition embodies the conflict between defiance and submission through contrasting color placements and emotional tonality. The palette, consisting of muted purple, deep blue, orange, and yellow, fosters a spectrum of warmth and coolness, emphasizing a dynamic transformation between two emotional states. The bottom-weighted layout asserts a sense of gravity and pressure, symbolizing submission, while lighter hues and higher contrasts evoke the spirit of defiance. Key collisions occur in the upper left and lower right, where vivid contrasts between intense and muted colors reflect tension. The unbalanced arrangement further suggests an unresolved battle, as elements spiral outward in a chaotic yet deliberate fashion.
