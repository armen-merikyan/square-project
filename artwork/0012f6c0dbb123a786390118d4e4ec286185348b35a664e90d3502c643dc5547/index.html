Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between fear and hope, represented through contrasting colors and jagged edges. The dark palette reflects fear's weight and pressure, while soft gold and pale green evoke hope's delicate brightness. Main contrast occurs in the diagonal flow, with bursts of hope breaking through the fear-laden darker hues, creating a dynamic interplay. The pattern echoes a struggle and a faint glimmer of light, reminiscent of the inner conflict expressed in the seed.
