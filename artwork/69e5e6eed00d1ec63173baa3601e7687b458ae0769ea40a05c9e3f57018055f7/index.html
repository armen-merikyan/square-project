Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between trust and suspicion, using the palette of warm shadows and cool blues to evoke an emotional landscape. The contrast is present in the layered patterns that intertwine, where the warm tones signify trust and stability, while the cool shadows suggest suspicion. The collision happens in the center where dark colors confront lighter ones, portraying a dynamic struggle. The chosen colors reflect an intimacy of distances, swapping places in an aurora of pulse and static, as captured in the seed's themes.
