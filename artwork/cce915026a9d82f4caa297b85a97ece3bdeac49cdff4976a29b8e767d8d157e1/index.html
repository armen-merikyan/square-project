Artist Statement

The composition conveys a tension between restlessness and peace, achieved through a structured yet variable pattern. The palette blends soft neutrals with stark contrasts, emphasizing both calm and chaos. The light hues represent peace, while darker shades symbolize restlessness. The main collision occurs in the central area, where irregular patches disrupt the radial peace pattern, reflecting an emotional battle between conflicting feelings. Broken pixels embody slight disruptions, reinforcing the theme of unsettled tranquility.
