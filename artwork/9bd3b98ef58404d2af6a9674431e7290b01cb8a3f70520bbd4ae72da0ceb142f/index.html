Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between joy and grief through contrasting palettes and arrangements. The left side represents joy with warm, spark-like yellows and oranges, while the right side uses deep blues and muted purples to convey grief. The patterns ‘flame’ for joy and ‘target’ for grief illustrate a fight between liveliness and heaviness. The clash occurs at the center, where bright colors are interrupted by darker, more subdued tones, creating a dynamic interplay that reflects the complexities of human emotion. The decision to split horizontally underlines the duality, while the presence of broken pixels throughout infuses a sense of imperfection, adding depth to the emotional narrative.
