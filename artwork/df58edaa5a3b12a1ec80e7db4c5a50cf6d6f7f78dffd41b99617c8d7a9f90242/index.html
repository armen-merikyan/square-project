Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a battle between the chaotic energy of rage and the serene presence of calm. The selected palette of red, black, pale blue, and white creates a strong contrast, with red as the dominant color representing rage encompassing cool blues conveying calmness. The main collision occurs between a dense cluster of hot red dots symbolizing explosive emotions against the structured horizontal lines of pale blue, signifying order and containment. The layout mimics a mirror effect with intentional disruptions, evoking themes of emergence and fracture, shaped by the seed into a dynamic yet harmonious abstract composition.
