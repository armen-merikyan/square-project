Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the delicate balance between vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette of melancholy wonder—with rain blue and muted plum—embodies the soothing depth of vulnerability while the lavender gray and soft cyan symbolize resilience and clarity. The primary collision occurs at the center where the 'hook' pattern rises amidst a dense grid, creating tension between the supportive edges and the unshielded center. Broken pixels introduce intentional flaws, reflecting vulnerability in the structure, while maintaining a cohesive flow upward, signifying hope against pressure.
