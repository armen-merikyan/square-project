Artist Statement

The emotional tone suggests a dance between desire and determination. The palette, reflecting cosmic indigo and void black, creates depth while the cold gold offers a sharp accent of resolution against the overwhelming calm of deep teal. The artwork contrasts the soft spirals of longing, portrayed through muted rings of color, and the strong, dynamic pushes of resolve represented by sharper placements. The collision occurs at the center, where the themes intertwine, suggesting a push and pull of emotion, reflecting the seed's intricacies.
