Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the artwork captures the duality of envy and gratitude through vibrant warm hues of yellow and orange, contrasting with the deep, muted blues and purples. The palette reflects the complexity of these intertwined emotions, suggesting moments of brightness against shadows. The main collision occurs at the transition between warm and cool colors, creating a rhythm of tension and release that mirrors the intended feeling of the seed. The layout is structured to rise, symbolizing hope amidst emotional conflict, while the deliberate placement of broken pixels introduces imperfection, suggesting the unpredictability of human emotion. Overall, the pattern flows in a zigzag, embodying an emotional spectrum that evokes a sense of both chaos and resolution.
