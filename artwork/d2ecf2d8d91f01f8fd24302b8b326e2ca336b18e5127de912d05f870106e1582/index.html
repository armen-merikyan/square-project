Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between emptiness and desire, utilizing a palette that juxtaposes saturated warmer tones against cool, quiet hues. The contrast is centered in the middle where the hot coral and blood orange create a pulsing effect, suggesting relentless craving, while the surrounding hollow blue and charcoal act as a calming containment. The clipped edges and broken pixel placements inject disorder amidst order, reflecting the struggle inherent in the emotional themes. The overall layout achieves a balance between dense islands of desire in the center against a broader field of restraint, symbolizing the constant push and pull of human emotion.
