Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between safety and exposure using a contrasting palette of muted greens and warm browns representing stability against soft tones of pale skin and cold white for vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center where the noise pattern of safety interrupts the stair pattern of exposure, creating a dynamic shift that evokes a sense of both comfort and risk. The falling pixel layout mimics tears or droplets, enhancing the narrative of emotional release and pressure in balance.
