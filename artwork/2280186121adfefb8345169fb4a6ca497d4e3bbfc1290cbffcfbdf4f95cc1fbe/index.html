Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts clarity with the chaos of confusion. The palette utilizes electric blue and magenta against a black foundation to evoke tension, reflecting the push and pull between structured symmetry and broken patterns. The placement of colors creates a disjointed rhythm, where the vibrant hues pulse against deeper tones, suggesting an ongoing conflict of emotions. This layout embodies overlapping paths and false symmetry through striped and bordered patterns, visually narrating the struggle for clarity amidst noise.
