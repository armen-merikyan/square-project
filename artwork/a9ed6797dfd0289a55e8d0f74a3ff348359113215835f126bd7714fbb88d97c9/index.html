Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between clarity and chaos, inspired by the concepts of signal versus noise. The palette of deep navy and black creates a foundation of weight, while the bright dim cyan serves as a piercing accent, echoing the idea of a clean pulse fighting through interference. A quiet channel of negative space cuts through dense patterns, mimicking the struggle of a clear signal amidst overwhelming static. The layout illustrates a layered density that collides at the center, where hard edges meet smoother transitions, representing the continuous battle between the ordered and the chaotic. The broken pixels intentionally disrupt the flow, adding imperfection and evolving the narrative of emotional mechanization into a dynamic interplay of echoes and interruptions.
