Artist Statement

This artwork conveys a tension between emptiness and desire. The palette of dark violet as a symbol of depth contrasts with the warm tones of soft gold, establishing an emotional pull. The arrangement features a strong vertical movement representing rising signals against a weighty dark base, while 'burned edges' provide a sense of fragility and decay. The patterns of stripes and staircases reflect the complexities of longing, evoking the feeling of striving towards unattainable desires set against a backdrop of isolation.
