Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition integrates chaos and calm, reflecting restlessness against peace. The chosen palette features a core of dark maroon, adding intensity, paired with soft pinks and grays that evoke a sense of tranquility. The collision occurs centrally, where bright sparks of color emerge against darker, more subdued backgrounds, illustrating the inner conflict of memory versus motion. Broken pixels strategically disrupt symmetry, creating tension and drawing attention to the boundary between peace and chaos as patterns of ripple and spark intertwine in the grid's design.
