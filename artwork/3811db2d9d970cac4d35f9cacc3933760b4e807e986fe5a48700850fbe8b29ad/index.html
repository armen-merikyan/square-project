Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission. The palette is comprised of deep reds and grays, symbolizing love's heat and betrayal's chilling presence. The main collision occurs in the center of the square, where the fiery red sharply contrasts against cooler shades, creating a dynamic interplay that reflects the themes in the seed. The 'rain' and 'fault line' patterns create disrupted symmetry, highlighting areas of stability and tension that mirror the emotional landscape of conflict and resolution. The use of broken pixels symbolizes scars within this interplay, leaving intentional space that echoes the complexity of these emotions.
