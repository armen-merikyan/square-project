Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional interplay between defiance and submission, utilizing a palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue to evoke a sense of nostalgia and tension. The central line of darker colors represents defiance, standing in stark contrast against the lighter, more muted shades of submission, creating a visual collision. The layout emphasizes an 'empty room' perception, allowing negative space to breathe, while the 'broken dots' and 'stripes' patterns manifest the conflict and rhythm represented by the seed—suggesting falling weight and shifting emotions. The overall flow is from left to right, symbolizing movement and transition, invoking love and betrayal subtly through the imbalance of shapes. Each row exemplifies the structured chaos, culminating in a composition rich in emotional story.
