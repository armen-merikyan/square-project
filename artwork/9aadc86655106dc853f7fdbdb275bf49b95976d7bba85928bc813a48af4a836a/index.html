Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve through contrasting patterns and colors. The palette of deep reds, muted creams, and grays reflects a struggle of warmth vs. cool restraint, symbolizing a search for clarity. The frequent interruptions of pattern in the lower left corner create a sense of overflowing emotion, while the top right remains quiet and restrained. The central collision carries the weight of both emotions, encapsulating the pulse of desire amidst a broken frame.
