Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the chaos of restlessness and the serenity of peace. The palette combines warm tones like hot coral and blood orange to signify tension, contrasting with cooler shades like charcoal and hollow blue, representing tranquility. The placement of the colors creates a main collision in the center, where warm colors erupt amidst cooler tones, evoking the spark of motion amid stillness. The interaction echoes the seed's essence, creating a visual representation of struggle and resolution within a confined space.
