Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension of longing and resolve through a division in color placements. The cool hues (moss green and stone gray) evoke a sense of yearning, while the warmer shades (rust and small gold) provide a grounding resolve. Main contrasts collide diagonally, suggesting pressure and release. The broken pixels add imperfections, enriching the emotional depth. Each color interacts to form an abstract narrative of intimacy and distance.
