Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between peace and restlessness, using a palette that balances mourning blacks and deep blues with subtle yellows and greens to symbolize transformation. The layout mirrors with intentional breaks, suggesting the flow of memories and motion: a clash between calm, even channels and restless, noisy interruptions. The main collision occurs diagonally, emphasizing the argument between upward movement (peace) and downward pull (restlessness), creating a vivid visual narrative that captures the delicate balance between these emotional struggles.
