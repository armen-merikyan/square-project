Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the push and pull between dread and relief. The palette incorporates muted storm gray and near black to embody dread, woven with accents of pale mint and faded gold to provide moments of relief. The main collision occurs where dark hues meet brighter colors at the center, representing the struggle between heavy and light. The use of broken pixels enhances the chaos and imperfection, conveying the delicate balance between order and chaos, mirroring the spiral dynamics of pressure and release.
