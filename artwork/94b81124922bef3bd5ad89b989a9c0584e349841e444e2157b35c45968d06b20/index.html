Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional duality of longing and resolve, showcasing a vibrant clash of colors that suggests a narrative of striving and stability. The chosen palette of moss green and bone white reflects resilience amidst fragility, while rust and stone gray introduce a sense of chaos and blockage. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense clusters of deep colors interrupt quiet fields, symbolizing the friction between desire and certainty, achieving a dynamic flow. The patterns of 'spark' and 'islands' intermingle in an asymmetric rhythm, capturing the essence of unresolved hope reaching towards resolution.
