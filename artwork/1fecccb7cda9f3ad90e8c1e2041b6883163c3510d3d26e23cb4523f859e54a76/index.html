Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension of defiance versus submission through a vibrant palette and contrasting placement of colors. The dominant color is acid green, representing defiance, while muted deep olive shades speak to submission. The clash occurs diagonally from corner to corner, symbolizing the encroachment of defiance over submission. The pattern incorporates broken pixels to intentionally disrupt the order, creating an interaction between chaotic movement and structured calm, reflecting the seed's themes of tension and imbalance in dynamics.
