Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends the strength of rooted contrasts with the delicate vulnerability of pale hues. The palette of 'safety_exposure' provides warm brown and shelter green to signify strength, while cold white and pale skin represent vulnerability, illustrating a push-and-pull between protecting and exposing oneself. The main collision occurs at the center, where the two pulse motifs overlap, creating a dynamic tension that invites reflection on the interplay of resilience and fragility. In this layout, the weighted edges suggest stability while the muted center hints at an inner struggle for balance.
