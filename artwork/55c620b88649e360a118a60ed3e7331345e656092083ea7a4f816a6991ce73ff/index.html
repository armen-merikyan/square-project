Artist Statement

The arrangement captures the collision of restlessness and peace through a progression from calm, wide-spaced colors at the edge to a chaotic, dense center. The palette of muted off-white and compressed red contrasts with dark navy, suggesting a struggle between tranquility and turmoil. The main collision occurs in the center with the chaotic red, while the outer grid-like pattern in soft tones symbolizes peaceful order. The seed's themes are translated into an abstract narrative of emotional conflict, each edge and core selection echoing the tension between memory and motion.
