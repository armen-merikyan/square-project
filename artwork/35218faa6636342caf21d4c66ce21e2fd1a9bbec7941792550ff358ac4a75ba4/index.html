Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the tension between quiet and pressure through dense clusters of color on one side and soft hues on the other, mirroring the emotional contrasts evoked by the seed. The palette features muted shades like 'dust_gray' and 'off_white' to convey calmness, contrasted with warmer 'compressed_red' accents, representing pressure and intensity. The central collision of color occurs where the darker tones cluster, suggesting intense pressure, while the lighter hues fade toward the edges, providing a visual reprieve that invites contemplation. The use of broken pixels to disrupt the otherwise soft transitions reflects moments of confusion and clarity in balance.
