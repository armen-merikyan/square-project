Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension of 'longing vs resolve' through a careful interplay of muted and intense colors, with 'dust_gray' and 'off_white' providing a quiet base, signifying serenity, while 'compressed_red' disrupts as a bold accent illustrating resolve and urgency. The layout suggests falling pixels, creating a downward flow that conveys both the weight of longing and the clarity of resolve. The main collision occurs in the center, where sharper diagonal transitions of 'compressed_red' intercept the gentler tones, creating a stark yet harmonious contrast that symbolizes the quest for balance between longing and resolve.
