Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between warmth and cold, representing the dichotomy of love and betrayal. The palette intertwines pink and dark maroon, symbolizing soft affection clashing with harsh betrayal. The darker edges suggest a sense of confinement, contrasting with the lighter inner rows that evoke hope and connection. The collision primarily occurs through the juxtaposition of warm and cool colors, and the placement creates a rising pattern symbolizing the aspiration to transcend emotional turmoil.
