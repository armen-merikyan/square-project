Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between love and betrayal through contrasting shades of warm pinks and reds against muted grays and dark maroons. The layout mirrors itself with slight dissonance, representing the theme of symmetry distorted. A zigzag flow emphasizes the oscillation of emotions, while the 'stairs' pattern embodies the staircase of betrayal amidst a maze of warmth. The negative space adds a sense of emptiness, mirroring the void left by broken connections.
