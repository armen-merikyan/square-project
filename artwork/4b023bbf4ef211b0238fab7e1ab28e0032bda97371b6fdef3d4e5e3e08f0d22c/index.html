Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional contrast of pressure and quiet through dense clusters of color colliding at the bottom that dissolve into lighter tones toward the top. The palette, featuring dark navy and compressed red, symbolizes tension and internal struggle, while off-white and faint peach introduce a sense of calm. The dynamic diagonal flow introduces a sense of rising tension, with the main collision of deeper colors anchoring the lower two rows, creating a push upwards. This echoes the abstract theme of the seed, reflecting the interplay between hard-edged pressure and soft release, embodying both clarity and confusion.
