Artist Statement

This artwork grapples with the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve. The color palette—comprising soft cyan, muted plum, and lavender gray—carries a melancholic yet hopeful quality, reflecting deep yearning against a backdrop of quiet strength. The collision between delicate halos representing longing and the firm, target-like shapes of resolve creates a palpable tension in the center. The layout features a narrow negative-space path that cuts through dense patterns, emphasizing the contrast of isolated feelings amidst layered complexity. This encourages the viewer to contemplate the complexity of human emotions and their intricate patterns.
