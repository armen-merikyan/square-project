Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional dichotomy between trust and suspicion. The left side presents a clean grid pattern in soft golds and greens representing trust, symbolizing stability and growth. The chaotic right side employs dark tones and glitch patterns reflecting suspicion, where uneven tones and jagged edges create a sense of tension. The vertical split emphasizes the contrast, making the viewer experience a push and pull between clarity and uncertainty. This arrangement embodies resilience during fragility, suggesting a landscape where confidence meets doubt.
