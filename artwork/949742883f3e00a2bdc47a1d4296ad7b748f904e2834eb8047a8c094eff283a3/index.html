Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a struggle between panic and control, reflected through the use of stark contrasts in the palette. The choice of red and black suggests urgency and instability, while pale blue introduces a hint of calm, attempting to balance the chaotic elements. The layout is a grid where the left side employs a structured, framed pattern symbolizing control, while the right breaks into a maze-like chaos, exhibiting the conflicting emotions. The collision occurs where these two opposing patterns meet, intensifying the narrative of instability and sparking emotional tension.
