Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tug-of-war between envy and gratitude, represented through contrasting warm and cool colors. The palette of burnt orange and dark red captures the intensity of envy, while the softer shades of gold and charcoal evoke a sense of gratitude and reflection. The layout features a frame of darker hues representing envy, with a lighter inner core symbolizing gratitude; this contrast starkly collides in the center where emotions intermingle. The chosen shape of a moon informs the soft edges and dynamic tension—signifying distant longing against intimate warmth. The strategic placement of broken pixels disrupts the symmetry, echoing the idea of imperfection within the emotional spectrum.
