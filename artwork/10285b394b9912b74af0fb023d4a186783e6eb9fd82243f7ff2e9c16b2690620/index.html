Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captures the battle between calm and rage, using a palette of black for weight and red for emotional intensity contrasted with pale blue for soothing qualities. The layout suggests spacious negativity with a stark off-center presence, embodying a quiet yet powerful interplay of colors. The collisions occur mainly where the red sharply contrasts against the black and pale blue, creating a pulse of energy amid stillness. This relates to the seed as the emotions reflect the nuanced relationships between pressure and release, rhythm and pause.
