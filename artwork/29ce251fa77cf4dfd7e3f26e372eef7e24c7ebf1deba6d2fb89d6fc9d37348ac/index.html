Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension of vulnerability and strength through the interplay of color and pattern. The palette, featuring off-white and faint peach, conveys a sense of exposure and fragility, while dark navy and compressed red ground the composition in strength. The central collision of heavier color forms with lighter elements creates a narrative of safety versus exposure, symbolizing emotional layers. The quiet_channel layout, marked by an open path of negative space, enhances this tension, allowing the viewer to navigate between contrasting emotional states.
