Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes longing and resolve, where the deep navy and black create a contemplative base, representing longing, while the dim cyan introduces punctuated moments of clarity and strength, symbolizing resolve. The collision occurs centrally, where dense clusters of dark hues break through lighter areas, simulating the tension between desire and decision. The patterns of broken pixels interrupt the flow, echoing the imperfections in our journeys of repair and change.
