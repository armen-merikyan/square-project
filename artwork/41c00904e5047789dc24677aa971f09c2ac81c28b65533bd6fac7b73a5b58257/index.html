Artist Statement

The emotional tone derived from the seed contrasts the tension of envy against the clarity of gratitude, embodied through the choice of dark navy and muted tones juxtaposed with sharp whites. The palette represents a lonely signal against a backdrop of weight. The arrangement features dense clusters reflecting rage and calmness, with zigzag patterns indicating interruptions. The collision occurs at the center, where the chaos of broken lines leads to a moment of reflection, suggesting a journey from noise to quiet. This visual complexity and broken structure draws from the metaphor of a shattered mirror.
