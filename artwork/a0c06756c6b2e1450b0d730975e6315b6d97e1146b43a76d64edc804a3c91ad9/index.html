Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a battle between defiance and submission, visualized through contrasting patterns of dots and crosshatch. The palette features cosmic indigo and cold gold that signify the conflict's intensity, while void black serves as a unifying background, amplifying each color's emotional pull. The main collision occurs in the center, where bright, confident colors clash against softer, muted shades, indicating a struggle between the dominant defiant force and the encroaching submission. This creates a dynamic rhythm that plays out across the grid, suggesting both tension and release, mirroring the complex emotional landscape set by the seed.
