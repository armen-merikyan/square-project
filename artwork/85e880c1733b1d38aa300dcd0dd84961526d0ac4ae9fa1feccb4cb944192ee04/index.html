Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork explores the tension between vulnerability and strength, creating a visual narrative of exposure against shelter. The selected color palette, 'awe_terror,' features cosmic indigo and void black as foundational colors, highlighting the intensity of the struggle. The main contrast occurs centrally, where jagged, flame-like patterns of vulnerability collide with solid, tooth-like structures representing strength. This intersection creates disruption, mimicking the emotional friction outlined by the seed, instilling a sense of looming tension amidst quiet spaces.
