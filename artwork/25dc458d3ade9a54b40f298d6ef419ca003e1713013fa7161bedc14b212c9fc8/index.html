Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dance between vulnerability and strength, depicted through confronting contrasts in color. The palette includes warm coral and soft amber as dominant hues suggesting warmth and safety, while cold slate and blue-black introduce depth and tension. The main contrast occurs in the center where the lighter ring pattern of strength collides with a halo of vulnerability, creating a dynamic push and pull. Negative spaces allow for breathability, resonating with the themes of pressure and exposure. The layout encapsulates an almost framed composure shaped by broken pixels that introduce imperfections, embodying the ongoing struggle between sheltering oneself and remaining exposed. This creates a narrative of emotional complexity, embodying both strength and delicacy in the interplay of colors.
