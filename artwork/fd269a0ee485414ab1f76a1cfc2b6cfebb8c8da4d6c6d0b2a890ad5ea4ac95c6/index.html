Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between 'longing' and 'resolve' through a palette that oscillates between vibrant and muted tones. The warm hues of hot coral and blood orange symbolize the urgency and desire inherent in longing, while the cool charcoal and hollow blue reflect the steadiness and calmness found in resolve. The arrangement reflects a central collision, where warmth meets coolness, echoing the emotional struggle between these two states. Broken pixels are integrated to disrupt the flow, suggesting both damage and repair, emphasizing the fragility of the emotional state. Negative space is evident, providing a quiet channel that juxtaposes the denser, shattering echoes of longing, enhancing the sense of unresolved tension that is so integral to the theme.
