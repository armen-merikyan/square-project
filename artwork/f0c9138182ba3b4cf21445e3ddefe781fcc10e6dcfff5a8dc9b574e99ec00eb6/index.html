Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between dread and relief, a visual metaphor for the conflict within vacant spaces. The palette, inspired by 'ancient_fire,' uses burnt orange and dark red to invoke heat and tension, while charcoal offers a stark reminder of weight and absence. The main contrast occurs vertically, with a checker pattern suggesting respite from imminent pressure, interrupted by broken dots that introduce chaos and imperfection, echoing the journey from dread to relief. Each row reflects a rising energy, aligning with the layout of 'rising_signal' while maintaining at least 10% negative space to enhance the sensation of release.
