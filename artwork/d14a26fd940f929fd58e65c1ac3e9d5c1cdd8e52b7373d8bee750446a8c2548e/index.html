Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between restlessness and peace, using a palette that contrasts warm coral with cold slate to signify emotional friction. The main collision occurs diagonally, with bright colors representing moments of clarity fighting against darker, denser shades. The interaction of mirror patterns and constellations reflects the push and pull of memory and motion, echoing the constancy of existence against impermanence, while the structured quiet channel adds to the tension of unresolved feelings. Broken pixels interrupt the surface, embodying disruptions in peace, creating an imperfect harmony that embodies the theme.
