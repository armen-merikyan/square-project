Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional duality of awe and terror, juxtaposing the structured patterns of order against the chaos of broken elements. The palette of deep reds and pale blues creates a dynamic contrast, suggesting warm emotional tension between stability and fragility. The 'tears' motif symbolizes emotional release and broken memories, manifesting through scattered pixels that disrupt the flow, inviting viewer contemplation on how chaos can coexist with structured beauty. The alternating placement of colors emphasizes the push and pull of comforting familiarity against unsettling unpredictability.
