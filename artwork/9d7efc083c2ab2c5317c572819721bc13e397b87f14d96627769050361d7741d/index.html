Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between trust and suspicion. The palette combines calming hues like 'pale aqua' and 'mist gray' representing trust, with striking 'alarm red' to signify suspicion. The layout emphasizes a vertical split, creating an inherent clash: the left showcases soft fault lines of trust while the right supports jagged islands of suspicion. The pronounced contrast is centered, allowing the two emotional states to collide, creating friction that disrupts visual serenity, embodying the essence of resilience and fragility.
