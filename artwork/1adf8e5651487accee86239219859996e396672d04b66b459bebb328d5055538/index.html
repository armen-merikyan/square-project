Artist Statement

The emotional tone draws on the tension between vulnerability and strength, symbolized by a dense center of soft, muted colors transitioning to sharper, darker tones at the edges. The chosen palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green reflects the duality of fear and hope, highlighting the fragile balance. The main collision occurs at the central area, representing the battle of protection and exposure. The patterns of glitch for strength and noise for vulnerability collide around the center, creating an echo of frequencies, while broken pixels disrupt symmetry, suggesting imperfections in strength. This arrangement embodies a struggle between stability and fragility, resonating with the original seed's concepts.
