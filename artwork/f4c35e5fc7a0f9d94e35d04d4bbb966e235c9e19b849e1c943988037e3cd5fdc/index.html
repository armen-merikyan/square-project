Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a battle between defiance and submission, represented by contrasting patterns of barcode and ripple. The palette, 'intimacy_distance', features warm and cool tones that enhance emotional tension, suggesting clashing dynamics. The dominant color of cool blue in the center portrays the unmoved defiance, while the surrounding warm shadows suggest a receding submission. The collision is prominent at the center, where defiance marks disrupt the quieter ripple edges, embodying a narrative of interruption and contrast between the turbulent center and the serene borders.
