Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the struggle between tenderness and rage, reflecting both warmth and explosive energy. The palette transitions from saturated reds symbolizing rage to muted blues representing calmness, creating an emotional gradient. The main contrast lies in the hard-edged grid of red that collides with the soft staircase pattern of pale blue in the center, capturing the essence of these feelings. This tension manifests in a dynamic interaction where brighter colors pierce through muted tones, emphasizing a battle between compassion and turbulence. The layout employed serves to frame the core emotions, offering an abstract symphony of color interactions.
