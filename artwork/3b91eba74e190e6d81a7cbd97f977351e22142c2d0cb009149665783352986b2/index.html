Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of vulnerability and strength. The palette of muted purples and deep blues reflects melancholy and stability, while the vibrant yellows and oranges add an aspect of warmth and light, symbolizing hope amid fragility. The main contrast resides in the diagonal arrangement, where brighter colors invade from one corner, interrupting a deep blue base, creating a dynamic tension between exposed vulnerability and protective strength. Broken pixels disrupt the patterns like scars, emphasizing the fragility that exists within strength and offering a visual representation of internal conflict.
