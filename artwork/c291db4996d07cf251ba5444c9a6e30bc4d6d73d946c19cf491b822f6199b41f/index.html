Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects a struggle between confusion and clarity, depicted through a contrasting palette of deep reds and soft pales. The placement of bold colors represents the thick outlines mentioned in the seed, while the lighter shades suggest moments of clarity among the chaos. The collision occurs predominantly in the central pixels, where the darker shades hint at confusion, while the lighter ones provide glimpses of clarity. This arrangement echoes the idea of falling pixels that rise in intensity towards the edges, symbolizing a journey from confusion at the center to potential clarity in the periphery, offering a visual rhythm that moves from dense clusters to quiet spaces, thereby creating a compelling tension.
