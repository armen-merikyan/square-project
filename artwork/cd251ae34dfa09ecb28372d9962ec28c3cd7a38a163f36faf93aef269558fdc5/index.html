Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between fear and hope through contrasting dark and light tones. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes a sense of weight and fear while introducing glimmers of hope through the lighter elements. The split vertical layout emphasizes the conflict, with darker colors on one side suggesting heaviness and fear, while the lighter colors burst forth from the center, echoing moments of clarity and illumination. The soft edges create a sense of delicacy, while broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, symbolizing the unpredictable nature of emotions. Overall, this artwork encapsulates the struggle between heavy despair and the fragile threads of hope.
