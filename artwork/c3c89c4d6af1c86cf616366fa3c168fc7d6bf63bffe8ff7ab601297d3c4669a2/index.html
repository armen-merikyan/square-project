Artist Statement

This composition embodies the struggle between panic and control using a stark palette of black, red, and pale blue to reflect intensity and calmness. The arrangement of pixels follows a 'falling' pattern, suggesting descending emotions, while the jagged edges introduce a sense of chaos against the structured core. The contrast lies between dense black clusters and the lighter blue, evoking the collision of overwhelming pressure against moments of clarity. The control aspect is portrayed through symmetry that breaks apart, depicting the unresolved tension suggested by the seed.
