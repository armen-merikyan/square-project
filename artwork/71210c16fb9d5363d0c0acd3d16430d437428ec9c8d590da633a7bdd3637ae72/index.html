Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between longing and resolve. The palette, drawn from soft pastels and deep shades, reflects both the warmth of hope and the starkness of unresolved emotions. Colors like pale mint resonate with calmness, while storm gray introduces an element of uncertainty. The primary contrast occurs in the bottom half, where the rippling effect of lighter colors collides with darker tones, echoing a journey from chaos to clarity. The layout rises upward, embodying the theme of ascent amidst broken connections, represented by randomly placed dark pixels disrupting the flowing patterns of light.
