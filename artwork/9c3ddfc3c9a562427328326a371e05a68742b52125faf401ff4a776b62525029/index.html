Artist Statement

The emotional tone highlights the tension between emptiness and desire. Color choices reflect this duality, with warm vibrant tones representing desire in contrast to cool muted hues symbolizing emptiness. The hot coral and blood orange create a saturated pull, while charcoal and hollow blue depict containment and quietude. The collision occurs at the center where brighter colors overlap disrupted patterns, illustrating the struggle between longing and limitations. This abstract arrangement conveys a story of desire reaching for liberation against a backdrop of restraint.
