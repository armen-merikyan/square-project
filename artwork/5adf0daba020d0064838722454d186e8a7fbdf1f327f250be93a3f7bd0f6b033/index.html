Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between control and panic, using a palette that juxtaposes warm yellows and deep blues to illustrate the clash of emotions. The radial pattern lends a sense of order on one side while harsh stripes break in from the other, creating a visual echo of instability. The arrangement of colors intentionally leaves empty spaces, suggesting an 'inside vs outside' dynamic, while the contrasting sections provide a vivid narrative of conflict, centered around the concept of an 'empty room' filled with unexpressed emotions.
