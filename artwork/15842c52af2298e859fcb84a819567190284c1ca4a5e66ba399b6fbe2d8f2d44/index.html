Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts fear, represented by dark corners and dense, muted colors, against the hope, evoked by a central flow of lighter tones. The palette includes black and dark violet for the fear elements, while soft gold and pale green represent hope, creating a push-pull dynamic that captures both anxiety and optimism. The collision occurs towards the center where dark and light pixels intersect, mimicking a zigzag flow, suggesting movement and disruption. The layout emphasizes an argument grid, illustrating the struggle between light and shadow, ultimately leaving negative space to allow for breathing room amidst the tension.
