Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between envy and gratitude, using a palette evoking warmth and nostalgia. The broken symmetry of the layout reflects imperfect experiences. The contrasts emerge primarily at the center, where deep sepia clashes with lighter faded blue, symbolizing a weighty emotional core against lighter sentiments. The jagged distribution of colors creates a zigzag effect, enhancing the feeling of unevenness while the negative space allows for a breath of calm amidst the chaos, embodying the push and pull of conflicting emotions.
