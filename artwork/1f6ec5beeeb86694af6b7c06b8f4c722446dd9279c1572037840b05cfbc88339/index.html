Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the juxtaposition of awe and terror, reflecting the fragility of perceived order against underlying chaos. The 'ancient_fire' palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold evokes warmth and danger, mirroring the tension of awe and terror. The layout features mirrored sides with abstract broken pixels symbolizing disrupted stability. The contrast between the warm hues and darker shades creates a pulse-like rhythm, suggesting a wave of emotions that oscillate between harmony and disruption, embodying the broken and chaotic elements of the seed.
