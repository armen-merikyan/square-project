Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, utilizing a palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green to evoke a sense of low-key contrasts. The center is dense and dark, symbolizing confusion, while lighter, softer colors radiate outward like clarity spreading from a core. The jagged edges create a sense of disruption amidst harmony, while the broken pixels represent imperfection within the flow of clarity. The layout invites the eye to start from the center and navigate toward the edges, echoing the tension inherent in the seed. This composition maintains the necessary negative space to avoid overwhelming the viewer despite the density at the center, creating a balance between chaos and calm.
