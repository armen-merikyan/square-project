Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between loneliness and connection, mirrored through contrasting density and lightness. The chosen palette of deep maroon and various shades of gray represents the weight of isolation against the warmth of potential connection. The left side is weighted with darker colors featuring sharp, fragmented patterns, reflecting loneliness, while the right side employs lighter shades with gentle, repetitive dots, symbolizing connection. The overlapping colors create a collision of feelings, echoing the complexity of human emotion.
