Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between the vibrancy of spark and the muted essence of ash. The palette combines soft tones of warm ivory and stained browns to reflect stability, while deep wine and golden accents emerge sporadically, evoking bursts of energy amidst a backdrop of ashen gray that hints at lost memories. The collision occurs diagonally, creating a sensation of disarray as the structured flow battles fierce interruptions, making the viewer navigate the contrast between calm and chaos.
