Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress. The top half, representing nostalgia, uses darker tones to evoke a sense of depth and history, while the bottom half, symbolizing progress, uses lighter shades to suggest hope and forward movement. The palette of deep navy for nostalgia contrasts with soft dim cyan of progress, showcasing the ongoing battle between memory and future aspirations. The stronger colors towards the edges help illustrate the weight and pressure from external influences, symbolizing the collision of these two states. The grid pattern reflects the structured nature of progress while the imperfections (or broken pixels) signify the chaos of history lingering in the present.
