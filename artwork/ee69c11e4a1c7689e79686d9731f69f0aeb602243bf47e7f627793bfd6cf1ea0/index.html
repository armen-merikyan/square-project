Artist Statement

This composition evokes the emotional spectrum encapsulated in the seed. The contrast between the deep, saturated colors and the vibrant accents illustrates the collision of emotions: calmness interrupted by bursts of intensity. The dark colors provide a quiet backdrop, a nod to the underlying pressure, while the lighter hues disrupt this calm, creating an ongoing rhythm that suggests both tension and release. Each color pair acts like pulses of emotion—remnants of quiet moments interrupted by sudden bursts of feeling, embodying the dynamic interplay of calm and chaos.
