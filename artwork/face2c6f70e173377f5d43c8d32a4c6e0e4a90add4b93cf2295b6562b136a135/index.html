Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between certainty and curiosity, with the palette suggesting a weighty relief against chaos. The colors evoke a pulse of the courageous (storm gray) meeting the obscure (near black), while the golden accents highlight points of curiosity within the density of the arrangement. The contrast is prominent in the central region, where soft sky and faded gold subdue the more chaotic glitched edges of darker tones, creating a visual tension that composes the overall narrative of rising signals clashing with receding anxiety.
