Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes certainty and curiosity, creating a dynamic interplay between structure and exploration involving an upward movement. The palette incorporates vibrant shades like hot coral and blood orange, representing curiosity, against grounding tones of charcoal and hollow blue, symbolizing certainty. The overlapping clusters in the center represent the collision of these emotions, while the cooler colors recede, offering space for contemplation. This tension invites the viewer to navigate between control and exploration.
