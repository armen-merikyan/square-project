Artist Statement

The artwork captures the tension between longing and resolve through a palette of vibrant and muted tones, utilizing cool electric blues and hot magentas to embody desire and strength. The light static gray provides a quiet backdrop, representing the space between emotions. The dominant color creates a collision at the center, while the pattern reveals a falling motion, suggesting a journey of ascent and descent. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, reinforcing the theme of unresolved emotional contrasts.
