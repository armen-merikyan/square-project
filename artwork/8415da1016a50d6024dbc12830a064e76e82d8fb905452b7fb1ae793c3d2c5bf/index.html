Artist Statement

The emotional tone focuses on the contrast between longing and resolve, represented through an asymmetric pattern. The chosen palette—composed of soft cyan and muted plum—evokes a sense of melancholy wonder, capturing the tension between delicate beauty and the weight of unresolved feelings. The left side reflects longing with a 'noise' pattern, filled with subtle disruptions and muted backgrounds. The right side balances this with the 'spark' pattern, signifying resolve through bright, radiant colors and bold forms. The main collision occurs in the center, where these patterns meet, creating a visual discourse that echoes the complex interplay of human emotions.
