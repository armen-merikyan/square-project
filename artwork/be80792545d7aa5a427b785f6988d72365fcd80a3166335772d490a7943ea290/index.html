Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between quietness and pressure, utilizing a calm palette with pops of intensity. The colors are strategically placed to create a dense cluster at the bottom left, representing pressure, while softly transitioning to negative spaces that evoke quietude. The patterns of embers and a negative cross collide diagonally, enhancing the theme of tension. Bright accents and muted backgrounds embody the shattering and clarity theme from the seed.
