Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends bright vulnerability with warm enclosure, reflecting a tension between exposure and safety. The palette of shelter green, warm brown, pale skin, cold white, and open blue creates a balanced yet dynamic composition, with soft hues suggesting warmth countered by exposed spaces. The main collision occurs through a pale center framed by darker edges, denoting a protective yet vulnerable core. The patterns of mirror and fault line interact within the layout's quiet channel, highlighting interruptions and overlaps that evoke complexity in emotional states.
