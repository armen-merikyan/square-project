Artist Statement

This composition captures the duality of longing and resolve through contrasting color clusters and spatial arrangements. The chosen palette of blacks, dark violets, soft golds, and pale greens represents the emotional depth and transformation from confusion to clarity. The main collision happens at the center, where the 'ring' pattern of longing meets the structured 'constellation' of resolve, creating a vivid expression of tension and connection. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, evoking a sense of repair amidst fragmentation, reflective of both the healing process and the remnants left behind from damage.
