Artist Statement

This composition juxtaposes emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette features muted tones (dust gray and off-white) suggesting calm, combined with compressed red for tension and dark navy for depth. The central collision of red meets calm gray, mimicking the intricate struggle of yearning against determination. The layout presents an invasion from one corner, enhancing feelings of tension and restraint, while the broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, representing repairs amid damage.
