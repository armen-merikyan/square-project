Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between envy and gratitude through contrasting patterns and colors. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue reflects a broken memory, with warm tones suggesting nostalgia and cool tones echoing a sense of calm. The composition features upward and downward movement, with a center-heavy arrangement that creates a feeling of imbalance – mirroring the conflict between dense emotions. The flow of pixels creates an interruption, while cracks in the pattern represent the underlying discord of envy, juxtaposed with stairs symbolizing the elevation of gratitude. Surface-level variations like broken pixels add imperfection, enhancing the emotional narrative.
