Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between 'spark' and 'ash' through a contrast of vivid greens and blues against muted grays and blacks. The palette symbolizes scattered ignitions against the weight of ash, invoking a feeling of resilience amidst fragility. The central interruption—a jagged field of bright colors—suggests struggle, while the peripheries represent quiet decay, allowing for a visual narrative of conflict and memory. The layout echoes a worn symmetry, enhancing the overall tone of an unresolved argument.
