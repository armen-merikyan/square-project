Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between envy and gratitude, using a palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold to evoke warmth and depth. The contrast occurs where the dark, muted colors suggest heaviness and weight, colliding with the brighter, softer hues that represent lightness and hope. The layout mimics pixels falling, creating a sense of movement and emotional pendulum, while broken pixels introduce imperfection and vulnerability, echoing the complex interplay of fear and hope. This arrangement forms a pattern that reflects the struggle between rising aspirations and falling doubts.
