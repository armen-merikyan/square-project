Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects a tension between longing and resolution, where warmth meets resolve in a fragmented yet hopeful arrangement. The palette incorporates shades of green, brown, and pale tones to symbolize safety and exposure, emphasizing a journey from incompleteness to clarity. The central collision occurs where warmer brown pulses meet the dominant green field, suggesting a sense of movement and depth, while the broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, mirroring the complexity of repair amidst damage. This composition navigates themes of interruption and connection through scattered patterns, underlining how unresolved feelings can prompt a search for repair.
