Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between confusion and clarity, embodied in contrasting color blocks suggesting tension and chaos. The palette leans into warm burnt orange and deep dark reds juxtaposed against charcoal and gold, evoking warmth yet a sense of disturbance. The flow resembles falling droplets, echoing descending feelings, while sharp vs soft edges create dynamic movement across the grid. An imperfect symmetry emerges with strategically placed 'broken' pixels to disrupt order and suggest narrative turbulence.
