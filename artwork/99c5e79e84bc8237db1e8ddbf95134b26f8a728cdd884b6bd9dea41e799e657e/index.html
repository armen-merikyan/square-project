Artist Statement

This composition balances the emotional contrasts of vulnerability and strength with a focus on muted tones juxtaposed against sporadic vivid accents. The pale mint and faded gold symbolize fragility and light, forming an exposed center, while storm gray and near-black edges encase it, representing strength and stability. The layout spirals inward, suggesting a pressure that builds towards the center but interrupts rhythm with broken pixels to invoke a sense of sudden exposure. The collision of colors highlights the tension between the two emotions, creating a dynamic yet cohesive abstract narrative.
