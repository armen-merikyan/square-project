Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension of 'rage vs calm' through a contrast of vivid reds and muted blues. The warm, jagged patterns of rage collide with the soft, echoing regions of calm, suggesting an eruption within serene spaces. The main contrast occurs at the center, where hot colors disrupt the quiet edges, leaving a hollow space around. The interplay between the colors symbolizes the chaos of emotion intermingled with the desire for peace, reflecting the dichotomies in the seed text.
