Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between the clarity of signals and the confusion of noise. The chosen palette—comprising signal green, electric blue, dead black, static gray, and hot magenta—evokes a clash of exciting vibrance and oppressive depth. The bright accents of green and blue punctuate the dark hues to represent 'signals,' while the gray and black areas create a backdrop of noise. The main collision occurs in the center, where the signal colors overlap and interrupt the quieter areas, creating a pulse in an otherwise chaotic landscape. This embodies the seed by symbolizing the struggle between clarity (signals) and confusion (noise) within our experiences.
