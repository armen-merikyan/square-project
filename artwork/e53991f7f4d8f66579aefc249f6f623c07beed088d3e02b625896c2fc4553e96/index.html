Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension of longing versus resolve through a palette that oscillates between warm and cool colors, depicting the push-and-pull of emotional depth. The dense colors in the center create a feeling of chaos, while the lighter tones suggest moments of clarity. The diagonal gradients represent the cross currents and flows of intimate distances in relationships. The use of broken pixels is intentional, emphasizing imperfection and the idea of distance, while the halo effect in the edges provides a subtle framing that evokes a lingering presence.
