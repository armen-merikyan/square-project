Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a blend of tender warmth and painful distance, represented by the contrast between soft pinks and harsh grays. The palette features warm colors to symbolize love (pink, red) and cooler, darker hues (gray, dark maroon) to evoke betrayal. The arrangement reflects a spacious layout, with the soft noise pattern on one side interrupted by sharp dots of betrayal. The collision occurs where the two patterns meet, symbolizing the tension between love and betrayal. Negatively, empty spaces echo loneliness, reinforcing the feeling of an 'empty room'. This abstract approach embodies a story of conflicting emotions without literal representation.
