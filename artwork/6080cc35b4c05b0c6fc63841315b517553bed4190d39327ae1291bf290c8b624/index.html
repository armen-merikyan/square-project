Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork encapsulates the tension between loneliness and yearning, represented through a palette that juxtaposes warm and cool tones. The choice of a muted palette signifies emptiness, while bright yellow accents introduce a pulse of desire. The central contrast occurs at the pixel arrangement that directs flow from bottom to top, simulating rising hopes amidst the isolation. Equally, the radial pattern of the brighter colors intersects with the checker-like cool tones, creating an interrupted harmony, echoing the feelings stimulated by the seed.
