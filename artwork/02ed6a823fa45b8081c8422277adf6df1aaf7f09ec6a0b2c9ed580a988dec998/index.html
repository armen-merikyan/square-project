Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a compelling contrast between restlessness and peace, where dark colors symbolize density and unease, while lighter hues provide intervals of calm. The palette employs deep teal and void black to embody restlessness, with cosmic indigo and star white as accents signaling moments of clarity and hope against the backdrop. The diagonal layout establishes a collision in the center, where the tension between restless patterns and peaceful spaces meets, creating a visual harmony of disruption and stillness. Broken pixels are scattered intentionally to evoke the scars of memory amidst calmness, reinforcing the emotional narrative.
