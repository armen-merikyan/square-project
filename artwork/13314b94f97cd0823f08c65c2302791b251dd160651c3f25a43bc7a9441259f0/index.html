Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of confusion and clarity through a carefully structured layout. The top half represents clarity with vibrant shades of pink and red, arranged in a maze-like pattern that induces a sense of order and direction. The bottom half portrays confusion with overlapping target patterns in duller gray and dark maroon hues, suggesting intrusion and disorder. The jagged edges introduce a visual break, highlighting the contrast of a nearly mirrored image disrupted. The pulse-like rhythm is reflected by the arrangement where colors collide, creating an echoing tension beneath the surface, perfect for conveying emotional complexity.
