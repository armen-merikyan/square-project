Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between clarity and confusion, using a palette derived from deep navy and muted cyan to evoke both introspection and turbulence. The central contrast emerges from the dense cluster of dark colors that symbolize confusion, while lighter accents punctuate moments of clarity, suggesting fleeting insights amidst chaos. The layout emphasizes a diagonal flow, representing clarity emerging from a corner against a backdrop of disarray. Each pixel arrangement echoes the turbulent pulse of conflicting emotions, alluding to the jagged edges of broken symmetry without direct representation.
