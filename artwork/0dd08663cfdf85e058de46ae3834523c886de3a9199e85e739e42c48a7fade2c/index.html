Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional turbulence of 'confusion' versus 'clarity.' The selected palette of electric blue, magenta, and black introduces a stark contrast evocative of sharpness against the softness of the colors. The layout features a dense central area surrounded by rhythmic patterns resembling pulses, creating a sense of motion that reflects turmoil and resolution. The broken pixels scatter throughout, imbuing the piece with fragments of chaos amidst a primarily structured grid, effectively visualizing the seed's themes of symmetry and disruption.
