Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines elements of loneliness and connection, expressed through a balance of muted hues and vibrant accents. The palette features warm yellows and oranges to depict connection, contrasted with deep blues and muted purples to convey loneliness. The main contrast occurs in the shift from top to bottom, creating a visual representation of isolation versus togetherness. This abstract pattern emphasizes both the weight of solitude and the lightness of unity, with broken pixels suggesting the imperfections of memory.
