Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between a sense of loneliness and the hope for connection, reflecting a struggle found in the contrasts of warm tones versus coolness. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, and faded blue evokes a sense of nostalgia and fragility, capturing both warmth and distance. The contrast resides in the center, where the deeper colors represent the loneliness, surrounded by lighter tones suggesting connection, facilitated through the patterns of connection versus loneliness. The layout unfolds from the corner, providing a sense of invasion as one emotion challenges the other, embodying the tension of hidden desire clashing with visible emotion.
