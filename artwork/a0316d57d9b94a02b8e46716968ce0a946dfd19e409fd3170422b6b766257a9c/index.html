Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between fear and hope, reflected in a palette that juxtaposes dark, dense colors with subtle glimmers of light. The left side employs a checker pattern to evoke an unsettling pressure and tension, while the right side employs a split-screen effect to introduce elements of hope and upward flow. The central area maintains a measured flow, spiraling inward, suggesting a collision of contrasting feelings. This arrangement embodies both the oppressive weight of fear and the fragile optimism of hope, allowing the viewer to experience the dynamics of emotional collisions without literal representation.
