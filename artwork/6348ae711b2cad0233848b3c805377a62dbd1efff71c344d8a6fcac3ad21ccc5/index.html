Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tug-of-war between panic and control, showcasing a contrasting palette of dark navy and bright dim cyan. The left side constructs an ordered staircase pattern reflecting control, while the right bursts with mirroring chaos. This collision highlights the struggle as colors clash, presenting a visual narrative of pressure and liberation. The asymmetry serves as a reminder that chaos can seep into order, infusing dynamic energy into the composition.
