Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes tenderness and rage, creating contrasting energies. Using a palette of red (rage), pale blue (calm), black (depth), and white (light), the artwork achieves a balance between intense pressure and serene spaces. The main collision occurs diagonally, with a braid pattern representing rage in the lower corner, intertwining with a soft, stair-like pattern of tenderness. Broken pixels strategically interrupt the smooth flow, suggesting emotional scars and creating a dynamic rhythm across the grid. This layout emphasizes the conflict and interplay of feelings, allowing the viewer to experience both calm and chaos simultaneously.
