Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between love and betrayal, displayed through a contrasting palette of warm pinks and deep maroon against muted grays. The pattern flows in a diagonal fall, representing the collapse of order into chaos. The placement of broken pixels creates a sense of interruption and fragmentation, simulating the feeling of a heart divided. The chaos emerges where dark maroon collides with the warmth of pink, providing a visual echo of pain against the calmness of gray, representing the disarray of emotions.
