Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between quiet and pressure, reflected in a color palette that balances muted tones with sharp contrasts. The darker shades at the center symbolize pressure, while the lighter edges suggest openness and clarity amidst confusion. The main collision occurs at the diagonal center, where the density of darker colors confronts the airy lighter tones, illustrating a shattering against the tide of quietness. This dynamic interplay showcases a visual tension where hard edges converge with soft transitions, encapsulating the seed's essence.
