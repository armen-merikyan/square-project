Artist Statement

The composition embodies the emotional interplay between longing and resolve, captured through a dynamic palette that melds warm coral and cold slate. The soft amber creates gentle intersections, symbolizing both connection and disconnection. The design features punctuated sections where broken pixels appear, presenting moments of imperfection—representative of damage and repair. The central collision of pulse marks amidst expansive negative space emphasizes the tension between contained emotions and an overwhelming urge to break free.
