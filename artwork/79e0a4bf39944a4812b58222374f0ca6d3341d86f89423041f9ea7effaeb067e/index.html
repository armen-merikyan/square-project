Artist Statement

This abstract composition encapsulates the emotional dance between pressure and quiet. The palette features muted tones like off white and dust gray coupled with compressed red and dark navy, symbolizing the weight of pressure intertwined with the serenity of quiet. The main contrast occurs diagonally across the square, where the light colors abruptly encounter dense clusters of darker hues. This collision reflects the tension of opposing emotions stated in the seed, creating a dynamic flow that captures both hard edges and soft spaces. The arrangement hints at a rise from bottom to top, mirroring a struggle for clarity amidst confusion, echoing the overall narrative.
