Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional duality of longing and resolve through the use of contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, inspired by the themes of intimacy and distance, incorporates warm tones like rose clay and cool shades of distant lilac to evoke a sense of nostalgia, while also suggesting strength through the deeper golds. The main collision occurs in the top left corner, where dense clusters of broken dots create a visual pressure against the sparse islands of color that symbolize serenity. This interplay reveals the struggle between a desire for resolution and confronting the chaos of damage, translating the seed's themes into an abstract expression.
