Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a battle between the intense pressure of rage and the serenity of calm. The chosen palette of deep reds and cool blues represents this conflict, where bright warm colors burst against muted backgrounds. The main collision occurs centrally, with jagged red pixels suggesting chaos cutting into the cold, horizontal blue maze pattern that attempts containment. This abstract composition reflects the push and pull of emotions hinted at by the seed, creating a tension between dense and empty spaces, echoing both an outburst and a quiet retreat.
