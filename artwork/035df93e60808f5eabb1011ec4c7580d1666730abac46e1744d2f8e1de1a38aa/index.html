Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a careful balance of color intensity and placement. The palette features muted moss green and stone gray, representing the depth of longing, while rust and small gold introduce a sense of resolve and illumination. The center of the grid serves as the primary collision point where more static elements, depicted by the tightly packed colors, meet the pulse marks that suggest movement and yearning. The two contrasting patterns create a visual dialogue, signifying how order meets chaos, with negative space facilitating the ebb and flow of emotion.
