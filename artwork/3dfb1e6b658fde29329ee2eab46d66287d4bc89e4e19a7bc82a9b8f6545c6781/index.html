Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the warmth of love with the coldness of betrayal. The palette combines soft pinks (#FFC0CB) and deep dark maroon (#800000) to symbolize love's tenderness clashing against betrayal's intensity. The layout employs falling pixels to suggest tears or loss, while broken dots signify betrayal's abruptness, creating conflict with the gentle curvature of paired shapes representing love. The contrast lies primarily between the central warmth and the darker, more chaotic edges, illustrating the emotional collision and leaving space for reflection.
