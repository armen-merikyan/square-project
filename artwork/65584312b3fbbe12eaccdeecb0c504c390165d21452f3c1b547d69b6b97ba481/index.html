Artist Statement

This piece conveys the emotional tension between control and surrender, showcasing the struggle of warm hues attempting to overflow their boundaries while cooler tones calmly contain the chaos. The palette of warm coral and soft amber contrasts against cold slate and blue-black, emphasizing the push-and-pull dynamic. The central collision occurs within the center of the grid, where the patterns of shatter and flame intertwine, creating a visual disruption that invites contemplation. The application of broken pixels introduces intentional imperfections, reinforcing the theme of tension amidst an overarching structure.
