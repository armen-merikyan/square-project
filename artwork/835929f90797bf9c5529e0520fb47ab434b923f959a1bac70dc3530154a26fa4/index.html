Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional contrast between clarity and chaos, represented by sharp green and electric blue against a backdrop of muted gray and black. The predominant signal green represents the readable pulse, while hot magenta and static gray introduce tension and interruptions. The diagonal layout emphasizes the dynamic thrust of signal versus the encroaching noise, with soft areas contrasting against hard edges to convey a sense of struggle. The intentional broken pixels create imperfections, reflecting the flaws inherent in any signal received amidst noise.
