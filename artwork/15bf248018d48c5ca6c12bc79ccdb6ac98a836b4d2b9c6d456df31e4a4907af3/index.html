Artist Statement

This pixel art conveys the emotional interplay between joy and grief through its color choices and arrangement. The upper half employs brighter colors (yellow, orange) to emulate joy, with soft rounded patterns representing warmth and the gentle flicker of flame. In contrast, the lower half uses deeper tones (muted purple, deep blue) to evoke the weight of grief, creating a fragmented, shadowed feeling. This juxtaposition establishes sharp contrasts between the halves, punctuated by intentional placement of broken pixels reflecting the theme of chaos amidst order. The layout mirrors itself horizontally but disrupts it subtly, reflecting the intense emotional collision and balance inherent in the seed's themes.
