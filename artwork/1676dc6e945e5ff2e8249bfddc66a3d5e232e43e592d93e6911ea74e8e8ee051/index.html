Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between restlessness and peace, embodying the struggle of memory versus motion. The palette, drawn from hues of muted cream, blackened plum, and iron gray, suggests a void intertwined with a slight hint of warmth in small orange accents. The clash of the checker pattern against the quiet threads hints at the disruption caused by restless memories against a backdrop of serene peace. The main collision occurs in the center, where colors clash yet fuse, depicting a restless rebirth within a calm space. The layout depicts a mostly dark background with small light reflections representing fleeting moments of peace amidst agitation.
