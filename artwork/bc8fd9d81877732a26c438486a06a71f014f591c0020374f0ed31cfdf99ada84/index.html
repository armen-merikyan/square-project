Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between clarity and confusion, where readable signals battle against the noise of interference. The palette of deep navy and dim cyan represents shadow and static, while white serves as a contrasting clearing, symbolizing moments of clarity within chaos. The center pulse of white breaks through darker edges, creating a focal point. This collision of clear and muddled spaces generates a rhythmic flow reminiscent of a heartbeat trying to stabilize amidst static, embodying the conflict of memory vs. erasure as an abstract experience.
