Artist Statement

The emotional tone leans towards the juxtaposition of longing (desire) against the stark reality of absence (emptiness). The palette of warm yellows and cool blues reflects this duality, creating a visual tension between warmth and chill. The top half utilizes a dotted pattern to symbolize fragmented desires, while the bottom half enacts a more structured approach, embodying the heaviness of emptiness. The collision occurs in the middle, where contrasting elements meet, suggesting a struggle between these emotional states. Broken pixels introduce an imperfection that mirrors human emotion's complexity, enhancing the narrative of conflict and resolution. Overall, this piece serves as a palpable representation of that battle between light and weight.
