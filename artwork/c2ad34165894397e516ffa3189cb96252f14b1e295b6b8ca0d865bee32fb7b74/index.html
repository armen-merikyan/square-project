Artist Statement

This composition embodies a narrative of confusion and clarity, showcasing the tension between symmetry and breakage. The palette, drawn from the 'ancient_fire' theme, employs burnt orange and dark red for intensity against the grounding tones of charcoal and gold, emphasizing the emotional contrast of warmth versus coolness. The top half features a structured 'ring' pattern representing clarity, while the bottom disrupts this with a 'mirror' pattern, invoking chaos and noise. The presence of broken pixels adds intentional imperfection, enhancing the sense of conflict between the orderly and the chaotic, allowing the viewer to experience a pulse of emotion through the visual rhythm.
