Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the chaotic energy of rage with the soothing calmness of serenity. The palette features hot reds contrasting with soft pale blues and sharp whites, visually representing tension and tranquility. The collision is centrally located where jagged, fiery reds interrupt the smoother blue hues, embodying the struggle of containment against an overflow of emotions. The 'ladder' shape seed inspired layered patterns—a maze for calmness above, contrasting islands of chaos below, conveying a narrative of emotional conflict while maintaining an abstract form.
