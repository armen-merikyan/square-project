Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional interplay between longing and resolve through a delicate balance of color and abstraction. The chosen palette emphasizes resilience (moss green and stone gray) contrasting against rusty and bone-like hues to reflect a journey from fragility to strength. The main collision occurs towards the center, where brighter elements (small gold) represent the pulse of resolve amid surrounding quieter tones, embodying a tension between contained and overflowing emotions. The broken pixels simulate the scars left by prior damage, while the negative spaces create breathing room for reflection.
