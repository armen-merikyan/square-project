Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between quiet and pressure through a bold color palette that juxtaposes dark navy and compressed red, suggesting the weight of pressure, against lighter tones like off-white and faint peach. The contrast peaks in the center, where darker colors converge, creating a dense focus that symbolizes pressure, while the outskirts fade into quiet, airy colors. This reflects the seed's themes of conflict and balance, shattering expectations through sharp edges and soft tones that allow for both confusion and clarity in chaos.
