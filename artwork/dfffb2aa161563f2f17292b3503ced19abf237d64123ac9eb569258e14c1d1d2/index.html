Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between joy and grief through contrasting colors and patterns. The outer frame, representing grief, uses deep blues and muted purples to convey a sense of heaviness, while the vibrant yellows and oranges in the center represent joy, creating a pulsing effect that feels alive. The clear 4x4 grid of dots on the left symbolizes structured joy, while the broken patterns signify the disruption of grief on the right. The overall flow zigzags, resonating with the push and pull of these emotions.
