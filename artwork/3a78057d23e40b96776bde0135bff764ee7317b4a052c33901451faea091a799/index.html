Artist Statement

The emotional tone draws from the conflict between pressure and quiet, manifested in these 8x8 color arrangements. The palette balances muted fields of dust gray and off-white representing calm, juxtaposed with sharper accents like compressed red and dark navy to convey tension. The primary collision occurs along the diagonal, where the harder edges meet softer tones, signifying the shattering and resolving interactions described in the seed. This arrangement leads to a visual conversation between structured density and open space, mirroring the underlying emotional narrative.
