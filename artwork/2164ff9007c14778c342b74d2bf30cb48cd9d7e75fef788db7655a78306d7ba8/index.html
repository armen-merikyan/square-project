Artist Statement

This composition portrays the emotional tension between emptiness and desire. The palette is rooted in deep navy and black, evoking feelings of solitude, interspersed with dim cyan that hints at the yearning for connection. The layout transitions from dense patterns reflecting desire to more open, sparse areas representing emptiness. The jagged edges enhance the feeling of fragmentation, while the warm vs. cool colors collide to show contrast, echoing the ebb and flow of conflicting emotions, culminating in a rhythmic pattern that captures the essence of unfulfilled longing.
