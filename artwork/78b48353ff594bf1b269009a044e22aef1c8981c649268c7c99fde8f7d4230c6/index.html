Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between vulnerability and strength through color selection and arrangement. The palette leans towards muted pinks and reds, representing both emotional warmth and betrayal, while maintaining a stable gray to symbolize strength. The central area has brighter reds that collide with the darker borders, creating a vivid representation of tension where light and dark interact—reflecting the theme of exposure against protective barriers. Broken pixels add irregularities, enhancing the feeling of imperfection and complexity.
