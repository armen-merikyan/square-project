Artist Statement

The emotional tone wrestles with the duality of love and betrayal, where the warm hues signify affection and unity while the darker tones evoke feelings of dissonance and fracture. The chosen palette of pink, red, dark maroon, and gray reflects this combination. The artwork employs a symmetrical layout interrupted by asymmetrical broken pixels, highlighting the clash between harmony and disarray. Positive versus negative space creates a visual tension, with sharp contrasts and soft transitions framing the central core of emotion.
