Artist Statement

This composition embodies a contrast between longing and resolve, using a palette that evokes hunger and restraint with muted creams and dark plum. The dense center sparkles with small orange accents, representing resolve and urgency, surrounded by darker hues that express longing and introspection. The overall flow spirals from the center outward, creating a sense of movement while the broken pixels introduce imperfection, suggesting both repair and damage. The resulting pattern reflects a negotiation between serenity and urgency, encapsulated in an abstract flame shape.
