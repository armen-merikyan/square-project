Artist Statement

This composition captures the conflict between safety and exposure, showcasing a pale center against darker borders. The chosen palette of warm brown and soft greens signifies shelter, while cold whites and open blues represent vulnerability. The collision occurs in the central area where muted colors battle with brighter accents, suggesting a struggle between pressure and release.
