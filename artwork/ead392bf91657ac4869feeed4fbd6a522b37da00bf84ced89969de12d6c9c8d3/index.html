Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition embodies the tension between shelter and exposure. The palette includes warm brown and shelter green as foundational elements representing safety, while pale skin and open blue introduce vulnerability. The main contrast occurs where the color transitions clash in the upper center, symbolizing the delicate balance between being shielded and exposed. The patterns flow upwards, reflecting an ascent towards hope amidst uncertainty, while broken pixels disrupt the symmetry in places, signifying the fragility of safety in a chaotic world.
