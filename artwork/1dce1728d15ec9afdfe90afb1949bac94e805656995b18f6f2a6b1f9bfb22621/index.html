Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a clash between love and betrayal, captured through the juxtaposition of warm and cool colors. The palette includes pink and red tones representing love, contrasted sharply against dark maroon and gray, indicating betrayal. The split vertical layout creates tension, with the left side embodying warmth through dotted patterns, while the right side incorporates crack-like disruptions. This design evokes a sense of pressure and release, mirroring the emotional intricacies suggested in the seed.
