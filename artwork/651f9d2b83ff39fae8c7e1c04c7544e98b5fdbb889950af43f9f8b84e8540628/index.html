Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between pressure and quietness. The palette integrates muted tones like dark navy and dust gray to convey a sense of weight, reflecting the compressed energy of pressure. In contrast, off-white and faint peach represent soft, quiet spaces, inviting a release from density. The grid develops a diagonal flow that directs the eye across the square, with dense clusters at the top left and more open areas toward the bottom right, suggesting a journey from chaos to serenity. Broken pixels serve as intentional disturbances, adding a sense of imperfection and depth, echoing the theme of clarity interspersed with confusion.
