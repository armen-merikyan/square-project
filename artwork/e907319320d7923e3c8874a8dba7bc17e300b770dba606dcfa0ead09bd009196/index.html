Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a transition between nostalgia and progress, with the palette evoking melancholy (navy, black) countered by the freshness of growth (dim cyan, white). The layout utilizes a mirror-broken approach with slight imperfections, representing the fractured memory of the past against the clarity of future aspirations. The contrast is most evident in the top half's organized maze structure symbolizing nostalgia, and the chaotic drip pattern in the bottom half depicting progress. This shift creates a visual dialogue between stability and change.
