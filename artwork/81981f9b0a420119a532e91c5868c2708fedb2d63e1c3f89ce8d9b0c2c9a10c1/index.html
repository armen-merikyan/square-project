Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of restlessness and peace through a palette influenced by muted earth tones and subtle blues, suggesting memories intertwined with dynamic motion. The contrasting elements collide in the center, where a dense cluster of darker hues denotes turmoil and vibrant noise, while the lighter edges evoke a sense of calm and distance. The 'falling_pixels' layout emphasizes a downward pull, mirroring the experience of drifting thoughts. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, echoing the nature of fleeting memories amid calmer states. This juxtaposition forms a narrative of emotional tension—between movement and tranquility, yet remaining abstract and open to interpretation.
