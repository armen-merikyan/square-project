Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional conflict of defiance and submission. The palette, with stark contrasts of black and white coupled with muted blues and grays, embodies tension and complexity. The main collision occurs centrally, where glitch-like patterns disrupt the calm, conveying a struggle between chaos and order. Layered softness at the edges embraces the dominant central structure, reflecting moments of both collision and quietude, much like the seed’s concepts of thresholds and transitions.
