Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between love and betrayal, utilizing a palette of warm and cool tones to reflect vulnerability and conflict. The main contrast occurs where the deep reds meet the grays, creating a visual clash that suggests an emotional fracture. The pattern is intentionally fragmented, with a mix of dense clusters and open spaces, evoking feelings of both intimacy and distance, a reflection of the heart's fragmentation in moments of betrayal.
