Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional conflict of longing and resolve. The palette features clean white and ink black representing clarity and density, while ice blue introduces a soft atmosphere, contrasting the striking warning orange that punctuates the composition. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the cool fields of longing meet the warmth of resolve, suggesting both a tension and a potential for connection. The scattered repair layout allows for quiet spaces that reflect distance and intimacy, melding the emotional tones into a coherent abstract narrative.
