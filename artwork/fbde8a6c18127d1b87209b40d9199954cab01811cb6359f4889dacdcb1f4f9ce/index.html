Artist Statement

Emotional tone captures the volatility of spark and ash as forces of creation and destruction. The palette combines serenity and urgency with calm shades of pale aqua and mist gray contrasting against vibrant alarm red and ink blue. The dominant dark and muted colors represent the weight of ash, while the scattered bright accents of red embody the spark's vitality. The collision occurs primarily toward the center, where jagged edges interact with soft transitions, suggesting tension and balance. The abstraction of this layout echoes the dynamics of memory and resilience, creating layers which evoke the push and pull of emotional states.
