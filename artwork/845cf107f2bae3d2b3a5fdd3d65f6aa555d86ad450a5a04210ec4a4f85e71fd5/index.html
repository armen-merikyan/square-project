Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dialogue between longing and resolve, reflecting the tension of personal narratives shaped by the past (broken) and the present (repair). The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue encapsulates nostalgia and tranquility, creating a subtle atmosphere. The main contrast occurs in the arrangement where dark hues intersect with lighter ones, establishing a dialogue between tension and calm. Broken pixels disrupt the quiet field, suggesting imperfection amidst a longing for structure. This piece narrates the push and pull of emotion through thoughtful color placement and a dynamic, but non-literal, composition.
