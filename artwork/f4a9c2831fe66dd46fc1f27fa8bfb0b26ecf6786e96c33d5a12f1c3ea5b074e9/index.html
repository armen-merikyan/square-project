Artist Statement

The composition embodies the tension of vulnerability and strength, with a central focus representing stability surrounded by contrasting, softer edges that evoke feelings of fragility. The palette of electric blue, magenta, and lime creates a vibrant emotional clash against the deep black, representing the void of vulnerability. The primary contrast and collision occurs in the center, where the structured barcode pattern meets the more fluid channel pattern, symbolizing the interplay of contrasting emotions. This reflects the seed's themes through a dynamic composition that remains abstract yet emotionally resonant.
