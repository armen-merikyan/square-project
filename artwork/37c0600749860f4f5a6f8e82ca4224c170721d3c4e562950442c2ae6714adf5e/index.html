Artist Statement

This composition conveys an emotional push and pull between desire and restraint through a palette of warm, saturated hues clashing against muted cool tones. The central collision occurs between the bright hot coral and deep charcoal, representing the intense yearning that is held back by the locked edges and quiet areas around it. The halo and radial patterns of desire and restraint intertwine while creating a suffocating effect, echoing the spiral pressure of the seed's themes.
