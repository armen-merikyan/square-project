Artist Statement

This abstract composition evokes the tension between confusion and clarity through contrasting patterns. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon reflects emotional resonance—warm colors suggesting vibrancy and confusion, while cooler shades offer stability and clarity. The left side disrupts with a crack pattern symbolizing confusion, whereas the right introduces a spark pattern for clarity. The thick outline echoes a boundary, adding to the structural tension, while broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the sense of chaos. This interplay creates a dynamic rhythm of balance and disruption across the canvas.
