Artist Statement

This piece explores the duality of tenderness and rage through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of deep reds and soft blues manifests the emotional tension, where the saturated rage on the left side collides with the muted tenderness on the right. The 'broken_frame' pattern evokes a sense of fragility amidst the intensity, while 'thread' introduces dynamic movement that interrupts the calm. The main contrast occurs at the center, visually depicting internal conflict and unresolved layers of emotion.
