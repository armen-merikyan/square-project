Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress, split horizontally to represent the rise vs fall dichotomy. The palette of burnt orange and dark red reflects warmth and underlying intensity, while charcoal introduces an aspect of distance and muted tension. The top half, filled with circular rings, hints at growth and movement, while the bottom half is weighted with a heavier grid, suggesting the anchoring force of memory. The use of broken pixels introduces an element of imperfection, echoing the unresolved nature of the theme. Overall, this artwork invites contemplation on how contrasting emotions and experiences collide, forming a complex narrative of human sentiment.
