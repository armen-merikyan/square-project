Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through contrasting warm and cool colors. The sepia tones suggest a sense of forgotten warmth, clashing with the faded blues that resonate feelings of isolation. The main contrast occurs in the center where bursts of warm colors meet the cool surround, symbolizing the struggle between longing for connection and the weight of solitude. The chosen palette embodies the essence of broken memories, enhancing the feelings imbued in the arrangement while maintaining negative space to evoke introspection. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, mirroring the fragility of our emotional states.
