Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional contrast between vulnerability and strength through monochromatic fluctuations of darker shades (strength) meeting the softer blues and whites (vulnerability). The palette emphasizes reds and blacks to represent the weight of strength, tempered with pale blue and whites to symbolize exposure. The central collision of tight, jagged edges symbolizing strength and the soft stripes of light hues signifies vulnerability creates a dynamic shift, allowing the emotions to interact rather than coexist. This echoes the duality of being exposed and sheltered, reflecting the abstract pattern influenced by the seed's essence.
