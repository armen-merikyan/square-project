Artist Statement

This composition embodies the struggle between order and chaos, represented by sharp contrasts of color and texture. The palette features a mix of electric blue as a signal accent, hot magenta for the chaotic interruptions, and deep blacks and grays to create a background noise. The central area displays a dense pattern, mimicking the solid 'signal', while lighter areas provide a stark contrast to evoke a feeling of disruption. Broken pixels serve as emotional scars throughout, disrupting the full rhythm of the piece, echoing the theme of a pulse fighting against overwhelming noise, thus creating a dynamic yet fragmented visual experience.
