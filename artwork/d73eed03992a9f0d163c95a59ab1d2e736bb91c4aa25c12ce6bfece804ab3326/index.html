Artist Statement

This composition embodies a palpable tension between defiance and submission. The palette combines warm shadow with cool blue, creating emotional spots that feel both intimate and distant. The warm shadow occupies the center, suggesting a strong, unmoving force (defiance), while the cool blue encapsulates the perimeter, representing a receding influence (submission). This collision of emotional forces happens at the edges, where symmetrical interruptions challenge the calmness of the central field, reflecting the seed's themes of open versus closed structures. The slight use of broken pixels adds imperfection, enhancing the overall layered density of thought and emotional complexity without literal representation.
