Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the tension between longing and resolve, symbolizing a push and pull dynamic. I chose a palette grounded in 'mourning_renewal', featuring dark and muted tones with highlights to express depth and resonance. The deep blue and black suggest a serious mood, while the pale yellow adds a hint of hope. The composition captures a collision at the center where dark 'teeth' patterns interrupt lighter 'fault line' elements, mirroring the struggle of containment against overflow. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional disruption, suggesting the scars of conflict and the moments that break through resolve. This arrangement forms a structured narrative of emotion, moving from the dark edges to a vibrant central clash.
