Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a palette of muted sepia, tan, and faded blue tones. The dim background represents submission, creating an enclosed field, while bright accents of concentrated colors represent defiance, creating an uneven border and allowing for collision in the center. The layout suggests a last light breaking through the chaos, emphasizing a pulse-like rhythm while still incorporating negative space to maintain a sense of quiet amid the noise.
