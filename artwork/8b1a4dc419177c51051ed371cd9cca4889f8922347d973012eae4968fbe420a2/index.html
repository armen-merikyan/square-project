Artist Statement

The artwork embodies a tension between quiet and pressure. The palette blends muted and darker tones, such as off-white and dust gray, creating a soft yet weighted atmosphere. The dark navy provides a sharp counterpoint, representing pressure. The main collision occurs diagonally, with darker colors dominating the top and lighter shades dispersing towards the bottom, echoing the falling pixels concept. The overall emotional tone suggests a narrative of a struggle between calm and turmoil, while the broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections that reflect the seed's themes of shattering against a tide.
