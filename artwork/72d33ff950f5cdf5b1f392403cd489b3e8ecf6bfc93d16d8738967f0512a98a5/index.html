Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional conflict of quiet versus pressure through color density and pattern arrangement. The palette of off-white and muted dusty tones contrasts sharply with deeper hues of compressed red and dark navy, creating a visual representation of tension. Dense clusters in the center symbolize pressure, surrounded by softer fields suggesting quiet, which gradually release towards the edges. Broken pixels disrupt the surface, hinting at the chaos within clarity, while diagonal flows break the symmetry, embodying a counterflow movement. Overall, the composition encapsulates an ongoing struggle between pressures contained and moments of soft release.
