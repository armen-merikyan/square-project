Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional clash between defiance and submission, utilizing a palette that emphasizes the contrast of strong, deep hues of mourning black and deep blue against lighter notes of pale yellow to represent moments of hope and renewal. The collision occurs at the center with a grid pattern of defiance, surrounded by a static flow of calmer, softer tones representing submission. The layout spirals outward, merging contrasting patterns that suggest tension building and releasing, a delicate balance between chaos and order echoing the seed's themes. Broken pixels are strategically placed to signify disruptions, enhancing the overall emotional depth.
