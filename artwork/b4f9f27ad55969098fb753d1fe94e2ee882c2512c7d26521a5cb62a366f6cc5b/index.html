Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between softness and severity. The palette combines powder pink and warm white to introduce warmth alongside sharp crimson and carbon black to provide a stark contrast. This interaction reflects the gentle pulse against a rigid grid—a metaphor for the turbulence of guilt and forgiveness. The dominant presence of darker tones, accentuated by warmer fields, embodies the weight of severity while maintaining softer undertones. The layout gradually ascends from darker to lighter hues, symbolizing hope breaking through clearer signals, with the center marked by a vibrant collision of colors representing the emotional struggle.
