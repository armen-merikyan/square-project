Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity (signal) and interference (noise), symbolizing the tension within communication and memory. The palette blends deep contrasts with electric highlights, where the tranquil static gray and dead black represent the noise, while the bright signal green and hot magenta offer bursts of clarity. The main collision occurs in the center, where the clean channel pattern is interrupted by the chaotic island shapes, echoing disruptions in communication. This interaction symbolizes the vulnerability and fragility of memories in a noisy world.
