Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a blend of certainty and curiosity, producing a narrative of tension and exploration. The palette utilizes black and white to signify order, contrasted by reds and pale blues that suggest curiosity and chaos. The main collision occurs around the center where bright colors intersect, disrupting the surrounding calmer areas, illustrating the conflict of rising signals against an otherwise contained space. The seed elements shaped the abstract pattern by pushing tension through the center, representing the cage of certainty with wisps of curiosity breaking free.
