Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension derived from the seed's themes. The palette oscillates between deep, muted colors representing calmness and bursts of brighter hues signifying emotional collisions and release. The central contrast occurs where the dark blues meet the vibrant yellows, suggesting both pressure and release, echoing the rhythmic dance between stillness and chaos. The arrangement evokes a sense of quiet fracturing amidst a dense emotional landscape.
