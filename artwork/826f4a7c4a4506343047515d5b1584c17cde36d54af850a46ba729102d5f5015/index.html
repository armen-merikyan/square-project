Artist Statement

This piece embodies a tension between confusion and clarity, using a palette that reflects the mood of broken memory—sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue. The central area bursts with clarity, symbolized by radial patterns, while edges give way to crosshatch confusion, suggesting an escape from uniformity. The main contrasts occur between the warm sepia tones at the center and the cooler faded blues at the edges, creating a visual conflict that mirrors emotional turbulence. The layout operates like a grid where patterns tussle for dominance, ensuring that negative space is preserved for moments of calm amidst chaos, resonating with the essence of a machine's emotional struggle.
