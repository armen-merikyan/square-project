Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, captured through a palette of muted blues and soft grays, representing the complexities of emotion. The two smaller centers of color pulse against a backdrop of negative space, echoing the contrasting dynamics of strength's resilience and vulnerability's raw openness. The collision occurs centrally with dark, rooted colors that suggest strength, while lighter, broken patterns suggest vulnerability, creating a dialogue of pressure and release. The 'ember' shape seed manifests through the warm and cool colors intertwining, depicting a narrative of clash and embrace.
