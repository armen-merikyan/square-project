Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between awe and terror, captured through contrasting colors that blur soft edges into stark interruptions. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green reflects a spectrum from despair to hope. The contrast falls heavily in the center, where dense patterns of dark and light collide, suggesting a split screen where two overwhelming emotions vie for attention. This pattern advances the idea of instability and fluidity through upward and downward movements, mirroring the wavering nature of human feelings—our memories both broken and elevated.
