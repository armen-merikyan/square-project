Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece balances the urgency of 'spark' with the weight of 'ash', visualizing a struggle between vitality and decay. The chosen palette of yellow and muted purple reflects this duality, with yellow symbolizing bursts of energy and muted purple suggesting quiet resignation. The main collision occurs diagonally, where vibrant yellow accents clash against deeper blue and purple tones, creating a dynamic north-east to south-west tension. Features like broken pixels serve as reminders of imperfection, embodying the concept of resilience amid fragility. The overall pattern echoes a conversation between chaos and calm throughout the grid, making it both visually striking and thoughtfully layered.
