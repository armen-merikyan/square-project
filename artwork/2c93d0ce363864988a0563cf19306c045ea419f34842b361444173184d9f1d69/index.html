Artist Statement

This abstract composition reflects the emotional contrast of restlessness and peace through a blend of muted and vibrant colors. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a nostalgic memory, while the dynamic crosshatch and radial patterns create a visual tension. The central area features a collision of a vibrant blue layer cutting through a sea of soft, muted tones, symbolizing the struggle of motion against static peace. The deliberate placement and 'broken' pixels add imperfection and chaos, representing interrupted thoughts within the calm. This arrangement conveys a story of memory layered with constant fluctuation, and a visual echo of fragmented states of mind.
