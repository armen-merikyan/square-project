Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between fear and hope, represented through a palette of deep blacks and soft golds that symbolize the shadow of fear against the glimmer of hope. The composition features upward patterns, echoing a rise while integrating broken pixels to imbue a sense of imperfection and unpredictability. The main contrast is between the darker, grid-like undercurrent and the lighter, radial bursts, encapsulating the essence of tension and release. The zigzag placements propose a feeling of chaos disrupted by moments of clarity, resonating with the keywords from the seed.
