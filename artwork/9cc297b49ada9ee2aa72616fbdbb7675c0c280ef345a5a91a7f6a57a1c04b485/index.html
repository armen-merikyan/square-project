Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the interplay of grief and joy, represented by a palette that blends deep blues and muted purples, reflecting heaviness, along with bright yellows and oranges for moments of relief. The contrast emerges prominently at the center, where joy's expanding noise intersects with grief's fragmented crack pattern, suggesting collisions of feeling. The layout follows a descending flow, echoing rain or tears to evoke a sense of gradual release, allowing for an emotional journey across the pixel grid. Each row carries swirling patterns that echo movement and emotional weight, creating a tension between happy bursts of color and the shadows of deeper emotions.
