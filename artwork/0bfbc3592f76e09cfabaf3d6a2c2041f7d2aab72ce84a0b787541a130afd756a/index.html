Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting patterns. The left side, representing trust, employs warm ivories and soft golds, suggesting stability and open connections, while the right side, embodying suspicion, uses deeper hues of stain brown and ash gray, creating a foggy barrier. The wave-like flow between the columns depicts an emotional shift, where the swirling patterns collide, reflecting the unpredictability of relationships portrayed as a soft glow interrupted by hard shadows. Broken pixels hint at imperfections and vulnerabilities, enhancing the narrative of trust challenged by suspicion.
