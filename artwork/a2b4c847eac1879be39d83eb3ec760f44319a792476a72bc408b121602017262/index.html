Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional push and pull between longing and resolve. The palette of faded gold, pale mint, and storm gray reflects the warmth of yearning contrasted with the weight of determination. The soft sky serves as a quiet backdrop, allowing the intense near-black to create the focal point of collision in the center, embodying the tension of crossing currents. Patterns of broken frames and tides intersect across the canvas, illustrating the chaotic journey of desire versus the firm axis of resolution. The composition adheres to a diagonal flow, symbolizing an approach to resolution, while maintaining open negative space, suggesting both intimacy and distance.
