Artist Statement

The piece embodies the tension between joy and grief, reflected in its top and bottom halves. The upper section features bright yellows and oranges, symbolizing joy through a stepping pattern, suggesting expansion and lightness. The lower half utilizes muted blues and purples to represent grief, arranged in a heavier, more concentrated ring pattern, creating a sense of weight. The fluidity of vertical waves introduces an ongoing oscillation, with occasional thin outlines suggesting interruptive moments that evoke emotional depth. The combination results in a visual dialogue expressing the dichotomy of emotions while allowing room for reflection and space.
