Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the emotional tension of tenderness and rage. The palette blends deep reds and blacks representing rage with soft pale blues and whites that evoke tenderness. The central collision occurs between lighter, muted fields of color and jagged, intense patterns symbolizing pulse marks of anger. The arrangements suggest a climb of emotions, where the dense cluster signals pressure from the left side bleeding into quieter, calmer fields on the right, embodying the push and pull of conflicting feelings.
