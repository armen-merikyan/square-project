Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between loneliness and connection, encapsulated by a central cluster of warm tones surrounded by cool, muted shades. The palette consists of sepia and faded blue, symbolizing the warmth of connection battling the chill of solitude. The contrasting sections highlight a jagged edge structure, illustrating the division between inner warmth and outer coldness. The inner grid represents loneliness, while the outer random accents evoke connection. Imperfections ('broken pixels') introduce a sense of vulnerability, echoing the core theme of hidden versus exposed elements.
