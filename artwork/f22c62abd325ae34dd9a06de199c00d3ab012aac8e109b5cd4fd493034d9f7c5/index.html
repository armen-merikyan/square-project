Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional interplay of love and betrayal through color contrasts and patterns. The palette of soft pink, deep maroon, and varying grays illustrates the warmth of love (pink/red) colliding with the harshness of betrayal (dark maroon/gray). The arrangement features a symmetrical flow disrupted by broken pixels, symbolizing fractured emotions, echoing the essence of the seed. The balance between dense clusters and quiet space amplifies the emotional tension, making the viewer feel the conflict between pressure and release.
