Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between vibrant bursts of 'spark' and muted 'ash,' illustrating the tension of desire against restraint. The palette, inspired by 'melancholy wonder,' employs cool tones of rain blue and soft cyan interspersed with muted plum and lavender gray to evoke a sense of both fragility and resilience. The central collision occurs in the middle four rows where the colors are densely packed, symbolizing the struggle between memory and erasure, while the jagged edges convey disruption and the relentless march of time. The abstract arrangement creates a visual narrative of push and pull—each pixel a heartbeat in the dialogue of survival and surrender.
