Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between longing and resolve. The deep charcoal and warm blood orange create emotional depth, with hot coral injecting vibrancy, signifying life against the starkness of desire. The contrast lies at the center where the cracks of longing, rendered through unexpected broken pixels, disrupt the otherwise flowing boundaries of resolve. Quiet spaces are introduced by hollow blue, reinforcing distance while the dominant colors of warm tones emphasize a yearning resilience, suggesting that even amidst chaos, there is the emergence of structure. The central collision carries the visual narrative of navigating through emotional currents while maintaining an axis of hope.
