Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between confusion and clarity through a contrasting palette of dark hues and soft highlights. The choice of deep blacks, dark violets, and hints of soft gold and pale green evoke a sense of layered emotional complexity. The layout follows a nearly mirrored composition with intentional disruptions, showcasing asymmetry as it flows from one corner, embodying chaotic energy transitioning into moments of clarity. The collisions of dense color blocks against the more open fields create visual noise, punctuated by soft golden accents representing clarity and calm amidst confusion.
