Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette featuring hot coral, blood orange, dust pink, and charcoal envelops the viewer in a warm yet subdued atmosphere, suggesting both desire and a sense of restraint. The layout of broken frames allows for the contrasting emotions to ebb and flow, highlighting 'near versus far' with a zigzag flow that energizes the grid. The main collision occurs in the center, where brighter accents pulse against soft corners, creating a dialogue of urgency held tightly by muted edges. The broken pixels symbolize imperfection and healing, reflecting the theme of repair amidst damage.
