Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve, realized through the palette of deep reds and soothing pale blues. The rich black serves as a boundary of chaos, while white areas suggest quiet space, highlighting moments of intimacy and distance. The central collision between dense intensity and open quiet reflects a persistent struggle, where longing stretches like a bridge over sharp contrasts, creating a rhythmic flow across the grid. The crosshatch pattern evokes yearning while the frame structure provides a solid foundation, echoing the idea of unresolved aspirations. Broken pixels introduce subtle imperfections, symbolizing scars from the journey of desire and strength.
