Artist Statement

The emotional tone is captured in the struggle between loneliness and connection. A dark palette represents heaviness and solitude, contrasted by brighter accents signifying fleeting connections. The arrangement introduces tension, with clustered lighter pixels suggesting warmth and connection, while the surrounding dark colors embody loneliness. This contrast and collision lead to a clear narrative of yearning amid isolation.
