Artist Statement

This composition reflects a tension between joy and grief, utilizing a palette of warm yellows and oranges against cooler deep blues and muted purples. The light and spark-like patterns symbolize joy, while the dispersed checker pattern evokes grief, embodying the collision of emotional states. The asymmetry in color placement creates a pulse-like activity, echoing the 'order vs chaos' theme, leading to intersections that suggest unresolved intersection. Such juxtapositions mirror the themes from the seed, emphasizing an abstract story of emotional conflict and balance.
