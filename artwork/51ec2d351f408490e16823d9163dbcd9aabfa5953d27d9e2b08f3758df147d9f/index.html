Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension of defiance versus submission, utilizing a palette of deep colors like black and electric blue contrasted with vibrant lime and magenta accents. The center feels defended by layers of darker colors, while the outer edges gradually fade, representing vulnerability and openness. The primary collision occurs at the center, where the embedded pulse marks from both emotions interrupt each other, suggesting a conflict; they suggest both strength and fragility. This reflects the seed's abstract qualities of dense versus empty and inward motion, with a flow that feels almost like a pulsating barrier between contrasting emotional states.
