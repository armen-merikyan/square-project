Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between envy and gratitude through a contrasting palette of warm pink and muted maroon against cool gray. The layout follows a central focus, symbolizing the inner struggle, surrounded by jittery patterns that represent noise and disruption. The placement of colors creates uneven pressure that ebbs from the center outward, suggesting rising emotions that collide with quieter moments, encapsulating the complexity of the emotional landscape inspired by the seed.
