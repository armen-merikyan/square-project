Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between longing and resolve, using a palette that juxtaposes muted purple and deep blue against bright yellows and oranges. The ‘broken pixels’ are strategically placed to create a sense of interruption within a smooth pattern that suggests both repair and damage. The main contrast occurs at the center, where warm colors collide with deeper tones, resembling a pulse amidst quiet spaces. The layout symbolizes the distance and closeness of emotional states, while the 'mirror' pattern suggests the reflective quality of the longing. Meanwhile, the 'grid' provides a structured sense of resolve, offering a rhythmic balance throughout the artwork. Overall, the chaotic yet contained arrangement embodies the struggle between serenity and urgency, capturing a narrative of unresolved emotions yet striving for repair and clarity.
