Artist Statement

This abstract piece explores the emotional tension between softness and severity through an interplay of colors that represent gentle warmth and stark coldness. The palette merges powder pink and warm white, embodying softness, with carbon black and sharp crimson evoking severity. The contrasting flows are organized in a way that allows warmer colors to dominate, preserving a sense of depth while inviting disturbances through the darker tones. The broken pixel elements introduce intentional imperfections to break the rigidity, echoing the message of emotional complexity inherent in guilt and forgiveness.
