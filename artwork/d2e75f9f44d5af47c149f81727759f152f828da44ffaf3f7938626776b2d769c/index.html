Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional conflict between clear signals and pervasive noise. The chosen palette of 'signal green' and 'electric blue' represents hope and clarity, while 'static gray' and 'dead black' embody static and interference. The layout emphasizes a central pulse surrounded by chaotic noise, creating an echoing effect. Contrasts occur in the jagged edges, where vibrant colors interject into darker fields, symbolizing disruption. The central collision of clarity and noise showcases a struggle for dominance, reflecting the seed's theme organically.
