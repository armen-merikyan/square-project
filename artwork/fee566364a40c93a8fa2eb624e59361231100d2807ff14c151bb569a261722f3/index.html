Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects both longing and resolve, highlighted by a palette that interweaves warm and cool tones. The deep wine and soft gold punctuate the ash gray and warm ivory, creating a contrast that encapsulates the collision of desires and stability. The main contrast happens in the center where rich colors collide with quieter edges, echoing the dense yet fragile nature of desires that seek fulfillment but remain incomplete. This pattern symbolizes the pulse of emotions, where lines of longing intersect a background of resolve, resulting in a textured landscape of feeling that is rich yet unfulfilled.
