Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve. The palette of hot coral and hollow blue suggests warmth clashing with coolness, symbolizing desire versus emptiness. The central collision occurs through a spiral layout where warm colors pulse against cool shades, creating a sense of urgency and brokenness, while negative space remains to echo unfulfilled desires. The broken pixels enhance the themes of repair versus damage, offering a visual representation of scars and flaws in a repetitive yet chaotic pattern.
