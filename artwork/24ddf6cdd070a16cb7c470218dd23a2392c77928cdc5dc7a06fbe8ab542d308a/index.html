Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional contrast between quiet and pressure. The palette includes muted colors like off-white and faint peach that create a calm base, while darker shades like dark navy and compressed red introduce a sense of tension. The layout emphasizes a crowded outer border with a more relaxed interior, manifesting a clash where crowded density meets spaciousness. Broken pixels introduce subtle imperfections that echo the theme of shattering pressure against stillness. The composition uses this contrast to express a narrative of tension release, where both qualities coexist in a delicate balance.
