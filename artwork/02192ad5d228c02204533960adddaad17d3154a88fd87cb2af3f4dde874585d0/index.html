Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional duality between joy and grief, captured through a contrasting color palette of bright yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples. The arrangement creates a sense of upward movement on the left, symbolizing joy and hope, while the darker hues on the right evoke a feeling of heaviness and sorrow. The intentional placement of broken pixelations introduces imperfection and instability, aligning with feelings of betrayal, creating a rhythm that echoes between extremes, ultimately inviting contemplation of resilience through asymmetric patterns.
