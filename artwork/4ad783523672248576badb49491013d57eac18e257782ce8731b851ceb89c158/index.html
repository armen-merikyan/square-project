Artist Statement

The emotional tone is characterized by a dance between restlessness and peace, embodied through opposing color placements. The palette utilizes deep blues and pale yellows to suggest a tension between darkness and light, embodying the struggle of memory versus motion. The left half employs a crosshatch pattern, hinting at a disordered pulse, while the right demonstrates a softer scar pattern symbolizing calm and tranquility. The main collision occurs diagonally, where light interacts with darker hues, creating dynamic fits of color that ripple across the canvas, echoing the themes of fragmented memory and the fluid motion of time.
