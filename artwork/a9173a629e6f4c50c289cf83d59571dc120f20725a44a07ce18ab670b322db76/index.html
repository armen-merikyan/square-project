Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between intense, chaotic energy and the tranquil, restrained aspects of calm. The palette combines striking reds and deep blacks, representing rage, with soft pale blues and whites for calmness. The main collision occurs in the center where sharp, jagged reds intersect with horizontal bands of soothing blue, creating a visual tension that embodies the turmoil described in the seed. This arrangement echoes the concept of containment versus overflow, with broken pixels introducing intentional imperfections that add depth to the chaotic harmony.
