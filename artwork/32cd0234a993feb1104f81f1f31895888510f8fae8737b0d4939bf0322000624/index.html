Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the contrast between vulnerability and strength, embodied in bold yet delicate colors. The palette of warm shadows contrasting with soft lilacs evokes intimacy and distance. The bright accents create a central tension against the dim fields, with a collision occurring in the lower center where the patchwork of strength meets the stepwell of vulnerability. The pattern reflects the introspection of safety against the risks of exposure, with broken pixels adding intentional disruptions and fractures in the composition.
