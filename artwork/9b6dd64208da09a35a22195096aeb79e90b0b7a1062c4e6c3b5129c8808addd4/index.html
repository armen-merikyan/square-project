Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional interplay between tenderness and rage through a contrasting palette. The saturated red hues symbolize the intensity of rage, while the soft pale blue represents tenderness. The layout, with clustered sections of rage interrupted by lighter colors, depicts the struggle between these emotions. The strict structure conveys a locked tension, with broken pixels symbolizing both scars and moments of vulnerability in the emotional landscape.
