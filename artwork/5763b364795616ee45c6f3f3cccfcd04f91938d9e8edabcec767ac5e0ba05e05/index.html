Artist Statement

The artwork captures a dialogue between fear and hope. The left side, steeped in dark and muted tones of black and dark violet, represents emotional pressure and tension through dense patterns. The right side contrasts this with lighter, glowing accents of soft gold and pale green, symbolizing hope. The jagged edges and broken pixels create an impression of imperfection and instability, while the split layout reflects the collision between these two emotional states. The flow from left to right accentuates the transition from a weighted density to a soft illumination, embodying the journey from pressure to release.
