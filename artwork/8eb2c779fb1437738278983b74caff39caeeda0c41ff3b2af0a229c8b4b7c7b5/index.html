Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between quiet and pressure. The color palette balances soft tones of off-white and faint peach against darker, more intense shades like dark navy and compressed red, creating a sense of depth and contrast. The top left corner is densely packed with a flood of quiet colors, giving way to a more open, chaotic lower right, echoing the concept of pressure exerting control while simultaneously inducing confusion. The layout leads the eye diagonally, capturing the essence of struggle and release within a seemingly ordered space that ultimately disrupts the viewer's perception.
