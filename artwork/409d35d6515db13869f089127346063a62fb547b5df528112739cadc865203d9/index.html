Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captures the tension between quiet and pressure, using a palette of muted and dark shades to represent density, while also introducing lighter flecks for moments of clarity. The main collision occurs at the center, where static and broken dots interact. The layout of two pulses against a negative space creates an emotional push-pull effect, mirroring the themes of the seed. This complexity captures the essence of balance and disruption without literal representation.
