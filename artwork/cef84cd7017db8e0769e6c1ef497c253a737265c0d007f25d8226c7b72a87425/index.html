Artist Statement

This artwork illustrates the emotional collision between fear and hope. The visual tension is created by contrasting dark and light colors. The palette features black and dark violet representing fear, while soft gold and pale green symbolize hope. The center holds a warm gold hue, a focal point of optimism, surrounded by jagged edges of dark colors that suggest an attack or pressure. The flow directed outward creates a sense of expansion from the center, embodying the struggle between quiet relief and intense pressure. The broken pixels add imperfection, evoking a sense of vulnerability and authenticity.
