Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tensions of joy and grief through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette blends warm yellows and oranges, representing joy, with deep blues and muted purples for grief. The upward movement of warm colors collides with the downward pull of cool tones, allowing for a dynamic interplay between expansion and contraction, reflecting the tension of emotional release. The absence of a central focal point enhances the feeling of an empty room, leaving space for interpretation while still conveying depth and layers of feeling.
