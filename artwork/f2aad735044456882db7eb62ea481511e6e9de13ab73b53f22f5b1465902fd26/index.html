Artist Statement

This abstract artwork captures the emotional battle between defiance and submission through a radial pattern representing outward force against a spiraling inward motion. The color palette, 'clarity_confusion', juxtaposes stark blacks and whites with vibrant touches of orange to symbolize moments of clarity amidst chaos. The collision primarily occurs at the center, where intense dark hues clash against lighter tones, signifying the push and pull of opposing forces. This composition resonates with themes of pressure and release, inviting the viewer to reflect on the tension within emotional landscapes.
