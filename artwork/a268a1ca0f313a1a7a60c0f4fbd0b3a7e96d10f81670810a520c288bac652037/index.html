Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between loneliness and connection through a blend of muted grays and warm sepia tones. The sparse arrangement reflects loneliness, with large negative spaces emphasizing isolation, while occasional bursts of warmth suggest fleeting moments of connection. The layout flows upward, signaling hope and escape, with pixels concentrated in the central column to create a protected core under attack from the edges, embodying the emotional conflict. Broken pixels evoke the fragility of memory, enhancing the contrast between full and hollow experiences.
