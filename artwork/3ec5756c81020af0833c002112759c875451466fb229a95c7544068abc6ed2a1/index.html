Artist Statement

This composition reflects the interplay between calm and rage. The palette of deep black, vivid red, and soft pale blue establishes a tension between explosive elements and serene spaces. The main collision occurs in the top left corner, where sharp red pixels represent fury, spilling into a quieter stretch of pale blue that seeks to contain the explosion. Broken pixels disrupt symmetry, symbolizing emotional scars and the complexity of human feeling, allowing for an energetic contrast that conveys the mood described in the seed.
