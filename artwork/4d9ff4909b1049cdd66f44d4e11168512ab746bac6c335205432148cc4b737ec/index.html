Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the duality of restlessness and peace through its asymmetric pixel distribution, with a faint center emphasizing peace surrounded by restless dark edges. The selected colors create a juxtaposition between quiet moments and tense pulsations. The broken pixel elements showcase disrupted calm, while remaining patches of light suggest fleeting memories, reinforcing the theme of conflict and resolution. The contrast between the lighter center and darker borders portrays an emotional push and pull, mirroring life's chaotic yet peaceful contrasts.
