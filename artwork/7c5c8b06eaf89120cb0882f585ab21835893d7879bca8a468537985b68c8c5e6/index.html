Artist Statement

This composition expresses the dynamic emotional tension between panic and control by juxtaposing warm and cool colors, with dense clusters of pink and red contrasted against the starkness of gray and dark maroon. The palette reflects the complex interplay of joy and grief, while the arrangement suggests a falling rain-like pattern that symbolizes the descent into chaos, interrupted by moments of order. The primary collision is between the chaotic density of the upper rows and the more sparse, controlled lower rows, representing a struggle for balance amidst emotional turmoil.
