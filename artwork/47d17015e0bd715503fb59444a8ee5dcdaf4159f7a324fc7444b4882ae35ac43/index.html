Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts tenderness’s gentle overlap with rage's explosive compression. The palette of deep reds alongside muted blacks and pale blues evokes conflicted feelings—rage represented in saturated colors, while tenderness is suggested through softer shades. The main collision occurs in the middle with a shatter pattern interspersed among flooding elements, creating an imbalance that echoes emotional conflict. The layout resembles falling pixels, echoing the rain theme, suggesting a weighty, downward flow where some pixels break, representing moments of imperfection and vulnerability amidst the storm.
