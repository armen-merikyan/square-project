Artist Statement

The artwork captures the tension between feelings of loneliness and connection through its deliberate color placement. The palette of deep navy, black, and soft dim cyan evokes a sense of isolation, while the single white pixel serves as a hopeful connection amidst the density. The layout mimics falling pixels, where some colors cluster tightly to create pressure while others leave open spaces, suggesting moments of release. The soft edges further emphasize the gentle collision of emotions and the ebb and flow of expression.
