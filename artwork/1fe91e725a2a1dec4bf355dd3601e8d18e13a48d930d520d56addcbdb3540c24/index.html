Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy between love and betrayal through a blend of warm and cool colors. The palette is dominated by reds and pinks, representing love, while grays and dark maroon convey the undercurrents of betrayal. The pattern employs a grid for the left side (love) and a maze-like approach on the right (betrayal), illustrating a clash of emotions. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, symbolizing the cracks in relationships. The layout and rising signal design evoke a sense of hope and tension, encapsulating the struggle between harmony and chaos.
