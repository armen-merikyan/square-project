Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional contrast of defiance and submission through color placement and density. The palette of muted elements like 'dust_gray' and 'faint_peach' creates a quiet yet tense backdrop, while the bold 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy' punctuate the center, representing defiance. The arrangement follows a halo void layout, emphasizing the protected yet vulnerable core against a textured, braided pattern in the edges. The broken pixels scattered throughout serve as moments of chaos, reflecting the themes of invasion and disruption.
