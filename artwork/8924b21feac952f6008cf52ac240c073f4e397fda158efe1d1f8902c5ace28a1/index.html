Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between trust and suspicion, represented through contrasting patterns of stripes (trust) and echoing disturbances (suspicion). The palette features muted tones like sepia and dust gray for a foundational warmth, punctuated by faded blue that suggests a chill of uncertainty. The main collision is at the center where dark and light transitions create a visual tension, reflecting the core theme of balance and disruption. The quiet channel from the layout invites space for contemplation, while broken pixels scatter throughout to symbolize moments of fracture within the whole.
