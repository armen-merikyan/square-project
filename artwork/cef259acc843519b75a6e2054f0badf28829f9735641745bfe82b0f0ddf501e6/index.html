Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between restlessness and peace, with the palette reflecting a melancholic wonder through muted colors juxtaposed against brighter accents. Darker colors dominate, representing the heavier themes of restlessness, while the lighter colors symbolize fleeting moments of peace. The arrangement encourages an eye movement from corner to corner, with the central horizontal line showcasing the main contrast. Various broken pixels create disruptions within the layers, echoing memories of motion and providing an abstract reflection of both stasis and disturbance.
