Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes longing with resolve, creating a tension that flows diagonally across the grid. The palette of electric blue and magenta brings vibrancy, while intervals of black ground the composition, representing both distance and intimacy. The main contrast occurs where the lattice of longing (magenta) collides with the flame of resolve (electric blue) creating a rhythm of pressure and relief. This dialogue embodies the seed's imagery as an abstract representation of emotional currents.
