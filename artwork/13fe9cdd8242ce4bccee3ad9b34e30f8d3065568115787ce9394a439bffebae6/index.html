Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between desire and restraint. The color palette, featuring red oxide, blackened plum, and muted cream, reflects the warmth of desire intertwined with the coolness of restraint. The dense center invokes a saturated pulse that competes against the sealed edges, creating a visual narrative of yearning contained. The main contrast occurs at the intersection of the rich reds and muted fields, emphasizing the collision between the intense longing and the boundaries that hold it back. Broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, echoing the imperfections of this emotional struggle. The layout pushes desire towards the center, while restraint solidifies at the edges, creating a unique interplay of colors and emotions.
