Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress. The dark palette represents the weight of past memories, while the lighter shades signify hope and forward motion. The layout follows a split vertical design, providing a visual representation of the rising vs falling dichotomy. The contrast lies in the abrupt juxtaposition of dense clusters on one side and hollow spaces on the other, creating a rhythm of interruption and flow. The 'drip' concept is integrated through gradual color transitions that evoke a sense of movement, enhancing the dynamic between holding on and releasing.
