Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between clarity and confusion using a stark palette of black, electric blue, lime, and magenta. The left side maintains a sense of symmetry with a repeating pattern while the right introduces breaks and disarray, promoting an unstable feeling. The chaos of overlapping pixels creates a pulse-like rhythm contrasted against the solid structure of the left, signifying the push and pull of emotions. The edges are carefully outlined to create a dichotomy of permeability and constriction, enhancing the visual narrative of instability versus stability.
