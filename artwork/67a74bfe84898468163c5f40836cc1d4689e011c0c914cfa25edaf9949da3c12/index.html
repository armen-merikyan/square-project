Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between exposure and safety through a split vertical layout, reflecting the duality of vulnerability and protection. The palette, derived from earthy greens and warm browns contrasted with pale skin tones and cold whites, illustrates the interaction between comfort and openness. The central focus is a blend of colors that represents an echo of pale skin, surrounded by darker hues that emphasize the safety boundary. The patterns of 'embers' and 'stepwell' collide, forming a rhythmic tension that mirrors the heartbeat of existence, where pressure meets release.
