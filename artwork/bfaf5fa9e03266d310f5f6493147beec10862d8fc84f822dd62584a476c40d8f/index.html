Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional clash between vulnerability and strength through contrast in color and pattern. The palette of muted reds and grays, resonating from the 'love_betrayal' theme, reflects the nuances of these emotions: warmth disrupted by cold. The dominant presence of dark maroon symbolizes strength, serving as a boundary against the pale pink accents that embody vulnerability. The central pixels clash, creating a focal point of tension, while the soft edges contain moments of quiet space, inviting the viewer to explore the contrast without literal interpretation.
