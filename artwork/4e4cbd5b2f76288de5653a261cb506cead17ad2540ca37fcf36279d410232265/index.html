Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the dynamic between panic and control, utilizing a palette that reflects love betrayal with rich reds and subtle grays. The outer rows manifest a chaotic, noise-like pattern that suggests panic, while the inner rows implement a radial control pattern, framing the chaos. The main contrast occurs at the boundary where the outer noise meets the stability of the inner core, echoing the idea of an empty room filled with unseen tension. Each color placement is intentional; darker shades create depth, while lighter colors emerge as sparks of hope against the chaotic backdrop, suggesting the ongoing struggle between stability and unrest.
