Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between restlessness and peace, using a palette that juxtaposes warm and cool tones. The colors, inspired by intimacy and distance, create a visual narrative echoing patches of serenity amidst disruptions. The diagonally layered edge style introduces a dynamic flow, contrasting the quiet fields with the intense halo patterns. The main collision occurs toward the center where cool blues and warm shadows intertwine, creating a sense of movement against a peaceful backdrop.
