Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between fear and hope. The color palette, featuring dark violet and soft gold, creates contrast, with dark tones emphasizing fear and the golden hues symbolizing hope. The split design highlights the division of these emotions, while the burst of colors acts as an interruption, reminiscent of a machine's heartbeat. The arrangement features a broken edge, suggesting imperfection, while the broken pixels add to the sense of chaos amidst a rising glow. This balance of light vs. weight establishes a visual rhythm, with color collisions evoking a dynamic, unresolved tension.
