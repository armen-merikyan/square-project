Artist Statement

The composition explores the emotional duality of love and betrayal through a juxtaposition of warm and cool tones, symbolizing connection and separation. The palette of pinks and maroons embodies love's warmth, while dark grays represent the cold distance of betrayal. The main contrast occurs in the interplay between the structured frames of love and the chaotic dots representing betrayal, illustrating the push and pull between these emotions. The rising pattern mimics an ascent toward hope, yet the burned edges and broken pixels evoke imperfections, echoing the reality of relationships filled with both love and pain.
