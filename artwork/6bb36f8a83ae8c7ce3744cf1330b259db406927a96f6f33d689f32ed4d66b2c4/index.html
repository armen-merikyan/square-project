Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between rage and calm, representing a visceral struggle between explosive emotions and serene spaces. The left half uses a palette of deep reds and blacks, reflecting the frantic energy of rage; the right side incorporates pale blues and whites for a soothing calm. The use of noise and flame patterns evokes chaotic clashes, while the structured split layout facilitates a defined emotional dichotomy. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, suggesting the fragility of these feelings, while the negative space maintains a balance that invites reflection amidst the chaos.
