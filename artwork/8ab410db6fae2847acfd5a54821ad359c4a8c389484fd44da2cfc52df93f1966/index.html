Artist Statement

The emotional tone here juxtaposes joy and grief, creating a narrative of tension and release. The palette fields bright yellows and muted purples, representing joy's spark against grief's heaviness. The left side follows an orderly pattern of crosshatching depicting joy, while the right displays broken dots in muted colors illustrating grief. The main contrast occurs at the center, where the structured and chaotic collide, creating a visual interplay that evokes both emotion and introspection.
