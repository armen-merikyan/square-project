Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a fragmented structure. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon conveys sentiments of love and betrayal, echoing the complexities in relationships. The outer rows represent defiance with darker shades that weigh heavily, while the inner pattern of brighter colors suggests submission's lighter emotional texture. The interplay of colors creates a dynamic push-and-pull, where the viewer perceives a dance between tension and openness, reflecting on the seed's themes of contrast and emotional complexity.
