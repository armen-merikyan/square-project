Artist Statement

The piece captures the tension between rage and calm. The palette features bright, explosive reds clashing with muted blues, representing the emotional extremes. The central collision occurs in the middle rows, where warm reds spill over cooler tones, mimicking a visual overflow. The spiral layout emphasizes movement and tension, creating an abstract sense of diagonal pressure. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, echoing the inner tumult of emotions.
