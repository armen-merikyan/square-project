Artist Statement

The emotional tone depicts an interplay between rage and calm, with brighter reds expressing overflow and compression against softer blues and whites that attempt to contain them. By using a palette of intense red and calming pale blue, the contrast reinforces a dynamic struggle. The chaotic placement of red, showcasing jagged thresholds, collides with structured rings of blue, focusing the interaction toward the center. Broken pixels are intentionally scattered, creating a disrupted flow that echoes the themes of erosion and repair. This composition captures tension through both color and form, inviting the viewer into an abstract world of emotional complexity.
