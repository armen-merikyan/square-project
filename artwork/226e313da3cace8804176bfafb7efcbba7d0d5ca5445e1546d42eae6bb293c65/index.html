Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a dance between longing and resolve, visualized through a palette reflecting vibrancy and softness. The selected colors—ripe green and acid green for tension, honey gold for warmth, and deep olive for grounding—contrast heavily with cream for a tranquil space. The main collision occurs in the center where dense colors pulse around lighter tones, creating an echo of emotional conflict. This pattern evokes a narrative of striving against the backdrop of serene acceptance, underscoring the struggle between repair and damage without literal identifiers.
