Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a tension between control and panic, highlighted by an asymmetric layout that visually suggests a subtle conflict. The chosen palette of sepia and faded blues evokes a nostalgic yet uneasy atmosphere, reminiscent of a memory that feels both comforting and distressing. The arrangement uses a split-screen influence to create a visual dichotomy, where one side is calmer and the other breaks into chaotic splashes, reflecting disruption. The broken pixels introduce imperfections that symbolize the unpredictability of emotion, while maintaining enough negative space to prevent overwhelming density, suggesting the breath between moments of tension.
