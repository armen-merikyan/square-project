Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between longing and resolve through the interplay of muted grays with vibrant reds and pinks. The color palette captures emotional conflict: the soft warmth of longing is juxtaposed with the compressed strength of resolve. Bold strokes represent broken pixels that act as intentional disruptions, suggesting moments of fracture alongside attempts at repair. The main contrast occurs in the center where gray and red collide, symbolizing the coexistence of vulnerability and determination. The upward flow of color reflects a rising signal, embodying a journey from uncertainty to empowerment.
