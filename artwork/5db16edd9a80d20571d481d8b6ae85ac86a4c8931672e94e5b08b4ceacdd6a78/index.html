Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends loneliness and connection, creating a tension between isolation and warmth. The palette features hues like burnt orange and dark red, representing both the ancient fire of connection and the charcoal depths of loneliness. The contrast emerges sharply where warm colors intersect cold darks, showcasing moments of connection amidst vast emptiness, with broken pixels evoking the fragility of these feelings. The layout emphasizes division with four columns each for loneliness (left) and connection (right), creating interplay and collision. The vertical split suggests inner vs outer worlds, enhancing the narrative of fear and comfort. Negative space peppered throughout allows for breath, promoting an introspective atmosphere.
