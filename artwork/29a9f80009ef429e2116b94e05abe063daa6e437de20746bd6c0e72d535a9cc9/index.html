Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between trust and suspicion, illustrated through contrasting colors. The palette of soft gold and deep wine suggests warmth and fragility; while ash gray instills a sense of caution. The central area has higher color density reflecting pressure, with broken pixels scattered to create imperfection, symbolizing the fractures in trust. The collision is prominently placed in the center where warm textures interrupt cooler tones, echoing the push and pull of emotional dynamics.
