Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the contrasting emotional tones of restlessness and peace. The palette features deep blacks and vibrant electric blues, creating a juxtaposition of calm undercurrents and frenetic energy. The collision between light and dark occurs through the arrangement of colors, capturing the tension of restless movement against an expanse of quiet space. The layout reflects scattered repair, portraying moments of disruption and light amidst the shadows. This tension speaks to the vulnerability within calmness, emphasizing the intricate dance between movement and stillness.
