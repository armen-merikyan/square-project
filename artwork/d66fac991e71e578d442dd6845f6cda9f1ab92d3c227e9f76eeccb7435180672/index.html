Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between restlessness and peace, utilizing a palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple to signify warm versus cool emotional tensions. The contrast lies in the bright patches that interrupt and repair a darker field, symbolizing attempts to restore balance amidst chaos. The collision of colors is concentrated towards the center, creating a dynamic pulse that conveys unease yet is patched by serene accents. The broken pixels represent moments of disruption, contributing to a narrative of navigating through conflicting emotions.
