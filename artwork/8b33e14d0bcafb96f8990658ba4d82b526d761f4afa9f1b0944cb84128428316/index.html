Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between clarity and chaos, with a palette emphasizing the duality of clean signal (signal_green, electric_blue) against a backdrop of interference (static_gray, dead_black) with accents of hot_magenta. The main collision happens centrally, where the rhythmic pulse of green and blue is interrupted by erratic bursts of magenta, suggesting a dance of memory amidst noise. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, enhancing the feeling of fluctuation and tension, signifying moments of clarity within a fragmented spectrum.
