Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tone of vulnerability versus strength through a balance of warm and cool colors. The palette features warm clay and shadow tones colliding with cool blues and distant lilacs, representing the push and pull of opposing emotions. The main collision occurs in the center, where the lattice pattern of strength overlaps with the grid of vulnerability, suggesting a flux of pressure and release. The placement of broken pixels introduces an element of imperfection, symbolizing the cracks in strength and the delicate nature of vulnerability, creating a dynamic visual narrative.
