Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional spectrum of relief against dread through contrasting color dynamics and an asymmetrical arrangement. A palette of deep blacks and violets conveys a sense of weight and tension, while soft gold and pale green add hints of lightness and hope. The layout is intentionally asymmetrical, embodying a battle between structured order and random breaks, reflecting the seed's themes of symmetry and fractures. The pattern suggests a push and pull between dense, darker areas and scattered, lighter sections, evoking a sense of unresolved emotion.
