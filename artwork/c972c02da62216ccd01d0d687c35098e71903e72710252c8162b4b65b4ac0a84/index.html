Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between stillness and compression, utilizing a palette that captures subtle contrasts. The dark navy represents pressure and density, while the faint peach embodies moments of quiet release. The main collision occurs at the center, where static patterns form a dense cluster that begins to break towards the edges, suggesting an escape from overwhelming pressure into spacious clarity. The arrangement flows diagonally, creating an argument between hard edges and softer areas, reflecting the seed's narrative of shattering against a tide.
