Artist Statement

This composition conveys a delicate balance between longing and resolve, illustrated through the use of warm and cool colors that collide yet coexist. The palette of powder pink and sharp crimson evokes a sense of urgency, contrasted by the stability of carbon black and steel gray, mirroring the complexity of human emotion. The patterns of 'hinge' and 'constellation' create a rhythmic separation, where small centers of pulsating energy play against a quiet background, suggesting a conversation between repair and damage, further underscored by the feathered edges that let color bleed into one another. This arrangement manifests the idea of threads pulling apart yet connected, emphasizing the balance of desire and determination.
