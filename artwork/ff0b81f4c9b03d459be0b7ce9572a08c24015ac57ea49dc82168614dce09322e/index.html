Artist Statement

This abstract work encapsulates the emotional dance between clarity and confusion using a contrasting palette. The chosen colors draw from a 'broken_memory' theme, featuring muted tans and grays conflicting with the undefined edges of deep blues. The pattern of falling pixels creates a visual rhythm that evokes a sense of weightlessness against the dense confusion of stripes at the center, signifying the breakdown of clear imagery. The asymmetry creates tension, where the chaotic aspects of the design interrupt the potential for harmony, reflecting the theme of 'symmetry vs break.'
