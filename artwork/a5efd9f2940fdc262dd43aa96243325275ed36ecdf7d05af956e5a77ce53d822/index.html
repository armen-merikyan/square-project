Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates a vivid emotional tension between fear and hope. The top half uses a monochromatic palette of dark colors signifying fear – creating a feeling of pressure and enclosed space, with sharp contrasts denoted by pixels clustered tightly. The bottom half introduces lighter hues, representing hope, with scattered patterns that evoke upward movement and light. This arrangement suggests a rising signal amidst a storm, reflecting the complexity and duality present in the seed. The use of broken pixels adds imperfection, embodying the jagged experience of navigating fear and hope.
