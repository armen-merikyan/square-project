Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, expressed through a mix of dark hues and intermittent bright spots. The color palette, influenced by the 'lonely_signal', includes navy and black for depth, while white and dim cyan create striking highlights, introducing moments of clarity amidst noise. The layout echoes falling pixels, with colors cascading down in a top-to-bottom flow, characterized by broken symmetry and sharp contrasts. The use of negative space allows feelings of both isolation and potential connection, as certain pixels break the uniformity, creating a sense of disruption and unresolved motion that resonates with the seed's themes of confusion and clarity.
