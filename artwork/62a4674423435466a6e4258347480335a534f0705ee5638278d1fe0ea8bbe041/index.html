Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between feelings of loneliness and connection, using a palette of dark and soft colors to represent these contrasts. The central pixels are more muted, suggesting a sense of isolation, while the outer pixels utilize warmer tones to represent the yearning for connection. The top-weighted layout emphasizes this emotional descent, where negative space reflects loneliness, and clusters echo the desire for interconnection. The crosshatch pattern introduces a rhythmic quality, suggesting a pulsating heartbeat amidst silence.
