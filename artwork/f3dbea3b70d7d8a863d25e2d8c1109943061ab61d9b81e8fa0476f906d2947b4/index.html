Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the delicate balance between joy and grief, reflected in a palette of warm yellows and muted purples. Bright yellows represent joy's spark, while muted purples convey a sense of grief. The layout, with joy starting from the bottom left corner, creates a diagonal invasion of brightness, contrasting with the deeper, heavier colors that crowd the upper right. This shift suggests a collision of vibes between joy's light and grief's weight, with the edge style emphasizing this transition through a coarse, hard texture. Broken pixels enhance the feeling of fragmentation, allowing the emotions to bleed into one another, creating a tension that is both unsettling and beautiful.
