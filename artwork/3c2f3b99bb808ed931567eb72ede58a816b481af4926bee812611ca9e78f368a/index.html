Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes nostalgia with progress, reflecting on how memories can clash with modernity. The chosen palette—black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green—symbolizes a blend of muted historical tones with accents that hint at hope and growth. The grid-like arrangement represents stability and nostalgia, while broken pixels in dark violet and gold create a sense of fragmentation, echoing the struggle between the comfort of the past and the uncertainty of the future. The bottom-weighted layout emphasizes the heaviness of memories in contrast to the soaring aspirations of progress, with abrupt interruptions suggesting conflict and unresolved tension between these emotions.
