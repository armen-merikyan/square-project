Artist Statement

The emotional tone evokes a struggle between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise). The chosen palette of navy for depth, black for isolation, and dim cyan for a muted pulse emphasizes the contrast of structured paths against unsettling disruptions. The main collision occurs in the center where the structured crosshatch pattern (signal) meets the irregular ladder pattern (noise), implying an intrusion into clarity, while the broken pixels serve as intentional scars of disruption. The layout suggests falling pixels, reflecting a rain-like descent of chaotic memory against the backdrop of static calm, effectively portraying the theme of interference against clarity.
