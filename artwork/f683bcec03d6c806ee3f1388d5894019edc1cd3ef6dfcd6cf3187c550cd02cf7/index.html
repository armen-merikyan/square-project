Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between the stability of certainty (represented by deep blacks and crisp whites) and the unpredictability of curiosity (seen in the lighter, swirling colors). The palette of black, pale blue, and white evokes a stark contrast suggesting both tension and calm. The main collision occurs at the center, where chaotic impressions of pale blue disrupt the surrounding order of black, symbolizing the internal conflict. The seed's qualities of 'cage' and 'rising signal' inspired structured patterns that echo a sense of containment versus freedom.
