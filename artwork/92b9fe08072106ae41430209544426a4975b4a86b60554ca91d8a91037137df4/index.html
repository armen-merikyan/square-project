Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional duel between rage and calm. The palette consists of stark contrasts with vivid red representing rage and muted pale blue representing calm. The dynamic clash occurs centrally, where jagged red pixels loosely interweave with serene blue 'steps.' This chaotic interplay echoes the erratic energy of signal versus noise, where form erupts unpredictably amidst quietude, embodying both tension and tranquility. The broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, reinforcing the emotional narrative of chaotic convergence against a calm backdrop.
