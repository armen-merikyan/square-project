Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between fear and hope, depicted through contrasting dark and light colors. The palette emphasizes the duality: black and dark violet representing fear with their oppressive weight, while soft gold and pale green signify hope, offering a luminous counterpoint. The main collision occurs at the center, where upward-flowing hope interacts with the surrounding pressure of fear. The composition's jagged edges and a central pixel mimic the turmoil of conflicting emotions, while the broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, hinting at the fragility amidst struggle.
