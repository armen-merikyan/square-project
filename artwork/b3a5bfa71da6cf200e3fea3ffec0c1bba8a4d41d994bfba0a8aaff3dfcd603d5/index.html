Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a balance between vulnerability and strength, symbolizing the delicate interplay of rigidity and freedom. The palette, named 'melancholy_wonder', utilizes soft cyan and muted plum to convey calmness contrasted with lavender gray representing encroaching strength. The core conflict occurs centrally, where strong rippled patterns (strength) collide with more fragile veil-like distributions (vulnerability), suggesting emotional tension. Negative space is left intentionally to evoke a sense of emptiness amid the pressure, reminiscent of the flood against an unwavering stack.
