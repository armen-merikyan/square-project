Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys both loneliness and connection, reflected in the use of harsh contrasts alongside softer hues. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue suggests a sense of nostalgia and broken memory. The arrangement shows clusters of darker colors that create pressure in the center, interrupted by lighter colors on the periphery, illustrating the collision between isolation and a yearning for connection. The broken pixels enhance the 'imperfection' of memory, maintaining negative space as a metaphor for absence.
