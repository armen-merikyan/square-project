Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between tenderness and rage, utilizing a palette that contrasts the starkness of black and deep red with the softness of pale blue and white. This encapsulates dissonance and subtle quietude in the artwork. The main contrast occurs in the central pixels, where jagged red shapes collide with a gentle blue field, suggesting a break from calm to chaos. The abstract pattern reflects a fluid shift of emotions, with broken pixels indicating imperfection and the unpredictable nature of feelings—perfectly embodying the seed's themes without literal representation.
