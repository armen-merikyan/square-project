Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes awe against terror, creating a tension-filled composition. The palette of deep blues, muted purples, and warm yellows symbolizes the fight between calmness and underlying chaos. The use of soft edges and broken pixels introduces a sense of imperfection and memory, mirroring the 'broken line' shape seed. The main contrast occurs between warm and cool colors, suggesting emotional collision while the layout flows from bottom to top, embodying a journey from stability to instability.
