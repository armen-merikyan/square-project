Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise), creating a dynamic tension. The palette combines deep navy and shadowy black to represent noise, with singular accents of white and dim cyan illustrating the moments of signal clarity. The primary collision occurs in the vertical split, where muted chaos layers meet sharp accents, illustrating the constant battle of interference against a fleeting readable pulse. This composition captures the essence of the seed by evoking a sense of layered complexity and interruption, reminiscent of memories being overshadowed by static noise.
