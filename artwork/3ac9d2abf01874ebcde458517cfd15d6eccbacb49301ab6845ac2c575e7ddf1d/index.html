Artist Statement

This piece evokes the emotional clash of defiance and submission, with dark colors symbolizing resistance and light tones representing yielding. The palette transitions from deep black to pale blue, forging a contrast between heaviness and lightness. The arrangement of colors from left to right illustrates the struggle as bold frames of black push against gentle flames of pale blue, creating a tension that resonates with the seed. The central focus is marked by clusters of deep colors, denoting density and weight, while the edges are more dispersed, suggesting an open-ended resolution. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, mirroring the conflicts inherent in the emotional dynamics of love and betrayal.
