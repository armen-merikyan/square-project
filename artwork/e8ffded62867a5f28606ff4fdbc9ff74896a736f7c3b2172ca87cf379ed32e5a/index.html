Artist Statement

This artwork conveys a struggle between nostalgia and progress through contrasting color patterns. The sepia and faded blue palette reflects a sense of memory and movement towards the future, creating a dynamic yet contemplative atmosphere. The upper half features cool tones moving upward, symbolizing growth and forward momentum, while the lower half employs darker, muted tones that suggest the weight of the past. An intentional distribution of negative space allows for a visual breathing room, reinforcing the theme of balance amidst two opposing emotional forces. The broken pixel elements create texture, symbolizing imperfections in memory, adding depth to the experience of nostalgia without literal representation.
