Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the clash between defiance and submission through a dynamic interaction of colors. The palette of pale mint and soft sky suggests a sense of calm, while storm gray and near black introduce pressure and tension. The intersecting veils and stripes create a collision at the center—emphasizing defiance within a framework that attempts to submit. The asymmetrical distribution of colors ensures that the piece maintains visual interest while also suggesting a narrative of struggle and surrender.
