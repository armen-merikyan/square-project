Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dichotomy where dark, weighty forms represent control while lighter colors signify surrender. The palette, inspired by 'hunger_restraint', uses muted tones like 'blackened_plum' and 'iron_gray' for the dominant control area, while 'small_orange' and 'muted_cream' introduce a soft, yielding element. The collision occurs centrally, creating a textured boundary between dense and open spaces. The layout's diagonal flow underscores the tension arising from control versus freedom, embodying the conflict inherent in the seed's themes. The broken pixels subtly interrupt patterns, reinforcing the sense of struggle and imperfection inherent to the experience.
