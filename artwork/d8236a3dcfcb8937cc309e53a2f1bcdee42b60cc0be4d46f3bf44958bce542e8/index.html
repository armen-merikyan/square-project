Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between defiance and submission, where a central, darker hue represents defiance surrounded by softer, lighter tones that symbolize submission. The jagged edges create a chaotic border, while the dense lattice pattern in the center battles with the flowing tide-like pattern in the outer regions, reinforcing the contrast and tension prominently. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue reflects a muted memory, suggesting the weight of the themes and an unresolved emotional cadence, enhancing the abstract narrative without depicting literal forms.
