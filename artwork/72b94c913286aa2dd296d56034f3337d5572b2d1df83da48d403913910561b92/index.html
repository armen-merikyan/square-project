Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between quietness and pressure through a juxtaposition of dense colors and softer tones. The dominant dark navy and compressed red symbolize pressure and intensity, while faint peach and off-white provide a soothing contrast. The layout follows a pulsating form from corner to center, embodying the idea of two small centers interacting across negative space. The broken pixels disrupt the flow, echoing impermanence and fragility, aligning with the themes of clarity and confusion. Overall, it presents a complex narrative of tension, release, and the dance between emotional states.
