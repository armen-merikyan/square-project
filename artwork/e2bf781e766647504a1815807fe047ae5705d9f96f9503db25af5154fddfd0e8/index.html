Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between trust and suspicion through a palette of vibrant and muted colors. The juxtaposition of electric blue and lime as vibrant accents plays against the deep black, creating a stark contrast symbolizing uncertainty and watchfulness. The arrangement includes broken pixels, representing flaws and imperfections in trust, while the structured lines suggest clarity and boundaries. The collision of patterns—'glitch' and 'split_screen' type arrangements—symbolizes the struggle between feeling secure and feeling vulnerable, fostering an emotional dialogue within the grid.
