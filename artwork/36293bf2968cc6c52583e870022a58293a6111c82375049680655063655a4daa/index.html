Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between vulnerability and strength through color contrast and placement. The palette of icy blues, stark blacks, and soft whites creates a visual dialogue reminiscent of fragility against a darkening weight. The central collision of the pale center, combined with broken dots and an overall spiral pressure layout, conveys a sense of interruption and reflection amidst chaos, mirroring the emotional weight of the seed's themes. The use of negative space allows for a breath, underscoring the fragility inherent in strength.
