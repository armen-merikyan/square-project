Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional clash between defiance and submission, reflected through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette—warm sepia and muted gray tones—provides a somber backdrop, while the interaction of noise (defiance) and static (submission) suggests the push and pull of these themes. The central area is bold and dark, indicating defiance, while the surrounding space softly recedes, symbolizing submission. Quiet colors balance against sharper edges, creating a visual tension that encapsulates the seed's essence.
