Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between ephemeral sparks and enduring ash through its contrasting colors and dynamic layout. The warmer tones of rose clay and lamp gold evoke brief, lively moments, while the cooler distant lilac and cool blue suggest the weight of memory and quiet aftermath. The falling pixels pattern symbolizes the descent from a peak of intensity into reflective calm. The center features a jagged interruption where warm and cool collide, representing the struggle between resilience and fragility.
