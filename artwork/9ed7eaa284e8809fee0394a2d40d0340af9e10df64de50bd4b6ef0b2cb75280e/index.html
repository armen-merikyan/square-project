Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between warmth and coolness through a palette of muted and vibrant colors. The warm hues represent 'spark,' creating sudden bursts against a backdrop of dusty, soft 'ash' shades. The main contrast occurs in the center, where the pressure bands of warmer colors collide with jagged edges of cooler hues, symbolizing the struggle between hunger and restraint. The layout of stacked bands reflects layers of memory and emotion, suggesting both resilience and fragility while adhering to the theme of broken memory vs. movement.
