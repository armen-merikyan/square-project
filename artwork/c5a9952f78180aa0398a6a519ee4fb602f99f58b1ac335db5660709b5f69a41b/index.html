Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between clarity and confusion. The deep blacks and dark violets evoke a sense of confusion, while the soft gold and pale green represent clarity. The structured chaos of the crack pattern against a solid backdrop creates a feeling of conflict, as rigid forms collide with flowing movement, giving the impression of a fractured symmetry. The edge style introduces burnt elements, enhancing the feeling of tension and interruption in the composition, suggesting the pulse of turbulent emotions.
