Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional contrast between nostalgia and progress through a grid that suggests a balance between past memories and future aspirations. The palette of muted purple, deep blue, yellow, and orange reflects the 'joy_grief' theme, where warm and cool tones collide, representing the echoes of nostalgia and the brightness of progress. Interruption occurs at the midline, where darker hues sink below lighter, creating a dynamic tension. Broken pixels disrupt the symmetry, enhancing the illusion of imperfection and the struggle between the retained past and the striving forward. Each cluster of colors drips into its neighbor, promoting feelings of weight and flow, capturing the essence of conflict within the artwork.
