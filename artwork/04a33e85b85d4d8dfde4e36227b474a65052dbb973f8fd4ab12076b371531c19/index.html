Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts tenderness and rage, depicted through a palette balancing muted and saturated colors. The colors #FF6347 (red) and #87CEFA (pale blue) represent rage and tenderness, respectively, with a collision occurring at the center through ripple and shatter patterns. The layout showcases a sense of space, emphasizing the emotional duality as the softer elements break into sharper edges, suggesting a quiet tension within a chaotic environment. Broken pixels intentionally disrupt the smoothness, embodying the struggle between calmness and intensity as suggested by the seed.
