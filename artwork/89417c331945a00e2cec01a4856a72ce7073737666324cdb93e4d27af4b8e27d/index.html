Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between loneliness and connection, revealing a struggle for balance. The chosen palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold reflects the warmth of ancient fires juxtaposed with cold isolation. The center is dense with color clashes, representing the burst of emotion, while the edges fade into softer colors, creating a contrast between hidden and exposed elements. The grid and maze patterns intertwine, emphasizing the conflict of structure versus chaos, leading to echoes of warmth amidst loneliness.
