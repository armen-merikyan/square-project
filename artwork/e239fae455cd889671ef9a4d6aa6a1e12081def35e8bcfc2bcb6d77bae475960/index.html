Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the struggle between relief and dread, where warmth clashes with heaviness. The chosen palette of warm yellows and muted blues reflects joy and grief, establishing a psychological dichotomy. Main contrasts occur at the intersections of bold, warm hues against expansive darker fields, creating a visual echo of struggle and resolution. The pattern flows from dense to lighter strokes, suggesting an upward motion while acknowledging breaks in symmetry as a nod to the mentioned cracks and breaks, enhancing the complexity birthed from the seed.
