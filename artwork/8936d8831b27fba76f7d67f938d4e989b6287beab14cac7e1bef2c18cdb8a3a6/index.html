Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through vibrant contrasts and patterns. The palette of lime, electric blue, magenta, and black reflects energy and depth, evoking feelings of warmth and isolation. The contrast between the deep blacks and bright colors suggests a collision between isolation (black) and the potential for connection (the vivid colors), with the structured grid representing the loneliness, while the burst of bright colors reflects hopeful connections. The flow symbolizes a rising signal towards connection, yet maintains a deliberate negative space to reflect isolation.
