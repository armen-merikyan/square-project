Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional tension between rage and calm, utilizing a palette of stark blacks, whites, and soft blues to enhance this dichotomy. The contrast happens sharply, with darker colors representing rage colliding against the calm, pale blues, suggesting a fragile peace disrupted by intensity. The seed's abstract qualities informed a swirling, fragmented pattern mimicking the irregularity of emotions, creating a visual narrative of collision and release.
