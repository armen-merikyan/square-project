Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, captured visually through contrasting colors. The selected palette of ripe green, acid green, and honey gold suggests a spectrum of resilience (strength) against the soft cream, which emulates vulnerability. The inner 4x4 area, depicting strength, bursts forth with repetition in a dot pattern representing stability, while the outer edges are marred by flame patterns demonstrating fragility and disruption, reflecting the seed's theme of collision and interplay. The main contrast occurs between the vivid inner core and the muted outer, signifying a dynamic boundary between two emotions.
