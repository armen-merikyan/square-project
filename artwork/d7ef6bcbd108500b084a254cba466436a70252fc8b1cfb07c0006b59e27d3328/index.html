Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tumultuous contrast between restlessness and peace, symbolizing a struggle for balance. The selected palette of vibrant signal green and electric blue offsets the somber dead black and static gray, embodying both pulse and stillness. The main collision occurs centrally, where the braiding of restless colors interrupts the peace-like stacked colors. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, reinforcing the narrative of motion against stillness, mirroring anxious memories juxtaposed with tranquil thoughts.
