Artist Statement

This composition embodies the contrasting emotions of tenderness and rage. The palette, featuring saturated red, pale blue, and muted blacks, creates an emotional tug-of-war. The bright colors dominate one clustering edge, where the explosive energy of rage collides with tender interactions of the blue and black. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, reflecting inner conflicts and hidden guilt. Overall, the arrangement flows from a dense, chaotic cluster to a quieter space, representing the climb from pressure to release.
