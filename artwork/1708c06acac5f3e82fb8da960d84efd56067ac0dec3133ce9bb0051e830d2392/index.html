Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission by utilizing contrasting color placements. The left side, embodying defiance, showcases stronger and darker hues that evoke a sense of resilience. It contrasts sharply with the right side, representing submission through softer and lighter tones, suggesting withdrawal. The center serves as the collision point, where a flicker of brightness breaks through the surrounding darkness, signaling tension and interruption. This arrangement follows the emotional dynamics outlined in the seed, reflecting the interplay of strength and surrender, pressure and relief.
