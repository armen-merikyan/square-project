Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between 'emptiness' and 'desire' through a palette of muted sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue. The chosen colors contrast sharply yet harmoniously, suggesting the inner struggle of noise versus silence. The layout begins in the top left corner, expanding diagonally, embodying the idea of 'corner invasion' where quietness yields to emotional pressure. Broken pixels add an imperfect, nostalgic quality, reinforcing the memory theme, while the negative space remains significant, enhancing the feeling of desire amidst emptiness.
