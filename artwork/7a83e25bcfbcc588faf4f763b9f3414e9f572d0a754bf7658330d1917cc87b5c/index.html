Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between panic and control. The palette of navy, black, and muted cyan expresses the depth and weight of these feelings, with stark contrasts illustrating the collision of chaos and order. The outer edge of the grid represents control, while the inner signifies panic through disruption and noise. The arrangement showcases broken pixels to symbolize imperfections, embracing the theme of fragmentation. This results in a swirling pattern of contrast, evoking both discomfort and resolution.
