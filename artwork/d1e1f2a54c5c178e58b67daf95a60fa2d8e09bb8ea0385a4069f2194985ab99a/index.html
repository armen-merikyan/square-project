Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The palette of navy, black, single white, and dim cyan captures loneliness and signal loss, creating a deeply resonant atmosphere. Dark colors dominate the edges, while white pixels across the center evoke clarity, resembling shards of hope breaking through confusion. The layout suggests downward flow, mirroring how thoughts descend from chaos to coherence, while the jagged edge style introduces a sense of fragmentation and dynamism. The broken pixels serve as imperfections that reflect the complexity of emotions rather than a smooth narrative, highlighting the conflict within the title's themes.
