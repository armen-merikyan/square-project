Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between trust and suspicion using a contrasting palette of soft and harsh colors. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the soft glow of warm white interacts with the sharp crimson, suggesting a struggle between openness and defensiveness. The placement of darker tones creates a centered rhythm, embodying the push and pull of trust's comfort against suspicion's watchfulness, framed by a boundary of deep black that signifies a boundary or pressure.
