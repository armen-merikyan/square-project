Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between loneliness and connection through color and pattern. The warm palette of burnt orange, dark red, and gold reflects ancient fire, symbolizing warmth and coldness. The left side features a sparse crosshatch pattern, embodying isolation, while the right bursts with spark-like connections, creating a contrast between density and emptiness. The jagged edges and broken pixels add emotional imperfection, emphasizing the complexity of human emotions amid a structured grid layout.
