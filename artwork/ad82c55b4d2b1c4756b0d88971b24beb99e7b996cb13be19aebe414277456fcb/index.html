Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a stark contrast of colors and patterns. The palette, featuring electric blue and hot magenta, denotes defiance with angular lattice forms, while the softer tones of static gray and dead black represent submission in a broken frame layout. The layout flows diagonally, suggesting a rise amidst chaos. The central clash reflects conflict and resolution, while the jagged edges evoke uneven borders, echoing unease yet inviting exploration of boundaries.
