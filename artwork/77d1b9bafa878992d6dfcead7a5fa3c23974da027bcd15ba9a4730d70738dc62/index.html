Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a duality of longing and resolve, expressed through color contrasts of soft warmth against sharp severity. The palette blends powder pinks and warm whites with the starkness of carbon black and sharp crimson, evoking a sense of both tenderness and urgency. The main contrast lies at the center where warm colors collide with structured, dark edges, illustrating the push and pull between hope and restraint. The arrangement captures a flow that zigzags between flooded softness and haloed strength, with deliberate broken pixels providing signs of mending amidst the tumultuous backdrop of emotion.
