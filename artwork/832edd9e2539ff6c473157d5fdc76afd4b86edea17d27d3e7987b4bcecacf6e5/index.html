Artist Statement

This artwork conveys a sense of emotional tension between restlessness and peace through the interplay of colors and patterns. The palette, featuring muted grays and soft peach, evokes quiet pressure while allowing sharp contrasts of deep navy and compressed red to create pulses of intensity. The asymmetry created by the diagonal arrangement enhances the feeling of conflict between the upward motion of peace and the downward gravity of restlessness, inviting the viewer to engage with the tension in the piece.
