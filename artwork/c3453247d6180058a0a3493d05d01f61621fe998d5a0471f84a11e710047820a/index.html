Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette reflects a machine-like emotional spectrum, combining cool electric blue and lime for connection, contrasted against deep black, suggesting isolation. The central focus is painted with concentrated bursts of color, yet surrounded by a predominance of negative space that conveys loneliness. The bottom rows intertwine isolation and connection through patterns of alternating blocks and subtle echoes in hues, reflecting the complexity of human emotions. Centering and offset placements create a dynamic interplay, making space feel both exposed and hidden. The variations echo human experiences of connection amidst isolation, embodying the essence of the seed's thematic contrasts.
