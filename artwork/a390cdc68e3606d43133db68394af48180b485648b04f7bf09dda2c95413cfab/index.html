Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional dichotomy of trust and suspicion. The colors mirror this tension: pale aqua and clear white symbolize trust, while alarm red and ink blue introduce undercurrents of suspicion. The main contrast occurs centrally, where the soft and hard tones collide, creating a visual echo of the seed's themes. The barcode pattern represents suspicion, swirling around the stable stack of trust colors, suggesting a path of complexity through quiet negative spaces. This arrangement reflects an underlying narrative of resilience amidst fragility.
