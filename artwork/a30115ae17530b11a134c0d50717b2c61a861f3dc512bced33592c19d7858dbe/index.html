Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the oscillation between restlessness and peace, where dense clusters of black and dark blue symbolize restlessness, fighting against quiet expanses of white and ice blue that represent peace. The patterns collide in the center of the grid, creating a dynamic tension. The clean white acts as a calming field, while the warning orange accents interrupt, symbolizing moments of unrest. This collision conveys memories jostling against tranquil emotions, echoing the seed's themes of contrast and depth across the canvas.
