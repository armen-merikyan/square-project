Artist Statement

This abstract artwork captures the emotional interplay between longing and resolve. The palette features warm ivory and soft gold to embody an underlying warmth and comfort, contrasting with deep wine and ash gray to articulate the weight of longing and the sharpness of resolve. The outer border, following the broken dots pattern, represents longing, creating a textured frame filled with disruption. The inner 4x4 grid utilizes a barcode pattern that signifies order amidst chaos, symbolizing resolve. The interactions between chaotic outer spheres and structured inner core suggest a dynamic tension, illuminated by lit edges that evoke a sense of urgency intertwined with serenity. The careful placement of broken pixels emphasizes the imperfection inherent in this emotional struggle, inviting contemplation.
