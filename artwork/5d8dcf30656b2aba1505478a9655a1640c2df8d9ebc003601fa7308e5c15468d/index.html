Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the delicate balance between trust and suspicion. The palette, featuring warm brown and shelter green contrasted with cold white and open blue, symbolizes the warmth of trust against the chill of suspicion. The dominant checker pattern on the left suggests an organized, trusting space, while the echoing dots on the right introduce a sense of fragmented uncertainty. The main collision occurs vertically, creating a visual tension that echoes the dichotomy expressed in the seed. The use of broken pixels adds to the sense of imperfection, enhancing the narrative and creating emotional depth.
