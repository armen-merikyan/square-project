Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional duality of restlessness and peace, exploring the contrast through a curated palette of muted colors representing 'broken memory'. The palette juxtaposes dense dark shades with lighter highlights to convey conflict. The diagonal arrangement of brighter hues against richer darks suggests the tension of movement versus stillness. The uneven pixel distribution and broken edges amplify feelings of disturbance and partial resolution within the piece.
