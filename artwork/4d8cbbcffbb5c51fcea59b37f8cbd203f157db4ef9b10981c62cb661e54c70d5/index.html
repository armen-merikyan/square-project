Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy between pressure and quiet. The palette features off-white and faint peach to embody the serene, while dark navy and compressed red evoke a sense of intensity. The main contrast resides in the central diagonal where the two emotional states collide, blending warm and cool tones to showcase their tension. The chosen patterns create a dynamic flow, with the densely packed constellation pattern interrupting the quieter ash field, inviting the viewer to explore the interplay between order and disruption that mirrors the seed's narrative of conflict and clarity amidst chaos.
