Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the juxtaposition of loneliness and connection through a balance of dense contrast and expansive negative space. The palette uses dark navy and soft dim cyan to portray a deeply introspective feeling, with bursts of white that suggest moments of connection amidst shadows. The main contrast occurs in the center, where dark tones collide against the bright core, reflecting the tension between hidden emotions and exposed vulnerability. The arrangement forms a grid of noise in the outer regions and an inner frame suggesting connection, encapsulating the essence of the seed through abstract patterns that embody emotional depth.
