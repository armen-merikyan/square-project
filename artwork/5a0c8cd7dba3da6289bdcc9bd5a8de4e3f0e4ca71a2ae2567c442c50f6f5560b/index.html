Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between clarity (signal) and distortion (noise). The palette features 'signal green' and 'electric blue' for clarity, contrasted by 'static gray' and 'dead black' to represent noise. The layout and flow emphasize a hollow center surrounded by energetic disruption, where clean pulses are interrupted by static interference. The collision occurs at the center, illustrating the tension of maintaining a clear path amidst surrounding chaos.
