Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a battle between longing and resolve, represented through a palette of soft gold and deep wine to create warmth reminiscent of intimacy and determined strength. The contrast is most evident at the center where the warm gold clashes with deeper tones, symbolizing the struggle. The diagonal staggered arrangement offers movement akin to a ladder against a fault line, imposing a narrative of conflict and resolution, while allowing for quiet fields to create breathing space. The inclusion of broken pixels signifies the imperfections inherent in navigating emotions.
