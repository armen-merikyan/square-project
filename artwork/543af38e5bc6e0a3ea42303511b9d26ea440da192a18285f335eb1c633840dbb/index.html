Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between desire and restraint, expressed through a dynamic palette that leans into saturation and muted tones. The bright coral and blood orange evoke a sense of heat and urgency, while charcoal and hollow blue introduce a cool containment. This pattern emerges through a central collision at the corner where warm colors overflow, symbolizing desire, against the muted static marks that represent restraint, illustrating a powerful push and pull. The careful placement of colors creates echoes of interaction and interruption, emphasizing the complexity of the emotional landscape.
