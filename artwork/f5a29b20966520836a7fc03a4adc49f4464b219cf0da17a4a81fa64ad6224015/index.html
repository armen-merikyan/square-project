Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of restlessness and peace, using a palette that reflects muted warmth contrasted with darker, intense shades. The collision of these emotions is positioned centrally within the square, where warmer hues create islands amid a turbulent, darker backdrop, suggesting a struggle between quiet moments and chaotic memories. The patterns of 'hinge' and 'islands' interweave to represent the passage of time and emotional motion, while broken pixels interrupt the flow, embodying the imperfections of memory. This artwork evokes a sense of dynamic balance, inviting contemplation of peace amidst an underlying restlessness.
