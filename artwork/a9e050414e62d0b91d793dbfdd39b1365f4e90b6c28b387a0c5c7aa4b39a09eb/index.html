Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional struggle between clarity and chaos, represented by the contrasting colors of signal and noise. The palette combines vibrant electric blues and greens with muted grays and blacks to juxtapose the tension of readable rhythms against static scatter. The main collision occurs at the center, where the ripple of signal green confronts the barcode static gray, creating an interrupted flow that echoes the seed's themes of exposure and shelter, memory versus erasure. The outer edges create a frame of noise, emphasizing the core's pulse, suggesting an inward spiral of clarity fighting through the void of distractions.
