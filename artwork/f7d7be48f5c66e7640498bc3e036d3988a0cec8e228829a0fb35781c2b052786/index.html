Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between fear and hope through a flow that shifts from dark hues to bright accents. The palette utilizes deep blacks and dark violets to represent fear, sharply contrasted against the soft glow of pale green and soft gold, embodying hope. The layout mimics a mirror-effect, introducing intentional misalignments that signify disruption. The jagged progression reflects a 'zigzag' pattern of fear, while the brighter color placements in the center create moments of uplift, suggesting a beacon of hope amidst chaos.
