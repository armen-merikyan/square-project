Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tug-of-war between 'spark' and 'ash', embodying brightness versus quiet decay. The palette, drawn from the 'broken_memory' family, utilizes deep sepia and muted dust gray to underscore the contrast between vitality and stillness. The central collision occurs through the use of glitch patterns versus dotted textures, embodying quick bursts of energy against the weight of settled dust. Negative space amplifies the effect of distant memories versus present tension, while broken pixels create intentional disruptions that mimic the jaggedness of memory itself.
