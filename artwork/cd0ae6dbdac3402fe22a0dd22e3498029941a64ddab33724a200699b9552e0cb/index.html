Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between loneliness and connection. The chosen palette of deep blue, muted purple, yellow, and orange reflects the warmth of connection against the starkness of loneliness. The main contrast is between the deep blue and vibrant yellow, where blue manifests isolation, while yellow represents fleeting moments of connection. The tearing and crosshatch patterns suggest a chaotic interaction between opposing emotional forces, creating a burst of energy against an underlying quietness.
