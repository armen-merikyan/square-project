Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between quiet and pressure, capturing the shift in dynamics across the grid. The palette features muted tones like faint peach and dusty gray to represent the quietness contrasted by compressed red and dark navy, which symbolize the pressure. The placement of colors captures a feeling of density concentrated in one corner, while the opposite side opens up into a softer, quieter area, illustrating the ebb and flow of emotions. This tension is accentuated by a few broken pixels that disrupt the continuity, creating a sense of imperfection and tension in the overall composition.
