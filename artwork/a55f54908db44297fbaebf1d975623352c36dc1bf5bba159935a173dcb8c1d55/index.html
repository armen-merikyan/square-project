Artist Statement

The emotional tone of defiance vs submission creates a palpable tension throughout the grid. The palette, which consists of earthy tones like moss green, bone white, and rust, reinforces a sense of resilience and fragility. The contrast of bold colors in the center, representing defiance, clashes with the softer tones around the edges, symbolizing submission. The main collision occurs centrally, creating a visual spiral that draws the eye inward while the edges feel like they are collapsing, creating an echo of pressure against quiet spaces. The result is an abstract pattern reflecting dynamic emotional currents.
