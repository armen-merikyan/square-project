Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the juxtaposition of joy and grief, using a palette of bright yellows and a muted purple to symbolize the vibrancy of joy against the heaviness of grief. The outer layer is composed of darker tones, suggesting weight and somberness, while the inner section features brighter colors, hinting at hope and lightness. The arrangement balances between symmetry and deliberate breaks, emulating the tug between stability and disruption in emotional experiences. The grid formation represents structure, while the scattered dots introduce elements of chaos, inferring the unpredictable nature of human feelings. The placements deliberately create tension through contrasts and collisions between bright and muted colors, inviting the viewer to contemplate the complex interplay of emotions.
