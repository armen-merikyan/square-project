Artist Statement

This composition conveys the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold reflects warmth and depth while creating a contrast between subdued and vibrant tones. The central collision occurs where vibrant patterns of 'embers' clash with the structured 'stepwell' forms, representing a struggle between chaos and clarity. Negative space allows for breathing, hinting at moments of serenity. The arrangement embodies a broken mirror, reflecting elements of repair and damage in a shared visual language of color and form.
