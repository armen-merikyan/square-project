Artist Statement

This piece reflects the tension and interplay between certainty and curiosity. The chosen palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green suggests a clash between the familiar and the unknown, embodying the emotions of dread and hope. The central clash occurs at the spiral's core, where structured lines (representing certainty) juxtapose with fluid, chaotic colors and textures (representing curiosity). The arrangement of colors creates a sense of pressure and release, echoing the theme of rising signals against the backdrop of receding edges, emphasizing both movement and stillness within the confines of the square.
