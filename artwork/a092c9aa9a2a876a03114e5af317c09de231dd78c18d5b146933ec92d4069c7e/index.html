Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between defiance and submission through contrasting colors and arrangements. The palette blends pale aqua and mist gray to suggest serenity while introducing alarm red and ink blue as striking accents, creating a visual dialogue. The layout features a rising signal, with colors climbing upward from the bottom, embodying hope and escape amidst chaos. This aligns with the seed's narrative of conflicting forces and emotional landscapes, where desaturated echoes of submission reside near the edges while bold, glitch-patterns of defiance dominate the center. The intentional interruption of quiet spaces with vibrant tones further emphasizes the conflict visualized through the abstract arrangement.
