Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between confusion and clarity, illustrated through a checker pattern that hints at a broken symmetry. The warm hues of yellow and orange contrast sharply with the cooler deep blue and muted purple, representing emotional highs and lows. The arrangement creates clashes and interruptions that mimic a pulse of energy, where sharp edges evoke a sense of urgency yet are contained within a structured 8x8 grid. The use of fragmented pixels enhances the idea of imperfection, embodying the emotional complexities of the seed.
