Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tones of safety and exposure through a contrast of soft and harsh elements. The palette, consisting of shelter green (#A8D99F), warm brown (#844B3D), pale skin (#FBEDD1), cold white (#F4F4F4), and open blue (#84C2E0), plays into this duality, with cool tones representing exposure and warm tones cushioning safety. The main collision occurs in the center, where the soft pale skin intersects with the dark warm brown, symbolizing a vulnerable heart encased within a protective shell. This arrangement adheres to the 'halo_void' layout, creating a hollow center ringed by more vibrant colors, providing a visual echo of the emotional conflict suggested by the seed.
