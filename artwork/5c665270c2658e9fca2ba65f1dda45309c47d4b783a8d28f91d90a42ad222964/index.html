Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the tension between strength and vulnerability, embodied through contrasting density and softness. The palette of clean white and ice blue signifies clarity and vulnerability juxtaposed with grounding ink black and violet gray representing strength. The primary collision occurs at the center where a dense grid symbolizes strength and a surrounding soft ember pattern depicts vulnerability, creating a dynamic sense of push and pull. Broken pixels are intentionally scattered, representing the scars of exposure amidst the quest for safety, all while maintaining a readable structure in this abstract composition.
