Artist Statement

The emotional tone here plays with the tension between longing and resolution, embodied through the fragmented mirror layout. The palette combines sepia and faded blue evoking nostalgia while suggesting movement toward clarity. The main collision occurs in the center, where warm sepia tones clash with cooler blues, symbolizing the struggle between chaos and order. Broken pixels disrupt the grid, creating a narrative of unresolved emotion, while maintaining a negative space that offers breathing room against the dense pixel clusters.
