Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the juxtaposition of vulnerability and strength. The palette of navy and dim cyan conveys coolness and depth, while the subtle use of black and white emphasizes stark contrasts—invoking feelings of exposure against a backdrop of protective layers. The main collision occurs in the center where light and dark merge, creating a visual representation of strength intersecting with vulnerability amidst a hollow void layout. This abstract configuration whispers narratives of connection and alienation, stirring a sense of unresolved tension.
