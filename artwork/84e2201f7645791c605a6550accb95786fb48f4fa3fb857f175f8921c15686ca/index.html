Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the juxtaposition between vulnerability and protection. The palette, with warm browns and greens representing safety, contrasts starkly with the pale and blue tones of exposure. The main collision occurs in the center, where the dense channel layout meets the maze-like exposure, creating energy and conflict that resonates with the seed's themes. Broken pixels subtly disrupt the textures, imbuing the work with an imperfection that enhances its emotional weight.
