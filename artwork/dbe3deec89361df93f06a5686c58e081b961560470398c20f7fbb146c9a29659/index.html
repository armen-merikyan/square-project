Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork embodies the contrast between confusion and clarity, representing the turmoil of emotional collisions. The palette incorporates electric blues and magentas against a black backdrop to evoke both pressure and release, amplifying the intensity of feelings. The layout of falling pixels suggests a descent, reflecting the weight of emotions. By applying the 'target' pattern for confusion, the arrangement creates moments of density interrupted by open spaces, drawing from the juxtaposition of solidity and fluidity in emotion. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, enhancing a sense of instability, as mirrored patterns clash with disruptions to evoke a journey through emotional landscapes.
