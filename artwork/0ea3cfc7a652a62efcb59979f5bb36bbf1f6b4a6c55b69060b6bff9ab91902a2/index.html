Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through a divided palette reflective of warmth and coolness. The left side uses muted purples and deep blues to symbolize isolation, contrasting with bright yellows and oranges on the right that signify the warmth of connection. The grid-like structure represents loneliness while jagged bursts suggest moments of potential connection, creating a dynamic interplay of tension and release.
