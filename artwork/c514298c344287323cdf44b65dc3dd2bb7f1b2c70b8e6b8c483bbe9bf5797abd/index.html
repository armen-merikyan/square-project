Artist Statement

This composition contrasts the tension between panic and control by juxtaposing chaotic crosshatch patterns with structured dots. The palette employs deep blacks and vibrant reds to evoke the struggle of intense emotions, guided by pale blue to suggest moments of calm amid the chaos. The arrangement of broken pixels introduces imperfection, embodying the disruption inherent in arguments and emotional conflicts, while the flow from right to left highlights the shift from order to disorder, creating a sense of unease and unresolved conflict.
