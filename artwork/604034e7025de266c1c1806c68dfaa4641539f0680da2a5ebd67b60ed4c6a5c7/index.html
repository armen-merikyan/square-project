Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tone of relief and dread through contrasting color density and arrangement. The palette mixes muted storm gray and near black for a sense of impending weight, while faded gold and pale mint introduce moments of clarity and exhale. The interplay of crowded areas (dread) versus open spaces (relief) mirrors a spiral pattern, suggesting tension gradually loosening. Broken pixels enhance the feeling of imperfection and disruption, emphasizing the emotional collision at the center of the square.
