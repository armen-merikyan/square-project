Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a dynamic interaction between loneliness and connection. The palette, consisting of electric blue, lime, and magenta, reflects the vibrant undercurrents of connection against the starkness of loneliness. The use of black serves as a grounding force and a canvas for the bursts of color, suggesting hidden depths and the interplay of exposure and concealment. The spatial arrangement features a dark central tear-like shape, surrounded by crystalline bursts of color, symbolizing the tension and release of emotions. The layout invites the viewer to contemplate the finite boundaries of connection, where vibrant clusters confront the quiet voids of loneliness, echoing the feelings of hidden versus exposed.
