Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the dynamic tension between strength and vulnerability, conveyed through a palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold. The dark colors representing strength form a thick edge, while the lighter tones suggest vulnerability in the center, creating a soft break. The central collision occurs in the middle rows, where strength and vulnerability overlap, suggesting a push-pull dynamic that reflects the complexities of safety and exposure. The broken pixels add intentional imperfections, enhancing the raw emotion of the piece.
