Artist Statement

The artwork reflects the tension between longing and resolve, using a warm color palette of burnt orange and dark red against a backdrop of charcoal, symbolizing the struggle between desire and clarity. The broken border amplifies the sense of unresolved tension, suggesting emotional leakage. The central collision of pulse marks and rings embodies this conflict, creating a dynamic narrative where one feeling attempts to dominate while the other pushes through in fragmented bursts.
