Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the feelings of emptiness and desire, using a palette that juxtaposes warm reds with cool blues. The edges feature darker shades to create a sense of contention and pressure, reflecting the struggles inherent in desire clashing against emptiness. The arrangement conveys upwards movement akin to rise versus fall, while the cracked patterns reflect interruptions in continuity, echoing conflict. Color placements alternate with moments of density and negative space, suggesting times of quiet amidst the noise of yearning.
