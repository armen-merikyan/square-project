Artist Statement

This artwork represents the emotional contrast between restlessness and peace through a collision of dynamic and static elements. The palette blends deep black and blue with softer shades like pale yellow and green, creating a dialogue between darker, weighty tones and lighter, uplifting ones. The main contrast occurs in the lower corner, where restless hues clash against broader peaceful areas, mirroring the tension of memory versus motion. This setup conveys a feeling of unresolved energy, interrupted by moments of tranquility.
