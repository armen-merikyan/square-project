Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the delicate dance between vulnerability and strength. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon evokes themes of love, betrayal, and emotional depth. The center is densely packed with strong colors (dark maroon and red), representing strength, which struggles against the softer edges created with pink and gray hues, suggesting vulnerability. The collision occurs primarily in the center where intense saturation confronts lighter tones, creating a tension that speaks to the struggle articulated in the seed. The lattice pattern represents vulnerability while the echo pattern signifies strength, both colliding and intertwining to evoke depth and complexity in the emotions conveyed. The broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, manifesting the theme of pressure against release and echoing the emotions expressed in the seed.
