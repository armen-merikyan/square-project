Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between love and betrayal, illustrated through contrasting warm and cool colors. The palette combines pink and red for warmth with dark maroon and gray to signify betrayal. The arrangement of colors reflects a downward flow, mimicking the feeling of falling and separation, as if love is giving way to betrayal. The edges are softened and interrupted, symbolizing the fragility of emotions, while the placement of darker shades amid lighter tones suggests a collision of feelings, creating a tension-filled visual narrative.
