Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The upper half utilizes the 'noise' pattern in dark shades, creating an environment of solitude and heaviness. The lower half employs a 'flame' pattern in lighter hues, suggesting warmth and connection. The clash of dark and light across the center point reflects the ongoing struggle between isolation and the desire for closeness. The 'burned edges' give a weathered feel to the composition, embodying both fragility and resilience.
