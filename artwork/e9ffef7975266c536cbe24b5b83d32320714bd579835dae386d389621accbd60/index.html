Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional clash of panic and control through a carefully selected palette of deep reds juxtaposed against pale blues and stark whites, creating sharp contrasts that embody chaos and order. The layout disperses colors in a diagonal flow, suggesting an unequal struggle between tension and calm, while broken pixels represent fractures in control. Central to this piece is a zigzag pattern that suggests both a spark of chaotic energy and controlled movement, conveying a narrative of pressure and release.
