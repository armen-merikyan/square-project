Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines longing and resolve, captured through a palette that oscillates between warm and cool shades to visually narrate tension. The colors represent distant warmth (rose clay, warm shadow) against the clear boundary (cool blue, distant lilac). The arrangement reflects two pulsing centers with a central collision point, primarily situated in darker shades, contrasting against lighter fields, representing complexity and emotional depth. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, mirroring life’s unpredictability amidst quests for resolution.
