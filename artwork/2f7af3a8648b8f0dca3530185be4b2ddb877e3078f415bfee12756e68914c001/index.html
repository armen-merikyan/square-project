Artist Statement

The artwork captures the tension between joy and grief through an upward flow of bright colors, contrasting with deeper, muted tones that suggest heaviness. The palette features bright yellows and oranges symbolizing joy, while deep blues and muted purples represent grief. The placement creates a zigzag movement, suggesting a bridge between these opposing feelings, while broken pixels introduce imperfection and chaos amid order, echoing the emotional complexity of the seed.
