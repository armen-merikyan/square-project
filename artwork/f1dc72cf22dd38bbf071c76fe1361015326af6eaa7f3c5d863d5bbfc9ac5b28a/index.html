Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between loneliness and connection, captured through a palette of deep navy and soft dim cyan, evoking feelings of isolation under a cloaked warmth. The center features a burst of lighter tones suggesting connection, contrasted by the denser shades surrounding it, symbolizing loneliness. The layout flows from top to bottom, with mirrored elements that are intentionally broken to create a sense of imperfection and disruption, embodying the crosshatch theme from the seed.
