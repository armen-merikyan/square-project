Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between defiance and submission, represented by bold contrasts and soft receding elements. The palette of warm coral, soft amber, cold slate, blue black, and signal cyan embodies conflict and connection, evoking warmth against coldness. The main collision happens centrally, where dense ring patterns of defiance clash with the glitch-inspired submission edges, creating a dynamic tension. The layout incorporates a quiet channel, allowing negative space to evoke a sense of calm amid chaos, aligned with the seed's themes of conflict and resonance.
