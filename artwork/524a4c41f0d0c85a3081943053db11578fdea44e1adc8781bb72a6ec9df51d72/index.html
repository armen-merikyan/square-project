Artist Statement

This composition juxtaposes the structured certainty of a dark maroon outer border with the erratic curiosity of vibrant pink and gray inner fields. The dark maroon encapsulates order, while the playful patterns of broken dots inside symbolize curiosity and exploration. A split flow adds tension, suggesting a rising signal countering downward pulls. The broken pixels disrupt the patterns, reflecting the chaos of navigating between certainty and curiosity, while leaving sufficient negative space enhances the overall balance.
