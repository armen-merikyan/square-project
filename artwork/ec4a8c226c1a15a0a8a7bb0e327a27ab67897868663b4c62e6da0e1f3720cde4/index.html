Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay between nostalgia and progress, symbolizing a rise versus fall through color contrast and placement. The palette includes deep reds and blacks symbolizing rage infused with pale blues and whites representing calm, creating a visual tension. The split horizontal layout divides the canvas, suggesting dual narratives—one side filled with structured grid-like patterns (nostalgia) and the other showcasing a chaotic noise (progress). This collision creates an echoing rhythm that embodies both conflict and synthesis, inviting reflection on the passage of time and evolution.
