Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a clash between sudden brilliance and settled dust, embodied by a palette of muted and saturated colors. The palette incorporates warm gold and cool grays to symbolize resilience against fragility. The pattern emphasizes a diagonal arrangement where the vibrant gold and rust colors intermittently disrupt the gray crosshatch, suggesting an echo of memory vs erasure. The jagged edges of colors create interruptions, reflecting the tension of safety and exposure. The main collision occurs diagonally, providing a narrative tension between spark and ash.
