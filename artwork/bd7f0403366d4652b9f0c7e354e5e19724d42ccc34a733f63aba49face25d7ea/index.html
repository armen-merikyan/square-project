Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the piece contrasts the soft warmth of tenderness with the explosive intensity of rage. The palette combines deep crimson and dark noir to evoke the fiery aspect of anger, while pale blue and white are used to represent calmness and softness associated with tenderness. The main collision occurs in the center, where jagged, shattering patterns of red disrupt the serene blue bands, symbolizing the conflict between these emotions. The layout of stacked bands allows for the gradual transition from rage to calmness, creating a dynamic flow that echoes to the right, reflecting the journey of emotional processing. Overall, the arrangement captures the essence of a universe filled with intense feelings colliding and merging.
