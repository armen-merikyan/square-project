Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a dance between restlessness and peace, represented through a palette of warm and cool colors that clash and harmonize. The main contrast occurs in the center, where dark, muted hues blend with vibrant brush strokes of warmer shades, symbolizing the tension between stillness and movement. The pattern emerges from a layered structure that echoes memories while suggesting swift motion, forming an abstract landscape of emotional strata.
