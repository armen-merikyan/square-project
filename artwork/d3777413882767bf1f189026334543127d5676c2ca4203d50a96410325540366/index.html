Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between joy and grief, exploring themes of symmetry and breakage. The left side, representing grief, employs muted purple and deep blue tones with a ring pattern to evoke a sense of drooping heaviness and fragmentation. In contrast, the right side bursts with brighter yellow and orange in a checker pattern, symbolizing joy's optimism and expansion. The glowing edges create a visual contrast that emphasizes the struggle between these emotions, with asymmetrical placements adding layers of complexity. The center seed acts as a pulse point, pushing against the stark divide, suggesting an arrow moving through the emotions.
