Artist Statement

This artwork captures the duality of joy and grief through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette blends bright yellows and oranges representing joy with muted purples and deep blue symbolizing grief. The layout is intentionally left-weighted to suggest a shift in emotional balance. Circular patterns emerge amidst the structured grids, representing the collision of structured joy and broken grief. The piece encapsulates moments of pulse and release through varying densities, creating visual tension.
