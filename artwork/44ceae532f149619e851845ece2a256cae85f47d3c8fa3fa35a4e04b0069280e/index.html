Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional interplay of fear and hope through contrasting dark and light hues. The palette, derived from the fear_hope theme, uses deep blacks and soft golds to create tension, representing fear with darker tones and hope with brighter accents. The vertical split illustrates a battle between these emotions, while jagged edges invoke a sense of anxiety. The patterns infused with flame and mirror add complexity, creating an echo of struggle. The use of 'broken pixels' introduces a sense of imperfection, allowing moments of quiet space, which is essential in contrasting dense clusters of color.
