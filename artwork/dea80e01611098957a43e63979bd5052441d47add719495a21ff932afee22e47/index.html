Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between clarity and distortion, encapsulated in a palette that balances the deep, still navy against the sharpness of black and the subtle light of dim cyan. This composition illustrates conflict, where a central 'pulse' of brighter tones attempts to break through layers of noise, evidenced by the solid blocks of dark colors around it, creating a dynamic edge style. The broken pixels disrupt the flow, mirroring the chaotic essence of the seed and reinforcing a sense of fractured communication amidst order.
