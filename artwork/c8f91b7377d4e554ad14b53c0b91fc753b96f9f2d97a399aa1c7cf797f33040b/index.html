Artist Statement

The composition captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve, using a palette that reflects both resilience and fragility. Moss green and rust suggest organic growth tainted by past struggles, while bone white and stone gray represent clarity amidst confusion. The dramatic collision predominantly occurs in the lower corner where dense patterns of longing (stairs) interrupt the space of resolve (echo), creating a palpable pressure that gradually dissipates across the grid. This balance of tension and release embodies the emotional complexities of moving from longing to a state of resolve.
