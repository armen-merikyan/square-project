Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a carefully selected palette of deep reds, blacks, pale blues, and whites, symbolizing intense pressure and calmness. The central collision occurs where strong echoes (in deep reds and blacks) clash with soft threads (in pale blue and white), visually representing the heartbeat of resilience against fragility. The layout follows a quiet channel, maintaining negative space to create contrast and allow for reflection. The broken pixels disrupt the symmetry, hinting at imperfections and the beauty found within vulnerability.
