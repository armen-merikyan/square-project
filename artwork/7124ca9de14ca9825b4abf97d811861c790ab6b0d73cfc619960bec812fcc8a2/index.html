Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between defiance and submission, evoking visual tension through hard, angular contrasts against softer, muted colors. The chosen palette of 'ancient_fire' reflects intense emotions—burnt orange and dark red denote defiance, while charcoal introduces a quiet, grounding element. The dominant warm colors create a subtle pressure, symbolizing resistance, while the cooler shades provide moments of retreat, embodying submission. The collision occurs centrally, where patterns of density and glitch interplay, suggesting turmoil within an established center. This layout designed as a quiet channel emphasizes the push-pull of emotion, allowing the eye to flow through with a sense of both instability and calm.
