Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between restlessness and peace. The warm tones of 'small_orange' and 'red_oxide' act as punctuating accents against a field of muted 'blackened_plum' and 'iron_gray', representing moments of agitation amidst calm. The layout reflects a banded structure that echoes the push and pull of memory and motion, with the central diagonal filled with warmer colors creating a striking contrast that captures the tumult of feelings outlined in the seed. Broken pixels reinforce the theme of disruption, indicating scars of memory layered over the essence of peace.
