Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve through a palette that balances warm and cool tones. The chosen colors reflect clarity and confusion: clean white and ice blue representing serenity, while ink black and warning orange evoke urgency. The collision of linear patterns across the 8x8 grid illustrates the struggle between tension and release, with the dominant emotion of longing occupying the lower left, suggesting a pull upwards towards resolve in the upper right. Sparse use of broken pixels disrupts the order, hinting at imperfections in our pursuit of completion, enhancing the narrative of repair versus damage.
