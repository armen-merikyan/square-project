Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of longing and resolve, with elements of brokenness and repair implied in the arrangement. The chosen palette, predominantly from the 'safety_exposure' family, uses shelter green and warm brown to evoke a sense of solidity and warmth, while pale skin tones introduce vulnerability. The sharp contrast between the broken lattice pattern expressing longing and the shatter pattern representing resolve creates a collision at the center of the square. The placement favors density on one side, allowing wider negative spaces to suggest unresolved tension and longing for connection.
