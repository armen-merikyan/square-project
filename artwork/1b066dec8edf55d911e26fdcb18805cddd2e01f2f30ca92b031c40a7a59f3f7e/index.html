Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between joy and grief through a split layout, representing order and chaos. The bright yellows and oranges symbolize joy, contrasting with the deeper, muted purples representing grief. The hard edges create a sense of instability while the stair-step pattern reflects an upward movement, suggesting a journey through mixed emotions. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, embodying the unpredictability of feelings, while leaving negative space enhances contemplation.
