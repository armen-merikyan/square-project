Artist Statement

The artwork reflects the emotional tension of 'collisions' and 'pressure' through a contrasting palette dominated by shades of deep red, gray, and muted maroon, symbolizing both conflict and release. The left side showcases a structured pattern reminiscent of nostalgia with a crosshatch motif, while the right breaks into chaos with mirrored but disrupted elements. The edge style is top-weighted, emphasizing a feeling of weight pressing down. Broken pixels introduce imperfection to signify emotional anomalies, creating an interplay between order and disorder across the canvas.
