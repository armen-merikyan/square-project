Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, using colors from the 'melancholy_wonder' palette to convey muted beauty and resilience. The central colors feature a blend of soft cyan and moon white, representing exposed vulnerability, while dense plum shades anchor the edges, portraying strength. The contrast sits at the center where the patchwork of soft hues interplays with the thicker layers at the periphery, symbolizing the coexistence of fragility amidst a strong outer barrier. Broken pixels intermittently disrupt the pattern to evoke feelings of upheaval and complexity in this emotional narrative.
