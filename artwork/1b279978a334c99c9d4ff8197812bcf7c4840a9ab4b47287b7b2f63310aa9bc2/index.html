Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the interplay between longing and resolve. A palette of nostalgic colors—pink for warmth, dark maroon for depth, gray for neutrality, and red for intensity—creates a backdrop for tension. The main collision occurs in the center, where braiding patterns of resolve meet the crosshatch of longing, creating a dissonance that resonates throughout. Imperfections represented by broken pixels introduce an element of disruption, reflecting the complexities of emotional states. The art piece captures the push and pull of contained versus overflowing sentiments, accompanied by deliberate negative space to enhance the feeling of longing.
