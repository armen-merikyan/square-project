Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between the soft residue of ash and the scattered brightness of sparks, suggesting emotional conflict. The dark maroon and gray evoke subdued memories and restraint, while accents of pink and red introduce moments of ignition and passion. The dense center, filled with warm hues, contrasts sharply with the jagged edges of cooler colors, creating a visual representation of resilience against fragility. The composition flows outward as if breaking free from the weight of memories, making the center a focal point of collision, with irregular placements serving as echoes of the emotional struggle.
