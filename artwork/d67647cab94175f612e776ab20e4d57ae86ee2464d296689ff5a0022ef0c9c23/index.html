Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through a deliberate arrangement of dark and light tones. The palette of navy and dim cyan symbolizes loneliness and the spark of connection amidst chaos. Each color interacts with its neighbors, creating a visual pulse that suggests both harmony and disruption—reflecting the core of the seed. The contrast between the dense, dark areas and lighter pixels introduces a rhythm that captures the essence of breaking symmetry while maintaining a sense of balance.
