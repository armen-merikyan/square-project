Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the struggle between distinct signals amidst scattered noise. The chosen palette of greens, blues, and grays functions to evoke clarity fighting through chaos. The main contrast occurs in the center, where the radial signal pattern clashes with the checker noise, creating a dynamic tension that echoes the confrontation between memory and erasure. This pattern showcases the tension of a single clear path trying to prevail, symbolizing resilience in the chaos of life.
