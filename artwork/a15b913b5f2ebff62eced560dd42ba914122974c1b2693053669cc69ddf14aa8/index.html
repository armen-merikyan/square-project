Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork captures the tension between defiance and submission, using a contrasting palette of deep black and soft pale blue to illustrate the push and pull of these emotions. The layout reflects falling pixels, suggesting a descent from chaos to calm, where dark colors represent weight and oppression, while the lighter shades signify hope and openness. The main collision occurs at the center where darker colors meet lighter hues, reinforcing the idea of conflict and resolution. This pattern encapsulates the struggle inherent in love and betrayal, as color blocks intrude upon each other, creating a sense of unresolved tension across the canvas.
