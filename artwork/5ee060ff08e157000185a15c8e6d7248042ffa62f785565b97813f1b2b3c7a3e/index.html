Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between defiance and submission, manifesting as contrasting patterns of rain and shatter. The brightness of the small gold interjects amidst darker tones, suggesting defiance pushing against the subdued calmness of the bone white and stone gray, creating a dynamic flow from right to left. The center marks a collision of intensity, while the edges recede into a quieter space, embodying the emotional journey expressed in the seed.
