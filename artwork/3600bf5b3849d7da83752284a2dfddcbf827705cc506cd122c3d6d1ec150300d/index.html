Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the emotional tone of longing for connection set against the firm ground of resolve. The palette combines warm tones like burnt orange and dark red to convey passion, contrasted by cooler charcoal and gold marks, signifying strength and clarity. The main collision occurs centrally, where flood-like texture of longing meets the sharp, defined lines of resolution, producing a dynamic tension. The layout captures a threshold, suggesting a bittersweet moment of choice, pulling between emotional weight and steady composure.
