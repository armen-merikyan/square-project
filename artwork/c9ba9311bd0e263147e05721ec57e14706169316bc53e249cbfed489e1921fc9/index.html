Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional dance between joy and grief. The warm palette of yellows and oranges represents joy, while deep blues and muted purples convey grief, presenting a spectrum of emotion. The arrangement features dense clusters of warm colors in the center, symbolizing joy, surrounded by cooler hues that evoke grief, creating an emotional contrast. The thin outlines serve to fracture the symmetry, reinforcing the theme of balance versus disruption. The waves and checkered patterns further enhance the feelings of layered complexity, oscillating between density and emptiness.
