Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between longing and resolve, using a muted palette that evokes nostalgia and strength. The warm undertones of red oxide and small orange speak to a sense of yearning, while blackened plum and iron gray bring a shadowy weight, representing solidity and determination. The tension is emphasized at the center where the two emotional qualities collide, creating a dynamic interruption. The chosen lattice pattern for longing mingles with the mirrored structure of resolve, allowing the viewer to experience a layered emotional narrative.
