Artist Statement

This composition explores the interplay between vulnerability and strength, represented through a palette that harmonizes warm accents with cool bases. The use of ripe green and cream suggests growth amidst fragility. The main collision occurs in the center where dense patches of dark green interact with lighter greens and cream, symbolizing how strength can shelter vulnerability yet also expose it. The diagonal layout creates a tension as vibrant colors confront muted tones, embodying the emotional themes of exposure and shelter throughout the abstract pattern.
