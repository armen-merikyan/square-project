Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of restlessness and peace, achieved through contrasting shades. The palette of melancholy wonder, featuring soft cyan and muted plum, reflects the soothing aspect of peace, while deeper tones introduce underlying restlessness. The arrangement creates a central collision of tension, where darker hues disrupt lighter bands, mimicking the emotional push and pull reflected in the seed. Shapes and patterns overlap, creating a visual narrative of unresolved motion and contrast among the colors.
