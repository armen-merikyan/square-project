Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between restlessness and peace. The chosen palette of sepia tones reflects the memory and warmth of moments, with quiet fields like tan and faded blue evoking a sense of calm. The contrasting darker shades introduce interruptions and tension, especially evident in the diagonal flow of darker colors against the lighter ones. The composition leaves negative spaces that symbolize unfilled moments, while broken pixels add intentional disruptions, imitating the interruptions in memory.
