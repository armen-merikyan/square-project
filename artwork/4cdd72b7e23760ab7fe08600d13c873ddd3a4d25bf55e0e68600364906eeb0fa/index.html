Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension of desire and restraint, represented through a palette balancing saturated and muted colors. The contrast occurs where the warm hues of hot coral and blood orange collide with the muted tones of charcoal and hollow blue, suggesting a struggle between vitality and containment. The arrangement creates a flow that spirals toward a central lively cluster, while soft edges enforce a sense of sealed space, highlighting the dynamic between expansion and restriction.
