Artist Statement

The composition explores the emotional tension between rage and calm through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette highlights the clash of intense reds representing rage against the cool, soft blues symbolizing calm. The warm pixels dominate the composition, reflecting the overarching emotion of rage, which bursts through an inlaid structure of cooling tones. The lower rows present broader, calmer horizontal sequences reminiscent of noise, while the top rows introduce jagged, ember-like interruptions, creating a layered visual narrative that signifies conflict and dynamic emotion.
