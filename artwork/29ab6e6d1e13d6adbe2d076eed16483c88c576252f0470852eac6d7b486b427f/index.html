Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between panic and control. The palette embraces warm yellow and orange tones juxtaposed with deep blue and muted purple, suggesting a struggle between light and dark, calm and chaos. The contrast occurs primarily at the center, where warm colors disrupt the cooler blues, representing the spark of chaos in an otherwise empty and stable room. The grid-like layout mirrors the desire for control while the asymmetric application and broken pixels evoke a sense of instability, capturing the essence of the seed's themes beautifully.
