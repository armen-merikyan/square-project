Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents the interplay between joy and grief, using a palette of warm yellow and orange for joy to contrast with the deeper blue and muted purple signifying grief. The main contrast occurs in the center where the warm tones pulse outward amidst jagged textures reflecting grief, creating a visual balance and disruption. The pattern allows for echoes of joy to clash against the structured grid of sadness, representing life’s dichotomies. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, emphasizing vulnerability.
