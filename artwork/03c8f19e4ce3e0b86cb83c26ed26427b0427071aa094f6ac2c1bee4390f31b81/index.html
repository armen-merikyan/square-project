Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional collision between 'emptiness' and 'desire.' The palette of deep reds and blacks contrasted with pale blue and white suggests a battle between overwhelming pressure and the yearning for calm. The arrow shape seed informs an upward and downward movement in the pixel arrangement, creating a sense of dynamic tension. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, symbolizing the jarring nature of unfulfilled desire amid a contrasting emptiness. The flow spirals counterclockwise, enhancing the emotional journey from dense clusters to open spaces, reflecting the push and pull of emotional release.
