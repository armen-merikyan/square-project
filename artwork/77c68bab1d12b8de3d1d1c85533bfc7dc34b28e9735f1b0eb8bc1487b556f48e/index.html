Artist Statement

This composition explores the interplay between softness and severity, as expressed through contrasting colors and patterns. The chosen palette of powder pink, warm white, carbon black, steel gray, and sharp crimson mirrors the emotional weight of the seed, suggesting tenderness juxtaposed with stark intensity. The main collision occurs in the center where sharp crimson and carbon black create a dramatic entrance, while the surrounding noise pattern of powder pink and warm white offers gentle relief. This dynamic reflects the tension of signals competing with noise, invoking a feeling of both openness and confinement. The layout divides the square into sections of heavy color and lighter tones, creating a threshold where emotions meet and interact.
