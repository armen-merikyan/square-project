Artist Statement

The emotional tone is one of tension between clarity and confusion, represented through the juxtaposition of dense black and pale blue contrasted with whites and grays. The palette creates a visual weight portraying a throbbing confusion at the center, with black representing the confusion and pale blue illustrating calm clarity. The edges are thick and dark, framing a chaotic center which implores the viewer to navigate the confusion. The use of broken pixels adds to the sense of imperfection and interrupts the flow, reflecting disarray while maintaining an 8x8 grid. The overall arrangement symbolizes the struggle between chaos and order, embodying the essence of the seed.
