Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the duality of loneliness and connection through contrasting colors and spatial weight. The palette combines warm yellows and oranges signifying connection, with deeper purples and blues representing loneliness. The center is visually weighted with dense pixel arrangements that create a 'burst' effect, while the edges feature more negative space, hinting at feelings of isolation. The broken line pattern echoes the tension between the two emotions, showcased through sharp, contrasting areas clashing with softer, muted tones, resulting in a visual dance of feelings.
