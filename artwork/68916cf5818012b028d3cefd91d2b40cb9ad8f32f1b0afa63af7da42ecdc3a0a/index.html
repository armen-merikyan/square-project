Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of lime, black, electric blue, and magenta reflects the contrast between energetic vibrancy and dark depths, inviting viewers to perceive layers of emotion. The collision occurs in the center, where the soft echo pattern representing vulnerability is interrupted by a braided structure signifying strength. Broken pixels evoke imperfection and reinforce the narrative of exposed resilience.
