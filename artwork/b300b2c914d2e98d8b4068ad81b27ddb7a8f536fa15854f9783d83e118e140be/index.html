Artist Statement

This piece conveys the emotional dance between loneliness and connection through a structured layout that juxtaposes calming dark tones and bright signals. The color palette embodies a spectrum from deep navy to stark white, representing the tension and release inherent in emotional collisions. The main focus is a central pattern of dim cyan rings, enveloped by darker colors that create a sense of pressure, echoing the dynamic interaction of emotions. A few broken pixels (imperfections) add to the feeling of disconnection while simultaneously inviting connection through their arrangement. The layout mirrors the contrast of feeling lost yet surrounded, reflecting the inner and outer emotional landscapes.
