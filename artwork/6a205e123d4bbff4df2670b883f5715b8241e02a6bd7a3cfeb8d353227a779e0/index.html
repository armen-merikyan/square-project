Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, symbolized through a radial pattern contrasting with split-screen chaos. The bold palette of electric blue and magenta reflects a vibrant intensity, while black grounds the composition. The collision occurs in the central cluster, where luminosity from the outer edges clashes against the darker core, embodying the struggle between clarity and confusion. The broken pixels interrupt the symmetry, mirroring the tumultuous feelings represented in the seed text.
