Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between joy and grief, balancing vibrant and muted colors. The palette of yellows and oranges represents joy, while deep blue and muted purple symbolize the weight of grief. The left side focuses on bright radial patterns, expressing expansion and warmth, contrasted by the broken dots that disrupt the right side, reflecting loss and chaos. Each color placement emphasizes a push-pull between the two emotions, creating a clash that resonates with the seed's themes of order and chaos. This composition invites a dialogue about safety and threat, formed through sharp edges that indicate division and inner conflict.
