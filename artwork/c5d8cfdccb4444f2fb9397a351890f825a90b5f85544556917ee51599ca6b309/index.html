Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between certainty and curiosity, captured through a contrasting palette of deep blues and soft yellows. The center features a dense cluster of dark colors, signifying certainty ('mourning black' and 'deep blue') while the edges are interspersed with lighter, hopeful hues ('new leaf' and 'pale yellow')—a metaphor for curiosity seeping in. The layout is open, with a palpable flow towards the center, where chaotic shapes meet the orderly surfaces, characterized by subtle broken pixels that introduce a sense of imperfection and organic movement. The overall emotional tone speaks to the interplay of structure versus exploration, encouraging viewers to engage with the ongoing dialogue between these two forces. Each color in the rows creates layered density, echoing a sense of rising tension before a release. The patterns reflect fragments of an unraveling narrative, hinting at an encroachment of the enigmatic upon the safe.
