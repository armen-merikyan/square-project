Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between defiance and submission. The central area, marked with stronger, brighter colors, conveys defiance. In contrast, the softer, muted edges represent submission, creating an emotional juxtaposition. The palette is drawn from warm and cold tones, reflecting emotional warmth vs. distance. The dominant dense center is interrupted by lighter bands, illustrating the main collision between bright and subdued. Each broken pixel adds intentional disruptions, evoking a sense of imperfection and life within the abstract.
