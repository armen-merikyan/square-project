Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between quietude and pressure. The palette blends muted tones of dust gray and faint peach, creating a soft atmosphere contrasted by compressed red representing pressure. The layout suggests density in the bottom-left corner, where colors stack, echoing the feeling of weight. The interplay of light and dark enhances the emotional argument, with the soft edges providing a gentle resistance to the sharpness of the overlaid patterns. This arrangement signifies the struggle between calmness and the overwhelming force of pressure, embodying the seed's thematic complexity.
