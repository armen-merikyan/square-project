Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between pressure and quietude, creating a visual representation of that tension. The chosen palette of off-white, dusty gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach reflects the contrast—dark colors signify pressure and the lighter hues suggest quiet moments. The main collision occurs in the center, where clusters of dense dark shapes disrupt the soft, muted field, echoing the idea of a shattering boundary—where symmetry meets chaos. This arrangement mirrors the seed's themes of disruption and quiet strength, while the broken pixels serve as intentional imperfections, enhancing the overall narrative of tension and release.
