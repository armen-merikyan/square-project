Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional conflict between restlessness and peace. The outer rows feature darker tones, illustrating a heavy weight of unrest, while the inner rows introduce lighter colors that suggest a whisper of calm and resolution. The contrast between the chaotic black and subdued reds of the outer layers collides sharply with the gentle blues and whites of the core, creating a dynamic tension. The arrangement reflects a diagonal flow that symbolizes movement and memory, ultimately forming a beautiful yet turbulent patchwork that resonates with the seed philosophy.
