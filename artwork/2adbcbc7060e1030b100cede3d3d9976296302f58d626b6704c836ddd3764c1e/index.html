Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dichotomy of peace against restlessness where moss green and bone white dominate the composition to evoke tranquility. Rust and stone gray pop in glitch-like patterns, representing restless energy that disrupts the calm, with the main collision occurring diagonally across the center. The palette's themes of resilience and fragility highlight the struggle between control and chaos, echoing the seed's essence through dynamic placements and deliberate broken pixels.
