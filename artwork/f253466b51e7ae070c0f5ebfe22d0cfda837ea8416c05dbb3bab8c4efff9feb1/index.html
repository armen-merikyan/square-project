Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the struggle between longing and resolve, creating tension through contrasting elements. The palette, characterized by muted grays and a striking red, reflects the somber yet dynamic interplay of feelings: the red symbolizes urgent longing while the grays denote serene resolution. The top four rows are filled using thin pulse-like marks, presenting a rhythm that beckons while the bottom four rows settle into a static, stable pattern, embodying clear boundaries. The main collision occurs between the soft edges and compressed forms in the center, creating an echo of feeling that pulls the eye inward, bridging the dichotomy expressed in the seed.
