Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between sparks of energy and the softness of ashes, with a palette evoking a lonely signal: deep navy, quiet black, along with an accenting dim cyan, creating a space filled with both warmth and coolness. The primary collision occurs in the center, where bright colors disrupt softer backgrounds, illustrating the imbalance between resilience and fragility. This arrangement mirrors the push and pull of struggle and stability, emphasizing broken edges and memory, with a distinctly patterned layout of static versus grid-like organization creating depth and texture across the square.
