Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between panic and control, set against a backdrop of spaciousness that symbolizes the empty room. The palette merges warm tones of yellow and orange with the deep blue of control, suggesting a dance of warmth against the coolness of stability. The main contrast emerges through the bold clash between vibrant oranges indicating panic and muted blues representing calm. The arrangement features periods of density with 'teeth' patterns interrupting the cycle, illustrating the chaos intruding upon the structured. The off-center distribution introduces balance, suggesting that even amidst turmoil, there's a potential for harmony.
