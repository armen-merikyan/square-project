Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through a deliberate juxtaposition of sharp contrasts and soft gradients. The palette of electric blue, magenta, and rich black provides a modern, machine-like quality, evoking feelings of both calmness (clarity) and vibrant chaos (confusion). The thick outlines define shapes that clash and intertwine, creating a push-and-pull effect that embodies the struggle between symmetry and disruption. The placement of vibrant colors against darker tones enhances this sense of conflict and unresolved motion, inviting the viewer to navigate the dense areas that echo confusion, contrasted with clearer, purposeful spaces that embody calm.
