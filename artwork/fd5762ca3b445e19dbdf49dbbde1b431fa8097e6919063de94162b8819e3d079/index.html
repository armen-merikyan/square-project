Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between restlessness and peace, using contrasting colors to evoke feelings of unsettled motion alongside quiet symmetry. The palette consists of vibrant lime and electric blue set against the starkness of black and magenta, embodying both dynamic energy and stillness. The collision occurs prominently in the center, where the pressure bands (suggestive of restlessness) overlap with braided patterns (representing peace). This interplay creates a structural discord that reflects the brief moments of tension before resolution, mirroring the complexities of memory and movement.
