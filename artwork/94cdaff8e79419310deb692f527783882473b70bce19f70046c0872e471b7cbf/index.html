Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between the clarity of signal and the chaos of noise. The palette of deep navy, muted black, and bright dim cyan suggests both weight and hope, mirroring the duality found in the seed. The arrangement allows for a significant area of negative space, emphasizing the struggle of a single readable pulse amid static, represented through horizontal stripes of dark color contrasted with brighter bursts. The main collision occurs centrally, where the structured ascent of the pulse clashes against a murky field, embodying the concept of a 'broken mirror' reflecting confusion and clarity.
