Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the vitality of sparks against the somber quality of ash, illustrating a constant tension between growth and decay. The palette embraces deep wine and soft gold to introduce warmth and vibrancy, contrasted by ash gray and stain brown to ground the composition. The main collision occurs at the center with a dense cluster of warmer tones framed by cooler hues, suggesting conflict and resolution. This arrangement resonates with the seed's themes of memory and resilience, transforming feelings into an abstract interplay of light and shadow.
