Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between emptiness and desire, using the earthy palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold. The clash between warm colors emerging from the dark mass symbolizes desire breaking through the surface of emptiness. The layout subtly rises, suggesting hope and tension, while employing broken edges to convey the imperfections inherent in emotional collisions. This arrangement creates a visual echo of pressure and a release, where contrasting dark and bright pixels invite contemplation of the unseen emotions.
