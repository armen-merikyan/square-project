Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission, emphasizing a dynamic narrative of pressure and release. The selected palette of electric blue and magenta (indicative of vibrancy and emotion) contrasts sharply against the depth of black, juxtaposing intense feeling with moments of quiet. The diagonal rise flows evoke a sense of hope amidst turmoil, while the broken pixels introduce imperfection, reinforcing the theme of collision. The arrangement creates an abstract landscape where color clashes signal emotional responses, drawing the viewer into a cyclical rhythm of intensity and calm.
