Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between vibrancy and subtle decay, inspired by the contrast of spark and ash. The palette mixes luminous pale mint and faded gold to represent sparks of memory against a backdrop of storm gray, suggesting ashen feelings. The layout embraces a halo of colors, interrupted by periods of stark dark near black, thereby displaying both the vitality of a 'spark' and the somber restraint of 'ash.' The dominance of grays complements the serenity of mint in the center, while jagged edges in placement suggest the fragility and resilience of memories amidst interruptions. The broken pixels enhance this sense of imperfection, echoing the emotional tension of memory and forgetting.
