Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between envy (represented by dark maroon and red) and gratitude (shown through pink and gray). The checkerboard pattern creates a collision of coloration that feels both dynamic and unresolved, echoing the push and pull of these contrasting emotions. The palette emphasizes warmth through reds and coolness through shades of gray, creating a rhythmic visual experience that fluctuates between dense clusters and quiet spaces. The placement of colors hints at a narrative of pressure and release, emphasizing both chaos and comfort, encapsulating the emotional score derived from the seed.
