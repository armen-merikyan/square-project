Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a balance between longing and resolve, reflected in the palette that intertwines warm honey gold and deep olive with the serenity of cream as a quiet space. The contrast between saturated acid green as a bright collision against the muted tones builds tension, emphasizing the echoes of longing. The main collision occurs towards the center, where the patchwork pattern of resolve meets the echo pattern of longing, creating an interplay of contained emotions against overflowing disruptiveness, resonating with the central idea of repair amidst damage.
