Artist Statement

The emotional tone is rooted in the tension between loneliness and connection, reflected in the palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold. These colors symbolize warmth and yearning amidst desolate echoes. The central area contains warmer tones that evoke connection, while the periphery features heavier, darker colors to express isolation. The contrast manifests as a collision between the vibrant core and the muted borders, emphasizing the push and pull of emotional weight. The layout rises from the bottom, suggesting a movement towards hope against despair, resonating with the seed's themes without depicting literal images.
