Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between calmness and rage through a carefully selected palette of intense reds and soothing pale blues juxtaposed against deep blacks and whites. The primary contrast is established through sharp rises of jagged red elements intersecting with softer, horizontal blue patterns, creating a sensation of pressure and release. This arrangement manifests the seed's essence by showcasing the upward movement of emotions clashing against descending calmness, translating the theme of collision into an abstract pattern filled with rhythmic tension and release.
