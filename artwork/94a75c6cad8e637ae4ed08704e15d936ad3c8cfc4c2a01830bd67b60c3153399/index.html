Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a tension between emptiness and desire, represented by a warm color palette of burnt orange and gold contrasting against deep charcoal and dark red. The layout presents an argument grid where the warm 'desire' pattern frames the cooler 'emptiness' layout. Collisions of colors occur in jagged transitions across the canvas, suggesting both the intensity of yearning and the weight of absence. The pattern creates a sense of rising movement, echoing the feelings of desire that punctuate the quieter, emptier areas. Broken pixels enhance authenticity, revealing flaws inherent in the human experience of longing.
