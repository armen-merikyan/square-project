Artist Statement

The artwork expresses the emotional tension between defiance and submission, using a palette that oscillates between assertive and muted tones. The cool blues of 'rain_blue' and 'moon_white' represent submission, while the bold 'lavender_gray' signifies defiance. The arrangement causes a clash in the center, where dense, dark blues suggest pressure from the edges while the lighter tones attempt to break through. This collision creates a dynamic rhythm, mirroring the push and pull of the emotional themes presented in the seed.
