Artist Statement

This pixel art composition explores the tension between longing and resolve through an arrangement that initiates from the bottom left corner, echoing a diagonal fall through the grid. The palette, consisting of shelter green for growth and warmth, played against open blue for tranquility, creates a dynamic that speaks to the struggle between chaos and order. The collision resides centrally where rich colors intermingle, highlighting moments of connection and separation, exploiting broken pixels to symbolize imperfections in the emotional journey.
