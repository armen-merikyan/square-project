Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between loneliness and connection. The palette of vibrant yellow and muted purple reflects this dichotomy, where yellow symbolizes connection and warmth, while muted purple suggests introspection and solitude. The main collision occurs along the diagonal flow, with a division of bright and subdued colors acting as an echo of memory — bright patches of yellow expand against the deep blue and muted purple, carving out a single pixel of impact amidst the background chaos. The pattern embodies a split-screen atmosphere, emphasizing both loneliness and connection through disconnected yet vibrant blocks of color, invoking feelings of isolation interspersed with hopeful connection.
