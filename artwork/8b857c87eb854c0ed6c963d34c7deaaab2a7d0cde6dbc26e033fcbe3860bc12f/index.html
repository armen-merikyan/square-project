Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a duality of relief and dread, expressed through spirals of color that converge at a corner. The palette of pale mint and faded gold invokes a contrast between uplift and heaviness, while storm gray steadies the visuals, creating a perturbing edge. The collision between dense dark and light accents creates both tension and release, echoing the spiral motif as it twists around the edge, embodying the themes from the seed.
