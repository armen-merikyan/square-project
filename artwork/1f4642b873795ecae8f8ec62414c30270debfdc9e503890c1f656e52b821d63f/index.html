Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between loneliness and connection through an interplay of colors and structural patterns. The palette of pinks and reds conveys warmth, while the gray and dark maroon offer a stark contrast, emphasizing feelings of isolation against connection. The pixels are arranged to create a visual conflict: the center is dense with broken symbols representing loneliness, surrounded by dots that suggest connection. The flow is right to left, inducing a sense of disruption reflective of emotional struggles, highlighted by a central burst of color that interrupts the grid-like arrangement. This composition evokes a story of hidden emotions battling for expression.
