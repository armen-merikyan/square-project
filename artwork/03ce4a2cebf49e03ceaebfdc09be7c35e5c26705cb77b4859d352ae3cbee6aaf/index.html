Artist Statement

This composition intertwines the duality of vulnerability and strength through color and form. The palette, inspired by 'awe_terror', uses deep teal for a calm yet heavy base that represents vulnerability, punctuated by cosmic indigo and cold gold as bright accents that suggest strength and resilience. The main contrast occurs centrally, where densely packed colors reflect the clash of emotions—the soft break of pale fields against the darker depths creates a visual echo of tension and release. Broken pixels are strategically placed to symbolize scars or interruptions in the journey from vulnerability to strength, with an overall sense of asymmetry to convey the unpredictable nature of these forces.
