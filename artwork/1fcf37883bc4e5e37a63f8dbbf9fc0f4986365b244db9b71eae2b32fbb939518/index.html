Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a delicate balance between 'quiet' and 'pressure', reflecting an interplay where dense clusters of color interact with open spaces. The palette, featuring muted off-white, soft dust gray, and deeper accents like compressed red against dark navy, creates a feeling of tension and release. The main collision occurs in the central pool of compressed color, symbolizing the shattering of calm into chaotic pressure and the interruption by softer tones. This juxtaposition echoes the seed concept, embodying both clarity and confusion.
