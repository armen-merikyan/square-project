Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between longing and resolve through a juxtaposition of soft, muted colors and sharper, more defined ones. The palette primarily consists of muted cream and iron gray, symbolizing restraint and longing, while accents of red oxide and small orange add vibrancy and urgency. The layout features a hollow center that is encircled by these stronger colors, creating a 'halo void' effect. The contrast between densely packed colors on one side and open, quiet spaces on the other highlights the duality of intimacy and distance. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, suggesting imperfection and the inherent struggle between chaos and order, reflecting the emotional journey implied in the seed.
