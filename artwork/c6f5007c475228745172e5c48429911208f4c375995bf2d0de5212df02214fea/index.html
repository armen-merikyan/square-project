Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension and interplay between pressure and quietness. The color palette of muted hues like 'off_white' and 'dust_gray' represents a soft landscape while the accents of 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy' introduce disturbance. The left and right sides reflect a near symmetry but are broken with irregular pixel adjustments, symbolizing the unpredictability of pressure against a serene backdrop. This collision creates a feeling of emotional weight against expansive quiet, drawing attention inward through the dense center while allowing for expansion on the edges.
