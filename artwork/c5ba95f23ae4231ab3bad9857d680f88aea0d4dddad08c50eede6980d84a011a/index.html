Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between longing and resolve through a carefully chosen color palette of soft, muted tones contrasted with vivid bursts. The main collision occurs in the center, where pulse marks of deep lavender-gray collide with flowing echoes of moon white, creating a dynamic push-pull effect. The broken pixels scattered throughout serve as reminders of past damages, adding depth and a sense of repair to the piece. The layout of falling pixels suggests a downpour of emotion, evoking feelings of serenity intertwined with urgency.
