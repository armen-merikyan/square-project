Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tone of longing, represented by a series of muted, low-density colors and interrupted patterns, contrasting with the resolute structure defined by bold, clear colors and steady repetition. The palette of deep hues reflects an underlying tension, the warm 'small_orange' pop against cool muted 'blackened_plum' symbolizes the struggle between chaos and order, while maintaining an air of intimacy through soft negative space. The main collision occurs centrally, where longing's soft pulse meets resolve's rigid shapes, creating a visual tension that echoes the seed's themes.
