Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension of vulnerability versus strength through a layered palette of pale mint and storm gray, symbolizing exposure and stability. The dense central area contrasts with a quiet channel of negative space that runs diagonally, representing a pathway of unresolved emotion. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, adding to the narrative of conflict. The crosshatch and ash field patterns collide and overlap, embodying the struggle between pressure and release.
