Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between defiance and submission, represented through stark contrasts in color: the warm, earthy tones symbolize warmth and acceptance while the cooler shades evoke detachment and pressure. Palette choices from the 'broken_memory' evoke nostalgia, adding depth to the emotional narrative. Key contrasts occur in the central grid, where dark, heavy colors collide with lighter shades, suggesting a shift from chaos to calmness. The arrangement of colors hints at a downward motion, echoing the bottom-weighted theme while maintaining negative space to emphasize the essence of quiet against a backdrop of noise.
