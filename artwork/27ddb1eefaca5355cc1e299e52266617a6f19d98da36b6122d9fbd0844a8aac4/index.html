Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a duality of defiance and submission, using angular clashes of dark colors against soft gradients. The palette of mourning black, deep blue, new leaf, pale yellow, and stone suggests a cycle of reformation and renewal amidst chaos. The main contrast occurs in the center where rigid black cracks interrupt the nurturing pale background, creating a sense of conflict. This design mirrors the seed's concept through aggressive upward movements in layout, embodying hope against submission, represented as a spiral of color around the center.
