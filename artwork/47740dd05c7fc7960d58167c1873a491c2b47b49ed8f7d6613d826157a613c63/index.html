Artist Statement

This composition conveys the emotional struggle between certainty and curiosity. The palette reflects the nuanced feelings, using warm ivory and soft gold for certainty, contrasted with deep wine and ash gray to express curiosity and chaos. The layout features stacked bands where the warm hues dominate, representing a clear order, while areas of darker colors with uneven borders suggest curiosity's unpredictability. The main collision occurs towards the center, where lighter colors interact with darker edges, creating a visual tension that embodies the push-pull nature of the concepts.
