Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tug-of-war between control and surrender. The combination of muted sepia tones and faded blue evoke a sense of nostalgia and reflection. The organized clusters in the center represent containment, while the scattered, broken dots around the edges symbolize a gentle release. The main contrast lies in the center where the dense patterns clash with the opened outlines, suggesting a struggle between pressure and freedom.
