Artist Statement

This composition creates a narrative of emotional collision, centering on the themes of restlessness and peace. The palette, made up of dark maroon, red, gray, and pink, embodies conflict and warmth, suggesting a sense of both anxiety and comfort. The piece primarily leans into restlessness with an uneven pulse provided by the static pattern, disrupted by diagonal strokes representing fleeting moments of peace. The transition from darker regions to warmer pink tones illustrates a struggle between tumult and tranquility, with the central collision creating a focal point of visual tension.
