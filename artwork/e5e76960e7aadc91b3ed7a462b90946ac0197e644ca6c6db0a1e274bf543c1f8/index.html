Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, using a palette of warm yellow and cool muted purple to represent this duality. The outer frame, dark and dense, symbolizes confusion with a jagged edge layout, while the inner core radiates lightness, offering clarity. The collision is prominent at the center where the warm brightness meets the dark, creating a visual echo that mirrors the emotional conflict. Broken pixels add layers of imperfection, invoking a sense of fragmentation which is core to confusion, while the overall radial layout slows the eye's movement, suggesting a gentle pull towards understanding.
