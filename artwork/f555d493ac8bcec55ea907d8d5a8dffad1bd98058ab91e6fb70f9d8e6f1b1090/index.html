Artist Statement

The emotional tone toggles between restlessness and peace, embodied through a palette that juxtaposes warm shadows against cool blues. The collision occurs in the central region, where chaotic stepwell patterns of restless colors intersect with the stabilizing presence of the pressure band, creating a sense of tension. Broken pixels are scattered, representing the imperfections of memory and motion, allowing for a sense of unresolved energy that aligns with the seed concept of emotional contrasts and a balance test. This grid layout subtly whispers to the viewer about the ongoing struggle between calmness and chaos, framed within an abstract representation.
