Artist Statement

The emotional tone is one of contrast, with loneliness represented by darker hues creating a sense of isolation, while connection emerges through lighter tones that hint at movement and potential engagement. The palette, which includes navy and dim cyan, evokes depth and subtle brightness. The main collision occurs in the center, with a burst of light colors surrounded by darker ones, illustrating the tension between exposed connection and hidden loneliness. The crosshatch pattern against a rising signal layout embodies the struggle and interplay between intimacy and isolation.
