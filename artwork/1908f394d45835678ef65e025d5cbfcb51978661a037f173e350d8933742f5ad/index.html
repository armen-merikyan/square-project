Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between defiance and submission, using a palette that juxtaposes warm and cool colors. The selected colors embody a narrative where vibrant pink and red accents disrupt the subdued gray and dark maroon fields, illustrating conflict and resolution. The main contrast occurs at the center where the patchwork and thread patterns collide, suggesting an inward spiral of resistance against surrounding pressure. The arrangement embraces dynamic tension, with broken pixels representing the scars of experience, encapsulating the emotional messiness of the concepts.
