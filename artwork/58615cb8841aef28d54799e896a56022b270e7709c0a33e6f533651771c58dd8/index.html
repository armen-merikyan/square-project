Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines defiance and submission, illustrated through a mix of warm and cool colors. The palette of burnt orange and dark red contrasts with the muted charcoal and gold shades, evoking a sense of conflict and resolution. The patterns represent defiance (stepping stairs) emerging from the submission pattern (target) through fragmented and falling pixel arrangements, creating a sense of movement from the bottom left to the upper right. The arrangement employs a mostly empty space to embody tension, with heavier colors weighted towards the bottom, signifying a struggle against closure. The interactions within the composition create an emotional conversation between open versus closed, resonating with the complexities of love and betrayal.
