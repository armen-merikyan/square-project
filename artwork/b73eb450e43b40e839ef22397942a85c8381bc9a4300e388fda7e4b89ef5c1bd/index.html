Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the contrast of longing and resolve, symbolizing a struggle between entrapment in thoughts and the desire for clarity. The palette, consisting of burnt orange and dark red, evokes warmth and intensity, representing longing, while charcoal provides a backdrop of steady resolve. The collision occurs at the center where a dense cluster of burnt orange pixels signifies desire, which interrupts and collides with the structured threads of charcoal. This blend creates a powerful visual narrative of emotional tension and resolution that reflects the seed’s attributes without becoming literal.
