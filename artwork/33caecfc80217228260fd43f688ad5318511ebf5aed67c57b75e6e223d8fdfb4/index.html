Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between fear and hope through a contrast of dark and light. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green reflects the emotional spectrum, with dark colors representing fear's jagged edges and the golden hues signifying light and hope's upward flow. The split vertical layout conveys a sense of opposing forces, while the delicate crosshatch pattern echoes the struggle between the two emotional states. The broken pixels add imperfection, suggesting the unrest inherent in both fear and hope.
