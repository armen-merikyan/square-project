Artist Statement

This piece echoes the emotional duality of longing and resolve. The interplay of dense areas and quiet fields embodies the contrast between unfulfilled desires and steadfast determination. A palette of ripe greens and honey golds suggests growth and warmth, while deep olive and cream introduce tension and calmness. The focus lies at the center, where overlapping motifs—a lattice of longing against fragmented dots of resolve—create a core of collision, encouraging the viewer to navigate through an emotional landscape of unfinished thoughts versus defined boundaries. Broken pixels subtly disrupt the flow, reflecting the theme of repair amidst damage, enhancing the narrative complexity.
