Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional interplay between longing and resolve through contrasting textures and patterns. The selected palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold evokes warmth against a backdrop of depth and shadow. The patterns of checker and teeth collide in the center, emphasizing the tension between yearning and stability. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, subtly reflecting the complexity of human emotions while maintaining negative space that allows for breathing room amidst the density. The overall structure follows an eroded symmetry, signifying the transformative journey between chaos and order.
