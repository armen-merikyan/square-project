Artist Statement

The emotional tone resonates with the struggle between defiance and submission. The palette of soft and muted colors contrasts with sharp accents to evoke tension. The outer rows embody a 'pressure border' representing submission, while the inner 4x4 features a 'constellation' pattern embodying defiance, creating a central collision of colors. The uneven arrangement reflects chaos vs order, symbolizing the unresolved nature of the interaction.
