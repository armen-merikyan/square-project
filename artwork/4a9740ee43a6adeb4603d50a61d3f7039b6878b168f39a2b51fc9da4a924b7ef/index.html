Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between certainty and curiosity, with a strong presence of certainty represented by darker shades providing a grounded background, while curious accents of light colors introduce disruption and inquiry. The palette blends warm coral and soft amber against cold slate and blue-black, creating a nuanced scene of tension. The main collision occurs at the intersection of dark backgrounds and vibrant accents in the center of the grid, symbolizing the struggle between established patterns and emerging uncertainties.
