Artist Statement

The emotional tone is driven by the dichotomy of spark and ash, representing life vs. stillness. The selected palette of pale aqua and alarm red introduces a vibrant tension against the muted mist gray and ink blue, allowing the contrasting emotions to resonate. The collision occurs primarily in the center where vibrant colors meet softer tones, representing an emotional struggle. The patterns of tide and checker interact at jagged edges, creating a dynamic visual rhythm that embodies conflict and resolution. Negative space accompanies this tension, inviting contemplation amidst the density.
