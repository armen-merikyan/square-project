Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the softness of tenderness with the explosive nature of rage. The selected palette, comprising saturated reds and muted blacks with touches of pale blue and white, reflects both the intensity and calmness in this duality. The vivid red lines represent rage's jagged intrusions, while the ripple patterns in pale blue act as counterpoints, suggesting moments of tenderness. The main contrast occurs centrally, where vibrant reds clash with soft blues, creating a visual conflict that embodies the seed's themes of turmoil and peace. The design includes intentional 'broken pixels' in dark colors to add depth and imperfection, emphasizing emotional scars and healing.
