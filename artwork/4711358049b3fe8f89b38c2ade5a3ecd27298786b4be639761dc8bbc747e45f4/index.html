Artist Statement

This piece contrasts the emotions of loneliness and connection, with a palette that blends warm pinks and deep maroons to create tension. The arrangement features a top-weighted flow that suggests tears or falling ash, while broken pixels add an element of imperfection, reflecting hidden vulnerabilities. The main contrast emerges in the center, where bolder colors pulse against muted grays, illustrating the push-pull between isolation and the yearning for connection.
