Artist Statement

This composition embodies the juxtaposition of joy and grief, with a warm palette representing joy (yellow and orange) contrasted against muted tones of deep blue and muted purple illustrating grief. The zigzag flow symbolizes the ups and downs of emotional experiences, while the broken edges evoke a sense of fragmentation and complexity in feelings. Main contrasts occur where vibrant colors collide with deeper hues, creating tension and a sense of unresolved emotion. This abstraction tells a story of rising signals of hope amidst the creeping weight of grief.
