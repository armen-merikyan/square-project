Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve, utilizing a palette that balances warm and cool tones. The warm ivory and soft gold represent the yearning for connection, while deep wine and ash gray symbolize the weight of resolve, creating a complex emotional backdrop. The main collision occurs in the center, where the softer hues clash with bolder tones, reflecting the struggle between hope and fortitude. The pattern of scattered 'constellations' evokes the sense of yearning amidst structured noise, alongside broken pixels that represent disruptions in harmony, reinforcing the theme of resolution amidst chaos.
