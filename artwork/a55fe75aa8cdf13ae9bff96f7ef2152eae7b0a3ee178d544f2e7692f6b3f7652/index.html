Artist Statement

This composition expresses the tension between envy (upper rows) and gratitude (lower rows) as the palette transitions from dark, dense hues to lighter, muted tones. The navy blue represents deep envy, while the dim cyan evokes feelings of gratitude and hope. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, reflecting the unresolved emotions stemming from the contrasting feelings. The edge style and the upward vs. downward flow embody a struggle between rising aspirations and falling fears, emphasizing the inner conflict. The placement creates a visual dialogue of density and emptiness, making way for an impactful abstraction.
