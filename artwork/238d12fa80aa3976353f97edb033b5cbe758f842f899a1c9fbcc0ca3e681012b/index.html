Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the piece is rooted in the tension between panic and control, symbolized through sharp contrasts and layered patterns. The selected palette of electric blue and magenta against the deeper blacks evokes a feeling of instability and urgency, creating a conflict that resonates with the prompt. The bright accents represent moments of hope or clarity battling against the engulfing dark tones, which convey the weight of isolation and fear. This duality shapes an abstract narrative of struggle, with the arrangement creating an upward flow that hints at aspiration amid disarray.
