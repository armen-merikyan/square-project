Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between the softness of pale hues and the severity of deep, dark accents. The chosen palette highlights this contrast, with a powder pink representing softness as it softly envelops areas, while sharp crimson and carbon black disrupt the calm. The main collision occurs at the center, where the darker colors interrupt the lighter, creating a dynamic push-pull. This arrangement echoes themes of 'exposure vs shelter' as more muted tones recede into a background while vibrant accents choose to assert themselves outward, embodying the complex interplay suggested by the seed.
