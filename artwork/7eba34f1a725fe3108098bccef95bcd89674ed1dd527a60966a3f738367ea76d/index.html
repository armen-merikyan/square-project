Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrast between softness and severity. The soft palette invokes warmth with powder pink and warm white, while carbon black and sharp crimson introduce a harsh tension. The center holds a denser arrangement, reflecting severity, surrounded by a patchwork of softer hues that resonate with gentleness. The collision occurs at the center, where chaos meets order, hinting at a struggle between opposing forces. The broken pixels disrupt the flow, emphasizing the theme of imperfection within the emotional spectrum.
