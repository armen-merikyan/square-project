Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between softness and severity through contrasting colors and placements. The soft top half is composed of warm whites and powder pinks, symbolizing tenderness and gentle edges. The bottom half shifts into darker tones of carbon black and steel gray, representing a stark severity. The delicate threads of the softness pattern intertwine with sharp dots of the severity pattern, creating a visual tension that echoes the emotional conflict noted in the seed. The central collision occurs through overlapping colors, suggesting a pulse of unresolved emotion, echoing themes of guilt and forgiveness.
