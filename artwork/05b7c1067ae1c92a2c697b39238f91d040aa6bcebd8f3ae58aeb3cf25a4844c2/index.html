Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the calmness of peace against the disruptive energy of restlessness. The palette employs warm coral and soft amber to symbolize peace, while cold slate and blue-black represent the turmoil of restlessness. The arrangement creates a central island of warm tones that confronts cooler, darker pixels around it, symbolizing a struggle. The pattern features a ring representing peace and a thread signifying restlessness; they interrupt and interlace, creating a sense of tension and release. This interplay of chaos and tranquility reflects the numerous interpretations of the seed's complexities.
