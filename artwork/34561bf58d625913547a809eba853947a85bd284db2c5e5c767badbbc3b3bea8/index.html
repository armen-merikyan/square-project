Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'spark vs ash' suggests a contrast between vibrant energy and subdued aftermath, reflected in the palette of fiery red and muted grays. The arrangement follows a descending flow, embodying memory collapsing into restraint, with the brightest colors clustering along the edges, creating a jagged tension where quick bursts collide with quiet decay. Thus, the spark sharply interrupts the ashen calm, forming a dynamic visual echo within the constrained layout.
