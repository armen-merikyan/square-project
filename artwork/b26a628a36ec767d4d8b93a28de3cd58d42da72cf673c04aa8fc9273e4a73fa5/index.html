Artist Statement

This artwork explores the dynamic tension between restlessness and peace. The palette, featuring deep navy, black, and soft dim cyan, invokes a sense of quiet yet unsettling contrast. The main collision occurs in the center, where the 'embers' pattern of restless pixels disrupts the 'flame' calm, creating a spiral pressure that draws the eye while leaving negative space to emphasize the struggle. The arrangement suggests a dance between motion and stillness, mirroring the seed's themes.
