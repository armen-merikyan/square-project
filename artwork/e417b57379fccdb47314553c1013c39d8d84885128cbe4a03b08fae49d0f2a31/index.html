Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the fight between softness and severity, captured through a palette balancing warm whites and stark blacks. The collision occurs in the center with sharp crimson as an accent against a backdrop of muted tones, creating a tension that resonates with the idea of pulse surviving amidst noise. The layout offers a glimpse of structure as the 'frame' pattern of softness and the 'crosshatch' of severity intersect. Broken pixels disrupt the composition, suggesting scars and echoes of unresolved contrasts.
