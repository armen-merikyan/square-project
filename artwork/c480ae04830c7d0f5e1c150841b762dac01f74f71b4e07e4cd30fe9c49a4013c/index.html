Artist Statement

The emotional tone expresses the duality of softness and severity through a palette that juxtaposes warm pastels against dark accents. The main contrast occurs diagonally across the square, creating a feeling of invasion where the softer colors (powder pink, warm white) spread from the corner, echoing the theme of openness, while the sharp crimson and carbon black introduce tension and severity. This collision not only signifies a battle between light and dark but also waves of pulsing emotion, embodying the complex interplay of guilt and forgiveness through textured layering.
