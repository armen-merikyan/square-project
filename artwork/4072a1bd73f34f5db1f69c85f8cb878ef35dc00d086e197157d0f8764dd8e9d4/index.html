Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between safety and exposure through a vertical split in color density. The palette combines warm browns and greens representing safety with pale blues and whites to signify exposure, creating a sense of both protection and vulnerability. The collision occurs at the center where warm tones meet cooler shades, illustrating the struggle between pressure and release. Broken pixels disrupt flow, adding layers of complexity and echoing the intricate balance of emotions suggested by the seed.
