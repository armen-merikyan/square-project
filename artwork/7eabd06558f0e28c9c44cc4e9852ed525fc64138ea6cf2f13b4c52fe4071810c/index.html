Artist Statement

The emotional tone is an interplay between tenderness and rage. The palette features saturated red and muted pale blue to depict conflict, evoking warmth against coolness. The central dense area, representing rage, is contrasted by a quieter border suggesting tenderness. This represents the struggle between overwhelming emotion and calm, with the colors bleeding into each other symbolizing the chaos of these feelings. The seed 'tear' inspired the jagged interruptions and soft pulses as colors collide in the center.
