Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between strength and vulnerability, creating a central tension. The palette of cosmic indigo and cold gold signifies a juxtaposition of deep introspection against bright aspirations, while void black serves as a grounding contrast. The broken frame layout introduces intentional gaps, mirroring breaks in strength and moments of vulnerability. The collision happens at the center, with dense blocks of strength that break into softer, pale stair patterns, symbolizing emotional shifts where one feels both stable and exposed.
