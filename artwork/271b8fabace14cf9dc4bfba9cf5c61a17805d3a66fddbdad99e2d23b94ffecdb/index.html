Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between clarity and confusion, represented by the contrasting colors of electric blue and magenta against the deep black. The pattern contrasts dense segments against sections of negative space, embodying the struggle of symmetry versus break. The collision occurs where vibrant accents of electric blue disrupt the dark fields, enhancing the sense of chaos within clarity. The arrangement is reminiscent of fragmented thoughts, embodying the seed's themes of confusion and clarity, represented abstractly rather than literally.
