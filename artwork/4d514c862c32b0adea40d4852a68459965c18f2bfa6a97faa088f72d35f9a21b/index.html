Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the contrast between softness and severity, emphasizing how these elements interact in life. The palette combines gentle tones like powder pink and warm white with the starkness of carbon black and sharp crimson, symbolizing the gentle power of vulnerability against harsh realities. The collision occurs predominantly in the center of the square, where fractured pixels (the 'shatter' pattern) disrupt the stable framing of the 'frame' pattern, representing a delicate boundary between acceptance and guilt. This arrangement creates a visual experience of tension and flow, embodying the pulse of conflicting emotions.
