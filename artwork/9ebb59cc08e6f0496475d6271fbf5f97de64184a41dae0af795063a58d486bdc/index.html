Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork expresses the tension between vulnerability and strength using contrasting colors. The chosen palette incorporates 'ripe green' and 'deep olive' representing strength and density, while 'honey gold' and 'cream' evoke a soft, vulnerable feel, creating an interplay of shadows and lights. The main collision occurs in the center, where the 'echo' pattern of dense greens interrupts the 'lattice' pattern of creamy hues. This creates a rich narrative of exposure through tension and interplay of colors, capturing the essence of strength emerging from vulnerability.
