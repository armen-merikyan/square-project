Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of loneliness and connection, using a stark black and white palette with shades of red and pale blue to reflect tension and calm. The black and white grids suggest isolation while the red serves as impactful bursts of connection, creating a stark contrast against the negative space. Each pixel placement enhances the feeling of a hidden connection in a sparsely populated universe, embodying the essence of the seed through intentional asymmetry and pressure. The overall composition flows from outer noise to inner calm, making the piece resonate deeply with the themes of hidden versus exposed.
