Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between clarity (signal) and distortion (noise). The palette balances vibrant accents like hot magenta and electric blue against the overwhelming presence of static gray and dead black, embodying moments of breakthrough amidst chaos. The main collision occurs in the center, where the signal's pulse prevails against the backdrop of shatter-like noise patterns. This artwork captures the essence of fluctuating memories and voices, creating interference that both disrupts and emphasizes the vibrancy of moments remembered.
