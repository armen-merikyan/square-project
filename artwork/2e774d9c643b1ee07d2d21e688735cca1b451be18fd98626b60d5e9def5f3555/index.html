Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and protection. The chosen palette merges warm brown and cold white, evoking a soft barrier against harsh exposure. The main collision occurs vertically through the use of color patterns: barcodes signify exposure while static elements symbolize safety. The arrangement dances upward, suggesting hope amid pressure, and the presence of broken pixels adds intentional disruptions, enhancing the feeling of imperfection within the balance of safety and exposure.
