Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, featuring muted gray with sharp pink and red accents, reflects a struggle between stability and tension. The left side employs broken dots representing trust, creating an open, unbroken path, while the right is disrupted by a maze-like pattern embodying suspicion. The central collision invites the viewer into a dynamic space where emotion intersects, emphasizing a delicate balance of resilience and fragility, echoed by the use of jagged edges.
