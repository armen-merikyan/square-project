Artist Statement

This artwork conveys emotional tension between defiance and submission using a minimal yet impactful palette. The dominant deep navy represents calm yet assertive defiance, while the lighter dim cyan signifies submission and openness. The collision occurs centrally, with heavy dark colors contrasting against lighter tones, suggesting weight and lightness. The diagonal flow from bottom to top creates a feeling of rising tension, complemented by broken pixels scattered to evoke imperfection and fragility in emotional struggles.
