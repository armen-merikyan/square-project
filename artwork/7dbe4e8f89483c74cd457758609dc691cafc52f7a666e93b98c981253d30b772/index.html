Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between relief and dread through a contrasting palette of deep shadows and soft highlights. The colors shift from dark violet encapsulating dread to pale green suggesting relief, with a pattern of maze and target motifs creating a dynamic conflict. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, echoing the theme of uneven emotional landscapes. The flow from top to bottom mirrors the weight of dread pressing down, while the bright accents punctuate the darkness with glimpses of hope.
