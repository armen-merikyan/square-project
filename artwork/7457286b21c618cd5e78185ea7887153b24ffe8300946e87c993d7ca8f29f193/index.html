Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between relief and dread. The contrasting palette of storm gray and faded gold represents the struggle between dark foreboding and warm comfort. The density of pixel clusters at the bottom evokes a sense of pressure, while the soft transitions to pale mint and soft sky colors express moments of relief. The diagonal movement of dread collides with the lattice of relief patterns, emphasizing a crossing point at the center, embodying a struggle where chaos meets order.
