Artist Statement

The artwork explores the tension between restlessness and peace, creating a dialogue between static noise and gentle pulsations. The palette evokes warmth with 'rose_clay' and 'lamp_gold' to symbolize moments of peace, contrasting these with cooler 'cool_blue' and 'distant_lilac' shades representing restlessness. The diagonal flow indicates a movement from chaos in the bottom corner to attempts at calm in the upper area, creating a dynamic interplay of dots and sparks that interrupt and echo each other. This design embraces broken pixels as intentional disruptions that reflect the imperfections of memory and the fragmentation of experiences.
