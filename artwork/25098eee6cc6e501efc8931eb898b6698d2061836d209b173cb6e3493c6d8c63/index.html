Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional dichotomy of rage and calm through the interplay of sharp, disruptive elements against soft, tranquil spaces. The palette features hot reds clashing with cool pale blues and whites, embodying the tension between explosive feelings and serene containment. The central collision of bright colors dominated by the chaotic red threads encapsulates the intersection of rage, while calm frames in soft blues create a protective boundary, allowing for moments of quiet. This results in a layered narrative that echoes the seed's themes of signal vs. noise and eroded symmetry, creating an abstract arrangement that resonates with unresolved emotional dynamics.
