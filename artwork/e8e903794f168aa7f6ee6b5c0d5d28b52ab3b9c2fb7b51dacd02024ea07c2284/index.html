Artist Statement

This artwork explores the dynamic interplay between joy and grief, represented through contrasting colors. The palette, featuring vibrant yellows and deeper blues, captures the duality of hope and sorrow. The composition employs a split horizontal layout, allowing for a vivid wave of warm tones (representing joy) to clash against the darker, muted hues (symbolizing grief). The emotional tension arises from the interruptive nature of the pixel arrangement; bright accents punctuate the more subdued fields, creating a ripple effect reminiscent of waves. The overall rhythm follows a clockwise spiral, reinforcing the cyclical nature of these emotions, while the careful placement of broken pixels introduces an element of imperfection, suggesting the raw humanity behind these feelings. This results in a complex narrative depicted through abstract form rather than defined symbols.
