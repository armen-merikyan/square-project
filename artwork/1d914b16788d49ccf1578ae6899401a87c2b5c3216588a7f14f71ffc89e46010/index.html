Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between confusion and clarity. The emotional palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green emphasizes depth and warmth, with stark contrasts to evoke uncertainty. The outer edge in deep colors represents confusion, while the inner core, touched by hints of gold and pale green, suggests clarity. The thick outlines and varying densities create a flowing, pulsating rhythm, suggesting movement and disruption through the falling pixels motif. The arrangement balances hard edges against softer gradients, presenting a story of fracture and unity.
