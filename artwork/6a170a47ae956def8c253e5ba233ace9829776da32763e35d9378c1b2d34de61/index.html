Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through contrasting warm and cool tones. The palette, inspired by 'intimacy_distance', embodies a feeling of distant warmth (rose_clay) meeting firm presence (cool_blue), creating a pulse that encapsulates both serenity and urgency. The layout, featuring two small centers of activity separated by expansive negative space, reflects a delicate balance between connectivity and isolation. The strategic placement of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, symbolizing scars that tell a story of repair and damage, further enhancing the narrative of longing versus resolve.
