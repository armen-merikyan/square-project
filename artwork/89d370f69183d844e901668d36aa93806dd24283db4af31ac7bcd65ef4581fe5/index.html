Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between love and betrayal through an interplay of warm and cool tones. The palette, featuring pinks and reds contrasting against grays and dark maroon, evokes a feeling of emotional warmth shadowed by betrayal. Symmetry is subtly disrupted by asymmetry in the color distribution, creating a visual echo of the theme. The main collision occurs along the diagonals, suggesting upward movement yet interrupted by darker, sharper edges, which signify conflict. The layout aspect of 'rising_signal' reinforces the theme of hope amidst despair.
