Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between pressure and quietude, conveyed through the contrasting colors of dark navy and faint peach. The dense clusters of dark colors suggest pressure, particularly along the left edge, while the lighter colors represent a release, creating an opening on the right side of the square. This interplay is reflected in the zigzag flow of the pattern that interrupts the calm with sharp edges, capturing the essence of the seed's themes of contrast, collision, and chaos.
