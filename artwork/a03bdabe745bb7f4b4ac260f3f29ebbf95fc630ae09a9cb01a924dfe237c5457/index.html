Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this square reflects a tension between vulnerability and protection. The chosen palette mixes warm browns and soft greens to convey safety, while pale skin and open blue evoke exposure. The main contrast occurs at the diagonal axis where the lighter hues of exposure clash with darker shades of safety, creating a visual echo that captures the essence of conflict and resolution. The grid employs a tidal pattern to signify the gentle weight of safety while allowing disruptions, delivering a sense of layered complexity and interaction without literal representation.
