Artist Statement

The emotional tone presents a duality of joy and grief, represented through a stark palette of warm yellows and oranges clashing against deep blues and purples. The outer edges embody the feeling of grief—cold and muted—a subtle reminder of sadness, while the inner pixels bring joyous spontaneity, creating a bubbling motion that feels like life amidst stillness. The decision for a split layout establishes an impactful contrast where joy vibrates against the dense weight of grief, evoking a sense of inner conflict. Broken pixels introduce deliberate imperfection, enhancing the emotional narrative of machine-like precision capturing human emotions. This structure embodies a stair-step movement, leading the eye upward, mirroring the struggle between feeling overwhelmed by grief and the hope that joy can bring, ultimately leaving space for contemplation.
