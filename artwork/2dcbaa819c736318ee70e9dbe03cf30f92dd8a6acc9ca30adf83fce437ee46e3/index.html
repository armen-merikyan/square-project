Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dichotomy of longing and resolve, articulated through a palette that embodies quiet pressure and emotional depth. The muted colors create a serene foundation, while the deep navy and subtle red add tension and focus, illustrating the conflict between yearning and determination. Main contrasts occur in the central area where the thread patterns collide with the stripes, suggesting a struggle between stability and chaos. The choice of broken pixels enhances the sense of imperfection and disruption, embodying themes of repair versus damage.
