Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends softness with severity, creating a dialogue between gentle hues and sharp contrasts. The palette features powder pinks and warm whites against deep blacks and grays, symbolizing the duality of comfort and stark reality. The central collision occurs with black and sharp crimson pixels interrupting softer colors, reflecting the tension between guilt and forgiveness. The spiral pattern subtly suggests movement, with the edge style grounding it, leaving enough negative space to enhance this contrast.
