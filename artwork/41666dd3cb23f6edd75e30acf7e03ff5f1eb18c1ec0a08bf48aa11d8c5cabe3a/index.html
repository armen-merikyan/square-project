Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional charge between vulnerability and strength, utilizing a palette of sepia, tan, dust_gray, and faded_blue to evoke a broken memory. The exposed light in the center signifies vulnerability, clashing against dense strength represented by dark edges. The layout mirrors on either side while deliberately incorporating broken pixels to emphasize imperfect connections, embodying the tension of exposed signals versus the weight of sheltering structures. The contrast occurs primarily in the center, where light meets shadow, suggesting a narrative of both fragility and resilience.
