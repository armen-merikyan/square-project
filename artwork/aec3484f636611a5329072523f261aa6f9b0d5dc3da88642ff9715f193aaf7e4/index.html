Artist Statement

This artwork portrays the emotional tension between fear and hope through a jagged yet rhythmic pattern. The chosen palette consists of deep blacks and dark violets to represent fear, contrasting against vibrant soft gold and pale green hues that suggest hope and growth. The arrangement plays with density and negative space, where the bottom half is loaded with dark tones, creating a feeling of pressure, while the lighter colors rise from the center to symbolize the ascent of hope. The zigzag flow enhances this movement, making the viewer feel both the weight of fear and the lifting potential of hope. Overall, this abstract embodies a collision of emotions, structured to invite reflection on the balance between these two powerful forces.
