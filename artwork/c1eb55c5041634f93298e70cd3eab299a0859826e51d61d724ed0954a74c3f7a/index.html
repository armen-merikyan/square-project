Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between longing and resolve, illustrated through contrasting colors and shapes. The palette connects to the theme of loneliness and connection, favoring dark navy with hints of dim cyan to represent quiet urgency. The main collision occurs in the center where the pressure bands of longing create dense clusters that are interrupted by mirror-like reflective spaces of resolve, suggesting moments of clarity amidst chaos. This square evokes feelings of yearning tempered by strength, resulting in an abstract narrative of pulse and reflection.
