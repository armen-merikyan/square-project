Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between confusion and clarity through a juxtaposition of chaotic and structured patterns. The palette, inspired by 'broken_memory', evokes nostalgia with tones of sepia and faded blue, offering a contrast between warm and cool hues. The density on one side reflects turmoil, while the lighter colors create a sense of calm and clarity on the opposing side. Broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, mirroring the concept of false symmetry and broken features, implying a deeper narrative of fractured emotions.
