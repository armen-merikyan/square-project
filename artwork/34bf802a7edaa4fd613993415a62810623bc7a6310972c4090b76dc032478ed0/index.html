Artist Statement

This piece captures the duality of control and surrender through contrasting color placement and patterns. The emotional tone reflects a struggle between contained pressure (represented by the structured, muted plum and rain blue) and the yielding softness of surrender (suggested by soft cyan and lavender gray). The color palette is intentionally melancholic, evoking feelings of longing and quiet reflection. The central focus, where intense colors collide, symbolizes the overwhelming nature of conflicting emotions, while the edges, rendered in quieter tones, signify moments of introspection. The 10% negative space serves to underscore the dialogue between chaos and calm, allowing the viewer to feel both the weight of contentment and the push of tension.
