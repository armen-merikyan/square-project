Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional contrast between awe and terror through a duality of structured stripes and chaotic flame patterns. The warm palette of yellow and orange represents awe, while the deep blue suggests a lurking terror. The vertical split layout reinforces the division of emotions, with the left side conveying a sense of stability and curiosity, and the right side expressing instability and chaos. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, symbolizing fragmented memories impacting perception. The layering suggests a shift from calm into pressure, reflecting the dynamic interplay of emotions.
