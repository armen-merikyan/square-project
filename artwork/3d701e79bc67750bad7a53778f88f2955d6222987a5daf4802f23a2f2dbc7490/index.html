Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional contrast of defiance and submission through the interplay of color and pattern. The palette, comprising soft pinks, warm whites, and sharp crimson, evokes a sense of conflict, where warm hues suggest defiance and cool tones hint at submission. The center predominantly features the defiant 'ladder' pattern in bold colors, symbolizing strength and resistance, while the surrounding area represents submission through the more subdued 'lattice' pattern. The jagged edges suggest an erosion of symmetry, creating a dynamic tension that pulls the viewer inward while maintaining negative space for visual rest. The broken pixels disrupt the patterns, echoing the imperfections inherent in the struggle between these opposing emotions.
