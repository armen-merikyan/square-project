Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between turmoil (envy) and serenity (gratitude). The palette incorporates warm oranges and yellows against cool muted purples and deep blues to reflect this clash. The contrast peaks with jagged interruptions in an otherwise harmonious arrangement, emphasizing the tension between rising and falling emotions. The layout suggests an empty room filled with both light and dark, with 'soft edges' creating a feeling of lingering echoes and unresolved feelings.
