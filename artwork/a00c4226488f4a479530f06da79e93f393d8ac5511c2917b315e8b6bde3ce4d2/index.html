Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting patterns and colors. The left side carries a soft, dot-like pattern symbolizing trust, using light hues from the palette to create a feeling of openness and stability. The right side fractures into a scarred pattern, embodying suspicion and creating a feeling of darkness and anxiety. A central diagonal flow with contrasting colors invokes disruption and collision. The palette consists of muted shades like dust gray and off-white, with touches of compressed red for emotional spikes, illustrating the struggle between quietly unsettling suspicion and more inviting trust. This conflict embodies the complexities of human emotion—the artwork encourages the viewer to contemplate the nuances of perception and connection.
