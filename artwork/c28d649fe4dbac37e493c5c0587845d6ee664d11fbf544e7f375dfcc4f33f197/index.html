Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between quietude and pressure. The palette of off white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach conveys a soft yet weighted atmosphere, where dense pixels gather in the upper left, contrasting with a more spacious and muted lower right. The interplay of sharp edges and softer tones creates a visual argument, echoing the seed concepts of tension and release, alongside a subtle diagonal flow that suggests movement against stillness.
