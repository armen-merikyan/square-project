Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork captures the collision between control and panic, illustrating the struggle between a structured grid and chaotic interruptions. The chosen palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold symbolizes raw energy and tension. The contrast between the orderly grid pattern and the jarring burned edges conveys a sense of impending chaos, while the symmetry is disturbed through intentional disruption in the placement of colors. Negative spaces represent moments of calm amidst the tension, anchoring the piece.
