Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension of longing contrasted with the solidity of resolve. The palette of deep blues and muted purples evokes a sense of depth and reflection, while the warm yellows and oranges represent the reaching out against barriers. Color placement highlights the central collision between warmth and coolness, creating a dense center that spirals subtly towards emptier edges. This builds a narrative of seeking connection while facing the uncertainty that distance brings.
