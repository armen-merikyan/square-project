Artist Statement

This piece explores the interplay between compression and release, reflecting the rhythm of emotional collisions. Darker shades create areas of pressure, contrasting with bursts of brighter colors that suggest moments of release and echo. The palette includes dense grays that convey a sense of weight, interrupted by vivid accents expressing the sudden shifts in emotional landscapes. The pattern suggests a narrative of tension giving way to unexpected release, echoing the seed's themes.
