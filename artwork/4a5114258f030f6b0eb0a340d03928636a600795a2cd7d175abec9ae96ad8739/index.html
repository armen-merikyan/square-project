Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between confusion and clarity, illustrated through a palette derived from a broken memory theme. The colors oscillate between dark sepia and faded blue, representing emotional layers of depth and disorder. The arrangement captures falling pixels that interact asymmetrically across the canvas, creating a visual rhythm where dense clusters and quiet spaces collide. The main contrast lies in the thick outlines against the hollow centers, suggesting a mechanical quality trapped within organic chaos.
