Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the contrast between longing and resolve; the palette of yellow and muted purple suggests warmth and melancholy. The dominant yellow on one side creates a sense of urgency, interrupted by dark blue spots symbolizing broken connections. The layout features a mirrored structure, with intentional broken pixels disrupting the symmetry, embodying the feeling of repair amidst damage. The arrangement shows a dance between dense clusters of color and wide negative spaces, mirroring the pull between hope and despair.
