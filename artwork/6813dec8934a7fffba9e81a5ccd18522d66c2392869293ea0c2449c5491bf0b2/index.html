Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece embodies the contrast between vulnerability and strength, conveyed through a palette of deep blues and muted blacks, interrupted by patches of soft yellow and vibrant green. The layout emphasizes a central fractured pattern suggesting protection and exposure. Broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, while the radial flow radiates from the center, highlighting the struggle between stability and fragility. The main collision occurs in the center, where deeper hues clash with lighter accents, illustrating the emotional narrative of hidden tension and illumination.
