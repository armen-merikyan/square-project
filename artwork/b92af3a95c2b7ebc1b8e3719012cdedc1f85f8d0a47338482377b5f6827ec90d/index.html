Artist Statement

This piece conveys a sense of emotional contrast through the juxtaposition of dark and light colors. The palette incorporates cool navy and dim cyan against a backdrop of deep black, embodying the themes of emptiness and desire. The central light pixel breaks into surrounding dense dark, symbolizing rising hope amidst profound loneliness. The patterns echo a feeling of struggle, with stripes symbolizing emptiness and rain representing desire. This collision suggests a movement from dark heaviness to flickers of light, laying bare the raw dynamics of human emotion.
