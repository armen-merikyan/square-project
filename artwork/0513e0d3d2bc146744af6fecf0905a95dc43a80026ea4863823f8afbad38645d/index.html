Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts between joy and grief reveals a struggle between brightness and darkness. The palette includes bright yellow and orange representing joy, while deep blue and muted purple symbolize grief. A zigzag pattern captures the tension between these emotions, signaling a rising conflict. The use of glowing edges adds a glimmer of hope amidst the chaos, while broken pixels introduce an imperfection that echoes the complexities of human emotion. The row symmetry subtly interrupted emphasizes the contrast of order and chaos.
