Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece explores the duality of vulnerability and strength, represented through an interplay of warm and cool colors. The palette features vibrant yellows and muted purples, suggesting a flooding warmth contrasted with the cool depth of blue, embodying tension and release. The dominant central pattern reflects strength with its warmer hues, while the peripheral elements introduce breaks and soft transitions, indicating vulnerability. The collision occurs at the center where warmth meets cooler shades, simulating pressure and balance, encapsulating the conflict described in the seed.
