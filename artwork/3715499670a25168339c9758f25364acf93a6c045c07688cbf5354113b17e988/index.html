Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the tension between longing and resolve, embodied through a palette that shifts from muted tones to sharper contrasts. The quiet pressure of colors like off-white and dust-gray evokes a sense of calm and yearning, while the compressed red adds urgency against a dark navy backdrop, drawing the eye to the center as a collision point that emphasizes the emotional struggle. The top half of the square, representing longing, features flowing tide patterns with broken pixels indicating unresolved feelings. The bottom half, representing resolve, adopts a static approach, with solid blocks reinforcing strength. This juxtaposition captures the essence of the seed: the intertwining of desires and boundaries, with negative space allowing the emotional weight to breathe, suggesting both serenity and urgency in a cohesive abstract narrative.
