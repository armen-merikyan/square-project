Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between control and surrender. Utilizing a palette of clean white and ink black contrasted with soft violet gray and warning orange enhances the contrasting themes. The left side of the square embodies control with a 'veil' pattern creating a structured yet soft arrangement, while the right side features a 'glitch' pattern depicting chaos and interruption. The main collision occurs in the center, where both patterns overlap and introduce broken pixels, symbolizing the fraying edges of emotional pressure. This piece encapsulates a feeling of conflict and coexistence within boundaries, suggesting a dialogue between containment and overflow.
