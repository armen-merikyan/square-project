Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between nostalgia and progress. A palette of dark violet, soft gold, pale green, and black evokes a sense of hope against the backdrop of fear. The split horizontal layout suggests conflict, with the upper half hinting at the calm reminiscence of nostalgia portrayed through soft gold and pale green, while the lower half bursts with dark violet and black, representing the heavy weight of progress. Broken pixels create an imperfect finish, representative of unresolved feelings. The main contrast occurs at the horizontal division, where nostalgic hues collide with the weighty progress, creating a layered narrative of tension and release.
