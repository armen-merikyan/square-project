Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the tension between clarity (signal) and confusion (noise) within an environment marked by hard edges and disruptions. The palette of navy, black, white, and dim cyan evokes feelings of isolation and struggle, where navy represents hope (the signal) and black embodies chaos (the noise). The central collision occurs primarily around the center, where the 'braid' pattern of signal meets the 'barcode' noise pattern, creating a turbulent interplay. The broken pixels reflect the imperfections of both experiences, enhancing the artwork's narrative of an ongoing struggle between clarity and confusion.
