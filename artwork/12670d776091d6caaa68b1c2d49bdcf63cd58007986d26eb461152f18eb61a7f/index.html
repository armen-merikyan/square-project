Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork oscillates between the hot intensity of rage and the soft embrace of tenderness. Using the 'rage_calm' palette, the red signifies panic and intensity, while pale blue and white introduce calm restraint. The main contrast occurs in the center, where jagged, mirror-like red pixels clash with smooth, rain-like blue tones, suggesting the struggle between rage and tenderness. The overall structure begins dense and tight at the center, gradually easing outwards, creating an inward spiral flow that visually narrates the journey from pressure to release. This layout embodies the tension and resolution inherent in the seed's emotional landscape.
