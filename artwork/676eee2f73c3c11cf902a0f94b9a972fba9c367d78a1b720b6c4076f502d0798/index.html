Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional contrast between panic and control by juxtaposing soft grays and vibrant reds within a mirrored layout, subtly disrupted by broken pixels. The palette of pink, red, and dark maroon evokes feelings of tension and emotional betrayal, while the diagonal flow introduces a sense of movement. The dominance of darker tones encapsulates an underlying weight, suggesting instability amidst the controlled grid pattern, creating a visual echo of emotional conflict.
