Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a battle between longing and resolve, where muted undertones reflect the ache of desire contrasted by bursts of assertive color. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue embodies memories—muted but present. The main collision occurs centrally, with the 'hinge' and 'ring' patterns intertwining, creating a dynamic interplay of structure and vulnerability, reflecting the seed's theme of broken yet yearning forms.
