Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the tension between envy and gratitude, where dark colors symbolize envy and light colors reflect gratitude. The chosen palette, consisting of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green, emphasizes the weight of envy against the lightness of gratitude, showcasing the essence of pressure and release. Main contrast arises from the dark edges paired with soft gold centers, where they clash and balance throughout the piece. The zigzag flow represents the tumultuous interaction between the emotions, creating a visual rhythm that echoes both lightness and weight. The circular ‘moon’ seed translates into a dense core surrounded by chaotic edges, maintaining a fragmented yet harmonious abstract narrative.
