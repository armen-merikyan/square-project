Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece juxtaposes feelings of envy and gratitude, representing a dynamic between density and emptiness. The palette of deep navy and subtle dim cyan signifies loneliness and connection, creating a foundation that conveys complex emotions. The main contrast arises from the dense central patterns reflecting envy, which are interrupted by softer edges that symbolize gratitude. The arrangement of colors creates a pulse of pressure that ascends and descends, evoking a rhythm of rising hopes and falling fears. This abstract pattern embodies the dualities present in the seed while maintaining a non-literal interpretation.
