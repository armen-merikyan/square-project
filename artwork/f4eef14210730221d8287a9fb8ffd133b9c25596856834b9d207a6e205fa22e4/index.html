Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores a vibrant clash of desire and restraint, juxtaposing saturated colors to symbolize the warmth of longing against muted hues representing control. The palette of rich reds and deep plums captures hunger, while faint creams and grays offer a sense of calm order. The main contrast occurs at the center, creating a dynamic collision where bright marks push against a restrained grid of darker colors, evoking feelings of pressure and desire held back by social boundaries. The abstract patterns emerge from the themes of spiral pressures, suggesting a tension between freedom and confinement, resulting in a visual dance of tension and release.
