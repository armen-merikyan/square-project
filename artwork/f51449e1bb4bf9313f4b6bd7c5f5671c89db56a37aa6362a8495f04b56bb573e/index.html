Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between 'spark' and 'ash', representing fleeting moments of vitality against the weight of residue. The palette intertwines warm accents of 'lamp_gold' and 'rose_clay' with cool undertones of 'cool_blue' and 'distant_lilac', reflecting the contrasting nature of vitality against stillness. The dominant presence of angular, jagged edges and the lattice pattern create a radiant conflict centered on the grid, emphasizing the collision of chaotic energy and settled silence.
