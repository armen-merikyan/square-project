Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts peace and restlessness, reflected through a color palette of ripe green and deep olive against lighter cream and honey gold interruptions. The layout features a dim overall environment with one bright, active cluster, symbolizing fleeting peace amidst chaos. The main collision occurs at the center with a bright honey gold core, sharply contrasting the surrounding darker tones, creating a dynamic tension. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, suggesting memories that are scattered but impactful, embodying the subtle disturbances within a peaceful mind. This abstract pattern captures unresolved motion and glimpses of tranquility with chaotic undertones.
