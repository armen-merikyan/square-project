Artist Statement

The emotional tone of awe and terror creates a dynamic conflict, illustrated through stark contrasts between dark and light. The chosen palette of navy, black, white, and dim cyan balances heaviness against illumination, symbolizing a struggle between overwhelming presence and fleeting hope. The arrangement suggests a rising tension, where jagged edges fragment the calm, creating a sense of interruption. The placement of broken pixels introduces imperfection, acknowledging the chaotic interplay of these emotions.
