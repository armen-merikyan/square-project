Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between restlessness and peace, using contrasting colors to depict motion and memory. The palette of yellows and purples introduces warmth and calmness, while deep blues introduce depth, reflecting inner turmoil. The layout follows a diagonal argument, creating an unstable edge through color placement that suggests internal conflict. The collision of vivid yellow against muted tones represents sparks of inspiration battling against dark memories, enriching the abstract narrative.
