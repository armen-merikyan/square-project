Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette, consisting of soft off-white and faint peach tones contrasted with darker navy and compressed red, reflects the fragile yet powerful interplay. The main collision of colors occurs at the center, where a pale interior is set against a halo-like border, emphasizing exposure and shelter. The juxtaposition of the ladder pattern symbolizing vulnerability against the halo of strength creates a visual rhythm that represents resilience amidst tension.
