Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between calmness and agitation, depicted through a palette of warm corals representing warmth and tension, contrasted with cold slates suggesting distance and discomfort. The main collision occurs in the diagonal arrangement where vibrant colors interact chaotically, suggesting the push-and-pull of conflicting emotions. The broken pixels create interruptions resembling moments of doubt amidst clarity, supporting the abstract notion of internal conflict inspired by the seed.
