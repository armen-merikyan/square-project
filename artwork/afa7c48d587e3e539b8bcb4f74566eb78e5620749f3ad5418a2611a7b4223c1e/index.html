Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve using a palette of ripe green and honey gold, which contrast with deep olive and cream tones. The left side embodies 'longing' through stacked patterns, while the right showcases 'resolve' with a patchwork of colors. The main collision occurs in the center, where warm hues interrupt cooler tones, symbolizing a struggle between containment and overflow. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, enhancing the theme of repair amidst damage, creating visual echoes that resonate with the emotional narrative.
