Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between joy and grief through a contrast in color density and arrangement. The palette of muted purple and deep blue represents grief's heaviness, while the bright yellows and oranges symbolize joy's vibrancy. The zigzag pattern of colors creates an upward motion, suggesting a rising signal of hope against a backdrop of chaotic emotions, where collisions happen at the borders. The arrangement has an uneven distribution, reflecting the imperfections inherent in balancing these two contrasting feelings.
