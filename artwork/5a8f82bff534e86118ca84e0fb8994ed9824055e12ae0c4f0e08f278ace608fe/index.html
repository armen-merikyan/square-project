Artist Statement

This artwork channels the tension between love and betrayal through a delicate balance of contrasting colors and patterns. The palette focuses on soft pink and warm red representing love, contrasted sharply with the deep gray and dark maroon symbolizing betrayal. The checker pattern of betrayal collides with a split-screen layout for love, creating visual dissonance. This leaves open spaces that evoke feelings of loneliness and emptiness, mirroring the 'empty room' concept, while the glowing edges suggest both clarity and fracture, embodying the emotional collisions highlighted in the seed. The arrangement also promotes a directional flow from darker to lighter areas, capturing the thematic journey from despair to connection, yet feeling unresolved at times, reflecting the emotional complexity of the themes.
