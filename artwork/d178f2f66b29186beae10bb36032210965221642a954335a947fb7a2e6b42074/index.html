Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between structured clarity and swirling intrigue. The chosen palette of muted tones from dark navy and dust gray to faint peach evokes a sense of quiet pressure, while a segment of bright compressed red introduces an element of curiosity that disrupts the solid foundations of dark tones. The main collision occurs in the center, where the bright red interacts with the darker colors, symbolizing rising signals through a chaotic tide, reflecting both hope and uncertainty. The layout emphasizes upward movement, suggesting the pursuit of understanding amidst confusion, while the negative space allows the colors to breathe, reinforcing a sense of tension.
