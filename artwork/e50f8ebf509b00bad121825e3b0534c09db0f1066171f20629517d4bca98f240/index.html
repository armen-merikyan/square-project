Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between defiance and submission. The palette, featuring muted tones of off_white and dark_navy, contrasts with bursts of compressed_red to symbolize resistance. The collision occurs primarily in the diagonal layout, where defiant starkness meets subdued submission, creating tension. The seed's themes of invasion and spirals suggested a chaotic yet structured arrangement that echoes the feeling of conflict and resolution within the square.
