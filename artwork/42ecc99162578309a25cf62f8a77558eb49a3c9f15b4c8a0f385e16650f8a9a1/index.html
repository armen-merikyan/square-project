Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers around the conflict between defiance and submission, depicted through a jagged diagonal layout that represents angular resistance against soft collapse. The palette of dark violet juxtaposed with soft gold conveys a struggle between tension and warmth, with the dark vignettes symbolizing the chaotic edges opposing the quiet center. The central collision is emphasized through broken pixels—each representing an imperfection or disruption in the orderly flow, suggesting the complexities of confronting and yielding in emotional states.
