Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts 'spark' and 'ash', illustrating a struggle between vitality and decay. I selected a palette of soft, muted colors with shimmering accents to create a sense of melancholy wonder. The central collision of chaotic jagged edges against a mirrored field reflects the struggle of memory and erasure, with distinct bright pixels radiating from a darker background. This presentation embodies the tension between bright moments of life and the surrounding quietness, creating an ongoing dialogue within the composition.
