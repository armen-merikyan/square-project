Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional dichotomy of defiance and submission. The palette of pink, dark maroon, gray, and red signifies the warmth of defiance clashing with the cool, muted tones of submission. The center pulsates with bold red, representing strength and focus, surrounded by gray and maroon in a spiral layout, creating a tension-filled boundary. Broken pixels add imperfection, mirroring the disturbances of conflict and resolution. The interplay of dense clusters and negative space captures the essence of a spiraling confrontation, leaving the viewer contemplating the balance of power.
