Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between rage and calm through a clash of colors. The palette juxtaposes the vibrant emotional heat of red against the coolness of pale blue and black, suggesting a battle between passion and tranquility. The broken frame pattern instills a sense of disruption, while patches of calmness attempt to contain the energy. The main collision occurs towards the center, where the bright red pixels clash with quieter fields, emphasizing the struggle within disturbances of noise and feeling.
