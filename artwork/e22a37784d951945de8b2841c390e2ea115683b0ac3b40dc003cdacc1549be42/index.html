Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic struggle between desire and emptiness, explored through warm and cool hues clashing across the canvas. The palette features deep blacks and soft gold against muted greens, highlighting a desire for warmth amidst the starkness of emptiness. The layout follows a mirror-broken structure that introduces intentional asymmetry, allowing for emotional interruptions to emerge through color placement. The main collision occurs at the center, where warm golds radiate outward, conflicting with cooler tones that suggest a lingering hollowness. The arrangement captures the tension of rising aspiration versus the weight of unfulfilled longing in a series of layered, abstract signals.
