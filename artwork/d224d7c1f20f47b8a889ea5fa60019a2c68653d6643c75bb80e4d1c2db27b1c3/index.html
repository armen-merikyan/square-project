Artist Statement

The emotional tone is built around the tension between clarity and chaos, with the contrast between the rich 'signal_green' and electric intensity of 'hot_magenta' representing pure emotional pulses against chaotic noise. The 'static_gray' acts as a neutral field, allowing bursts of color to emerge, while 'dead_black' has been used to create fragmented boundaries. The pattern of 'maze' and 'constellation' creates a visual collision, making the clarity of the green pulse feel tenuous amidst surrounding distortion. The main contrast is located centrally and expands outward, suggesting the struggle for order amid overwhelming noise.
