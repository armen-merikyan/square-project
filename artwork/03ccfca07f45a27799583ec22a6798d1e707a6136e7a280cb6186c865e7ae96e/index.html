Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dualities of longing and resolve, illustrated through the palette of muted purples and vibrant oranges, suggesting warmth and grief. The primary contrast occurs at the top-left corner, where warm colors densely converge, symbolizing the urgency of desire. This juxtaposes against a cool, quiet field of deep blues, evoking serenity and calm. The patterns of glitch create a sense of interruption, while the ring forms represent structure and clarity, reflecting the 'vessel' shape seed abstractly through interwoven threads. Broken pixels intentionally disrupt the flow, embodying the imperfections of repair against damage.
