Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between desire and restraint. The palette employs saturated tones of hot coral and blood orange to embody desire, contrasted with muted charcoal, suggesting restraint. The structure of broken frames and islands creates visual collisions and interruptions, emphasizing the push and pull of feelings. The central focus highlights an uneven border, as if a potent energy is contained yet yearning for release, echoing the themes of a trapped longing amidst an atmosphere of isolation.
