Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension of joy and grief through a blend of warm and cool tones. The palette features yellow and orange to represent joy, contrasting with deep blue and muted purple for grief. The layout simulates falling pixels, creating a sense of descent and pressure, where clusters of colors interrupt the quieter spaces, evoking a rhythm of emotional collision. The arrangement suggests a struggle between light and dark, with a deliberate use of broken pixels to create imperfection, alluding to the fragmented nature of feeling.
