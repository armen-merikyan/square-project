Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional contrast between longing and resolve. The palette blends the warmth of 'shelter_green' and 'warm_brown' with the coolness of 'cold_white' and 'open_blue', supporting a narrative of tension and serenity. The broken pixels disrupt the flow, simulating the repair vs damage motif. The main collision occurs at the bottom left, where densely clustered tones intermingle, creating a visual pulse that reflects urgency amidst a quiet surrounding. The pattern suggests a braid that feels unified yet jagged, embodying both connection and fractures.
