Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the piece captures a dynamic interplay of pressure and calm, expressed through a palette that juxtaposes deep blues and blacks against vibrant oranges and yellows. The contrast lies in the collision of saturated warm colors each breaking through muted shades, suggesting moments of emotional release amidst quiet. The pattern of color placement evokes echoes of rhythm—clusters of intensity met by open spaces, portraying the dance between pressure and relief as dictated by the seed's narrative on emotion.
