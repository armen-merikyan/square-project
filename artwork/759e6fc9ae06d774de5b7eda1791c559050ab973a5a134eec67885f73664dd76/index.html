Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of control and surrender, where the palette of cosmic indigo, void black, star white, cold gold, and deep teal represents both tension and release. The main collision occurs in the center, portraying an overflowing abundance of color that resonates with feelings of pressure and surrender. The patterns embody spark and ripple effects, mirroring a delicate balance between sharp interruptions and soft yielding, resulting in a visual narrative that evokes both calm and chaos. The layout descends like tears, adding to the emotional weight and sense of release.
