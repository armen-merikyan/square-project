Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts between tension and calmness, represented by the duality in colors and patterns. The palette of subdued hues like 'dust_gray' and 'faint_peach' interacts with bolder elements like 'compressed_red,' creating visual tension that embodies 'pressure.' The distribution of colors generates a diagonal flow, with denser patches on one side representing pressure that gradually loosens into negative space on the opposite side, symbolizing clarity. The collision occurs primarily on the left side where darker colors cluster, contrasting sharply with lighter shades, evoking emotional conflict while highlighting the broken symmetry, inspired by the seed's themes of tension and release.
