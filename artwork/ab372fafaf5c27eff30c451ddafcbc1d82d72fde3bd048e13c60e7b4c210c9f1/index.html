Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between love and betrayal through a palette of warm and cool tones. The soft pink and vibrant red evoke love, while the dark maroon represents the sharp sting of betrayal. Contrasting shapes subtly interact, with broken pixels evoking imperfection and dissonance, while the diagonal rise suggests a struggle between ascent and downfall. The pattern arrangement creates a rhythmic flow, counterpointing the central collision of emotions with areas of negative space, emphasizing the unresolved tension within the abstract narrative.
