Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the interplay of vulnerability and strength, reflecting the tension between exposed and protected elements. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon conveys warmth and conflict. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense patterns of strength (red) intertwine with lighter shades representing vulnerability (pink). The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, emphasizing the tension of connection and alienation depicted in the seed.
