Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the explosive nature of rage against the calming influence of tranquility. This is expressed through a palette of intense reds juxtaposed with cooler blues and whites. The collision occurs in the central region, where sharp, jagged patterns (representing rage) interact with the soothing, stepwell waves of calm. The broken pixels signify moments of disruption, echoing the chaotic nature of noise against the ordered calmness. This interplay reflects the struggle between containment and overflow, aligning with the notions of eroded symmetry and the zigzag flow suggested in the seed.
