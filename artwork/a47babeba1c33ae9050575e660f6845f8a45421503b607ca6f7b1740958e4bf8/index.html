Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulated by 'awe vs terror' is refracted through a palette of muted sepia and dust gray, evoking nostalgia and fragmented memory. The color arrangement results in stark contrasts, with darker tones flowing upwards to suggest struggle and a sense of looming terror on one side, while lighter colors cascade downward, imbuing the composition with an ambiance of wonder. The jagged edges create a visual tension that mirrors life's unpredictable nature, echoing the instability of memories. The layout balances between center and chaos, alluding to the breakage of clear paths in memory and emotion.
