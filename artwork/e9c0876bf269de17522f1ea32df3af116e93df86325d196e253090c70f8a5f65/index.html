Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional interplay between safety and exposure through contrasting colors and textures. The palette combines warm tones of shelter green and brown representing safety, with cool pale skin and blue hues illustrating exposure. The central area features a juxtaposition of softness and weight, creating a rhythmic pulse. The 'two_pulses' layout creates a split that visually narrates the tension between vulnerability and protection, while broken pixels introduce imperfections symbolizing the fragility of this balance.
