Artist Statement

This piece embodies the interplay between clarity and chaos, represented by contrasting colors. The dense center showcases a vibrant, pulsating core filled with the rhythm of clean signals, while the surrounding areas are layered in muted hues, highlighting a static noise field. The navy and black palette conveys a sense of depth and introspection, with the single white representing fleeting clarity amidst confusion. The collision occurs at the center, where the dominant signal interrupts the surrounding noise, creating a visual metaphor for the struggle to maintain clarity against overwhelming static.
