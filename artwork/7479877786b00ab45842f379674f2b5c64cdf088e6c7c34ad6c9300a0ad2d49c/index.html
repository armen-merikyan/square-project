Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission, captured through a juxtaposition of warm and cool tones. The palette, named 'ancient_fire', draws from burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold, signifying both a fierce refusal and a quiet retreat. The central collision lies in the contrast of bright colors against the surrounding muted shades, creating an inner spiral that suggests a struggle. The 'broken_frame' layout allows emotional leaks, representing the conflict between containment and release, while broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, emphasizing the chaotic interplay of emotions.
