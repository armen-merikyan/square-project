Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between safety and exposure through contrasting color placements that suggest a central vulnerability surrounded by protective hues. The palette features warm browns and soft greens suggesting shelter, juxtaposed with pale skin and cool whites representing exposure. The layout, which clusters brighter colors in the center, creates an active echo of vulnerability amidst darker, muted fields of safety, emphasizing the contrast between the two emotional states.
