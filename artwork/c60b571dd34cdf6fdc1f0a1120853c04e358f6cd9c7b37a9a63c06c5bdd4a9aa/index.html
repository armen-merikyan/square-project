Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between restlessness and peace, using the palette of warm coral and cold slate to depict the collision. The warm tones suggest comfort while the cool tones pull toward chaos. The layout is structured with a pressure corner that expands dynamically into space, illustrating the struggle between inner calm and external agitation. This pattern features strong color contrasts at the edges, where restlessness sharply meets the tender balance, reflecting the seed's themes of motion and response.
