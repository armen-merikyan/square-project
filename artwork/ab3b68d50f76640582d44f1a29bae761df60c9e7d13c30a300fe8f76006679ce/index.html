Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between warmth (love) and desolation (betrayal). The chosen palette of pinks and dark maroons emphasizes love's softness against betrayal's harshness. A checker pattern symbolizes order, while broken, jagged edges represent chaos. The main contrast occurs between vibrant pinks disrupted by deep maroon spots, reflecting emotional interruptions and fractures. This grid-like composition reveals density versus emptiness, telling a story of a heart fragmented yet surrounded by chaotic beauty.
