Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between defiance and submission, creating a rhythm of resistance and acceptance. The color palette of navy, black, white, and dim cyan conveys loneliness and a signal of conflict. The jagged edges indicate chaos, while the scattered repair layout suggests attempts to bridge gaps. The center features a dense cluster of dark colors symbolizing defiance, while the lighter pixels disrupt and attempt to 'repair' the edges, evoking a sense of struggle and unresolved tension.
