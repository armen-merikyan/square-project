Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional duality of vulnerability and strength, utilizing a palette of soft pastels and dark contrasts to depict an exposed center surrounded by protective boundaries. The patterns of braided flow and ladder-like structures represent the interplay between fragility and stability. The collision of the soft sky and storm gray colors indicates a shift from calmness to tension, evoking a sense of resilience amid disruption. The arrangement allows for a quiet channel of negative space, accentuating the contrasting feelings.
