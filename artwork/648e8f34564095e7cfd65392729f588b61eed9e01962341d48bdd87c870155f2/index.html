Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between softness and severity through a gradient of color pressures. The palette juxtaposes soft powder pink and warm white against stark carbon black and sharp crimson, illustrating a dance of delicate warmth against a backdrop of cold austerity. The main contrast builds along the central axis, where dense clusters of severity clash with softer transitions, while broken pixels signify emotional interruptions, embodying guilt and forgiveness. The overall flow from corner to corner evokes a spiral movement that subtly releases tension through varying densities.
