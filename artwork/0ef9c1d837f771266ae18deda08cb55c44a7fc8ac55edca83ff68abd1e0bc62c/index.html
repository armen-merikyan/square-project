Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the juxtaposition of awe and terror, captured through a contrast of structured clarity and chaotic disarray. The palette features warm yellow and orange symbolizing awe, blended with muted blues and purples to evoke a sense of terror. This results in a visual tension where orderly patterns clash with fragmented textures, emphasizing instability. A central light section suggests stability, while surrounding darker hues suggest chaos—conveying an emotional narrative of a wavering balance between wonder and fear.
