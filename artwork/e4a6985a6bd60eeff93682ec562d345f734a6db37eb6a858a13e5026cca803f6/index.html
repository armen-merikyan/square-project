Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between vulnerability and protection, embodied through a palette of warm brown and cold white. The layout allows colors to descend like anticipated rain, where the central section contrasts softly with a dense border. This collision allows for a sense of impending exposure against a stable frame, creating a continuous echo of weight and lightness, enhancing the feeling of softness meeting severity. The broken pixels disrupt the flow subtly, representing scars of experience.
