Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the emotional tension of 'quiet' versus 'pressure' through distinct color clusters. The palette of off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach establishes a balance between softness and intensity. The arrangement places heavy, darker colors on the left, conveying weight and pressure, while the right side lightens into quieter tones, suggesting release. The dynamic flow of jagged edges and a spiral layout creates a visual tension—sharp contrasts overlap with softer patches, embodying the struggle and interplay of emotions. Broken pixels add imperfection, symbolizing disruption amidst the order.
