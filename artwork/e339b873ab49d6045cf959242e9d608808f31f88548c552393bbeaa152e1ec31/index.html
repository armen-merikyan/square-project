Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional dynamism between desire and emptiness. The hot coral and blood orange symbolize a saturated pull of desire, contrasting against the cool hollow blue and charcoal representing emptiness. The central area is dense, filled with vibrant colors, while the perimeter features low saturation, indicating containment and unfulfilled longing. The arrangement juxtaposes a warm core surrounded by cold emptiness, reflecting an ongoing tension between growth and restraint. Broken pixels introduce disruptions, mimicking the fragility of desire encased within limits.
