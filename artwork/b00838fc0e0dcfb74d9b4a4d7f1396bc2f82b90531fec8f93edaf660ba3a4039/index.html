Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between calm and rage, illustrated through the juxtaposition of soft pale blues and stark reds. The palette emphasizes these emotions; the deep red represents rage and compression while the pale blue signifies calmness and quietness. The layout frames an inner core of compressed reds with a calming outer edge, creating a tension that embodies 'collisions of emotion.' The broken pixels scattered throughout interrupt the symmetry, capturing moments of chaos against an otherwise peaceful backdrop.
