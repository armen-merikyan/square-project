Artist Statement

The artwork evokes a dichotomy of awe and terror through the use of contrasting dark and luminous colors. The color palette, featuring deep blacks, dark violets, soft golds, and pale greens, creates a sense of depth and tension. The black and dark violet represent terror's weight, while the soft gold and pale green intersperse as glimmers of awe. This artwork plays with stability and imperfection through broken pixel placements that hint at a corrupted symmetry, resonating with the emotional conflict expressed in the seed. The rising signal layout creates upward motion, while the checker pattern conveys order, interrupted by rain-like streaks that symbolize chaos. Overall, the composition captures a nuanced interplay between hope and anxiety.
