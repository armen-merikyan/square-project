Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise). The palette, consisting of rich navy and dim cyan, evokes a sense of isolation and subtle energy. The contrast occurs predominantly at the center, where bright color channels intersect with dark, static noise patterns. This collision encapsulates the struggle of a singular, readable rhythm attempting to emerge amidst overwhelming interference, mirroring the core ideas from the seed. The use of hard edges and broken pixels adds an element of disruption, emphasizing the theme of memory versus erasure.
