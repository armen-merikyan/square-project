Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve, using a palette that reflects mourning and renewal. The colors depict a subtle conflict: deep blues signify longing while pale yellow represents resolve. The main collision of color occurs diagonally, symbolizing the struggle between the push towards connection and the pull of individuality. The broken pixels introduce elements of imperfection, reminding viewers of the beauty of unresolved situations.
