Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between clarity and distortion, reflecting the essence of the seed through contrasting elements of signal (the clear pulse) and noise (static interference). The palette features a lonely signal with navy and black as dominant colors, representing a heavy backdrop, while dim cyan peeks through, suggesting flickering moments of clarity. The collision occurs centrally, where the clear paths of the signal clash with static patterns, creating a compelling dynamic across the canvas. Patterns of 'stairs' and 'rain' visually convey the struggle as they intermittently overlap, symbolizing how memories can both rise and drown in static.
