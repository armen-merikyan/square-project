Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between confusion and clarity, explored through a palette of muted earth tones contrasting with softer hues. The left half embodies confusion with darker, overlapping colors suggesting chaos, while the right side invokes clarity with lighter, mirrored tones. The arrangement signifies falling pixels, where the left side appears heavier, leading into a lighter, more open space on the right. Broken pixels are introduced throughout to create a sense of imperfection, emphasizing the narrative of fragmented clarity and chaotic confusion.
