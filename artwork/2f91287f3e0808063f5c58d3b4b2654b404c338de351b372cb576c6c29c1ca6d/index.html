Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional dichotomy of panic and control through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of sepia, tan, dust_gray, and faded_blue embodies the tension between warmth and coolness, creating a sense of instability and grounding. The layout suggests a rising signal, capturing the struggle of hope against rising stress, while the noise pattern introduces a chaotic element. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, symbolizing moments of interruption in the narrative. The contrast between densely clustered colors and calmer spaces highlights the contrast of overwhelming emotion versus the search for control.
