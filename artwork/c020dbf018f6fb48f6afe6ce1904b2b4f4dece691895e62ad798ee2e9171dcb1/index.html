Artist Statement

This abstract composition embodies the tension between loneliness and connection, resonating through a warm and cool palette. The inner concentration of deep blue symbolizes the isolation and nostalgia of loneliness, contrasting with bursts of orange and yellow that represent fleeting moments of connection. The thick outline reinforces the divide between inside and outside, framing the chaos of muted purples that brush against vibrant bursts, creating a sense of conflict. The broken pixel elements introduce imperfection, reflecting the unpredictable nature of emotions and relationships in this structured grid, while maintaining at least 10% negative space for introspection.
