Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between vulnerability and strength, framed by the contrasting themes of exposure and shelter. The palette of greens and golds conveys a sense of vitality intertwined with fragility, representing hope against dysfunction. The collision of the stronger, deeper greens forms a sturdy base, while the lighter tones offer a gentle disruption from above, resembling raindrops falling into a deep well. This interplay creates a rhythmic falling pattern that emphasizes both hard edges and soft breaks, alluding to the complexities of connection and alienation. The central focus is a heavier stroke of deep olive, surrounded by lighter hues that create a sense of both impact and breadth, allowing the viewer to feel the interplay of strength and vulnerability together.
