Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between pressure and quiet through a gradient of density, where darker pixels representing pressure cluster in the center while lighter hues spread outward, suggesting release. The chosen palette of off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach emphasizes this contrast. The dark navy and compressed red create a collision at the center, while off-white and faint peach illustrate moments of clarity and softness towards the edges, creating a delicate balance between chaos and serenity.
