Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the struggle between defiance and submission. The palette blends soft grays and pale aqua to evoke calmness, punctuated by the urgency of alarm red and ink blue, creating tension. The central collision occurs where the dark ink blue meets the bright alarm red, suggesting a battleground of emotions. The chosen patterns of hinge (defiance) and tide (submission) create a contrast that flows from the outer edges inward, symbolizing how chaos interacts with order. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, enhancing the feeling of disruption and unpredictability, culminating in a dynamic abstract story.
