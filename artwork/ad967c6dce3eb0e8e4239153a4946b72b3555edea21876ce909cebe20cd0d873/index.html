Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a balance between the fragility of exposure and the solidity of safety. The palette draws from soft greens and warm browns, evoking feelings of warmth and shelter while punctuated by pale skin tones and cold whites, suggesting vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center, where bright colors clash against darker shades, symbolizing the tension between protection and exposure. The abstract arrangement of colors creates a rhythm of pulsating contrasts, forming a visual echo of the themes represented in the seed.
