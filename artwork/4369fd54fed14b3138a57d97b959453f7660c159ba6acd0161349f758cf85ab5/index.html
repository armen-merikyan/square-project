Artist Statement

The emotional tone evoked by the seed contrasts tenderness with underlying rage. I selected a palette featuring deep blacks and muted blues to portray calmness, interspersed with sharp reds to signify disruption. The layout 'empty_room' permits a spacious feel, accentuating a central clash where rage bursts through layered gentle textures, creating a quilt of emotion. The saturated colors dominate at certain intersections, symbolizing moments of intense conflict, while 'broken pixels' intentionally mar the clarity, akin to how emotions often bleed into one another. This impact creates a stark narrative of vulnerability within chaos.
