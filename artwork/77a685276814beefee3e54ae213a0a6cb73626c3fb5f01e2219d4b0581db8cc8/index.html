Artist Statement

This artwork contrasts the tension between confusion and clarity. The left half, representing confusion, features a muddled palette of muted purples and deep blues, creating a sense of overwhelming noise and disruption. The right half, symbolizing clarity, employs brighter yellows and oranges to convey vibrancy and balance. The arrangement echoes the dissonance of broken pixels, leading to a collision of emotions where the left side's density meets the right side's openness. This contrast illustrates the emotional spectrum of anger transitioning into calm, resonating with the seed's narrative of symmetry disrupted by chaos.
