Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between control and panic, illustrated through a palette that juxtaposes the coolness of electric blue and the warmth of lime against the grounding impact of black. Edges are jagged to symbolize disruption and chaos, while the arrangement builds a zigzag flow to encapsulate the argument grid from the seed. The contrast between dense clusters and negative spaces suggests conflict, with vibrant pixels sparking against darker backgrounds, mirroring the struggle of joy opposing grief.
