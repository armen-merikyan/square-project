Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between panic and control through stark contrasts and jagged edges, highlighting the struggle within organized chaos. The color palette of deep navy and black represents the weight of panic, while the white spark signifies moments of clarity or control, allowing glimpses of optimism amidst turmoil. The inner 4x4 grid subtly contrasts with the chaotic outer pixels, creating an echo of structured disorder. Broken pixels remind us of imperfections and add depth to the visual narrative of this piece.
