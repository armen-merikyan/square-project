Artist Statement

This piece juxtaposes the emotional tension of vulnerability and strength using a palette rich in contrast. The left side embodies vulnerability with soft, pale tones that are exposed, while the right side asserts strength through deeper, more saturated colors that create a structured density. The central collision occurs between clear elements of ice blue and stark black, suggesting a wave of rising signal that interrupts the calm. The seed guided the abstract pattern by emphasizing the relationship between exposure and protection, crafting a piece that oscillates between vulnerability and strength.
