Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, utilizing a palette rich in contrasts. The 'desire_emptiness' colors create a rhythmic juxtaposition of warmth and coolness, with saturated accents of hot coral and blood orange colliding against the muted fields of charcoal and hollow blue. The density is skewed towards muted tones to represent the overarching theme of safety and exposure, while a cluster of bright colors suggesting resilience emerges in the center. The 'last_light' layout principle echoes the central contrast, creating a focal point that draws attention while the edges remain soft and somewhat broken. This friction delivers a dynamic interplay between exposed and sheltered emotions reminiscent of life's delicate balance.
