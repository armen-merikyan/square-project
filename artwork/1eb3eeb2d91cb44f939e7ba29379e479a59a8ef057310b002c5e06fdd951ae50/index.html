Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between emptiness and desire. The palette, evoking sepia tones and faded blues, represents a nostalgic yet fractured memory. The contrast lies in the dense edges filled with darker hues against a lighter core, symbolizing the bursts of emotional release amidst pressure. The central square is densely packed with color, suggesting protective desire, while the periphery offers a hint of emptiness, resulting in a push-pull dynamic that reflects the collisions of emotion. The layout emphasizes a frame-and-core concept, where the outside influences the interior through a wave vertical flow.
