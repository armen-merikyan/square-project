Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette combines sepia and faded blue to express warmth and distance, while dust gray adds a quiet contemplative space. The diagonal layout creates a strong dynamic, with longing occupying the lower left through a split screen pattern, emphasizing its wide quiet field and an echo of distant memory. Resolve, on the upper right, uses a maze pattern that interrupts the longings flowing through the middle, suggesting struggle against inner chaos. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, reinforcing the theme of collision within structured yet fragile emotional frameworks.
