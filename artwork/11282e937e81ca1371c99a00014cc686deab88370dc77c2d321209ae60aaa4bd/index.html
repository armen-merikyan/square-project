Artist Statement

The composition embodies the struggle between clarity (signal) and confusion (noise) through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of deep blacks and vibrant greens creates a dichotomy between the dense static and the bright signal, where electric blue and hot magenta introduce collisions that heighten the sense of tension. The falling pixel layout enhances the emotional narrative of overwhelming interference from noise while allowing the signal to attempt rise from the chaos. The central clash occurs primarily in the middle rows, with the rhythm being visually interrupted by darker tones, symbolizing the layers of memory and erasure.
