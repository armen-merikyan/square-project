Artist Statement

This artwork utilizes a tension between certainty and curiosity. The palette of deep olive and honey gold represents a grounding solidity, while the ripe green and cream accentuate bursts of exploration and surprise. The layout transitions from dim tones dominating the base to clusters of brightness that evoke peaks of curiosity rising through orderly chaos. The central collision sparked by the warped 'barcode' pattern interrupts the stable 'thread' design, creating a sense of movement suggestive of rising signals against receding edges. In this configuration, careful placement paves the way for tension and release across the grid, embodying a balance between restraint and exploration.
