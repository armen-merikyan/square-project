Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dichotomy of restlessness and peace, represented through a palette of greens and golds that signify growth and calmness mixed with deeper shades suggesting agitation. The layout features a quiet channel that cuts through a dense stepwell pattern, emphasizing the collision of motion with stillness. The off-center composition flows diagonally while the interrupted lines in the stepwell pattern create an unsettled border, translating the seed's essence into abstract forms.
