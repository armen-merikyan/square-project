Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting pattern choices. The palette blends soft pastels with deep black, suggesting a dance between softness and severity. The top left corner, cluttered with intense color clusters invoking suspicion, sparks an energetic clash with the calm areas of the right side, where orderly pixel arrangements symbolize trust. The collision at the central diagonal axis creates a dynamic flow that mirrors the struggle between these two emotions.
