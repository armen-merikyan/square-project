Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between restlessness and peace. Using deep teals and cosmic indigo for the chaotic elements, the design creates flame-like patterns that clash against tranquil star white patches that represent calmness. The arrangement showcases a diagonal invasion of chaos, creating a movement that feels energetic yet offset by quiet areas, embodying the seed's themes of memory versus motion. The contrasting colors evoke a sense of flickering emotions—dynamic yet contained.
