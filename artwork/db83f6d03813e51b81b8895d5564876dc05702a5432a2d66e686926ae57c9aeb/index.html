Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition reflects a delicate tension between longing and resolve. The palette, featuring stain brown, ash gray, warm ivory, soft gold, and deep wine, provides a rich emotional depth and contrast, illustrating the scars of longing and the strength of resolve. The soft browns and grays evoke a sense of nurturing yet urgent serenity, while the gold brings an accent of hope and light, highlighting moments of clarity amidst the chaos. The main contrast emerges strongly at the center, where broken pixels disrupt the flow, symbolizing the tension between aspiration and closure. The layout showcases a mirror effect with distinct interruptions, emphasizing the emotional pull and flow described in the seed. This abstract pattern captures the pulse of conflicting emotions with a deceptively simple visual structure that invites contemplation.
