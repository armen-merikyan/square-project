Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece contrasts panic with control, embodying the tension between chaos and stability. The chosen palette of deep blacks, dark violets, soft golds, and pale greens reflects the struggle—darkness representing panic and gold hinting at control and hope. The main collision occurs in the central area, where dense clusters of dark and light colors interact, symbolizing the break between stability and instability. This is further emphasized by the mirror-like qualities of the surrounding pixels, creating an uneasy symmetry that feels both comforting and unsettling, echoing the seed's themes of balance and fracture.
