Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between confusion and clarity by juxtaposing organized and chaotic patterns. The palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple reflects the contrast of warmth and calmness against intensity, suggesting an emotional oscillation. The main collision occurs in the top two rows where bright yellows intersperse with deep blues, representing the internal struggle between chaotic emotions and serene clarity. The falling pixel layout enhances the visual flow, creating a sense of movement that captures the pulse of emotion, while the broken pixels introduce imperfections that evoke a sense of disorder within the structured pattern.
