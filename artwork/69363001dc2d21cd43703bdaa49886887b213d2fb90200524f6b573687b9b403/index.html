Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of restlessness versus peace, exemplified by a stark contrast in color and arrangement. The palette, dominated by ice blue and accented by warning orange, creates a visual tension that reflects the interplay of calmness and unsettled energy. Restlessness is represented by a chaotic, mirrored pattern that appears in the overfilled corner, while peaceful, gentle noise spreads across the remaining space, creating an atmosphere of contrast. The use of broken pixels adds intentional disruption, echoing the theme of emotional scarring and imperfection, invoking a feeling of unresolved motion.
