Artist Statement

This abstract artwork embodies the emotional duality of longing and resolve, using a palette that evokes a sense of melancholy and wonder. Colors like rain blue and muted plum create a moody base, representing the weight of longing, while soft cyan and moon white introduce subtle light, hinting at the resolve. The main contrast occurs between the tightly packed vertical 'thread' patterns and scattered 'shatter' elements, symbolizing the push and pull of emotions. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, creating a sense of imperfection and fluidity that echoes the themes of repair versus damage.
