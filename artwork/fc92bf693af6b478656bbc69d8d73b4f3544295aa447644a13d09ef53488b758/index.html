Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette, featuring shades of red oxide and muted cream, suggests warmth and distance, creating a contrast between the intimate yearning and the steadiness of resolve. The diagonal layout reinforces a sense of falling, while the arrangement of darker tones colliding with lighter tones creates visual tension and depth. The chosen pattern types echo the emotional narrative: 'rain' representing longing in soft, falling lines, and 'ripple' suggesting the steadiness of resolve. The central collision occurs around the diagonal where the colors meet intensely, signifying the heart of the emotional journey.
