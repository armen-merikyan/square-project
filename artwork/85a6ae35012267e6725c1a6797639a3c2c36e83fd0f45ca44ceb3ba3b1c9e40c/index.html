Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between a focused signal and chaotic noise. The chosen palette of navy and dim cyan represents the clarity of the signal against the backdrop of black, symbolizing noise. The layout is eroded symmetry, suggesting a central pulse disrupted by scattered interference. The use of broken pixels creates a sense of imperfection, embodying fractured memories amidst an overwhelming static environment. The center features a dominant dark color with subtle cyan highlights, illustrating the struggle of clarity surviving within chaos.
