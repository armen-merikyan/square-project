Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the push and pull between vulnerability and strength, illustrated through a palette of deep reds and soft grays, symbolizing love and betrayal. The layout features a hollow center surrounded by vibrant colors, suggesting a protective barrier. The main collision occurs where the softer 'vulnerable' colors mix with the stronger 'defensive' shades, leading to tension in the central pattern. This visualizes the struggle between opposing forces, creating a narrative of resilience against adversity.
