Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional clash between restlessness and peace. The chosen palette, comprising deep blacks and warm creams, juxtaposes the unsettling elements against a soothing background. The main collision occurs diagonally—a representation of motion battling against the calmness of structured forms. The patterns of ripples and stair-like progressions create a sense of movement while maintaining anchored stability, embodying the seed of 'restlessness vs peace.'
