Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the delicate tension between vulnerability and strength, mirrored in the contrasting colors. The selected palette embodies resilience through earthy tones (moss green and rust) intertwined with the fragility of bone white and stone gray. The main collision occurs at the center, where the 'tiny plus' shape seed is represented by a dense cluster of warm hues, surrounded by cooler, quieter colors that evoke safety and exposure, reflecting the underlying themes of strength and vulnerability.
