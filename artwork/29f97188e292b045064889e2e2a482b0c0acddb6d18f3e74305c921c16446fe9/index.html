Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between relief and dread through a contrasting color palette of dark maroon and light pink. The arrangement showcases a top-weighted heaviness in the upper part, paralleling life's stresses, while a lighter ring pattern radiates from the lower corner, representing hope. The collision between dense dark colors and lighter tones creates a dynamic interplay, reflecting the unresolved emotions suggested by the seed. The maze-like structure implies complexity, while leaving negative spaces enhances the feeling of emptiness, mirroring an 'empty room' vibe.
