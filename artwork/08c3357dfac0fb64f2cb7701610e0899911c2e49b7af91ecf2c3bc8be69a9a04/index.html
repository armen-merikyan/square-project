Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional interplay of restlessness and peace through contrasting color placements and patterns. The palette uses deep navy and black to symbolize weight and unease, contrasted with soft dim cyan and white for moments of clarity and calm. The arrangement features central clusters of restless dots interrupted by gentle spark patterns on the edges, creating a dynamic movement that ebbs and flows, reflecting memory and motion. The primary collision occurs diagonally, where deeper colors intrude upon lighter fields, embodying the tension outlined in the seed.
