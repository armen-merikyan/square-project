Artist Statement

The emotional tone is centered around the tension between quietness and pressure, reflected through the interplay of color density across the square. The palette includes soft tones like off white and faint peach alongside deeper colors like dark navy and compressed red to illustrate the waves of pressure against a serene backdrop. The main collision occurs where the dense 'flood' pattern meets the softer, maze-like structures, emphasizing the conflict of these emotions. Shapes and placements symbolize the push and pull between chaos and calm, crafting an abstract landscape that evokes a complex emotional experience.
