Artist Statement

The piece captures a dynamic tension between longing and resolve. The palette marries deep wane and warm ivory, portraying a rich narrative of desire intertwined with stability. The layout positions a dense center with chaotic accents towards the border, suggesting a struggle between forces of order and chaos. The broken pixel elements represent both imperfection and a sense of openness, enhancing the feeling of intimacy alongside distance. Overall, the arrangement invokes a spiral of emotional currents that echoes the seed's complexity.
