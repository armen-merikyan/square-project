Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between hope and fear, with dark corners representing uncertainty and a soft gold at the center suggesting resilience. The contrasting patterns of spark and maze celebrate this collision of emotions, while the bottom-weighted edge creates pressure from below. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green mirrors this dichotomy, showcasing the interplay of density and light within the composition.
