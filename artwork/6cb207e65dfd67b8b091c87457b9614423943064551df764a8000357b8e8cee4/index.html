Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength through a palette of muted and bold colors. The soft pale interior represents fragility while the deeper hues create a protective proximity. The dominant color clusters symbolize strength, while subtle empty spaces remind us of vulnerability. The central collision happens in the spiral flow, where strength and vulnerability intertwine, creating a dynamic and emotive abstract pattern that invites contemplation.
