Artist Statement

This piece captures the fragile interplay between vulnerability and strength using a contrasting palette of warm reds and muted grays. The central area, drenched in deeper hues, symbolizes strength through dense radial patterns, while lighter crosshatching around the edges signifies vulnerability. The dominant color shifts create a collision at the center, where intensity meets calm, echoing the seed's themes of exposure and protection. The disruptions from broken pixels enhance the sense of fragility, reinforcing the emotional narrative.
