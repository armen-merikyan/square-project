Artist Statement

This piece embodies the push and pull between clarity and confusion through the interplay of structured and broken patterns. Utilizing a muted palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue, the composition explores emotional tumult while leaving space for reflection. The thick outlines contrast against hollow areas, suggesting a confrontational space where clarity collides with scattered confusion, underscored by a flow of falling pixels that create a rhythm of descent.
