Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay between softness and severity, which is expressed through a palette combining gentle, warm shades (powder pink and warm white) with stark, cold tones (carbon black and steel gray). The layout pulls heavily from the corner, creating an overpressure effect with the intensity of colors colliding primarily in the bottom left. The broken dot pattern introduces imperfection and chaos, while the flood pattern portrays the severity, bridging the emotional divide. This piece encapsulates the quiet chaos of unresolved emotions, inviting the viewer to navigate through layers of tension.
