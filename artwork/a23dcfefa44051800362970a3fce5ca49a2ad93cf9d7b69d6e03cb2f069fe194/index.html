Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through contrasting bold and muted colors. The palette of hot coral, blood orange, dust pink, charcoal, and hollow blue evokes desire and emptiness, showcasing a dialogue between warmth and coolness. The layout reflects a mirrored but disrupted symmetry, with intention behind the placement of broken pixels creating scars of experience. The central collision of warm hues suggests urgency, while the cooler edges embody serenity, symbolizing the struggle between being drawn into longing and the resolve to find balance. The 10% negative space amplifies the tension, creating a visual rhythm that resonates with the feeling of pulse and gesture.
