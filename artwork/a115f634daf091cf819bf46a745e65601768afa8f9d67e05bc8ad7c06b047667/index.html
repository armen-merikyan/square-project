Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork encapsulates the tension between vulnerability and strength, where the central pixels evoke an exposed quality surrounded by a more robust framing pattern, allowing for a reflective dialogue. The palette features warm shades like hot coral and blood orange to imply vitality and struggle, while cooler tones like charcoal and hollow blue bring in a contrasting sense of calmness and introspection. The main collision occurs at the center with brighter colors, illustrating the spark of strength amidst the overwhelming flood of vulnerability that frames it. The structure allows for a lingering sense of negative space, adding to the thoughtful observation of exposure versus protection, creating an abstract narrative of connection and isolation in its pixel arrangement.
