Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between fear and hope, using a palette of deep blacks and dark violets representing fear, contrasted with soft golds and pale greens for hope. The layout reflects a mirroring effect with slight asymmetry, symbolizing the delicate balance between these emotions. The overall flow moves from a dense center outward, echoing the inner conflict of rising hope amid dark pressures. The pattern of rain juxtaposed with grid-like formations embodies the struggle between chaos and order, while broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, emphasizing vulnerability.
