Artist Statement

The emotional tone translates to a conflict between assertive defiance and the subtle pull of submission. The chosen palette of electric blue and magenta clashes against the darkness of black, embodying a vibrant struggle within a predominantly closed field. The center's bright colors pulse boldly, disrupted by the surrounding quiet spaces filled with black, suggesting an uneven battle. Broken pixels interrupt the flow, symbolizing glitches in this emotional channel, enhancing the sense of chaos and order. The overall composition reflects a diagonal rise, starting from the corner, as an assertive brightness fighting for space against an enclosed, subdued field.
