Artist Statement

This abstract artwork explores the interplay of relief and dread, utilizing a palette that juxtaposes cool, calming tones with darker, oppressive hues. The soft sky and pale mint create a breath of fresh air, symbolizing relief, while near black and storm gray represent the heaviness of dread. The clash between these colors reveals a defined threshold, a center collision where emotions meet and contend. The layout emphasizes this shift, allowing negative space to breathe, while broken pixels interrupt the structure, representing imperfections in emotional states, evoking the duality of calmness and chaos.
