Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition explores the collision between nostalgia (warm, soft colors) and progress (cool, sharp colors). The chosen palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon evokes feelings of warmth and sadness, creating a deep contrast. The layout splits the square vertically, allowing nostalgic warmth to clash with the cooler progress on the right. This creates a visual tension—where vibrant reds and soft pinks disrupt the gray foundations, akin to memories colliding against future aspirations. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, reflecting the complexity of emotions and the negative space highlights the quiet amid the emotional chaos.
