Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between emptiness and desire through a distinct palette of warm and cool tones. The selection of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon embodies desire's warmth colliding with the cool, quiet aspects of emptiness. The use of a checker pattern represents the feeling of emptiness while dotted patterns invoke the sporadic nature of desire. The layout emphasizes negative space, suggesting a vast emotional landscape filled with longing and isolation, reflecting the contrasts outlined in the seed.
