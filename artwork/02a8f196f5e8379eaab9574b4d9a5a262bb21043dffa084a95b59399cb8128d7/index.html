Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional conflict of loneliness versus connection through a contrasting palette that plays on depth and floating fragments. The outer layer employs a rich, dark violet to evoke solitude and its weight, while the inner spaces use soft gold and pale green, suggesting warmth and potential connection. The irregular arrangement of broken pixels scattered among these contrasting colors represents the disturbance of memory and fragmented feeling, while also allowing areas of quiet space to breathe. This composition echoes the shift between heavy isolation and the lightness of connection, emphasizing the emotional depth.
