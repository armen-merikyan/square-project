Artist Statement

The emotional tone echoes the conflict between vulnerability and strength, symbolized through warm honey golds representing illumination clashing with darker olives that suggest density and protection. The layout features a diagonal split, with the cream and light ripe greens forming a halo of exposure, while deep olives and acid greens create a stark contrast of shelter. The main collision occurs at the center, where the 'glitch' of vulnerability meets the 'halo' of strength, capturing the pulse-like feeling of connectivity interspersed with moments of alienation. This arrangement evokes the rise of a signal amidst an overwhelming flood of emotion.
