Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a tug-of-war between control and surrender, depicted through the palette's stark contrasts. The muted palette (red_oxide, blackened_plum, muted_cream, iron_gray, small_orange) conveys deep introspection and weight. The interaction of dense clusters on one edge contrasts with quiet spaces elsewhere, representing the overflow and containment themes. Key collisions occur centrally, where vibrant accents disrupt the muted background, symbolizing sudden awakenings against a backdrop of restraint. The pattern of stripes against islands creates a rhythmic stutter, embodying the tension of control ebbing into surrender.
