Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between love and betrayal, using a palette of warm and cool colors to reflect the contrasting emotions. The blend of soft pinks and deep maroons with stark grays creates a feeling of complexity. The grid layout mirrors the argument between two opposing forces. The checker pattern (betrayal) intersects with soft noise, emphasizing the collision of sharp edges against gentle hues. The negative space enhances the feeling of emptiness in the midst of chaos, suggesting a struggle between connection and disconnection.
