Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between the reliability of trust and the shadow of suspicion. The color palette, with shades of pink, dark maroon, and gray, illustrates this duality with warmth meeting coolness, symbolizing emotional conflict. The main collision lies along a diagonal axis, where lighter and softer hues interact with deeper, more intense shadows. This reflects the concept of an open gate leading into a stormy territory, resembling the fracture between safety and uncertainty as highlighted in the seed. The 'cage' shape is reflected in the stark contrasts and structured yet fragmented patterns, suggesting limitations and the struggle within that frame.
