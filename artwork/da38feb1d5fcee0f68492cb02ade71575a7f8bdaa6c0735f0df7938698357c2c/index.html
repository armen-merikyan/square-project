Artist Statement

This composition conveys the emotional tension between safety and exposure, utilizing a palette of soft greens and warm browns juxtaposed with pale and cold tones. The center features denser, warmer colors, representing safety, while surrounding areas utilize cooler tones, emphasizing exposure. The main collision occurs in the center, creating an undulating flow that suggests a push and pull of pressure and release. The contrasting patterns of split screen for exposure and stacked layers for safety reveal an abstract narrative of vulnerability intersecting with stability.
