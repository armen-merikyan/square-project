Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between the yearning nature of longing and the firm grounding of resolve. The palette combines soft off-white and faint peach for warmth against a deeper dust gray and dark navy representing shadows and complexities. The main contrast emerges at the center, where the maze-like longings collide with structured target shapes, symbolizing the struggle between chaos and order. The seed of 'thread' introduces a sense of connection and division across the pulsating negative space, echoing the emotional pull of both longing and resolve.
