Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the tension between defiance and submission. The chosen palette, featuring sepia and dust gray, conveys a broken memory, adding depth and weight. The arrangement flows from top to bottom, suggesting a descent of thoughts or feelings, while the use of alternating patterns—checker for defiance and spark for submission—creates a visual conflict. The main contrast is found in the balance between darker hues at the top, evoking heavier emotions, and lighter tones cascading down, symbolizing a release or surrender. This creates a layered effect, illustrating an intricate dance of confrontation and acceptance.
