Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and distortion—the 'signal' attempts to penetrate through 'noise'. The selected palette includes vibrant green and blue as signals of clarity, juxtaposed against muted black and gray representing static and interference. The main contrast is concentrated in the middle rows, where an echo of signal disruptions is visually palpable, suggesting interruptions in communication and memory. The arrangement creates a sense of diagonal flow, invoking both tension and release as light penetrates dark, embodying the essence of the seed's themes.
