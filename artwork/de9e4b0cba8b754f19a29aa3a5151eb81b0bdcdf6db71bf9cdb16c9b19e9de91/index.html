Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dichotomy of desire and restraint, using a palette that juxtaposes vibrant and muted colors. The brighter hues, like hot coral and blood orange, evoke a sense of yearning, while the cooler tones of charcoal and hollow blue provide a structure of containment. The layout features a corner invasion, with desire radiating from the lower left corner, creating an impression of overflowing emotion that confronts the more muted lattice of restraint. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where bright colors collide with darker backgrounds, expressing an internal struggle. The broken pixels suggest imperfections in the control, enhancing the complexity of this emotional landscape.
