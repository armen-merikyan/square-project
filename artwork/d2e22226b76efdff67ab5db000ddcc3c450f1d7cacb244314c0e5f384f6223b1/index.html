Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of spark and ash through its interplay of warm and cool tones. The palette is drawn from the softness of powder pink and warm white, contrasted sharply with the severity of carbon black and sharp crimson. The layout expresses an 'open gate' where the center showcases a collision of lively 'island' patterns against the static 'lattice' formations on the periphery, echoing the dynamic argument between energy and decay. The 3 broken pixels disrupt the grid, representing memories caught between tension and restraint, amplifying the feeling of resilience and fragility.
