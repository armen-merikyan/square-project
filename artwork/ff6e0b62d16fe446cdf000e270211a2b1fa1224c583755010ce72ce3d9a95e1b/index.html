Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between love and betrayal. The palette, featuring shades of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon, evokes warmth combined with a sense of somberness. The diagonal flow mimics tears falling, creating a narrative of split feelings. The colors are intentionally arranged to create a stark contrast in the center, where warm reds collide with muted grays, suggesting conflict. The broken pixel elements introduce imperfection, representing the cracks in trust and connection without illustrating them literally.
