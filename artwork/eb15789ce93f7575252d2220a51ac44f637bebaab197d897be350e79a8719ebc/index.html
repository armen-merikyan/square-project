Artist Statement

This abstract composition embodies the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue reflects the warmth of memory contrasting with the cooler tones of advancement. The layout features a mirrored pattern to illustrate the rise and fall, with intentional disruptions to convey the rawness of longing and the push towards innovation. The bottom-weighted distribution emphasizes gravity, while light drip effects suggest ephemeral moments within a rigid grid, creating a sense of weight and release. The smooth edges enhance this gentle oscillation, whereas the broken pixels introduce imperfection, echoing real-life transitions.
