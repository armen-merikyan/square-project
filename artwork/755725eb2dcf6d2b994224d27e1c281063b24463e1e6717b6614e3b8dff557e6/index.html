Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between rage and calm, using contrasting colors to represent these opposing feelings. The selected palette of red and pale blue evokes intensity and serenity, while the presence of black and white enhances the visual dialogue. The layout mirrors both sides of the square, creating a feeling of balance disrupted by a few misplaced pixels that symbolize breaking tension. The arrangement employs striped patterns on the calm side and disruptive sparks on the rage side, encapsulating the emotional conflict as it flows from one state to another.
