Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, with a warm palette of muted purples, oranges, and deep blues to represent emotional contrasts. The central area uses brighter colors to suggest strength, while the edges employ darker, muted tones, reflecting vulnerability and darkness. The layout uses a chaotic distribution of color to demonstrate the collision and interplay of these feelings, with broken pixels creating a visual narrative of disruption and repair. This piece captures the essence of the seed’s themes through contrast and emotional weight.
