Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise). The palette utilizes deep navy for the calm signal, contrasted by muted blacks and dim cyan, representing noise and uncertainty. A structured flow from bottom to top captures a rising tension, with noticeable echoes of broken pixels creating intentional disruptions, showing the fragility of the underlying pattern. The left side (signal) displays clear channels amidst static interruptions on the right (noise).
