Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension of joy versus grief, framed within a structured chaos. The palette of bright yellow and warm orange signifies joy, contrasted with deep blue and muted purple that add a sense of grief. Main contrast surfaces through the stark juxtaposition of warm dots scattered against a cool striped background, symbolizing the conflict between lightness and heaviness. The layout's descending flow evokes the feeling of tears or rays of light, while strategic placement of broken pixels introduces imperfections, reflecting human emotions. Overall, the piece aims to evoke complex emotional dialogues through abstract means, highlighting the persistent chase between joy and sorrow.
