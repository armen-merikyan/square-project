Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a contrast of colors and patterns. The chosen palette of deep hues conveys a sense of stability (deep blue, mourning black) while the pale yellow suggests fragility and exposure. The arrangement features a diagonal flow, with the deep blue occupying the upper left, signifying strength, counterbalanced by the pale yellow in the lower right, representing vulnerability. The inclusion of broken pixels adds an element of imperfection, suggesting scars of experience, while the echo and threshold patterns collide in the center, capturing the essence of connection and alienation.
