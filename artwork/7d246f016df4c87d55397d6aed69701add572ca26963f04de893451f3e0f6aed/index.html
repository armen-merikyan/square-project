Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between 'restlessness' and 'peace', where contrasting colors represent the struggle. The palette of signal green, electric blue, and hot magenta conveys vibrancy against the static gray and dead black, symbolizing the collision of tranquility and agitation. The main conflict is felt in the center with the dense colors vying for space, while the edges fade into quieter hues, suggesting a boundary between calm and chaos. This arrangement echoes memories intertwined with motion, accentuated by broken pixels that represent disjunction and interruption.
