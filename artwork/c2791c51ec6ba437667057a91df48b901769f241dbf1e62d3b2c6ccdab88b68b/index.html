Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension of defiance against submission, utilizing a palette derived from themes of love and betrayal — vibrant reds contrasting with muted grays and dark maroon. The center shows a 'pulse' of rebellious intensity while the edges dissolve into quieter tones, representing submission's influence. The arrangement creates an imperfect collision where pressure bands of color vie for prominence, allowing an asymmetry that conveys unresolved motion and dynamic tension between control and chaos, echoing the seed's emotional breadth.
