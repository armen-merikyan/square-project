Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension of vulnerability against strength, utilizing a palette of warm oranges and cool blues to reflect this duality. The central area features a pale, soft yellow that emits an exposed quality, while the darker edges provide a protective barrier that contrasts with the openness of the center. The arrangement features thin, jagged lines that represent tension, colliding against the smoother, more tranquil areas. The layout allows for an intentional asymmetry that enhances the sense of imbalance and disruption, suggesting the complex interplay between exposure and shelter.
