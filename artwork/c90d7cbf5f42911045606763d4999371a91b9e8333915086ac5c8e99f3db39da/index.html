Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a conflict between the yearning for peace and the drive towards resolution. The chosen palette reflects a wistful blend of pale aqua and mist gray, representing longing, alongside sharper alarm red and deep ink blue symbolizing resolve's strength. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where a dense cluster of warm colors meets cooler fields, creating a visual pulse of tension. Broken pixels are intentionally scattered to hint at past damage yet point towards potential repair, making the composition resonate with dynamics of interruption and continuity.
