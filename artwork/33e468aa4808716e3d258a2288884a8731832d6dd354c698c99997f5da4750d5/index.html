Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between control and panic, captured through jagged patterns that signify disorder set against a backdrop of dense blacks. The palette's interplay of electric blue and lime against dark blacks denotes sparks of hope or clarity amid chaos. The main contrast resides at the intersection of bright colors intruding into heavy dark areas, emphasizing the battle between light and weight while incorporating 'broken' elements to represent imperfection and disruption.
