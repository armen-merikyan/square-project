Artist Statement

This artwork expresses the tension between love and betrayal through a fragmented layout that divides warm and cool tones. The left side utilizes a gradient of reds and pinks, symbolizing warmth and affection, while the right features grays and dark maroons to evoke feelings of coldness and separation. The broken pixels create a sense of imperfection, contrasting the idealized love with the harsh realities of betrayal. The central diagonal pattern suggests a rise that disrupts symmetry, embodying the conflict between stability and chaos, reflecting the emotional struggle depicted in the seed.
