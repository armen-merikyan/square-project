Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between quiet and pressure, juxtaposed through dense clusters on the left and soft fading areas on the right. The palette of off-white, dust gray, and faint peach conveys calmness, while compressed red and dark navy introduce conflict and depth. The ripple pattern clashes with the quiet field, creating a central collision where color meets texture, embodying the struggle between rigidity and softness that the seed suggests.
