Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional push-pull of longing versus resolve, showcased through a palette of ripe greens and honey gold, suggesting both growth and yearning. The central collision of a dense pattern (resolve) disrupts the quiet field (longing), embodying the concept of intimacy and distance. The structured chaos of diagonal arrangements hints at the 'ladder against fault line,' while broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, symbolizing the struggles of crossing emotional currents. The composition balances between engagement and calm, with a clear separation enhancing the emotional narrative.
