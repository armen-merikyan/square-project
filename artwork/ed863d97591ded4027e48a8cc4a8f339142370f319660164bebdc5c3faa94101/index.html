Artist Statement

This composition slices through the dichotomy of quiet and pressure. The palette, featuring muted off-white and gray alongside the compressed red and dark navy, creates a layered emotional landscape where the darker hues symbolize pressure and density, while the lighter tones introduce moments of quiet clarity. The main contrast occurs along the diagonal, employing ladder and hinge patterns to create a dynamic intersection that feels both restrained and expansive. The deliberate placement of broken pixels represents the shattering against the tide, adding an element of intentional imperfection reflecting inner turmoil and clarity.
