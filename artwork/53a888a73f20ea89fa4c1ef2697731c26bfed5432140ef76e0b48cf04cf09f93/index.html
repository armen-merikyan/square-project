Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the relationship between quietude and pressure, reflecting a balance between densely packed colors and open spaces. The palette combines muted hues with a vibrant red accent, symbolizing urgency interlaced with calm. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where clusters of compressed tones clash against softer backgrounds, creating a visual argument in the piece. This aligns with the seed's themes of clarity and confusion, representing the interplay between tension and release.
