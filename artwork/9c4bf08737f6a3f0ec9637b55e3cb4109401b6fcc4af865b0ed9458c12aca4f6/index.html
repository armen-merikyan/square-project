Artist Statement

This artwork captures the interplay between emptiness (represented by darker hues) and desire (represented by warm, glowing elements). The black and dark violet tones dominate the borders, evoking feelings of void and isolation. In contrast, the soft gold and pale green create a luminous core that symbolizes yearning and aspiration. The pattern is a blend of structured maze shapes reflecting desire and dispersed dots suggesting a feeling of emptiness. The collision occurs where the warm and cool colors interact, highlighting the tension of longing against desolation. The broken edges amplify imperfection, embodying a chaotic beauty within the emotional spectrum.
