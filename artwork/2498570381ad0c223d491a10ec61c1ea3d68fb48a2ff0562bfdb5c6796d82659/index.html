Artist Statement

This abstract composition captures the emotional conflict between defiance and submission through a striking contrast of warm and cool colors. The palette, inspired by 'intimacy_distance', combines rose clay and lamp gold to evoke warmth and resistance, while cool blue and distant lilac represent a soft collapse. The main collision occurs in the center where the warmer tones converge sharply against the cooler, creating tension. The corner invasion layout suggests a forward push of defiance, while hints of broken pixels introduce imperfection, reinforcing the chaotic interplay between stability and disruption. Overall, the arrangement balances density and quiet space, ensuring a dynamic visual rhythm.
