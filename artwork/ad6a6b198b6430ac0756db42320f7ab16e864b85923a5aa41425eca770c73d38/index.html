Artist Statement

This composition captures the duality of joy and grief through a horizontal split, using a palette of warm yellows and oranges juxtaposed against deep blues and muted purples. The top half embodies joy with vibrant, expanding colors, while the bottom, marked by flame-like pixels, invokes grief through heavier, drooping patterns. The palette reflects lightness contrasted by depth, creating emotional collision. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, reinforcing the theme of hidden vs exposed sentiments, where structured order meets chaotic bursts of color. The flow is a gentle wave that carries the viewer from warmth to a poignant pause, suggesting the timeless dance between these two emotional states.
