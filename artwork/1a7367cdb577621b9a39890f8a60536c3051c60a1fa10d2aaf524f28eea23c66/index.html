Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of strength and vulnerability. The palette combines deep olive and cream to create an interplay of dark and light, suggesting both stability and fragility. The main contrast lies at the center where the edges are tightly framed, creating a visual tension. Patterns of strength frame a softer, more chaotic inner section, echoing the seed's concept of emotional conflict. The layout emphasizes an argument grid, illustrating the battle between control and chaos, with broken pixels serving as intentional disruptions.
