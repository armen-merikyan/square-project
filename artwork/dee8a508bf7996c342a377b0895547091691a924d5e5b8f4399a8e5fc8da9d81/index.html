Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between loneliness and connection, represented through a palette of warm burnt orange, dark red, and gold against muted charcoal to evoke warmth amid isolation. The stark contrast between darker edges and a lighter center creates a visual collision reflective of connection's fragile nature amid loneliness. The pattern flows downward, akin to falling pixels like distant memories, while broken pixels introduce subtleties suggesting imperfections in human connection, resonating with the seed's narrative of full vs hollow.
