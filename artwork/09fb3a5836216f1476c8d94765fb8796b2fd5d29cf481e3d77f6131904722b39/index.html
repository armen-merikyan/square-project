Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional duality of clarity and confusion. The palette blends warm hues of orange and yellow with deep blue and muted purple, symbolizing the warm vibrancy of clarity contrasted by the darker tones that evoke confusion. The use of symmetrical and broken patterns creates an argument on the canvas, with areas of dense color interrupted by lighter spaces, emphasizing the notion of confusion fighting for clarity. The broken pixels add a layer of imperfection, reflecting the disruption caused by chaos. The structured grid layout features a bottom-weighting that visually anchors the piece while allowing echoes of lighter areas to shift the viewer's focus, inviting contemplation of the balance between clarity and chaos.
