Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the juxtaposition of vulnerability and strength through a palette that oscillates between cosmic indigo and void black as backdrops, contrasted by star white highlights. The main collision occurs at the center, where broken dots suggest vulnerabilities amidst halo-like structures symbolizing strength. The spiral pressure layout contributes to a fluid, dynamic composition, conveying a sense of movement and emotional complexity.
