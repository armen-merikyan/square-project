Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension of longing and resolve. The use of dark maroon and red reflects the intensity of resolve while the softer pink introduces a sense of longing. The heavy contrast in the edge styles creates a feeling of boundaries, with the red mirroring the compressed strength at the borders and the pink ladder pattern, symbolizing longing, flowing within the inner zones. The intentional broken pixels introduce disruptions that signify repairs needed amid damage, enhancing the emotional complexity. The separation between the core and frame allows for a reflection of the duality in feelings.
