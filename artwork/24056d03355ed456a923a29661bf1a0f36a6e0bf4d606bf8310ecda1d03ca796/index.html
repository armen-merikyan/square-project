Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a clash between rage and calm, utilizing a palette of intense reds against serene pale blues and whites. The placement of vibrant reds, particularly positioned towards the center, collides sharply with the softer hues surrounding it, representing an emotional eruption amid a calm backdrop. This tension embodies the seed concepts through contrasting patterns of structure and chaos, with broken pixels strategically disrupting flow, suggesting a battle between containment and overflow.
