Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents a duality between joy and grief, reflected through a palette of yellow, orange, muted purple, and deep blue. The contrasting use of warm colors (joy) with cool, muted tones (grief) illustrates the collisions of these emotions. The pattern flows horizontally, evoking a wave-like rhythm, interrupted by jagged edges that suggest a breakdown or an imperfect balance. The arrangement creates a visual narrative where one emotional force collides with another, allowing viewers to feel both warmth and melancholy in the same frame, highlighting the beauty of imperfection amidst symmetry.
