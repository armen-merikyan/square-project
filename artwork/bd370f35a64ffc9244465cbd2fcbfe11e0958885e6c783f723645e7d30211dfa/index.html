Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes restlessness with peace, creating a visual narrative of tension and release. The chosen palette of sepia tones and faded blue reflects a melancholic memory interspersed with moments of calm, embodying these contrasting feelings. The collision occurs predominantly at the center, where dense dark patches (representing restlessness) encounter lighter hues that suggest tranquility. This interplay suggests a journey from tension to release, reflecting the conceptual seeds of motion and memory.
