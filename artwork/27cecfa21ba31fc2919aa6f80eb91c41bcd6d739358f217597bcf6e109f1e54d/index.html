Artist Statement

This artwork captures the dichotomy of quiet and pressure, utilizing a color palette that oscillates between cool and warm tones to explore emotional collisions. Darker colors (#3C2A65, #1D1F38) represent pressure and depth, with bright accents (#FBAF39, #FF6E40) suggesting moments of release and contrast. The arrangement induces a rhythmic flow, where quiet spaces (the blues) are interrupted by bursts of warmth, embodying the tension and release present in the emotional landscape. This composition mirrors the journey through conflicting feelings, ebbing towards a hopeful release.
