Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between control and surrender through a palette evocative of faded memories. The muted tones of sepia and dust gray represent containment, while the faded blue provides a soft surrendering contrast. The primary focus is on the collision in the center, where stripes of controlled structure meet a jagged pattern representing release. This unfinished convergence encapsulates the struggle between pressure and relaxation, creating a dynamic visual narrative without invoking literal imagery.
