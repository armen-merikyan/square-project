Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between rage and calm through a dynamic color palette of red and pale blue, symbolizing explosive energy versus serene containment. The central collision of jagged red pixels aligns with the calm, horizontal flow of blue, interrupted by scattered white accents resembling sparks. The edge styles frame the conflict while maintaining a soft, quiet background, embodying the interaction of noise and silence inherent in the seed's themes. The edge and broken pixel placements add intentional imperfections, enhancing the emotional depth of the artwork.
