Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between stability and exploration. The palette blends warm coral and soft amber for certainty with cold slate and blue-black representing curiosity. This creates a contrast where the bright coral clusters (55% coverage) signify moments of clarity against the darker, chaotic background representing unanswered questions. The main collision occurs in the center where the bright coral disrupts the slate and black, invoking tension and release, influenced by the idea of a rising signal amidst a backdrop of uncertainty.
