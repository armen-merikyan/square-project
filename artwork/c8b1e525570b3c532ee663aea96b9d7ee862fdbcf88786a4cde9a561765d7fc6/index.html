Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between longing and resolve, utilizing a palette that reflects calm pastels contrasted with deeper hues to convey depth and introspection. The strong gradients of storm gray represent longing through ripples, while the flooded structure of faded gold signifies resolve within a broken mirror layout. The intentional disruption of broken pixels emphasizes the fragility of connection amidst the pulsating flow, creating visual collisions that resonate with feelings of serenity and urgency.
