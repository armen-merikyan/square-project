Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission through an interplay of angular and stacked patterns, embodying both rigid and soft elements. The palette of moss green and bone white serves to convey resilience amidst fragility, while the rust and stone gray reflect underlying struggles with their earthiness. The piece predominantly features a central collision of strong, dark tones contrasted against softer lighter hues, suggesting a struggle for dominance. Broken pixels add intentional disruptions, representing moments of imperfection in this emotional narrative.
