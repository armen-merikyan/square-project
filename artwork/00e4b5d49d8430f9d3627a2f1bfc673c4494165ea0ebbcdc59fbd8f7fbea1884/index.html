Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between trust and suspicion, with a color palette that oscillates between warmth and coolness. The contrasting patterns reflect the clash between structured paths of trust (represented by maze-like patterns) and fragmented forms of suspicion (represented by veil-like interruptions). The main collision occurs in the center where the two patterns intersect, symbolizing the center of decision-making amidst uncertainty. This abstract pattern evokes a dialogue between clarity and confusion, capturing the essence of the seed.
