Artist Statement

The artwork captures a tense dialogue between vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette of muted cream, red oxide, and blackened plum evokes a sense of desire mixed with restraint, while the interplay of colors suggests both protection and invasion. The concentrated density of color in the upper left corner represents strength, which unravels into softer hues in the surrounding area, embodying a feeling of yielding or vulnerability. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions to highlight imperfection, contrasting sharply with the foundational strength of the dominant colors. The collision occurs primarily in the corner, where dense colors meet lighter shades, creating a visual tension that resonates with the seed's themes of duality and hidden forces.
