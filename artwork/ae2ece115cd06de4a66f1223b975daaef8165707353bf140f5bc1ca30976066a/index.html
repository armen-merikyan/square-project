Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the contrast between 'spark' and 'ash,' illustrating a delicate balance between vibrant energy and subdued remnants. The palette—comprised of warm shadows and cool blues—creates a resonance between warmth and distance, enhancing the emotional dichotomy. The dominant half is a vibrant gradient suggesting sudden brightness, while the muted colors represent settled dust, with intentional broken pixels suggesting imperfections. This collides mainly towards the center, where colors oscillate, embodying tension and release in the overall pattern.
