Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional conflict between longing and resolve through a palette of rich greens and warm honey tones. The deep olive creates a firm base, symbolizing resolve, while the vibrant acid green interjects like a whisper of longing. The grid and checker patterns collide at the center and edges, creating a push and pull dynamic. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, representing moments of intimacy and distance, emphasizing an abstract narrative of intertwining emotions amidst the tension between chaos and order.
