Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between clarity and chaos, represented by the contrast of bright moments against a darkened background. The palette of navy, dim cyan, and black evokes distance and isolation while offering a vibrant point of light. The main collision occurs in the center where a halo of bright colors disrupts the overwhelming noise of darker shades, emphasizing the tension of trying to find clarity amidst static. The broken pixels act as scars of noise, adding to the narrative of memory and erasure through irregular interruptions of color.
