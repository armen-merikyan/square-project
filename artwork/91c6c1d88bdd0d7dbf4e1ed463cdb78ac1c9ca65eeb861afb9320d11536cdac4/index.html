Artist Statement

This composition balances elements of longing and resolve, using a palette that oscillates between warm and cool tones. The emotional tone is conveyed through the stark contrast of deep olive and ripe green, creating a field of tension and serenity. Broken pixels disrupt an otherwise steady flow, representing the 'repair vs damage' concept, while the structured bands reflect the 'contained vs overflowing' sentiment. The main collision occurs in the center, where honey gold emerges against the darker colors, embodying the tension of pulse amidst stillness.
