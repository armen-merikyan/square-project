Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting textures and patterns. The left side features a stable 'pressure band' of soft golds and pale greens representing trust, while the right side displays chaotic 'stairs' in dark violet and black symbolizing suspicion. The diagonal fall in color distribution indicates a sense of gradual conflict and resolution. The palette of fear and hope accentuates this emotional dichotomy, leading to a central collision of colors that speaks to the fragility and resilience embedded within relationships.
