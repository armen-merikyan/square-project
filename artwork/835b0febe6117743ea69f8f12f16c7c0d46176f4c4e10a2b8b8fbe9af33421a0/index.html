Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the tension between trust and suspicion, creating an atmosphere of anticipation. The chosen palette represents a contrast between the stability of deep navy and the frailty of dim cyan, tapping into themes of uncertainty and reliance. The collision happens along the vertical line where dark shadows from suspicion meet bright clusters, illustrating a dichotomy that echoes within the layout 'last_light' — where most pixels provide softness, yet a muted center draws focused attention. The use of broken pixels gives a sense of imperfection and reinforces the feelings of tension and complexity.
