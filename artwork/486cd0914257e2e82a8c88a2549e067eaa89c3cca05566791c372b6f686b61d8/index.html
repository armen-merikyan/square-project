Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional juxtaposition of safety and exposure through a contrast of warm and cool tones. The palette, composed of 'warm brown' and 'shelter green' against the 'open blue' and 'cold white,' serves to highlight the struggle between feeling enclosed and facing the open space. The main collision occurs at the center, where dense, warm colors conflict with colder hues, echoing the themes of shelter and vulnerability. The patterned 'embers' represent safety's warmth while the 'grid' symbolizes exposure, intertwining to create a dynamic visual experience.
