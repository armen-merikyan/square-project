Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the tension between love and betrayal, with warm pinks and reds contrasting against the cold gray and dark maroon. The palette represents warmth and affection traditionally associated with love, juxtaposed with the cooler tones illustrating betrayal. The layout evokes a mirrored structure, symbolizing the longing for balance disrupted by unexpected breaks, illustrated through misplaced pixels creating a fractured boundary. The pattern flows from top to bottom with radial forms representing love transitioning into chaotic rings representing betrayal, creating an undefined space that prompts reflection.
