Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the push and pull of vulnerability and strength, reflected in the contrast of bold and muted colors. The palette, combining dark violet and soft gold, represents the tension between fear and hope. The main collision occurs in the center where warm tones of gold collide with deep violet, symbolizing conflict and resilience. The falling pixel layout evokes a sense of descent and a gradual build-up of emotional pressure, with broken pixels adding imperfection and dynamic layers to the overall composition.
