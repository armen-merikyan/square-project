Artist Statement

This composition navigates the emotional landscape between 'longing' and 'resolve.' Using a palette inspired by the 'broken_memory' theme, muted sepia and dust gray represent the reflective nature of longing, while bright highlights of faded blue capture the steady resolve. The core area includes broken dots, symbolizing repair and connection amidst imperfections, while the outer frame evokes a quiet backdrop, allowing longing to linger. Interruption between the two patterns creates a tension that embodies a push and pull of emotions. The main collision occurs in the center, drawing the eye into the complex interplay of feelings.
