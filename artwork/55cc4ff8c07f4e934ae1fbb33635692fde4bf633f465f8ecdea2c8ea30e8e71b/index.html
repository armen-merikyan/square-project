Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the dynamic tension between rage and calm, using a color palette of deep blacks, sharp reds, and soft pale blues. The contrasting colors serve as visual metaphors for emotional pressure (black and red) clashing against quiet relief (pale blue and white). The composition showcases a zigzag flow, where bursts of red interrupt the quiet blue fields, creating marked collisions that symbolize the volatility of emotion. The use of broken pixels implies a sense of imperfection and disruption, enhancing the feeling of unresolved tension while allowing moments of calm to emerge in the spaces around explosive color placements. Overall, this pattern encapsulates the push and pull of intense emotional experiences, favoring negative space that invites reflection.
