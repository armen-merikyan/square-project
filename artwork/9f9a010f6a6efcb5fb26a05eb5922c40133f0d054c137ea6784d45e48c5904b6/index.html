Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise), using a palette of vibrant and muted tones to express this duality. The contrast primarily occurs in the bottom left corner, where electric colors clash against more subdued grays, illustrating the struggle of signal amidst overwhelming noise. The pattern is shaped by the seed which emphasizes conflict and resistance in a world saturated with static, reflected in the uneven distribution of colors that evoke memories and fragmented connections.
