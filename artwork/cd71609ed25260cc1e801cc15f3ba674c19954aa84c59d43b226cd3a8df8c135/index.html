Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of restlessness and peace through the interplay of dense clusters of blacks and dark violets contrasting with soft golds and pale greens. The left side exhibits sharper patterns indicative of restlessness, while the right side smoothly transitions into lighter tones, symbolizing peace. The diagonal arrangement creates a tension that reflects the internal arguments of conflicting emotions, while broken pixels disrupt the flow, hinting at vulnerability within the structure. The balance of negative space punctuates the overall tension, guiding the eye toward the center where the emotional collision occurs.
