Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional conflict of longing and resolve through a palette that balances softness and severity. The combination of warm whites and sharp crimson conveys both urgency and calm, while carbon black and steel gray provide a contrasting backdrop that suggests a magnetic tension. The main collision happens in the center, where the clusters of sharp crimson break through the muted tones, representing bursts of resolve against a sea of longing. The patterns of channels and constellations suggest a flow of emotion, echoing the interplay of memory and urgency.
