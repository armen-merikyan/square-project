Artist Statement

This artwork embodies a tumultuous clash of rage and calm through an intentional layout, where the left side is saturated with vibrant reds, symbolizing intensity, while the right features soft pale blues, representing tranquility. The combination creates an emotional tension reflected in the 'ring' pattern merging with 'broken dots'. The collision occurs predominantly at the vertical central line, illustrating the struggle between the overflowing energy of the left side and the containment of the right, forming an echo of contrast across the canvas.
