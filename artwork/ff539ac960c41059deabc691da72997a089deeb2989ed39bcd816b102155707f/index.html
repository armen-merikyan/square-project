Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between vulnerability and strength, manifested through contrasting color zones. The palette chosen reflects a spectrum of quietness (off-white and faint peach) against bolder shades (compressed red and dark navy), creating a sensation of pressure and release. The collision occurs predominantly in the center, where the brighter colors interact with the darker edges, suggesting a battle between exposed fragility and surrounding stability. The interplay of the ladder pattern (representing vulnerability) and mirror pattern (representing strength) encapsulates the dynamic tension requested in the seed, culminating in a complex yet harmonious visual narrative.
