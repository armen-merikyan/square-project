Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, portrayed through a stark contrast of colors. The choice of a palette with deep olive and cream embodies a grounded yet delicate duality. The center (representing strength) showcases strong, rich hues like deep olive, while the edges (reflecting vulnerability) are softer with creams that suggest fragility. The main collision occurs in the center, where the rich and light colors meet, symbolizing a break or imbalance, reminiscent of a broken mirror that distorts the reality of strength. This arrangement reflects the ongoing struggle between safety and exposure, evoking a feeling of tension and unease.
