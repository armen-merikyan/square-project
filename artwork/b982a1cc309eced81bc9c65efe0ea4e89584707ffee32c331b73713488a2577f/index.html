Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tensions of tenderness and rage through color interactions. The palette of deep reds against muted blues embodies the struggle between chaos and calm. A central interruption of bright red pixels crashes into a field of soft blue tones, representing moments of intense feeling amidst a backdrop of quiet. The choice of broken pixels hints at imperfections and scars, layering the story of human emotions over the structured descent of color.
