Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of 'longing' and 'resolve' through a contrasting palette of deep teal and cosmic indigo against void black, suggesting depth and introspection. The arrangement respects the layout of two pulse centers, creating a dynamic tension between the two emotional states. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, symbolizing the damage and repairs inherent in emotional journeys. The pulse marks clash with the crosshatch pattern, creating a visual echo of their contrasting natures, reflecting the simultaneous feelings of serenity and urgency.
