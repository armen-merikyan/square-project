Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances the heat of rage with the tranquility of calm. Bright red and white signify explosive moments, creating a stark contrast against the cooler, muted shades of pale blue and black that signify quietude and containment. The palette represents the tension between chaos and serenity, with red pulse marks disrupting horizontal calm fields in a zigzag pattern, embodying the emotional collision. The central focus on red marks against the dimmer backdrop illustrates an eruption, accentuating how light can highlight darkness. This intersection encapsulates the idea of contained overflow, symbolizing silent battles beneath a seemingly calm surface.
