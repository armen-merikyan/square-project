Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between quiet and pressure, illustrated through a color palette that shifts from softer tones to sharper contrasts. The use of muted colors such as off-white and faint peach creates a calm foundation, while compressed red and dark navy introduce a sense of pressure and weight. The collision between the distinct ripple pattern of pressure and the scattered broken dots of quiet reflects the ebb and flow inherent in the emotional theme. The composition is marked by a dense center that disrupts the tranquil edges, symbolizing the struggle to contain overwhelming feelings within a manageable space. This arrangement narrates a story of seeking clarity amidst confusion, where the jagged edges create intentional broken pixels, introducing imperfection and reinforcing the abstract theme.
