Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a dynamic balance between pressure (represented by dark colors) and quiet (represented by lighter tones). The palette, inspired by sepia and faded elements, creates an atmosphere of nostalgia and complexity. The contrast occurs at the edges where dark lines intersect the muted core, symbolizing the collision of defiance against submission. The pattern echoes a maze interspersed with stair-like gradients, representing a journey through emotional noise to find clarity amidst chaos.
