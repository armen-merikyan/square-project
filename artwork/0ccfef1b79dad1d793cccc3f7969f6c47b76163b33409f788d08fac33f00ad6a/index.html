Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between defiance and submission, captured through vibrant lattice patterns that collide with softer halo elements to evoke conflict. The chosen palette of moss green and stone gray symbolizes resilience amid fragility, while the rust and bone white add dramatic contrast to represent the pulses of defiance. The dense center is emphasized against a quieter border, illustrating a tension as if a strong pulse emerges from an otherwise tranquil space, embodying both chaos and order.
