Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tensions of defiance and submission. The palette features melancholy hues of rain blue and muted plum juxtaposed with soft cyan and lavender gray, creating a sense of layered complexity. The main collision occurs in the center where sharp angular resistances (fault line patterns) intersect with softer, receding textures (teeth patterns). This embodies the struggle between inward spirals of submission and outward projections of defiance, offering a visual narrative filled with both tension and release.
