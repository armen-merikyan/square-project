Artist Statement

This composition expresses the emotional dichotomy of confusion versus clarity through a zigzag flow. The outer frame in muted tans and dust grays suggests a sense of calm amid chaos, while the central noise pattern in faded blue introduces tension with uneven placements creating visual 'broken pixels' that represent the emotional chaos. The palette reflects a memory of broken clarity, evoking nostalgia and dissonance. The collision occurs at the transition from the calm outer frame to the chaotic inner core, illustrating a juxtaposition of stability versus disruption.
