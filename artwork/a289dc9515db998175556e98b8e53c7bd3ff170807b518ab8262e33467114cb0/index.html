Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between defiance and submission, expressed through a contrasting palette. The cool tones of pale aqua and mist gray suggest serenity, while alarm red disrupts the calm, asserting defiance. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense patterns of contradiction interact with negative space, creating a quiet channel that draws the eye. This layout captures the struggle between rigid patterns versus chaotic bursts, mirroring the seed's themes of order versus chaos and the threshold between opposing forces. Broken pixels are used as intentional disruptions that enhance the sense of disarray, creating a visual narrative of emotional tension.
