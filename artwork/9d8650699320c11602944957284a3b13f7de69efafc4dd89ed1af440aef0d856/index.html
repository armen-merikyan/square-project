Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and chaos, where clear signals strive to emerge from a static noise. The palette combines deep navy and black to evoke solitude and interference, while a single white color provides a bright accent, representing the hopeful signal amidst the noise. The main collision occurs along the center, creating a dynamic split between orderly grid patterns and chaotic lattice structures, highlighting the emotional conflict of signal vs. noise.
