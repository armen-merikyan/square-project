Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on a dichotomy of grief (represented by deep blues and muted purples) and joy (expressed through yellows and oranges). The palette evokes a balance between somber weight and vibrant spark, capturing the essence of the described emotional collisions. The layout suggests upward movement of joy that interacts with downward flows of grief, creating an impression of pressure and release. The use of a crosshatch pattern for joy intertwines with a grid for grief, resulting in dynamic tension. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, suggesting the bittersweet nature of these experiences. The main contrast occurs through the juxtaposition of warm and cool tones, creating an abstract narrative of emotional complexity.
