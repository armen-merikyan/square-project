Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a clash between longing and resolve. The palette combines deep teal and cosmic indigo for a foundational calm, contrasted by bright, cold gold accents which symbolize urgency and pulse. The outer border of dark colors embodies longing, while the lighter, clearer colors in the center represent resolve, creating a dynamic interaction as they collide. Broken pixels suggest scars and imperfections, reflecting the repair vs. damage theme incorporated in the seed, forming a narrative of tension and contrast.
