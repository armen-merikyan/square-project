Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the tension between loneliness and connection. The color palette of muted purples, oranges, and deep blues evokes both warmth and distance, suggesting an interplay of joy and grief. The main contrast is established between the vibrant corner colors and the subdued center, creating a 'frame and core' layout that highlights isolation against a backdrop of potential connection. The arrangement showcases asymmetries where bright colors disrupt the grid-like loneliness at the edges, simulating memories—some vivid, some faded.
