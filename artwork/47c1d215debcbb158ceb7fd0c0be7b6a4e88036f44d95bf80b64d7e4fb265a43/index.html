Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of softness and severity, with warm whites representing gentleness contrasted against depth in carbon black. The palette of powder pink and warm white suggests a feeling of comfort, while sharp crimson introduces a striking interruption. The layout combines dense areas of sharp contrast with soft transitional fields, creating an engaging push-pull dynamic within the square. Key collisions occur at the center, where warm and cool colors overlap, reflecting the complex interplay between guilt and forgiveness. The use of broken pixels adds a layer of imperfection, symbolizing scars from past emotional battles.
