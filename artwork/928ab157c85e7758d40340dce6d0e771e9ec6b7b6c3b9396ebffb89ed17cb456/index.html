Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the conflict between desire and restraint, utilizing contrasting colors to symbolize tension. The palette blends warm saturated hues (hot coral, blood orange) representing desire with muted shades (charcoal, hollow blue) embodying containment. The main collision occurs in the center, where saturated stripes weave through a subdued hinge pattern, creating a dynamic tension that reflects the struggle against sealed edges. This layout forms a compact island of vibrant heat set against a sea of quiet.
