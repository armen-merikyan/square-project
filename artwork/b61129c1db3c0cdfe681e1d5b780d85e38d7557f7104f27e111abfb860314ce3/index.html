Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between awe and terror using a palette of dark and light colors. The contrasting black, white, and pale blue represent the emotional extremes, with black illustrating terror's weight and white reflecting awe's brightness. The soft edges of the palette create a tension that merges and separates experiences, while the central placement of lighter colors amidst darker fields represents a confrontation between stability and instability, echoing the dualities in the seed text. The arrangement suggests a wave-like rhythm, reinforcing the ongoing conflict of emotions.
