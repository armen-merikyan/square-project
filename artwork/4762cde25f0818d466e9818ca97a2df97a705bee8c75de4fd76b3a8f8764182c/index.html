Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between awe and terror through contrasting colors and patterns. The top half uses a serene palette of soft pink and gray, invoking a sense of calm and wonder, while the bottom half sharply contrasts with deep reds and dark maroons representing chaos and fear. The patterns mimic a vertical wave, suggesting movement and disturbance, while the broken pixels introduce imperfection, reinforcing the theme of fragmented memories. The sharp contrasts create collisions of feeling that evoke both stability and instability, embodying the core of the seed's emotional narrative.
