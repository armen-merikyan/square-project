Artist Statement

The emotional tone suggests a tension between love and betrayal, highlighted through a palette of warm pinks and reds clashing with muted grays and dark maroon, symbolizing conflict and contrast. The arrangement features a grid-like pattern on one side representing love's warmth, while the disrupted pixelation on the other conveys feeling of betrayal. This juxtaposition creates a meaningful dissonance, echoing the theme of an empty room where emotions collide, the sharpness of a broken edge against soft gradients pushing the narrative of symmetry vs. break.
