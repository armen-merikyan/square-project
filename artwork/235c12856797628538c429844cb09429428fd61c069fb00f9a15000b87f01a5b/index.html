Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional duality of loneliness and connection through a deliberate interplay of color and pattern. The chosen palette of yellows and deep blues represents warmth and distance respectively, allowing for a conversation between the opposing emotions. The contrast of densely packed colors versus negative space mirrors the feelings of isolation amid crowds, while the top-weighted layout suggests the weight of hidden burdens. This tension culminates in a burst of muted pulses, symbolizing both moments of connection and the pangs of loneliness.
