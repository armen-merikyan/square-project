Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts longing's distant warmth with resolve's steady rise. A palette of safety and exposure captures these feelings, with shelter green and warm brown conveying a grounded comfort against the colder hues of open blue and cold white. The main collision occurs in the central row where broken pixels and dense marks represent the struggle between reaching and being held back, creating a sense of urgency within calm. The interplay of lattice and shatter patterns further emphasizes the duality, evoking an abstract story of tension and resolution.
