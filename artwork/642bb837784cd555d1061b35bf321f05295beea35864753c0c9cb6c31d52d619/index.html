Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the dichotomy of restlessness and peace, with a palette that reflects serenity (pale aqua, mist gray) and urgency (alarm red, ink blue). The main contrast occurs in the center, where a pale field (pale aqua) attempts to resist the encroaching pressures of darker accents and restless patterns (alarm red and ink blue). The chosen flow and patterns create a visual conflict, with ripples of tranquility interrupted by the chaotic veil of restlessness, suggesting tension and unresolved emotion.
