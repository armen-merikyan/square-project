Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between calm and pressure, depicted through a sharp vs. soft contrast of colors. The chosen palette combines electric blue, lime, black, and magenta, evoking both vibrancy and heaviness. The layout mirrors itself on left and right, yet with slight disruptions (broken pixels) to illustrate imperfect balance—capturing the essence of chaos within control. The primarily dark surface is intermittently interrupted by striking colors, suggesting an unresolved rhythm that shifts between calm and a gradual build-up of emotional pressure.
