Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between nostalgia and progress through contrasting colors and patterns. The left half is populated with muted sepia and gray tones reflecting nostalgia's subdued memories, while the right side bursts with brighter colors, suggesting progress and future potential. The maze-like pattern on the left evokes the complexity of past emotions, while the ring pattern on the right illustrates the cyclical nature of growth. The overall design creates an asymmetric balance where the collision of dense dark colors meets the lighter, more vibrant colors, embodying the push and pull between yesterday and tomorrow.
