Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between pressure and quiet, depicted through a palette that transitions from muted to striking colors. The color choices emphasize the quiet field of faint peach and dust gray, which occupy the inner core, while the outer edges are defined by deeper hues of dark navy and compressed red to symbolize the intense pressure. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where the jagged edges meet the soft constellation pattern, embodying the clash between these two emotions and allowing a sense of release amidst the chaos.
