Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the chaos of rage with the serenity of calm. The chosen palette—intense reds and blacks contrasts with pale blues and whites—captures the frenetic energy of rage attempting to overflow the calm nature of the background. The main collision occurs at the center, where the channels of red clash with serene horizontal rain patterns of pale blue. This abstract pattern reflects the constant tension between control and chaos, echoing the seed's qualities with an evocative rhythm of interruption and quiet space.
