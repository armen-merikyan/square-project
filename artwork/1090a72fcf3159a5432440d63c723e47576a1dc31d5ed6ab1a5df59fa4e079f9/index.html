Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between confusion and clarity, expressed through a palette of warm (yellow, orange) and cool colors (deep blue, muted purple). The arrangement creates a collision of dense clusters and quiet spaces, as the 'spark' pattern for chaos interacts with 'rain' for clarity, manifesting a symbolic fight. The broken pixels introduce subtle imperfections, suggesting the unpredictable nature of clarity amid confusion.
