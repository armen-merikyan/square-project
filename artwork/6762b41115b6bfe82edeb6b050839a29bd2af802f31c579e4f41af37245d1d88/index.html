Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between control and surrender. The selected palette evokes guilt and forgiveness with deep wine, ash gray, and warm ivory juxtaposed against stain brown and soft gold, creating a rich interplay of muted and vibrant tones. The main contrast occurs in the center with saturated colors that collide, surrounded by a halo of quieter hues, effectively embodying the theme of a saturated pull against a locked border. The pattern combines islands of control with dotted softness of surrender, emphasizing a chaotic yet structured approach to the tension at play.
