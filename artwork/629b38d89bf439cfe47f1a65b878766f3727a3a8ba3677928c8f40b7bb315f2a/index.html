Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece explores the tension between vulnerability and protection, using a palette that juxtaposes warm, earthy tones with pale, exposed hues. The contrast pits the gentle weight of safety against the bright vulnerability of exposure, with a main collision occurring in the center where warm brown encircles the pale skin tone. The layout suggests a near-enclosure, though broken pixels disrupt the continuity, evoking a feeling of tension and release in the context of safety's soft embrace battling exposure's starkness.
