Artist Statement

The composition reflects an emotional tension between 'spark' and 'ash,' where 'spark' is represented by warm, vibrant tones creating sudden bursts, while 'ash' is depicted in muted, cooler shades that evoke a sense of quiet residue. The palette of 'melancholy wonder' balances the dynamic brightness against soft, introspective backgrounds. The main collision occurs in the center, where the lattice pattern of warmth disrupts the smoother rain pattern of softness, reflecting the push and pull of memory against erasure. The subtle broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, symbolizing the fragility of resilience in this abstract interplay. This layout embodies an eroded symmetry, hinting at forgotten memories that emerge sharply against their faded counterparts.
