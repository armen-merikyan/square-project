Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between pressure and quietness. The chosen palette of muted tones with hints of warmth suggests an emotional landscape — with dark navy and compressed red representing the weight of pressure while faint peach and off-white convey quietness and clarity. The main contrast occurs where the crosshatch pattern overlaps with the negative space, mainly concentrated towards the upper left, creating a visual echo of tight clustering breaking into softer fields. This reflects the seed's themes of conflict and release, visually depicting a shattering against the quiet backdrop.
