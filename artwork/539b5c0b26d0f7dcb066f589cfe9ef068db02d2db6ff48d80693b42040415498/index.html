Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between coherence (signal) and disruption (noise). The palette features a stark contrast of cool (electric_blue) and warm (hot_magenta) tones, creating a dialogue of persistence amidst chaos. The collision of fault lines and islands represents the push and pull of organized rhythm fighting against static. The distribution favors a landscape of static-gray and dead-black with sharp interruptions of magenta pulses, reflecting the initial seed’s juxtaposition of hope and disarray. The broken pixels disrupt this structure, adding a layer of complexity that enriches the chaotic narrative.
