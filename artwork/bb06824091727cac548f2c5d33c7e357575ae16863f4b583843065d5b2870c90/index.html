Artist Statement

This artwork embodies a juxtaposition of loneliness and connection, reflecting emotional collisions through contrasts. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes a struggle between despair and hope. The dark tones fill the outer rows, representing pressure and quiet isolation, while the lighter, warmer colors imply connection and rhythm at the center. Negatively spaced areas allow for emotional breathing, encouraging the viewer to sense an unresolved tension that ebbs and flows. The grid pattern in the edges collides with the wave-like motion at the center, suggesting an emotional push and pull, resonating with the seed's themes.
