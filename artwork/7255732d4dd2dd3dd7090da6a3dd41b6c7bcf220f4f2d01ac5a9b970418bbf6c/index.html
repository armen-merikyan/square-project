Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances restlessness and peace, creating an interplay of clashing and harmonious elements. The palette features warm colors that invoke a sense of desire (hot coral and blood orange) clashing against cool tones symbolizing tranquility (charcoal and hollow blue). The composition utilizes a grid for peace, with sporadic stripes introducing an element of chaos, focusing on the central diagonal collision that illustrates tension. Broken pixels disrupt the visual flow, representing the shattering of calm into motion, resulting in a dynamic abstract narrative.
