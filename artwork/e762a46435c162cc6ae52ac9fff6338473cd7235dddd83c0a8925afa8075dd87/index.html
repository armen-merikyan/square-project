Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of defiance and submission through a vivid contrast of color and placement. The chosen palette of ripe green, acid green, honey gold, deep olive, and cream suggests both vitality and a sense of overwhelming envy, creating an effect of tension swirling around a serene core. Contrasts between the braiding 'defiance' pattern and the starry 'submission' pattern generate dynamic collisions in the center, where the colors evoke a struggle. Negative space emphasizes the core while maintaining an atmosphere of unresolved complexity. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, reinforcing the theme of conflict within structural beauty.
