Artist Statement

This composition represents the emotional conflict between rage and calm, illustrating a turbulent core surrounded by a cooling maze. The palette of deep black, fiery reds, soft pale blues, and ethereal whites reflects the intensity of emotions at play. The main collision occurs in the center, where bright reds break through a soft maze of cold colors, suggesting an interruption of calmness. This layout embodies an 'open gate' through which hot energy surges, depicting 'contained vs overflowing' through abstract layering and fragmented structure.
