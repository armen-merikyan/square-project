Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the deep void of emptiness with the vibrant flicker of desire. The palette consists of warm tones (red, pink) battling against the cool shades (dark maroon, gray), creating a visual tension. The layout features an outer frame of muted colors denoting emptiness, while the inner core bursts with desire—manifested through jagged, crack-like patterns. The density of color towards the center captures the pulse of longing, while the negative space suggests a lingering absence, reinforcing the overall theme of struggle between yearning and void.
