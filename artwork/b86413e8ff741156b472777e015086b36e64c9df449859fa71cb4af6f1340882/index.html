Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between softness and severity, embodying a struggle between gentle warmth and rigid boundaries. The palette features powder pink and warm white as soft bases, contrasted with carbon black and sharp crimson to introduce severity and tension. The arrangement emphasizes a central collision of colors, where the darker shades intrude upon the lighter, mimicking guilt against layers of forgiveness. The radial and channel patterns create a sense of flow and interruption, suggesting a movement from chaos to calm, embodying the emotional complexities highlighted in the seed.
