Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between quietness and pressure, reflecting a central collision where density confronts tranquility. The darker tones at the left side signify the pressure, presenting a compressed feel, while the muted grays and soft peach colors on the right evoke a sense of quiet release. The palette merges off-white and faint peach to suggest calmness amidst darker elements. The diagonal arrangement creates a dynamic tension, disrupting symmetry and introducing a flow from left to right, illustrating a narrative of struggle towards clarity, while broken pixels form intentional disruptions symbolizing the shattering of convention against the tide of emotion.
