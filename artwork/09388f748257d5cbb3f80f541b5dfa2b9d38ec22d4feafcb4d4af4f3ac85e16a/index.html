Artist Statement

The emotional tone channels the conflict of panic against control, embodying an unsettling rhythm that juxtaposes chaotic noise with structured calm. The selected palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon symbolizes the warmth of passion mingling with the dark undertones of anxiety. The main collision occurs where brighter hues interrupt the darker fields, suggesting sparks of clarity amidst the chaos. The zigzag layout serves to reflect the stepwise ascent from control to vulnerability, with broken pixels adding imperfection to the tension, hinting at a narrative of joy and grief intertwined.
