Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the duality of vulnerability and strength, reflected through a palette that juxtaposes dark and soft hues. The choice of black and dark violet conveys strength, while the soft gold and pale green provide the essence of vulnerability. The compelling contrast occurs at the lower-left corner, a dense assembly contrasting against an empty top quadrant, embodying the theme of pressure and exposure. The visual disruption caused by broken pixels adds a layer of imperfection, echoing the uncertainties within strength. The abstract arrangement, defined by heavy textures and open spaces, allows the viewer to experience the collision of emotional forces without a literal interpretation. This composition thus serves as a metaphor for the struggle between protection and exposure, resonating with the essence of the seed.
