Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts 'trust' with 'suspicion,' using the palette from 'ancient_fire'. The left side is structured with a target pattern in burnt orange and gold, conveying warmth and reassurance, while the right side disrupts with chaotic checker patterns in dark red and charcoal, suggesting tension and uncertainty. The edges are crisp, defining each half clearly yet allowing for interaction between them. Broken pixels add imperfection, hinting at emotional friction, allowing areas of quiet space to balance out density, creating a narrative of conflict and resolution.
