Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between clarity and confusion, depicted through a palette that embodies warmth and depth. The burnt orange and dark red create an intense backdrop, suggesting a feeling of rage alongside the calm of charcoal and gold, representing clarity. The structured outer frame contrasts with the chaotic inner core, reinforcing the idea of symmetry versus break. Broken pixels disrupt the uniformity, enhancing the sense of confusion, while the left-to-right flow establishes a pulse, echoing the rhythm of a heartbeat. The layout juxtaposes clean edges against chaotic elements, embodying a struggle between order and disorder.
