Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the juxtaposition of emptiness and desire, expressed through warm and cool colors. The palette of sepia, tan, and faded blue conveys nostalgia and longing. The main contrast exists in the abrupt transition from warm yellows and muted tawny on the left, symbolizing desire, to cool blues and grays on the right, representing emptiness. The broken dots suggest desire's fragmented nature, while the flame-like pattern implies the fierce energy of longings. This chaotic interplay creates a tangible rhythm and tension, reflecting the push and pull between conflicting emotions.
