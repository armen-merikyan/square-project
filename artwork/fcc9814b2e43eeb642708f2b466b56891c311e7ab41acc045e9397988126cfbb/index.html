Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional conflict between longing and resolve through the juxtaposition of muted and vibrant colors. The chosen palette of `rain_blue`, `lavender_gray`, `moon_white`, `soft_cyan`, and `muted_plum` conveys a sense of melancholy while allowing moments of brightness to pierce through. The main contrast occurs centrally, where vibrant colors collide with subdued tones, symbolizing the struggle between emotional repair and damage. The falling pixel layout reflects a constant motion, embodying both serenity and urgency, while broken pixel placements introduce an element of imperfection, reinforcing the narrative of being caught between hope and despair.
