Artist Statement

The composition embodies a dance between tenderness and rage, using a palette of saturated reds and calm blues to illustrate emotional tension. The strong contrast occurs vertically in the square, where jagged red pulses intersect softer blue channels, creating a sense of clash and overlap. This configuration evokes a story of internal struggle, as if the elements of guilt and forgiveness battle in a confined space, reflecting the delicate balance between calm and chaos.
