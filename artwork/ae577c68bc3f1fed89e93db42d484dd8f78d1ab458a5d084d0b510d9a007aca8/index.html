Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between envy and gratitude, illustrated through a color palette of warm reds contrasted with cool pale blues. The pattern echoes a zigzag movement, signifying the rise and fall of emotions. The broken pixels create intentional disruptions, embodying the idea of a mirror that is not quite whole, enhancing the contrast. This composition focuses on creating an atmosphere of unresolved conflict, with clustered reds representing envy and expansive blues symbolizing gratitude. The interplay is balanced yet interrupted, invoking a sense of tension and depth.
