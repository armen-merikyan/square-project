Artist Statement

This composition captures the dualities of rage and calm through stark contrasts in color and placement. The palette features deep blacks representing rage alongside soft pale blues symbolizing calm, creating an emotional tension that pulses through the grid. The top left half is dominated by dense black pixels, suggesting compression and chaos, while the bottom right features lighter colors spaced out, embodying the serenity of calm. The jagged interruptions of red pixels represent points of anger that contrast against the softer blue horizontal bands, evoking feelings of collision and unresolved tension, akin to a crack against a mirror.
