Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece is a representation of tension between clarity and chaos, embodied by the pulse of signal fighting through layers of noise. The color palette features deep navy as a dominant field, suggesting depth and complexity, contrasted by bright accents of cyan that symbolize clarity and intensity. The arrangement is structured in an argument grid, where the luminescent pulses interrupt the static noise represented by darker shades. Key contrasts collide at the center of the frame, allowing the eye to move around the composition while maintaining focus on the vibrant, coherent center. Broken pixels create intentional imperfections, echoing the theme of conflict and evolution within this abstract dialogue.
