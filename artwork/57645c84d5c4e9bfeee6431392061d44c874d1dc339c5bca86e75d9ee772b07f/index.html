Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between loneliness and connection through a carefully curated palette of muted sepia and faded blue tones. The top-weighted composition allows darker colors at the top to signify heavy emotions contrasted by lighter accents below, creating a feeling of release. Each color's sharpness or softness interacts at points of collision, suggesting moments of connection. The chosen patterns—sparks of loneliness and veins of connection—intertwine, echoing the complexities of emotional isolation amidst human connectivity.
