Artist Statement

The emotional tone is captured in the juxtaposition of awe and terror, representing the push-pull between stability and chaos. The palette of dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes a sense of mysterious beauty against darker undertones, creating a visual tension. The contrast lies in the orderly stripes of awe and the jagged, chaotic stairs of terror, manifesting an interplay of contrasting emotions. The layout draws a boundary—where the outer ring embraces the calm yet disorienting chaos, while the center offers a reflective and unstable core. This composition embodies the split-screen concept through color placement, ensuring enough negative space to maintain the theme of broken memory and emotional weight.
