Artist Statement

The emotional tone evokes a struggle between joy and grief with a layout favoring weight on one side. The palette combines vibrant yellows and oranges reflecting joy, juxtaposed against deep blues and muted purples that express grief. The left side employs a striped pattern representing joy's vibrance, while the right features broken dots, symbolizing fragmentation and heaviness. The main contrast occurs between the structured joy and the chaotic grief, creating a visually arresting dialogue between feelings.
