Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between vibrancy and decay, reflecting on resilience contrasted with fragility. The palette combines muted browns and greens representing ash and quiet, with punctuations of bright colors symbolizing sparks of hope and interruption. The central diagonal collision represents the tension of competing forces—highlighted by the jagged edges of the layout that disrupt the flow, invoking the dissonance of memory versus erasure. The placement of broken pixels emphasizes imperfection and repair, adding to the narrative of struggle and transformation.
