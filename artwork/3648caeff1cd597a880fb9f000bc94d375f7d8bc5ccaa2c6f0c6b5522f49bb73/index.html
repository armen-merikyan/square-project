Artist Statement

This composition embodies the juxtaposition of 'quiet' and 'pressure'. The palette features a mix of muted and stark colors—selected to evoke feelings of tension and release. The dark navy and compressed red create an overwhelming presence along the edges, while faint peach and off-white suggest moments of calm and clarity in the center. The main contrast emerges at the diagonal center, where quiet soft hues counterbalance the dense dark shadows, simulating a dynamic argument between peace and stress. The movement radiates outward in an outward spiral, with broken pixels disrupting the surface, adding depth to the narrative that invites contemplation.
