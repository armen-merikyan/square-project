Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through color placement and pattern. The warm yellows and oranges represent connection and life, while muted purples and deep blues signify isolation and depth. The center contains bursts of brightness surrounded by darker tones, suggesting an inner conflict between feeling hidden and exposed. The patterns create a crosshatch effect that symbolizes barriers, while the jagged edges of color contrast with the more fluid center, echoing the struggle between connection and loneliness.
