Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a delicate balance between feelings of loneliness and connection. The selected palette of deep blacks and soft pale blues evokes a visual tension—darkness representing isolation and light signifying potential connection. The rows contrast heavily on the left, creating a 'weighted' feeling as if the loneliness gathers there, while the lighter colors on the right suggest hope or the yearning for connection. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, representing memory and the imperfection of past experiences. The edge style incorporates uneven placement, creating an off-balance mirror that reflects the tumult of human emotion, embodying the push and pull inherent in relationships.
