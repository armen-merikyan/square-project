Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional contrast between restlessness and peace through a carefully selected palette that signifies fear and hope. The dark hues of black and dark violet represent the weight of restlessness, while soft gold and pale green add a touch of peace. The main collision occurs centrally, where lighter accents disrupt the denser fields, illustrating a struggle between calm and chaos. The use of broken pixels simulates the imperfections of memory and motion, embodying both tension and unresolved feelings.
