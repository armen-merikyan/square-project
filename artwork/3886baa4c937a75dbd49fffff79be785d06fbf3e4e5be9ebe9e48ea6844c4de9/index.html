Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between relief and dread through contrasting colors and patterns. The chosen palette features muted storm gray and near black to evoke a sense of weight, while faded gold and pale mint introduce moments of brightness, suggesting relief. The collision occurs centrally, where dense blocks meet lighter hues, creating a sense of pushing against or pulling away. The patterns of channels and hinges emphasize movement and interruption, mirroring the emotional tug-of-war between the two forces represented in the seed.
