Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between the depths of longing expressed through sprawling combinations of cool blues and grays, suggesting a distance or yearning essence, while resolve is characterized by firm and vibrant accents creating a structured counterpoint. The palette includes ice blue for longing's coolness, ink black for depth, and warning orange for assertive resolution. The main contrast occurs diagonally, with a deep, somber black creating a stark collision against bright orange, illustrating the push and pull of these competing emotions. This configuration mirrors an inner turmoil where quiet longing meets the structured resolution, manifested through the patterns of stepwell and ripple that weave throughout the square, binding them together without literal representations, maintaining the abstract narrative.
