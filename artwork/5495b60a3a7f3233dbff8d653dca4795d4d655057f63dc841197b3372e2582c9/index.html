Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between softness and severity, presenting a swirling embrace of a pulse amidst the static of noise. The palette comprises powder pink and warm white to express gentleness, juxtaposed against the sharp crimson and carbon black that allow the severity of the experience to resonate. The main contrast occurs at the center, where the softer colors ripple outward, colliding with the severity of the black, creating a spiral of emotion. The open gate layout invites the viewer into this emotional landscape, where echoes of intensity and whispers of softness intertwine, forming an abstract story of navigating guilt and forgiveness.
