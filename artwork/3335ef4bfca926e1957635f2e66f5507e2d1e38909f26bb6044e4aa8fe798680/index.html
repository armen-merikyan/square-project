Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity. The palette of melancholy_wonder—a mix of muted plums, soft cyans, and lavender grays—contrasts moments of solid, defined areas with softer, open spaces that invite exploration. Pixels ascend from the bottom to the top, suggesting hope or growth, while the interspersed lattice pattern interrupts with unexpected bursts of color, echoing curiosity. The main collision occurs centrally, where static halos from certainty clash against the flowing lattice of curiosity, creating a visual dialogue of tension and release.
