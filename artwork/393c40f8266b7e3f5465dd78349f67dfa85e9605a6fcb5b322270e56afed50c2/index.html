Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between envy and gratitude through a palette of deep navy, black, dim cyan, and hints of white. The predominant contrast arises from the dense black and navy that symbolize envy, clashing with the lighter dim cyan representing gratitude. The misalignment of the broken pixels introduces a sense of imperfection, mirroring the complexities of these emotions. The diagonal flow creates a visual tension, suggesting an unresolved struggle that invites reflection.
