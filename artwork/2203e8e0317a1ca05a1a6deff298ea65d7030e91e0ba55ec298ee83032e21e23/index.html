Artist Statement

The emotional tone reveals a struggle between vulnerability and strength, expressed through a palette balancing the muted depth of dark shades with the electric vibrance of bright colors. The layout symbolizes an empty room with a fragile center; a pulse of color in a quiet space suggests tension between exposure and protection. The collision occurs off-center, where the strength of dark colors creates a stark contrast against flashes of bright hues which signify vulnerability. The broken pixels introduced in strategic areas add imperfection, enhancing the abstract narrative.
