Artist Statement

This piece embodies the contrast between joy and grief, reflecting their complex coexistence. The palette of bright yellows and deep blues brings warmth and coolness into a visual dialogue, while muted purples serve as a space of tranquility amid the emotional turmoil. The layout maintains symmetry disrupted by strategically placed 'broken' pixels, symbolizing emotional interruption and the unbalanced harmony between order and chaos. Dense areas of color collide with quieter spaces, creating a rhythm that speaks to inner conflict and resolution.
