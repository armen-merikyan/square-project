Artist Statement

In this composition, the emotional tone of joy (represented in warm yellows and deep oranges) collides against the somber tones of grief (rich muted purples and deep blues). The checker pattern of grief contrasts sharply with the flame-like movement of joy, reflecting a dynamic tension between order and chaos. The layout emphasizes the border of darker colors encasing the vibrant core, symbolizing grief overshadowing joy, yet sparks of brightness emerge through the dark—echoing a rising signal through the chaos. The use of broken edges and strategically misplaced pixels contributes to a fragmented yet cohesive narrative, suggesting both disruption and connection amidst the emotional turmoil.
