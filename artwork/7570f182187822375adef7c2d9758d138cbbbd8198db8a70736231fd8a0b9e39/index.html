Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional duality of restlessness and peace, using a restrained palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold. The central area features a warm, inviting flame pattern suggesting peace, while the surrounding edges disrupt this calm with chaotic thread-like patterns symbolizing restlessness. The color contrasts are sharp; the warm hues of burnt orange clash with the deep shades of charcoal and dark red, evoking a sense of a struggle between tranquility and turmoil. The dynamic tension is accentuated through broken pixels strategically placed to interrupt the flow, adding an element of imperfection. This layout emulates the feeling of a patchwork of memories, interwoven with moments of stillness and chaotic motion. Overall, the 8x8 composition creates a visual narrative of conflict and resolution, inviting contemplation on the balance between these emotional states.
