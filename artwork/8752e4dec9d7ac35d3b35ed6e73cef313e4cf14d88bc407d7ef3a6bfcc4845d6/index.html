Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between softness and severity. The selected palette of powder pink, warm white, and sharp crimson represents softness and gentility, while carbon black and steel gray evoke a harsh, intense contrast. The main collision occurs centrally, where a dense cluster of darker colors rings a lighter core, symbolizing the struggle between gentle and oppressive forces. The crosshatch pattern of softness intertwines with the ember-like severity, creating a vibrant pulse that navigates through the halo void, reminiscent of a heartbeat contrasting with static noise.
