Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between panic and control, creating a tension-filled backdrop. The warm tones of yellow and orange contrast against the cool deep blue and muted purple, representing the chaos and order inherent in the theme. The jagged edges evoke brokenness, while the structured placements suggest attempts at control. Scattered rings reflect the idea of both confinement and bursts of emotion. The balance between densely packed colors in the edges and emptier centers creates an echo of unresolved feelings.
