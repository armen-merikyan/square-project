Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional interplay between strength and vulnerability through a palette of dark navy and dim cyan, contrasted by bright whites to emphasize moments of exposure. The center pulses with the tension of a pale, soft light, symbolizing vulnerability at the heart amidst darker, denser edges embodying strength. The chosen layout emphasizes two small centers, creating a rhythmic push and pull that highlights the contrast between dense and lighter fields, but without using literal representation.
