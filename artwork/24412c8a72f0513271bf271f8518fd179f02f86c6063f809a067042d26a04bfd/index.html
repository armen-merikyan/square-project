Artist Statement

This abstract piece reflects the tension between fear and hope, represented by contrasting colors and patterns. The darker shades symbolize fear, enveloping the outer edges, while soft gold and pale green hues suggest hope and light at the center. The jagged edges and broken pixels introduce chaos and imperfection, mirroring emotional fragmentation. The layout flows vertically, like drops of emotion cascading from dark to light, creating a wave of contrast, emphasizing the collision between weight and lift.
