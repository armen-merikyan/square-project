Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between longing and resolve through its contrasting colors and fragmented patterns. The palette combines muted plum and lavender gray to evoke a sense of melancholy, while soft cyan introduces a hint of urgency, allowing for a quiet yet vibrant clash. The main collision resides in the center where longing experiences an interruption from resolve, creating a visual dialogue that captures feeling caught between hope and despair. The layout strategically positions longing in one diagonal wedge, symbolizing a steady rise interrupted by the ash-like embers of resolve, reflecting the struggle of overcoming brokenness. Broken pixels strategically placed add depth and dimension, suggesting repairs that are imperfect yet meaningful.
