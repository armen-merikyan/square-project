Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between clarity and confusion, represented by the interplay of signal and noise. The palette of signal green and hot magenta offers a stark contrast, with electric blue underscoring the tension while static gray provides a grounding element. The dominant use of green creates an overall calm, interrupted by aggressive bursts of magenta, which evoke a sense of chaos across the surface. The collision between orderly green stacks and chaotic magenta embers suggests a struggle between coherence and disruption, aligning with the seed's themes of pulse and interference.
