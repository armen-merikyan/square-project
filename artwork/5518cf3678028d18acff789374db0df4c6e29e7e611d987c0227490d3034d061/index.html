Artist Statement

The artwork captures the tension between signal and noise, embodying interference and clarity through distinct color placements. The palette comprises vibrant greens and blues for signal and muted grays and blacks for noise, establishing a stark contrast. The bottom half evokes static through horizontal stripes, while the top offers a pulsing energy with stair-like patterns that create rhythm. The main collision occurs in the center, where the clear pulse meets chaotic noise, symbolizing an ongoing struggle between clarity and obstruction.
