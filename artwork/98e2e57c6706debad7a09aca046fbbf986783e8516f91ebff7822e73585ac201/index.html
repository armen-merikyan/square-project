Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the delicate balance between safety and exposure. The palette combines soft greens and warm browns with pale skin tones and cold whites, symbolizing a protective boundary against openness. The main contrast occurs where the soft, warm shades collide with colder, exposed center colors, creating a sense of tension and release. The pattern embodies a rising motion, invoking feelings of hope while acknowledging vulnerability, as seen in the vertical split, where clarity emerges amidst the clutter.
