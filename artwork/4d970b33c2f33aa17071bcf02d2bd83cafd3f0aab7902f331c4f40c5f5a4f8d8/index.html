Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes restlessness against peace, capturing the flickering sensations of both. The palette draws from muted cream to blackened plum, symbolizing a soft tranquility clashing with vibrant energy. The layout accentuates a small bright cluster (yellow tones) amidst a darker, muted background, illustrating the constant struggle between serenity and agitation. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfection, echoing the abruptness of memory versus motion, inviting moments of reflection intertwined with disarray.
