Artist Statement

This composition expresses the struggle between clarity and distortion, embodying the emotional dichotomy of 'signal' against 'noise.' The palette juxtaposes deep blacks and grays with vibrant greens and electric blues, highlighting the fight for readability in a chaotic environment. The strong contrast occurs at the center, where bursts of signal colors collide with the dense patchwork of noise. The chosen layout suggests an invasion from the corner, creating an organic flow across the square, with broken pixels subtly introducing imperfections to represent memories and fractured paths.
