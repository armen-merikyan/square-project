Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tension between restlessness and peace. The palette features pale aqua and mist gray as calming fields, contrasting sharply with vibrant alarm red to symbolize unrest and urgency. The diagonal nature of color placement signifies movement and tension, while the scattered repairs represent attempts to achieve stability amidst chaos. The broken pixels create imperfections that mirror emotional scars, enhancing the thematic depth. Key collisions occur at the center, suggesting a clash of experiences, while quiet colors frame the unrest, creating a visual dialogue about memory and motion.
