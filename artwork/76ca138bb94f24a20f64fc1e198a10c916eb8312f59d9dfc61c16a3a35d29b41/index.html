Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the duality of envy and gratitude, illustrated through contrasting color patterns. The warm palette suggests an emotional ebb and flow, while the lighter shades represent gratitude juxtaposed against deeper tones of envy. The chaotic outer layer reflects the intense feelings of envy, leading inward to a calmer central space symbolizing gratitude. The lack of literal shapes allows for a personal interpretation of the struggle between these emotions, creating a piece that resonates with the viewer's own experiences of balance and conflict.
