Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of joy and grief through vivid colors and stark contrasts. The palette of yellow and orange represents joy, while deep blues and muted purples evoke grief. The central pattern symbolizes a 'bridge' connecting these emotions but emphasizes the clash through jagged edges and an uneven distribution, creating both harmony and tension. The alternating light and dark colors evoke a play of rising signals against a backdrop of chaos.
