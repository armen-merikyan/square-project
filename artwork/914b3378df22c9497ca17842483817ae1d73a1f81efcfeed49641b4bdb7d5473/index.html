Artist Statement

This abstract composition embodies the emotional duality of love and betrayal through juxtaposed color fields and patterns. The warm tones of pink and red evoke love, while the dark maroon introduces an element of betrayal. The layout features an argument grid that contrasts symmetrical shapes with intentional disruptions, representing broken connections. The diagonal rise adds a sense of movement, while broken pixels create imperfections that echo the emotional turmoil. This piece recalls the struggle between unity and fracture, captured in a balanced yet dynamic visual language.
