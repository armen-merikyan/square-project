Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition reflects the tension between control and surrender, achieved through a palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold. The warm colors evoke a sense of pressure while the cooler shades provide areas of release. The main collision occurs in the center, where the dense maze pattern representing control meets the softer ring of surrender. This juxtaposition captures the essence of the seed, suggesting an overflowing center constrained by borders of chaos and calm. Broken pixels create intentional disruptions, enhancing the narrative of struggle and continuity within the piece.
