Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between clarity and confusion. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of faded memories and contrasts against the more chaotic radial pattern of confusion. The structured grid element for clarity is intermittently disrupted by random color placements, suggesting moments of clarity veering into chaos. The use of broken pixels introduces inconsistencies that symbolize the struggle between the two emotional states.
