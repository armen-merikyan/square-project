Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The muted palette of sepia, tan, and dust gray conveys a sense of nostalgia and distance, while the faded blue introduces a concept of clarity and strength. The collision occurs in the center, where tightly grouped colors (representing resolve) contrast against the expansive, quieter edges (symbolizing longing). The broken pixels subtly disrupt this tension, allowing for an exploration of repair and damage through color. The layout, inspired by an island-and-sea metaphor, enhances this narrative by nestling an intense island of color within a vast, serene field, capturing both urgency and serenity.
