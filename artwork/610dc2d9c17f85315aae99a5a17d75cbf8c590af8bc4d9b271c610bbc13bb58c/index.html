Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between confusion and clarity through a color palette of deep reds and grays, evoking a sense of emotional depth and turmoil. The sharp contrast between dark maroon and vibrant pink signifies the struggle between overwhelming noise and moments of clarity. The layout emphasizes a rising signal, with asymmetrical clusters creating interrupted flow, echoing the seed concept of broken symmetry and falling pixels. The balance of dense and negative space highlights the delicate interplay between rage and calm, suggesting a narrative of unresolved emotions.
