Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity, embodied by a color palette reflecting both calmness and disruption. The use of pale mint and soft sky offers a serene base, while storm gray and near black introduce contrast, emphasizing conflict. The pattern mimics an upward spiral on one side (certainty) and a chaotic layering on the other (curiosity), creating a visually abstract collision at the center. Broken pixels enhance imperfections, reflecting the wrestle between structured paths and unresolved themes, encapsulating the essence of the seed into an emotional narrative of rising tension and uncertainty.
