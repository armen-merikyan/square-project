Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between clarity and confusion. The palette combines deep blacks and bright electric blues with pops of magenta and lime, contrasting the dark backdrop against vibrant elements. This collision creates emotional tension, as the structured rows of blues represent clarity, while the erratic introduction of magenta and lime reflects chaotic energy. The arrangement follows a mirrored layout disrupted by intentionally misplaced colors, exemplifying the theme of broken symmetry and emphasizing the underlying uncertainty. Overall, the piece oscillates between dense clusters and quiet spaces, capturing the complex dance between calm and rage, harmony and disruption.
