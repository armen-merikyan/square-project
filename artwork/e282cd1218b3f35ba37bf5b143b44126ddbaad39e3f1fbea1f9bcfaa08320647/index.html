Artist Statement

This composition explores the juxtaposition of joy and grief, using a palette of warm yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples. The contrasting colors create a vivid emotional tension, symbolizing the tug-of-war between bright moments (joy) and darker shadows (grief). The layout mirrors but includes broken pixels, emphasizing imperfection and the disruptions inherent in emotion. The diagonal flow suggests rising signals, while zigzag patterns create an echoing rhythm - reflecting the complexities of human experience. The edges are burned and weighted at the top, presenting a sense of protection as well as chaos, perfectly encapsulating the emotional narrative behind the seed.
