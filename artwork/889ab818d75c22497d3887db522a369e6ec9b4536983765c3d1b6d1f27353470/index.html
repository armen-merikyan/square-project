Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between fear and hope through a carefully selected palette and structure. The darker shades symbolize fear's grip, with a jagged edge suggesting sharpness and tension. In contrast, the lighter tones of soft gold and pale green represent hope and rising signals. The arrangement creates a dense outer layer that gradually gives way to a more open, lighter core, reflecting the push and pull of inside versus outside. The broken pixels introduce an imperfect quality, mirroring the struggle inherent in the emotions depicted.
