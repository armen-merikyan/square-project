Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captures the duality of panic and control. The palette, featuring deep blacks against bright whites with soft blue highlights, portrays the struggle between chaos and order. The main contrast occurs in the abrupt transition from the dark, dense left side to the lighter, contrasting right, showcasing the jagged boundaries between the two states. The zigzag pattern represents the erratic nature of panic, while the organized flow patterns depict the yearning for control, mimicking the push and pull of emotional states.
