Artist Statement

The emotional tone of joy versus grief manifests through contrasting colors and patterns within this abstract square. The palette includes the warm yellow and orange hues representing joy, juxtaposed against deep blue and muted purple that signify grief. This arrangement mimics the structural intensity of the emotional conflict, with warm colors initiating pulses, while cooler tones resonate deeper into the grid. The broken pixels introduce a sense of disruption within symmetry, suggesting themes of love and betrayal. The heavy use of dark colors toward the left side indicates a weighted grief that subtly encroaches upon the joyful pulses on the right, creating a visual and emotional tension that captures the essence of collision and unresolved feelings. The tonal shift creates both a rhythm and an interruption, highlighting the coexistence of these emotions within a structured yet fractured framework.
