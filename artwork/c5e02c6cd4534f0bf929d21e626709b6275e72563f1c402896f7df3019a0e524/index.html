Artist Statement

This composition explores the interplay between confusion and clarity through a distinct color palette representing love and betrayal. The left side features a chaotic 'stairs' pattern symbolizing confusion with deeper shades like dark maroon and red, creating a dense, overwhelming sensation. The right side employs a ring pattern depicting clarity, where lighter gray and pink hues evoke a gentle, soothing feel. The jagged edge style enhances the theme of 'broken symmetry', allowing visual tension where the colors collide, particularly at the boundary, echoing the emotional struggle of finding clarity amidst chaos.
