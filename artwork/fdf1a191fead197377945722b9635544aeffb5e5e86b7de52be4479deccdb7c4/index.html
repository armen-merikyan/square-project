Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional struggle between restlessness and peace. The selected palette, a mix of warm ivory, deep wine, and ash gray, evokes a sense of conflict and resolution. The collision of densely packed colors at the top, depicted through dark tones, transitions into lighter fields below, representing a journey towards tranquility. Broken pixels from the edges create a disarray that interrupts the flow, mirroring the tension experienced between motion and stillness. The diagonal pattern suggests a movement that is both directed and erratic, rooted in the idea of memory versus motion, leading one’s eye across the piece in an echo of restless energy.
