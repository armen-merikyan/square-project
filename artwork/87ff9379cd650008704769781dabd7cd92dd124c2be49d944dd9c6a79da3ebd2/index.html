Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between a clear, vibrant signal and chaotic, static noise. The palette juxtaposes vibrant 'signal green' and 'electric blue' against darker 'dead black' and 'static gray', signifying the tension between clarity and interference. The arrangement showcases a center pulse interwoven with interrupted static patterns, emphasizing the collision of harmonious flow and disruptive echoes. The broken pixels add imperfections, reflecting the influence of memory and erasure within this abstract landscape.
