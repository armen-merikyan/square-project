Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between certainty and curiosity, creating a sense of dynamic balance. The palette leans towards warm greens and golds that evoke feelings of growth and vibrancy. The main contrast emerges from the dark, dense corners representing certainty, gradually loosening into softer hues of cream and gold that suggest curiosity and openness. The patterns of 'rain' and 'flood' interact, causing visual collisions in the central area while maintaining erosion at the edges, illustrating the chaos of curiosity against the structured certainty. Overall, this piece embodies a struggle between order and chaos, with an asymmetric shape that invites exploration.
