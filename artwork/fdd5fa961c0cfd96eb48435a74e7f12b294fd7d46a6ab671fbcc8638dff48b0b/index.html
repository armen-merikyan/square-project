Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension of relief against dread through contrast and pattern. The palette features soft mint and storm gray creating a dialogue between the lively and oppressive. The collision occurs in the center, where the dark hues of gray and near black press against lighter lattice patterns in mint and faded gold. Broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, while the diagonal layout highlights the conflict between the two emotional states, suggesting a slow release from chaos towards order.
