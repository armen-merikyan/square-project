Artist Statement

The artwork captures the essence of 'signal vs noise' through a divided grid where one side embodies chaos (darkness, disarray) and the other strives for clarity (lightness, structure). The palette, featuring navy and dim cyan as dominant colors, creates a somber yet vibrant mood, reflecting the collision of a readable pulse against a backdrop of static interference. The main contrast occurs in the center, where light interruptions break through an otherwise dense arrangement of darker hues, embodying a struggle to maintain clarity amid chaos. Each broken pixel represents an imperfection, reinforcing the theme of resilience and disruption. This arrangement visually suggests a narrative of navigating through a fractured system toward an emerging signal.
