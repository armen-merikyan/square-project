Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of desire and restraint through contrasting color zones. The warm tones, representing desire, spill over cooler muted areas signifying restraint. The central collision occurs between brights and darks, creating visual tension and a sense of movement. The selection of colors from 'desire_emptiness' suggests a battle between vibrant aspirations and quieter realities, reflecting the spiral pressure of conflicting feelings.
