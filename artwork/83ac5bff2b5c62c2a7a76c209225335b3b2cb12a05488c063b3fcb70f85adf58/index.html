Artist Statement

The emotional tone of confusion versus clarity is reflected in the contrasting patterns of dense block colors and scattered pixels. The palette of red, black, pale blue, and white emphasizes tension and calm, creating a visual dialogue. The primary clash occurs between the structured dark rows and lighter blues, showcasing a struggle for dominance. The presence of broken pixels adds an element of imperfection, mirroring the seed's theme of 'symmetry_vs_break' while maintaining an abstract aesthetic.
