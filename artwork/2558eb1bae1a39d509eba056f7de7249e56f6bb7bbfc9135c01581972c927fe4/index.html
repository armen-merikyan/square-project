Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between quiet and pressure. The palette includes muted tones representing quietness (off white, faint peach) and deeper shades denoting pressure (dark navy, compressed red) to create an uneven response. The collision occurs in the top left corner, where the dense, grid-like structured pressure colors push against the softer, crack-like quiet colors, signifying a shattering against the tide. This interplay mirrors the complexities of clarity and confusion, with organized patterns broken by softer interruptions.
