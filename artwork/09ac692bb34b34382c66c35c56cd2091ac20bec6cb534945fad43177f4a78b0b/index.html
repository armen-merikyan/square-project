Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional interplay between calm and rage, utilizing a palette of warm and cool tones: deep reds and blacks contrast with soft, pale blues and whites. The edges are sharply defined, reflecting the explosive nature of rage while the maze-like pattern represents the ambiguity of calm. The diagonal flow suggests an invasion of calm space by bursts of warm color, echoing the seed's theme of tension between emotions. The arrangement captures an unresolved conflict, creating visual tension without depicting literal imagery.
