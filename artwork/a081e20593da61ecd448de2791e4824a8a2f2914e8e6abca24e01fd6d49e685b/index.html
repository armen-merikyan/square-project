Artist Statement

The composition embodies the emotional dynamic between longing and resolve. The warm hues of honey gold and ripe green suggest yearning and hope in the center, contrasting with the cooler deep olive and acid green at the edges that signify resolve and boundaries. The overlapping patterns represent the struggle between containment and overflow, with density at the center hinting at urgency, while the negative spaces echo a quiet longing. The intentional disruption of broken pixels adds a fractured beauty to the piece, symbolizing repairs against underlying damage. This combination leads to an abstract narrative that balances tension and continuity throughout the arrangement.
