Artist Statement

This artwork portrays the emotional struggle between rage and calm through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of black, red, pale blue, and white represents the heat of rage in juxtaposition with the serene qualities of calm. The sharp contrasts of the grid pattern signify the explosive nature of rage, invading the calm sections marked by split-screen patterns. The primary collision is between the dense black and sharp red accents against the soft pale blue background, depicting the turmoil that arises from inner chaos. Overall, the arrangement captures the essence of emotional imbalance, oscillating between tensions that reflect both confinement and release, inspired by themes of weight and lightness from the seed text.
