Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between awe and terror through a palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple. The colors suggest warmth and sadness, embodying the deep emotional contrasts suggested by the seed. The layout features a near mirror effect disrupted by a few broken pixels, emphasizing imperfection within order. The dominant collision occurs at the split where deep blue meets vibrant warm hues, creating a wave-like flow that contrasts stability with instability, echoing both the awe of beauty and the terror of chaos.
