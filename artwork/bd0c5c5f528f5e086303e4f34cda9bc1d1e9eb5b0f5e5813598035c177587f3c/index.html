Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the tension between loneliness and connection, evoked through the use of stark contrasts and strategic placements. The palette of deep black, electric blue, and magenta serves to illustrate the complexity of emotions—black representing isolation and depth, while magenta hints at fleeting connections. The center is dominated by a single, luminous blue pixel, symbolizing a seed of connection surrounded by darker tones that suggest loneliness. This creates an engaging visual tension, paired with the spiral flow that guides the eye through the composition, suggesting movement from isolation towards connection. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, emphasizing the struggle inherent in balancing these emotional states.
