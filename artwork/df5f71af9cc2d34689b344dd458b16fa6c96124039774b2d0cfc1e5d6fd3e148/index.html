Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a palette that juxtaposes the softness of pale skin against the solid depth of shelter green and warm brown. The shatter and glitch patterns clash in the center, where pale and warm tones intersect, creating a focal point of imbalance, representative of the struggle for safety amidst exposure. The asymmetrical distribution of color enhances the sense of conflict and resolution, portraying a narrative of internal struggle captured in a simple abstract composition.
