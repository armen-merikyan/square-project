Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between warmth and pressure, as tender shades of pale blue whisper against jagged shards of red, embodying rage. The palette juxtaposes soothing hues against dark, stark elements, creating a sense of conflict. The main collision occurs centrally, where red and black meet, disrupting the softness of the quieter tones. The layout of two pulses suggests a rhythmic push and pull, encapsulating the struggle within subtle noise and stark, penetrating shapes, represented by a knot-like arrangement that weaves through the canvas.
