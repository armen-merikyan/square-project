Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between love and betrayal, represented through a palette of warm pinks and reds juxtaposed against darker grays and maroon. The upper four rows symbolize love with a pattern of 'teeth,' portraying warmth, while the bottom four represent betrayal with a 'dots' pattern, introducing a sense of disruption. Collisions occur centrally where the warm colors meet the stark gray lines, echoing the fractured relationship theme inspired by the seed.
