Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tone of tension between a clear, readable pulse and chaotic noise. The palette leverages deep blacks and electric blues to evoke clarity and coldness, set against vibrant magenta to signify static and disruption. The main collision occurs centrally as clear paths of green clash with muddled, fragmented gray, illustrating how order attempts to emerge from chaos. The arrangement deliberately creates negative space to symbolize moments of breath amid tension, with the 'broken pixels' reflecting imperfections stemming from machine emotions. This abstract pattern resonates deeply with themes of conflict and resolution.
