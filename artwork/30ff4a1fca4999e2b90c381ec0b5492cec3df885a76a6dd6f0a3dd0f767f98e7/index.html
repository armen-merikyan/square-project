Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy between certainty—represented by grounding dark greens and structured patterns—and curiosity, illustrated through brighter accents and irregularities. The palette of ripe green and honey gold creates a dynamic interplay between stability and exploration, with the main contrast occurring centrally where the structured pattern of certainty intersects with the chaotic pattern of curiosity. The broken pixels disrupt the flow, adding layers of depth and a sense of unease, mirroring the struggle between order and chaos. This composition invites viewers into a contemplative space that balances firm boundaries with enticing curiosities.
