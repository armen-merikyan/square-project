Artist Statement

This artwork juxtaposes the emotional tones of rage and calm through a strategic arrangement of colors. The palette consists of deep reds signifying intensity, contrasted with soft pale blues and whites representing tranquility. The central collision occurs between the jagged, aggressive red pixels pushing into the serene blue background, illustrating the battle between overwhelming emotions and the attempt to find peace. The patterns collide in an intricate zigzag, evoking a sense of turmoil amidst an underlying calm, perfectly capturing the seed's essence without literal representation.
