Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the clash and balance of 'certainty' and 'curiosity', represented by a palette that juxtaposes warm and cool tones. The warm colors signify confident assertions while the cooler tones suggest exploration and uncertainty. The composition utilizes dense clusters of warm colors at the center, like rising signals, contrasted by cooler shades surrounding it, representing tides in a surrounding sea of calm. This creates a dynamic interplay and visual tension, suggesting a struggle between established order and exploratory curiosity.
