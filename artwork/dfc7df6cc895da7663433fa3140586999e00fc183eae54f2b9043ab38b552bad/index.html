Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between restlessness and peace. The selected palette, 'serenity_urgency,' reflects soft aqua and gray hues harmonizing with striking red and deep blue accents. The composition embodies a quiet channel layout where a narrow negative space intersects dense constellations of color, signifying conflict and unresolved motion. The collision occurs at the center where haze merges with vibrant interruptions, emphasizing the chaos within calm. The broken pixels serve as imperfections that suggest moments of disruption and memory, adding to the layered depth of emotion.
