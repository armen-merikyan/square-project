Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional struggle between relief and dread. The warm colors on the left, representing relief (pinks and reds), create a stark contrast against the cooler, darker shades on the right (grays and dark maroon) symbolizing dread. The top-weighted structure emphasizes the heaviness of dread, while broken dots on the left suggest moments of hope and relief. The split layout reflects the tension between the two moods, creating a dynamic interplay that speaks to the complexity of human emotion and experience. Negative space is utilized to suggest emptiness and absence, enhancing the overall narrative without cluttering the composition.
