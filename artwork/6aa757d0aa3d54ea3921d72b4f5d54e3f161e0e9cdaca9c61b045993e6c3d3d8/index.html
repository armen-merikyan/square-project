Artist Statement

Emotional tone explores the tension between crowded pressure and spacious quiet. The palette combines deep dark navy signifying pressure with muted off-white and faint peach that evoke quietness. The main collision occurs along a diagonal that divides the piece, with a dense braid of darker colors on one side contrasting with the soft, muted colors feeling like a releasing wave. This reflects the seed-driven themes of conflict and balance: the spiral layout directed toward an off-center calm suggests an ongoing struggle between dense clusters and open space, embodying a dynamic interplay of feelings.
