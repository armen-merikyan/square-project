Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between vibrancy and quietude. The palette includes 'pale_aqua' for calmness, 'mist_gray' for restraint, 'alarm_red' for urgency, 'ink_blue' for depth, and 'clear_white' for stark contrasts. The dominant color, mist_gray, occupies much of the space, illustrating the weight of restraint. Vibrant accents lie sporadically, creating tension and sparks of energy across the canvas, embodying the collision between spark and ash. The jagged edges introduce a sense of disruption, while the negative space emphasizes the quiet channel cutting through the density, allowing patterns to collide and interleave thoughtfully.
