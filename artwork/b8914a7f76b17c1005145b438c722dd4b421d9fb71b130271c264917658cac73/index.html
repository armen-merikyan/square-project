Artist Statement

The emotional tone highlights the contrast between loneliness and connection, represented through a palette of deep maroon, gray, and soft pink hues. The darker shades evoke a sense of solitude, while the pink accents signify fleeting moments of connection. The design employs a mirrored layout with intentional disruptions, capturing both the overwhelming weight of loneliness and the delicate bursts of connection. This interplay creates a visual tension that reflects the theme of hidden versus exposed emotions, punctuated by the cracked patterns that suggest fragility among the grid structure. The overall composition invites viewers to ponder the nuances of emotional experience through contrasting densities and colors.
