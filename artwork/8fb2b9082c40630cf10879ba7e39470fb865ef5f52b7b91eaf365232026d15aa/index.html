Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between 'spark' and 'ash' through contrasting colors, with warm tones representing energy and cool tones embodying restraint. The maze-like pattern of 'spark' collides with the halo-like presence of 'ash,' creating a rich dialogue in the center while jagged edges disrupt the flow, signifying resilience against fragility. The palette's intimacy contrasts with the distance suggested by the arrangement, evoking feelings of urgency and quiet reflection.
