Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between restlessness and peace. Utilizing a palette of serene colors like pale aqua and mist gray contrasted with alarm red and ink blue, tension surfaces visually. The arrangement emphasizes the collision at the diagonal, where dense veil patterns (representing restlessness) disrupt the calm dot patterns (symbolizing peace). This interaction evokes a feeling of a struggle between motion and stillness, illuminated by bursts of color that suggest memory imprints amidst the ebb and flow of existence.
