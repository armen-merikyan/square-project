Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the interplay between restlessness and peace through contrasting colors and patterns. The selected palette of moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold conveys both fragility and resilience, perfectly embodying the essence of conflicting emotions. The main collision occurs in the center, where restless dot patterns converge with tranquil patchwork, creating a tangible tension. The broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, highlighting imperfections and the struggle between static and resonance. The flow from top to bottom gives a sense of movement and groundedness, aligning with the seed's themes of motion and disruption.
