Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting color densities. The palette of greens and golds symbolizes growth and potential against the backdrop of deeper olives, reflecting complexity and richness in emotional depth. The central collision occurs where bright accents of honey gold interrupt the shadowy deep olive, suggesting an exposed center—vulnerability—framed by layers of stable color. The use of broken pixels adds imperfection, further embodying the concept of exposure vs. shelter, while ensuring there remains a sense of negative space, allowing the viewer to reflect and feel the weight of emotion.
