Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between restlessness and peace, illustrated through the use of a muted palette with explosive accents. The colors transition from deep olives and greens to brighter honey gold and acid green, representing a movement from calmness to agitation. The main contrast happens in the center where bright hues clash against the darker edges, symbolizing how fleeting peace often fights against underlying memories of turmoil. The broken pixels interject moments of disruption, mirroring the experiences of memory and motion, while the sealed edges suggest confinement, creating a sense of tension within the square. Overall, the arrangement evokes a narrative of conflict and resolution, echoing the complexity of human emotion.
