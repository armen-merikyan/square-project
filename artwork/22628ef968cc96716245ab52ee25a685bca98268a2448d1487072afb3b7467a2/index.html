Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances relief and dread through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette combines muted tones of dark gray and black representing dread with gentle soft mint and faded gold symbolizing relief. The collision occurs in the center where the heavy dark mass disrupts the lighter open spaces, creating a visual tension that echoes the emotional narrative. This captures a spiraling movement from chaos at the edges towards an opening in the center, paralleling the seed's essence of conflict and release.
