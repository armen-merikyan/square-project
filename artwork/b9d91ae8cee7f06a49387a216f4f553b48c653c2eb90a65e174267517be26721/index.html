Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay between longing and resolve, manifesting as a delicate dance between rich, warm colors and cool, muted tones. The palette combines warm yellows and oranges representing 'longing' with deep blues and muted purples symbolizing 'resolve.' The main contrast occurs at the center, where intersecting patterns echo both longing's glitch and resolve's structural integrity, creating tension. The broken pixels reflect imperfections, suggesting past damage yet also hinting at repair, captured through fragmented bursts of color in an 'open_gate' layout. This configuration fosters an emotional dialogue of contained versus overflowing, mirroring the push and pull of human experience.
