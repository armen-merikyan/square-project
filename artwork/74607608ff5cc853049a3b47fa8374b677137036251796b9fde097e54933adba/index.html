Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the dense, weighted presence of pressure and the gentle, soft quality of quiet. The palette combines muted tones like 'dust_gray' and 'faint_peach' to capture quietness, while 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy' evoke a sense of pressure. The central collision occurs at the bottom left, where dense reds disrupt the softer edges, symbolizing the breaking of tension. The 'hook' shape seed manifests through a flowing diagonal of tension, surrounded by scattered, softer tones, suggesting a dynamic conversation between the two emotional states. This layout mirrors a stacked band effect that captures the essence of emotional strata—moving from pressure to release.
