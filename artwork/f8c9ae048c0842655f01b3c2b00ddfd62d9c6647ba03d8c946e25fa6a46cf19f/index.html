Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the tension between loneliness and connection, encapsulated in a palette of dark violet and soft gold for warmth against black and pale green for depth. The contrast is strategically placed in the center, signifying an exposed vulnerability. The grid-like flow reflects a structured arrangement while the maze pattern alludes to complexity, creating a narrative of hidden emotional struggles amidst an attempt to connect. Broken pixels disrupt the pattern, hinting at imperfection and the struggle for connection, ultimately leaving significant negative space to evoke feelings of isolation.
