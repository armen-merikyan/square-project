Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the struggle between defiance and submission. The palette of soft pinks, crisp whites, and deep grays evokes softness contrasted with severity—each color playing its role in this emotional duel. The carbon black serves as the primary aggressive interruption amidst the powder pink's gentle embrace, emphasizing resilience and fragility. The main collision occurs in the center where sharp crimson and steel gray interact, suggesting a focal point of tension. This is accentuated by broken pixels throughout, depicting both the remnants of chaos and an incomplete repair path, creating a narrative of ongoing conflict and resolution.
