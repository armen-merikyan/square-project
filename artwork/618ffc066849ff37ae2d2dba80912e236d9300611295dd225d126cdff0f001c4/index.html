Artist Statement

This composition conveys the emotional tension between 'quiet' and 'pressure' through a contrast of soft colors interspersed with hard edges. The palette emphasizes quietness with soft grays and off-whites, while bursts of compressed red inject a sense of urgency and crowding, creating vibrations across the grid. The main collision occurs through diagonal stripes that cut against a gentle stair-step background, representing a dynamic interplay of release and intensity, echoing the seed's themes of tension and resolution.
