Artist Statement

The artwork reflects the emotional duel between 'emptiness' and 'desire' through a palette that evokes warmth and depth. The contrast between vibrant colors like 'hot coral' and subdued tones like 'charcoal' captures the tension of hungry aspiration against a contained border. The main collision occurs centrally, where the warm coral shades ripple against a stark border of blue, representing both yearning and the hollowness of unmet needs. The chosen patterns—patchwork for desire and barcode for emptiness—interrupt each other, enhancing the sensation of conflict and unresolved longing, resonant with the seed topics.
