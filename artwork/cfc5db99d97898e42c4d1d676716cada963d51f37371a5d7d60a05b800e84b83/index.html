Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the tension between relief (softness and release) and dread (pressure and darkness). Colors like 'pale_mint' and 'soft_sky' evoke a sense of openness alongside 'storm_gray' and 'near_black' that embody a looming dread. The arrangement forms a gradient of density from the bottom to the top, where the darker colors clump together, suggesting the weight of pressure, while the lighter colors unfurl like hope. The collision occurs in the center with a cluster of darker hues, surrounded by lighter ones, creating a dynamic balance that resonates with the seed's themes. This pattern transcends literal interpretation, representing emotions through abstract interaction and color placement.
