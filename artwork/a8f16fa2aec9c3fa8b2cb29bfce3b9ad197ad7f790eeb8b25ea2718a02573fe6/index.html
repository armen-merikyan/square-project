Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional collision between trust and suspicion, reflecting the dichotomy through contrasting color patterns. The palette employs deep blues for trust and ashen tones for suspicion, establishing a gradient of warmth and coldness. The main collision occurs in the center, where the soft noise pattern disrupts the more static dot pattern of suspicion, creating a pulse of dynamics. The chosen colors and arrangements convey a sense of pressure and release, suggesting resilience amidst fragility, as indicated by the seed's narrative of complex emotional states.
