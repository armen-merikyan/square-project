Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the delicate balance between vulnerability and strength. The palette of ripe green, acid green, honey gold, deep olive, and cream juxtaposes grounded strength against the delicate nature of vulnerability. The collision occurs in the central area where darker colors evoke density, while lighter shades reflect exposed moments. The layout of two small centers creates a pulse of interaction, surrounded by intentional negative space that invites contemplation.
