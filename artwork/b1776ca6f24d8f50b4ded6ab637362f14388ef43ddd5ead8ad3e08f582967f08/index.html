Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength using a palette inspired by melancholy and contrast. The dominant soft cyan color in the center suggests vulnerability, while the dense lavender gray and muted plum around it create a protective frame, indicating strength. The main collision of color occurs at the center, where the lightness of the soft cyan meets the heaviness of the darker hues, interrupted by strategically placed broken pixels that introduce a sense of chaos, echoing the concept of exposure versus shelter. This overall composition speaks to the delicate balance between security and fragility, where both emotions coexist yet collide.
