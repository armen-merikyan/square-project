Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between confusion and clarity, utilizing a palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon to emphasize the tension. The contrast manifests in the stark juxtaposition between thick outlines and broken dots, creating an echo of collisions. The layout fights for dominance with broken pixels scattered throughout, symbolizing imperfection radiating from the center outward. This design creates an unsettling atmosphere while also suggesting moments of clarity amidst chaos.
