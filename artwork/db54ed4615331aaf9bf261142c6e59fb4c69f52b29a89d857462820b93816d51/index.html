Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy between rage and calm through a converging flow. The warmer colors, mainly red, pierce through the cooler, more muted tones of pale blue and black, creating a visual tension that speaks to explosive feelings battling against a serene backdrop. The chosen palette aims to juxtapose heat against coolness, where the radial disruptions of red create asymmetric bursts, echoing the chaos of emotion. The centerpiece serves as an arena for collision, highlighting a struggle between overflowing intensity and the containment of calm. The overall arrangement carries an abstract narrative of interruption where the soft, echoing calm reflects resilience against the sharpness of rage.
