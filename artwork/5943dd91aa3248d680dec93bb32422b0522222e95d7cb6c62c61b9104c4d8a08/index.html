Artist Statement

This composition evokes the emotional duality of longing and resolve through contrasting density and quietness. The palette of lime, electric blue, black, and magenta creates a dynamic balance between vibrant chaos (resolve) and the softness of longing through the use of quiet fields of black. The main collision of color occurs at the center, symbolizing a pivotal interaction between the two emotional states, allowing for feelings of tension and release while also providing space for introspection and warmth. The use of broken pixels emphasizes imperfection and vulnerability, reminding us of the fragility in the pursuit of connection and understanding.
