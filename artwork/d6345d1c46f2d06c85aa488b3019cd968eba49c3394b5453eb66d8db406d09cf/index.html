Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension of 'longing' and 'resolve' through an interplay of warm and cool tones, representing inner conflict and resolution. The palette combines the grounding element of 'shelter green' with 'open blue' to create a sense of depth and distance. The contrast of densely packed colors in the center merges with open quiet fields towards the edges, emphasizing the concept of collision between chaos and order. The diagonal patterns signal unresolved motion, evoking an intimate distance. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfection, reflecting emotional scars and the complexity of feelings.
