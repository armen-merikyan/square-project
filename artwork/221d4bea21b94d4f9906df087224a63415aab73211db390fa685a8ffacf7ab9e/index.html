Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between panic and control, balancing dark, muted tones with sporadic brighter sparks to create a sense of unease and uncertainty. The chosen palette, 'broken_memory', enhances the emotional dichotomy through warm sepia tones juxtaposed against cooler faded blues. The central heavy edge aligns with a dense lower layer, which contrasts with lighter spots, symbolizing fleeing moments of control amidst overwhelming chaos. The layout offers a corner invasion, suggesting the encroachment of panic, while breaks in color represent the inherent instability of these emotions.
