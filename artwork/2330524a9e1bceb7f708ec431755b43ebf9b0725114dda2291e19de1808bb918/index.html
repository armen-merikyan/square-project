Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional interplay between softness and severity through contrasting palettes of warm and cool tones. The transition from sharp crimson to soft powder pink illustrates a struggle between light and dark, manifesting as fragmented echoes against a dense, muted background. The layout emphasizes a sense of scattered repair, embodying emotional tension and unresolved conflict, while simultaneously employing chaos within structured fields for visual interruption. The central focus highlights the contrast of a quiet warmth struggling against a weighted darkness, embodying the essence of the seed's themes.
