Artist Statement

The composition reflects the tension between rage and calm through a balanced palette of stark red and cool pale blue. The diagonal flow represents the invasiveness of intense emotions, while softer noise patterns emerge from a dark base, suggesting hidden layers within. Strong contrast arises from red pixels intersecting blue spaces, illustrating the push and pull of conflicting feelings, captured in a field that is both dense and open. The interplay of sound and silence is encoded in this pattern of color.
