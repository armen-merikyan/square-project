Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a contrast of colors and patterns. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes a sense of hope against a backdrop of uncertainty. The diagonal spread of darker tones depicts the longing, punctuated by brighter patches representing moments of resolve and clarity. The central density of dark colors collides with sporadic bright pixels, embodying the fight between chaos and order, echoing the seed's themes. Broken pixels are deliberately placed to accentuate imperfections and the ongoing struggle within.
