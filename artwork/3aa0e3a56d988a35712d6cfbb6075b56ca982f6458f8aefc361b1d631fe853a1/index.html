Artist Statement

The composition captures the emotional tension of longing versus resolve through a deliberate interplay of warmth and coolness. The palette features warm coral and blood orange against the cooler hues of charcoal and hollow blue, symbolizing the push and pull of these emotions. The structured grid represents longing, while the flame pattern interrupts this order, introducing an element of chaos. The main collision occurs in the center, where warm tones and cooler shadows clash, encapsulating the conflict inherent in the seed's sentiments of aspiration and limitations.
