Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through a dynamic interplay of colors. Utilizing a palette that juxtaposes warm tones of yellow and orange against the cool depths of muted purple and deep blue, the piece evokes a powerful contrast that resonates with the themes of harmony versus chaos. The falling pixel layout creates a sense of descending motion like tears or rain, while the broken edges suggest the idea of imperfection inherent in clarity. The broken pixels introduce interruptions, fostering a visual narrative of struggle between symmetrical order and chaotic disruption, creating an abstract rhythm that mirrors the emotional pulse described in the seed.
