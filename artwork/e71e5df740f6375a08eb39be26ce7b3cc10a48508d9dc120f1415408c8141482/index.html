Artist Statement

This artwork captures the duality of vulnerability and strength through a delicate balance between soft, pale colors and jagged, darker edges. The use of muted tones like 'rose_clay' and 'distant_lilac' represents exposure, while dense colors like 'warm_shadow' showcase stability. The falling pixel layout evokes a sense of descending pressure, emphasizing the collision between chaos (noise) and structure (frame). The interplay of colors speaks to the tension between vulnerability and strength, reflecting the emotional complexity of the seed.
