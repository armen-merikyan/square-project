Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between quiet and pressure. The palette features muted tones like 'dust_gray' and 'dark_navy' that evoke heaviness and weight, contrasted by soft 'faint_peach' to suggest the lightness of quiet moments. The lattice pattern in the denser left region instills a sense of pressure, while the fault line pattern on the right indicates openness and release. The main collision occurs along the diagonal axis, where the pressure meets the quiet, symbolizing the conflict between compression and calm. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions within this interplay, enhancing the narrative of sharp edges meeting soft tones, reflecting both clarity and confusion.
