Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional contrast between longing and resolve, conveyed through a palette of warm and cool tones. The use of broken pixels represents interruptions and imperfections, while larger areas of quiet space echo the 'halo void' layout. The collision occurs centrally where muted tones intermingle with brighter accents, symbolizing the tension between desire (longing) and fulfillment (resolve). The arrangement of circular 'ring' patterns, coupled with the structured 'frame' design, expresses a narrative of repair emerging from chaos, allowing for a serene yet urgent undertone throughout the piece.
