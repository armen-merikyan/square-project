Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between vulnerability and strength, represented through contrasting color placements. The selected palette of deep blacks, vibrant signal green, and electric blue emphasizes the struggle of resilience against exposure. The collision occurs mainly in the center with a ripple-like arrangement of soft tones surrounded by hard edges, signifying the protective frame around the vulnerability. The patterns of strength (frame) and vulnerability (ripple) clash against each other, evoking an emotional dance of stability and fragility.
