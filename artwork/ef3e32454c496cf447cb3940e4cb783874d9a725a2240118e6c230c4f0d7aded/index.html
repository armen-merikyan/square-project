Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between joy and grief, structured through a rising layout to symbolize hope against despair. The color palette combines vibrant hues of yellow and orange, representing joy, with deeper tones of muted purple and deep blue, reflecting grief. The juxtaposition of two distinct patterns—joy in a crosshatch and grief as stripes—creates emotional collisions, illustrated through the placement of colors. The thin outlines introduce a sense of specificity amidst chaos, while remnants of broken pixels evoke the fragmented nature of human emotion.
