Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between trust and suspicion, signifying the inherent conflict in their coexistence. The palette of stain brown, ash gray, warm ivory, soft gold, and deep wine expresses warmth and depth, suggesting vulnerability and resilience amidst the contrasting feelings. The main collision occurs along the vertical split, where the mirror broken layout allows the right side filled with soft islands of trust to collide with jagged mirrors of suspicion on the left, hinting at the imperfections and disruptions of emotional boundaries. Overall, the arrangement leans towards suspicion while allowing glimmers of trust to peek through the patterns, simulating an atmosphere of unease with moments of hope.
