Artist Statement

The emotional tone depicts the tension between longing and resolve, creating a push-pull dynamic. The palette blends soft, muted colors from the 'intimacy_distance', representing warmth and calm with sharper accents like golden hues. The main collision occurs diagonally, symbolizing the struggle between compression and expansion, with broken pixels as marks of disruption, suggesting the natural chaos of experiences. This arrangement vibrates with patterns of tension, where distance and closeness reflect emotional complexity.
