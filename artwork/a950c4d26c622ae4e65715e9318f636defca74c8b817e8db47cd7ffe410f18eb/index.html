Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures an interplay between defiance and submission, where the central area bursts with vivid colors symbolizing vibrant struggle, while the edges contain softer hues suggesting withdrawal and enclosure. The palette of ripe green, acid green, honey gold, deep olive, and cream conveys contrasting emotions: an internal fire against a quiet backdrop. The collision occurs predominantly in the center, where bold colors interact dynamically, while the fragmented, uneven border emphasizes the tension created by defiance pushing against the confines of submission. The arrangement reflects the qualities of a bridge, uniting opposing forces in a harmonious yet charged visual pulse.
