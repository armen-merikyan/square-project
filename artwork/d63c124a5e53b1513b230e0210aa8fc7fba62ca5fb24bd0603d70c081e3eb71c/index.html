Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the duality of 'longing' and 'resolve'. The palette, inspired by muted tones, suggests an underlying tension, using rich reds and deep plums to evoke a sense of urgency and depth, while warm creams provide a soothing contrast. The center of the square is dense with color, encapsulating a wave of emotion that spills towards the borders, which are less saturated. The collision occurs predominantly at the center, creating an overwhelming sensation that's both captivating and contained. The pattern reflects the ripples of longing, represented by the cascading waves against structured halos of resolve, illustrating the push and pull of desire and commitment. Imperfections, represented by broken pixels, add a layer of disruption, reminding us of the fragility within these interactions.
