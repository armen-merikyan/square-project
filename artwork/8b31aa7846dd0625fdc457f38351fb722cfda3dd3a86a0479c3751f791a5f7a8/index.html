Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances defiance and submission, captured through contrasting colors and patterns. The selected palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue creates a sense of broken memory, symbolizing tension with muted fields and vibrant accents. The collision occurs centrally with darker colors framing lighter ones, embodying the struggle between guarding and yielding. The seed inspired the use of boundaries and interruptions, where patterns of mirror and pulse marks create a dynamic visual conversation that suggests depth and complexity without defining specific icons.
