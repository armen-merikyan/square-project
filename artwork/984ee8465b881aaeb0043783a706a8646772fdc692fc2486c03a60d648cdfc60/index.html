Artist Statement

This artwork captures the duality of awe and terror through a striking contrast of rich, dark colors against bright, warm accents. The central motif creates a sense of focus yet evokes a feeling of uncertainty with the interplay between jagged edges of darker colors and the softer patterns of warmth. The palette of burnt orange and gold denotes a flicker of hope contrasted against the oppressive dark reds and charcoals, embodying the struggle between order and chaos. The pattern reflects a balance of maze-like complexity on one side and a systematic approach on the other, illustrating the emotional tug-of-war inspired by the seed.
