Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that emphasizes warmth and depth. The top four rows, filled with soft, pale tones, depict vulnerability while the bottom four utilize more intense colors to illustrate strength. The collision lies at the center where the threads of color intermingle, symbolizing tension and resilience. The subtle 'broken pixels' disrupt the flow, suggesting the fractures found in frailty and support, creating an echo of conflict within the pattern as it moves from light to dark.
