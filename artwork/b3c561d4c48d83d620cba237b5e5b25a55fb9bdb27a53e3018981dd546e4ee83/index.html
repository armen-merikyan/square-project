Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional pulse between restlessness and peace through the contrasting colors of the palette. The reds and muted creams convey a warmth of tension, while the deeper blacks and grays offer a sense of calm. The layout emphasizes a collision at the center, where the light vs. dark elements shift into one another, symbolizing the struggle of memory against motion. Broken pixels are strategically placed to hint at imperfections and disruptions in this interplay, embodying a fragmented narrative as emotion ebbs and flows.
