Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines joy and grief, showcasing a struggle between uplifting expansion and heavy fragmentation. The palette reflects this duality: bright yellows and oranges signify joy while muted purples and deep blues hint at grief. The layout creates a mirror effect disrupted by broken pixels and pattern shifts, representing chaos within order. The main collision lies in the transition from the warm joy side to the cool, heavy grief side, suggesting a journey of emotional duality.
