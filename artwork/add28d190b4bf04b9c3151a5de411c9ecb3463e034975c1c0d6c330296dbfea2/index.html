Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional struggle between control and surrender by utilizing a palette of contrasting colors—warm coral and soft amber against cool tones of blue-black and cold slate. The central clash occurs in the middle of the square, with brighter accents surrounding darker fields, suggesting moments of tension and release. The introduction of broken pixels disrupts symmetry, reflecting the chaos of the emotional state conveyed by the seed. The spiral flow evokes movement while creating a visual tension that embodies the struggle between containment and overflow.
