Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional interplay of softness and severity, expressed through a palette that transitions from warm whites and powdery pinks to deep, sharp crimson and carbon black. The layout emphasizes a diagonal conflict where the gentle broken frames of softness collide subtly with the harsh radial patterns representing severity. The darker colors anchor the bottom part of the square, conveying a sense of weight, while lighter hues illuminate the upper section, suggesting an open gate towards forgiveness amidst the severity of guilt. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions that symbolize the imperfections of human emotion, enforcing an impression of echoing contrast without literal representation.
