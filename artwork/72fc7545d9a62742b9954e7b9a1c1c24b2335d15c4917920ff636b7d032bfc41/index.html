Artist Statement

The composition embodies the emotional conflict of defiance versus submission through the juxtaposition of hard interruptions and soft collapses. The palette, drawn from 'gratitude_envy', features vibrant greens and muted tones to illustrate the tension between these emotions. The bright 'acid_green' and 'honey_gold' disrupt the muted fields of 'deep_olive' and 'cream', creating a sense of collision at the center, symbolizing the struggle. The arrangement suggests a spiral motion inward, representing the inward reflection of these emotions, while areas of negative space emphasize the tension and invite contemplation.
