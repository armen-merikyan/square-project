Artist Statement

This artwork contrasts the emotional pull of desire through saturated colors against the muted tones of restraint, suggesting a tension of unfulfilled yearning. The palette features vibrant warm hues to represent desire, while cool muted tones embody the feeling of restraint. The arrangement emphasizes a central collision, where desire's vibrant pulse marks meet the noise of restraint's edges, creating an impactful visual narrative. The spiral flow mimics the internal struggle between achieving closeness and being pushed back, encapsulated in the pixel arrangement.
