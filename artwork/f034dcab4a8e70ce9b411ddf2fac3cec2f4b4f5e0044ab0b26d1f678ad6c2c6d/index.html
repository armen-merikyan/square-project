Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between joy and grief through a juxtaposition of vibrant and muted colors. The light yellows and oranges symbolize joy while deep blues and muted purples echo grief. The layout features a central area of warmth, expanding outward in a grid-like pattern that is disrupted by broken pixels, suggesting the fragility of happiness amidst sorrow. The top-weighted edge creates a feeling of pressure, while the disorder introduces a dynamic collision that mirrors the emotional struggle.
