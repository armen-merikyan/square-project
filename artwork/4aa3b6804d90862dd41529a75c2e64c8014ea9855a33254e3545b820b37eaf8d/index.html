Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic interplay between fear and hope, using a color palette that transitions from the darkness of black and dark violet to the warmth of soft gold and pale green. The burst of color represents hope's emergence against fear's shadows, highlighted by thin outlines that create paths of movement without confinement. The split vertical layout emphasizes asymmetry and tension, where dark edges offer a sense of enclosure, while glowing center pixels suggest a flickering light of resilience. The pattern evokes conflict and balance, manifesting through jagged textures that address fear, contrasting sharply with warmer, framed areas of soft gold, suggesting a beacon amidst turmoil.
