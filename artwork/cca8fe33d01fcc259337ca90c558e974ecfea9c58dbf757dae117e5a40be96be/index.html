Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between fear and hope through a mix of dark and light colors. The palette combines blacks and dark violets to represent fear, contrasted with soft gold and pale green symbolizing hope. The pattern flows from the edges inward, embodying a sense of rising signal amid jagged interruptions. The arrangement utilizes broken pixels to evoke imperfection and tension, creating a dialogue between sharp contrasts and soft cadences, with the center left relatively empty, enhancing the feeling of unease and possibility.
