Artist Statement

The emotional tone here reflects the tension between longing and resolve, portrayed through the juxtaposition of deep teal and cosmic indigo against stark contrasts of cold gold. The arrangement creates a visual spiral pressure, suggesting a movement toward an off-center focal point where longing is dense in the lower left, symbolizing yearning, while resolve intensifies in the upper right through a checker pattern. The broken pixels intentionally disrupt the flow, embodying the conflicts of intimacy and distance. The layout creates a visual echo, a reminder of the unresolved state, swirling in the space of memory and desire.
