Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional dichotomy between confusion and clarity. The top half uses broken patterns in shades of gray to evoke confusion, representing disorder with uneven pixel placements illustrating fragmentation. The lower half transitions to warmer tones of pink and red in a structured 'stairs' pattern, suggesting clarity and focus. The color palette reflects a spectrum from warm to cool, enhancing the contrast between calmness and chaotic energy. Strings of broken pixels provide imperfection, reinforcing themes of sudden interruptions amidst a structured flow.
