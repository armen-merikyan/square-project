Artist Statement

The artwork embodies a tension between vulnerability and strength, represented by strong colors colliding with soft hues. The palette of moss green and rust evokes resilience against fragility, while the bone white introduces light and exposure. The central collision of deeper tones within the negative space serves to emphasize resilience, as stronger colors forge paths through vulnerable backgrounds. By using a mix of defined borders and softer edges, the piece reflects the dynamics of protection and exposure, creating a narrative of emotional interplay.
