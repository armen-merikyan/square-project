Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the struggle between safety and exposure. The left half, using darker tones like #6F8F7A (shelter green) and #A0522D (warm brown), conveys a sense of protection and weight, while the right half features lighter hues like #FDEFD3 (pale skin) and #DFE6E8 (cold white), suggesting vulnerability and openness. The center forms a gentle wave of contrast, illustrating the emotional tug-of-war between these states. Broken pixels strategically placed symbolize imperfections and emotional scars, enhancing the depth of the narrative.
