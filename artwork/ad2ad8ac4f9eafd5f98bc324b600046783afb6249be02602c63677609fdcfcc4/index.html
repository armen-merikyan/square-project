Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between nostalgia and progress through a split composition. The palette, a combination of muted dark tones and soft highlights, reflects a struggle between the past's weight and the brightness of potential. The top half features a cracked pattern symbolizing nostalgia's irregularities, while the bottom half employs a dotted layout to embody pushing forward into progress. The contrast is emphasized by the horizontal split and a bottom-heavy distribution, invoking a sense of gravity and movement.
