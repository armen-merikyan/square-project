Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between trust and suspicion, with a deliberate split down the center representing their dynamic relationship. The warm coral and soft amber hues signify trust, while the cold slate and blue-black offer a sense of suspicion and darkness. The main contrast lies in the center where the warm colors collide with the darker tones, creating a ripple effect that speaks to the waves of emotion in communication. The palette draws from the concepts of connection and alienation, with broken pixels introducing a sense of imperfection and chaos within the artwork, reflective of reality’s noisy signals and silent whispers.
