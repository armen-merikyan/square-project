Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a spectrum between longing and resolve, depicted through a contrasting palette that balances muted and vibrant colors. The chosen colors (off_white, dust_gray, compressed_red, dark_navy, faint_peach) represent a journey from calm serenity to pressing urgency, interplaying 'quiet pressure' against stark interruptions. The main collision occurs in the center where hues clash, symbolizing the tension between hope and despair, while the overall split layout emphasizes the dichotomy of the emotions. This arrangement captures the feeling of a ‘tide’ in the top rows, flowing into a more ‘static’ bottom, mirroring the pattern of longing versus resolution.
