Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts loneliness with connection, using a palette of deep reds, grays, and pinks to convey tension and warmth. The pattern adopts a crosshatch to represent connection sparking through separation. The top-weighted arrangement emphasizes the contrast with heavy colors in the corners spilling into lighter tones, creating a dynamic interplay that feels like hidden emotions being exposed.
