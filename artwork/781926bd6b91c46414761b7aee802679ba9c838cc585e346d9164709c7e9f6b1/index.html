Artist Statement

The composition explores the emotional contrast between awe and terror by using a palette that balances deep, dark hues with vibrant, softer accents. The black and dark violet create a sense of overwhelming weight and chaos, while the soft gold and pale green provide glimmers of hope and wonder, introducing a dynamic interplay. The layout favors negative space, emphasizing the conflict as the light shapes disrupt the dense arrangements of dark tones. This tension mirrors the seed's concepts of stability versus instability, fragmented memories, and the duality of existence through the broken pixel placements throughout the piece.
