Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic tension between defiance and submission, illustrated through the use of deep blues and mourning black as a solid foundation. The contrasting pale yellow marks signify submission, gently interrupting the darker colors. The diagonal rise captures movement and struggle, while the uneven border creates an impression of chaos against the calm center. Broken pixels add a layer of imperfection, emphasizing the narrative of disruption and resilience.
