Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a dynamic interplay of longing and resolve. The pastel palette of rose clay and distant lilac conveys intimacy and softness, while accents of warm shadow and lamp gold interrupt with urgency. The main contrast lies in the border between the stacked vertical patterns on the left (symbolizing longing) and the glitch-like structures on the right (representing resolve), showcasing a collision of emotions. This is reminiscent of threads weaving together yet displaying imperfections and breaks, akin to the complexities of human emotions.
