Artist Statement

This composition captures the duality of vulnerability and strength through a dense base of soft colors that envelop a central, brighter cluster representing resilience. The palette of pale aqua and mist gray promotes a serene yet urgent atmosphere, while sharp contrasts from the vivid alarm red and ink blue create tension and disruption. The strong, grid-like structure in the center embodies strength, pushing against the fluid, flood-like edges that signify vulnerability. The collision is palpable in the chaotic arrangement of the brighter colors intertwined with softer tones, embodying the struggle between protection and exposure.
