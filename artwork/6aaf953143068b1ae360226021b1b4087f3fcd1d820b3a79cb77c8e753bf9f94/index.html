Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between relief and dread, evoking a tension that mirrors the struggle between order and chaos. The palette features soft sky blue and faded gold representing relief clashing with storm gray and near black embodying dread, creating an interplay of heaviness and lightness. The collision occurs in the center, where saturated dark colors disrupt lighter spaces, establishing a visual conflict consistent with the spiral and broken line ideas from the seed. The broken pixels are interspersed to enhance the sense of imperfection and tension.
