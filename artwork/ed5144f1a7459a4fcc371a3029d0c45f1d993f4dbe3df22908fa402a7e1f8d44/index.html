Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects an interplay between vulnerability and strength, symbolized by a muted palette contrasting with brighter accents. The central area showcases a concentrated 'strength' motif using deeper blues and subtle purples framed by the warm yellow and orange 'vulnerability' accents, creating a visual pulse. The collision occurs primarily at the center with gentle interruptions along the edges, emphasizing the tension between solidity and fragility. The seed guided the use of texture and layering, portraying complex emotions rather than literal representation.
