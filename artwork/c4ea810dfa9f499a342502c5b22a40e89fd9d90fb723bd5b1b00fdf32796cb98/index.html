Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a complex interplay between quiet and pressure. The chosen palette includes muted off-whites and greys that create a sense of spaciousness, while the deeper reds and navy add intensity and weight. The main collision occurs where the dark navy meets the faint peach, representing the struggle between rigid structure and fluid chaos. The pattern shifts from tight, concentric rings symbolizing pressure on the left, to broken fault lines on the right, echoing the shifting dynamics indicated by the seed text. The overall composition builds a narrative of tension gradually dissipating into calm.
