Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between emptiness and desire through the clashes of bold and muted colors, creating a visual rhythm that spirals around the center. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green represents a balance between dark voids and hopeful highlights. The main contrast occurs between the soft gold and dark violet, suggesting a collision of intensity and calmness. This piece reflects the quiet pressure and release inherent in emotional experiences, making each pixel a part of the narrative without forming recognizable themes.
