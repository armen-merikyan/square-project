Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between control and surrender, reflecting an interplay of dense, warm colors against muted and cool hues. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue suggests a memory-like quality, where warm colors symbolize pressure and control, while cooler tones imply freedom and a soft yielding. The primary collision occurs at the center, creating an overflow effect surrounded by a muted frame, embodying a visual tension between contained pressure and moments of release. This arrangement emphasizes the struggle between confinement and abundance, leveraging asymmetry and broken pixels for added depth and complexity.
