Artist Statement

This work embodies the emotional interplay between emptiness and desire, captured through a palette of warm red tones contrasting with cool blues and blacks. The center glows warmly, representing desire, while the edges soften into a cool emptiness, creating a dynamic tension. The composition follows a spiral counterclockwise flow, suggesting a rise and fall. The intentional placement of darker colors along the edges provides a sense of confinement, while the vibrant center suggests a beacon of hope amidst isolation, reflecting the chaos and longing hinted at in the seed.
