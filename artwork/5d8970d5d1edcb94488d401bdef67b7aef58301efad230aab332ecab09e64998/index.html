Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional contrast between isolation and the desire for connection. The warm tones of burnt orange and dark red convey the intensity of ancient fire and deep feelings of loneliness. The central elements formed by lighter gold hues represent connection, while darker colors disrupt this flow, creating tension. The grid layout signifies a battle of patterns—split screens symbolize loneliness clashing against expansive, cracked surfaces that depict connection. The broken pixels create a sense of imperfection and vulnerability, enhancing the emotional depth.
