Artist Statement

This composition balances the emotional tones of longing and resolve through contrasting color placements and patterns. The muted navy and black represent the depth of longing while the bright white and dim cyan create a pulsing sense of resolve. The broken pixels disrupt the uniformity, hinting at imperfections and the complexity of emotions. The layout emphasizes a rising signal, creating a visual ascent that conveys hope amidst the tension. The central collision showcases the interplay of yearning and determination, capturing the essence of the seed.
