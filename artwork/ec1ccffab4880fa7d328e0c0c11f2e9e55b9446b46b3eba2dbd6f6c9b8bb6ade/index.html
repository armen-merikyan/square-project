Artist Statement

This artwork embodies a tension between longing and resolve, expressed through the interplay of a static pattern representing yearning, contrasted with a crosshatch denoting strength and resolution. The palette of cosmic indigo and cold gold reflects the emotional depth and contrast. The negative space offers a quiet channel through the dense pattern, symbolizing the distance between intimacy and separation, rendered through broken pixels that suggest moments of imperfection and disruption.
