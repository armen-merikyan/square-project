Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts longing and resolve, achieving a dynamic interplay. The selected palette of warm coral and cold slate evokes feelings of intimacy and distance. Dots (for longing) collide with a checker pattern (for resolve) creating tension and interruption, while a quiet space is established through the use of ample negative space, emphasizing the struggle between dense and empty. The most vivid densities are concentrated towards the center-left, suggesting a point of unresolved tension.
