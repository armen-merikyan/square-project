Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the duality of longing and resolve, represented through a contrast of colors. The palette blends soft pastel tones reminiscent of hope ('pale_mint', 'soft_sky') against the deep intensity of uncertainty ('storm_gray', 'near_black'). The layout's split vertical design allows longing to dominate the left section (55% coverage) with glitch patterns, while resolve forms the right with veiled textures. The arrangement implies tension via a central collision where colors overlap and interact, using broken pixels to suggest the fractured nature of emotion while maintaining intentional negative space for quiet reflection. This structure embodies a journey between yearning and certainty, symbolized through the metaphor of a ladder reaching towards clarity against the chaos below.
