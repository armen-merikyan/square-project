Artist Statement

The composition juxtaposes the emotional turmoil of confusion against the serene clarity, utilizing a palette of dark violet, soft gold, pale green, and black. The outer layer embraces chaos with irregular patterns that evoke a flame-like confusion, while the inner core maintains a structured frame for clarity. The dense black highlights the broken pixels that create intentional 'imperfections', embodying the struggle between symmetry and disruption. This arrangement emphasizes a pulsing rhythm, represented by contrasting textures that draw the viewer’s eye across the layout. The chaotic outer layer creates tension and noise, while the core remains a quiet anchor within the vivid emotional landscape.
