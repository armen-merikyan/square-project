Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the clash between light and dark, representing vitality (spark) versus desolation (ash). The palette, inspired by ancient fire, employs burnt orange and dark red to embody the unpredictable nature of energy and decay. The primary contrast occurs at the center with disruptive jagged patterns clashing against the quiet fields, illustrating the core tension of resilience facing erasure. The layout’s colliding currents symbolize the inner conflict of desire versus restraint.
