Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension of 'spark vs ash,' showcasing a dynamic clash between vibrant energy and muted remnants. The muted palette of off-white, dust gray, and faint peach sets a foundation of quiet pressure, while the bold use of compressed red and dark navy introduces intense bursts, representing sparks of vitality amidst a backdrop of stillness. The dominant presence of grayish tones achieves a juxtaposition of memory and erasure, creating a poignant visual narrative. The main collision occurs at the top left, where warm red bursts disrupt the calmer fields, symbolizing a moment of ignition amidst the muted memories, echoed by the jagged edges that suggest struggles and interruptions in flow.
