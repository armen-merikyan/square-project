Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dichotomy of quiet against pressure. The palette of off_white, dust_gray, compressed_red, dark_navy, and faint_peach captures the tension between soft tranquility and the weight of pressure. The loose, scattered layout with more generative pressure (darker tones) at the top left suggests an accumulation that gradually dissipates towards the right, invoking a sense of conflict and resolution. Key contrasts occur at the intersection of darker, dense clusters and soft, muted areas, portraying the tension and unexpected breaks introduced by sharper colors.
