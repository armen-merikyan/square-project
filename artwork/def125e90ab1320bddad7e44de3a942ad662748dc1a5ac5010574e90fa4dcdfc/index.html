Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The chaotic outer borders in burnt orange and dark red represent confusion, using a ring pattern that disrupts perceived symmetry. The inner core, in shades of gold and charcoal, fosters clarity through a rain-like gradient that brings order. The edges are thick to suggest a strong boundary while remaining imperfect with broken pixels, emphasizing interruption within structure. Overall, the piece reflects a struggle between harmony and disarray.
