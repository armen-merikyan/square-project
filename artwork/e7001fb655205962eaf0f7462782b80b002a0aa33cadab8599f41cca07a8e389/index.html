Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension of loneliness clashing with connection. The palette, rooted in 'love_betrayal', employs shades of pink and gray to echo the warmth of connection and the chill of isolation. The contrast of dark maroon against lighter pinks symbolizes the interplay between lightness and weight, where brighter colors create sharp interruptions against muted backgrounds. This arrangement reflects the seed's themes of fullness versus hollowness through a scattered maze of broken dots and asymmetric placements, emphasizing contrast and emotional collisions throughout the composition, while maintaining a central focus disrupted by cracks of negativity, echoing memories lost in connection.
