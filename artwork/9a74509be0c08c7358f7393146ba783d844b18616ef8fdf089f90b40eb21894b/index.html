Artist Statement

The emotional tone emerges from the dynamic tension between vulnerability (represented by the soft hues and faint contrasts) and strength (embodied in the deep, bold colors). The palette includes cosmic indigo, void black, and star white, suggesting both depth and light amidst chaos. The primary collision occurs in the center, where stronger colors intertwine with lighter ones, echoing a cross-current of emotions. The rising layout signifies hope, with darker colors at the bottom acting as a weight while lighter shades bring an uplifting feel, representing the struggle between pressure and release.
