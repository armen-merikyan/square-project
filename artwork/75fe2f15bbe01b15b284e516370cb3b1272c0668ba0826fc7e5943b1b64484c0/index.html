Artist Statement

The emotional tone derived from the seed suggests a duality of pressure and release. The palette, 'ancient_fire,' embodies an intense emotional spectrum with burnt orange and dark red representing pressure and shame, while gold evokes a sense of pride. The layout is split vertically, creating a stark contrast between the two emotional states. The diagonal flow complements the tension, as patterns of pride clash against those of shame. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, signifying unresolved feelings amidst supposed symmetry.
