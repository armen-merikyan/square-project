Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The chosen palette features soft hues of rose clay and distant lilac paired with striking warm shadow and lamp gold accents. The contrast between muted backgrounds and sharp, warm edges creates rhythmic pulsations, echoing the 'pulse' aspect of the seed. The central collision occurs subtly off-center, with a cluster of broken pixels providing a sense of imperfection and disruption, embodying the repair vs damage theme. Overall, the piece conveys a narrative of both serenity and urgency, portraying how emotions evolve through visual contrast.
