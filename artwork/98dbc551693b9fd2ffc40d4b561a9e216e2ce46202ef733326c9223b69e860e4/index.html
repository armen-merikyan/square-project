Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The color palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold evokes warmth and intensity, reflecting the underlying chaos of the seed's themes. The sharp contrast between dark and vibrant shades represents the intersection of clarity amidst confusion, while the edge style of thin outlines contributes to a sense of fragmentation. Broken pixels are intentionally scattered to enhance the feeling of distorted balance. The layout creates a symmetry where one side feels orderly and the other chaotic, reflecting the thematic tension of the seed.
