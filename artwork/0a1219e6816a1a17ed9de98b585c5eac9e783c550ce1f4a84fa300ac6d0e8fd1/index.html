Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between dread and relief, utilizing a palette that blends dark storm gray with lighter hues of pale mint and soft sky. The collision occurs in the lower left corner where a dense cluster of dark colors gives way to light warms, embodying the push-and-pull between heavy pressure and soft release. The broken pixels add textural imperfection, hinting at disruptions and echoes of both feelings, translating the seed concept into an abstract representation of emotional dynamics.
