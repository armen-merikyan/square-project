Artist Statement

This composition plays on the emotional tensions between restlessness and peace, achieved through the juxtaposition of bold, warm tones against cooler, muted shades. The palette of soft gold and deep wine introduces warmth, while ash gray serves as a grounding element. The lively pressure_band pattern interrupts the quieter thread pattern, creating a dynamic center where the emotions collide. The arrangement evokes a sense of motion and tension centrally, symbolizing the chaotic memories against the stillness of peace.
