Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between clarity and confusion. The palette features deep maroons and grays to evoke a sense of intensity and emotional weight, while the integration of pink hints at an underlying softness. The contrasting patterns of solid fields against jagged breaks create a push-and-pull dynamic, symbolizing the struggle between order and chaos. The scattered placement of colors reflects falling pixels, illustrating a chaotic unraveling that parallels the search for clarity amidst the noise.
