Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension of longing against the grounded nature of resolve, visualized through deep contrast and fragmented patterns. The palette contains cosmic indigo and deep teal for a sense of depth and uncertainty, while star white serves as a stark yet calming accent, highlighting areas of intersection and collision. The grid is structured with a notable central vortex of activity, where darker patterns of longing collide with lighter, resolved spaces, creating an echo of conflict and serenity, reminiscent of the feeling of a fragmented mirror. The usage of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, evoking the sensation of damage and repair amid the overall composition, where emotion creates and alters the form.
