Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between love and betrayal, represented through warm pinks and reds juxtaposed with darker maroons and grays. The palette conveys intimacy yet evokes a sense of impending fracture. The layout divides the square into halves: warm and expressive on the left, chaotic and dense on the right. The broken dots pattern represents love, while the target pattern evokes feelings of betrayal and anxiety. This careful positioning and contrast leads to an unresolved visual conflict, mirroring the emotional struggle intrinsic to the seed's themes.
