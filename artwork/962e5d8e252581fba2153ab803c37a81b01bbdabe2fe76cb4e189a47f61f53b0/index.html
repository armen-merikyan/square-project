Artist Statement

This abstract artwork explores the tension between restlessness and peace through its color palette and pattern. The use of muted sepia and tan combined with accents of faded blue creates a dichotomy: the warm tones suggest a grounded stillness, while the cooler color introduces a sense of motion and uncertainty. The center features a clash of darker hues that represent restlessness, while the lighter edges form a protective boundary, evoking peace. The cracks in the pattern echo memories of shattering moments, with intentional broken pixels enhancing the theme of disruption. The rising layout symbolizes upward hope amidst chaotic feelings, allowing viewers to engage with the emotional narrative of striving for calm amidst turmoil.
