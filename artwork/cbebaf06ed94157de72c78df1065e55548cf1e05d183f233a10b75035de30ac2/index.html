Artist Statement

This piece conveys the emotional struggle between defiance and submission through a tension-filled arrangement where bold colors confront muted tones. The palette, imbued with cosmic indigo and void black, creates an awe-inspiring yet terrorizing atmosphere. The main collision occurs in the central row, where deep teal and cold gold disrupt the surrounding quiet fields, echoing the themes of resistance and submission. The broken frame pattern suggests fractures in the confrontation, further emphasizing interruption and unresolved motion. Each pixel placement creates a rhythm reflective of an inward spiral, symbolizing a conflict within while retaining a spacious echo around it.
