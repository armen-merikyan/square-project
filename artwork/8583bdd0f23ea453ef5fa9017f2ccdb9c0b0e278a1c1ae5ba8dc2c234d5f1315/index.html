Artist Statement

The composition explores the tension between softness and severity, captured through a palette of powder pink, warm white, and sharp crimson against a backdrop of carbon black and steel gray. The palette reflects a gentle dichotomy—soft colors representing tenderness and warmth, while darker shades signify rigidity and depth. Main contrast occurs centrally, where the stacked and radial patterns collide; the softness radiates upwards while severity looms downwards, creating a visual pulse that embodies the flowing tension of the emotional seed. Broken pixels disrupt the breeze of softness with sharp interruptions indicating moments of guilt and forgiveness, enhancing the dynamic between light and dark without creating literal interpretations.
