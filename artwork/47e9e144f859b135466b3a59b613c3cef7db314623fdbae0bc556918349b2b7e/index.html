Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the tension between fleeting brightness (spark) and settled silence (ash), using a contrasting palette of muted grays and warm golds to suggest warmth meeting cold, memory against erasure. The primary collision occurs diagonally across the grid where intense waves of soft gold ripple through the darker, ashy braids of deep violet, creating a fragmented but dynamic space. The intentional broken pixels introduce disruption, reflecting scars of memory amidst the complexities of resilience and fragility.
