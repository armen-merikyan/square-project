Artist Statement

This abstract piece embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a contrasting color palette and asymmetric patterns. The combination of deep teal and cosmic indigo creates a dark, rich atmosphere disrupted by bursts of cold gold. The intersection of warm and cool colors suggests conflict, while the uneven border and broken pixels allow for visual interruptions that evoke both resistance and vulnerability. The primary contrast occurs in the center, where the density of colors converges, illustrating the collision of opposing forces derived from the seed.
