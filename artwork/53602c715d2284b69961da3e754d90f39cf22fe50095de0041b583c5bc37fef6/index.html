Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the dense presence of pressure against the stillness of quiet. The chosen palette of muted tones (off_white, dust_gray, faint_peach) contrasts with the deeper compressed_red and dark_navy to evoke a sense of conflict and resolution. The collision occurs predominantly in the central rows, where sharper edges of intensity break against softer, more expansive areas. The layout harnesses stacked bands to visually represent emotional strata, shifting from dense clusters to quieter, negative spaces, reflecting the seed's narrative of tension and release.
