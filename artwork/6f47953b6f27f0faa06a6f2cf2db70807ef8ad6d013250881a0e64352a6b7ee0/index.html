Artist Statement

This piece captures the dichotomy of longing and resolve. The palette, drawn from 'ancient_fire', utilizes burnt orange and dark red to evoke urgency and depth, while charcoal provides a quiet backdrop, symbolizing the unseen void of longing. The collision occurs in the center where the bright burnt orange threads intersect with darker shattered pixels, representing the tension between holding on and letting go. The distribution reflects a significant presence of longing with scattered bursts of resolve, mirroring the complex interplay of emotions inherent in the seed's themes.
