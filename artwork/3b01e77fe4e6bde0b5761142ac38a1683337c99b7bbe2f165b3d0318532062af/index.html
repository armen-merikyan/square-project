Artist Statement

The artwork explores the duality of confusion and clarity, with dark hues juxtaposed against vibrant whites to symbolize the struggle between clarity and chaos. The palette, drawn from a spectrum of red, black, pale blue, and white, evokes feelings of rage and calm. The central tension arises from clusters of broken dots against thick outlines, creating a visual narrative of disruption and resolution. The layout suggests a spacious void filled with uneven pixels, emphasizing a feeling of imbalance while the flow moves outward, representing the expansion from confusion to potential clarity.
