Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional conflict between desire and restraint through contrasting palettes of saturated warm tones against muted cool shades. The left half embodies a passionate warmth, swirling with brightness, while the right half, with its dots, suggests contained restraint. The main collision occurs at the center, where these contrasting patterns intersect, creating a dynamic visual tension that resonates with the inherent struggle of the seed concept.
