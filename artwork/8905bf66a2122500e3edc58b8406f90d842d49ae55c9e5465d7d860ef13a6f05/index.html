Artist Statement

The composition explores the duality of control and surrender through contrasting color placements. The palette, featuring deep teal and cosmic indigo, evokes a sense of depth and mystery, creating an emotional tug-of-war. The intersection of dense clusters in the center reflects pressure versus the quiet edges representing release. By utilizing the patterns of 'shatter' and 'noise', the artwork hints at the tension of an overfilled center, skillfully disrupted by the uneven border, echoing the theme of control breaking through a release, while the subtle use of broken pixels enhances the overall emotional complexity.
