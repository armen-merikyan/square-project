Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the juxtaposition of joy and grief, resulting in an interplay of bright and muted colors. The palette of warm yellows and oranges (representing joy) contrasted with deep blues and muted purples (symbolizing grief) creates a visual tension. The main contrast occurs within the dense cluster at the center, disrupted by the sharp outlines hinting at edges of chaos. The choice of the 'checker' pattern for joy and 'crack' for grief encapsulates the underlying emotional turmoil while leaving necessary negative space to avoid overwhelming the viewer and maintain an abstract narrative.
