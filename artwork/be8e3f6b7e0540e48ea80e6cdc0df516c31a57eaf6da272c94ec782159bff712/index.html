Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a constant push and pull between strength and vulnerability. The selected palette evolves from deep blues symbolizing stability and strength to more ethereal and soft hues, representing vulnerability and exposure. The contrast between dense blue blocks on the left and lighter hues on the right illustrates a powerful collision at the center, embodying tension and harmony. The interplay between the spark (bright, directional colors) and floods (soft, expansive colors) creates a sense of dynamic rhythm and engagement across the canvas.
