Artist Statement

This square artwork explores the tension between clarity and confusion, utilizing a palette of black, electric blue, magenta, and lime to symbolize emotional contrasts. The layout employs a central thick outline (representing clarity) broken by intersecting lines of color (signifying confusion) that create a dynamic pulse. The arrangement generates collisions, especially where bright magenta interrupts deep black, reinforcing the conflict of emotions. The pattern echoes the complexity of thoughts, creating a rhythm between order and chaos.
