Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between desire and restraint, employing a palette that balances warm, vibrant reds with muted creams and blacks to symbolize the push-and-pull of conflicting feelings. The contrast centers on the left, where saturated colors spill into muted spaces, creating a narrative of overflow against containment. The spiral pressure in placement suggests a dynamic flow, with the outer edges representing restraint surrounding a vivid core, embodying the tension between freedom and limitation.
