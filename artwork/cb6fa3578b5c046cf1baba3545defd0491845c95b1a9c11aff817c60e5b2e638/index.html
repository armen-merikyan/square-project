Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between 'longing' and 'resolve.' The palette features warm reds and pinks expressing yearning, contrasted against cool grays and dark maroon that signify steadiness and resolve. The central focus of tension rises from the collision of grid patterns symbolizing longing with quiet fields suggesting resolve, creating a vibrant core surrounded by more negative space. The broken pixels introduce organic disruptions, reflecting the imperfections of desires clashing with resolution.
