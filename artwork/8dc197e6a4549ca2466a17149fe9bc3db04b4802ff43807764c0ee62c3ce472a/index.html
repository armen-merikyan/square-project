Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between clarity and confusion, echoing the emotional dynamics of release amidst pressure. The selected palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue creates a muted backdrop that suggests memories that are both comforting and melancholic. The interplay of lighter and darker shades introduces a visual rhythm that resonates with emotional collision, where darker patches signify pressure while lighter colors suggest moments of release. The arrangement reflects a diagonal flow, emulating falling pixels, evoking feelings of tears or raindrops, reinforcing the theme of emotional collisions. Such placements create deliberate conflict as more concentrated clusters oppose sparser regions, leaving room for introspection.
