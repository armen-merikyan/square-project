Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the conflict between control and surrender, with a palette evocative of ripe and muted colors. The colors suggest a gentle transition from vibrant to more subdued tones. The main collision occurs at the center, where the brighter greens cut through the richer olive and honey shades, symbolizing a breakthrough against containment. The lattice and target patterns create a dynamic flow that feels like a push and pull, embodying the essence of pressure against release within a regulated space.
