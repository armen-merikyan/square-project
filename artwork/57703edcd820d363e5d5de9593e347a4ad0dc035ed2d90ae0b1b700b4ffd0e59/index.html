Artist Statement

The composition captures the emotional tension between joy and grief through the palette of vibrant yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples. The contrast of the bright 'joy' hues on one side clashes with the darker, 'grief' shades on the other, creating a sense of collision and unresolved tension. The asymmetric arrangement, with a left-weighted emphasis, disrupts symmetry, while the stripes for grief juxtapose the rings for joy, enhancing the idea of conflict. The grid-like structure hints at a pulse, resonating with the seed's themes of love and betrayal.
