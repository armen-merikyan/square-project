Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional interplay between vulnerability and strength, represented by a predominance of darker shades scattered with bright accents. The palette features storm gray and near black serving as the dense base, while faded gold interjects hope and warmth, encapsulating the clash of exposure and strength. The layout, characterized as 'last light', introduces a small cluster of brighter elements in the top-left corner, representing a fragile point of safety amid an overwhelming expanse of charged, darker tones. The broken pixels create intentional disruptions that highlight the fragility embedded within strength, and the radial vulnerability pattern pulses outward, suggesting an emotional release or echo.
