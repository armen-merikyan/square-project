Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic interplay of softness and severity, creating a contrast that invites contemplation. The selected palette incorporates muted tones of powder pink and warm white to evoke a sense of softness, balanced against sharp crimson and carbon black, introducing severity. The arrangement emphasizes a dichotomy, with the pattern of lattice in the top half expressing gentle structure, while the teeth pattern of severity in the bottom half disrupts this harmony, creating a sense of tension. The main collision occurs near the center, where the softer and harsher elements interact, manifesting the complexity of emotions suggested by the seed.
