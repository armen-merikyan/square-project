Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between panic and control, captured by a balance of warm and cool colors from the 'broken_memory' palette. The left-side harmony is disrupted by erratic bright dots symbolizing the chaotic elements of panic, while the calm sepia tones represent control on the right. This contrast provides a dynamic collision, with the zigzag pattern hinting at the underlying struggles. Broken pixels manifest a sense of imperfection, emphasizing the concept of symmetry versus chaos.
