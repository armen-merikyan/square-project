Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a struggle between vulnerability and strength. The selected palette, featuring pale mint and soft sky, suggests a softness that embodies vulnerability, contrasted by storm gray and near black, representing strength’s dense, protective qualities. The major collision happens in the center, where the split screen pattern of strength meets the intertwined braid of vulnerability, asserting a visual tension. The broken frame layout encompasses these emotions, emphasizing the leaks of vulnerability at the edges while maintaining a strong core. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional disruption, echoing the fragility of safety as it clashes with exposure.
