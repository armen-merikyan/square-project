Artist Statement

The emotional tone expresses a conflict between fleeting sparks of vitality and the settling ash of restraint. The palette combines shelter green for vitality, warm brown for the weight of memory, and pale skin for vulnerability against a backdrop of cold white that suggests erasure. The main clash of colors occurs at the center, where vibrant accents like shelter green spark against the calming presence of warm browns, illustrating the tension between resilience and fragility. The arrangement flows from the upper-left corner, symbolizing pressure that unfolds across the grid, while jagged edges at the grid's borders provide a broken, imperfect quality, reinforcing the theme of conflict and coexistence.
