Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of rage and calm through jagged edges and contrasting colors. The palette combines deep blacks with sharp reds against soft pale blues and whites, creating a visual tension. The pattern features radial spikes of red radiating from a central pale blue calm, symbolizing turmoil breaking through tranquility. The layout of falling pixels suggests the descent of feelings, with negative space representing moments of introspection, enhancing the collision between chaos and serenity that the seed evokes.
