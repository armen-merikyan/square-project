Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a complex interplay of love and betrayal, achieved through a palette that combines warm pinks and reds with deep maroon and gray to highlight the dichotomy of compassion and pain. The left side resonates with love via a checker pattern, while the right side evokes betrayal through crack formations, creating a sharp contrast. The occasional broken pixels act as a reminder of unresolved emotions, disrupting the structured patterns to symbolize conflict and tension. The layout emphasizes a split vertical design, instilling feelings of division, where the most intense contrasts emerge at the center, reflecting the clash of deep emotions.
