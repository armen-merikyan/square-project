Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between restlessness and peace. The palette of deep navy, black, and a touch of dim cyan represents the depth of memory and motion. The gentle balance of pale fields contrasts with wandering accents of darker colors, creating an unsettling border. The main collision occurs towards the center, where the contrasting patterns of 'fault_line' and 'threshold' interrupt each other, suggesting the fight between calm and chaos. The intentional broken pixels symbolize disruptions in memory, further enhancing the theme of emotional turbulence.
