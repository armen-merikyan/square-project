Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between clarity and chaos, drawing from the seed’s tension between signal (defined patterns) and noise (disruption). The palette uses stark contrasts of deep black and bright magenta to symbolize the persistent pulse against jarring static. The main collision occurs in the center of the grid where the bright colors clash with muted tones, creating a sense of struggle between coherence and interference. The organization reflects a 'split_vertical' layout, fostering a clear yet dynamic contrast in emotional density, suggesting the complex interplay of memory and distraction.
