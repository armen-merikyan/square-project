Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tumultuous yet harmonious interplay of emotions through stark contrasts and rhythmic patterns. The palette features deep, muted tones interspersed with bright accents, reflecting moments of pressure against quiet space. Each row is designed to mirror the cyclical nature of tension and release, where dark colors represent depth and weight, while lighter shades introduce moments of relief and interruption. The 8x8 grid captures the essence of collision in emotional landscapes, with bursts of color acting as echoes of feeling.
