Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a structured layout inspired by an open gate. The chosen palette, ranging from deep charcoal to warm coral, illustrates the struggle between the known (resolve) and the unknown (longing). The collision at the center highlights paths diverging, echoing a pulse of conflict and resolution. By using a broken pixel approach, the artwork embraces imperfections, reflecting the turmoil of mending versus the nature of damage. The interplay of sharp edges and soft tones creates a resonance between serenity and urgency, guiding the viewer into a space that is at once inviting and introspective.
