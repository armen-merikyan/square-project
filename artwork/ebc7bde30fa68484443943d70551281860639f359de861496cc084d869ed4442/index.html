Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the tension between exposure and safety, using a muted palette of warm browns and soft greens to evoke a sense of enclosure against the pale center representing vulnerability. The checker and split-screen patterns collide in the center, creating a dynamic interplay while the broken pixels add intentional disruptions, echoing the struggle between protecting and revealing. The layout draws the eye towards the center, where the contrast of the warm brown against the pale skin color signifies the heart of the emotional narrative: an oasis of safety amidst a vast openness.
