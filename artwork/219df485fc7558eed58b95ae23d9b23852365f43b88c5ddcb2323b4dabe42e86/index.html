Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents a tension between longing and resolve, manifested through contrasting areas of density and emptiness. The chosen palette—ripe green, acid green, honey gold, deep olive, and cream—reflects both warmth and a cool, precise edge. The primary collision occurs in the center, where dense clusters of green reflect longing, while the lighter, more neutral cream and honey gold create spaciousness, symbolizing resolve. This composition speaks to an emotional journey from scattered tension to cohesive steadiness, framed within a refined structure.
