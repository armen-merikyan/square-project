Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional dichotomy between vulnerability and strength, using a palette of rich greens and subtle yellows to symbolize growth and hope against decay and fear. The dense base colors interact with softer tones, creating a central focus that embodies exposure amidst a surrounding hard-edged structure. The pattern incorporates a hinge-like transition, emphasizing the intersection of strength and vulnerability. Through strategic placement of broken pixels, this piece mirrors the scars of experience and the complexity of connection versus alienation, with the main collision occurring at the center, representing the struggle between protection and exposure.
