Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the struggle between clarity and chaos, where a vibrant center pulse wrestles against muted noise. The palette draws from deep navy and subtle cyan to create a lonely yet impactful atmosphere, while the dominant dark colors symbolize noise and interference. The main collision occurs in the center, where lighter claustrophobic elements interrupt the heavy static ring, embodying sparks of clarity in a sea of noise. This reflects the seed's themes of isolation and uncertainty, suggesting an emotional journey through reminders and memories.
