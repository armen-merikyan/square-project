Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of joy and grief, with warm colors representing joy and deep blue hues signifying grief. The palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple establishes a vibrant contrast that showcases love against loss. The layout splits the composition vertically, with bright patterns on the left symbolizing joy, while crosshatched elements on the right embody grief. Interruption occurs as bright pixels occasionally invade the darker, muted space, illustrating the collision of feelings. The overall flow is outside-inward, suggesting the pull of emotions toward a center of complexity. The seed's abstract qualities evoke flames symbolizing passion and intensity; yet, their imperfection through broken pixels introduces a sense of tension within the harmony.
