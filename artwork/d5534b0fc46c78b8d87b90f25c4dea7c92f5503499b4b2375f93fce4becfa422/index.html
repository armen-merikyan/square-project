Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates the tension between trust and suspicion, with the left side representing order through a radial pattern in calming pinks and grays, while the right side breaks into a chaotic grid of deeper reds and dark maroons. The soft glow of light hues meets the hard shadows of darker tones, illustrating the push and pull of these emotional states. Main collision occurs at the center, creating a focal point of contrast that speaks to the conflict in the seed.
