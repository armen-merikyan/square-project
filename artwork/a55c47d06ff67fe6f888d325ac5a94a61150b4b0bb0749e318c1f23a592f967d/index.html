Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional push and pull of desire and emptiness. The palette combines saturated colors like hot coral and blood orange to evoke desire, contrasted with hollow blue and charcoal to suggest emptiness. The main collision occurs in the center where heat and saturation meet low saturation, creating a dynamic tension. The structured yet broken pixel arrangement highlights both containment and overflow, echoing the seed's themes.
