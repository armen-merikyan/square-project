Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a layered structure that shifts from bold, aggressive colors to softer, muted tones. The palette of burnt orange and dark red reflects intensity, while charcoal and gold suggest complexity and drama. The ring pattern signifies defiance, providing a strong central focus, while broken dots represent submission, creating a sense of retreat. The primary collision occurs at the center where vibrant contrasts meet subdued backgrounds, echoing the dynamic between energy and inertia.
