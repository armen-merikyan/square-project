Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between quiet and pressure using a carefully curated palette of muted tones and sharp contrasts. The main colors include off-white and faint peach to represent the quiet, interspersed with deep dark navy and compressed red to invoke pressure. The diagonal arrangement of the colors guides the eye across the piece, suggesting movement and conflict, while the broken pixels introduce imperfection, symbolizing disruption. The collision occurs where dark hues meet lighter ones, representing the struggle between density and openness, embodying the seed's themes of clarity versus confusion.
