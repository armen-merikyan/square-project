Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dance between tenderness and rage through a divided palette of soft blues and deep reds. The pattern introduces a collision of soft checkerboard tender colors flowing left, contrasting sharply with jagged, explosive patches on the right. The layout, with its two pulsating centers, allows for negative space to emphasize the tension. Broken pixels disrupt a otherwise calm rhythm, suggesting the struggle between these opposing feelings.
