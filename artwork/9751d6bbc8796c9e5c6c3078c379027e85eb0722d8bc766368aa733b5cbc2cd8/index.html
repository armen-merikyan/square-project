Artist Statement

This pixel art embodies the emotional tension between love and betrayal using contrasting warm and cool tones. The colors represent warmth and connection (pink, red) clashing with darker, conflicted emotions (dark maroon, gray). The layout mimics symmetry through broken mirroring, creating an interplay between dense and empty spaces. The inclusion of broken pixels adds imperfection, emphasizing the fragility of relationships. The overall pattern suggests a narrative of longing and distortion, merging hope with hurt in an abstract form.
