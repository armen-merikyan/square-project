Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'love vs betrayal' guides the visual conflict in the piece. The warm, vibrant colors of pink (#FF6F61) and soft red (#BF5B5B) evoke feelings of love, while darker shades of gray (#7F7F7F) and dark maroon (#5A2E2E) introduce betrayal and tension, creating significant contrast. The palette juxtaposes warmth against the cool, muted tones to suggest an emotional collision. The layout mimics a mirrored image but disrupts the symmetry with strategically placed 'broken' pixels, reflecting the theme of discord within unity. The pattern arrangement focuses on tension, suggesting a diagonal fall that creates a dynamic flow from left to right, capturing the nuances of rising signals and descending emotions.
