Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork oscillates between isolation and connection. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue reflects memories both warm and cold, suggesting a mingling of comfort and distance. The composition has a strong contrast where the dense center hints at emotional pressure—bursts of color representing connection within a fragmented exterior. This tension mirrors the seed's themes of hidden versus exposed, creating an abstract narrative of vulnerability and strength through its chaotic placement and occasional broken pixels.
