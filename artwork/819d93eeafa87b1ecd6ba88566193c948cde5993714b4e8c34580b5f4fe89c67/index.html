Artist Statement

This piece contrasts restlessness and peace, conveyed through the tension of color choices. The palette of navy and dim cyan suggests depth and emotion while black represents weight and shadow. The main energy collision occurs in the center where the lighter dim cyan disrupts the heavy black, simulating a struggle for balance amid motion. The broken pixel elements provide intentional disruption, mirroring the stuttered essence of restlessness within a framework meant to evoke calmness, echoing the seed's themes.
