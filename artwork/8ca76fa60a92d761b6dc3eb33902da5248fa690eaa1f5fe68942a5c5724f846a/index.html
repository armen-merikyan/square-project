Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between love and betrayal through a division across the layout. The left side utilizes warm shades of pink and red representing love, while the right side contrasts with dark maroon and gray to signify betrayal. The patterns of 'teeth' on the left suggest a gripping affection while 'rain' on the right conveys sadness and loss, creating an echo of emotions. The sharp edges contrast soft hues, reflecting the dissonance between two opposing feelings and the imperfection of broken pixels adds to the narrative of turmoil.
