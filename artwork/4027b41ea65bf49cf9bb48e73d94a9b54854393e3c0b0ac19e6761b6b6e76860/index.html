Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between longing and resolve through contrasting colors and spatial arrangement. The warm coral and soft amber evoke feelings of intimacy and a desire for connection, while the cold slate and blue black introduce moments of distance and isolation. The layout emphasizes two small pulse areas against a negative space, conveying the push and pull of emotion. The broken frame pattern brings an element of fragmentation, illustrating the collision of emotions. The deliberate placement of broken pixels suggests a sense of imperfection, resonating with the theme of unresolved feelings amidst stark clarity.
