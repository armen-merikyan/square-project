Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between longing and resolve. The palette of deep blue and pale yellow illustrates a journey from dense, quiet spaces into bright accents, embodying hope and clarity amidst chaos. The primary collision occurs in the center with dense colors juxtaposed against lighter hues, suggesting the tension between chaos (longing) and order (resolve). The structure reflects the crack metaphor, emphasizing interruption and asymmetry, which mirrors the complexity of the emotions present in the seed.
