Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between loneliness and connection through a palette of soft gold, dark violet, and green against a stark black background, representing emotional states. The outer layer reflects loneliness with a disengaged pattern and significant negative space, while the inner core bursts with clusters that symbolize connection. The arrangement emphasizes a contrast between heavy, dark edges and vibrant inner structures, pulsing diagonally to depict an emotional journey from isolation to interaction. The imperfect placement of colors evokes a sense of broken memory and quiet longing.
