Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances 'restlessness' and 'peace,' using contrasting colors of calm (pale greens, electric blues) against vibrant, darker accents (magenta, black). The outer edges reflect restlessness with a chaotic pattern, while the inner core symbolizes peace with smoother, structured patterns. The collision occurs diagonally, representing an argument between stability and disruption, driven by the interplay of dark and light areas, creating a visual narrative of unresolved motion.
