Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors and broken patterns. The palette features deep blacks and vibrant reds suggesting strength, with pale blues and whites representing vulnerability. The main collision occurs toward the center, simulating an exposed point amid darker, thicker edges that create a protective barrier around it. The falling pixel layout embodies the unpredictability of emotions, while the broken edges reflect the fragility of connection. Each pattern type showcases a unique approach to embodying strength and vulnerability, creating a dynamic interplay that captures the essence of the seed themes.
