Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the struggle between restlessness and peace, represented through a palette balancing warmth and coolness. The colors evoke a quiet field with accents reminiscent of hidden intensity. The main collision occurs in the diagonal arrangement where the restless stripes disrupt the calming ripples, creating a dynamic tension across the piece. The interplay between muted and vibrant colors reflects a memory in motion while edges of color seem to crawl, suggesting a hesitance to break free from the restraint.
