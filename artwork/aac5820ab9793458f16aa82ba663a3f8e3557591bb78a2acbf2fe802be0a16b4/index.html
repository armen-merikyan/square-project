Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional dynamic between safety and exposure through a palette that balances warm browns with cool greens and blues. The dark outer colors create a feeling of safety, framing the lighter center that suggests vulnerability and exposure. The main contrast occurs where the warm brown meets the pale skin, suggesting a collision of emotional states while echoing the vulnerability of the exposed center. The broken frame allows for interruptions, resembling the conflict described in the seed, leading to a visual narrative of tension and release.
