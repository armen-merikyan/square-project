Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors and patterns. The chosen palette features soft, muted tones like 'faint_peach' and 'dust_gray' to represent vulnerability, while deep colors like 'dark_navy' provide a sense of strength. The lattice pattern in the central area establishes a strong core, while the stacked arrangement around it allows for a sense of exposure, creating a dynamic collision at the center, where strength meets vulnerability. Broken pixels enhance this contrast, representing scars and interruptions in the flow.
