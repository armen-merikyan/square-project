Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the conflict between fear and hope, using a palette of dark violet, soft gold, and pale green to contrast the sharpness of jagged edges with a central glow that suggests rising hope amidst surrounding darkness. This is depicted through a dense pattern focusing on the central seed, which symbolizes hope, against a backdrop of chaotic color collisions that represent fear. The arrangement allows for negative space that evokes both emptiness and potential, conjuring the duality of the human experience.
