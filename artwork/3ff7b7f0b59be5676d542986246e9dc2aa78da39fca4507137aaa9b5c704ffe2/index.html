Artist Statement

The piece embodies the emotional tension between love and betrayal through contrasting shades of pink and dark maroon. The left side reflects warmth and intimacy, while the right side expresses dissonance through jagged patterns. The vertical split evokes a sense of internal conflict, enhanced by the sharp contrasts and soft edges, creating a dynamic interplay between opposing emotions.
