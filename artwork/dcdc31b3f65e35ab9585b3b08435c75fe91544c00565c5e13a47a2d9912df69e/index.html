Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tensions of vulnerability and strength, reflected in the palette of warm reds and soft pinks contrasting against dark maroon and gray. The central area, where the colors collide, creates a feeling of intimacy amidst pressure, suggesting a vulnerable core that is simultaneously protected and exposed. The arrangement follows an outward spiral, illustrating a flow from explosion to containment, while random broken pixels disrupt the uniformity, symbolizing the complexity of these emotions.
