Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece encapsulates the tension between 'spark' and 'ash,' representing bursts of energy clashing with subdued remnants. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue creates a nuanced backdrop that symbolizes memories and fragmented thoughts, allowing the vibrant 'spark' contrasts to stand out against the quiet fields of ash. The central collision of the two contrasting patterns, 'constellation' and 'stairs,' occurs diagonally, which reinforces the notion of conflict and resolution. Jagged edges introduce imperfection, suggesting the resilience amid fragility, further enriching the story.
