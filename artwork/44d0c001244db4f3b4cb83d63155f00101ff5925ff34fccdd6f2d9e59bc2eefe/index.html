Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between pride and shame by employing a palette of pinks and dark maroons to denote warmth and intensity juxtaposed against muted grays. The 'falling_pixels' layout suggests a descent and the breaking flow, reminiscent of tears or release, while asymmetrical placements within the pixel grid create a sense of collision and disturbance. The introduction of broken pixels serves to emphasize imperfection, enhancing the overall feeling of vulnerability amid the dense interplays of color.
