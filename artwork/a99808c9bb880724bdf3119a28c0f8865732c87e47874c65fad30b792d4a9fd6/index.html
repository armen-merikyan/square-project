Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional pull between longing and resolve through a dynamic arrangement with warm and cool tones. The burnt orange and dark red juxtapose against charcoal and gold to create a vivid contrast that feels both urgent and serene. The main collision occurs in the corner where saturated colors create pressure, while the spreading marks of the lighter tones reflect an unresolved yearning. This blend of pressure and release echoes the seed's theme of repair versus damage, with broken pixels acting as intentional disruptions to suggest imperfection amidst a quest for wholeness.
