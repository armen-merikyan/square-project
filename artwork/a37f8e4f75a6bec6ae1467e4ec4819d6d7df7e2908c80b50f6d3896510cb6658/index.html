Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tensions of defiance and submission through a stark interplay of color and pattern. The palette of moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold reflects resilience amidst fragility, signifying the struggle between rigidity and fluidity. The main collision occurs toward the center, where intense colors interrupt softer hues, illustrating the battle of emotional forces. The broken pixels represent imperfections and scars that coexist with quiet flows, accentuating the contrasting emotional states inherent in the seed.
