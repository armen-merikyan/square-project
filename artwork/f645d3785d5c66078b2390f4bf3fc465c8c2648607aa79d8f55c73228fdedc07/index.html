Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the complexities of vulnerability and strength, using a palette of muted hues from 'melancholy_wonder' to evoke a sense of both security and openness. The dense outer maze pattern represents strength, with a clear boundary, while intermittent soft pulses in the center embody vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center, where marks flow toward and interrupt each other, creating a dialogue of pressure and release, perfectly paralleling the seed's themes of exposure and emotional safety.
