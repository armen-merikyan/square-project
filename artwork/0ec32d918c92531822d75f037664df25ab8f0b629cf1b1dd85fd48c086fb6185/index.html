Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'quiet vs pressure' is reflected through a dense color accumulation in the top left corner, symbolizing pressure, while a gradual softening into lighter tones suggests quiet release. The selected palette of muted colors with a vivid accent creates a visually striking contrast, where deep navy collides with faint peach, evoking a push against calm. The flow is outward, resembling an emotional escape from tension, and the layout features a central disturbance with uneven edges that imply shattering clarity amidst confusion.
