Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of softness and severity through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette combines muted tones with sharp accents, emphasizing a gentle tension between the warm whites and stark blacks. The central area features a denser arrangement of powder pink and warm white, symbolizing softness. In contrast, the sharp crimson and carbon black punctuate the space, creating a sense of severity. The main collision occurs near the center, where the softer tones are disrupted by darker accents, capturing the essence of conflict. The spiral-like distribution against a linear background echoes the themes of unfolding emotions and resolutions.
