Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between longing and resolve, capturing a dichotomy through contrasting colors and forms. The selected palette, 'intimacy_distance', incorporates warm and cool colors to suggest emotional depth—warm shadows (#6F4C3E) representing longing and cool blue (#3F89A8) embodying resolve. The layout radiates from the center (a threshold), creating patterns that echo the pressure of 'hinge' against the 'radial' flow of longing. Main collisions occur at the center, where vibrant hues punctuate muted backgrounds, signifying the complex interplay of repair and damage, essence and absence, threaded throughout the composition.
