Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between control (structure) and surrender (fluidity). The palette features deep navy and black, representing containment and pressure, contrasted with dim cyan and white symbolizing open edges and release. The broken pixels create intentional disruptions, reinforcing the feeling of an interrupted signal in a controlled environment. The center's density (more black and navy) represents a tightly packed, overfilled control, while surrounding broken dots of white and cyan suggest moments of surrender and escape. This interplay forms a visual narrative capturing the push and pull of hope against the weight of pressure.
