Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between confusion and clarity, with an intentional interplay designed to evoke a sense of tension. The palette blends deep blacks with pale blue and stark white, contrasting dark confusion and light clarity. The main collision occurs at the center, where chaos meets structure in a dense arrangement surrounded by negative space, echoing both claustrophobic pressure and the potential for release. The use of thick outlines and pixelated noise hints at a fractured symmetry, embodying the struggle of broken elements amidst a rhythmic pulse, reflecting the complexities of human emotion.
