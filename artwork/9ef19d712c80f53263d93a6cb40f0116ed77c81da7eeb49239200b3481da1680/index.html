Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional contrast of control and surrender through an 8x8 pixel grid. The palette reflects a struggle against overwhelming impulses, utilizing warm tones like red_oxide and small_orange against the subdued blacks and grays of iron_gray and blackened_plum. The composition showcases a density at the center, surrounded by a soft yield as hints of muted_cream infiltrate the darker tones, mimicking pressure versus release. The arrangement features broken pixels to signify the disruption and inherent imperfection in the struggle, making the piece feel alive and responsive to its underlying themes.
