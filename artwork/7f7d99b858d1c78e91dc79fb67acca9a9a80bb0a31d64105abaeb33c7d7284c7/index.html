Artist Statement

The emotional tone in this composition reflects the push and pull between longing and resolve, manifested through a palette that intertwines dark violet and soft gold. The contrast between dense clusters of dark colors represents the weight of longing, while lighter accents suggest moments of resolve. The spiral layout creates an inward flow, emphasizing how emotions coil around a central point of tension. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, echoing the imperfections found in attempts to mend what is shattered. The interplay of contrasting spaces reinforces the idea of unresolved emotions, where quiet areas allow for reflection amidst bursts of color, creating a visual dialogue between serenity and urgency.
