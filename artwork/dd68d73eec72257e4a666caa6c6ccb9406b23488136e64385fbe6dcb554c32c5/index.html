Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tension between joy and grief. The palette combines vibrant yellows and oranges representing joy against deep blues and muted purples for grief. The design employs a split layout to create symmetry, yet the 'wave' pattern introduces an element of interruption, reflecting the interplay of these contrasting emotions. Overall, the arrangement suggests fluctuating intensity—dense areas collide with empty spaces to embody the push and pull of feeling.
