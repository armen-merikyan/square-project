Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the conflict between awe and terror, represented through warm and cool contrasts in the palette. The sepia and tan colors evoke a sense of nostalgia, while the dust gray and faded blue introduce an unsettling depth. The layout rises vertically, illustrating an inward flow of emotions where broken pixels inhabit order, echoing memories, while the maze-like patterns represent the chaos of fear against the stability of awe. Main collisions occur where soft hues clash, creating tension in the viewer's perception.
