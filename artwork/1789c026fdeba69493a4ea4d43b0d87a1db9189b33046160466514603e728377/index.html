Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional complexity of vulnerability versus strength, conveyed through a deliberate color palette that sharpens the contrast between warm and cool tones. The dominant colors, 'hot coral' and 'charcoal', create a dynamic tension, symbolizing the clash of strength against fragility. The layout suggests a rising signal, with pixels ascending from the bottom, illustrating hope amidst pressure. The use of 'broken dots' introduces imperfection, enhancing the narrative of resilience. The collision occurs in the center with bold, dense patterns disrupting the softer, exposed areas, suggesting layers of emotional struggle.
