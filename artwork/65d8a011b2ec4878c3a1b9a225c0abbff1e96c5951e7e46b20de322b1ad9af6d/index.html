Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the tension between trust and suspicion, represented through a blend of soft and hard tones. The palette of warm shadow and cool blue suggests intimacy and distance, with a dominant warm presence that embodies trust. The main collision occurs with the stark contrast of dark edges symbolizing suspicion, etched against lighter backgrounds, creating a sense of eroded symmetry. Broken pixels disrupt this interplay, representing moments of doubt amidst a cohesive flow of connection, articulated through patchwork and broken dots patterns. Overall, this composition invites viewers to reflect on the dynamic balance between these opposing emotions.
