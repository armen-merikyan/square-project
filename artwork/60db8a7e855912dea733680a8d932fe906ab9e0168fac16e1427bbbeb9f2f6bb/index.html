Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the struggle between defiance and submission, creating a landscape filled with tension. The palette mixes dark violet representing the gravity of submission contrasted with soft gold and pale green that symbolize bursts of defiance and hope. The main collision occurs in the center where upward movements of gold spark against the dark borders, evoking a rising signal of resistance. The broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, echoing the imperfections inherent in navigating such emotional states. The layout reflects a rising flow, as if ascending towards resolution yet leaving space for contemplation.
