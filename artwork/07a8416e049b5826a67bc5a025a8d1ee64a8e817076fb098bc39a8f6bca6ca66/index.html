Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between light and heavy feelings, captured through stark contrast. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon communicates both warmth and tension, creating a field where gratitude (light) and envy (heavy) collide. The layout emphasizes a vertical split, which enhances the visual conflict. The 'target' pattern for envy creates dense clusters of color, while the 'flame' pattern for gratitude provides more space, forming a dynamic rhythm. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, mirroring the complexities of emotion. Negative space ensures breathing room, offering a moment for reflection amidst pressure.
