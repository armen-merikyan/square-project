Artist Statement

This composition embodies the contrast of vulnerability and strength through an interplay of exposed and protected areas. The palette, 'hunger_restraint', utilizes muted creams and deep oxides to evoke a sense of warmth against a backdrop of dependence and weight. The central collision occurs with the checker and flame patterns meeting in the center, suggesting an unstable equilibrium where strength holds sway yet remains threatened by vulnerability. The eroded symmetry reflects the fragility of safety against the tides of exposure. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional disruption, mirroring the imperfection inherent in the emotional landscape.
