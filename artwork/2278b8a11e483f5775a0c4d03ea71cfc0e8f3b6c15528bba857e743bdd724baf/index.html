Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duel between joy and grief through a palette that oscillates between vibrant yellows and muted blues. The arrangement places brighter colors on the periphery, gradually colliding with deeper blues towards the center, embodying the struggle of finding light among shadows. Broken pixels create imperfections, while the flow reflects a zigzag pattern that symbolizes the unpredictable nature of emotion. The negative space allows for contemplative pauses amidst the chaos of color, enhancing the emotional tension.
