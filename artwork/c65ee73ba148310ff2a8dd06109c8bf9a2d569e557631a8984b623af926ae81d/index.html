Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. Using a palette of muted purples, deep blue, vibrant yellow, and orange, it contrasts warmth against coolness, embodying the struggle between understanding and chaos. The thick outlines suggest a boundary between the orderly left half and the broken right half, where five disrupted pixels create feeling of an interrupted flow. This layout builds a pulse of energy through dense clusters on the left and a grid-like disarray on the right, highlighting the collisions between coherence and random distortion.
