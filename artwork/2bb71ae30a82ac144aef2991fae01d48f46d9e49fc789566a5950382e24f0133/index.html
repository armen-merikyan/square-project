Artist Statement

This composition conveys a tension between confusion and clarity. The palette of deep blacks and soft golds creates a 'fear_hope' contrast, with dark colors indicating confusion and gold representing moments of clarity. The arrangement of colors descends like falling pixels, where solid columns of color disrupt with chaotic patches, symbolizing the interplay of emotions. The sharp contrasts help to visualize unresolved tensions, while the negative space allows for breathing room, enhancing the feeling of unease amidst the structured flow.
