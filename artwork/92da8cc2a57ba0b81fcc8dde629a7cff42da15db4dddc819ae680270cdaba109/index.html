Artist Statement

The composition conveys a struggle between restlessness and peace through a juxtaposition of dense clusters and open spaces. The palette, filled with cosmic indigo (representing depth), void black (symbolizing stillness), star white (suggesting clarity), cold gold (for warmth), and deep teal (as a calming base), allows for both tension and tranquility. The main collision occurs in the center, where chaotic scars of color interrupt the calm areas, symbolizing the emotional turmoil of memory against the motion of time. The sparse distribution evokes a sense of unease alongside quietude, highlighting the emotional journey implicit in the seed.
