Artist Statement

The emotional tone of defiance vs submission leads to a palette that juxtaposes bold and muted colors. The chosen yellows and deep blues create a vivid contrast, symbolizing bright refusal against a backdrop of soft collapse, reflecting the tension inherent in the seed. The main collision occurs in the center of the square where bold yellow bursts through muted purple shards, representing the core struggle. The layout reflects an eroded symmetry, enhancing the sense of an ongoing conflict that is both powerful and disordered.
