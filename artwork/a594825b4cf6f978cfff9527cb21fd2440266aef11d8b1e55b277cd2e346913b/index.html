Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between quiet and pressure, utilizing a palette of muted and contrasting tones. The darker colors on one side represent pressure, creating a weighted border that pushes against the softer hues of off-white and faint peach, symbolizing quietude. The collision occurs predominantly in the center, where the density of darker colors meets the lighter palette, illustrating a struggle between introversion and external force. The patterns of 'teeth' and 'scar' evoke rhythmic interruptions, suggesting a wave of emotion surging inward while maintaining a sense of turbulence and calm.
