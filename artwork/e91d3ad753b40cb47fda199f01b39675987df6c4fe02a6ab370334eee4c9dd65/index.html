Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes defiance and submission against a backdrop of density and quiet spaces. The palette, drawn from 'joy_grief', features bright yellows and oranges that clash with muted purples and deep blues, symbolizing vibrancy amidst struggle. The main collision occurs centrally, where shattered patterns of brightness interrupt a grid-like submission, suggesting an intense battle for space. This reflects the seed's themes of conflict and duality, with broken pixels emphasizing imperfection and disruption in this emotional landscape.
