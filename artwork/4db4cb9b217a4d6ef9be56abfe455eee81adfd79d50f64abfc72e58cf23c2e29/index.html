Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between relief and dread through contrasting colors and patterns. The chosen palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon represents warmth juxtaposed against heaviness, illustrating the struggle of light against dark. The top-heavy arrangement conveys pressure, while the broken pixel elements create an interrupted flow, enhancing the sense of discomfort. The checker and stripe patterns invoke feelings of dread and relief, respectively, allowing for a visual melody of oscillating emotions.
