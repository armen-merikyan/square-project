Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between protection and exposure. The palette merges earthy greens and browns (symbolizing safety) with cool whites and blues (representing vulnerability), creating a dynamic tension. The main collision occurs at the center where darker hues of brown meet lighter cool colors, evoking feelings of pressure in the dense center while creating an echo-like effect around the edges. The broken pixels intentionally disrupt the pattern, suggesting the imperfections in both safety and exposure that the seed represents. This arrangement embodies a rhythmic interplay between security and vulnerability, reflecting a journey from pressure to release.
