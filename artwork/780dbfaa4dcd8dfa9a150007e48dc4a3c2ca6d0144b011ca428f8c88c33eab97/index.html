Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension of envy and gratitude through a juxtaposition of warm and cool colors. The jagged edges symbolize the broken mirror, representing fractured perceptions. The upward and downward movements in the pixel placement create a rhythm of rising ambition versus settling appreciation. The palette of deep reds and pale blues accentuates this emotional dichotomy, while the imperfections scattered throughout enhance the idea of an unsteady balance between the two feelings.
