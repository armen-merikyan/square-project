Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the tension between clarity and chaos, embodying the essence of 'signal vs noise.' The selected palette of electric blue and signal green provides a stark contrast to the static gray and dead black, visualizing the central pulse amid surrounding disturbances. Color placement captures the collision of clear, structured paths with erratic glitch patterns. The diagonal layout reinforces the struggle between coherence and interference, allowing the green 'pulse' to dominate while the magenta and black encroach. This evokes themes of memory, shelter, and disruption, effectively translating the seed’s concepts into an abstract narrative.
