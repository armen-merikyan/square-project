Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between loneliness and connection, expressed through a palette that contrasts warm sepia tones with muted cooler shades. The main collision occurs in the left-weighted design, where darker hues gather, suggesting loneliness, while lighter colors push towards the right, representing connection. The use of broken pixels creates an imperfect sense of memory, enhancing the emotional depth and highlighting the struggle between feeling full and hollow. The asymmetrical arrangement captures the essence of longing and the faint echo of togetherness, merging shadow and light.
