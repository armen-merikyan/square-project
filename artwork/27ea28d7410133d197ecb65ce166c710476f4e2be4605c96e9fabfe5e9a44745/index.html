Artist Statement

This piece captures the essence of tension between longing and resolve through the emotional contrasts of muted tones and brighter accents. The palette, inspired by 'broken_memory,' balances sepia with faded blue to suggest memories entwined with urgency. The dominant coral hue acts as an accent against softened background colors, creating a visual representation of a pulse. The central area features a collision of warmer and cooler colors, embodying the push and pull of emotional strains. Broken pixels are scattered, symbolizing the irregularities of human experience, while the layered bands reflect emotional strata, allowing whispers of serenity alongside urgent impressions.
