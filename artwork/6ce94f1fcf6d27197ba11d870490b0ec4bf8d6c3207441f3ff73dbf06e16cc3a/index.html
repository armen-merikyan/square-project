Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between desire and restraint, with saturated colors representing yearning and muted tones embodying containment. The dynamic interplay in the center reflects a collision of warm hues against cold, muted borders, mirroring the struggle between expansion and limitation. The layout features a narrow negative space that signifies emotional release, while clustered pixels create momentum around the edges, evoking pressure and anticipation.
