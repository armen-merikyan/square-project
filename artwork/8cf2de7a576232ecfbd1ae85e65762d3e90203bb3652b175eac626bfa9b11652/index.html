Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a contrast between longing and resolve, with a gentle play between quiet space and dense clusters. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes depth and hope, creating a balance between shadow and illumination. The main collision occurs in the center, where the longing channels push against solid resolute edges. The seed's themes of brokenness and repair are abstractly expressed through disjointed pixels that hint at imperfection, while the spiral layout guides the viewer’s eye inward amidst varying densities, capturing the tension of serenity against urgency.
