Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between desire and emptiness. The palette embodies 'broken memory' using warm and cool tones to signify the push-pull dynamic. The main contrast occurs in the jagged edges of warm colors juxtaposed with cooler, muted shades, creating an echo of emotion that reverberates across the space, symbolizing the collision of hope and resignation. The abstract arrangement of pixels follows a left-to-right flow, embodying the rise against the backdrop of emptiness, with sections of intentional negative space reflecting loneliness but also the potential for desire.
