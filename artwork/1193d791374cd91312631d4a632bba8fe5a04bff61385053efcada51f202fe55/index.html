Artist Statement

This piece embodies the interplay of fear and hope, creating a visual narrative of tension and release. The palette, featuring deep blacks and vibrant soft golds, captures the stark contrast between the unsettling shadows of fear and the shimmering light of hope. The layout ascends like a signal of rising resilience, with jagged edges contrasting against smoother, warmer flows. The inclusion of broken pixels adds imperfection, echoing life's unpredictable nature, while maintaining sufficient negative space to evoke a sense of quiet amidst the chaos.
