Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes rage and calm, using a palette that merges stark black and vibrating reds with soft blues and whites to create tension. The chaotic reds suggest an explosive energy, while the cool blues represent tranquility, leading to a visual echo of anxiety clashing with serenity. The main contrast arises in the center, where jagged edges intrude upon smoother, horizontal patterns, exemplifying the conflict inherent in the seed. Broken pixels create subtle imperfections that evoke the feeling of interruption and disruption, aligning with the themes of a crack against a mirror and the loneliness of a signal in the void.
