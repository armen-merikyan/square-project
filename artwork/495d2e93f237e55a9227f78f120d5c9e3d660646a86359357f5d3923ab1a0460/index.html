Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captures the interplay of longing and resolve, reflected in the contrasting use of color and geometric density. The palette, characterized by off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach, embodies a quiet pressure that oscillates between warmth and firmness. The central collision occurs diagonally, where the chaotic checkerboard representing longing meets the static, dense field of resolve. Broken pixels interrupt the pattern, adding a sense of imperfection and depth, echoing the theme of intimacy and distance. This arrangement ultimately invites the viewer to navigate the emotional space between tension and stability, making the composition resonate with a balance of feelings.
