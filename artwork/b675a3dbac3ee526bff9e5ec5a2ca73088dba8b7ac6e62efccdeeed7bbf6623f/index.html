Artist Statement

This piece embodies a tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of navy, black, single white, and dim cyan reflects solitude and depth, evoking feelings of quiet intensity. The arrangement creates a collision of dense dark areas against lighter spaces, symbolizing the push and pull of opposing emotions. The flow is zigzag, suggesting movement and rhythm, while broken pixels add imperfection. The central focus remains elusive yet compelling, embodying the concept of 'inside vs outside'.
