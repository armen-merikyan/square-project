Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and chaos, using a palette that contrasts muted signals with vibrant noise. Dark tones represent the dense chaos, while the electric blue and hot magenta serve as the bright, attention-grabbing pulses of order amidst the static. The arrangement creates a hollow center that emphasizes the collision of patterns—where the order (electric blue) intersects with the chaotic noise (static gray) in the center, suggesting both conflict and harmony within the square. This juxtaposition encapsulates the seed's essence, illustrating the timeless conflict of signal amidst noise.
