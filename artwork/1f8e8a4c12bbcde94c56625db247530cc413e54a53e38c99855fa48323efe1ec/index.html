Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between nostalgia and progress, depicted through a palette that balances deep navy and black with touches of dim cyan. This creates a feeling of weight and density contrasted with lighter segments, suggesting an ongoing push and pull. The bottom-weighted layout allows for a visual interplay between the full depth of color and more hollow spaces, embodying both remembrance and advancement. The drip and checkerboard patterns lend a rhythm indicative of time's passage, with selected broken pixels enhancing the imperfection of memories.
