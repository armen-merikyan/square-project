Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional interplay between certainty and curiosity through contrasting patterns and colors. The palette of ripe green, acid green, and honey gold evokes warmth and optimism, while the deeper shades of olive and the cream provide grounding elements. The main collision occurs in the center, where structured stripes of certainty meet the exploratory grid of curiosity, creating a tension that resonates with the seed's themes. The upward flow represents hope and escape, while the broken pixels disrupt the overall order, symbolizing chaos and curiosity that challenge the straight paths of certainty.
