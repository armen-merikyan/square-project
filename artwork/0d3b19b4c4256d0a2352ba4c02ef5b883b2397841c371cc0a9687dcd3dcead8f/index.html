Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between feelings of panic and control, as represented by the contrasting colors. The chosen palette of red and pale blue embodies intensity and calmness, creating tension and resolution. The layout features a core surrounded by an outer frame, enhancing the contrast of chaos against order. Broken pixels introduce imperfection to symbolize the jagged nature of panic, while smooth transitions in the center evoke a sense of control, illustrating the struggle between these feelings. The arrangement creates a rhythmic zigzag flow emphasizing emotional collision and symmetry within fragmentation.
