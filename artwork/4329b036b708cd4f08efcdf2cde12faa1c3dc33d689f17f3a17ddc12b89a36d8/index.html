Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between trust and suspicion, represented by contrasting dark and light hues and their interaction. The palette, composed of deep blues and muted yellows, symbolizes the transition from a shadowy environment (suspicion) to a softer light (trust). The central collision occurs with some squares emphasizing a hard shadow against softer glows, while broken pixels create intentional disruptions in an otherwise cohesive flow. The split pattern showcases the balance between chaos and clarity, reflecting the seed's themes.
