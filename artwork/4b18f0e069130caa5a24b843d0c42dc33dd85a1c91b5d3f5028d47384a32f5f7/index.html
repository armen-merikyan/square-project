Artist Statement

This composition conveys the emotional tension between softness and severity through a deliberate color choice: warm pastels teaming with stark blacks and grays. The collision occurs mainly in the center, where a pulse-like rhythm of brighter colors gently interacts with darker tones, creating an echo effect that implies a pull and restraint. The pattern follows a ripple for softness in the left half and a ring structure on the right for severity, visually battling each other yet coexisting in a state of dynamic conflict. Broken pixels add intentional disruption, reflecting the imperfections in balancing these emotions.
