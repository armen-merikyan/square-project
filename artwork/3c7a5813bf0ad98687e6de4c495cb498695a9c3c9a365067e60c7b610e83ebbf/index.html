Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the interplay of defiance and submission through contrasting color placements. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of nostalgia, hinting at memories that carry weight. The outer pixels embody a dense rhythm, suggesting pressure, while the inner square provides a quieter core that releases tension. The central void contrasts sharply with the densely packed edge, highlighting the emotional collision at the heart of the piece. Broken pixels create an imperfect surface, inviting contemplation of the layered emotions.
