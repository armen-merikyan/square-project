Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts a clear, vibrant pulse against a static cacophony, capturing the struggle between clarity and interference. The chosen palette features a vibrant signal green to represent clear communication, contrasted with static gray and dead black to symbolize noise. The collision occurs through a central flow where green channels overlap with bursts of hot magenta, creating tension and interruption. This composition visually narrates the experience of seeking connection amidst chaos, effectively merging the emotional aspects of the seed into an abstract arrangement.
