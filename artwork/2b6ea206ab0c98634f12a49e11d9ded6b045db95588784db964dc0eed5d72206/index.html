Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional contrast of love and betrayal through a balance of warm and cool colors. The palette includes soft pinks to denote love, juxtaposed with dark maroons and grays representing betrayal, leading to a visual tension. The layout features an asymmetrical arrangement, where clusters of color create a spiral flow, suggesting a motion of rising hope interrupted by sharp elements of betrayal. Broken pixels enhance this theme of imperfection and emotional distortion, echoing the conflict between stability and instability.
