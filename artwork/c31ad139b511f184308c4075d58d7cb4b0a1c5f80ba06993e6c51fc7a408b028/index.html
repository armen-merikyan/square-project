Artist Statement

The emotional tone suggests a battle between loneliness and connection. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of nostalgia and quiet reflection. The main contrast occurs between darker and lighter shades, where the dark edges indicate loneliness, framing a lighter central area that symbolizes connection. This interplay creates tension and release, with broken pixels adding to the sense of imperfection and memory, reflecting the complexities of human relationships. The overall layout is designed to encapsulate a small central 'seed' amidst pressures from surrounding colors, depicting a fight for connection.
