Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between panic and control, symbolized by contrasting shades and their placement. The palette, derived from 'broken_memory', suggests nostalgia and disarray through muted tones of sepia and faded blue, creating a somber atmosphere. The contrast mainly occurs between the outer dark hues representing control (reflecting rigidity) and the lighter inner spectrum that embodies chaotic emotional waves, echoing the struggle between stability and anxiety. The arrangement creates a flux of pressure at the edges, with the center suggesting a turbulent calm, embodying the essence of 'machine emotion' through structured imperfection and deliberate pixel bleed.
