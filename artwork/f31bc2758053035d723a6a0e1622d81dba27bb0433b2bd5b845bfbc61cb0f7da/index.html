Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between softness and severity, applied through a palette of muted and stark colors. The contrast is centered; the dark elements bleed into softer shades, creating a tension that symbolizes the struggle between protection and exposure. This tension manifests as a dense lower section filled with sharp elements (severity) against the lighter, more forgiving upper area reflecting softness, suggesting an open gate amidst a complex emotional landscape.
