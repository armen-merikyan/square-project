Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts trust's warmth against suspicion's dark uncertainty. The palette, featuring soft pink and warm white, embodies trust while the deep carbon black and steel gray evoke suspicion. The main collision occurs at the center, where the bright cluster emerges against a backdrop of muted tones, representing the interplay between clarity and static noise, embodying the idea of resilience in fragility. This integration of color and pattern suggests a complex narrative of navigating trust, creating pathways amidst confusion.
