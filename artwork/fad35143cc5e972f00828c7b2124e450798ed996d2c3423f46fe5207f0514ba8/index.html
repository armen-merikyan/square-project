Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dichotomy of fear and hope, with a palette balancing dark hues of black and violet representing fear against soft gold and pale green symbolizing hope. The contrast lies at the center, where the lighter tones break through the darker frames, suggesting a rising signal amidst the heavy corners. The zigzag flow and radial patterns of dark on the edges collide with soft mirrors in the center, creating a dynamic interaction that echoes the struggle between safety and threat. This arrangement expresses a narrative of rising hope punctuated by periods of tension.
