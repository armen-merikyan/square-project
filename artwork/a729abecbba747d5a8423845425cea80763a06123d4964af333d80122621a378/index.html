Artist Statement

The emotional tone of longing and resolve is expressed through a palette that juxtaposes soft, muted tones with moments of brightness, signifying hope amidst depth. The primary colors create a sense of distance and intimate connections, reflecting the tension between order and chaos. The main collision occurs in the center where dense patterns of 'resolve' (crosshatch) integrate with more organic 'longing' (stack), suggesting a powerful yet delicate interplay. The quiet spaces enhance the emotional pull, allowing for both calm and turbulence to coexist.
