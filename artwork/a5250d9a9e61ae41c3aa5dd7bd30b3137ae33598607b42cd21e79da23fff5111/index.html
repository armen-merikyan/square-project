Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic interplay between control and surrender, utilizing a palette of lime, electric blue, black, and magenta. The control aspect is depicted through the structured grid pattern, while the surrender is represented by a chaotic, dissolving negative cross shape. Main contrast occurs at the center where dense, dark colors intermingle with vivid accents, creating an 'overfilled center' sensation. This captures the struggle of containment amidst overwhelming forces. The clear division and flowing motion from left to right add to the tension, reflecting a narrative of stuttering progress and emotional echoes throughout the composition.
