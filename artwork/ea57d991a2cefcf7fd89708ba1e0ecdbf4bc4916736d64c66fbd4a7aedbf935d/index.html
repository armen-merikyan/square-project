Artist Statement

This pixel art conveys emotional tension between 'panic' and 'control' through a juxtaposition of colors and patterns. The palette, including black, electric blue, lime, and magenta, embodies a mechanical emotion, representing the clash of structured control versus chaotic panic. The left side features a stable pattern resembling rain, while the right diverges into a checker pattern, symbolizing fragmentation. The central tension is emphasized by disruptive placements of bright colors against a mostly dark palette, creating a feeling of instability and unpredictability. The imperfect nature of 'broken pixels' enhances the chaos, reinforcing the emotional theme.
