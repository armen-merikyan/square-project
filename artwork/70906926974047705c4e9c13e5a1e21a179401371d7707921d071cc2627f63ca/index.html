Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional interplay of longing and resolve, depicted through a careful blend of contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, labeled 'relief_dread', employs soft mint and sky tones to evoke a sense of calm and yearning, while storm gray and near-black inject moments of turmoil and strength. The primary collision occurs in the upper-left corner, signifying overwhelming intensity that spills into the quieter areas, emphasizing the tension between dense clusters and open space. The checker pattern of longing contrasts with the target pattern of resolve, creating a visual dialogue of conflict and connection, mirroring the meditative back-and-forth of intimacy and distance.
