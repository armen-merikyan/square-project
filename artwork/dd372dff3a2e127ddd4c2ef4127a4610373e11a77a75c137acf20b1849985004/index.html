Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tones of loneliness and connection. The choice of the palette, encompassing burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold, captures the essence of ancient fire—warmth contrasted against a backdrop of shadows. The arrangement features a burst pattern from the center, contrasting the heavy, dark edges with bright core colors, symbolizing connection amidst isolation. The dark colors around the edges create a sense of separation, while the center's warmer tones suggest warmth and intimacy, embodying the struggle and desire for connection in a vast emptiness. The sporadic placement of broken pixels emphasizes imperfection and fragility in human relationships, aligning with the themes of hidden vs. exposed. Overall, the piece visualizes the interplay of emotion through structured chaos, where the isolation (dark colors) contrasts sharply with zones of collective warmth (lighter colors) that ignite memories of connection.
