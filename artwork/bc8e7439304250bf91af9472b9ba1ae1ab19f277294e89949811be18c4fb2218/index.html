Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through the juxtaposition of structured and chaotic elements. The palette, drawn from 'broken_memory', features muted tones that evoke a sense of nostalgia and introspection. The outer layer conveys confusion with a spark pattern, while the central space introduces clarity through dot patterns, creating a balance of pressure and release. The contrast lies at the edges, where thick outlines define a sense of boundaries disrupted by 'broken pixels', instigating a visual sense of disturbance and unresolved motion, mirroring the struggle between chaos and order.
