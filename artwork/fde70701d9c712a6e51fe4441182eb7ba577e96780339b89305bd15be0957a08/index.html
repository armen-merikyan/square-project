Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a conflict between restlessness and peace, reflected in the use of contrasting colors. The palette features deep blacks and vibrant accents to illustrate the tension, while gray and green patches symbolize tranquility. The central collision occurs diagonally, embodying the friction between static and fluid movement. This arrangement evokes a rich narrative of unease and calm through the interplay of colors and patterns.
