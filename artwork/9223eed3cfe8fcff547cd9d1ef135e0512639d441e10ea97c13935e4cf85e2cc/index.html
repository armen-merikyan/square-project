Artist Statement

This composition embodies a tension between trust and suspicion through a stark contrast of colors and patterns. The palette, highlighting signal green and electric blue against the static gray and dead black, amplifies emotional undercurrents. The grid rhythm evokes uneven reflections of suspicion with ‘ladder’ patterns clashing against ‘ripple’ forms of trust. The falling layout, reminiscent of descending pixels, serves to symbolize the weight of these opposing emotions. The central focus captures an interplay of hard and soft edges, revealing that within trust lies fragility. A delicate balance exists in the composition, with more space given to trust while suspicion disrupts its calm.
