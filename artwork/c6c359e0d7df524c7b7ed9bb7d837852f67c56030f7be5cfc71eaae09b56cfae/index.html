Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between trust and suspicion, manifested through a palette that juxtaposes deep blues and soft yellows. The contrast primarily occurs at the center of the grid, where deeper hues clash with occasional bursts of pale yellow, symbolizing moments of clarity amidst uncertainty. The layout embodies scattered repairs as bright patches interrupt the darker fields, representing resilience against the backdrop of fragility. The presence of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, echoing the duality of stability and unrest brought forth by the seed concept.
