Artist Statement

This composition embodies the duality of vulnerability and strength. The palette of vibrant colors like electric blue and hot magenta (representing strength) contrasts sharply with the softer hues of static gray and dead black (representing vulnerability). The central area, dominated by dense patches of color, evokes a sense of protection, while the surrounding lighter pixels create an echoing vulnerability. The broken pixels punctuate the design, symbolizing imperfections and scars. The flow moves from the edges inward, intensifying the emotional collision of strength battling with exposure, capturing a narrative of resilience against fragility.
