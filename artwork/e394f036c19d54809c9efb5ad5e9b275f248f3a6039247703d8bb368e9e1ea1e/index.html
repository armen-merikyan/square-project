Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between emptiness and desire, reflecting a tension that rises through the composition. The chosen palette of deep blacks, muted violets, soft golds, and pale greens evokes feelings of longing and quietness against the backdrop of hope. There’s a sharp contrast between the dark and cool tones versus the warm golden hues, suggesting moments of aspiration and the pressure of unfulfilled dreams. The placements create a rhythm of upward movement that invokes rising signals of desire, while also incorporating imperfections through broken pixels to symbolize the jagged journey of seeking fulfillment amidst loneliness.
