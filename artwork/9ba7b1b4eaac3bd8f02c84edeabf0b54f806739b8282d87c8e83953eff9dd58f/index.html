Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a mix of compression and calm, visually represented through a palette that contrasts the muted warmth of faint peach against the starkness of dark navy. The soft channels of off white and dust gray represent the quiet throughout the layout. The diagonal arrangement creates a sense of tension on one wedge, where the compressed red breaks through, resembling pressure shattering against the tide. This arrangement captures the interplay of quiet moments interrupted by sudden bursts of intensity, ensuring that the predominantly dark colors are punctured by lighter hues, inviting contemplation amidst the chaos.
