Artist Statement

This composition conveys the tension between sudden brightness (spark) and the heaviness of burnt remnants (ash). The palette of mourning and renewal contrasts dark, muted tones with bright yellows and greens that represent fleeting moments of energy and hope. The main collision occurs diagonally, where bright accents clash against darker backgrounds, creating a visual argument of resilience and fragility. The jagged edges echo the instability of memories being formed and erased, while maintaining an abstract form that invites interpretation without literal representation.
