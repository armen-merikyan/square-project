Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the clash between the warmth of nostalgia (reflected in soft gold and pale green) and the starkness of progress (represented by deeper, darker tones). The palette combines soft and assertive colors to depict the tension of hope amidst a backdrop of heaviness. The bottom-weighted layout symbolizes an imbalance, reflecting both a rise towards progress and a fall into memory. The collision occurs where lighter hues seep into the deeper tones, creating an interrupted flow that mimics the struggle between full and hollow spaces, mirroring both the drip of nostalgia and the structured demands of progress.
