Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes envy and gratitude, establishing an ongoing internal battle. The palette uses deep blacks and pale blues to symbolize darkness and clarity, representing envy and gratitude respectively. The striking contrast between black and blue, alongside the imperfect zigzag pattern created by broken pixels, highlights the conflict between these emotions. The layout emphasizes a diagonal fall, suggesting a descent from chaotic envy towards the clarity of gratitude. Overall, the composition reflects unresolved tension and contrasts, inviting viewers to contemplate the duality of these sentiments.
