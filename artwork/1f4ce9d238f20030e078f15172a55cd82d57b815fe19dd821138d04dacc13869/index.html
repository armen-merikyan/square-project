Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional interplay between restlessness and peace through color and structure. The palette of soft pinks and stark blacks represents a collision of gentleness and severity. The main contrast occurs in the upper left corner, where darker colors create a dense cluster of unrest, while soft whites and warm grays recede into the background, allowing quiet space to emerge. The overall structure is influenced by the seed's themes of memory and motion, creating a dynamic yet reflective experience that hints at unresolved tensions.
