Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette, inspired by ancient fire, utilizes burnt orange and dark red to evoke a sense of warmth and intensity, contrasting with the cool, charcoal tones that suggest isolation. The top-weighted composition creates a visual imbalance, simulating the pressure of loneliness contrasted by bursts of connection in the center. The flow of colors descends like tears, revealing hidden strength within vulnerability—a conceptual interplay of exposure and concealment.
