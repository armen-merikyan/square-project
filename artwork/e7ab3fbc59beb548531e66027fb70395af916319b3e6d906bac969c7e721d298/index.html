Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between restlessness and peace. The palette of mourning black and deep blue forms a contemplative base, while pale yellow introduces a spark of hope amidst darker tones. The main collision occurs upwards from the bottom, creating a visual pulse that suggests upward motion, symbolizing escape or aspiration. The jagged edges reflect disorder against the serene fields, contrasting calm with the chaotic grid pattern of deeper colors. The intentional broken pixels evoke scars, amplifying the feeling of interruption, mirroring the heart's restlessness uniquely.
