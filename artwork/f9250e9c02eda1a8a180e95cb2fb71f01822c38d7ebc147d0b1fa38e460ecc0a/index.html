Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes vulnerability and strength, captured through a palette that blends softness and severity. The main contrast occurs in the center, where a pale field is encircled by deeper, more assertive colors, illustrating a confrontation between protection and exposure. The chosen colors, with their broken pixels, highlight imperfections while creating a sense of layered interaction, reminiscent of a braid intertwining strength and fragility.
