Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between the clarity of signal and the chaos of noise, where vibrant colors clash against muted tones, symbolizing the struggle of memory versus erasure. The palette features greens and blues for clean signaling, contrasted by shades of gray and a striking magenta, representing noise. The collision occurs prominently in the center, where the bright accents interrupt the static-like background, embodying moments of clarity amidst the confusion, while the structured layout allows for a readable flow between emotions across the grid.
