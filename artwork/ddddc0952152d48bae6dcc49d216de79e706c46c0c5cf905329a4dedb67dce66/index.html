Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the tension between saturated desire and muted restraint, using a palette that embodies warmth against a backdrop of calm. The selected colors create a contrast of deep reds and muted creams, where the dynamic arrangement reflects overlapping pulses of longing and containment. The main collision occurs centrally, marked by a saturation of reds contrasting with scattered muted tones, symbolizing the struggle within the ‘diamond’ shape, suggesting both allure and restriction.
