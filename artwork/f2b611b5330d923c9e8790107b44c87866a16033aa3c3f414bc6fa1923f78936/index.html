Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between vibrancy and decay, captured through a palette of moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold. The composition features a dense cluster of colors (spark) in the top left corner, gradually dispersing into a softer chaotic arrangement (ash) toward the bottom right. The collision occurs diagonally, showcasing the contrast between a lively emergence and a settling stillness, echoing themes of resilience against the weight of memory. Broken pixels disrupt the structure, adding intentional imperfections that suggest scars of past experiences.
