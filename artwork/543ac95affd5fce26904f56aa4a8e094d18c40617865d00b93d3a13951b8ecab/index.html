Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork oscillates between restlessness and peace, with a focus on the struggle of memory versus motion. The palette, drawn from warm greens and soft creams, suggests a tension between stability and disruption. The main contrast manifests in the central diagonal where vibrant patches of acid green collide with grounding deep olive, representing moments of clarity amidst noise. This interplay of color captures the essence of struggle and resolution, echoing the seed's themes through abstract expression.
