Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured is that of **defiance versus submission**, emphasizing a centered strength surrounded by softer, retreating influences. The palette of **pale mint**, **soft sky**, **storm gray**, **near black**, and **faded gold** conveys this dynamic by creating stark contrasts and ensuring a balance of quiet spaces with bold interruptions. The left half of the grid, representing defiance, uses mirror patterns of darker tones to create a sense of rigidity and strength, while the right half reflects submission with ring patterns of lighter, softer colors, suggesting a gentle dissipating quality. The main collision occurs in the center, where darker colors encroach upon the lighter, illustrating the tension between these emotions, while broken pixels create intentional disruptions throughout, reinforcing the conflict. This arrangement narrates an inward spiral of emotions, echoing both resilience and vulnerability.
