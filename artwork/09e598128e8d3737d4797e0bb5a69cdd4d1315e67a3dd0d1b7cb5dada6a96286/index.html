Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension of trust versus suspicion, illustrating the struggle between openness and obstruction. The palette balances cool aquas and grays with sharp red accents, conveying serenity mixed with urgency. The composition's main collision occurs on the left side, where the structured checker pattern of trust interrupts the chaotic crosshatch of suspicion, culminating in a visual tug-of-war. The layout incorporates broken frames to symbolize leaks of emotion while preserving space for quiet contemplation, allowing the viewer to navigate through the contrasting feelings.
