Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between restlessness and peace. The palette, drawn from 'mourning_renewal', uses deep blue and pale yellow to convey depth and warmth, creating a contrast between cool and warm tones. The main collision occurs between restless wandering accents near the center, represented by a chaotic interplay of darker colors, while the pale yellow and new leaf hues softly frame the edges, symbolizing peace. The structure emphasizes open gate layout with tension flowing inward, creating an intentional imbalance that evokes both memory and motion.
