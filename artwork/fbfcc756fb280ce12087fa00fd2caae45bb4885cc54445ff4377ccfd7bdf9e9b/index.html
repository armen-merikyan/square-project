Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting light and muted colors. The top half uses pale, soft shades, suggesting an ephemeral quality, while the bottom half employs richer, deeper hues that evoke stability. The gradient flow from soft to strong creates a dynamic push and pull, embodying a sense of exposure versus protection. The arrangement features broken pixels that add imperfection, representing the fragility inherent in strength. The overall balance resonates with a feeling of suspended tension and unresolved motion.
