Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between loneliness and connection through a stark contrast of colors on a muted background. The palette, predominantly of dark maroon and gray, evokes feelings of isolation, emphasizing a sense of void while gentle pinks interrupt the rigid structure, symbolizing fleeting connections. The asymmetric pattern utilizes a mirror-broken layout to convey disrupted harmony, with heavier dark tones at the top reflecting a burden. The limited use of brighter hues conveys bursts of warmth against an otherwise cool, dense space, aligning with the emotional theme of hidden versus exposed feelings. The arrangement of colors, with a central focal point that feels both protected and vulnerable, evokes a narrative of inner turmoil and longing for connection, symbolized by scattered warmer tones in an otherwise dark void.
