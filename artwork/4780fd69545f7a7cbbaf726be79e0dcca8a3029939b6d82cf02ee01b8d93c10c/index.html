Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts between envy and gratitude create a visual dialogue, where darkness (envy) collides with lightness (gratitude). The palette of navy, black, and dim cyan accentuates this tension, fostering feelings of both heaviness and relief. The arrangement emphasizes a central distribution of lighter colors battling against the denser darker edges, resonating the notion of a soft edge fighting against noise. The pattern evokes a sense of imbalance—rising and falling—as colors move in a fragmented way, creating a narrative of interruption and resolution.
