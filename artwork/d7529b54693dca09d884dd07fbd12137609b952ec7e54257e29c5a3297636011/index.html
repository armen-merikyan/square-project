Artist Statement

This composition encapsulates a struggle between peace and restlessness. The palette utilizes cosmic indigo and void black for depth, complemented by the serene yet piercing star white, which hints at a quiet beauty amidst chaos. The arrangement sees dense, chaotic noise patterns dominating the center, symbolizing unrest, while softer, barcode-like structures near the edges represent a yearning for peace. The main collision occurs diagonally, creating a dynamic interaction that reflects the tension between movement and stillness.
