Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between chaos (panic) and order (control). The palette combines deep reds and dark maroon to evoke intense emotions, contrasted with soft gray for a sense of stability. The outer border juxtaposes dark tones to represent control, while the inner area features a zigzag pattern of red and pink, symbolizing conflict and energy. Broken pixels are applied to suggest imperfections, enhancing the emotional tension. The visual clash between the structured outer borders and chaotic inner core creates a narrative of struggle, balancing between two opposing states.
