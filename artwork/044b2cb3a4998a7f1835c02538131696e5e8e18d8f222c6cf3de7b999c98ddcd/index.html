Artist Statement

This composition evokes a tension between nostalgia and progress by contrasting dense dark areas with lighter, airy spaces. The palette features deep blacks and dark violets suggesting weight and reminiscence, while soft gold and pale green represent potential and forward momentum. The top half displays a broken dots pattern reflective of nostalgia, creating an interrupted echo of past memories, while the bottom half adopts a checker pattern embodying the structured nature of progress. The split layout enforces a visual separation that highlights the dichotomy, with an underlying pulse of both calm and chaotic emotions at play through the interplay of colors.
