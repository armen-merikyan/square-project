Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional conflict between joy and grief. The palette combines bright yellows and deep blues to represent joy's warmth and grief's coolness. The contrast emphasizes an oscillation between the vibrant central area and the darker edges. The upward flow suggests resilience and aspiration through layers of color that shift from vibrancy to muted tones, embodying the tension of hope amid sorrow. Each color placement, arranged asymmetrically, echoes a push and pull, capturing the complexity of human emotion.
