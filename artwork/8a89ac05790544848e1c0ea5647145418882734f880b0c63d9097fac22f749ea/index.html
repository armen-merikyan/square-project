Artist Statement

This abstract artwork explores the emotional tension between clarity and confusion as described in the seed. I utilized a palette mixing vibrant yellows and deep blues to create a striking balance between warmth and coolness. The use of radial patterns to represent confusion contrasts with tooth-like shapes for clarity, leading to a visual narrative of interconnected emotions. The thick outlines suggest fragmented symmetry, while the broken pixels introduce imperfection and noise, symbolizing the struggle for coherence amidst chaos. The layout pushes clarity from the bottom left to the top right, creating a dynamic flow that resonates with the theme of pulse and disruption.
