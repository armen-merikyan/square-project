Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional clash between loneliness and connection through a pattern of contrast and visual tension. The palette, inspired by broken memory, utilizes muted sepia and faded blue tones to resonate with feelings of nostalgia and disconnection. The main collision occurs in the diagonal flow of bright dots (representing fleeting connections) against the heavier muted background, creating a sense of disruption. The arrangement embodies both isolation, with off-center placements and stripes, and moments of connection, indicated by the brighter elements punctuating the darker tones, echoing the emotional narrative encapsulated in the seed.
