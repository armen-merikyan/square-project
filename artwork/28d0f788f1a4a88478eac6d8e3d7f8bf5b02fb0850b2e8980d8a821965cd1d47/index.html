Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional nuances of longing and resolve through contrasting palettes and spatial dynamics. The choice of 'melancholy wonder' colors (rain_blue, lavender_gray) emphasizes a feeling of wistfulness against the strength of muted_plum and soft_cyan, creating tension. The dominant color (lavender_gray) fills about 60% of the space, suggesting longing, while the broken pixels create intentional disruptions echoing the struggle between chaos and order. The interplay of light and dark creates an attractive rhythm that simulates a pulse towards intimacy and distance, interrupted by the halo-like edges suggesting unresolved tension as the two emotional impulses collide.
