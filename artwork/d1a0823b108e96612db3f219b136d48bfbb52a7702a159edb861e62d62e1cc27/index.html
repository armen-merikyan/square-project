Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between loneliness and connection, represented through contrasting color densities. The palette combines deep black with electric blue and vibrant magenta to evoke warmth against isolation. The center features a slight burst of color, suggesting an exposed emotion, with the surrounding pixels creating a grid-like pressure that signifies the weight of the outside world. The patterns reflect a crosshatch between structured chaos and moments of clarity, resulting in an unresolved interplay between feeling confined and the desire to connect.
