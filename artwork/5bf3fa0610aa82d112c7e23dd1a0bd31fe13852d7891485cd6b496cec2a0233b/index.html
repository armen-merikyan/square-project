Artist Statement

This abstract composition encapsulates the duality of longing and resolve through a delicate balance of warm and cool tones. The palette draws from 'intimacy_distance', where muted colors represent yearning and brighter hues signify determination. The collision occurs centrally with dense clusters contrasted against expansive negative space, suggesting a struggle between connectedness and separation. Patterns of pressure and noise intermingle, reflecting an emotional pulse where moments of clarity interrupt lingering feelings. The incorporation of broken pixels symbolizes imperfections in the journey towards resolve.
