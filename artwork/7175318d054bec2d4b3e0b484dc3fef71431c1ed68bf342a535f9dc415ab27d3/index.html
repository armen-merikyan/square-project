Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a fluidity between desire (longing) and assurance (resolve), using the palette of 'softness_severity' to contrast warm and cool tones. The starkness of carbon black meets the gentleness of powder pink, creating a palpable clash. The collision occurs centrally, where longing's delicate fault lines intersect with resolve's firm stripes, suggesting a dance of emotion. The composition is punctuated with broken pixels that symbolize interruptions in this push-pull dynamic, while preserving a quiet field to invoke the sense of distance and intimacy. Thus, the arrangement speaks to the complexity of emotions at play, reflecting both chaos and clarity.
