Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a turbulent yet calming interplay between restlessness and peace. The palette combines muted blacks and deep violets with accentuations of soft gold and pale green to suggest tension and hope. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where the static marks of restlessness collide with the serene shatter pattern of peace. This reflects the narrative of memory vs. motion, where chaotic elements weave through peaceful spaces, evoking an ongoing emotional dialogue within the 8x8 grid.
