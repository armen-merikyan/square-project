Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of joy and grief, manifesting through a color palette of bright yellows and oranges for joy, contrasted against deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The upper rows reflect order through a structured grid pattern, while the lower rows devolve into chaotic, jagged placements symbolizing grief's unpredictability. The edges exhibit a 'burned' style, enhancing the theme of imperfection and the tension between the rigid and the disordered. The zigzag placement of the joy colors introduces dynamic tension, interrupted by the heavier grief tones.
