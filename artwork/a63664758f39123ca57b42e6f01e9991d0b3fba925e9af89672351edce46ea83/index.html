Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between defiance and submission through its structured yet chaotic arrangement. The palette combines warm and muted tones—red and dark maroon for defiance intersecting with gray and pink shades for submission. The left half embraces an angular 'split_screen' pattern, while the right displays a fractured 'crack' motif, exemplifying the conflict and collision of these emotions. The main contrast centers on the division between the opposing sides, symbolizing the struggle between order and chaos.
