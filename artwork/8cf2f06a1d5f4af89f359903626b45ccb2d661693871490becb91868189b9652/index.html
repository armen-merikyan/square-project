Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the conflict between sudden brightness (spark) and the settled dust of resignation (ash), evoking a dynamic tension between hope and despair. The palette combines red oxide and muted cream to symbolize the hunger for life and the restraint of reality, with jagged edges to suggest disruption. The diagonal layout creates a visual flow from light to dark, emphasizing the interplay of emotions, with that collision occurring centrally where vivid colors intersect with muted tones.
