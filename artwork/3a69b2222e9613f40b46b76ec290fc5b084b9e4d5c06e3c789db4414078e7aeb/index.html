Artist Statement

The emotional tones of joy and grief are captured through a palette of bright yellows and muted purples, representing joy's brightness against the heaviness of grief. Contrasting patterns of noise (for joy) and flame (for grief) create a visual tension across the canvas. The layout, starting from the lower left corner, inherently weighs the composition towards one side, symbolizing the burden of grief. The center acts as a point of conflict, surrounded by broken pixels that suggest interruption and fragility, emphasizing the emotional struggle within the polished grid. This arrangement is meant to evoke the push and pull between joy and grief, culminating in an abstract expression of life's complexities.
