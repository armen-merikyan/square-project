Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between restlessness and peace through contrasting dark and light tones. The chosen palette includes deep wine (#5D3A3A) symbolizing restlessness and soft gold (#FFEA92) that represents peace. The pattern is designed with a diagonal flow, showcasing a dance of density and openness reminiscent of memory's ebb and flow. The center is intentionally disrupted with 'broken pixels' to reflect moments of interruption, enhancing the story of unsettling tranquility. Each color placement captures a volatile conversation of tension and release, echoing the themes of the seed throughout the 8x8 grid.
