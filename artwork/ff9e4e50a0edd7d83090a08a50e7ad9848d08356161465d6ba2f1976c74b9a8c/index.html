Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette of muted purple, deep blue, and bright yellow accents. The central bright cluster represents vulnerability, while dimmer surrounding areas symbolize strength, creating a soft glow against a stark contrast. This collision occurs prominently in the center, echoing the emotional resonance of the seed, as jagged edges hint at broken boundaries between safety and exposure. The irregular distribution reinforces feelings of instability, encouraging contemplation of one's internal landscape.
