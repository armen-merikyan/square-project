Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional duality of restlessness versus peace through an interplay of dense clusters and quiet fields. The selected palette of moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold symbolizes resilience and fragility, emphasizing contrasts between warmth and coolness, echoing the theme of memory versus motion. The collision between rust accents representing restlessness and the soft bone white conveys a struggle between chaos and tranquility—a moment of tension at the center amidst more muted tones, creating a sense of unresolved conflict in an abstract narrative.
