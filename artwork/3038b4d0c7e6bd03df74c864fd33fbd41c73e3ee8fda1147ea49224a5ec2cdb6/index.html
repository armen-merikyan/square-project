Artist Statement

The emotional tone expresses tension between love and betrayal, using a palette of warm pink and red hues contrasted with cool gray and dark maroon. The opposing colors represent the duality of emotions. The composition features a broken pattern that evokes feelings of strain and connection, with density on one side and soft, empty space on the other. The arrangement of colors creates a rhythm that suggests both conflict and harmony, reflecting the seed's themes of conflict and unity through diagonal placements and intersecting patterns.
