Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of trust and suspicion, with a palette that evokes 'melancholy wonder.' The arrangement reflects a rising signal with pressure borders highlighting the tension. The soft cyan and moon white create calmness while muted plum adds a subtle weight, suggesting layers of both warmth and unraveling doubts. The main contrast occurs where darker hues meet lighter shades, creating a visual echo of the theme. The diagonal arrangement symbolizes shifting perspectives, while broken pixels introduce an aspect of fragility. Overall, it tells a story of resilience challenged by hidden suspicions.
