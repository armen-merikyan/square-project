Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional juxtaposition of restlessness and peace, using a palette that balances serene pale hues with intense accents. The background is dominated by soft colors like pale aqua and mist gray, representing the tranquil aspects of peace. In contrast, alarm red represents moments of unrest, creating a visual tension against the calming fields. The central broken dot pattern collides with the frame-like edges, symbolizing the battle between chaos and stability. The layout is intentionally open on one side, suggesting a flow of emotions as they intersect with quiet moments, showcasing the struggle and dance of memory and motion.
