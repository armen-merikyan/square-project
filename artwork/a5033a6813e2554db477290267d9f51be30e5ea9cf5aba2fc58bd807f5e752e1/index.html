Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of envy and gratitude, where colors contrast both in warmth and coolness. The palette includes muted sepia tones and soft blues to represent the complexity of these emotions. The arrangement features a central crack in the pattern, symbolizing disruption, surrounded by sparks of hopeful colors and muted fields of grayish hues reflecting the noise of fear against rising hope. The asymmetry and fragmentation reflect the delicate balance of emotion that flows from left to right, suggesting a movement from chaos towards calm while maintaining sufficient negative space for contemplation.
