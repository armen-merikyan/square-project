Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between trust and suspicion, creating a lively tension. The palette, derived from the love_betrayal theme, includes warm pinks and reds contrasting with darker maroons and grays, evoking a deep emotional landscape. The main collision—intense reds and soft grays—nestles in the center, where vibrant lines mimic the pattern of a 'ladder' against 'constellations', symbolizing suspicion and trust respectively. The edges give a jagged feel, representing the tension of pressure and a soft glow. This structure leaves space for contemplation while also provoking intrigue through density and interruptions, truly reflecting the complexity of the seed.
