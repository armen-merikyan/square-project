Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between control and surrender, using bursts of color to express the concepts. The chosen palette of warm coral, soft amber, cold slate, blue black, and signal cyan conveys a balance between warmth and coolness, evoking both comfort and detachment. The main contrast occurs at the center where the bright accents collide with muted fields, suggesting a struggle between an overfilled emotion and the surrounding emptiness that tries to contain it. This collision embodies the conflict inherent in the seed's themes, with an asymmetric layout that emphasizes the pressure in one corner and the release towards the edges.
