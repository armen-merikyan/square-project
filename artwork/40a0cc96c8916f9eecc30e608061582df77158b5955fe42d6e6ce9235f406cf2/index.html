Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the assertive nature of defiance with the serene characteristics of submission. The palette includes vibrant greens and calming cream tones to reflect the tension between these emotions. The main collision occurs in the center, showcasing dynamic blocks of deep olive and acid green against softer honey gold fields, creating a visual pulse that suggests movement and inner conflict. The layout follows an 'island and sea' concept, where the denser, darker pixels symbolize defiance amidst a tranquil backdrop of lighter tones, embodying both struggle and acceptance.
