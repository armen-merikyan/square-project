Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between longing and resolve, layered with hints of repair and damage. The palette blends sharp crimson for urgency against soft warm white and carbon black for depth. The collision occurs through the diagonal layout, where subtle pulse-like patterns of crosshatch weave through a rain-like saturation on the opposing side, suggesting a dynamic interplay between the ideas of containment and overflow. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, symbolizing the fragility of both longing and resolve.
