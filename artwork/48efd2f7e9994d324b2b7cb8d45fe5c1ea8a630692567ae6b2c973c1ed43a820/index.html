Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, highlighting the fragile nature of existence against an ominous background. The palette of 'hunger_restraint' evokes a depth of feeling—rich reds represent strength and determination, while muted creams signal vulnerability and fleeting moments of calm. The central broken dot pattern signifies strength, while the subtle ring patterns radiate vulnerability, creating a striking contrast in the center. The collision occurs centrally but is disrupted by the edges, which suggest a dark invocation of closed corners reflecting a constant tension. Overall, the abstract pattern serves as a visual representation of navigating emotions, where moments of safety coalesce with existential exposure.
