Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between defiance and submission, using a palette that embodies quiet pressure with warm and cool contrasts. Dark navy and compressed red create a dichotomy of energy, while off-white and dust gray suggest calmness and negative space. The central diamond shape punctuates the arrangement, creating a dominant collision point, surrounded by radial submissions, and cracked defiance, illustrating a battle of forces. The layout's eroded symmetry enhances the narrative of imperfection, echoing the theme of fractured boundaries.
