Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between warmth and a sense of rupture, encapsulating the tension of love versus betrayal. The palette of pinks and reds evokes warmth, while dark grays and maroons suggest an underlying sorrow. The main contrast lies in the chaotic crosshatch patterns against soft edges, representing the struggle between hope and despair. The interplay between sharp and soft forms reflects the diamond’s brilliance, which holds an inherent fragility, conveying both beauty and pain.
