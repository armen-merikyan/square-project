Artist Statement

This abstract composition captures the tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors and uneven patterns. The palette features deep navy and black representing strength and solidity, while dim cyan and white evoke vulnerability with their softness and lightness. The center holds a pale area symbolizing exposure, surrounded by dense, darker corners, suggesting the tension of a fragile shield against a hard edge. The noise pattern evokes strength through chaotic density, while the frame-like outline represents vulnerability with its soft boundaries. The collision of these patterns at the center creates a visual dialogue of safety versus exposure, inviting contemplation on their fragile balance.
