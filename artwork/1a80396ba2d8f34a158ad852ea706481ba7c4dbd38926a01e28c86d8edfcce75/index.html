Artist Statement

This composition communicates the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity through its pixel arrangement and color choices. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue creates a sense of nostalgia, representing the weight of past experiences while inviting a spark of curiosity through the use of color contrast. The strong diagonal flow from the corner reflects the chaos emerging against a structured certainty, as the soft edges intermingle with sharper hues in the center, creating a collision of feelings. The broken pixels act as scars within this evolving narrative, hinting at imperfections that echo the complexity of human emotion.
