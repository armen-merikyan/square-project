Artist Statement

The emotional tone evokes a struggle between explosive intensity and serene containment. The palette mixes warm reds with cooler pale blues to create a contrast of heat vs calmness. The main collision of colors occurs centrally, emphasizing the transition from pulsating rage to soft rings of calm. The pattern reflects the shape seed of 'fault', visualizing a jagged tension layered over gentle fields, portraying both chaos and tranquility merging.
