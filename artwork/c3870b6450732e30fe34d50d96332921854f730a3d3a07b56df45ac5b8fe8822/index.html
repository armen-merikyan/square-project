Artist Statement

The composition reflects the tension between joy and grief, capturing the interplay of contrasting emotions through color. The palette features vibrant yellows and oranges for joy against deep blues and muted purples for grief, creating a split between a lively upper half and a somber lower half. This split results in unexpected color collisions, emphasizing the emotional conflict present in the design. The 'hard edges' create a stark division, while 'broken pixels' add an element of imperfection, enhancing the narrative of fragility amid strength. The 'stair-step' pattern reinforces the tension, suggesting a rise from grief towards joy, while also depicting a sense of ascending through emotion, resonating with the overarching themes of the seed.
