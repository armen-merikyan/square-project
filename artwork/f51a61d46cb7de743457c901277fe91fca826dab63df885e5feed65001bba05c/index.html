Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between stillness and overwhelming force. The palette of muted off-white, soft gray, and deep navy represents a subtle calm intersecting with vibrant compressed red, signifying pressure. The main contrast occurs in the central area where the soft hues collide sharply with darker edges, creating a visual narrative of tension and release. Inspired by the seed, abstract shapes of islands emerge subtly within the flow of dense color clusters, symbolizing moments of clarity amidst confusion.
