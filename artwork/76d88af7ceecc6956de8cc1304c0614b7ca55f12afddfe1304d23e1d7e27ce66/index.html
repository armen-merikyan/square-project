Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a structured grid layout that alternates between calm and pressure. The muted palette of off-white, dust gray, faint peach, and compressed red represents the balance of serenity and urgency. The central collision happens between the stark compressed red and soft tones, creating a focal point of intensity surrounded by a thread-like arrangement of subtle stripes and stairs, invoking both a sense of lost connection and the hope of repair.
