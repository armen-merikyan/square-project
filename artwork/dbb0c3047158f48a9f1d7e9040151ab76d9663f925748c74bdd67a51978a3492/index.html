Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the tension between vulnerability and strength, represented through a palette of warm and cool colors. The dominant charcoal provides a dense background, contrasting against the lively hot coral and blood orange, which symbolize sparks of strength trying to emerge. The layout suggests a mostly empty room with the sparks of color off-center, while soft edges and broken pixels create intentional disruptions that echo the theme of exposure versus shelter. The main collision occurs at the center, where vibrant colors push against the dark, creating a dynamic interplay of light and shadow that reflects the complexities of connection and alienation.
