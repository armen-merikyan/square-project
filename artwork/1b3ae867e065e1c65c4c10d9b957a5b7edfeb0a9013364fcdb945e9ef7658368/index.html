Artist Statement

The composition embodies the emotional contrast between restlessness and peace, with a predominant dark palette signaling restlessness through chaotic arrangements, while lighter colors introduce moments of peace. The high contrast between warm shadow hues and cool blues creates tension, while the broken pixels symbolize interrupted motion, aligning with the seed's themes. The central collision occurs where the dark and light pixels meet, drawing the eye and representing the struggle between movement and stillness.
