Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the space between vulnerability and strength, highlighted through a palette of cool and warm tones. The signal green and hot magenta create a dynamic tension, while dead black serves as a grounding element. The collision of crosshatch and flame patterns suggests an interplay between structure and fluidity. The central area remains pale, acting as a focal point amid the darker surroundings, embodying exposure amidst shelter. A few broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, reflecting the fragility of the emotions at play.
