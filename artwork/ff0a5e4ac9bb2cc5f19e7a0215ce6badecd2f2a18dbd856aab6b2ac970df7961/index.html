Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of restlessness and peace through a palette that evokes warmth and depth. The clash between vibrant hues, like small_orange and muted_cream, and darker shades like blackened_plum and iron_gray, suggests an inner conflict. The main collision occurs at the center, where chaotic patterns of restlessness interrupt smoother, tranquil areas, embodying a pulse of memory versus motion. The placement of broken pixels fuels the feeling of disintegration while preserving a sense of quiet amid the chaos.
