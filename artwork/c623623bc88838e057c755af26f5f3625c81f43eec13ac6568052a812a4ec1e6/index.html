Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between longing and resolve, using a palette of muted sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue to suggest a sense of faded memory and introspection. The primary emotional tension manifests in a checker pattern that interjects into a more structured hinge pattern, symbolizing the collision between chaos and order. The broken pixel elements introduce intentional disruptions, echoing the inner struggles of intimacy and distance while maintaining a loose symmetry across the grid, ensuring that the primary emotion of longing dominates without overwhelming the negative space. The dominant colors in the center create a compact strength while the edges fade, suggesting the quiet distance of unresolved emotions.
