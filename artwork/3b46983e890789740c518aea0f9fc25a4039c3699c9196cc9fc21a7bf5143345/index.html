Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the juxtaposition of loneliness and connection through a careful balance between dark and light colors. The chosen palette of navy, black, white, and dim cyan evokes a sense of isolation contrasted with fleeting signals of connection, represented by sparse bright pixels. The dominant dark colors create an enveloping pressure, while the single bright pixels echo hope and connection amid the void. The arrangement flows from the left towards the center, evoking a subtle invasion that suggests a tension between a weighted left and an open right, embodying a memory that feels both heavy and fragile.
