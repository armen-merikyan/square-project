Artist Statement

This artwork embodies an emotional dichotomy between fear and hope. The palette combines soft gold to symbolize hope with dark violet representing fear, creating a stark contrast. The pattern of crosshatch against burned edges maximizes the tension of conflicting emotions, where chaotic lines clash with softer glows, suggesting a struggle. The use of broken pixel elements enhances imperfection and depth with jagged edges evoking unease, while the glowing center signifies potential and aspiration amidst a darkened backdrop.
