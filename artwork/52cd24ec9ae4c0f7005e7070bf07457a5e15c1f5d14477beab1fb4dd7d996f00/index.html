Artist Statement

The emotional tension between loneliness and connection is captured through a stark juxtaposition of colors and patterns. The palette, combining deep blue and muted purple, conveys a sense of quiet sorrow contrasted against the warmth of yellow and orange, representing fleeting moments of connection. The patterns represent a struggle: the grid and ring motifs clash, illustrating the push-pull between being seen versus concealed. Contrasting warm colors break through the cooler tones, creating visceral collisions and emotional bursts. The arrangement emphasizes negative space to evoke isolation while still suggesting a hint of community amidst the chaos. The overall composition tells a story of yearning for connection amidst the feeling of being deeply alone.
