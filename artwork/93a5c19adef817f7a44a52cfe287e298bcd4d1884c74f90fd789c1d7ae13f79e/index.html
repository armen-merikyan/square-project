Artist Statement

This composition embodies a juxtaposition of emotional tones—quiet and pressure. The off-white and dust gray hues create a muted field representing calmness, while the compressed red and dark navy colors impose a weighty density, suggesting emotions of pressure and conflict. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where dark navy disrupts the lighter tones, embodying a push against stillness and creating a dynamic tension. Broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, symbolizing disruptions and the inability to maintain control amidst overwhelming feelings. The layout mimics the effect of falling pixels, enhancing the sense of descent from pressure to quietude.
