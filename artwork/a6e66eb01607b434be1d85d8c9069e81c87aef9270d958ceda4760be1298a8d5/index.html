Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between clarity and confusion through a palette of muted reds and contrasting dark grays. The main collision occurs in the central column, where a gradient of colors reflects the clash of structured symmetry and unexpected breaks. The radial pattern evokes confusion while the almost symmetrical placement pulls towards clarity. The jagged edges create interruptions that enhance the emotional turbulence, resulting in an abstract narrative that resonates with the seed's themes.
