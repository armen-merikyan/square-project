Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength through its contrasting colors and patterns. The deep blues and blacks symbolize strength, while the pale yellows and greens suggest vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center where the strength colors dominate, interrupted by the softer tones, creating a palpable tension. This reflects the seed's emotional themes of exposure and protection, manifesting as a dynamic push and pull within the arrangement.
