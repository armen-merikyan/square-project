Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve, reflecting the struggle and harmony through color and placement. The palette, inspired by 'hunger_restraint', juxtaposes muted creams with rich blacks and deep reds, creating a dynamic yet serene atmosphere. The central collision occurs at the core of the grid, where dense, jagged patterns clash against quieter colors, representing the fight between desire and stability. Broken pixels are utilized strategically to symbolize flaws and disruptions within this emotional narrative, maintaining a balance of negative space to invite contemplation.
