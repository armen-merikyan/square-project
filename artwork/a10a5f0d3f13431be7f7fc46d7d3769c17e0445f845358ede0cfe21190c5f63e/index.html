Artist Statement

This composition captures the duality of longing and resolve through a palette that balances warmth and depth. The colors of ripe green and honey gold evoke a sense of yearning, while deep olive and cream provide structure and clarity. The main collision occurs in the center where the braided pattern of longing overlaps with the stair-like resolve. This juxtaposition creates a rhythm of tension, echoing the delicate nature of repairs amidst fractures. The organized disarray speaks to the complexity of emotions represented in the seed, crafting a narrative of both connection and solitude.
