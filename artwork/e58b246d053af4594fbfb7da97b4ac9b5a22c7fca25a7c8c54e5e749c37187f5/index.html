Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic conflict between desire (represented by warmer colors) and restraint (cooler shades), creating a push-pull effect. The palette combines hot coral and blood orange for intensity, with charcoal and hollow blue providing calm and containment. The main collision occurs at the top-left corner, where saturated colors burst against muted tones, forming an asymmetric pattern that suggests tension and release, echoing the seed's themes of pressure and interplay.
