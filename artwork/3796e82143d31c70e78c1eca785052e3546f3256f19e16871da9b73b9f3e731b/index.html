Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between restlessness and peace, represented by the contrast of deep navy against bright white, creating a visual conflict. The palette of navy, black, dim cyan, and a sliver of white suggests depth and isolation, embodying the struggle of calm against chaos. The dominant dark colors form a heavy base, interrupted by the sharp brightness of white, symbolizing fleeting moments of clarity. The diagonal arrangement of stripes and lattice reflects the contrasts in life's rhythm, while the intentional broken pixels serve as reminders of disruption amidst the flow.
