Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a balance between the chaos of restlessness and the calm of peace. The palette combines soft lavenders and blues with darker hues, creating a play of muted serenity and vibrant disruption. The main contrast occurs in the grid's left diagonal, where darker shades collide with lighter ones, representing the struggle between quiet order and noisy unrest. The selected patterns of noise and shatter visually narrate this conflict, with an overall sense of fluid energy against a restrained space that’s marked by broken pixels, echoing the tension in the seed.
