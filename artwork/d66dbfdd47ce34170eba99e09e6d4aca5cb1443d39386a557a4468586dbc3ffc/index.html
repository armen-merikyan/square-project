Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve, utilizing a palette that evokes warmth and depth. The contrasting colors illustrate journeys of hope contrasted against feelings of emptiness. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense clusters of warm hues intersect with cooler, empty spaces, representing the struggle between chaos and order. The diagonal flow symbolizes a rising signal of hope, while broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, suggesting unresolved feelings. Together, the arrangement reflects an emotional tapestry of intimacy and distance.
