Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between love and betrayal through its color palette and arrangement. The warm tones of pink and red represent love, while the darker maroon signifies betrayal. The asymmetry of color application creates a visual echo of conflict— a fine line between warmth and discomfort. The use of soft dots on one side contrasts sharply with the harshness of crosshatching on the other, suggesting both tenderness and rupture, all while maintaining a rising diagonal flow that embodies hope despite disruption.
