Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a tension between the protection of safety and the vulnerability of exposure. The palette transitions from earthy warmth to coolness, reflecting the duality while maintaining an abstract cohesion. The dominant colors create a stable frame representing safety, while broken pixels and minimal negative space echo the fragility within the exposed area. The central collision occurs through the interplay of the warm brown against the cool blue, suggesting a protective embrace encountering open vulnerability.
