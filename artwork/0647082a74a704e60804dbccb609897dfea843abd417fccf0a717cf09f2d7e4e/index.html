Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the tension between restlessness and peace. The palette features muted cream for calmness, blackened plum to evoke depth, and small orange for a hint of disruption. The main contrast occurs at the center, where brighter accents collide with darker, maze-like patterns, reflecting the struggle of memory against motion. The arrangement spirals inward, suggesting an ongoing dynamic tension as colors interrupt each other, embodying the seed concept as an abstract dialogue of exposure vs. shelter.
