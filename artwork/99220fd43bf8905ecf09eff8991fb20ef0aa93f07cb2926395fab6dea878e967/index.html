Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the duality of nostalgia and progress through a deliberate palette of dark navy interspersed with light dim cyan. The jagged edge design fosters a sense of interrupted flow, while a zigzag pattern suggests movement that oscillates between pressure and release. The main contrast emerges through the collision of deep, dense colors against lighter tones, reflecting the tension between quiet moments and emotional upheaval. This interplay of color and form invokes a storyline of conflict and balance as suggested by the seed's themes.
