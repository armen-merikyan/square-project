Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between nostalgia and progress, conveying a journey through emotional contrasts. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green suggests a shift from weighty memories to lighter aspirations. The dark colors represent nostalgia's density and the burdens of the past, while brighter hues reflect the potential of progress. The split horizontal layout emphasizes this duality: the top section features a rhythmic pattern akin to progress (soft gold in scattered motifs), while the lower half embodies nostalgia through a more grid-like disposition of darker hues. Edge variations and broken pixels introduce imperfection, symbolizing the jagged path between these emotional states, creating a dynamic visual interplay of tension and release.
