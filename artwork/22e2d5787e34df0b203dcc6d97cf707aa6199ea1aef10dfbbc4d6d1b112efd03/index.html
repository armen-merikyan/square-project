Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between confusion and clarity, using a palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold to evoke warmth amidst chaos. The arrangement emphasizes a diagonal flow, with a corner invasion layout suggesting a gradual buildup of pressure. Broken pixels create imperfection, enhancing the sensation of conflict between vibrant areas and spaces of tranquility. The main collision occurs in the center, where dark red and charcoal clash, visually representing the chaotic interplay of emotions.
