Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between the quiet and the impactful, reflecting the emotional turmoil suggested by the seed. The color palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue conveys a subdued yet warm atmosphere, encapsulating feelings of nostalgia and unresolved sentiments. The main contrast arises at the intersection of the warm tans and cool faded blues, representing the collision of gratitude (warmth) with envy (coolness). Patterns iterate through mirrored placements, creating a rhythmic flow that suggests alternating pressure and release across the composition, inviting the viewer into a dance of emotional colors.
