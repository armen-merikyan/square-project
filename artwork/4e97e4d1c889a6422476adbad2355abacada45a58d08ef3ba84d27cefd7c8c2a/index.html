Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between restlessness and peace. The color palette embodies warmth and coolness—conveying a dialogue between calm and chaos. The contrasting colors create visual collision at the central axis, illustrating an argument between structured calm (left) and chaotic energy (right). Patterns of calm gradients clash against disordered dashes, echoing themes from the seed references. Each element suggests an unresolved motion, drawing the viewer into a narrative of emotional tension.
