Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured here is one of confusion amidst clarity, illustrating the tension between contradictory feelings. The palette draws from a 'love_betrayal' theme, using shades of pink and dark maroon to evoke warmth counters pressure. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, creating a jagged interaction that embodies the 'symmetry_vs_break' concept, conveying an almost mirrored look that is disruptive. The zigzag flow creates dynamic movement, suggesting the collision of emotions, while maintaining essential negative space to allow for breathing room amidst the pressure.
