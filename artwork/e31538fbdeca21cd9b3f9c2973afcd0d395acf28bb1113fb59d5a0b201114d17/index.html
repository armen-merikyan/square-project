Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through a combination of dense and empty spaces, symbolizing the push and pull of human relationships. The contrasting palette of dark violet and soft gold represents the heaviness of isolation against the lightness of reaching out. The arrangement, with a left-weighted structure, echoes the idea of corners being both protective yet invasive, while the scattered pixels suggest broken memories and the elusive nature of connection.
