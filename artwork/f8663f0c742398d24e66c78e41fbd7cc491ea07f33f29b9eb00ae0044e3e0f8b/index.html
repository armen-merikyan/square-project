Artist Statement

This artwork embodies a tension between 'certainty' and 'curiosity', symbolized by the clash of structured and chaotic elements. The palette, infused with blues and soft grays, evokes a melancholy wonder, suggesting introspection amidst exploration. The bright accents of lavender and soft cyan emerge from a grounding background, representing curiosity breaking free from confinement. The main collision occurs at the top-left corner, where vibrant colors burst into a more restrained area, representing a chaotic yet hopeful exploration against a backdrop of order.
