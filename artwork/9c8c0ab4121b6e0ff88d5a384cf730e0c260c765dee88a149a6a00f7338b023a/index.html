Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the warmth of safety and the chill of exposure. The chosen palette weaves together the tension between shelter green and cold white, suggesting stability against fragility. The focus is on the left side, where dense warm colors meet the softer cool tones, creating a striking contrast. This arrangement embodies the split vertical concept, with the barcode and hinge patterns colliding in the center, interrupted by intentionally broken pixels, allowing for a sense of disarray within the comfort of structure.
