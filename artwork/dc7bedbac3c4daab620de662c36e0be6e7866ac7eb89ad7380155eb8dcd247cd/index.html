Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tone of confusion versus clarity through a juxtaposition of saturated and muted colors, evoking a sense of tension and release. The palette combines warm reds and pinks with cooler grays, symbolizing the contrast between emotional turmoil and moments of clarity. The main collision occurs where the organized spark pattern representing confusion meets the structured maze pattern depicting clarity. This duality highlights the nature of our perceptions, pulling between moments of symmetry and disruption, as represented by the 'broken edges' that create an imperfect yet deliberate composition.
