Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dance between restlessness and peace, manifesting through the contrast of dark and light shades. The palette includes muted sepia and faded blue to evoke a sense of broken memories interlaced with clarity. The outer edges feature darker hues, suggesting heaviness and weight, while the inner 4x4 grid presents lighter colors in a crosshatch pattern, symbolizing energy and motion. The main collision occurs diagonally, creating tension and a push-pull between the crowded dark space and the airy light core, representing the dialectic of chaos and tranquility found in our memories.
