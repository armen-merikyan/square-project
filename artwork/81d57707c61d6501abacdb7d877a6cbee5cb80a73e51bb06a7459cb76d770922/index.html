Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the delicate balance between longing and resolve, using a palette that juxtaposes clean whites with darker hues to signify clarity against confusion. The main contrast occurs at the center where overlapping patterns of stripes (signifying resolve) collide with thresholds (representing longing), creating a dynamic interplay of light and dark. The layout emphasizes an outer border in darker tones with a brighter, more hopeful core, mirroring the push and pull of emotions expressed in the seed.
