Artist Statement

This abstract composition reflects the duality of control and surrender, visually emphasizing the conflict through a palette that juxtaposes warm rust tones with cool moss greens. The arrangement creates a central concentration of rust colors, suggesting overwhelming pressure, while the surrounding cooler tones evoke a soft yielding—symbolizing containment. The pixel arrangement descends like falling rain, echoing the emotional flow from control at the center to surrender towards the edges. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, reinforcing the narrative of pressure versus release. The overall balance leans towards dominance of the warmer tones, embodying a sense of chaotic energy contained by the cooler, softer hues.
