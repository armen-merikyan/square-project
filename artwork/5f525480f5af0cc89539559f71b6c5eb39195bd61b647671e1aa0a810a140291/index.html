Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between the comfort of safety and the vulnerability of exposure. The palette combines warm brown and shelter green for safety, with bold accents of open blue and cold white representing exposure. The main contrast occurs at the center, where warm tones collide with cooler colors, symbolizing the interplay of comfort and risk. The rising flow and tide versus crosshatch patterns create an upward movement, suggesting hope amidst uncertainty.
