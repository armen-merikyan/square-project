Artist Statement

The piece embodies the tension between longing and resolve through a defined palette of muted sepia and faded colors, evoking feelings of nostalgia and strength. The main collision, marked by brighter edge colors, suggests resolve, arching over a backdrop of longing represented by dots. The quiet channel of negative space accentuates the contrast, making the presence of either emotion palpable without a literal depiction of the shape seed.
