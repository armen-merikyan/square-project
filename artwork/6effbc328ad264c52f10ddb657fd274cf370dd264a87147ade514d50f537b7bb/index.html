Artist Statement

This abstract piece explores the emotional dichotomy of 'quiet' versus 'pressure' through a structured layout of colors representing soft and hard edges. The palette integrates muted tones like 'off_white' and 'dust_gray,' juxtaposed with heavier colors such as 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy,' emphasizing tension. A loose pattern of broken dots for pressure collides with a more calm ember pattern, creating a dynamic interplay in the upper left corner while leaving a significant portion of the grid open, symbolizing silence and clarity amid chaos. The composition leans heavily on clarity in sections to avoid overwhelming the viewer, maintaining a balance where 'quiet' dominates amidst fragmented color disruptions.
