Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes restlessness and peace, embodied through contrasting shades of gray, pink, and dark maroon. The palette captures the tension between calm (pale tones) and agitation (stronger hues), with a clear rising pattern symbolizing hope or escape. The dominant gray fields convey tranquility, interrupted by jagged accents of dark maroon and red that represent unrest. The main collision occurs in a diagonal pattern, creating an unsettling disruption amidst the serene background, reinforcing the emotional struggle of the piece.
