Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between panic and control, mirroring the struggle of maintaining order in chaotic moments. The selected palette of navy, black, and dim cyan conveys feelings of isolation and density, with contrasting elements of white creating stark highlights that symbolize sudden bursts of control amidst turmoil. The arrangement features a structured outer layer representing control (crosshatch pattern), while the chaotic, irregular inner section embodies panic (rain pattern), emphasizing a collision of emotion. This split layout creates visual tension as the edges bleed into one another, evoking a sense of discomfort and unresolved struggle.
