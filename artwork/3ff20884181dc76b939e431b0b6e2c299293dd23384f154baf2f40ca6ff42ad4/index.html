Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette—soft hues of pale aqua and mist gray representing vulnerability—contrasts sharply with the bold alarm red and deep ink blue that symbolize strength. The layout emphasizes two pulses that interact in the center, suggesting a collision of feelings, while maintaining a quiet negative space around them. Broken pixels break the symmetry and provide an imperfect beauty, echoing real-life struggles. The diagonal flow creates a sense of rising energy against stability, illustrating the duality of exposure versus shelter.
