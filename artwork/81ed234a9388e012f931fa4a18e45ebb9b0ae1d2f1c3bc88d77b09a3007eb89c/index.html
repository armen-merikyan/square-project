Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between love and betrayal, represented through a cool color base (grays) contrasted by warm accents (reds and pinks). The palette conveys warmth yet carries an underlying chill, symbolizing the tension between connection and disruption. The pattern features asymmetrical placements and broken pixels, which create a compelling visual conflict, mirroring the theme of 'symmetry_vs_break.' The use of negative space evokes an 'empty room' feeling, enhancing the sense of loss and solitude.
