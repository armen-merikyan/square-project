Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes vulnerability's fragility against strength's density. The palette of cosmic indigo, void black, star white, cold gold, and deep teal reflects the awe-terror dichotomy, with darker tones suggesting shelter and lighter ones embodying exposure. The central collision occurs in the grid's heart—where cold gold dots resemble a fragile beacon, interrupted by crack-like patterns in deep teal. This setup emphasizes the contrast between exposed connectivity and the weight of hidden strength, creating a rhythmic tension encapsulating the seed's themes.
