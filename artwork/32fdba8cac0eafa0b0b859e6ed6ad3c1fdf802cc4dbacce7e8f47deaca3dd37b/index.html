Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a nuanced struggle between safety and exposure. The palette, combining warm browns, soft greens, and pale whites, suggests both a protective enclave and an open, vulnerable center. The collision arises from a central vertical division where the maze-like order of safety is interrupted by stepwell patterns of exposure, creating a tension between dense shelter and airy openness. Broken pixels add an element of imperfection, reinforcing the fragility of the depicted emotions.
