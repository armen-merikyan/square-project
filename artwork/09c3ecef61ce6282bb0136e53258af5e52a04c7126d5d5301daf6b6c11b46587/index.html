Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between rage and calm, with the outer border representing the intensity of anger in deep reds and blacks while the center embodies a lighter, more serene quality with pale blue and white. The chaotic arrangement of dots on the edges collides with the structured frame of the core, illustrating the emotional pressure of rage as it pulses into the calm, creating a compelling contrast that invites contemplation of release. The layout guides the viewer's eye from chaos to tranquility, revealing an abstract journey of emotional collision and resolution.
