Artist Statement

This abstract artwork captures the emotional tension between clarity and chaos. The selected palette of navy, black, dim cyan, and white reflects the loneliness of a clear signal amidst overwhelming noise. The layout emphasizes a diagonal contrast, with the 'signal' occupying the lower left as clean lines against the 'noise' in the upper right, creating a dynamic interaction. The use of broken pixels introduces disruptions that symbolize interference, emphasizing the theme of memory versus erasure as these influences collide with harsh static patterns. The composition reads as an ongoing conflict between readable rhythm and disorienting depth, portraying the struggle to maintain clarity amidst confusion.
