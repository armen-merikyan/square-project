Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between tenderness and rage, using a palette that juxtaposes the warmth of red (rage) with the calmness of pale blue (tenderness). The composition features a centralized collision between sharp jagged red patterns and softer cracked textures in blue, creating a dynamic interaction that suggests both pressure and release. Broken pixels are employed as intentional disruptions, enhancing the abstract nature and allowing for a sense of unresolved emotion. The intersection of these emotional elements fosters an engaging visual narrative about the struggle and coexistence of opposing feelings.
