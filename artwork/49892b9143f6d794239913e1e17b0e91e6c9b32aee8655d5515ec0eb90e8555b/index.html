Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the tension between trust and suspicion, depicted through a contrasting palette of serene and urgent colors. The cool blues symbolize trust, creating a calm base, while the alarm red cuts through as a burst of suspicion, creating interruption. The layout follows a spiral pressure, reflecting a central collision where the chaos of suspicion meets the order of trust. This pattern allows for a visual representation of resilience and fragility through the irregularities of the chosen shapes, while broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, embodying life's disruptions. The arrangement creates a rhythmic flow emphasizing both comfort and anxiety.
