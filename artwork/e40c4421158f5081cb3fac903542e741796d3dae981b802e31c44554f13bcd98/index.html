Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between envy and gratitude through contrasting colors and a layout that emphasizes negative space. The subtle colors from the 'broken_memory' palette suggest a sense of nostalgia and softness, while the fractures formed by lighter shades disrupt the calm, mirroring the internal conflict of rising hope against the falling fear. The pixel arrangements create a dynamic flow, with a sense of softness at the center and sharper contrast at the edges, embodying the emotional dialogue inherent in the seed.
