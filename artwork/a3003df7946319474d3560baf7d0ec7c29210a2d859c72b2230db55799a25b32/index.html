Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, represented through a palette that juxtaposes coolness with warmth. The muted darkness of 'near_black' and softness of 'pale_mint' evoke feelings of longing, while the rich 'storm_gray' suggests strength and stability. The main collision occurs in the central double pulse, where patterns of 'fault_line' and 'checker' create visual tension and overlap, symbolizing the inner conflict of desire versus determination. The composition balances dense areas with open spaces, reflecting the push-and-pull of intimacy and distance.
