Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between safety and exposure. The palette features muted greens and browns for safety, while pale skin and cold whites represent vulnerability. The collision occurs off-center, creating a protected space against an encroaching texture, echoing the contrasts of softness and severity. The jagged edges evoke a sense of interruption, delivering a visual narrative of pressure yielding to release within the confines of a designated shape, reinforcing the emotional duality of the seed.
