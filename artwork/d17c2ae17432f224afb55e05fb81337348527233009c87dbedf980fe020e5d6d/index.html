Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a juxtaposition of love and betrayal, expressed through contrasting colors and textures. The palette utilizes soft pinks and deep maroons, signifying warmth and emotional depth yet also a hint of darkness. The layout initiates from the bottom left, invoking a sense of ‘bottom to top’ struggle, while the jagged edges represent burnt and scarred emotions. The pattern flows with checker-like disruptions to symbolize conflict and misalignment, capturing the essence of ‘crosshatch against noise.’ The collisions occur at the interface of warm and cool colors, suggesting moments of tension and resolution in a fragmented emotional landscape.
