Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between order and chaos, representing the struggle of a discernible signal amidst a backdrop of static noise. The chosen palette of greens and blues symbolizes clarity and tranquility (signal) against darker greys and blacks that signify confusion and interference (noise). The arrangement features a pronounced upward flow echoing hope with bright accents piercing through denser static interference, creating moments of clarity amidst overwhelming noise. The central collision of bright emerald green against deep black highlights the central struggle, while random placements of static grey introduce imperfection, resonating with the concept of memory and interruption.
