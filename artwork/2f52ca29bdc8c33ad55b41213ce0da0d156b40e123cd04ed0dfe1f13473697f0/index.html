Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between love and betrayal through contrasting colors and patterns. The warm pink and red evoke love, while dark maroon and gray introduce feelings of betrayal. The layout employs a nearly mirrored arrangement, disrupted by a few random pixels, symbolizing betrayal's interruption of love. The dense cluster of colors on one side and empty spaces on the other highlight a contrast in emotional density, reflecting the narrative of conflict. The pattern of dots represents the fragile memories of a paired relationship, disrupted by sharp edges signifying the pain of betrayal.
