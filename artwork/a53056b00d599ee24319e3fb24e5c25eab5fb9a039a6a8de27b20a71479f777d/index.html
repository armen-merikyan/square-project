Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension of trust and suspicion through contrasting colors and arrangements. The palette of deep navy and black represents the weight of suspicion, while light dim cyan symbolizes fleeting moments of trust. The placement of colors in a zigzag flow creates a rhythmic collision, where quiet spaces are shattered by clusters of darker tones, embodying the pressures inherent in relationships. This pattern reveals the inner turmoil of balancing trust against a backdrop of uncertainty.
