Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a juxtaposition between love's warmth and the cold traces of betrayal. The chosen palette of pinks and dark maroons represents affection overwhelmed by a backdrop of gray, signifying uncertainty and turmoil. The central clash occurs along the diagonal line where warm hues begin to fade into colder shades, suggesting ruined symmetry and emotional conflict. The arrangement incorporates broken pixels to introduce imperfection, aligning with the theme of disruption as signals of betrayal interrupt the flow of love. This design evokes a complex emotional sphere, balancing warmth against shadows of doubt.
