Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional dichotomy of defiance and submission through a structured yet chaotic composition. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon evokes themes of love and betrayal, suggesting a turbulent emotional landscape. The left-to-right flow represents a struggle between forces; the dark hues weigh down the lower half, while the lighter colors disrupt and break through, introducing tension. The checkerboard pattern symbolizes the harsh contrasts of submission against a rigid structure, while the ring pattern reflects defiance and strength in the centered elements. Broken pixels introduce an intentional imperfection, hinting at unresolved emotions, enhancing the narrative of conflict.
