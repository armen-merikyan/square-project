Artist Statement

This abstract composition contrasts the emotional weights of pressure and quietness through a dense accumulation of colors on one side, gradually dispersing into lighter hues. The palette combines muted grays and slight warm accents to embody the rumbling tension of pressure against the soft release of quiet spaces. The main collision occurs diagonally, where darker colors push against lighter areas, creating a visual argument between two emotional states. The layout follows a 'scattered_repair' concept, representing a gradual shift from crowding to calm, hence encapsulating the essence of the seed in a visually striking format.
