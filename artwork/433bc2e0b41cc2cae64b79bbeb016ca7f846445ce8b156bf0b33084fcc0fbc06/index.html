Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional essence of 'longing vs resolve' through a dynamic interplay of muted and vibrant colors. The palette of pinks, reds, and grays reflects feelings of warmth and tension. The upper half, representing longing, embodies a braided pattern that suggests an unfinished approach, while the lower half, embodying resolve, uses a stair-like structure symbolizing clarity and strength. The intentional use of broken pixels introduces imperfections that evoke the theme of repair amidst damage. Main contrast occurs between the soft pinks and sharp reds, centered to illustrate the collision of these opposing emotions.
