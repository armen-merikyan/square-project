Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the artwork is a juxtaposition of fear and hope, embodying the struggle between darkness and light. The palette combines black and dark violet to symbolize fear, while soft gold and pale green represent the budding hope. The main contrast occurs between the heavy dark colors clustering in the corners and the lighter, upward-rising elements in the center, echoing a rising signal amidst the heavy weight. The zigzag flow contributes to a sense of tension and movement as if battling between the elements of fear and hope, while the broken pixels introduce imperfection and chaos, aligning with the complexity of the emotional theme. Each row has been carefully structured to maintain visual balance and abstract storytelling without forming explicit imagery.
