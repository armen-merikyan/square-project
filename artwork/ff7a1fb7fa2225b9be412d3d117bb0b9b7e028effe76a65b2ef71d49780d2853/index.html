Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting color patterns and placements. The palette, anchored in dark navy and black with accents of dim cyan and white, conveys depth and tension. The main collision occurs in the center, with stronger noise patterns interrupting softer fault lines, suggesting a dynamic interplay of pressure versus release. The rising signal layout metaphorically represents hope amidst chaos, reflecting the essence of the seed.
