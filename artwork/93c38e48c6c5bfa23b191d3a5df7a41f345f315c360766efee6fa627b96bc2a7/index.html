Artist Statement

This abstract piece embodies the emotional duality of confusion and clarity. The palette combines warm yellows and oranges representing clarity with deep blues and muted purples symbolizing confusion, creating an interplay of tension and warmth. The layout, a split vertical design, emphasizes the stark contrast: the left half feels vibrant and symmetrical, while the right half becomes disordered with irregular placements, suggesting fragmentation. Broken pixels disrupt the pattern, echoing feelings of imperfection and chaos. This arrangement evokes a pulse, as if the artwork itself breathes amidst the contrasting emotions, capturing the essence of the seed in a visual dialogue of clarity versus confusion.
