Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic contrast between envy and gratitude, visualized through an interplay of warm yellows and oranges against cool deep blues and muted purples. The palette signifies warmth striving upward, battling against the cool depths representing fall and release. Main collisions happen where warm tones disrupt the cooler background, suggesting emotional peaks and troughs. The layout captures the central 'bridge' shape abstracted in soft curvature, signifying pressure points where emotions collide. Broken pixels introduce imperfect interruptions into the grid pattern of envy, complemented by fractal cracks of gratitude, thus emphasizing the complexities of emotional landscapes.
