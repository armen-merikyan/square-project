Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the interplay between clarity and chaos, represented by the use of contrasting colors. The palette of navy, black, and dim cyan mirrors the deep pulls of static noise and the sharp accents of the signal, creating a palpable tension. The predominant dark elements fill one corner, symbolizing pressure, while the brighter accents pulse in the center, hinting at moments of clarity amongst the noise. The chaotic 'shatter' and 'target' patterns collide, generating interruption and echo, while leaving intentional gaps for negative space to breathe.
