Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tension between loneliness and connection, representing the fragmentation felt in solitude while also hinting at moments of interaction. The palette features vibrant lime and electric blue, symbolizing connection and energy, contrasted by deep blacks and magentas representing isolation and weight. The sharp and soft contrast between colors creates a dynamic rhythm, while the broken pixels deliver a sense of imperfection, evoking memories that are both vibrant and fading. The arrangement forms a wave flowing from left to right, reflecting the invasion of connection into the quiet, hollow spaces.
