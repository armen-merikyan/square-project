Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of emptiness and desire through the juxtaposition of warm and cool tones. Black and pale blue serve to create a contrast that reflects depth and longing, while white hues introduce a sense of space and fragmentation. The broken pixel patterns introduce intentional imperfection, symbolizing the interruptions of desire amidst the void. The composition flows from a dense center indicative of the heart's yearning to more airy edges, emphasizing the pull between fullness and absence.
