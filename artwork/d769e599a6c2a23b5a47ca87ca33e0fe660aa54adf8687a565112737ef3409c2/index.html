Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents the clash of defiance and submission, visualized through a rising signal from the bottom, suggesting hope and escape. The palette of signal green, electric blue, and hot magenta embodies vibrancy amidst muted shades of dead black and static gray, reinforcing the idea of noise versus clarity. The layout moves upward, echoing a dynamic tension, particularly where bright accents collide with darker backgrounds in the center. Broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, symbolizing the struggle that persists in trying to rise above chaos.
