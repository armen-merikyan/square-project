Artist Statement

This artwork evokes the emotional tension between softness and severity through a contrasting palette of muted and intense colors. The softer elements in powder pink and warm white represent forgiveness, while the sharp crimson and carbon black depict guilt and severity. Central to the composition is a collision of these contrasting elements, with areas of dense pressure from the severity patterns interrupted by lighter tones suggesting quiet spaces or reflections. This interplay embodies the seed’s themes of emotional conflict and resolution, creating a pulsating rhythm across the square.
