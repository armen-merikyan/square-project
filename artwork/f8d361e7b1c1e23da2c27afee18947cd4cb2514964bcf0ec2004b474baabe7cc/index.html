Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between clarity and chaos, encapsulating the struggle of a clear signal within a tumultuous noise. The color palette consists of deep navy and black reflecting the dense noise, while dim cyan and white serve as accents, reflecting moments of clarity. The main collision occurs in the center, where the rhythmic echo of clear white pulses through fields of darker tones, interrupted by broken pixels that symbolize scars and interruptions in the signal, aligning with the seed's themes of friction and interplay of emotion.
