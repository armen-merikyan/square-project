Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts control and surrender, creating a dynamic interplay between structured forms and soft yields. The palette, 'melancholy_wonder', with colors like rain blue and soft cyan, embodies a serene yet poignant atmosphere, fostering both reflection and movement. The main collision occurs as the cool blue floods from the top left, facing the warmer lavender gray which suggests containment. The pattern reflects a central overfilled space that symbolizes both pressure and release—echoing the themes of overflow and control. The broken pixels disrupt the flow, introducing imperfections that suggest the weight of emotion in flux, resonating with the seed's narrative.
