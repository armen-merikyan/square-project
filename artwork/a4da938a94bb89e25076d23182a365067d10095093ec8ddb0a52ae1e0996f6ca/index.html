Artist Statement

This composition navigates the emotional spectrum between panic and control, embracing the chaos of overlapping symbols and gestures. The black and red hues represent panic's intensity, while pale blue and white symbolize calmness and control. The pattern of dense, jagged stripes on one side gives a chaotic feeling that contrasts with the structured grid of the opposite side, emphasizing the emotional collision. The composition rises and falls, reminiscent of an unstable orbit, with broken pixels introducing a sense of imperfection that enthralls the viewer.
