Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional juxtaposition of awe and terror through a carefully arranged palette of red, black, pale blue, and white. The outer squares convey a sense of order with soft edges in a stable rhythm, while the inner squares are chaotic with broken dot patterns that introduce tension. This interplay serves to highlight the contrast between the calmness of stability and the tumult of chaos, epitomizing the emotional essence of the seed text.
