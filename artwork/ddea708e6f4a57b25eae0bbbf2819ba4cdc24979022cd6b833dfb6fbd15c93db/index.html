Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through a dynamic interplay of dark and light colors. The chosen palette, consisting of navy, black, single white, and dim cyan, reflects a balance of weight and lightness, conveying a sense of depth and contrast. The layout, split vertically, allows for a sharp transition from the left's maze-like loneliness to the right's spark of connection with the bright white pixel interrupting the weight of black. The broken pixels throughout the composition suggest fragmented memories, enhancing the theme of connection amidst isolation.
