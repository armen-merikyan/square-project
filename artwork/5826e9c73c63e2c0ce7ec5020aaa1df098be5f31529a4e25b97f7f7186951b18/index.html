Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a muted color palette that highlights the interplay of light and dark. The dominant use of dark navy promotes a sense of depth and isolation, while off-white accents suggest bursts of fragile hope. The central cluster of faint peach acts as a rising signal, breaking through the dense gray lattice of vulnerability. The asymmetric placement of colors speaks to the uneven balance between protection and exposure. Broken pixels are scattered to create intentional disruptions, symbolizing scarring and resilience.
