Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a delicate balance between vulnerability and strength. The palette 'melancholy_wonder' features muted blues and soft grays that create a serene yet introspective atmosphere, with 'rain_blue' and 'soft_cyan' representing vulnerability through echoing patterns and light exposure. The main contrast occurs in the center where dense blocks of color collide with lighter, more fragmented hues, suggesting a struggle between hard edges and soft breaks. This aligns with the seed's themes of exposure and shelter, creating a visual narrative of tension and support.
