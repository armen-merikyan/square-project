Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the conflict between restlessness and peace, using a palette that juxtaposes warm and cool colors to highlight tension. The darks (from 'stone_gray' and 'rust') suggest weight and restlessness, while the light colors (from 'bone_white' and 'small_gold') represent fleeting moments of tranquility. The main collision occurs in the center, where the crack pattern of restless colors interrupts the structured negative cross of peaceful colors, creating a visual dialogue of opposing feelings, echoing the essence of the seed.
