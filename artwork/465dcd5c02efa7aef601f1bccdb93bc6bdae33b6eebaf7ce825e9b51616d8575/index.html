Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between 'longing' and 'resolve' through a split horizontal layout. The top half utilizes cooler colors from the 'signal_noise' palette to evoke a sense of yearning and distance, represented through the crosshatch pattern. The bottom half portrays a stronger, more defined energy with brighter accents, utilizing the target pattern to illustrate a sense of resolution and clarity. The gentle collision of colors in the center reflects the intersection of hope and determination, while the strategically placed broken pixels introduce a necessary imperfection, mirroring the fragility of emotion. The balance of these elements results in a narrative of striving toward serenity amidst chaos.
