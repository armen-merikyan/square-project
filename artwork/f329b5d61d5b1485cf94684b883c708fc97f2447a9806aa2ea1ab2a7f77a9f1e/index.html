Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette includes clean white for clarity, ice blue for cold vulnerability, and warning orange as a striking accent representing strength. The main collision between patterns occurs around the center, where the strength of crosshatch interacts with the delicate softness of dot patterns, creating a dynamic quality that embodies the push and pull of emotions. The choice of negative space around these patterns enhances the feeling of exposure and safety, resonating with the seed's themes.
