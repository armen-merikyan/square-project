Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece balances trust and suspicion, creating a sense of tension between stability and unease. The palette features pale mint and soft sky as soothing fields contrasting against storm gray and near black that evoke shadows and doubt. The main collision occurs in the center, where the dark colors interrupt the lighter hues, echoing the struggle between clarity and noise. The diagonal flow with broken pixels represents the resilience and fragility found in navigating this emotional landscape.
