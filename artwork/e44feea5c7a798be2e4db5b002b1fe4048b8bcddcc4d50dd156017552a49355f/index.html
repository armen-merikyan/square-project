Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dialogue between softness and severity. The contrast in the palette, with powder pinks and sharp crimson against steel gray and carbon black, reflects a duality of warmth and coldness. The main collision occurs in the center, where the mirrored softness meets a more fragmented, severe pattern, creating tension and movement. The layout suggests a small core of softness that is hemmed in by a surrounding noise of severity, representing the emotional interplay of protection versus vulnerability.
