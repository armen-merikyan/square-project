Artist Statement

This composition explores the duality of 'signal' and 'noise' through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, incorporating navy and dim cyan, reflects a lonely yet vibrant emotional landscape, symbolizing clarity amid static. The center hosts the main collision of bright signal colors against muted noise, creating tension and dynamism, while intentional broken pixels disrupt harmony, signifying interference. The layout's mirroring quality allows echoes of color to resonate on both sides, embodying feedback and reflection in its chaotic order.
