Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between defiance and submission, using a palette that contrasts dark and light to reflect tension. The choice of colors like dark violet conveys resistance, while soft gold represents vulnerability—a visual interplay that suggests a battlefield. Each color choice creates a collision, especially where the vibrant tones clash against the muted shades in the center. This arrangement fosters a sense of drama and unresolved motion, echoing the theme of conflict, as patterns of lattice and rain converge unevenly within the defined edges.
