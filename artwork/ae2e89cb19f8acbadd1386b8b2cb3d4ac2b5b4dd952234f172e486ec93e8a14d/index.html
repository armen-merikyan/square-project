Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of restlessness versus peace through contrasting colors and patterns. The chosen palette of greens, golds, and creams reflects both vibrancy and calmness. The layout features two small centers that pulse toward each other, creating a rhythmic tension throughout the composition. The prominent use of negative space heightens the feeling of unrest in certain areas, while the gentle glitch patterns suggest fleeting moments of calm amidst the chaos. Imperfections introduced through broken pixels symbolize disruptions that evoke memories, aligning with the seed's themes of movement and emotion.
