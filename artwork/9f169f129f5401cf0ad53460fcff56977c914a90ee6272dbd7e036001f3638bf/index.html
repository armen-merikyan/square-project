Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes love and betrayal, reflected in the warm colors of pink and red representing love, interrupted by the coolness of gray, suggesting betrayal. The arrangement contrasts the solidity of structured grid patterns (love) against the chaotic flame motifs (betrayal), creating a visual narrative of conflict. The broken pixels add a sense of rawness and imperfection, while the layout ensures a significant negative space that embodies loneliness, imparting a sense of emotional vacuity amid tension. The layout, flowing from the bottom to top, echoes a rising emotional struggle, creating depth and height within the contained atmosphere of the empty room.
