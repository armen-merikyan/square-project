Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the tension between loneliness and connection, seen through its dual-patterns and color density. The dark navy and black signify solitude, while the dim cyan introduces a hint of connection, creating contrast within the dense arrangement. The pixels are arranged to form an off-center heart fragment, symbolizing a yearning for connection within isolating circumstances. Broken pixels add imperfection, hinting at hidden vulnerabilities. The contrast between weighted sections and sparse spaces evokes a narrative of longing and fleeting contact.
