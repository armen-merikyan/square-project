Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between love and betrayal, echoed through the chosen palette of pinks and maroons, creating warmth contrasted by cool gray. The diagonal flow introduces a tension where the warm colors cluster heavily in one corner, suggesting love's intensity, while the gray and dark maroon spiral outward, serving as a quiet release. The broken pixel elements create imperfections that symbolize betrayal, resulting in a dynamic yet unresolved composition that expresses the complexity of emotions in a succinct abstract form.
