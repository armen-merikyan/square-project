Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between fear and hope, visualized through jagged edges symbolizing anxiety and soft golds representing hope. The palette creates a balance, with black and dark violet invoking a sense of weight and pressure contrasting against hints of pale green and soft gold, suggesting uplifting potential. The central conflict occurs between the dense patterns of fear represented by noise along the edges and the flowing pattern of hope symbolizing an upward journey breaking through the center. The abstract arrangement emphasizes the struggle between these emotional states, with a deliberate choice of broken pixels to indicate imperfection and uncertainty.
