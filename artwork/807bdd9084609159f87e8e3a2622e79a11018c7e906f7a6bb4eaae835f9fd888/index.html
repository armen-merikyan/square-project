Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between loneliness and the desire for connection. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon creates a resonance of warmth and betrayal. Main contrast occurs centrally where vibrant reds burst against muted grays, symbolizing the clash between hidden emotions and exposed yearnings. This interaction shapes the abstract pattern as pixels pulse downward, hinting at the weight of tears and the quiet echo of connection in a stark visual landscape.
