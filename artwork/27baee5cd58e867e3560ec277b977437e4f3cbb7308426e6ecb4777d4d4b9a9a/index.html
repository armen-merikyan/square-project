Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork captures the tension between longing and resolve, translated into a color palette that juxtaposes warmth with darker, cooler elements. The primary color themes of gray and dark maroon suggest a somber yet intense emotional struggle, while brighter shades of red and pink provide bursts of intensity that reflect moments of clarity and resolve. The layout rises, creating a sense of upward movement that embodies hope, interrupted by broken pixels representing imperfections and scars, which align with the seed's themes. This composition features contrasting sections where the maze pattern of resolve intersects sharply with the fragmented longing, creating a dynamic and complex visual narrative.
