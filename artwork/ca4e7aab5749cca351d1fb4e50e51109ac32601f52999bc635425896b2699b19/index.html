Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the chaos of panic and the stability of control, reflected through a palette that contrasts vibrant electric blue and magenta with grounding black and lime. The main collision occurs at the center, where dense colors evoke a spark of tension against a more subdued surrounding, symbolizing the push and pull of different emotional states. The jagged edges and patterns represent broken pixels, enhancing the visual chaos while still maintaining a sense of formality in the layout, akin to a structured yet chaotic argument grid.
