Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between quiet and pressure through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, consisting of muted tones like off-white and dust gray, creates a tranquil field, while compressed red and dark navy introduce intensity and pressure. The main collision occurs in the central-left section, where the tight, thread-like patterns of pressure intersect with the open, ladder-like structure of the quiet, embodying a beautiful chaos that resonates with the essence of the seed. The uneven borders enhance the sense of disruption and tension.
