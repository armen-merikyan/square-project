Artist Statement

This piece represents the emotional dynamics of vulnerability and strength through opposing yet intertwining patterns. The palette, 'mourning_renewal', comprises deep blues and muted earth tones to evoke a sense of conflict between stability and fragility. The contrast is primarily at the center, where soft breaks meet the bold solidity of darker colors, signifying the struggle of strength rising against the tide of vulnerability. The layout features an argument_grid where jagged pixel interruptions symbolize fleeting moments of tension and release, allowing for a visual narrative of emotional interplay.
