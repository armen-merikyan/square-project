Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the pull between longing and resolve, using a palette that juxtaposes warm, inviting colors with deep, somber tones to express strength versus vulnerability. The central area is densely packed with a glitch-like pattern of oranges and corals, representing urgency and desire, while the edges soften into quiet spaces of blue and charcoal. The contrast occurs primarily in the center, where the vibrant colors collide against the subtle border, embodying a dynamic tension that mirrors the essence of repair versus damage.
