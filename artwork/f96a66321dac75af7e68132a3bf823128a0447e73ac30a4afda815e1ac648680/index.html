Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension of pressure and quiet through dense clusters of deep colors clashing against lighter fields. The dark navy represents pressure, while the faint peach and off-white provide a soft contrast, suggesting the movement from chaos to tranquility. The grid invites a sense of symmetry, yet upon closer inspection, the broken pixels disrupt consistency, embodying confusion against clarity. The main collision occurs in the center, where pressure meets release, illustrating the struggle between intensity and calm.
