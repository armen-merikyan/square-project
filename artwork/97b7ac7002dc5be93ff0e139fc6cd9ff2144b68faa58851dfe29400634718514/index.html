Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional interplay between shame and pride through a balance of dark and light tones. The palette, drawn from warm sepia and cool faded blue, accentuates the contrasts of feelings—dark hues representing shame and lighter tones symbolizing pride. The patterns of intersecting pressure create a rhythmic flow, with softer edges contributing to an overall sense of dynamic tension. Broken pixels add an element of imperfection, embodying the collisions of emotions, while negative spaces create breathing room, symbolizing release amidst pressure.
