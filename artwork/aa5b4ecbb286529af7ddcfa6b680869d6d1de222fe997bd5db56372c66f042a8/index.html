Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between nostalgia and progress, balanced through a muted palette of sepia and faded blues that evoke a sense of memory yet beckon forward movement. The layout divides the piece horizontally, with heavier, darker colors at the bottom symbolizing gravity and weight, while lighter shades above suggest a lift towards progress. The radial pattern for nostalgia contrasts with the more structured rings of progress, reflecting the tension between past comfort and future ambition. The deliberate broken pixels introduce imperfections, resonating with the theme of fragmented memories and transitions.
