Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength. The selected palette, 'broken_memory', features muted tones (sepia, tan, dust_gray, and faded_blue) creating an ambience of introspection and fragility. The application of two contrasting patterns—'negative_cross' for strength and 'scar' for vulnerability—results in a visible collision at the center, where stronger tones break through softer shades. The layout features a broken frame that allows light elements to bleed into darker paths, representing exposure and shelter. The arrangement of colors suggests movement from heavy density in the corners to lighter hues in the center, echoing the emotions linked with resilience amidst fragility.
