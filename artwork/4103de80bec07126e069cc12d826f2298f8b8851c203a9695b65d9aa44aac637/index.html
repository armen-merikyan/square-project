Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between joy and grief through a contrasting palette of vibrant yellows and muted purples. The arrangement features a flowing pattern of warm tones at the top, suggesting joy’s expanding nature, which meets a dense, fragmented lower section in cooler shades, symbolizing the weight of grief. The interplay creates a hypnotic wave effect, resembling an ancient battle of emotions. The absence of outlines allows for a blending of borders, emphasizing the fluidity and complexity of feelings. Broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, representing life's unpredictability and chaos intertwined within emotional experiences.
