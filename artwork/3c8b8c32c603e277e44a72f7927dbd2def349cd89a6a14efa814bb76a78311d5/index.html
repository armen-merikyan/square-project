Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrast between fear and hope, represented through a split vertical layout that segregates dark, dense colors on the left with lighter, illuminated hues on the right. The palette consists of black and dark violet on one side to evoke feelings of unease, while soft gold and pale green offer a sense of uplift and optimism on the other. The glowing edges imply potential escape routes from darkness, while the jagged and soft textures create visual tension that resonates with the themes of inherent struggle. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfection, highlighting the unpredictability of emotions. This arrangement fosters a rhythmic ebb and flow between fear and hope, crafting an abstract dialogue on the dynamic interplay of human emotions.
