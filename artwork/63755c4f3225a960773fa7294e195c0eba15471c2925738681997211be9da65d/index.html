Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a complex interplay between longing and resolve, suggested by a palette that oscillates between void black and cosmic indigo. The contrast is achieved with cold gold accents against deeper colors, forming a dialogue of hope amidst despair. The central collision occurs in the composition’s hub, where dark tones meet bright edges, echoing a suffused pulse. The arrangement features cracked patterns symbolizing longing, while the fault lines signify resolve. Negative space is embraced to convey a sense of distance, resonating with the feelings of incomplete restoration.
