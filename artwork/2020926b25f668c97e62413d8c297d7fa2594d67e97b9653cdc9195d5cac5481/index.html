Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional contrast of loneliness and connection using a palette of warm oranges and deep blues to invoke feelings of warmth versus isolation. The placement of brighter colors in the corners symbolizes connections sparking in a landscape of muted tones that represent solitude. A pattern of mirrored shapes extends from the center, suggesting the struggle of reaching out versus retreating inward, creating a tension that is both sharp and soft, as highlighted by the zigzag flow, which feels both dynamic and unresolved.
