Artist Statement

The artwork encapsulates a tension between clarity and confusion, represented by structured patterns and jagged edges. The color palette of electric blue, lime, black, and magenta reflects a high-energy emotional dichotomy, where electric blue and lime signify clarity and vibrancy, while black brings depth and chaos. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, hinting at flaws within symmetry. Central to the composition is a balance between dense color patches and negative space, evoking a sense of struggle between calm and rage, simplicity and disorder, reflecting the seed's themes of pulse and rhythm.
