Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a balance of soft and strong tones. The halo of powder pink surrounds deep carbon black and sharp crimson, creating a dreamlike contrast. The layout centers on a corner that feels burdened yet diverges into lighter swathes, suggesting a struggle between density and emptiness: longing is represented by the halo pattern, while resolve manifests as a strong ash field. The echoing marks create an intimate distance, aligning with the seed's themes of unresolved emotional currents.
