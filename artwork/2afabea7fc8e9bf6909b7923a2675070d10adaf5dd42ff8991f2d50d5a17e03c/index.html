Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between longing and resolve, illustrated through the chosen palette of resilience and fragility. The moss green and rust represent the interplay of desire and damage, while the bone white acts as a quiet field that balances the composition. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense segments of color collide, suggesting a pulse or thread, while broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, enhancing the feeling of repair amidst damage. The layout emphasizes separation yet connection, echoing the theme of searching for resolution. The interplay of feathered edges contrasts with sharp central figures, creating a dynamic rhythm of the artwork.
