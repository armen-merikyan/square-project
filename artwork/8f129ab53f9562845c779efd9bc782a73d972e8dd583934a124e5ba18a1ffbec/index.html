Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional pull between vitality (spark) and decay (ash), using a palette that balances vibrant cosmic indigo and cold gold with the depths of void black, suggesting a contrast between hope and desolation. The jagged edges representing tension interrupt the calmness while the checker and thread patterns collide, predominantly favoring ash to reflect the weight of restraint. The two forces clash centrally, creating a visual narrative of resilience against fragility, echoed in the structure's layout, leaving room for negative space to breathe.
