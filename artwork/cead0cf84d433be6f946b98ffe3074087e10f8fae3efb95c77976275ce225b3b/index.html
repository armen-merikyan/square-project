Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic interplay between control and surrender. Warmer colors symbolize the pressure of control, while cooler hues represent surrender's gentle descent. The palette consists of sepia and faded blue shades, conveying both an organic and a memory-like quality. The main contrast occurs centrally where warm tones overflow from the structured rings into the soft islands, suggesting the emotional conflict of overextending versus containment. This tension translates into an abstract pattern through the descending pixel layout, evoking sensations of patterns colliding and interweaving amidst moments of both stillness and movement.
