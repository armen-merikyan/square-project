Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances the warmth of love with the cool detachment of betrayal. The palette of soft pinks and deep maroons evokes passion and pain, while the contrasting grays signify the chaos between order and disruption. The pattern features dense clusters of color interruption signifying emotional ruptures, creating an abstract visual representation of a heart fragmented by discord. The broken pixels add imperfection and depth, resonating with the theme of love and betrayal.
