Artist Statement

The emotional tone plays with the tension between 'envy' and 'gratitude', paired through contrasting colors. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green conveys depth and complexity, representing both oppressive feelings and moments of relief. The layout employs a vertical split, with the left side conveying chaotic disturbances through a 'cracked' pattern, while the right awakens a structured rhythm with a 'maze' appearance. The main contrast emerges at the center, where dark shades against lighter hues create a palpable collision of feelings, reminding of how conflicting emotions can both clash and harmonize.
