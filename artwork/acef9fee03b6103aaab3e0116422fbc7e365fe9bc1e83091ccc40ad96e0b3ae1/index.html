Artist Statement

The emotional tone of confusion versus clarity flows through the contrast of saturating warm colors against muted cool tones, representing the struggle between clarity and chaos. The warm palette of yellow and orange suggests turbulence, while deep blue adds depth, hinting at clarity amid confusion. This dynamic is captured in the diagonal rise flow and broken patterns, where stripes collide with radial motifs, causing a visual tension that encapsulates the seed's themes. The deliberate placement of colors creates a stark contrast at the center, where the hues collide and evoke a sense of unresolved emotion. The resulting composition suggests a movement from the chaotic edges toward a central clarity, yet remains abstract and non-representational.
