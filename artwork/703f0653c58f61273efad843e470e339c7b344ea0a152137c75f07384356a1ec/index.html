Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of tenderness and rage, with contrasting patterns flowing from left to right. The left side, in softer, muted tones, suggests a calm pressure, while the right bursts with explosive reds, symbolizing chaos. The palette of deep reds and soft blues speaks to the intense feelings of guilt and forgiveness intertwined, creating a sense of tension that collides at the central boundary. Broken pixels at the edges add an element of imperfection, enhancing the theme of emotional struggle.
