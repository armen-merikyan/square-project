Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional struggle between loneliness and connection. The palette combines vibrant magenta and electric blue with stark black, embodying the push and pull of emotional tension. The arrangement of colors creates a contrast between dense clusters of connection (top rows) and negative space representing loneliness (bottom rows). The off-center placement of dark shades suggests isolation while vibrant hues burst around it, signifying the yearning for connection amid solitude. This layout evokes themes of hidden desires and emotional exposure, reflecting the ambiguous relationship between being inside oneself and reaching outward.
