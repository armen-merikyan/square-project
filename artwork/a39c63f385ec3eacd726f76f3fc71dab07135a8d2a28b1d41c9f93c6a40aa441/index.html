Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the tension between restlessness and peace. The color palette of electric blue, lime, magenta, and black creates a vibrant dialogue, representing the energy of conflict and the calmness of resolution. A predominant dark field interplays with bright accents, suggesting an underlying chaos softened by moments of tranquility. The main collision occurs diagonally, with vibrant pixels emerging like sparks amidst a predominantly dark surface, creating an echo of movement and stillness, a metaphor for the push and pull of memory and motion.
