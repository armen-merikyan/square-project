Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between defiance and submission, with dense clusters of aggressive reds contrasting soft gray elements, symbolizing the push and pull of opposing forces. The selected palette reflects tension through vibrant reds and muted grays, suggesting warmth in conflict and calm in surrender. The main collision occurs around the center, where bold red shards cut against the tranquil gray ground, evoking a pulse through the channel layout. The deliberate inclusion of broken pixels interrupts the flow, echoing the idea of imperfection amidst strong emotional undercurrents.
