Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between softness and severity, utilizing muted tones of gray and stark contrasts of black and crimson to reflect the intricacies of guilt and forgiveness. The palette of soft powder pink and warm white embodies a comforting softness, while carbon black and sharp crimson introduce a severity that interrupts the gentle flow. The composition strategically uses a rising signal layout, where deeper, darker colors anchor the bottom, suggesting weight and gravity, while lighter tones pulse upward in a spiral rhythm, creating a dynamic interaction of hope and heaviness. This pattern, balanced by broken pixels, resonates with imperfections and echoes of past emotions, establishing a rich narrative of conflict and resolution.
