Artist Statement

The emotional tone stems from the contrast between the suffocating loneliness of dark colors and the subtle warmth of connection represented by lighter hues. The use of black, pale blue, and red forms a visual narrative of tension and resolution. The palette juxtaposes stark loneliness against potential connection, creating a dynamic framework. The center's darker tones hint at isolation, while the lighter borders suggest warmth, leading to a clash of feelings—a burst of isolation framed by the possibility of connection. The grid-like arrangement echoes organized chaos, with bursts of color representing memories of connection attempting to break through the weighted loneliness.
