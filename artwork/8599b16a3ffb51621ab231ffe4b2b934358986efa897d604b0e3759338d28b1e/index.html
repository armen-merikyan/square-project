Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of restlessness and peace, utilizing warm and cool tones to represent tension and tranquility. The palette comprises earthier tones, like rose clay and warm shadow, juxtaposed with distant lilac and cool blue, hinting at both closeness and distance. The arrangement features spiraling patterns to illustrate the restless energy clashing with moments of calm, focused at the center where contrasts collide, suggesting a perpetual push and pull. Broken pixels intentionally disrupt the almost organic flow of color, embodying the feeling of interrupted thoughts and fragmented memories.
