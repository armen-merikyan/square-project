Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional struggle between defiance and submission through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of black, navy, and dim cyan conveys a sense of isolation and depth, while the intentional placement of light and dark creates a visual tension. The asymmetrical pattern, balancing broken dots against rhythmic stripes, echoes the conflict of love and betrayal. The bottom-weighted layout emphasizes the heaviness of submission, while the scattered upper elements depict the lightness of defiance, creating a dynamic visual story within the abstract constraints of the seed.
