Artist Statement

This composition explores the clash of emotions represented by the colors in the 'joy_grief' palette. The placements evoke a battle between desire (represented by warm yellows and oranges) and emptiness (deep blues and muted purples). The layout of patterns reflects an argument between these two forces, with sharp contrasts in color density representing emotional pressure and lightness. The broken pixel effect adds imperfection, symbolizing the real human experience amid conflict. The soft edges offer a sense of fluidity, echoing the emotional waves throughout the piece.
