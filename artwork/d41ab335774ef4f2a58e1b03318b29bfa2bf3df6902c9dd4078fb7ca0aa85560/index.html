Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between trust and suspicion, expressed through the contrasting use of a soft glow against hard shadows. The palette of pale aqua and mist gray softens the tension, while alarm red and ink blue introduce interruptions. The layout features falling pixels, evoking the feeling of descent and fragmentation, with the conflict taking place primarily in the center where dark colors collide with lighter tones, suggesting a dialogue between resilience and fragility.
