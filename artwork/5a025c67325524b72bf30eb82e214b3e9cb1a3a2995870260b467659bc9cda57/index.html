Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tug-of-war between certainty and curiosity. The chosen palette of mourning black, deep blue, and pale yellow creates a somber yet hopeful contrast. The main collision occurs with a bold central focus of pale yellow, strategically placed in the center, clashing with the deep blue of the surrounding pressure, resonating the tension of rising signals amidst the chaos. The spiral pressure layout emphasizes movement from a contained core outward, invoking the idea of expanding curiosity facing solid boundaries of certainty, while broken pixels create interruptions symbolizing fleeting moments of uncertainty.
