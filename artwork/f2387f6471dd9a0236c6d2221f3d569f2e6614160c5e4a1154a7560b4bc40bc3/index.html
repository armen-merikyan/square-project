Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between nostalgia and progress, represented through a palette of muted pinks and dark maroons that evoke warmth and depth. The main contrast exists where lighter colors disrupt the darker, heavier hues, creating a dynamic clash suggesting both the weight of the past and the brightness of potential futures. Inspired by the seed's themes, the grid-like layout symbolizes the structured, sometimes oppressive nature of progress, while the scattered patterns reflect the unpredictable essence of nostalgia. The mix of 'spark' and 'ring' patterns creates an atmosphere of unrest, embodying the duality of movement and stasis.
