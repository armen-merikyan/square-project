Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional struggle between love and betrayal through contrasting warm and muted colors. The pinks and reds represent love and warmth, while the dark maroon and gray signify betrayal and despair. The pixel arrangement balances dense clusters of warm tones against larger areas of empty space, underscoring the tension between connection and disconnection. The interrupted patterns evoke a sense of fractured relationships, embodying both the gentle and harsh elements of love's duality.
