Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between restlessness and peace. The warm colors symbolize peace, while the darker shades represent restlessness, creating a vibrant yet unstable backdrop. The contrasting patterns of 'stripes' and 'teeth' create a visual conflict that resonates with the diagonal argument of the seed, where the center seems to pulse with peace as the edges clash in motion. The chosen colors evoke depth and emotion, with warm ivory providing a quiet foundation amidst the volatility of deep wine and stain brown, enhancing the sense of inner turmoil while allowing for pockets of tranquility. The balance tilts slightly to the warm tones, establishing a predominance of peace over restlessness.
