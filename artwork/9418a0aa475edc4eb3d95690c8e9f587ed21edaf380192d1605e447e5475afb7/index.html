Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional contrast between trust (represented by warm tones) and suspicion (indicated by cool, muted colors). The carefully orchestrated arrangement features stripes and fault lines that create a visual tension. The ash field muted purples contrast with vibrant yellows and oranges, emphasizing the emotion pair. The central collision occurs where warm tones and cool shadows meet, forming a stark division, with broken pixels introducing imperfections, reinforcing the theme of resilience amidst fragility. Through these emotional expressions, the grid encapsulates a dialogue of trust and suspicion without literal imagery.
