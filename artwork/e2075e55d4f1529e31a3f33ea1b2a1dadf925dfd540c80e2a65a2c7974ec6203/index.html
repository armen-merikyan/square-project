Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of vulnerability and strength, with a predominance of dark colors representing strength and light touches for vulnerability. The pallette of navy, black, and dim cyan introduces a deep, heavy base, while strategically placed white pixels symbolize fragile moments and interruptions. The main collision occurs centrally, where the contrasting patterns—dense halos of strength overlapping with softer, jagged edges of vulnerability—interact to create a feeling of both pressure and release. The quiet channel layout of this piece ensures a balance of negative space, inviting contemplation of safety against exposure, echoing the conflicts present in the seed text.
