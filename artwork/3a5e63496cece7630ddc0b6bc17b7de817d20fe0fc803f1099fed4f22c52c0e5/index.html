Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between panic and control, illustrated through a layout that contrasts dense regions with spaces of breathing room. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, and charcoal embodies the fiery intensity of panic while gold introduces a touch of hope or control stability. The main collision creates tension in the center, with darker, chaotic colors surrounding lighter, controlled shades, giving a distinct feeling of an inner battle. The broken dot pattern on one side signifies the fragmentation of thoughts in panic, while the structured frame suggests stability against chaos, echoing the seed's theme.
