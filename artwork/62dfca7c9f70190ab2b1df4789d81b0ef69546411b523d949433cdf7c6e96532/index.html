Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between nostalgia and progress through contrasting color placements and patterns. The upper rows embody nostalgia with muted tones, reminiscent of faded memories, while the lower rows represent progress with brighter, more vibrant colors, suggesting energy and forward motion. The soft gradient drips downward, creating a visual 'falling' that captures the essence of transition. The interplay of radial and checkerboard patterns enhances the emotional narrative, suggesting both collision and harmony as different phases of experience overlap. The chosen palette of deep blue, muted purple, yellow, and orange creates a juxtaposition of warmth and coolness, manifesting in the push and pull of the emotional score.
