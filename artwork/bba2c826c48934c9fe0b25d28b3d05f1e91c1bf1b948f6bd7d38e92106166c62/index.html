Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional contrast between defiance and submission through a dynamic interplay of color and pattern. The palette consists of red oxide and muted cream to evoke strength and delicacy, respectively, while iron gray and blackened plum ground the piece, creating depth. The vibrant small orange acts as an accent, interrupting the subdued tones, reminiscent of sparks amid a dense background. The falling pixel layout suggests a downward pressure of submission, while the organized chaos of defiance represented by noise patterns in warmer colors collides in the center, creating a microcosm of tension in the abstract.
