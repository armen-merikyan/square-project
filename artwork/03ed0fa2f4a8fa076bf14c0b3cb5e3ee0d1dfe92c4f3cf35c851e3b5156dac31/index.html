Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the clash between defiance and submission, visualized through sharp contrasts and dense interactions. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold captures the warmth of defiance against the subdued nature of submission. The main conflict arises in the center, where bright, bold colors (representing defiance) collide with softer, muted colors (symbolizing submission). The upward flow of the row arrangement echoes a sense of rising hope, while the broken patterns represent the imperfections of this emotional struggle. Patterns of ladders and dots intertwine, creating an engaging dynamic that feels both structured and chaotic, reflecting the core ideas of tension and interdependence.
