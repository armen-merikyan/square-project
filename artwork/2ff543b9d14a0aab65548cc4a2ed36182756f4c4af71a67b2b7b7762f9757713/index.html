Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through a palette inspired by ancient fire. The use of dark red and charcoal represents the weight of isolation, while the bursts of burnt orange symbolize fleeting moments of connection. The irregular pattern evokes a crosshatch effect, suggesting the chaotic interplay between what is hidden and exposed. The contrast between dark and vibrant shades creates a dynamic pulse, embodying the struggle of finding warmth in solitude.
