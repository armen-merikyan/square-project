Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between rage and calm, with the central area reflecting a fiery intensity through sharp reds, while the periphery embodies a cool, serene blue. The palette juxtaposes hot reds and blacks with quieter pale blues and whites, creating a visual clash. The dense center disrupts the calm surrounding it, illustrating a contrast between aggression and serenity. The broken pixels act as disruptions, simulating scars of emotional turmoil, emphasizing the struggle for containment around the vibrant heart of rage. Instead of a central shape, the patterns of embers and dots abstractly echo the emotional collision, making this artwork a metaphor for contained chaos.
