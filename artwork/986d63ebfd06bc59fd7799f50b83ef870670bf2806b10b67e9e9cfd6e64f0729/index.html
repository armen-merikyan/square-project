Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts dramatically between the intense heat of rage and the soothing coolness of calm. The palette blends hot reds with soft pale blues, suggesting a conflict that is both energetic and subdued. The collision occurs in the center, where jagged shapes disrupt the calm braiding patterns, creating an imbalance that represents the struggle between containment and overflow. Patterns of falling pixels imply descent into chaos amidst an undercurrent of tranquility, embodying the dynamic interplay of the defined and the reckless.
