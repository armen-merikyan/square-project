Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece embodies the push and pull of longing and resolve, depicted through a contrast of dense and empty spaces. The palette of 'melancholy_wonder'—featuring blues, grays, and gentle purples—symbolizes the delicate balance between hope and uncertainty. The collision occurs in the center, where dense markings symbolize resolve while softer fields represent longing. This contrast captures the complexity of human emotion, creating a spiral of engagement and retreat.
