Artist Statement

The artwork encapsulates the emotional conflict of signal versus noise. The palette evokes a sense of digital dissonance with the stark contrast between electric blue as the main signaling tone and hot magenta representing static interference. The center pulse in green represents clarity trying to emerge through the noisy edges of deep black and static gray. The 'ladder' and 'braid' patterns overlap with one another, signifying the ongoing struggle of maintaining purity amidst chaos. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, embodying the idea of memory and its erasure. The layout features an engaging central pulse surrounded by a protective yet chaotic border, enhancing the overall emotional tension.
