Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between clarity and confusion, captured through a careful arrangement of color and disruption. The palette incorporates deep blacks to evoke tension against pale blues, symbolizing calmness amidst chaos. The main contrast occurs where the lighter colors intersect with vibrant reds, suggesting moments of interruption amidst the dark, dense areas. The layout mirrors but is intentionally broken, with misplaced hues creating a visual dissonance that encapsulates the essence of 'confusion vs clarity'. The pattern of noise interspersed with dots illustrates the pulse of emotion echoing within the composition.
