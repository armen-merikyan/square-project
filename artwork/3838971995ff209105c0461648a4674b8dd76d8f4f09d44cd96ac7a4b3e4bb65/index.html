Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances vulnerability and strength, with black and dark violet creating a strong base, representing hard edges and rooted contrast. Pale green introduces a soft, hopeful quality, symbolizing vulnerability but also offering lift against the dense darkness. The top half utilizes halo patterns reflecting fragility, while the bottom employs pulse marks denoting raw strength. The main collision occurs in the center where these patterns merge, precipitating both tension and connection amidst disarray, encapsulating the struggle represented by the seed.
