Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dichotomy between longing and resolve, captured through a mix of vibrant and muted colors. The palette consists of lime and electric blue as vivid accents, contrasting against black to highlight both urgency and calm. The main collision occurs at the center, where the green and blue hues create a pulse-like rhythm, visually representing the battle between serenity and urgency. The 'spiral_pressure' layout shifts focus towards a central point, enhancing the dynamic flow while honoring the broken pixel concept through intentional disruptions, mirroring the themes of damage and repair.
