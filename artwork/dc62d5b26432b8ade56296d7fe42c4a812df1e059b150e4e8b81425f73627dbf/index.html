Artist Statement

The artwork conveys a dynamic tension between defiance and submission through contrasting color patterns. The palette, representing 'joy_grief', pairs warm yellows and oranges against cooler deep blues and muted purples, illustrating emotional collisions. The main contrast occurs where the angular defiance of the outer colors meets the softer submission in the center. This creates a visual rhythm that hints at interruptions and shifts, evoking feelings of unresolved tension and impending release.
