Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between tenderness and rage, depicted through a palette of saturated red and cool pale blue. The main contrast is located in the center, where the rigid checker pattern of rage meets the softer broken dots of tenderness, symbolizing the clash of these feelings. The layout features deliberate breaks in the edges, allowing for the 'leak' of softer emotions outside the rigid order, creating a sense of unresolved tension. This conflict speaks to the complexity of human emotions, where calmness is often interrupted by inner turmoil.
