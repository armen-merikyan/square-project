Artist Statement

This artwork portrays the tension between desire and emptiness, illustrating longing for connection while grappling with the confines of isolation. The palette, rich in hot coral and blood orange, reflects the warmth of yearning, contrasted by the deep charcoal and hollow blue that embody the sense of emptiness. The main collision occurs at the center, where a saturated burst clashes with a quieter field, creating an emotional pull from the edges inward. The edge style of cut and jagged shapes reinforces the motif of containment against overflow, allowing space to breathe while maintaining tension.
