Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between restlessness and peace, visually represented by dynamic, checker patterns juxtaposed with static fields. The chosen palette embodies mourning and renewal with deep blues and soft yellows, enhancing emotional depth. Light and dark areas clash around a central diagonal, signifying conflict between chaos and serenity. The composition echoes an open gate, inviting motion yet restraining it in certain sections, employing broken pixels to evoke imperfections in memory and experience, creating an unsettling yet reflective piece.
