Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the contrast between quiet and pressure through intentional color placement and patterning. The chosen palette, 'quiet_pressure', uses muted tones of off-white and faint peach to symbolize quietness, while the dark navy and compressed red injects a sense of pressure. The dense pixel cluster in the top-left represents compressed density, released by a fluttering diagonal pattern that unfolds towards the bottom right, allowing for lighter hues where the quietness breathes. The strategic placement of broken pixels adds tension, mimicking how clarity can shatter into chaos, reflecting the seed's narrative of conflict and balance between opposing forces.
