Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a tension between longing and resolve, expressed through the contrasting density of warm ivories and deep wines against soft grays. The palette reflects guilt and forgiveness, with deeper colors symbolizing resolve and lighter tones capturing a sense of longing. The main collision occurs at the center where dense, dark tones interrupt the lighter surrounding fields, creating a visual struggle between intimacy and distance. This arrangement, combined with stripes and pressure bands, conveys a teetering balance of emotions like unresolved tension and quiet acceptance.
