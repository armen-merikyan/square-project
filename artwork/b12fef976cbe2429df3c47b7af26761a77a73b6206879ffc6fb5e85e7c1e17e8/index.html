Artist Statement

This piece captures the duality of rage and calm through contrasting colors and arrangements. The use of black and deep red clashing against pale blue and white creates an emotional tension that reflects struggle and serenity. The palette emphasizes the heaviness of rage and the lightness of calm, while the layout features pixels descending like a rain of emotion, echoing the theme of weight versus light. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, symbolizing cracks in the mirror of calmness, while the softer blue and white provide areas of quiet reflection amidst the chaos.
