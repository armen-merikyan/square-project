Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional clash between defiance and submission through a split vertical layout, emphasizing a collage of angular resistance on the left and soft, broken patterns on the right. The palette blends deep black and dark violet for boldness, while soft gold and pale green introduce warmth and contrast, creating a dynamic interplay. The main collision occurs at the center, highlighting the tension as colors shift from solid and assertive to fragmented and subtle, echoing the inner struggles of these opposing emotions.
