Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between longing and resolve through a structured 8x8 grid. The emotional tone is expressed with a palette of deep blue and pale yellow, symbolizing melancholic yearning against determined clarity. The top half, representing longing, features dense clusters of muted, darker colors, while the bottom reflects resolve with more structured, vibrant oscillations. The collision is most visible in the horizontal separation where the deep blue bands starkly meet the lighter accents, evoking a struggle between chaos and order. The ‘ladder’ shape seed is abstractly interpreted through directional lines and spacing, creating a visual metaphor for upward aspiration versus grounding reality.
