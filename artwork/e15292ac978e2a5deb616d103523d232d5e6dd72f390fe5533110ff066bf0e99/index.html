Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between clarity and confusion, reflecting a struggle for balance through color. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue conveys a nostalgic yet disorienting atmosphere. The main contrast lies in the heavy dark colors battling the lighter palette, suggesting tension and resolution. The arrangement of colors in thick horizontal bands gradually breaks into erratic stripes, symbolizing the pulse and disruption stemming from conflicting emotions, reflecting both symmetry and disorder.
