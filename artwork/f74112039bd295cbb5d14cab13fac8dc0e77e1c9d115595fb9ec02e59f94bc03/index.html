Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional contrast between vulnerability and strength through a dynamic palette of deep blacks and vibrant accents. The use of signal green and electric blue introduces a stark contrast that represents the rising signals of strength battling the surrounding darkness of vulnerability. The layout invites the viewer's eye to the center, where clashes of sharp boundaries exist, suggesting a struggle. Random broken pixels imply imperfections and enhance the themes of interruption and repair, allowing the piece to maintain an abstract quality without literal representation.
