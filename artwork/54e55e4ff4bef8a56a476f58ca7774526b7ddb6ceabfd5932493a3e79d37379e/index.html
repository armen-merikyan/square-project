Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the tension between longing and resolve, utilizing a warm palette dominated by burnt orange and dark red to evoke urgency while interspersed with cool charcoal to create a counterbalance. The bold colors collide rhythmically, suggesting the thread-like connection between desire and commitment. There's a deliberate arrangement of broken pixel effects to create the sense of damage and repair, allowing the patterns of a lattice and halo to weave through the composition, symbolizing a journey that is both beautiful and fragmented. The collision occurs predominantly in the center, emphasized by tight clusters pushing against the void, representing the space where emotion flows in and out, evoking both serenity and urgency.
