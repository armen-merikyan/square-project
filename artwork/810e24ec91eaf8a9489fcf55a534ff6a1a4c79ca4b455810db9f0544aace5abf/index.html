Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between 'longing' and 'resolve'. The palette mixes ripe greens and honey golds, with muted tones of deep olive and cream representing both hope and yearning. The layout utilizes mostly negative space, emphasizing quiet against dense interruptions, culminating in a collision in the center where the 'flame' patterns of resolve meet the glitchy fragments of longing. The jagged edges symbolize the broken mirror of emotions throughout this piece, capturing the essence of repair versus damage. The placement of brighter colors creates a 'pulse' effect in contrast to darker hues, encapsulating the complex narrative of searching for completeness.
