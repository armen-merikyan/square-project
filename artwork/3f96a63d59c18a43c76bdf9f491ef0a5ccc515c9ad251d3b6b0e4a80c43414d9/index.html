Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between hope and fear, depicted through a twilight palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green. The contrast operates primarily in the central area, where softer colors collide against darker, jagged edges. This invokes a feeling of tension and release, as the brighter hues signal hope, fighting against the oppressive darkness surrounding them, symbolizing the struggle between fear and aspiration. The layout suggests a protected center amid a threatening perimeter, achieving a delicate balance between negative space and dense color patterns. The patterns chosen—checker for fear and rain for hope—create an engaging rhythm that mirrors the dynamics of emotional collision.
