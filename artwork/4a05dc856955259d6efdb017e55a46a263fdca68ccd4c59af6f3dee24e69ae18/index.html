Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between defiance and submission, depicted through a balanced yet chaotic arrangement. The vibrant palette of lime and electric blue serves as accents that signify defiance, whereas the black and dark magenta represent the heavier, more oppressive aspects of submission. The main collision occurs at the center where the halo pattern meets the rippling effect of submission, reinforcing the tension. This embodies the complex interaction of opposing forces, echoing the spiraling thoughts and conflicting emotions suggested by the seed.
