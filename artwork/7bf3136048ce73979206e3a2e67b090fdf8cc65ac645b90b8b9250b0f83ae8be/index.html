Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts suspicion and trust, depicting a visual struggle between warmth and cool shadows. The chosen palette channels 'guilt_forgiveness' through soft gold and deep wine against ash gray, embodying tension and release. The main contrast occurs in the top-left corner, where suspicion (dark tones) collides with trust (warm accents), while the rest of the square, interspersed with quiet spaces, suggests resilience amidst fragility. Broken pixels create intentional disruptions, reinforcing the concept of 'signal vs noise'.
