Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between rage and calm, using a palette that emphasizes sharp reds against muted blues and blacks to illustrate the conflict. The rage is represented by jagged, irregular patterns that cut through calmer horizontal lines, creating a stark contrast. The grid pattern symbolizes calmness but is disrupted by scar patterns, representing explosive energy. The main collision occurs at the center, where red and black forcibly interrupt the pale blue, evoking feelings of turmoil battling serenity. Thus, the abstract form captures a layered narrative of emotional conflict and resolution through spatial arrangements and color choices.
