Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captures the conflict between fear and hope. The palette consists of deep blacks and dark violets representing fear, contrasted with soft golds and pale greens, symbolizing hope. The layout implies a rising flow, with intense dark colors clustered in the corners to convey fear's weight and a scattering of lighter tones suggesting hope's emergence at the edges. The zigzag pattern, representing tension and interruption, creates a collision of emotion as soft and sharp contrasts interact throughout the composition, embodying the balance between despair and uplift.
