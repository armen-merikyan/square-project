Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dance between chaos and calm, symbolizing confusion juxtaposed with clarity. The palette of deep black, stark white, and soft pale blue creates a dynamic tension, with black embodying the weight of pressure and white offering a release of clarity. Color placement emphasizes a collision in the upper rows, where dark colors cluster against expansive whites, suggesting interference but also spaciousness. The broken pixel approach introduces moments of imperfection and disruption, enhancing the feelings of dissonance. The top-to-bottom flow creates a narrative movement from density to more open spaces, paralleling the journey from tumultuous emotion to a serene resolution.
