Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between longing and resolve, interpreted through a palette of muted plum and rain blue with accents of soft cyan and moon white. The main collision is central in the square, where the halo pattern of longing interacts with the firm structures of the target pattern, creating a dynamic visual pull. This interplay represents the push and pull of emotions and the state of being both broken and whole, illuminated by lit edges that suggest clarity amidst chaos.
