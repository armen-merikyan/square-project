Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between fleeting intensity and enduring stillness, reminiscent of sparks contrasted against the settling ash. The palette combines vibrant yellows and oranges for bursts of warmth while deep blue and muted purple lend a feeling of depth and restraint. The main contrast occurs in the center, where dynamic flow of colors seems to collide with the quieter, cracked edges of gray tones, emulating the struggle between memory and erasure. The fractured placement of the patterns furthers the narrative of resilience against fragility.
