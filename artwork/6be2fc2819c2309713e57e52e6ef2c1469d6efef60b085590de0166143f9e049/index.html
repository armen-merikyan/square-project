Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dialogue between joy and grief, using a palette that contrasts the bright yellows and oranges (representing joy) against deep blues and muted purples (symbolizing grief). The zenith of this contrast occurs in the central row, where a wave of brighter colors struggles against a denser, darker field. The chosen patterns reflect the fluidity and conflict inherent in emotions: joyful dots amidst the maze-like structure of grief. The slight asymmetry and presence of broken pixels introduce an imperfection that echoes the complexity of these feelings, creating a dense yet open emotional landscape.
