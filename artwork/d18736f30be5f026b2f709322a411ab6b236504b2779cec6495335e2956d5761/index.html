Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between defiance and submission through a carefully chosen palette that combines deeper, muted tones with bright, pulsating colors. The central colors symbolize defiance, with sharp contrasts against the softer edges representing submission. The main collision occurs at the center, where bright pulses rise amidst a more subdued background, creating a dynamic rhythm that reflects the struggle of an uneven border. The color placements weave in and out of a central channel, illustrating the idea of quiet versus chaos. The layout emphasizes the collision between emotion through the use of broken colors to introduce intentional imperfections.
