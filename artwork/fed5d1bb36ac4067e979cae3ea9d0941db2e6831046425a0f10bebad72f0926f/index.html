Artist Statement

The emotional tone across this artwork embodies a tension between warmth and coolness, as directed by the palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold. The contrast between the warmer tones and the otherwise muted darkness creates a visual dialogue of control versus chaos. The layout features upward and downward movements symbolizing competing emotional forces, enforcing the sense of collision. The irregular placement of broken pixels injects a layer of imperfection, inviting the viewer to contemplate the unpredictable nature of emotional experiences. Quiet spaces exist among denser regions to reflect moments of breath amidst turmoil, generating a sense of rhythm in the chaos.
