Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between defiance and submission. The chosen colors, with muted tones of dark navy and dusty gray opposing the bright compressed red, convey a constant struggle between two forces. The center is occupied predominantly by the quiet fields of off-white and faint peach, representing submission, while the edges are disrupted with glitch-like interruptions of darker colors, highlighting moments of resistance. The composition flows diagonally, creating rhythm and echo that encapsulates the essence of the seed’s themes.
