Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between longing and resolve, creating a dynamic contrast of desire and determination. The selected palette of soft pinks, stark black, and gradient grays emphasizes this dichotomy, with the warm shades symbolizing yearning and the darker hues representing resolute shapes. The dominant collision occurs in the center where the chaotic ash field pattern overlaps with the structured grid, creating a visually striking frustration between the ordered and the disordered. The layout evokes a cross current movement, symbolizing the interplay of opposites, and broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, adding depth and texture to the entire composition.
