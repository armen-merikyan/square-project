Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a contrast between strength and vulnerability, represented through a palette of mourning black, deep blue, new leaf, pale yellow, and stone. The presence of dark colors signifies strength and density, while the lighter shades represent the nuanced vulnerability. The main collision occurs centrally, where deep blue (strength) interacts with pale yellow (vulnerability), creating tension. This interaction aligns with the seed references of pressure and flow, forming a dynamic pattern that mimics the scene of a flood against a stack, illustrating emotional turmoil and complexity.
