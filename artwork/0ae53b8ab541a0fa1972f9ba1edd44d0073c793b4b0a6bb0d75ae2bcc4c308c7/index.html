Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the conflict between clarity and chaos, with the chosen palette of dark navy and muted cyan creating a stark contrast of signal versus noise. The darker colors represent the static and interference, while the cyan offers a clean pulse of clarity. The main collision occurs in the center, where a denser cluster of navy represents the noise, and the lighter areas provide breathing space, mimicking the tension suggested by the seed. The use of broken pixels gives an impression of disruption and memory erasure, enhancing the overall abstract experience.
