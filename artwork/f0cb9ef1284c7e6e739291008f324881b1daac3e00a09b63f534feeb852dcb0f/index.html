Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between defiance and submission, captured through a juxtaposition of sharp colors and softer, muted tones. The warm reds and deep blues suggest a collision of urgency and calm, fostering a visual tension that embodies the theme of struggle. The rhythm of the pulse marks against glitch is represented in the uneven patterns and color placements, primarily through the use of ripple (defiance) and ring (submission) patterns. The main contrast emerges from the center, where the darker, chaotic elements intersect with the lighter, enclosed areas, creating a sense of dialogue between the two states. The presence of broken pixels adds layers of imperfection and unpredictability, symbolizing the disruptions within the emotional spectrum.
