Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between loneliness and connection, illustrated through contrasting warm and cool palette choices. The primary colors, deep blue and warm orange, collide to evoke tension—while warm colors suggest connection, cool colors represent isolation. The arrangement emphasizes a central focal point, with hues radiating outward, creating a burst effect. Broken patterns of stripes and maze-like configurations reflect the complexity of human emotions—a struggle between hidden vulnerability and the persistent desire for connection. Sparse areas of negative space reinforce feelings of drifting away, symbolizing loneliness amidst the chaos.
