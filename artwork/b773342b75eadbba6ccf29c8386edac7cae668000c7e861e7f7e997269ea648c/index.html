Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between longing and resolve through a dynamic palette of warm greens and cool blues, grounded by stark black accents. The use of electric blue and lime signifies the vibrancy of hope that competes with the chaos represented by deeper shades. The dominant collision surfaces at the center, where braiding patterns of resolve intertwine with shattering elements of longing, echoed by broken pixels that represent tension and imperfection. The layout suggests an upward movement, depicting a journey towards resolution amidst chaos, leaving space for contemplation.
