Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a carefully chosen palette of soft greens, warm browns, and cool whites. The color choices signify safety and exposure, embodying the duality of yearning and stability. The main collision occurs in the center as patterned clusters of resolve (stack) contrast against sparse fields of longing (constellation), highlighting a push-and-pull dynamic. Broken pixels are incorporated as intentional disruptions, adding an element of imperfect harmony while suggesting the process of repair amidst damage. The layout emphasizes a scattered repair theme, creating quiet negative space that emphasizes the emotional narrative of striving for resolve amidst fragmented echoes of longing.
