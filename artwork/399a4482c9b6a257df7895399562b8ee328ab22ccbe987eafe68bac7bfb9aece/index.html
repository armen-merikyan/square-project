Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension of confusion and clarity, using a dark palette to evoke depth and mystery. The outer border reflects the chaos through a gradient of blacks and dark violets, symbolizing confusion and overlapping pathways. The inner core, employing soft gold and pale green in a staircase pattern, represents clarity and structured thought. The collision between the steady, bright core and the turbulent border illustrates the struggle between clarity and noise, creating an unsettling yet compelling visual rhythm.
