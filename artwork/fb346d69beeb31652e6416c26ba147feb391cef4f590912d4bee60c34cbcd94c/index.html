Artist Statement

This composition embodies the juxtaposition of envy and gratitude, with one side having a structured, clean pattern representing gratitude, depicted in light grays, while the chaotic aspect of envy is represented by dark maroon and contrasting pinks. The diagonal rise signifies hope and escape, with the clash of colors suggesting tension between calm and disturbance. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, creating a narrative of disrupted harmony, representing the collision of these conflicting feelings in an abstract manner.
