Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dance between joy and grief through a carefully selected palette of deep blues, muted purples, and bright yellows and oranges. The contrast between heavy, cool tones and vibrant, warm accents creates a push-pull effect, symbolizing the balance between despair and hope. The grid-like arrangement suggests structure, while the broken pixel elements introduce an element of imperfection, echoing the theme of broken symmetry and emotional fractures. The left-weighted layout amplifies the sense of heaviness on one side, inviting the viewer to consider the interplay of these conflicting emotions.
