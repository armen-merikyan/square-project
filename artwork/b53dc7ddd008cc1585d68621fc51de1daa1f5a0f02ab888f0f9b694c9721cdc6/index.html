Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dance between longing and resolve, captured through a palette mixing warm browns as a nod to yearning and cool greens representing stability. The main contrast lies in the center where mirrored depths of color collide, imitating the push and pull of emotions. Broken pixels act as intentional disruptions reflecting the seed’s themes of imperfection and interconnectedness, creating a visual narrative of layered experiences.
