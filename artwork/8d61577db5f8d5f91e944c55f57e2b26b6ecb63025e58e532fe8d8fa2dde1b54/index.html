Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts restlessness with peace through the use of jagged and soft forms. The palette features warm colors that evoke memory and vibrancy against cool dark tones representing stillness and introspection. The main contrast occurs in the diagonal spread of brighter colors, interrupted by darker shades, reflecting an emotional narrative of struggle versus calm. The pattern flow flickers between uniform calm and chaotic disruptions, symbolizing the oscillation between memory and motion, creating an uneasy yet soothing visual rhythm.
