Artist Statement

This abstract composition explores the tension between joy and grief. The palette combines warm yellows and oranges to symbolize joy with cool muted purples and deep blues representing grief. The left side follows a structured zigzag pattern, suggesting order, while the right side breaks into more chaotic transitions, embodying grief's fragmented essence. The interplay creates a visual narrative of interruption and balance, with color collisions hinting at the emotional turmoil layered within, reflecting the seed's themes of contrast and internal conflict.
