Artist Statement

This piece explores a spectrum of emotion through contrasting regions of warmth and firmness, using a palette of deep reds to symbolize longing, and stark grays to denote resolve. The main collision is centered along a diagonal streak, representing a cross-current of emotions where hope meets the boundary of reality. Broken pixels add a sense of imperfection, echoing intimacy and distance. The layout favors negative space to emphasize emotional weight, while layers of color create a depth that both attracts and repels.
