Artist Statement

The emotional tone pivots between panic and control, suggesting a chaotic but structured environment. The palette of electric blue, magenta, and black evokes a sense of urgency against a grounded background. The left side embodies control with a structured target pattern, while the right disrupts with a grid reflecting panic through broken pixels. The main contrast arises where the clean lines meet chaotic breaks, creating emotional tension and unease, embodying the conflict of the seed through an abstract pattern.
