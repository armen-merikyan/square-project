Artist Statement

This piece embodies the duality of love and betrayal through stark contrasts of warm and cool tones. The palette combines soft pinks and deep maroons to evoke intimacy and conflict. The layout invites imbalance with broken symmetry and interrupted patterns, suggesting emotional friction. The arrangement holds both dense clusters and negative space, capturing a pulse of contrasting feelings and an unresolved rhythm.
