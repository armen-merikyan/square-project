Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys the tension between confusion and clarity, realized through a dynamic palette of dark and light hues. The arrangement of colors creates a contrast between the tightly packed dark edges and the lighter, more open center, embodying the layout of ‘frame_and_core’. The use of broken pixels introduces a sense of imperfection and chaos, complementing the checker pattern that symbolizes confusion. Meanwhile, the central area exhibits stair-like clarity, creating depth and a pulse that ebbs and flows throughout the piece, representing emotional rhythms.
