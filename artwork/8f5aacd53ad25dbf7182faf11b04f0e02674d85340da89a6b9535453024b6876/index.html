Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between restlessness and peace. The palette integrates deep reds and muted creams, signifying a clash of intense feelings against calmness. The main collision occurs in the center, where dark edges intersect with lighter fields, representing the challenges in achieving tranquility. The channel and hinge patterns invite movement across the grid, mimicking the emotional tug between stillness and motion.
