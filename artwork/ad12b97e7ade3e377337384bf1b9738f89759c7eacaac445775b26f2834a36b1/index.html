Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends confusion with clarity, conveying a struggle between structure and disorder. The warm sepia and tan colors evoke nostalgia, while faded blue provides dissonance, highlighting moments of clarity amidst confusion. The edge style leans heavy on the left, accentuating the dense clusters at the center that radiate outward, creating a burst of overlapping patterns. The crosshatch pattern embodies chaos while the checker brings forth moments of calm, suggesting a narrative of falling from clarity into chaos, reflecting the seed's emotional journey.
