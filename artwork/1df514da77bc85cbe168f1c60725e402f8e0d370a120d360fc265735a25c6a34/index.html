Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dynamic tension between rage and calm. The palette incorporates rich reds representing rage alongside calming pale blue and white tones to emphasize the contrast. The main collision occurs in the top-left corner, where fiery, jagged patterns clash with horizontal waves of calm, creating a vivid interaction that is both chaotic and structured. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfection, symbolizing the struggle between the contained and the overflowing, while still maintaining an abstract representation of the seed's essence.
