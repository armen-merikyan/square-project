Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between confusion and clarity. The palette features deep, resonant colors that reflect a lonely signal, evoking a sense of depth and emotional weight. The strategic placement of darker shades suggests the presence of chaos against the clearing light, while lighter tones offer moments of clarity amidst confusion. The asymmetry of color placements represents broken symmetry, emphasizing collisions of feeling. The contrasting flow—where darker areas pulse with noise against brighter intervals—creates a heartbeat of emotion, with negative space fostering a sense of contemplation. Overall, the arrangement suggests an emotional story of navigating through layers of confusion towards clarity.
