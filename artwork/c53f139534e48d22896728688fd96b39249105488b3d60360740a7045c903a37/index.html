Artist Statement

The composition captures the tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting patterns of stripes (representing vulnerability) and maze-like structures (representing strength). The palette of cool 'melancholy wonder' colors evokes feelings of calmness amidst pressure, with the muted hues of lavender gray and soft cyan framing moments of rest against denser clusters. The center maintains a delicate balance—serving as both sanctuary and target while the outer edges collide with sharper contrasts. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, hinting at the imperfections inherent in both strength and vulnerability.
