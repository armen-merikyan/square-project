Artist Statement

This piece conveys the tension between restlessness and peace through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of shelter green, warm brown, pale skin, cold white, and open blue creates a balance of warmth and coolness. The arrangement features stacked colors representing peaceful moments interrupted by cracks signifying restlessness, with the main collision occurring diagonally across the square, fostering a sense of movement and instability. The negative space complements this tension, enhancing the emotional narrative of seeking calm while wrestling with a restless spirit.
