Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between 'longing' and 'resolve', using a palette of deep navy and bright dim cyan to reflect the interplay of solitude and clarity. The central area features a dense arrangement of darker colors representing longing—a sense of weight and unfulfilled desire—while the edges transition into lighter shades, symbolizing resolve and the clarity that comes with understanding. The collision occurs at the center, where the contrasts between the layers create a vibrational connection, merging quiet fields with radiant bursts, challenging the viewer with its layered narrative.
