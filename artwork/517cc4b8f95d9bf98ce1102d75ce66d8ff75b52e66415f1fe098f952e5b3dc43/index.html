Artist Statement

This pixel art embodies the emotional tension between joy and grief. The palette consists of bright yellows and oranges representing joy, contrasted with deeper blues and muted purples symbolizing grief. The alternating patterns evoke a feeling of conflict: bright sparks of joy attempt to break through darker, heavier sections while maintaining a sense of symmetry through the horizontal split. This arrangement fosters a tension-filled conversation between light and dark, suggesting both a struggle and an unresolved blend of emotions. The use of negative space allows the viewer to breathe amidst the density, echoing the fluctuating nature of emotions where joy and grief coexist.
