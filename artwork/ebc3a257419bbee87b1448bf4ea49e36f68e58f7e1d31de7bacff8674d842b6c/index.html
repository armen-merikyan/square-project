Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional duality between joy and grief through a dynamic interplay of color and pattern. The warm yellows and oranges evoke joy, pulsing like light, while the deep blue and muted purple represent grief, adding weight and depth. Central placement of brighter tones serves as a sudden burst of joy, contrasting with a surrounding grid pattern that suggests grief's intricate maze. This tension reflects the emotional push and pull, encapsulating the essence of human experience. The composition employs broken pixels to introduce imperfection, enhancing the narrative of emotional struggle.
