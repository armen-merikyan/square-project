Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional conflict between desire and emptiness. The palette combines fiery tones of hot coral and blood orange, symbolizing desire, with subdued hollow blue and charcoal representing emptiness. The central area is filled with softer colors in a 'stripes' pattern, clashing against the 'spark' accent colors. The main collision occurs in the center, embodying the tension of yearning versus containment. Broken pixels are strategically applied to represent intentional disruptions and scars in the field of desire, emphasizing the struggle for fulfillment amid containment.
