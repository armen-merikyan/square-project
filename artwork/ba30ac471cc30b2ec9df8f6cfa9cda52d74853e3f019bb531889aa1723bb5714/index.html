Artist Statement

The emotional tone here juxtaposes loneliness and connection, illustrated through a central cluster of warm 'burnt orange' and 'gold' hues representing connection, surrounded by darker, cooler shades suggesting isolation. The contrast is brought to life by the interplay of warm and cool colors, where lighter colors at the center draw the eye, while the outer levels of darker shades create a sense of weight and distance. Broken pixels scattered throughout mirror fragmented memories and the individual experience of loneliness, creating a rhythm of connection and isolation through spatial arrangement and color dynamics.
