Artist Statement

The emotional tone mirrors the tension between restlessness and peace, with a palette that incorporates deep teal and cosmic indigo for depth, contrasting against bursts of star white and cold gold to suggest moments of clarity amidst chaos. The dominant colors create pressure in the center, while lighter colors touch the edges, embodying peace as they recede. The patterns reflect restlessness with uneven constellation arrangements colliding with calm, rhythmic dots that beckon quiet. This choice connects to the seed's themes as it portrays the conflicts of memory versus motion and the delicate balance of emotion as a fluid experience.
