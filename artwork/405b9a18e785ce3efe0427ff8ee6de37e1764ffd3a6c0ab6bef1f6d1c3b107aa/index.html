Artist Statement

This 8x8 artwork embodies the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette, composed of muted pinks, deep reds, dark maroons, and grays, suggests an interplay of warmth and isolation. The center, marked by brighter red hues, is surrounded by darker shades that echo feelings of loneliness. The crosshatch pattern represents connection but is broken by the presence of heavy negative space, signifying isolation. The arrangement highlights the contrast between the vibrant core and the surrounding density, evoking a sense of longing amidst connections that feel strained or incomplete.
