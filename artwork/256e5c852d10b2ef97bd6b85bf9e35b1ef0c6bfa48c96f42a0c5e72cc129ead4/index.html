Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between control and surrender, represented through the dense center that suggests overflow contrasted with gentle descents at the edges. The palette combines deep wine and warm ivory to create warmth and depth, while ash gray provides a muted backdrop. A stuttered rhythm occurs as warm colors subtly intersperse into cooler tones, leading to a thick outline of darker colors that delineate chaos versus calm. The main contrast happens centrally where the largest concentration of texture lies, echoing the theme of containment versus release.
