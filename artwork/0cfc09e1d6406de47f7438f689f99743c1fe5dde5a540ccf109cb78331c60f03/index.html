Artist Statement

The emotional tone emerges from the interplay between loneliness and connection, with a foundational palette that recalls faded memories and feelings of separation. The dominant warm earthy tones of sepia and tan contrast with cooler notes of dust gray and faded blue, creating visual tension and depth. This tension resonates through the placement of colors, where a central burst of lighter shades symbolizes fleeting connection, surrounded by deeper hues that represent the weight of isolation. The choice of a corner invasion layout emphasizes how feelings of loneliness can encroach upon the space of connection, forming a pattern that ebbs and flows, with broken pixels scattered to suggest fragility.
