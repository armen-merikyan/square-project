Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The color palette, rooted in dark navy and black contrasted with stark whites and dim cyan, reflects a journey from chaos to order. The arrangement uses corner invasion to suggest movement from a dichotomous nature—where dense clusters of confusion radiate outward, interrupted by clearer sections. The crosshatch pattern embodies confusion while the use of a checker system represents clarity. The collisions happen where clarity breaks through the confusion, hinting at moments of insight amidst the disarray.
