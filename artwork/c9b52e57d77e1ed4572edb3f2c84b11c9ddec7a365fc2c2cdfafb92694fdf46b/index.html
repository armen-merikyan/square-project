Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captures the interplay between longing and resolve, where soft, distant hues suggest longing while deeper, more saturated colors represent resolve. The selected palette combines serene colors like pale aqua and mist gray to evoke ambiance, alongside alarm red and ink blue for intensity. The collision is primarily located in the center, where the pressure band of darker colors interrupts the lighter, dotted areas, illustrating the struggle between chaos and order. The seed of a 'ladder' manifests in the structured but fragmented arrangement of color, creating a dynamic tension reflective of the emotional push and pull.
