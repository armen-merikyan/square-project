Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through contrasting colors and spatial dynamics. Utilizing the 'clarity_confusion' palette, the stark contrast of ice blue against ink black highlights the feeling of yearning against a backdrop of steadiness. The collision of the spark pattern, suggesting longing, with the mirror pattern, indicating resolve, appears prominently in the center, where jagged elements represent the broken fragments of a 'crack.' The layout balances quiet negative space with bursts of tension, reflecting a serene urgency, creating an emotional pulse throughout.
