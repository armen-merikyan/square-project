Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional juxtaposition of relief and dread, showcased through a palette of soft mint and storm gray, symbolizing calmness and foreboding respectively. The dense lower-left corner represents dread with dark hues and layered patterns, contrasting against the light, airy waves that unveil towards the upper-right, embodying relief. Key collisions occur at the center where the tension builds before dissipating, creating a dialogue between opposing emotional states, influenced by themes of pressure and release from the seed.
