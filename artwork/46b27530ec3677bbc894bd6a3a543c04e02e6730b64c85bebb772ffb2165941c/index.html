Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the conflict between vulnerability and strength through a palette of warm and cool colors. The chosen colors symbolize warmth (rose clay, lamp gold) representing strength, while cool tones (cool blue, distant lilac) illustrate vulnerability. The primary collision occurs in the center of the grid, where dense, dark colors (indicating strength) interact with lighter, more fragile hues (indicating vulnerability). This creates an impression of exposure while maintaining shelter, reflecting the seed's underlying themes of an emotional tug-of-war. Each broken pixel introduces imperfection, adding depth to the narrative of connection and alienation.
