Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the contrast between vulnerability and protection. The palette, featuring 'shelter_green' and 'pale_skin', evokes warmth and security contrasting with the coolness of 'cold_white' representing exposure. The collision of 'ladder' and 'stairs' patterns creates a layered rhythm reminiscent of growth and descent. The pattern's flow outside-inward, combined with a hollow center, conveys a protected yet exposed feeling, with the main contrast occurring centrally where soft colors intersect starker tones.
