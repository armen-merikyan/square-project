Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dichotomy between defiance and submission through a calculated mix of density and openness. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon expresses love and betrayal, evoking a sense of warm tension. The outer border exhibits a darker, muted range that envelops lighter, more vibrant inner pixels, creating a feeling of weight and pressure that contrasts against the softness inside. The checker and stripe patterns interweave at random points, suggesting moments of connection and disconnection within the broader narrative.
