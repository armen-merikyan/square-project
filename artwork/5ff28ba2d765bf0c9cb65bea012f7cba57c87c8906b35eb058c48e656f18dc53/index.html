Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, represented through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, drawn from the 'hunger_restraint' family, includes deep, muted tones clashing with bright accents that evoke resilience amid fragility. The main contrast occurs centrally, where dense reds and blacks collide with lighter muted creams, symbolizing a protective barrier at the core surrounded by breaks in continuity, highlighting vulnerability. This disjointed assemblage speaks to the memories and echoes of strength amidst the fragility of existence, with broken pixels symbolizing disruptions in perceived safety. The overall layout mirrors to emphasize the duality while maintaining an air of unease through intentional imperfections and a 10% negative space for breathing room.
