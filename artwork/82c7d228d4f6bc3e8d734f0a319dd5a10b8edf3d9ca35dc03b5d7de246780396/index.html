Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between certainty and curiosity, represented by deep greens and honey-like accents that evoke tension and intrigue. The palette reflects gratitude and envy, combining dark olive and bright greens to visualize the ebb and flow of opposing forces. Contrast arises where the dense mass of dark colors meets the bright, pinched accents, creating a focal chaos in the center. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, representing the uncertainties and disruptions in the journey from bottom to top, while maintaining an 8% negative space for ambiguity.
