Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances severity with softness, using a palette that integrates powder pink and sharp crimson for contrast. The layout mirrors an ‘open gate’ allowing a wave of noise and softness to enter, creating an interruption in density. Main collision appears centrally, where the sharp crimson disrupts the cooler steel gray and carbon black threads, reflecting the struggle between clarity and interference. This arrangement evokes a heartbeat, pulsing between calm and unrest, embodying the essence of the seed.
