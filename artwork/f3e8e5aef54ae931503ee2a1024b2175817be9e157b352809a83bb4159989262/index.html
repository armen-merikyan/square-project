Artist Statement

This artwork represents the tension between control and surrender using color interplay. The deep olive and acid green evoke a sense of underlying pressure, reflecting control, while the softer cream and honey gold introduce gentle yields of surrender. The layout centers around a dense cluster, interrupted by lighter tones that suggest quiet moments, implying emotional overflow amid restraint. Broken pixels create intentional disruptions, giving the piece a sense of unresolved motion, akin to a fluctuating battle between tensions. The collision occurs in the center, where density meets openness, capturing the core of the emotional narrative.
