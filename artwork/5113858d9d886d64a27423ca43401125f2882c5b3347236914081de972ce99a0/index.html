Artist Statement

This composition explores the dichotomy of envy versus gratitude through contrasting color placements and forms. The palette blends warm tones of pink and red with cool shades of gray and dark maroon, establishing a strong tension. The jagged edges represent the disruptive nature of these emotions, while the central structure creates a focal point surrounded by softer pixel arrangements, conveying inner turmoil and aspiration. The palette reflects warmth battling the underlying weight of darker feelings. Collision occurs primarily where the lighter, more vibrant hues intersect with darker, muted tones, suggesting a space where emotions scream and whisper simultaneously.
