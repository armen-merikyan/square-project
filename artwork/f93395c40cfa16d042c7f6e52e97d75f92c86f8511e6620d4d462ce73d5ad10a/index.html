Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between joy and grief, represented through a contrasting palette of vibrant yellows and warm oranges clashing against deep blues and muted purples. The centerpiece represents the 'seed' of emotion, subtly pulsing with warmth while the surrounding pixels suggest a chaotic presence of grief through a checker pattern. The broken pixels introduce an intentional imperfection, enhancing the feeling of tension. The layout is upward-moving, signifying hope and the fight against despair, while emotional tensions dance in an asymmetrical pattern between light and dark.
