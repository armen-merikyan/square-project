Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between trust and suspicion, using a palette inspired by love and betrayal. The main contrast is established with a glowing soft pink against deeper gray and dark maroon, symbolizing the interplay between soft trust and hard suspicion. The stacked band layout creates a sense of shifting density, where the warmth of pink attempts to rise amidst darker elements. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, representing the scars of past disappointments, while the negative space holds breath, allowing the viewer to feel the balance of pressure and calm. The overall structure conveys resilience against fragility, echoing the ideas of stability and instability contained within the emotional narrative.
