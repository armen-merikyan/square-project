Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional juxtaposition of trust and suspicion. The dark colors represent suspicion and the lighter shades symbolize trust, creating a tension that resonates throughout the piece. The palette's use of cosmic indigo and void black captures the unsettling essence of suspicion, while the cold gold and star white provide a contrasting glow of trust. The central area is a chaotic clash, representing the collision of emotions, with a few broken pixels that hint at imperfection and the disruption of trust. The layout of a hollow center surrounded by a gradient of dark and light invokes a subtle pressure, symbolizing the ongoing struggle between reassurance and doubt.
