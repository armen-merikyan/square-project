Artist Statement

The emotional tone is one of struggle and harmony, utilizing the palette to embody warmth and coolness in contrast. The warm coral and soft amber evoke longing, symbolizing emotional threads weaving through serenity. The cold slate and signal cyan represent resolve, sharp yet calming. The design features a collision of checker and rain patterns, interrupted at the center to create a visual pulse, with broken pixels serving to signify repair amidst tension. This results in an abstract narrative of connection and fragmentation.
