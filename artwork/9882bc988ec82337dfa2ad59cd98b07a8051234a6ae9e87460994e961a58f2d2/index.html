Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between clarity and noise, using a dichotomy of colors that symbolize the persistent struggle between signal and static. The selected palette of deep black and vibrant magenta echoes the interference of memory versus erasure, where the vibrant hues cut through the muted tones, creating tension and dynamic breathing space. The center pulse in green suggests a readable rhythm amidst the overwhelming static (gray). Broken pixel disruptions create intentional scars across the composition, representing the invasive echoes that punctuate serene spaces.
