Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a tension between longing and resolve, represented through a palette of warm burnt orange and dark red for longing, contrasted against the cooler charcoal and subtle gold for resolve. This combination creates a visual conflict, with the main collision occurring centrally where the warm and cool colors intersect, embodying the dualities of repair and damage. The layout reflects broken mirroring, achieving an 'almost' symmetry that evokes the essence of a fractured reflection, suggesting unresolved emotions and the tightrope walk between hope and despair.
