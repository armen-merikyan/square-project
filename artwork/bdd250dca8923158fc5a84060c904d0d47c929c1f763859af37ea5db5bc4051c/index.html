Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic tension between tenderness and rage, expressed through a palette that balances vivid saturation with deep, muted tones. The palette includes black for intensity, pale blue for calmness, white to create negative space, and red as a crucial accent. The main contrast occurs in the center, where stacked red and calm blue pixels collide, representing the conflict between emotional extremes. The layout features two small centers that pulse around a quiet blue channel, embodying the theme of inner versus outer feelings. The arrangement embraces both harsh interruptions and gentle overlaps, creating a story of pushing against barriers while yearning for expansion, reflecting the complexity of forgiveness entwined with guilt.
