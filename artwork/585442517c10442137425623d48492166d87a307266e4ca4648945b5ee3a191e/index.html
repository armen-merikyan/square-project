Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional dialectic between quiet and pressure using a palette that juxtaposes muted tones with impactful contrasts. The darker borders channel the weight of pressure, while softer center colors suggest spaces of stillness. The core tension manifests at the diagonal seam where the hard edges of compressed red clash with faint peach, creating a visual narrative of shattering against the tide. This arrangement evokes a quiet response from the viewer while challenging them with bursts of intensity, mirroring the chaos of clarity and confusion.
