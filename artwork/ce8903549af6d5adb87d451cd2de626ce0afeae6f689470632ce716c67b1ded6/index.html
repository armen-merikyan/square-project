Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between rage and calm through vibrant contrasts. The warm reds symbolize the intensity of rage, clashing with muted pale blues representing calm. The structure employs an argument grid layout, where brighter colors dominate the corners, suggesting an eruption of emotion against a quieter center. The use of black defines boundaries, creating sharp contrasts that echo the chaotic dynamics of the seed concepts, while broken pixels introduce imperfections, symbolizing the fragility of containment.
