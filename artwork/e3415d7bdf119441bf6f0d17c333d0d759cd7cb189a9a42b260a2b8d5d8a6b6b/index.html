Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional duality of connection and loneliness. Utilizing a palette of deep navy, soft whites, and muted dim cyan emphasizes isolation against underlying warmth. The pattern features dense stripes that suggest loneliness, while echoing crack-like diagonals signal shattering connections. The top-weighted layout creates a tension that invites reflection, with the central burst representing a heart trying to connect amidst the surrounding chaos, employing broken pixels for imperfection and a touch of vulnerability. The negative spaces allow room for introspection against highly contrasted zones.
