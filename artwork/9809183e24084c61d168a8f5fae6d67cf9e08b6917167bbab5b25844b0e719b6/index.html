Artist Statement

This composition evokes a tension between the emotions of panic and control through its patterned approach. The palette, featuring warm yellows and oranges against muted purples and deep blues, captures the essence of joy and grief, representing the emotional dichotomy. The use of a grid-like structure combined with zigzag patterns creates a rhythmic conflict, while selectively broken pixels enhance imperfections, echoing the theme of a fractured sense of order. The contrast of vibrant colors interrupted by darker shades reflects the chaos woven into the experience of maintaining control.
