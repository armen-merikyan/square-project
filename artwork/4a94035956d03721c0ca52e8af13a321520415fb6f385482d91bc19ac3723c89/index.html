Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality between rage and calm. The palette combines intense reds and blacks representing emotional explosions with soft blues and whites that depict serenity. The main contrast occurs in the central area where sharp, jagged red pixels surge against the smoother blue flow, suggesting an interruption of noise against a quiet field. The randomness of broken pixels also echoes a sense of imperfection, embodying the eroded symmetry implied in the seed phrase. This blend of tension and tranquility invites viewers to recognize the coexistence of contrasting emotions.
