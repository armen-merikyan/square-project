Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between the emotions of safety and exposure. The color palette, comprised of shelter green and pale skin, reflects a gentle yet alert ambiance, suggesting a fragile balance. The sharp contrasts of cold white and warm brown create interruptions, embodying the conflicting areas of pressure and release. The main collision occurs diagonally from the corner, where soft tones encroach upon the more intense colors, mirroring the themes of invasion and protective boundaries. The arrangement employs a hinge pattern to suggest a dynamic spatial relationship, evoking feelings of vulnerability and security.
