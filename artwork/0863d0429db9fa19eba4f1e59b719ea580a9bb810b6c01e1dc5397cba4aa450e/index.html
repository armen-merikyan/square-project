Artist Statement

The emotional tone is built on the juxtaposition of longing and resolve, expressed through a palette that symbolizes broken memories with calming tones of sepia and faded blue, contrasted by stronger shades of dust gray and tan. The flow moves from dense clusters of longing (center) to more sparsely colored edges representing resolve, creating a visual tension. Broken pixels throughout introduce intentional disruptions, while the main collision happens at the center, where colors collide and overlap, suggesting an internal struggle between serenity and urgency, echoing the seed's themes in an abstract form.
