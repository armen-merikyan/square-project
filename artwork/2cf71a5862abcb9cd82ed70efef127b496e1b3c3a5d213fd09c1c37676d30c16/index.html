Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dance between longing and resolve, with muted fields meeting occasional vibrant interruptions. The palette of 'melancholy wonder' conveys depth and quietness, while vivid colors represent bursts of determination. The central collision occurs where soft cyan and lavender gray interact, creating a balance of tension and solace. The crown shape seed influences the arrangement, symbolically suggesting a hierarchical structure of feelings, but remains abstract through the interplay of colors. Broken pixels introduce deliberate imperfections, signifying past damage and repair in this emotional landscape.
