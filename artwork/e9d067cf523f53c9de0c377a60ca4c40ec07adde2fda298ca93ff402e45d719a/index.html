Artist Statement

This artwork expresses the tension between tenderness and rage through a contrasting palette of saturated red and muted blue with strategic placements that evoke a push-pull dynamic. The main collision occurs at the center, where the vibrant red interrupts the soft blue, suggesting a moment of conflict. The left and right sides mirror the emotional duality while the broken pixels serve as disruptions to the symmetry, reinforcing the theme of imperfection in emotional expression. This piece captures the essence of climbing tensions and calm reconciliations in a visual narrative.
