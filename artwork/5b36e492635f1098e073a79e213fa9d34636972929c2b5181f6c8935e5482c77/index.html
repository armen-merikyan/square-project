Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts peace and restlessness, represented by a palette that balances deep, warm tones with lighter accents. The central collision of deeper colors suggests restlessness, while lighter shades document an effort toward peace. The layout resonates with a cross-current, where vertical and horizontal movements create tension at the center. Broken pixels distribute disruption, illustrating memory and motion within a calm space, emphasizing a quiet battlefield between contrasting feelings.
