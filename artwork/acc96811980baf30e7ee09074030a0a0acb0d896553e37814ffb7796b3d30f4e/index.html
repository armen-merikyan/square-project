Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured is one of tension and resolution, reflecting the push and pull between calm and rage. The palette of deep reds and serene blues juxtaposes fiery intensity against tranquil coolness, embodying the struggle of conflicting feelings. The black frames the inner calm area, emphasizing the collision of the contrasting states. The split and stairs patterns visually represent the rhythmic flow from pressure to release, creating an abstract narrative of emotional transitions.
