Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional conflict between panic and control, represented through contrasting palettes of warm reds and cool grays. The jagged edges and broken pixels symbolize instability amidst the seemingly controlled structure of the grid pattern. The chaotic placement of red hues against the subdued gray tones creates a visual collision that reflects both joy and grief, while the negative spaces evoke a sense of interruption. Each row suggests a descent of emotions that fluctuate between order and chaos, echoing the complexities of human experience.
