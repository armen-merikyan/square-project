Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of love and betrayal, utilizing a palette of warm and muted tones. The contrast between pinks and dark maroon evokes warmth battling against the coolness of gray. The layout mirrors yet breaks symmetry, showcasing interruption and a fracture in harmony. The patterns of dots and checkers create a rhythmic tension, suggesting movement from density to emptiness, aligning with the themes of rising signals and diagonal falls. The burned edges add a sense of decay or resolve to the love-betrayal dynamic, culminating in a visual dialogue of emotional pressure.
