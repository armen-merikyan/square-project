Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between clear signals and chaotic noise through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette leans heavily towards dark navy and blacks, symbolizing the depth of noise, punctuated by bright dim cyan and stark white to represent the intermittent clarity of the signal. The center of the square is densely packed with bright colors, embodying a readable rhythm amidst a sparse outer frame that echoes the chaos. The main collision occurs at the center, where dense colors 'pulse', interrupted by sparse areas, portraying the struggle of clarity against encompassing disorder. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, adding a layer of imperfection akin to scars against a smooth background, reinforcing the theme of struggle amidst noise.
