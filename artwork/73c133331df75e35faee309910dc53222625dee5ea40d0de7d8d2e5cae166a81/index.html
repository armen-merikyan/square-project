Artist Statement

The emotional tone of loneliness juxtaposed with connection is represented through a dark palette of blacks and deep navies, conveying isolation, contrasted by sparse lighter colors symbolizing hope and connection. The main collision occurs where the deep colors swirl around isolated spots of brightness, breaking the dense dark, suggesting the tension between solitude and desire for warmth. As the layout is split horizontally, the top half expresses a somber, heavy weight while the bottom offers visual echoes of potential connection through lighter tones, implying a gradual interplay between the two emotions.
