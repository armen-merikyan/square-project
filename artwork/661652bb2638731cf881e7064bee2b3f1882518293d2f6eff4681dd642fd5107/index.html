Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between signal clarity and noise disruption. The palette—navy, black, dim cyan, and a touch of white—mirrors a struggle where dark muted tones amplify intermittent bright signals. The central pulse emerges from an intricate patchwork pattern, suggesting resilience amidst static disruption. The visual collision occurs as a bright patch of white emerges from a field of intense noise, creating a vibrant counterpoint. This arrangement echoes the seed's narrative of struggle and survival, embodying chaos juxtaposed with moments of clarity.
