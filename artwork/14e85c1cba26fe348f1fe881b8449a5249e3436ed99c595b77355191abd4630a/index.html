Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between emptiness and desire, translated into a stark color palette of warmth and coolness. The contrast arises from the checker patterns on the left representing desire, which is vibrantly punctuated, while the split-screen, muted tones on the right evoke the emptiness. This collision creates a visual rhythm that mirrors rising tension against a backdrop of fading signals, effectively narrating internal conflict through color distribution and density.
