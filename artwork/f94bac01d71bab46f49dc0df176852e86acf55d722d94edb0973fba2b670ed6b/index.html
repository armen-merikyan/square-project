Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional rollercoaster of control versus panic. The palette of electric blue, magenta, and black creates visual tension, with dark fields representing pressure and bright accents signifying bursts of emotion. The off-center arrangement evokes a sense of imbalance and quiet disturbance, inviting the viewer to explore the dynamic interplay of order and chaos. The introduction of several broken pixels enhances the sense of imperfection and unpredictability, embodying the emotions referenced in the seed.
