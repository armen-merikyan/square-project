Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between softness and severity, embodied through the contrast of warm hues against cool depths. The use of soft pastel tones from the palette juxtaposed against stark blacks suggests a push and pull between comfort and harshness. A ripple effect from the center marks the collision of emotions, with the main focus being lighter accents versus the darker backdrop, conveying a narrative of guilt and forgiveness. The broken pixels introduce a further layer of disruption, graphically representing the imperfections inherent in conflicts of emotion.
