Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the duality of confusion and clarity through the interplay of warm and cool hues. The palette 'ancient_fire' is chosen to evoke emotional warmth alongside stark contrasts. The left side uses burnt orange and dark red to symbolize confusion with jagged, overlapping stripes, creating a sense of chaos. In contrast, the right side employs gold and charcoal in clear, defined stripes representing clarity. The collisions occur where warm colors overlap cool tones, echoing feelings of tension. The broken pixels add subtle imperfections, enhancing the theme of disarray, while the central arrangement draws attention to the struggle between cohesion and chaos.
