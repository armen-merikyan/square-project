Artist Statement

This artwork expresses the emotional tension between tenderness and rage through a diagonal layout of contrasting colors. The vibrant reds invoke sharp, intense feelings of rage, while the surrounding pale blues and whites represent soft tenderness and calm. The main contrast occurs at the center where the red ignites against the calm hues, symbolizing an emotional collision. The deliberate placement of broken black pixels symbolizes disruptions and imperfections, reinforcing the theme of conflict and resolution inherent in the seed text. Overall, this piece captures the complexity of emotions by blending saturation with muted tones, crafting an engaging visual dialogue.
