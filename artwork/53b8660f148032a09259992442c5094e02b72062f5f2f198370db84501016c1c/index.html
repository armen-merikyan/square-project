Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents the tension between desire and emptiness. The color palette employs warm tones like hot coral and blood orange to evoke a saturated longing, juxtaposed with cooler shades like charcoal and hollow blue, representing vacuum and distance. The main contrast occurs in the center, where these colors collide, creating a visual push-pull. The arrangement incorporates broken pixels to symbolize disruptions in flow, encapsulating the notion of desire attempting to overflow a containing border. The pattern emerges from a glitch-like interpretation of desire, morphing into soft rings of emptiness, suggesting a battle between expansion and restraint.
