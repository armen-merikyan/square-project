Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a tension between fear and hope, represented through contrasting colors and spatial dynamics. The black and dark violet edges symbolize fear and confinement, while soft gold and pale green hints at hope and possibility of emergence. A clear division down the center indicates a split between these extremes, creating a crosshatch pattern that echoes the struggle. The use of glowing edges enhances the feeling of potential, while the maze-like arrangement reflects confusion and weight, allowing a glimpse of brightness amidst the darkness.
