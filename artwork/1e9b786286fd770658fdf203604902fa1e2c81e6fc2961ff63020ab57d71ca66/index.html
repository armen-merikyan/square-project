Artist Statement

In exploring the emotional tones of control and surrender, the color palette incorporates muted shades of gray and soft peach against stark dark navy and compressed red accents. The arrangement creates a focus in the center with dense clusters of intensity, while the edge remains muted, representing the struggle of overflow versus containment. The interplay of tight geometric patterns with loose, flowing threads suggests a conflict between presence and absence, weight and lightness. The central area is thickly outlined, where colors collide dynamically, highlighting the core interaction of these emotions.
