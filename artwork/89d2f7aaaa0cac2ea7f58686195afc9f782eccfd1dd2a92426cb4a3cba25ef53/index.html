Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the dichotomy of joy and grief through contrasting color placements. The top half utilizes bright yellows and oranges to radiate warmth and joy, while the bottom half employs deep blues and muted purples, reflecting grief. The chaotic interplay of rigid edges against soft gradients speaks to the emotional shifts between safety and threat, illuminating the tension inherent in these feelings. The layout highlights a stark separation with a framed core, suggesting a split-screen narrative of these complex emotions.
