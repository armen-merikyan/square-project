Artist Statement

The artwork reflects the emotional dichotomy between restlessness and peace. The palette, drawn from deep navy, black, and dim cyan, embodies a lonely yet serene atmosphere, accentuating the struggle of quiet moments disrupted by flashes of intensity. The main collision occurs diagonally across the grid, where structured patterns of peace (stair-like) interrupt the chaotic veil of restlessness. This tension symbolizes the ongoing battle between calm and chaos, accentuated by broken pixels that represent the fragmented emotions encountered in memory and motion.
