Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic struggle between defiance and submission, using the palette of red, black, pale blue, and white to symbolize tension and calm. The contrast arises from dense clusters of black and muted reds depicting defiance, which collides against the coolness of pale blue, suggesting submission. The layout echoes the pulse of conflict as patterns of rain and checker collide, creating visual tension that draws the eye from left to right, reflecting the theme of weight versus lightness. The use of negative space enhances the feeling of interruption, leaving the canvas open yet fraught with unexpressed tension.
