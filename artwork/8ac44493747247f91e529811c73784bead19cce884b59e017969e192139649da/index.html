Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between loneliness and connection. The palette includes sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue, evoking nostalgia and memory, which complements the theme of hidden vs exposed emotions. The contrast in colors creates collisions between lighter shades that symbolize fleeting connections and darker hues representing loneliness. The layout introduces a mirror image with intentional breaks, creating a feeling of imperfection and disruption, much like interrupted communication or relationships.
