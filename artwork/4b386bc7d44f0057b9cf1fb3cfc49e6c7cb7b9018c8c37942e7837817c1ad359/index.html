Artist Statement

This pixel composition embodies the tension between fear and hope. The palette, featuring deep blacks and dark violets contrasted by soft gold and pale green, represents the heavy corners of fear battling against the light of hope. The placement of darker colors in the edges reflects fear's grip, while the brighter central pixels symbolize hope's emergence. The sharp vs. soft theme is echoed by the jagged edges of dark shades against smooth, luminous accents, suggesting a struggle within a defined space, as emotions collide and blend within the grid.
