Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a complex interplay between longing and resolve, utilizing a palette of soft warmth contrasted with severe accents. The arrangement highlights the main collision at the center where the shatter pattern intersects the negative cross, creating a visual representation of tension and conflict. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional imperfections, echoing the theme of damaged repair and the fragility of emotions. Quiet negative space is balanced with density from the warm and cool patches, mirroring the concepts of containment versus overflow, making the artwork a dimensional exploration of emotional depth.
