Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between emptiness and desire, manifested through a juxtaposition of saturated hues and expansive negative space. The palette, comprising hot coral and blood orange as accent colors juxtaposed against desaturated charcoal and hollow blue, illustrates a warm pull towards fulfillment contrasted by a cool sense of containment. The main collision occurs through dense dot patterns representing desire that interact dynamically with the grid elements surrounding them, evoking a sense of yearning constrained by external boundaries, enhancing the overall narrative of restraint and hunger.
