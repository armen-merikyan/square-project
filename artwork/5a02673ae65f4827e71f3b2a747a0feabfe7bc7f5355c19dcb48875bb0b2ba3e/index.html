Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between relief and dread. The palette features warm burnt orange and gold shades representing a flicker of hope against the oppressive dark reds and charcoals that signify dread and weight. The deliberate arrangement creates a rising pattern, evoking the feeling of upward progress amidst disturbance. The conflicts between these color contrasts mirror the emotional turmoil suggested by the seed text. The placement emphasizes a gradient shift towards light, indicating a struggle for clarity against looming darkness, embodying themes of symmetry and fracture.
