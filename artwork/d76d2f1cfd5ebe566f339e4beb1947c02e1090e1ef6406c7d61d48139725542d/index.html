Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tone of pressure juxtaposed with moments of quietude. The palette, featuring dark navy, off-white, and faint peach, reflects a subtle interplay between weighted density and airy lightness. The corner densely brims with deep colors to symbolize pressure, while the opposing area releases into softer tones, creating tension. The arrangement prioritizes movement from a pressured corner to an expanding, quieter region, highlighting emotional contrasts and interruptions. Broken pixels serve as dynamic disruptions, enhancing the feeling of shattering calmness amidst tension.
