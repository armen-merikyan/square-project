Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the dual emotional states of tenderness and rage, using contrasting colors to evoke a visceral experience. The palette focuses on deep reds to embody rage alongside soft shades of pale blue and white, reflecting tenderness. The pattern of collision is at the center where the braid of rage intersects with a radial softness, creating tension against a broken frame that implies escape and vulnerability. Broken pixels highlight imperfections and emotional scars, establishing a narrative of conflict and resolution, echoing the themes of inside vs outside.
