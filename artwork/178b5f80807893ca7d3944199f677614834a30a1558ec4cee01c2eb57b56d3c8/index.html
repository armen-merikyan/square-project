Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between warmth and betrayal, reflecting a subtle turbulence. The palette combines warm pinks and deep maroons to highlight love, while cool greys represent the chill of betrayal. The contrasting sections create a dynamic tension, with a core of warmer tones interrupted by darker edges, symbolizing the battle between hope and loss. The broken pixels add to the complexity, suggesting imperfection and the chaos of human emotions.
