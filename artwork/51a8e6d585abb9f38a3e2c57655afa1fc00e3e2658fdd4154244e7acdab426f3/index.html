Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between clarity and chaos, depicted through a carefully curated palette of navy, black, white, and dim cyan evoking a lonely signal within noise. The central collision of colors, transitioning from dark to light, symbolizes the struggle of order within chaos, resonating with the seed. Broken pixels illustrate interruptions and scars, enhancing the overall theme of disruption amidst a wave-like horizontal flow. The layout's erosion at edges encapsulates the decay of structure, keeping the viewer engaged in the contrast between densely packed colors and negative spaces.
