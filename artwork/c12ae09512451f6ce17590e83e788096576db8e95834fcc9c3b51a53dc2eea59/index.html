Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the dance between control and surrender. The palette reflects warm coral and soft amber representing control's pressure, contrasted with cool slate and blue-black hues hinting at surrender's yielding nature. The main collision occurs centrally with warm tones at the edges that softly yield to a cooler, darker center, mimicking the push and pull of emotional tension. This arrangement subtly mirrors the seed's themes of balance and conflict, translating them into an abstract rhythmic flow.
