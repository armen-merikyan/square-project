Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between quietude and pressure, using a palette that juxtaposes softness with muted intensity. The dark navy serves as a focal point, creating a dense area that contrasts with the off-white, mimicking a weighted anchor against a soft horizon. This visual conversation echoes the seed's themes of fragmentation and flow, where sharp edges (hard colors) collide with soothing, scattered soft tones. The broken frame layout accentuates these interruptions while leaving room for negative space, enhancing the feeling of release amidst compression.
