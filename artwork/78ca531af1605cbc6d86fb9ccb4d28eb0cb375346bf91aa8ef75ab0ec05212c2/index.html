Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a balance of confusion and clarity, depicted through a palette of deep navy, stark black, soft white, and muted cyan. The primary contrast emerges between the dense, dark edges that evoke uncertainty and the lighter center hints at clarity and release. The pattern features irregular arrangements that symbolize 'falling pixels', enhancing the sense of brokenness while maintaining a bed of negative space to support a feeling of lightness amidst chaos. The overall composition embodies a tension between a structured center and scattered edges, illustrating the journey from clarity to confusion, reverberating emotionally.
