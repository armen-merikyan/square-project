Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the juxtaposition of rage and calm through intentional color choices that evoke emotional contrasts. The palette is framed by deep reds to convey rage, contrasted with pale blues and whites representing calm. This tension captures the struggle between chaotic emotion and serene restraint. The main collision occurs centrally, where red spikes invade the blue surroundings, mirroring the emotional push and pull suggested by the seed's themes. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, hinting at eroded symmetry in the emotional landscape.
