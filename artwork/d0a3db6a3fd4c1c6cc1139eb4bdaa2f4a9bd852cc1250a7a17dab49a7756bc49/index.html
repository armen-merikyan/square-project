Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between panic and control, represented by a palette that focuses on warm reds and cool grays. The outer borders exhibit a sense of urgency and chaos, while the inner 4x4 grid suggests calmness and structure. The contrast ignites in the collision of dense reds against muted grays, portraying the tension within chaos and order. The design incorporates broken pixels, evoking imperfection and echoing the emotions inherent in the seed's complexity.
