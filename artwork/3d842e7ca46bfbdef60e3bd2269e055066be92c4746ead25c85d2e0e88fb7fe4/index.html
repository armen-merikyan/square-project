Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the warm, inviting colors of trust against the sharp, contrasting hues of suspicion, creating a palpable tension. The palette of icy blues and stark blacks emphasizes clarity and confusion, while the glowing orange serves as an accent to symbolize moments of revelation amidst uncertainty. The main collision occurs toward the center-left, where stripes of suspicion intersect with a softer frame of trust, allowing for an interplay of both emotions while maintaining an overall balance between positive and negative space. The layout alludes to an eroded symmetry, suggesting both stability and fragility within its boundaries.
