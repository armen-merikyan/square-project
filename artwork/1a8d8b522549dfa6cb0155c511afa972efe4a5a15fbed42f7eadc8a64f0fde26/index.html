Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional conflict of defiance against submission. The muted palette of moss green, stone gray, and bone white creates a sense of resilience, while accents of rust and small gold punctuate moments of opposition. The dominant presence of dark tones shows submission, contrasting with clusters of lighter, more vibrant colors representing defiance in the center. The density of dark pixels juxtaposes lighter areas, suggesting tension and interruption. This layout encapsulates the struggle between being enveloped and breaking free, resonating visually with the seed's themes.
