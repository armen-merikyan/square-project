Artist Statement

The composition embodies the tension between joy and grief, manifested through sharp contrasts of color and texture. The palette reflects this duality, with vibrant yellows and oranges suggesting moments of joy, contrasted against deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The layout's descending flow mirrors the weight of tears, while the pattern of broken dots emerges against rings, suggesting fragmented memories. A dense center gradually transitions to empty spaces, creating a rhythmic pulse across the canvas, evoking fluctuations between hope and sorrow.
