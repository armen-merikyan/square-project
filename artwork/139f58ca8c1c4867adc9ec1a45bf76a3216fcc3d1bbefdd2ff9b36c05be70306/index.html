Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects a tension between defiance and submission. The palette of deep navy and dim cyan introduces a sense of weighty intensity against the starkness of black and white, illustrating the stark contrasts of emotion. The main collision occurs at the center where a halo of bright sparks disrupts the surrounding quiet fields, embodying the push-pull of assertiveness versus retreat. This collision gives voice to the idea of inner turmoil in response to outward pressure, effectively translating the seed's abstract qualities into a dynamic visual narrative.
