Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork captures the tension between fleeting sparks of hope (represented by bright yellows and oranges) and the settled quiet of ash (depicted through muted purples and deep blues). The palette reflects a contrasting duality: warmth signaling resilience against the coolness embodying fragility. The main collision occurs in the center, where rippling light bursts meet hinge-like edges, creating a visual dialogue of tension and release, echoing the themes of memory versus erasure. The overall structure maintains quiet negative space, allowing the eyes to breathe while grappling with the complexities of their emotional state, urging contemplation amidst the chaos.
