Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tone of conflict between vulnerability and strength. The soft palette of pale mint and soft sky represents vulnerability, while the storm gray and near-black denote strength. A broken frame feeling emerges from the layout that accentuates contrast, with dense dark edges encapsulating a softer center, simulating an ongoing tension between exposure and shelter. The main collision lies at the diagonal edges, where strong colors crash against serene hues, evoking a sense of instability and emotional undercurrent.
