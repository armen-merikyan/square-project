Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional struggle between defiance and submission. The chosen palette of warm coral and soft amber represents defiance, while cold slate and blue-black embody submission, establishing a vivid contrast. The main collision occurs centrally, where stacked and braided patterns intertwine, evoking a dynamic tension. The broken pixels create a sense of imperfection, echoing an incomplete repair amid the chaos, drawing on the seed's themes of invasion and density, forming an abstract narrative of resistance and enclosure.
