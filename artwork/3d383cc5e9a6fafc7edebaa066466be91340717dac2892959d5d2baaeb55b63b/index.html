Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a conflict of tenderness and rage, with warm reds and muted blues representing tenderness and rage, respectively. The juxtaposition of the 'tear' shape seed creates a central focal point of collision. Central pixels bleed with saturation from sharp reds while outer layers maintain a muted tone. This contrast suggests an inner battle that is both chaotic and serene, fostering a sense of complexity as emotions collide.
