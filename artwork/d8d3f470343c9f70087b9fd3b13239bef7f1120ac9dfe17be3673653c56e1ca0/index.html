Artist Statement

This composition captures the essence of emotional collisions through contrasting colors and patterns. The burnt orange and dark red embody the intensity and pressure of conflicting emotions, while the muted charcoal represents quiet introspection. The carefully arranged pixels exhibit a mirroring effect that hints at confusion but is intentionally disrupted by a few broken pixels, emphasizing the narrative of tension and release. The layout starts from the corner, demonstrating the invasive spread of these emotions, creating a rhythmic yet chaotic interplay throughout the grid.
