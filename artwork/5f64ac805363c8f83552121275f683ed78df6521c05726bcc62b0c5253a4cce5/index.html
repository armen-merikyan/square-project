Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the interplay between clarity and confusion, utilizing a palette that juxtaposes dark and light shades to express emotional tension. The choice of red and pale blue mirrors the conflicts of warmth and coldness, fostering a sense of urgency against calmness. The pattern flows from dense clusters of color at one edge, suggesting confusion, to lighter, more organized spaces toward the center, hinting at clarity's emergence. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, while strong contrasts accentuate the collision between structured spaces and chaotic energy.
