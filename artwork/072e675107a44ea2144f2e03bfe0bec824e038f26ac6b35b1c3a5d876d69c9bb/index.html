Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, represented through a contrast of soft, muted tones against a harsh, dark base. The pale colors signify exposure and fragility, while the darker hues provide a sense of shelter. The collision occurs diagonally, symbolizing the struggle between the open space of soft light and the enclosing walls that provide strength. The pattern reflects a ripple versus noise dynamic, calling to the concept of opposing forces meeting within the grid, resulting in a balanced yet dynamic abstract narrative.
