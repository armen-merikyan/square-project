Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece revolves around the contrast between 'quiet' and 'pressure.' The palette chosen reflects a muted field of 'off_white' and 'dust_gray,' juxtaposed with sharper bursts of 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy.' This contrast simulates the tension of pressure against the calmness of quiet. The main collision occurs in the center of the piece, where 'compressed_red' interrupts the gentle stack of 'off_white' and 'faint_peach,' creating a focal point that embodies the dynamic dialogue between intensity and softness. The layout invites the viewer to navigate from the dense top to the more relaxed bottom, echoing the seed's narrative of tension and release.
