Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy between joy and grief. The palette of yellows and deep blues reflects the bright, expansive nature of joy as counterposed with the heavy, muted tones of grief. The contrasting zigzag pattern represents the chaotic intertwining of these emotions, interrupted by jagged edges, suggesting tension and volatility. The central region remains more subdued, symbolizing the weight of grief, while the edges radiate warmth, embodying the resilient sparks of joy. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, emphasizing the unpredictability of emotion, creating a visual pulse of memory and feeling.
