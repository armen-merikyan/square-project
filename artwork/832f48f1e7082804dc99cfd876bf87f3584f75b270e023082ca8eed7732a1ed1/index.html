Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette features soft pinks and deep maroons that evoke warmth and depth, with grays introducing a contemplative space. The grid layout symbolizes 'longing' while 'resolve' emerges in the quiet fields, creating distinct areas of density and calm. The main collision occurs in the center where vibrant accents disrupt the serene backdrop, conveying the struggle between desire and firmness.
