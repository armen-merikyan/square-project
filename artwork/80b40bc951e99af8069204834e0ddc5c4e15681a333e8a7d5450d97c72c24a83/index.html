Artist Statement

This abstract composition explores the emotional dichotomy of envy and gratitude, manifested through contrasting bright and dark colors that embody the struggle between lightness and heaviness. The palette incorporates hues of red and gray to reflect the intensity of emotions—red symbolizing envy contrasted with cooler gray tones representing gratitude. The arrangement follows a downward flow, imitating tears or falling debris, creating a stark division that emphasizes the push and pull of these feelings. The staggered placements evoke a zigzag pattern akin to stairs, symbolizing ascent amidst noise, while the broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, echoing the theme of shattered mirrors. This interplay creates a visual narrative of conflict—a battle between chaotic noise and quiet reflection.
