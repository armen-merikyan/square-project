Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between emptiness and desire, reflected in the interplay of warm and cool colors. The palette of reds and blues creates a stark contrast, symbolizing conflict. The layout alternates between 'grid' for desire and 'ring' for emptiness, allowing negative space to accentuate emotions. The placement leads the eye from outside inward, embodying a movement from chaos to calm, with broken pixels lending a sense of imperfection and human touch. This arrangement creates a narrative of longing resonating through the colors' density and contrasts.
