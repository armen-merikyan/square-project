Artist Statement

The composition embodies the emotional tension between 'quiet' and 'pressure,' evoking a calming presence while facing an overwhelming force. The 'quiet_pressure' palette reflects muted tones with 'off_white' and 'dust_gray' establishing a soft base, complemented by 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy' to inject vibrancy and depth. The arrangement demonstrates a dense cluster at the top left, where 'pressure' collides with the more dispersed 'quiet' elements, hinting at an unsettled balance. By exploring the concept of hard edges against soft hues, the piece demonstrates a visual narrative of tension and release as one emotion interacts and reshapes the other.
