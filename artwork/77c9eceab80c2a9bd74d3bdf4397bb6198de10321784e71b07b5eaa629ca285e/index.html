Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between envy and gratitude, creating a dynamic visual tension. The chosen palette of lime, black, electric blue, and magenta evokes a clash of warmth and coolness, enhancing the conflict. Contrast is achieved through jagged edges and a rising layout, suggesting an upward struggle. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, symbolizing tears amidst hope. Each color placement illuminates the contrast between the vibrant (gratitude) and the muted (envy), culminating in an abstract representation of internal conflict and emotional release.
