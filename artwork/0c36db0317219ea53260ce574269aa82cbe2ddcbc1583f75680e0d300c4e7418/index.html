Artist Statement

This composition illustrates the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through the interplay of warm and cool colors, creating a sense of dissonance. The dark maroon and red evoke intensity and passion, while the gray and pink introduce calmness. The left side maintains a patterned continuity, suggesting clarity through mirroring, while the right side disrupts this symmetry with blocks of color and broken pixels, symbolizing confusion. The jagged edges contrast against smoother forms, enhancing the emotional narrative of a fractured balance.
