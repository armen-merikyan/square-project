Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the struggle between defiance and submission, illustrated through a pattern that evokes falling pixels. The palette of deep reds and pale blue creates a stark contrast, with blacks grounding the composition while whites give it a sense of openness. The collision happens predominantly in the central rows, suggesting a 'protected' core amidst darker surroundings. This interplay reflects the tension of belonging versus isolation. With broken pixels introduced to suggest imperfection, the piece embodies the struggle between chaos and order, highlighting the ongoing battle of love, betrayal, and the weight of decisions.
