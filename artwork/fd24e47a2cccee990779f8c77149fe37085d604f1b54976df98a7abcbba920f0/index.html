Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between trust and suspicion, with a palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue to evoke nostalgia while emphasizing contrasts between warmth and coolness. The main collision occurs in the diagonal from the top left to the center, where soft stripes of trust intersect with jagged, echoing patterns of suspicion. This reflects the tug-of-war between security and doubt inherent in the seed text, translating into color placements that suggest an ongoing emotional conversation.
