Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between joy and grief through contrasting colors and patterns. The selected palette features vibrant yellows and oranges to represent joy, while deep blues and muted purples depict grief. The left-weighted arrangement reflects a sense of imbalance, symbolizing love betrayal. The 'broken dots' pattern introduces moments of imperfection, aligning with the theme of loss and disruption. The primary collision occurs between the bright hues against the soft, muted tones, creating a visual echo of the emotional duality.
