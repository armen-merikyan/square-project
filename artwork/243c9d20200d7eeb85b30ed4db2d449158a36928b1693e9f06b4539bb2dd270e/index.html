Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, reflected through a palette of muted sepia, tan, and soft blue hues. This choice evokes a sense of warmth and nostalgia alongside a steady, serene strength. The main contrast lies in the collision of the diagonal threads of longing (soft colors on one side) and the glitch patterns of resolve (sharp edges and darker notes) at the center. This abstract expression mirrors the broken nature of the seed, encapsulating emotional echoes through intentional imperfection and negative space.
