Artist Statement

This artwork juxtaposes the emotional tones of longing and resolve through contrasting patterns and colors. The palette combines soft mint and sky colors suggesting serenity, while storm gray and near black introduce a sense of conflict and density. The lower left reveals a broken pixel disturbance, signaling imperfections in resolution. The layout employs a grid pattern clashing with a stepwell motif, creating a fight for dominance, while the diagonal rise suggests a pulse seeking to escape over the calm expanse, embodying the struggle between the desire for serenity and the urgency of longing.
