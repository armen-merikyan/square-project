Artist Statement

This abstract composition represents the emotional interplay between desire and restraint. The palette features rich reds and muted creams to symbolize warmth and longing against the cooler tones of blackened plum and iron gray, reflecting containment. The central area displays bright, saturated colors that escalate outward, symbolizing desire expanding and challenging the boundaries of the cooler, restrained outer edges. The use of broken pixels creates intentional disruptions, adding a tactile quality like echoes of unresolved feelings. The main collision occurs in the center, where the pulse marks of desire meet the ash fields of restraint, creating a dynamic tension. The overall structure maintains visual clarity while embodying a complex emotional narrative.
