Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between clarity and chaos, where the pulse of a signal emerges through layers of noise. The palette of deep navy and subtle dim cyan reflects both isolation and illumination. The main contrast occurs in the center, where increased intensity of the signal color (brighter accents) interrupts the muted noise (darker colors) in a dynamic dance of clarity against distortion, fostering a visual rhythm that resonates with the seed's themes of memory and momentary clarity against static noise. The broken pixels represent interference, maintaining an imperfect consistency.
