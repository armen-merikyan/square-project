Artist Statement

The composition reflects the tension between joy and grief through a warm palette of yellows and oranges contrasting against deep blues and muted purples. The outer rows embody the weight of grief while the inner squares radiate joy, creating a zigzag flow that hints at the rise amidst chaos. Broken edges add an element of imperfection, mirroring the emotional fractures of life. The alternating warm and cool colors create a visual collision, echoing the inner struggle between happiness and sorrow.
