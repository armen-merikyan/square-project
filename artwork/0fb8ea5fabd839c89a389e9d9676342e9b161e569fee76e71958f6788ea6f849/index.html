Artist Statement

This artwork embodies a struggle between clarity and chaos, with a palette of deep blacks and vibrant signals like electric blue and hot magenta to create tension. The contrast emerges diagonally, where the clarity swath pulses through static layers, capturing the essence of a signal striving amidst noise. Broken pixels intentionally disrupt the smoothness, adding to the theme of imperfection in communication.
