Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the delicate balance between softness and severity. The palette incorporates soft pinks and whites against stark blacks and grays to create a contrast of vulnerability and harshness, symbolizing the conflict of guilt and forgiveness. The layout emphasizes a hollow center, suggesting introspection, surrounded by a mix of quiet fields and veils, where light meets dark. The collision occurs around the center, where light pulses clash with darker, more static elements, representing the emotional struggle captured in the seed's themes.
