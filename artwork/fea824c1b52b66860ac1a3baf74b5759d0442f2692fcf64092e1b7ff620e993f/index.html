Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dance between vulnerability and strength, depicted through the contrast of pale colors and deep accents. The chosen palette of moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold embodies resilience against fragility. The main collision occurs in the center with dense textures of rust, surrounded by the quiet of bone white and stone gray, evoking a sense of safety and exposure. Broken pixels add intentional disruption, heightening the sense of vulnerability in an otherwise structured composition.
