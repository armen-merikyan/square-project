Artist Statement

This pixel composition captures the tension between emptiness and desire through bold warm tones and cool contrasts. The palette of yellows and muted purples evokes a sense of longing paired with the void. The rising pattern suggests hope with colors climbing from the bottom to the top, while the layered cracks introduce an element of disruption, mirroring the journey between aspiration and absence. The main contrast occurs between warm and cool shades disrupting the flow, reinforcing an emotional tension echoing through the layout.
