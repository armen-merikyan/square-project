Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through the use of contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, dominated by electric blue and magenta against stark black, suggests a pulsating energy, embodying both warmth and isolation. The defined edges create a sense of separation, while the top-weighted layout emphasizes a pull towards the center, representing a yearning for connection. The 'spark' pattern evokes moments of solitary bursts amidst the grid-like structure of life, where echoes of connection are frequent yet fleeting.
