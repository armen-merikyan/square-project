Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between panic and control, emphasizing the struggle for balance in a chaotic environment. The palette of deep blacks contrasts with sharp whites and cool pale blues, representing the fight between order and chaos. The nearly symmetrical layout is disrupted by deliberate 'broken' pixels, creating visual tension that echoes the theme of balance amidst disorder. The arrangement suggests a pulse of activity against quiet negative space, symbolizing moments of clarity within overwhelming emotions.
