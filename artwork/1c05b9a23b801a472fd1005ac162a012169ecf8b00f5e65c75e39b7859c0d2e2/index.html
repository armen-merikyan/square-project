Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the artwork captures the dichotomy of panic and control through contrasting colors that embody warmth and depth. The palette, 'ancient_fire', with burnt orange and dark red, evokes intensity, while charcoal and gold provide grounding and complexity. The left side adheres to a crosshatch pattern representing control, while the right diverges into a 'spark' pattern, illustrating chaos. This collision of structured and chaotic patterns, alongside the asymmetric broken pixels, creates tension that draws the viewer into the struggle between order and disorder, strongly reflecting the seed's themes.
