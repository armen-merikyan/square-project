Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the longing of desire contrasted with the suffocating emptiness. The palette of hot coral and blood orange symbolizes desire's intensity, while hollow blue and charcoal evoke a sense of void. The main collision occurs predominantly in the center, where there is an interplay of maze-like patterns depicting desire battling against the calm, tide-like expanse of emptiness, creating a visual rhythm that feels both chaotic and structured. This tension between saturation and void embodies the seed's themes.
