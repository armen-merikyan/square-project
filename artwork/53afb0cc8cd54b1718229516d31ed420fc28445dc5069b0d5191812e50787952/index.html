Artist Statement

The piece reflects on the duality of loneliness and connection, manifesting through an interplay of warm and cool tones. The palette is drawn from sepia and faded blue, emphasizing the weight of memory contrasted against fleeting connections. Darker pixels gather in one corner, evoking solitude, while lighter hues encroach from the opposite side to symbolize connection, creating a visual tension. Broken pixels are scattered to suggest imperfections in memory and connection. The spiral flow of color reinforces the idea of movement from isolation to a semblance of communal presence, yet never fully uniting, echoing the theme of 'full vs hollow'.
