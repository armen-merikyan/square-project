Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between fear and hope. The chosen palette of dark violet and soft gold reflects the oppressive nature of the inner struggle, while pale green introduces elements of light and hope. The dense black and dark hues create a backdrop representing pressure and chaos, contrasting against the lighter gold which serves as a symbol of release and aspiration. The arrangement features a central cluster of light against expansive dark areas, suggesting a fight against overwhelming emotions, echoing the theme of collisions in life's rhythms.
