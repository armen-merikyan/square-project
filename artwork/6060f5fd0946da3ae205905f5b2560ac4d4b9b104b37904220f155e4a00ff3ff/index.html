Artist Statement

The artwork captures the tension between clarity and confusion through a dynamic interplay of colors. The navy and dim cyan represent calm and clarity, while black introduces depth and confusion, creating visual tension. The arrangement features an asymmetrical layout that suggests a migration from dark to light, echoing rising signals of hope amidst uncertainty. A few broken pixels disrupt the consistency, representing interruptions in thought or emotion, aligning with the theme of falling pixels and the pulse of an emotional landscape.
