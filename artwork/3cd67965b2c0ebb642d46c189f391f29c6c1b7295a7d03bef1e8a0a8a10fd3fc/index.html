Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between softness and severity, illustrated through a palette that juxtaposes warm whites and sharp crimson against deep blacks and steely grays. The density resides on the left, suggesting a weighted presence where severity emerges from soft edges. The collision occurs centrally, where broken frames of color disrupt the otherwise radial pattern, forming an interplay of interruptions and smooth transitions, leading to thoughts of guilt and forgiveness amidst tension.
