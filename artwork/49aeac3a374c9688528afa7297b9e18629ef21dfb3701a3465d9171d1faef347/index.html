Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between trust and suspicion, depicting the contrast of structured calmness against unexpected interruptions. The selected palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon evokes love and betrayal, symbolizing underlying pressures and emotional collisions. The top four rows feature a stair pattern representing trust and rhythm, flowing downward, while the bottom four rows break into a ring pattern for suspicion, suggesting abruptness and fragmentation. The interplay between these structured and chaotic patterns captures an abstract narrative of inner turmoil and resolution.
