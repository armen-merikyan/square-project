Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the tension between longing and resolve, mirrored by a palette evoking burnt orange and dark red against charcoal and gold. The contrasting colors initiate a pulse—a rhythmic interplay between warm, glowing tones of longing and the solid, structured approach of resolve. The arrangement flows from a dense buildup in the center to quieter edges, suggesting an open gate that invites reflection. Broken pixels act as intentional scars, embodying repair amidst damage; their placement disrupts uniformity, enhancing the sense of both urgency and serenity. This visual composition tells a story of emotional collision and interconnectedness, where one half feels densely clustered and heated, while the other offers a cool, contemplative space.
