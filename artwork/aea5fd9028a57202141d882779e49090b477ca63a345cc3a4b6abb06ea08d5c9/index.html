Artist Statement

This composition juxtaposes feelings of loneliness and connection through a balance of dark colors representing isolation and bright colors symbolizing warmth and interaction. The grid pattern in the top half evokes a feeling of confinement and separation, while the flame pattern in the bottom half suggests warmth and emotional bursts. The use of lime and magenta introduces vibrant connections amidst the darkness, reflecting the intricate dynamics of relationships. The broken pixels in contrasting areas enhance the sense of imperfection, mirroring the realities of emotional experiences.
