Artist Statement

This abstract piece embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette, drawn from 'melancholy_wonder', features cool tones (lavender_gray, soft_cyan) juxtaposed with subtly stronger accents (rain_blue, muted_plum). The central area is kept lighter to represent exposure, surrounded by deeper hues that suggest strength and protection. The collision of the maze and ash field patterns visually narrates the conflict between feeling exposed and being shielded. The dominant color is soft_cyan, taking up approximately 60% of the space, enhancing the sense of vulnerability amidst the thicker, darker edges that embody strength. This layout encourages inward reflection with a flow that invites thoughts of safety and exposure simultaneously.
