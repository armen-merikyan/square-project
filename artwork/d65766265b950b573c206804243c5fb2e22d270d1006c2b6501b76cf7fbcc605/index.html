Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts delicate sparks of warmth and energy against the muted grayness of ash. The palette incorporates warm coral and soft amber to represent the vibrancy of life, while cold slate and blue-black echo the quiet, heavy presence of desolation. The main collision occurs in the central section, where the bright colors disrupt the dark, creating dynamic tension. The layout emulates an open gate, suggesting the transition from restraint to freedom, reflected in the jagged edges and broken pixels throughout, which symbolize imperfections and memories that linger amidst the clash.
