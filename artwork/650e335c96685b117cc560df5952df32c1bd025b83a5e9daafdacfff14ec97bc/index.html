Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between bright sparks of vitality and the muted, ashen remnants of restraint. The palette, featuring warm coral and orange hues against stark charcoal and cool blue, evokes a complex emotional landscape. The central collision is achieved through jagged edges of bright colors interrupting the denser areas, representing the struggle between the dynamic and the static, creating a visual push and pull that invites contemplation on resilience and fragility.
