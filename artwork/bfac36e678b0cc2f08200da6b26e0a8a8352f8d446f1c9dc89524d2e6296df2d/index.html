Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between restlessness and peace through a dynamic interaction of textures and contrasts. The chosen palette of deep navy, muted dim cyan, punctuated by bold black and stark white, creates a visual conflict where calm meets chaos. The arrangement emphasizes a passage or seam, where dense clusters of dark tones collide with lighter patches, suggesting a delicate dance of memory and motion—an echo of flickers in a restful space. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions that reflect the complexities of emotion, celebrating the beauty found in imperfection.
