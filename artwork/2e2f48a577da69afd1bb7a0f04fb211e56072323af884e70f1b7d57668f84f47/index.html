Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional interplay of 'signal vs noise,' portraying a struggle between clarity and chaos through contrasting colors and placements. The palette of signal green, electric blue, and hot magenta introduces dynamic tension, while static gray and dead black serve as the background noise, embodying a fractured environment. The dominant left side, enriched with bright accents, collides with muted fields on the right, representing a narrative of isolation amidst interference. The patterned arrangement reflects moments of connection and disconnection, translating the seed's themes of exposure and shelter into a visual language of emotional resonance.
