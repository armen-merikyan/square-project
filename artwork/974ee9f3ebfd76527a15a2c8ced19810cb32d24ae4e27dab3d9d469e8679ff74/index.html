Artist Statement

This composition embodies the struggle between clarity and confusion, using a palette of stark contrasts to evoke emotional tension. The black and white colors represent clarity, while shades of red and pale blue introduce chaos and disruption. The arrangement features broken pixels that create unexpected interruptions, emphasizing moments of confusion against a more ordered crosshatch that represents clarity. The flow creates a sense of moving outward, reinforcing the dynamics of tension and resolution.
