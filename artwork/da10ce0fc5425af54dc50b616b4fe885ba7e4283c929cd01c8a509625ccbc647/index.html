Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through the juxtaposition of light and dark colors. The chosen palette contains clean white and ice blue to represent light and vulnerability, while ink black and violet gray embody strength and weight. The arrangement features a rising signal layout where dark tones dominate the upper edge, giving a sense of pressure and conflict. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, echoing the concept of a 'mirror broken,' symbolizing disruption in balance. The overall rhythm suggests an upward flow of hope despite the surrounding pressure, perfecting the story of inner strength against external vulnerabilities.
