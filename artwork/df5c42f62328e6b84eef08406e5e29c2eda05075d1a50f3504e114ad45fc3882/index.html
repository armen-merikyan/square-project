Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between confusion and clarity, represented by the juxtaposition of sharp edges and broken patterns. The palette of black, lime, electric blue, and magenta creates a stark contrast, heightening the feelings of tension and resolution. This is seen in the top half, filled with radial patterns echoing chaos, while the bottom half embraces a more mirrored approach offering clarity. The collision occurs in the transition where the dense radial patterns break against the precise mirror, embodying the unsteady balance of confusion vs. clarity.
