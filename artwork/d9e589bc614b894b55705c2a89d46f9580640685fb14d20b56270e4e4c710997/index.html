Artist Statement

The artwork portrays the intricate dance between love and betrayal, using a color palette of pinks and reds against cool grays to embody warmth and conflict. The design features a diagonal rise, reflecting the tension in relationships. Broken edges enhance the sense of imperfection, suggesting unresolved emotions and fragmented connections. The interplay of ring patterns (representing love) and broken dots (representing betrayal) creates visual dynamics that echo the complexity of human feelings.
