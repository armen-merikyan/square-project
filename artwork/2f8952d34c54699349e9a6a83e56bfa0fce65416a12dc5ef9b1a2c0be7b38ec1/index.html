Artist Statement

This composition embodies the clash between defiance and submission, illustrated through contrasting patterns and colors. The warm coral and soft amber emerge as dominant, representing defiance, while cold slate and blue-black create submission's quietness. The zigzag flow suggests movement and interruption, especially at the center where echoing colors collide. The broken pixels signify imperfection, giving texture to the explosive tension that exists in the dynamics between the emotions. Overall, the combination leads to a visual representation that is both chaotic and structured, reflecting the complexities of conflict.
