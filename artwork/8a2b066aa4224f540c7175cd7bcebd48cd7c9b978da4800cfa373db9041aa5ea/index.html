Artist Statement

This work captures the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue speaks to nostalgia and warmth juxtaposed with a colder distance. The main contrast lies at the center, where dense glitch patterns intertwine with threshold structures that rise, symbolizing hope colliding with emotional chaos. Each pixel arrangement suggests a soft tension between the push for ascent and the pull of absence, capturing the essence of the seed through abstract patterns while maintaining a delicate balance with negative space.
