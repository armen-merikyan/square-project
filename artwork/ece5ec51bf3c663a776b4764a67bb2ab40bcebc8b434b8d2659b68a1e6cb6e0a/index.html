Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve; the palette of sepia and faded blue invokes warmth and distance, representing the yearning while grounding it with the steadiness of tan and dust_gray. The primary collision occurs at the center, where warm hues intermingle with cooler tones, suggesting an unresolved meeting point between desire and determination. The layout reflects a diagonal spread from the corner, embodying the urge to reach while still anchored by boundaries, echoing the seed's themes through abstract patterns that suggest tension and harmony.
