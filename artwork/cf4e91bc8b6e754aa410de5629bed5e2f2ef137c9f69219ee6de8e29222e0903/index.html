Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tension between desire and restraint. The palette of red oxide and muted cream signifies a saturated longing against a backdrop of blackened plum and iron gray, creating a stark contrast between emotional intensity and the barriers of restraint. The collision occurs at the center, where pulse marks of warmth disrupt the muted split-screens that try to contain them. This interplay of saturation and muting directly relates to the seed's concept of hunger versus restraint, embodying a struggle that is effortlessly abstract yet deeply felt.
