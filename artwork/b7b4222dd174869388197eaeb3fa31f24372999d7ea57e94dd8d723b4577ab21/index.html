Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the warmth of love against the coldness of betrayal, utilizing a palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon. The placement of colors creates contrast: warm pinks are often interrupted by darker edges, symbolizing both intimacy and rupture. The layout suggests an empty space with a strong grip of colors at the top left, while the right side takes on a muted, lonely gray. The broken pixels add texture and imperfection, echoing the complexities of relationships and emotional states. This arrangement captures a dynamic flow of inner turmoil, subtly suggestive of the seed's conflicted themes.
