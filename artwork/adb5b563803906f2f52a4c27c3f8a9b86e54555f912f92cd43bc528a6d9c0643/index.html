Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a tension between trust and suspicion, represented through a dynamic interplay of soft and severe colors. The palette of soft warmth (powder pink and warm white) contrasts sharply with the cool, hard tones of carbon black and steel gray, mirroring the emotional conflict. The design features a pressure corner layout where colors collide violently in one area, creating an echoing effect that resonates outward. This embodies the struggle of trust battling suspicion and showcases the complex nature of relationships. The central halo shape represents an inner glow that is partially obscured by dark shadows, demonstrating the fragility and resilience inherent in the emotional theme.
