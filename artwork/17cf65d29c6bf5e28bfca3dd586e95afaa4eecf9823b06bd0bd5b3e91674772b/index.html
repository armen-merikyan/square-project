Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between the pulsating desire for connection and the quiet emptiness of restraint. Utilizing hot coral and blood orange to signify desire, contrasted against the muted hollow blue and charcoal represents the emptiness. The central collision occurs at the grid's center, where vibrant colors burst against stark boundaries, symbolizing conflict. The jagged edges create a feeling of interruption, capturing the essence of an overwhelming yet contained emotion, derived from the seed's thematic elements.
