Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between envy (represented by dense dark colors) and gratitude (reflected in lighter tones). The palette, with its deep blacks and pale blues, captures the struggle between overwhelming feelings and moments of clarity. The arrangement shifts between dense noise and structured stripes, creating a visual 'argument' that reflects the seed's themes of internal conflict. Key contrasts occur through the density of dark pixels colliding with lighter elements, symbolizing how these emotions can clash within us, while broken pixels add a touch of imperfection, mirroring the fractured nature of these feelings.
