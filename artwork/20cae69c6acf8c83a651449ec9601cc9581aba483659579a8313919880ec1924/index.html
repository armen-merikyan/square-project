Artist Statement

This piece expresses the tension between vulnerability and strength through a careful palette of soft gold and deep wine, representing light and weight. The placement plays on both the exposed center and the jagged edges, creating a haunting feel of safety interrupted by pressure. The chosen patterns create an oscillation between a stable center and chaotic influences from the surroundings, illustrating the collision of emotions and themes as dictated by the seed.
