Artist Statement

The emotional tone focuses on the juxtaposition of safety and exposure, creating a dynamic interplay of warmth and coolness, evoking both comfort and unease. The palette draws from nature-inspired colors, with soft greens symbolizing shelter and browns indicating stability, contrasted against pale skin and cold white reflecting exposure and vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center, where radial patterns of cool colors create an echo effect, suggesting a soft space pushing against a thicker, glitch-like border representing safety. The layout climbs upwards, reinforcing a sense of hope amidst tension, while intentional 'broken pixels' disrupt the flow, symbolizing weaknesses in the perceived safety. Overall, the composition balances negative space to provoke thought and reflection on the dichotomy of feeling secure yet exposed.
