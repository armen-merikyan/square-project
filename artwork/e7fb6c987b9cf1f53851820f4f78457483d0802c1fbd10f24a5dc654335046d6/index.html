Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between envy and gratitude, capturing their contrasting nature through a palette of muted reds and grays to reflect emotional turmoil. The central placement symbolizes the 'hole' concept, suggesting emptiness or longing, while the surrounding colors create a sense of pressure and noise. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, adding imperfection to the visual story of rising versus falling emotions, where darker reds evoke heaviness while lighter tones suggest fleeting joy or relief.
