Artist Statement

This composition embodies the duality of envy and gratitude, using a palette of soft pinks and deep maroons to create tension and contrast. The outer edges display a clean, rhythmic pattern, while the inner four-by-four core erupts into chaotic arrangements, illustrating emotional collisions. The stark mix of dark maroon and soft pink creates a striking contrast that captures the essence of quiet versus pressure, representing moments of introspection disrupted by intense feelings.
