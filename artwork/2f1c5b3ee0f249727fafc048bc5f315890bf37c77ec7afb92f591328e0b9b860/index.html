Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. Using a palette of warm and cool colors, the deep charcoal contrasts with lively hot coral to express desire and emptiness. The breaking points in the pattern symbolize the damage amidst repair, creating a fragmented design filled with anticipation and resolution. The central collision of bright coral against muted blue illustrates the active pulse of emotion amidst the surrounding softness, reflecting the dynamic between serenity and urgency.
