Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional interplay between vulnerability and strength through its color palette and structure. The chosen colors, such as off-white and dust gray, provide a muted base that symbolizes vulnerability, while the stronger hues like compressed red and dark navy introduce a sense of depth and resilience. The contrast type 'near_vs_far' is emphasized by alternating dense rings of strength and thin tides of vulnerability, creating a visual tension. The main collision occurs in the center where pale colors meet the stronger darker edges, resembling a protective frame that stands strong against exposure, encapsulating the essence of shelter amidst vulnerability.
