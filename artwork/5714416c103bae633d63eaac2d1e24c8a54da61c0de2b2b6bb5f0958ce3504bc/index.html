Artist Statement

This composition reflects the interplay between feelings of loneliness and connection, with the central pixels denoting moments of tension. A palette of deep navy and dim cyan suggests isolation, while the off-center white dot symbolizes the potential for connection. The design embodies a maze-like pattern on one side, representing loneliness, while a more structured framing suggests connection. The contrast occurs mainly in the center, where the darker colors collide with the lighter tones, creating a narrative of emotions tangled between despair and the yearning for connection.
