Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes longing's warmth against resolve's compression, reflecting a struggle between desire and the need for stability. The palette of burnt orange and dark red represents the heat of longing, while charcoal offers a grounding contrast. The central collision occurs where the channel patterns of resolve disrupt the flame-like formations of longing, highlighting the dance between urgency and serenity. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, symbolizing imperfect repair amidst longing, encapsulating the essence of the seed in a dynamic yet abstract composition.
