Artist Statement

This composition conveys the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve through a vivid palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue. The upper left corner, rich in dark tones, symbolizes a dense feeling of pressure, while lighter colors emanate from there, mirroring the struggle to break free and heal. Broken pixels introduce intentional scars that suggest imperfection, heightening the emotional conflict. The collision of heavy channels against lighter flood patterns embodies the tension between aspiration and acceptance, wrapping the viewer in a narrative of unresolved yearning.
