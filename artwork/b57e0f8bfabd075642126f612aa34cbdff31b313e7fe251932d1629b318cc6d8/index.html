Artist Statement

This piece reflects the tension between love and betrayal through a dichotomy of warm and cool colors. The palette of pinks and reds symbolizes love's warmth, contrasted by the grays and dark maroon representing betrayal. The arrangement follows a falling pixel layout, with colors cascading downwards to indicate a sense of loss. The jagged edges create an unsettling interruption, echoing the emotional turmoil of betrayal. The placement of colors forms a pattern that suggests cracks and fractures, symbolizing the heart's fragility amidst conflicting emotions.
