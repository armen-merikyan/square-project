Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between defiance and submission, expressed through a palette that reflects a memory of contrasting emotional weights. The dark sepia and tan colors evoke a sense of nostalgia, while the faded blue adds a layer of coolness, suggesting lightness against the heaviness of the surrounding hues. The design flows from left to right, embodying the 'falling pixels' theme, where pixels loosely descend, creating an open versus closed conversation within the frame. The broken pixel elements disrupt the visual flow, mirroring the theme of conflict and resolution in relationships, enhancing the asymmetrical tension that keeps the viewer engaged.
