Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension of awe and terror through a contrasting palette of warm reds and cool grays, representing warmth and depth. The upward motion on one side suggests hope, while the downward flow introduces a sense of fall, creating a dynamic collision. The fragmented patterns enhance the sense of broken memory, while the soft edges provide instability, evoking emotions of both reverence and fear.
