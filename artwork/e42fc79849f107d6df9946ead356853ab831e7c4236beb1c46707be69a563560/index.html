Artist Statement

The artwork illustrates the emotional tension between longing and resolve using a palette that juxtaposes warm and cool tones, reflecting intimacy and distance. The left corner overflows with dense, checker-like markings symbolizing longing, while the right side introduces quiet fields, representing a sense of resolution and calm. The main contrast occurs diagonally, creating a conflict between chaotic density and serene space. The placement of broken pixels adds intentional disruptions, enhancing the narrative of emotional complexity.
