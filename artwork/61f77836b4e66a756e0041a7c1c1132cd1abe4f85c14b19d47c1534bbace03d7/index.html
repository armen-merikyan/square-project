Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between trust and suspicion, manifesting through a palette of 'shelter green' and 'warm brown' that represent safety, contrasted against 'cold white' and 'open blue' symbolizing doubt and distance. The placement of darker colors in the edges provides a pressure border that contrasts with lighter hues at the center, echoing a transition from suspicion to trust. The maze pattern of darker shades creates an intricate sense of chaos, while the broken dots representing trust gradually reveal a more open space toward the center, illustrating the struggle and resolution within the relationships defined by the seed.
