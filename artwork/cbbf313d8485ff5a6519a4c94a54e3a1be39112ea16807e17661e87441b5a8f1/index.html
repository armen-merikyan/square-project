Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between confusion and clarity through a juxtaposition of asymmetric patterns and a constrained color palette. The dark maroon and gray evoke feelings of confusion, while the sparks of pink and red symbolize moments of clarity. The design features thick outlines that segment the canvas into a chaotic exterior and a structured core, embodying the idea of broken symmetry. The intersection of these discussions creates visual pulse and dynamic movement within an otherwise structured framework, suggesting both disruption and resolution.
