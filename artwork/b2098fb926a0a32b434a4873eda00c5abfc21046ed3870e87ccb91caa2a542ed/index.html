Artist Statement

The emotional tone merges the structured certainty of a rising signal with the chaotic curiosity of flame patterns, suggesting a journey upwards through uncertainty. The chosen palette of muted off-white, dust gray, and faint peach reflects a quiet pressure, while compressed red and dark navy add accents of strength and chaos. The main contrast occurs diagonally in the center, where the pulse marks and flame patterns collide, symbolizing the tension between clarity and chaos as they intertwine. This arrangement illustrates the push-pull of rising hope against the receding edges of doubt, encapsulated within a structured grid yet disrupted by the patterns converging in a disordered manner.
