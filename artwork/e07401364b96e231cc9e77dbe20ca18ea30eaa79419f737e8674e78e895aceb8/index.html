Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between dread and relief, where dense clusters of muted colors suggest tension, contrasted by lighter tones that represent release. The selected palette of deep grays and a hint of gold introduces a somber yet uplifting dynamic. The main collision occurs along the shifting border, where the overwhelming dark shades bleed into the lighter hues, simulating an escape from pressure into openness. The seed's qualities inspire a rhythm of tightening and loosening that resonates throughout the grid.
