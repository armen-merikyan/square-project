Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured here suggests a tension between warmth (love) and the coldness of distance (betrayal). The selected palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon emphasizes this duality. The sharp contrasts within the composition create areas of collision, notably where the bright pinks clash against the darker maroons. This collision, represented by the broken pixel elements, hints at the cracks in a relationship, embodying both warmth and the potential for pain.
