Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between restlessness and peace. The palette combines muted tones like sepia and dust gray for a calm foundation, with brighter accents of faded blue introducing moments of motion and disruption. The main collision occurs in the center, where brighter colors intermingle with deep, dark tones, representing the chaotic interplay of memory and motion. This clash creates a visual echo of the seed's qualities, suggesting a narrative of tension and its eventual resolution.
