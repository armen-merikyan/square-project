Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys the collision between warmth (love) and disarray (betrayal). The palette combines warm pinks and reds with darker maroon and gray, suggesting both passion and shadow. The main contrast resides in the diagonal flow where bright, paired colors from the 'love' side clash with dark, chaotic voids from the 'betrayal' area. This tension embodies a heart fragmented by emotions, creating an abstract narrative of rising intensity from calmness into pressure, mirrored by the broken pixels reflecting imperfections of memory.
