Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The warm burnt orange and dark red colors suggest a pulse of conflicting emotions, while charcoal and gold provide a contrasting clarity. The layout plays on a split vertical composition - with dense chaotic 'stairs' on the left and a calmer 'rain' texture on the right, reflecting the struggle between symmetry and break. Broken pixels add to the sense of imperfection, embodying the fragmented thoughts of confusion. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense color sections meet, creating a visual tension that echoes the seed's themes.
