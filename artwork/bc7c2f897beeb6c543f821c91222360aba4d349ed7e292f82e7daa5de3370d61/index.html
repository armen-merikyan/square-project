Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between vitality (spark) and decay (ash). The palette features vibrant yellows and oranges for the spark, balanced by deep blues and muted purples to represent ash, creating a visual dialogue of tension and calm. The main collision occurs in the center where bright colors interact chaotically with the darker, more subdued tones, manifesting a battle between impulse and restraint. This arrangement evokes the push-pull of memory and experience that life encapsulates.
