Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between rage and calm, employing a palette of deep black and vivid red against pale blue and white. The upper half, influenced by rage, uses sharp, jagged patterns symbolizing chaos, while the lower half features softer, horizontal elements representing calmness. The collision between dark, rich colors and gentle pastels creates a nuanced emotional narrative, illustrating the struggle and coexistence of amplified feelings and tranquility, hinting at inner conflicts and peaceful resolutions.
