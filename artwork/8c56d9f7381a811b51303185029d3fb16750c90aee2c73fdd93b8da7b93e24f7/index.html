Artist Statement

This artwork represents the ongoing struggle between tenderness and rage, using a palette that juxtaposes deep reds and muted blues. The reds capture the intensity of rage, while the blues evoke a sense of calm tenderness. The dominant color is red, reflecting the raw emotional push, while the blues provide a soothing contrast, symbolizing quiet spaces amidst turmoil. A diagonal layout creates collision and interruption between the two emotional states, with broken pixels scattered throughout to signify the chaos and interference present in these feelings.
