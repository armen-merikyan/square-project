Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between rage and calm. The chosen palette of deep reds and soft blues illustrates this contrast significantly. Intense red dots collide with muted blue horizontal bands, representing the chaotic interruptions of rage breaking through tranquil calmness. The arrangement adheres to a falling flow, symbolizing descending feelings of pressure and release, which echoes the themes of isolation and connection inherent in the seed. The edge style emphasizes stark transitions, and the broken pixels add an element of imperfection, enhancing the narrative of disruption.
