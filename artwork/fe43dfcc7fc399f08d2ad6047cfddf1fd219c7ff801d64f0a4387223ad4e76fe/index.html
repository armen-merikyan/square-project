Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between vulnerability and strength, contrasting soft, pale colors with deeper, more vibrant hues. The palette includes hot coral and blood orange as emotional accents against charcoal and hollow blue, representing strength. The collision occurs primarily in the center, where vibrant colors interrupt the softer, more exposed edges. This arrangement reflects the seed's themes of tension, dynamics, and fragmentation, creating an abstract representation of the emotional qualities drawn from the seed.
