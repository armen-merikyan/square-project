Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the juxtaposition of joy and grief, with a rise in warmth opposing a foundational coolness. For the palette, vibrant yellows and oranges signify joy, while muted blues and purples represent grief, creating a tension that captures the oscillation between the two emotions. The conflict arises in the central rows where bright colors clash with deep hues, suggesting a chaotic undercurrent of hidden feelings. Patterns of dots and grids enhance this sense of structure against disorder, embodying the complex narrative of exposure versus concealment, while leaving negative space on the corners to evoke a sense of unresolved emotion.
