Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the tension between restlessness and peace, highlighted by a color palette that embodies both contrasts with sepia and faded blue. The main collision occurs through a diagonal division, where the restless patterns of interrupted frames intersect with static peace, creating a visual discord that evokes memories evanescing yet clashing with calm. This reflects the seed's complexities of motion, memory, and edges that refuse to reconcile.
