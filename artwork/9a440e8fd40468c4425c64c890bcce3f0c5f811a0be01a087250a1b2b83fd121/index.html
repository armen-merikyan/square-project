Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional duality of defiance and submission using a split horizontal layout. The top half, representing defiance, employs a stack pattern with bold, darker hues to signify strength and interruption. The bottom half, embodying submission, transitions into lighter, softer tones representing a quiet collapse and gentle enclosure. The palette combines sepia and faded blue tones to evoke a sense of nostalgia and complexity, while the broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions that symbolize the tension points between chaos and order. The central contrast emerges where both patterns collide, suggesting an unresolved emotional dynamic between the two states, ultimately creating a rhythmic yet dissonant flow throughout the composition.
