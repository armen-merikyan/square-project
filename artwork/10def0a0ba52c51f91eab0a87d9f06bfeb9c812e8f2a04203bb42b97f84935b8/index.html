Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes restlessness with fleeting moments of peace, represented through a palette that reflects both turmoil and tranquility. The colors navigate between deep teal as a calming base and accents of cold gold that interject energy, reflecting the discord between memory and motion. A bold bright cluster at the center draws the eye, symbolizing a moment of clarity amidst the chaos, while the darker hues create a boundary that contains and contrasts the active brighter blocks. This layout underlines the tension of edges, expressing interruption and unresolved conflicts, reflecting the seed's themes of diagonal arguments and crawled edges.
