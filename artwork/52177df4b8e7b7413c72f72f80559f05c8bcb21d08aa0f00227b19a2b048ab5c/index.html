Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between restlessness and peace through a juxtaposition of sharp, crack-like patterns and smooth, ring-like formations. The palette features muted tones of shelter green and warm brown to evoke a sense of grounding and safety, contrasted with the starkness of cold white and open blue, representing a more unsettled state. The dominant presence of dark colors in the upper left corner signifies restlessness, while the lighter, smoother areas to the right provide a contrasting sense of peace. Broken pixels throughout disrupt uniformity, illustrating moments of interruption and tension. The main collision occurs diagonally, suggesting movement and emotional contention.
