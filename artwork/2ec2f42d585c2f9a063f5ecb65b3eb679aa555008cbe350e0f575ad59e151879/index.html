Artist Statement

This artwork represents the juxtaposition of pressure and quiet, structured through a 'rising_signal' layout which suggests motion and hope. The palette balances soft and muted tones ('off_white', 'dust_gray', 'faint_peach') against the weight of 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy'. The main collision occurs in the center where intense color breaks against softer backgrounds, embodying the push-and-pull of emotions. The arrangement of broken pixels throughout introduces imperfection, reflecting the chaotic beauty of clarity amidst confusion.
