Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a tension between restlessness and peace, represented through a palette that dances between serene aquas and urgent reds. The contrast occurs mainly in the diagonal arrangement where calm hues like pale aqua and mist gray intersect with fiery colors like alarm red. The structure employs broken pixels to signify moments of unrest, with gentle textures pushing against the edges of insecurity. Ultimately, this composition suggests a narrative of emotional push and pull, invoking a feeling of movement across a static frame while summarizing the delicate interplay of memory and sensation.
