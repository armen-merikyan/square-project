Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of awe and terror through contrasting colors that reflect pressure and release. The palette of yellow and deep blue represents warmth and coolness, creating visual tension. The placement of the brighter colors in the corners suggests a corner invasion of joy into the surrounding muted purple, illustrating a battle between emotions. Jagged edges enhance this sense of chaos, while broken pixels symbolize the imperfections inherent in emotional experiences. The piece maintains a negative space to foster a feeling of quiet amidst the emotional collision.
