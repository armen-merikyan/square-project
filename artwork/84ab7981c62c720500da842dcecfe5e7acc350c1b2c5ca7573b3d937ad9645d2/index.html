Artist Statement

This composition embodies the push and pull of tenderness and rage. The sharp blacks and reds evoke a sense of volatility, while the cooler blue and white hues represent the calm amidst chaos. The crowded corner expresses overwhelming pressure, with a barcode pattern signifying tenderness clashing with the jagged ladder-like pattern symbolizing rage. The collision is strategically placed to disrupt the calm space, while broken pixels serve as scars from emotional conflict, enhancing the overall narrative of unresolved tension.
