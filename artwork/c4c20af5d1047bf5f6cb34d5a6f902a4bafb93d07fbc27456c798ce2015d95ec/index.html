Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork explores the tension between vulnerability and strength, with a visual narrative of exposure and shelter. The selected palette of moss green and bone white reflects resilience and fragility, while the rust and stone gray evoke a sense of groundedness amidst vulnerability. The main collision occurs centrally where the softer, pale tones meet stronger, darker edges, suggesting an incongruous balance of feelings. The abstract pattern embodies the conflict of exposure and protection through broken pixels, representing scars and resilience within the emotional landscape.
