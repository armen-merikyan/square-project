Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances nostalgia with the push for progress, embodied in a palette that transitions from dark reds and charcoals, suggesting heaviness and past memories, to burnt orange and gold, evoking a sense of hope and ambition. The top-heavy layout captures the idea of falling, representing layers of history crashing against the aspirations of future. The row placement enhances the idea of a drippy descent across the grid, echoing the struggle between full vs hollow, with sharper contrasts interrupting smoother flows, suggesting moments of tension and release.
