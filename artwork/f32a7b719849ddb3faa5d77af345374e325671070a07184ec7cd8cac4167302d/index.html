Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between desire and restraint through contrasting colors and patterns. The use of warm, saturated hues like hot coral and blood orange suggests a strong pull of desire, while muted tones like charcoal create a sense of containment. The diagonal flow from the corner creates a dynamic tension, as vibrant colors weave through soft edges, echoing conflict. Main contrast occurs where saturated colors intersect with muted fields, emphasizing the struggle between yearning and limitation.
