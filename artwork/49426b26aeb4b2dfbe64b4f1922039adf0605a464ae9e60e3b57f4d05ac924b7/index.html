Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of trust and suspicion through its layered color composition. The palette combines soft greens and warm browns to represent trust, while cold whites and pale tones introduce an element of suspicion. The main collision is observed diagonally, where brighter colors intersect darker shades, symbolizing the struggle between these opposing feelings. The pattern flows upward, emphasizing the notion of ascent and hope, while strategically placed broken pixels disrupt the uniformity, embodying the fragility intertwined within resilience.
