Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between trust and suspicion, invoking a patterned chaos versus a stable center. The palette, drawing from warm shadows and cool blues, contrasts warmth with distance, embodying the emotional conflict within the seed. The broken pixels disrupt the clarity of the patterns, symbolizing moments of doubt and uncertainty that collide with a foundational trust—a soft glow against deep shadows. The main collision occurs at the center, where patterns of 'veil' and 'stepwell' intersect, creating an emotional heartbeat that pulsates through the artwork, representing resilience amidst fragility.
