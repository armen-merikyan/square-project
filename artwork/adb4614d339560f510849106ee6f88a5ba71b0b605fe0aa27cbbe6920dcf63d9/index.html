Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional contrasts of defiance and submission through a pulsating grid that captures a rising vs falling energy. The palette blends warm tones of yellow and orange with deep blue and muted purple, representing conflict. Bright colors speak to defiance, creating vibrant centers of energy, while the softer tones introduce a sense of submission surrounding them. The arrangement evokes a dynamic tension, with colliding areas of structured emotion and quiet negative space—allowing both conflict and calm to coexist.
