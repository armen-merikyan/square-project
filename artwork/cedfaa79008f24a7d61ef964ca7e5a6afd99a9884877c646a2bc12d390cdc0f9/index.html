Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the contrast between the dense, warm connections and the void of loneliness. The chosen palette of burnt orange and dark red suggests warmth and intensity, while charcoal adds depth and quiet. The arrangement emphasizes a struggle against solitude, with the top rows representing connection through grid-like structures and the bottom rows signifying loneliness with radial patterns and broken pixels interrupting continuity. The resulting abstract form captures the collision and interplay of these feelings, creating a visual narrative of companionship overshadowed by isolation.
