Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dialogue between control and surrender through a strategic interplay of dark and light colors. The chosen palette employs deep blacks and vibrant reds to represent confinement and pressure, contrasting with soft pale blues and whites symbolizing release and freedom. The central area bursts with darker hues, creating a visual tension that suggests an overflow, while lighter shades ascend from the edges, embodying hope and surrender. The collision is felt most in the center, where the colors overlap yet retain a delicate balance, reflecting the struggle between these opposing forces. The use of broken pixels adds a layer of imperfection, emphasizing the movement between confinement and liberation. The overall pattern maintains a recognizable strength while allowing for emotional nuance, ensuring the piece reads as a meditation on tension and release.
