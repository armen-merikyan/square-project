Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a dynamic struggle between defiance and submission, with an interplay of dense clusters (defiance) contrasted against quiet fields (submission). The palette of navy, black, single white, and dim cyan reflects isolation amidst conflict, highlighting the tension through dark and light interplay. The main collision occurs at the center, where a maze pattern of defiance is interrupted by the stair-like submission structure, allowing a visual narrative of pressure melting into release. This composition evokes a feeling of both hope climbing upward and confrontation, visually telling the story of a struggle for dominance in a finely balanced abstract arrangement.
