Artist Statement

The piece embodies the conflict between clarity and confusion through the use of a warm and cool color palette, which illustrates tension. The dark deep blue (#001F3F) and muted purple (#6F42C1) juxtapose vibrant yellow (#FFD700) and orange (#FF851B), creating an emotional collision. Patterns of radial confusion punctuate the structure, while broken pixels introduce imperfection against a grid that strives for symmetry, capturing the heart of the seed's themes.
