Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between peace and restlessness, illustrated through the contrasting palettes of off-white and dark navy. The dominant gray forms a quiet space, interrupted by scattered accents of compressed red and faint peach that symbolize bursts of energy or tension. The arrangement reflects a center with a protective yet unsettled aesthetic, emphasizing the contrast by placing lighter colors near the middle and darker edges. This configuration resonates with the seed's themes of memory and motion through its asymmetric patterns and broken pixels, suggesting disturbances in a restful field.
