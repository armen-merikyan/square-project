Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the tension between clarity and confusion. The palette of navy and black serves as the quiet background, establishing a feeling of isolation against the brighter dim cyan which represents the pulsing signal trying to break through the noise of darker shades. The layout sees an overfilled corner, symbolizing pressure as chaotic noise spills into orderly segments. The broken pixels intentionally disrupt the flow, signifying the scars that mar both clarity and chaos, creating a visual echo of frustration and fleeting moments of clarity amidst density.
