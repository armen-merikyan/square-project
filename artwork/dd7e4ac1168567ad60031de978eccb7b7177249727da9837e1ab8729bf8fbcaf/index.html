Artist Statement

This abstract composition reflects the tension between exposure and protection through color and arrangement. The selected palette of 'guilt_forgiveness' conveys a mix of warmth and depth, using soft gold and warm ivory as emotional anchors contrasted by deep wine and ash gray that evoke feelings of strength and vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense warm tones clash with cooler, pale edges, symbolizing the dynamic between vulnerability and strength. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections reminiscent of scars, while negative space helps convey emptiness, echoing the themes of the seed text.
