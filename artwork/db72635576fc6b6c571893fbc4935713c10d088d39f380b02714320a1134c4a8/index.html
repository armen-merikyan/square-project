Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional struggle between loneliness and connection through a carefully chosen palette of muted purples, deep blues, yellows, and oranges. The contrast of dark colors against lighter shades creates a dynamic tension, suggesting the push and pull between isolation and warmth. The off-center arrangement and the broken lines evoke a sense of fragmentation, while the occasional bright dot surrounded by darker hues represents fleeting moments of connection in a sea of loneliness. The layout leans heavily on negative space, enhancing feelings of emptiness while also allowing the small bursts of color to resonate as significant visual nodes, mirroring memory fragments. Overall, the piece communicates a narrative of longing and the complexity of emotional experience in an abstract form.
