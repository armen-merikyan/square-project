Artist Statement

The composition captures the emotional pull between love and betrayal through color contrasts and placements. The warm palette of pink and red signifies love, while the stark grays represent betrayal. The layout flows upward, mimicking a search for hope amidst strife. The broken pixels create a sense of disruption in the mirrored sections, echoing the theme of unresolved emotional conflicts. The crosshatch pattern for betrayal contrasts sharply with the paired shapes for love, symbolizing a split in relationships.
