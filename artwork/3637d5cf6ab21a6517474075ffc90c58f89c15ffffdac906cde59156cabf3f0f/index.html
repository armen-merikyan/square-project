Artist Statement

The emotional tone emphasizes the conflict between softness and severity, symbolizing a pull towards openness while maintaining a harsh boundary. The chosen palette combines warm pastels and cool darks to create an interplay of light and shadow, fostering a sense of tension. The main collision occurs at the center of the square, with punctuated accents of crimson breaking through a softer base. This arrangement reflects the struggle between gentle and firm forces, as captured in the seed's exploration of polarity and release.
