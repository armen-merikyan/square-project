Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between panic and control. The palette, consisting of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green, evokes a blend of fear and hope, creating a layered emotional landscape. The pattern contrasts structured control (dark tones) against chaotic bursts (gold and green). The use of broken pixels introduces irregularities, reflecting the themes of disorder in symmetry, enhancing the narrative of a struggle between calm and turmoil. Central to the composition is the interplay of dark and light, suggesting a 'spark against target' while leaving ample negative space to symbolize the void of unresolved feelings.
