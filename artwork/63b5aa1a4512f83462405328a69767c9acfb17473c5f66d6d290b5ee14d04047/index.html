Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission through color and form. A palette rooted in sepia tones and muted blues reflects the complexities of memory — warm versus cool. The left side symbolizes defiance with interwoven braids of darker colors, showcasing strength and interruption, whereas the right side, composed of subtle stripes in lighter hues, suggests a soft collapse and a quiet retreat. The main collision occurs in the center, where the dense patterns clash, creating a textured interplay of emotion. The edges are veiled with lighter shades, emphasizing the boundary between the two contrasting emotional states.
