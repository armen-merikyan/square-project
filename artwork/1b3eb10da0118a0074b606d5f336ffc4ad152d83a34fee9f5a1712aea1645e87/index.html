Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The color palette, featuring deep blue and muted purple against bursts of warm yellow and orange, expresses the contrast between warmth and isolation. The layout is top-weighted, suggesting heaviness in the center where connection emerges, surrounded by darker, emptier spaces. The dense vs. empty contrast creates an echo of hidden emotions while the 'wall' shape seed manifests as fragmented patterns of warmth breaking through, invoking both the pain of isolation and the longing for connections. The use of broken pixels contributes to a sense of imperfection, reminiscent of real emotional experiences.
