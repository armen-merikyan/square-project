Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines warmth from love with the starkness of betrayal. The chosen palette of pink, red, dark maroon, and gray signifies this duality. The soft hues contrast sharply with the darker edges, creating a feeling of conflict. The arrangement emphasizes left-to-right flow while maintaining mirror-like symmetry with intentional breaks to depict disconnection and isolation. This results in an overall pattern that suggests the tension between hope and doubt, reflecting the complexity of emotional relationships.
