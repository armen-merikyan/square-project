Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between safety and exposure. The palette features soft shelter greens and warm browns as barriers, juxtaposed with the open blues and pale skin tones representing exposure. The main contrast occurs along a vertical split, creating an interruption in the flow, embodying the 'pressure vs. release' theme. The center remains vulnerable, while the outer edges frame it, reflecting the dual nature of protection and openness present in the seed.
