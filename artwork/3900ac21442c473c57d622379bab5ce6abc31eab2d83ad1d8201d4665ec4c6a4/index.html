Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tone of the seed through a balance of pressure and quiet. It employs a palette of deep, dark hues to indicate weight and tension, contrasted by bright accents that serve as moments of release. The arrangement creates a rhythmic flow, where darker squares (representing pressure) interrupt lighter areas (representing quiet), creating collisions of emotion. The strong contrast between saturated colors and soft pastels evokes feelings of both calm and turbulence, capturing the essence of emotional complexity.
