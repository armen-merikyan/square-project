Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through stark contrasts and irregular patterns. The palette of deep blue represents calmness and clarity, while vibrant orange and muted purple suggest the turmoil of confusion. A thick outline in the outer frame symbolizes a barrier between these emotions, creating a turbulent core that echoes a sense of chaotic pulses amidst structured clarity. The placement of colors suggests a rhythmic collision, with warmer tones merging into cooler fields, capturing an ongoing struggle between symmetry and disruption.
