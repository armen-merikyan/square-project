Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between spark and ash through a palette that oscillates between warm and cool tones. The use of alarm red and ink blue creates a striking contrast, symbolizing bursts of energy against the backdrop of settled dust. The crosshatch pattern on the lower half conveys a sense of restraint, while the target pattern above introduces interruptions that suggest hope and aspiration. The central collision occurs where the two patterns overlap, emphasizing the struggles of resilience against fragility.
