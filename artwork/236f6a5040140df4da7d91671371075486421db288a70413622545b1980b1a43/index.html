Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength through a dynamic contrast of color fields. The use of black represents a stable center while vibrant colors like electric blue and magenta signal energetic tension, hinting at a struggle that feels both confrontational and beautiful. The gentle wave-like patterns of vulnerability collide against the reliable echoes of strength, embodying growth through conflict. The choice of colors illustrates the emotional story—deep shadows versus bright highlights, where the center pulses against a backdrop of negative space, allowing the viewer to sense both isolation and connection.
