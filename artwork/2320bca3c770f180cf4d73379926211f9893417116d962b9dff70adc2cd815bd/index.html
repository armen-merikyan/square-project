Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a clash between calm and rage, captured in a palette of stark red and soft blue with interspersed blacks and whites. The arrangement features upward movements reminiscent of hope colliding with abrupt downward pressures, creating a visual tension. The colors alternate sharply, embodying the emotional contrast, with soft blue horizontal bands echoing against jagged red stripes, suggesting both calm and rage. The placement of broken pixels hints at imperfection in the emotional landscape, while strategic negative space juxtaposes density against emptiness, mirroring inner turmoil against moments of clarity.
