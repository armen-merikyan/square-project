Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette, consisting of earthy greens and warm browns contrasted with cold whites and open blues, evokes a sense of shelter from overwhelming emotional surges. The central collision of colors signifies the push and pull of repair versus damage, with jagged edges creating a visual tension that mirrors the broken aspects of the seed. The arrangement of darker colors at the top descends toward lighter hues to represent a journey from heavy longing to a steady resolve, echoing the flowing tide and braided patterns. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, enhancing the composition's emotional depth without representing a literal form.
