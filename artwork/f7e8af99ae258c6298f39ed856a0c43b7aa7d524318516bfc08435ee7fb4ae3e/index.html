Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured here is the tension between defiance and submission, represented by the collision of solid, dark colors against softer, lighter hues. The palette of compressed red and subdued tones like dust gray and off-white creates a landscape of pressure and release, highlighting the struggle between fortitude and yielding. The main contrast occurs in the center, where the dark navy and compressed red assert their presence against the faint peach and off-white, embodying the struggle of forces. The arrangement reflects a balance of shapes and actions, with broken pixels representing disruptions from both emotional states, creating an abstract wave-like flow that echoes a moment caught between resisting and giving in.
