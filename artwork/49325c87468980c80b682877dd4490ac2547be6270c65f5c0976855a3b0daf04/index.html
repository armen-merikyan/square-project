Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects a battle between calm and rage, using contrasting colors to signify this relationship. The palette features deep reds to represent rage, paired with soft pale blues and whites symbolizing calm. The main collision occurs at the center where jagged red stairs abruptly intersect with the smooth ripples of the blue pattern, creating a visual disturbance that encapsulates the struggle between containment and overflow. This arrangement evokes a sense of tension and unresolved dialogue between emotions, effectively transforming the seed into a vibrant abstract narrative.
