Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances longing and resolve, showcased through the diagonal flow of colors. The palette incorporates warm tones of coral and blood orange, representing intense longing, contrasted against the cool hollow blue, embodying resolve. The collision occurs at the center where the colors blend, intersecting with quiet charcoal fields that create emotional tension and depth. This reflects the cross-current theme of desires, while the arranged colors descend like rain, suggesting a journey of introspection. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, reinforcing the narrative of longing against an often chaotic background, symbolizing moments of intimacy and distance within the depths of emotion.
