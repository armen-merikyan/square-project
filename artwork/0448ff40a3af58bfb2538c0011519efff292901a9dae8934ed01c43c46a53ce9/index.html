Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tension between longing and resolve. Using a palette of muted greens and warm golds fosters a sense of yearning, while the contrasting sharpness of darker tones evokes strength. The placement of dense greens in the center represents yearning that collides with the lighter, more defined lattice edges, embodying resolution. The overall structure suggests both chaos and order, capturing the essence of the seed's emotional narrative, where the longing for connection faces the comfort of resolve.
