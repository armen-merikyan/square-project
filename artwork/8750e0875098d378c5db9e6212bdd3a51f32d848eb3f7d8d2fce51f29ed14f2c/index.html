Artist Statement

This composition illustrates the tension between vulnerability and strength through a carefully curated palette contrasting cool and warm tones. The central area is pale, symbolizing exposure, while darker, more solid colors frame it, suggesting strength and protection. The layout features a broken frame where the warmth of orange intermittently interrupts the cold blues, symbolizing emotional conflict and connection, while broken pixels create deliberate imperfections that evoke memories of past encounters. The arrangement encourages a flow that draws the eye inward, capturing the push and pull of feelings.
