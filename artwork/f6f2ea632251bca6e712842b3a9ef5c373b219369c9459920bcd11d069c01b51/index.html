Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between confusion and clarity, using a palette of warm and cool tones to create emotional contrast. The chosen colors of dark maroon, pink, and gray echo feelings of encroaching calm amidst chaotic noise, while the structural asymmetry captures a near-mirrored balance broken by subtle disruptions. Broken pixels evoke a sense of imperfection, emphasizing the confrontation between structured clarity and disorder. The diagonal flow leads the eye across the tension points, celebrating the narrative of both chaos and resolution.
