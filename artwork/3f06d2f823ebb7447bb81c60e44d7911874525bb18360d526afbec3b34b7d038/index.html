Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension inherent in the struggle between clarity and chaos. The chosen palette—greens representing the signal, blues as calm yet vibrant noise, and the striking magenta to draw attention—portrays a landscape of sound and silence. The brighter tones emerge like bursts amidst a mostly muted background, symbolizing intervention within disorder. The primary collision occurs in the center, creating a focal point of organized noise surrounded by expansive quiet, echoing the concept of islands resisting echo. The arrangement embodies a dynamic pulse, inviting contemplation on the balance of memory and erasure in the midst of surrounding static.
