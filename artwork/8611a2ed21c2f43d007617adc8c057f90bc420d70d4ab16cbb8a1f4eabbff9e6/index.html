Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between restlessness and peace through the use of a vibrant palette juxtaposed with darker tones. The signal green and hot magenta serve as bright accents that create tension against the muted dead black and static gray areas, echoing the idea of peace disrupted by restlessness. The main collision occurs in the center where bright and dark colors intertwine, emphasizing an unresolved emotional state. The arrangement of colors follows an eroded symmetry reflecting both quiet moments and chaotic bursts, evoking the seed's concepts of motion and memory.
