Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a struggle between longing (represented by softer, muted colors) and resolve (asserted through darker, stronger tones). The palette features navy and black to signify depth and a sense of void while including a dim cyan to create a glimmer of hope or light amidst the darker spaces. The central contrast is the intense collision of these colors, predominantly around the center, reflecting the unresolved tension of the seed. The 'ladder' seed manifests an implied structure of vertical and diagonal patterns suggesting an upward or forward motion that is interrupted, capturing the essence of the wanderlust and the pull of the resolved state. This artwork reflects a moment of suspended journey and the stark balance between emptiness and fullness.
