Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of rich greens and honey golds represents vitality and growth, while the deep olive introduces a sense of grounding and stability. The pattern of 'islands' indicates strength amid chaos, offset by 'stack' flows that imply vulnerability, especially in the center where colors collide. The main contrast emerges at the borders, simulating a break as vulnerability seeps through, creating an echo of desire amidst emptiness. The balanced chaos reflects the seed's essence, inviting a reflective exploration of strength in the midst of vulnerability.
