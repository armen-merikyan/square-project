Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dance between vulnerability and strength, evident in the contrasting textures and colors. The palette, derived from soft and muted tones, represents fragility against the firmness of the deeper hues. The main collision occurs at the center, where lavender_gray and soft_cyan meet starkly against the muted plum and rain_blue. The layout employs a mirror-broken structure that emphasizes the tension; while left and right sides echo each other, subtle disruptions reveal underlying turmoil. The checker and pressure band patterns express the push and pull of exposure versus shelter, forming a cohesive yet conflicted abstract story.
