Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional dichotomy of signal versus noise, capturing a clean rhythm battling through interference. The palette combines deep navy for quiet reflection and sharp, bright accents of white and dim cyan for contrast, depicting moments of clarity within static chaos. The broken pixels intentionally disrupt the otherwise mirrored layout, representing the unpredictable nature of memory and echoes. The main collision occurs in the center, where the clean pulse confronts noise, creating a dynamic yet contained visual narrative.
