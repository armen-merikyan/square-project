Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork juxtaposes the clarity of 'signal' against the chaos of 'noise', capturing a struggle between two states. The palette features 'signal green' to symbolize the pulse of clarity, accented with 'hot magenta' as chaotic interference, creating a stark contrast. The layout starts in the corner, spreading diagonally, which mirrors the invasion of 'noise' as it seeks to disrupt. Broken pixels, represented by muted shades like 'dead black', introduce intentional imperfection, evoking scars within the composition. The central clash happens as green and magenta confront each other, suggesting a dynamic interplay between the calm and the chaotic, embodying the seed's themes effectively through abstract aesthetics.
