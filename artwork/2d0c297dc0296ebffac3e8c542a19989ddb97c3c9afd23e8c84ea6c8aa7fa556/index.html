Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional nuances of fear and hope through contrasting patterns and colors. The palette balances dark tones representing fear with softer hues for hope, creating a visual tension. The dark violet and black create an oppressive center, while soft gold and pale green introduce uplifting strokes, embodying the duality of emotions. The collision of rigid black edges against the fluid spark of the lighter colors encapsulates the push-pull of these feelings, resonating with the zigzag motif of rising signals.
