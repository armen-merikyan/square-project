Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes vulnerability and strength. The palette features muted greens and golds to symbolize nature's duality, encapsulating growth and decay. The center is soft and pale, suggesting vulnerability, with darker edges creating a protective frame. The collision occurs with bright accents interrupting muted fields, illustrating tension and the push-pull of exposure versus shelter. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, embodying scars that reinforce the main theme of emotional balance.
