Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the push and pull between defiance and submission, creating a lively tension across the square. The chosen palette—warm yellows and oranges contrasted with deep blue and muted purple—creates a vivid interplay of heat and coolness, emphasizing the emotional weights. The main collision occurs in the center where bright and dark pixels intersect, mirroring the conflict described in the seed. The patterns of 'target' and 'barcode' disrupt each other, hinting at structure being both formed and invaded, while broken pixels add intentional imperfection reminiscent of scars and struggles.
