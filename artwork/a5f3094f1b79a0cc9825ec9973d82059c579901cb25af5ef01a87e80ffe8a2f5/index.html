Artist Statement

The artwork explores the tension between longing and resolve, translating these emotions into an abstract pattern. The palette of warm coral and cold slate reflects the warmth of longing against the cool steadiness of resolve. The spiral layout suggests a coiling push and pull, with the brighter signal cyan representing moments of clarity breaking through dense pressures. Central collisions of color symbolize moments of connection, while the broken pixels evoke imperfections in the human experience. The placement creates a dance between open space and dense clusters, embodying a narrative of emotional struggle and intimacy across distances.
