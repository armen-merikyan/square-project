Artist Statement

This composition explores the duality of joy and grief, using a palette that contrasts warm (yellow and orange) with cool (deep blue and muted purple) tones. The structured wave patterns reflect rising hope, while deliberate disruptions in pixel placement symbolize emotional interruptions. The density in the bottom rows suggests pressure, transitioning to calmer areas at the top, exemplifying the release of pent-up feelings.
