Artist Statement

This abstract artwork reflects the emotional tension between longing and resolve through contrasting color placement. The palette combines deep blues and soft lavenders to evoke a sense of melancholy and warmth. The central collision occurs where darker tones meet lighter ones, representing the struggle between chaotic feelings and structured resolve. The chosen pattern types, resembling targets and braids, intertwine to suggest a flow of emotions, interrupted by careful placements of negative space, allowing the eye to rest and reflect.
