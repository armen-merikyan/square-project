Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dynamics of vulnerability and strength through a stark contrast of colors. The muted sepia and gray tones represent vulnerability, while deeper, bolder hues introduce the strength. The arrangement forms a central channel where vulnerability spills over into strength, reflecting a rising signal amid a flood. The broken pixels hint at the fragility within strength, creating an emotional tension that resonates throughout the piece. The interplay creates a rhythm of quiet space juxtaposed against dense patterns, capturing an unresolved interplay between exposure and shelter.
