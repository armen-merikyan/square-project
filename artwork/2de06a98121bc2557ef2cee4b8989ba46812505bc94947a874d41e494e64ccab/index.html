Artist Statement

The emotional tone presents love versus betrayal, captured through a duality of warm and cool colors. The palette features pinks and reds to express warmth and affection, contrasted with dark maroon and gray to evoke feelings of betrayal and emptiness. The main contrast occurs diagonally, illustrating the conflict between opposing emotions—softness colliding with sharp edges. The layout reflects a broken symmetry where warmth (represented by maze patterns in the inner square) is disrupted by noise (a more chaotic outer border), echoing the essence of a broken relationship while maintaining an abstract arrangement free of literal representation.
