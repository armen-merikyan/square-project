Artist Statement

The emotional tone evokes a contrast between vulnerability and strength, symbolizing a swirling conflict. The palette, selected from 'fear_hope' colors—black for depth and absorption, dark violet for introspection, soft gold for fragile hope, and pale green for gentle resilience—creates a dynamic backdrop. The main collision occurs in the center, where dark violet and soft gold interlace, representing the tension of vulnerability protecting a soft core. The layout imitates falling pixels, suggesting a cascading flow of experiences. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the sense of rupture and resolve within the abstract pattern.
