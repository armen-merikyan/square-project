Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green symbolizes fear and hope, contrasting dark and light to represent the push-pull dynamic of the seed's themes. The collision occurs at the center with dense blocks of dark colors interrupted by lighter shades, reflecting the central tension of protection versus exposure. Broken pixel placements disrupt symmetry and create an emotional echo, inviting the viewer to engage with the interplay between structure and fragility.
