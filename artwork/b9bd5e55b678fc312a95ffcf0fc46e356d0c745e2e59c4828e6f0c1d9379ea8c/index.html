Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional spectrum of joy and grief through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, with bright yellows and oranges embodying joy, is disrupted by deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The zigzag pattern flows from a central light, symbolizing rising emotion, while the edges reflect a chaotic frame, suggesting the dichotomy of order vs chaos. This collision between warmth and coolness evokes an unresolved tension. The use of broken edges introduces an element of imperfection, inviting a layered reading of emotion encapsulated in a checker-like interplay.
