Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dissonance between clarity and chaos, embodying the 'signal vs noise' theme. The palette comprises signal green and electric blue as vibrant accents, representing the coherent signals amidst the static gray and dead black, which evoke noise and interference. The key collision occurs in the center where the brighter colors attempt to break through a swirling gray ring, simulating the struggle of order against disorder. Broken pixels add intentional disruptions, visually representing static and erasure while maintaining a readable rhythm to create an engaging contrast between motion and stillness.
