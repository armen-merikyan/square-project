Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a dynamic interplay between longing and resolve, suggesting a narrative of construction and deconstruction. The palette includes warm ivory and deep wine, invoking a sense of nostalgia versus firmness. The main collision occurs centrally, where the soft, braided patterns of longing overlap with the structured echoes of resolve, creating tension. This layout contains a dense island amidst a calmer sea, capturing the essence of yearning amidst the solidity of resolution, embodying a story of inner conflict.
