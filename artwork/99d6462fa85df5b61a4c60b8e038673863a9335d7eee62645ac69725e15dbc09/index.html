Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around love versus betrayal, creating a dynamic interplay of warmth and chill. The palette employs warm pinks and reds symbolizing love, contrasted with deep gray and dark maroon representing betrayal. The layout features pixels descending like tears, reflecting the emptiness of an inside space flooded with emotion. The crosshatch for love intertwines with a maze-like structure for betrayal, creating a visible tension and disruption. The jagged edges add a feeling of fragmentation, while the presence of broken pixels suggests the scars of love and betrayal, encapsulating the duality of these emotions.
