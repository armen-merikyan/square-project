Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between safety and exposure through a blend of soft and stark colors. The warmer tones encapsulate the safety motif, while the cooler, more brittle colors evoke exposure. The central area pulses with light and color variations, illustrating vulnerability amidst a protective border. This intersection signifies the intricate balance, where the delicate and the robust coexist, with sporadic broken pixels disrupting the rhythm, adding to the textured sensation of both pressure and release.
