Artist Statement

This composition embodies the conflicting emotions of joy and grief, utilizing a palette with bright yellows and oranges for joy, contrasted against muted blues and purples representing grief. The layout begins with brighter colors in the bottom left, signifying an emergence of joy, while darker tones invade from the top right, illustrating the encroachment of grief. The flow is scattered, reflecting the chaotic interplay between emotions, while the jagged edges create a sense of tension. Broken pixels add disorder, enhancing the emotional landscape and preserving negative space for a breath amid the turmoil.
