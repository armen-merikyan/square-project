Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between love and betrayal, reflecting warmth and fragmentation. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon evokes both affection and the sting of loss. Contrasts occur where warm colors collide with grayscale, representing the tension in the theme. The placement creates an asymmetric pattern, suggesting a broken heart, with dense textures symbolizing betrayal in places while leaving negative space to echo love's fleeting warmth. The rhythm fluctuates—dense intensity on the left meets quiet emptiness on the right, embodying a battle between order and chaos.
