Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrast of joy and grief through a dynamic clash of colors. The palette combines bright yellows and oranges to symbolize joy against deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The layout features a central block that shifts towards chaotic edges, creating an interplay of order and chaos. The zigzagging patterns of grief collide with the bright broken dots of joy, suggesting a complex narrative of emotional duality. Broken pixels provide a sense of imperfection and raw emotion, enhancing the overall impact.
