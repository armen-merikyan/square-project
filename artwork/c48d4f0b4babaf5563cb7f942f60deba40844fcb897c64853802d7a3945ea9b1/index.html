Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured here is the tension between restlessness and peace, representing a struggle to find calm amid chaos. The palette features deep pinks and dark maroons contrasted with gray, illustrating the weight of memory and motion. The central positioning of darker hues evokes a sense of encumbered calm, while lighter colors break through, indicating fleeting moments of peace. The collisions lie at the edges where active spark patterns of restlessness intersect with more static gray patterns symbolizing peace, creating a dialogue between continuity and disruption.
