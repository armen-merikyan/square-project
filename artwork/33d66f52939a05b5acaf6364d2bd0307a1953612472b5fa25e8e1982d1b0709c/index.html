Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional duality of control and surrender, where the intense contrast of the predominant dark colors symbolizes control while the lighter hues suggest a yielding softness. The palette, featuring grave tones against punctuations of alarm red and clear white, enhances the perception of pressure versus release. The focus is on the central area with darker hues colliding with lighter accents, creating a graphical tension that evokes an emotional struggle. The structured chaos and echoes of colors emulate feelings of confinement as well as a yearning for freedom, reinforcing the overall theme without resorting to literal representation.
