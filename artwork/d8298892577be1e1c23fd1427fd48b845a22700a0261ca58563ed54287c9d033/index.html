Artist Statement

The composition reflects the struggle between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise). The selected palette of navy, black, and dim cyan illustrates a stark contrast, symbolizing a muted backdrop (noise) with bursts of brighter tones representing signal interruptions. The center pulses (color patterns) collide and disrupt, creating bursts of activity amidst the serene darkness. The layout of two pulsing centers suggests movement and interaction against the isolating echo of negative space, embodying a dance between exposure and shelter.
