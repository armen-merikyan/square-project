Artist Statement

The emotional tone is centered on the tension between vulnerability and protection. The palette employs soft greens and warm browns to evoke a sense of shelter, contrasted by pale skin and cold white that hint at exposure. The dominant color split from left to right creates a visual echo, emphasizing the dynamic interplay between pressure and release. The disrupted pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, symbolizing scars from past exposures. This pattern generates a tactile sense of both softness and severity, mirroring the collision of emotions derived from the seed.
