Artist Statement

The emotional tone suggests a balance between vulnerability and strength, represented through a palette of muted purple and deep blue with bright accents of yellow and orange. The collision occurs in the center where the radial pattern of vulnerability engages with the dense ladder of strength. This interplay symbolizes a fight between exposure and the desire for safety, with broken pixels representing scars of past experiences and imperfections in seeking protection.
