Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between defiance and submission, represented by a palette of deep, warm colors signifying intensity (burnt orange and dark red) contrasted with muted tones (charcoal and gold) indicating vulnerability. The arrangement features a bottom-weighted composition symbolizing heaviness as it flows left-to-right, with the zigzag pattern creating an echo of movement. The broken lines add complexity and imperfection, suggesting tension and unresolved conflict.
