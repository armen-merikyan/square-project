Artist Statement

The artwork explores the tension between confusion and clarity through a contrasting palette of muted sepia and faded blue tones. The colors suggest a sense of nostalgia and memory, aiming for a delicate balance between calm and chaos. The arrangement plays with broken symmetry; the left side is composed of structured, spark-like patterns while the right features layered noise, creating a pulse of disruption. The thick outlines serve to frame this conflict, while the specific placement of 'broken' pixels adds to the overall sense of fragmentation and unresolved dialogue. This fusion of order and chaos embodies the essence of the seed, creating a narrative without literal representation.
