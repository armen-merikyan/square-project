Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends confusion with clarity through an interplay of dark and muted tones symbolizing confusion, against bright accents that evoke clarity. The palette utilizes navy and black for depth and emotional weight, while dim cyan introduces clarity as a pulsating contrast. Collisions occur as lighter colors disrupt denser areas—illustrating a battle between abstract emotions like rage and calm. The layout mimicking falling pixels leads to a sense of rain-like descent, where broken pixels represent imperfections in the search for clarity.
