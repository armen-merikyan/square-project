Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dichotomy between trust and suspicion, captured through a palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue. The collision of 'glitch' patterns against 'ladder' formations suggests an interplay of unease and stability, manifesting mainly in the bottom corner, where a denser array of darker colors battle lighter hues above. This pattern creates a tension exemplifying resilience amid fragility, resulting in a striking visual with intended broken pixels for disruption.
