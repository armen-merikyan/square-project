Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the conflict between clarity and interference, reflecting the struggle of a clean pulse amidst chaotic noise. The palette combines deep blacks, vibrant greens, and electric blues to evoke both the essence of signal integrity and the chaotic patterns of noise. The primary collision occurs in the center, where sharp contrasts between the vibrant green and static gray create a focal point that draws attention while being surrounded by softer shades. This layering creates an echo effect, signifying trapped memories and fluctuating signals, encapsulating the dynamic interplay of exposure and shelter.
