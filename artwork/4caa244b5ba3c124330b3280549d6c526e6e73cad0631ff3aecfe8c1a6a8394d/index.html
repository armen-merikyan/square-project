Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a complex interplay between control and surrender, manifested through colors that communicate warmth and coolness, representing the struggle between containment and release. The chosen palette of 'melancholy wonder' emphasizes soft tones to invoke a sense of quiet reflection, layered with the tension of deeper hues to illustrate pressure against release. The main collision, at the center, creates a vibrant pulse with the bright 'moon_white' and 'soft_cyan' accentuating the muted 'lavender_gray' and 'muted_plum'. This conflict signifies the overflow against containment, unfolding in a structured yet disrupted grid pattern, where broken pixels symbolize the imperfections of this emotional journey.
