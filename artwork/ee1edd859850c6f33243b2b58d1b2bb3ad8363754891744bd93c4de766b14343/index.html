Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts longing's soft, stretched diagonal with resolve's clear, steady lines. The selected palette of moss green and bone white suggests resilience against the backdrop of rust and stone gray's fragility. The layout creates a seam where chaos and order occur, with broken pixels representing the disruptions of longing amidst the structured barcode patterns suggesting resolution. This balance yields a visual narrative of emotional tension and the interplay between density and quiet space.
