Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve through a layered interplay of muted and vibrant tones. The palette, drawn from 'hunger_restraint', conveys a sense of contained emotion with iron gray and blackened plum grounding the piece, while small orange represents bursts of unresolved energy. The checker and broken frame patterns create a sense of interruption, with the collision concentrated towards the center, signifying the heart of this emotional struggle. This arrangement reflects the push and pull between desire and stability, encapsulating brokenness and potential repair.
