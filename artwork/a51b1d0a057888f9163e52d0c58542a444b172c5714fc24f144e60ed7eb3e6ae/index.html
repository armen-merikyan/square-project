Artist Statement

The emotional tone represents the tension between control and surrender, using a palette of stark contrasts. The color choices of black and ice blue symbolize containment and depth, while the warm warning orange accentuates moments of release. The artwork's main collision occurs in the center, where the darker, shattering elements push against lighter, dot-like bursts, suggesting an overflow into freedom. This arrangement channels a sense of battling pressures, with a broken, layered texture representing imperfection and human experience.
