Artist Statement

The emotional tone of defiance against submission is expressed through a blend of sharp contrasts and soft elements, embodying tension and resolution. The chosen palette features muted tonalities of pale mint and storm gray interspersed with sparks of faded gold to suggest an impactful interruption. Central collision occurs in the juxtaposition between dense darker tones and softer lighter hues, visualizing the push-pull of emotions. The spiral flow symbolizes inward conflict, while the mirroring layout reflects the ‘mirror_broken’ concept, ensuring intentional disruptions within. Broken pixels create imperfection, amplifying the theme of unresolved struggle and disruption, yielding a visually dynamic yet abstract representation of the seed.
