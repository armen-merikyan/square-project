Artist Statement

This artwork captures the duality of loneliness and connection through a palette of muted earthy tones suggesting distant memories. The use of sepia and tan evokes warmth, while the gray and faded blue introduce a somber contrast. The arrangement creates a pronounced center with bursts of color representing moments of connection, surrounded by subdued hues that embody loneliness. The layout displays a mirrored quality, interrupted with imprecise placements to suggest the inherent imperfections of emotional experiences.
