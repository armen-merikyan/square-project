Artist Statement

This composition embodies the duality of love and betrayal through a purposeful arrangement of color and pattern. The contrasting palette of warm pinks and dark maroons creates a tension between comforting love and the coldness of betrayal. The grid-like structure represents betrayal's rigid nature, while the softer edges suggest the fluidity of love. The placement favors open spaces to symbolize isolation and emotional distance, while the collision of colors now invites the viewer to navigate through feelings of warmth versus coldness. Each pixel approaches the emotional theme of longing and distortion, captured within the quiet confines of an empty room.
