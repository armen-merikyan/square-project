Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between fear and hope through contrasting color and placement. The palette of dark violet, soft gold, and pale green emphasizes this duality, with dark tones suggesting fear's heavy pressure and lighter hues symbolizing hope's emergence. The sharp edges of the dark colors cut through the softer, rising signals of gold and green, creating a zigzag pattern that represents the tumultuous interplay between anxiety and optimism. The composition features broken pixels to induce an element of imperfection and capture the fractured nature of emotion, while maintaining negative space to prevent overwhelming chaos, thus offering a glimpse of hope amidst the heaviness.
