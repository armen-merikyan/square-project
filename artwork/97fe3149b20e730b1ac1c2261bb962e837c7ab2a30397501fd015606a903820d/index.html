Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, epitomized by a soft, pale inner core surrounded by a stronger, contrasting border. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue creates a nostalgic yet fragile atmosphere, suggesting a memory or moment under pressure. The outer border reflects the strength with a ripple pattern, providing stability, while the central square exhibits a stairs pattern, symbolizing the layered complexity of vulnerability. The pixel asymmetry and intentional disruptions introduce moments of collision, echoing the narrative of protection against exposure.
