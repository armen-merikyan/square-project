Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between vulnerability and strength, represented through contrasting light and dark colors. The palette of off-white and compressed red suggests delicate tones colliding with striking intensity. This juxtaposition creates a spiraling flow from the lighter center, signifying exposure, towards darker edges, symbolizing shelter. The main contrast occurs centrally with a gentle transition into quiet fields, evoking a dialogue of pressure and release, illustrating the emotional tension outlined in the seed.
