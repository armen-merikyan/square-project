Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between love and betrayal using contrasting colors. The warm tones of pink and red symbolize love, while darker shades of gray and maroon introduce feelings of betrayal. The pattern combines a 'target' layout for love and a 'maze' for betrayal, creating visual pressure and disorientation. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, evoking a sense of loss, while the hard edges suggest stark contrasts in feelings. The interplay of warm and cool colors, along with an asymmetric arrangement, represents the chaotic emotional landscape arising from the juxtaposition of intimacy and pain.
