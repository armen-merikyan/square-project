Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between restlessness and peace through varied textures and colors. The palette combines warm shadows and cool blues to signify emotional depth while creating an atmosphere of intimacy and distance. The main contrast occurs diagonally across the grid, showcasing a collision between bright, punctuating accents and muted fields. This interplay reflects the seed's themes of disruption and continuity, suggesting an unresolved motion with bursts of energy against calmer spaces.
