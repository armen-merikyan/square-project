Artist Statement

The emotional tone leans towards distrust, as evidenced by the heavier application of darker colors suggesting suspicion and static. The palette includes deep navy and black representing the weight of uncertainty, contrasted with muted whites and dim cyan, which signify the fragility of trust peeking through. The main contrast and collision occur in the center, where darker shades overlap with transitions of lighter tones, suggesting an inner struggle between clarity and noise. The choice of a 'tiny_cross' shape seed manifests as negative spaces created by broken pixels, signifying disruption and instability while reflecting on resilience amidst fragility.
