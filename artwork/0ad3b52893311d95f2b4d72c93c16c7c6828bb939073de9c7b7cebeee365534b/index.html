Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between fear and hope through a split vertical composition. The left side uses dark, jagged colors to evoke fear, while the right employs softer, glimmering hues representing hope. The contrast of black and dark violet against soft gold and pale green creates a striking division, ensuring a dynamic interplay of emotions. The layout reflects a flow from heavy pressure to lightness, subtly inviting the eye to navigate the tension and release expressed in the patterns.
