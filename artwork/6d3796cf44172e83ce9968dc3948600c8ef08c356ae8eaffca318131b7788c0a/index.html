Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional spectrum of pressure and quiet through contrasting color placements. The darker hues represent the weight of emotion, while bright accents evoke moments of release. The arrangement reflects collisions and rhythmic flows, illustrating how tension can oscillate within a space, creating a dynamic pulse that speaks to both conflict and harmony.
