Artist Statement

The artwork conveys a tension between quietness and pressure, embodying a struggle of soft and sharp contrasts. The palette, composed of off-whites and dusty grays merging with a compressed red and dark navy, represents the complex interplay between tranquility and restrained strength. The main collision occurs in the central region, where intense colors intersect with softer tones, creating a visual disruption that echoes the seed's themes of breaking boundaries and the push-pull of emotions.
