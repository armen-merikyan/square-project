Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts fear and hope, depicted through a palette of dark hues (black and dark violet) contrasted by soft gold and pale green accents. The patterns suggest a split between oppressive weight and uplifting light, reflecting tension through diagonal flows and disrupted symmetry. The placement of colors creates collisions, with dark areas representing fear at the edges contrasting against the joyous light of hope at the center, embodying the struggle of emotions in an abstract narrative.
