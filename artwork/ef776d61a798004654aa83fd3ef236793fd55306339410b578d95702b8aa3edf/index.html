Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between joy and grief, with colors representing warmth and coldness. The palette combines bright yellows and oranges, symbolizing joy, against muted blues and purples reflecting grief. The left side radiates warmth with radial patterns, while the right counteracts with cooler, stripe-like textures, creating a deliberate collision of emotions. The split horizontal layout enhances the contrast, suggesting a wave of rising joy against the backdrop of descending grief.
