Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between joy and grief, using a palette rich in yellow and orange to evoke joy clashing with the muted depth of blue and purple representing grief. The center of the grid is filled with brighter colors, symbolizing a pulse of joy, while the edges feature darker tones creating a contrast that feels both weighted and light. The thicker outlines give structure to the oscillating patterns, suggesting a frame of stability amid emotional chaos. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, creating a sense of unresolved feelings that echo the joys and burdens of love and betrayal.
