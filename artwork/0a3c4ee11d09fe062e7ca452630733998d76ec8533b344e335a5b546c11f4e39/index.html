Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between signal and noise, with a clear readable pulse contrasted against chaotic static interference. The emotional tone is established through a palette of navy, black, white, and dim cyan that represents clarity in a turbulent field—navy as a grounding dark contrast, and the white providing a breath of clarity amid the chaos. The main collision occurs centrally, where bright accents (white) clash against the darker backgrounds, dancing around layers of broken pixel distortions, symbolizing disruption and a broken signal amidst the noise. This arrangement represents the struggle of maintaining clarity amid disorder, echoing the seed's themes of conflict between order and chaos, with broken frames suggesting the fragility of communication.
