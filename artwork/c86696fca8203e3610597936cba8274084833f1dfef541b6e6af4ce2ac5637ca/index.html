Artist Statement

The emotional tone of nostalgia meets the relentless drive of progress, creating a dynamic tension. The chosen palette reflects the weight of memory and the brightness of innovation, with dark colors signaling the past and lighter tones representing future possibilities. The main contrast occurs in the middle rows, where lighter colors break into the heavier, darker fields, echoing the rising conflict within the emotions. The split layout accentuates the collision between static and motion, while the jagged edges suggest tensions that remain unresolved, as they drip into one another, embodying the themes of weight and lightness.
