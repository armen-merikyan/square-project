Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve. The palette reflects vibrant aspirations (electric blue and magenta) against the heaviness of black, embodying pressure and distance. The main collision occurs in the center, where densely packed vibrant colors meet the empty space, suggesting an overwhelming need to bridge a gap. The arrangement mirrors a diagonal flow, emphasizing both chaos and clarity, representative of the emotional currents in the seed. The use of broken pixels throughout adds intentional imperfection, reflecting the struggle between yearning and commitment, with quiet spaces enhancing the tension.
