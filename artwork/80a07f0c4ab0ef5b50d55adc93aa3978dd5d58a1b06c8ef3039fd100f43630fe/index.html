Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between clarity and static noise. The color palette of deep navy and dim cyan represents the emotional depth of signal amidst overwhelming interference. The left half embodies the 'signal' with dot patterns suggesting a readable pulse, while the right half introduces 'noise' through channel disruptions. The central collision occurs within the heart of the square, emphasizing how moments of clarity are eclipsed by chaos. Broken pixels are interspersed, echoing interruptions in both memory and communication, leaving negative spaces as contemplative pauses. Overall, the composition illustrates the struggle between presence and absence, sound and silence, forming a unique abstract narrative.
