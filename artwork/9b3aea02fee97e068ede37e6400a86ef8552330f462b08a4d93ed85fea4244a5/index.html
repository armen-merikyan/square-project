Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a stark contrast between peace and restlessness. The palette, containing deep olive and honey gold, suggests a battle between calm warmth and intense unrest. The arrangement illustrates a conflict between structured patterns representing peace (with fault lines) and chaotic, textured areas symbolizing restlessness (thread-like patterns). The central collision, where the two emotional patterns meet at the diagonal edge, creates a visual tension reflecting the inherent struggle within the seed concepts. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, embodying the interruptions and scars left by inherent energy and memory.
