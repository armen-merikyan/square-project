Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between softness and severity through a palette that balances warm whites and powder pink against the starkness of carbon black and sharp crimson. The braiding pattern symbolizes softness, engaging with the steeper stairs of severity, suggesting a conflict. The center serves as the collision point where bright crimson juts out against muted backgrounds, creating a dynamic rhythm. The overall flow is diagonal, hinting at movement or struggle, with a slight presence of negative space to suggest unresolved tensions.
