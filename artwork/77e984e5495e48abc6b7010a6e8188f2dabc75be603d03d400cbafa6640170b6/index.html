Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tone of vulnerability versus strength through a pale center surrounded by darker, more intense colors, representing the tension between exposure and shelter. The chosen palette highlights muted earth tones juxtaposed against soft blues, creating a contrast between warmth and coolness, suggesting both comfort and uncertainty. The collision occurs in the center, where thin marks of pale colors struggle against denser, darker edges, echoing the conflict between protective barriers and unguarded openness. This interplay embodies the essence of exposure and shelter, rendered in abstract patterns to evoke deep emotional resonance.
