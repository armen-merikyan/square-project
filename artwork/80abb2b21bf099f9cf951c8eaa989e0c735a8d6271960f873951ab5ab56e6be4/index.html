Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional contrast of defiance and submission through a structured yet chaotic layout. The palette blends warm tones of hot coral and blood orange with cool shades of charcoal and hollow blue, symbolizing the conflict between resistance and yielding. The main collision occurs at the center, depicting the intense struggle, while the surrounding colors indicate submission's quieter influence, creating an intricate balance of pressure and release. The uneven distribution hints at broken moments of defiance, mirroring the layer of complexity inherent in the seed's themes.
