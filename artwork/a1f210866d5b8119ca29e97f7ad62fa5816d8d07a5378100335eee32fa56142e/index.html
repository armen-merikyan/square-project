Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional duality of restlessness and peace, reflected in the stark contrast between light and dark colors. The palette, dominated by pale field tones and dark navy, suggests tranquility interrupted by moments of kinetic energy in red. The broken pixel design reflects imperfections and the struggle between opposing feelings, with the main collision occurring in the central region where the bright accent interacts with the muted background, creating a compelling narrative of tension and resolution.
