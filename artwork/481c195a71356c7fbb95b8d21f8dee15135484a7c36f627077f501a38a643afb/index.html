Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between the warmth of trust and the cold suspicion through a balanced yet fractured composition. The color palette, characterized by soft sepia and muted blues, emphasizes a sense of nostalgia and broken memories. The left side exudes quiet balance, while the right introduces darker tones and fragmented patterns, illustrating the collision between the two emotions. The stark contrast occurs in the middle, where the organized 'rain' pattern of light colors meets the chaotic 'pressure_band' of shadows, evoking a visual tension between structure and disorder.
