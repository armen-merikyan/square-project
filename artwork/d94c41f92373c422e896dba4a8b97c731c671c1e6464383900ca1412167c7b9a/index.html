Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between tenderness and rage, embodying the chaos of feelings through jagged edges of dark reds against cool, muted blues. The palette of deep red, black, pale blue, and white fosters a stark contrast that underscores the battle between softness and aggression, with a dominant dark red section clashing against gentler hues in a diagonal layout. The 'spark' and 'pulse_marks' patterns collide, symbolizing broken relationships and emotional overlaps while maintaining a scattered flow. Broken pixels add intentional disruptions, reflecting the imperfections in the narrative of guilt and forgiveness, allowing for a whisper of quiet amidst the emotional overwhelm.
