Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between trust and suspicion, visualized through a layout where one corner bursts with color and detail reflecting chaos, while the rest embodies calmness, symbolizing trust. The palette merges earthy tones and cool shades, illustrating warmth conflicting against cold detachment. The collision occurs prominently in the upper left corner, where vibrant accents disrupt the structured calm of the surrounding area. The overall arrangement evokes a sense of resilience amidst fragility, mirroring the complexities of relationships and perceptions.
