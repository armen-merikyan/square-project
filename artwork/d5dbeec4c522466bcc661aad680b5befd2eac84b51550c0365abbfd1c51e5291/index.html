Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between joy and grief, represented through contrasting color palettes of bright yellows and deep blues. The layout flows from a central series of bright pixels outward, reflecting joy as the force radiating away, while the darker colors surrounding them create a sense of encroaching grief. The arrangement suggests an inward spiral, mimicking how joy can feel threatened yet remains central, thereby embodying resilience amidst sorrow. The jagged edges introduce a 'hard' feeling of disruption, enhancing the emotional landscape.
