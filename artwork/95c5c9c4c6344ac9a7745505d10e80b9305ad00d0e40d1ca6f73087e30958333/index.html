Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional struggle between restlessness and peace, employing a color palette that contrasts warmth and coolness. The combination of lush, soft pastels with darker, more muted tones reflects the emotional collision of comfort and unease. The structure follows a spiral flow to suggest the motion of memory, evoking a sense of upward growth that meets resistance in downward pulls. The primary tension is evident through the asymmetrical distribution of colors, with brighter hues signaling moments of clarity against the weight of darker shades, thus articulating the journey from motion into stillness.
