Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork encapsulates the tension between emptiness and desire, with cool blues reflecting a sense of longing and warmth from yellows and oranges suggesting hope. The contrast is stark yet symbiotic, with the left half embodying desire through a grid pattern, while the right represents emptiness with jagged, broken pixels. The collision of these patterns creates an unsettling pulse, echoing the push and pull of emotions. This interplay presents a visual narrative of a heart fragment caught between yearning and void, mirroring the seed concepts into an abstract expression.
