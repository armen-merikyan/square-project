Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between loneliness and connection. The palette of warm yellows and muted purples suggests the warmth of connection but contrasts it with cooler, darker tones, representing isolation. The central area is lighter, symbolizing potential connection, surrounded by heavier, darker colors to imply pressure and separation. The arrangement of broken dots versus layered colors reflects the dual nature of hidden versus exposed emotions, creating a rhythmic pulse that echoes the core sentiment of the seed.
