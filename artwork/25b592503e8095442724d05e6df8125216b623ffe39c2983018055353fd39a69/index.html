Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork captures the tension between desire and restraint, reflecting the push and pull of longing and limitation. The palette features deep hues like red_oxide and blackened_plum representing desire, while muted_cream and iron_gray embody restraint. The main contrast occurs in the center where saturated, irregular markings collide with the stable stripes of cooler shades, highlighting the tension. The layout, inspired by a halo void, accentuates this conflict by leaving negative space that echoes both presence and absence, reinforcing the idea of longing being both expansive and confined.
