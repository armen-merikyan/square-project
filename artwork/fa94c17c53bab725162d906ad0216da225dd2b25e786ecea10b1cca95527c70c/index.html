Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of navy, black, dim cyan, and occasional white represents a blend of distant warmth and steady strength. The irregular pattern of crosshatches surrounded by noise embodies the yearning for connection amidst chaos. The central collision of serenity and urgency suggests a space where emotion intertwines, with broken pixels creating intentional disruption, echoing the complexity of human experience.
