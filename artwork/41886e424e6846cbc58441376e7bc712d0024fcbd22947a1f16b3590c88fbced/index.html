Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, where soft pastels meet deeper grays. The color palette of pale mint and faded gold expresses a sense of longing, while storm gray and near black convey resolve. The layout features a sparse negative space channel cutting through dense, overlapping colors, creating a pulse of urgency. The main collision occurs centrally with densely packed elements, evoking a sense of both pressure and serenity—reflecting the 'anchor' shape seed through implied structural strength without literal representation.
