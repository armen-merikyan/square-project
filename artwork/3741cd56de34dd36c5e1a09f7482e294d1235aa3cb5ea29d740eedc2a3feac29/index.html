Artist Statement

This piece embodies the duality of panic and control through the juxtaposition of warm and cool tones. The palette, drawn from the emotional depths of joy and grief, builds a sense of unrest and stability. The darker shades near the edges signify chaos, while the softer inner colors represent a longing for order. The asymmetrical placement of the bright yellows and oranges against muted purples creates a striking contrast, visually narrating the conflict between calm versus pressure, with hints of broken symmetry suggesting imperfection amidst striving for balance.
