Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity, utilizing a palette that juxtaposes warmth and coolness. The main colors—powder pink and sharp crimson—contrast sharply with the deep tones of carbon black and steel gray, creating a sense of urgency amidst a background of quiet spaces. The layout leans towards 'empty room,' leading to a dominant area of certainty, shaped by stiff ladder-like structures, interrupted by softer, channel-like formations that suggest curiosity. The main collision occurs at the center, inviting viewers into the chaos of the emotional interplay without depicting literal shapes. The selection of broken pixels subtly suggests the frailty amid the rigidity of emotional states.
