Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between calm and pressure, using a contrasting palette of deep blues and vibrant yellows. The arrangement emphasizes clusters of intensity (the warm yellows and oranges) colliding with cooler, quieter colors (the blues and grays), representing an emotional push and pull. Main contrasts occur in the central rows where brightness interrupts calmness, suggesting a narrative of unresolved feelings. This interplay of colors captures the essence of rhythmic release amidst emotional collisions.
