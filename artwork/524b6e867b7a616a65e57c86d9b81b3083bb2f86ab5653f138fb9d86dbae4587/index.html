Artist Statement

This abstract artwork juxtaposes the emotional tones of clarity and confusion, reflecting the battle between structured patterns and chaotic interference. The selected palette of black, red, pale blue, and white emphasizes strength and vulnerability—black and red suggest intensity and underlying rage, while pale blue and white invoke calmness. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense clusters of color create tension against the quiet negative space around them, echoing the 'falling_pixels' and suggesting a pulse that shifts from chaos to clarity. This is manifested through a broken dot pattern interspersed with distinct targets, embracing the concept of nearly symmetrical layouts disrupted by colored disruptions.
