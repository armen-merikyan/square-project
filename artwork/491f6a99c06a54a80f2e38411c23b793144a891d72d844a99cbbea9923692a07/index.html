Artist Statement

The piece encapsulates the tension between clarity and confusion. The left half, representing confusion, employs muted purples and oranges, creating a sense of overlapping paths and false symmetry. Contrastingly, the right half embodies clarity with vibrant yellows and deep blues, exhibiting a sense of structured flow. The chosen palette juxtaposes warm and cool tones, where the pulse of colors hints at an emotional rhythm. The broken pixels interject misleading calm into an otherwise tense composition, reflecting on the clash between harmony and disruption. The overall design manifests as a visual dialogue, resonating with layers of emotional depth through color and placement.
