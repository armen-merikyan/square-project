Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between vulnerability and security, utilizing a color palette that contrasts warm browns and greens (safety) against cool whites and blues (exposure). The collision occurs primarily in the center, where the softer tones tussle with deeper shades, creating a pulse between tension and calm. The split vertical layout suggests a dynamic response to pressure, interspersed with broken pixels that add deliberate irregularities, simulating emotional scars and releases, emphasizing the dialogue between these opposing forces.
