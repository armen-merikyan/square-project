Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between clarity and distortion, creating a dynamic resonance of tension and release. The palette, featuring deep blacks, vibrant greens, and hints of electric blue, evokes the clear yet chaotic essence of sound waves battling against interference. The central collision manifests as an off-center spiral of green teeth patterns clashing with static gray and hot magenta, symbolizing the fight between signal and noise. Broken pixels dispersed throughout add disruption, enhancing the narrative of fragmentation versus coherence.
