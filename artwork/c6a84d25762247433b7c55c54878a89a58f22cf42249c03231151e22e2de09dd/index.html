Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between restlessness and peace. The palette features muted sepia and tan tones alongside the calming faded blue, encapsulating a sense of nostalgia while interrupting it with flashes of contrast. The main collision is found within the center, where brighter colors peak through the softer edges, embodying a spark of life amidst the calm. The layout suggests a field of stillness, disrupted by jagged patterns that echo the underlying conflict between calm and chaos.
