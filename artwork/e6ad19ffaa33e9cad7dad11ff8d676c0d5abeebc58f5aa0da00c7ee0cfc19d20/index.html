Artist Statement

This abstract composition evokes the tension between longing and resolve through layered emotional contrasts. The muted yet warm palette featuring soft pink, warm white, and deep black reflects the interplay of hope and despair. The central collision of sharp crimson against the cooler tones illustrates an urgent pulse amidst a sense of contained overflow, echoing the seed's theme of tension between repair and damage. The broken frame layout enhances the feeling of incompleteness and unresolved emotion.
