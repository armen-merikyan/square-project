Artist Statement

This composition embodies the interaction between vulnerability and strength. The selected palette of deep blue, pale yellow, black, and muted stone evokes a sense of mourning contrasted with renewal. The edges are jagged, allowing for a collision between the soft, lighter colors that symbolize vulnerability and the deeper tones representing strength. The arrangement flows upward, echoing a rising signal against the weight of the darker hues, while the broken pixels disrupt the composition intentionally, suggesting that even in strength, there are fragile elements. The tension between density and lightness creates an emotional narrative of resilience amidst fragility.
