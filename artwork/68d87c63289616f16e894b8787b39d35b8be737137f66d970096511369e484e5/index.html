Artist Statement

The emotional tone draws from a blend of confusion and clarity, utilizing a stark palette of navy and black to symbolize depth and void, while dim cyan and white punctuate the canvas to create focal points of clarity amid chaos. The pattern, characterized by opposing flows, uses the 'stairs' arrangement to depict a layered journey of perception. The contrast between heavy, filled spaces and delicate, broken pixels introduces collisions of feeling, suggesting an unsettling negotiation between clarity and confusion at the center versus edges. This imbalance is intended to evoke an unresolved emotion, as if clarity intermittently breaks through but is incessantly clouded by encroaching confusion.
