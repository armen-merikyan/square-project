Artist Statement

The emotional tone is anchored in the tension between loneliness and connection, explored through a palette weighted towards dark, muted colors (navy, black) juxtaposed with rare bright accents. The arrangement creates a stark contrast; the outside is heavy and oppressive, suggesting loneliness, while the inner core lightens with hints of exposure, indicating connection. Patterns of noise and frame clash in an asymmetric layout, echoingancient fire burning against isolation. The presence of empty space enhances the feeling of loneliness, while the bursts of lighter color represent fleeting moments of connection amid the darkness. The grid pattern symbolizes the struggle between barriers and connections, driving the abstract narrative.
