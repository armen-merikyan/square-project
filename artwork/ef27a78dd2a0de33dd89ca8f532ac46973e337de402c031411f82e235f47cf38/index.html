Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the tension between restlessness and peace. The palette employs deep wine and soft gold to represent warmth and calm, with ash gray as a quiet field color. The main contrast occurs at the center, where pulse marks signify motion and disarray, interacting with the barcode patterns that suggest a sense of order and repair. Broken pixels interrupt the smoother patterns, symbolizing disruptions in memory and ongoing motion, ultimately creating an abstract environment where chaos and calm coexist.
