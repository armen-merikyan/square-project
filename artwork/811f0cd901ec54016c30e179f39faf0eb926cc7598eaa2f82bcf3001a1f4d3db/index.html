Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances longing and resolve through fragmented textures that engage in a dance of potential and certainty. The chosen palette of moss green and bone white represents warmth and fragility, while rust and stone gray embody the tension between repair and damage. The main contrast occurs at the center, where layers of dashed lines and glitch patterns collide, creating a pulse-like rhythm that encapsulates both serenity and urgency, echoing the struggle of connection amidst disruption.
