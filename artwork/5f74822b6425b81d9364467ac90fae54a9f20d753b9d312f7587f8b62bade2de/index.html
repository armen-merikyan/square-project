Artist Statement

This square explores the emotional tumult between rage and calm through contrasting patterns and color palettes. The selection of red as an accent against the backdrop of cooler tones symbolizes the tension created by fiery emotions clashing with serenity. The layout mimics falling pixels, representing chaotic sparks nested within a structured calm, embodying the conflict from noise (rage) against quiet fields (calm). The dominant presence of darker colors encapsulates the feeling of 'contained vs overflowing', while brighter pixels break through this calm, creating a striking visual dialogue around the edges.
