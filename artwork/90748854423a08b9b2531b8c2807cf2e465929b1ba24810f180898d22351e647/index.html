Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between panic and control through contrasts of color and space. The central darker pixels signify control and stability, while the surrounding vibrant colors suggest the chaos of panic. The use of broken dots pattern connects both emotions, representing fragmented thoughts. The chosen palette reflects a mechanical emotion, grounding the piece while allowing bursts of color to disrupt and create visual tension, highlighting the constant battle between feeling secure and the fear of chaos.
