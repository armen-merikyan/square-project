Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between safety and exposure, manifesting in a visual interplay of soft and harsh colors. The palette, including shelter green and cold white, creates a contrast between nurturing safety and stark exposure. The collision occurs at the central 4x4 area, where the patterns of safety (embers) and exposure (ash field) overlap, creating a vibrant yet tense interaction. This structure symbolizes a core of vulnerability surrounded by a protective outer layer, embodying the emotional dynamics implied in the seed.
