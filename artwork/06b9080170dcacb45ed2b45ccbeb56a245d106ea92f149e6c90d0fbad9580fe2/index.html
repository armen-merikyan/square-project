Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional duality of strength and vulnerability. The palette of deep teal and cold gold creates a stark contrast—strength represented by the dense, dark tones, while vulnerability finds expression in the pale, soft accents. The primary collision occurs at the center, where the heavier colors interact with lighter hues, symbolizing the dynamic push and pull of inner strength challenged by vulnerability. The eroded symmetry layout further emphasizes this conflict, portraying the notion of a protected core under the weight of external pressures, while broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions that mirror the imperfections of human connection.
