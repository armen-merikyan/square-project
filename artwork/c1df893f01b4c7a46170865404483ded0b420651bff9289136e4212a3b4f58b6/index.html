Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, portraying a spectrum of feelings through the selected palette. The colors shift from the warm softness of powder pink and warm white, representing serenity and hope, to the starkness of carbon black and sharp crimson, depicting urgency and damage. This layout employs stacked bands to mimic emotional strata, with a primary collision at the center where warmth meets severity. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, emphasizing flaws and the sense of shouting within a whisper, mirroring the struggle between repair and damage. The pattern arises as a flame and spark merge, hinting at a quiet underlying pulse of strength founded in vulnerability.
