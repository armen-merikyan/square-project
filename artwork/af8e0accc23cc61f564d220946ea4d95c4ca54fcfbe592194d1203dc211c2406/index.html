Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional duel between dread and relief. The chosen palette features deep blacks and cool tones, symbolizing dread, counterbalanced by stark whites and soft cyans to suggest moments of relief. The main contrast occurs where dark and light colors collide, creating tension and punctuations of silence amid the heavier masses. The pattern's layout leads from dark at the top, suggesting a weighted heaviness, toward brighter accents below, offering a subtle escape—a narrative of ascent amidst confinement.
