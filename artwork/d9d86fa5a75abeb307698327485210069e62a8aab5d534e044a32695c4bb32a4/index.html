Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between control and surrender, depicted through a dense central pixel area framed by softer, quieter spaces. The palette of soft powder pink, warm white, and sharp crimson enhances the feeling of pressure and release, with stark contrasts in color emphasizing breakdowns and interruptions. The thick outlines signify control while the faded colors around create a sense of surrender, with collision evident at the center where the most intense colors converge, suggesting an overflowing signal fighting against noise.
