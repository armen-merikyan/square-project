Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a battle between safety and exposure, using a palette of soft greens and browns with sharp white accents to illustrate vulnerability within protection. The main collision occurs at the center, where warmer tones suggest exposure and cooler tones embody safety, creating a visual tension. This conflict is enhanced by the use of broken pixels that interrupt the smoothness, indicating imperfections in both security and threat, shaping the abstract pattern into a narrative of emotional contrast.
