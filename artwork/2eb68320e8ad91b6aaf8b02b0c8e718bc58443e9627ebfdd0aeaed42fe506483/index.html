Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between defiance and submission, represented by refreshing pale green and imposing dark violet. The palette suggests hope amidst chaos, where the soft gold serves as a luminous clash against the deeper hues. The core 4x4 section is dense, embodying quiet but pressing pressure, while the jagged edges repeat the conflict, creating a swirling motion that embodies the chaotic rhythm and release of emotions. Overall, the arrangement of colors emanates an abstract story of pushing against restraints and finding freedom within constraints.
