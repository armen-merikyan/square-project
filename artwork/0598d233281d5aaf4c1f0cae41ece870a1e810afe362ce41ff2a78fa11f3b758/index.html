Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between defiance (bright, angular interruptions) and submission (soft, receding corners). The palette of sepia, tan, and faded blue contrasts starkly, reflecting emotional complexity through both warm and cool tones. The main collision occurs in the central square, marked by a broken pixel technique that interrupts the flow. This reflects chaos amid order, echoing the seed's themes of connectivity and disruption, forming an abstract representation of emotional thresholds and layered experiences.
