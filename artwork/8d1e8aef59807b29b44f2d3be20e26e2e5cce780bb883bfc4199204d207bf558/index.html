Artist Statement

This piece captures the tumultuous relationship between defiance and submission through a clash of warm and cool tones. The palette, inspired by 'guilt_forgiveness,' mixes deep wine for emotional weight, soft gold as a subtle focal point, and ash gray to denote the quiet spirals of submission. The main tension radiates from the center, where the darker shades press against lighter, airy colors, creating a dynamic interplay that suggests both the struggle and acceptance inherent in the emotional themes. Broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, signifying the scars of conflict and the push-pull of inner turmoil. The overall structure maintains clear readability, yet offers complex layers beneath the surface.
