Artist Statement

The piece captures the struggle between loneliness and connection through a palette of deep blacks, soft golds, and pale greens. The contrast showcases a grid-like structure, where dark hues dominate the top, representing emotional weight and isolation, while lighter shades suggest a subtle burst of potential connection at the center, reflecting hope and exposure. Broken pixels evoke imperfection, emphasizing the transient nature of emotions. The emotional flow is dictated by a top-heavy design, pooling darker tones that fade into lighter colors—symbolizing a journey inward from chaotic noise to a hint of connection.
