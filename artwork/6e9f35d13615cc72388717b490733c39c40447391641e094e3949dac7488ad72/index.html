Artist Statement

This piece explores a dichotomy of longing and resolve, using a palette that embodies 'broken memory' with sepia and faded blue tones which suggest nostalgia and emotional weight. The sharp contrasts between deep blues and softer tans create a dynamic interplay, capturing the tension between feelings of serenity and urgency. The central grid represents the structural resolve, while the irregularities reflect the imperfections of longing, achieving a balance that feels both contained and overflowing. The intentional use of broken pixels embodies the 'repair vs damage' theme, creating echoes within the composition.
