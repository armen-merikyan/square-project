Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between clarity and confusion, illustrated through a palette of red, pink, dark maroon, and gray, representing love and betrayal. The arrangement invokes a center-outward flow, creating a sense of warmth in the middle, contrasting with cooler, darker edges. The falling pixel layout contributes to a sense of descent and fragmentation. Main collisions happen where bright reds and pinks clash with cooler grays, creating tension and unresolved motion within the square, embodying the complexities of emotional states.
