Artist Statement

This composition embodies the duality of fear and hope through contrasting edges and patterns. The dark violet serves as a foundation, representing fear's heavy grip, while soft gold and pale green provide spikes of hope, creating tension and resolution. The jagged edges introduce chaos, while small areas of light symbolize resilience. The interaction between the densely packed dark pixels and the scattered bright pixels hints at a narrative of struggle and emergence.
