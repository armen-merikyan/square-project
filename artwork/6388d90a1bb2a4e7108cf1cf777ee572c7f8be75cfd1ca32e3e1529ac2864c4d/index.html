Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between spark and ash, portraying both vitality and decay. The warm pink and red accents represent the ignition of passion and desire, while the gray and dark maroon provide a grounding overlay, symbolizing restraint and fading memories. The layering of ring patterns on one side colliding with fault line patterns on the other illustrates the struggle of a quick burst against settled dust, creating a dynamic yet imperfect balance. The main contrast resides centrally, where vibrant colors meet muted backgrounds, allowing for visual tension that reinforces the emotional nuances of the seed.
