Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional struggle between panic and control, employing a palette of yellow, orange, deep blue, and muted purple to represent joy and grief. The arrangement presents a nearly symmetrical layout disrupted by subtle, asymmetrical adjustments to convey chaos amid structure. The dominant contrast occurs between warm, vibrant colors and darker, muted tones, evoking a sense of unresolved tension and collision, mirroring the conflict of feelings within the seed. The combination of dot patterns and noise enhances this dynamic, creating an abstract narrative of pressure and release with a feel of balance and discord.
