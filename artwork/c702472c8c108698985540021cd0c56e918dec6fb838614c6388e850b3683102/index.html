Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between relief and dread, depicted through contrasting warm and cool palettes. Bright yellows and oranges symbolize hope and relief, while deep blues and muted purples represent the weight of dread. The layout employs a rising flow, where lighter elements spill upwards, facing dark, dense clusters that interrupt the visual rhythm. Broken pixels enhance imperfections, echoing the feeling of tension against relief. The arrangement conveys an unresolved dynamic, merging moments of brightness with overarching heaviness.
