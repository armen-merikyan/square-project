Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the tension between vulnerability and strength, illustrated through a palette of burnt orange and dark red representing strength, standing against charcoal and gold, which symbolize vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center, where structured 'noise' patterns blur into softer 'ash_field' textures, creating an echo of strength meeting vulnerability. The broken pixels accentuate moments of disruption in an otherwise balanced grid, suggesting a threshold where these emotions coalesce.
