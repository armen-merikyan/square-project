Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic interplay of vulnerability and strength, with a pale center suggesting exposure surrounded by darker edges, symbolizing shelter. The palette features 'melancholy wonder' colors, where soft cyan and lavender gray evoke vulnerability and openness, while deeper hues of muted plum and rain blue assert strength and protection. The main collision occurs at the center, where the flame and constellation patterns intersect, creating a tension between the light interior and heavy edges, manifesting the complexities of connection and alienation. Broken pixels disrupt this balance, enhancing the narrative of imperfections and scars in the pursuit of shelter.
