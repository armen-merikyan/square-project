Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between fear and hope through a contrasting palette. The outer edges, represented by darker shades, evoke a sense of encroaching anxiety, while the subtle hints of soft gold and pale green signal the presence of hope within the darkness. The jagged pattern introduces a sharpness that clashes dynamically with smoother, lighter spaces in the center, representing the struggle between opposing emotions. The flow of the layout draws the viewer’s eye inward, encapsulating a quiet resilience amidst chaotic noise. The broken pixels add a sense of imperfection, suggesting that even in fear, glimpses of hope can be found.
