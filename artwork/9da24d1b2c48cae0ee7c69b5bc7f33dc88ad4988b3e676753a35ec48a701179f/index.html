Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional dialogue between defiance and submission, reflected through a juxtaposition of dark and light tones. The palette chosen, 'rage_calm', features stark contrasts between warm reds and cool pale blues, creating a push-pull dynamic that embodies tension. The central pixels evoke a feeling of weight and pressure, gradually lightening towards the edges, symbolizing submission or opening. Broken and solid patterns contrast throughout, representing the chaos of falling pixels against a structured frame. The rhythm flows from bottom to top, suggesting a movement that starts with dense pressures and resolves into lighter spaces, illustrating the complex interplay of emotions. Negative space is intentionally left, allowing the viewer to breathe among the intensity, echoing the unresolved tension within the narrative.
