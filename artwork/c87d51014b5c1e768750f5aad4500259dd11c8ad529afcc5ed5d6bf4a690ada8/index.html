Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between the chaos of panic and the structure of control. The selected palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green emphasizes depth (black/violet) and fleeting hope (gold/green). The contrasting areas create a dance of stability and instability, with the chaos primarily situated on the right side represented by broken or dispersed pixels. The layout mirrors asymmetrically - providing intentional breaks in the flow, suggesting moments of confusion amidst a solid framework.
