Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, reflected in the use of contrasting colors to create a feeling of collision. The palette of warm browns, greens, and cool whites signifies shelter amidst a chaotic backdrop, while the broken edges carry a sense of imperfection through a few scattered pixels. The main contrast occurs at the center, where intimate closeness meets emptiness. The diagonal spread of the color shapes resonates with ideas of structured chaos, embodying a journey from quiet introspection to the clarity of resolve.
