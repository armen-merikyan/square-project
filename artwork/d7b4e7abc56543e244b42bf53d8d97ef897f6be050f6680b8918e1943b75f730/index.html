Artist Statement

This composition juxtaposes the contrasting emotions of joy and grief through a vibrant palette of yellow and orange against deep blue and muted purple. The arrangement features heavier, darker tones in the corners, symbolizing grief, while lighter, expanding shapes run diagonally, representing joy. Broken pixels provide a sense of imperfection, capturing the unresolved nature of these feelings as they collide and ebb. The layout flows from the corner, suggesting an invasion of emotions that causes tension and release across the grid.
