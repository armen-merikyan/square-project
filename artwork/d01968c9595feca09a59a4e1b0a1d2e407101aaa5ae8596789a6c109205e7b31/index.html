Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the pull between longing and resolve, captured by alternating warm and cool colors. The palette, featuring rose clay and distant lilac, suggests warmth and intimacy while contrasting with the cool blue that symbolizes emotional distance. The main collision occurs in the center, where tighter clusters of colors ripple against a quiet backdrop, invoking a sense of tension and release. This dance of pattern reflects the push-and-pull dynamic inherent in the seed's themes, manifesting as waves of layered complexity and simple tranquility.
