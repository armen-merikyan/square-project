Artist Statement

This abstract artwork embodies the emotional tension between softness and severity. The left side, dominated by muted pinks and warm whites, reflects the gentle, feathered touch of softness. Meanwhile, the right side juxtaposes darker shades like carbon black and steel gray, signaling severity and a sense of hard cuts. The pattern creates a visual pulse that suggests a struggle against static and noise, with abrupt interruptions where colors collide. The placement of colors shifts from the light, airy emotions on the left to a weighty presence on the right, creating a dynamic flow that captures the essence of the provided seed. Broken pixels disrupt order, embodying the imperfections of communication and emotional expression, while negative space accentuates the conflicting tones.
