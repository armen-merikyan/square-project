Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between love and betrayal. The palette blends deep reds and dark maroons to evoke passion, contrasted with grays that symbolize coldness and distance. The arrangement creates a flow from bottom to top, embodying the rise of emotions from the depths of despair to the light of hope. The stark contrast between warm and cool colors signifies the collision between affection and disillusionment. Broken pixels are intentionally distributed to reflect imperfections and interruptions in the emotional journey.
