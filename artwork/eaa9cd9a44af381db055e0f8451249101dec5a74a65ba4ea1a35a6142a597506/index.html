Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and chaos, embodying the tension from the seed's themes. The palette combines deep navy and dim cyan to evoke depth and calm against the starkness of black and white, illustrating the pulsing signal amid static noise. The main contrast lies at the center where dense dark colors layer over lighter hues, creating a pulsing effect that interrupts the sense of calm around it. This arrangement reflects the conflict and resonance of memory versus erasure as it ripple across the grid.
