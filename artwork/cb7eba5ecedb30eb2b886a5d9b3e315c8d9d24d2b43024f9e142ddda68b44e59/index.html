Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between confusion and clarity, represented through a palette that oscillates between soft gold and dark violet, symbolizing hope against fear. The main contrast occurs where the soft gold interrupts the dense black, creating a collision of light and dark that evokes both clarity and chaos. This abrupt interaction reflects the seed’s themes of symmetry versus break, suggesting a complex narrative through overlapping forms and a structured yet fractured design.
