Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a tug-of-war between certainty and curiosity, reflecting a dynamic where structure meets exploration. The palette of deep wine, warm ivory, stain brown, and soft gold invokes feelings of warmth contrasted with the pressures of restraint. The main collision occurs centrally, where the barcode pattern of certainty overlaps with the rain-like patterns of curiosity, creating an area of focus that holds tension. The layout of dense 'island' colors within a quieter 'sea' builds a narrative of solid absence surrounded by exploratory space.
