Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between pressure and calm, represented through a stark contrast of deep blacks and soft golds alongside subdued greens. The palette captures fear and hope, creating a visual equilibrium where dense clusters of dark and light collide. The arrangement references the layout of 'corner_invasion,' allowing one emotion to surge diagonally against another. The patterns of target and stripes ensure an interplay of rhythm and disruption, maintaining a sense of unresolved tension throughout the piece.
