Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission, contrasting vibrant yellows and oranges with subdued blues and purples to illustrate the interplay of light and shadow. The palette evokes feelings of warmth against coldness, symbolizing the struggle within the seed's themes. The main collision occurs in the center, reflecting an upward pulse against a shattering field, ensuring the tension between chaos and order is front and center.
