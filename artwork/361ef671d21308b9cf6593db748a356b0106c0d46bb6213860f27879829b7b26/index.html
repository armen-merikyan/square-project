Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between restlessness and peace, represented through jagged edges and interrupted patterns. The palette blends calm pale aqua and mist gray with the urgent alarm red, fostering a tension between serenity and chaos. The main collision occurs at the center, where the hinge pattern of restlessness disrupts the thread of peace. This dynamic evokes a sense of conflict and balance, drawing from the seed's exploration of memory and motion.
