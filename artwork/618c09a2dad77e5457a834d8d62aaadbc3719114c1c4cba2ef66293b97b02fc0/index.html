Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a conflict between the chaos of panic and the stability of control. The palette of yellow and deep blue evokes feelings of joy and grief, representing the light and dark sides of emotion. Main contrast occurs between the warm center, embodying control, and the cooler, chaotic edges suggesting panic, creating a dynamic tension. The diamond shape at the core represents a focal point of stability amidst jagged, fractured edges, embodying the contradictions inherent in the seed.
