Artist Statement

This artwork embodies a struggle between confusion and clarity, reflected in the choice of deep, moody colors contrasted by sharp, piercing accents. The palette includes navy and black for a sense of depth and uncertainty, balanced by patterns of dim cyan that evoke clarity as they spiral outwards. The thick outline represents boundaries, while broken pixels suggest imperfections in clarity, echoing the emotional tension between structured form and chaotic disruption. The collision of dark and light across the grid represents the push-pull between clarity and confusion.
