Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends the expansiveness of joy with the density of grief. The left side, representing joy, employs bright yellows and muted oranges, evoking warmth and buoyancy. The right side reflects grief's heavy lows with deep blues and muted purples. The contrast occurs at the boundary where joy's lightness collides with grief's weight, forming an intricate dance between expanding warmth and constricting depth. The seed inspired the split vertical layout, introducing an asymmetric break in symmetry that echoes the complexity of human feelings.
