Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between feelings of envy and gratitude, presenting a duality in color and form. The palette, featuring warm yellows and oranges contrasted with deep blues and muted purples, reflects the emotional weight of these sentiments. The left half contains vibrant, jagged patterns symbolizing envy and noise, while the right side features softer, crosshatched forms alluding to gratitude and calm. The arrangement creates a zigzag flow, emphasizing the collision of emotions at the center and suggesting a visual dialogue of pressure and release.
