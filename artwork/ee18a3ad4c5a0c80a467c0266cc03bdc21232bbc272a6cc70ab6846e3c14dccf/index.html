Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve. The chosen palette of soft cyan and muted plum reflects the melancholy yet hopeful nature of the seed, creating a serene field amidst tension. The main contrast occurs in the central area, where bright clusters evoke urgency, while outer regions fade into quietness. The patterns intermingle, one suggesting islands of memory, and the other creating a flood of persistence, embodying the push-and-pull of emotions.
