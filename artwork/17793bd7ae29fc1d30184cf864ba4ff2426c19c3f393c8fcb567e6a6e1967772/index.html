Artist Statement

This composition captures the intense emotional struggle between confusion and clarity using contrasting colors from the 'love_betrayal' palette. The dark maroon and gray represent confusion, while pink and red introduce clarity, clashing with thick outlines to enhance tension. The zigzag flow and broken pixel pattern disrupt symmetry, implying dynamic motion and emotional collision. The negative spaces play into the contrast, allowing for moments of quiet against overwhelming density, embodying the duality central to the seed.
