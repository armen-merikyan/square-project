Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the piece juxtaposes the steadfastness of certainty with the unpredictability of curiosity. The palette—comprising ice blue for calmness, ink black for depth, and violet gray for unresolved tension—creates a dialogue between these forces. The collision occurs primarily at the center where dark and vibrant hues intermingle, suggesting struggle and release against the framework of blocked edges. The abstract pattern reflects a shift from structured, linear flows to chaotic explorations, embodying the tension outlined in the seed.
