Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between 'emptiness' and 'desire' through strong contrasts of warm and cool hues. The palette, based on ancient fire tones, evokes warmth and sentiment, juxtaposed against dark charcoal, symbolizing emptiness. The central focus shifts from the bright, fiery corners that suggest desire, to the heavier darker edges representing emptiness. The color arrangement creates a dynamic tension, where the bright burnt orange and gold accents collide with the thick charcoal boundaries, reinforcing the feeling of longing. The wave vertical flow mirrors the rise and fall of emotions, while the broken edges add a sense of imperfection and fragmentation, echoing the heart's yearnings.
