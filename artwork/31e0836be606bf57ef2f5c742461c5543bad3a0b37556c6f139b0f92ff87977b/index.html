Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional clash between relief and dread through the use of color density and placement. A dominant calm mint green (#B2E0D9) represents relief, occupying approximately 65% of the grid, contrasting against the ominous near-black (#212121) that introduces an unsettling weight. The spiral pattern flows from the corner, creating tension as it moves outward, interrupted by stark, jarring blocks of storm gray (#7A7B7D) that evoke a sense of chaos. The use of broken pixels enhances this narrative of disruption, suggesting scars of conflict within the overall calm. The light accents of faded gold (#E4C111) punctuate the darker sections, providing brief moments of brightness.
