Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between defiance and submission through a diagonal division in the color palette. The dominant use of dark navy establishes a sense of protective defiance, contrasted sharply with the soft faint peach, representing submission. The muted dust gray acts as a quiet channel, allowing for a calm yet complex interplay of presence and absence. Each interaction of the crack pattern (defiance) and halo (submission) visually collides at the center point, creating a threshold that sparks emotional tension while preserving enough negative space to suggest an ongoing struggle.
