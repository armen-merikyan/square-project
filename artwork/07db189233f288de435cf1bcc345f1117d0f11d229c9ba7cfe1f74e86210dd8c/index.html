Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of restlessness and peace through a defined yet chaotic layout. The chosen palette of warm tones (hot coral, blood orange) juxtaposed with cooler shades (dust pink, charcoal, hollow blue) symbolizes the internal struggle between a yearning for calm and the echoes of unresolved motion. The diagonal layout reinforces a sense of movement while the broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, reflecting the impact of memory against a backdrop of tranquility. The primary collision occurs along the diagonal, where the warm, active colors interrupt the cooler, grounded hues, creating a dynamic visual narrative that encourages the viewer to navigate through emotional layers.
