Artist Statement

The emotional tension between panic and control manifests through the warm palette of yellows and oranges contrasting with deep blues and muted purples, embodying a dynamic interplay of light and shadow. The patterns represent rain and flames, signifying a push and pull between stasis and tumult. The layout engages in an 'argument grid' where warm colors (indicative of panic) clash against cooler hues (representing control), with a spiral flow suggesting movement and disruption. Broken pixels introduce randomness, mirroring the imperfect nature of real emotions, while negative space emphasizes the conflict and breathing room between chaos and calm. This pixel arrangement tells a story of striving for equilibrium within tumultuous feelings, evoking unresolved emotions and interactions.
