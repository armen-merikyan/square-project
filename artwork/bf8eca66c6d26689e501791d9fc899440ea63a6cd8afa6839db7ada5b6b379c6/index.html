Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between life (spark) and decay (ash). The palette features vibrant colors like electric blue and hot magenta, symbolizing energy and vitality, contrasted against static gray and dead black representing stillness and fatigue. The layout follows a vertical split, where the left side is dominated by brighter colors, evoking a sense of urgency and a small burst of hope. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, reflecting the fragility of resilience in the face of erasure. The main collision occurs diagonally, creating a dynamic tension that echoes the underlying struggle of the seed concept.
