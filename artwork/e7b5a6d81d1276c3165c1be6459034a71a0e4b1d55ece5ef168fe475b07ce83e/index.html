Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between longing and resolution, depicted through contrasting warm burnt orange and dark red against cooler charcoal and gold. The layout, dominated by warm colors, suggests an overarching sentiment of yearning, while the cold tones provide moments of clarity and structure. The collision occurs centrally, where the stacked longing patterns invade the lattice structured resolve, creating an interrupted flow that embodies repair versus damage. The broken pixels add intentional imperfections, reinforcing the theme of a fractured but resilient spirit.
