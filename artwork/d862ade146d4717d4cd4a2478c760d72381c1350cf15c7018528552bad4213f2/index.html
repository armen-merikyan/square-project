Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the notions of safety and exposure. The palette combines a soft shelter green and pale skin with stark cold whites and warm browns, creating a dialogue between warmth and coolness. The dominant colors occupy the upper left quadrant, demonstrating a protective warmth, while ripple patterns in cold blues create tension in the lower right, echoing exposure. This leads to an asymmetric composition where soft barriers interact with open spaces, revealing the push-pull dynamic of the seed's themes.
