Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension of 'panic vs control' through distinct patterns and colors. The top four rows utilize a checker pattern in warm colors, representing control with muted burnt oranges and dark reds, suggesting a structured but oppressive atmosphere. In contrast, the bottom four rows implement a chaotic grid in cooler dark hues, creating a sense of instability and fragmentation. The edge style is bottom-weighted, visually grounding the chaos underneath the orderly top. The juxtaposition of the structured warmth against the fractured coolness encapsulates the emotional dissonance defined by the seed.
