Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension of rage and calm through a juxtaposition of sharp reds and cooler blues. The palette reflects this dichotomy, with black representing deep calmness contrasting against the explosive heat of red. The arrangement emphasizes a spiral clockwise flow, symbolizing the way chaos (sharp, jagged patterns) breaks down the calm (horizontal, soft bands), suggesting a narrative of intrusion and disruption. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the emotional theme of collision between states of being.
