Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The palette combines dark and warm tones of burnt orange and dark red against crisp charcoals and golds, creating a strong contrast that echoes the struggle between clarity and chaos. The layout of falling pixels suggests a descent into disorder, with broken pixels strategically placed to disrupt symmetry, reinforcing the theme of false harmony. Each row conveys a pulse: some dense and energetic, others soft and open, reflecting the quiet versus noise dynamic. Overall, the artwork embodies a narrative of collision while remaining abstract, allowing emotional interpretation without a literal representation.
