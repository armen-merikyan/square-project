Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a struggle between dense pressure and a quiet openness. The palette features muted tones of gray and off-white to suggest a calm atmosphere while employing a deep navy and compressed red to create moments of tension. The main collision occurs at the diagonal from the top left to the bottom right, where the darker colors break through the lighter tide-like effects, symbolizing shattering against the tide of quiet. The 'stairs' pattern representing pressure clashes with the 'tide' representing quiet, echoing the theme of contrast throughout the canvas.
