Artist Statement

This piece explores the contrast between softness and severity, illustrated through a palette of gentle yet impactful colors. The powder pink and warm white represent softness while the carbon black and sharp crimson embody severity. The arrangement creates a dynamic visual tension, where the lighter hues spiral upwards, creating a sense of rise, mirrored by the darker shades that pulse beneath. The broken pixels scattered throughout serve as disruptions, echoing the underlying emotional struggles between guilt and forgiveness, leading to a layered narrative of complexity and resolution.
