Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that balances soft pinks with stark blacks. The contrast emerges at the center, where the strong dark shades collide with lighter, pale colors, symbolizing the push-pull between exposure and protection. The jagged arrangement reflects the 'broken mirror' idea, creating imperfect symmetry that speaks to the emotional chaos of life's balances. Broken pixels are interspersed to disrupt the grid, reinforcing the theme of vulnerability in an otherwise structured environment.
