Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between love and betrayal through contrasting colors and asymmetrical arrangements. The palette blends warm colors like pink and red to symbolize love with dark maroon and gray to introduce themes of betrayal and chaos. The main collision occurs diagonally, as lighter shades merge into darker, creating a dynamic shift from warmth to coldness. The broken pattern with intentional 'flaw' in some pixels introduces the imperfection of relationships, invoking feelings of fragmentation amidst the striving for connection.
