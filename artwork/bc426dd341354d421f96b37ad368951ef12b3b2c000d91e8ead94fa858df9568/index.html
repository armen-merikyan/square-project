Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'longing vs resolve' is explored through a collision of soft, muted colors and stark contrasts. The palette of deep navy and black establishes a sense of quiet reflection, while the inclusion of a bright dim cyan serves as an interruption that captures urgency and tension. The patterns formed by 'mirror' and 'hinge' elegantly clash in the center, drawing attention to the space where these emotions collide. This creates a feeling of unresolved movement, where the delicate balance between desire and determination is visually represented, suggesting a dance between serenity and urgency.
