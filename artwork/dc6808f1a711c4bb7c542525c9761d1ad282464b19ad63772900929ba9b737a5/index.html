Artist Statement

The artwork encapsulates the tension between quietude and pressure through contrast in color and placement. The chosen palette combines off-white and faint peach with darker tones, suggesting a struggle between light and weight. The collision of dense dark navy at the edges with fragmented red slices through the center evokes a shattering effect, symbolizing counterflow against the encroaching compression. Overall, it embodies a rising tension through blocky collisions and softer interruptions, reflecting the seed's themes.
