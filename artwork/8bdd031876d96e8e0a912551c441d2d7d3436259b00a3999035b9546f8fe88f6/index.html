Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional interplay between longing and resolve. The palette includes ripe green and honey gold, symbolizing the warmth of yearning, contrasted against deep olive, creating a sense of firmness and boundary. The spiral layout emphasizes the flow of energy, with clusters of thread-like patterns (representing longing) juxtaposed against patchwork fields (symbolizing resolve). The main collision occurs around the center, where deeper colors intertwine, highlighting the tension and beauty of existence in a fragile yet firm dance.
