Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of joy and grief through a harmonic balance of contrast and texture. The color palette, combining bright yellows and deep blues, creates a visual tension that reflects the push and pull of these opposing feelings. The structured ring pattern in the upper half symbolizes joy's order, while the chaotic checker pattern below embodies grief's fragmentation. The collision occurs in the horizontal split, where ordered brightness meets muted chaos, exemplifying the emotional journey of struggling between hope and sorrow. Negative space is utilized to create a sense of breathability amidst the dense color interactions.
