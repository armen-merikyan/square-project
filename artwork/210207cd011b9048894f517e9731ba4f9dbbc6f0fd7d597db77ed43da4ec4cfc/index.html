Artist Statement

This artwork captures the duality of vulnerability and strength through a stark contrast between solid and soft pixel arrangements. The primary colors, a blend of signal green and electric blue, symbolize strength with dense, rooted contrasts at the edges, while soft static gray evokes vulnerability, creating delicate channels within the central area. Broken pixels add intentional disruption to this equilibrium, emphasizing the emotional tension of strength being both protective and commanding, yet susceptible to exposure at its core. The layout of falling pixels suggests a downpour of thoughts, swirling in a chaotic yet structured manner.
