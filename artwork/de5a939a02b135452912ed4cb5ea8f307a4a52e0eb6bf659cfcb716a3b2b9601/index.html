Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the contrast between restlessness and peace. The palette blends soft pinks and warm whites with stark blacks and grays, highlighting a struggle between calm and chaos. The central collision occurs diagonally, where warm colors interrupt dark fields, suggesting a push and pull between memory and motion, invoking a sense of hesitation amid desire. The chosen patterns—stairs representing peace and a broken frame for restlessness—interact to portray emotional tension that ebbs and flows across the canvas.
