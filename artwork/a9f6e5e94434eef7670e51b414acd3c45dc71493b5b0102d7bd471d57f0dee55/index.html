Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional juxtaposition of softness against the severity through a dynamic interplay of powder pinks and carbon blacks. The use of soft hues wrapped in a structured layout evokes an internal dialogue between chaos and calm. The main contrast occurs where the warm whites collide with sharp crimsons, illustrating an interruption in the flow of emotion. This pattern embodies the spiral motion of the seed text, suggesting a journey that is both trapped and liberated.
