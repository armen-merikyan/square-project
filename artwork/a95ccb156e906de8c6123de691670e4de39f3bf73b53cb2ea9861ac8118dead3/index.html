Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension of joy and grief through contrasting colors and spaces. The palette incorporates bright yellows and oranges representing joy, interspersed with deep blues and muted purples expressing grief. The checker pattern for grief creates a rigid structure that embodies heaviness, while the bright, spark-like yellows form a zigzag pattern suggesting rising emotions. An intentional imbalance enhances the emotional narrative, with bright pixels fighting against darker backgrounds, echoing the central themes of order and chaos.
