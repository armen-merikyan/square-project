Artist Statement

The artwork represents a dynamic emotional contrast embodying 'control' through the dense, dark tones that create a thick outline in the corners. In contrast, 'surrender' is evoked by soft pinks that spiral inward in a gentle descent, representing an overflow of emotion. The collision occurs at the center where pressures meet soft yields, creating a visual tension. The selected palette enhances feelings of love entwined with betrayal, emphasizing the conflict between containment and overflow.
