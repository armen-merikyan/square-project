Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tension between control and surrender. The selected palette, combining soft pinks and sharp blacks, creates a contrast reflecting the struggle between rigidity and fluidity. The central arrangement features a denser pattern of black and gray, embodying control, while the surrounding lighter pinks symbolize surrender. The collision occurs dominantly in the center, where the stark black glimmers with touches of crimson, suggesting an overflow of emotions. The broken pixel elements introduce imperfection, representing unexpected disruptions in a seemingly ordered space, emphasizing the theme of pressure versus release.
