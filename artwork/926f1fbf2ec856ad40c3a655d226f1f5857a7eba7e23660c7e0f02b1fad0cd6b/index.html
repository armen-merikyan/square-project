Artist Statement

This composition reflects a tension between envy (cool tones like navy and dim cyan) and gratitude (warm gestures from black and white). The center-heavy layout encapsulates feelings of isolation contrasted with warmth, portraying a journey from deeper emotions evolving towards softer edges at the periphery. The noise against a stark gradient illustrates the push-pull dynamic, narrating an emotional story with asymmetric placements that echo vulnerability and hope.
