Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dance between longing and resolve. The chosen palette of ripe green, acid green, honey gold, deep olive, and cream creates a dynamic contrast evocative of warmth and yearning against a backdrop of structured resolve. The flowing pressure bands denote a sense of longing, while the scattered dots represent moments of consolidation and strength. The main collision of colors occurs in the central area, symbolizing the exploration of intimacy and distance, leading to an immersive tension throughout the piece. The patterns of pressure versus dots engage in a dance of chaos versus order, effectively bringing the seed's themes to life.
