Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tug-of-war between strength and vulnerability. The palette transitions from a bright yellow (representing strength) to deep blue and muted purple (symbolizing vulnerability). The dominant yellow's presence at the corner is set against a stark transition to muted tones elsewhere, creating a pressure-filled environment, akin to a flood against a spark. The 'stairs' and 'braid' patterns create movement and tension across the grid. The 'pressure_corner' layout emphasizes a dynamic imbalance, defining where the greatest emotional collision occurs. The use of broken pixels further illustrates imperfections, mirroring the struggles present in the themes of exposure and shelter.
