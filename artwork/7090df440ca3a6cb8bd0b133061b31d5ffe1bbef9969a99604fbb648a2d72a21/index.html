Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the duality of panic and control, represented through the tumultuous interplay of dark and light colors. The palette, featuring soft gold against dark violet and pale green, evokes a sense of hope emerging from chaos. The main contrast occurs at the edges, where jagged dark hues collide with bright, stable centers, suggesting a protective yet unstable core. The seed's themes of fragmentation (broken dots and sparks) influence the pattern's rhythmic oscillations, creating a deliberate disruption that captures the tension observed when love falters amidst control.
