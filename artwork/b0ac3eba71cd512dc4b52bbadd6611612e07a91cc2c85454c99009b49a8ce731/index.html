Artist Statement

This piece contrasts the tension between openness and protection. The palette features soft greens and warm browns representing safety, while pale and cold hues symbolize exposure. The center holds a lighter area that invites attention, while the darker edges provide a sense of shelter and weight, disrupting the outer calm. The flow echoes to the right, suggesting a movement from pressure to release, encapsulated in the diagonal layout that creates a visual argument of tension and release.
