Artist Statement

The emotional tone expresses the clash between envy and gratitude, conveyed through a palette that creates a dialogue between muted sepia and faded blue. The contrast arises from soft edges meeting abrupt interruptions of dense color, depicting a tension that oscillates between hope and fear. The arrangement of colors defines a central pattern that feels both fragile and overwhelming, echoing the seed's themes of rising vs falling and noise against crack.
