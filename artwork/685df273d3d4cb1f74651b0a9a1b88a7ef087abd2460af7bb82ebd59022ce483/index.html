Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between loneliness and connection through contrasting color placements. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a nostalgic and muted emotional tone, representing the fragility of connections. The center features a burst of energy amidst a grid-like structure, illustrating the struggle of reaching out while feeling isolated. The top-weighted arrangement creates a sense of pressure, with darker hues reflecting the weight of loneliness, while lighter tones suggest flickers of connection. This arrangement captures the collision of feelings, as the central symbol is punctuated by fading echoes of color that symbolize hidden emotional layers.
