Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through color and composition. Light colors dominate the center to evoke a sense of exposure, while darker tones form a battling perimeter, suggesting shelter. The muted palette of pale mint, storm gray, and faded gold conveys a quiet undercurrent of dread, creating contrasts that evoke instability and resilience. The dominant center generates a rising signal, while the uneven edges symbolize hard edges against a soft core. The interplay of 'glitch' and 'constellation' patterns further enhances the feel of organized chaos, suggesting a constant push-pull of emotion. This tension culminates at the center, surrounded by broken pixels that emphasize disruption in the narrative flow.
