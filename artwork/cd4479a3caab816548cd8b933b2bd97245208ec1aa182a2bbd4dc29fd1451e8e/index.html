Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a battle between joy and grief, represented through contrasting color patterns. A palette of muted purples, deep blues, and spark-like yellows evokes joy amid the sobering presence of grief. The layout flows downwards in columns, resembling tears or cascading thoughts, with a jagged edge style that suggests brokenness. The collision occurs in the left-weighted distribution where joyful colors disrupt the somber blues, creating visual tension that embodies the emotional dichotomy. This arrangement reflects the push-pull of emotions, invoking a sense of unresolved longing.
