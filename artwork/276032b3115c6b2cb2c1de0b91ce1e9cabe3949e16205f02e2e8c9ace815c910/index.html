Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The selected palette of 'melancholy wonder' colors—soft blues and muted purples—creates a serene yet poignant atmosphere. The main collision occurs at the center, where the lattice pattern of vulnerability meets the stable dots symbolizing strength. This juxtaposition evokes feelings of exposure yet encased in a protective layer, reflecting the conflict inherent in the seed's emotional narrative. Each broken pixel introduces imperfection, enhancing the artwork's depth and dynamic interplay.
