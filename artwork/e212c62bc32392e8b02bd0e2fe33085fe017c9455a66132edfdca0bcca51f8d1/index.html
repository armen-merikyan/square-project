Artist Statement

This piece embodies the duality of joy and grief through contrasting color fields. The top half is a radiant grid, representing joy with bright yellows and oranges, symbolizing expansion and warmth. In contrast, the lower half showcases muted blues and purples, reflecting the heaviness of grief with a stair pattern that suggests descent. The zigzag movements within the patterns create an echo of rising signals, suggesting a struggle between order and chaos. The thick outlines provide definition, while the broken pixels create imperfection, highlighting the fragility of emotions. The interplay of colors conveys the story of transitioning from moments of joy to the weight of sadness.
