Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional interplay of longing and resolve through a palette inspired by ancient fire, contrasting warmth with darkness. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, and charcoal creates tension and a feeling of urgency, symbolizing both repair and damage. The pattern merges broken dots with rain, representing the collision of hopes and fears, manifesting in a spiral flow. The central collision occurs in the center with a dense cluster of warm colors, interrupted by cooler shades and broken pixels that symbolize scars and disarray—a reminder of unresolved emotions in a serene yet urgent landscape.
