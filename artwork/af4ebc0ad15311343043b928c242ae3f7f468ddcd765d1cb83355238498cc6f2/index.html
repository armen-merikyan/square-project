Artist Statement

This piece conveys the push and pull between clarity and chaos by utilizing an intense palette of signal green, electric blue, and hot magenta against a background of static gray. The collision occurs diagonally, where the smooth transitions of the signal colors meet jagged patches of noise, invoking a sense of pulsating tension within the structured chaos. The composition reflects on the emotional themes of interference and discomfort juxtaposed against a fleeting, clean pulse, mirroring the complexities of human emotion in a machine-dominated world.
