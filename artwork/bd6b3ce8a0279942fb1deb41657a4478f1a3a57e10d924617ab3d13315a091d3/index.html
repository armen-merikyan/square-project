Artist Statement

The artwork expresses the complex interplay between longing and resolve through color and structure. A palette of signal green and hot magenta creates a vibrant tension against a static gray backdrop, embodying both warmth and distance. The collision occurs in the center, where dense clusters of colors (the longing) contrast with the quieter spaces (the resolve), interrupted by broken pixels that represent imperfections in emotion. This evokes a story of desire and purpose intertwined, reflecting the duality of the seed's themes.
