Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between confusion and clarity, employing a palette of dark navy and black contrasted with stark white and dim cyan. The layouts suggest emptiness broken by a single, pulsating rhythm represented by the bright contrast of white amidst a spectrum of dark tones. The arrangement features a 'ring' pattern for the chaotic elements, blending with graduated dark areas that invoke a sense of ungraspable clarity. The majority of dark colors exemplify dense emotions, while the white pixels, representing clarity, provide brief glimpses of relief within the composition. The intentional placement of broken pixels suggests imperfection amidst order, contributing to the overall abstract narrative of conflict and convergence.
