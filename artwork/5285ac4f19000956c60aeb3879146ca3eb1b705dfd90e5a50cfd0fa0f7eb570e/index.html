Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between 'longing' and 'resolve,' with a flow that descends like rain, symbolizing tears or ash. The palette incorporates deep blacks and soft pale blues to create contrast, where black represents the weight of longing while pale blue suggests the clarity of resolve. The halo border introduces a soft confinement that enhances the sense of intimacy versus distance. The main collision occurs in the gradient from dark to light, emphasizing the struggle between chaos and order, and layering to suggest depth while still allowing for negative spaces that whisper of unfulfilled desires.
