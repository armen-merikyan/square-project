Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension of 'longing' and 'resolve'. The warm brown and shelter green suggest a sense of comfort and the urge to find solace. Pale skin tones intersperse with cold white to provide haunting echoes of longing, while the open blue emerges as a stark accent, representing clarity and decisive strength. The pattern reflects a rhythmic pulse with close-set marks that contrast the quiet negative spaces, conveying a steady hope amidst the chaos. The central collision is in the middle: warm notes meeting cold edges, framed by scattered brighter tones that aim to repair the emotional rift.
