Artist Statement

The emotional tone is crafted to balance restlessness and peace, using a stark palette of clean white and ice blue against ink black and violet gray, symbolizing the conflict between calm and disturbance. The dominant collision occurs in the center, where jagged edges of restlessness disrupt a smooth field of peace. This tension mirrors the seed's themes of memory and motion, presenting a rhythmic yet disjointed pattern that evokes an unresolved, oscillating state.
