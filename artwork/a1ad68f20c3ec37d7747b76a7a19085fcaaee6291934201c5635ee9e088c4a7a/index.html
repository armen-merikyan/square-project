Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts between the dense, heavy presence of 'pressure' and the lightness of 'quiet'. The palette features dark navy and compressed red representing pressure, while faint peach and off-white symbolize quiet. The main collision occurs in the upper area, where the darker colors build up, invoking a sense of crowding, giving way to lighter shades that suggest release and openness. This pattern reflects a balance between chaos and calm, with a notable density at the bottom gradually transforming into airy space towards the top, illustrating a journey from confinement to freedom, inspired by the tension in the seed.
