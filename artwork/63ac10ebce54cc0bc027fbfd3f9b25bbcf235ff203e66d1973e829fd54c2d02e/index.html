Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between panic and control, using a palette reflective of memory and distance. The dark tones represent feelings of confinement and pressure, while the lighter colors introduce moments of release and clarity. The central area is densely packed with darker shades, suggesting control, while the outer edges are lighter and more fragmented, signifying the encroachment of panic. The interplay of dark and light colors creates a rhythm of contrast and collision, echoing the push and pull of emotional states. The layout invokes a falling pixel effect, enhancing the sense of descent into chaos or uplift towards clarity, depending on which side the viewer leans into.
