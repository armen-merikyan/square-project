Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between panic and control using contrasting colors to convey tension. The palette, 'ancient_fire', evokes warmth and intensity with burnt oranges and dark reds representing chaos, while gold hints at an attempt at composure. The main contrast surfaces in the center with the warm colors bleeding outward, creating a sense of pressure. The pattern resonates with radial elements encroaching on a central space, as the star shape is referenced subtly, illustrating the theme of protection versus exposure. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, aligning with the emotional narrative, suggesting disruption in the perceived order.
