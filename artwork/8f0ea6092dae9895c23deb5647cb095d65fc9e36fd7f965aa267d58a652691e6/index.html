Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between confusion and clarity through a fragmented grid of colors. The palette—roots in black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green—contrasts light and dark, symbolizing the push and pull of conflicting emotions. The main focus is the broken symmetry in the arrangement, creating a sense of disarray while maintaining a consistent pulse through rhythmic placement. The negative space allows breathing room for the chaotic elements, eliciting a feeling of unresolved tension, akin to a heartbeat caught between rage and calm.
