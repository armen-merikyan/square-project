Artist Statement

The emotional tone emphasizes the tension between vulnerability and strength, with the palette capturing this conflict through stark contrasts. The deep black symbolizes strength while the pale blue reflects vulnerability. The dominance of black in the center creates a sense of pressure, interrupted by the gentle braids of blue and white that symbolize vulnerability trying to emerge. The arrangement directs the viewer's gaze outward, emphasizing a quiet battle within the structured grid—symbolizing the interplay of control versus chaos.
