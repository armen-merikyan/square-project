Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic tension between vibrancy and desolation, represented through contrasting colors from the mourning_renewal palette. A jagged feel interrupts the static backdrop (ash) with vibrant accents (spark), emphasizing the duality of resilience and fragility. The pattern flow zigzags through the space, creating a sense of movement, while intentional broken pixels enhance the ‘tear’ shape, embodying the struggle between memory and erasure. The central collision occurs along a diagonal axis, reinforcing the concept of an inner fight amidst outer chaos.
