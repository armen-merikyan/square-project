Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a juxtaposition between calm and rage, invoking feelings of tension and release. The palette of deep blacks and reds creates a stark contrast with the softer pale blue and white, reflecting the duality of the emotions. The left side, inspired by calm, uses horizontal and low-contrast arrangements, which are occasionally interrupted by sharper diagonal elements of rage on the right. The broken pixels signify imperfections, enhancing the narrative of a cracked and asymmetric harmony, echoing the core theme of the seed.
