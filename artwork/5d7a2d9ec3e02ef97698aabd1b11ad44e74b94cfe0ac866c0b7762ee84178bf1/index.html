Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between envy and gratitude, with warm versus cool colors creating a dynamic interplay. The palette of yellow and muted purple reflects the warmth of gratitude against the coldness of envy. The mirrored layout presents a subtle discord, where slight variations introduce an element of imperfection, while the zigzag flow emphasizes the wavering between emotions. The contrast lies in the center-heavy design, with denser colors juxtaposed against lighter negative space, mirroring the feeling of weight carried by envy, yet leading to a sense of lightness through gratitude.
