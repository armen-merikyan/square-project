Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork explores the tension between clarity and confusion. The palette incorporates electric blue, magenta, and black to invoke both energetic clarity and chaotic confusion. The contrast emerges through the interplay of clear, defined blocks of color versus muddled, chaotic areas that evoke a sense of disruption. The arrangement reflects the seed's themes of breaking symmetry, where the left half focuses on uniform patterns while the right depicts fragmented, overlapping colors, embodying the confusion of falling pixels. The presence of negative space ensures calmness amid the chaos, grounding the composition.
