Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dialogue between control and surrender through a structured pattern that suggests conflict and resolution. The color palette features warm shades of rose clay and lamp gold, symbolizing the warmth of human emotion against the cooler, introspective tones of distant lilac and cool blue. The outer border, rich in deep colors, represents control, while the inner section exhibits an intricate, softer flow, indicative of surrender. The collision occurs at the center where warm and cool shades meet, capturing the tension of overflow versus containment. This abstract form resonates with the seed's themes, illustrating an emotional journey.
