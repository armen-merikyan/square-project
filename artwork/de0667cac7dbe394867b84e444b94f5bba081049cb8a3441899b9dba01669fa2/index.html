Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a complex interplay of trust and suspicion, set within a dense center that reflects the noise and static of confusion. The chosen palette of dark navy, black, dim cyan, and touches of white represents the struggle between clarity and obscurity. Main contrasts appear between the dense center—representing a signal of intention—and the quieter edges that signify suspicion and uncertainty, creating patterns of both trust and betrayal. The broken pixels represent imperfections within this dynamic, enhancing the sense of interference and echo.
