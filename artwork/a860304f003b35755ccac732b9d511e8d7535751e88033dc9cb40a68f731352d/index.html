Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between awe and terror, captured by a contrast of structured order in the left half and chaotic disruption in the right. A carefully chosen palette of navy and dim cyan symbolizes depth and unease, while broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, emphasizing the split between stability and instability, encapsulating the essence of the seed's thematic tension.
