Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts between envy and gratitude, represented by a division of structured order on the left and chaotic elements on the right. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes a mix of weight and hope. The left side employs a crack pattern in darker colors suggesting the heaviness of envy, while the right side employs a maze pattern in lighter tones, hinting at the intricate nature of gratitude. The breaking pixels introduce a layer of imperfection, reflecting the collisions of emotion and creating a dynamic visual rhythm.
