Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, symbolized through a palette evoking warmth and weariness. The colors, such as dusty sepia and faded blue, suggest a memory that lingers while interspersed with darker tones, echoing the feeling of repairs held in a broken state. The layout features a central tension, highlighted through a dense cluster of colors (the resolve) surrounded by quieter spaces of dust gray (the longing), reflecting a struggle between containment and overflow. The broken pixels introduce moments of imperfection, creating scars within the overall cohesiveness and amplifying the internal conflict expressed in the seed.
