Artist Statement

This artwork evokes a tension between the warm tones of love and the colder shades of betrayal. The palette combines soft pinks and reds with dark maroon and gray to suggest emotional clash. The layout emphasizes symmetry with one side showcasing a radial pattern representing betrayal, while the other exemplifies a balanced grid for love. The broken pixels create a sense of imperfection that mirrors the theme of disruption in relationships. The diagonal flow add dynamism, while avoiding complete chaos ensures moments of reflection.
