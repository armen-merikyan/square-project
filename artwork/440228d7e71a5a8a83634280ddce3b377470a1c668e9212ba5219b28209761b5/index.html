Artist Statement

This abstract composition reflects the emotional tension of longing and resolve. The palette combines warmth with depth—hot coral and blood orange suggest urgency and desire, while charcoal and hollow blue introduce calmness and depth. The central collision is framed by a quiet field, resembling a boundary where soft yet vibrant colors meet darker ones, embodying the push-pull of yearning versus steadfastness. The introduction of broken pixels interspersed within echoes the theme of imperfection and resilience, capturing the essence of repair amidst a sense of damage. The resulting grid conveys rhythm and a gentle pulse, mirroring the conflict of containment versus overflow in an abstract representation.
