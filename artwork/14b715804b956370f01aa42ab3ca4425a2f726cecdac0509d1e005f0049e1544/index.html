Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece embodies the tension between confusion and clarity, manifesting through an interplay of structured and chaotic patterns. The palette, consisting of bold black, stark whites, and soft pale blue, creates a dichotomy of rage and calm, which reflects the underlying conflict of the emotional score. The contrasting colors are intentionally placed to suggest noise against clarity, creating a sense of broken symmetry across the grid. The introduction of 'broken pixels' through scattered dark hues disturbs the otherwise harmonious arrangement, symbolizing confusion. This juxtaposition clusters certain areas while allowing others to breathe, forming a distinct narrative of tension and release, portraying the core essence of the seed.
