Artist Statement

The piece reflects the emotional tension between fear and hope through a palette of dark violet representing fear and soft gold for hope. The composition features asymmetrical patterns that suggest a conflict between sharp edges and gentle curves, evoking a sensation of rising signals against a backdrop of darkness. The contrast occurs where the brighter colors punctuate deeper shadows, giving a sense of struggle and potential, while the broken pixels introduce imperfection, hinting at vulnerability within the chaotic existence. The balance of negative space allows for an interpretation of breath and release amidst tension, resonating with the theme of an internal battle.
