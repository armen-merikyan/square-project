Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional dichotomy of tenderness and rage through a contrast of saturated and muted tones. The primary palette of deep reds and soft blues represents the collision of explosive feelings against gentle overlap, capturing the essence of emotional conflict. The composition favors a predominant dark base with a small cluster of highlighted pixels, signifying a quiet but vibrant struggle against an oppressive background. The main contrast occurs from the center, where the gentle softness meets sharp reds, creating a compelling visual tension.
