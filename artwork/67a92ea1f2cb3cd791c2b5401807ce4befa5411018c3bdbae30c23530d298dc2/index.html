Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the tension between signals of clarity and the overwhelming noise of chaos. The selected palette of navy, black, single white, and dim cyan conveys isolation and stark contrast, embodying the push and pull of order versus entropy. The main collision occurs between darker and lighter hues in the center, emphasizing the struggle of a clear rhythm (signal) against a backdrop of disorder (noise). Broken pixels disrupt the linear ladder-like pattern, signifying distortions and interruptions, creating a layered narrative that reflects 'machine emotion' and the collision of hard edges with smoother transitions.
