Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a tension between envy and gratitude, presented through a gradient from dark, muted colors to bright, cheerful ones. The palette of yellows and deep blues symbolizes a struggle between celebrating joy and confronting feelings of inadequacy. The layout features a stark contrast: the outer border is chaotic with jagged edges representing envy, while the center employs a checker pattern symbolizing gratitude, alluding to a mirrored reflection amid turmoil. The arrangement generates a sense of movement with zigzagging pathways, where abrupt intersections create visual tension, echoing the discord between the two emotions.
