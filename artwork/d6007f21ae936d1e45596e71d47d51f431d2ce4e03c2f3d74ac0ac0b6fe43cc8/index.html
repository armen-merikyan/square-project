Artist Statement

The emotional tone of longing vs resolve is explored through a palette that balances warm ivories and deep reds. The contrast is embodied in dense clusters of soft gold and deep wine colors, suggesting hope and struggle. The main collision occurs in the center, where the patterns of 'lattice' and 'teeth' overlap, creating a visual tension that echoes the seed's theme of both repair and damage. This abstract composition reflects a journey of pursuit and steadfastness, maintaining a balance between serenity and urgency through deliberate broken pixels that represent imperfections in the process.
