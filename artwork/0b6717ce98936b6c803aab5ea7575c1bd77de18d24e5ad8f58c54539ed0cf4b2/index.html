Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the delicate struggle between vulnerability and strength, captured through a thoughtful interplay of color blocking and rippling patterns. The selected palette of melancholy wonder—rain blue, lavender gray, moon white, soft cyan, and muted plum—serves to evoke feelings of introspection and subtle warmth. The flow from bottom to top, mimicking an upward rise, accentuates hope against an imperfection-rich backdrop, embodying the metaphor of a pressure block that battles against the undercurrent of soft breaks. The main collision occurs in the center where strong, vibrant blocks meet softer, more fragmented hues, representing a balance of forces at play. Broken pixels create intentional disruptions, echoing moments of vulnerability while contributing to the overall dynamic of the piece.
