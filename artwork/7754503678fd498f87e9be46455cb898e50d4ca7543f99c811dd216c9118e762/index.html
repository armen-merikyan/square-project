Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tone of vulnerability highlighted against a backdrop of strength. The chosen palette, 'gratitude_envy,' with its contrasting greens and honey tones, creates tension between safety and exposure. Strength is represented by deep olive shades at the edges, reinforcing a protective boundary, while the softer cream and honey colors suggest vulnerability at the center. The layout mirrors at the split, allowing weighty colors to collide with lighter, braided textures, symbolizing the complex interplay of protective and exposed feelings. Broken pixels are strategically placed to disrupt harmony, emphasizing imperfection in both strength and fragility.
