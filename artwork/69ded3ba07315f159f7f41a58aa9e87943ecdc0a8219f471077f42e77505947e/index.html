Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork captures the tension between joy and grief using a palette that juxtaposes warm, vibrant colors with deeper, muted tones. The bright yellows and oranges represent joy, expanding from the left side, while muted purples and deep blues portray grief, fragmenting the canvas with dotted patterns. The center showcases the pulse of emotion, with intense contrast between these areas, emphasizing both collision and coherence as they interact. The layout flows diagonally and includes broken pixels to create a sense of imperfection and vulnerability, mirroring the theme of love's betrayal amid emotional struggle.
