Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between trust and suspicion, using a palette that reflects warmth and coolness. The contrasting colors of warm ivory and deep wine signify trust, against shadows of ash gray representing suspicion. The main collision happens in the center with more vibrant tones emerging from muted backgrounds, emphasizing the delicate balance. The pattern features soft dots on one side versus shattered elements on the other, visually representing the tension in the seed. Broken pixels hint at moments of disruption, providing an imperfect yet cohesive narrative.
