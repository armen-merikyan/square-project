Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between a readable pulse and the surrounding static noise. The palette is dominated by deep blacks and grays to create a sense of isolation, complemented by vibrant greens and magentas that represent fleeting signals within. The central collision point features bold accents clashing with a predominantly dark grid, invoking an emotional struggle between clarity and confusion. The design embodies the chaos of memory and distraction, illustrated through split patterns that isolate bursts of vibrancy against murky backgrounds.
