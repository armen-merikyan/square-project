Artist Statement

This abstract composition embodies the emotional collision between vulnerability and strength. The palette, featuring soft pinks and deep maroons, reflects warmth intertwined with intensity, showcasing the delicate balance between exposure and shelter. The center is occupied by lighter tones symbolizing vulnerability, with darker edges framing it as a protective barrier but allowing for a stuttering flow of deeper colors that interrupt the space, signifying strength. The patterns of islands (soft, scattered colors) and a maze (complex, dense sections) collide in rhythm, creating a visual tension that mirrors the emotional theme.
