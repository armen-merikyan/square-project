Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between feelings of loneliness and connection, juxtaposed against the idea of lightness and weight. The chosen palette of dark maroon, gray, pink, and red highlights tension, with dark shades representing loneliness pressing against the brighter tones of connection. The layout climbs upward, creating a sense of hope alternated with bursts of energy emerging from the depths. Boundaries are delineated using burned edges to signify the imperfections of memories and emotions. The collision between muted darks and vibrant highlights underscores a constant push and pull, inviting viewers to contemplate the complexity of their own emotional landscapes.
