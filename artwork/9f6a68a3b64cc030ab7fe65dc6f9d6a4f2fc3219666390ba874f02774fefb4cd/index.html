Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between restlessness and peace. The dominant dark colors signify depth and unrest, representing memory and motion that clash with moments of lightness. The palette includes a variety of greens and golds to depict both calmness and intensity. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where the chaotic lower colors clash with the serene upper hues, embodying the emotional dialogue of the seed. Broken pixels are introduced to disrupt uniformity, emphasizing the imperfections of this emotional experience.
