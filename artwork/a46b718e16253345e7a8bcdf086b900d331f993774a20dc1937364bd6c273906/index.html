Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional duality of trust and suspicion, represented through a palette evoking safety and exposure. Soft greens and warm browns create a sense of shelter, while contrasting cold whites and open blues introduce the tension of suspicion. The layout collides horizontal and vertical movements at the center, creating a focal point where trust softens suspicion, yet echoes of doubt permeate. The broken pixels disrupt uniformity, symbolizing the fragility within resilience. The patterns intersect, allowing for overlapping emotions to unfold through color placement and contrast, delivering a reflective narrative on the challenges of establishing trust amidst suspicion.
