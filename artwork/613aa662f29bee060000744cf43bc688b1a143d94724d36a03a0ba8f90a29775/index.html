Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between love and betrayal, represented through contrasting colors and disrupted patterns. The palette features warm reds and pinks to symbolize love, contrasted by deep grays and dark maroons representing betrayal. The layout with the top half reflecting love's gentleness through the crosshatch pattern, while the bottom half distorts this with a jagged edge and chaotic arrangement, showcases a split in symmetry that evokes discomfort and unresolved tension. The use of broken pixels introduces deliberate imperfections, enhancing the sense of vulnerability and creating a narrative of emotional conflict.
