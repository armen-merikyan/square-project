Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle of clarity amidst overwhelming noise. The palette of deep blacks and vibrant greens signifies the interplay between the starkness of noise and the vibrancy of a singular signal. The main contrast occurs in the center where a bright ring of green interrupts the static gray and black background, symbolizing the moments of clarity that pierce through chaos. The arrangement of colors creates a falling pixel pattern, emulating descent and fragmentation, resonating with the seed's themes of interference and isolation.
