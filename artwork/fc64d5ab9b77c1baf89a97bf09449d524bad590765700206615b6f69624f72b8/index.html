Artist Statement

The emotional tone highlights the tension between safety and exposure, creating a juxtaposition of warm enclosure and bright vulnerability. The chosen palette, with shades of green and brown representing safety, contrasts with pale and cold hues signifying exposure. The main collision occurs toward the right, where the lighter colors abruptly meet the dense, darker shades, mirroring the split vertical nature of the seed. This arrangement evokes a feeling of hesitation, where softness meets severity, reflecting the broader themes of protection against the incoming pressures of exposure.
