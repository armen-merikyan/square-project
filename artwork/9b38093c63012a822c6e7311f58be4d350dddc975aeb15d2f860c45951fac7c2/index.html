Artist Statement

The emotional tone merges the explosive energy of rage with the subtlety of calm. The palette combines stark reds and deep blacks to express the weight of rage, with gentle blues and whites representing calmness. The central collision occurs between the jagged red patterns that fragment through the soft pale blue, conveying both a sense of pressure and a rising signal. This layout speaks to the ongoing conflict between powerful emotions, portraying an abstract rhythm of tension and release.
