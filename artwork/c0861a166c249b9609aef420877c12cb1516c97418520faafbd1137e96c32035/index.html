Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between love and betrayal, accentuated by warm colors of pink and red against the cooler gray and dark maroon. The diagonal rise suggests hope and escape, representing love, while the broken edges introduce tension and imperfection linked to betrayal. The collision occurs at the intersecting lines of stripes and rings, creating a dynamic where love's warmth meets betrayal's chill, invoking a feeling of conflict yet resolution in this abstract narrative.
