Artist Statement

This artwork captures the interplay between panic and control using a contrasting palette of dark violet and soft gold, symbolizing emotional extremes. The layout reflects a mirror effect to suggest instability, with slight disruptions (broken pixels) enhancing the tension. The flow from bottom to top evokes a sense of rising anxiety contrasted by the grounding darker tones, creating a visual pulse that evokes feelings of pressure and release.
