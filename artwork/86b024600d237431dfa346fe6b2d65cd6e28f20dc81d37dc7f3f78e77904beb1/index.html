Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a palette inspired by the 'guilt_forgiveness' theme. The soft gold and warm ivory suggest warmth and hope amidst the shadows of ash gray and stain brown. The pattern employs a grid to depict longing, capturing the idea of separation, while the noise elements depict resolve, portraying strength and compression. The main collision occurs at the center, where the two patterns intersect, symbolizing a tension-filled balance between chaos and order. The use of negative space emphasizes the feelings of solitude and distance.
