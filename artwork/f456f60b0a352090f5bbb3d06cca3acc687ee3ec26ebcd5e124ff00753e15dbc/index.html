Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between vulnerability and strength, using contrasting colors to represent these themes. The palette consists of muted off-white, gray, and faint peach to convey vulnerability, while dark navy represents strength. The main collision occurs centrally, where dark navy meets pale interior colors, embodying a dynamic tension. This interaction creates a visual metaphor for strength encroaching upon vulnerability, reflecting the seed's essence through an asymmetric layout.
