Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between loneliness and connection. The palette merges deep blue, muted purple, and vibrant warmth of yellow and orange—a juxtaposition of grief and joy. The central space contrasts a solitary dark square against burst-like bright colors that represent energy and connection. The patterns reflect a crosshatch against a grid, embodying hidden tensions and the push-pull of emotional depth. The imperfect broken pixels add texture, fostering the sense of both noise and quiet that defines human experience.
