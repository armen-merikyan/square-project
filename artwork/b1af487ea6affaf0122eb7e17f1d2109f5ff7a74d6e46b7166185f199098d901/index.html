Artist Statement

The composition embodies the tension between joy and grief through a dynamic interaction of warmth and coldness. The palette features bright yellows and oranges representing joy, contrasted with deep blues and muted purples symbolizing grief. The overall arrangement utilizes a zigzag flow depicting the conflict and collision of emotions, while the pattern juxtaposes broken dots for grief against a split-screen layout representing joy. Negative space offers a reflective quality that embodies moments of vulnerability amidst chaos. The result is a striking balance between fragmentation and expansion, echoing the struggle between light and dark emotions.
