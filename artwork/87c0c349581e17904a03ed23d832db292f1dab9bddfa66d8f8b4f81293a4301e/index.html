Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between longing and resolve, illustrated through contrasting patterns and colors. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold captures a warm yet urgent essence, evoking feelings of a broken mirror against a thread of hope. The arrangement features densely packed colors at the center, representing resolve, while the edges incorporate a quiet field, allowing for a sense of overflow, echo, and disruption. The central collision of noise patterns against the stretching sparks of longing represents a delicate interplay of emotion.
