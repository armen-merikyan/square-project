Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dichotomy of fear and hope. The left side, representing fear, employs darker shades with jagged patterns reflecting broken dots, creating a claustrophobic feel. In contrast, the right side uses lighter colors and crosshatch patterns that evoke a sense of rising light and escape, suggesting hope. The collision occurs in the center where dark and light colors meet, embodying the tension of these opposing emotions. The layout reinforces a split vertical contrast, mirroring the internal struggle expressed in the seed.
