Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends restlessness and peace, reflected in a palette juxtaposing soft mint and storm gray. The center is saturated with color to convey tension and energy, symbolizing restlessness, while the edges fade into quieter tones to suggest peace. The collision occurs diagonally, where divergence and overlap create dynamic tension, illustrating the push and pull of memory against motion. The use of broken pixels adds an element of imperfection, emphasizing the fragmented feelings evoked by the theme.
