Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The deep, dark colors represent the weight of longing while brighter gold and green accents signify moments of resolve and hope. The collision of dense clusters in the corner symbolizes the turmoil of unfulfilled desires, while the lighter areas represent clarity and intention. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfections, echoing the concept of chaos within our structured desires, allowing a glimpse of warmth amidst the intensity.
