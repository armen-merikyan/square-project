Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines clarity and chaos, reflecting the tension between controlled signals and intrusive noise. The chosen color palette emphasizes dark tones to signify noise, offset by vibrant accents representing the signal. The collision occurs at the center, where bright greens assert their presence amidst the darker gray and black tones, mirroring the struggle of clear thoughts in a sea of distractions.
