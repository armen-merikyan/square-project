Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between loneliness and connection, utilizing a palette of warm and cool colors to illustrate the contrast. The deep blue symbolizes solitude, while the brighter yellow and muted purple represent attempts at connection. The contrast occurs in the grid layout where the darker colors cluster at the center, symbolizing hidden emotions, while lighter colors spread outward, suggesting exposure. The arrangement creates a burst of emotional resonance, with the top-weighted feeling of connection battling against the isolation of the center, effectively conveying the struggle expressed in the seed.
