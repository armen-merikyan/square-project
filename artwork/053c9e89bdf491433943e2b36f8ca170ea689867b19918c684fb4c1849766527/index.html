Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between restlessness and peace through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, featuring pale aqua and alarm red, reflects serenity against urgency, while the diagonal flow introduces dynamic movement that collides with quiet areas. The texture of stairs symbolizes peace, while the target pattern evokes restless motion. The dominant flow from the corner creates a sense of encroaching energy, interrupted by soft edges, allowing for a sense of both tension and release.
