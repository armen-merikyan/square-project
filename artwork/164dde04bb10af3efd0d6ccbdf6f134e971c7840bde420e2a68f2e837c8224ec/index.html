Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between solitude and connection. The palette, drawn from 'broken_memory,' relies on muted sepia and faded blue tones to emphasize a sense of nostalgia and introspection. The main contrast manifests through the stark differentiation between dark colors in the center, symbolizing loneliness, and lighter hues that echo connection, indicating a pattern of dissonance. The arrangement exhibits a burst effect, juxtaposing simplicity against dense clusters, effectively embodying the push and pull between hidden versus exposed feelings. Asymmetrical placements suggest disruptions, while open spaces convey a sense of longing and unresolved emotion.
