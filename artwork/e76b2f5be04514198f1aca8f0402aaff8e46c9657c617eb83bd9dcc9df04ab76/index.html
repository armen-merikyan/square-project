Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances chaos and clarity, reflected through the use of dark navy and black as the foundation of noise, disrupted by the brighter dim cyan and isolated whites representing signal. The contrast occurs centrally, where the clear accent colors push back against the darker tones, mirroring the theme of 'signal vs noise'. The layout presents scattered disruptions (broken pixels) emerging from dense patches, contrasting with quiet negative spaces, embodying the struggle of clarity amidst chaos as indicated by the seed.
