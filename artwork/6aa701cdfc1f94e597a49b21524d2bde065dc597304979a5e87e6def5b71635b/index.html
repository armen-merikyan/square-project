Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between relief and dread using a palette that blends pale mint and faded gold for a sense of lightness against storm gray and near black to evoke heaviness. The composition features a dense accumulation of darker colors in the bottom left corner, easing into lighter hues toward the top right, symbolizing a release from pressure. The contrast is established through a crosshatched pattern in the upper region that interrupts the stacked heaviness, reflecting a transition from chaos to order. Broken pixels create intentional disruptions, suggesting scars or memories that resonate with the journey of escaping dread towards relief.
