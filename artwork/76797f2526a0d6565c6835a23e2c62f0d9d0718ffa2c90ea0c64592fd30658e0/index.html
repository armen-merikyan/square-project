Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dichotomy between longing and resolve through a blend of deep wine and warm ivory hues. The palette reflects warmth and depth, suggesting both vulnerability and strength. The primary contrast lies in the collision of the structured 'stairs' pattern of resolve (represented by soft gold) against the organic 'teeth' motif of longing (in warm ivory), particularly noticeable in the center and creating a tension-filled core. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, symbolizing the nuances of emotional experience, enhancing the overall narrative of struggle and acceptance.
