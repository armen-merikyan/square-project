Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between vibrant sparks of energy and subdued ashes of restraint, captured in a palette of melancholy blues and muted lavenders. This evokes a sense of struggle and resilience within a canvas of imperfection. The palette alternates between soft cyan and muted plum, with a contrast at the center, where radial patterns collide with crosshatch marks, creating an interruption in flow. The jagged sensations from memory and erasure are echoed through broken pixels, suggesting a layered complexity of feeling.
