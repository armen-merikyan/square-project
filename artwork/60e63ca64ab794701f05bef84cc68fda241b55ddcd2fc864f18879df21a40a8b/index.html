Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between the dense gathering of dark colors on one edge, suggesting pressure, contrasted with lighter, softer shades creating a quiet space. The selected palette encapsulates this duality, using muted tones for the quiet fields while the darker accents depict the pressure zone. The main collision occurs diagonally, where these two emotional realms intersect, creating a dynamic interplay that embodies the tension of quiet versus pressure.
