Artist Statement

This composition embodies the struggle between restlessness and peace, highlighted by the contrasting patterns of 'flood' and 'stairs.' The palette, featuring muted off-white and dark navy, reflects the quiet pressure and dense clusters of emotions. The central focus is a collision of warm and cool tones, emphasizing the push against the calm edges and inviting visual tension. The broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, allowing for moments of reflection amidst the chaos, echoing the theme of memory versus motion. Each row portrays shifting densities and tonal contrasts, capturing the essence of emotional conflict in an abstract visual form.
