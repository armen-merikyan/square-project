Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between control, represented by structured layers and a darker palette, and surrender, shown through chaotic bursts of color and lighter tones. The palette comprises warm cosmic indigo and deep teal, implying underlying intensity mixed with hints of cold gold for interruption. The main collision occurs at the center with a dense concentration of dark colors, contrasting against a lighter, releasing space creating a push-pull effect reminiscent of the themes of containment versus overflow.
