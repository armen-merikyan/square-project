Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates a dynamic emotional interplay between rage and calm through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette includes deep blacks and reds representing the intensity of rage, while soft pale blues and whites symbolize calmness, creating a sense of emotional conflict. The grid layout introduces important negative space, emphasizing the off-center small bright area that represents moments of calm breaking through the intensity of a heavier dark mass. The 'maze' pattern for rage creates a feeling of complexity and chaos, clashing against the 'radial' calm pattern which offers a sense of balance and serenity. This collision creates an abstract story of struggle and resolution, inviting the viewer into an emotional journey.
