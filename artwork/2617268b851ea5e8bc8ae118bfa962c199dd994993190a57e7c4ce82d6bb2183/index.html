Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes restlessness against peace, inspiring a balance of anxious bursts and calm areas. The palette, drawn from 'ancient_fire' colors, reflects intense emotion and warmth through burnt orange and dark red while grounding it with charcoal and gold. The contrast emerges diagonally across the square, where the chaotic crack pattern of restlessness collides with the glitch pattern of peace, marking a shift from heavy density to lighter spaces. The layout of spiral pressure emphasizes the journey through memory, echoing a heartbeat that disrupts the tranquility, embodying both a longing and resolution.
