Artist Statement

This composition contrasts the tension of panic and the calm of control. The palette incorporates soft pinks and deep reds to represent emotional intensity, while grays add a sense of stability. The layout features central concentration with bursts of warmth suggesting chaos surrounded by cold clarity, embodying the struggle between feeling overwhelmed and seeking balance. Strong contrasts occur between the densely packed colors in corners and more open spaces, allowing for dynamic breathing room amidst emotional turbulence.
