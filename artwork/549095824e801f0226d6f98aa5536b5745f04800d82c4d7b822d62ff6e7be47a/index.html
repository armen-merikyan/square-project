Artist Statement

This composition embodies the dichotomy of restlessness and peace through a dynamic interplay of color and pattern. The contrast between deep teal and star white advocates for tranquility, while cosmic indigo hints at unease. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the soft halo pattern of restlessness interrupts the calm tide of peace, emphasizing the emotional struggle. Broken pixels create intentional disruptions that echo the theme of unsettled balance against a quiet backdrop.
