Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between restlessness and peace. The palette, drawn from 'awe_terror,' combines deep teals, cosmic indigos, and stark whites to evoke a sense of contrast and conflict. The main collision happens diagonally, where dense clusters of dark colors battle against lighter, more serene hues, creating a visual dialogue that pulls the viewer in multiple directions. The interaction of stacked patterns—both structured and chaotic—reflects the push and pull of memory versus motion, echoing the seed's themes of internal struggle and fragmented calm.
