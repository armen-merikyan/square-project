Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between warmth and tension, captured through a palette of pinks and dark maroons, which juxtaposes love and betrayal. The contrast is evident where the warm pinks clash against the deep, dark colors symbolizing the pain of betrayal. The 'flame' shape seed translates to a diagonal rise motif, evoking a sense of conflict and movement. The patterns of grids and mazes create a dynamic texture that suggests entrapment, while the broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the theme of broken love. The layout leaves enough negative space, reflecting an empty room, suggesting emptiness amidst emotional chaos.
