Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dichotomy of softness and severity, translating into a palette of warm whites and deep blacks to represent contrast. The chosen palette of powder pink, warm white, carbon black, steel gray, and sharp crimson evokes a feeling of tension and resolution. The patterns—checker for softness and lattice for severity—collide at the center, emphasizing the push and pull of emotions captured in the seed. Broken pixels create intentional disruption, resembling the imperfect nature of feelings.
