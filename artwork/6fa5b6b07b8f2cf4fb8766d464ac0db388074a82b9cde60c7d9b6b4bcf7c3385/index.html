Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of panic and control, conveyed through a palette of pinks and reds grounded by dark neutrals. The dominant colors create a strong contrast that hints at instability while the 'mirror_broken' layout suggests a fractured perspective on these emotions. The zigzag flow introduces movement and tension, while the placement of 'broken pixels' adds an element of chaos to stabilize tension, tightly packed around a central light, acting as both a spark and a beacon amidst an overwhelming surround.
