Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between love and betrayal, characterized by warm pinks battling dark maroons. The palette's warmth embodies love, whereas the darker tones introduce betrayal's shadow. contrasting colors clash at the center, symbolizing an emotional confrontation, highlighting unrest between softness and sharpness, much like an inside vs. outside conflict. The pattern emerges through a crosshatch of warmth versus a chaotic noise, emphasizing desolation and connection. Negative space allows for breathing room, maintaining balance amid pressure.
