Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between joy and grief through a contrasting palette of warm and cool colors. The left side employs a scattered 'noise' pattern evoking grief with deep blues and muted purples, while the right side showcases a structured 'checker' pattern symbolizing joy in bright yellows and oranges. The center holds an intentional break, achieving a pulse-like rhythm that conveys the heart's beats of conflicting emotions. The jagged edges signify the rawness of emotions and the spatial symmetry hints at an unresolved narrative.
