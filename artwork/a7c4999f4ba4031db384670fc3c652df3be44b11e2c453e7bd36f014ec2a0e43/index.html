Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between fear and hope through a carefully chosen palette of dark violet and soft gold. The central area radiates a glow, representing hope amid darker edges that suggest fear. There’s a deliberate split between darker colors on the left and lighter colors on the right, signifying the struggle between weight and lightness. The pattern incorporates broken dots to symbolize optimism against a backdrop of maze-like complexities, creating a rhythmic interplay of chaos and calm, echoing the theme of contrast from the seed.
