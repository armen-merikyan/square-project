Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between joy and grief, utilizing a color palette of bright yellow and orange to symbolize joy, contrasted against deep blue and muted purple representing grief. The layout presents a near mirror, with purposeful 'broken' pixels introducing a sense of chaos amid order, creating an emotional rhythm that reflects the complexity of feelings. The palette draws on warm hues that spark emotion and cooler tones to invoke introspection. The main contrast occurs at the center, where the transition between joyful brightness and poignant depth happens, embodying the core of the seed concept.
