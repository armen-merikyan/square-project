Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette of soft hues like rain blue and lavender gray contrast with darker tones. The collision occurs centrally with soft cyan breaking the density of muted plum, representing strength in vulnerability. The layout utilizes stacked bands to imply shifting density and emotional strata, while broken pixels create intentional disruptions, suggesting the fragility amidst strength. The interaction between light and shadow reflects the themes of exposure against shelter.
