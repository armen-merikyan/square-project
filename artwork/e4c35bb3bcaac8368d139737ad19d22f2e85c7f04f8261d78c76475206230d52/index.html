Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates between confusion and clarity, represented through a clash of dark and vibrant hues. The palette includes black for grounding, electric blue for bursts of clarity, magenta for emotional depth, and lime for vibrant tension, reflecting a mechanical emotion. The main contrast arises in the center, where bright colors clash against darker, more chaotic edges, mirroring the central theme of confusion. Patterns of broken dots and maze-like forms extend outward, suggesting a fall into disorder while keeping a firm outline, encapsulating both the full and the hollow aspects of emotional experience.
