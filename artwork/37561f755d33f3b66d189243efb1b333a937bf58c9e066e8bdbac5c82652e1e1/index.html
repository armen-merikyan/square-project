Artist Statement

The emotional tone is one of tension between clarity (signal) and interference (noise). The palette was chosen to reflect this dichotomy, with bright greens and magentas signifying clarity and static gray embodying interference. The main collision occurs in the center, where the brighter pixel bursts (signal) interact with the quieter fields (noise), suggesting the struggle of a singular pulse amidst a chaotic backdrop. This arrangement echoes the seed's essence, illustrating exposure versus shelter in vibrant contrast with layered patterns that interrupt each other, creating a dynamic tension.
