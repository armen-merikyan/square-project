Artist Statement

This artwork depicts the struggle between desire and emptiness through a combination of vibrant and muted colors. The use of warm tones (#FF6F61, #FF4E50, #F9D35E) represents the push of desire, creating a saturated cluster in the center, while the cooler hues (#4A4A4A, #B7BEC3, #A3C6E2) suggest the surrounding empty spaces, illustrating a quiet pressure. The stuttered arrangement of color creates a sense of interruption and rhythm, echoing the emotional tension inherent in the theme. Broken pixels add intentional discord, reflecting the imperfections in the pursuit of desire.
