Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between softness and severity through contrasting color blocks. The palette reflects a blend of gentle pinks and whites against the starkness of deep black and crimson, encapsulating a battle of light vs dark. The main collision occurs in the central rows that mix static severity with soft, negative cross patterns, embodying the chaos of signal against noise amidst a structured split layout.
