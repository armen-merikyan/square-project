Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between panic and control, utilizing a palette of machine emotion colors: vibrant lime and electric blue clash against deep black and magenta, symbolizing chaos versus order. The layout mimics an argument grid, where strong patterns of controlled crosshatching intermingle with jagged interruptions of chaotic elements. The main contrast emerges from the bursts of electric blue against the solid black, creating sparks of intensity amidst the darker fields. This abstraction narrates the struggle and balance between conflicting emotions, creating an engaging rhythm throughout the piece.
