Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the tensions of defiance and submission, depicted through sharp contrasts and fluid patterns. The palette, 'guilt_forgiveness', features deep wine and ash gray for intensity and warmth, framing the interplay of a vibrant central area versus softer edges. The collision lies in the center, where the glitch pattern interrupts the lattice background, reflecting a chaotic pulse against a quiet foundation. This results in a dynamic clash of emotions, enclosed within an uneven border that hints at openings and closures, creating a narrative of struggle and resolution.
