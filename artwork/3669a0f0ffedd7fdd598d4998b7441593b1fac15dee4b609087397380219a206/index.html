Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension of 'quiet vs pressure' through a contrasting palette of muted colors against more vibrant accents to reflect the collision of elements. The choice of off-white and faint peach provides a soft foundation, while the darker hues of compressed red and dark navy introduce the theme of pressure. The main collision occurs towards the center, where dense clusters of red oppose the lighter fields, echoing the tension of a rising signal towards clarity. The abstract pattern is defined by broken lines, symbolizing disruption and imperfection, which structurally lies within a framework repeating this contrast dynamically.
