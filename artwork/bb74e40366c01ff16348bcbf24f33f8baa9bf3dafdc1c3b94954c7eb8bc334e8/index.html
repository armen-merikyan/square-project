Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dance between defiance and submission. The warm tones of rose clay and lamp gold represent defiance's brightness, while the cool blues and distant lilac suggest submission's quiet surrender. The layout illustrates a diagonal argument, with the warm colors dominating one wedge, suggesting an assertive push against the cooler, more subdued tones. The broken pixels intermittently disrupt the flow, representing moments of conflict. As the colors blend and collide, the result captures a dynamic tension and a sense of unresolved dialogue, reflecting the complex interplay of the emotions stated in the seed.
