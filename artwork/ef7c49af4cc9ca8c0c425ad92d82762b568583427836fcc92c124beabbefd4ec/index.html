Artist Statement

This artwork captures the duality of longing and resolve, presenting a tension between soft hues of intimacy and bold flashes of clarity. The palette uses warm clay and distant lilac to evoke a serene yet urgent atmosphere, while cool blue adds distance, contrasting with rich lamp gold that highlights key moments in the pixel arrangement. The collision is dominant in the center, where crosshatch and negative cross patterns intersect, illustrating the struggle between desire and fulfillment. Broken pixels disrupt the overall structure, suggesting imperfections and life's complexities, aligning with the theme of a 'mirror broken.' The layout mirrors yet diverges, allowing for a sense of unresolved motion and emotional depth.
