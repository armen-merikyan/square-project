Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between love and betrayal, symbolized through a palette of warm pinks and reds clashing against darker, muted grays and maroons. The use of stripes for love contrasts sharply with betrayal's frames, creating a push-pull dynamic in the center where the colors collide. Broken pixels add imperfection, enhancing the sense of loss and conflict, while the mirrored layout introduces a sense of confusion and distortion in the pattern, depicting the fracture in relationships. The composition captures both warmth and a sense of emptiness, suggesting the consequences of broken trust.
