Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes nostalgia with progress, creating a rift in the abstract composition. The palette, favoring lime and electric blue against black and magenta, embodies a mechanical heartbeat—soft yet electric, hinting at the duality of calmness and chaos. The contrast is significant: the bottom half reflects a heavier density with darker shades, while the top echoes lightness with sporadic lime, symbolizing growth from the past into potential. The broken pixels and falling layout enhance the notion of disintegration amid emerging clarity, leaving pockets of negative space to suggest unresolved emotions.
