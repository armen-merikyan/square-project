Artist Statement

This artwork evokes the emotional tension between trust and suspicion through the use of contrasting palettes and patterns. The chosen colors, warm reds and soft pinks, embody warmth and connection, while dark maroon and gray represent doubt and interference. The diagonal placements create a sense of motion and conflict, where the soft glow of trust attempts to break through the dense layering of suspicion. The layout engages the viewer with an argument grid, showcasing a visual struggle between space and density. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, emphasizing the fragility of trust against static elements. The overall composition reflects resilience against an uncertain backdrop, making the artwork resonate with a profound emotional narrative.
