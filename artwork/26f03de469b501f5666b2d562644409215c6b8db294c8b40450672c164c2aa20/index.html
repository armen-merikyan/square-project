Artist Statement

This artwork evokes an emotional tension between love and betrayal through a deliberate arrangement of contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, dominated by warm pinks and reds, suggests love and warmth, punctuated by darker maroon and gray tones that signify betrayal and loss. The pattern juxtaposes crosshatching and stair-stepped lines, representing a complex dance between unity and discord. The left side retains integrity while the right side fractures, suggesting a break in harmony, echoing the seed's themes of symmetry and contrast. The layout creates a visual dialogue of rising versus falling, achieving a delicate balance between chaos and calm that reflects the narrative of the seed text.
