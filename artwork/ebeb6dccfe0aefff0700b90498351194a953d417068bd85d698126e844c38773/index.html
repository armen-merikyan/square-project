Artist Statement

This composition evokes a complex emotional tone by contrasting vulnerability and strength through a balance of warm and cool colors. The palette combines rose clay and lamp gold to represent warmth and strength, while cool blue serves as a contrast to show vulnerability. The center is more densely packed with color and structure, representing strength, while the edges feature softer, broken pixel elements illustrating vulnerability. The tension lies predominantly in the middle rows where impactful contrasts occur, creating a visual echo of strength surrounded by delicate vulnerability.
