Artist Statement

This composition conveys a tension between recognizable forms and chaotic noise, embodying the struggle between clarity (signal) and confusion (noise). The navy and dim cyan create a deep undertone, suggesting depth and memory, while the sharp contrasts formed by single white amidst muted colors create focal points that disrupt and draw attention, akin to bright signals within darkness. The distribution reflects a central pulse being invaded by surrounding noise, highlighting its vulnerability and strength simultaneously. The layout shifts from the quiet margin toward a center of commotion, embodying the seed's essence.
