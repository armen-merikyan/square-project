Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension of longing against resolve. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue provides warmth and depth, suggesting both longing and a quiet strength. The broken frame pattern intersects with dots, creating collision points while maintaining negative space that induces reflection, representing the quiet channel that runs through the chaos. The central collision occurs at the middle, weighing heavy on longing but hinting at strength.
