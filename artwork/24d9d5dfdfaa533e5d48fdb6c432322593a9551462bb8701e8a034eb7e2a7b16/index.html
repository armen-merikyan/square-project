Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between safety and exposure, utilizing a cool palette to signify vulnerability and a warm palette to suggest protection. The main collision occurs in the center, where the cool pale skin tones interlace with warm brown frames, evoking a struggle between openness and enclosure. The soft edges allow for subtle transitions, while broken pixels represent interruptions in the emotional flow, mimicking the tension of the seed's narrative. The layout follows an 'empty room' aesthetic, with negative space enhancing the sense of quiet contemplation amid invisible pressures, suggesting organic rhythms of life.
