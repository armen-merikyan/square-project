Artist Statement

The artwork captures the tension between loneliness and connection through a deep contrast of colors and patterns. The palette, dominated by navy and black, reflects a somber, introspective mood, while dim cyan represents a longing for connection. The layout features a stark central core surrounded by a dense, chaotic frame, symbolizing both protection and isolation. The checker pattern in the outer border contrasts with the dots of connection in the center, embodying the push and pull between these emotional states.
