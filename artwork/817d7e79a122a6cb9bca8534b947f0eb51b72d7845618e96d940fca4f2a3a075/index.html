Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission, utilizing a palette inspired by melancholy to emphasize contrast. The left half features dynamic, bright colors that interrupt the muted, softer tones of the right side, creating a visual clash that evokes the struggle inherent in the seed's themes. The central area serves as a battleground where the vibrant 'islands' of defiance collide with the receding 'crosshatch' patterns of submission, symbolizing the ongoing interplay of chaotic pressure and calm quietude.
