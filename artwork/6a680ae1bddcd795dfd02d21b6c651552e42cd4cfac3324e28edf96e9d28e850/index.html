Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between love and betrayal using a palette of warm and cool hues. The juxtaposition of pink and dark maroon represents warmth battling against the chill of betrayal, creating a rhythm of rising and falling in the arrangement. The overall pattern embodies an argument grid, with fractured motifs suggesting interrupted connections. The interplay of dense clusters against negative space reflects inner turmoil and the push-pull of emotions, while broken pixels add an intrinsic flaw that echoes the complexities of relationships.
