Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the tension between safety and exposure, where a gentle weight of colors suggests protection and a stark contrast signals vulnerability. The palette includes soft greens and browns representing safety, flavored by a delicate touch of pale skin and blue, creating a feel of warmth fighting against coldness. The collision of colors occurs in the center, where channel patterns clash with patchwork textures, embodying the ongoing struggle between secure boundaries and external pressures. The composition features intentional disruptions through broken pixels, highlighting the fragility of the shelter amidst a ripple of exposure.
