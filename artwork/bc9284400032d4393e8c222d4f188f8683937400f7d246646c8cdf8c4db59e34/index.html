Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional duality of longing and resolve, using soft greens to represent yearning nestled against vibrant browns symbolizing stability and structure. The contrast between the muted pastel area of pale skin and the saturated cold white creates a pulse of tension, akin to a heartbeat, radiating from the central ember shape. The broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, invoking a sense of repair amidst the chaos, reminding us of the fragility in our pursuits. The interplay of expanses and clustered patterns invites viewers to reflect on the balance between serenity and urgency.
