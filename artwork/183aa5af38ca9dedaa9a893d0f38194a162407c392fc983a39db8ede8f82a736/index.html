Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional juxtaposition between joy and grief. Using a palette of bright yellows and muted deep blues, it captures the tension of joy attempting to break through the layers of grief. The patterns of stripes for joy and rain for grief create movement, while the arrangement of colors offers collision and contrast. The zigzag flow contrasts the stable checker pattern, hinting at chaos amid order, resonating with the themes of rising signals and emotional complexities. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, symbolizing how grief interrupts joy.
