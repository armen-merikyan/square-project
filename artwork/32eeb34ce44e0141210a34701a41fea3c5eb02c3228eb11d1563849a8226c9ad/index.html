Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay between vulnerability and strength, achieved through a duality in color where muted hues evoke a sense of fragility against bolder tones expressing resilience. The palette comprises earthy greens and browns for grounding strength, complemented by pale skin and cold whites to illustrate vulnerability. The central area is densely packed with a radial structure representing strength, while the outer regions transition into lighter, radial patterns suggesting vulnerability. The main collision occurs where the strength in the center meets the softness of the edges, articulating the tension between protection and exposure.
