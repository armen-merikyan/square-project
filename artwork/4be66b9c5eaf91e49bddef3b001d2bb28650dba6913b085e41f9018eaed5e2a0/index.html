Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between warmth and despair, capturing the tension of love and betrayal. The palette features shades of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon, illustrating warmth clashing against cold emptiness. The broken pixels reflect imperfections and the jagged nature of heartbreak. The central composition creates a flow from the warmth of love (upper left) to the desolation of betrayal (lower right), where colors collide and create a visual spark. The arrangement suggests a ripple effect, evoking a sense of movement and unresolved emotions.
