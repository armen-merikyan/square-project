Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece contrasts vulnerability and strength. The palette, derived from 'broken memory', with sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue, symbolizes a sense of nostalgia and fragility while invoking a feeling of grounded stability. The layout of 'falling_pixels' creates a rain-like descent to illustrate the exposure and weight of emotions, while a collision occurs predominantly in the center where a denser concentration of muted tones interacts with lighter hues and broken pixels, creating a central void suggesting vulnerability amidst the surrounding strength. The patterns emphasize a soft, pressure band against the robustness of a negative cross, symbolizing the delicate balance between holding on and letting go.
