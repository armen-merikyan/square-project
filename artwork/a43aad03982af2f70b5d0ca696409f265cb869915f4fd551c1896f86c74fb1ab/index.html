Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captures the tension between trust and suspicion, manifesting in a divided space that shimmers with layers of density against a backdrop of quiet. The cool, muted palette of deep olives and ripe greens represents suspicion, while the soft golden hues interject moments of trust. The main contrast occurs in the central diagonal, where dark shadows clash with brighter, textural elements. The pattern logic reflects the duality of feelings, capturing the fragmentation of perception through broken pixels that suggest imperfections and scars in relationships.
