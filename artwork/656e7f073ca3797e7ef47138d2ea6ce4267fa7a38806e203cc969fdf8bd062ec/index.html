Artist Statement

This abstract composition explores the duality of fear and hope. The outer border, depicted in darker shades, symbolizes fear with jagged edges creating a sense of tension and uncertainty. In contrast, the inner 4x4 core showcases lighter colors and gentle patterns, representing hope and possibility. The soft gold and pale green elements break through the weighty darkness, evoking an upward flow of optimism. The arrangement of darker colors at the corners versus glowing accents at the center forms a striking collision that mirrors the seed's theme of light against weight, articulating the emotional struggle between despair and possibility. Broken pixels add an element of imperfection, reinforcing the complexity of emotions.
