Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between longing and resolve through a delicate interplay of color and form. The palette balances muted grays and off-whites with sharp, compressed reds and deep dark navy. The main contrast manifests at the center, where the rhythm of 'echo' from longing collides with the 'target' of resolve, creating a vibrant pulse amidst a background of calm. The broken pixels serve as intentional scars, embodying the theme of repair versus damage in a journey filled with interruptions and subdued energy.
