Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork is rooted in the tension between longing and resolve. The burnt orange and dark red colors represent the urge and warmth of longing, while charcoal signifies a solid foundation of resolve. The central placement of the vibrant gold accent creates a pulse against the muted backdrop, symbolizing an impending breakthrough in a fractured scenario. The 'shatter' pattern represents the inevitable interruptions and scars amid the smooth, flowing rhythms of 'pulse_marks.' The portfolio of colors, particularly with the broken pixels, highlights the beauty within the chaos, symbolizing repair amidst damage. The collision occurs around the center, where the gold accent pushes against the darker hues, suggesting hope battling inertia. This creates a visual narrative of contained energy straining against the edges, completing the conceptual bridge set by the seed emotion.
