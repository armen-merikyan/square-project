Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a spectrum between longing and resolve, using a palette that juxtaposes vibrant electric blue and magenta against deep blacks. This choice evokes both urgency and serenity, suggesting a struggle to maintain balance in a chaotic world. The main contrast occurs centrally, where stacks of longing converge with expansive floods of resolve, creating a visual tension. The pattern utilizes broken pixels as interruptions, representing the fragmented nature of experiences and emotions, while leaving negative space to allow the viewer to breathe amidst the intensity.
