Artist Statement

This composition captures the duality of longing and resolve through a blend of soft and severe colors. The dominance of warm white and sharp crimson suggests a tension where longing rises against the compressed strength of carbon black and steel gray, creating a push-pull dynamic. The densely saturated center echoes the intensity of emotions while the scattered, broken pixels introduce imperfection, reflecting the struggle of resolving chaos. The quiet edges provide a contrast that invites the viewer to the dense core, representing intimacy and distance. The arrangement reinforces feelings of unresolved tension and emotional complexity.
