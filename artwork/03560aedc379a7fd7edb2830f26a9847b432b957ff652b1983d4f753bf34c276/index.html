Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension of defiance against submission. The palette of pinks, reds, and grays suggests both warmth and intensity, representing the collide of strong emotions. The central area is dense and charged with the halo pattern (defiance), while softer echoes of color (submission) create a surrounding field that interrupts the dominance, particularly in the corners. The quiet channel cuts through, signifying a path of resistance against collapse. Broken pixels add imperfections, embodying the chaos that accompanies this tension.
