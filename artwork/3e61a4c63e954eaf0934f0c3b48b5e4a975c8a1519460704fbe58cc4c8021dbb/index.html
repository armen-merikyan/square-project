Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between joy and grief, portraying a split between warm and cool tones. The chosen palette juxtaposes bright yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples to represent joy's spark and grief's heaviness. The arrangement features upward and downward movement with bursts of energy in warm colors colliding with cooler, somber tones, suggesting the struggle between resilience and sorrow. Overall, the patterned density allows a quiet release within structured chaos, echoing the emotional narrative of the seed.
