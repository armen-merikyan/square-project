Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between rage and calm, illustrated by a palette of stark contrasts. Red symbolizes rage with sharp, jagged placements, while pale blue represents calm, spreading evenly and softly. The collision occurs in the center, where the chaotic red overlaps with tranquil blue, creating a rhythmic push-pull dynamic. The inclusion of broken pixels deliberately disrupts the flow, echoing a machine-like emotional pulse against a quiet background, emphasizing the balance between ordering chaos and contained calm.
