Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between clarity and confusion. Using an 'ancient fire' palette, the burnt orange contrasts with the dark red and charcoal, representing the adversarial duality. Clarity emerges from symmetry, while broken pixels and a split pattern introduce noise, creating visual tension. The layout offers a spacious feel, with thick edges suggesting strength against the backdrop of confusion, and negative space allowing the mind to wander. The placement creates a pulse-like rhythm that echoes the chaos and calm.
