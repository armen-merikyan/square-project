Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between yearning (longing) and steadfastness (resolve). The palette blends warm clay with cool blue to highlight the interplay of intimacy and distance. Main contrast occurs at the center where the dense patterns collide, suggesting both closeness and divergence, echoing the complex nature of emotional relationships. This juxtaposition captures the essence of the seed, with broken pixels representing scars of past connections amidst the structured frame of resolve.
