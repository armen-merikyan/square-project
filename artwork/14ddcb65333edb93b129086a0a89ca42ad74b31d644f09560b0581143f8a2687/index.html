Artist Statement

This composition illustrates the emotional tension between fear and hope through contrasting patterns and colors. The chosen palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green embodies a struggle where dark hues represent fear and lighter tones signify hope. Jagged edges collide with soft appearances, creating an interplay of sharp and soft impressions. The layout emphasizes two opposing forces vying for control, with a slight upward flow suggesting an escape from fear towards hope. The broken pixels add a sense of imperfection and disruption, effectively influencing the theme of rising signals amidst chaos.
