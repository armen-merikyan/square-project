Artist Statement

This abstract artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette mirrors resilience and fragility, using moss green to evoke calmness and bone white for airy lightness, while rust and stone gray introduce elements of inner turmoil. The main collision occurs in the center where warm colors intertwine with darker tones, expressing a push and pull that reflects repair against damage. The patterns of pressure bands and flame simulate a pulse, a rhythmic heartbeat of emotion, appearing as threads weaving through space, while broken pixels disrupt the flow, highlighting imperfections naturally embedded in our emotional experiences.
