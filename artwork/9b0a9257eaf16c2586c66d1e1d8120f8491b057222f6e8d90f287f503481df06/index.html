Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the tension between control and surrender, manifesting through a central dense area of deep reds and pinks that symbolize overwhelming emotion and pressure. The borders fade into softer grays, representing a gentle letting go, creating a stark contrast between the vibrant center and the subdued edges. The collision occurs at the center, where the heavy outlines form a knot-like shape, suggesting an ongoing struggle to contain intensity. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfection, embodying the unavoidable disruption in our emotional landscapes.
