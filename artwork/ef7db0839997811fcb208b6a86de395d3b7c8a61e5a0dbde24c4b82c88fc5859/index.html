Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between awe and terror, capturing a spectrum of emotional collisions through its color choices and patterns. The 'ancient_fire' palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold suggests warmth and danger, forming an evocative yet harmonious blend. The outer edges, characterized by dark and muted tones, create a protective barrier of pressure, while the inner section erupts with fiery stripes symbolizing awe, contributing to a feeling of release. The contrast between the deep darkness and the vivid inner colors represents the clash between calm and chaos, with broken pixels introducing an element of imperfection that enhances the emotional narrative.
