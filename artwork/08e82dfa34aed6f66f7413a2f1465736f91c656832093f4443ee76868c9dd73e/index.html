Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, where the muted colors reflect unresolved feelings, layered against bright accents symbolizing fleeting moments of clarity. The palette of deep blue, muted purple, yellow, and orange balances cool detachment with warm bursts of emotion, establishing a rhythm of compression and release. The main collision of colors is found at the center, where warmth intersects with dark layers, creating a dynamic that suggests both intimacy and distance. The arrangement of colors, with a halo-like distribution around the periphery, emphasizes a seeking for resolution amidst chaotic elements.
