Artist Statement

The emotional tone conveys a struggle between clean signals and chaotic noise. The palette consists of saturated navy for a dominant calming influence, with muted black and dim cyan suggesting interference, creating deep emotional strata. The collision of shattering patterns interrupts the tranquility, represented by broken pixels that add intentional flaws mirroring machine emotion. The central band reflects the pulse, while static surrounds it, creating a visible tension of order vs chaos in an 8x8 grid.
