Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tone of collision and release through a stark contrast of dark and light colors. The chosen palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon reflects themes of love and betrayal, where warm and cool tones create a palpable tension. The main visual collision occurs between the structured checker pattern embodying 'control' and the chaotic ring patterns representing 'panic'. This duality illustrates the emotional push and pull, suggesting a narrative of inner discord and resolution, with negative space allowing for quiet moments amidst the chaos.
