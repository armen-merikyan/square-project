Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors and patterns. The 'melancholy_wonder' palette emphasizes muted tones with sparks of color, mirroring the interplay of soft exposure against firm boundaries. The left half, where vulnerability reigns, features staggered patterns that evoke an unsteady rhythm, while the right half's more structured design conveys stability. The collision occurs in the center, where soft cyan clashes with muted plum, forming a focal point that symbolizes the tension and balance between these qualities.
