Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts panic and control, portrayed through a palette that combines warm and cool hues. The chosen colors—sepia, tan, dust_gray, and faded_blue—evoke feelings of nostalgia and uncertainty. The arrangement is characterized by a center-heavy design, with upward and downward movements reflecting the struggle between chaos and order. The soft transition between warm and cool colors suggests tension, while the strategic placement of broken pixels creates an atmosphere of fragmentation, echoing feelings of instability. The pattern of dots surrounding the central filled square embodies the unpredictable nature of emotions, adding depth and echoing the seed's themes of internal conflict and exploration.
