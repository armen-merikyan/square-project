Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional spectrum between longing and resolve. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold reflects a fiery conflict, where warm hues suggest intensity and urgency, while cooler shades introduce serenity. The layout is characterized by scattered areas of dense color clashing with open spaces, representing feelings of pressure against a backdrop of calm. The central collision is emphasized by a vibrant patch of gold at the center, creating a focal point that symbolizes a bridge attempting to repair fragmented connections. Broken pixels create intentional disruptions throughout, echoing the theme of imperfection and the attempts to mend the brokenness. Overall, the arrangement captures a dynamic interplay of emotions, transforming the seed into a complex abstract narrative.
