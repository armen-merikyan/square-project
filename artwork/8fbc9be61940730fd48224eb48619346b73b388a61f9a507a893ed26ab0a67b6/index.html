Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension of control versus surrender, represented through a structured ring pattern on one side and dot bursts on the other. The palette of dark maroon and varying shades of gray symbolizes the weight of control overlaid with moments of emotional overflow, while the broken pixels introduce imperfection, echoing the struggle between confinement and freedom. The central collision reflects a pivotal tension, striking at the very core of the piece and allowing visual chaos to encroach upon the ordered structure.
