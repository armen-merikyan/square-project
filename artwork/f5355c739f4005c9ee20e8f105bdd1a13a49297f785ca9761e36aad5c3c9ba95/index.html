Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between trust and suspicion through color contrasts and structural placement. The palette features warm and soft tones like hot coral and dust pink, reflecting trust, while the dark charcoal symbolizes suspicion. The main collision occurs along the diagonal, where the soft rain pattern intermingles with the lattice of suspicion, suggesting the push and pull between emotion states. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfection, serving as disruptions that mirror moments of doubt within a trusted connection.
