Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between calm and chaos, reflecting restlessness on the left through a scattered dot pattern and peace on the right through a smooth flood. The palette, including warm shadows and cool blue, represents the push and pull of contrasting emotions. The dominant collision occurs diagonally across the center, as the soft hues of tranquility clash with the vibrant dots of unrest, depicting a journey of memory and motion.
