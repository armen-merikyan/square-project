Artist Statement

The emotional clash between vulnerability and strength is expressed through a palette of soft, muted colors with a few stark contrasts. The focus is a pale center suggesting vulnerability, surrounded by darker edges to symbolize strength. The arrangement features a collision between a dense, lattice-like structure and a thinner, ladder pattern, evoking tension. This resonates with the seed's themes of exposure and protection, reflecting a struggle between safety and risk.
