Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dichotomy of defiance and submission, utilizing colors from the 'serenity_urgency' palette to convey both tension and calm. The focus on dark ink blue and alarm red creates a collision of intensity and quiet, with red hints sparking against the cooler tones. The spatial arrangement delves into a mostly empty center surrounded by dense noise, representing the struggle in the periphery, which resonates with the inner turmoil suggested by the seed's emotional themes. As a result, a visual interplay of steady presence (submission) and auditory chaos (defiance) emerges.
