Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional duality of vulnerability and strength. The palette balances soft blues and grays (representing vulnerability) against deep blacks and sparkles of faded gold (symbolizing strength). The central area employs broken dot patterns, surrounded by a more tranquil field, creating an effect where the edges feel overpowering, maintaining a 'dense island in a sea'. The central collision occurs with dense color contrasts meeting softer shades, fostering emotional tension and engagement.
