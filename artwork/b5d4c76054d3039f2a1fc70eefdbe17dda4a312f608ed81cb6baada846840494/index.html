Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between 'longing' and 'resolve,' where a central broken energy is surrounded by softer tones suggesting negative space. The palette combines 'ripe green' for warmth and optimism with deeper hues like 'deep olive' and muted 'cream,' representing complexity and reflection. The collision occurs centrally, represented by darker pixels against lighter ones, mirroring the embrace of connection and the pain of distance, embodying the emotional dynamics of the seed through overlapping grids and scattered dots.
