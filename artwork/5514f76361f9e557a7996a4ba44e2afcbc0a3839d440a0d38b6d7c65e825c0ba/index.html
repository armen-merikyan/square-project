Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between softness and aggression, mirroring the interplay of tenderness and rage. The chosen palette of deep blacks and vivid reds conveys powerful contrasts, while the pale blue and white suggest quiet spaces amidst the turmoil. The contrasting colors collide in the center, highlighting the struggle between light and heavy, muted and saturated. The arrangement follows an eroded symmetry pattern, establishing an emotional flow that suggests upward movement against a backdrop of harshness. Broken pixels add intentional disruptions, echoing unresolved feelings.
