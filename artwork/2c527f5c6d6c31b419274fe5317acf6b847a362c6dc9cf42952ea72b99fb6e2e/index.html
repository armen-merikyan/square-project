Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between longing and resolve. The palette incorporates safety-exposure colors: shelter green and open blue evoke a delicate calm, while warm brown and pale skin introduce warmth and vulnerability. The main contrast occurs in the center where the flame pattern (resolve) collides with the lattice pattern (longing), creating an interaction that feels both soothing and unresolved, suggesting an incomplete path of repair amidst the fragments. Broken pixels disrupt the grid, enhancing the sense of imperfection and echoing the theme of damage and repair.
