Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between loneliness and connection, represented through a palette of muted sepia and faded blue, evoking nostalgia and warmth. The density of deeper colors in the upper rows contrasts with the lighter tones below, simulating a heavy top weight, while the scattered brighter pixels evoke bursts of connection amidst an overarching texture of loneliness. The pattern aligns with the crosshatch and corner invasion concept, promoting an active visual tension where hidden sparks of connection disrupt the surrounding expanse of negative space.
