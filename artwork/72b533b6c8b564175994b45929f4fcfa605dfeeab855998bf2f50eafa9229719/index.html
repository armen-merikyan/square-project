Artist Statement

This composition captures the dynamic interplay between longing and resolve. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of nostalgia and weight. The darker hues suggest the heaviness of longing, while the lighter tones offer glimpses of resolve and clarity. The main collision occurs centrally, where the circular patterns representing longing disrupt the angular shapes embodying resolve, recommending a struggle for balance. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, enhancing the tension and reflecting moments of repair amidst damage.
