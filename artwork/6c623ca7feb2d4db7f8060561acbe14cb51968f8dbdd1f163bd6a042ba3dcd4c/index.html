Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between emptiness and desire through contrasting warm and cool colors. The palette combines vibrant yellows and oranges with deep blues and muted purples, suggesting an emotional push and pull. The upward flow symbolizes hope, while broken pixel elements suggest imperfection and vulnerability, contrasting with the clean lines of desire. The composition captures a rhythmic dance between density and negative space, representing echoes of longing amidst feelings of isolation.
