Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes clarity against confusion, embodied through the broken grid of vibrant colors. The palette of soft gold, dark violet, and pale green evokes a spectrum of feelings—from hope to uncertainty—intertwining the serene aspects of clarity with the disruptive energy of confusion. The main contrast arises from the diagonal flow of vibrant yellows and greens atop a foundation of darker tones, symbolizing the struggle between order and chaos. The broken pixel elements enhance the impact of this tension, creating interruptions that signify emotional echoes and unresolved states.
