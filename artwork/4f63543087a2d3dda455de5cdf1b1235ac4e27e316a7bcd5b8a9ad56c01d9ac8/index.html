Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork embodies a struggle between dense pressure and soft quietness. The palette blends muted shades of 'dust_gray' and 'dark_navy' with the subtle warmth of 'faint_peach'. This equilibrium reflects the contrast where soft hues guide the eye while darker colors loom heavily at the edges. The collision occurs in the center where tightly packed colors pulse outward, mimicking the emotional turmoil suggested by the words in the seed. The spiral layout creates a sense of motion, symbolizing counterflow against a backdrop of calm. Broken pixels add imperfections that evoke feelings of shatter and confusion, responding to the seed's 'clarity_confusion'.
