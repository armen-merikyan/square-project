Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece explores the tension between control and surrender, using a palette of stark contrasting colors. The outer layer, framed in dark blues and blacks, represents the controlled space, while the softer inner area envelops a burst of vibrant oranges and whites, suggesting liberation and overflow. The collision emerges at the center where these two emotional aspects interact, creating visual tension. The pattern flows from a structured outer frame to a chaotic, starry inner core, embodying the duality of containment and release.
