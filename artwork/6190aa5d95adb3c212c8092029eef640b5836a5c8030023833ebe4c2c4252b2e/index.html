Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between envy (represented by darker hues) and gratitude (represented by lighter, more vibrant colors). The selection of a black and pale blue palette embodies the juxtaposition of rage and calmness. The main contrast lies in the zigzag pattern, creating visual tension through the horizontal and vertical placements, while broken pixels introduce a sense of disruption. This approach consciously embraces asymmetry to evoke the feelings tied to imbalance, mirroring the complex emotional landscape suggested in the seed.
