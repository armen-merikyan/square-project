Artist Statement

This artwork captures the dualities of love and betrayal through contrasting colors and placements. The palette of warm reds and pinks suggests passion and connection, while muted grays underscore tension and loss. The emotional collision is represented through asymmetrical patterns that echo the seed's themes of brokenness and warmth. The sharp vs. soft contrast creates an unresolved feeling, inviting the viewer to reflect on their own experiences of love and betrayal. The layout emphasizes a central disruption, suggesting a heart fragmented amidst the chaos of emotions. The inclusion of broken pixels adds an imperfection that enhances the narrative of conflict within unity.
