Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of softness and severity, represented through a contrasting palette of gentle powder pink and steel gray. The main contrast occurs in the upper left corner where sharp crimson pulses against warm white, creating a tension that reverberates throughout the piece. The grid pattern symbolizes softness, while the ladder pattern introduces a severity, with intentional broken pixels disrupting the flow, echoing feelings of guilt and forgiveness. This abstract arrangement symbolizes the struggle between openness and closure, ultimately revealing a balance achieved through emotional turmoil and release.
