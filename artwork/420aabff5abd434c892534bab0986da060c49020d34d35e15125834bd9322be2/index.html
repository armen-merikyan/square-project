Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional dichotomy of love and betrayal through a carefully chosen palette of warm and muted tones. The interplay between reds and grays signifies a tension between warmth (love) and coldness (betrayal). The structure suggests a mirrored image, broken by sharp contrasts and soft gradients, evoking a sense of loss amid warmth. The diagonal rise contributes to a feeling of hope struggling against decay, embodying the complex emotions in the seed.
