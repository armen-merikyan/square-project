Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between pride and shame, utilizing a palette of navy and dim cyan to signify depth and emotional contrast. The top half features broken dots representing pride, suggesting a scattered momentum, while the bottom half uses crack patterns to illustrate shame, resulting in a feeling of fragmentation. The overall flow evokes a diagonal fall, where light and dark interplay, symbolizing the collision of vivid emotions and the release therein.
