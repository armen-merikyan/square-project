Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional conflict between nostalgia and progress, illustrated through a warm vs. cool contrast palette. The sharp juxtaposition of electric blue and magenta against the starkness of black creates a visual tension that suggests confrontation and unresolved motion. Combining these traits with a layout that emphasizes negative space allows for a reflective quality amidst the chaos. The arrow shape influences the asymmetric patterns where one color interrupts another—creating a sense of dynamic movement while invoking feelings of both pressure and release.
