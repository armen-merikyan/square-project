Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of longing and resolve, with the palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon evoking both warmth and intensity. The main contrast occurs in the center, where the framework of longing (the pink and gray frame) meets the structured lattice of resolve (the reds). This collision symbolizes a pulse of emotion against a backdrop of negative space, creating a dynamic rhythm within the arrangement. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, symbolizing the complexities of repair versus damage that resonate with the seed's theme.
