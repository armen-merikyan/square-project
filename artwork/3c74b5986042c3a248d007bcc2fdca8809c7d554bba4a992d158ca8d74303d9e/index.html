Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a careful balance between tension and tranquility, embodying the conflict of restlessness against the search for peace. The palette blends warm browns and greens with cool whites and soft blues to illustrate this contrast. The dense center bursts into looser segments towards the edges, creating an impact where the two emotional states collide, symbolizing the dynamics between memory and motion. The 'filled_square' shape represents cohesion amidst chaos, while broken pixels introduce intentional imperfection. The layout favors density at the core, making a statement of pressure that gradually disperses, channeling both flickers of energy and moments of calm.
