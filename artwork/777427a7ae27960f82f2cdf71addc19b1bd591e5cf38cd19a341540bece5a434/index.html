Artist Statement

This artwork contrasts restlessness and peace through a mix of warm and cool tones. The palette includes shelter green and warm brown for grounded peace, while cold white and open blue introduce restlessness and tension. The challenge of the diagonal invasion layout echoes memory and motion, creating a strong collision of a warm central glow pressed by radial patterns of cool tones at the edges, resulting in an emotional pull between calm and chaotic.
