Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve, represented through a palette that synergizes cosmic indigo and deep teal, evoking both depth and contemplation. The contrasting structures of horizontal stripes (resolution) meet an irregular array of pixels (longing) that suggest chaotic beauty and brokenness. The collision occurs primarily in the center, where the rigidity of black rectangles meets the softer, textured edges of the longing patterns. This intentional disruption embodies the struggle between order and chaos, mirroring the tensions outlined in the seed, producing a visual narrative filled with echoes, interruptions, and a sense of pursuit.
