Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between connection and loneliness, with a palette that suggests fear and hope through deep blacks and soft golds. The layout is center-heavy, with more intense colors at the core representing pressure, while surrounding pixels in muted hues symbolize quietness. The main contrast occurs where the dark violet collides with pale green, creating a pulse that reverberates across the composition. This arrangement embodies the theme of rhythm and interruption, mirroring life's emotional collisions.
