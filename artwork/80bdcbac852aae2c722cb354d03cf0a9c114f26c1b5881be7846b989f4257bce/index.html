Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between love and betrayal, with the palette selected to emphasize warmth and depth. The pinks and reds represent love’s warmth, while the dark maroon and gray denote betrayal’s coldness. Main contrasts arise in the geometric grid pattern against broken symmetry, where warm colors on one side clash against cool hues on the other. The diagonal flow and burned edge style encapsulate the tension and unresolved intersections suggested by the seed, with each broken pixel symbolizing a fracture in emotional clarity, invoking a feeling of both protection and emotional chaos.
