Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between longing and resolve, creating a rhythmic pull and push through contrasting colors. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green reflects the tension between desire and certainty, with dark colors representing depth and longing, while soft gold introduces warmth and hope. The main collision occurs centrally where the dark violet and soft gold intersect, symbolizing the struggle between chaos and order. The broken pixels throughout add texture, hinting at compromise and the human condition’s imperfections, echoing the abstract narrative of the seed around intimacy and distance.
