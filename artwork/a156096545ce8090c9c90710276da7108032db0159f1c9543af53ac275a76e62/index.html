Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve, expressed through contrasting patterns and color dynamics. The palette features calm shades of pale aqua and mist gray, disrupted by the urgent presence of alarm red and ink blue. The collision arises at the center, where patchwork patterns of longing meet the organized 'islands' of resolve. The use of broken pixels serves as scars of damage, signifying the tension of repair versus destruction. Overall, this piece captures the struggle between clarity and chaos.
