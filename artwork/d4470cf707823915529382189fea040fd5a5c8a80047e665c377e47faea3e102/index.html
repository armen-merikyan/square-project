Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between trust and suspicion through contrasting color fields. The muted cream signifies trust, while darker tones like blackened plum evoke suspicion. The patterns clash at diagonal edges, suggesting the struggle between these emotional pairs, creating an interplay of hard shadows and soft glows. The palette reinforces an atmosphere of conflict and resilience, reflecting on fractured symmetry.
