Artist Statement

This composition contrasts the emotional tones of softness and severity, using a palette of powder pink and sharp crimson against deep black and gray to express both gentle and harsh elements. The arrangement creates a significant collision at the center where energized crimson pulses against the stabilizing gray, while the surrounding softness provides a quiet backdrop. This reflects the dynamic balance of guilt and forgiveness, marking the boundaries of emotional duality.
