Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between clear signals and chaotic noise. The palette of dark navy and dim cyan conveys a sense of isolation and introspection, while the sharp contrasts of black and occasional white introduce moments of stark clarity. The placement of colors creates an ebb and flow reminiscent of fluctuating signals, with darker edges suggesting noise encroachment. Broken pixels serve to disrupt the flow, adding intentional scars that emphasize disorder within structure. The general pattern suggests islands of clarity amidst a backdrop of overwhelming interference, visually narrating a battle between coherence and confusion.
