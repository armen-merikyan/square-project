Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between emptiness and desire. The palette of deep navy and black evokes a sense of loneliness and a desire for connection. The asymmetry in the color distribution, with warmer tones sporadically interrupting the cooler base, symbolizes the flickering hope against overwhelming emptiness. Radial patterns contrast with the linear flow from left to right, suggesting the constant pull of desire amidst a quiet sea of black. Broken pixels intersperse the design to imply fragility in this yearning journey.
