Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette of soft off-white and faint peach contrasts sharply with the deep energy of dark navy and compressed red, creating a visual juxtaposition. The central shapes pulse toward one another, disrupted by breaks in the surrounding layers, embodying both protection and exposure. The densely packed colors on the periphery meet the empty spaces in the center, suggesting a conflict between pressure and release, mirroring the themes of strength and vulnerability.
