Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between joy and grief through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette merges vibrant yellows and oranges representing joy with muted blues and purples that evoke grief. The split screen layout introduces chaos and order within the emotion pair. The interaction of warm and cool colors denotes lively sparks amid low tones, creating an emotional conflict. This clash is accentuated through fractured patterns, ensuring a dynamic balance while leaving intentional negative space, emphasizing the emotional struggle as a central theme.
