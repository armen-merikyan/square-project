Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between softness and severity, using a palette of muted tones and sharp accents. The soft pinks and warm whites create gentle blends, contrasting with the somber carbon black and sharp crimson, which punctuate the composition sharply. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the subtle braiding of warmth meets the flooding severity of darker hues, suggesting an underlying 'battle' between gentle and harsh emotions. This balance hints at the spiraling influences of guilt and forgiveness, rippling through the art as an echo of complex emotional states.
