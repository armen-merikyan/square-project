Artist Statement

This artwork navigates the interplay between vulnerability and strength through a palette anchored in darkness and light. The use of black and dark violet creates a strong base, while soft gold and pale green infiltrate as accents, signifying fragile beauty. The central collision occurs where these opposing emotions meet, highlighted by a veil pattern suggesting vulnerability interwoven with a hinge structure representing strength. The composition forms a threshold, symbolizing the continual balance and tension between these feelings.
