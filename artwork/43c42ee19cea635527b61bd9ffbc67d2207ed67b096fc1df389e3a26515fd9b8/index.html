Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between clarity (signal) and confusion (noise) through a palette that contrasts vibrant colors with muted tones. The chosen palette of 'signal green,' 'electric blue,' and 'static gray' represents moments of clarity against overwhelming noise. The main collision occurs where lighter colors clash with darker, denser areas, symbolizing the fight for visibility amidst chaos. The zigzag flow aligns with contrasting patterns - a central clean path disrupted by bursts of color, illustrating how signals can ripple through static, creating a sense of movement and tension.
