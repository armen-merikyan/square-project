Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between restlessness and peace through its color choices and arrangement. The palette utilizes 'shelter_green' and 'cold_white' to create a field of calm contrasted with deep 'warm_brown' accents that symbolize disturbance and movement. The arrangement is denser at the left, suggesting pressure, while lighter spaces to the right evoke the sense of release. The collision is intentional, with 'warm_brown' interrupting pale spaces, illustrating the conflict of emotions and dynamic flow across the grid.
