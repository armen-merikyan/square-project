Artist Statement

The emotional tone arises from the tension between longing and resolve, expressed through a palette emphasizing deep contrasts between warm and cool tones. The dark, muted colors evoke a sense of yearning while the brighter hues suggest assertive resolve. The main collision occurs centrally, where two patterns – the staircase of longing and the sparks of resolve – intertwine, creating a visual echo of tension and release. The negative space embodied by the ash gray represents quiet introspection amid the emotional turmoil, emphasizing a journey from serenity to urgency.
