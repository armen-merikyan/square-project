Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between safety and exposure, showcasing a spiral pattern where colors collide and echo softly in the center. The palette merges earthy tones representing safety with pale, exposure-related hues, creating a gentle yet stark contrast. The main collision occurs in the central area, with a predominant shelter green framing open blue and pale skin tones, reflecting vulnerability and protection. Broken pixels add intentional disruption, embodying the push and pull of pressure and release inherent in the theme.
