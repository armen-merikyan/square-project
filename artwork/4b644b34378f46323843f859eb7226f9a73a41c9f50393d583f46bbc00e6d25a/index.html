Artist Statement

The composition captures the emotional tension between control and surrender, utilizing a palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold to create warmth and introspection. The overfilled corner contrasts with the quieter areas, symbolizing pressure versus release while mirroring the idea of containment. The jagged edges and interrupted patterns provoke a sense of chaos that complements the center's calm, embodying the seed's conceptual landscape effectively.
