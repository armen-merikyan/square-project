Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between love and betrayal through color contrast and asymmetry. The warm tones of pink and red represent love, while the stark grays and dark maroon evoke feelings of betrayal. The layout reflects a nearly mirrored image with deliberate misalignment of colors, creating a visual rift. The crosshatching pattern plays against the stripes, suggesting complexity in emotions, while a few broken pixels introduce imperfections, symbolizing the fragility of relationships. The negative space enhances the sense of emptiness and the unresolved nature of the feelings involved.
