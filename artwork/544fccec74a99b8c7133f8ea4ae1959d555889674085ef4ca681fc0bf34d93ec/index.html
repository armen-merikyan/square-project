Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between spark and ash — vitality juxtaposed against decay. The selected palette of vibrant coral, oranges, and soft grays represents this duality: the warmth of the spark countered by the cool neutrality of ash. The layout creates a spiral flow, where dynamic crosshatch patterns collide with halo-like quiet fields, asserting each emotional state while leaving space for reflection. This results in a piece where jagged edges meet smooth gradients, symbolizing resilience within fragility.
