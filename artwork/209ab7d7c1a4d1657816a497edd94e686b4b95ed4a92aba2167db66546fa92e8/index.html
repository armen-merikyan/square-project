Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional contrasts of strength and vulnerability, using a palette that embodies rich greens and soft cream to balance loud strength and quiet vulnerability. The color placements create a layered rise, mimicking hope and escape, with the deep olive and acid greens representing strength anchoring the top while softer hues evoke vulnerability at the bottom. The main contrast occurs at the center, creating a collision of dense weight against light echoes, suggesting movement from one state to another amidst the broken pixels symbolizing fractures in perspective.
