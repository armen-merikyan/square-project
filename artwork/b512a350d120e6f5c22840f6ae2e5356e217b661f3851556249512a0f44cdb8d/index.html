Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through a falling pixel pattern. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon conveys a sense of warmth against emotional disappointment and betrayal. The main contrast occurs at the edges where dark colors collide with lighter hues, creating a feeling of interruption and echoing the seed's essence. The pattern of dots reflects confusion while the central arrangement suggests clarity, symbolizing the conflict and yearning for resolution.
