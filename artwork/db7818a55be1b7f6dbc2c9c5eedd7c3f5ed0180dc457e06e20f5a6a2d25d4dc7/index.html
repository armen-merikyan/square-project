Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the piece navigates the tension between joy and grief, symbolizing a struggle between order and chaos. The selected palette of bright yellows and oranges represents joy and hope, while deep blues and muted purples evoke a sense of sorrow and melancholy. The left side, dominated by muted tones irregularly patterned, contrasts with a vibrant, patterned right side. This collision highlights the unresolved emotional pull, embodying a pulse of conflicting feelings through structured chaos. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, mirroring the complexities of personal emotions while leaving negative space to symbolize melancholic reflection.
