Artist Statement

The composition evokes the duality of strength and vulnerability through a jagged contrast of colors. The palette, defined by quiet greys and muted reds, represents the often invisible weight of emotional burdens. The dense patterns of crack and threshold clash diagonally, suggesting an ongoing struggle to balance exposure and safety, culminating in a central collision that challenges the viewer. The intentional broken pixels serve as reminders of imperfection amid the quest for stability, echoing the sentiment of shattered mirrors turning into reflective surfaces that reveal deeper truths.
