Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a delicate balance between loneliness and connection, utilizing warmer burnt orange and gold tones against cooler charcoal hues to enhance the sense of tension. The palette represents warmth juxtaposed with the weight of isolation, creating a space that feels both full and hollow at the same time. The placement of colors shows a left-weighted design, with a fiery orange at the center gradually transitioning to darker tones towards the edges, embodying a rhythmic flow of connection fighting against solitude. The broken pixels create an imperfection that hints at the fragility of memory, amplifying the theme of echoes within the composition.
