Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends longing and resolve, represented by contrasting colors and patterns. The chosen palette reflects safety (shelter green, warm brown) against exposure (cold white, open blue), highlighting tension in their collision. The pattern 'stairs' embodies a feeling of ascension yet unfinished paths, while the 'ripple' suggests fluidity and resolution. The main contrast is central, where warm brown meets open blue, creating a dynamic interplay of density and emptiness, echoing the seed's themes of unresolved emotional currents.
