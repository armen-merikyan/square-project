Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional complexities of calm and rage through stark contrasts. The chosen palette of deep blacks and vibrant reds juxtaposes an explosive fervor against a calm backdrop of pale blue and white. The main collision occurs diagonally, where chaotic red grid patterns disrupt soft blue horizontal lines, embodying a constant struggle between pressure and quiet. The pattern shifts as it moves outward from the center, honoring the seed's influence on rhythm and texture, and creating a space that feels both tense and hopeful.
