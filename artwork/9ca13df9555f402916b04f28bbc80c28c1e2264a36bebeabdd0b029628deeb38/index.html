Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of envy and gratitude, represented through contrasting colors of passion (red) and calm (gray). The zigzag pattern and broken dots convey a sense of disruption and movement, illustrating the struggle between weighty emotions and lightness. The use of negative space emphasizes the inner conflict, while the glowing edges highlight tensions at the intersections.
