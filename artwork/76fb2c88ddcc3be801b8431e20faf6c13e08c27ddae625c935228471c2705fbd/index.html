Artist Statement

The artwork expresses the duality of longing and resolve, achieved through bold contrasts and a mixture of textures. The palette of sepia, faded blue, and earth tones creates a sense of nostalgia and weight, with the broken pixels adding intentional disruption, symbolizing the struggle between damage and repair. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense, layered colors evoke a feeling of urgency against quieter surrounding fields, illustrating the conflict of emotions captured in the seed.
