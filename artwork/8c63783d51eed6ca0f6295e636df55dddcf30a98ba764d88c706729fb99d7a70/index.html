Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of control and surrender. The palette combines warm shadow tones with bursts of lamp gold, suggesting a warm intensity against cooler blues that evoke distance and introspection. The dominant dark colors represent a feeling of pressure, while the lighter tones in the center suggest release and softness. The collision occurs at the center where the warm golden hues interact with the cooler shades, reflecting the battle between containment and overflow. The insertion of broken pixels adds a layer of imperfections, illustrating struggles and scars in the flow of emotions.
