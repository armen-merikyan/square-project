Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy between vulnerability and strength, reflecting a central protected core disrupted by external pressures. The palette features a muted moss green and stone gray, representing stability and resilience, contrasted with the softness of bone white and rust, symbolizing exposed fragility. The arrangement creates visual tension, where the busy elements suggest conflict, while the quiet fields provide necessary relief. The core achieves a stable symmetry, with bursts in the surrounding area creating a palpable sense of pressure and disruption.
