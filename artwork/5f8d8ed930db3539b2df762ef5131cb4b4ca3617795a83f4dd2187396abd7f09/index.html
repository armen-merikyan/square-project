Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between defiance and submission, characterized by a strong yet vulnerable center. The chosen palette of red oxide, blackened plum, muted cream, iron gray, and small orange serves to emphasize contrast, with the dark hues contrasting the lighter one, suggesting layers of tension and dynamism. The main collision transpires around the center where brighter colors collide with muted fields, infusing energy within an almost subdued layout, signifying the conflict and interdependencies articulated in the seed. Randomly placed broken pixels symbolize the imperfections in the emotional narrative, adding depth and texture to the arrangement.
