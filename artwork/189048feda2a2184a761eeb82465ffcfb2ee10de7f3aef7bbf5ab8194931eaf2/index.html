Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between spark and ash, reflecting emotional states of hunger and restraint. The chosen palette represents safety against exposure, with the warm browns contrasting sharply with cool whites, echoing the internal struggle between vibrant urges and subdued realities. Main collision occurs in the top left corner with a lattice pattern of vibrant colors that starkly interrupts the surrounding grid-like structure, symbolizing the intense yet fleeting nature of memories and desires amidst the weight of restraint.
