Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional score of collision and contrast through a dynamic interplay of dark and light colors, representing pressure and quiet. The palette oscillates between deep blues and vibrant oranges, suggesting a tension between calmness and bursts of energy. The rows create a rhythmic pulse, with colors clashing and coalescing to evoke feelings of memory and unresolved emotion, capturing the essence of the seed's themes in a fluid yet structured manner.
