Artist Statement

This composition embodies the struggle between clarity and interference, utilizing the contrast between vibrant signal and subdued noise. The palette, featuring 'signal_green' and 'hot_magenta,' emphasizes sharp, emotional contrasts, while 'static_gray' and 'dead_black' provide a grounding backdrop. The rising layout represents hope, with colors climbing from muted base to bright peaks, illustrating a battle of clarity against chaos. Broken pixels are introduced to evoke disruptions akin to memory erosion amidst a loud background, echoing the emotional pull of the seed.
