Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional duality of loneliness and connection through a palette that evokes both hope and fear. The deep blacks contrast with soft golds to represent the interplay between isolation and warmth, while muted greens symbolize a yearning for growth. The sharp contrasts occur at the left side, where strong, dark pixels invade lighter tones, embodying the push-pull of connection and loneliness. The layout flows from top to bottom, creating a sense of tears or rain—suggesting both release and the weight of solitude—while broken pixels enhance the feeling of fragmented memories, making the viewer contemplate the subtle dynamics at play.
