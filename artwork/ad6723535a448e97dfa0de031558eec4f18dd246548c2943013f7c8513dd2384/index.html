Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through color and density. The palette, inspired by 'resilience_fragility,' features earthy tones (moss green, rust) alongside the stark contrast of bone white and stone gray. The arrangement creates a dense center that feels both protected yet exposed, with 'pulse_marks' and 'halo' patterns colliding visually. The open corners communicate a sense of safety amid vulnerability, while broken pixels scatter like distant constellations in the night sky, adding layers of imperfection. The main collision occurs at the center, reinforcing the juxtaposition of strength and vulnerability, invoking a narrative of resilience facing external pressures.
