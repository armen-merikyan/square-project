Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between relief and dread, conveying a struggle between light and dark through color juxtaposition. A palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green highlights the tension and contrast, with pale hues suggesting moments of relief amidst the overwhelming darkness. The layout's diagonal spread represents a corner invasion of feelings, while the mirror and stripes patterns create a dynamic echo of contrasting emotions. The use of broken pixels introduces imperfection, enhancing the overall narrative of collision and release.
