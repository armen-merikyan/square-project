Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between calm and chaos. The palette, featuring pale mint and soft sky, suggests tranquility while storm gray and near black introduce a sense of disturbance. The main collision occurs at the center, where halo-like patterns of restlessness (like fleeting memories) intertwine with the rain-like serenity representing peace. The sealed edges emphasize a longing for expansion but maintain control, mirroring the emotional struggle outlined in the seed.
