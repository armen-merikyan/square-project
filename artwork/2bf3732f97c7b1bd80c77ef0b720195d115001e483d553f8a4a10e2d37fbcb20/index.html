Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between loneliness and connection, represented by warm (sepia and tan) and cool (faded blue and dust gray) colors. The dominant left-heavy layout echoes the weight of solitude, while the interspersed checker pattern emphasizes broken connections. The main collision occurs across the center, where warm pixels clash against cooler backgrounds, symbolizing the struggle for connection amidst loneliness. Negative space is carefully integrated to allow the emotions to breathe, drawing on the theme of fragmented memory.
