Artist Statement

The emotional tone in this piece contrasts the turbulent rage with the serene calm, creating a dynamic dialogue between them. The chosen palette incorporates bold reds to evoke intensity and deep blues for tranquility. The colors clash where the red shards interrupt the calm blue spaces, particularly prominent in the center where confrontation erupts. This creates a sense of collision and tension against a backdrop that strives for containment, reflecting the seed's themes of machine emotion and structured chaos.
