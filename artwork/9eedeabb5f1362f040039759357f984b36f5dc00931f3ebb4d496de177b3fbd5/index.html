Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between vulnerability and strength through a delicate balance of colors. The palette transitions from the soft hues of off-white and faint peach to the deeper tones of dark navy and compressed red, illustrating the interplay between fragility and resilience. The placement creates a spiral pressure, drawing chaotic elements toward the exposed center while allowing for a sense of safe retreat. The collision of patterns suggests a graceful disarray where strength emerges from a foundation of vulnerability, with broken pixels subtly interrupting the flow to evoke a sense of imperfection.
