Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional conflict between restlessness and peace. The nuanced palette of soft greens and warm browns serves as a backdrop of tranquility, while sharper lines and contrasting darks suggest agitation and memory. The main contrast arises from a bold band across the center, clashing against the softer edges, illustrating an emotional push-pull. Color placement disrupts the symmetry, creating an intentional tension that reflects the restless nature of the seed's themes.
