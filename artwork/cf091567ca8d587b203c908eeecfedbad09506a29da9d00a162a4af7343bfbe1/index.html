Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between restlessness and peace, illustrated through a palette that contrasts warm fiery hues with chilling deeper tones. The bright yellows and oranges symbolize brief bursts of energy and memory, while the muted purples and deep blues create a calming, reflective space. The central collision occurs diagonally, disrupting the serene top layer with sparks of warm color, suggesting the constant fight between peace and restlessness. Overall, the arrangement creates a sense of movement that flows downwards, akin to falling ash, highlighting the push and pull of conflicting emotions.
