Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a juxtaposition of soft and hard edges. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue reflects a sense of memory and nostalgia, crucial to the emotional expression of the piece. The main collision occurs at the center, where radial strength patterns intermingle dramatically with pressure bands, creating an underlying narrative of conflict and connection. This abstract arrangement invites viewers to explore the complexities of juxtaposed emotions, leaving intentional negative space to enhance the sense of exposure within the sheltering framework.
