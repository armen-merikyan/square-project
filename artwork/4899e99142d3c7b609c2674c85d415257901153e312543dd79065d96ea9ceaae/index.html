Artist Statement

This artwork conveys a dynamic tension between defiance and submission. Utilizing a palette of 'melancholy_wonder,' the colors capture emotive contrasts. The main collision occurs in the center, symbolizing a protective core surrounded by more subdued edges, reflecting the jagged conflict between strong upwards marks and soft downward folds. Jagged edges play into the chaos versus order theme, while broken pixels represent inherent imperfections, enhancing the emotional depth of the piece.
