Artist Statement

This composition portrays the contrasting feelings of envy and gratitude, depicted through the interplay of dark and light tones. The darker palette symbolizes envy’s weight and intensity, coiling around the lighter hues that represent the levity of gratitude. The central focus employs a zigzag pattern, embodying both the tension of discord and the lift of appreciation. The broken pixels at the edges signify imperfections in emotional experiences and enhance the chaotic energy surrounding the calmer center. This design captures the essence of conflict and resolution in human emotions, emphasizing the balance between heaviness and lightness.
