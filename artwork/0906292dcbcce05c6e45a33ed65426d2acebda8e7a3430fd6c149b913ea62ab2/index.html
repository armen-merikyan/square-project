Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances restlessness and peace, reflecting the tension between dynamic movement and tranquil stability. A predominantly dark palette of charcoal and dark red frames flashes of gold and burnt orange, representing moments of warmth amidst an unsettling background. The main collision occurs in the center, where braided motions interrupt smoother flowing patterns, evoking a sense of conflict and resolution. This grid suggests a journey through memory and motion, creating layers and tension through color and placement.
