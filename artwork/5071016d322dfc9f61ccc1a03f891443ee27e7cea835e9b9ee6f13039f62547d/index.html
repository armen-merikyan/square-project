Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension of love and betrayal through the use of warm and cool colors juxtaposed against a backdrop of quiet and pressure. The palette, featuring shades of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon, reflects warmth intertwined with underlying sorrow. The layout emphasizes a nearly symmetrical form that is disrupted by carefully placed broken pixels, symbolizing emotional fractures. The main contrast lies in the rhythm of the color placements: bold reds and soft grays collide, creating a dynamic interplay of chaos and calm. This abstract arrangement captures the essence of the emotional collisions described in the seed, resulting in a visually striking narrative of tension and release.
