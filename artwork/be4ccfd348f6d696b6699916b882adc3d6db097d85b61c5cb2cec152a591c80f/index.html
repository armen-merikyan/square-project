Artist Statement

The composition captures the emotional pull between restlessness and peace, using a palette that juxtaposes calm moss green and bone white with the warm yet tumultuous rust and stone gray. The main contrast is found centrally, where pulse marks of rust collide with the gentle veil of green and white, suggesting the struggle of memory against motion. Broken pixels disrupt the smooth flow, embodying moments of interruption and echo throughout the artwork, with empty space allowing the tension to breathe.
