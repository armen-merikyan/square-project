Artist Statement

This abstract piece captures the tension between control and panic. The warm yellows and oranges evoke the heat of panic, while the muted purples and deep blues signify an undercurrent of control. The zigzag flow suggests continuous movement, with color clashes representing emotional collisions. The broken pixels add imperfection, hinting at chaos, while maintaining a significant amount of negative space to suggest moments of calm amidst turbulence.
