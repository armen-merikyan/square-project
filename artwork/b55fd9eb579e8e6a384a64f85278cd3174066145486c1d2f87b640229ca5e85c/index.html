Artist Statement

The emotional tone draws from the tension between brightness and muted remnants, using a palette representing resilience and fragility. The contrast between vibrant tones of small gold and rust against muted stone gray and bone white emphasizes a dynamic clash, representing the struggle between spark and ash. The primary collision occurs in the central diagonal, creating an upward flow that suggests hope amidst chaos. Broken pixels disrupt the patterns, symbolizing the erratic nature of memory and erasure.
