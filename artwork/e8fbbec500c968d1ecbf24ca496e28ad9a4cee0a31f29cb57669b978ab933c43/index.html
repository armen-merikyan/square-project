Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional push and pull of desire and restraint through a saturated color palette that evokes warmth and longing, contrasted by muted tones reflecting limitations. The central collision arises from a spiral pattern suggesting dynamic motion yet is framed by restrained borders that create an inner tension, emphasizing the struggle of desire against locked edges. This design allows for a narrative of conflict, drawing the eye towards the intensity at the center while maintaining a quiet edge.
