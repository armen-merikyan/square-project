Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between clarity and chaos, echoing the duality of 'signal vs noise'. The palette of navy, black, dim cyan, and a stark white reflects the lonely, yet hopeful, path of the signal cutting through the black static. The collision occurs prominently in the center, where brighter pixels disrupt darker areas, illuminating a readable rhythm amidst the chaos. The broken pixels create intentional disruptions, amplifying the failure and repair theme without overt literalism.
