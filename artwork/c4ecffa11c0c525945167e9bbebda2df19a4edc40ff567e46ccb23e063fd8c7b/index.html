Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between tenderness and rage, highlighted through dark and vibrant colors. The chosen palette of deep reds and blacks signifies rage, while pale blues and whites represent tenderness. The main contrast emerges around the center, where jagged rain-like patterns of red intersect with soft crosshatch textures in blue, visually narrating the push and pull of conflicting emotions. The deliberate placement and choice of 'broken pixels' evoke imperfections and emotional scars. The chaotic flow upwards contrasts with the calmer areas of quiet, suggesting moments of disruption within the broader emotional landscape.
