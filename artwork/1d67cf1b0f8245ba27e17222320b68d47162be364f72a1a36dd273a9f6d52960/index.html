Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the tension between envy and gratitude, represented through contrasting patterns of rising stair motifs against a backdrop of chaotic noise. The palette embodies muted sepias and faded blues to express depth and nostalgia, with broken pixels creating intentional imperfection. The main contrast unfolds in the center, echoing a collision of stable structure versus chaotic disarray. This interplay of colors suggests a journey of growth amidst inner turmoil, encapsulated by the visual tension of light against weight.
