Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between vulnerability and strength, with a focus on exposure contrasted by shelter. The palette features deep reds and muted creams to illustrate tension and connection, while blackened plum offers a heavy, protective edge against the lightness of soft hues. The collision happens in the central area where the patterns of strength and vulnerability intertwine, creating an echo of interaction. The arrangement of colors reflects the dual nature of human emotion, leading to a dynamic visual rhythm that feels both unsettling and grounding. The broken frame layout emphasizes gaps to symbolize exposure, inviting the viewer to consider what lies beyond while maintaining a presence of refuge.
