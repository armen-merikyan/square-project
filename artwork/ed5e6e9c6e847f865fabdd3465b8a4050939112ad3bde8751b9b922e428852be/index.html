Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the tension between restlessness and peace, highlighted through a conflicted color palette. The chosen colors (cosmic indigo, void black, star white, cold gold, deep teal) create both a quiet field and sharp accents. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where warm gold sparks interrupt cool indigo, suggesting a struggle between tranquility and chaos. The abstract patterns of stepwell for peace and flame for restlessness intermingle, embodying memory and motion through dynamic placements that echo the theme of tension.
