Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the structured control of the grid with the gentle surrender of the flowing patterns. The deep teal and cosmic indigo represent containment, while cold gold accents suggest tension in the softer static areas. The overlap in the center creates a collision of emotions, symbolizing the struggle between control and flow. The black edges contain and set apart the vibrant center, emphasizing the choices we face at the threshold of pressure versus release.
