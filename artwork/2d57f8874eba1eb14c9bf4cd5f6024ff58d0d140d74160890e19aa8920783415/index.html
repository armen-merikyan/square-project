Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The palette draws from soft, muted tones of sepia and dust gray, representing vulnerability, against deeper hues of faded blue that suggest strength. The main collision occurs in the center where pale colors gently intersect with darker shades, illustrating the duality of exposure and protection. The cascading pixel layout mimics falling raindrops, signifying the weight of emotions and the fragility of safety. Each broken pixel acts as an intentional disruption, highlighting the scars of the emotional landscape without defining a singular narrative.
