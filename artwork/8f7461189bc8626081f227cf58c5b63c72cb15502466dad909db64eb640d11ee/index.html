Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duality of restlessness and peace through contrasting color clusters and patterns. The muted palette evokes a sense of calm while the vibrant orange disrupts with sudden intensity, reflecting the tumult of memory and movement. The diagonal arrangements suggest conflict and fluidity, merging the soft 'flame' patterns of tranquility with the 'braid' of restless motion. The space is primarily occupied by dark tones, evoking quiet moments, while bursts of color interrupt, generating tension. The design embraces both stasis and movement, reflective of the seed's thematic elements.
