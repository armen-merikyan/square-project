Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a battle between tenderness and rage, using a palette of saturated reds to represent anger and muted blues for calmness. Colors clash and intertwine, with the deeper reds dominating to signify rage, juxtaposed against softer pastels that whisper of tenderness, creating a tension point in the center with pixel bleed patterns. The collage of colors embodies confrontation and resolution, hinting at a latent conflict of feelings shaped by the seed's themes of inner versus outer struggles.
