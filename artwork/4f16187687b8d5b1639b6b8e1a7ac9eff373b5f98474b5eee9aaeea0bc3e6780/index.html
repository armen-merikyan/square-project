Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between desire and restraint, with warm colors spilling over a quiet foundation. The chosen palette reflects 'hunger_restraint', using saturated hues for desire juxtaposed against muted tones to signify containment. The main contrast occurs at the center, where vibrant colors spiral outwards, interrupted by softer tones, creating a dynamic interplay of emotional currents. The diamond shape seed influences the organization of warm and cool tones, manifesting as a disrupted lattice, emphasizing the pressure and conflict intrinsic to longing and boundaries.
