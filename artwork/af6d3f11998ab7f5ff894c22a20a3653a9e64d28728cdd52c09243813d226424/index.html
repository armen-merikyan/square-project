Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between longing and resolve, using warm yellows and oranges to express urgency fused with muted purples and deep blues representing calm. The palette suggests a dynamic push and pull within the grid, where brighter colors collide at the center, embodying the struggle and repair of broken continuity. Broken pixels create disruptions that symbolize the imperfections of life, amplifying the emotional narrative woven into this abstract composition.
