Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity. The palette includes deep blacks and vibrant accents, with static gray suggesting control amidst chaos. The clean lines derived from certainty descend into a burst of chaotic hues that pierce through the swirling background, representing curiosity. The main collision occurs in the center with a clash of sharp colors amidst smoother transitions, illustrating the struggle between order and disruption while retaining a sense of negative space to allow for reflection.
