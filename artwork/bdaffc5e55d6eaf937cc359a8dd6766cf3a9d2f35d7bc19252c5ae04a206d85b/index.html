Artist Statement

The emotional tone centers on the tension between vulnerability and strength, represented through an interplay of dark and light colors. The palette utilizes navy and dim cyan to symbolize safety and exposure, while intensifying the contrast with stark black and white to accentuate the theme of broken edges and fragile centers. The main collision occurs in the upper left, where soft and aggressive patterns intersect, embodying the struggle of protection versus exposure. This grid encapsulates a narrative of conflicting feelings, showcasing how delicate layers coexist with dense structures.
