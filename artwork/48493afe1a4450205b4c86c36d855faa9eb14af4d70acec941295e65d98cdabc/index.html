Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between certainty and curiosity, illustrated by dense border colors receding towards lighter centers, akin to a rising signal battling noise. The used palette includes warm rose clay and distant lilac for emotional depth, with lamp gold accents acting as sparks amidst chaos. The main contrast occurs at the center, where increased color saturation collides with lighter edges, suggesting tension and resolution.
