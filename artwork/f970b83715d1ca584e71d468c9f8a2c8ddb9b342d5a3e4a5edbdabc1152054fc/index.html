Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve, using a palette that evokes both depth (deep teal, cosmic indigo) and warmth (cold gold, star white). The center features a collision of orderly ripples and stair-like patterns, symbolizing the conflict of resolution amidst lingering desires. Imperfections from broken pixels introduce a sense of disruption, emphasizing the fragility of the mirrored layout. The balance of negative space allows for quiet reflection, creating a feeling of unease interspersed with moments of clarity.
