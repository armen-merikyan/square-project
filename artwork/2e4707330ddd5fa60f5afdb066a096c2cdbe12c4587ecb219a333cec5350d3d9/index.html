Artist Statement

The composition juxtaposes the emotional tones of confusion and clarity, achieved through a split layout where the left side embodies confusion with darker hues and a chaotic pattern, while the right represents clarity with lighter, more structured placements. The palette of navy, black, white, and dim cyan evokes a lonely signal, indicating tension yet echoing a calm pulse in the arrangement. The main contrast occurs at the vertical center with the clash of foggy darkness against bright simplicity. Broken pixels in the dense, chaotic left enhance feelings of instability, while the right side offers relief, emphasizing the seed's exploration of symmetry and disruption.
