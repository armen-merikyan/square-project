Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the tension between clarity and distortion—signal representing the singular, coherent path amidst the chaotic noise. The palette of deep navy and black reflects darkness and interference, while the dim cyan and white serve as bright accents, illustrating moments of clarity. The main collision occurs in the center, where the structured grid of the signal pattern meets the quiet field of noise, creating a dynamic interplay that embodies conflict and resolution. This abstract composition captures the essence of the seed by portraying a struggle between structured pathways and overwhelming static, emphasizing the emotional weight of memory and the fragility of clarity.
