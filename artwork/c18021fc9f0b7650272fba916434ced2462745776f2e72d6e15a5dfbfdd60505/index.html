Artist Statement

The emotional tone celebrates the push and pull of confusion against clarity. The palette of warm yellow and orange juxtaposed with a deep blue and muted purple creates a dynamic tension that hints at both warmth and depth. The colors collide at the center and edges, illustrating broken symmetry amidst overlapping paths. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, further expressing the emotional turmoil of confusion. This composition reflects the theme of rising signals of clarity emerging from the chaos, with intentional spacing that embodies pause and negative space, creating a balance that suggests both hope and escape.
