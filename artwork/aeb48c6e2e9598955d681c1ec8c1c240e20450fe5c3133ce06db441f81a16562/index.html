Artist Statement

This abstract composition captures the emotional tension between relief and dread, using color to depict their nuanced interplay. The palette of muted purple and deep blue evokes a somber atmosphere, while vivid yellow and orange punctuate, representing flickers of joy and warm light against heavy shadows. The top-weighted layout mimics a surge of emotions, where light colors occupy the upper half, symbolizing relief, while darker tones dominate the bottom, suggesting dread. Strategic placements of contrasting colors create collisions that further enhance this emotional dialogue, inviting the viewer to engage with the underlying narrative of tension and calm. Imperfections are introduced through broken pixel placements, evoking a raw, unpolished quality to the experience.
