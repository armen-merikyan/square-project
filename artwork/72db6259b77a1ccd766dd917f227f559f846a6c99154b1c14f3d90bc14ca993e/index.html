Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay between defiance and submission, with bright colors representing the former and muted tones symbolizing the latter. The chosen palette combines vibrant yellows and oranges for defiance and deep blues and muted purples for submission, creating a sharp contrast. The layout emphasizes pressure in one corner, symbolizing an invasion, while the rest of the square remains more subdued, echoing the idea of retreat. Broken pixels add chaos to represent emotional disruptions, contrasting the orderly center with its radical edges. The swirling and angular patterns reflect a struggle between these opposing forces.
