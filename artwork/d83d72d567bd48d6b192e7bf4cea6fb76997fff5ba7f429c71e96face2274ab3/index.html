Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between vulnerability and strength, represented by a palette that juxtaposes deeper, stable colors with lighter, softer hues. The top half uses a radial pattern reflecting strength with dense, earthy colors that create a sense of solid ground—symbolizing resilience. The bottom half contrasts this with dotted patterns of muted pastels, depicting the fragility inherent in vulnerability. The collision occurs in the center, where these two patterns meet, creating a visual tension that echoes the interaction between resilience and frailty, while leaving negative spaces that allow the eye to rest amidst the chaos.
