Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the push-and-pull between joy and grief, with a palette that balances vibrant yellows and oranges with muted purples and deep blues. The central contrast occurs at the horizontal split, where warm colors radiate against the cooler, denser regions. The patterns of joy (radial) and grief (ring) create a flowing yet fractured narrative, echoing the heart’s oscillation between lightness and heaviness, while the broken pixels introduce an imperfection that suggests the chaos within. This abstract design captures a moment of emotional collision, embodying layered feelings.
