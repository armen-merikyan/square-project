Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between love and betrayal. The warm palette of pink and red contrasts against the cold gray and dark maroon, representing the conflict of emotions. The outer border, composed of darker tones, evokes a sense of betrayal, while the inner 4x4 core, using lighter shades, suggests love. The broken pixels introduce an imperfection that embodies the themes of disconnection and loneliness, enhancing the overall emotional impact. The pattern of cracks and rings reflects the fragmentation of trust, creating a visual wave of fluctuating emotions.
