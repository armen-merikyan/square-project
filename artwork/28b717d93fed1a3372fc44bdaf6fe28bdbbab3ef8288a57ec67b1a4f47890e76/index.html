Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between longing and resolve through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of soft warmth (powder pink, warm white) against stark severity (carbon black, steel gray, sharp crimson) illustrates the gradient of emotional conflict. The central collision occurs in the middle, where delicate broken dots intermingle with solid rings, suggesting a struggle between the airy moments of desire and the firm grounding of decision. The layout presents an argument between chaotic randomness and structured rings, allowing a dynamic visual flow that echoes the essence of the seed.
