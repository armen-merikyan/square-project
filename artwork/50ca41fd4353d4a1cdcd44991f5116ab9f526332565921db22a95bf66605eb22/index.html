Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a dynamic palette of vibrant and muted colors. The combination of electric blue and lime introduces brightness, suggesting a pulse of anxiety in contrast to the deep black, which represents the grounded feeling of resolution. A central collision is created where the pulsing lines of longing disrupt the steady patterns of resolve, embodying the struggle of a broken mirror reflecting both chaos and clarity. The quiet negative space enhances the emotional push and pull, while the scattered 'broken' pixels simulate unpredictability and disruption, ensuring the piece remains vibrantly abstract without literal interpretation.
