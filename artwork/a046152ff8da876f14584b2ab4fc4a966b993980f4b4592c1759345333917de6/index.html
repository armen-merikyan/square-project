Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between vulnerability and strength through a deliberate arrangement of color and pixel structure. The palette, titled 'ancient_fire', comprises burnt orange and dark red to suggest intensity and urgency, while charcoal provides grounding depth. Gold adds a hint of brightness, representing moments of hope amidst struggle. The layout, 'open_gate', symbolizes exposure, with a diagonal rise that invites an energetic flow from the bottom left to the top right, signifying strength rising against vulnerability. The main contrast occurs around the center, where the lighter and softer colors of vulnerability collide with the surrounding stronger, darker edges, emphasizing a struggle against confinement. Broken pixels introduce elements of disruption that speak to the imperfect nature of both emotional states, while maintaining negative space allows for reflection and clarity within the chaos.
