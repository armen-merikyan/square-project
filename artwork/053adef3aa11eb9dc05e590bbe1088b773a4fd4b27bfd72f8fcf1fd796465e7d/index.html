Artist Statement

This composition combines the emotional resonance of longing and resolve, represented through a structured division of left and right. The palette reflects solitude and tension, with dark navy and black symbolizing depth and longing, while bright dim cyan punctuates areas of resolve. The main contrast occurs at the center, where the patterns of longing's grid encounter the strength of resolve's ash-like fields. The seed concept of a star acts metaphorically, with points of light caught in a dichotomy, suggesting the interplay of aspiration against the weight of reality.
