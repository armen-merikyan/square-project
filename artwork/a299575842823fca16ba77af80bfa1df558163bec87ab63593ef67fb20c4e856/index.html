Artist Statement

This artwork plays with the emotional tensions of vulnerability and strength, where soft mint and sky colors suggest a gentle light of vulnerability, while darker tones like storm gray and near black provide a contrast of strength. The circular halo layout emphasizes an exposed center, creating a conflict where light struggles against shadow. Key contrast are in the border areas, where these emotions collide, suggesting a profound narrative of protection vs exposure. Broken pixels scatter throughout, embodying imperfection and the pulse of emotion as they interrupt the smooth flow.
