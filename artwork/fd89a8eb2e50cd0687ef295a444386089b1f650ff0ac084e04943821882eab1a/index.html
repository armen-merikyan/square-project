Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, expressed through a palette of soft mint and storm gray, representing the struggle between dense emotional clusters and quiet spaces. The arrangement emphasizes a central collision with broken pixel disruptions to mirror unresolved feelings and instability. The layout of eroded symmetry suggests a symmetric order that has faced chaos, intermingling the feelings of intimacy and distance. The collision occurs in the center where the rippled resolve meets the braided longing, creating a vivid contrast that anchors the artwork.
