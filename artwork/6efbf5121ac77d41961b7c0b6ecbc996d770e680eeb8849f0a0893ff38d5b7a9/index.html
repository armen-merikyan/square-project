Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a contrasting palette of clean white and ink black with moments of ice blue and warning orange. The use of ripple patterns symbolizes the yearning extending into a peaceful space, while the target patterns represent decisive clarity. The central collision occurs at both the center and the lower edge, where the stable resolve collides with the chaotic longing, portraying the complexity of emotions. The broken pixels introduce imperfections and disruptions that symbolize repair and damage, suggesting the narrative of mending a fractured connection.
