Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the contrasting themes of loneliness and connection, using the palette inspired by 'ancient fire' — burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold. The main contrast occurs between deep, absorbing hues suggesting isolation and vibrant accents that evoke fleeting connections. The layout emphasizes an off-center burst, symbolizing a hidden pulse of connection amidst an expansive negative space. Patterns of split screens and crosshatches create a dialogue of pressure versus release, inviting the viewer to traverse between isolation and warmth.
