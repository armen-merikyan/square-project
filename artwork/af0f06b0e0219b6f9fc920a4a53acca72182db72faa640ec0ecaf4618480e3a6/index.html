Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional interplay between vulnerability and strength, primarily displaying a sense of unshielded light against rooted stability. The chosen palette, featuring shelter green and pale skin, invokes a feeling of safety intersected with exposure, while warm brown introduces warmth and a solid foundation. At the center, bold contrasts create a collision of colors that depict tension, with darker shades of blue and muted whites surrounding a paler center. Broken pixels interrupt the otherwise structured flow, symbolizing the imperfections and disruptions inherent in the themes of strength and vulnerability. Overall, the piece conveys a story of fluctuating emotions and the delicate balance between exposure and protection.
