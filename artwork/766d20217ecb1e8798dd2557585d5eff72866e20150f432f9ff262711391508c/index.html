Artist Statement

This piece navigates the tension between vulnerability and strength, utilizing a palette of signal green and electric blue to represent a dynamic interaction of emotions. The center is a pale area disrupted by stark contrasts of dead black and hot magenta, symbolizing exposure within a protective, yet eroding, boundary of static gray and darker tones. The rows alternate between dense patterns and quiet spaces, suggesting pressure against the frame of vulnerability. The collision occurs centrally, inviting a visual exploration of the contrasts present.
