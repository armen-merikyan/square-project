Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the turbulence of love and betrayal, expressed through a contrasting palette of warm pinks and cool grays. The chaos of betrayal emerges in irregular pixel arrangements, while the warmth of love attempts to maintain symmetry but is disrupted by intentional 'broken pixels'. This creates a visual narrative of conflict, where parts of the 'heart' are represented through vibrant warm colors that collide with cooler hues, suggesting a struggle between opposing emotions. The diagonal patterns evoke a sense of rising signals interrupted by cracks, reflecting tension within harmony.
