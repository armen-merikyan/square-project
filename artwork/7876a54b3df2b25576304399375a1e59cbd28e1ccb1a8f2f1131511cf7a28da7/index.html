Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through an uneven, fractured symmetry. The 'broken_memory' palette of sepia, tan, dust_gray, and faded_blue establishes a soft yet complex environment, where warmth gently meets cooler hues. The dense central vertical column symbolizes clarity, while the surrounding disrupted patterns illustrate confusion, evoking a sense of pulse and disruption in the flow from top to bottom. The textured arrangements, resembling falling pixels, together with intentional broken pixels, create a sense of movement and layered depth, echoing emotional resonance without literal representation.
