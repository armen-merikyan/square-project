Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between vulnerability and strength, represented through the interplay of smooth flowing colors and sharp contrast. The selected palette of deep hues like dead black and vibrant hot magenta symbolizes the struggle between a strong foundation and the fragility that surrounds it. The main contrast occurs at the center, where light breaks through darker pixels, suggesting an emergence from pressure. This arrangement encapsulates the seed's themes of conflict and balance, translating the narrative into an abstract pattern using flowing and eroded structural motifs.
