Artist Statement

This composition conveys the tension between vulnerability and strength through a careful selection of colors and patterns. The muted tones of lavender gray and soft cyan represent vulnerability, while the deeper rain blue and muted plum signify strength, creating an emotional push-pull. The maze pattern represents vulnerability's presence, weaving through the dominant flood of strength that pushes from the center to edges. The layout reflects a broken mirror symmetry, with subtle disruptions in color rhythm, accentuating the feeling of discomfort amidst stability. The main contrast forms a central collision, where lighter tones blur against darker shades, suggesting a complex emotional interplay.
