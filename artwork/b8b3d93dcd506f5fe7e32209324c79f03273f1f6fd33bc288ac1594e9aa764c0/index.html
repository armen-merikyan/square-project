Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the struggle between loneliness and connection through contrasting colors and spatial arrangements. The dark tones symbolize isolation (black and dark violet), while the soft gold and pale green represent fragile connections. The juxtaposition of dense areas against significant negative space creates an emotional tension, echoing the themes of hidden vulnerabilities versus exposed fragility. The layout suggests a mirrored existence, while broken pixel imperfections reflect the imperfections of human connection. The overall composition flows inward, drawing attention to the center, symbolizing the burst of emotion that can emerge from loneliness.
