Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between tenderness and rage. The palette combines stark reds to symbolize rage and muted blues for tenderness, creating a visual conflict. The collision occurs predominantly in the center where red pulse marks interrupt the soft horizontal bands of blue, serving as metaphors for offensive emotions against calming forces. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, reflecting the complexity of conflicting feelings and suggest a journey through various emotional landscapes.
