Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between joy and grief through a zigzag pattern that alternates between bright and muted tones, creating a dynamic flow. The palette combines warm yellows and oranges to express joy, contrasted with deep blues and muted purples to evoke grief. The interplay creates a collision of emotions, where brighter colors rise amidst darker fields, suggesting an unresolved balance. The broken pixels introduce imperfections, symbolizing the rawness of emotional experiences.
