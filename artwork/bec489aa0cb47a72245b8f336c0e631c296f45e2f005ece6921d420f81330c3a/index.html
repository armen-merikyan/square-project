Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional contrast between longing and resolve through a jagged flow that intertwines moments of hope and despair. The palette reflects gratitude and envy—ripe greens suggest growth and energy, while deep olives represent grounding barriers. The collision occurs centrally, where the structured resolve (crosshatch) clashes with the fluidity of yearning (thread), creating a charged space that speaks to unresolved emotional currents. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the narrative of struggle between clarity and confusion.
