Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the contrasting feelings of love and betrayal, represented through a palette of warm reds and pinks juxtaposed with darker hues of gray and maroon to symbolize intensity and depth. The upper half of the artwork portrays love's warmth through a framed pattern, while the lower half embodies the chaos of betrayal with targeted disruptions and chaotic placements. The contrast arises from the interruptive collisions of bright colors against muted backgrounds, suggesting emotional conflict. The arrangement creates a sense of spiraling tension, representative of the push and pull between affection and distrust, and echoes the themes of rising signals versus diagonal falls throughout the layout.
