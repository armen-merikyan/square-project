Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional struggles of tension and resolution. The color palette of faded blues and sepia tones reflects nostalgia and memory, creating an intimate and quiet atmosphere. The contrast between darker and lighter sections provides a visual dialogue between weight and illumination. The pattern's flow, moving from center outward, simulates a release of emotions, while the broken pixels represent imperfections in memories – echoes of past collisions felt in tandem, invoking a sense of rhythm amid chaos.
