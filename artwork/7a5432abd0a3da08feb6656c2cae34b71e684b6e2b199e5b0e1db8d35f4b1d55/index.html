Artist Statement

The emotional tone interweaves panic and control, creating visual tension. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold plays into the duality of warmth versus weight. The outer frame embodies a structured, controlled feel, while the inner core contrasts with chaotic checker patterns, suggesting conflict and disruption. Broken pixels hint at imperfection, enhancing the overall concept of strife. This arrangement reflects the jagged push and pull of emotions captured within a confined space, conveying unresolved energy.
