Artist Statement

This artwork captures the duality of vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors and patterns. The warm tones of yellow and orange hint at strength, while the cooler deep blue and muted purple suggest vulnerability. The central pixels, representing a quiet field, dominate the composition, embodying a sense of rooted strength while the surrounding lighter pixels create a mirror-like effect of exposure. This interplay of color and pattern suggests a gradual shift between calm and pressure, mirroring the emotional tension implied by the seed. The diagonal layout reflects the conflict of these contrasting emotions, as they simultaneously collide and coexist.
