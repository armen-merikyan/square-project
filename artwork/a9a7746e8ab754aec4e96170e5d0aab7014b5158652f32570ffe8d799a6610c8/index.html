Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a dance between longing and resolve, encapsulated in the palette. The cool tones of 'ice_blue' and 'violet_gray' evoke a sense of distant yearning, while the 'warning_orange' and 'clean_white' punctuate moments of clarity and decision. The pattern incorporates broken pixels to symbolize repair amidst damage, creating intentional disruptions—like scars—within the structure. The layout follows a quiet channel, allowing negative space to coexist with dense clusters, signifying the tension between emptiness and fullness. The main collision occurs centrally, merging fractured flows that mirror the inherent conflict and harmony in the seed's themes.
