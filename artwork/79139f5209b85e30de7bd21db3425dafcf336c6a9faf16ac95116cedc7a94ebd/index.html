Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between 'relief' and 'dread' through a dynamic arrangement of colors. The color palette, featuring soft pastels of 'pale mint' and 'soft sky' juxtaposed against the deep 'storm gray' and 'near black', symbolizes the emotional struggle. The main collision occurs at the bottom left corner, where dense dark colors reflect dread, breaking into spaces of lighter tones that suggest an escape to relief. The patterns of 'mirror' and 'barcode' illustrate a contrast of order against chaos, woven throughout the grid. Broken pixels create a sense of imperfection and disruption, reinforcing the theme of unresolved emotional tension.
