Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between rage and calm through a split vertical layout, where the left side showcases a calm checker pattern using pale blue and white, evoking tranquility and space. The right side contrasts sharply, employing a radial pattern of deep reds and blacks to illustrate explosive emotions and density. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, suggesting the cracks in calmness that rage can create. This combination captures the conflict between serenity and turmoil, making the experience both reflective and dynamic.
