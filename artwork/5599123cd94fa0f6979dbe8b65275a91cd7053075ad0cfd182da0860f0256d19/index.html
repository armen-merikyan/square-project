Artist Statement

This composition embodies the struggle between quiet and pressure, using a palette that communicates these emotional tensions. The soft hues of off-white and faint peach reside in the corners, representing quietude. In contrast, the compressed reds and dark navy dominate the center, illustrating the build-up of pressure. The pattern of echo merges into flame, creating dynamic disruptions at the diagonal, symbolizing the counterflow and interruption mentioned in the seed. The overall clash between density and space resonates with the balance of noise and calm, where clarity frequently meets confusion.
