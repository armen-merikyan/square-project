Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission through the interplay of sharp contrasts and soft gradients. The palette of 'melancholy wonder' instills a subdued yet poignant atmosphere, with the brighter rain blue symbolizing defiance cutting through the quieter, muted plum that represents submission. The layout emphasizes a hollow center surrounded by dynamic crosshatched and striped patterns, suggesting a push and pull between contrasting forces. The collision occurs centrally, where the vibrant colors intersect with the muted surroundings, creating a narrative of tension and release.
