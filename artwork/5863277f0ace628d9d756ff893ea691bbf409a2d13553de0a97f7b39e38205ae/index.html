Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the frailty of vulnerability against the fortitude of strength, creating a dynamic interplay. The chosen palette resonates with intensity and calm: deep blacks emphasize strength, while pale blues evoke vulnerability. The central pixels are more saturated, suggesting weight, contrasted by lighter, scattered edges reminiscent of fragility. The main collision occurs towards the center with darker hues pressed against lighter edges, encapsulating the tension of protection versus exposure, culminating in an unresolved push and pull throughout the grid.
