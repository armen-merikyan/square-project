Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional dichotomy of joy and grief, leveraging a palette of deep blue and muted purple to represent melancholy, contrasted against bright yellow and orange signifying joy. The arrangement flows vertically and horizontally, with dense blocks of colors suggesting grief's weight and lighter spaces invoking fleeting moments of joy. The patterns are chaotic yet structured, mimicking the inherent conflict between these emotions, creating a resonance of layered experiences. The thin outlines add complexity without overt symbolism, while broken pixels introduce elements of imperfection, echoing the fractured nature of our feelings.
