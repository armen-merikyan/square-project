Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between panic and control, represented by an uneven arrangement of contrasting colors. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue captures the nostalgic yet conflicting feelings of grief and joy. The primary contrast occurs at the edges, where darker colors collide with lighter ones, embodying the chaos against the structure. The arrangement of colors creates a zigzag pattern that defines both pressure and relief, evoking the restless push and pull of emotions inherent in the seed.
