Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a juxtaposition of joy and grief, represented through a palette that combines warm yellows and oranges symbolizing joy with deep blues and muted purples indicating grief. The contrasting colors create a dynamic tension throughout the piece: the top half bursts with brightness, while the bottom dwells in darker tonalities, embodying hope versus despair. The spiral counterclockwise flow evokes a sense of motion while maintaining asymmetry; warmer colors erupt amidst the darker fields, echoing the collision of emotions. The overall pattern suggests a wave, with colors rising and falling, creating visual rhythm and depth.
