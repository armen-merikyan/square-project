Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, contrasting an exposed pale center with a darkened, protective frame. The soft glow of muted oranges and yellows signifies a fragile light amidst shadows, while deep blues and purples create the stability of the surrounding edges. The collisions happen centrally where clusters of light intertwine with the shadows, suggesting emotional complexity. The palette reflects an interplay of joy and grief, echoing themes of intimate exposure, while carefully maintaining substantial negative space even amidst strong contrasts.
