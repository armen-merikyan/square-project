Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dualities of love and betrayal, exploring the boundaries of intimacy versus isolation. The palette utilizes warm colors like pink and dark maroon to represent love, contrasted with cooler gray tones symbolizing betrayal. The arrangement creates a sense of pressure emanating from the edges towards a softer, empty center, establishing a flow that suggests confinement within an open space. The checker pattern suggests unity and warmth, interrupted by 'broken pixels' that inject a sense of chaos and loss, echoing themes of distortion and memories of connection.
