Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional dichotomy of loneliness and connection through a stark contrast of colors and patterns. The use of a dark maroon and gray palette represents the heaviness of isolation, while vibrant pinks and reds hint at the yearning for connection. The arrangement features dense areas of dark colors interrupted by bursts of brighter hues, illustrating the tension between feeling hidden and the desire to be exposed. The 'rising signal' layout suggests growth amidst despair, aligning with the notion of hope.
