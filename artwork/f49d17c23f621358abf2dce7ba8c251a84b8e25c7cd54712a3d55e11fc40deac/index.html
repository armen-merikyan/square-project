Artist Statement

The artwork captures the tension between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise), expressed through the use of a vibrant green that represents the dominant signal contrasted against disruptive magenta and static gray fields. The broken pixels introduce intentional flaws, mirroring memories' imperfections and enhancing the abstract clash of order and disorder. The central area embodies a pulsating rhythm, surrounded by fragmented static that creates a feeling of unease, echoing the emotional weight of the seed’s themes.
