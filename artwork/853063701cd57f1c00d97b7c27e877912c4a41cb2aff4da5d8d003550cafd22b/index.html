Artist Statement

This piece conveys the emotional tension between 'rage' and 'calm'. The palette combines intense reds with cool blues, symbolizing the battle between overheating emotions and serene stillness. The main collision occurs in the center, where hot red jagged patterns disrupt the calmness of the pale blue and black radial shapes. This juxtaposition showcases the conflict between chaotic sparks and quiet spaces, making for a visually dynamic and emotionally charged composition.
