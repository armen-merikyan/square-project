Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a carefully selected palette and arrangement. The colors evoke serenity (pale aqua and mist gray) contrasting with urgent tones (alarm red and ink blue). The collision occurs near the center, where these contrasting motifs intersect, symbolizing the struggle between aspiration and stability. The use of broken pixels signifies interruptions in this emotional journey while maintaining an intentional sense of negative space, reflecting the incomplete nature of longing. The layout, inspired by an open gate, suggests an invitation to move through the emotional landscape, revealing layers of both danger and calmness, resonating beautifully with the seed's thematic depth.
