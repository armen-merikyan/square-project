Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between restlessness and peace through a juxtaposition of muted tones and vibrant accents. The palette utilizes warm oranges and cool blues, with shades like hot coral and hollow blue representing chaotic energy and serene calm, respectively. The main collision occurs off-center with dense clusters of coral and blood orange competing against a quiet background of charcoal and dust pink, creating a visual dynamic of struggle and tranquility. This reflects the seed's themes of motion and memory through their abstract representation in tension and resolution.
