Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a juxtaposition of clarity and confusion, using contrasting colors to evoke the struggle between order and chaos. The muted sepia and tan evoke a sense of memory, while the dust gray and faded blue introduce a tension of confusion through overlapping patterns. The placement centers around a core of darker colors, radiating outward with lighter shades, illustrating a flow from the dense to the hollow. The broken pixels disrupt uniformity, representing the imperfection in the clarity of thought.
