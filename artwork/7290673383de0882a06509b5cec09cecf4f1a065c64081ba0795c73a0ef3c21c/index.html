Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between pressure and quietude. The palette combines dark navy and compressed red to reflect the weighted pressure, while off-white and faint peach suggest areas of stillness and clarity. The dominant dark tones provide a heavy center, creating a visual push, while scattered lighter colors around it evoke a gentle release. The layout of 'falling_pixels' instills a sense of descending tension, with clusters of colors creating interruptions along the diagonal flow, embodying the seed's themes of counterflow and shattering against the tide.
