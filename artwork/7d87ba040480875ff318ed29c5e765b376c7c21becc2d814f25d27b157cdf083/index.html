Artist Statement

This composition conveys the tension between pressure and quietness through contrasting color densities. The palette includes muted tones of 'dust_gray' and 'off_white,' signifying calmness and spaciousness, while 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy' introduce density and pressure. The main collision occurs diagonally across the grid, where densely packed dark colors interrupt the lighter spaces, evoking a narrative of conflict and resolution. The layout focuses on a bright cluster in one corner, depicting clarity amid confusion, aligning with the seed's themes without literal representation.
