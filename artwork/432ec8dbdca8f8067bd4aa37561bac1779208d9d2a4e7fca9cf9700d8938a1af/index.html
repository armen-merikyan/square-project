Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the conflict between defiance and submission, using a palette of electric blue, magenta, and black to symbolize tension and contrast. The pattern features a near-mirrored layout with subtle disruptions, creating a sense of unease. Collisions occur where vibrant colors break through darker backgrounds, representing love and betrayal in an unresolved emotional state. Negative space reinforces the struggle between openness and closure, reflecting the falling pixels theme. This composition captures the essence of duality and emotional push-pull.
