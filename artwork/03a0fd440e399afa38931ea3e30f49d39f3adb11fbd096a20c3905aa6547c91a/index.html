Artist Statement

This composition conveys the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through the use of a muted palette that embodies melancholy, with deep blues and soft grays balancing against stark whites. The center of the square shows a burst of variance (reflecting strength) while the edges fade gently (capturing vulnerability). The layered patterns of mirrored and spark-like textures create a visual representation of collision while maintaining a sense of flow towards an unresolved emotional state, echoing the seed's themes of pressure and quiet desire.
