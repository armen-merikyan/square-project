Artist Statement

This composition embodies a dialogue between softness and severity, emphasizing the interplay of pressure and release. The palette features soft, powdery hues to represent gentleness and warmth, while deep blacks and sharp crimson introduce moments of stark severity. The pattern alternates between delicate dots symbolizing softness and sharp flame-like outlines representing severity, colliding primarily in the center, creating a vibrant tension that draws the eye. The negative space is deliberate, creating a sense of calm amidst the chaos, echoing feelings of guilt and forgiveness as contrasting pulsing marks emerge and dissolve. This arrangement invites contemplation, echoing the complexity of emotional experiences.
