Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and chaos, captured through a palette emphasizing both electric vibrancy and muted stress. The vibrant greens and blues symbolize the 'signal'—clarity navigating through soundwaves, while blacks and grays represent 'noise'—the distortions and interruptions. The main collision occurs in the center where vibrant threads of green are intermittently disrupted by patches of static gray, symbolizing the fractured, yet resilient pulse of the signal amidst overwhelming noise. Utilizing the 'split_horizontal' layout, the top half of the square communicates the signal's focused clarity, contrasted against the chaotic noise below, creating a compelling and immersive engagement with the viewer.
