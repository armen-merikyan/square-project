Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a battle between longing and resolve, captured through a palette of warm and muted tones. Hot coral and blood orange provide urgency, while hollow blue and charcoal introduce a calm depth. The contrast arises in the center, where warm colors collide with the cooler tones, creating a stark dichotomy reflective of the seed's themes of repair and damage. The split-screen pattern suggests a stark division yet also an intermingling, emphasizing the thread of unresolved emotions like a pulse against a backdrop of quiet space.
