Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes awe and terror, depicting a struggle between stability and chaos. The palette, comprising electric blue, lime, black, and magenta, reflects the vibrancy of awe contrasted against the somberness of terror. The main contrast occurs between deep black and the colorful highlights representing emotional bursts. The layout creates an echo of rising and falling, with the left side uplifted by electric blues while the right is grounded with darker tones. This interplay balances chaos against a stable center, encapsulating themes of memory and transient moments.
