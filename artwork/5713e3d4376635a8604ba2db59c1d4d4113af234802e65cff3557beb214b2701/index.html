Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of tenderness and rage through a contrasting palette of saturated red and muted blues. The intensity of red pixel floods represents rage, creating a dense core, while the soft blue checker pattern signifies tenderness, interweaving gently around the edges. The main collision happens centrally, where these two emotional patterns disrupt and intermingle. This embodies a climb from chaos to calm, reflecting the abstract nature of memory disrupted by vibrant emotion.
