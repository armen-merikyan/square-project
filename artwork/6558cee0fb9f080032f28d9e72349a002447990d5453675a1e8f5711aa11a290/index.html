Artist Statement

This composition captures the push and pull between longing and resolve, utilizing contrasting colors to express tension. The palette is anchored by burnt orange and dark red, representing passion and depth, while accents of gold symbolize clarity and illumination amidst chaos. The structured flow allows visual pathways to emerge, highlighted by the placement of denser colors against quieter spaces, creating a sense of layered intimacy and expansiveness. The collision mainly occurs in the center of the grid, where darker tones meet brighter accents, embodying the unresolved nature of desires clashing with determinations.
