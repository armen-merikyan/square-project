Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between calmness and pressure. The palette features muted colors like off-white and dust gray for a quiet atmosphere, contrasted by the deeper compressed red and dark navy, suggesting weight and intensity. The dense central island is created with overlapping pressure patterns, creating a visual collision in the center. The outer quieter pixels form a soft frame, emphasizing the dynamic contrast within. The color choices echo the push and pull of the seed's duality, where clarity and confusion intermingle.
