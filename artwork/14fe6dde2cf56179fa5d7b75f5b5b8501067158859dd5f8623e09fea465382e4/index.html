Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional turmoil of joy and grief through an interplay of colors and patterns. A palette of deep blue, muted purple, warm yellow, and vibrant orange reflects the duality of emotions. The center exhibits brighter colors that fade into darker tones at the edges, suggesting a transition from joy to grief. The arrangement features clusters echoing the pressure of grief, contrasted by the lighter, more expansive areas that represent fleeting joy. The broken pixels create intentional imperfections reminiscent of disrupted memories, enhancing the complexity of emotional experiences and making space for interpretation.
