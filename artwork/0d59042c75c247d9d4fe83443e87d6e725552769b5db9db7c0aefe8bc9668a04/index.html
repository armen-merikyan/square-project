Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between clarity and disruption. The palette of deep black, electric blue, and hot magenta conveys the vibrant chaos of noise against the calm of signal. The main collision occurs at the center, where the signal (green and blue tones) pulses upwards, interrupted by chaotic magenta and gray ripples, symbolizing interference. This mirrors the seed's themes of struggle and fragmentation, creating a visual tension between order and chaos.
