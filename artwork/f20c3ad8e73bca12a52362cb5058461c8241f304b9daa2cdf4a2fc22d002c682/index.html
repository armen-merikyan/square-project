Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between joy and grief, using a palette of vibrant yellows and oranges representing joy, contrasted with muted purples and deep blues symbolizing grief. The visual layout is split, with the upper half introducing a sense of order through warm colors adopting an expanding pattern, while the lower half disrupts this harmony with cool colors in flame-like, fragmented forms. This duality creates a collision of emotion, charge, and rhythm, mirroring the push and pull of dense feelings encapsulated in the seed's narrative.
