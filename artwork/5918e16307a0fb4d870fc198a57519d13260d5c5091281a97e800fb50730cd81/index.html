Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The color palette of deep navy and dim cyan suggests a somber atmosphere, capturing feelings of isolation while also whispering hints of connection through soft, muted tones. The arrangement places weight in the upper rows, symbolizing a burden of solitude, while introducing disrupted patterns in the lower half to denote the potential for bonds and interaction. Main contrasts arise through the shift from dark to light shades, where the upper density represents static loneliness and the lower, more open patterns suggest the emerging possibility of connection.
