Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tug-of-war between longing and resolve. The palette of shelter_green, warm_brown, pale_skin, cold_white, and open_blue embodies a balance of warmth and coolness, suggesting an intricate interplay of hope and hesitation. The central dense island of deeper colors is surrounded by quieter fields, presenting a clear contrast that emphasizes collision. The patterns of 'teeth' and 'veil' imply interrupted connection and a protective layer, mirroring the idea of a 'mirror_broken.' Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, portraying the scars of damage as well as the thread of repair, resonating with the tension of the seed's themes.
