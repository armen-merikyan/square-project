Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between dread and relief. The selected palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold evokes a sense of ancient fire, symbolizing warmth amidst darkness. The arrangement emphasizes collision through a predominant dark background punctuated by bright, checker-like segments representing fleeting moments of relief. The layout suggests a top-to-bottom flow, mirroring the descent of weighty thoughts, while providing areas of negative space to evoke calmness amidst pressure.
