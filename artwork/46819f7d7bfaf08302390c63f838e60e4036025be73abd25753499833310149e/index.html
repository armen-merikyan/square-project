Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the persistent struggle between defined clarity (signal) and the chaotic murmur of noise. The palette of dim cyan, navy, and black creates an atmosphere of isolation, while the splashes of white act as fleeting moments of brightness, representing clarity amidst confusion. The primary collision occurs in the center, where an interplay of static (patchwork) and clear accents (static) cause disruption within a tempered, structured frame. The abstract pattern reflects both the chaos of interference and the throbbing clarity of a consistent pulse, emulating the persistent conflict described in the seed.
