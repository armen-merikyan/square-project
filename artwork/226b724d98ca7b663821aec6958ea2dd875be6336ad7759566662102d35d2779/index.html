Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between trust and suspicion through a contrasting palette and asymmetrical patterns. The dark colors represent suspicion, creating a pressure field, while the warm hues advocate for trust, forming a vibrant cluster. The collision occurs in the center where the warm and cool colors meet, symbolizing the delicate balance of emotions. The use of stairs as a pattern evokes a fractured yet ascending nature of resilience, while broken pixels add to the chaos of mistrust.
