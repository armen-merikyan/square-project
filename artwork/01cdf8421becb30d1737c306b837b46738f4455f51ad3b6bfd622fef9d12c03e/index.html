Artist Statement

The emotional tone emphasizes a tension between the weight of pressure and the stillness of quiet. The chosen palette—off white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach—serves to illustrate the deep contrast between dense, darker elements on the left and lighter, more open spaces on the right. The main collision occurs centrally, as compact clusters of deep colors confront wider areas filled with soft, muted tones, embodying the ebb and flow of stress and calm. This landscape of emotions embodies the seed's themes through an abstract arrangement that captures the essence of symmetry breaking and pressure release within a confined space.
