Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tension between love and betrayal through a palette of warm pinks and reds contrasted against deep maroons and grays. The layout flows from left to right, suggesting a journey from chaos to order, mirroring love's warmth against betrayal's cold edge. The color placement creates a sense of movement, accented by broken pixels that evoke the imperfections in both emotions. The collision of dense clusters of warm tones against sparse, cooler areas enhances the feeling of conflict, illustrating the complex relationship captured in the seed.
