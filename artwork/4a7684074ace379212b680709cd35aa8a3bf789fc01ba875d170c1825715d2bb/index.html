Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends joy and grief, creating tension through contrasting colors. The palette incorporates warm yellows and oranges for joy, offset by deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The left side begins with a structured grid pattern depicting grief, dissolving into a more chaotic dotted pattern of joy to reflect the emotional collision. Imperfect alignment illustrates the ‘broken pixels’ concept, emphasizing the disarray amidst an overarching balance.
