Artist Statement

This square engages with the emotional tension between quietude and pressure through a contrasting palette of muted and vibrant colors. The use of dark navy (#1A1D24) forms the dense cluster on the left, representing pressure and crowd, while soft peach (#F5C0A0) accentuates the quieter side on the right, suggesting an airiness that allows for escape. The broken pixels enhance the texture, mirroring emotional disruptions. The diagonal flow augments the contrast, allowing the eye to experience the tension between direction and stillness.
