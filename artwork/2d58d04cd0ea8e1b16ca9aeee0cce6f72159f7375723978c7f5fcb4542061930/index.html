Artist Statement

The emotional tone is one of tension and balance, presenting the struggle between defiance and submission. The selected palette of clean white and ink black prominently contrasts purity and depth, creating a striking visual narrative. The collision occurs in the center, where the bright white disrupts the encroaching darkness, reflecting the battle of light and dark. The layout creates an uneven border, symbolizing chaos within a structured form, mirroring the seed's thematic essence of negotiation between order and disorder.
