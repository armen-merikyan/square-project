Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the conflict between softness and severity, represented by the palette's contrast between warm whites and cool blacks. The palette reflects warmth and intimacy against stark boundaries. The main collision occurs in the center, where pressure bands of soft colors meet sharp, dark edges, creating a dynamic tension. This layout suggests a spiral pull against hard constraints, echoing the seed's themes of conflict and resolution through an abstract narrative.
