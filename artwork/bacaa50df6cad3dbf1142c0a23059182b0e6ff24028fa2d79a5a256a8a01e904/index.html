Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The juxtaposition of dense areas of black (#000000) representing confusion clashes against sharp boundaries defined by electric blue (#00FFFF) and magenta (#FF00FF), evoking a sense of clarity. Broken pixels are introduced along the edges, enhancing the feeling of an imperfect symmetry that resonates with the idea of fractured clarity. The flow spirals outward, suggesting a movement from chaos at the center to moments of resolution at the edges, creating a dynamic visual rhythm.
