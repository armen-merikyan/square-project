Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a journey from longing to resolve, captured in a color palette of soft, melancholy hues. The primary colors - muted plum and soft cyan - embody the dichotomy of yearning and strength, while the serene lavender gray serves as a quiet space amidst the pulse of darker tones. The main collision is intended to occur at the center where static and channel patterns intermingle, creating a vibrant tension that echoes the struggle between repair and damage. The layout embodies a corner invasion, allowing longing to spread diagonally, encroaching upon areas of resolve, punctuating the interplay of emotion with broken pixels representing imperfections and interruptions.
