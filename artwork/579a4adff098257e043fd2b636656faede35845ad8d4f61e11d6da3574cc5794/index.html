Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a striking palette of warm reds and cool grays. The defiant colors dominate the top left, marked by angular patterns, while the submission colors recede in the bottom right, creating a balance of forces. The collision occurs along a diagonal flow, where the sharp contrasts of dark maroon press into lighter fields, suggesting an inward spiral of tension. This interplay of colors symbolizes the struggle for control in the face of inevitable pressure.
