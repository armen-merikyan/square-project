Artist Statement

The artwork seeks to convey the tension between vulnerability and strength through a palette that evokes emotional contrasts. The muted tones of dark maroon and gray establish a solid, dense base representing strength, while pops of pink and red signify vulnerability and exposure. The main collision occurs at the center, where the jagged 'crack' pattern for strength disrupts the 'grid' of vulnerability, inviting the viewer into a reflection on what it means to feel both exposed and secure. This interplay echoes the emotional complexity inherent in the seed's themes.
