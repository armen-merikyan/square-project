Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between confusion and clarity through stark contrasts in tone and texture. The chosen palette—dark maroon to symbolize deep confusion contrasted with softer pinks representing clarity—creates a visual rhythm that oscillates between dense clusters and airy spaces. The top half features a grid pattern, chaotic and layered, embodying confusion, while the bottom half is structured in a checker pattern, symbolizing clarity. The intentional placement of broken pixels introduces disruption, resonating with the theme of a fractured perception. Overall, the piece reflects a pulse-like flow, capturing the emotional oscillation between order and chaos.
