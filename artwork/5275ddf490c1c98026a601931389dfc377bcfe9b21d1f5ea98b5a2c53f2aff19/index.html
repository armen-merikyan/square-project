Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional pull between 'longing' and 'resolve.' The left half represents yearning with soft pinks and a patchwork pattern suggesting fragility and connection. In contrast, the dark maroon and firm edges on the right express a sense of strength and resolution. The interplay of both sides creates a dynamic tension, amplified by broken pixels that symbolize scars and repairs. The overall composition flows from left to right, emphasizing a journey from longing into resolve.
