Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between control and surrender, utilizing a palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green to represent contrasting emotions. The dense center represents control, surrounded by empty space that reflects the act of surrender. The collision of patterns—measured ash field in the center against a soft-framed edge—creates a pulse of pressure versus release. Broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, symbolizing interruptions in the signal. The artwork speaks to the struggle of finding clarity amidst chaos, echoing the seed's themes.
