Artist Statement

This artwork portrays the tension between love and betrayal, using a palette of warm reds and soft grays contrasted against dark maroons. The placement of colors resembles a struggle, where warm tones symbolize love, while harsh gray blocks represent betrayal. The mirror-like layout hints at reflection, yet broken pixels create a sense of disruption and imperfection. The abstract pattern emerges from the seed's emotional conflict, creating a palpable tension and unresolved narrative across the composition.
