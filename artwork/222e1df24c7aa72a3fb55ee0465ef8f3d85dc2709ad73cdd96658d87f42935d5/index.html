Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the tension of 'love vs betrayal', where warmth converges with sharp edges. The chosen palette—ranging from soft pinks to deep maroons—evokes warmth intertwined with darker hints of emotional turmoil. The top half features a staircase pattern embodying love—soft, hopeful gradients, while the bottom half bursts with noise and chaotic patterns representing betrayal, creating a stark contrast. The collision happens between the warm tones of love and the harsh grays, suggesting the fragile nature of trust amidst betrayal. The seed's themes shape the abstract narrative through juxtaposed patterns and colors, capturing the push and pull of intimacy against isolation.
