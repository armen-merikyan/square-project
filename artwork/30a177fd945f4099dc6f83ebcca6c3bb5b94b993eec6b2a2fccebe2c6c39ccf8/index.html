Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a tension between joy and grief, symbolizing a zigzag flow between warmth and coolness. The palette features bright yellows and oranges representing joy, contrasted with muted blues and purples for grief. The main contrast emerges from the bright ring patterns of joy colliding against the fragmented mirrored patterns of grief, creating a dynamic visual tension. The layout reflects falling pixels, contributing to the sense of chaos and the emotional undercurrent of rising signals amidst disorder.
