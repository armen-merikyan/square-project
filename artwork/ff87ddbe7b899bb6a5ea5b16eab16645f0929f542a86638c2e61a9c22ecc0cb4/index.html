Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, captured through the interplay of soft and hard colors. The palette combines muted sepia and tan with a faded blue accent, evoking a sense of nostalgia and wistfulness. The primary collision occurs in the center, where the bright blue halo of vulnerability meets the darker, more stable edges symbolizing strength. The overall composition features areas of negative space to represent quiet reflection, while broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, echoing the fragility within resilience. This abstract pattern emphasizes the struggle of exposure against the need for shelter.
