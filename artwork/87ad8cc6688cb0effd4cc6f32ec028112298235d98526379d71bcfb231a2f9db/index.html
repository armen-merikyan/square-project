Artist Statement

The artwork captures the tension between envy and gratitude through a contrasting palette of dark violet and soft gold against a backdrop of black and pale green. The warm vs. cool contrast symbolizes the pressure of emotion colliding with a feeling of release. The 'corner_invasion' layout starts with the dark hues in the corner, spreading diagonally to create a sense of quiet space interrupted by vibrant patches of color, embodying the push and pull of emotion. The chosen patterns evoke a sense of scatter and spark, reinforcing the theme of chaotic yet beautiful emotional collisions.
