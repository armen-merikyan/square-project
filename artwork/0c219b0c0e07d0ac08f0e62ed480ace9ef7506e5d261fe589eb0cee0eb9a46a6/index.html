Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between love and betrayal through contrasting palettes and patterns. The warm tones of pink and red resonate with love, while the grays and dark maroon introduce a sense of betrayal and conflict. The layout of falling pixels creates movement from top to bottom, symbolizing an emotional descent, echoing feelings of emptiness in an inside vs outside dynamic. The use of stripes against rings reflects the clash of sharp and soft elements, suggesting disruption and unresolved feelings. The broken pixels introduce an organic imperfection that represents the fragility inherent in relationships.
