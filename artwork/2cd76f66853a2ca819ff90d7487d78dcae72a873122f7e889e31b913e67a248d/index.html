Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a balance between longing and resolve, visualized using a palette that evokes fear and hope. The darker colors symbolize the depths of longing, while the soft gold introduces the warmth of resolve. The main collision occurs at the center, where clusters of dark violet and pale green interact, suggesting an unresolved push and pull. The arrangement conveys a spiral pressure, inviting the viewer to explore the tension of a broken yet hopeful state.
