Artist Statement

This abstract piece captures the emotional tone of control and surrender through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of soft cyan, muted plum, and lavender gray evoke a melancholic and contemplative mood, while the higher density of darker tones represents control, pushing against the lighter hues, symbolizing surrender. The collision occurs at the center, where muted tones seep into vibrant ones, creating a visual stutter effect, reminiscent of pressure against underlying release. The final composition embodies a gradual transition from containment to overflow, maintaining asymmetry throughout, with deliberate broken pixels adding elements of imperfect beauty.
