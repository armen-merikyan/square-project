Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional dichotomy of rage and calm through the intentional clash of vibrant and muted colors. The palette features stark contrasts, using black and deep red to embody rage juxtaposed against pale blue and white for calm. The main collision occurs centrally, where jagged, ring-like shapes disrupt the softer halo pattern surrounding them. This reflects an emotional narrative of explosive energy confined within delicate boundaries, embodying the tension between chaos and tranquility.
