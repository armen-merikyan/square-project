Artist Statement

The emotional tone resonates between fear's darkness and hope's subtle breaks of light. The palette mixes deep blacks and dark violets to portray a weighty atmosphere, contrasted by soft gold and pale green to represent rising hope. The main contrast occurs where the dense black corners push against softer colors, suggesting a confrontation between pressure and potential. The arrangement employs radial patterns for fear and flame patterns for hope, illustrating the struggle and intersection of emotions, characterized by irregularities and broken pixels creating organic tension.
