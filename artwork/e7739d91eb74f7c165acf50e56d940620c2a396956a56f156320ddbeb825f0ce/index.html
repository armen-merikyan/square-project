Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured a struggle between defiance and submission, depicted through a contrast of sharp, assertive angles with softer, maze-like patterns. The palette of navy and dim cyan speaks to a lonely signal amidst the chaos, while black serves as a grounding void. The main collision occurs in the center, where pressure bands meet the maze, creating an interruption that invites contemplation of the tension between each emotional state. Broken pixels symbolize the intentional imperfections of life. The layout features a hollow center, echoing the theme of vulnerability against external forces.
