Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between joy and grief, using a palette that contrasts warm and cool colors: yellows and oranges evoke joy, while deep blues and muted purples signify grief. The composition features a mirrored layout with subtle disruptions that indicate disorder amidst perceived order. A noticeable contrast occurs along the horizontal split, where warm tones diminish and cool tones rise, reflecting the emotional tide. Patterns of crosshatch on the joyful side clash against a grid from grief, symbolizing the interplay of these emotions. The edge style, presenting a burned effect, reinforces the rawness of these feelings, enhancing the sense of hidden versus exposed vulnerability.
