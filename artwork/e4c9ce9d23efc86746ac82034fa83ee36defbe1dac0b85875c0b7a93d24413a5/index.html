Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts vulnerability with strength, represented through a color palette emphasizing muted creams and deep colors to reflect exposure and protection. The use of muted cream as a central element symbolizes vulnerability, surrounded by stronger colors that create a protective frame. The collision occurs at the center, where dense patterns of halo-like colors meet the negative-cross disruptions, suggesting a dynamic interplay between tension and calm. This dialogue of colors embodies the themes of vulnerability and strength through their contrasting densities.
