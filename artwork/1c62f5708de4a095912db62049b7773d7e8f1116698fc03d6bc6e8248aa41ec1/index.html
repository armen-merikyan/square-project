Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional struggle between longing and resolve, embodying a visual prayer of tension. The palette of deep blue and pale yellow contrasts the weight of mourning black, suggesting a push and pull between grief and hope. The crowded center with black and deep blue implies the compression of longing, while the yellow suggests brightness and distance on the edges, a potential resolve. Broken pixel elements scattered throughout introduce moments of imperfection, referencing the chaos inherent in the journey of emotion. The diagonal flow reflects the unresolved nature of these feelings as they intermingle.
