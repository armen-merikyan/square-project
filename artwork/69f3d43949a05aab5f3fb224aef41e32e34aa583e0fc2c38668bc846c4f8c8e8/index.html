Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional interplay of longing and resolve through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette combines soft mint and pale sky to evoke serenity, interspersed with deep storm gray and near black to suggest a sense of weight and reflection—a mirrored conflict within. The dominant presence of muted colors implies a state of resolve, with brighter accents of faded gold representing moments of hope or urgency that break through. The main contrast occurs along a diagonal flow, with dense patterns colliding at the center, suggesting a dynamic tension. The quiet negative space enhances the feeling of longing, creating a visual journey from calm to urgency, embodying both repair and fragility within the abstract structure.
