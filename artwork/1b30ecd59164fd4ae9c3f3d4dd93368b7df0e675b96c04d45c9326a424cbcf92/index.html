Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional spectrum between chaos and control through contrasting warm and cool tones. The chosen palette reflects feelings of joy and grief, with yellows and oranges representing an underlying joy amidst tension and burnt edges suggesting unresolved emotional clashes. The arrangement features a zigzag pattern to symbolize the conflict, with sharper diagonal lines representing panic colliding with more structured, controlled tones and textures. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the idea of tension and chaos within the ordered framework. This allows for a visual representation of the struggle between these contrasting emotional states.
