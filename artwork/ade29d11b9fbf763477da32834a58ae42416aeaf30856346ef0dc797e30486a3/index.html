Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the juxtaposition of joy and grief, creating a tension between vibrancy and melancholy. The palette of vibrant yellow and muted purple represents the bright bursts of joy and the deep sobering tones of grief. The pattern embodies a maze-like structure that suggests the complexities of these feelings, while the dots emulate the fragmentation of grief. The composition features a mirror layout with intentional breaks, allowing for glimpses of chaos and order, echoing the seeds of contrast and movement. The primary collision occurs through the vibrant yellows breaking into the quieter, deeply purples, creating an unsettling yet beautiful tension.
