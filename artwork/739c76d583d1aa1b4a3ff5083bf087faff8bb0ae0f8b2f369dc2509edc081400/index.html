Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of joy and grief through a stark contrast of colors and patterns. The palette, featuring vibrant yellow and orange juxtaposed against deep blue and muted purple, embodies brightness and heaviness. The grid layout exposes a battle between the ascendant maze of joy and the descending crosshatch of grief, signifying the duality of human experience. Sharp edges emphasize the tension, while imperfections in the pixel arrangement symbolize the unpredictability of emotions. The arrangement evokes a sense of conflict, yet retains spaces of negative space for reflection, capturing the essence of navigating through joy amidst grief.
