Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the conflict between clarity and confusion, with the palette evoking loneliness and tension. The chosen colors of deep navy, black, and dim cyan represent the surrounding noise, while the singular white serves as a poignant signal breaking through. The collision occurs at the center of the grid, where the readable pulse (the white) interacts dynamically with the darker, maze-like patterns reflecting noise. This interplay creates a visual story of trying to maintain clarity amidst chaos, embodying the transformative struggle of memory and distraction.
