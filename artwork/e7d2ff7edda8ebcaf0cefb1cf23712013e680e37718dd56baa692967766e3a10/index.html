Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional conflict of longing and resolve through color and arrangement. The palette of moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold reflects resilience and fragility, suggesting depth and warmth. The dense clusters towards the center represent resolve, while the quieter edges evoke longing. The sharp contrast of vertical and horizontal color placements creates a feeling of tension and movement, making the center the focal point where these emotions collide.
