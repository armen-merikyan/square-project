Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the duality of tenderness and rage, illustrated through a palette of deep red as the dominant force, contrasted with pale blues representing quiet moments. The placement creates a focal collision in the center, where softer checker patterns meet the intensity of radial bursts, suggesting a dynamic interplay. The organizing metaphor of 'echo_mark' manifests as whispers of warmth layered against sharp interactions, signifying struggle and reconciliation.
