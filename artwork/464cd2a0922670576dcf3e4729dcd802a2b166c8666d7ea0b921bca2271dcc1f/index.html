Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured in this artwork reflects the tension between longing and resolve, utilizing a palette that juxtaposes muted tones with a few saturated accents. Dark navy represents depth and urgency, while off-white introduces an element of hope and distance. The composition features a clear collision between the organic flow of longing, represented by rain-like patterns, and the structured resolve of broken dots. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions within the framework, representing imperfections in the pursuit of clarity. This abstract pattern navigates through spaces that feel both overflowing and contained, creating a feeling of both serenity and urgency.
