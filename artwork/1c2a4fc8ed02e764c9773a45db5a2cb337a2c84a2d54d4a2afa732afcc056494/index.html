Artist Statement

This piece captures the duality of loneliness and connection through contrasting colors and placement. The chosen palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes a sense of fear and hope, highlighting the emotional collision. The central focus emerges through a maze-like pattern on one side, suggesting connection, while the off-centered mirrored dots express the loneliness that permeates the canvas. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the emotional tension, while retaining sufficient negative space to convey quiet reflection amidst the chaos.
