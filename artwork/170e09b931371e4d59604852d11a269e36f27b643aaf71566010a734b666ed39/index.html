Artist Statement

The composition explores the tension between emptiness and desire, represented by the clash of warm and cool colors. The palette draws from 'ancient_fire', invoking burnt orange and dark red to symbolize desire's heat against the coolness of charcoal and gold representing emptiness. The center is a jagged clash of burnt oranges echoing desire, while the surrounding cooler hues form stripes and rhythmic rain patterns, suggesting a dynamic pulse that ebbs and flows. This creates an unresolved narrative where the heart is fragmented amidst a sea of contrasting textures and pressures, ultimately depicted through the balance of density and negative space.
