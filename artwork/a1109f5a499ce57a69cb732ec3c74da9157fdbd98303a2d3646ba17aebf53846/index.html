Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between confusion and clarity, represented through the use of contrasting colors in a nearly mirrored layout. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon implies warmth and emotional depth; the warm tones suggest passion while the grays introduce a muted, calming counterbalance. The sharp contrasts and broken pixels introduce imperfection into the symmetry, mirroring the struggle between clarity and chaos. This results in a composition that pulses with emotional weight, achieving a balance between serene spaces and dense clusters, evoking a narrative of interruption and unresolved tension.
