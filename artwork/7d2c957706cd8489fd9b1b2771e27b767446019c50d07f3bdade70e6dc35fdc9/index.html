Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between signal and noise, reflecting the emotional struggle of clarity amidst chaos. The color palette is dominated by a bright, electric blue (#005EB8) and vibrant signal green (#A6E7B2) to signify the moments of clear signal fighting through the dense static of gray (#7D7F81) and black (#2F2F2F). The interruptive nature of hot magenta (#D7006D) adds a jarring beauty, creating a central collision point, while static gray serves as a disrupted background noise. The uneven border arranged in a broken frame symbolizes cracks in communication, enhancing the emotional nuance within the square. The interplay of pulse marks against fault lines embodies the theme of existence within disruptions, making the grid not only readable but filled with resonant meaning.
