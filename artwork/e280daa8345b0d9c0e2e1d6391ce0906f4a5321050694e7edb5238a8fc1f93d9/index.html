Artist Statement

This piece juxtaposes the tension of restlessness against the calming embrace of peace. The palette features soft pinks and reds representing peace, contrasted with deep grays and dark maroon that evoke a sense of weight and disturbance. The main collision occurs at the center, where elements of spark scatter against rippling waves, encapsulating the ongoing struggle between anxiety and serenity. Broken pixels, scattered throughout, emphasize imperfections, echoing the theme of disrupted harmony.
