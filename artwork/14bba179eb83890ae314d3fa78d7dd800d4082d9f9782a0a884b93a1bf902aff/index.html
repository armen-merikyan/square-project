Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between life and decay, illustrated through a palette that juxtaposes vibrant warmth against muted ash tones. The radial pattern representing 'spark' radiates energetic accents, while the braided elements of 'ash' create a subtle, woven backdrop, suggesting memories and remnants. The collision occurs in the center, where warmer hues break through the darker areas, echoing resilience amidst desolation. The layout fosters a visual dialogue of conflict and balance.
