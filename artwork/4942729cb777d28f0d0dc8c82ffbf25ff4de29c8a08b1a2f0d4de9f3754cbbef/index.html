Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece contrasts the vitality of 'spark' against the somberness of 'ash'. The palette predominantly features gray and dark maroon to evoke a sense of muted memory with flashes of hotter reds and pinks representing bursts of energy. The grid structure of 'ash' runs horizontally, interrupted by vertical stripes of 'spark', conveying a dynamic tension between restraint and vitality. The central collision between colors depicts a pivotal moment of transformation and disruption.
