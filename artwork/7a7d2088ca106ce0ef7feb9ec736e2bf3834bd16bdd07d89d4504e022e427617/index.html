Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between yearning ('desire') and frustration ('emptiness'). The color palette, dominated by warm coral and deep charcoal, arms the artwork with a sense of conflict. The layout sees pixels climbing upward, suggesting hopes that are persistently out of reach. The dominant warm colors create a saturated pull against a stark background. Key collisions occur at the center, where warm pulses interrupt the darker fields, embodying the clash of desires amidst constraints. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, echoing life's disruptions in the pursuit of fulfillment.
