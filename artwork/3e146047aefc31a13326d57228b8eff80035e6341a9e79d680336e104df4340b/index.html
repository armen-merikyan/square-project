Artist Statement

The composition captures a dynamic interplay between tenderness and rage, using a palette of saturated reds and soft pale blues. The main collision occurs in the center, where dense red blocks erupt against the calmer blue edges, highlighting emotional tension. The 'filled_square' shape seed manifests as intense color clashes that ripple outward, while intermittent 'broken_pixels' introduce a sense of imperfection, echoing personal conflict. This balance of saturated intensity and muted tranquility tells a story of struggle and resolution, embodying the essence of the seed's emotional dichotomy.
