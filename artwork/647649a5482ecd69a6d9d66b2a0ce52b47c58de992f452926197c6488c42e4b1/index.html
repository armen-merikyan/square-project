Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes vulnerability with strength, representing a core center of soft colors surrounded by darker, more intense edges. The palette combines gentle powder pink and warm white at the core, embodying vulnerability, while sharp crimson and carbon black dominate the outer frame, suggesting strength and protection. The collision occurs where the light inner square meets the darker perimeter—this tension highlights the struggle between exposure and shelter. The arrangement reflects the idea of interaction and influence, echoing the seed's themes.
