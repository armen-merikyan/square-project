Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension of 'panic' against 'control.' The selected palette of deep navy and subtle dim cyan evokes a sense of isolation and a distant calm, while the dark background surrounds brighter spots representing moments of anxiety or conflict. The layout mimics falling pixels, with quieter spaces contrasting the denser blocks of color, illustrating a dance between pressure and release. The interplay of dark shades captures weight, while lighter tones create an impression of intermittent bursts of emotion, echoing the seed's themes.
