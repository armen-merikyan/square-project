Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between quietude and pressure, visualized through a palette that contrasts muted tones with bursts of intensity. The chosen colors—off-white and faint peach represent quiet, while compressed red and dark navy symbolize pressure. The layout suggests scattered repair, where brighter tones disrupt darker clusters, highlighting a collision of feelings. The predominant dark navy creates a dense field on the left, with light colors gradually emerging towards the right, suggesting a movement from pressure towards a release into clarity.
