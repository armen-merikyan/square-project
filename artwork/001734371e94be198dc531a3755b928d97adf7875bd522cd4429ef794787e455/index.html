Artist Statement

The artwork captures the tension between awe and terror using contrasting colors that evoke feelings of stability and instability. The palette blends warm yellows and deep blues with muted purples, creating a dynamic balance reflective of the emotional duality. The vertical wave pattern suggests an upward flow from chaos to calm, while the fragmented elements symbolize memory and imperfection. The collision here is found in the interplay of vibrant and muted sections, creating a rhythmic visual narrative. This composition embodies the complex nature of emotions, where beauty and fear coexist.
