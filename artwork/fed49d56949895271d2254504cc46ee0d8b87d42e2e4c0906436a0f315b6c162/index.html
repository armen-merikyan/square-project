Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional duality of confusion and clarity. The chosen palette, inspired by 'ancient_fire', blends burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold to evoke warmth and intensity. The layout features a nearly mirrored composition disrupted by chaotic crosshatches that symbolize confusion. The main contrast arises from the tension between the thick outlines and the broken pixels, creating a visual pulse that resonates through the arrangement. This reflects the delicate balance of emotions, embodying the conflict between symmetry and disruption.
