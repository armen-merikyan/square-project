Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension of relief versus dread through color dynamics. The muted palette of storm gray and near black signifies the heaviness of dread, dominating the upper left corner. In contrast, the soft sky and pale mint emerge from the spiral layout, creating an opening towards the lower right, suggesting relief and escape. The palette's interplay, combined with intentional broken pixels, disrupts uniformity, encapsulating a chaotic yet structured experience that mimics the emotional tug of the seed's themes.
