Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity (the signal) and confusion (the noise). The palette, comprising electric blue and hot magenta against static gray and dead black, represents bright accents disrupting dark fields. The main collision occurs in the center where clear signals provoke interference. This mirrors the idea of 'islands against echo,' as contrasting colors float amidst a sea of dense noise, creating a visual representation of memory and erasure.
