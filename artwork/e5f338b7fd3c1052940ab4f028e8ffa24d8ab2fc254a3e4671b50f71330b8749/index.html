Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through jagged edges and contrasting patterns. The palette mixes faded blue and tan with darker shades to emphasize a feeling of broken symmetry, where sections are dominated by chaotic textures resembling 'flame' patterns interspersed with calm 'teeth' elements. The main collision occurs in the central rows, where colors clash and flow diagonally, creating a sense of pulsating unrest amidst structured calm.
