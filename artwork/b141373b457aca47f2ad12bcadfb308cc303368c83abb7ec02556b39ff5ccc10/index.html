Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between loneliness and connection, reflecting the struggle of hidden vs. exposed feelings. The palette is influenced by the 'broken_memory' theme, with muted tones contrasting against a few vibrant colors that serve as focal points. This creates an atmosphere where the shadowy colors represent isolation, while the brighter tones symbolize fleeting moments of connection, resulting in a rhythmic pattern that ebbs and flows. The arrangement suggests deep feelings of bursting emotions surrounded by quiet tension, effectively capturing the complex narrative from the seed.
