Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional dichotomy of confusion and clarity through an alternating pattern of dark and light tones. The palette of navy and dim cyan exudes a lonely signal amidst the chaotic arrangements, echoing the essence of falling pixels and broken grids. The stark contrast between dense patches of dark colors and lighter tones creates a visual fight that hints at a center radiating outward, simultaneously grounding the viewer while inviting chaos. The chosen patterns of stairs intermixed with radial placements illustrate the conflicting nature of clarity breaking through as clusters of confusion.
