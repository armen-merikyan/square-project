Artist Statement

This piece represents the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress. The palette, drawing from 'ancient_fire', uses burnt orange and dark red to convey intensity and warmth contrasting against cool, charcoal tones that symbolize depth and calm. The split horizontal layout creates a sense of rising vs falling, with the top half offering a lighter, more vibrant color reference, suggesting progress and vitality, while the bottom half is heavier, evoking nostalgia with darker, muted tones. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the emotional texture. Central color placements create echoing contrasts, embodying the tension of movement and stasis, thus transforming the seed's essence into a meaningful abstract narrative.
