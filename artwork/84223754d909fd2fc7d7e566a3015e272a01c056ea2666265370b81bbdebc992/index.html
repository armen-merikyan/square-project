Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the dichotomy of love and betrayal, using a palette of pinks and dark maroons to illustrate warmth alongside hidden danger. The contrast emerges through the juxtaposition of dense clusters on the edges versus an emptier core, emphasizing a sense of broken heart and misaligned symmetry, reflecting betrayal within love. The chaotic implications of 'crack' and 'burned edges' are visually represented by undefined, fragmented patterns across the grid.
