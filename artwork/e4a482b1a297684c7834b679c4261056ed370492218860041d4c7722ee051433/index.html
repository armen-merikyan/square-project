Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between control and surrender through a contrasting palette of warm reds and cool grays. The center appears overfilled, reflecting pressure, while the surrounding field exhibits a quiet release. The broken pixels disrupt the smooth flow, embodying unexpected interruptions and the struggle between contained pressure and the urge to surrender. The arrangement leans toward the dominant pressure at the center, creating a visual pulse that resonates throughout the square.
