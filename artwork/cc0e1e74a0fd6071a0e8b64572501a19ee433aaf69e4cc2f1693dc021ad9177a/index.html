Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes vulnerability and strength, captured through a palette that blends 'melancholy wonder' with soft, muted blues and warm plums. The left half symbolizes vulnerability with a delicate 'shatter' pattern using pale colors, while the right embodies strength with a 'thread' pattern in deeper shades, coiling toward the center. The main contrast occurs as the muted hues collide with bright accents in the center, suggesting the tension between exposure and shelter, enhanced by broken pixels that introduce intentional imperfections, echoing the theme of fragility within strength.
