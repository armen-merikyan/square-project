Artist Statement

The emotional tone is one of tension between clarity and confusion, highlighted through a palette of bright and deep colors. Black and electric blue create a stark contrast that feels both heavy and weightless, while lime and magenta punctuate the space with vibrancy. The placement emphasizes a break in symmetry that replicates the emotional push and pull described in the seed. The arrangement of contrasting colors creates collision points, expressing the inner turmoil represented by 'confusion' against the calm of 'clarity'. A broken pattern evokes a pulse, symbolizing a heartbeat through the shifting positions of colors.
