Artist Statement

This abstract artwork embodies the emotional tug-of-war between control and surrender. The palette chosen reflects a 'melancholy wonder' with deep cool tones interrupted by gentle highlights. The central area is densely layered with the darker shades representing control, while softer hues repair around the edges, symbolizing the release. The collision occurs predominantly at the center, where the patterned control clashing with softer surrender creates a tension that draws the eye. Broken pixels scattered throughout add imperfections, symbolizing the unpredictability of the emotional experience.
