Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between clarity and chaos, representing the emotional contrasts of 'signal' and 'noise.' The palette combines signal green and electric blue for vibrancy against a background of static gray and dead black. The hot magenta serves as an accent, creating visual interruptions reminiscent of static or interference. The layout emphasizes a pressure corner where noise collides with the signal pulse, creating an abstract representation of persistent struggle between clarity and confusion.
