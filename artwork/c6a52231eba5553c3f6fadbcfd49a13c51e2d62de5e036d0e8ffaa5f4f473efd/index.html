Artist Statement

This piece conveys the emotional duality of longing and resolution. The chosen palette of pale aqua and mist gray juxtaposes serene elements against alarming hints of red and ink blue, representing desire and determination. The main collision occurs in the central four pixels, creating a dynamic tension while the edges maintain a quiet field. Patterned ripples of longing scatter throughout, interrupted by the solid quiet fields of resolve, mirroring the broken threads of intent that bind them together.
