Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension of defiance versus submission through sharp contrasts and patterns. The palette, featuring deep reds and muted creams, symbolizes a struggle between bright refusal and receding focus, while the layout – climbing upward – conveys a sense of aspiration and conflict. Main collisions occur in the center, where a maze-like structure – representing defiance – intersects with crack patterns of submission, creating a visual complexity that evokes resistance amidst chaos.
