Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between restlessness and peace, contrasting chaotic spark-like colors with a soft, subdued palette. The interplay of warm and cool colors symbolizes a tension between movement and tranquility, where bright accents (like warm brown) unexpectedly punctuate a sheltering green backdrop. The upper left corner carries the most vibrant hues, representing the imminent intrusiveness of restlessness, while the cooler shades dominate the lower region, creating a balance reminiscent of peace. This layout highlights a motion inward, where external chaos meets an enveloping calm, reflecting the essence of the seed through visual disruption without literal representation.
