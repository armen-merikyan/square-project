Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between trust, represented by softer colors like soft gold and pale green, and suspicion, expressed through darker tones of black and dark violet. The palette conveys hope amidst uncertainty. The main collision occurs through the central band where soft gold replaces dark violet, creating visual tension and drawing the eye. This tension reflects the struggle between clarity (trust) and interference (suspicion), creating layers of meaning reminiscent of static noise against a clear signal, framed by a dark edge to evoke a sense of pressure and confinement, mirroring the broader themes of resilience and fragility.
