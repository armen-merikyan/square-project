Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between awe and terror through a juxtaposition of dark and light colors. The palette, including soft gold and pale green, introduces warmth against a backdrop of darkness, referencing hope amidst fear. The layout presents a solid outer border of darker shades representing chaos, while the central 4x4 area symbolizes order, enhanced with crack and maze patterns to create a sense of fractured memory. The edge style, characterized by jagged edges, emphasizes instability, allowing for a collision of emotions at the boundaries. The negative space preserves a feeling of breath amidst the weight of the overall design.
