Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a balance between trust and suspicion, showcasing varying pressures within a confined space. The palette of pale mint, soft sky, storm gray, near black, and faded gold creates a contrast between warmth and coolness, with storm gray embodying suspicion and the pale mint representing trust. The main collision occurs at the center, where soft sky overlaps with storm gray, creating an echo effect of fleeting certainty and lurking doubt. This duality is reflected in the arrangement: darker colors framing a lighter core, suggesting the tightness of suspicion around the vulnerability of trust.
