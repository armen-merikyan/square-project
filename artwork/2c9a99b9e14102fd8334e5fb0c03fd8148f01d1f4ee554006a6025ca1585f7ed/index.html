Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional interplay of relief and dread. The color palette of pale mint, soft sky, storm gray, near black, and faded gold evokes a transition from tension to space. The calmness of the lighter shades represents relief, while the darker, dense hues symbolize dread. The design utilizes a barcode pattern to reflect dread, clashing with a stepwell pattern suggesting relief. The main contrast occurs in the lower left corner, where darker colors cluster, creating pressure that disperses towards the upper right, suggesting a loosening of tension. The use of broken pixels evokes scars of past pressures, adding to the emotional complexity without taking on literal forms.
