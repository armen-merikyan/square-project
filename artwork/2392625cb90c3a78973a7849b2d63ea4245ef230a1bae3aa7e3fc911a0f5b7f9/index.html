Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a clash between relief and dread, embodied through contrasting warm and cool colors. The 'love_betrayal' palette provides a vivid tension: the light pink and red signify relief while the dark maroon and gray express dread. The pattern intersperses broken dots to suggest fleeting moments of relief amidst a dense undercurrent of pressure, with more vibrant colors colliding with muted ones, creating a visual rhythm that captures the essence of emotional turmoil. Negative space is maintained to avoid chaos, allowing each color to breathe while still suggesting a narrative of collision and contrasting emotions.
