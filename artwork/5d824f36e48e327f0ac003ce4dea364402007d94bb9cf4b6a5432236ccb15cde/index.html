Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece embodies a dialogue between longing and resolve, creating a visual narrative of tension and release. The palette, featuring melancholy blues and muted plums, conveys a sense of both tranquility and urgency, echoing the duality of the seed. The main collision occurs at the center of the grid, where soft, glowing colors collide with deeper shadows, illustrating the push and pull of emotional states. This arrangement is built on the idea of two pulses moving toward each other—broken pixels introduce intentional disruption, capturing the essence of damage and repair and enhancing the abstract story.
