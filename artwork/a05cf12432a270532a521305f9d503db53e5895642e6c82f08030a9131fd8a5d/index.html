Artist Statement

This composition captures an emotional tension between clarity and interference, where a bright 'signal' of electric blue suggests a clean pulse, sharply contrasting against a vast field of muted static gray and dead black. The bright greens and hot magenta disrupt the darker tones, creating a sense of collision and interruption, representing the struggle between memory and erasure. The layout adheres to the concept of a small bright cluster remaining active amidst a predominantly dark expanse, embodying the seed's narrative of exposure versus shelter.
