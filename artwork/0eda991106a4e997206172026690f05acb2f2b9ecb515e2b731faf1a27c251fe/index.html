Artist Statement

This piece conveys the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through a predominantly dark palette punctuated by soft gold highlights, symbolizing moments of hope amidst struggle. The main contrast is between the soft, radial patterns suggesting vulnerability in the center and the structured, hinge patterns representing strength at the edges, creating a dynamic interplay that echoes the theme of conflict and coexistence. The central area remains less dense, evoking a sense of emptiness but also potential, while the edges exert pressure, enforcing a feeling of protection.
