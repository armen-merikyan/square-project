Artist Statement

This composition balances the tension between the deep desires represented by warm, saturated colors and the encompassing emptiness shown through muted, darker hues. The palette highlights a struggle, with vibrant colors like #FF6F61 and #FFABAB signifying desire emerging against the backdrop of cool and calm shades like #101820 and #2C3E50, which symbolize restraint. The main collision occurs towards the center—a vibrant cluster disrupts the surrounding quiet fields, echoing the concept of overflow versus containment, creating a visual narrative of a 'pull' against a barrier. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional imperfection, reflecting the inherent conflicts described in the seed while adhering to the grid layout, resulting in a structured yet fluid composition.
