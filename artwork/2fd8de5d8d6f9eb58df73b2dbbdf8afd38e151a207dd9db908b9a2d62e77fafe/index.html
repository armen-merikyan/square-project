Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, depicted through jagged edges and a fragmented arrangement. The chosen palette of gray, dark maroon, pink, and red contrasts heavy intensity against lighter, muted tones. The central flow moves upward, representing hope and clarity amidst an encroaching chaos. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the feeling of disorder as they collide with the more structured bright segments, symbolizing the fight for clarity within confusion.
