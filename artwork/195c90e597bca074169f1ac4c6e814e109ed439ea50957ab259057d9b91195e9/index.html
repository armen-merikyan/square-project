Artist Statement

This composition expresses the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through a measured contrast of color and layout. The palette of deep black, vibrant red, pale blue, and stark white embodies raw emotional contrasts—black and red suggesting isolation and tension, while blue and white evoke the presence of potential connection and calm. The top-weighted layout creates a grounding sensation, with densely clustered reds and blacks forming a tumultuous zone at the top, representing internal struggle, while lighter hues filter down, suggesting clarity and emergence. The pattern, inspired by a burst and crosshatch, creates an interplay of connections and separating lines, where the broken pixel inclusion signifies the imperfections inherent in relationships. Overall, the arrangement tells a story of striving for connection amidst the weight of solitude.
