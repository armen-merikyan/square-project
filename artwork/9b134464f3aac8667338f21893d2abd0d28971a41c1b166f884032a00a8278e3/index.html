Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between panic and control. The palette of sepia, tan, and dust gray embodies a broken memory, evoking a sense of nostalgia mixed with unease. The upper half features a structured pattern reflecting order, while the lower half breaks apart, conveying chaos and instability. This contrast is heightened by the light vs. dark dynamic, with sharper contrasts creating points of collision that symbolize emotional turmoil.
