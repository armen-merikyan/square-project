Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between peace and restlessness, creating a dynamic interplay in the composition. The selected palette features both muted and vivid tones, where 'pale mint' serves as a quiet field that suggests calmness, while 'storm gray' and 'near black' introduce intensity and depth. The collision occurs at the center, where a 'negative cross' pattern representing restlessness disrupts a 'halo' of peaceful colors, embodying their emotional struggle. The diagonal flow hints at the inevitable movement between these contrasting emotions, with broken pixels reinforcing the chaotic nature of restlessness.
