Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional contrasts of joy and grief, reflected through stark and soft color shifts. The palette of warm yellows and deep blues evokes a sense of warmth against the coolness of sorrow. Patterns of joy emerge in stripes that rise diagonally, suggesting an upward battle through the structured chaos. The zigzag flow represents the push and pull of emotions, while broken pixels introduce imperfection, reflecting fragmented feelings. The contrast between the bright yellows and muted purples highlights collisions in the emotional narrative, creating a visual tension that resonates with the complexity of the seed.
