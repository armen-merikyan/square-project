Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional dichotomy between clarity and chaos. The palette, featuring deep navy and bright cyan, represents the stark contrasts inherent in 'signal' and 'noise.' The left side employs static patterns with darker tones to illustrate interference, while the right side utilizes lighter shades that suggest clarity and presence, embodying a pulsing signal. The central tension coalesces at the midline, illustrating the struggle between these two states. Intentionally placed broken pixels serve as scars within the structure, adding an element of imperfection and disruption reflective of the theme.
