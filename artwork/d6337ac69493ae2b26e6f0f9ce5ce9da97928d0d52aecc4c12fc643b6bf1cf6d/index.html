Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional clash between vulnerability and strength. The palette, featuring moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold, evokes resilience against fragility. The central pixels are adorned with soft, pale colors (bone white and moss green) to suggest vulnerability, contrasted by the dense, darker hues of rust and stone gray at the edges, symbolizing strength. The collision occurs at the center where lighter tones meet the darker borders, creating a feeling of tension and release. The broken pixels disrupt the symmetry, embodying the interplay of chaos and control sparked by the seed's themes.
