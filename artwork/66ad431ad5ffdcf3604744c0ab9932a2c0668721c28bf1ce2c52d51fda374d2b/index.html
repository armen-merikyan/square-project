Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured here reflects the struggle between defiance and submission, visualized through a juxtaposition of sharp lines and soft curves. The selected palette includes 'rain_blue' and 'muted_plum', suggesting a melancholic wonder that underlines both tension and quiet moments. The central contrast is defined by the hard interruption of the crosshatch pattern symbolizing defiance, colliding with the gentle ring pattern of submission. The tension and emotion arise from the dissonance created in this threshold space, where structured chaos meets soothing calm, embodied in the layout's uneven border and the presence of broken pixels that disrupt and reinforce the flow.
