Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength through a palette that mixes soft golds with deeper hues, presenting warmth juxtaposed against density. The collision occurs centrally where pale and dark colors meet, symbolizing the dialogue between safety and exposure. Broken pixels emulate scars and open wounds, reminding of the fragility within strength. Overall, the composition whispers narratives of healing amidst imperfection, encoded in the echoing patterns of warmth and weight.
