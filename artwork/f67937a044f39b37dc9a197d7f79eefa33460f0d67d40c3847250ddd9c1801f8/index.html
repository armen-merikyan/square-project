Artist Statement

This artwork captures the push and pull between certainty and curiosity. The top half uses a warm palette with burnt orange and gold to symbolize the definite, structured impulses of certainty, while the bottom half embraces deeper hues of charcoal and dark red, embodying chaos and curiosity. The abrupt transition in the middle represents the conflict of emotions and thoughts breaking against each other. The placement of colors suggests waves pushing and receding, evoking a sense of rising signals. Disrupted pixels serve as a reminder of imperfections within the emotional journey, enhancing the overall sense of tension and resolution.
