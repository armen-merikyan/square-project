Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between panic and control, represented through a dichotomy of structured patterns and disrupted pixels. The selected 'broken_memory' palette conveys nostalgia with its muted sepia tones, evoking a sense of realization against the resignation. The left side utilizes cleaner, orderly dots to signal control, while the right hints at chaotic dispersion, simulating the feeling of panic. The collision occurs in a subtle interplay of tightly arranged colors versus sporadic placements, embodying the clashing emotions the seed suggests.
