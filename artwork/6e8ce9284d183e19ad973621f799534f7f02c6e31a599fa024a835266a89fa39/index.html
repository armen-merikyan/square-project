Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a conflict between clarity and chaos, symbolized by the interplay of 'signal' and 'noise'. The palette utilizes vibrant greens and blues (for signal) contrasting with deep blacks and grays (for noise), creating a sense of tension and vibrancy. The main collision occurs in the center with clean patterns subtly interrupted by static specks, representing the struggle for clarity amidst distraction. The arrangement mirrors the idea of an 'echo mark', where bursts of signal are surrounded by chaotic noise, speaking to memory versus erasure.
