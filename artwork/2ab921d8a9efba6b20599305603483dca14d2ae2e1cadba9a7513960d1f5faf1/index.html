Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between chaos and structure, represented by a palette of sepia and faded blue, evoking a sense of nostalgia intertwined with clarity. The contrast emerges where warm hues clash with cooler shades, creating a fractured symmetry in the composition. This arrangement disrupts the expected flow, drawing the eye toward collisions between dark and light, symbolizing confusion emanating from the center and diffusing outward. The formation of broken pixels adds to the complexity by introducing elements of glitch, enhancing the overall narrative of conflict and resolution.
