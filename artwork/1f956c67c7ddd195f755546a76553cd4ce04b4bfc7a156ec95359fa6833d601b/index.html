Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a juxtaposition of clarity and confusion reflecting the struggle between order and chaos. The palette employs dark navy and stark black to invoke depth against touches of dim cyan, representing moments of clarity. The left side employs a noise pattern to create an overwhelming, disorganized feel, while the right embodies mirrored clarity. The collision occurs where those tones meet, creating powerful tension, and scattered 'broken' pixels introduce an imperfection, implying the presence of chaos even within clarity.
