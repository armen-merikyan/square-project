Artist Statement

This piece captures the duality of tenderness and rage through a contrasting palette of black, red, and pale blue. The layout suggests an empty room, emphasizing solitude. The upward-moving action represented by saturated red pixels collides with the soft, muted blues and whites, projecting an emotional climb that hints at tension. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, mirroring the chaotic interplay between the two emotions. The space is designed to provoke reflection on the internal struggle between tumult and calm, resonating with the concept of 'echo_mark'.
