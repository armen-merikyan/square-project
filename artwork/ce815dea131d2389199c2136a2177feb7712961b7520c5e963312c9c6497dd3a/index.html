Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between love and betrayal through contrasting color patterns. The warm pinks and reds at the top represent love, presented in a crosshatch pattern to convey intimacy and connection. In contrast, the bottom half features cold grays and dark maroon, arranged in a grid that suggests an unsettling rigidity of betrayal. The diagonally flowing arrangement and 'burned edges' create a sense of emergence and tension, embodying a rise and fall of emotion. The interplay between soft and sharp colors highlights the emotional dichotomy, creating a poignant rhythm throughout the artwork.
