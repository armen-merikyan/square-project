Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between loneliness and connection, using an expressive palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon to illustrate these dynamics. The chaotic arrangement of colors illustrates the contrast between pressure and release; dense clusters of dark maroon and gray represent emotional weight, while scattered pink accents suggest fleeting moments of connection. The symmetry in layout brings a broken mirror effect, reinforcing the tension between connection and isolation, where off-centered elements evoke a sense of unease but also invite interaction. This piece embodies a rhythmic pulse of emotions, shifting in and out of focus, representing the collisions of feelings.
