Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a duality of trust and suspicion, contrasting warm, inviting hues with cooler, shadowed tones. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue illustrates the warmth of trust while juxtaposing the cooler shadows representing suspicion. The primary collision occurs in the lower-left corner, where a dense cluster of muted colors embodies the pressure of suspicion, while the right side offers a softer glow, representing trust. The abstract pattern flows in a diagonal manner, mirroring the tension between these opposing emotions and creating an overall feeling of unresolved dialogue.
