Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the conflict between control and surrender. The palette incorporates deep wine and warm ivory, suggesting guilt and forgiveness, while ash gray serves as a quiet field to cool the intensity. The central collision occurs with a dense cluster of deep wine surrounded by softer hues, visualizing the pressure of the overfilled center against the boundary. The broken frame layout leads to an organic ebb and flow, representing the push and pull between containment and overflow.
