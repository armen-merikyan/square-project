Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a dance between longing and resolve, illustrated through a palette of deep black, vibrant red, soft pale blue, and serene white. The black conveys depth and chaos, while the red symbolizes passion and intensity. Pale blue introduces a serene contrast, and the white offers a feeling of space and resolution. The main collision occurs centrally where the barcodes of longing meet the interwoven braids of resolve, creating a rhythmic structure that feels chaotic yet anchored. The seed inspired an alternating pattern that reflects movement along a diagonal flow, representing the motion of tension and release.
