Artist Statement

The emotional tone derived from the seed speaks to the complex interplay between longing and resolve, expressed through color and arrangement that suggest a struggle between control and chaos. The palette of stain brown, ash gray, warm ivory, soft gold, and deep wine evokes a sense of warmth and muted urgency. The dominant color of warm ivory fills much of the piece with a sense of calm, while deep wine provides a pulsating contrast at the center, signifying the collision of longing and resolve. The radial pattern interacts with the checker pattern to symbolize the tension between the yearning for serenity and the push for action, creating a dynamic surface with moments of disruption. Broken pixels scattered amidst the otherwise structured layout serve as scars or reminders of the imperfections inherent in this emotional journey, creating a narrative without literal representation.
