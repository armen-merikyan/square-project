Artist Statement

The artwork conveys the emotional tension of rage and calm, where explosive energy meets a stillness. The palette combines bold reds to represent rage with soft blues and whites for calm, symbolizing a clash of feelings. The arrangement features a checker pattern for calm juxtaposed against jagged, static disturbances of red, creating a sense of overwhelming yet contained emotion, particularly focused in the corners, where calm edges erode under the pressure of rage.
