Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a balance of strength and vulnerability, echoing the dichotomy of protection and exposure. The palette, drawn from 'ancient_fire', employs burnt orange and dark red to ground the artwork in a sense of stability while utilizing gold for moments of light and hope. Charcoal adds depth, evoking shadows of vulnerability. The collision occurs predominantly at the center, where the dense 'echo' pattern of strength meets the spaced 'barcode' echoes of vulnerability. This juxtaposition reflects the complexity of the seed, transforming it into an intricate dance of color and emotion, both clamoring for attention yet undeniably intertwined.
