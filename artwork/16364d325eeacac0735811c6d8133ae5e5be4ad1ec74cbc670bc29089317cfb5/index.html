Artist Statement

The composition embodies the emotional tug-of-war between rage and calm, with a contrast that plays out between jagged red and soothing pale blue. The palette symbolizes this tension, utilizing warm intensities and cooler tones to embody both explosive energy and tranquil containment. The 'stairs' pattern for rage collides with the 'fault line' for calm, creating an asymmetrical balance with emphasis on the red zones at the center while maintaining negative space around the edges. This layout reflects the subject of 'vessel', capturing the essence of intention versus emotional overflow.
