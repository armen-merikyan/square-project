Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between chaos and serenity. The dark palette represents rage, with blacks and deep reds dominating the left half. In contrast, pale blues and whites, suggestive of calm, occupy the right, juxtaposing soft echoes against sharp interruptions. The fiery red pixelation zigzags inward, colliding with the delicate blue fields, while disrupted cycles hint at eroded symmetry. This leads to an abstract representation of containment versus overflow, with broken pixels signifying ruptures and emotional scars. The flow of colors merges in the center, accentuating the tension inherent in the struggle between the two emotions, embodied through the arrangement of vibrant clashes and tranquil spaces.
