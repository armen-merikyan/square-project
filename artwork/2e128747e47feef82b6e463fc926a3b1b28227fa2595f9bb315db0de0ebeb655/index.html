Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork juxtaposes clarity and confusion, using a palette that contrasts dark tones with soft accents. The black and dark violet in the top half reflect confusion through a broken grid pattern, while the soft gold and pale green in the bottom evoke clarity with a smoother noise texture. The placement of broken pixels adds intentional imperfection, representing disruptions in perceived order. This interplay creates a visual tension, embodying the pulse of emotion through zigzag flow, capturing the essence of both chaos and calm.
