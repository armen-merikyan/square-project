Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between strict control and gentle surrender, leading to an 8x8 composition characterized by a central intensity that suggests pressure. The palette of 'ancient_fire' evokes warmth, intensity, and conflict—burnt orange and gold represent the glow of control, while deep red and charcoal embody the overarching surrender and tension at the edges. The central pixel arrangement, which employs a thread-like pattern in a rising signal layout, creates a collision of dense, upward energy interrupted by broader, gentle fields, showcasing a layered narrative of both restraint and release.
