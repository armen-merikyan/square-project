Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional contrast of intense desire battling against the boundaries of restraint. The palette, drawn from the 'hunger_restraint' theme, employs saturated reds and muted creams to signify heated passion colliding with muted self-control. The edge style is soft, creating a quiet channel through the dense, vibrant patterns that represent desire, while glitch-like interruptions symbolize restraint. The main contrast occurs in the center, where bright colors pulse against muted tones, evoking an emotional conflict between longing and limitation.
