Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between envy and gratitude, depicted through contrasting colors that shift from muted tones to brighter accents. The chosen palette includes soft sepia and faded blue, representing warmth against cooler hues, creating an interplay of tension. The main contrast occurs diagonally across the square, emulating a push and pull, embodying the seed's notion of rising and falling. The use of broken pixels adds imperfection, enhancing the sense of unease typically associated with the emotions presented.
