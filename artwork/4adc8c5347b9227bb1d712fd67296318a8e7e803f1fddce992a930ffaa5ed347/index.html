Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy of love and betrayal through juxtaposed colors and patterns. The palette of warm pinks and reds contrasts with dark gray and maroon to evoke feelings of warmth and heartbreak. The main collision happens in the center where vibrant pink meets deep, dark maroon, representing the clash of love and betrayal. The layout mimics falling pixels, cascading down like tears, while the broken edges symbolize imperfections in relationships. The pattern mixes a grid suggestive of stability with displaced elements, creating tension and movement. Overall, the artwork captures the complexity of human emotions, oscillating between warmth and despair.
