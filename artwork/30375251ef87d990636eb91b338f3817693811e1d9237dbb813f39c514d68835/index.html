Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines the warmth of love and the sharp edges of betrayal. A palette of pink and dark maroon creates a tension that contrasts with softer grays. The layout suggests falling pixels, echoing the descent of emotions. The broken dots pattern conveys fragmentation, with subtle clashes of color mimicking misalignment. The main contrast resides in the interplay of soft pinks against the deeper maroons and grays, representing a split between warmth and pain, evoking a restless heart yearning for completeness.
