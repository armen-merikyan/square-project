Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines calmness and rage, embodied through a contrasting palette of reds and blacks with soft pale blues and stark whites. The arrangement represents the collision of emotions, where sharp, intense colors interrupt the softer space, suggesting an ongoing struggle between chaos and tranquility. The chosen layout mirrors calm and rage, with disruptions creating a tension that reflects the seed's essence. The edge style, resembling burned textures, emphasizes the depth of emotion through unexpected broken pixels, introducing subtle imperfections that mirror real emotional experiences.
