Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the juxtaposition of awe and terror, reflected through a palette of deep black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green. The top half represents awe with a staircase pattern, balancing warm accents against a cool, dark foundation. The bottom half channels terror through a flame-like chaotic dispersion of color, creating a visual tension. The central broken pixels symbolize fragmented memories and instability, while the layout's split emphasizes the dichotomy between order and chaos, creating a pulsating rhythm across the canvas.
