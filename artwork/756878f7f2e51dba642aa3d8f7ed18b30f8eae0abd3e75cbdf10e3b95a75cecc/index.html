Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional dichotomy of envy and gratitude, capturing their inherent tension. The chosen palette of yellows and deep blues represents the warmth of gratitude contrasting sharply with the colder tones of envy. The arrangement maintains a central focus, with soft edges embodying the delicate interplay of feeling. Clusters of muted colors represent the noise of envy, while light and vibrant colors offer bursts of gratitude, generating a rhythmic flow from the edges toward the center, creating a balance of structure and chaos.
