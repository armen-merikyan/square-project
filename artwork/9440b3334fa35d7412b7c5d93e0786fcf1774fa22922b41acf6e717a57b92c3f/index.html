Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional dichotomy between confusion and clarity, illustrated by a stark palette of deep navy and muted blacks, intersected with brighter dim cyan notes to signify moments of clarity piercing through chaos. The pattern employs a crosshatch approach, reflecting overlapping feelings and a tension between structured chaos and calm. The layout suggests rising hope through visual ascent, yet disruptions occur in the form of broken pixels, indicating unresolved emotions. The overall composition captures the push-and-pull of clarity emerging against entanglement, mirroring the internal struggle of the seed's emotional landscape.
