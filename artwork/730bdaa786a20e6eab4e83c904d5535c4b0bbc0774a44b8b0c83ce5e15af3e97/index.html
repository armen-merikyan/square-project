Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between love and betrayal, where the warm pinks and reds symbolize love while darker maroons and grays indicate betrayal. The collision is represented by diagonal lines and crosshatched patterns that fight for dominance, creating a deep contrast between soft and sharp edges. The arrangement introduces interruptions and echoes of emotion, highlighting the struggle between the warm and cool colors, manifesting the duality suggested in the seed.
