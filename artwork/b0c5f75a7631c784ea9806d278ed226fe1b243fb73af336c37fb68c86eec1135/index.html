Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of joy and grief, with vibrant yellows and oranges contrasting with deep blues and muted purples. This palette embodies a warm vs. cool tension, where the left side suggests joy through bright, structured patterns, while the right embodies grief with darker, fragmented stripes. The central stair-step shape creates a dynamic shift from order to chaos, emphasizing the collision of these conflicting emotions, evoking both safety and threat within the abstract space.
