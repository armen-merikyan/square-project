Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition reflects the duality of fear and hope, represented through contrasting dark and light colors. The palette, featuring deep blacks and soft golds with touches of violet and green, embodies the gravity of fear in jagged edges while allowing moments of hope to shine through as small, concentrated bursts. The arrangement prioritizes a sharp central contrast between the dark, heavy corners and the soft, radiating pixels, creating an evocative tension between protection and vulnerability. This layout visually narrates a cycle of rising signals against the backdrop of brokenness, illustrating the coexistence of these emotions, with subtle pixel imperfections enhancing the rawness of the experience.
