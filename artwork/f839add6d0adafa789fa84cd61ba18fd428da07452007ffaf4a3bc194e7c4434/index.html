Artist Statement

The emotional tone merges the energy of 'spark' with the decay of 'ash', embodying tension between vitality and fading. The palette of hot coral and blood orange injects warmth amid the reflecting coolness of charcoal and hollow blue, suggesting a battleground of life and quietude. The arrangement reflects diagonal arguments, where bright dots representing sparks confront pulse marks of ash, achieving collision that commands attention in the center of the square, surrounded by darker accents that threaten to consume the lighter elements, echoing the theme of resilience against erasure.
