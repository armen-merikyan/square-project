Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between pressure and quiet. The palette includes soft off-whites and faint peaches to evoke a quiet space, contrasted by deep dark navy and compressed red representing pressure points. The main collision occurs where the bright peach cluster interacts with the dark navy, illustrating a push-pull sensation. The patterns of spark and patchwork embody the clash of feelings while maintaining an abstract harmony, shaped by the seed's notions of pressure and clarity crossing.
