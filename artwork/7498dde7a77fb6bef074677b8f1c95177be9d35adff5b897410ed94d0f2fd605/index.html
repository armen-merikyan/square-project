Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between trust and suspicion by utilizing a diagonal layout where trust in soft greens and creams occupies the lower left, while suspicion in deep olives and accentuated shadows fills the upper right. The chosen palette evokes a sense of contrasting warmth and coolness, embodying security with the lighter tones and tension with darker elements. The collision occurs at the diagonal edge, creating an echo of uncertainty against a soft glow, illustrating the struggle between hope and doubt, framed within sealed edges for a stark boundary.
