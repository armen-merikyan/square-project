Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dichotomy of defiance and submission. The deep navy and black serve to create a weighty atmosphere, representing submission, while the lighter, dim cyan colors evoke defiance and resolve. The pattern reflects a falling motion, echoing the conflict between openness and closure. The broken pixels add an element of imperfection, suggesting the unpredictability of emotional experiences. The primary contrast occurs between densely packed dark colors and the scattered lighter ones, generating a push-and-pull effect that mirrors the inner struggle represented in the seed.
