Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between defiance and submission, where bright accents punctuate the darker tones, signifying both a push against complacency and a pull towards acceptance. The chosen palette, with strong greens and a warm honey gold, communicates growth and hope amidst turmoil. The central collision occurs in the middle of the square, where chaotic glitch patterns (defiance) clash against the more structured target patterns (submission), reinforcing the interplay of opposing emotions. This tension is amplified by the small areas of broken pixels, which serve as subtle reminders of imperfection in both struggle and surrender. The layout spirals towards the center, symbolizing the inward conflict that heightens pressure, but leaves ample negative space to allow for breathing room, echoing the complexity of human emotion.
