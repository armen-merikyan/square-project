Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the interplay between feelings of pressure and quietude. The selected palette consists of muted tones—off-white and dust gray represent quiet moments, while compressed red and dark navy evoke deep pressure. The main contrast occurs diagonally, where the compressed red meets faint peach, symbolizing conflict amidst stillness. The layout creates a spiral motion where dense clusters shift into quieter spaces, evoking a sense of ebb and flow, thus reflecting the seed's theme of fluidity versus rigidity.
