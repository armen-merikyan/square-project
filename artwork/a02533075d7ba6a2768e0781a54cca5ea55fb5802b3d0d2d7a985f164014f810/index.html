Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the duality of joy and grief, portrayed through juxtaposition of bright yellows and muted purples. Colors like #F2C94C and #E6E6FA signify joy with lightness, while deeper tones like #4B3D85 represent the heaviness of grief. The arrangement features a left-weighted composition where the bright hues pulse against darker colors, creating visual tension. The occupancy of broken pixels introduces an element of imperfection, symbolizing the unresolved nature of love and betrayal. The grid pattern represents a stable foundation, interrupted by the scattered dots of grief, inviting contemplation.
