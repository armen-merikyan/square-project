Artist Statement

This artwork suggests a tension between panic and control through contrasting color placement and patterns. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold evokes a sense of ancient intensity, where the warmth of the colors is juxtaposed against deeper shadows, representing chaos in a controlled environment. The placement creates an echo of order versus chaos through a mirrored but broken layout, accentuating the emotional push and pull suggested by the seed. The soft edges add to the sense of disruption, allowing for a fluid transition from calm to clash in this abstract narrative.
