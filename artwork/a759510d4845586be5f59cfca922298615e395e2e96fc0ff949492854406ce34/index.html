Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between vibrant desire and muted restraint. The palette features warm accents of 'hot coral' and 'blood orange' representing desire, contrasted by darker tones of 'charcoal' and softer 'hollow blue' to signify restraint. The main collision occurs in the center where saturated colors invade muted ones, illustrating the conflict. The shatter and maze patterns intertwine to evoke a dynamic tension between freedom and constraint, creating a narrative of emotional push and pull.
