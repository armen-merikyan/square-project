Artist Statement

This composition embodies the push and pull between desire and restraint, where vibrant reds and oranges burst from the edges, encapsulating the warmth of yearning. The muted creams and grays anchor the piece, providing a steady counterbalance that reflects restraint. The layout features a pressure corner where bright colors collide with softer tones, creating an intentional disruption that suggests tension. The checker pattern of desire contrasts sharply with the radial restraint, capturing the essence of emotional conflict inherent in the seed's narrative.
