Artist Statement

The composition juxtaposes intense emotional tones of defiance and submission, creating a dynamic interplay. The vibrant coral and blood orange evoke defiance, while the subdued charcoal and hollow blue symbolize submission, creating tension. The collision occurs at the center, with dense, warm tones battling against cooler, more subdued colors, reflecting the push and pull between these two emotions. This abstract pattern narrates a story of conflict through color placement and density variations.
