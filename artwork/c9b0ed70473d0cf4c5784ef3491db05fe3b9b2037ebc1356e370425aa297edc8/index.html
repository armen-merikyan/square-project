Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional contrast between vulnerability and strength. The palette, dominated by soft muted colors like off-white and dust gray, communicates vulnerability, while deeper hues such as dark navy signal strength. The arrangement features a fractured layout where a pale internal space is encircled yet intermittently broken by darker colors, suggesting the tension between exposure and shelter. The broken frame serves to highlight the ongoing struggle between protection and openness, with pixel irregularities representing moments of disruption and imperfection. The overall effect is one of quiet introspection paired with underlying strength.
