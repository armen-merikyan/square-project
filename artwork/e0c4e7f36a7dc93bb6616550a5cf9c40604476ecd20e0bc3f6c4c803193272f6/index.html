Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between joy and grief, showcasing bright yellows and oranges for joy against deeper blues and muted purples signifying grief. The left columns have a structured frame-like pattern representative of joy, while the right introduces a staggered staircase of grief, creating an interruption in flow. The central bridge shape suggests a connection yet maintains an off-center balance, enhancing the feeling of emotional conflict. Broken pixels add a layer of imperfection, echoing the rawness of these feelings.
