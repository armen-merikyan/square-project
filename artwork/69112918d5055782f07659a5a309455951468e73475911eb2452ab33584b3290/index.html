Artist Statement

This piece embodies the contrasting emotions of love and betrayal. The chosen palette of warm pinks and deep maroons creates a tension that reflects the heart's dual nature. The layout descends, resembling the unpredictable flow of grief and joy. Patterns of dots and radial arrangements interact, with moments of chaotic interruption signifying betrayal amidst the softer warmth of love. The broken pixels suggest imperfection and fragility, echoing the challenges found within deep emotional connections. This abstract composition transcends literal representation, focusing instead on feelings and emotional intersections.
