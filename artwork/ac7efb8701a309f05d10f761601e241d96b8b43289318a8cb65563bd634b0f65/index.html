Artist Statement

This composition captures a dialogue between longing and resolve through contrasting hues and fragmented patterns. The palette features intense colors like electric blue and hot magenta, evoking urgency and vibrancy, while static gray provides a quiet space, enhancing the emotional pull of the artwork. The collision occurs at the center, where glitch-like patterns of longing intersect with fault lines of resolve, emphasizing a dynamic tension. The use of broken pixels expresses both damage and repair, creating a narrative of transformation and continuity.
