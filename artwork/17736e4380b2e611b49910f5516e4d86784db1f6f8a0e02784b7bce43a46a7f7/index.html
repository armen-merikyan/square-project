Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the tension between loneliness and connection. The selected palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green contrasts warm and cool emotions, embodying the push and pull of feelings. The main contrast occurs at the center, where darker colors clash with vibrant accents, portraying a collision of emotions in a rhythmic outward flow. The layout creates a feeling of tension around a central point, emphasizing the quieter edges against denser clusters, reflecting the complex interplay of the seed's themes.
