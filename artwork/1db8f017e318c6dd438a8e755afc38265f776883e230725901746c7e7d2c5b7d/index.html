Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic tension between longing and resolve, using warm coral and soft amber to evoke warmth and intimacy, contrasted with cold slate and blue-black which evoke a sense of distance and introspection. The core collision occurs at the center of the grid, where glitches disrupt the smooth constellations of longing, creating visual tension. This pattern reflects the journey of repair amidst damage, with broken pixels symbolizing imperfections that enhance the narrative of both tension and flow.
