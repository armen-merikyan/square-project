Artist Statement

This composition evokes a tension between longing and resolve, where warm coral and soft amber convey a sense of yearning and intimacy, while cold slate and blue-black represent strength and stability. The layout indicates two pulsating centers that pulse toward each other, separated by quiet, negative space. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions within a structured radial pattern, hinting at both repair and damage, mirroring the dual nature of the emotional journey described by the seed. The contrast of quiet space against vibrant colors creates depth, suggesting underlying narratives of connection and alienation.
