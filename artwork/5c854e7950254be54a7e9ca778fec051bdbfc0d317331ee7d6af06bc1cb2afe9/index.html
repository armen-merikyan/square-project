Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the dance between certainty and curiosity: the upper half is dense with structured dots in moss green (#4A7C2E) and bone white (#E5D6C2), conveying a sense of ordered clarity. The bottom half introduces a grappling grid of rust (#9A5B42) and stone gray (#7C7B7A) that disrupts the calm. This collision highlights the tension of curiosity fighting against rigid boundaries. The palette implies resilience (greens) meshed with hints of fragility (grays and rusts) creating rhythm and flow, disrupted by intentional broken pixels, evoking a sense of unease and exploration. The layout fosters a visual journey from a controlled top to chaotic lower, with a quiet space accentuating the interactions.
