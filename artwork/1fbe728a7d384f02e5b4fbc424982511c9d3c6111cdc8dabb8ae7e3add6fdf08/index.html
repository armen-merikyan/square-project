Artist Statement

This abstract composition captures the emotional tension between 'quiet' and 'pressure'. The chosen palette of off-white, dust gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach represents the soft and muted qualities of quiet, contrasted against the dense and imposing presence of pressure. The darker colors cluster predominantly in the top left, simulating pressure, while lighter colors fall outward, suggesting release. The central collision between the sharp reds and the soft peach creates a dynamic balance, embodying both tension and release, as inspired by the seed terms that evoke confusion and clarity.
