Artist Statement

The composition balances the warm emotional gravity of love against the stark rawness of betrayal. Utilizing a palette that reflects passion (reds and pinks) juxtaposed with the somber tones of gray and dark maroon, it captures the tension between heart and fracture. The arrangement emphasizes an inner urgency: warm shades dominate the left, suggesting connected love, while cooler, darker shades on the right convey the weight of betrayal. The broken pixels introduce a layer of imperfection, resonating with the themes of fragmentation and emotional turmoil, while maintaining enough negative space to reflect unresolved feelings.
