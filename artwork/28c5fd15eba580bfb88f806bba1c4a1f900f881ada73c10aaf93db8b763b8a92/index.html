Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between certainty and curiosity, embodied by structured patterns colliding with more fluid, irregular forms. The selected palette of deep blue, muted purple, and warm yellows evokes feelings of both calmness and anxiety, representing the pull between organized thought and chaotic exploration. The main visual collision occurs in the central rows, where the jagged edge patterns of certainty intersect with the ladder-like motifs of curiosity, creating a tension between order and curiosity that resonates throughout the piece.
