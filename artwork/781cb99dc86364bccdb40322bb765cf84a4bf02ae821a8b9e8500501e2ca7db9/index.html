Artist Statement

This piece embodies a juxtaposition of dread and relief through a stark color palette that captures emotional intensity. The choice of dark maroon and gray creates heavy, foreboding areas, while the lighter pink injects moments of unexpected brightness, suggesting relief. The layout mirrors itself, presenting a balance disrupted by strategically placed 'broken pixels' that introduce an asymmetrical tension. The spark pattern against a checker pattern encapsulates the conflict between calmness and tumult. Main collision points arise in the juxtaposition of dark versus light, where the flow bursts across the canvas, leading the viewer's eye through peaks and valleys of emotional weight.
