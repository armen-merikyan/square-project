Artist Statement

This composition navigates the emotional dichotomy of loneliness and connection. The palette of electric_blue, lime, magenta, and black creates a tension between vibrancy and dark quietness. The top-weighted arrangement with dark colors at the corners evokes the feeling of being surrounded yet isolated, while bursts of bright colors at the center suggest moments of connection and warmth. The broken pixels introduce chaos within order, symbolizing hidden emotions. Ultimately, the piece embodies a narrative of strife and desire, mirrored sides with intentional disruption, creating an echo of the emotional struggle.
