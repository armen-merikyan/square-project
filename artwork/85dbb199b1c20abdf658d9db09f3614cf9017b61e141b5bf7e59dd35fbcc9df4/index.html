Artist Statement

The emotional tension of envy versus gratitude is captured through contrasting dark and light colors. The palette of soft gold and pale green shares warmth and calmness, while black and dark violet introduce weight and intensity. The placement of colors in zigzag patterns suggests movement and interruption, echoing the internal struggles of light and weight. The arrangement has broken pixels to symbolize imperfections, reflecting how contrasts aren't perfect but create a rich emotional landscape.
