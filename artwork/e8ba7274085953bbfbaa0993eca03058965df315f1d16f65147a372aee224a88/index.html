Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette transitions from dark, rich colors to lighter, softer hues, embodying a journey from isolation (black and dark violet) to hope (soft gold and pale green). The collision occurs in the center, where bold, broken pixels disrupt a halo of quiet, creating a contrast between the steady rise of resolve and the whispering gaps of longing. The layout mirrors a rising signal, symbolizing the struggle between upward hope and downward chaos, with negative space enhancing the feeling of both longing and intimacy.
