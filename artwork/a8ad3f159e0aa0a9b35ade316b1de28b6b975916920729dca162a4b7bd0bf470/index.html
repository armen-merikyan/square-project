Artist Statement

The emotional tone is derived from the duality of love and betrayal. The palette, with warm hues of pink and dark maroon, embodies love and its complications, while grays convey a sense of betrayal. Contrast arises from the sharp edges of dark colors meeting softer pinks, resulting in a visual tension that simulates the emotional conflict. The arrangement resembles a broken symmetry, with textured hints of orderly patterns interrupted by chaotic bursts, reflecting the underlying themes of confrontation and resolution in relationships.
