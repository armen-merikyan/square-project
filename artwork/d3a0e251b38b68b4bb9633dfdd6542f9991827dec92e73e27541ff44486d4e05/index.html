Artist Statement

The emotional tone is rooted in the interplay of fleeting sparks of energy against the muted remnants of ash, suggesting a tension between vibrancy and decay. The palette consists of sepia, tan, and faded blue—reflecting warmth in the sparks and coolness in the ash. The main collision occurs centrally where bright tones disrupt the ashen fields, creating sharp contrast and tension. This layout showcases the erosion of symmetry as the colors bleed into each other, illustrating resilience amid fragility. Each pixel placement engages with themes of memory and erasure, inviting the viewer to absorb a disjointed narrative of contrast.
