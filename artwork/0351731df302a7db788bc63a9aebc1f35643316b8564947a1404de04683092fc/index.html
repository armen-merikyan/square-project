Artist Statement

This piece contrasts the legible 'signal' amidst the chaotic 'noise.' The palette includes a vibrant green to symbolize the clean pulse interspersed with electric blue and hot magenta to represent the disruptive echoes of noise. The center of the grid features a dense layering of the signal colors, while the edges are left more muted, reflecting a struggle against containing the turbulence. The interplay of straight horizontal and broken vertical patterns reflects a memory of coherence disrupted by interference, dominating the central space while allowing quieter tones to linger at the borders, suggesting a quiet persistence against overwhelming chaos.
