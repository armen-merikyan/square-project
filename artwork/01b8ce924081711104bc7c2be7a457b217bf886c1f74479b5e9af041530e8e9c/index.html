Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a contrast between longing's expansive reach and the resolute boundaries of resolve. The palette invokes 'ancient fire' with burnt orange and dark red to symbolize warmth and tension, while charcoal adds depth and whispers of empty spaces. The collision of the patterns unfolds primarily in the center, where a patch of brighter pixels ('spark') disrupts the denser noise of longing, suggesting an attempt to mend or bridge emotional gaps. The 'scattered repair' layout reflects this striving for resolution amidst chaos, leaving intentional gaps to symbolize both intimacy and distance. The composition evokes a visual dialogue of conflict and harmony, echoing the seed's themes without literal representation.
