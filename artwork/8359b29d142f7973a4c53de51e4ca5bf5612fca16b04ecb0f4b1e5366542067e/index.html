Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends loneliness with connection, evoking a sense of yearning amidst a protective structure. The color palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue encapsulates memories, where warm tones represent connections and cool tones signify isolation. The primary contrast occurs at the center with warm colors clustered, colliding against cooler hues on the edges, creating visual tension. The layout suggests rising hopes and an upward struggle, while stripe patterns denote isolation yet allow for calm flow, resulting in a dynamic yet melancholic abstract conversation.
