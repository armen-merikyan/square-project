Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between love and betrayal, creating a complex interplay of warmth and coolness. The palette includes warm pinks and reds as representatives of love, contrasted against deep gray and dark maroon to evoke feelings of betrayal. The main contrast occurs in the center, with the warm maze pattern symbolizing love disrupted by the chaotic noise pattern around, indicative of betrayal. This blend cultivates a visual representation of emotional collision, echoing the dynamics within the seed.
