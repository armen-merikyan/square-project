Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece encapsulates the dissonance between confusion and clarity, conveyed through color collisions and rhythmic patterns. The palette, drawn from muted sepia and dusty blues, evokes memories of a fractured reality, embodying a sense of layered density. Contrasts arise primarily between darker shapes that disrupt the lighter fields, creating a tension reflective of unresolved emotions. The composition is intentionally asymmetric, with broken pixels mimicking the imperfection of thought processes, fostering a feeling of instability against a backdrop of structured emptiness.
