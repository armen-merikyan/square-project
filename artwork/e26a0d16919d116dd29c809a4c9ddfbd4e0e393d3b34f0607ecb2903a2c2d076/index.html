Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette of soft pinks and deep maroons represents vulnerability and warmth, while the gray adds weight and a feeling of isolation. The outer edges echo loneliness with a chaotic crack pattern representing fragmented connections, contrasting with the smoother, grid-like inner core that symbolizes the desire for connection. The arrangement creates a sense of pressure, enhancing the interplay between hidden feelings and their exposure, suggesting both the burden and beauty of social ties.
