Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional polarity of confusion vs clarity through a grid pattern that simulates controlled chaos. The palette of deep navy and stark black, contrasted by single accents of white and dim cyan, creates a tension that reflects the push-and-pull of clarity amidst confusion. The contrast lies within the layout, where one side leans towards order and the other distorts it through broken pixels, ensuring that the viewer feels the struggle between symmetry and fracture. Every pixel placement echoes the thematic duality presented in the seed.
