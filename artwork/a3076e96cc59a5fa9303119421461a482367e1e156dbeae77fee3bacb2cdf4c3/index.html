Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between clarity and static. The dominant colors are 'signal_green' and 'electric_blue,' which create a sense of hope and readable rhythm, representing the signal. In contrast, 'static_gray' and 'hot_magenta' disrupt this clarity, embodying the noise. The main collision occurs in the center, where these colors intersect, creating a dynamic flow upwards, symbolizing aspiration and continuity amidst distraction. The structure is intentionally broken, suggesting imperfection and interruption, mirroring the struggle between memory and erasure. Negative space is maintained to avoid overwhelming density, allowing viewers to grasp the pulse against the chaos.
