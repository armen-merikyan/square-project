Artist Statement

This artwork explores the juxtaposition of vulnerability and strength through a palette that balances deep, somber hues with delicate, bright accents. The dark colors represent the weight of vulnerability, while subtle glimmers of soft gold illustrate the strength found within exposure. The flow of diagonal lines implies a rising tension, where the darker colors dominate the background, leaving interruptions by lighter shades that symbolize fleeting moments of clarity amidst chaos. The broken pixels add a layer of imperfection that enhances the organic feel of this emotional battle. The overall structure reflects a central collision where the strongest hues meet the lightest accents, creating a visual tug-of-war between the two emotional states.
