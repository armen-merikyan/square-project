Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional duality of loneliness and connection through color contrast and spatial arrangement. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green creates a visual dialogue of fear and hope, with black symbolizing isolation and dark violet representing a deep, hidden weight. The center is burdened with darker shades, depicting loneliness, while the surrounding colors engage in a more vibrant array, suggesting connection. The ‘tear’ shape is abstractly represented in the center with the black core, while bursts of soft gold and pale green denote moments of connection spilling into the periphery. The top-heavy layout further emphasizes the weight of loneliness, creating a tension that invites the viewer to reflect on the contrast between hidden emotions and bursts of light.
