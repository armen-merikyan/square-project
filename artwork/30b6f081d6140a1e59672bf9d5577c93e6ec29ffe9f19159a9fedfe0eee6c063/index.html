Artist Statement

This abstract composition contrasts the emotional states of panic and control, using a palette that embodies warmth (yellow, orange) against cool tones (deep blue, muted purple). The control pattern, represented by sharp, crack-like structures in the center, clashes with the chaotic rain-like edges, creating a dynamic tension. The central squares are densely packed, symbolizing control's assertiveness, while the outer layers bleed into less structured forms suggesting the impending chaos of panic. The layout captures a battle between stability and disorder, evoking a compelling emotional resonance.
