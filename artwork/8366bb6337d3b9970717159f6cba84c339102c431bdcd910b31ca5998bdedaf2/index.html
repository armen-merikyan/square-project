Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes tenderness and rage, reflected by the palette choices of saturated reds symbolizing anger against a backdrop of muted blues suggesting calm. The top four rows embody 'rage' through a flood pattern of deep reds and blacks, illustrating a heavy, aggressive presence, while the bottom four rows represent 'tenderness' with dot patterns in pale blues and whites, implying safety and gentleness. The main collision occurs centrally, as the saturated tones bleed into the softer colors, suggesting a complex emotional interplay.
