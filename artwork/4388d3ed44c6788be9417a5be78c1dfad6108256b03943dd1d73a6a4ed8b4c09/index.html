Artist Statement

The emotional tone suggests a struggle between control (measured, framing colors) and surrender (fluid textures). The palette of cosmic indigo and cold gold evokes a sense of awe and tension, with deep teal providing depth amidst a soft void black that symbolizes the constraints of control. The main collision occurs at the center where the structured frame of control meets the soft rain-like patterns of surrender, creating a dynamic interplay that embodies the essence of pressure versus release.
