Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between restlessness and peace through its color choices and arrangement. The palette, inspired by 'awe_terror,' features cosmic indigo and void black creating a stark contrast against the bright star white, invoking the emotional pull between calm and chaos. The scattered repair layout showcases bright, patchwork areas that signify moments of peace amidst a turbulent background, emphasizing the layered memory aspect of motion. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, highlighting imperfections and adding an element of realism to the abstract narrative.
