Artist Statement

This square explores the emotional tension between restlessness and peace through contrasting color placements and patterns. The palette of soft cyan, lavender gray, and muted plum offers a sense of melancholy while allowing patches of bright white to signal moments of clarity and disruption. The outer edges, represented by a muted palette, evoke peacefulness, while the inner core of crosshatched textures suggests an unsettled motion. The main collision occurs in the center where bright elements of white and soft cyan disrupt the dark lavender, creating a focal point of dissonance and reflection, demonstrating the tension of the themes explored in the seed.
