Artist Statement

This composition evokes the tension between sudden brilliance and settled quietness, embodying the emotional shades of 'spark' and 'ash.' The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue captures the remnants of warmth against a backdrop of memory. The primary collision occurs in the upper left corner, where the vibrant patchwork colors clash with muted tones, creating a dynamic visual rhythm. This arrangement embodies the narrative of resilience amidst erasure, where clusters of brightness interrupt the overall dimness, mirroring the themes of life against decay.
