Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between trust and suspicion, symbolized through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, rooted in earthy tones like sepia and dust_gray, captures a nostalgic yet fragile memory, reflecting the uncertainty embedded in relationships. The main collision of emotional tones occurs at the central axis, where a soft, glowing tan intersects with a darker, muted gray band, representing the duality of feelings. The presence of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, enhancing the narrative of resilience amidst fragility. The overall composition creates an impression of pulsing energies, with trust emanating outward while suspicion lurks in darker recesses, echoing the complexities of human dynamics.
