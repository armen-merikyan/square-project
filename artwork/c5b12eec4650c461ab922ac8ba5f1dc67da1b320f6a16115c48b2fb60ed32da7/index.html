Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition navigates the tension between vulnerability and strength, aiming to depict a delicate balance. The palette, named 'gratitude_envy,' incorporates deep olive and honey gold to symbolize strength, while cream and shades of green represent vulnerability. The main contrast occurs at the center, where dense stripes of deep olive clash against thin barcodes of honey gold, suggesting a battle between heavy and light emotions. Broken pixels throughout the grid create intentional imperfections, echoing the theme of fracture amidst strength, with sections of negative space woven through to emphasize the unresolved nature of this conflict.
