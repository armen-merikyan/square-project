Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between restlessness and peace through a juxtaposition of warm and cool tones. The palette blends soft coral and warm white to create a sense of calm while using sharp crimson and carbon black to signify upheaval. The main collision occurs in the center, where the saturated colors grapple against softer edges, representing the battle between emotions. The diagonal flow enhances the feeling of motion and disruption, while the broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, reflecting the chaotic nature of memory and motion.
