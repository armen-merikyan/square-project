Artist Statement

The piece contrasts the feelings of loneliness and connection by using a palette that oscillates between deep black and vibrant red, creating a dynamic tension. The palette symbolically represents the weight of loneliness (black) against the warmth of connection (red). The layout features asymmetry and broken patterns, suggesting the invasion of connection amidst the pervasive loneliness. The introduction of sparse bright dots on a darker background illustrates fleeting connections, while broken pixels add to the imperfection of memory, echoing the theme of disruption and reflection. This contrast culminates at the central area, where the colors collide, amplifying the emotional narrative.
