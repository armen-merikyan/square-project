Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional conflict between panic and control. The left side, housing cooler tones, represents panic through a split-screen pattern that suggests chaotic energy. The dark blues and muted purples dominate this area, creating a sense of heaviness. In contrast, the right side portrays control with bursts of warm yellows and oranges in a radial pattern, radiating outward and inviting calmness. The deliberate placement of broken pixels introduces imperfections, evoking a sense of uncertainty, while the sharp vs soft contrasts enhance the tension. The composition captures a narrative of seeking stability amidst turmoil, with color interactions depicting the struggle of opposing emotions.
