Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dialogue between 'signal' and 'noise.' The palette of deep navy and muted black interplays to evoke a sense of isolation and intensity, while dim cyan introduces fleeting clarity. The layout features broken frames where chaos seeps into structured rows, highlighting the collision at the center where rhythmic patterns collide with scattered noise, suggesting an emotional pulse attempting to thrive amidst static disturbances. The arrangement aims for a weighted dominance of noise, with intention behind broken pixels to symbolize fractures in communication.
