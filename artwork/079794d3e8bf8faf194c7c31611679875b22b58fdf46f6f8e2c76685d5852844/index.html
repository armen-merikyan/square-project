Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between restlessness and peace, reflected through a palette of rich, warm colors like pink and dark maroon, contrasted by subdued grays. The pixel arrangement suggests a flow mimicking the descent of memories, with a primary density on the left encouraging a sense of pressure versus lighter, spaced-out pixels on the right conveying serenity. The broken pixels introduce disruptions, representing emotional scars and interrupted thoughts, while the dominant arrangement emphasizes peacefulness with an echo of restlessness in the texture. The diagonal argument seen here creates visual tension, mirroring the struggle between conflicting feelings.
