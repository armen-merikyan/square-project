Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a delicate balance between strength and vulnerability, represented through contrasting colors and placement. The palette combines shelter green, warm brown, and open blue for a soothing yet dynamic effect, indicating safety amid exposure. The collision between thick and thin textures predominantly occurs in the center, showcasing stability amidst disruption. Broken pixels introduce elements of chaos and imperfection, echoing the ebb and flow of strength against the tide of vulnerability.
