Artist Statement

The composition explores the emotional conflict between joy (represented by bright yellows and oranges) and grief (embodied by deep blues and muted purples). The warm tones radiate outward in a radial wave, while the cooler colors create a crosshatch effect, suggesting a tension between expansion and constriction. The interplay of dense clusters and quiet spaces captures the essence of this duality, with a thin outline representing the fragility of emotion. The contrast reflects the break in symmetry, creating a layered narrative of growth through distress.
