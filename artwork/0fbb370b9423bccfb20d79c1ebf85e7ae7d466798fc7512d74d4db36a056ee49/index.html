Artist Statement

The emotional tone here reflects the tension between love and betrayal, expressed through a contrasting palette of warm pinks and dark maroons. The outer rows are predominantly dark, symbolizing betrayal, while the inner area uses warmer tones to suggest intimacy and love. This contrast is disrupted by imperfect placements, representing emotional conflict and unexpected turmoil. The use of shapes and patterns aligns with the seed by embodying the idea of a broken symmetry, where a harmonious love is marred by betrayal, creating an inner void amidst external turmoil.
