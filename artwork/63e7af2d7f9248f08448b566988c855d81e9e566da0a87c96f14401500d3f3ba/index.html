Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between control and surrender, demonstrated through contrasting colors and placements. A palette of guilt and forgiveness, represented by deep wine, soft gold, and warm ivory, embodies warmth and healing amidst heaviness. The dominant dark shades suggest containment, while lighter tones evoke release, with a subtle ebb of color creating narrative flow. The collision happens centrally, where deep wine and ash gray interact, providing tension. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, symbolizing leaks and disruptions in the emotional spectrum, effectively capturing the essence of the seed in an abstract context.
