Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between joy and grief through a palette of bright yellows and muted purples. The bright yellow in the top half radiates warmth and optimism, while the bottom half, with its deep blue and muted purple, conveys heaviness and sorrow. The design splits horizontally to symbolize the division of feelings, with bright flame-like patterns against darker, rain-like textures. The thin outlines enhance this contrast, while maintaining a sense of balance and structure amidst the emotional chaos.
