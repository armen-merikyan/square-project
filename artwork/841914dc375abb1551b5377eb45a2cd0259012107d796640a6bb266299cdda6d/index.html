Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a stark juxtaposition of relief and dread, illustrated through the use of the 'ancient_fire' palette. The burnt orange and gold introduce warmth, representing relief, while the dark red and charcoal convey the weight of dread. The arrangement features descending patterns, symbolizing a quiet cascade against the pressure of darker colors, capturing the essence of heaviness descending into lighter zones. The use of broken pixels adds imperfections, echoing the emotional struggles intertwined within the contrasting tones.
