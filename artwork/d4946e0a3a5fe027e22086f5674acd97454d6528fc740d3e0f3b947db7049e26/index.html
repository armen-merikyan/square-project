Artist Statement

The piece embodies an emotional journey from dense pressure to a release of quiet. The palette juxtaposes deep blues and soothing pastel tones against sharp, vibrant oranges and yellows, creating tension and release. Darker tones suggest the weight of emotion, while brighter accents introduce echoes of joy or relief, representing the collision and interplay of these feelings. The arrangement offers both clustered intensity and spaces of calm, reflecting the dynamics of a troubled mind finding resolution.
