Artist Statement

The piece embodies a struggle between clarity and confusion, using a palette that oscillates between the dark tension of black and dark violet and the hopeful softness of pale green and soft gold. The arrangement showcases broken symmetry and harsh contrasts, where the crispness of pale green interrupts the darker backgrounds, suggesting clarity emerging from chaos. Negative space is intentionally preserved, allowing the viewer to experience the weight of confusion against the fleeting clarity, culminating in unexpected pixel collisions that echo the emotional dichotomy suggested by the seed.
