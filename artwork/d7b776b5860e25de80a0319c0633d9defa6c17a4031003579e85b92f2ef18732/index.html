Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette includes lime and electric blue to represent vibrancy amidst a backdrop of black, echoing the quiet spaces of longing against the boldness of resolve. The contrast lies predominantly at the center where the colors collide, symbolizing the struggle between the two states. The stepwell and crack patterns interplay throughout, echoing the emotion of urgency against serenity, while broken pixels add intentional disruptions, enhancing the theme of a broken mirror. This arrangement reflects the complexity of emotions and the delicate balance of feelings in play.
