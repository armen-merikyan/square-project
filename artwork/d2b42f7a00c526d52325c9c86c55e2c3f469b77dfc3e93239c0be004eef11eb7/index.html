Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the interplay between joy and grief through the use of a warm and cool palette, representing the struggle and balance of opposing forces. The chosen colors, predominantly yellow and deep blue, signify the light and heaviness within each emotion. The contrast is accentuated by the color wave pattern, where brighter colors surge upward, symbolizing hope, while the darker hues form a grounded base, representing heaviness and memory. This dynamic creates a visual tension reflective of the emotional score, leading to a fragmented yet beautifully resonant abstract pattern.
