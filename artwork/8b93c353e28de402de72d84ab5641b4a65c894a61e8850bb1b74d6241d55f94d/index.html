Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes vulnerability with strength through contrasting colors. A palette of warm brown and shelter green creates a grounded yet exposed feeling, while cold white and pale skin signify fragility and openness. The flow spirals counterclockwise, suggesting a dynamic push-pull effect, with a structure that implies a channel dividing denser areas from lighter ones. The collision occurs at the center, embodying the core struggle of the seed's themes, leading to a rich interplay between dense patterns and fragmented calm spaces.
