Artist Statement

This abstract 8x8 artwork explores the tension between joy and grief, representing their emotional duality through contrasting colors and placements. The palette comprises vibrant yellows and oranges for joy, juxtaposed with muted blues and purples reflecting grief. The layout incorporates a small dense center resonating with joy, surrounded by chaotic grays and muted tones reminiscent of grief. This dynamic captures the sense of internal struggle, with bright sparks interrupting heavier fields, illustrating the complex relationship between these emotions. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, emphasizing the unpredictable nature of feelings.
