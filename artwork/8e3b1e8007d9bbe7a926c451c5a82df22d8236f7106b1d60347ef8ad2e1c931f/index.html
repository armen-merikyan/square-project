Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional contrast between emptiness and desire through the use of dark and light pixels. The palette, consisting of deep blacks contrasting with vibrant reds and pale blues, suggests warmth battling coolness, underlining the core conflict of the seed. The layout juggles vertical and horizontal flows as sparse and dense patterns collide within the 8x8 grid. The intentional placement of broken pixels introduces imperfections, creating a sense of fragmented desire amidst the backdrop of empty space, echoing loneliness and longing.
