Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the duality of confusion and clarity by employing a stark contrast between dark and light colors, reflecting the chaotic nature of confusion while hinting at clarity through structured organization. The thick outline embodies the boundaries of the confusion, while the falling pixels around the center convey a sense of disarray. The chosen palette of burnt orange and charcoal offers warmth and depth, with gold accents suggesting moments of clarity piercing through confusion. Each segment’s pattern evokes the feeling of a collapsing grid, balancing the space between dense clusters and quiet gaps, mirroring the tension of the emotional theme.
