Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional dichotomy between tenderness and rage through a pattern of contrasting colors and textures. The chosen palette of deep black and pale blue represents rage and calmness, respectively, while white adds a dimension of space and reflection. The arrangement features a dominant chaotic central structure (representing rage) that draws attention, surrounded by softer, muted tones representing tenderness. The main collision happens at the center of the grid, where jagged patterns collide with smoother transitions, signifying the emotional turmoil and dichotomy captured in the seed text.
