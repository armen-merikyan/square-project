Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between clarity and confusion. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green evokes an emotional landscape where darkness and light collide. The contrast is accentuated by a crosshatch pattern in the center, radiating outward with broken pixels that represent the struggle for symmetry amidst chaos. This interplay creates a visual story of fragments striving for harmony, revealing an abstract experience of striving for clarity within confusion.
