Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay between confusion and clarity, manifested in a checker-like pattern that contrasts sharp dark and soft muted colors. The 'broken_pixels' theme translates into a clash between focused sections and chaotic disturbances, where the darker shades represent confusion while the lighter ones symbolize clarity. The use of sepia and tan from the 'broken_memory' palette suggests nostalgia and warmth while maintaining a sense of instability. The placement creates a balance of tension and flow, reflecting the conflict inherent in the seed. This arrangement embodies the struggle between order and disorder, drawing the viewer into its emotional narrative.
