Artist Statement

This composition balances the emotional tension of longing and resolve. The chosen palette of 'guilt_forgiveness'—stain brown, ash gray, warm ivory, soft gold, and deep wine—evokes a sentiment of nostalgia and healing. The clear contrast between the dark broken pixels and the lighter warmer tones, particularly in the center, symbolizes the collision of longing's unfinished emotions with the firmness of resolve. The broken frame suggests vulnerability while also depicting the ongoing struggle to contain powerful feelings, making the piece resonate with both serenity and urgency.
