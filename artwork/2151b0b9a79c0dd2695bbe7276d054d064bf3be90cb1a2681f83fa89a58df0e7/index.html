Artist Statement

The emotional tone captured here oscillates between love and betrayal, creating a dynamic juxtaposition. The chosen palette of soft pinks, deep reds, and muted grays evokes warmth and passion while simultaneously hinting at underlying discord. The layout follows a mirror-broken design, reflecting a sense of symmetry disrupted by intentional misalignments, symbolizing emotional conflicts. The central tension lies in the interplay of dense clusters of warm colors against cooler shades, creating a visual echo of pressure and release. This arrangement suggests a narrative of conflicting feelings, where lines of affection are intertwined with shadows of doubt, mirroring the complexities of human emotions.
