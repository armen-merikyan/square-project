Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity (signal) and distortion (noise). The chosen palette of deep navy, black, and dim cyan evokes feelings of isolation amidst static, while the single bright white offers a stark contrast, emphasizing the struggle for clarity. The main collision occurs in the center, where the brighter signals vie for space against the dense, dark noise. This interaction suggests a narrative of memory and continuity against erasure, illustrating how a single pulse can survive amidst chaotic interference.
