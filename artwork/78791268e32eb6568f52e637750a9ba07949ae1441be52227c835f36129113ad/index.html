Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between joy and grief, using a palette of warm, vibrant yellows and calming deep blues to create emotional contrast. The upper half hints towards a spark of joy, represented by bright tones and a noise pattern that suggests movement and expansion. In contrast, the lower half incorporates muted purples and deeper blues with a dotted pattern, evoking the heaviness of grief. The split horizontal layout reflects the dual nature of emotions, while the rhythmic flow implies a cyclical nature of these feelings. Overall, the piece conveys a dance between light and dark, joy and sorrow, reminiscent of ancient flames flickering against the constraints of order.
