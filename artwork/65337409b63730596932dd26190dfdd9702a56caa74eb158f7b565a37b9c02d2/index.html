Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional conflicts of nostalgia and progress. The dark colors evoke a sense of depth and contemplation, representing nostalgia, while the sharper contrasts showcase the push towards progress. The layout balances serenity with tension, using a split horizontal division to create a dialogue between stillness and urgency. The scattered placement reflects both chaos and control, embodying the drip and weight described in the seed through variations of color density.
