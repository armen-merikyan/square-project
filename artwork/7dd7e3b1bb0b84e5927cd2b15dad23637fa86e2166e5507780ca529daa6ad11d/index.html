Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between love and betrayal, using a palette of warm reds and cool grays that signify warmth against coldness. The outer rows represent a chaotic, burning feeling of betrayal, while the inner rows reflect a more muted, softer love, each creating distinct zones that echo contrast. The central focus is disrupted, with sharp edges clashing against softer tones, hinting at a fractured relationship. The main collision occurs at the center, where dynamic patterns echo the struggle between protection and exposure. Overall, the design offers a fragmentary yet balanced exploration of the emotional seed, rendered abstractly through intentional color placements.
