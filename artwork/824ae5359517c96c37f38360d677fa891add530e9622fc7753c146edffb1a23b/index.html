Artist Statement

The composition expresses an emotional duality between 'panic' and 'control,' with the structured stripes representing control, and chaotic 'target' patterns embodying panic. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green reflects contrasts between fear and hope. The encounter between muted tones and bright accents creates tension, primarily at the center where chaos disrupts order, symbolizing conflict. This echoes the seed's themes of balance between structured control and overwhelming disorder.
