Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional duality of longing and resolve. The chosen palette of navy, black, and dim cyan evokes a sense of distant warmth tempered by clarity. The dense center of dark colors represents longing, contrasted by lighter segments that symbolize resolve. The pattern alternates between sharp edges and broken frames, creating a visual pulse that mirrors the emotional tension expressed in the seed. The central collision of color creates a visual threshold, echoing the constant push-pull between these contrasting feelings.
