Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts confusion and clarity, leading to a dynamic visual narrative. The palette of muted purples and deep blues conveys a sense of depth and complexity, while the vibrant accents of yellow and orange punctuate the chaotic sections, representing moments of clarity. The arrangement features symmetry in the outer frame and breaks into a chaotic center, illustrating the tension between order and disorder. The zigzag flow and broken dots pattern replicate the 'pulse', while irregularities introduce an emotional charge throughout the piece.
