Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances defiance and submission through contrasting elements. The palette of clean white, ink black, warning orange, ice blue, and violet gray creates visual tension, reflecting the struggle between order and chaos. The upper rows dominate with angular patterns in defiant colors, while the lower rows soften with serene tones, suggesting a quiet collapse. The collision occurs in the center, where colors meet and disrupt, embodying the tension of conflict and resolution. The introduction of broken pixels adds intentional imperfection, echoing the rawness of each emotional state. The overall arrangement creates a diagonal rise, enhancing the sense of upward movement against a backdrop of quiet.
