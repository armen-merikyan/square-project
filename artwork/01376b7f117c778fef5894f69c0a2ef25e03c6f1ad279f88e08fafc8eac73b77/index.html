Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between bursts of vitality (spark) and the stillness of recollection (ash). The palette of deep reds, dark plum, and muted creams evokes a sense of urgency against a quiet backdrop, creating a feeling of hunger clashing with restraint. The primary contrast occurs through a centralized explosion of the brighter colors that pierce through the subdued tones, embodying a conflict of memory versus erasure. The diagonal layout introduces a dynamic flow, encouraging viewer engagement as contrasting patterns of static (spark) and stepwell (ash) collide and resonate throughout the grid, maintaining an asymmetrical balance.
