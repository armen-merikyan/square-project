Artist Statement

The composition embodies the tension between love and betrayal through a contrasting palette of warm pinks and deep maroons, symbolizing passion and heartbreak. The layout follows an upward flow, representing hope, with dense clusters in the lower half interrupted by fragments of lighter hues suggesting a fractured feeling. The burned edges instill an imperfection, reinforcing the theme of betrayal amid warmth. Each broken pixel adds to the narrative of conflict and emotion, creating a visual essence that speaks to the unresolved nature of love.
