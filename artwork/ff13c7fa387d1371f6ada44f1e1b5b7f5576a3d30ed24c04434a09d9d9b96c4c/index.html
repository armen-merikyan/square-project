Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts longing and resolve, illustrated through a palette of rich greens and calming creams. The deeper colors represent the weight of longing while the lighter shades introduce a sense of clarity and resolve. The central collision occurs with a thick band of dark green intersected by flickers of yellow-green, suggesting longing amidst heightened anticipation. Broken pixels are applied within the dense areas, serving as interruptions that symbolize the repair vs. damage concept, creating visual tension in the composition. The layout is a 'quiet channel' which emphasizes negative space juxtaposed against dense clusters, embodying serenity affected by urgency.
