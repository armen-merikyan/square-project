Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional interplay of desire and emptiness, reflecting a struggle between warmth and coolness through color choices. The palette of electric blue and magenta contrasts with deep blacks, emphasizing the void against vibrant aspirations. The arrangement features a spiral flow, signaling rising desires from the bottom left while the cooler colors exert pressure from the top right, creating a visual tension. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, suggesting that even desires come with fragments of solitude. Overall, the density of dark colors predominantly occupies the corners, reflecting emotional weight, while the center’s vibrancy suggests hope and yearning, creating an unresolved narrative of longing.
