Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension of 'signal vs noise' through a palette of dark and muted colors contrasted by bright accents. The dominant black and navy represent noise, while the intermittent dim cyan suggests a fragile pulse of clarity attempting to break through. The scattered arrangement of colors creates a sense of conflict and interruption, reflecting the broken mirror concept. The asymmetry and broken pixels enhance the sense of disturbance, resulting in an emotional landscape that captures a moment of chaotic rhythm and fragmented communication.
