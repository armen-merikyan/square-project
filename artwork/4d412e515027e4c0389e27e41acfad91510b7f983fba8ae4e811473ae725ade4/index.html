Artist Statement

The emotional tone shifts between relief and dread, employing a palette of cool mint, soft sky, and heavy storm gray highlighted by faded gold. The main contrast occurs as the dense dark colors clash along the borders with lighter fields, creating a tension that suggests a battle between oppressive weight and the breath of relief. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, symbolizing fractures within the emotional narrative, while the arrangement leads the eye in a spiral, instilling a sense of chaotic order shaped by the seed of a bridge. This reflects the interplay of structure and chaos, leaving moments of negative space to evoke the necessary calm amidst pressure.
