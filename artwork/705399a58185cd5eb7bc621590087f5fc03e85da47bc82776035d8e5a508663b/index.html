Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between love and betrayal, represented through a palette of warm reds and cool grays. The rising flow suggests hope amidst uncertainty, while the broken edges symbolize the fragility of relationships. The grid pattern reflects a structured approach to love, contrasting with sporadic bursts of noise that evoke feelings of betrayal. The deliberate placement of colors creates a visual conflict that expresses a story of duality and emotional complexity.
