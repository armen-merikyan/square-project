Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between clarity (signal) and chaos (noise). The palette includes a 'signal green' for moments of clarity and an 'electric blue' to evoke a sense of urgency, contrasted sharply with 'dead black' and 'static gray' to represent interference and shadowy echoes. The upper left quadrant dominates with a smooth arrangement of green dots signifying the pulse of clarity, while the right side exhibits scarred arrangements of gray and magenta showing noise. The collision, led by jagged edges and broken pixels, occurs centrally, creating a striking interruption that draws the eye.
