Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of restlessness and peace by utilizing a warm color palette juxtaposed against cold tones, representing the ongoing tension. The main contrast occurs diagonally through the canvas, where warm coral and soft amber signify peace, while cooler shades like blue-black and cold slate suggest restlessness. Patterns of ring and thread overlap to evoke motion and memory, while intentional 'broken' pixels disrupt the flow, illustrating the friction between the two emotional states. This structure maintains a clear abstract narrative, where the quiet fields invite contemplation amid an underlying pulse of chaos.
