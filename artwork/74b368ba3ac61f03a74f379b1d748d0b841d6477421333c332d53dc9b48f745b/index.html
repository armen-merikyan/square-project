Artist Statement

This piece embodies a complex emotional dynamic between tenderness and rage. The palette juxtaposes saturated reds symbolizing anger against muted blues for calmness, creating an interplay of intensity and softness. The sharp diagonal flows introduce aggressive energy, clashing with quieter, static patterns. The central pixel bleed represents the internal struggle, while careful arrangements of color create a sense of tension and pressure. Key collisions occur at the center and edges, creating a vivid narrative of emotional conflict.
