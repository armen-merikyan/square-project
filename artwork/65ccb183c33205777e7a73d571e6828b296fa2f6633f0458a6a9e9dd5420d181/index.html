Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between quiet and pressure, utilizing a palette that evokes muted softness and deep saturation. The primary colors of dark navy and compressed red create a stark contrast, while off-white and faint peach provide a soft backdrop, highlighting the fragmented feel of the piece. The arrangement features a centered density of darker colors that gradually break apart into lighter hues towards the edges, symbolizing the struggle between pressure and release. The patterns of target and barcode overlap to create an interruption in flow, echoing the emotional themes laid out in the seed.
