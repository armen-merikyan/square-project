Artist Statement

The artwork encapsulates the contrast between the clean pulse of signal represented by vibrant green and blue, against the chaotic noise illustrated through dark grays and blacks. The central area is bright, symbolizing clarity amidst the pervasive interference around it. The use of broken pixels in corners represents moments of disruption, creating a layered, textured feel that reflects the emotional conflict of memory and interference. The palette emphasizes a balance between vitality and obscurity, letting the viewer feel the tension between clarity and chaos.
