Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress. The palette, inspired by 'ancient_fire', blends burnt orange, dark red, and charcoal to evoke warmth and depth, suggesting heritage contrasted by evolving ideals. The top half is stark and dark, representing a heaviness and weight of nostalgia, while the bottom half incorporates vibrant accents illustrating progress, reflecting a gradual rise in intensity. The arrangement is asymmetrical with soft edges, encouraging visual movement that spirals along a horizontal divide, emphasizing the collision between full and hollow spaces.
