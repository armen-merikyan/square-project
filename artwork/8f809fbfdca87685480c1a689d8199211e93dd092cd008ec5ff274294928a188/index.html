Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between fear and hope through contrasting dark and light tones, suggesting a struggle between weight and uplift. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green represents the dichotomy, with darker hues creating a maze-like pattern embodying fear, while golden hues burst through, offering glimpses of hope amidst pressure. The layout creates a split that echoes the emotional battlefield, and the arrangement of pixels dances between dense clusters and quiet spaces, emphasizing unresolved collisions of feeling. The broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, further enhancing the emotional depth.
