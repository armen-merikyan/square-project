Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The palette of dark violet and soft gold against black introduces a contrast between despair and hope, with a soft vibrancy suggesting clarity. The left columns feature broken dot patterns representing confusion, while the right presents a radial pattern, hinting at clarity. The bottom-weighted edge creates weight, symbolizing depth and pressure, with intentional broken pixels capturing the theme of imperfection. The arrangement juxtaposes carefully organized shapes with chaotic interference, manifesting the seed's exploration of emotional collision.
