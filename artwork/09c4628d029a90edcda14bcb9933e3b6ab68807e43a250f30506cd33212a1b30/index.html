Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a battle between defiance and submission. The selected palette of red oxide, blackened plum, muted cream, iron gray, and small orange represents raw intensity, softness, and restraint. The main contrast occurs at the center, where bold colors interrupt subdued tones, symbolizing the clash between assertive and yielding energies. The layout and flow suggest a diagonal rise across the canvas, representing an upward struggle against darker, chaotic interruptions, forming a dynamic visual tension.
