Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional contrast between softness and severity. The palette features muted tones of carbon black and steel gray that represent severity, juxtaposed with the gentle warmth of powder pink and warm white to signify softness. The grid layout is weighted towards the lower left, capturing a sense of bottom-weighted tension, while the interaction of broken frame and shatter patterns creates a visual noise that emphasizes the conflict between open and closed spaces. The central intersection of these colors serves as the main collision, symbolizing the struggle for forgiveness amidst feelings of guilt. This abstract arrangement evokes a sense of both pressure and release, interpreting the complexity of human emotion without literal representation.
