Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, using a color palette that juxtaposes dark, mysterious tones with softer, hopeful hues. The choice of deep blacks and dark violet suggests confusion, while soft gold and pale green introduce clarity and hope. The left half embodies a distorted mirroring, where similar colors clash into chaos, while the right half transitions into structured stripes, symbolizing clarity. Broken pixels strategically disrupt the symmetry, creating a sense of tension and depth, evoking conflicting emotions. This arrangement emphasizes the dialogue between the two emotional states while maintaining an overarching abstract quality.
