Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between trust and suspicion, using a color palette that balances softness with severity. Warm whites and powder pinks represent trust with their welcoming essence, while the carbon black and sharp crimson introduce suspicion through stark contrast. The broken pixels disturb the surface, echoing fragility amidst resilient structures paralleling the diagonal flow, creating a layered conflict. The central collision occurs in the vertical axis, highlighting the split between emotions.
