Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the push and pull between loneliness and connection, represented by an uneven distribution of color. The palette consists of black for emptiness, dark violet for introspection, soft gold as a symbol of connection, and pale green suggesting growth. The collision occurs in the center with soft gold, contrasting against the deep tones, embodying the tension between the innate desire for connection and the weight of solitude. Areas of negative space enhance this feeling, creating a tension that resonates with the seed's theme.
