Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between warmth and coldness, represented by love and betrayal. The palette features warm pinks and reds juxtaposed with grim gray and dark maroon, symbolizing the conflict. The left side utilizes radial patterns of love's warmth, while the right employs broken, ring-like patterns of betrayal, creating a striking contrast. This collision accentuates the emotional dissonance and layered complexities of the themes. The jagged edges further emphasize imperfection and tension, reminding of the crack between love and betrayal.
