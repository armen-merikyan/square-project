Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dichotomy of confusion and clarity through its structured chaos. The palette, grounded in deep blacks and soft golds, contrasts the dense textures in the center with the lighter hues at the edges, representing clarity emerging from confusion. The interplay between dark and light areas depicts both tumultuous collision and a hopeful escape, with the zigzag flow creating a sense of tension and movement. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, a nod to the 'teeth against broken dots' concept, while maintaining intentional negative space, allowing the viewer to contemplate the chaotic beauty of the arrangement.
