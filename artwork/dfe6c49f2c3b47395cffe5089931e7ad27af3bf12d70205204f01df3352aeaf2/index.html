Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between emptiness and desire through a juxtaposition of cool and warm tones. The defined palette combines navy and black with hints of dim cyan, creating depth and resonance while suggesting a quiet loneliness. The upward flow embodies a rising signal toward hope, while broken edges introduce an imperfection that echoes the fragility of desire. The collision between the dark, immersive navy and the lighter shades creates stark contrast, enabling a visual rhythm of ascent and descent. Each cluster of lighter pixels amidst the darker background represents desires puncturing through feelings of emptiness, resulting in a textured emotional landscape.
