Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between longing and resolve through a palette of warm rust and cool stone gray. The colors symbolize a push-pull dynamic, with rust representing desire and urgency, while bone white serves as a calming, serene backdrop. Broken pixels scatter throughout, creating intentional disruptions that echo the themes of repair and damage. The composition features a center focus where colors collide, suggesting a moment of conflict and resolution, embodied in a rhythm of falling pixels that mirror the downward flow of emotions, constantly in flux yet interwoven.
