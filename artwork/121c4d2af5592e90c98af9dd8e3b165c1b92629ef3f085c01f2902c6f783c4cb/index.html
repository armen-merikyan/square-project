Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the complexity of envy contrasted with gratitude. The use of dark, muted colors like navy and black in the top four rows symbolizes the weight and pressure of envy, invoking a dense and jagged feeling. The bottom four rows introduce lighter, echoing shades, representing gratitude and a sense of connection. The central arrangement enforces a tension between the two emotions, while the broken pixel strategy disrupts harmony, emphasizing life's imperfections. The palette’s symmetry and contrast encourage an exploration of collision and balance, ensuring a negative space that breathes amidst emotional weight.
