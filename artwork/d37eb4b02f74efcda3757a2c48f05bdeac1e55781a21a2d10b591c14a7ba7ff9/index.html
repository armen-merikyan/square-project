Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional interplay between peace and restlessness. The soft sky and faded gold colors represent peace, while storm gray and near black serve as symbols of restlessness. The diagonal flow introduces dynamic tension, as if a flame flickers against an unseen boundary. The central collision between radial and veil patterns signifies the constant tug between calmness and anxiety, creating a visual narrative that evokes both stillness and movement.
