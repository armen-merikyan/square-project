Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between awe and terror, utilizing a palette of deep navy and black contrasted with hinting soft whites and dim cyan. The central axis of the wave suggests an underlying order battling chaos, with diagonal flows creating a visual rhythm akin to falling pixels. The composition interrupts itself with irregular placements of brighter accents, representing fleeting moments of awe amid the encompassing weight of darker colors, allowing for deep contemplation yet provoking an unsettling unease.
