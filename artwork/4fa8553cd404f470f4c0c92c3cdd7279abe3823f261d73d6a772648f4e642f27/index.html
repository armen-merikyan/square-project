Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between restlessness and peace, embodied in the contrasting colors of sharp crimson and warm white versus deep, offset blacks and grays. The palette resonates with 'softness_severity', aiming to depict underlying tension through flowing patterns. The primary collision occurs in the center where warmer colors pulse, symbolizing a spark of life against the cold edges that represent isolation and anxiety. The placement of broken pixels introduces intentional flaws, reflecting memories of motion and unrest, enhancing the dialogue between calm and chaos in this abstract arrangement.
