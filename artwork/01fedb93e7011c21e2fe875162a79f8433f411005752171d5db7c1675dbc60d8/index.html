Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and confusion, embodied by the contrasting patterns of structured grids and the randomness of falling pixels. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of nostalgia while capturing the weight of distraction. The primary conflict emerges from the central concentration of darker colors amid lighter surroundings, creating a dynamic interplay that suggests both pressure and release. This composition aims to encapsulate the narrative of attempting to find order in chaos, mirroring the provided seed concepts through abstract representation.
