Artist Statement

The artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between 'spark' and 'ash,' utilizing a palette that emphasizes contrasts between warmth and coolness. Dark hues symbolize restraint, while bright accents signify moments of brilliance and vitality. The diagonal arrangement creates a visual flow that suggests conflict and resolution, embodying the concept of collision—the bright elements occasionally interrupt the dark fields, reflecting memory and erasure. The patterns—'target' and 'stepwell'—collide visually, evoking a sense of disruption yet cohesion. Broken pixels serve as moments of imperfection, creating a narrative of resilience amid fragility.
