Artist Statement

This composition explores the duality of fear and hope through contrasting patterns and colors. The palette shifts between deep dark violets and soft golds, encapsulating the emotional tension. The main contrast occurs with jagged edges against the hope-filled upward flow, creating a collision that mirrors the rising signal from a place of fear. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, reflecting the chaos within this emotional landscape, while the diagonal flow suggests an almost escape-like movement toward hope. The arrangement maintains sufficient negative space, symbolizing the silence often felt amidst emotional turmoil.
