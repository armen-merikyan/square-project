Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission, represented by bright accents surrounded by darker, muted fields. The palette centers on warm colors like hot coral and blood orange for a sense of urgency and chaos, while charcoal and hollow blue offer a backdrop of calmness. The main collision occurs centrally, demonstrating the struggle and interaction of these conflicting emotions, creating an abstract narrative of internal conflict. The uneven border and broken pixels symbolize the unpredictability of these feelings, suggesting scars and disruptions in the emotional landscape.
