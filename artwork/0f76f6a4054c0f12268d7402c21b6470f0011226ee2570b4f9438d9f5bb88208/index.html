Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines heated chaos with serene stillness, illustrated through a palette of intense red contrasts against soft blue and muted boundaries. The collision is centered, where bright reds flood into a calmer pale blue field, suggesting a struggle between overflowing emotion and restrained calm. This composition reflects the seed's themes by presenting broken, jagged red areas that interrupt a steady horizon of blue, marking the tension between containment and overflow.
