Artist Statement

This abstract composition reflects the emotional duality of awe versus terror through a controlled palette of faded hues. The left side features soft tan and sepia stripes symbolizing order and stability, while the right side disrupts this harmony with erratic placements of dust gray and faded blue, representing chaos and broken memories. The arrangement creates a vertical flow that evokes rising tension and erosion, with broken pixels for imperfection. The chosen colors contrast effectively, suggesting a narrative of conflict beneath a façade of calm.
