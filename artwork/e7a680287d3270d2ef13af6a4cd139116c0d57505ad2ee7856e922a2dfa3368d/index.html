Artist Statement

This abstract composition illustrates the emotional dialogue between control and surrender. The palette, derived from earthy greens and muted golds, evokes a sense of groundedness while allowing for a hint of vibrancy. The dark green dominates the canvas, representing control, while the lighter tones disrupt this solidity and symbolize surrender's gentle force. The central collision happens where dense layers of green meet the lighter golds, suggesting an interplay of pressure and release. The broken pixel element introduces intentional imperfections, reflecting the imperfections inherent in maintaining control. Overall, the arrangement captures the nuanced tension found in the struggle between restraint and freedom.
