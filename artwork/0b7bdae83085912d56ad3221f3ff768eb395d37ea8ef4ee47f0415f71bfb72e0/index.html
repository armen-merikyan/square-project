Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of restlessness against peace, represented through a palette that juxtaposes soft warmth with stark severity. The swirling patterns of pinks and whites suggest rippling moments of tranquillity, while the contrasting darks introduce elements of disruption and tension, evoking a sense of both memory and motion. The diagonal arrangement creates a flow reminiscent of patchwork, where calm and chaos collude without forming a recognizable shape. The intentional placement of broken pixels adds unique imperfections, which symbolize scars of memory. The predominant edge style enhances a sense of unsealed borders, heightening the emotional interplay between light and dark.
