Artist Statement

This artwork expresses the tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon contrasts light and weight, reflecting the emotional complexities of safety and exposure. The dominant gray hues create a muted atmosphere, interspersed with strikes of pink and red signifying strength. This collision occurs in the center, where the intensity of colors looms against wider spaces of calm, echoing feelings of exposed vulnerability. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, reflecting the fragility in the balance of strength and vulnerability.
