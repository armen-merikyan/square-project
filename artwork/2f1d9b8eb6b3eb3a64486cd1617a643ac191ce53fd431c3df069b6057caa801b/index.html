Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes envy and gratitude, creating a tension that flows from the contrasting patterns of dots and rain. The palette of reds and pale blues expresses the fiery nature of envy against the coolness of gratitude, with black representing the noise and chaos. The main collision happens at the center where the darker colors interrupt the softer blue, suggesting both rising and falling emotions. The empty room layout allows for quiet spaces to evoke contemplation, reinforcing the theme of internal conflict.
