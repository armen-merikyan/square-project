Artist Statement

The artwork explores the emotional dichotomy of loneliness and connection, embodying a tense interplay through color and form. The chosen palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon reflects warmth and tumult, tying closely to feelings of both affection and betrayal. The central pixels are dense and warm, represented by reds and dark maroons, contrasting against cooler gray tones that form an outer boundary, conveying a sense of isolation. Through the grid-like pattern of connection clashing with flame-like bursts of loneliness, the arrangement suggests a struggle between togetherness and solitude, allowing the viewer to feel the weight of connection amidst the vastness of disconnection. The broken edges and negative spaces hint at the fragility of these emotions.
