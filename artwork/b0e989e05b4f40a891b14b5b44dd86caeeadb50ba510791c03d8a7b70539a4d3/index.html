Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'joy vs grief' influences the palette, balancing warm yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples. The contrast is structured horizontally: one side exudes brightness and openness, suggesting joy, while the other embodies heaviness and fragmentation, reflecting grief. The palette conveys a stark emotional landscape, while the flow mimics a wave, moving from exuberance to a deep layered blues, creating a dynamic interplay of pressure. As directives are followed, broken pixels introduce fragmentation, emphasizing the struggle between harmony and chaos.
