Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between clarity and chaos. The palette utilizes 'signal_green' (#00FF00) for a central pulse, contrasted by 'static_gray' (#A9A9A9) representing noise. 'Electric_blue' (#0000FF) emerges as an accent, integrating with muted 'dead_black' (#000000) and 'hot_magenta' (#FF00FF) for interruption. The layout spirals inward, suggesting a movement towards structure amidst the turbulence, with intentional broken pixels that represent echoes of memory and interference. The core sits toward the center, acknowledging the struggle between signal and noise while maintaining space for contemplation.
