Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a struggle between defiance and submission. The palette blends cool blues and muted purples to express melancholy while introducing soft cyan for contrast. The central area bursts with darker colors, representing defiance, while the lighter hues on the edges convey submission. The quiet channel layout evokes a narrow pathway of conflict, where pressure and density meet delicate tranquility, interrupted by broken pixels that symbolize chaos and disruptions in order.
