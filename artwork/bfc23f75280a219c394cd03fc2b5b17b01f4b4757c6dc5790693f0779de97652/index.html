Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve, reflecting the push and pull of desire against certainty. The palette, inspired by the 'mourning_renewal' theme, employs deep blues for depth and reflection alongside the pale yellow, symbolizing flickers of hope amid darkness. The broken pixel elements disrupt the ordered structure, echoing the sentiment of imperfection and repair. The dominant areas of dark contrast with lighter patches create a visual heartbeat, while the near-symmetrical layout introduces a sense of balance interrupted by intentional flaws, suggesting the complexity of human emotions.
