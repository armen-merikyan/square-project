Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the conflict between love and betrayal through the use of warm and cold colors to create tension. The palette incorporates shades of pink and dark maroon to signify affection and pain, while gray signifies the emptiness of betrayal. The layout features an argument grid that juxtaposes soft rings reminiscent of love against fractured mirror patterns symbolizing betrayal. The main collision occurs in the center, where warm and cool colors clash, creating a dynamic tension. Broken pixels add imperfections reflecting the complexity of emotions, amplifying the narrative of discord and inconsistency in relationships.
