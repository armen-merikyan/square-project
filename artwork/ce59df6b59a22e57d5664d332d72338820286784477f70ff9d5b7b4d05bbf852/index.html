Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts restlessness and peace, using a palette of deep red, black, pale blue, and white to signify intensity and calm. The colors are placed to create a tension-filled spiral from the darker center to lighter edges, suggesting a struggle between static noise and transient peace. The red accents portray flickers of unrest, while the pale blue lends a soft, serene quality, creating a balance. The composition employs broken pixels to indicate imperfections and interruptions in flow, reflecting the core theme of motion versus memory. The primary contrast occurs diagonally, illustrating a push and pull where energy collides with tranquility.
