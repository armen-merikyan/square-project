Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the contrast between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that evokes a sense of melancholy wonder with calm blues and muted plums. The main collision occurs diagonally, where dense, darker hues oppose lighter, thinner colors, symbolizing exposure to the elements against a framework of stability. This arrangement expresses the push and pull between feelings of weakness and resilience.
