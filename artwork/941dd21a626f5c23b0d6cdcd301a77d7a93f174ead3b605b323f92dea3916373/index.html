Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between quietude and pressure. The palette was chosen for its blend of muted and compressed tones, creating a dialogue between calm and density. The dark navy serves as a heavy foundation, while faint peach and off-white inject moments of light and softness. The main collision occurs in the top left, where compressed red disrupts the quiet fields, creating a ripple effect. This shatters the symmetry and creates a dynamic flow from the dense top to the looser bottom. The layout signifies an empty room with a meaningful offset, leading the viewer's gaze across the composition's complexity.
