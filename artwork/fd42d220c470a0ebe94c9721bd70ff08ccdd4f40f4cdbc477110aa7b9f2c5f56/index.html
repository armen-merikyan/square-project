Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between warmth and sharpness, representing love's nurturing essence contrasted with the jaggedness of betrayal. The chosen palette of pinks and dark maroons symbolizes this duality, while the placement of colors creates a narrative of conflict—warm hues in the center surrounded by darker, more aggressive tones. The pattern emerges as a crosshatch against stripes, symbolizing the complexities of relationships, where love intertwines with betrayal. Negative space ensures breathing room, representing pauses in turmoil.
