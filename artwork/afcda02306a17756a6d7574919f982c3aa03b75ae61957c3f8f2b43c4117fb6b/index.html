Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the tension between joy and grief, with a palette blending muted purples and deep blues against warm yellows and oranges. The contrast between the heavy blue-toned edges and the vibrant center suggests a layered emotional core under attack, while zigzag patterns evoke the conflict between order and chaos. The broken pixels introduce randomness, echoing the unpredictability of emotion, creating a visual story about the collision of two opposing feelings, framed by jagged edges to represent vulnerability amidst strength.
