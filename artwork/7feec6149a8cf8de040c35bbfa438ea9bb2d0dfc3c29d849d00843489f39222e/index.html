Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between confusion and clarity. The palette, consisting of deep blue, muted purple, yellow, and orange, evokes a sense of warmth and discomfort simultaneously. The design uses a stair pattern for confusion and a framing pattern for clarity, symbolizing structure against chaos. The sharp contrast between dark hues and lighter accents creates visual collisions, with broken edges suggesting imperfection. This complexity immerses viewers in a narrative that balances between dense clusters of emotion and serene spaces.
