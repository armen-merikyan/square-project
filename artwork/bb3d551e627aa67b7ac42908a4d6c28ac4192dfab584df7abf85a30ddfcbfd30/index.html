Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between chaos and order, where bursts of vibrant orange clash against deep charcoals, representing the panic that disrupts the control. The palette of 'ancient_fire' symbolizes raw energy and intensity, while the placement of colors creates a spiraling flow, contrasting centered control with off-center chaos. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the emotional discord and creating visual interest, allowing viewers to feel the struggle between joy and grief.
