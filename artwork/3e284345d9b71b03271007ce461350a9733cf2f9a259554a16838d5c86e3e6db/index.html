Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between tenderness and rage, reflected through contrasting colors. A palette of deep blacks and saturated reds resonates with anger, while pale blues and whites convey soft tenderness. The collision occurs in central rows, creating tension where sharp bursts of red disrupt calm blues, mirroring the emotional turmoil between gentleness and fury. The 'stacked bands' layout enhances this tension, establishing a rhythm of conflicting feelings that blend yet clash, visually narrating the complexities of emotional states.
