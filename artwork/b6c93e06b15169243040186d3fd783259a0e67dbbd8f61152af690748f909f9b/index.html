Artist Statement

This artwork expresses the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through a palette that blends dark shades of black and violet, symbolizing depth and isolation, with accents of soft gold and pale green that represent hope and connection. The contrast arises from the clustering of dark colors in the corners, which evoke feelings of loneliness, while lighter colors dominate the central area, suggesting connection. The flowing layout of pixels falling from top to bottom creates a sense of movement while maintaining a significant amount of negative space, embodying the emptiness felt within loneliness. Each pixel's placement creates an impression of fleeting connections against a backdrop of isolation.
