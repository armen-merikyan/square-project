Artist Statement

The artwork captures the tension between love and betrayal through an interplay of warm and cool colors, contrasting soft pinks against deep maroons. The top half embodies love with a crosshatch pattern, while the bottom introduces betrayal through a noise effect, featuring jagged edges. The primary visual conflict arises from the misalignment and broken pixels, symbolizing emotional dissonance. This structure draws the eye upward diagonally, echoing themes of rising tension and unresolved feelings, resulting in an abstract yet poignant composition.
