Artist Statement

The piece embodies the emotional tension between clarity and confusion, articulated through a palette representing love and betrayal—soft pinks contrasting with dark maroon and gray tones. The central space showcases radial patterns symbolizing confusion, while the edges exhibit a more structured layout hinting at clarity, creating a visual dialogue of chaos and order. The contrasting colors collide in the center, with thicker outlines accentuating the disruption, leading to a dynamic rhythm that pulses across the square's surface, evoking both rage and calm in an abstract narrative.
