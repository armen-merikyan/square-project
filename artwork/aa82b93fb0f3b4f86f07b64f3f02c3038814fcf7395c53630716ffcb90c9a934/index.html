Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece captures the tension between love and betrayal, using a palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon to evoke warmth and danger. The contrast lies in the asymmetrical placement of warm and cool colors, where warm pinks and reds signify love, while dark maroons and grays suggest betrayal. The arrangement creates a push and pull effect, with dense clusters of vibrant colors overtaken by stark gray spaces, symbolizing betrayal's coldness encroaching on love. The broken pixels are strategically placed to reinforce the theme of fractured connections, mirroring the complexities of love entangled with betrayal.
