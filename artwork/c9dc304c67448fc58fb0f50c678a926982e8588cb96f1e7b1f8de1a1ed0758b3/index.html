Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork captures the struggle between deep, heavy feelings and the lightness of relief. The palette, incorporating black, pale blue, and white, contrasts the darker edges with lighter central colors, suggesting a journey from heavy pressure to a moment of release. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, mirroring real emotional experiences. The flow spirals clockwise, guiding the viewer's eye through the dense outer borders towards a more tranquil inner core, embodying the transition from weight to lightness, symbolized by the moon's quiet contrast.
