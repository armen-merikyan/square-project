Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between vulnerability and strength, with a palette that balances serenity and urgency. The pale aqua and mist gray serve as a quiet backdrop for the bold alarm red, representing sparks against overwhelming floods. The main collision occurs in the center, where pulse marks and a ladder pattern interact, suggesting interplay between exposure and protection. The layout features a narrow negative-space channel that enhances the sense of opposing forces, embodying the complexity of connection and alienation without explicit representation.
