Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the conflict between longing and resolve, illustrated by the palette's balance of calm greys and intense reds emphasizing pressure. The pattern uses threads of longing represented in lighter colors, weaving through the darker, more defined forms of resolve, mirroring the internal struggle. The collision occurs in the center where red and navy clash, depicting the heart of tension, creating an energetic but unresolved dynamic within a grounding, quiet edge, consistent with the seed's complexity.
