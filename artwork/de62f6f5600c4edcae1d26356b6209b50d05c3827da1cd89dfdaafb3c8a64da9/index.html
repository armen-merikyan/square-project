Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between defiance and submission through the interplay of bright accents and muted background colors. The color palette, drawn from the theme 'melancholy wonder', includes hues that evoke deep emotional resonance. The center cluster of vibrant colors represents defiance, while the surrounding muted shades symbolize submission, creating a deliberate collision of feeling that echoes the core theme. The uneven border gives the piece an organic, chaotic edge, enhancing the sense of inwardness as well as exploration.
