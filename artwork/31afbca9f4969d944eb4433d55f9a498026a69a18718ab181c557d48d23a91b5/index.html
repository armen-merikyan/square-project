Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between control and surrender through its contrasting colors and patterns. The palette—a mix of warm coral and soft amber against a backdrop of cold slate and blue-black—reflects the push and pull of pressure versus release. Central clusters of warm hues represent control, while the cooler tones soften towards the edges, symbolizing moments of surrender. The uneven distribution, with broken pixels, interrupts the flow, hinting at uncertainty and layered emotional dynamics. This abstraction tells a story of balance, dominance, and the friction that lies within the interplay of these opposing forces.
