Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional dichotomy of love and betrayal. The chosen palette of pink and dark maroon evokes warmth and intensity, creating a contrast that mirrors the tension between these feelings. The diagonal rise in patterns symbolizes movement and conflict, with stripes representing betrayal cutting through the softer flame patterns of love. The broken pixels are scattered throughout to introduce imperfection, reinforcing the theme of a fractured relationship. The composition utilizes a grid layout to showcase the battle between two contrasting states of emotion within a confined space.
