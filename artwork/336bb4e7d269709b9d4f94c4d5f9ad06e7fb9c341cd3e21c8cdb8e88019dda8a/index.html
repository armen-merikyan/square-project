Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the tension between safety and exposure. The palette blends the warm brown and shelter green, suggesting a protective presence, while the pale skin and open blue evoke vulnerability and the essence of exposure. The main collision occurs through a vertical split; warmth is juxtaposed against coolness, enhancing the idea of sheltering pressure versus liberating release. The arrangement flows in stacked bands, with the layers creating both stability in some areas and vulnerability in others, showcasing a balance that suggests how one can shield while also being open.
