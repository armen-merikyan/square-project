Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the tension between chaos and peace. The contrast of the warm yellows and oranges against the cool deep blues and muted purples evokes a tumultuous feeling, reflecting both clarity and confusion. The left side mirrors the right, but selective broken pixels introduce disruption, representing the sudden shifts felt in turbulent emotions. The edges feel fragmented to emphasize the theme of conflict within harmony, while the layout suggests a pulse, rhythm of contrasting moods that echo throughout the square.
