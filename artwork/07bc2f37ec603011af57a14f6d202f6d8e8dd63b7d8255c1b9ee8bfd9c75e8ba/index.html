Artist Statement

This composition explores the duality of softness versus severity, using a warm and cool palette to create emotional tension. The slight bias towards dark colors reflects severity while the presence of warm whites and soft pinks disrupts it, bringing moments of lightness. The broken pixel pattern symbolizes imperfection and the conflict suggested by the seed, with collisions occurring predominantly in the middle where dark and light colors interlace. The layout suggests a mirroring effect with intentional disruptions, mirroring the interplay of emotion inherent in the theme.
