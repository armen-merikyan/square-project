Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between clarity and confusion, embodying the struggle of a signal trying to break through chaos and noise. The palette mixes deep navy for grounding with dim cyan for accents, creating a tension between stability and interference. The collision occurs predominantly at the center, where rhythmic patterns of organ-like shapes clash with jagged noise textures, visualizing the tension between order and chaos inspired by the seed concepts.
