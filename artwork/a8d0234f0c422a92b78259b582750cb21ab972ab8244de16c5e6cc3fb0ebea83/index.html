Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between softness and severity through a palette of contrasting colors. The top half utilizes soft tones of powder pink and warm white to create a sense of calm and gentle blending, representing softness. The bottom half shifts to stark carbon black and sharp crimson, reflecting a harsh and severe edge. This juxtaposition creates a distinct collision in the center of the grid, symbolizing the ongoing battle between guilt and forgiveness, where noise meets signal in an abstract flow. The broken pixels introduce deliberate imperfections, echoing the narrative of human experience within structured boundaries.
