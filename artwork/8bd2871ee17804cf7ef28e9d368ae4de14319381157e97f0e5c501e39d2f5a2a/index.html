Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional duel between rage and calm through a contrasting palette of deep, passionate reds and serene blues. The layout features a compromised center surrounded by flowing colors, representing how rage can creep into calm. The diagonal flow of reds intersperses with horizontal bands of pale blue, creating a dynamic tension that interrupts the calmness. The pattern mirrors around the center to echo the feeling of a fracture, while leaving negative space to suggest unresolved emotions and whispers of solitude.
