Artist Statement

This composition captures the duality of conflict and harmony through contrasting colors and patterns. The left side showcases the defiance theme with a pressure band pattern, embodying angular resistance and upward movement. The palette includes dark navy and compressed red, representing strength against a muted backdrop that conveys chaotic energy. The right side incorporates a negative cross for submission, using faint peach tones layered with dust gray, suggesting surrender and softness. The spark of movement is expressed through disrupted pixels, exemplifying the clash and interplay between defiance and submission. The main emotional conflict occurs predominantly in the center, where these two forces intersect and influence each other.
