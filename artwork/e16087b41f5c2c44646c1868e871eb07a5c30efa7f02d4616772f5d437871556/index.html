Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between clarity and interference, represented through a color palette of muted navy and black for the noise, contrasted with sharp whites and dim cyans symbolizing clean signals. The left side showcases a dense pattern of static sparks (noise), while the right displays a structured stack of clean pulses (signal). The main collision occurs centrally, where the intensity of the left disrupts the order on the right, creating an unresolved tension that mirrors the seed's themes.
