Artist Statement

The emotional tone illustrates a struggle between a clean pulse of clarity (signal green, electric blue) and the chaotic interference of noise (static gray, dead black, hot magenta). The green path sporadically breaks through the chaotic field of gray and black, representing the main collision. The seed's themes of contrast and brokenness manifest as an interplay of defined edges and disruptions, echoed in the layout that positions one emotion against the other. The palette contrasts vibrant clarity with muted chaos, creating a dynamic tension within the 8x8 structure.
