Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between nostalgia and progress, where the darker hues signify nostalgic weight and the brighter tones represent hopeful advancement. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green creates a contrasting tension between the heavy and light emotions. The layout features a nearly symmetric design disrupted by two misplaced elements, representing the clash between stability and chaos. The split pattern resonates with the duality of memories and aspirations, with broken pixels signifying imperfections in both memory and expectation.
