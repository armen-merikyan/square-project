Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece embodies the struggle between loneliness and connection, reflected in the palette of warm sepia and cool faded blue tones. The choice of broken pixels creates imperfection, simulating the discomfort of loneliness. The top-heavy flow leads the eye, while the contrast between warm tones and cool blues creates a vivid emotional collision, illustrating hidden vs exposed feelings. The grid-like layout captures an argument, with areas of noise suggesting isolation amidst connection, creating a dynamic tension throughout the piece.
