Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between 'emptiness' and 'desire', creating a visual dialogue through the interplay of colors and patterns. The chosen palette of warm pinks and dark maroon contrasts sharply against the cool gray tones—symbolizing the warmth of desire fighting against the hollowness of emptiness. The rising layout suggests an upward struggle filled with hope, while fragments of noise patterns evoke the chaotic nature of the heart's longings. The use of broken pixels represents imperfections within this emotional landscape, and the negative space allows breathing room, emphasizing the delicate balance of these feelings.
