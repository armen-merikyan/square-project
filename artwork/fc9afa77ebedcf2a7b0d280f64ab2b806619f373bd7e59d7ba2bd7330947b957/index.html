Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts feelings of envy and gratitude, embodied through a palette that balances muted sepia and tan shades with soft blue notes. The main collision occurs at the center, where warm colors suggest envy, while the outer edges with cool blues evoke gratitude. The layout follows an 'argument_grid,' creating tension between the central pattern inspired by 'rain' and the surrounding 'teeth' structure. The inclusion of broken pixels contributes to the imperfection and depth of the emotional experience, enhancing the sense of a noisy internal struggle. Negative space is used to symbolize unresolved feelings, maintaining an abstract quality throughout.
