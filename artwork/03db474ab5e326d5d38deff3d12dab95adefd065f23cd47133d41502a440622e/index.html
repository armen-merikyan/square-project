Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that evokes contrasts of fear and hope. The dominant dark colors represent strength, while pale gold and soft green punctuate the canvas, echoing fragility. The main collision is located in the middle, where dense dark colors collide with the lighter, softer tones, suggesting the struggle between protection and exposure. This translates the seed's theme into an abstract experience of emotional complexity, portraying how strength can shield yet also cage the vulnerable.
