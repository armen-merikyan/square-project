Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between nostalgia and progress, where the top half uses a palette of deep blues and subtle gray tones to evoke a sense of longing and reflection, while the bottom half introduces lighter hues that represent movement and forward-thinking. The contrasting density of colors creates a visual narrative of memory clashing with ambition. The pattern arrangement follows a drip-like flow that enhances the idea of time passing—memories seeping down into the progress of the future. The inclusion of breaking points in color provides imperfection, making the piece feel more alive and emotionally charged.
