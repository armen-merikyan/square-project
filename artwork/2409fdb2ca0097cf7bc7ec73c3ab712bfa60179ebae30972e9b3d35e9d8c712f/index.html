Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the clash between love and betrayal, creating a visual narrative of contrasting feelings. The warm palette of pink and red against the grays and dark maroon signifies warmth and passion struggling against the chill of betrayal. The main collision occurs in the top rows where brighter colors meet muted tones, representing hope against despair. The rising layout suggests an aspiration to escape these feelings, while burned edges introduce imperfection, reinforcing the theme of emotional conflict.
