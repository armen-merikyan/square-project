Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional duality of fear and hope. The dark colors symbolize fear, creating a sense of jagged edges and emptiness, while the softer gold and pale green suggest hope and a glowing center. This contrast is highlighted through a split vertical flow, where the left side feels heavier and more oppressive, while the right side offers a sense of lift and brightness. The treatment of colors embodies the abstract qualities derived from the seed, with broken pixels adding an element of imperfection and an unfinished narrative.
