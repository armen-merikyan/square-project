Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. The palette suggests a 'broken memory,' featuring sepia and faded blue hues that evoke nostalgia. The contrast lies in the heavy clustering of dark tones against lighter accents, representing the struggle between feeling isolated (dark colors) versus the yearning for connection (lighter colors). The center showcases a circular burst of warmth, symbolizing an emotional seed, surrounded by chaotic patterns of noise and crack, which reflect the push and pull of hidden vs exposed sentiments.
