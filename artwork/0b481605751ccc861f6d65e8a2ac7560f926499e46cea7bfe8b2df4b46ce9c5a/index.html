Artist Statement

The emotional tone comes from the tension between defiance and submission, represented by the use of dark versus light colors. The palette features deep navy and black to symbolize resistance and weight, contrasted with single whites and dim cyan for moments of clarity and fragility. The main collision occurs in the center, where the dense textures meet the more chaotic noise patterns, reflecting the tension of the emotional themes addressed. The left-to-right flow mirrors the struggle, while the layout introduces intentional disarray, representing the broken pixels as metaphors for imperfections within a broader emotional narrative.
