Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'softness vs severity' is represented through the contrasting palette of powder pink, warm white, carbon black, steel gray, and sharp crimson. The pattern invokes a sense of tension, with the softness of pink and white occupying the lighter spaces and the severity of black and crimson clashing in dense regions, creating a narrative that reflects the struggle between gentle acceptance and harsh reality. The central collision of colors occurs at the intersection of light and dark, visually echoing the push and pull of forgiveness and guilt. Broken pixels are intentionally placed to disrupt the symmetry, symbolizing the imperfections within emotional states. The overall composition resonates with the themes of radial movement and layered complexity, embodying both a pulse of intensity and a faint whisper of tranquility.
