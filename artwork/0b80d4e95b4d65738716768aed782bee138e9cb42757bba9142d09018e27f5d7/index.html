Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between tenderness and rage, framed within the concept of an 'inside vs outside' emotional landscape. The selected palette of red, black, pale blue, and white reflects the heated core of rage contrasted with the calmness of tenderness, invoking a sense of emotional push and pull. The clash between the 'lattice' pattern of tenderness and the 'stepwell' pattern of rage creates a jagged contrast, with red and dark tones dominating the center, representing the intense feelings within, while the serene blues offer a muted background, allowing for moments of reflective quiet. This setup results in a palpable emotional tension that ensures the viewer feels both the comfort of tenderness and the explosive nature of rage in a juxtaposed manner.
