Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between pressure and quietness, achieved through color density and placement. Dark navy is used as the boundary, contributing to a weighted feeling, while faint peach introduces a tender contrast, representing the release of pressure. The patterns of islands and teeth clash diagonally, disrupting harmony. This collision occurs at the center, symbolizing a break in expected flow, enhancing the sense of confusion and clarity. The palette combines muted tones with sharp edges to evoke a feeling of both density and lightness, illustrating the narrative of pushing against constraints while seeking freedom.
