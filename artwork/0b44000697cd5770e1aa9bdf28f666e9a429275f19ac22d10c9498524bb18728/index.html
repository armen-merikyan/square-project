Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between control and surrender through color and placement. The palette, including sepia and faded blue, represents memories and calmness interrupted by darker hues, suggesting a stuttering pulse in a sea of noise. The sharp contrast between quiet fields and abrupt echoes creates a collision in the center, emphasizing the struggle against a thick outline that frames the piece, instilling a sense of imprisoning control. The overall arrangement resonates with feelings of loneliness and tension, reflecting the seed's essence through abstract patterns and spatial disruptions.
