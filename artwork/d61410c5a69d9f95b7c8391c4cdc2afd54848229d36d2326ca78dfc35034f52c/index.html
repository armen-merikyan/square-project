Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between joy and grief, illustrated through a palette of deep blues and vibrant yellows. The joy element is represented by the sparking orange and scattered yellow accents interrupting the heavy blue backgrounds, symbolizing the push and pull of emotions. The layout balances symmetry against asymmetry, creating a visual conflict that echoes the duality of love and betrayal. The pattern logic mirrors the seed by emphasizing a pulse-like movement where colors collide and recede, allowing open spaces that echo feelings of loss amidst bursts of joy.
