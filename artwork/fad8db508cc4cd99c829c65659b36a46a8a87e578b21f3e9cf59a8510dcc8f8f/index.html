Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional tension between longing and resolve, using a palette that embodies hunger and restraint. The dark, muted colors signify a dense emotional landscape while the off-white provides contrast, creating a halo effect around the central cluster. The collision of dense pixels at the center, representing resolve, disrupts the surrounding quiet fields, echoing the longing for intimacy through space. The arrangement suggests a diagonal flow, reminiscent of a pathway that leads to higher resolution, with broken pixels subtly included to indicate imperfections.
