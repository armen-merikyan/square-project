Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tensions between fear and hope through a jarring contrast of colors. The palette includes deep blacks and dark violets to evoke fear, alongside soft golds and pale greens that convey hope. Jagged edges enhance the sense of anxiety while the central glow hints at an emerging light. The flow is from bottom to top, suggestive of rising hope amidst darkness, creating a visual story of pressure against serene resolution. Each pixel arrangement embodies collisions of feeling and the intricacies of human emotion.
