Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tug between longing and resolve, using a palette that blends muted tones with sharp contrasts. The compulsion of longing is represented by softer colors, while resolve is signaled through striking reds and dark navy. The arrangement creates a central halo effect, suggesting a moment of clarity amidst chaotic emotions. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, mirroring the 'repair vs damage' theme. The diagonal rise in color density hints at both urgency and serenity, marking the pulse between conflicting feelings.
