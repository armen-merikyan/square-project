Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between restlessness and peace through color and pattern collisions. The palette includes soft gold and warm ivory for a quiet backdrop, representing peace, while deep wine and ash gray introduce restless interruptions. The layout forms an argument grid, where broken dots clash against island patterns, illustrating a struggle between calmness and agitation. The use of broken pixels adds intentional disruptions, embodying echoes of conflict amidst stability.
