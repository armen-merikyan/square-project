Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between confusion and clarity through a juxtaposed use of color and space. The selected palette of yellows and muted purples illustrates warmth and calm, contrasting against deep blues that invoke a sense of depth and confusion. The arrangement disrupts the symmetry, creating a visual conflict that echoes the theme of broken symmetry in emotional experience. Main contrast lies in the distribution of warm and cool colors, creating an abstract pulse throughout the piece, while the chaotic arrangement resonates with feelings of uncertainty. The use of broken pixels emphasizes the theme of imperfection, simulating the sensation of a fractured clarity versus overwhelming confusion.
