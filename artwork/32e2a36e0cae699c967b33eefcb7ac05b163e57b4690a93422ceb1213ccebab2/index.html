Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates the tension between defiance and submission, represented through a palette of intense red and calming pale blue. The central area juxtaposes a rigid checker pattern indicating defiance, surrounded by softer striped areas that evoke submission. The color contrast draws a visual conflict where the strike of bright red pulses against the gentle pull of blue, while the fading edges instill a sense of quietude, creating a dynamic threshold. Broken pixels in key positions simulate disruptions, reinforcing the narrative of struggle between containment and release.
