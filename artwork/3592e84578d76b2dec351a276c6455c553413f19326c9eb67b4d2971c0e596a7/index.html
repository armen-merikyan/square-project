Artist Statement

The emotional tone navigates the tension between restlessness and peace, represented through an interplay of muted greens and warm ivory. The palette evokes a sense of conflict and resolution, emphasizing a small, active patch of color amidst the gray, which symbolizes fleeting clarity in a chaotic backdrop. The main collision occurs in the center with a cluster of warm tones surrounded by cooler, steadier shades, echoing the theme of 'memory vs motion'. This pattern creates a dynamic yet harmonious composition that reflects the concept of 'signal vs noise'.
