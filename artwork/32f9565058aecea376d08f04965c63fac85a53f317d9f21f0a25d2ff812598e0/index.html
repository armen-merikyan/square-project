Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dichotomy between longing and resolve, exhibited through a palette of soft and muted colors with concentrated accents. The quiet pressure of off-white and dust gray dominates, embodying longing with fragmented maze patterns that spill from the bottom-left corner, creating tension. The contrasting compressed red and dark navy disrupt the surface, symbolizing resolve. The collision happens primarily in the center, where light and dark play against each other, suggesting repair amid damage while emphasizing the ongoing struggle between containment and overflow. This becomes an abstract narrative of pulse and echo through color and placement.
