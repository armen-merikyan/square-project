Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional interplay between defiance and submission, capturing a clash of resilience and retreat. The palette, drawn from 'gratitude_envy', balances the intensity of acid greens and honey gold against rich, deep olive, symbolizing the collision of energy and softness. The pattern displays a barcode representing defiance intersecting with broken dots symbolizing submission, illustrating a chaotic yet ordered flow. The central collision is accentuated by an open border, creating a visual threshold and deepening the emotional tension.
