Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve, achieved through a contrasting palette of deep blacks and vibrant golds. The rectangular 'tear' pattern embodies longing with its checker layout, while the ripple pattern conveys resolve in the upper layers, suggesting movement and flow. The collision occurs centrally, where warm golds disturb the cool black, indicating conflict and tension. The design retains negative space, enhancing the sense of separation and reflection, symbolizing the mirror aspect in the seed, while the inclusion of broken pixels adds intentional disruptions, giving the composition an expressive, layered depth.
