Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic interplay of strength and vulnerability, employing contrasting colors to symbolize resilience amidst fragility. The palette, combining moss green and stone gray with accents of rust and small gold, suggests a grounded stability alongside shimmering lightness. The main contrast occurs in the center, where dense dark greens and grays collide with the lighter colors, creating an echo of collision between security and exposure. The pixel arrangement, divided vertically, embodies the struggle between stability (left) and fragility (right), with broken pixels introducing intentional imperfections that represent emotional scars and the unpredictability of life's pressures.
