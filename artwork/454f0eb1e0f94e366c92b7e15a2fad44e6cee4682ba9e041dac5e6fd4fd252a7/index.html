Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition straddles the line between confusion and clarity. The chosen color palette of dark black (#000000), vibrant electric blue (#0000FF), and magenta (#FF00FF) represents a clash between emotions: the dark tones symbolize confusion, while the bright colors hint at clarity. The rows feature structured organization interrupted by bursts of electric blue and magenta, creating a tension that simulates a pulse. The mix of nearly symmetrical elements and the introduction of 'broken' pixels disrupts the flow, creating a visual narrative of tension versus release. This chaos is illustrated through dense clusters of dark colors contrasted with vivid highlights, evoking a sense of struggle and eventual clarity amidst noise.
