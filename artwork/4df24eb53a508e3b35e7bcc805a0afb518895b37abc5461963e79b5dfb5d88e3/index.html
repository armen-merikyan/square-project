Artist Statement

The emotional tone emerges from the juxtaposition of dread and relief. The palette, dominated by storm gray and near black, invokes a heavy presence, while accents of pale mint and faded gold hint at escaped light, creating a nostalgic warmth. The arrangement features jagged edges with broken pixels disrupting the flow, symbolizing the chaos of emotion. The central collision occurs where dark tones meet lighter accents, reflecting a dynamic tension between heaviness and lightness throughout the grid.
