Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tug-of-war between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that reflects a sense of fear and hope. The dark violet signifies strength and stability at the edges, contrasted by soft gold representing fragile light in the center. The arrangement features a thin path of pale green that disrupts the heavy patterns, resulting in a quiet channel amidst the dense textures. The intentional placement of broken pixels symbolizes the scars and ruptures inherent in both themes, reinforcing the notion of exposure amidst the protective surfaces.
