Artist Statement

The artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between restlessness and peace through contrasting dark and light colors. The palette of navy, black, single white, and dim cyan creates a swirling feeling of unresolved movement and calm. The collision occurs along the diagonal, where dark patches intermittently break the quiet spaces of the lighter hues, suggesting memories colliding with motion. The imperfect mirror layout represents the struggle between stability and chaos, reflecting the emotional dualities defined in the seed.
