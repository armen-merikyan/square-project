Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between desire and restraint. The palette, featuring vibrant coral and muted charcoal, evokes a struggle of saturated warmth against contained coolness. The layout emphasizes a pressure corner, where bold colors flow into quieter areas, suggesting a collision of desires spilling into organized spaces. The use of broken pixels introduces a sense of imperfection, reinforcing the theme of unresolved tension and emotional complexity.
