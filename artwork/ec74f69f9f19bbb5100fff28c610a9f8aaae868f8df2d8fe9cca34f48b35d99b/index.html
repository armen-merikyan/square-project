Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between chaos and tranquility. The chosen palette blends warm hues of orange and yellow representing moments of clarity against cooler deep blues and muted purples, embodying confusion. The contrast occurs in the asymmetrical placement of colors, where vibrant clusters collide with more muted areas, suggesting interruption and unresolved feelings. The broken pixel theme introduces disorder, with intentional misses in symmetry amplifying the chaos.
