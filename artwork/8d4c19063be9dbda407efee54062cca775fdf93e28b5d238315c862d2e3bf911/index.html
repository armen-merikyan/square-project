Artist Statement

The composition explores the emotional tension between defiance and submission. The palette featuring deep blacks (#000000) and navy (#001f3f) represents the bold assertion of defiance, while lighter shades of dim cyan (#7fdbff) embody submission's surrender. The sharp contrast between dark and light denotes a struggle, with the dark colors forming a lattice pattern that collides with quieter fields in lighter blue tones. This visual interplay signifies an inward spiral of conflict, where the dominant darkness (about 65% of the grid) asserts itself against the softer submission colors, creating an unsettled rhythm. The broken pixel elements add an imperfection that mirrors the emotional struggle, revealing cracks in both defiance and submission. The chaotic rhythm at the center of the arrangement captures the essence of noise amid signals, resulting in a rich, abstract narrative of resilience versus resignation.
