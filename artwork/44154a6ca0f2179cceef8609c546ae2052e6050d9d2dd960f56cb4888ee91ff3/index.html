Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between desire and emptiness. The palette, featuring warm hues like hot coral and blood orange, encapsulates a yearning, while the cool shades of charcoal and hollow blue convey a sense of containment and spacing. The main contrast arises in the left half, where saturated colors collide with the emptiness of muted shades on the right, symbolizing the push-pull between longing and restraint. The broken pixels symbolize imperfections and disruptions, inherent to the emotional experience of desiring something out of reach.
