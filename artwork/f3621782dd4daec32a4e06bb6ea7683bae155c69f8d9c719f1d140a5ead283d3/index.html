Artist Statement

This piece captures the tumultuous emotional contrast between confusion and clarity, embodied in a palette of deep blacks and soft blues. The layout reflects an argument between a structured grid and chaotic stripes, creating a pulse-like rhythm. The central area buzzes with tension, highlighted by overlapping pixels that symbolize broken symmetry. The composition plays with negative space—allowing visual breath within the chaos—which invites contemplation amidst confusion.
