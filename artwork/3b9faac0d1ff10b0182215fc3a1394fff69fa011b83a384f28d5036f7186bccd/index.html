Artist Statement

The emotional tone expresses a strong contrast between rage and calm, utilizing a palette of red, white, pale blue, and black to embody this dichotomy. The top half features jagged patterns in red reflecting explosive energy and tension, while the bottom half incorporates more subdued arrangements of pale blue and white to symbolize tranquility. The chaotic intersection of colors creates a dynamic visual that evokes both conflict and serenity, illustrating a narrative of emotional struggle. The negative space allows the contrasting elements to breathe, enhancing the overall impact.
