Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece explores the juxtaposition of vulnerability and strength. The palette, drawn from deep navy and black against softer dim cyan and a highlight of white, creates a stark contrast that speaks to pressure and release. The center, with a dense cluster of dark colors, represents strength, while the pale areas signal vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center, where these opposing forces interact, creating a palpable tension that embodies the seed's themes. This abstract pattern emerges from the suggested movement and flow of 'cross_current', representing a dynamic interplay of emotional states, while broken pixels introduce imperfection, enhancing the concept of exposure wrapping around structured shelter.
