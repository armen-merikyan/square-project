Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between tenderness and rage through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette includes saturated reds representing rage, balanced by muted black and pale blue to evoke calmness and tenderness. The main collision occurs at the center where heavy, jagged reds clash with soft, glitchy patterns suggesting an inner turmoil. The falling layout enhances the feeling of rain as emotional pressure descends, while broken pixels introduce disruption, mirroring guilt and forgiveness. The arrangement reflects a constant battle between inner emotions and external pressures.
