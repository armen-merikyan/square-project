Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the dichotomy between the warm saturation of desire and the subtle tension of restraint. The palette, featuring rich reds and muted creams, accentuates this clash, while the layout emphasizes a split between these emotions. The central collision occurs where saturated colors of desire physically interrupt the restrained pattern of muted tones, illustrating the struggle for release against confined boundaries. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, echoing real-life complexities in desire versus restraint dynamics.
