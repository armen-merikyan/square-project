Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between isolation and the yearning for connection, using a palette that transitions from warm yellows and oranges representing the warmth of human connection to cool blues and muted purples symbolizing loneliness. The main contrast occurs in the central pixels, where bright colors burst against the darkness, creating a dynamic collision of feelings. This arrangement reflects the contrast between the inner experience of loneliness, which is intricate and dense, and the branching out towards connection, symbolized by the cracks in the radial pattern that disrupt the surrounding noise, evoking a narrative of struggle and search for intimacy.
