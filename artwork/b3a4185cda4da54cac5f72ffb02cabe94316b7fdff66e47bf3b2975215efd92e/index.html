Artist Statement

The emotional tone grapples with the duality of pressure and quiet. A palette of muted colors conveys tension while maintaining a soft field. The dark navy and compressed red form a dense wedge, creating a striking encounter with the quieter off-white and faint peach areas. This layout facilitates a diagonal argument, where chaos of pressure collides with serene quiet, evoking a shattering against a tide. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, enhancing the contrast between the two emotional states.
