Artist Statement

This artwork captures the dynamic tension between restlessness and peace. The palette blends soft sepia and faded blue to evoke memories of motion while contrasting with sharp, darker edges representing turbulence. The main collision occurs through the denser central area, where brighter accents clash against a muted background, symbolizing unresolved emotional tension amidst a backdrop of quietness. This reflects the seed's themes of edge crawling and motion.
