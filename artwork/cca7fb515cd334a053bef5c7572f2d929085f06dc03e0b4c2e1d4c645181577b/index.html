Artist Statement

The emotional tone showcases a conflict between joy and grief, represented through the top half in bright hues of yellow and orange, creating a flame-like pattern symbolizing joy. The bottom half is composed of muted purple and deep blue forming a radial pattern, suggesting the heaviness of grief. The contrast is highlighted through the hard edges where joyful tones meet the darker, denser grief, creating a visual tension between warmth and heaviness. The seed plays out in the split layout, emphasizing the battle between uplift and weight.
