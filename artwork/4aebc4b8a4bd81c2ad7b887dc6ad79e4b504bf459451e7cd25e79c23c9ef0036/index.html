Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between tenderness and rage through a contrasting color palette of muted blues and reds. The central square exhibits a radial pattern symbolizing explosive rage that COLLIDES with a scarred texture representing tenderness. The softer hues create a sense of warmth while the darker, vibrant reds radiate outward, creating a stressful, chaotic tension. This duality effectively converts the seed's themes into a narrative of conflicting emotions, highlighted by the main collision at the center, surrounding negative space provides a breathing room that echoes the quiet channel.
