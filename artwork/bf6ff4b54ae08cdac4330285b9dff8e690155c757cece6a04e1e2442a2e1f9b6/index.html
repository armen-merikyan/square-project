Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional clash between vulnerability and strength, represented through a split layout. The left side uses softer tones to depict vulnerability with a pattern of quiet fields, while the right side employs strong, thicker lines in deeper colors to reflect strength. The collision occurs centrally, where the patterns intersect, illustrating the tension between exposure and shelter. The color palette combines warm coral and soft amber for warmth and invitation, contrasted by cold slate and blue-black for depth and introspection. The deliberate placement of broken pixels adds imperfection and uniqueness, enhancing the theme of vulnerability. Overall, the artwork reflects a dynamic interplay between light and shadow, softness and rigidity.
