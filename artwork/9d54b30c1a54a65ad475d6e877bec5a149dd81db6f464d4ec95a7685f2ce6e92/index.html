Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrast between certainty and curiosity. The color palette of warm yellows and oranges interspersed with cooler deep blues and muted purples creates a dynamic tension, suggesting the push and pull of order versus chaos. Here, bright colors attempt to overflow from the structured center, representing curiosity, while cooler tones hold their ground, embodying certainty. The central pixel is a bright accent, representing the main collision and drawing the eye, surrounded by a more subdued arrangement that echoes the ordered layout, allowing for negative spaces that evoke areas of quiet contemplation amidst the chaos of color. This pattern tells a story of conflict and resolution, where the palette flows and ebbs like waves, reflecting the tensions expressed in the seed.
