Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between control and surrender, using a palette that balances muted earth tones with cool accents. The main collision occurs in the center with a cluster of warmer shades, radiating outward into cooler hues, suggesting a release of pressure. The channels symbolize a contained flow while the rain pattern introduces a stuttering descent, capturing the essence of tension and the release that follows. The broken pixels disrupt the expected patterns, embodying moments of imperfection and emotional scars.
