Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'spark' pulls against 'ash', suggesting a battle of energy and stillness. The palette of warm and cool tones reflects intimacy and distance, creating a visual conversation between the presence of life and the absence left behind. The main contrast occurs through the edge layout, where jagged edges and structured rings intersect, embodying memories and interruptions. The spark elements emerge as brighter sections that disrupt the calm, soft ash fields, visually interpreting the struggle of resilience against the quiet fragility of memory.
