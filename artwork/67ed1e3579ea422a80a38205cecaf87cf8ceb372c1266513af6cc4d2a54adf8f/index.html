Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between vitality (spark) and desolation (ash). The palette intertwines warmth with coolness, featuring colors like lamp gold and distant lilac to evoke both brightness and depth. The pattern features a jagged edge with faint echoes of bright hues struggling against shadowed fields—representing the constant push-pull between memory and erasure. The contrast lies prominently in the central area, where lively golden bursts disrupt the darker tones, mimicking moments of resilience against the backdrop of fragility.
