Artist Statement

This artwork represents the tension between light and darkness, where the 'spark' of the palette's bright colors symbolically clashes against the subdued, muted tones of 'ash.' The main collision occurs in the center, where the vibrant colors surmount the dense charcoal, signifying resilience amidst fragility. The choice of colors creates a lyrical flow, enhancing the emotional narrative of remembrance and erasure, embodying the complexities of existence captured in a simple 8x8 grid. The layout mimics descending pixels, creating a visual echo of falling memories.
