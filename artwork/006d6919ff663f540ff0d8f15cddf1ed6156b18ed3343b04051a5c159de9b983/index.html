Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, employing a palette that contrasts warm yellows and oranges with cooler blues and muted purples. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfection, suggesting disruption in growth or clarity. The structured yet nonlinear arrangement evokes a pulse and rhythm that follows a counterclockwise flow, enhancing the sensation of swirling thoughts. The edge style is left-weighted, providing a sense of imbalance, while the negative space allows for breathability within the chaos, amplifying the experience of confusion against moments of clarity.
