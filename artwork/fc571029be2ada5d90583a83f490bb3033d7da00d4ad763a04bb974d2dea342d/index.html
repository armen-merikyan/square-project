Artist Statement

The composition reflects the emotional tension between emptiness and desire through contrasting warm and cool tones. The outer rows evoke a sense of solitude with muted colors, while the central 4x4 grid bursts with warmth, suggesting desire's persistence. This collision emphasizes a rising signal amid a quiet, lonely backdrop, enhancing the abstract narrative. The palette mirrors 'ancient_fire', emphasizing depth and warmth against charcoal's emptiness while creating a rhythmic flow that progresses from muted to vibrant, resembling a heartbeat.
