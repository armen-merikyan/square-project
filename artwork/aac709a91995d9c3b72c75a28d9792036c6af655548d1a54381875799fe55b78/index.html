Artist Statement

This composition conveys the emotional tension between longing and resolve through an uneven arrangement of colors. The soft off-white and faint peach capture a sense of distance and warmth, reflective of ‘longing,’ while the deeper hues of dark navy and compressed red represent ‘resolve,’ providing a strong visual anchor. The predominant use of dark colors creates a dense atmosphere, interrupted by lighter accents that symbolize moments of hope amidst struggle. The collision occurs near the center where the patterns of noise (representing resolve) clash with the rhythmic split-screen structure of longing, encapsulating the emotional journey. The 'repair vs damage' aspect is reflected in broken pixels disrupting the flow, adding visual scars that enhance the narrative of imperfection and healing.
