Artist Statement

This composition contrasts the emotional states of longing and resolve, symbolizing a journey between these feelings. The muted palette of off-white, dust gray, and faint peach embodies a sense of serenity, while the compressed red and dark navy introduce emotional intensity and a focal push. The quiet channel structure creates a narrow path of negative space, allowing for a dense clash between the echoing longing patterns and target marks of resolve. The heart of the piece erupts in the center, showcasing a collision of calm and chaos, reflecting the complex narrative of emotional tension.
