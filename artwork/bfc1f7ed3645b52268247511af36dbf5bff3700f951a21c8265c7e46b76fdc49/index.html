Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional contrast of certainty and curiosity through a dynamic interplay of colors and patterns. The cool, muted tones convey a sense of stability and order, while the vibrant accents represent a desire for exploration and intrigue. The collision occurs at the center where the dense blue-black clashes with the soft amber, suggesting the tension between certainty (the structured border) and curiosity (the chaotic inner pattern). Broken pixels create intentional disruptions, highlighting a sense of imperfection and exploration. Altogether, this composition reflects an emotional journey from the assurance of the known to the depths of the unknown.
