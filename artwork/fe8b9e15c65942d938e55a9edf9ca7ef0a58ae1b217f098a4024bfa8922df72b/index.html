Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the juxtaposition of love and betrayal through a palette of warm pinks and cold grays, creating a visual tension. The main contrast erupts in the middle, where dense blocks of red and dark maroon clash against softer pink and gray areas, embodying the chaos of a broken relationship. The overall diagonal flow hints at movement, suggesting both rising hopes and falling realities, resonating with the emotional events represented in the seed. The choice of broken pixels in the layout enhances the feeling of imperfections and interruptions within the harmony of love's warmth.
