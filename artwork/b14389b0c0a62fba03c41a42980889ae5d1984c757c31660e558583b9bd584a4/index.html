Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a duality of love and betrayal, represented through a palette of pinks and reds for warmth, contrasted by darker maroons and grays symbolizing loss. The layout features a corner invasion, creating a sense of burgeoning pressure that contrasts against areas of negative space, symbolizing emotional emptiness. The radial patterns of love collide with the sharp checker patterns of betrayal, creating a visual narrative of unresolved tension and interrupted harmony.
