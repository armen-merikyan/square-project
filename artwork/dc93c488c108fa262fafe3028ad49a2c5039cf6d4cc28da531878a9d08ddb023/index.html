Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the tension between clarity and chaos, embodying the struggle of a signal seeking expression amidst the noise of interruption. The palette, anchored by navy and dim cyan, creates a deep space that evokes isolation yet promises clarity. The collision occurs predominantly at the center, where brighter cyan pulses break through darker shades, representing the signal's struggle against the intrusive background noise. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, resembling fragmented memories or interrupted thoughts, emphasizing the theme of memory versus erasure. The overall composition is dense, with clear structure and space that allows the viewer to feel both the pulse of the signal and the weight of static interference.
