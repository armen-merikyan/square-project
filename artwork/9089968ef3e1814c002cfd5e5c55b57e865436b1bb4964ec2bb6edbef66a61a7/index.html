Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between awe and terror through its use of contrasting colors and patterns. The palette blends soft pinks with darker maroons and grays, evoking a sense of love intertwined with betrayal. The left side features a checker pattern suggesting awe, while the right employs a ring motif to represent terror, creating a visual collision. Broken pixels introduce imperfection and disrupt order, staying true to the seed's themes of chaos and fragmentation.
