Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between awe (represented by lighter, soothing colors) and terror (indicated with darker, heavier hues). The palette of red, black, pale blue, and white creates striking contrasts, highlighting the push and pull between these emotions. The layout's rising signal contrasts with pixel clusters to evoke an upward movement against dense darkness, symbolizing hope fighting against chaos. Broken pixels disrupt the flow, reflecting fragmented memories. The interplay of patterns, where light bursts and dark teeth intertwine, adds depth to the abstract narrative.
