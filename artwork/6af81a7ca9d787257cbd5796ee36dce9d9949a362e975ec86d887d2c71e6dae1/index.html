Artist Statement

The emotional tone resonates with the tension between intimacy and isolation, reflected in the pinks and reds symbolizing connection, contrasted against the gray and dark maroon representing loneliness. The layout mirrors the contrasting emotions with slight disruptions to create a sense of imperfection and fragmentation, like memories in disarray. The use of broken pixels introduces an element of chaos, while the negative space adds to the feeling of absence. The pattern of density on one side and emptiness on the other suggests the push and pull of relationships, ultimately presenting an abstract narrative of yearning and belonging.
