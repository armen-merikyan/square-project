Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes defiance and submission, creating a dynamic tension. The chosen palette of pale aqua, mist gray, alarm red, ink blue, and clear white embodies serenity against a backdrop of urgency. The dominant colors are placed to reflect an aggressive invasion from the corner, with darker hues pushing into lighter zones, creating visual conflict. The abstract pattern emerges from the collision of structured 'flame' and fluid 'braid', visualizing the silhouette of engagement that feels both protective and intrusive. Broken pixels suggest the scars of struggle, enhancing the narrative of resilience amidst chaos.
