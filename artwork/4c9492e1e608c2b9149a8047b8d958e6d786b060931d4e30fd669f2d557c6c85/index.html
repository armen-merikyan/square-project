Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts love's warmth with betrayal's chill, embodied by the palette of soft pink and deep dark maroon. The top half illustrates love with a cracked pattern, suggesting warmth through pink and gray tones, while the bottom half embodies betrayal with a stair-like structure in dark maroon, introducing tension. Key contrasts appear between the soft colors above and the harsher tones below, creating a sense of conflict, as if battling between warmth and betrayal. Broken pixels strategically disrupt expectations, enhancing the portrayal of fractured relationships, while leaving intentional negative space to evoke solitude.
