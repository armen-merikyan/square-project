Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional struggle between control and panic through structured arrangements and scattered broken pixels. Utilizing a contrasting palette of warm sepia and cool faded blues emphasizes the tension: the outer edges display orderly patterns (representing control) while the inner core is filled with chaotic, broken dots (representing panic), creating distinct clashes. The overall flow mimics a diagonal descent, suggesting an unwinding of control. Centralized tension juxtaposed with negative space allows for an interpretation of emotional weight and release.
