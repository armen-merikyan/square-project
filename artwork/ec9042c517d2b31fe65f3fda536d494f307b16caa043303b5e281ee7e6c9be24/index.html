Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between clarity and confusion, using a palette of sepia, tan, and dust gray that symbolizes blurred memories. The left half, representing clarity, engages with broken dot patterns in lighter shades, while the right half, embodying confusion, employs dark, sharp tooth-like structures. The contrast is highlighted in the jagged edge transitions, creating a pulse of spatial dynamics that reflects a chaotic, fractured state. Colors intersect in the center, reinforcing the emotional collision.
