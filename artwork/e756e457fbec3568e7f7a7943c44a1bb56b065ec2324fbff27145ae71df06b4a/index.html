Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between loneliness and connection. A dense cluster of muted colors on the left represents isolation, while bright contrasting colors on the right evoke feelings of connection. The sepia and faded blue colors from the palette suggest nostalgia and warmth against a cool backdrop, emphasizing the hidden vs. exposed contrast. The center is dominated by darker tones that create a feeling of weight, balancing the lightness on the edges, reflecting a heart bursting within a contained space. The pattern echoes both disconnection and seeked connection through broken pixels, hinting at imperfections in relationships, making the piece resonate with an emotional depth.
