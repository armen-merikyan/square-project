Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a dynamic interplay between tenderness and rage. The palette features saturated reds representing rage, juxtaposed with muted pale blues that evoke tenderness—highlighting the tension of contrasting emotions. The main collision occurs along the center diagonal, where explosive jagged red pixels interact with soft blue fields, illustrating a struggle. The abstract pattern embodies the concept of broken pixels as remnants of emotional scars, emphasizing the complexities of guilt and forgiveness. This grid hence represents the push and pull of inner turmoil and serene moments, reflecting a quiet channel against a backdrop of intense feelings.
