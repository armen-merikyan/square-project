Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between softness and explosive energy. The palette features saturated reds against muted blues and whites, illustrating a dense clash of rage against calm. The broken pixels disrupt a continuous flow, symbolizing the interference of internal clashes. This abstract pattern forms two dominant spaces—rage with its jagged contours and tenderness with soft threads, colliding chiefly at the center to create complexity while preserving quiet negative space around it.
