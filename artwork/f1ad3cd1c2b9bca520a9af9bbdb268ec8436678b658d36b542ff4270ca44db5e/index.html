Artist Statement

This artwork reflects an emotional journey from tension (terror) to calm (awe) through its contrasting color dynamics. The palette, featuring stark blacks with softer hues of pale green and dark violet, creates an echo of quiet amidst the pressure of the darker tones. The upward and downward flow of pixels represents the internal conflict of emotions colliding, with bold strokes suggesting moments of intensity against quieter spaces—symbolic of the underlying rhythm and release felt within these collisions.
