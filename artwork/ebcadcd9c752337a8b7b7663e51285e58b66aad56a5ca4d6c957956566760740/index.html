Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dichotomy of longing and resolve, utilizing contrasting tones to depict the tension of repair and damage. The palette blends warm white and powder pink with harsher accents of carbon black and sharp crimson, illustrating the interplay between gentle yearning and firm resolution. The central collision occurs between clusters of broken frames representing resolve and distant rings symbolizing longing, layered across the grid to create an impression of both space and urgency. The layout's cross currents evoke interactions reminiscent of pulsating threads caught between feelings, enhancing the emotional weight across the 8x8 canvas.
