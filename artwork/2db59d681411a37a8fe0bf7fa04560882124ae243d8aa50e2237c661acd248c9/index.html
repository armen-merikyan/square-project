Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between desire and restraint. The palette combines hot coral and blood orange to evoke intense longing, while charcoal and hollow blue provide a soothing counterbalance. The collision occurs in the center with a saturated pulse of warm colors, rising upward amidst cooler, muted hues. The stepping pattern of 'stairs' creates a feeling of ascent, symbolizing hope and aspiration, while 'fault lines' provide the restraint, echoing the limitations faced. This dynamic interplay crafts an abstract narrative of yearning and the struggle against confines.
