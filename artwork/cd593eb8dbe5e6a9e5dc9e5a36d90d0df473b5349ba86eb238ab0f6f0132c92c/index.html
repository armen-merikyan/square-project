Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between 'safety' and 'exposure'. The palette reflects earthy greens and browns for safety, contrasted with colder whites and pale tones representing exposure. The central area is left lighter, echoing vulnerability, while a bolder framing of dark tones signifies protection. The layering of colors and the asymmetry suggest an ongoing interplay between comfort and risk, aligning with the seed's emotional themes and creating an abstract flow that resonates with dualities.
