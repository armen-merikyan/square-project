Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension of love versus betrayal. The warm palette of pinks and reds, representing love, is contrasted against cooler grays and dark maroon, symbolizing betrayal. The pattern arrangement reflects an almost symmetrical layout, disrupted by strategically placed 'broken pixels', emphasizing the theme of imperfection. The rows exhibit a diagonal rise, echoing the tension between soft warmth and sharp breaks, creating a rich narrative of emotional complexity.
