Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional struggle between restlessness and peace. The palette, consisting of muted greens and soft golds, evokes a sense of calmness interrupted by restless energy represented by darker pixels. The main collision occurs in the center where bright clusters push against a dense, dark field, illustrating the interplay of motion and stillness. The composition’s edge style creates a feeling of confinement while maintaining an open space for the lighter accents, suggesting a yearning for peace within disturbance.
