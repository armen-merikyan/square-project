Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional dichotomy of clarity and confusion, using a palette of deep navy, black, and muted cyan to create a lonely yet vibrant signal amidst the chaos. The layout plays on the idea of near-symmetry interrupted by broken pixels, enhancing the sense of instability. The contrast between dense dark areas and unexpected light interruptions fosters a pulsing rhythm that seeks balance against a backdrop of noise.
