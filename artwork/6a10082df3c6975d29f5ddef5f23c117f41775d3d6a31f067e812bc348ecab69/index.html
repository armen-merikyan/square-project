Artist Statement

This abstract composition embodies the emotional tension between strength and vulnerability through a color palette rich in contrast. The left half, representing vulnerability, utilizes a patchwork of softer hues to convey fragility, while the right half illustrates strength through bold, structured barcodes. The central area serves as a collision point where these two emotions interrupt each other, reflecting the delicate balance of exposure and shelter. The colors – deep blacks and vibrant greens punctuated by electric blues and magentas – symbolize the fight between the soft essence of vulnerability and the stark edges of strength.
