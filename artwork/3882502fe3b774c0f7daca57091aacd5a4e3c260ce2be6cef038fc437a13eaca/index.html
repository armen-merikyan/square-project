Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts restlessness with peace, captured through a palette that balances earthy rust and moss green with quiet bone white and stone gray. The placement creates a diagonal argument where the warmth of rust is pressed against the cooler tranquility of greens and grays, suggesting conflict. The broken pixels introduce imperfection and interruption, embodying the flame and shatter idea, while maintaining a sense of rhythmic motion. The arrangement fosters a dynamic tension between chaotic pressure bands and serene islands, inviting the viewer to reflect on the interplay of memory and motion.
