Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tension between fleeting sparks of energy and settled ashes, embodying resilience interlaced with fragility. The palette of sepia, tan, and faded gray evokes a sense of burnt memories and quiet introspection, while vivid accents disrupt the muted backdrop, representing sudden clarity amidst obscurity. The collision is central, where dark colors represent ash and trap serene sparks of lighter hues, highlighting cycles of loss and renewal.
