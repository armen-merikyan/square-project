Artist Statement

The composition captures the tension between softness and severity through a diverse palette where rounded pastels collide with stark depths. The main collision occurs at the center, representing the pulse of feeling against the radial context of life. Colors like warm white and powder pink evoke subtle tenderness, while the carbon black and sharp crimson introduce stark contrasts, representing severity. The spiral counterclockwise motif is echoed in the arrangement, giving a sense of movement within a tightly controlled space, reflecting an emotional journey of tension and resolution.
