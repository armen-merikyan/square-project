Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between loneliness and connection through an interplay of dark and light colors. The use of black and dark violet emphasizes the sense of isolation, while soft gold and pale green provide glimpses of potential connection and hope. The asymmetry of color placement creates fracture points that disrupt flow, paralleling memories of connection that are often broken or incomplete. The rising pattern reflects the struggle of moving upward from dense feelings of loneliness toward lighter connections.
