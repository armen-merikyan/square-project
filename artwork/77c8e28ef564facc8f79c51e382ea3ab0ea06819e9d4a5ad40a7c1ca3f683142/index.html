Artist Statement

The emotional tone is rooted in the tension between envy and gratitude, represented through an upward zigzag pattern on one side (gratitude) contrasting with a downward cluster on the opposite side (envy). The palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold signifies warmth and intensity, enhancing the emotional weight. The dominant contrast occurs in the center, where colors amplify their competition, suggesting an unresolved clash of emotions. The layout emphasizes movement and flux while retaining negative space that embodies calm amidst chaos.
