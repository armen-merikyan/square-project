Artist Statement

This composition embodies a tension between defiance and submission through contrasting patterns and colors. The lattice of defiance (represented by #ff3e3e, #5a7c99) seems to push outward while the checkerboard of submission (shown with #ffffff, #d1d1d1) offers a soft retreat. The dark #1f1f1f at the center represents the unmoved core, buffered by lighter hues that recede, creating a palpable emotional gravity. The layout reflects a mirroring tension that emphasizes a central collision, where density meets emptiness in a jagged, broken manner, enhancing the narrative of conflict.
