Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength. The muted palette conveys quiet pressure, with 'off_white' and 'dust_gray' as fields of vulnerability contrasted by 'compressed_red' and 'dark_navy' representing strength. The center is frail, bathed in lighter hues, interrupted by strong, dark edges, depicting strength's encroachment. The collision emerges at the center, where contrasting patterns collide, inspiring a sense of conflict between openness and protection. The layout mirrors the described emotional currents, encapsulating the essence of exposure against shelter.
