Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between the calm containment of peace and the sharp agitation of restlessness. The palette, 'melancholy_wonder', harmonizes cool blues and soft whites with muted plum accents, suggesting the push and pull of tranquility challenged by disruption. The main collision occurs around the center, where the pattern types 'hinge' and 'thread' intertwine, creating visual tension through intersecting colors. Broken pixels reinforce the feeling of imperfection amidst the spiral layout, acknowledging that calm often coexists uneasily with turmoil.
