Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional struggle between defiance and submission through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette incorporates deep blacks and reds to evoke intensity alongside stark whites and pale blues to suggest calmness, illustrating the push and pull of these themes. The layout, resembling falling pixels, adds a visual rhythm suggesting weight descending, while broken pixels introduce elements of imperfection, enhancing the narrative of conflict. The primary collision occurs at the center, where contrasting colors intermix, creating visual tension that reflects the emotional turmoil inspired by the seed text.
