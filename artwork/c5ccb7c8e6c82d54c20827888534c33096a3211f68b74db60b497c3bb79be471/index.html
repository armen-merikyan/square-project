Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between confusion and clarity through a palette that juxtaposes soft gold and dark violet, representing warmth against coolness. The color choices create a pulse-like quality, where soft gold emerges in bursts against the deeper background, suggesting fleeting moments of clarity amidst chaos. The layout of descending pixels varies in density, creating an echo-like effect, while the contrast types provide a sense of collision with abrupt transitions. The use of broken pixels adds to the sense of disarray, reinforcing the emotional weight of confusion and the struggle for clarity.
