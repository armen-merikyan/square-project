Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the interplay between longing and resolve, using the palette to differentiate between the muted whispers of desire and the solid forms of intent. The chosen colors—stain brown, ash gray, warm ivory, soft gold, and deep wine—convey warmth and depth, creating a visual metaphor for the emotional landscape described in the seed. The arrangement features a dense center suggesting longing juxtaposed against a soft halo border representing resolve, which provides a sense of protection and closure. The contrast emerges from dense clusters of warm colors in the center, hinting at intimacy, while the cooler shades of gray form the reassuring outer layer. This duality captures both the tension and release found in personal conflicts, ensuring the patterns overlap and intersect without creating a literal interpretation of the shape seed, maintaining the abstract nature of the artwork.
