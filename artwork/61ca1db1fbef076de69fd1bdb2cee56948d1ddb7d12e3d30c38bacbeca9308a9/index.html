Artist Statement

This artwork captures the duality of noise and signal, using vibrant green and blue to represent clarity amidst the static of grays and blacks. The density of colors contrasts with the irregular paths of quiet negative space, illustrating the interplay of memory and erasure. The main collision occurs in the center where the vibrant pulse of signal meets the darker noise, embodying the struggle for clarity in chaos.
