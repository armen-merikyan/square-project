Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a palette that embodies warmth and softness, creating a backdrop that evokes nostalgia and quiet reflection. The deep wine and soft gold colors suggest warmth that contrasts sharply with ash gray and stain brown, indicating the scars of damage and repair. The layout features a central collision of colors symbolizing the interplay of broken mirror reflections, with a deliberate asymmetry capturing the essence of 'mirror_broken'. The use of broken pixels introduces disruptive elements, emphasizing the theme of impermanence amidst an otherwise serene arrangement.
