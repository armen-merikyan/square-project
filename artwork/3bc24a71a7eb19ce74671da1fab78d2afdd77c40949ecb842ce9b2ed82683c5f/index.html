Artist Statement

The emotional tone intertwines trust's warmth against the underlying tension of suspicion. I selected a palette that balances deep reds and muted creams, grounding the piece yet reflecting complexity. The main contrast resides at the center, where saturated hues collide with quieter tones, forming a visible pulse amidst static. This resonates with the idea of a stable core struggling within an ambient noise. The pattern embodies resilience and fragile beauty, echoing the seed's themes of signal versus noise.
