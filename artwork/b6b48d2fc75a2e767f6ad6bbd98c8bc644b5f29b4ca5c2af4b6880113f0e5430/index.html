Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a battle between clear signals and pervasive noise, capturing both pulse and interference. The selected palette of deep black, electric blue, lively green, muted gray, and vibrant magenta embodies tension and excitement, with the green representing the readable pulse amidst chaos. The primary collision occurs at the center where the bright signals overlap with chaotic ripples of magenta and gray, illustrating the clash of order against disorder. This focus creates a dynamic where the viewer's eye is pulled into the center, balanced by quiet areas of black and blue that provide visual breathing space, enabling an interplay of presence and absence.
