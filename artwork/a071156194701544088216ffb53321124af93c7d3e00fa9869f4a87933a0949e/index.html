Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between loneliness and connection. It uses a muted palette of sepia and faded blue to evoke nostalgia and introspection, contrasting the heavy tones with lighter accents. The central area feels weighted, representing loneliness, while lighter colors on the edges suggest connection and fleeting moments. The broken pixels disrupt the continuity, echoing fragmented memories and the struggle between fullness and hollowness.
