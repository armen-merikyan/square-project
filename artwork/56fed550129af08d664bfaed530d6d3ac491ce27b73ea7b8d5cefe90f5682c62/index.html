Artist Statement

The emotional tone fluctuates between feelings of panic and control, creating an interplay between chaotic and structured energy. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of nostalgia infused with unease, imitating the fading memories of an empty space. The main contrast arises from the central cluster of dense, structured noise against a backdrop of broken dot patterns, highlighting the tension between stability and instability. The arrangement echoes a spiral flow, suggesting the pull of an orbit while leaving enough negative space to represent the void of the room.
