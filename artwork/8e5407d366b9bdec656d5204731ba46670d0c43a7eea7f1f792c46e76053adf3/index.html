Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between control and surrender. The palette features a mix of soft, quiet whites and grays that represent restraint and stability, contrasted with bolder, darker colors reflecting chaos and pressure. The central collision occurs at the midpoint, creating a visual echo that embodies the struggle of control vs surrender, with channels of soft colors yielding to bursts of darker tones. The structured layout mimics a ladder's ascent and descent, suggesting both guidance and descent into chaos, creating a layered emotional experience.
