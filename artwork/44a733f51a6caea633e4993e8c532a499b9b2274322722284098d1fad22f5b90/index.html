Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a tension between joy and grief, with bright yellows and oranges representing joy, contrasting sharply against deeper blues and muted purples symbolizing grief. The arrangement reflects a left-weighted asymmetry, showcasing an off-center dynamic where bright 'dots of joy' pulse against a grid of darker shades, signifying the struggle between uplift and heaviness. The broken pixels introduce subtle imperfection, mirroring the jagged edge of emotional experience, leaving deliberate negative space to emphasize the feeling of absence within presence.
