Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional tone of collision and release through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of black, dark violet, soft gold, and pale green symbolizes the battle between emptiness and desire. The stark contrasts between the darker shades on the left and the lighter shades on the right create tension, while the use of broken pixels disrupts the patterns to emphasize chaos and imperfection. The mirrored layout provides a sense of order versus chaos, encapsulating the emotional push and pull of the seed concept.
