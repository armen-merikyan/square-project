Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional tension between tenderness and rage, creating a dynamic interaction in a controlled chaos. The palette combines deep reds with muted grays and blues—red representing the intense core of rage, while the blue signifies softness and tenderness. The main collision occurs in the center, where densely packed dark pixels (representing rage) intersect with gentle, lighter patterns (tenderness), suggesting a conflict that doesn’t resolve but coexists. This pattern embodies the nuanced spectrum of emotions derived from the seed, integrating both quiet and dynamic elements into a cohesive abstract narrative.
