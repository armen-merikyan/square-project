Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between clarity and chaos, showcasing a pulse against an overwhelming backdrop of noise. The chosen palette—dark navy, deep black, and the occasional vibrant dim cyan—reflects solitude and urgency. The central collision occurs in the upper half, where the greater density of scars (symbolizing noise and disruption) clashes with fleeting moments of clear signal, embodied by lighter accents. The arrangement's upward flow resembles a striving signal attempting to rise above chaos, mirroring the struggle found in the seed's themes.
