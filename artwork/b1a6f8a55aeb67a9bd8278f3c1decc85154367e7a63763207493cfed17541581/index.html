Artist Statement

This piece reflects the emotional interplay between joy and grief, employing a palette of vibrant yellows and oranges to evoke joy, contrasting with deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The left side embodies a structured maze, while the right side disrupts this order with broken dots, illustrating tension and contradiction. The hard edges create a visceral impact, and the chosen layout introduces intentional imperfections to accentuate the emotional collision of stability versus fragmentation.
