Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through sharp contrasts in color and placement. The palette, including rich blacks and soft golds, creates a dichotomy reflecting isolation and warmth. The flow descends from top to bottom, suggesting tears or falling embers, while broken pixels hint at imperfection and vulnerability. The central arrangement features bursts of lighter colors surrounded by darker hues, portraying an inner struggle for connection amidst a broader sense of loneliness. The checker and dot patterns contrast to embody connection and solitude respectively, allowing the viewer to feel the interplay of these emotions.
