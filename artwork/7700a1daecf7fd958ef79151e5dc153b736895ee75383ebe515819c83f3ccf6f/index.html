Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the dichotomy of softness and severity, visualizing the conflict between gentle hues and stark, dark tones. The palette incorporates powder pink and warm white to represent softness, anchored by carbon black and steel gray embodying severity. The main collision occurs in the center, where the rippling pattern of severity interrupts the echo of softness, creating an intentional disruption. The placement of colors visually narrates a struggle: the heavier black tones at the bottom weight the frame while the soft hues ascend to create a sense of rising tension. Each row thoughtfully maintains a balance of stark contrasts interrupted by echoes of gentility, encouraging the viewer to feel the push and pull of guilt and forgiveness, evoking a spectrum of emotional resonance without depicting literal images.
