Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional interplay between 'panic' and 'control.' The palette incorporates warm oranges and yellows alongside cool deep blues and muted purples to express a spectrum of emotions. The main contrast occurs where vibrant bursts of orange collide with the tranquil blue, suggesting a disruption of stability. In the layout, colors flow from the top left corner, echoing the concept of inside vs outside, where the bright colors press outward against darker hues. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, reflecting the chaotic nature of panic, while the radial control pattern evokes stability amidst chaos. The arrangement not only forces visual tension but also speaks to an inevitable push and pull within the emotional landscape defined by the seed.
