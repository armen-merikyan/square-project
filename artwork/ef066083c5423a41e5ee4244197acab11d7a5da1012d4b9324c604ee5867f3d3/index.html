Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional struggle between certainty and curiosity, described in the seed. The warm tones of burnt orange and gold suggest feelings of certainty, while the cool shades of dark red and charcoal introduce a sense of curiosity and chaos. The main collision occurs centrally, where these contrasting colors meet as a dense cluster, creating visual tension, evocative of an echoing disruption. The patterns reflect a balance between structured flame-like motifs that represent certainty, and irregular flood-like waves that signify curiosity. This interplay between certainty's stability and curiosity's unpredictability dominates the grid, forming a rich narrative of emotional conflict.
