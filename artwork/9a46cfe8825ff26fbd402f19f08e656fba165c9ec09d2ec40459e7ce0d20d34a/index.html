Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between vulnerability and strength. The warm tones of pink and red represent the raw energy of vulnerability, while the subdued grays symbolize a protective shield of strength. The center features a dense cluster of warm colors that collide with cooler tones, creating a sense of exposure against a solid frame of calmness. The main collision occurs towards the center, evoking an emotional struggle between open vulnerability and solid strength. This reflection of the seed's themes is expressed through the warm vs cool contrast, generating a visual rhythm that mirrors the push and pull of these emotional states.
