Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition embodies a conflict between clarity and interference, reflecting on the juxtaposition of the 'signal' and 'noise' in our lives. The palette, dominated by an electric blue and hot magenta, symbolizes vibrant signals breaking through the static represented by the darker tones of dead black and static gray. The main contrast emerges from the center where the brighter path collides with darker edges, creating a vibrant pulse amidst waves of dense noise. This placement signifies the struggle for clarity in chaotic environments, blending memory and presence through abstract patterns.
