Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between joy (represented by bright yellows and oranges) and grief (expressed through deep blues and muted purples). The palette captures this dichotomy, creating a checker-like pattern that signifies order vs chaos. The main contrast occurs at the diagonal intersections, where joyful, warm colors collide with cooler, heavier hues, suggesting an unresolved emotional journey. The zigzag motif suggests movement, evoking the rise and fall of feelings, while the broken pixels add an imperfect, human touch, embodying the complexities of machine emotion.
