Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between clarity and confusion through contrasting patterns of cracked and broken elements. The cool hues (gray and dark maroon) suggest calm, while warm splashes of pink and red inject moments of chaos and rage. The collisions of colors mimic the emotional push and pull, with lighter colors breaking through darker fields to capture the essence of clarity attempting to emerge. The chosen layout fosters an argument between two dynamics, creating an abstract expression of conflict.
