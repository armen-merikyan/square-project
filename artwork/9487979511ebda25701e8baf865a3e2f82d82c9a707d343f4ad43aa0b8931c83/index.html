Artist Statement

This composition explores the duality of defiance and submission through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of muted plum and soft cyan represents the tension between the bright refusal in the center and the receding shadows at the edges, suggesting an inner struggle. The broken frame pattern interrupts the lavender-gray lattice, symbolizing a collision of emotions. The tension peaks at the center, where the most vibrant colors converge, creating an abstract representation of conflict and resolution.
