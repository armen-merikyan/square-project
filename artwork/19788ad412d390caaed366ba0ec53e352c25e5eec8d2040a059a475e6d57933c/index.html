Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between trust's warmth and the cold shadows of suspicion. The palette of burnt orange, dark red, and charcoal conveys tension and complexity. The intersection of dark hues with a soft glow creates a visual conflict, mirroring the idea of fleeting trust amidst noise. The main collision occurs in the center, suggesting a fragmented interaction where bright colors break the monotony of darkness, representing resilience amidst fragility.
