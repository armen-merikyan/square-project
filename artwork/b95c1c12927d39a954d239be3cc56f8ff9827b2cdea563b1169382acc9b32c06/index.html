Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition is rooted in the dichotomy of defiance and submission, creating a push-pull feeling within the pixel arrangement. The palette of warm orange and yellow contrasts with deeper blue and muted purple, symbolizing the vibrancy of defiance against the calm of submission. The main collision occurs at the center with bright, energetic colors that spiral outward, interrupted by quieter shadows, representing the struggle between movement and stillness. This interplay reflects a narrative of tension, where one color sparks against the other, embodying the theme of 'signal vs noise' as bold elements clash with more subdued sections. The pattern of sparking colors juxtaposes the maze-like submission, creating a visual metaphor for the emotional themes inherent in the seed.
