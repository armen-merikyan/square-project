Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between envy and gratitude, expressed through a dynamic color palette of navy, black, and dim cyan, symbolizing depth and contrasting emotions. The layout rises upward, suggesting hope amidst an empty room’s quiet, while hard edges create a sense of collision, highlighting a conflict between soft and harsh elements. The arrangement observes a 10% negative space rule, creating breathing room in the composition, while the broken pixels introduce an element of imperfection, echoing life's inherent uncertainties. The main contrast occurs at the edges, where dark hues meet lighter tones, repeating the emotional interplay of shadow and light.
