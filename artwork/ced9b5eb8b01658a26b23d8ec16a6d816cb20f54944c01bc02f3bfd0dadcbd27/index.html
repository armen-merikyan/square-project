Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between control and surrender through a striking contrast of dark and warm hues. The palette of burnt orange and dark red suggests underlying intensity, while muted charcoal allows for a restful contrast, embodying struggle and release. The central area holds denser color overlapping, symbolizing an 'overfilled center' where control falters, while the softer edges suggest a gentle surrender to the flow. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, enhancing the narrative of collision between stability and fluidity.
