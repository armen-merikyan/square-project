Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a duality of longing and resolution, with each influencing the pixel arrangement. The palette of 'quiet_pressure' includes muted tones of off-white and dust gray, contrasting with the more vibrant compressed red and dark navy, creating a sense of tension. The collision occurs centrally, where pulse marks of resolve interact with the shatter-like patterns of longing. This spatial interplay reflects the struggle between the need for intimacy and the distance felt, as if emotions are represented in a dance of density and emptiness across the canvas.
