Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The chosen palette of warm coral and cold slate evokes a sense of balance between warmth and distance. The pattern of barcodes symbolizing longing intersects with structured frames representing resolve, creating a dynamic collision in the central area. The rising signal layout allows colors to ascend, suggesting hope and aspiration, while retaining a quiet space that punctuates the rising tension.
