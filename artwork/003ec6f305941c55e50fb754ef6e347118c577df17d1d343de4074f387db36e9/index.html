Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between clarity and chaos, using a palette that juxtaposes serene greens and assertive magentas against subdued blacks and grays. The distinct contrast occurs in the center where 'signal' (bright green and magenta) collides with 'noise' (dark and gray tones), suggesting a struggle to maintain clarity amidst overwhelming interference. The pattern reflects falling pixels, creating a sense of descent that echoes the themes of memory and erasure.
