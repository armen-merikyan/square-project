Artist Statement

This composition contrasts the dense, dark elements symbolizing envy against bright accents symbolizing gratitude, embodying a struggle. The palette of navy, black, and dim cyan creates a lonely atmosphere, while flashes of white juxtapose this heaviness, representing moments of clarity. The arrangement emphasizes a flowing top-left to bottom-right diagonal layout with stripes on the left and scattered spark-like pixels on the right, echoing the emotional tension between weight and lightness.
