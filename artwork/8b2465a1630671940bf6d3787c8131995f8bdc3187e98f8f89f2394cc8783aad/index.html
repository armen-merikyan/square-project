Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece juxtaposes warmth with discord, encapsulating the tension between love and betrayal. The color palette features soft pinks and reds representing love, contrasted against deeper grays and dark maroon suggesting betrayal. A diagonal layout creates a visual rise, with patterned rotations symbolizing fluctuating emotions. The broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection, further highlighting emotional conflict and the fragmented nature of relationships. This abstract pattern emerges from the duality of warm vs cool, inviting viewers to explore themes of hope and loss.
