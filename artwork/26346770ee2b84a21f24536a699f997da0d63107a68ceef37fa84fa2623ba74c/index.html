Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between control and surrender, depicted through a central pulse surrounded by static pressure. The palette reflects resilience with moss green and rust creating clashes against softer components like bone white. The contrast is pronounced with a dense center representing control, while lighter dots suggest surrender in a feathered edge layout. The intentional broken pixels add disruption, echoing the fluctuation of the emotions at play.
