Artist Statement

The emotional tone oscillates between weight and calmness, embodied by the contrasting palette of compressed red and soft peach with darker navy grounding elements. The layout reflects the tension and release, as denser colors cluster in the bottom left, resonating with the pressure theme, while the top right offers quiet respite. Edge styles contrast hard borders with soft regions, and broken pixels create intentional disruptions, enhancing the theme of conflict—both visually and emotionally. The central diagonal pierces the square, symbolizing the argument between quiet and pressure, while the subtle gradients reinforce echoes of both intensity and calm.
