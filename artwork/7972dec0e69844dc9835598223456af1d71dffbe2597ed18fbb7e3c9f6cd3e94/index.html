Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition oscillates between confusion and clarity, represented by the juxtaposition of dark and light colors. The navy and black form a dense background with isolated light elements creating a confused atmosphere of overlapping patterns. The palette suggests loneliness and isolation, while the asymmetrical placement of colors illustrates a break from expected symmetry. The collision occurs between the dense areas and the negative space, emphasizing the tension of the given seed's themes of clarity versus chaos. The pattern is derived from the idea of broken paths, utilizing broken pixels to enhance imperfection and introduce uncertainty into the composition, inviting viewers to explore the contrast between structure and disarray.
