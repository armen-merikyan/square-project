Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a dance between defiance and submission, manifesting through sharp contrasts and broken patterns. The palette, dominated by muted sepia and tan, evokes nostalgia while the faded blue serves as an accent, suggesting moments of clarity amidst chaos. The main collision occurs in the center, where angular defiant pixels interrupt softer, flowing submission patterns, illustrating a push-pull dynamic. This layout creates a narrow channel of negative space, emphasizing the struggle in the composition.
