Artist Statement

This artwork conveys a striking contrast between the oppressive sensation of pressure and the release of quietude. The palette blends muted off-whites and soft peaches to signify tranquility, offset by dark navy and compressed red to evoke density and tension. The main collision occurs at the center, where tighter clusters of colors break apart and dissolve toward the edge, suggesting a narrative of hope rising against adversity. The selection of colors and arrangements captures the push-and-pull dynamic implied by the seed text, allowing for visual oscillation between constriction and liberation.
