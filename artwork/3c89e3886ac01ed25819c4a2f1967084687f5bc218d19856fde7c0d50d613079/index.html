Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between trust and suspicion, utilizing a palette that merges cool and warm tones. The main contrast occurs at the center, where the heavy, dark colors of suspicion collide with the lighter warmth of trust, forming a visual threshold that suggests resilience amidst fragility. The choice of cosmic indigo and deep teal represents the depths of uncertainty, while cold gold introduces a glimmer of hope. Broken pixels are strategically placed to symbolize disruptions in the flow of trust, enhancing the 'island and sea' layout and creating visual interruptions that evoke instability, suggesting a struggle between clarity and chaos.
