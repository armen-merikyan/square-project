Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic tension between defiance and submission, captured through a palette of muted cream, blackened plum, and accents of red oxide. The main collision occurs around the center with an upward block of bright refusal, while the edges softly recede, representing the complexity of submission. The arrangement resonates with layers of pressure and release, creating a visual spiral flow that contrasts a dense center against a quiet perimeter.
