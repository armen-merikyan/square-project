Artist Statement

The composition captures the emotional clash of control and surrender through sharp contrasts of color and texture. The palette consists of warm browns and cool greens creating a dialogue of containment versus overflow. The center features an intense clash of open blue and pale skin tones, symbolizing the struggle at the threshold where pressure builds yet yields to soft releases. Broken pixels create intentional scars mimicking interruptions in this emotional landscape. The flow and placement suggest a dynamic rhythm, echoing the stutter step between firmness and yielding.
