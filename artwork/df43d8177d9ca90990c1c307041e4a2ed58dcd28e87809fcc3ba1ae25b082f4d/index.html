Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between defiance and submission. The palette, with ripe greens and deep olives, evokes a sense of growth and resolve contrasted by underlying anxieties. The central focus, influenced by the shape seed of 'broken_line', symbolizes the tension of rigidity amidst softening edges, while broken pixels interrupt symmetrical flow, introducing an essence of unpredictability. The collision occurs towards the lower left, where dense patches of green converge with lighter tones, illustrating a struggle for dominance, aligning with the contrasting themes in the seed.
