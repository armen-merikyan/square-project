Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between desire and restraint through a dynamic interplay of warm and cool colors. The palette features vibrant reds and muted creams that suggest a clash of passion and limitation. The central focus of saturated warm tones mimics the pulse of desire, while the surrounding cooler tones reflect the boundaries of restraint, creating an island of emotional intensity within a sea of calm. The arrangement employs deliberate asymmetry, allowing for interruptions that symbolize the struggle between these two forces, resulting in a visually compelling piece that invites contemplation.
