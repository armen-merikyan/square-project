Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a battle between vulnerability and protection. The palette contrasts warm browns and soft greens representing safety against cooler hues of pale skin and white depicting exposure. The pressure and release are depicted through a dense cluster on the left suggesting a heavy weight of safety, while the right softly eases into quieter spaces. The collision occurs at the center where the stacked and split pattern intertwine, reflecting the complexity of shelter amidst exposure.
