Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional contrast between envy and gratitude through juxtaposed patterns of order and chaos. The selected palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue evokes a sense of nostalgia, reflecting the complexities of human emotion. The left side employs dense stripes representing envy, while the right side breaks into chaotic patterns reflecting gratitude. The interplay of weighted, muted tones against lighter accents creates moments of conflict and resolution in the grid layout, visually narrating a battle between heaviness and lightness, mirroring the emotional dynamics suggested in the seed.
