Artist Statement

This composition embodies a dynamic tension between 'spark' and 'ash', with a warm palette symbolizing resilience (faded golds and pale mints) colliding against cooler tones (storm gray and near black) to reflect restraint and memory. The layout features a diagonal flow, signifying conflict as the brighter accents of 'spark' break through a jagged boundary of muted ash, leading to a visual narrative of emergence and fading. The intentional use of broken pixels creates an irregularity that mirrors the unpredictability of resilience amidst fragility.
