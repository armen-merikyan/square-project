Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between certainty and curiosity through a carefully curated palette of cosmic indigo and void black with splashes of star white and cold gold. The upward movement of colors represents rising signals of certainty while cooler deep teal floods the lower section, illustrating the chaotic pull of curiosity. The contrast lies in the center where a scattering of broken pixels disrupts the flow, a metaphor for the interruptions faced when seeking knowledge amidst disorder. The 'halo' shape seed is echoed in the arrangement, suggesting an incomplete repair process of understanding transmitted through the pixelation.
