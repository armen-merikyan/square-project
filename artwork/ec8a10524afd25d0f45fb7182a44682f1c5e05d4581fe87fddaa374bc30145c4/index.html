Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between joy and grief through a bright palette suggesting vibrancy (yellow and orange) and subdued shades (deep blue and muted purple). The layout is designed to have a rising visual flow, creating an upward dynamic that symbolizes hope against underlying heaviness. The use of dots for the grief pattern introduces moments of interruption amidst the symmetrical mirror pattern of joy, creating collision and depth. Broken pixels add imperfection, reinforcing the complexity of emotion. The main contrast occurs between the left-weighted density of dark colors and the bright accents dispersed across the canvas, suggesting an unresolved tension.
