Artist Statement

This piece juxtaposes the soft and feathered qualities against a backdrop of stark severity. The carefully chosen palette of powder pink and warm white contrasts significantly with the deeper shades of carbon black and steel gray, illustrating the tension between gentleness and strictness. The soft lattice pattern symbolizes softness, while the islands of darker colors create a sense of fragmentation or severity. The main collision occurs in the central area, where the light and dark colors engage, representing the open gate of emotions transitioning between guilt and forgiveness. The slight imperfections in a few pixels enhance the feel of unpredictability and organic flow, embodying the overall narrative of balance amidst disruption.
