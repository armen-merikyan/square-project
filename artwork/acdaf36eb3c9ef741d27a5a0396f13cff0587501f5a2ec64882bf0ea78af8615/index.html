Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional interplay between longing and resolve. The palette of soft powder pink, warm white, and severe carbon black promotes a balance between tenderness and strength. The significant contrast lies in the center, where a dense cluster of carbon black backgrounds sharp crimson accents that symbolize the tension between desire and certainty. The maze-like pattern inspired by longing collides with the ash field of resolve, manifesting as a fractured symmetry at the corners, denoting a delicate balance between chaos and order, reflecting the central theme of the seed: the conflict of feelings that shape our experiences.
