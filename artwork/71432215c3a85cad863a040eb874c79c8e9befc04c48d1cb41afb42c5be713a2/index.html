Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the struggle between quietude and pressure, with a stark contrast where density collides into soft, wide spaces. The grayscale palette is punctuated by muted red and navy, creating a sense of compression in one diagonal while the opposite gently releases into lighter tones. The pressure is present in the heavy bordering pixels, giving a sense of hard edges, while the lighter colors suggest fragments of clarity within confusion.
