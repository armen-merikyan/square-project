Artist Statement

The emotional tone evokes a struggle between vibrant sparks of life and soft remnants of ash, suggesting resilience against fragility. The colors are drawn from a palette of warm corals and cool blues, creating a dynamic tension where warmth collides with coolness. The central clash occurs in the middle rows, where hot corals sharply contrast against the muted charcoal, symbolizing the ongoing argument of vitality versus restraint. Broken pixels disrupt the otherwise structured flow, mirroring the jagged edges of memory and erasure found in the seed. The layout embodies an eroded symmetry, preserving a spacious feel while retaining bursts of color that symbolize fleeting moments of memory and desire amidst calmness.
