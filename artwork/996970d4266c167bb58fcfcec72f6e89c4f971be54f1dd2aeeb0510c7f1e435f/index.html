Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The textured palette of reds, pale blues, black, and whites creates an interplay of intensity and calm. The main collision occurs diagonally across the canvas, where chaotic clusters of red interrupt expansive quiet spaces of pale blue and white, symbolizing the push and pull of desires and conclusions. The patterns—checker for longing and rings for resolve—blend in an asymmetric dance, reflecting the complexities of emotional duality.
