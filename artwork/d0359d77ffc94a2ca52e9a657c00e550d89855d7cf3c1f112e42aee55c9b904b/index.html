Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the tension between love and betrayal, manifested through the use of contrasting colors and patterns. The palette features warm and muted tones—pinks and reds represent love, while dark maroon and gray capture the essence of betrayal. The layout flows diagonally, creating a sense of descent and inner turmoil. The abrupt interactions between radial patterns and stair-like arrangements evoke conflict and resonance. The inclusion of broken pixels introduces imperfection, highlighting the fragility of emotional connections as well as the very nature of love and betrayal.
