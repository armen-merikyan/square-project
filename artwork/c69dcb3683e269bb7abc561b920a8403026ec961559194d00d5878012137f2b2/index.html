Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the ongoing struggle between longing and resolve through a stark contrast of colors and patterns. The palette features a mix of warm whites and muted grays, symbolizing warmth against cold resolution, while the deep crimson serves as an accent that disrupts the tranquility of the background. The arrangement follows a falling pixel layout, where pixels descend, echoing the feeling of tears or rain, coupled with a static pattern that evokes the strength of resolve. The center holds a dense cluster of dark colors representing chaos, surrounded by lighter hues, amplifying the emotional tension between emptiness and density. This interplay forms an abstract narrative of desire caught in a moment of clarity amid confusion.
