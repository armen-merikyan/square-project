Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between nostalgia (represented by softer, faded colors) and progress (through sharper contrasts and denser patterns). The palette of sepia, tan, and faded blue evokes a sense of memory, while areas of darker shades introduce a conflicting weight reflecting the harshness of change. The split horizontal layout enhances the contrast of lightness and depth, creating a push-pull dynamic. Broken pixels throughout introduce imperfection, mirroring how memories fade. This dip between connected dots signifies the call for nostalgia as a grounding contrast to the advancing pressures of progress.
