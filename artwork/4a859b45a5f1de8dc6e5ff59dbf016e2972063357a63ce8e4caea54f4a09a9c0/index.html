Artist Statement

This composition explores the juxtaposition between clarity and confusion, utilizing the 'broken_memory' palette to embody the emotional tension between clarity and chaos. The top half employs a staggered, stair-like pattern representing clarity, while the bottom half features a jagged, teeth-like structure to signify confusion, creating an asymmetry that underlines the theme of broken symmetry and collision. The color palette, with muted tones of sepia and dust gray, evokes a sense of muted memory, contrasting with faint accents of faded blue to illustrate tension points within the piece. Simple, hard edges define regions, enhancing the feeling of fractured clarity against dense chaotic areas, effectively visualizing the emotional battle within the seed.
