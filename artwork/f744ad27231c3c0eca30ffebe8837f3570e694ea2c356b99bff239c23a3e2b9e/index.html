Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional duality between trust and suspicion, illustrating a split between stable patterns and disrupted noise. The left side utilizes soft hues of pink and gray, suggesting warmth and openness, while the right side employs darker tones of red and maroon to invoke suspicion and tension. The chaos is introduced through broken pixels, serving as imperfections that highlight feelings of fragmentation and vulnerability. The main contrast occurs along a weighted diagonal, reinforcing the emotional tension between security and doubt, resulting in a rhythmic echo of unresolved dynamics.
