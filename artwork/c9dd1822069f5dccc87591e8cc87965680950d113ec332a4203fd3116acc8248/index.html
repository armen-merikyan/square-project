Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the conflict between clarity and static, symbolizing a 'signal' fighting through 'noise'. The color palette consists of deep navy, rich black, and a dim cyan to evoke a sense of depth and isolation, while the single white serves as a stark point of contrast—like a single pulse of clarity in chaotic interference. The arrangement focuses on a central collision of clean edges from the 'signal' competing against the dense, disrupted patterns of 'noise', using broken pixels to create a sense of imperfection and organic interruption. This abstract pattern reflects the struggle of finding clarity amid chaos, with a dominant field of noise interrupted by occasional signals in the central region.
