Artist Statement

The composition captures the tension between loneliness and connection by embedding a ring pattern indicative of isolation in the top half and a checker pattern representing connection in the bottom half. The palette features a stark contrast of electric blue and lime against deep blacks, embodying the relational dynamics and emotional waves suggested by the seed text. The grid-like structure brings structure but is interrupted by bursts of color that evoke ancient fire—a metaphor for warmth and connection amidst the solitary void.
