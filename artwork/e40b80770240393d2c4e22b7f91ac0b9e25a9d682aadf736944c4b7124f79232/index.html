Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the juxtaposition between vulnerability and strength, represented through a palette balancing warm and cool hues. The dominant colors are warm coral and soft amber, illustrating strength, while cold slate and blue-black signify vulnerability. The pattern combines a radial strength emanating from the center, disrupted by a veil-like texture from the edges, conveying a complex interaction of emotions. The central pixels reflect stability, contrasted by surrounding thinner, muted colors that expose tension along the borders, suggesting a constant push and pull between safety and exposure.
