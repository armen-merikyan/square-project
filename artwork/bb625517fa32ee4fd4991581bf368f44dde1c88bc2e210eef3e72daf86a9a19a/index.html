Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between fleeting sparks of hope and lingering ashes of memory, representing resilience and fragility. The palette, composed of soft pinks and stark blacks, highlights this contrast; the warm whites convey a sense of fragile safety against the severity of carbon black. The main contrast occurs in the center with a jagged interplay of sharp crimson and soft edges, symbolizing the collision between vibrant moments of clarity and the overwhelming shadow of loss. This design emulates the wave-like quality of the shape seed, reflecting movement and emotional conflict through overlapping patterns of luminosity and shadow.
