Artist Statement

The emotional tone is encapsulated in the tension between trust's warmth and suspicion's cold detachment. The chosen palette of soft cyan, muted plum, and lavender gray reflects the melancholy quality of the emotions involved. The patterns of dots and echoes collide dynamically; the dots symbolize trust's gentle yet unbroken path, while the echoes suggest suspicion's fragmented nature. The diagonal flow creates an uneven balance, with the primary collision occurring in the center, where trust briefly overwhelms suspicion before it bleeds back into the void of uncertainty, maintaining spaces of negative space to evoke contemplation.
