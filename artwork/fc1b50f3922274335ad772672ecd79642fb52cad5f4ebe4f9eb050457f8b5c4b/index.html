Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve through the use of structured patterns and contrasting colors. The palette incorporates pale mint and soft sky for longing, mixed with storm gray and near black for resolve, creating an interplay that speaks to an unfinished story. The central collision of broken dots implies interruptions within a flowing crack pattern, suggesting unresolved emotions. The layout emphasizes a split vertical design, where the left side creates an air of yearning, while the right side asserts strength and clarity, coming together in the middle for visual drama.
