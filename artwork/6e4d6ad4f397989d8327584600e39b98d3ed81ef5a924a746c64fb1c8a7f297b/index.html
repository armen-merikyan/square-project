Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dichotomy of restlessness and peace through dense, muted backgrounds contrasting with vibrant accents. The palette of greens and blues symbolizes tranquility and agitation, embodying memory and the motion of fluctuating emotions. The main collision of colors occurs at the bottom left corner, where chaotic halos collide with a structured pulse at the edges, creating a sense of disruption against an otherwise calm field. The layout fosters a sense of scattered energy juxtaposed with serene quiet, mirroring the complexities of the seed's narrative.
