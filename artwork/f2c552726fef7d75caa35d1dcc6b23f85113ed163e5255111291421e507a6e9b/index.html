Artist Statement

Emotional tone reflects the tension between joy and grief, using a palette that blends warm yellows and oranges with cooler deep blues and muted purples. The arrangement creates a visual wave rising from bottom to top, symbolizing hope amid turmoil. Juxtaposition of bright, spark-like forms against deeper, drooping tones expresses the collision of safety and threat, creating a sense of both protection and vulnerability. This results in a composition that captures the essence of emotional conflict without literal interpretation.
