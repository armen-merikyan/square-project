Artist Statement

The emotional tone expresses the dynamic between defiance and submission, symbolized through a clash of structured patterns. The color palette consists of soft sky and faded gold to evoke warmth contrasting with storm gray and near black, representing darker undercurrents of pressure. The central collision occurs in the grid-like arrangement of warm tones clashing against cooler shades, instilling a sense of struggle between external pressures and internal defenses. The placement of broken pixels introduces intentional disruption, enhancing the emotion of defiance against the backdrop of submission.
