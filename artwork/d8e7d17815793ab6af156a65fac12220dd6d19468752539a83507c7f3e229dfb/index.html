Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional dichotomy between fear and hope through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, balancing black and dark violet with soft gold and pale green, mirrors the interplay of heaviness and lightness. The design employs jagged edges on the right to represent fear, while lighter hues rise from the corner, suggesting hope. The collision happens where the dark tones meet glimmers of gold, encapsulating the struggle between despair and optimism. The arrangement embodies a flow that spreads diagonally, emphasizing the tension inherent in trying to escape fear while holding onto hope.
