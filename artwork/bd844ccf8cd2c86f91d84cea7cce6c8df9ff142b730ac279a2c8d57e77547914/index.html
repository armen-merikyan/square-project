Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional duel of desire and restraint using a palette that oscillates between warmth and coolness. The palette consists of rich red oxide and muted tones, suggesting yearning against a backdrop of containment. The flow leans towards a spiral, indicating the cyclical nature of these emotions. The central collision, represented by alternating patterns of saturated 'desire' and muted 'restraint', creates a stark division, while the broken pixels introduce tension and disturbance—symbolizing unresolved conflicts. This abstract arrangement serves as a metaphor for the push and pull of love and betrayal, inviting the viewer into a complex emotional landscape.
