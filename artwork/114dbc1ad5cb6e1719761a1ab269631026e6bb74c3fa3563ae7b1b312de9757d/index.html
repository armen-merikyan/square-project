Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional dialogue of defiance versus submission, portrayed through angular and soft contrasts. The palette symbolizes urgency (alarm red) against serenity (pale aqua) with an overlay of mist gray, evoking a sense of inner conflict. The spiral layout suggests movement inward, with a dense cluster of dark colors in the center signaling resistance, while lighter edges wash outward, indicating a retreat. The intermixing of red and blue showcases the collision of energy and calm, creating a narrative about struggle and acceptance as patterns overlap and interact. Broken pixels create interruptions, enhancing the dynamic tension without clarity, suggesting imperfections inherent in emotional truth.
