Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional clash between fear and hope, using a palette of deep blacks and soft golds, representing the dark corners of anxiety and the small lights of optimism. The main contrast is displayed in the jagged lines framing the central area, creating an oppressive feel that is pierced by soft gold pixels, suggesting moments of hope. The seed's abstract qualities manifest through a zigzag pattern, invoking rising signals, while the broken edges signify fragmentation in emotions, achieving a delicate balance between pressure and release.
