Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between joy and grief, employing a palette that oscillates between vibrant yellows and muted blues. The warm hues evoke feelings of expansion and openness, symbolizing joy, while the cooler tones introduce a drooping sensation associated with grief. The abstract arrangement flows from bottom to top, mimicking the idea of rising signals amidst the chaos of feelings, creating an upward motion that feels simultaneously hopeful and melancholic. The use of imperfect edges reflects the unpredictability of emotional experiences, while the zigzag patterns introduce rhythm and contrast, balancing the cheerful warmth against the somber coolness.
