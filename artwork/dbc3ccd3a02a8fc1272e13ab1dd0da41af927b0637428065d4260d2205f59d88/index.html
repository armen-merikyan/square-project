Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between the bright ignition of 'spark' and the subdued depth of 'ash'. The chosen palette blends off white for hope, dust gray for reminiscence, and compressed red for emotional friction, creating a landscape of contrast. The pattern alternates between rings and targeted placements, causing the 'spatial climb' to reflect the growing tension and rising hope, with negative space allowing the eye to rest amidst the complexity. The main collision occurs diagonally, symbolizing the intersecting ideas of hunger and restraint.
