Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between loneliness and connection. The palette mixes deep reds and blacks with pale blues and whites to evoke a sense of urgency against calmness. The grid layout contrasts the busy outer edges with a more serene center, suggesting a core of warmth (connection) surrounded by desolation (loneliness). The arrangement of colors creates a burst effect, hinting at hidden emotions breaking through the surface, portraying the juxtaposition of hidden vs. exposed feelings.
