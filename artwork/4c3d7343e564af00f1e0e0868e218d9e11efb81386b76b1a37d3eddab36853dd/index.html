Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the contrast between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that embodies muted resilience and an exposed essence. The colors feature earthy tones of red oxide and iron gray juxtaposed with the brightness of small orange, creating a feeling of urgency and unease. The main collision occurs in the center, where the strong, channel-like pattern of dark against lighter bands interrupts, echoing the push and pull of vulnerability amidst strength.
