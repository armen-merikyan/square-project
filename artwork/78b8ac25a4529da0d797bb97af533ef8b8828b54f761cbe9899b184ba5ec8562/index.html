Artist Statement

The emotional tones of joy and grief contrast vividly through the interplay of warm and cool colors. The palette features bright yellows and orange, symbolizing joy, juxtaposed with deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The composition reflects a rising signal, emphasizing hope amidst grief, with lighter shades clustered centrally echoing joy and darker tones spilling outward, creating a sense of density and fragmentation. The use of broken pixels introduces an imperfection that speaks to the struggle between these emotions, while the pattern suggests a wave-like movement, evoking an ebb and flow of feelings.
