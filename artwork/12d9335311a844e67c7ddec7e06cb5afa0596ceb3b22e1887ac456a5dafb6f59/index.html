Artist Statement

This composition captures the complex emotion of control versus surrender. The palette utilizes soft, earthy tones like 'rose clay' and 'warm shadow' contrasted with cooler 'distant lilac' and 'cool blue.' The dominant hues create a calming effect, while brighter accents of 'lamp gold' offer a jolt of intensity, resembling moments of pressure against a quieter background. The center creates a collision between structured stripes representing control and freeform crosshatching suggesting surrender, centralizing emotional tension. The layers of density pulse upward, emphasizing a subtle rise, where concentration of color at the center denotes overflowing sentiment, and quieter corners suggest a retreat into calmness.
