Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional tension between longing and resolve, represented through a split vertical layout. The left side conveys 'longing' using a flowing rain pattern with deep teal and cosmic indigo, suggesting a depth of yearning and the weight of unresolved emotions. Meanwhile, the right side embodies 'resolve' through a dotted pattern in cold gold and star white, symbolizing clarity and determination. The palette evokes a sense of awe and uncertainty, blending the tranquility of void black with vibrant highlights, capturing the push and pull between these feelings. The main contrast occurs in the center, where the two emotional narratives collide, producing a dynamic, visually striking interaction. Broken pixels are strategically placed to represent scars of past damage, reinforcing the theme of repair versus destruction without literal depictions.
