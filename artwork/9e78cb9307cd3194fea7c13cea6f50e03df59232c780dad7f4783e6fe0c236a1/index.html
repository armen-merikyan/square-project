Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tug-of-war between joy and grief through contrasting brightness and depth. The palette, featuring yellows and deep blues, showcases joy as expanding bursts, while muted purples signify the heavy, drooping quality of grief. The layout begins in the top-left corner, gradually encompassing the grid, creating an invasion of emotion. Broken pixels create an imperfection that heightens the tension of nostalgia and loss amidst joy, reflecting love and betrayal in a visual rhythm of pulse and interruption.
