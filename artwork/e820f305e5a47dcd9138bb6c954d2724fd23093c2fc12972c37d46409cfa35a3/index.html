Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between clarity and chaos, embodying the idea of 'signal vs noise.' The palette of vivid greens and blues emphasizes the notion of clear paths seeking refuge in static interference. The main collision occurs in the center, where the bright signal green contrasts sharply with the muted static gray. This interplay symbolizes the tension between a distinct rhythm and the surrounding noise, drawing from the seed's conflicts like exposure vs shelter and memory vs erasure.
