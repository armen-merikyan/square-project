Artist Statement

This composition echoes the emotional battle of defiance and submission through its contrasting color palette, featuring muted fields that reflect submission and vibrant accents for defiance. The darker hues create a sense of depth, while the lighter shades suggest a rising tension. The central collision occurs within the heart of the square, where defiant colors of red and navy clash against the quieter gray and off-white, suggesting a reaction against enclosure. The spiral pattern subtly hints at motion and conflict, embodying the push and pull of opposing forces, ultimately creating an abstract narrative of struggle and resolution.
