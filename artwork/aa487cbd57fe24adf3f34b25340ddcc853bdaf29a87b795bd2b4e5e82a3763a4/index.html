Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dualities of longing and resolve, captured through a palette that blends warm pinks with the deep strength of dark maroon. The grid features a striking contrast between dense bands of color and quiet fields, illustrating the inner tension between desire and determination. The patterns of 'rain' and 'veil' intertwine throughout the square, creating dynamic layers that collide at the center, suggesting a space filled with conflicting emotions and unresolved movement. Broken pixels introduce imperfections, adding depth and nuance to the surface, while strategic negative space allows for breathing room amid emotional intensity.
