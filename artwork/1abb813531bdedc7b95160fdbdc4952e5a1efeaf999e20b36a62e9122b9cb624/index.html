Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the intricate balance between strength and vulnerability. The chosen palette of greens and golds symbolizes growth and warmth, while the contrasting deep olive introduces a sense of weight and stability. The main collision occurs at the center where a series of maze-like patterns interact with soft, lattice-like structures, representing the struggle between exposure and shelter. The arrangement creates a tension that reflects inner conflict and connection amidst isolation, encapsulating the essence of the seed's themes in abstract form.
