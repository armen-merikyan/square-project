Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a delicate interplay between vulnerability and strength, expressed through contrasting colors. The palette of pink, red, dark maroon, and gray creates an emotional landscape of warmth versus coldness, with soft and harsh edges delineating the tension. The main collision occurs in the center with a soft pink greyscale background interrupted by darker maroon and vibrant red, illustrating the struggle of dominance. The patterns of 'hinge' and 'noise' create a dynamic interplay, resonating with the idea of exposure vs. shelter.
