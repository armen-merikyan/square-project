Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the struggle between clarity and chaos, with a strong sense of isolation emphasizing the central pulse. The palette, anchored in dark navy and black, represents the depth of noise, while the dim cyan serves as a beacon of signal, suggesting an emotional contrast where the quiet field of black surrounds a hint of clarity. The main collision occurs centrally, where the bright dim cyan clashes with deeper tones, creating a focal point amidst the surrounding veil of muted colors, symbolizing the delicate nature of retaining focus amidst distractions. The broken pixels add intentional disruptions, embodying the concept of memory effacing and echoing, reinforcing the emotional tension.
