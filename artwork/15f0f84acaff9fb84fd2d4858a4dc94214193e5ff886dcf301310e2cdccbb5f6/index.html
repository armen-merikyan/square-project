Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional juxtaposition of longing and resolve, articulated through the chosen palette and layout. The colors pale aqua and clear white introduce a sense of serenity, while alarm red and ink blue provide a contrasting urgency. The layout, described as 'argument_grid', emphasizes collision, where the quiet field of soften colors meets the sharp intensity of the darker hues. Main contrast happens centrally, enhancing the dynamic interplay between the anticipatory stretch of longing and the grounding stability of resolve. Broken pixels were strategically placed to imply scars of tension and moments of repair, making the visual narrative richer and more textured.
