Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between yearning and stability, captured through a palette of deep navy for isolation and dim cyan for subtle hope. The main contrast occurs in the collision where longing's radial patterns surge into the structured channels of resolve, creating a dynamic flow that embodies both urgency and serenity. The broken pixels introduce aspects of imperfection, symbolizing the fragility within both emotions. The split layout underscores the dichotomy, allowing the viewer to feel the push and pull of these emotional states.
