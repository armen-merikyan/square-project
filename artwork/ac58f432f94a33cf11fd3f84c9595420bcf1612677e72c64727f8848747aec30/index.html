Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between quietness and pressure. The palette combines soft tones like faint peach and off-white against darker, more demanding colors like compressed red and dark navy. The arrangement features concentrated clusters of pressure-like colors on the left, while the right side opens up into a more muted field, signifying release. The main contrast occurs at the center, where sharp shapes collide with gentle curves created by the patterns. The 'two pulses' layout features broken pixels that disrupt the symmetry, reinforcing the theme of fragmentation amidst an overarching structure.
