Artist Statement

This piece captures the dual nature of loneliness and connection through a palette of warm yellows and muted purples, suggesting a contrast between warmth and distance. The pattern mimics falling rain, reflecting isolation, while the scattered light dots symbolize momentary connections, creating a dance of tension. The density towards the left and the fragmented colors represent broken memories, contributing to a poignant emotional tension that resonates throughout the composition.
