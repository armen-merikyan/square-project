Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between certainty and curiosity. The palette combines warm shadows with cool blues to evoke a dance between safety and exploration. Main contrast occurs in the center, where warm hues collide with cooler tones, creating a spiral of interaction reminiscent of a decision point. This layout embodies a push and pull between structured paths and curious deviations, echoing the complexities within 'certainty' and 'curiosity'.
