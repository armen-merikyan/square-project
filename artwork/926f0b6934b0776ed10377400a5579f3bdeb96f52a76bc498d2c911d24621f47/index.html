Artist Statement

This piece embodies the tension between restlessness and peace through a contrasting palette that balances cold blues with warm accents. The main collision occurs in the center where darker colors intersect lighter ones, representing the struggle between calmness and chaos. The chosen palette of cool blues and soft violets conveys a sense of melancholy—a gentle yet restless atmosphere echoing memories and movements. The 'falling_pixels' layout echoes a descent, creating a rhythm where peace occasionally breaks through the weight of darker tones. This abstract arrangement reflects the emotional journey through conflict and serenity.
