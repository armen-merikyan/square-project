Artist Statement

This piece captures the interplay between defiance and submission, represented by a stark contrast of fiery colors and cool grays. The top four rows use deep reds and soft pinks evoking passion and resistance, symbolizing defiance. The bottom rows transition to muted grays, suggesting submission and weight, creating a layered narrative. The pattern flows diagonally with a center-weighted emphasis, illustrating noise against the edges with jagged transitions, adding to the tension. Broken pixels represent imperfection and the struggle of balance between the emotional states.
