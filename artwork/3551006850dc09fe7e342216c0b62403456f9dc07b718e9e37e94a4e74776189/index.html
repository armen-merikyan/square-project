Artist Statement

This abstract piece captures the emotional tension between quiet and pressure through carefully arranged color contrasts and patterns. The palette combines muted fields of off-white and faint peach, representing quiet corners, with intense shades of compressed red and dark navy to symbolize pressure. The top edge cascades with dark colors, creating a dense weight that descends into lighter areas below, evoking a sense of release. The central collision emerges where dark hues abruptly mix with softer tones, establishing a dynamic rhythm of light versus dark that mirrors the struggle of clarity against confusion.
