Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes joy's warmth against grief's cool desolation. The palette of yellows and oranges represents joy, while deep blue and muted purple evoke grief. A symmetrical pattern represents order, while breaking it horizontally into chaos supports the emotional duality of the seed. The interplay of dense line structures and sparse spaces echoes the theme of feeling simultaneously full and empty.
