Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a struggle between loneliness and connection. The palette, featuring muted sepia tones and faint blues, evokes a sense of nostalgia and memory, reflecting both isolation and subtle hints of warmth. The contrast between darker anchors and lighter accents suggests tension, with dark colors occupying the left and lighter colors diffusing toward the right, embodying the idea of 'corner invasion.' The arrangement mirrors the pattern of cracks in memory, emphasizing brokenness amid attempts at connection. Each pixel placement encourages a rhythmic ebb and flow, with clusters of dark tones contrasting with lighter areas to represent the push and pull of social ties versus solitude.
