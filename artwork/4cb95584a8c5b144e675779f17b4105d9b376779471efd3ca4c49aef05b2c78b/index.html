Artist Statement

This artwork juxtaposes tenderness and rage through a carefully selected palette that includes vibrant red and muted black. The red interrupts the calm, pale blue, creating a dynamic collision at the center where warmth struggles against weight, evoking the tension within the emotional pair. The patterns of 'negative_cross' and 'thread' manifest the internal conflict visibly, suggesting movement and interplay. The broken frame allows glimpses of emotion bleeding through, hinting at vulnerability amidst composure. The layout of the breaking border and the placement of colors emphasize the narrative of both resistance and surrender, capturing complexity.
