Artist Statement

This artwork explores the emotional dichotomy of 'rage' represented by bold, broken patterns and 'calm' expressed through stable horizontal lines. The palette of deep reds, pale blues, and black conveys intensity against a backdrop of tranquility, suggesting the struggle between chaotic energy and serene space. The contrast is most evident in the central placement of dark hues, surrounded by lightened tones, creating a feeling of collision and release. The arrangement embodies an echo of tension, where the viewer can sense the push and pull between opposing emotions while maintaining a significant amount of negative space.
