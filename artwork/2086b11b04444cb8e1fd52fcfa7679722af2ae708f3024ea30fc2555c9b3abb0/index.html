Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional push and pull of love and betrayal. The contrasting palette of soft pinks and deep maroons establishes warmth yet hints at underlying tension. The intentional distortion of color patterns symbolizes misalignment, evoking a sense of fractured relationships. The central pixel arrangement creates an echo of intimacy, disrupted by bursts of dark edges, giving it a chaotic beauty that reflects the complexity of emotional experiences.
