Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the tension of envy against the lightness of gratitude. The palette of deep blue and muted purple introduces a depth that embraces both strong emotions while hints of yellow and orange punctuate moments of release. The layout emphasizes a zigzag flow, creating a dance between heaviness and lightness, while broken pixels disrupt symmetry to underscore the broken mirror theme. The main contrasts occur at the center, where darker tones collide with lighter accents, suggesting unresolved emotions.
