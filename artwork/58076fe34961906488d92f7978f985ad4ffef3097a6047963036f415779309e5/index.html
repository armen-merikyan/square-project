Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between joy and grief through an interplay of light and dark colors. The palette features bright yellows and oranges representing joy against deep blues and muted purples symbolizing grief, creating a dramatic contrast. The outer layer employs a jagged frame, representing grief, while the inner core bursts with warm, target-like patterns of joy, creating a conflict that draws the eye inward. The arrangement of broken pixels introduces a sense of imperfection, echoing the chaotic balance between structured joy and fragmented grief.
