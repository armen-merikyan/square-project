Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between tenderness and rage through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of deep reds for rage against pale blues and whites for tenderness creates an evocative dialogue. The left-leaning flow emphasizes a gradient from intense warmth to quiet coolness, with a central collision of jagged red shapes clashing with softer blue targets. This arrangement mirrors the layers of guilt and forgiveness, fostering a deep emotional resonance, while the broken pixel interruptions create a sense of imperfection and dynamic movement within the quiet channel.
