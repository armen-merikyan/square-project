Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a palette that balances soft warmth and deeper tones. The colors reflect the themes of repair (soft gold) and damage (deep wine), while organized disruptions in the layout illustrate the push-pull of tension and calm. The main contrast occurs at the center with dense barcode patterns amid broken pixel accents, creating dynamic interaction that evokes a sense of pulse and unexpected echoes.
