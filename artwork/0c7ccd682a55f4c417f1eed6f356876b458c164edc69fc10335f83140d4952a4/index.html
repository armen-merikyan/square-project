Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a battle between vibrant sparks of energy and the muted, settling ash of memory and restraint. The chosen color palette, featuring 'signal_green' and 'hot_magenta' contrasting with 'static_gray' and 'dead_black', embodies this conflict through vivid accents interrupting the more subdued fields of ash. The main collision occurs vertically in the center, creating a sense of tension, while the asymmetrical distribution of colors creates a restless rhythm and evokes an unresolved feeling of yearning against inhibition.
