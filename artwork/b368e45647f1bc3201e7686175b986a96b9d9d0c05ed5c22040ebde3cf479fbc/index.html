Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the conflict of love and betrayal, expressed through a palette of pinks and grays that evoke warmth and sadness simultaneously. The checker pattern represents betrayal's sharpness, while the soft dots symbolize love's gentleness. The contrast emerges in the division of the composition, where two halves oppose each other while still being connected, creating a tension that reflects the emotional struggle. The placement from bottom to top suggests ascension towards hope, interrupted by sharp edges representing betrayal. This balances negative space and density, creating a feeling of complexity and unresolved conflict. Overall, the arrangement captures the inner turmoil of contrasting emotions and highlights the fragility of relationships.
