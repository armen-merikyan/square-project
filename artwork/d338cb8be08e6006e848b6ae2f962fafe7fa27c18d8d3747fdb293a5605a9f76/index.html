Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between nostalgia and progress through an interplay of dark and warm hues, contrasting elements evoking both calm and intensity. The palette draws on ancient fire colors, representing the warmth of memories and the fierce drive of progress. Main contrast occurs vertically, suggesting a split between the anchored fullness of nostalgia and the hollow, upward aspirations of progress. The arrangement reflects a rising signal, as brighter contrasts emerge amidst heavier shades, embodying an emotional climb from warmth into new terrain. The gentle drip pattern, alongside the disrupted edges, conveys the imperfection of memories colliding with the forward motion of change.
