Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between love and betrayal through a palette of warm pink and red hues juxtaposed with cold gray and dark maroon tones. The upper rows symbolize love's warmth with layered frames, while the lower rows reflect betrayal with sharp contrasts and broken patterns. The diagonal rise suggests the tumultuous journey from love to betrayal, creating a visual echo of emotional disruptions. The arrangement also emphasizes tension, with negative spaces reflecting the emptiness often felt in betrayal, thereby enhancing the overall narrative of conflict and contradiction.
