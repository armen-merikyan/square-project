Artist Statement

The emotional tone transitions between trust and suspicion, evident in the contrasting colors and patterns. The palette, featuring deep reds and soft grays, highlights tension: the darkness of suspicion is intertwined with the warmth of trust. The collision occurs diagonally across the square, where structured patterns of trust are interrupted by fractured elements, suggesting instability. The split layout emphasizes the contrast, as pixels fall like ash, creating a narrative of resilience amidst fragility.
