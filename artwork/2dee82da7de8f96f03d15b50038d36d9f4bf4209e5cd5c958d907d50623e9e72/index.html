Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes 'longing' and 'resolve', creating a tension that reflects both vulnerability and strength. The selected palette of 'signal green', 'electric blue', 'dead black', and 'hot magenta' evokes urgency and energy against the backdrop of 'static gray', representing the quiet yet palpable distance of longing. Main contrast occurs centrally, where energetic colors intersect with muted tones, suggesting the collision between chaos and calmness. The falling pixels layout mimics tears or pulses; the broken pixel strategy underscores the theme of repair amidst damage, adding intentional imperfections that represent the scars left by emotional experiences.
