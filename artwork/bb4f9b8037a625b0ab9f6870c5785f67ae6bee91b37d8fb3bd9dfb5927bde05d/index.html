Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the conflict between love (represented by warm hues) and betrayal (depicted through darker colors). The palette combines shades of pink and red for warmth, contrasted with gray and dark maroon, symbolizing coldness and treachery. The rows create a jagged dance of color, with sharp pixels contrasting against softer tones, creating a sense of chaotic harmony. The seed influenced the swirling patterns of interference, suggesting an 'inside vs outside' echo in the arrangement, where noise meets serenity, punctuated by negative spaces that evoke loneliness.
