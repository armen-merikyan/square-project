Artist Statement

This composition embodies a struggle between the forces of defiance and submission, creating an emotional tension. The palette utilizes muted tones of sepia and dust gray juxtaposed with vibrant hints of faded blue to represent the conflicted nature of the emotions. The main contrast occurs through a ripple pattern indicating defiance that collides with softer dot patterns suggesting submission, creating a dynamic visual narrative. The layout emphasizes quiet channels of negative space, enhancing the feeling of unresolved tension through dense clusters around the edges that push inward.
