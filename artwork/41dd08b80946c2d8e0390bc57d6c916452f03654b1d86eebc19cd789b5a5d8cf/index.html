Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension of vulnerability and strength through the interplay of the chosen colors and their arrangement. The palette features deeper tones like 'deep_olive' and 'ripe_green' representing strength, contrasted against lighter hues such as 'cream' and 'honey_gold' that signify vulnerability. The central pulses suggest a dynamic tension, as the colors interact both closely and distantly, creating a sense of both protection and exposure. The use of stripes for vulnerability and cracks for strength emphasizes this duality, with broken pixels appearing throughout to symbolize the imperfections and scars that come from facing exposure. The collision of warm and cool tones accentuates the ongoing battle between these emotions, while maintaining a soft negative space that allows viewers to breathe amidst the chaos.
