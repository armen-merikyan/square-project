Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. It uses a palette of deep blue and pale yellow to represent the collision of these forces—where the darker colors symbolize strength and stability while the soft yellows suggest exposure and fragility. The center is dense with saturated colors, representing resilience, while the borders bear pale hues, hinting at vulnerability and the struggle of containment. The interplay of ring and lattice patterns illustrates the collision between solidity and subtlety, with broken pixels offering intentional disruptions that signify imperfections and paths of healing.
