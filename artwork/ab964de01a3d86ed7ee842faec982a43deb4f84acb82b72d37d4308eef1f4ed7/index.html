Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes bright and muted colors, reflecting joy's brightness against grief's somberness. The palette features warm yellow and orange contrasted with deep blue and muted purple. This duality creates collisions within the patterns—the maze-like structure of joy is interrupted by the split-screen grief. The zigzag and checker patterns suggest a sense of tension and resolution, emphasizing the emotional push and pull dictated by the seed's themes.
