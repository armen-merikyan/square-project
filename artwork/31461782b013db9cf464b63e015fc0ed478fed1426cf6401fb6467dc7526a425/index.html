Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between control and surrender, with a contrasting palette of warm yellows and oranges against deep blues and muted purples. The center is filled with warm colors suggesting overfill or a dense emotion, while cooler edges provide a space of release and calm. The arrangement reflects the collision between the organized, glitch-like control and the more chaotic, scar-like edges of surrender. This results in an echoing effect where warmth meets a quiet, calm atmosphere.
