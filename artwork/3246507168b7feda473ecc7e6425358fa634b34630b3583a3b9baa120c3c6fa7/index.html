Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between love and betrayal. The palette of pink, red, and gray embodies warmth and depth, creating a juxtaposition of nurturing and pain. The arrangement favors a dense lower section, suggesting betrayal rising like heat, while the upper row cools into gray tones, representing a quiet space of emptiness and reflection. By applying broken pixels, there's an intentional imperfection echoing the theme of fractured relationships. The contrast between the sharp edges and soft tones reflects the complex dynamics of attachment and separation, yielding a tension-filled aesthetic that balances chaos with calmness.
