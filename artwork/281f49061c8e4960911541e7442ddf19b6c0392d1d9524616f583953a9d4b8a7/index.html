Artist Statement

This composition reflects the emotional clash between panic and control, using a palette that embodies fear (black and dark violet) versus hope (soft gold and pale green). The contrasting colors create a dense center with warm accents, signifying a target of control surrounded by chaotic grids of darker shades. The placement of colors reflects a patterned tension, where structured order meets jagged edges, illustrating the complexity of emotional states. The intentional broken pixels add imperfection and disrupt the grid, enhancing the underlying narrative of struggle and balance.
