Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional interplay between longing and resolve, expressed through contrasting colors and patterns. The muted, earth tones symbolize longing with negative cross patterns in the upper half, suggesting unfinished journeys enveloped in softness. In contrast, the lower half employs a structured frame with assertive tones representing resolve, symbolizing stability and growth amidst disruptions. Jagged edges reflect the imperfections inherent to the emotional experience, making chaos visible within the defined boundaries, highlighting the collision of the emotions. The dominant muted palette anchors the presence of quiet fields (grays) while cooler accents (small orange) provide sparks of urgency against the calm, embodying the push and pull of human experience.
