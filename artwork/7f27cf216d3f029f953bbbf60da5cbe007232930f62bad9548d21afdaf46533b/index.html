Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension of 'quiet vs pressure' through a visual dialogue between dense clusters of color and open space. The palette balances muted tones of off-white and dust gray with the accent of compressed red that bursts against deeper dark navy. The color arrangement favors the lower left corner, where the hues collide, creating a dynamic contrast that suggests both compression and release. The stepwell pattern provides a structured flow that contrasts with the rough noise representing pressure, while the diagonal layout hints at a conflict between stability and chaos, echoing the seed's themes.
