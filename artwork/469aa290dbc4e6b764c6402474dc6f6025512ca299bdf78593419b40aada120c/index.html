Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional dichotomy between relief and dread, embodying a subtle tension throughout the composition. The chosen palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue reflects an atmosphere of muted memories, weighted emotional states. The contrast occurs predominantly in the upper left corner, where darker shades symbolically weigh down against lighter areas, signifying the push and pull of contrasting emotions. The flow rises diagonally, echoing the idea of ascending from a dark place (dread) towards hope (relief). Broken dots and split screens create an imperfection that evokes unresolved feelings of interruption, resulting in a deeply abstract representation of the complexities instigated by the seed.
