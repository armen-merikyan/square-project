Artist Statement

This composition embodies the interplay between quietness and pressure through a split layout. The left side embraces soft hues, invoking calmness with shades like faint peach and off-white, while the right side introduces the compressed red and dark navy, symbolizing density and tension. The arrangement shatters into patterns, disrupting symmetry with diagonal breaks, reflecting how clarity can collide with confusion, enhancing the emotional depth. A few broken pixels are strategically placed to reinforce the theme of imperfection throughout the piece, making for an expressive abstract artwork.
