Artist Statement

This composition reflects the tension between fear and hope through the juxtaposition of sharp contrasts and soft gradients. The palette includes deep blacks and dark violets representing fear, and soft gold and pale green to symbolize hope. The central area is lighter, portraying an upward glow, while the edges are darker and jagged, expressing a barrier. The use of broken pixels signifies imperfection and vulnerability, creating a rhythmic flow between pressure and optimism.
