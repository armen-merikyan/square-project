Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a balance of vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold symbolizes a grounding strength contrasted against an exposed, delicate vulnerability. The main collision occurs in the center, where checker patterns of lighter colors disrupt the denser, robust hues, suggesting a push-pull dynamic of protection versus exposure. This aligns with the seed's call for interaction between emotional extremes and creates a visual narrative of complex emotional landscapes.
