Artist Statement

The piece reflects the emotional tension between joy and grief using a palette of yellow and deep blues, suggesting warmth and coolness. The top half employs circular patterns in brighter hues to express joy, while the bottom half features a checker pattern of muted purples and deep blues to depict grief. The contrasting patterns create a visual narrative of conflict and balance, interrupted by hard edges that symbolize the sharpness of feelings. Negative space allows for breathing room, evoking relief amidst the heavier emotions.
