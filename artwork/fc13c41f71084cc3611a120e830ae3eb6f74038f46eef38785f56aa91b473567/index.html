Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece balances vulnerability and security, with colors representing shelter and exposure. The warm brown and shelter green form a protective border, creating a gentle weight around the pale skin center, which suggests raw openness. The collision occurs in the center where warm and cool colors interplay, symbolizing the tension between safety and exposure. The pair of flame and braid patterns collide, creating a visual dynamic of tension and release across the canvas, embodying the complex emotional narratives suggested by the seed.
