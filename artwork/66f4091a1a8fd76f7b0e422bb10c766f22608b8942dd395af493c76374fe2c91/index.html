Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength through juxtaposition. The palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue invokes a sense of broken memories and soft nostalgia. The main contrast lies in the dense central cluster of dark hues which creates a stark collision against the lighter background, symbolizing the exposed center protected by a larger quiet field. The broken pixels throughout add intentional disruptions to reinforce the idea of fragmented vulnerability within a framework of strength.
