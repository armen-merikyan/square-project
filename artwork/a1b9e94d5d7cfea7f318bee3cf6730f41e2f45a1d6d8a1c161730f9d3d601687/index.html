Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional dynamic between longing and resolve, where the warm coral and soft amber colors evoke a sense of warmth and gentle yearning, while the cold slate and blue-black introduce a stark contrast, creating boundary and depth. The layout features a nearly enclosed border that invites attention while also allowing emotion to seep through deliberate gaps. The patterns suggest a lattice of resolve structured by the collisions of colors, representing both the firm axis of strength and the delicate threads of repair. Broken pixels add intentional disruptions, embodying the fragility of both longing and resolution. The primary collision occurs in the center and extends outward, inviting viewers into an exploration of tension and balance.
