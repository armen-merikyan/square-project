Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension of love and betrayal, utilizing a palette of warm reds and pinks contrasted against cooler shades of gray and dark maroon. The sequence moves from light to dark, representing a shift in emotions, with dense patterns of broken dots to suggest fragmentation and connection. The arrangement features a subtle invasion pattern, where colors collide and interact across the space, creating visual echoes of warmth interspersed with moments of coldness, embodying both intimacy and isolation.
