Artist Statement

This artwork captures the tension between desire and restraint. The palette reflects bright and muted colors, representing the interplay of saturated emotions and held boundaries. The central area uses intense colors in a checker pattern symbolizing desire, while the edges echo restraint's influence with softer shades and interruptions. The collision of vibrant color clusters in the center against cooler, muted tones creates a visual narrative of emotional struggle.
