Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the duality of fear and hope through contrasting color choices. The dark shades of black and dark violet symbolize fear and pressure, creating a dense environment that feels compressed. Conversely, the soft gold and pale green represent hope and lightness, emerging in an upward pattern like aspirations breaking through the weight of fear. The flow of the pixels is diagonal, suggesting a gradual release. The jagged edges evoke instability, amplifying the sense of collision, while strategic negative space allows for moments of calm, inviting viewers to reflect amidst the chaos. Overall, this composition captures the emotional journey from pressure to resolution, creating a layered experience of contrasting feelings.
