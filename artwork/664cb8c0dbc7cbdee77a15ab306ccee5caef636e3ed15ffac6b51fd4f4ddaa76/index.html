Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the discord of fear and hope; the palette of deep blacks and dark violets contrasts with soft gold and pale green, suggesting a duality. The main contrast emerges in the split composition: the left heavy with darkness, echoing fear, while the right embodies lighter colors, symbolizing hope. The radial and rain patterns represent the chaotic interactions of these emotions, creating a sense of rising tension and potential release, while strategic broken pixels hint at imperfection and vulnerability.
