Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional duality of safety and exposure, showcasing warmer earthy tones contrasting against cooler, serene colors. The soft greens signify shelter and protection, while the pale hues represent the vulnerability of exposure. The main collision occurs at the center, where dense stripes of warmth clash with the open blue, echoing a retreating ripple from an incoming wave. This dynamic creates visual tension, evoking a narrative of conflict and solace.
