Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional duality of softness and severity, with the warm hues of powder pink and sharp crimson contrasting against the cooler tones of carbon black and steel gray. The palette reflects a blend of gentle touches and colder edges, capturing the essence of tension and release. The main collision occurs in the center with a cluster of warmer colors, symbolizing the pulse of emotional conflict, while the cooler, darker borders signify containment. The eroded symmetry layout is reflected in the uneven distribution of colors, creating a sense of a central spiral that draws the eye in a counterclockwise motion, echoing the theme of conflict and resolution.
