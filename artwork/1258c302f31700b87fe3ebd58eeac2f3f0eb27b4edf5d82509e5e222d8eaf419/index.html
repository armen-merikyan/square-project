Artist Statement

This composition channels the conflict between 'spark' and 'ash' through a dynamic palette of soft and severe colors. The contrasting hues of warm white and sharp crimson create a sense of tension and urgency, juxtaposed against the muted tones of carbon black and steel gray that signify restraint and memory. The arrangement of colors in a diagonal pattern suggests a movement across the square, echoing the sense of wave and radial disruptions, while broken pixels introduce a deliberate imperfection, enhancing the narrative of resilience and fragility. The main collision occurs in the center, where bright and muted colors interact, symbolizing dynamic emotional interplay.
