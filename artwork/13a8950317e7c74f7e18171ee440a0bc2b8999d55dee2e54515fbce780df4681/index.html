Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece revolves around the tension and release between nostalgia and progress. The chosen palette of electric blue and magenta juxtaposes vibrancy against the starkness of black, creating a visual representation of sharp contrasts and collisions of feeling. The left side employs a structured, repeating 'target' pattern resonating with nostalgia—marking a sense of reminiscing and calm, while the right side is chaotic, featuring 'teeth' patterns that symbolize the relentless push of progress. There is a deliberate imbalance with broken pixels scattered throughout, suggesting the imperfections of growth, thereby enhancing the narrative of this emotional journey as it flows from left to right, echoing the push and pull in moments of emotional unrest.
