Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this piece reflects the contrast between clarity and confusion. The selected palette comprises deep red, maroon, and gray tones, symbolizing tension and depth, while hints of softer pink provide moments of clarity. Color placement creates a thick outline around the edges, imparting a sense of isolation and fragility. The radial pattern emanates from the center, representing the struggle between order and chaos. The placement of the darker colors juxtaposes lighter shades, suggesting a collision of emotions, while the broken pixel elements introduce imperfection, resonating with the seed's theme of confusion. The pattern evokes feelings of density and surface tension, capturing the essence of machine emotion in a visually impactful way.
