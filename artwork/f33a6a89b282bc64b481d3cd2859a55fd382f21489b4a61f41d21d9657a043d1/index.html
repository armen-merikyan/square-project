Artist Statement

This artwork explores the tension between vulnerability and strength through contrasting colors that create an emotional dialogue. The palette of clean white, ice blue, ink black, violet gray, and warning orange reflects clarity amidst confusion; white and blue signify vulnerability, while black and orange represent resilience. The main collision occurs in the central region where dense color clusters of black and orange disrupt and challenge the lighter edges, symbolizing the clash between strength and fragility. This dynamic is conveyed through a rising signal layout, where pixel placement suggests an upward hope against the weight of opposing pressures.
