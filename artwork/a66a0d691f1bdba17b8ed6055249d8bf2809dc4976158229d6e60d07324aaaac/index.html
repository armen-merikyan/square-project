Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between control and surrender through a deliberate imbalance. The chosen palette of 'ancient_fire' utilizes burnt orange and dark red to evoke intensity and urgency, contrasted against the muted charcoal. The center radiates lighter tones creating a halo effect of release yet is constrained by heavier outlines representing control. The main collision resides in the center where light colors clash with dark, symbolizing a crucible of emotions—structured yet yearning for freedom.
