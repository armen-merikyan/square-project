Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the juxtaposition of rage and calm, with a palette invoking intense red and soft pale blue interacting dynamically. The contrast is showcased through an asymmetric arrangement where explosive red segments collide with structured horizontal lines of soft blue, creating an interplay of noise and quiet. This mirrors the seed's concept of conflicting emotional states, manifesting as a visual 'threshold' where chaos meets containment. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, hinting at the disruption of calm by bursts of rage.
