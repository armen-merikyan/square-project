Artist Statement

This piece embodies the struggle between clear signals and overwhelming noise, using a palette of deep navy, stark black, and dim cyan to convey the emotional weight of isolation and interference. The sharp edges of color contrast create visual tension, while the broken pixels reflect imperfections and scars of communication. The main collision occurs towards the center, where the contrasting signals grapple for attention amidst the chaotic backdrop. The layout spirals outward, evoking a sense of movement that echoes the pulse of connectivity disrupted by chaos.
