Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength by using a contrasting palette of navy and dim cyan against a predominantly black backdrop. The collision occurs in the center where the darkness meets the vibrant blues, suggesting an exposed vulnerability fighting against a robust presence. This reflects a merging of emotions, with rhythmic, glitch-like patterns breaking through the quiet fields, creating a drip-like flow that embodies change and tension, each color represents a distinct feeling of pressure and release.
