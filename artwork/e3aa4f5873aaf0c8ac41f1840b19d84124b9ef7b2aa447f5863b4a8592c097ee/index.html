Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between softness and severity. The color palette balances gentle powder pink and warm white with stark carbon black and sharp crimson, embodying emotional contrasts. The central collision occurs between the delicate pink and harsh black, representing the push and pull of emotions. Patterns of dots signify severity, while split screens reflect softness, creating a dynamic interplay within an 8x8 grid that signifies a pulse of conflict and resolution. The negative space amplifies this tension, allowing each color to breathe within the arrangement.
