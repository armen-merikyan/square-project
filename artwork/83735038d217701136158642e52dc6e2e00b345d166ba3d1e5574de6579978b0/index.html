Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between safety and exposure, utilizing contrasting elements in its palette and arrangement. Shades of shelter green and warm brown dominate, representing safety, while a cluster of pale skin and open blue colors at the center symbolize exposure. The composition flows towards an echo-like rhythm on the right, while broken pixels disrupt the structure, evoking imperfections in our protective layers. The vertical split emphasizes the dichotomy, with a brighter center contrasting against the darker edges, showcasing the fragile balance between vulnerability and security.
