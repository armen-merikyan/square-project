Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the tension between control and surrender, utilizing a palette that interweaves cool and muted tones to create depth (lavender_gray, muted_plum) while punctuating it with celestial hints (moon_white). The main collision is located at the center, where dense, radial forms suggest pressure that fractures into soft edges, reflecting the transition from containment to dispersal. This reflects the seed's themes of boundaries both respected and breached, crafting a narrative of struggle and release. The architecture of the composition follows a horizontally split layout that emphasizes control at the top and surrender at the bottom, allowing the viewer to visually journey from rigidity to softness.
