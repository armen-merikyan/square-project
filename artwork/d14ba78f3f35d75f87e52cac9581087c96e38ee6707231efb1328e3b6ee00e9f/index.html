Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between love and betrayal, portrayed through contrasting warm and cool colors. A palette of pinks and reds against muted grays and dark maroon enhances this tension. The layout symbolizes descending emotions, resembling falling pixels, which represent tears of sadness, loss, or echoes of nostalgia. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, hinting at the sharpness of betrayal against the softness of love. This creates a collision where warmth meets cool detachment, symbolizing the complexity of human emotions and relationships.
