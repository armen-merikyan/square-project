Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between envy and gratitude through contrasting warmth and coolness of color. The palette, drawn from 'joy_grief', merges vibrant yellows and muted purples, signifying fluctuating emotions. The jagged edges create a sense of broken symmetry, reflecting the theme of imperfection, while the floral patterns of orange flames against dotted deep blue evoke a lively contrast in energy. This interplay is crucial to demonstrate the duality of feelings, with a clear collision happening in the center where color intensifies, illustrating the dichotomy of lightness and weight.
