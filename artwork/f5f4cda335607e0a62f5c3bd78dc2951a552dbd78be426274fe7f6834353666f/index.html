Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional spectrum of defiance versus submission by juxtaposing dark and light tones. The palette embodies a visual conflict—black and pale blue represent the struggle, while the whites and muted grays suggest surrender. The arrangement emphasizes falling pixels, creating a dynamic where colors collide at the center, echoing the weight of decisions. Breaks in the pattern introduce imperfection, aligning with the seed's theme of conflict. Negative space at the corners symbolizes unresolved feelings, while the density towards the center signifies the weight of emotions at play.
