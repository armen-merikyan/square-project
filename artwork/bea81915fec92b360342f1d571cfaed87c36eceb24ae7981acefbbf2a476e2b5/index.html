Artist Statement

The composition embodies the tension between loneliness and connection. The use of yellow and muted purple reflects a contrast between warmth and sorrow, echoing the emotional disparities suggested by the seed. The palette invokes a feeling of struggle with interconnected warmth (flame) against isolation (broken dots). The layout climbs from the left to the right, portraying a visual ascent intertwining with broken pixels, signifying feelings that both ignite hope and reflect fragmented memories. The collision occurs on the left side where the vibrant yellows clash against the cooler purples, suggesting a dynamic interaction between joy and grief.
