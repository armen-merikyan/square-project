Artist Statement

This abstract piece explores the tension between envy and gratitude through a contrasting use of muted and bright colors, creating a visual argument of order versus chaos. The dark maroon and gray represent heavy emotions, while pink offers a touch of warmth and hope, allowing for a dynamic interplay. The jagged edges and spiraling flow suggest disruptions, echoing the discontent and connectivity in these emotions. The mix of dots and sparks visually represents the discordant nature of feelings that come together yet resist resolution.
