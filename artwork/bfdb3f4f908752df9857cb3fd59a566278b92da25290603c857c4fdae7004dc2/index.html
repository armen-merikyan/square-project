Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between safety and exposure, establishing a dynamic tension. The palette, combining muted shelter green and warm brown with pale skin tones, reflects safety, while open blue and cold white represent exposure. The main contrast collides in the vertical split of colors, creating a sense of guarded vulnerability. The rising signal flow reflects hope, climbing towards a brighter echo while maintaining soft edges to symbolize protection amidst exposure.
