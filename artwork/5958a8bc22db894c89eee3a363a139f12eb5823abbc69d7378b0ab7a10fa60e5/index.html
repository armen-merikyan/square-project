Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between tenderness and rage. The choice of colors—saturated reds and soft pale blues—reflects the collision of these feelings. The upward flow suggests escape and hope, while the patterns intersect chaotically to echo conflict. The central area highlights a dramatic contrast, creating an abstract narrative of emotional complexity.
