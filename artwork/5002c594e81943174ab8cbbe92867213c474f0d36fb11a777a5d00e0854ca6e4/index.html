Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission. The central highlight of electric blue represents defiance, contrasting against softer static gray and dead black that embody submission. The use of signal green as a pulse mark accentuates the interaction between chaos and order. The collision occurs predominantly in the center, with a sharp division mirrored by the uneven borders, creating a complex emotional rhythm that embodies the tension described in the seed.
