Artist Statement

This artwork explores the duality of 'awe' and 'terror' through the contrast of dense, dark colors with sharp, striking accents. The palette transitions between deep blacks and steely blues, evoking pressure and signal noise, while the bright whites introduce moments of release and interruptions. The placement follows a rise vs. fall pattern – dark lower sections contrasted by radiant, upward accents suggesting the push-pull of emotion. Broken pixels add imperfection, hinting at the chaos underlying calm structure. The overall composition resonates with abstract emotions, allowing viewers to engage with both the turbulence and tranquility of life's collisions.
