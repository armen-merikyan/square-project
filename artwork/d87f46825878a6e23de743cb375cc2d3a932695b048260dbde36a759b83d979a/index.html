Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures a battle between longing and resolve, using a palette of vibrant and muted colors. The contrasting colors of electric blue and hot magenta create tension, while static gray acts as a quiet grounding field. The centerpiece reflects a collision of patterns, suggesting broken echoes and repair with intentional breaks across the structure. The design's flow moves subtly from the edge inward, simulating a pulling heartstring.
