Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects a tension between vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette of pale mint, soft sky, storm gray, near black, and faded gold captures this duality, with softer hues representing vulnerability and darker shades signifying strength. The central area, marked by clear barcodes, symbolizes a stable core under threat, while the outer edges fade into veils of soft colors, suggesting exposure and fragility. The main collision occurs at the center where the barcodes overlap with the thin, pale layers, interrupting the otherwise soft texture. This reflects a narrative of resilience against external pressures, forming the abstract pattern of strength rising from vulnerability.
