Artist Statement

The emotional tone draws from the dynamic tension between emptiness and desire, illustrating a journey of contrast through color. The warm colors of yellow and orange represent desire, bursting forth in blocks that suggest movement and intention on the top half. The cooler tones of deep blue and muted purple signify the expanse of emptiness below, framed by edges that echo a sense of isolation. The split layout enhances this dichotomy, with broken pixels providing an imperfect, human touch that reflects fragility. The main contrast lies between the vibrant upper section filled with energetic bursts and the quiet, contemplative lower half. This arrangement captures the essence of a heart yearning yet constrained, positioned between the beckoning signals of warmth and the chill of loneliness.
