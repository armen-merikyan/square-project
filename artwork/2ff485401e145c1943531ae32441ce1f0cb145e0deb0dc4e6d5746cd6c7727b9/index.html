Artist Statement

This composition captures the dual forces of control and surrender, represented by the structured grid of muted colors contrasted with vibrant bursts. The palette of 'safety_exposure' represents a blend of grounding and vulnerability, embodying both the desire to contain and the risk of overflow. The main tension occurs in the overfilled center, where warmer tones collide with cooler shades, echoing the struggle between restraint and freedom. Broken pixels hint at imperfection, enhancing the emotional depth.
