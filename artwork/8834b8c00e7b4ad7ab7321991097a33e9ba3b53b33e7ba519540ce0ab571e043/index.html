Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a balance between stability and exploration, representing certainty with structured colors and curiosity with fragmented patterns. The palette combines warm brown and shelter green to signify grounding, paired with cold white and open blue to evoke wonder. The main contrast is found diagonally, where the structured elements of certainty collide with the unpredictable aspects of curiosity, echoing the theme of rising signals against chaotic channels.
