Artist Statement

This abstract composition plays on the emotional contrast of vulnerability versus strength. The palette, drawn from 'desire_emptiness', creates an unsettled warmth with a base of charcoal, punctuated by the brightness of hot coral and blood orange. The positioning features a dense dark area with small bright clusters in the center, representing a fragile spark amidst overwhelming pressure. Broken pixels intersect the bright areas, symbolizing the tension between exposure and shelter, leading to moments of disruption throughout the piece.
