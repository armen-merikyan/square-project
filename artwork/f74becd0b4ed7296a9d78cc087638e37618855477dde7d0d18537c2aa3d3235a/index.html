Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the dual emotions of restlessness and peace, portrayed through a palette of signal green and electric blue against a backdrop of dead black and static gray. The use of split-screen and patchwork patterns creates a collision of chaotic energy versus calmness across its surface. The main contrast occurs diagonally, with dark colors dominating the corners but interrupted by bright accents, symbolizing hidden tensions bursting through the calm. The closed corners emphasize containment, while the broken pixel elements act as 'scars' that suggest moments of rupture in the peace. The overall layout mirrors across the center, evoking a sense of balance disrupted by slight imperfections, mirroring the emotional landscape of our struggles between restlessness and tranquility.
