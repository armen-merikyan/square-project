Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the tension between safety and exposure, utilizing a color palette that contrasts warm browns with cool whites and greens. The pale center embodies vulnerability, offset by a denser, earthy border, symbolizing shelter. The color placement creates visual echoes of ripples and braids, suggesting movement while maintaining a central calm, interrupted by the fractured edges. The flow is diagonal, offering a dynamic feel, with intentional broken pixels to invoke a sense of disruption and imperfection, capturing the delicate push and pull between two emotional states.
