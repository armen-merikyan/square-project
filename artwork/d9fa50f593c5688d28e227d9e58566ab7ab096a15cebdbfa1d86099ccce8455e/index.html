Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends softness with severity, navigating through gentle gradients and sharp contrasts. The palette combines warm white and powder pink representing softness, offset by carbon black and steel gray for severity. This creates a tactile duality, where the left side is dominated by warm tones, symbolizing vulnerability and tranquility, while the right side introduces harsher edges and deeper shadows to evoke tension and control. The main collision occurs at the center, where soft colors blend into starker contrasts, reflecting an interplay of guilt and forgiveness, echoing the seed's themes of emotional complexity. The broken pixels add intentional disruption and depth throughout the visual flow, enhancing the narrative of pulse and rhythm.
