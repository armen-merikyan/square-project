Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between loneliness and connection through a palette that juxtaposes deep black and soft pale blue. The outer border, dominated by black, symbolizes solitude and deep reflection, while the inner squares burst with pale blue, suggesting shimmering connections and the hope of communication. The pattern, with its cracked texture in the lonely areas and ring-like forms in the center, narrates a story of hidden vulnerabilities and exposed feelings. The arrangement aims for a quiet density contrasted by bursts of color, making the viewer resonate with the undercurrents of isolation amidst a yearning for connection.
