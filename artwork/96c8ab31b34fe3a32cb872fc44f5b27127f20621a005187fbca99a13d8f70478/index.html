Artist Statement

This composition contrasts the heaviness of fear with the lightness of hope through a split vertical layout. The dark, dense colors dominate one side, while lighter, glowing accents emerge from the center and gradually transition outward. The symmetrical yet broken pattern evokes a sense of collision and imbalance, illustrating unresolved emotional tension. The palette, with its soft gold and pale green, creates a hopeful glow that is tempered by the surrounding darkness, reflecting the complexities within the seed's themes.
