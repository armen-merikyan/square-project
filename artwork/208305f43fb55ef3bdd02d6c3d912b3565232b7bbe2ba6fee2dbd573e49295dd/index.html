Artist Statement

This piece encapsulates the tension between tenderness and rage, utilizing a palette of stark contrasts to reflect emotional states. The chosen colors—deep black symbolizing rage, soft pale blue representing tenderness, and occasional fiery red for intensity—work together to create a balanced discord. The structure follows a central pattern focus indicating hidden emotions, while lattice-like textures collide and bleed into one another, visually interrupting calm with hints of pressure. The pixel bleed adds imperfection, emphasizing emotional complexity.
