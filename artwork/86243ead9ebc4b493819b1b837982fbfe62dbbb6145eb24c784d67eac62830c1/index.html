Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between longing and resolve through a rich palette and diverse pixel arrangement. The soft pinks and moody dark maroon represent a duality of longing and strength, while the gray serves as a quiet space that both separates and connects the dense elements. The main collision occurs in the center, where more chaotic colors meet the calming contrast of gray, embodying the push and pull between resolution and desire. The placement of the 'veil' and 'flame' patterns creates an abstract rhythm, echoing their emotional tones without resorting to literal interpretations.
