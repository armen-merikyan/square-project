Artist Statement

This piece captures the ongoing tension between restlessness and peace, with a color palette of deep blacks and soft golds indicating an emotional struggle. The contrast between dark shades on the left (representing restlessness) and golden highlights on the right (symbolizing peace) creates a visible disturbance, particularly evident in the central collision area. The static pattern of the soft gold reflects an aspiring tranquility, while the crack pattern in dark tones interrupts that peace, suggesting a battle between memory and motion.
