Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes joy and grief. The palette incorporates bright yellows and oranges to symbolize joy, contrasted with deep blues and muted purples representing grief. This tension illustrates a split horizontal flow, where one side is dense and vibrant while the other is more subdued and lighter, creating a visual rhythm. Discrete color placements symbolize waves of emotion—ups and downs echoing the seed's theme of contrast. The thin outlines and broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection and complexity within the arrangement, reinforcing the emotional collision between joy and grief.
