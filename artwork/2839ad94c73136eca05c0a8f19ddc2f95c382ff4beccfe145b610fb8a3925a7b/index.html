Artist Statement

This composition explores the tension between restlessness and peace. The palette combines rich dark maroons, soft pinks, and subtle grays to evoke a spectrum of emotional depth—from chaotic movement to serene stillness. The broken pixel disruptions simulate scars of past memories, while the patchwork reflects a resolution towards peace. The main contrast occurs diagonally, creating an energetic flow that implies conflict while still allowing room for calm.
