Artist Statement

The emotional tone arises from the tumult of 'panic vs control,' weaving a tapestry of conflict and resolution through tension in hues. The palette of red and black juxtaposed against pale blue and white expresses the visceral clash of overwhelming emotions and moments of clarity. The main contrast occurs between the bright, dynamic ring pattern representing panic and the steady, targeted formation symbolizing control. This interaction creates an overall sense of unresolved motion, echoing the central theme of the seed.
