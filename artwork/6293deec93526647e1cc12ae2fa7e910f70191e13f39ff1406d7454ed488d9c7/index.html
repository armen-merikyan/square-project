Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between joy and grief, using a palette that transitions from warm yellows and oranges to deep blues and muted purples. The warm hues represent joy's rising signal, cascading like a rain effect, while the cooler colors ground the composition with a sense of heaviness. Contrast occurs at the vibrant intersections, where bright pixels collide with dark spaces, suggesting an ongoing struggle. The layout is designed with rising patterns, conveying hope amidst conflict, enhanced by the jagged edges that evoke a sense of burned emotion, emphasizing imperfection and complexity.
