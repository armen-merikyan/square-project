Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes quiet with pressure, invoking a sense of struggle between calm and intensity. The selected palette echoes this with muted tones of off_white and dust_gray to symbolize quietude, contrasted by compressed_red for pressure. The pattern of a maze, applied in the darker tones, represents the dense pressure, while the softer braid captures the quiet flow, creating a gentle disruption across the grid. The primary collision occurs at the center where dark reds and grays interplay with lighter hues, suggesting both tension and transient calm. This addresses the seed's notion of counterflow and shattering, presenting a visually engaging exploration of emotional complexity.
