Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional contrasts of longing and resolve through a color palette of soft pinks and deep maroons intertwined with grays, symbolizing the complexity of desire versus determination. The quiet fields of gray serve as a backdrop, while the bursts of red and pink convey urgency and emotion. The dominant resolution (70%) appears in deeper tones, creating a strong foundation, interrupted by lighter accents representing longing. The patterns suggest a pulse of movement through the rising layout, capturing the essence of climbing from damage to repair. Broken pixels add intentional disruptions, mirroring the fractures in aspirations. Each color and placement interacts, creating a dynamic composition that evokes a sense of upward momentum amidst contrasting emotions.
