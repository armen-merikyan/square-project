Artist Statement

This piece captures the emotional contrast between loneliness and connection through a focused center surrounded by a dense, darker border, symbolizing isolation. The color palette of deep reds and pale blues conveys warmth and cool distance, reflecting yearning and comfort, respectively. The darker edges emphasize a heavy emotional weight. The arrangement applies a crosshatch of warmth against starkness, playing with both hidden and exposed elements. The use of broken pixels creates a sense of imperfection, reflecting the tears of loneliness within a structured outer grid.
