Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional conflict between restlessness and peace through color and form. The palette, inspired by soft tones of 'melancholy_wonder', reflects tranquility with 'moon_white' and 'soft_cyan', while 'muted_plum' and dark tones introduce a sense of chaos. The main collision occurs in the central rows, where bright colors interject into darker fields, creating visual tension. The diagonal arrangement represents movement, suggesting a landscape of memory caught between stillness and disruption—a dance of emotional currents.
