Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between awe and terror through soft edges and split-screen effects. The palette, rooted in shades of red, pink, gray, and dark maroon, showcases the collision of hope and despair. The dark tones dominate, providing weight and instability, contrasted by lighter accents that evoke awe. Main contrasts occur at the boundaries of dark and light, suggesting a cohesive wave-like motion amidst chaos. The arrangement mimics a wave, with colors flowing into one another yet interrupted by 'broken' pixels symbolizing fragmented memories and instability within a stable structure.
