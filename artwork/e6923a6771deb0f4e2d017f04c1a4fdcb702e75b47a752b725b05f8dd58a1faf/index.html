Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between defiance and submission, where the bright signals of defiance (represented by coral and signal cyan) pierce through muted submission fields (soft amber and cold slate). The layout features a mirrored structure with intentional breaks, symbolizing interruption and uneven borders, suggesting conflicting forces. The four-color palette evokes warmth and coolness, broadening the emotional impact. The dominant central area contains the 'tiny plus' formation, while the outer layer presents a softer dot pattern, illustrating the complex dynamics of pressure and release within the square.
