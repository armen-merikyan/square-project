Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional turmoil of love and betrayal. A divided palette of warm shades of pink and red in the left half represents love, while cool grays and dark maroons on the right signify betrayal. The stark contrast creates an uneasy tension, reflecting the complexities of emotion. The pattern introduces a radial formation on the love side for warmth and connection, contrasting with interrupted rings on the betrayal side that symbolize disconnection and heartbreak. The edge style features glowing transitions to convey lingering feelings despite emerging cracks, resulting in an abstract portrayal of a fractured relationship.
