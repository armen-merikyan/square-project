Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension and interplay between joy and grief using contrasting colors and sharp edges. The warm yellows and oranges reflect moments of joy, while the muted purple and deep blue signify grief. The upper section features a rain-like pattern representing joy, while the lower section's checker pattern conveys fragmentation and unresolved feelings. The main contrast arises between the vibrant yellows and the somber blues, creating a collision of emotions that reflects the complex human experience.
