Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a contrasting palette dominated by deep blue and mourning black, symbolizing strength and quiet surrender respectively. The introduction of pale yellow as an accent color creates pivotal interruptions against the darker tones, akin to pulses in a grey landscape. The pixel arrangement features a central area of high density, surrounded by smoother, quieter gradients, symbolizing an unmoved center amidst the chaos of the edges—reflecting the idea of a stronghold against encroaching turmoil. Broken pixels serve as scars, highlighting the emotional tension and imperfections in the composition, while the uneven border suggests an ongoing struggle between order and chaos.
