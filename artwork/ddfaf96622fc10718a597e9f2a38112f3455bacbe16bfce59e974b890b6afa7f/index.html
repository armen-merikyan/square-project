Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between envy and gratitude through the use of dark and light colors. The palette, chosen from deep navy and black to shimmering dim cyan and a singular white, represents the heaviness of envy versus the uplifting essence of gratitude. The contrast of deep, dark tones with bright accents creates a collision of feelings, representing the struggle between negative and positive emotions. The layout reflects a nearly mirrored symmetry that is broken by subtle imperfections in color placement, illustrating the challenge of maintaining balance in emotional states. The selected zigzag flow creates movement, symbolizing the wavering journey of emotion, with the broken pixels introducing intentional imperfection. This pattern is deliberately balanced yet complex, suggesting unresolved tension and moments of calm within the chaos.
