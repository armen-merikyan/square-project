Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between isolation and connection, represented through the interplay of warm and cool colors. The palette draws from the duality of yellow and orange representing warmth and connection, contrasted with deep blue and muted purple symbolizing loneliness and introspection. The main contrast occurs in the outer layers, where dark and muted colors create a sense of pressure, while the center bursts with warmth and light, anchoring the composition. This reflects the seed by portraying hidden feelings vying against a backdrop of exposure and imperfection.
