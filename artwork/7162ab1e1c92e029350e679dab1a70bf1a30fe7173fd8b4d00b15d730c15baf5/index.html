Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional dichotomy of vulnerability versus strength through a contrasting color scheme. The palette consists of warm ivory and soft gold to represent vulnerability, intertwined with deep wine and stain brown to signify strength. The central collision occurs where the strength colors intersect with vulnerability at the diagonal, creating tension. The asymmetrical distribution of colors reflects the unpredictable nature of emotional experiences, allowing for a conversation between exposed light and dense structure, embodying the narrative of protection versus exposure.
