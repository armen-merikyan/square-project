Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between clarity and confusion. The selected palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue reflects a muted yet warm ambiance, reminiscent of memories. The arrangement utilizes a grid pattern for confusion and a crosshatch for clarity, contrasting sharply in density and rhythm. The broken pixels introduce intentional imperfections, mirroring the theme of asymmetry and unpredictability. The main contrasts occur between the dense, darker colors, suggesting confusion, against lighter spaces representing clarity, creating a visual pulse that echoes the emotional weight of the seed's themes.
