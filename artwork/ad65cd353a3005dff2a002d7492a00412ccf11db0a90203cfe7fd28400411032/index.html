Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a delicate balance between the softness of warm hues and the severity of dark contrasts. The palette features warm whites and powder pinks that evoke gentleness, set against the starkness of carbon black and steel gray, generating a dynamic tension. The main collision occurs in the center, where bright colors breach darker boundaries, symbolizing the conflict between containment and overflow, creating a visual echo. The non-uniform placement and broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, emphasizing the narrative of guilt and forgiveness.
