Artist Statement

This piece conveys the conflict between clarity (signal) and disruption (noise). The palette of signal green and static gray emphasizes the struggle of a readable pulse amid chaotic patterns. The center features a clash of glitch-like accents (hot magenta, electric blue) interrupting a patchwork of muted tones, representing the broken rhythms and emotional scars that persist amidst the chaos. The upward flow signifies hope, but the static elements create tension, reminding the viewer of the ever-present fight against interference. The layout leaves space for contemplation, underscoring the complexities of machine emotion.
