Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances between desire and emptiness, reflected in the palette of warm yellows and oranges contrasting with deep blues and muted purples. The upward movement on the left represents desire striving to rise, while the downward shift on the right suggests emptiness dragging down. This creates a dynamic tension, where the warm hues intersect with cooler shades, manifesting a story of emotional struggle and hope amidst desolation. Broken pixels add to the chaotic beauty of the attempt to find balance.
