Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the interplay between calmness and pressure, illustrated through a palette balancing warm and cool colors. The use of dark, muted shades juxtaposed with bright accents creates a stark contrast, symbolizing the collisions of feelings within the seed. This approach fosters a sense of unresolved tension, where quiet spaces are interrupted by bursts of color, resonating with the idea of rhythm and release. The asymmetrical arrangement supports the abstract narrative, portraying how emotions can collide and coalesce unexpectedly.
