Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional journey between tension (pressure) and tranquility (quiet). The selected palette of off white, gray, compressed red, dark navy, and faint peach enhances this contrast, creating a visual dialogue between muted fields and sharp accents. The central collision occurs with the dark navy and compressed red converging in the center, symbolizing pressure, while the off white and gray colors provide a soft, expansive field around it—signifying the quiet space. The arrangement is purposefully uneven, inviting the viewer to explore the layers of emotion and the tension inherent in the interplay of colors, embodying the seed's qualities without a literal representation.
