Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores the juxtaposition of defiance and submission, highlighted through the stark contrast of black and white against a soft pale blue. The palette choice of 'rage_calm' reflects tension through dark colors and relief through lighter hues, creating an emotional interplay. The main collisions occur where black edges interrupt the calm expanse of pale blue and white, representing the pressure and subsequent release. The pattern embodies a spiral flow which encourages a sense of movement, while broken pixels in strategic locations enhance the sense of imperfection and tension.
