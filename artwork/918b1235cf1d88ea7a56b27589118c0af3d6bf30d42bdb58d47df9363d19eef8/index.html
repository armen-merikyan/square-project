Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of love and betrayal, using warm reds and soft pinks contrasting against sharper dark maroon and gray for depth. The palette reflects warmth battling against the cold edge of betrayal. The main contrast lies in the arrangement: a warm gradient providing a backdrop for sharper, more aggressive patterns, suggesting a collision between affectionate and fractious emotional states. The layout starts from the bottom left, expanding diagonally, symbolizing a rise from tension towards resolution, while broken pixels introduce imperfections, echoing the idea of love's fragility as characterized by the seed themes.
