Artist Statement

This composition expresses the emotional dynamic between quiet space and pressing intensity. The palette combines soft off-white and faint peach to evoke calm, juxtaposed with compressed red and dark navy to represent pressure. The corner-focused layout creates a visual migration from density to emptiness, illustrating a flow from crowded to expansive areas. The collision of hard edges in the upper left and softer static patterns elsewhere emphasizes the struggle between these emotions, leading to moments of clarity amidst confusion.
