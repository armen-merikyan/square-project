Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between restlessness and peace, represented by warm burnt oranges and golds against deep, soothing charcoals and dark reds. The collision occurs diagonally, where the veil pattern of restless colors interrupts the ring of serene pixels. This arrangement suggests a narrative of motion caught within a stable frame, echoing the seed's themes of memory and interruption through color placement. The use of broken pixels adds an intentional imperfection, symbolizing the scars of past unrest amidst a quest for calm.
