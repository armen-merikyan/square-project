Artist Statement

This piece explores the emotional tension between awe and terror, illustrated through a palette of muted sepia, tan, and faded blue colors to represent broken memories. The contrast between soft edges and jagged disruptions conveys a feeling of instability, evoking the tension between safety and threat. The center is a calm blue, suggesting stability, surrounded by a turbulent mix of darker colors that collide against lighter patches. This arrangement produces a sense of pressure radiating outwards, capturing the essence of the seed through an abstract narrative of conflict and harmony.
