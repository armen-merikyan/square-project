Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork encapsulates the tension and interplay between longing and resolve, represented through a contrast of saturated colors and muted tones. The palette, featuring bright yellow and deep blue, reflects duality: warmth against depth, akin to a pulse of life amidst serene shadows. The dominant color is a vibrant yellow, occupying the center, as it symbolizes resolve in the face of damage, surrounded by areas of muted purple that represent longing. The collision occurs mainly in the center, where densely packed colors of broken dots intersect with hinge-like patterns, creating a rhythm of interruption and echo. This arrangement mirrors the complexities of emotional conflict vis-a-vis repair, allowing the viewer to traverse the nuanced gradients of feeling.
