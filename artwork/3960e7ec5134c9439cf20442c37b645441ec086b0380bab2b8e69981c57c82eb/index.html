Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes the darkness of fear with the glimmer of hope, represented through a palette that balances deep black and violet with hints of soft gold and pale green. The main contrast occurs across the vertical split, with darker colors anchoring the bottom, inspired by fear, while lighter, hopeful colors ascend toward the top in a rising signal. The structured grid pattern reflects the tension of fear while the mirroring effect of hope introduces a sense of balance and potential clarity. The broken pixels add imperfection, highlighting the unresolved nature of the emotional conflict.
