Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies a dance between fear and hope, illustrated through the interplay of dark and light colors. The palette includes deep blacks symbolizing fear and soft golden hues representing hope. The zigzag pattern creates a sense of rising tension, conveying an upward drive but interrupted by dark 'rain' dots, suggesting disruption and a breaking point. This reflects the conflict of emotion captured in the seed, creating a visual journey from the intense pressure in the corners toward a soft glowing center while maintaining negative space to avoid chaos, allowing the viewer to breathe amidst the turbulence.
