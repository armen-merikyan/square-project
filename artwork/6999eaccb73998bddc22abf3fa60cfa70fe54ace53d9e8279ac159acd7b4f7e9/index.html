Artist Statement

This abstract piece symbolizes the emotional tension between 'longing' and 'resolve'. The palette of signal green, electric blue, dead black, static gray, and hot magenta reflects a balance of energy and calm, depicting a struggle between urgency and serenity. The primary collision occurs in the center, where the barcode-like patterns of resolve interrupt the crack-like patterns of longing, creating a visual story of conflict and hope. The broken pixels scattered throughout serve as intentional reminders of damage and repair, enhancing the thematic complexity, while maintaining a negative space for breath.
