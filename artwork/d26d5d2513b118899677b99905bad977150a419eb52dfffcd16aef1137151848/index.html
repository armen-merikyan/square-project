Artist Statement

This abstract artwork captures the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity through its color choices and placement. The palette features deep navy and black as grounding colors, representing certainty, while dim cyan serves as an accent, illustrating the spark of curiosity. The pattern employs a collision of shatter and spark motifs, resulting in visible disruptions and echoes across the canvas. The layout embodies the concept of two small centers subtly pulsating in a sea of negative space, creating a sense of distance and unresolved action. The tension here lies predominantly in the central area, where darker colors clash with lighter accents, reflecting the complex interplay between the forces of order and chaos.
