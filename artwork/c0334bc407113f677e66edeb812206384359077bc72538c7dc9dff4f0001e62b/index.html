Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a battle between restlessness and peace, captured through the palette of warm and cool tones that symbolize intimacy and distance. The collision occurs diagonally, with cooler colors in one region interrupting warmer tones, echoing the tension between movement and stillness. The patterns evoke a steady ladder-like peace against uneven ember-like forms representing restlessness. Broken pixels serve as reminders of imperfection, aligning with the theme of memory and motion, culminating in an art piece that expresses unresolved emotional dynamics amidst contrasting states.
