Artist Statement

The emotional tone balances vulnerability and strength, mirrored in the contrasting colors and patterns. The chosen palette of pink, dark maroon, and gray adds tension, with pink representing strength and gray embodying vulnerability. The main contrast occurs at the square's center where broken dots disrupt the outer border of saturated colors, embodying an echo of conflict between exposure and protection. The uneven distribution of colors signifies the struggle and interdependence of these opposing forces, creating an abstract narrative of resilience amidst fragility.
