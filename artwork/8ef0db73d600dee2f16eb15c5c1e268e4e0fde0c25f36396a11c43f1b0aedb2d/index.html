Artist Statement

This abstract artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between tenderness and rage. The palette of deep reds, soft pale blues, and stark black and white is chosen to reflect a battle of warmth against coldness. The dominant reds represent the raw energy of rage occupying the center, while the cooler tones strive to contain it, suggesting an internal struggle. The checker and mirrored patterns collide at the center, serving as a chaotic accumulation of emotion, hinting at moments of vulnerability against the backdrop of turbulence. Broken pixels create intentional disruption, reflecting imperfections in the emotional narrative.
