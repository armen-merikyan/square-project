Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between relief and dread through a carefully selected palette and arrangement. The dark colors represent the heaviness and dread that lingers, while the golden accents introduce moments of relief. The top-heavy layout suggests an imbalance, with darker hues dominating the upper section, creating a visual 'weight.' Jagged edges and broken pixels contribute to a feeling of imperfection and disruption, enhancing the emotional collision. The patterns of cracks and mazes reflect the struggle between order and chaos.
