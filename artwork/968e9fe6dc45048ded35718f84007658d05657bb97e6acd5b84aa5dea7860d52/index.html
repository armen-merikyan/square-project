Artist Statement

The emotional tone expresses a struggle between clarity and interference, represented by a palette of sharp greens and vibrant magentas against muted grays and blacks. The contrasting elements create a sense of dynamism, suggesting distortion within clarity. The signalgreen creates a focal point at the center, clashing with the surrounding noise of static gray, embodying pulse versus chaos. The layout establishes a visually dense center, surrounded by quieter regions, portraying an 'island in an ocean' effect that amplifies the feeling of isolation amidst overwhelming noise.
