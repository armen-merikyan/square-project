Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between relief and dread. The warm palette of yellows and oranges on the left contrasts with deep blues and muted purples on the right, reflecting warm vs cool emotional experiences. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, while the radial and ring patterns suggest a struggle between harmony and chaos. The composition indicates a flow from bottom to top, symbolizing a journey from darkness to light, yet the echoing patterns evoke unresolved feelings.
