Artist Statement

The artwork embodies the emotional dichotomy of dread and relief, with a dense accumulation of dark tones on the top-left corner representing the weight of dread, contrasted by the lighter, freeing hues swirling outward. The palette features a balance of storm gray and faded gold that contribute to the sense of somber tension against soothing calm. The collision is noted in the top left where darkness meets soft mint, evoking an emotional shift. The spiral layout with broken pixel disruptions enhances the theme of imperfection amidst search for clarity, creating a captivating narrative of pressure and subsequent release.
