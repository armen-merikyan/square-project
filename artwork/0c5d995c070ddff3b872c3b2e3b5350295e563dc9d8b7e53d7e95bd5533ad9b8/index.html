Artist Statement

This piece embodies a dynamic contrast of strength and vulnerability using a palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue. The central region is dominated by strong, clear blocks that represent stability, while thin shells and softer colors at the edges depict exposure and vulnerability. The collision occurs in the center where colors blend, evoking the tension and complexity of human emotions. The broken frame layout allows colors to seep through, symbolizing the constant interplay of shelter and exposure.
