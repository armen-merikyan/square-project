Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the tension between feelings of loneliness and the desire for connection. The palette, composed of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon, evokes a sense of warmth and betrayal, emphasizing the contrast between inviting and isolating hues. The main collision occurs in the center, where clustered warm colors suggest bursts of connection, while the surrounding gray tones create spacious loneliness. This configuration symbolizes a struggle, where vibrant connections attempt to pierce through dense layers of isolation, creating patterns that reflect a both an ancient and primal sense of fire—capturing the rawness of emotion in abstract form.
