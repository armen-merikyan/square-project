Artist Statement

This abstract artwork evokes the emotional tension between desire and restraint through a collision of warm and cool tones. The palette features saturated hues of red and small orange, symbolizing desire, while muted creams and dark grays address restraint. The placement reflects a spiral pattern pushing outward, suggesting the overwhelming nature of longing against a boundary of coolness. The main collision occurs centrally, where the warm tones create vibrant ripples interrupted by the rigidity of muted colors, illustrating the struggle between containment and release.
