Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition explores the pull between longing and resolve, represented through a transition from muted to vibrant colors. The selected palette ('melancholy_wonder') utilizes a blend of soft blues and grays to symbolize longing, contrasted with assertive patches of lavender and cyan that signify resolve. The main collision occurs centrally with a strong contrast of soft cyan interrupting the muted plum background, embodying the tension between chaos and order. The abstract pattern evokes a feeling of upward movement, inviting contemplation on distance and intimacy while maintaining a serene negative space that emphasizes emotional weight.
