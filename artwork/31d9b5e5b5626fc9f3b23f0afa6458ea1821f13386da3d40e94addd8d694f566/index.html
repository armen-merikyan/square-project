Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the tension between longing and resolve, utilizing a palette of moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold to embody fragility and resilience. The contrasting hues create an interplay of light and shadow, with the central hook shape suggesting both a quest for connection and the weight of disconnection. The collision occurs centrally, where the strength of the constellation pattern meets the softness of the hinge, illustrating a visual narrative of pressure against tranquility, with broken pixels introducing deliberate disruptions that echo the theme of repair amidst damage.
