Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the push and pull between softness and severity, reflected through a palette of warm whites and stark blacks to emphasize contrast. The pulse marks pattern weaves through the grid, suggesting tension and unresolved motion, while the darker colors are concentrated towards the bottom, evoking a weighted sensation. The main collision occurs in the center where light pulses against dark, enclosing a halo of soft tones, indicative of the dynamic tension embedded within the emotional scores of the seed.
