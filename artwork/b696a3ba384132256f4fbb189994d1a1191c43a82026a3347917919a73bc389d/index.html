Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between joy and grief through a carefully balanced palette of vibrant yellows and deep blues, evoking both brightness and melancholy. The composition uses a structured layout with a sharp contrast between orderly patterns on one side and chaotic brushstrokes on the other, representing the collision of emotion. The zigzag flow connects colors that create a rhythm across the canvas, while the broken pixels offer a nod to imperfection and the complexity of human experience, leading to an unresolved tension that mirrors the struggles implied by the seed.
