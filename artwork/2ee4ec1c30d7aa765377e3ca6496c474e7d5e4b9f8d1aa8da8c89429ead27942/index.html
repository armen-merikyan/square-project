Artist Statement

The emotional tone of 'longing vs resolve' is expressed through a palette that juxtaposes calming neutrals with striking red. The grid starts with longing colors in the top left corner, gradually transitioning to the resolute tones in the bottom right, creating a clear diagonal movement that breaks into a pulsating contrast. The use of dark navy allows the compressed red to stand out, symbolizing urgency intertwined with serenity. This formation embodies a thread of interconnected emotional pulls, represented by the intentional placement of broken pixels scattered throughout, echoing the feeling of imperfection amidst resolution.
