Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength. The chosen palette of dark navy and dim cyan against the starkness of black and white creates a vivid clash—a metaphor for exposure and shelter. The central pulse markers clash with stepwell patterns, illustrating the collision of fragile exposure piercing through a dense protective layer. The upward flow signifies hope amidst struggle, while the broken pixels disrupt order, echoing the imperfections in our emotional states.
