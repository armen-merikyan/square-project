Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts nostalgia, represented by the earthy, muted tones, with progress symbolized by vibrant, luminous colors. The palette of yellow and deep blue evokes both joy and grief, encapsulating the dichotomy of memory versus ambition. The pattern is designed to embody both the upward and downward flows, creating a visual tension that suggests conflict between two sides of the canvas. The deliberate placement of broken pixels imparts an irregularity aligning with the themes of imperfection in life's journey, while leaving significant negative space enhances the feeling of unresolved motion.
