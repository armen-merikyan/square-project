Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle of longing versus resolve, captured through a palette that evokes warmth and distance. The contrasting calmness of distant lilac and the intensity of lamp gold illustrate the push and pull of emotions, creating collision and tension in the central area. The arrangement embodies both the healing process and the impact of damage with scattered broken pixels, representing scars in a seamless yet disjointed flow.
