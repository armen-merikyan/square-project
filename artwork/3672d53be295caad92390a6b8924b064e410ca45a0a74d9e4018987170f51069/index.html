Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between ignition and decay. The palette features warm shades of rose clay and lamp gold, representing the spark, contrasted with cool blues and distant lilacs, symbolizing ash and restraint. The interplay of crosshatch patterns on warm fields surrounded by jagged edges emphasizes the collision of memory versus erasure, creating both a calm center and dynamic interruptions. The broken frame layout allows the emotion to leak through, signifying resilience amidst fragility.
