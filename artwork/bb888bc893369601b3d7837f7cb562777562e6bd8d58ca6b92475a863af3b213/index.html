Artist Statement

This composition explores the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, using a palette that balances warm and cool tones to represent the conflict and intersections of these abstract qualities. The chosen colors—ripe green and honey gold as warm accents against deep olive and acid green cool shades—suggest resilience amidst fragility. The layout features a nearly enclosed frame with intentional breaks, where the weight of the ‘strength’ pattern collides with the ‘vulnerability’ sparks, mainly at the center, embodying an emotional push-and-pull. Broken pixels create subtle disruptions, enhancing the sense of imperfect connection, while creating areas of quiet amidst dense clusters, telling a story of tension and release.
