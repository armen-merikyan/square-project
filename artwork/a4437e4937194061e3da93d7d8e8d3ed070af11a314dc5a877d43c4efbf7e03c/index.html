Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between confusion and clarity, utilizing a contrasting palette of burnt orange and dark red to signify warmth amidst chaos, against a backdrop of charcoal that embodies confusion and noise. The checker and mirror patterns create rhythm and tension, where symmetry is gently disrupted, evoking a sense of uncertainty. The arrangement culminates in collision points where brighter accents interrupt denser fields, echoing the emotional push and pull of the narrative.
