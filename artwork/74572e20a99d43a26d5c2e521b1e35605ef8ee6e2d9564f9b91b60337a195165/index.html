Artist Statement

This composition embodies the tension between defiance and submission through contrasting color dynamics. The dominant dark tones represent defiance, accentuated by bright patches that symbolize the push against submission, creating a sense of interruption and glitch. The muted orange and deep blue establish a complex emotional landscape, where the brighter yellow areas collide with the darker hues in the center, emphasizing the struggle of the vessel shape as it attempts to break free from its confines, reflecting the theme of thresholds and barriers.
