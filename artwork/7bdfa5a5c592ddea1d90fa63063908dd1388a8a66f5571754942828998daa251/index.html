Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between pressure and quiet, represented by a dense center that gradually dissipates towards the edges. The palette of muted whites, soft grays, and a single accent of compressed red emphasizes tension against soft light. The contrast is mainly found in the center, where deep hues collide with airy fields, symbolizing the fight between chaos and calm. The patterns of rain and barcode reflect the push and pull of emotional experiences. Broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, reinforcing the theme of shattering against tides of feeling.
