Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between vulnerability and strength, creating an emotional landscape where soft breaks meet rooted contrasts. The palette of ripe green, acid green, and honey gold reflect a dynamic interplay of vitality and fragility. The central pattern—a grid representing vulnerability—intersects with a lattice of strength, creating a pulse of color that echoes through the square. The broken pixels disrupt the harmony, symbolizing the fractures of vulnerability amidst stability, enhancing the overall narrative without defining it literally.
