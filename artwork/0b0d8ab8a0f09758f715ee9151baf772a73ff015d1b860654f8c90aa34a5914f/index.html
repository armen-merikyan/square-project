Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between envy and gratitude—two opposing forces represented through a palette of soft pinks and deep reds. The colors are arranged to create a sense of rising hope on one side, contrasted against the darker maroons that imply heaviness and envy. The layout directs the eye upwards, symbolizing a yearning for escape or elevation. This juxtaposition becomes a dance between soft edges and stark contrasts, creating an emotional tension that embodies the fragility of desire versus appreciation.
