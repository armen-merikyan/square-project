Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette, rooted in 'mourning renewal', blends deep blues and soft greys that evoke a sense of depth and reflectiveness, contrasted by pale yellow, suggesting fleeting hope. Main collisions occur in the center where the muted tones of longing meet the structured resolve - a tension that invites the observer into a space of contemplation. The deliberate inclusion of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions, mirroring the imperfection inherent in our experiences.
