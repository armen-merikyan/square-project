Artist Statement

This artwork expresses the tension between relief and dread, encapsulating a sense of weight and relief through contrasting palettes. The warm reds symbolize rage and intensity, while the cool tones of pale blue and black evoke calm and depth. The layout features a split core, with the outer border darker and more oppressive, punctuated by warmer, lighter hues within. The introduction of broken pixels captures the imperfections in life, while the interplay of flame and stair patterns suggests a journey upwards amidst uncertainty.
