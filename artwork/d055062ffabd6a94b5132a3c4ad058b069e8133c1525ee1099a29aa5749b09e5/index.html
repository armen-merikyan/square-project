Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between defiance and submission, showcased through an eruption of scattered intensity amidst more subdued tones. The palette features muted hues of dark navy and faint peach, bridging the assertive presence of compressed red against the quiet backdrop of dust gray and off-white. The central collision happens diagonally, where bright colors pierce through the calm, echoing the idea of a 'threshold.' Broken pixels disrupt the layout, symbolizing imperfections in both chaos and order.
