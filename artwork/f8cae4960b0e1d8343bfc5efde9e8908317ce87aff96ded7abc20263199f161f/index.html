Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette of clean white and ice blue suggests clarity and serenity, while ink black and warning orange punctuate the composition, symbolizing urgency and disruption. The collision occurs diagonally, with longing expressed through ripples of cool blues negotiating against the firm channels of the resolving tones, creating a dialogue of tension and resolution. The layout features a central seam where the two emotions meet, yielding a dance of organized chaos and quiet spaces, honoring the seed's themes of brokenness and repair.
