Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this artwork reflects the tension between envy and gratitude, using a color palette of lime, electric blue, and magenta against a black background to create vivid contrasts. The arrangement focuses on a central energy of 'seed', suggesting growth or a core feeling, while broken pixel placements introduce disruption to the mirrored concept, evoking the theme of imperfection within symmetry. The zigzag pattern captures movement, hinting at the ups and downs of emotional shifts, while allowing negative space for breath and reflection. The contrast between vibrant colors and black provides a collision of intensity against calmness, representing the duality of emotions effectively.
