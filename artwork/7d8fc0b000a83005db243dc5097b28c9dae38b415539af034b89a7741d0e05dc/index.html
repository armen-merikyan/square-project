Artist Statement

The emotional tone explores tension between vibrant sparks and the soft residue of ash, reflective of hunger and restraint. The palette of moss green, bone white, rust, stone gray, and small gold embodies the resilience-fragility dichotomy. The main contrast is centered, where flame-like patterns collide with ash stripes, creating an intense emotional space marked by quiet and explosive energy. This design reflects the ideas of conflict and memory through abstract representations of movement and stillness.
