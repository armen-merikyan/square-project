Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between longing and resolve, embodied through a palette inspired by warmth and depth. The use of stain brown and deep wine evokes a sense of attachment and sorrow, while soft gold and warm ivory signify hope and clarity. The main collision occurs centrally, where the glitch pattern of resolve meets the softer ash field of longing, creating a rich tension and echoing the interplay of these emotions. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, signifying repairs amid the emotional landscape, while the quiet fields provide necessary space for reflection.
