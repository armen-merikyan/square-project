Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between certainty and curiosity through a collision of colors and patterns. The palette, inspired by 'hunger_restraint,' employs dark, muted tones contrasted by brighter accents. The outer edge is dominated by the certainty of deep blacks and grays, emphasizing a strong boundary, while the inner core explodes in a chaotic yet rhythmic arrangement of brighter colors, symbolizing curiosity's unresolved nature. The composition evokes the push-pull of rising signals and receding edges, creating a visual representation of tension and release.
