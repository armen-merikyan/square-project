Artist Statement

The emotional tone juxtaposes restlessness and peace, capturing a tension between unsettled borders and quiet symmetry. The palette of deep olive and ripe green evokes a vigorous contrast, while honey gold acts as an accent. The main collision occurs in the center, where a dense scar pattern interrupts the maze of peaceful colors, illustrating an internal argument. The use of broken pixels introduces intentional disruptions that mirror life's imperfections, leading to a compelling abstract narrative.
