Artist Statement

This piece conveys the emotional struggle between 'signal' and 'noise', utilizing a palette of navy and black against the brightness of single white and dim cyan. The prominent use of dark colors suggests weight and confusion, while brighter colors represent clarity and hope amidst the chaos. The key contrast occurs between the center and edges, with crowded textures in darker hues colliding against voids of white. The layout mimics a 'scattered repair' motif, where interconnected paths in white and cyan attempt to break through the static, filling the chaotic composition with intention.
