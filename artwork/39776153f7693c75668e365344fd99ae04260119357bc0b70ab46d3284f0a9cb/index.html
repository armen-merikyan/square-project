Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a struggle between restlessness and peace, encapsulating their tension with dark, rich colors against soft highlights. The palette combines deep black and dark violet to convey restlessness, while soft gold and pale green add a sense of calm and light. The main collision occurs diagonally, where the maze-like pattern of black and dark violet contrasts with structured stacks of gold and pale green, representing peace. This interplay creates a visual dialogue of tension and release, echoing the seed's thematic elements without literal representation.
