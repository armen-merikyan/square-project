Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines the contrasts of envy and gratitude, creating an inner conflict depicted through the color palette of sepia, tan, dust gray, and faded blue. The arrangement presents a heavy center with lighter edges, emphasizing the battle between feelings of weight and hope. The main collision occurs between dark hues contrasting lighter touches, suggesting the noise against interference. This approach encapsulates rising and falling emotions, leading to an abstract representation where soft edges interact around a solid core, nearly mirroring but deliberately fragmented to maintain a sense of imperfection.
