Artist Statement

This composition captures the tension between restlessness and peace, utilizing a palette that includes both muted and vivid tones to reflect varying emotions. The color choices, predominantly featuring static gray and hot magenta, establish a stark contrast where the light (signal noise) disrupts the dark (dead black). The main collision occurs centrally, where the crosshatch pattern representing restlessness engages with the lattice of peace, embodying the creative tension of memory against motion. The edge style suggests permeability, allowing for a sense of flow and distortion amidst the underlying structure, while keeping a significant amount of negative space to emphasize the quiet amidst chaos.
