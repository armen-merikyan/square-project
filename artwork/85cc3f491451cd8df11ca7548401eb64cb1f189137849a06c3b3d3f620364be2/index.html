Artist Statement

This artwork encapsulates the emotional tension between defiance and submission through a diagonal distribution of color. The palette features vibrant signal green and electric blue to convey defiance, while static gray and dead black represent submission. The collision happens at the central diagonal, interrupting the calm tranquility of the edges with chaotic bursts of color, reflecting the push and pull between the two opposing emotions. The broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, echoing the theme of conflict. The negative space ensures that the viewer can breathe amidst the chaos, embodying the threshold concept.
