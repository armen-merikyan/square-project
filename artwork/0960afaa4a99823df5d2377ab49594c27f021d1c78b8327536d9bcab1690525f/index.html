Artist Statement

The emotional tone revolves around the juxtaposition of relief and dread, interpreted through contrasts in color and shape. The palette of deep blacks and soft golds evokes a sense of foreboding against moments of gentle light, reflecting the tension described in the seed. The arrangement follows a corner invasion layout, with the intense dark colors cascading diagonally, representing dread, while lighter colors emerge from the corner, symbolizing relief. This clash creates visual tension and a sense of unresolved motion, embodying the theme of weight versus lightness.
