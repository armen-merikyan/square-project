Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the friction between defiance and submission, illustrated through the palette of warm and cool colors. The contrast arises from the saturated warmth of hot coral and blood orange clashing against the subdued tones of charcoal and hollow blue. The central pixels create a soft rise, embodying subtle resistance, while the surrounding pixels echo chaotic disruptions. This uneven layout fosters an abstract dialogue of tension, capturing the essence of conflict without literal representation.
