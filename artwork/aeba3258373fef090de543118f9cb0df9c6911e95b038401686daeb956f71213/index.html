Artist Statement

This piece navigates the emotional contrast between pressure and quiet through a carefully chosen color palette. The muted tones of faint peach and dust gray represent the calm, while the compressed red introduces an element of intensity and pressure. The layout consists of stacked bands, embodying the shifting densities of emotion, with a pronounced collision occurring in the center, where the pressure is most evident. Broken pixels infuse the composition with intentional imperfections, reflecting the chaos of shattering tides. The overall composition invites the viewer to sense both the weight of pressure and the serene moments of quiet.
