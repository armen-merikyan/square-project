Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional conflict between restlessness and peace. The chosen palette, featuring lime, electric blue, black, and magenta, embodies a modern, machine-like tension. The symmetry at the center represents a calm core disrupted by jagged edges that reflect restlessness and interruptions. The collision occurs diagonally, where contrasting colors meet, suggesting a push and pull between order and chaos, echoing the struggle to find tranquility amidst disturbance.
