Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the tension between trust and suspicion, depicted through a color palette that captures warmth and unease. The 'love_betrayal' palette of pink, red, gray, and dark maroon illustrates this duality, with dark maroon dominating to represent suspicion. The layout features a spiral pressure where the center is dense yet softened by pink and gray threads, representing trust. The collision occurs at the center, where warm colors meet darker tones, representing conflict. Broken pixels detract from perfection, echoing fragility, while maintaining quiet spaces enhances overall tension and emotional depth.
