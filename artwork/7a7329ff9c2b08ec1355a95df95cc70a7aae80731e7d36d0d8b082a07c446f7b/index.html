Artist Statement

This piece captures the tension between control and surrender through a densely packed center that contrasts with softer outer edges. The selected palette of ripe green and deep olive represents growth and stability, while honey gold acts as a warm accent, suggesting moments of brightness. The collision occurs in the center where the thick outlines of 'control' interrupt the softer, flowing patterns of 'surrender,' highlighting a battle between rigidity and fluidity. The layout offers an island of pressure surrounded by a quieter field, evoking a story of internal conflict and external pressures.
