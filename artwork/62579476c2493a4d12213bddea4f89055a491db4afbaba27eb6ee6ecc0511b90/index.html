Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the struggle between light and dark, softness contrasted with severity. The palette consists of powder pink and warm white suggesting gentleness, while carbon black and steel gray represent harshness, creating a push and pull in the composition. Main contrast occurs in the center, where sharply contrasting colors collide, embodying overlapping tensions. The broken frame layout further enhances the feeling of disruption, allowing glimpses of expanded emotional space. This rhythmic arrangement echoes the pulse of guilt and forgiveness, visualizing a journey of conflicting feelings through color.
