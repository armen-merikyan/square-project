Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the struggle between control and surrender, represented by deep blues and blacks for containment against the softer yellows and greens that overflow, suggesting an emotional crescendo. The collision occurs at the center with clusters of pale yellow interrupting the black, symbolizing the struggle for control amid chaos. Each color supports the theme of pressure vs release, while the imperfect edges emphasize the nuanced complexity of control.
