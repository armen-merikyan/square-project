Artist Statement

The emotional tone is a juxtaposition of envy and gratitude, represented through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette of navy and dim cyan evokes a sense of depth and introspection, while black accentuates moments of emotional intensity. The arrangement leans heavily towards symmetry but disrupts it with broken edges, creating tension. This reflects the inner conflict suggested by the seed, where the rising and falling emotions create waves across the square. The flow is horizontal, imparting a pulse-like rhythm that symbolizes ongoing internal dialogue and reflective moments.
