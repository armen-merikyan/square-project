Artist Statement

The emotional tone combines feelings of panic and control, illustrated by contrasting patterns: orderly stripes against chaotic pixel bleed. The palette of navy, black, white, and dim cyan emphasizes a lonely signal amid turmoil. The main collision of color occurs where structured lines of control intersect with blocks of unstructured chaos, creating a tension that feels almost tangible. The arrangement reflects a battle between calm and pressure, with negative space allowing the chaos to breathe.
