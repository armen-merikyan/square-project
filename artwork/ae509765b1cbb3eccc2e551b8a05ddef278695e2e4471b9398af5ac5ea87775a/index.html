Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional struggle between control and surrender. The palette, dominated by cosmic indigo and void black, evokes a sense of awe and terror. The thick outlines of the control patterns clash against the softer forms of the surrender patterns, creating a tangible tension. The central collision of dark colors around a release of cold gold subtly represents the overflow versus containment concept, with denser shapes near the center releasing towards the edges, symbolizing a stuttering journey of pressure and release.
