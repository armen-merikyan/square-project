Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tone of the seed through a balance of warm and cool colors, representing the interplay between pressure and serenity. The arrangement features soft blues and greens that suggest a quiet space interspersed with vibrant reds and yellows, creating a sense of disruption and release. Main contrasts collide at the center, where deep blues meet fiery reds, symbolizing emotional collisions that resonate through rhythm and abrupt changes. The overall pattern features clusters of intense color that draw the eye while maintaining softer tones in the background, echoing the layered complexity of feelings like tension and calm.
