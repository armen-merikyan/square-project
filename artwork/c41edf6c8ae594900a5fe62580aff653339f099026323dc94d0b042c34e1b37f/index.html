Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the tension between longing and resolve. The palette combines deep blacks and dark violets to create a sobering atmosphere, contrasted with hints of soft gold and pale green, suggesting moments of hope amidst density. The layout, reminiscent of 'last light', includes a dim field with a bright cluster that subtly breaks through the surface—a reflection of intimacy in distance. The main collision occurs in the center, where the softer colors challenge the darker tones, manifesting the struggle between chaos and order. The split patterns symbolize unresolved emotions and the diagonal edge suggests movement amid stillness.
