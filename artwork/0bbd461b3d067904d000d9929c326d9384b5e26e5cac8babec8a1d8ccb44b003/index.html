Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends panic with control, illustrating an internal struggle between chaos and stability. The palette, structured around deep blacks and dim cyans, underscores a lonely signal amidst the stark contrast. The left side uses broken dots to evoke panic while the right employs cracks to suggest fragmented control. The movement flows diagonally, enhancing the feeling of tension and unresolved balance as brighter elements clash with darker fields, culminating in an abstract dance of emotion that highlights a struggle for equilibrium.
