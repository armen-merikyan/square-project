Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts the undercurrents of trust with rising suspicion, utilizing a palette that incorporates warmth and softness against colder shadows. The warm hues signify trust and openness, creating an inviting space, while the darker colors introduce suspicion and pressure, expressing tension. The layout centers on an interaction of radial patterns symbolizing trust, which collide with stacked patterns of suspicion, particularly at the edges and center. The strategic placement of broken pixels enhances the narrative of resilience amidst fragility, while maintaining necessary negative space that suggests an unfolding story of emotional depth.
