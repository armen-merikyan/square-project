Artist Statement

This artwork conveys the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The use of dark colors like blackened plum (#3b0a45) represents a depth of longing, while lighter hues like muted cream (#f2e6d3) suggest moments of clarity and resolve. The placements create a stark contrast in the center, symbolizing the collision of feelings. The scattered broken pixels hint at imperfection and the disruptions in our paths, mirroring the idea of broken mirrors and unresolved emotions.
