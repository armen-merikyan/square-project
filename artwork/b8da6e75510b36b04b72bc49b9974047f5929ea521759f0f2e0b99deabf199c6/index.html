Artist Statement

The emotional tone of this composition reflects a dynamic interplay between vulnerability and strength. The palette combines the soft aqua and mist gray with the striking alarm red and deep ink blue, creating layers of serene urgency. The main collision occurs centrally, where dense, clear blocks of strength weave through thin, split-screen patterns of vulnerability, suggesting a flood against a spark. The broken pixels serve as intentional disruptions, representing the scars of exposure in contrast to the protection of shelter, giving the piece an engaging texture and emotional depth.
