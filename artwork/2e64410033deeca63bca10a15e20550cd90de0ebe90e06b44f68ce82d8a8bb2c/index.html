Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between dread and relief using a palette dominated by navy and dim cyan, conveying depth and hidden weight. The left side represents dread through a darker, more chaotic arrangement, while the right side embraces a serene pattern of rings, suggesting relief. The main contrast occurs at the vertical midpoint, where dark, broken pixels clash against lighter, symmetrical patterns, illustrating a struggle between chaos and calm. The emotional tones of both relief and dread resonate through the juxtaposition of patterns—'crack' on the left versus 'ring' on the right—creating a visual narrative of conflict and harmony.
