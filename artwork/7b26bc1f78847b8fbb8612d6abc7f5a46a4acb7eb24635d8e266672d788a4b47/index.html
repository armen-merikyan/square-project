Artist Statement

This abstract composition embodies the emotional collision between love and betrayal. The outer frame is a mix of deep reds and maroons, representing the intensity of betrayal, while the inner 4x4 core uses softer pinks, symbolizing love. The edge style is heavy with dark colors, creating pressure, while the subtle dot pattern conveys an underlying sense of imperfection. The balance between warm shades and dark hues gives a visual tension, portraying an emotional push and pull reflective of inner struggles and releases.
