Artist Statement

The artwork explores the tension between fear and hope, using a palette of dark violet, soft gold, and pale green that represents both emotions' complexity. The layout features falling pixels to evoke both the feeling of rain and descending pressure. The predominantly dark colors at the bottom create a sharp contrast with the soft gold, suggesting a rising signal of hope. The pattern incorporates crossed and split designs, signifying disruption from fear and the potential triumph of hope, creating a visual flow upwards that signifies an escape from darkness.
