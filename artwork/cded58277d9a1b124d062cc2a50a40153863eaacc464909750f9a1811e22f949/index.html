Artist Statement

The emotional tone of the artwork embodies a duality of clarity and confusion, employing a palette of dark blues and black alongside a striking white to create sharp contrast. The use of thick outlines against a backdrop of broken pixels models the collision of structured clarity against chaotic confusion. The spiral clockwise flow from the center outward mimics ‘falling pixels’, evoking a sense of descent or disarray, while the center-heavy edge style emphasizes the idea of concentration versus dispersion. The resulting abstract pattern speaks to an emotional narrative of tension and resolution.
