Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional dichotomy of joy and grief, expressed through contrasting colors and patterns. The palette combines warm yellows and oranges symbolizing joy against deep blues and muted purples representing grief. The rising layout suggests hope and upward motion, while the hard edges introduce a sense of threat and chaos. The split pattern mirrors the emotional tension, where brighter colors encounter the darker tones, creating powerful collisions of feeling. Randomly placed broken pixels introduce a sense of imperfection and complexity, reflecting life's unpredictability.
