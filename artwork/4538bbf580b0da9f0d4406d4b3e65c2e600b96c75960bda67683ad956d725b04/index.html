Artist Statement

The chosen colors reflect the emotional tension between tenderness and rage. Dark reds signify the intense passion of rage, while pale blues suggest a calm, tender resistance. The dense cluster of dark colors in the center symbolizes turmoil, contrasting sharply with the lighter edges that represent tenderness and quiet. The palette blends saturated hues with gentle tones, embodying the collision of feelings. Rows of darker pixels bleed into softer colors, evoking introspection amidst turbulence, reflecting the nuances of guilt and forgiveness.
