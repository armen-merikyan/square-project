Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the interplay of joy and grief, represented through a palette of warm yellows and oranges contrasting with deep blue and muted purples. The juxtaposition is evident in the zigzag flow, where warm colors dominate the upper half, suggesting the brightness of joy, while cooler tones sink into the lower half, embodying grief. The visible clashing within rows creates a pattern of emotional tension, echoing the themes of exposed joy against hidden grief. The chosen palette facilitates a vivid contrast, accentuated by randomly placed broken pixels, which represent the unpredictability of emotions. This abstract arrangement resonates with the seed's themes of order and chaos, embodying a quiet battle of feeling without explicit imagery.
