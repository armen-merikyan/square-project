Artist Statement

The emotional tone contrasts longing's distant warmth and resolve's compressed strength. The palette, inspired by 'mourning_renewal', combines deep, cool colors with pale hues, reflecting the tension between urgent desire and steady stability. The main collision occurs in the center, where patchwork longings interrupt the flame patterns of resolve, creating an intriguing balance of tension and release. The hard edges provide a striking definition to the flowing patterns, symbolizing the boundary between yearning and fulfillment.
