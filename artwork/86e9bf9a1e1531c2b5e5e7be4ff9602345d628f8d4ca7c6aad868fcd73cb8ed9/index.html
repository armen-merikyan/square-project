Artist Statement

This composition balances the emotional tones of 'quiet' and 'pressure'. The chosen palette features muted 'off_white' and 'dust_gray' that provide a tranquil surface, juxtaposed with the ‘compressed_red’ and ‘dark_navy’ representing deeper emotional pressure and intensity. The colors interact through uneven clusters, with 'quiet' tones flowing into the denser 'pressure' regions—creating a pattern where pixels seem to descend, mimicking falling droplets that signify both weight and release. The main collision occurs in the center, emphasizing chaos amidst stillness, inspired by the seed's themes of 'clarity_confusion'.
