Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tension between desire and emptiness, using a palette that reflects warmth and coolness. The rich tones of hot coral and blood orange clash with deep charcoal and hollow blue, illustrating a struggle between overwhelming desire and the restraint of nothingness. The main collision occurs in the corner, where vibrant colors overflow, contrasting with the muted gradient across the grid. This represents the push of overwhelming desire against a structured boundary of emptiness, creating a visual rhythm of pressure and release.
