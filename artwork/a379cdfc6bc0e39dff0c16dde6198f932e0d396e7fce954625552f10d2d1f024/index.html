Artist Statement

The emotional tone struck is one of tension (pressure) contrasted with serenity (quiet). The palette blends deep navy for density and off-white for soft expansiveness, creating a sense of conflict. The center builds density with darker shades, surrounded by off-white and gentle colors representing light airiness. The main collision occurs at the center, where sharp edges meet soft expanses, embodying the idea of islands of clarity amidst turbulent forces. This captures the struggle between pressure and the desire for quiet space, while utilizing broken pixels to introduce intentional chaos against the order.
