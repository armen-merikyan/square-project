Artist Statement

The emotional tone encapsulates a struggle between longing and resolve, depicted through the juxtaposition of warm and cool colors. The palette, derived from 'relief_dread', centers on muted yet rich colors: storm gray symbolizes uncertainty, pale mint signifies hope, and faded gold suggests warmth. The arrangement creates a visual echo of pressure and release, with flows cutting diagonally across the square. Main collisions are found where crosshatch patterns (resolve) intersect with rain patterns (longing), generating tension in the middle. Broken pixels enhance imperfections, illustrating the unpredictable nature of human emotions. The layout enforces a quiet channel, encouraging contemplation amidst chaos.
