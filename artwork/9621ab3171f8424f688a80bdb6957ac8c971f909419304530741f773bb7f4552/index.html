Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the tension between longing and resolve through a contrasting palette of muted creams and deep plums, suggesting a weight of emotions. The warm red-oxide accents break the quiet spaces, representing moments of urgency amidst serene backgrounds. The layout rises from the bottom, echoing the hopeful energy of overcoming challenges, while sharp vs. soft contrasts highlight the collision of emotions. The arrangement of broken pixels introduces intentional imperfection, symbolizing the remnants of repair against the backdrop of unresolved feelings.
