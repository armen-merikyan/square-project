Artist Statement

This abstract pixel art explores the emotional range found in the juxtaposition between pressure and calmness. The palette features deep, dark colors symbolizing compression (#00334E, #021E24) and muted tones representing quiet spaces (#BAC5D6, #F2F5F7). The bright accents (#FF6B6B, #FFEDB0) create collisions that suggest a vibrant tension among the muted areas. The arrangement uses contrasting colors to evoke the dynamics of rhythm and release, capturing the essence of emotional turbulence responding to stillness.
