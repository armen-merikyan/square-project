Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the chaos and release inherent in conflicting feelings. The palette uses dynamic contrasts of deep black and vibrant red against calming pale blue and crisp white to explore the duality of confusion and clarity. The left side embodies chaotic 'rain' patterns in dark shades, invoking pressure, while the right delivers a 'grid' of orderly light hues, symbolizing release. The broken pixels introduce imperfection, suggesting unresolved emotions amidst the structured calm.
