Artist Statement

The artwork captures the emotional tension between nostalgia and progress through a palette of burnt orange, dark red, charcoal, and gold. The upper half signifies nostalgia with radial patterns evoking warmth and memory, while the lower half represents progress through crack patterns suggesting growth and disruption. The main contrast occurs at the split between the two, where warm hues meet cooler, more muted tones, echoing a conflict within the transition and creating visual tension. Broken pixels introduce imperfection, hinting at the unresolved nature of the emotional journey.
