Artist Statement

This artwork reflects the emotional duality of tenderness and rage through a careful arrangement of stark contrasts and soft gradients. The black, deep reds, and pale blue represent explosive pressure, while muted greys serve as fields of quiet. The main collision occurs at the center, where harsh edges from rage abruptly blend into softer, rounded forms of tenderness. The layout evokes an open gate, suggesting an intrusion of contrasting emotions leading to a layered narrative visualized through asymmetric patterns and broken pixels, symbolizing disruptions and unresolved feelings.
