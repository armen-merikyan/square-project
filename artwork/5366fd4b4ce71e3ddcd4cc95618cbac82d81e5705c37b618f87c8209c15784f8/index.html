Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects the dichotomy of quiet and pressure, using a palette that shifts from soft to dark hues. The contrast between muted colors and sharp edges symbolizes the collision of calm and chaos. The main concentration of darker colors on the lower diagonal conveys a sense of weighted tension, while the lighter hues rise starkly against it in a suggestive diagonal movement, embodying the struggle between release and restraint.
