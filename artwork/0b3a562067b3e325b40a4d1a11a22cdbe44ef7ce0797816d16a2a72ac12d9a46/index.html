Artist Statement

The emotional tone embodies the tension between love and betrayal, revealed through an 8x8 pattern that juxtaposes warmth against shadow. The palette features deep reds and grays symbolizing love, decay, and conflict. The layout emphasizes a transition from a dense left side representing betrayal to a lighter right side symbolizing the warmth of love. Broken pixels reflect imperfection and disruption. The central diagonal flow creates an unresolved tension, capturing the dynamic of rising signals amid chaos, echoing the jagged reality between these contrasting emotions.
