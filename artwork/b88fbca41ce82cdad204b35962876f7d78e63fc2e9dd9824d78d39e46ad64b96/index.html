Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tug-of-war between longing and resolve. The chosen palette of melancholy wonder includes soft and warm hues that create a sense of depth and introspection. The saturated center is filled with rich colors, suggesting intensity and urgency, while the more muted edges represent a longing for serenity and calm. The broken pixels disrupt this ideal structure, signifying damage yet also repair, creating an echo of collision in the artwork that resonates with the core emotions of the seed.
