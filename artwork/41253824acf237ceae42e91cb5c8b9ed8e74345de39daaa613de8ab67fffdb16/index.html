Artist Statement

This piece explores the tension between confusion and clarity through a duality of color and pattern. The palette consists of stark black to symbolize confusion and pale blue for clarity, providing a visual tug-of-war. The broken symmetry pattern reflects the emotional shift, with dark, clustered areas breaking against lighter spaces, suggesting moments of clarity amidst chaos. The layout imitates falling pixels, reinforcing the sensation of descending into confusion but also creating a rhythm that offers hope through contrast. The irregularities, or 'broken pixels', enhance the sense of imperfection—giving depth to the experiences of rage and calm. This tension is echoed throughout the composition, creating a visual language that speaks to the emotional narrative of the seed.
