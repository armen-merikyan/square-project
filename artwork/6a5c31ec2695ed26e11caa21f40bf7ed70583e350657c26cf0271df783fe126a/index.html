Artist Statement

The emotional tone captures the duality of love and betrayal—warmth vs coldness drawn from the chosen palette. Colors like pink and red symbolize love's warmth, contrasted by darker grays that shadow betrayal. The composition utilizes a split pattern strategy, where soft shapes of pink and red resonate against the harsher edges of the darker hues. The main collision lies in the intersection of warm and cold—highlighting love's joy through vibrant reds, disrupted by dark shadows that invoke feelings of broken trust, creating an overall rhythmic asymmetry through the arrangement of pixel colors.
