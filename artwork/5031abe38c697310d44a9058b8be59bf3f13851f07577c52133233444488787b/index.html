Artist Statement

This pixel art embodies the conflicting emotions of defiance and submission through contrasting patterns and colors. The 'mourning_renewal' palette of deep blacks, blues, and hints of pale yellow captures the depths of grief and renewal. The central spiral diverges into a ripple of broken frames, representing defiance colliding with the enclosing field of submission. Sharp versus soft contrasts are evident, creating a sense of pressure that emphasizes the emotional struggle. Negative spaces are maintained to allow breath within the intensity, representing a delicate balance between resilience and vulnerability.
