Artist Statement

This artwork captures the juxtaposition of peace and restlessness through color and pattern. The palette features a gradient from deep olive to cream, conveying calmness while accentuating tension. Restless patterns reflect motion, while ripples symbolize calm. The central area reflects chaotic interactions, signaling the struggle between these two emotions, thereby creating visual rhythm and disturbance. Light and dark areas create depth, emphasizing the contrast between memory and motion.
