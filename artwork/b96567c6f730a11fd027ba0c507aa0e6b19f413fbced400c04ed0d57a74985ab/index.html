Artist Statement

This artwork captures the emotional tension between defiance and submission, represented through a contrasting palette of bold blacks and whites paired with muted grays and a striking orange accent. The central area showcases clusters of dark tones pushing against the quieter, lighter edges, creating a sense of both conflict and connection. The use of broken pixels suggests disruption and repair, embodied by the harsh diagonal patterns and uneven borders that serve as thresholds between chaos and order. Overall, the piece feels dynamic, filled with unresolved emotion but balanced by subtle moments of calm.
