Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional tension between vulnerability and strength, demonstrated through contrasting color choices and placement. The palette of hot coral, blood orange, dust pink, charcoal, and hollow blue represents the clash between exposure and shelter. Arranging the stronger colors at the periphery emphasizes resistance, while softer hues fill the central area, suggesting a vulnerable core. The broken pixel inclusion symbolizes the imperfections and scars of vulnerability, creating intentional disruptions. The layout reflects a scattered repair, with brighter pixels illuminating the path of resilience amidst the chaos.
