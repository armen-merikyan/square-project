Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a dynamic interplay between pressure and release, conveying a sense of unmoored connection amid chaos. The palette, a spectrum from deep blue to vibrant yellow and muted purple, encapsulates joy and grief, suggesting a push-pull of emotions. The arrangement of colors creates a contrast where dark and muted tones are periodically interrupted by bright accents, embodying collision and dynamics. The layout of falling pixels furthers the sense of cascading emotions, while the broken pixel elements introduce imperfection, enhancing the overall theme of unresolved tension.
