Artist Statement

This piece embodies the emotional tension between longing and resolve. The palette reflects intimacy and distance, using warm clay tones to evoke longing and cool blue shades to depict resolve. The main contrast occurs in the center, where 'pulse marks' collide with a 'frame' pattern, creating a dynamic interplay of emotion. The layout suggests an argument grid that captures the push and pull between these feelings, with broken pixels adding intentional disruptions to symbolize the scars of emotional struggle.
