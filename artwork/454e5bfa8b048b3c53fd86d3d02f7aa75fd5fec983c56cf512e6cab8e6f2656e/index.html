Artist Statement

This composition captures the emotional tension between 'spark' and 'ash,' juxtaposing vibrant and muted tones. The palette of soft gold, dark violet, and black evokes a sense of hope against desolation. The main contrast occurs diagonally, with a dense cluster of the spark hue in one corner disrupting the quieter ash tones, embodying the conflict of resilience and fragility. The broken pixels create intentional disruptions, mirroring the jagged edges and collision of memory versus erasure.
