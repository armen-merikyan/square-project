Artist Statement

The emotional tone reflects a tension between vulnerability and safety, represented by contrasting colors. The palette mixes gentle greens and warm browns to suggest a protective environment, intersected by pale and cold colors that illustrate exposure. The main collision occurs in the center, where lighter tones of pale_skin and cold_white blend, symbolizing the intersection of protection and exposure, creating an echoing effect. The layout fosters a sense of a hollow center surrounded by fluctuating color, imitating waves of pressure and release. Broken pixels introduce intentional disruptions, mimicking scars or imperfections in the emotional landscape.
