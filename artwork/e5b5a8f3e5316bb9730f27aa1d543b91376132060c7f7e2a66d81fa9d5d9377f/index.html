Artist Statement

This artwork embodies the emotional duality of awe and terror. The palette draws from deep navy and black to evoke a sense of solitude and introspection, while the bright dim cyan offers sharp contrast, creating a dynamic tension. The composition flows from a structured center, symbolizing order, extending outward into chaotic disruptions, reflecting the collisions of contrasting emotions. This arrangement prompts viewers to navigate a space of density interrupted by moments of relief, suggesting an emotional journey.
