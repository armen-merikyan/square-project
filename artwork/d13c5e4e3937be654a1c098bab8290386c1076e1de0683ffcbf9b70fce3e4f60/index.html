Artist Statement

This composition embodies the emotional tug-of-war between longing and resolve. The palette, drawn from 'melancholy_wonder,' uses soft cyan and lavender gray to evoke a sense of yearning, contrasted against the muted plum and rain blue that imply strength and clarity. Broken pixels throughout illustrate the scars of past damage, while the more vibrant colors form a path of potential repair, highlighting the interplay of hope and loss. The main collision occurs in the center, where longing's delicate rings intersect with the firmer split-screen resolve, creating tension that feels alive yet unsteady.
