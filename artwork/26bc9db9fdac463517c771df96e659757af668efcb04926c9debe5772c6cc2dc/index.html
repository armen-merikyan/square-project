Artist Statement

The emotional tone blends nostalgia and progress, juxtaposing feelings of warmth with stark contrasts. The selected palette of deep red, pale blue, stark black, and crisp white conveys a sense of tension and release. The sharp vs. soft contrast plays out horizontally—darker hues at the bottom symbolize a heavier past, while lighter colors above suggest hopeful progression. The pattern is generated by radial shapes for nostalgia, contrasting with dotted arrangements representing forward momentum. The layout reflects a nearly symmetrical division, interrupted by imperfections, embodying the struggle between stability and chaos, echoing the themes of rise vs. fall.
